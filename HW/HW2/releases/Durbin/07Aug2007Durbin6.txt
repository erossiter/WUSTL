     Durbin Statement on Flooding in Rockford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         8 7 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Statement on Flooding in Rockford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Tuesday  August 7  2007       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today expressed his concern for the residents of Rockford and surrounding areas    My thoughts and prayers are with the residents of Rockford who are dealing with power outages  flooded roads and evacuations  said Durbin  I have asked members of my staff to work with local government officials  community leaders and the state and federal emergency management agencies today and in the coming weeks     As the rain continues to fall  it is increasingly important that everyone is working together in a coordinated effort  My staff and I will do everything in our power to see that this happens in order to keep residents out of danger and to minimize damage to homes and businesses    Heavy rains that began on Monday  August 6 caused overnight power outages  flooded roads  home evacuations and property damage in Rockford  Illinois and surrounding areas in Winnebago County  The Rock River Valley received almost 7 inches of rain as of early Tuesday morning with the heaviest amount falling in Rockford and Belvidere  More rain is expected in the area on Tuesday evening and into Wednesday morning    Durbin  who is leading an official Senate delegation to Afghanistan  Pakistan  Iraq and Jordan  was alerted to the situation in Rockford via phone call earlier today
