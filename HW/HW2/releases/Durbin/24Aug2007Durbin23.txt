     Durbin Statement on Storm Damage in Chicago and Surrounding Suburbs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              8 24 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Statement on Storm Damage in Chicago and Surrounding Suburbs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Friday  August 24  2007        U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today expressed his concern for the residents of Chicago and surrounding areas    My thoughts are with the people of Chicago and surrounding suburbs who are dealing with massive amounts of debris  power outages  flooded roads and flight cancellations  said Durbin   Earlier today  I spoke with officials at the Illinois Emergency Management Agency to discuss the damage   They informed me that preliminary estimates show that about 400 500 homes in the greater Chicago region are affected by flooding   My staff and I will be working today  and in the coming weeks  with state and federal emergency management officials  as well as local and state officials  to address fallout from the storm
