     DURBIN  DEMOCRATS ANNOUNCE HONEST LEADERSHIP AND OPEN GOVERNMENT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             1 18 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  DEMOCRATS ANNOUNCE HONEST LEADERSHIP AND OPEN GOVERNMENT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wednesday  January 18  2006       Saying that the Federal government belongs to the American people and not to highly paid lobbyists representing special interests  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today joined Senate Democratic Leader Harry Reid  D NV   House Democratic Leader Nancy Pelosi  D CA  and more than 70 other Democrats to announce an aggressive new ethics and lobbying reform proposal   Democrats are coming forward with specific proposals  not vague notions  to restore honesty and integrity to Congress  It is a real reform initiative that addresses the abuses of power the American people have witnessed in the past ten years  Durbin said  Our reforms are aimed directly at the need for accountability in our government   The Democratic bill  known as the Honest Leadership and Open Government Act will increase accountability  enforce and strengthen ethics and lobbying rules  impose strict new limits on gifts and travel  and close the revolving door between Congress and K Street  to stop people at the highest levels of government from cashing in on government service   Specifically  the bill will   ban all gifts and travel from lobbyists  increase transparency on lobbyists by requiring additional disclosure  shut down pay to play schemes like the K Street Project  where lobbyists contributed to Republican campaigns in exchange for a chance to set the agenda  prevent dead of night provisions in legislation and require conference committee meetings to be open to the public   Powerful corporate special interests have controlled the agenda and the American people have paid the real price  hundreds of billions of dollars to Big Oil  HMOs  drug companies and special interest tax breaks     Democrats believe that the American people should be able to turn to their representatives in government when they need help and not to the highest paid lobbyists   We have a real opportunity and a responsibility to end the stranglehold of special interest on the Federal government   We intend to do just that  Durbin said   The legislation will also create an Office of Public Integrity to investigate lobbyist disclosures and suspicious activity and will put in place new criminal penalties for lobbyists who attempt to cover up unethical behavior similar to those committed by lobbyist Jack Abramoff   Yesterday  Durbin announced that his Senate office will voluntarily comply with the provisions in the Democratic legislation  including the gift and privately funded travel ban
