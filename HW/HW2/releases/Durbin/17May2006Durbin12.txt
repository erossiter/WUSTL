     DURBIN ASKS AMTRAK PRESIDENT TO MEET REGARDING ROCKFORD SERVICE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  5 17 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ASKS AMTRAK PRESIDENT TO MEET REGARDING ROCKFORD SERVICE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Wednesday  May 17  2006   WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today sent a letter to the Acting President of Amtrak  David Hughes  requesting a meeting between Amtrak officials and Rockford community leaders to discuss restoring Amtrak service to Rockford          The  24 million commitment from the State of Illinois and four new train sets from Amtrak have put Illinois Amtrak expansion on the right track     said Durbin      It   s time to take advantage of this momentum  get Rockford on board and bring Amtrak to the table   As the state   s third largest city  Rockford and its residents deserve to have convenient and affordable access to the city of Chicago and the nationwide Amtrak network          In the letter to Amtrak  Durbin noted that while    Amtrak ended its Black Hawk route from Chicago to Rockford in 1982    since that time  the amount of commuters that travel from Rockford to the Chicago area has increased significantly          Just last month  Durbin  a member of the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee  helped secure a commitment from Amtrak to supply four new trains for additional round   trip service on the three Illinois routes   The 2007 Illinois budget committed to finance the service for these trains by doubling their Amtrak funding to  24 3 million   As result of this state and federal cooperation  beginning this October  there will be new service on the Carbondale  Quincy and St  Louis lines       Text of letter appears below           David Hughes  President  National Passenger Railroad Corporation   Dear Mr  Hughes    I am writing to request a meeting between Amtrak officials and Rockford  Illinois area community leaders   This meeting would be held to discuss the possibility of restoring Amtrak service on the Chicago to Rockford corridor       As you know  Amtrak ended its Black Hawk route from Chicago to Rockford in 1982   However  since that time  the amount of commuters that travel from Rockford to the Chicago area has increased significantly even though public transportation options are severely limited   Reinstatement of the Chicago to Rockford Amtrak corridor would serve not only those commuters  but would serve the thousands of other people that currently seek to travel to Chicago but are unable to do so due to the lack of quick and reliable public transportation   Moreover  Chicago to Rockford service would also connect Rockford area residents to Amtrak   s nationwide network via Chicago   s vibrant Midwest Amtrak hub  thereby growing Amtrak   s ridership numbers significantly       I thank you for your recent willingness to work with State officials to provide train cars for expansion on its three intrastate Amtrak routes   This expansion is in response to Amtrak   s growing popularity in the state  as almost every route in the state of Illinois recorded a double digit percentage increase in 2005   Accordingly  I believe Amtrak expansion to Rockford would be well received in Rockford  Chicago  and communities throughout northeastern Illinois       I look forward to working with you to improve Amtrak service in Illinois and around the nation    I stand ready to assist you in facilitating a meeting between Amtrak and Rockford area officials       Sincerely   Richard J  Durbin  U S  Senator
