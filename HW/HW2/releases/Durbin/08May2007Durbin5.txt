     Durbin Announces  750 000 Dot Grant For Jet Bridge At Chicago Rockford International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     5 8 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces  750 000 Dot Grant For Jet Bridge At Chicago Rockford International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Tuesday  May 8  2007    WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  USDOT  has awarded a  750 000 grant to the Greater Rockford Airport Authority to allow for the installation of a jet bridge        Today s grant will help expand the capacity of the Chicago Rockford International Airport   The jet bridge will provide all weather dry access to aircraft and enhance the security of terminal operations   said Durbin    This improvement will go a long way towards ensuring that the Rockford airport continues to attract additional carriers and serve one of the region s premiere airports        A jet bridge is a moveable bridge  normally enclosed  which extends from an airport terminal gate allowing passengers to board an airplane without having to go outside  Prior to the introduction of jet bridges  passengers would normally board an aircraft by walking along the ground level tarmac and climbing a set of movable airstairs       According to the USDOT  the grant will provide federal funding for the acquisition and installation of a jet boarding bridge to serve the international terminal at the Chicago Rockford International Airport which includes any related terminal modifications       In 2005  Durbin secured nearly  4 million for the Greater Rockford Airport Authority including  950 000 for a separate jet bridge project
