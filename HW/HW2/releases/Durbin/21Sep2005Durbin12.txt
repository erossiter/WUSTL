     DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS CHAIRMAN AGREES TO INCLUDE DROUGHT ASSISTANCE IN UPCOMING RELIEF MEASURE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          9 21 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS CHAIRMAN AGREES TO INCLUDE DROUGHT ASSISTANCE IN UPCOMING RELIEF MEASURE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Wednesday  September 21  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin said today that Senator Thad Cochran  R MS   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Committee  has agreed to include drought assistance for farmers in upcoming federal disaster relief legislation       In an exchange on the Senate floor today  Durbin noted that this year has been difficult for farmers across the country      Hurricane Katrina has decimated production all throughout the Gulf Coast     said Durbin      In addition  we have had a terrible drought in the Midwest     in my home state of Illinois  in Missouri and in parts of Iowa and Minnesota   We   ve had the worst drought in 105 years in some parts of my state   Every county but one in Illinois has been designated a disaster area by the Secretary of Agriculture             I would like to ask the Senator from Mississippi to include agriculture losses incurred due to Hurricane Katrina and other natural disasters     including the drought in the Midwest     in the next Katrina supplemental appropriations bill     Durbin said       Senator Cochran agreed to work with Durbin and other Senators whose states have suffered agricultural losses to include relief in the next emergency supplemental appropriations bill      I have agreed to work with the Senator form Illinois and the Chairman of the Subcommittee to craft language and funding that would be approved by the Senate     Cochran said      And in that legislation  when it does come before the Senate  we   ll work together to ensure that an appropriate provision is included        Durbin noted that this month   s estimates by the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  finds the value of corn and soybean production in Illinois has been reduced by more than  792 million for 2005 compared to what would be expected under average growing conditions   USDA estimates released today estimate Hurricane Katrina related agriculture losses at nearly  900 million
