     DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING PROPOSED CUT TO AMTRAK FUNDING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        5 26 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING PROPOSED CUT TO AMTRAK FUNDING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Friday  May 26  2006    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  made the following statement regarding a vote in the House of Representatives last night to cut Amtrak federal funding by a third       In the same month that the State of Illinois doubled their funding commitment for Amtrak  the U S  House of Representatives proposed to cut federal funding by more than a third   The measure approved by the House panel last night matches President Bush s inadequate  900 million request for Amtrak   700 million short of what Amtrak needs to maintain routes and services           With the state   s plans to provide additional service on all three of Illinois    intrastate routes  full funding of Amtrak has never been more important   If Congress and the President  under fund Amtrak  money the state intends to use for improvements in service and expansion will go towards maintaining current service and compensating for the lack of adequate federal funds             Last year  Amtrak service in Illinois saw a major increase in ridership  This year  the state recognized the need for expansion and responded by offering to work with Amtrak to increase service to nearly 30 communities   The State   s commitment will provide for the first Midwest Amtrak expansion in almost 20 years and has even prompted proposals to reinstate service in large metropolitan areas  such as Rockford  IL   If the House and the President   s proposals are adopted  Illinois    Amtrak expansion will be stopped dead in its tracks           This Memorial Day weekend  with families across Illinois paying almost  3 dollar a gallon to travel  Congress needs to remember downsizing Amtrak means more traffic congestion  more pollution and fewer affordable travel choices for America   s families
