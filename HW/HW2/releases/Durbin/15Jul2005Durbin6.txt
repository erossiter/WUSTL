     SENATE APPROVES DURBIN AMENDMENT TO DESIGNATE MASCOUTAH   S MIDAMERICA AIRPORT AS A U S  PORT OF ENTRY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             7 15 2005                                                                                                                                                                            SENATE APPROVES DURBIN AMENDMENT TO DESIGNATE MASCOUTAH   S MIDAMERICA AIRPORT AS A U S  PORT OF ENTRY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Friday  July 15  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      An amendment offered by U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  to designate MidAmerica St  Louis Airport in Mascoutah  Illinois as a port of entry for international air cargo was unanimously approved by the Senate this week   Senator Durbin   s measure was an amendment to the fiscal year 2006 Homeland Security Appropriations bill  which passed the Senate Thursday night        Last year  I met with Congressman Jerry Costello and community leaders in St  Clair County     said Durbin      They made a very compelling case for MidAmerica Airport   s need for port of entry status and gave several recent examples of problems encountered at the airport on both passenger flights and freight shipments  due to MidAmerica   s lack of port of entry status   The Senate   s action will help move the airport and the community closer to that goal        MidAmerica St  Louis Airport is the civilian counterpart to Scott Air Force Base   MidAmerica is currently classified as a Foreign Trade Zone and is a finalist to be classified as an interior transshipment point for international air cargo     Durbin noted that international air cargo transport is presently non existent in the region   Granting MidAmerica port of entry status would give the airport a means to enhance the economy in the Metro East area        In addition to the homeland security benefits  a port of entry designation for MidAmerica would help spur economic development in the Metro East and St  Louis region     Durbin said      I look forward to working with Congressman Costello to ensure that this important provision remains in the bill when the Homeland Security Appropriations bill is considered by a joint House Senate conference committee in the coming weeks
