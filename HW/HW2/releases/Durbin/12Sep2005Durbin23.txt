STATEMENT REGARDING FEMA DIRECTOR   S RESIGNATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Monday  September 12  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the announcement that Michael Brown  Director of the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has resigned       By offering his resignation  Michael Brown did the right thing   Mr  Brown   s mismanagement of the federal response to Hurricane Katrina was well documented  and his resignation now gives us an opportunity to bring leadership to the task of relief and rebuilding             I call on President Bush to direct his Administration to do everything within their power to ensure the success of the ongoing relief effort and to ensure that the billions of dollars we have appropriated are spent responsibly   In appointing a new FEMA Director  the President must find a real professional to take the reins of America   s lead disaster relief agency
