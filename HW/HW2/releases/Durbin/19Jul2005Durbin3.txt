     DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS SUBCOMMITTEE APPROVES FUNDING FOR NEW COURTHOUSE IN ROCKFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      7 19 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS SUBCOMMITTEE APPROVES FUNDING FOR NEW COURTHOUSE IN ROCKFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  July 19  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today said a U S  Senate panel has approved  50 million in funding for construction of a new federal courthouse in Rockford   The funds will provide nearly all of the necessary funding to complete construction on the courthouse     Durbin is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee   The funding was included by the Transportation  Treasury and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Subcommittee today in the fiscal year 2006 spending bill        This funding will modernize the Rockford courthouse to meet the needs of the judicial system     Durbin said      Judges  law enforcement officers and the citizens of Rockford who depend on the courthouse have made a convincing case for this investment        The Rockford courthouse  which was built more than 25 years ago  is filled to capacity and has structural problems that would be prohibitively expensive to repair and impede efficient functioning of the court   In addition  the lack of a separate prisoner entrance to the building compromises security at the facility   Both the General Services Administration  GSA  and the U S  Judicial Conference  which determines policy for federal judges  have made construction of a new Rockford courthouse a priority   Funding for the project was authorized by Congress in 2000        I have worked closely with Congressman Don Manzullo to secure nearly  7 million in planning and pre construction funding for a new courthouse in Rockford  and we will work together to ensure that this  50 million for construction is included in the final appropriations bill this fall     Durbin said
