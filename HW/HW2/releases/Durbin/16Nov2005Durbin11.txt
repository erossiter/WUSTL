     DURBIN  MORE THAN  4 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS PROJECTS APPROVED BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          11 16 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  MORE THAN  4 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS PROJECTS APPROVED BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Wednesday  November 16  2005    WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   today announced that the Senate has approved  4 105 000 for projects in the state of Illinois in the Commerce Justice Science spending bill       The Fiscal Year  FY  2006 appropriations bill for the U S  Departments of Commerce  Justice and State includes funding for the following projects in Illinois      Department of Justice   Ceasefire  Chicago   950 000 for the Chicago Project for Violence Prevention to expand Ceasefire  an accelerated community based prevention  intervention  treatment and advocacy initiative   School Based Violence Prevention and Mentoring  Chicago   300 000 for Uhlich Children s Advantage Network to support its school based violence prevention  mentoring  leadership  and vocational training programs   Courthouse Security  Chicago    850 000 for the Cook County  Illinois Sheriff   s Office to increase security at court facilities countywide  including technology and law enforcement upgrades   Technology Upgrades  Mt  Vernon    250 000 for the Jefferson County  Illinois Sheriff   s Department to upgrade law enforcement and communications technologies and to modernize equipment   Springfield Police Department  Illinois    100 000 for acquisition and training for impairment screening devices   Public Allies  Chicago   125 000 for recruitment and leadership training for young men from at risk backgrounds   Kids Hope United  Illinois   150 000 for family preservation services for methamphetamine involved families   City Year  Chicago   200 000 for Young Heroes program  a leadership development and delinquency prevention program for middle school students   Methamphetamine Awareness Campaign  Illinois   180 000 for the Illinois Sheriff   s Association campaign to raise awareness of the drug and its long term effects   Department of Commerce   Shedd Aquarium  Chicago   250 000 to conduct Great Lakes conservation education activities  National Oceanic   Atmospheric Administration   NASA   Henry Crown Space Center  Chicago   750 000 for the development of science based educational programming associated with the Henry Crown Space Center at Chicago   s Museum of Science and Industry   Durbin worked with other Members of the Illinois Congressional Delegation on this project
