     Durbin Welcomes Tuskegee Airmen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  3 29 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Welcomes Tuskegee Airmen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Thursday  March 29  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin today hosted a reception to honor 11 Illinois members of the Tuskegee Airmen in his Capitol office in D C   The reception followed a ceremony in the United States Capitol Rotunda at which the Tuskegee Airmen received the Congressional Gold Medal  the nation s highest civilian honor        Despite facing discrimination  the Tuskegee Airmen volunteered to dedicate their lives to serving our country   said Durbin    The Tuskegee Airmen did more than just fly planes  they paved the way for millions of people of color  They are American heroes in every sense of the word        The Tuskegee Airmen were the military s first black pilots  Their exemplary performance overturned racist assumptions about the abilities of African Americans and ultimately led to the integration of the military   Illinois members of the Tuskegee Airmen were a driving force behind the creation of the National Organization of Tuskegee Airmen  which serves to honor and perpetuate the accomplishments of the group  introduce youth to aviation  and provide annual scholarships to deserving individuals  The Tuskegee Airmen are the largest group ever to be awarded the Congressional Gold Medal       Honored at today s reception were  Charles McGee of Chicago  Beverly Dunjill of Chicago  Juilius Echols of Chicago  William Loving of Glencoe  John Lyle of Chicago  Robert Martin  Sr  of Chicago  John Rogers  Sr  of Chicago  Laverne Shelton of Chicago  George Taylor of Chicago  Shelby Westbrook of Chicago  and Oscar Wilkerson  Jr  of Markham
