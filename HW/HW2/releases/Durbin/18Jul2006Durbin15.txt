     DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS SUBCOMMITTEE APPROVES MORE THAN  46 MILLION FOR NEW COURTHOUSE IN ROCKFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        7 18 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS SUBCOMMITTEE APPROVES MORE THAN  46 MILLION FOR NEW COURTHOUSE IN ROCKFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Tuesday  July 18  2006   WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today said a U S  Senate panel has approved an additional  46 227 million in funding for construction of the new federal courthouse in Rockford   Today   s amount represents the last piece of funding needed to complete the project   The funding was included at Durbin s request in the fiscal year 2007 spending bill by the Transportation  Treasury and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Subcommittee of which Durbin is a member          We have secured the final necessary funding in today   s Senate subcommittee meeting and can look forward to breaking ground for the courthouse this fall     said Durbin      Congressman Manzullo and I have teamed up on every step of this project so far  and he and I will be working to keep the full  46 227 million in the bill as it goes to conference   The new federal courthouse in Rockford will be a source of pride for the entire community   one that will be secure  efficient and meet the needs of the judicial system          Durbin secured  34 5 million in the Senate spending bill last year and  6 9 million in earlier spending bills   The U S  General Services Administration  GSA   which oversees federal construction projects  estimated last year that only  34 million more would be needed to complete funding for the project   Earlier this month  GSA reported that due to a rising building material costs  especially the cost of steel  the project would cost an additional  12 million dollars in order to complete the job   These funds were approved by the Senate Transportation Subcommittee today   The total cost of the project is now  87 66 million       Senator Durbin has worked with Congressman Don Manzullo  D IL  to secure funds for the new Rockford Courthouse since the project   s inception
