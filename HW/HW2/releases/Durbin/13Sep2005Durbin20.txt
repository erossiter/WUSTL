     DURBIN AND OBAMA ANNOUNCE  2 6 MILLION DOT GRANT FOR DECATUR AIRPORT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             9 13 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN AND OBAMA ANNOUNCE  2 6 MILLION DOT GRANT FOR DECATUR AIRPORT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Tuesday  September 13  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senators Dick Durbin  D IL  and Barack Obama  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  2 641 816 in federal funding to the Decatur Airport   The funds will be used for runway improvements  specifically the widening of taxiways  to meet the needs of aircraft taking off and landing at the airport        Illinois airports are vital arteries that support our economy and national defense     Durbin said      This grant to Decatur will help the airport serve its customers and carriers effectively           This grant will help ensure that the Decatur Airport operates safely and efficiently     said Obama
