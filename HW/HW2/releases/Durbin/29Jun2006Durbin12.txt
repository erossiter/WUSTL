     DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE APPROVES  2 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS BRIDGE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 6 29 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN   SENATE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE APPROVES  2 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS BRIDGE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  June 29  2006   WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today said that the Senate Appropriations Committee  of which he is a member  has approved  2 million for an Illinois bridge project       The Fiscal Year 2007 appropriations bill for the Department of Homeland Security includes funding for the following project      U S  Coast Guard     EJ Railroad Bridge  Morris    2 million to improve the EJ Bridge on the Illinois River  near Morris  IL   To date   12 million has been appropriated for the EJ Bridge  which is hit by marine vessels more often than any other bridge in America   Repair of the bridge is a priority for the United States Coast Guard
