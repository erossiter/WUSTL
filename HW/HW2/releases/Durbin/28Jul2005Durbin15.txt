STATEMENT REGARDING ILLINOIS DROUGHT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  July 28  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement after his meeting with U S  Secretary of Agriculture Mike Johanns  Senator Barack Obama  and Illinois Director of Agriculture Chuck Hartke regarding the severe drought affecting Illinois       Today  I thanked Secretary Johanns for designating the majority of Illinois counties as agriculture disaster areas   USDA   s decision comes at an important time     the period from March to June of this year was the third driest in Illinois in more than 100 years             But the disaster designation is only a first step   If USDA does not devote sufficient funds and efforts to addressing the drought  I am ready to offer legislation to direct any additional funds to Illinois that may be necessary             Last week  I invited Secretary Johanns to Illinois so that he may see the affected farms and communities first hand   I look forward to working with him and USDA to deliver relief to Illinois farm families
