     SENS  DURBIN AND OBAMA   CONGRESSMEN COSTELLO AND SHIMKUS ASK FEMA TO EXTEND FEDERAL DISASTER ASSISTANCE TO 10 COUNTIES IN SOUTHWESTERN AND CENTRAL ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     1 9 2007                                                                                                                                                                            SENS  DURBIN AND OBAMA   CONGRESSMEN COSTELLO AND SHIMKUS ASK FEMA TO EXTEND FEDERAL DISASTER ASSISTANCE TO 10 COUNTIES IN SOUTHWESTERN AND CENTRAL ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Tuesday  January 9  2007    WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senators Dick Durbin  D IL   Barack Obama  D IL  and Congressmen Jerry Costello  D IL  and John Shimkus  R IL  sent a letter late last night to FEMA director  David Paulison  requesting that President Bush extend federal disaster assistance to 10 counties in Southwestern and Central Illinois after a severe winter storm involving freezing rain  heavy snow and high winds caused extensive property damage and disrupted public services throughout Illinois in December of 2006       The severe winter weather system hit on November 30  and continued through December 1  2006   It resulted in record or near record snowfall in some counties and extraordinary ice formation on roads  electric power distribution systems  trees and other structures in many other counties in the State of Illinois  The storm disrupted power to hundreds of thousands of households and businesses throughout the stricken areas       Federal disaster assistance was provided to 26 counties after members of the Illinois delegation sent a letter to the President on December 7  2006   In the letter  members of the Illinois delegation supported Illinois Governor Rod Blagojevich s request for federal disaster assistance   In the letter sent last night  the members asked that the same federal assistance be provided to ten additional counties  including Madison  Monroe  St  Clair  Bond  Christian  Greene  Jersey  Montgomery  Sangamon  and Shelby Counties       Text of letter below            January 8  2007   The Honorable R  David Paulison  Director  Federal Emergency Management Agency  500 C Street S W   Washington  DC 20472   Dear Director Paulison    As you continue to evaluate the request from Illinois Governor Rod Blagojevich for federal emergency disaster assistance for the State of Illinois due to the severe winter storms that occurred November 30  2006 to December 1  2006  we respectfully request that you give strong consideration to Madison  Monroe  St  Clair  Bond  Christian  Greene  Jersey  Montgomery  Sangamon  and Shelby counties       As you are aware  President Bush recently issued an emergency declaration for 26 counties in Illinois   However  none of the counties in Southern Illinois received a declaration   Thousands of homes and businesses were without power in this region and an emergency declaration is needed to assist the people and governments to successfully rebuild and recover from this natural disaster   As a result of extraordinary ice formation during these winter storms  many of these counties in Southern Illinois continue to struggle with the costs of ongoing debris removal       We look forward to hearing from you in the near future   Please let us know if we can provide additional information or be of assistance in any other way       Sincerely    Richard J  Durbin                                                                       United States Senator   Barack Obama  United States Senator   Jerry Costello                                                                            United States Representative   John Shimkus  United States Representative
