     Durbin Announces Nearly  12 Million in DOT Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   5 11 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  12 Million in DOT Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  May 11  2007   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  11 707 054 in grants to transportation sites in the state of Illinois      Department of Transportation   Federal Transit Administration  Rockford Mass Transit District   95 179 for continuation of alternatives analysis along planned Metra Union Pacific West Line extension from Elgin to Rockford   Rockford Bus and Bus Facilities   75 000 to purchase a paratransit vehicle    Federal Aviation Administration  Chicago Rockford International Airport  Rockford   2 204 974 in funding for the reconstruction of Taxiways   This project will restore structural integrity to the pavements thereby enhancing safety at the airport   Chicago Midway International Airport  Chicago   5 706 849 in funding for the construction of a portion of the public use area of the new passenger terminal concourse area located on the west side of Cicero Avenue   Quad City International Airport  Moline Rock Island   1 760 000 in funding for a 500 foot runway extension  The project will provide for a runway length that meets the need of aircraft currently utilizing the airport   Chicago O Hare International Airport  Chicago   1 865 052 in funding for services to be performed in support of the Environmental Impact Statement  EIS  processes necessary for the O Hare Modernization Program
