BUSH TO SHOW HIS COMPASSIONATE CONSERVATISM BY SUPPORTING EMBRYONIC STEM CELL RESEARCH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Monday  July 17  2006   Washington  D C       On the first day that the Senate began debating legislation to expand the federal policy concerning embryonic stem cell research  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today called on the president to show his compassion by supporting this promising medical research          The President has told the American people that he is a compassionate conservative  but the issue of embryonic stem cell research will test his compassion   Vetoing life saving research would say to millions of Americans suffering from Parkinson   s  diabetes  Alzheimer   s and spinal cord injuries that their hopes are diminished so long as President Bush is in the White House     Durbin said          Many Democrats and Republicans in the Senate agree that research using stem cells that would be destroyed is about breakthrough science  strong ethical guidelines and saving the lives of millions of Americans  I am hopeful that the Senate will pass this bill tomorrow and that the President will realize the scientific realties and hope of stem cell research     Durbin added
