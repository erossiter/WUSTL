     DURBIN TO CHAIR NEW SUBCOMMITTEE THAT WILL OVERSEE TREASURY DEPARTMENT AND FINANCIAL MARKETS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     1 11 2007                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN TO CHAIR NEW SUBCOMMITTEE THAT WILL OVERSEE TREASURY DEPARTMENT AND FINANCIAL MARKETS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Thursday  January 11  2007    WASHINGTON  DC      A restructuring of the Senate Appropriations Committee will place U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  in the position of overseeing much of the funding for the nation s financial services  as well as oversight of the U S  Treasury Department   The new subcommittee  announced by Senate Appropriations Chairman Senator Robert C  Byrd  D  WV   will be known as the Financial Services and General Government Subcommittee       Durbin will be the Chairman of the new subcommittee  which will have jurisdiction over the U S  Treasury Department  Internal Revenue Service  the Federal Trade Commission  the Securities and Exchange Commission  the Small Business Administration  the Commodities Futures Trading Commission  GSA  the U S  Mint and the Bureau of Printing and Engraving among others   Senator Sam Brownback  R KS  will serve as Ranking Member of the subcommittee       The subcommittee will also have jurisdiction over the federal courts system  including the issue of judicial security which Durbin has worked on since the tragic shooting of the family of a federal judge in Chicago two years ago       Additionally  the subcommittee will have jurisdiction over the Commodity Futures Trading Commission  CFTC  which regulates both the Chicago Mercantile Exchange and the Chicago Board of Trade       Durbin has served on the Appropriations Committees in the House and Senate for the past 20 years
