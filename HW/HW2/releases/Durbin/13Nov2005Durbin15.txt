                                                                                                                                                                                                  Sunday  November 13  2005    ROCKFORD  IL     Today  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  made the following statement regarding United Airlines service from Rockford to Denver       Rockford   It   s Time to Fly          As I   ve often mentioned  there are six words you never want to hear if you re the owner of a company and you   re trying to decide where to open a new business     You can   t get there from here      Those are six words you won   t ever hear about Rockford   United Airlines is coming to town and the sky over this city is looking friendlier by the minute          The Chicago Rockford International Airport has gone from one of the state   s most underutilized resources to one of Illinois    fastest growing airports   Two years ago  Rockford had no passenger air service   With today   s announcement  six airlines service Rockford   The quantum leap in service means 2006 is on track to be a very good year for Rockford          The persistence of Chairman of the Board of Commissioners of the Greater Rockford Airport Authority Mike Dunn and the countless others who have worked for this service has certainly paid off          Over the course of the past year  Durbin set up meetings between community and airline officials and personally encouraged United Airlines CEO Glenn Tilton to enter the Rockford market
