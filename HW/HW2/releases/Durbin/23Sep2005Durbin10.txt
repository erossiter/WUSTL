     DURBIN STATEMENT ON THE RESIGNATION OF FDA COMMISSIONER CRAWFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 9 23 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT ON THE RESIGNATION OF FDA COMMISSIONER CRAWFORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  September 23  2005    Chicago  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  issued the following statement on the resignation of Food and Drug Administration  FDA  Commissioner Lester Crawford       The Food and Drug Administration is facing nothing short of a crisis in leadership  Dr  Crawford has resigned only two months into his term and after two years as interim Commissioner             The FDA is responsible for the safety of foods  drugs  medical devices and thousands of other products American families count on  It regulates one quarter of the U S  economy             The controversy surrounding Vioxx and other pharmaceuticals has exposed weak oversight  conflict of interest and poor management at FDA             The Bush Administration cannot discount the critical importance of leadership at the FDA   There has not been a permanent chief at the FDA for two thirds of President Bush   s time in office  Now  more than ever  the President must name a trusted professional to head the FDA who is not beholden to the companies it regulates and has the strength to put the health and safety of American families ahead of any special interest group
