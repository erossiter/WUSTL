STATEMENT REGARDING PRESIDENT BUSH SIGNING FEDERAL TRANSPORTATION BILL IN AURORA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Wednesday  August 10  2005    AURORA  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding President Bush   s visit to Aurora to sign the Safe  Accountable  and Efficient Transportation Equity Act  A Legacy for Users  SAFETEA LU        Thankfully  we have reached the end of this very long road   After years of delays  Congress has agreed to  and the President has signed  the federal transportation bill   This legislation is a critical step toward funding our nation   s transportation needs and creating jobs in a troubled economy             Illinois will now receive more than  6 billion over the next five years from the bill   s highway formula    a 33 percent increase over our state   s funding in the last transportation bill   This transportation bill also increases funding for Illinois mass transit by 128 percent             With these additional funds  the Illinois Department of Transportation will be able to move forward on major construction projects throughout the state  and thousands of new Illinois jobs will be created in the process             Senator Obama helped write this bill and was able to ensure that Illinois received its fair share of highway and transit funding   I was pleased to work with him  Speaker Hastert  and our House colleagues to deliver a transportation bill that will move our state forward    addressing critical highway  bridge  and mass transit needs
