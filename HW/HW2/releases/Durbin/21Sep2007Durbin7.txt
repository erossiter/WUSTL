     Durbin Announces Nearly  6 7 Million In DOC Grants For Illinois Economic Development Districts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   9 21 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  6 7 Million In DOC Grants For Illinois Economic Development Districts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  September 21  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Commerce  DOC  has awarded  6 685 000 in grants to five Economic Development Districts in Illinois  According to the DOCs Economic Development Administration  the funding will be used to establish an economic development planning framework  process and strategy that supports private capital investment and job creation in the region  This is designed to bring together the public and private sectors in the creation of an economic development roadmap to diversify and strengthen the regional economy  Districts receiving funding include   City of Olney  Olney   1 825 000 in funding for the construction of an access road  water tower  sewer systems and a rail spur extension to accommodate the expansion at the Eastside Industrial Park in Richland County  These improvements will likely create 175 jobs and  17 million in private investment    Pembroke Township  Hopkins Park   1 600 000 in funding for the renovation and expansion of an existing facility for use as a small business incubator in Kankakee County  The funding will also be used for the construction of an adjoining new warehouse facility  These improvements are expected to enhance the economic viability of the area and by fostering the creation of new businesses  approximately 110 new jobs and  190 000 in private investment    City of Moline  Moline   1 060 000 in funding for multiple infrastructure improvements to the RiverTech Business Park to promote the creation of approximately 100 jobs and  21 5 million in private investment in Rock Island County  These improvements are intended to contribute to the economic diversity of the Quad Cities region    City of Marion  Marion   2 200 000 in funding for a water system improvement project performed in conjunction with the Rend Lake Conservancy District in Williamson County  This funding will support the construction of a water main and storage tank to assure sufficient water capacity to accommodate industrial and commercial growth  These improvements will likely save 2 600 jobs while creating an additional 1 500 jobs
