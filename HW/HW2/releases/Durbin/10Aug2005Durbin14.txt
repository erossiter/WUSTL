     DURBIN ANNOUNCES  350 000 FOR BENEDICTINE UNIVERSITY SCIENCE LAB                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 8 10 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ANNOUNCES  350 000 FOR BENEDICTINE UNIVERSITY SCIENCE LAB                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Wednesday  August 10  2005    LISLE  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced he has included  350 000 in federal funding in the Fiscal Year 2006 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill for the science lab at Benedictine University   The next steps in the funding process include approval by the full Senate and House Senate Conference Committee   Final action on the bill is anticipated this fall        The funding I   m working to secure will help Benedictine University modernize it   s labs by acquiring the next generation of research and development instrumentation     Durbin said      Modern equipment has a direct effect on a school   s ability to prepare students for the workplace and doctoral level education        In recent history  Benedictine University has received acclaim as a top notch school that graduates high quality scientists   many of whom have gone on to make substantial contributions in the fields of medicine  science and engineering     The funding will allow the university to purchase laboratory and research equipment for various biology and physics projects     Before making the funding announcement  Durbin met with the 21 students from the U S  Department of State   s Middle East Partnership Initiative   The students are high school leaders from four Middle Eastern countries who are studying U S  government and public policy over a seven week period this summer        I hope they will return home with a greater understanding of what democracy in action is all about     Durbin said      These students may very well be the next generation of leaders in their respective countries
