     DURBIN ANNOUNCES  100 000 FOR ST  MARY   S GOOD SAMARITAN HOSPITAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 8 25 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ANNOUNCES  100 000 FOR ST  MARY   S GOOD SAMARITAN HOSPITAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  August 25  2006    MOUNT VERNON  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced  100 000 in federal funding has been included in a Senate appropriations bill to help St  Mary   s Good Samaritan Hospital acquire new surgical technology        Modern equipment has a direct effect on a hospital   s ability to provide top notch care to its patients   This funding helps Good Samaritan Hospital take the first steps toward acquiring the next generation of technology needed to modernize its operating room     Durbin said     The new surgical system the hospital is working to acquire will promote minimally invasive surgery   The project  which is still in its early stages  is aimed at reducing postoperative pain  shortening hospital stays  lowering the risk of infection and allowing for faster recovery     The technology has many applications and has been used in urology  cardiothoracic surgery  general surgery  and gynecology   The surgical system would be the only one of its kind in Southern Illinois     Durbin secured the funding in the fiscal year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services Departments Appropriations bill   The full Senate is expected to approve the bill when Congress reconvenes in September
