    3 3 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN MOVES BILL NAMING POST OFFICE FOR ROBERT T  FERGUSON THROUGH SENATE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Friday  March 3  2006    WASHINGTON  DC   The U S  Senate today passed legislation championed by U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  in the Senate and U S  Congressman John Shimkus  R IL  in the House to name the post office  located at 312 East North Avenue in Flora  IL  the    Robert T  Ferguson Post Office Building      The measure was previously approved by the House  so today   s Senate action means the bill is now ready for signature by the President          Mr  Ferguson was a distinguished public servant who worked tirelessly on behalf of postal workers in Southern Illinois     said Durbin      There is no more fitting way to permanently and publicly recognize Robert Ferguson   s work than to name the Flora  Illinois post office in his honor   With the passage of this bill  we commemorate Mr  Ferguson   s exemplary service to the Flora community and to postal workers and patrons throughout Southern Illinois          In addition  Durbin supported two other bills passed by the Senate today naming Illinois post offices  including the    John F  Whiteside Joliet Post Office Building    at 2000 McDonough Street in Joliet  Illinois  and the  J M  Dietrich Northeast Annex    at 1927 Sangamon Avenue in Springfield  Illinois
