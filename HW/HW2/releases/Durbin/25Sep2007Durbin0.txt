Congratulates SIUE on 50th Anniversary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Tuesday  September 25  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced the Senate passage of a resolution late last night congratulating Southern Illinois University Edwardsville  SIUE  as it celebrates its 50th anniversary with a yearlong celebration beginning September 24  I have seen some truly exceptional and innovative educational programs taking place on the campus of Southern Illinois University Edwardsville  said Durbin  What started as a small university of 1 776 has grown into diverse student population representing 101 Illinois counties  43 other states and 46 nations  The University has become a tremendous asset to students and citizens of Illinois and I look forward to working with them for years to come  SIUE is a public university built by the people of Illinois for the people of Illinois in response to the clear need for a campus of higher education in the Metro East area of greater St  Louis  Since the creation of the University  the number of college graduates in Madison and St  Clair counties has risen from three percent to twenty percent  largely made up of SIUE graduates  These graduates give back to the community every day and the highly educated  skilled workforce they form is one of the greatest resources in Southern Illinois  In 2007  the Universitys Senior Assignment Program  an integrative learning experience required of all seniors  was ranked as a national model for learning assessment by the Association of American Colleges and Universities  The SIU School of Dental Medicine  the only Illinois dental school outside Cook County  is rated among the top dental schools in the nation on national board dental exams and serves as a primary oral healthcare provider for Southern Illinois  The Universities commitment extends beyond its students and into the community through innovative programs and investment  SIUE continues to serve the community that initiated its founding and has helped improve the quality of like for all citizens of the area  Its East St  Louis Center provides social services to families in East St  Louis and surrounding urban communities  Each year  more than 8 000 individuals benefit from the programs and services housed there
