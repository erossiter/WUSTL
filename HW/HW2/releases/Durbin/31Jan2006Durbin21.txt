STATEMENT ON DR  BERNANKE S CONFIRMATION AS FEDERAL RESERVE CHAIRMAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  January 31  2006           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   who met earlier today with Dr  Ben Bernanke  issued the following statement after he was confirmed by the Senate to head the Federal Reserve        By confirming Dr  Ben Bernanke to be the next Chairman of the Federal Reserve  the Senate has put a qualified and respected economist in charge of our monetary policies   I was pleased to vote for his confirmation           As Chair of the Federal Reserve  Dr  Bernanke   s challenge will be to maintain a growing economy at a time when our nation faces record long term debt  increased competition from countries like China and vulnerabilities in our pension systems
