     DURBIN SECURES  75 000 IN FEDERAL FUNDING FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN DUQUOIN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    7 7 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN SECURES  75 000 IN FEDERAL FUNDING FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN DUQUOIN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Friday  July 7  2006   DUQUOIN  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin announced  75 000 in federal funding for the economic development in DuQuoin   The funding was used to extend Bob Greene Drive to Route 51   providing an entrance to the new Best Western Fairground Inn   and to bring sewer and power lines across Route 51 to the hotel     The funding was included as part of the United States Department of Agriculture   s Rural Business Enterprise Grant program     Durbin believes the improvements will aid economic development in the DuQuoin area        The infrastructure projects completed with this funding will have long term economic benefits for the region     Durbin said      The hotel will attract people planning to attend events at the adjacent Southern Illinois Center or nearby DuQuoin State Fairgrounds   By bringing new jobs to the area and more potential customers to area businesses  the hotel strengthens economic development opportunities throughout the region        The hotel has created 12 full time jobs and four part time jobs   The 100 room Best Western  which opened on June 1  has bookings through October and is fully booked for this weekend   s events at the new Sparta World Shooting and Recreational Complex     The Southern Illinois Center is a 5 000 seat entertainment complex   The DuQuoin State Fairgrounds host events year round on its 600 acre site
