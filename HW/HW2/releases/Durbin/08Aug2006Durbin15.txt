     DURBIN  LAHOOD INTRODUCE BILL TO OFFER PROTECTION FOR LEBANESE PEOPLE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            8 8 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  LAHOOD INTRODUCE BILL TO OFFER PROTECTION FOR LEBANESE PEOPLE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Tuesday  August 8  2006    PEORIA  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and Congressman Ray LaHood  R IL  met with members of the local Lebanese community on Tuesday to discuss the Lebanese Temporary Protected Status Act of 2006   Durbin introduced the bi partisan bill in the Senate last week in response to the crisis in Lebanon and LaHood plans to offer similar legislation in the House in September   The bill would make Lebanon eligible for temporary protected status  TPS  for an initial twelve month period and would allow Lebanese nationals currently in the United States to remain here because ongoing hostilities make it unsafe for them to return to Lebanon        Innocent civilians are bearing the brunt of Hezbullah   s provocative actions     Durbin said      At this delicate moment in U S  Arab relations  giving temporary protected status to Lebanon will send a positive signal about U S  concern for the suffering of innocent Lebanese civilians  This is an affirmative step that Congress can  and should  take to alleviate the humanitarian crisis in Lebanon
