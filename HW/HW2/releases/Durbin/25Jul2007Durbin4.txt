     Durbin Announces Over  1 8 Million in DOJ Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    7 25 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Over  1 8 Million in DOJ Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Wednesday  July 25  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice  DOJ  has awarded  1 827 100 in grants for justice programs in Illinois  Office of Justice Programs  Juvenile Accountability  Chicago   1 477 100 in funding for the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority to reduce juvenile offending through accountability based programs focused on both the juvenile offender and the juvenile justice system  This funding is part of the Juvenile Accountability Block Grant  JABG  program  Enforcing Underage Drinking Laws  Chicago   350 000 in funding for the Illinois Department of Human Services to enforce laws prohibiting the sale and consumption of alcoholic beverages by minors   individuals under 21 years of age  This funding is part of the 2007 Enforcing Underage Drinking Laws  EUDL  block grant program
