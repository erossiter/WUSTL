     Durbin  Emanuel Call on BP to Publicly Commit to Protecting the Great Lakes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      10 9 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin  Emanuel Call on BP to Publicly Commit to Protecting the Great Lakes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  October 9  2007       Following their meeting with President of BP America Robert A  Malone in early September  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and U S  Representative Rahm Emanuel  D IL  sent a letter calling on BP to advertise its intention to operate under the old dumping permit as public commitment to protecting the Great Lakes  Today  Emanuel and Durbin reiterated their call for BP to publicly commit to the Great Lakes  The text of the letter appears below  Dear Mr  Malone  We write to follow up on our letter of September 12  2007  regarding our September meeting on BPs decision to stay within the old wastewater effluent permit limits as you move forward with the expansion of the Whiting  Indiana refinery  We have not received a response from your office and want to reiterate our recommendation that BP communicates clearly to the public its intention to operate under the limitations of the previous permit  Just as your company ran full page ads explaining your position prior to this decision  we hope that a public expression of BPs commitment to protecting the Great Lakes will be forthcoming  As we discussed in our meeting and mentioned in our previous letter  since your company does not intend to amend the NPDES permit or otherwise codify plans to operate under the previous permit  we urge you to consider a public declaration to show BPs public commitment to protecting the Great Lakes  We look forward to hearing from you soon  and we hope to soon see a public disclosure of BPs commitment to its plan for containing pollution from the Whiting facility and protecting the Great Lakes  Sincerely  Richard J  Durbin  U S  Senator Rahm Emanuel  Member of Congress
