    3 8 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN TELLS HOMELAND SECURITY SECRETARY CHERTOFF AMERICANS WANT ASSURANCES THAT GOVERNMENT CAN RESPOND TO NEXT MAJOR DISASTER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Wednesday  March 8  2006       With hurricane season fast approaching  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today told Homeland Security Secretary Michael Chertoff that Americans want assurances that the federal government will be able to respond to the next major disaster   We need to hear what America is prepared to do to help these members of America s family  who are still suffering six months after Katrina  We need to ensure that the catastrophe within the catastrophe    the preparation and response to the hurricane    never happens again  Durbin said during a Senate Appropriations Committee hearing on the Presidents request for emergency funding for recovery efforts in the Gulf Coast   Durbins opening statement  as delivered  is below     Secretary Chertoff  on September 3rd  six days after the briefing from Max Mayfield of the National Hurricane Center  you and several top officials in the Bush Administration held a press conference in Washington to tell America and the world what had been done and what would be done to respond to Hurricane Katrina   At the press conference you said  The United States  as the president has said  is going to move Heaven and Earth to rescue  feed  shelter and restore the life and health of the people who are currently suffering   Mr  Secretary  six months later  many of Katrinas victims are still suffering  still without homes  still without jobs  still without basic health care   All of them and all of us are wondering when this nightmare is going to end   Not only has our government and FEMA failed to move Heaven and Earth  we havent been able to move FEMA trailers to the right location   There are many unmet needs of Katrina survivors  not only in that region but now those scattered across the country   We need to hear what America is prepared to do to help these members of America s family  who are still suffering six months after Katrina  We need to ensure that the catastrophe within the catastrophe    the preparation and response to the hurricane    never happens again   We knew before Katrina hit that it was going to cause massive damage   We now know that the president  that you  that others in the administration were warned in advance by the director of National Hurricane Center that Katrina would do massive damage to the Gulf Coast and quite possibly to the levees protecting New Orleans   We ve all seen the videotape   Director Max Mayfield said during a videoconference on August 28th  I don t think any model can tell you with any confidence right now whether the levees will be topped or not  but that s obviously a very  very grave concern   Despite that explicit warning  Administration officials from the president on down repeatedly insisted in the days after Katrina that no one anticipated the breach of the levees   Instead of indignation and determination from this Administration  too often we sense resignation and more bureaucratic doubletalk   We need serious and experienced emergency managers who listen to the warnings of their professional staff and act on those warnings   With the start of a new hurricane season only a few weeks away and the continued threat of a terrorist attack  we are told that those threats are always with us   We don t have a day to waste
