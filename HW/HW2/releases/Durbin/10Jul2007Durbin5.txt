     Durbin  Senate Appropriations Subcommittee Approves More Than  58 Million for New Courthouse in Rockford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         7 10 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin  Senate Appropriations Subcommittee Approves More Than  58 Million for New Courthouse in Rockford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Tuesday  July 10  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced that the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Financial Services and General Government has approved an additional  58 792 million in funding for construction of the new federal courthouse in Rockford  Todays amount represents the last portion of funding needed to complete the project  In the appropriations bill approved today  we have included the final funding needed to move the Rockford Courthouse project forward  I believe that by the end of the week  this provision will be approved by the full Appropriations Committee and will be headed to the Senate floor for a vote  said Durbin  Congressman Manzullo and I have teamed up on every step of this project so far  and he and I will be working to keep the full  58 792 million in the bill as it advances  The new federal courthouse in Rockford will be a source of pride for the entire community   one that will be secure  efficient and meet the needs of the judicial system  Durbin has secured more than  41 million for this project in previous years spending bills  Senator Durbin has worked with Congressman Don Manzullo  D IL  to secure funds for the new Rockford Courthouse since the projects inception
