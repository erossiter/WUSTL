                                                                                                                                                                                                             3 23 2007                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN PUSHES LEGISLATION TO PRESSURE IRAN TO DISMANTLE NUCLEAR WEAPONS PROGRAM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Friday  March 23  2007    WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  joined Senator Gordon Smith  R OR  this week in introducing legislation to strengthen sanctions against Iran until that country dismantles all nuclear enrichment and reprocessing programs        Iran s leaders face a choice between the peaceful development of nuclear power for their citizens or the dangerous development of nuclear weapons   said Durbin    This legislation provides pressure and incentives to help them choose the path to peace and nuclear non proliferation       The Iran Counter Proliferation Act of 2007  Tightens economic sanctions on Iran   Addresses Russia s role in exporting nuclear and military technology to Iran by prohibiting U S  entry into a nuclear cooperation agreement with Russia until the President has verified that this cooperation has ceased   Encourages the peaceful development of nuclear power by authorizing  50 million in contributions to the International Atomic Energy Agency  IAEA  to establish an international nuclear fuel bank that would be available to countries that are in full compliance with IAEA safeguards and do not operate uranium enrichment or spent fuel reprocessing facilities   Reiterates the requirement to produce a National Intelligence Estimate on Iran mandated under last year s Defense Authorization bill    The IAEA is the world  s center of cooperation in the nuclear field  It was set up as the world  s  Atoms for Peace  organization in 1957 within the United Nations family  The Agency works with its Member States and multiple partners worldwide to promote safe  secure and peaceful nuclear technologies
