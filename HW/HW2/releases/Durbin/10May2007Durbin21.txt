     Durbin Announces  33 Million in DOT Grants for O Hare International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      5 10 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces  33 Million in DOT Grants for O Hare International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  May 10  2007    WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  33 000 000 in grants to the City of Chicago        Illinois airports are vital arteries that support our economy and national defense   Durbin said    Today s grants will go a long way towards ensuring that these airports continue to serve their customers with maximum efficiency        According to DOT  the grants will be used for      City of Chicago    18 000 000 toward noise mitigation measures for residences within a certain area surrounding O Hare International Airport   City of Chicago    15 000 000 to improve runway safety areas at O Hare International Airport
