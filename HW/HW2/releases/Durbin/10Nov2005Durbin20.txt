     DURBIN  SENATE MISSED OPPORTUNITY TO MAKE MILITARY RETIREMENT SYSTEM MORE EQUITABLE FOR GUARD AND RESERVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        11 10 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  SENATE MISSED OPPORTUNITY TO MAKE MILITARY RETIREMENT SYSTEM MORE EQUITABLE FOR GUARD AND RESERVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Thursday  November 10  2005           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  said the Senate missed an opportunity to revise the military retirement system to make it more equitable to members of the National Guard and Reserve and a more powerful incentive to continued service       Currently  a reservists who completes 20 years of service is eligible to receive retirement pay but not until the age of 60    Durbin   s amendment  which received 40 votes during debate on the Department of Defense Authorization bill  offered a one year reduction in the retirement age for every year of service beyond 20 years   Under Durbin s proposal  a reservist would have been able to begin to draw retirement pay as early as age 55 if he or she serves an additional five years          Today  a member of the Guard or Reserve can retire at the age of 38 after serving for 20 years  but must wait until the age of 60 before receiving the first penny of any retirement pay   That   s just too long  Our reserves are making enormous sacrifices  risking their lives in combat zones  and dying for their country  The age to draw retirement pay must be lowered     Durbin said          I am disappointed that the Senate did not pass this important measure  but the fight is far from over     Durbin added       Durbin said he will continue to work with organizations that have publicly supported his amendment to get this measure passed by the Senate   Organizations that have endorsed the amendment include The National Guard Association of the United States  The Military Officers Association of the United States  The Reserve Officers Association  and The Enlisted Association of the National Guard of the United States
