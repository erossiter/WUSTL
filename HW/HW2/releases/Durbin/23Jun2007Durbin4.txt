     Durbin Discusses Energy Bill During Visit to Pike County Wind Farm                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               6 23 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Discusses Energy Bill During Visit to Pike County Wind Farm                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Saturday  June 23  2007   PITTSFIELD  IL   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  visited Pike County Wind Farm today  less than 48 hours after the Senate passed a bill that promotes energy independence   The energy bill includes higher fuel economy standards for cars and trucks  a provision for which Durbin was instrumental in securing Senate passage  It also boosts production of renewable fuels over the next decade and takes critical steps to begin the fight against global warming   The energy bill we passed Thursday night will help move us in a new energy directiona direction that will enhance national security  strengthen the economy and protect the environment  said Durbin   Provisions of the energy legislation approved by the Senate include   CAFE standards  The Senate reached a bipartisan compromise to raise vehicle fuel economy standards for the first time in 22 years  The standards will increase from the current 25 miles per gallon to 35 miles per gallon by 2020  In 2020  the new fuel economy standards will save 18 billion gallons of gasoline annually  reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 206 tons of carbon dioxide annually in 2020  the equivalent of taking over 30 million cars off the roads  and save consumers nearly  25 billion in gasoline costs     Renewable Fuel Standard  The bill will increase the amount of ethanol produced  displacing 36 billion gallons of gasoline by 2022  This provision will save 1 4 million barrels of oil per day and reduce emission of greenhouse gasses into the environment  It will also drive the market toward next generation biofuels  including cellulosic ethanol which can be produced from many feedstocks     Energy Efficiency  The bill will make significant advances in reducing energy consumption in federal buildings and improving energy efficiency in lighting and appliancesan area often ignored and one that can save Americans billions of dollars  Reducing energy use by the federal government could save taxpayers  4 billion     Consumer Protections at the Pump  The legislation provides protection from oil companies taking advantage of consumers in an energy emergency  The bill explicitly prohibits market manipulation and gives the Federal Trade Commission the power to investigate and prosecute cases of potential price gouging     Clean Energy  The bill requires the federal government to purchase 10 percent of electricity from renewable sources by 2010 and 15 percent by 2015  If we are going to ask our fellow Americans to make greener choices  then we have to lead by example in Washington  Durbin said     Durbin also pushed for a provision that would require that 15 percent of electricity generated in the U S  come from renewable sources by 2015  The provision was not included in the final bill   The entire nation needs to follow this lead  we need to create energy in a clean  sustainable way  said Durbin of the Pike County Wind Farm  In addition to the clean source of electricity  the wind farm also means jobs and tax revenue for Pike County  which Durbin applauded   For the first time  I think we ve reached a point where the vast majority of our nation agrees that it is time to take global warming and energy independence seriously  Durbin said  That includes scientists  the Supreme Court  Fortune 100 leaders and military leaders  Now is the time to get legislation on these issues signed into law  I can tell you we are eager to do so and we need more places like the Pike County Wind Farm to help us achieve our goals
