     DURBIN REACTS TO PRESIDENT BUSH S SPEECH ON IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 10 6 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN REACTS TO PRESIDENT BUSH S SPEECH ON IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  October 6  2005           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after President Bush delivered an address on Iraq        The White House promised new details in this speech   Instead  the President delivered old generalities   President Bush failed to answer the questions Americans and Democrats are asking   How do we know if progress is being made   How do we measure success   When will there be enough Iraqi battalions ready to fight so U S  troops can begin coming home   What is the President   s plan to ensure that our troop commitment in Iraq does not compromise our safety here at home           The president has offered America a false choice  between resolve and retreat   The real choice is between a strategy of accountability and more vague generalities  We must move beyond policies of fear to a forceful commitment to protect the United States and its values        Roughly 7 000 troops from Illinois are serving in Iraq   Seventy seven Illinoisans have been killed in Iraq
