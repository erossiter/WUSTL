STATEMENT REGARDING DISMISSAL OF AMTRAK PRESIDENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Wednesday  November 9  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the dismissal of Amtrak President David Gunn    Today   s dismissal of Amtrak President and CEO David Gunn by the Amtrak Board of Directors is a clear demonstration that the Bush administration is determined to end Amtrak and seriously curtail passenger rail service in our nation       Under Gunn   s leadership  Amtrak increased ridership by over 2 million people since 2001 and revenues by  18 million in the last year alone   Amtrak has successfully expanded and become a viable mode of energy efficient transportation       David Gunn was no crony  he was a professional   He obviously made the White House uncomfortable          In Illinois  Amtrak serves 30 communities on three daily routes  employs more than 2 000 people  and has about two million annual riders  Chicago Union Station is the nation s fourth busiest passenger rail terminal and last year  ridership on the majority of routes originating in Chicago increased by 10  or more
