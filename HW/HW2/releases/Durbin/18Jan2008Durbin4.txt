Statement On The Death Of Former Cook County Board President John Stroger                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  January 18  2008   CHICAGO  IL   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement following the passing of former Cook County Board President John Stroger  John Stroger spent his political life breaking down racial barriers and working to lift up the less fortunate  His lifelong commitment to serve those who struggle every day to find affordable  quality medical care is his legacy  Many years ago John Stroger befriended me when I was an unknown candidate from Springfield with few allies in the Chicago political world  Today I mourn the loss of a real friend
