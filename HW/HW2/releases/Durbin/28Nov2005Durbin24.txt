     DURBIN ANNOUNCES NEW FUNDING FOR U of I AG RESEARCH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              11 28 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ANNOUNCES NEW FUNDING FOR U of I AG RESEARCH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Monday  November 28  2005    URBANA  IL      The agriculture industry in Illinois is stronger as a result of the cutting edge agriculture research the University of Illinois is conducting in partnership with the federal government  U S  Sen  Dick Durbin  D IL  said today     Durbin  a member of the Senate Agriculture Appropriations Subcommittee  met with University of Illinois officials and professors today in Urbana to announce  3 7 million in new fiscal year 2006 funding for agriculture research at U of I   Durbin has developed a record for supporting the University   s agriculture program  securing  3 5 million in fiscal year 2005 and  2 8 million in fiscal year 2004     Durbin pointed to the ag research specialty U of I has built over recent years and the positive effect those research projects are having on the state   s agriculture industry and ag economy        Agriculture has always been one of Illinois    largest industries     one that directly affects hundreds of large and small communities throughout the state   The University of Illinois is recognized across this nation for its world class agriculture research and I   m proud that some of the most important research in the nation is being conducted right here in Champaign Urbana   That has been the case for years and it will continue to be the case as long as I have a seat on the Agriculture Appropriations Subcommittee in the U S  Senate     said Durbin        The research funded at the federal level and conducted at the U of I is helping to create jobs and economic growth throughout the entire state   The faster the research findings can be delivered to Illinois farmers in the form of cutting edge technology  the stronger our ag industry will be   That   s a strategy that benefits everyone in the state     said Durbin
