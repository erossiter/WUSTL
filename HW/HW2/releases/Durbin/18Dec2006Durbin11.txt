     DURBIN PRESENTS PURPLE HEART TO VIETNAM VETERAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  12 18 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN PRESENTS PURPLE HEART TO VIETNAM VETERAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Monday  December 18  2006    CHICAGO  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today presented six honors  including a Purple Heart  to Vietnam Veteran Arthur Kimber in his Senate office   Kimber  who served in the United States Marine Corps from September 1965 to September 1968  earned a Purple Heart  the USMC Good Conduct Medal  the National Defense Service Medal  the Vietnam Service Medal  a Presidential Unit Citation Ribbon and a Combat Action Ribbon        Arthur Kimber volunteered to go to Vietnam and put his life on the line for the good of our country     Durbin said      He served America honorably  and it is my distinct pleasure to present him with his medals today        Kimber served as a Fire Team Leader in the Alpha Company of the USMC 1st Battalion  7th Marines   His unit received a Presidential Unit Citation from President Lyndon Johnson for    extraordinary heroism and outstanding performance of duty        Kimber  a lifetime member of the Disabled American Veterans  is currently retired after spending 25 years with the Chicago Park District   His father served in World War II and his daughter in law  Latisha  is a member of the Army Reserves     Joining Kimber for the presentation was his wife of 36 years  Renee  daughter Tanya  son David and daughter in law Latisha   He was also joined by his brother in law  John Babbitt  who served in the U S  Army for 13 years   Kimber   s son Arthur III  was unable to attend the medal presentation due to a work conflict     Kimber contacted Durbin   s office earlier this year seeking assistance in obtaining his medals  leading to today   s presentation   Durbin   s office is still working with Kimber to file the necessary paperwork to obtain a bronze star from the Department of Veterans Affairs
