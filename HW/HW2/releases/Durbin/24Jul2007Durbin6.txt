     Durbin Comments on Bipartisan Meeting With BP President                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          7 24 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Comments on Bipartisan Meeting With BP President                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Tuesday  July 24  2007   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement after a meeting with the Chairman and President of BP America  Bob Malone  BP had a wake up call at today s meeting  They now understand that on a bipartisan basis  the congressional delegations around Lake Michigan are determined to protect the lake  Lake Michigan is not just another body of water   it is a treasure to be protected  It is a source of drinking water for millions of families  We hope that it is in this spirit that BP will reevaluate their plans and present us with them when we meet again in September  We made it very clear that if they dont work to preserve and protect Lake Michigan  theyre in for a battle  Other members of Congress attending todays bipartisan meeting included  Senator Debbie Stabenow  D MI   Representatives Rahm Emanuel  D IL   Mark Kirk  R IL   Dan Lipinski  D IL   Jan Schakowsky  D IL   Judy Biggert  R IL  and Bobby Rush  D IL
