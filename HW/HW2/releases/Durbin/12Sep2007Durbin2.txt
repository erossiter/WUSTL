Appropriations Committee Approves  12 8 Million in Defense Appropriations for Rock Island Arsenal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Wednesday  September 12  2007   The Rock Island Arsenal plays a vital role both in our nations defense and in the regions economy  Durbin said  Funding for mission critical projects will help keep jobs at the arsenal and help the arsenal continue its work supporting our nations troops  The Fiscal Year  FY  2008 defense spending bill includes funding for the following initiatives at the Arsenal      Arsenal Support Program Initiative  ASPI  at the Rock Island Arsenal  RIA   Rock Island   11 5 million in funding to the RIA for efforts to market its part time manufacturing and related services as a non governmental contractor  The Arsenal Support Program Initiative  ASPI  renovates unused office or manufacturing space at Rock Island Arsenal to lease to commercial firms  It was enacted by Congress in FY 2001  This program creates jobs on Rock Island while collecting rent and offsetting base operating costs  There are currently over 20 tenants operating on Rock Island and nearly 500 jobs that have been created      Titanium Fabrication for Military Industrial Equipment  Rock Island   1 35 million to the RIA to create a consortium of titanium producers and manufacturers  Funding would establish a manufacturing laboratory at the Arsenal to concentrate on bending  forming  welding  and casting of titanium and will improve the governments ability to fabricate new lightweight materials  The funding bill will now go to the full Senate for approval
