     DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING SENATE ACTION TO PROVIDE HURRICANE RELIEF FUNDS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       9 1 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING SENATE ACTION TO PROVIDE HURRICANE RELIEF FUNDS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Thursday  September 1  2005      IL     U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the Senate   s passage of a  10 5 billion emergency supplemental appropriations bill to aid the victims of Hurricane Katrina       Tonight  the Senate acted swiftly to provide more than  10 billion in emergency funding that is critically needed   This is just the first step   In the coming weeks and months  as we begin to understand the true nature of this disaster  Congress will be asked to provide billions of additional dollars in assistance             I join the residents of Illinois who stand ready and willing to help their neighbors to the South and in commending our National Guard units and volunteers who have already traveled to the areas impacted by Hurricane Katrina   We pray for their safe return             During this time of national crisis  Congress is prepared to do whatever is necessary to assist the families in Louisiana  Mississippi  Florida and Alabama  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  I will work with my colleagues and the Administration to provide the federal aid necessary to help these communities recover             Americans are horrified by the devastation caused by Hurricane Katrina and the suffering that so many of our citizens experiencing  but I believe the American spirit will prevail
