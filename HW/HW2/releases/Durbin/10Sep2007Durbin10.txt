     Durbin  Emanuel Comment on Meeting with BP America CEO Bob Malone                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                9 10 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin  Emanuel Comment on Meeting with BP America CEO Bob Malone                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Monday  September 10  2007   This afternoon  we met with Bob Malone  the CEO of British Petroleum America  He gave us every assurance that BP is 100  committed to their decision not to dump additional waste into Lake Michigan  When we raised the issue of BP s air permit  and the potential pollution it poses to Lake Michigan  he asked us for additional time to assess the situation and promised to get back to us  We made it clear that we would stand firm and defend the environmental integrity of Lake Michigan on all fronts  air and water  Malone assured us he will consult with us as BP moves forward on this front
