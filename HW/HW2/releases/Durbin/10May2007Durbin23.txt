     Durbin Announces Nearly  22 Million in Homeland Security Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     5 10 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  22 Million in Homeland Security Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Thursday  May 10  2007    WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS   Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has awarded nearly  22 000 000 in federal funding grants to the state of Illinois       According to FEMA  this year s grants are as follows      Chicago  Illinois   6 811 741 for the City of Chicago Office of Emergency Management and Communications for port security   Chicago  Illinois    111 456 for Kinder Morgan Liquids Terminals  LLC   Chicago  Illinois   44 580 for Mittal Steel USA  Inc   Chicagoland Area   12 837 834 to enhance security measures for critical transit infrastructure including bus  rail and ferry systems   State of Illinois   1 540 000 to the state of Illinois for buffer zone protection programs
