    9 21 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Statement On The Recall Of 1 Million Cribs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Friday  September 21  2007    Washington  D C     United States Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  issued the following statement today  after the a recall of 1 million Simplicity cribs  The recall was announced after a dozens of children were injured and two killed  from an improperly installed piece of hardware    When parents put their children to bed at night  they trust that the crib they re using will be the safest place for them  outside of their arms  When you look at the series of recalls of children s products over the last few months  one thing becomes clear  greater steps need to be taken to test and certify the safety of products designed for children    I ve introduced a bill  along with Senator Bill Nelson  D FL   that would require ALL children s products be tested and certified safe by third party testing facilities  In addition  I m working with my Senate colleagues to strengthen the resources and authority of the Consumer Product Safety Commission  CPSC  to ensure that our nation s consumer watchdog is up to the task of protecting us all    I urge parents to please check your cribs against information found on the CPSC website  More info can be found at www cpsc gov
