Announces Nearly  2 7 Million in DOT Grants for Illinois Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Friday  July 13  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  2 679 819 in grants to Illinois airports  Department of Transportation  Williamson County Regional Airport  Marion   1 000 000 in funding to remove obstructions in the Runway 20 approach to enhance safety  overlay the general aviation apron pavement to provide for safe operations  expand the terminal building to enhance capacity  and reimburse for the acquisition of land that provides for approach protection of Runway 29  Mount Vernon Airport  Mt  Vernon   138 165 in funding under the Illinois State Block Grant Program for runway safety area improvement project  Quad City International Airport  Moline   1 541 654 in funding for the Metropolitan Airport Authority of Rock Island to improve the runway safety area
