     DURBIN S STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF 2500 AMERICAN SERVICE MEN AND WOMEN IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   6 15 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN S STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF 2500 AMERICAN SERVICE MEN AND WOMEN IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Thursday  June 15  2006   Washington  D C    Assistant Democratic Leader Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement on the death of 2500 American soldiers in Iraq   America has reached another sad milestone  2 500 of Americas best and bravest soldiers have been killed in Iraq   On this sad day  we observed a moment of silence in the U S  Senate to honor their sacrifice and devotion to our nation   As we honor our war dead  and those men and women who remain in harms way  we must ask  what can we do now to bring our troops home with their mission truly accomplished   The President has told the American people that as Iraqi soldiers stand up  our troops will stand down   Now he says that we must not leave too soon    That is not a plan for success nor is it a clear message to the Iraqi people   Ultimately  it is the Iraqi people  the Iraqi leaders  and the Iraqi armed forces and police who will deliver peace  security and prosperity to their nation  We must let the Iraqis know that we will not give up American lives indefinitely   The Iraqis have to show us that they will stand and defend their own soil
