STATEMENT REGARDING CHUCK SCHOLZ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Thursday  August 17  2006          U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after the announcement that former Quincy Mayor Chuck Scholz is withdrawing from the race for the 93rd District General Assembly seat          I have known Chuck Scholz for more than 25 years  He is a good friend and has been a tremendous ally   Chuck has an important battle to win before he can return to public service  I respect the very tough decision he has made today             I am honored to have worked with Chuck Scholz and wish him the best as he prepares to leave the public arena for private life  We will always be by his and Becky s side
