     DURBIN SAYS SENATE VOTE CLEARLY CALLS FOR CHANGE IN IRAQ POLICY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  11 15 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN SAYS SENATE VOTE CLEARLY CALLS FOR CHANGE IN IRAQ POLICY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Tuesday  November 15  2005    Washington  D C       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after the Senate voted to change the Bush Administration   s strategy on Iraq        Today   s vote on Iraq was significant because Democrats and Republicans joined together to call for change   The message to the Administration is clear  more of the same is unacceptable           We need leadership and a new direction   The Bush Administration does not appear to have a clear plan or strategy for how we will succeed in Iraq  This amendment though watered down by the Republicans calls for a clear break from current policy and requires timely reports from the President to the American people           We owe our troops  their families  and all Americans nothing less than a clear path to success in Iraq rather than a long term  open ended commitment
