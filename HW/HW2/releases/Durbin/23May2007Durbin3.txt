     Statment Of Senator Durbin On The Withdrawal Of The Baroody Nomination                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           5 23 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Statment Of Senator Durbin On The Withdrawal Of The Baroody Nomination                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Wednesday  May 23  2007         U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  issued the following statement today with regard to the decision by the White House to withdraw the nomination of Michael Baroody to chair the Consumer Product Safety Commission        This is the right decision for the consumers of America  This agency is far too important to be headed by someone whose body of professional experience has been spent opposing the mission of the agency       I hope that the President will to nominate someone with the experience and expertise to run this watchdog agency  More importantly  I hope the President will send us a candidate who understands the fundamental obligation this agency has to safeguard the lives and health of American consumers  I look forward to working with the Senate to confirm such a nominee
