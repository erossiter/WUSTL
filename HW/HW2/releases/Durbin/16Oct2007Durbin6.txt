     Durbin Statement Regarding Report of Cook County Bureau of Health Services Review Committee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      10 16 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Statement Regarding Report of Cook County Bureau of Health Services Review Committee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  October 16  2007        U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement about the Report of the Cook County Bureau of Health Services Review Committee  The Cook County Bureau of Health Services cannot survive without immediate  far reaching reform  The health professionals and civic leaders who accepted the challenge to review the critical issues facing the Cook County Bureau of Health Services have produced a comprehensive  professional and honest analysis  I thank them for the many hours they volunteered and especially thank Dr  Larry Goodman of Rush University Medical Center for his exceptional leadership chairing this review  The governance issue is a threshold requirement for credible reform  The President and County Board will have to show vision and courage in creating an independent entity that will work to save the important health services provided by the County  Amidst all of the shortcomings of the current system  the Review Committee found one thing that should inspire everyone who reads this report  In their words  Overall  the Bureau of Health Services provides high quality health care  The most vulnerable people in our state can take heart that the health care professionals of Cook County continue one of the finest medical traditions in our nation  I thank President Stroger for accepting my suggestion to convene this committee and empowering it to review every aspect of the Cook County Bureau of Health Services  Now we must turn this Committee s recommendations into positive change and we have no time to lose
