S  SENATOR DICK DURBIN REGARDING TODAY S BID BY INTERCONTINENTAL EXCHANGE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Thursday  March 15  2007         U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding today s unsolicited  9 7 billion all stock bid for the Chicago Board of Trade by IntercontinentalExchange        As with all such offers  today s proposal by IntercontinentalExchange needs to be evaluated by the proper federal regulatory and oversight authorities  but I believe that the Chicago Mercantile Exchange and the Chicago Board of Trade have created a merger agreement that makes sense and is beneficial to all involved         I m sure the Board of Trade leadership will review the new proposal and will make the decision that is in the best interest of the Chicago financial community as a whole
