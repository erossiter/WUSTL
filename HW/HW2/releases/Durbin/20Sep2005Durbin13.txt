MOURNS PASSING OF SIMON WIESENTHAL  CALLS HIM    CONSCIENCE OF THE HOLOCAUST                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Tuesday  September 20  2005    Washington  DC      Today  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  issued the following statement about the passing of Simon Wiesenthal          I was saddened to learn of the passing of Simon Wiesenthal  a man who for so many was the conscience of the Holocaust   Mr  Wiesenthal was an unrelenting advocate for justice on behalf of the six million Jews who perished in the Holocaust  He dedicated his life s work to ensuring that the world would never forget and that the perpetrators of the world s most heinous crimes were brought to justice  While his passing is a loss to the world  Simon Wiesenthal   s commitment to humanity will endure
