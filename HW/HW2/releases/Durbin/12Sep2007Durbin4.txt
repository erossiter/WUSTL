     Durbin  Emanuel  Call on BP to Publicly Commit to Protecting the Great Lakes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     9 12 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin  Emanuel  Call on BP to Publicly Commit to Protecting the Great Lakes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Wednesday  September 12  2007      Following their meeting with President of BP America Robert A  Malone  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and U S  Representative Rahm Emanuel  D IL  released the following letter calling on BP to advertise its intention to operate under the old dumping permit as public commitment to protecting the Great Lakes  The following is the text of the letter  Dear Mr  Malone  Thank you for taking the time to meet with us on Monday  We appreciated the opportunity to discuss your companys decision to stay within the old wastewater effluent permit limits as you move forward with the expansion of the Whiting  Indiana refinery  More importantly  we were pleased to hear that the commitment to forgo increased discharging into Lake Michigan goes beyond a personal commitment and reflects BP policy  In light of that promise  we would like to reiterate the recommendation that BP publicly advertise its intention to operate under the old permit just as your company ran full page ads prior to this decision  Since you do not intend to amend the permit or otherwise codify plans to operate under the old permit  an advertisement would be a welcome illustration of BPs public commitment to protecting the Great Lakes  We appreciate your understanding of just how important Lake Michigan is to the City of Chicago and the entire Great Lakes region  We look forward to continuing to work with you to make sure that BPs green reputation is both deserved and recognized  Sincerely  Richard J  Durbin  U S  Senator Rahm Emanuel  Member of Congress
