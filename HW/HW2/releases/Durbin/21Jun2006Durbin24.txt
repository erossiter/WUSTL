     DURBIN INTRODUCES BILL NAMING NAVY AND MARINE CORPS RESERVE CENTER IN ROCK ISLAND FOR CONGRESSMAN LANE EVANS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     6 21 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN INTRODUCES BILL NAMING NAVY AND MARINE CORPS RESERVE CENTER IN ROCK ISLAND FOR CONGRESSMAN LANE EVANS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Wednesday  June 21  2006    WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  introduced an amendment to the Department of Defense Authorization bill last night to name the Navy and Marine Corps Reserve Center at Rock Island  Illinois  the    Lane Evans Navy and Marine Corps Reserve Center      In March  Congressman Evans announced he would not seek reelection in November to the U S  House of Representatives        There are two kinds of courage in this world  There is physical courage  which is uncommon  But there is an even rarer commodity  moral courage  Once in a great while you find someone who has both and Lane Evans is that person     said Durbin      There is no more fitting way to permanently and publicly recognize Congressman Evan   s work than to name the Rock Island Navy and Marine Corps Reserve Center in his honor        Congressman Evans grew up in Rock Island  Illinois  the son of a firefighter   He joined the Marine Corps right out of high school and served during the Vietnam era from 1969 to 1971  After the Marines  Evans went to college  then to Georgetown Law School   He was elected to Congress in 1982 and has served as a ranking member of the Committee on Veterans Affairs and a senior member of the Committee on Armed Services        In his time in Congress  Lane has worked harder for military troops and veterans than virtually any other member in the House or the Senate     said Durbin      Naming the Navy and Marine Corps Reserve Center for him will ensure that the legacy of Lane   s service will continue to be a source of inspiration for our troops and veterans for years to come
