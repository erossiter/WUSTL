     Durbin And Manzullo Secure More Than  58 Million In Final Spending Bill For Rockford Courthouse                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  12 17 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin And Manzullo Secure More Than  58 Million In Final Spending Bill For Rockford Courthouse                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Monday  December 17  2007    WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Committee on Financial Services and General Government  and Congressman Don Manzullo  R IL  today announced that the House of Representatives has approved the final omnibus spending bill which provides an additional  58 792 million in funding for construction of the new federal courthouse in Rockford  This amount represents the last portion of funding needed to complete the project  The bill will be considered in the Senate this week and could be sent to the President for his signature as early as this weekend About six years ago  Congressman Manzullo and I started a conversation with the Rockford community about the need for a new federal courthouse  said Durbin  Thankfully  with the final funds secured in today s bill  those constructing the courthouse will be doing the heavy lifting from here on out  We are looking forward to seeing Rockford build a new federal courthouse that will be secure  efficient and meet the needs of the judicial system  After several years of hard work  Senator Durbin and I have secured the funding necessary to build the new federal courthouse in Rockford  Manzullo said  The new courthouse will dramatically improve the safety of the judges and the public  help rejuvenate downtown Rockford  and create several hundred construction jobs and contract opportunities for area small businesses  Todays announcement should pave the way for a groundbreaking in the spring  Durbin and Manzullo have been working together to secure funds for the new Rockford Courthouse since the projects inception  more than  41 million in previous spending bills  The existing Rockford courthouse was built more than 25 years ago  It is filled to capacity and has structural problems that would be prohibitively expensive to repair and impede efficient functioning of the court  In addition  the lack of a separate prisoner entrance to the building compromises security at the facility
