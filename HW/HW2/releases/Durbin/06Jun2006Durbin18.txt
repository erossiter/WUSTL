     DURBIN MEETS WITH UNITED STATES TRADE REPRESENTATIVE NOMINEE SUSAN SCHWAB TO DISCUSS ILLINOIS ISSUES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             6 6 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN MEETS WITH UNITED STATES TRADE REPRESENTATIVE NOMINEE SUSAN SCHWAB TO DISCUSS ILLINOIS ISSUES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Tuesday  June 6  2006    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today met with United States Trade Representative  USTR  Nominee  Susan Schwab  to discuss issues related to US trade including the need to enforce regulations of trade agreements  so that American businesses and employees are treated fairly        Lowering trade and political barriers has made it possible to do business with billions of other people across the planet     said Durbin      With this freedom comes a responsibility to protect our American companies and employees   I made it clear to Ms  Schwab that we must do more to enforce trade laws so that businesses in Illinois  and throughout the country  will have both the protections and opportunities they need as the world market continues to expand        In today   s meeting  Durbin asked Schwab to support strengthening company and employee protection rights in trade agreements so that American companies can compete fairly in international trade while holding competitors to the highest standards of worker   s rights   The Senator expressed concern with the vulnerabilities of American companies in today   s market  citing the example of a small Chicago business  ROTEC  which is now in bankruptcy   ROTEC invented and marketed a world class concrete transport system that the Chinese contracted to use abroad   However  midway through the project  Chinese project managers copied the technology  sued ROTEC in Chinese court  won the case  and used that ruling to seize the U S  assets of the company        It is an outrage that the World Trade Organization   s rules would allow a Chicago company   s technology and assets to be seized     said Durbin      We must strengthen our trade regulations so that Illinois companies  like ROTEC  can compete abroad without being exploited        Schwab is currently the Deputy U S  Trade Representative and was nominated to take over the top U S  trade post from Rob Portman
