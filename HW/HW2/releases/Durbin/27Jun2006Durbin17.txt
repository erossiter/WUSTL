    6 27 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  SENATE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE APPROVES  2 MILLION FOR STATEWIDE TEACHER TRAINING INITIATIVES   INSTALLATION OF NEW ETHANOL PUMP                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Tuesday  June 27  2006    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  said that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved  2 million for Illinois projects as well as a provision that expands the nation   s use of ethanol  E 85  fuel  a significant source of revenue for Illinois farmers and businesses       The fiscal year 2007 legislative branch appropriations bill includes funding for the following Illinois projects        Illinois    An Adventure of the American Mind    Programs  Statewide    2 million through the Library of Congress for continuation of teacher training initiatives in Illinois      An Adventure of the American Mind    is a teacher training initiative that instructs educators in ways to incorporate the Library   s digital collections into school curricula       The following Illinois educational institutions will receive continued funding through this program   Southern Illinois University  Carbondale and Edwardsville   Loyola University Chicago  DePaul University  Chicago  Governors State University  Kankakee  Federation of Independent Illinois Colleges and Universities  Springfield  Barat Education Foundation  Lake Forest  Eastern Illinois University  Charleston  Illinois State University  Normal  and Quincy University  Quincy        Ethanol Pump on Capitol Hill  A provision which directs the Architect of the Capitol to install the first ethanol  E 85  fuel pump on the Capitol complex   The provision also requires existing legislative branch flex fuel vehicles to use the ethanol pump and all future legislative branch vehicles to be E 85 compatible and use E 85 fuel   The pump will set an example and encourage automakers to increase their production of flex fuel vehicles and gas stations across the country to construct new E 85 pumps   Illinois is currently second in the nation in ethanol production and second in the nation in corn production   Illinois     1 billion dollar investment in ethanol has already created 4 800 jobs for businesses and farmers in the state
