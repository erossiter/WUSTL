     DURBIN WELCOMES FIRST NEW C 40C TO SCOTT AFB                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     2 26 2007                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN WELCOMES FIRST NEW C 40C TO SCOTT AFB                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Monday  February 26  2007    SCOTT AFB  IL    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  helped celebrate the arrival of the first of three new C 40s to the 932nd Airlift Wing at Scott Air Force Base today        Today marks a new chapter at Scott AFB  as the 932nd takes on a new  valuable mission with the C 40 aircraft   The state of the art plane we re welcoming today reinforces and strengthens the presence of the 932nd Airlift Wing here at Scott Air Force Base   The transition from the C 9 to the C 40 mission will help keep more than 1 000 reservists and civilians employed at 932nd Airlift Wing        The C 40C will primarily be used to provide safe  comfortable and reliable transportation for U S  leaders to locations around the world   The airplane is the first to offer a wide range of seating capacity options and that flexibility will allow the aircraft to serve a broad range of missions   On occasion  the aircraft may also be tapped to help with homeland security needs  flying Special Forces to the scene of a natural disaster following an emergency  Durbin said       The arrival of the aircraft is also noteworthy because the purchase was the first in a new  streamlined acquisition process for the Air Force  Durbin said       Scott AFB expects to welcome delivery for the second aircraft in July and the third aircraft in December
