     Durbin Presents Jefferson Award to Richard Daley                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 6 19 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Presents Jefferson Award to Richard Daley                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Tuesday  June 19  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  will present the Jefferson Award for Public Service to Chicago Mayor Richard Daley this evening  Tuesday  June  19  2007 at 8 30 p m  at Union Station  Mayor Daley is being recognized for his achievements in education  public safety  neighborhood development  and the environment as Mayor of the City of Chicago  Mayor Daley understands that a great city is more than gleaming glass and steel  it is a community  A great city celebrates its history  but looks to its future  Durbin said  That is what Chicago is doing  thanks in large part to the leadership of Richard Daley  Mayor Daley is receiving the U S  Senator John Heinz Award for Greatest Public Service by an Elected or Appointed Official  one of four Jefferson Awards being awarded this evening  The Heinz Family Foundation endowed the award and Senator Durbin will present it onstage with Tereza Heinz Kerry  The other 2007 Jefferson Award winners are noted economist and author Dr  Jeffrey T  Sachs  Harlem Childrens Zone President Geoffrey Canada  and Strong Women Strong Girls founder Lindsay Hyde  The Jefferson Awards were founded in 1972 by Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis  Senator Robert Taft  Jr   and Sam Beard to encourage community and public service  Jefferson Awards recognize leaders for extraordinary accomplishments and dedication in community service and volunteering  Former Jefferson Award winners include Justice Sandra Day OConnor  former astronaut and Senator John Glenn  and Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice
