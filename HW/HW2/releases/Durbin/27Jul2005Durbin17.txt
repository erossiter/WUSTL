     DURBIN  OBAMA   APPROPRIATIONS CONFERENCE COMMITTEE APPROVES MORE THAN  7 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS PROJECTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          7 27 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  OBAMA   APPROPRIATIONS CONFERENCE COMMITTEE APPROVES MORE THAN  7 MILLION FOR ILLINOIS PROJECTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Wednesday  July 27  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senators Dick Durbin  D IL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  and Barack Obama  D IL  today said that a House Senate conference committee has approved  7 7 million for Illinois projects   The bill will now go to President Bush to be signed into law       The Fiscal Year 2006 appropriations bill for the U S  Department of Interior  U S  Forest Service and Environmental Protection Agency includes funding for the following projects      Department of Interior  National Park Service      Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum  Springfield    1 million to continue construction of the Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum   Jens Jensen Park  Highland Park    175 000 to restore and preserve Jens Jensen Park  a landmark of the Ravinia neighborhood in Highland Park and named for the famed landscape architect   Fish and Wildlife Service      Crab Orchard Rehabilitation Project  Williamson County    2 625 million for a visitor center and dam rehabilitation project at the Crab Orchard National Wildlife Refuge   Department of Agriculture  Forest Service      Chicago GreenStreets Program  Chicago    350 000 to improve urban forestry in the City of Chicago through a tree replanting program  including trees damaged by the Asian Longhorned Beetle infestation   Shawnee National Forest  Southern Illinois    250 000 for land acquisition from willing sellers within the boundaries of the Shawnee National Forest   Environmental Protection Agency   Science and Technology      Clean Air Counts  Northeastern Illinois    800 000 to continue the Clean Air Counts project  an innovative and cost effective method to reduce smog causing emissions in the Chicago metropolitan region   Environmental Programs and Management      Midwest Technology Assistance Center  Urbana Champaign    500 000 for the Midwest Technology Assistance Center at the University of Illinois  a member of the national Small Public Water System Technology Center initiative   State and Tribal Assistance Grants      Wauconda Drinking Water Improvements  Wauconda    750 000 to the Village of Wauconda for a water system expansion project that will bring safe drinking water to local residents   Durbin and Obama worked with U S  Representative Melissa Bean on this project to provide safe drinking water to residents with wells contaminated by vinyl chloride   Springfield Drinking Water Infrastructure Improvements  Springfield    250 000 to the City of Springfield for drinking water infrastructure improvements including the Spaulding Dam Gate replacement   Port Byron Wastewater System Upgrade  Port Byron    250 000 to the Village of Port Byron for upgrades to its wastewater lagoon system   Lake County Wastewater Improvements  Lake County    500 000 to the communities of Lake Bluff  Highwood  Highland Park  and Lake Forest to address overflow of sanitary and storm sewers   Pecatonica Wastewater Treatment  Pecatonica   250 000 to the Village of Pecatonica for a new wastewater treatment facility
