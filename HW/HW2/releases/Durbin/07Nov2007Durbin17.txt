     Durbin Welcomes Oak Park Officials As Special Guests To The Joint Session Of Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            11 7 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Welcomes Oak Park Officials As Special Guests To The Joint Session Of Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Wednesday  November 7  2007       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today welcomed Oak Park Village President David Pope and Village Trustee Ray Johnson as his guests to an address given by the President of France  Nicolas Sarkozy  to a special joint session of Congress     Before attending the joint session  Durbin met with Pope  Johnson and Village Manager Tom Barwin in his Capitol office to discuss transportation and development issues in Oak Park particularly the cap over a portion of I 290 that splits Oak Park  The cap would provide space to build parks above the highway and likely reduce the amount of pollution that would come from the increased traffic after the Illinois Department of Transportation completes the expansion of I 290  Senator Durbin secured  2 million in funding for the project in the most recent Transportation Appropriations bill
