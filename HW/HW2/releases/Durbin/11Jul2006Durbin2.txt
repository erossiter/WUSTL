     DURBIN SAYS AMERICANS WILL PAY HEAVY PRICE UNDER PRES  BUSH   S SCHEME TO PRIVATIZE SOCIAL SECURITY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                7 11 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN SAYS AMERICANS WILL PAY HEAVY PRICE UNDER PRES  BUSH   S SCHEME TO PRIVATIZE SOCIAL SECURITY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Tuesday  July 11  2006   Washington  D C       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today said Americans will pay a heavy price under President Bush   s scheme to privatize Social Security   By including Social Security privatization in his mid session review of the federal budget  President Bush made it clear he has no intention of giving up privatization  In addition  the President   s proposal to privatize Social Security is now estimated to cost  9 billion more over the next 10 years from  712 to  721 billion        President Bush and Congressional Republicans are determined to privatize Social Security     Durbin said      That means benefit cuts for millions of Americans and billions of dollars in new debt           When retirement plans are under attack  Social Security is the bedrock of every American   s retirement security   Democrats stopped the President   s plan to dismantle Social Security last year and we will do it again     Durbin added
