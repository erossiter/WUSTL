     DURBIN SECURES VICTORIES FOR ILLINOIS VETERANS IN SPENDING BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  11 18 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN SECURES VICTORIES FOR ILLINOIS VETERANS IN SPENDING BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Friday  November 18  2005           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  said that he was pleased that two provisions he authored to assist veterans were included in the final version of the Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Conference Report which was passed by the Senate today  Illinois    junior senator  Barack Obama  D IL   was a cosponsor of both provisions     The first provision will prevent the Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  from proceeding with a review of the 72 000 cases where veterans receive the maximum amount of the post traumatic stress disorder  PTSD  disability compensation until the department presents Congress with a detailed report   Most importantly  the provision prevents the VA from cutting veterans    benefits as a result of this review  unless there is evidence of fraud     Earlier this month  the VA had announced that it had suspended such a review after pressure from Durbin and Obama  who expressed concern that veterans will face cuts in benefits and services        This is a victory for veterans in Illinois suffering from PTSD   Veterans suffering from mental or emotional problems should not have to suffer through the stress of an ill advised review of their cases years after the fact     said Durbin     The second provision will require the VA to notify veterans living in states such as Illinois with below average disability compensation about the history of past under compensation   The information campaign will be conducted in states whose average annual disability compensation payment was less than  7 300   In 2003  Illinois veterans received an average of  6 802   The information campaign will provide veterans living in these states with instructions for submitting new claims and requesting reviews        Americans have a fundamental dedication to fairness     Durbin said     This provision will help restore a level of fairness that Americans expect for our disabled veterans   Disabled veterans should not receive different amounts of disability payments based on where they live
