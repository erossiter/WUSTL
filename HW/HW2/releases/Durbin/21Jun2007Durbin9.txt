     Durbin Commends Committee Approval of Higher Education Act Reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       6 21 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Commends Committee Approval of Higher Education Act Reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Thursday  June 21  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   The Senate Committee on Health  Education  Labor and Pensions has approved legislation  written by U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  that would help protect student borrowers and provide better security on college campuses  Key provisions from two of Durbin s bills    the Student Loan Sunshine Act and Campus Law Enforcement Emergency Act    were included in the Higher Education Act reauthorization bill that was approved by committee and will now go to the Senate floor for consideration   We need to shine a bright light on the incentive based relationships that exist between some universities and lenders  said Durbin  This legislation takes an important step in requiring stricter standards for lenders and universities  We must make sure college students dont fall victim to unprincipled lenders   The portion of the bill based on Durbin s Student Loan Sunshine Act aims to protect students and parents from exploitation by private lenders and lenders who offer gifts to colleges as a way to secure loan business  The Higher Education Act reauthorization bill would    Ban lenders from offering gifts worth more than a nominal amount to college financial aid office employees  including travel  lodging  entertainment  and in kind services that lenders provide to college financial aid offices    Require full disclosure of the reasons why an institution of higher education has selected a lender for its preferred lender list  including any special arrangements the lender has with the school    Prohibit special arrangements that lenders and institutions of higher education have to offer loan products at the institution   After the terrible tragedy at Virginia Tech  it was clear that we need to address the vulnerability of students in college  said Durbin  The language added to this bill will better protect college students and their families from threats to their security  I look forward to debating this bill on the Senate floor and will work toward its quick passage   The portion of the bill based on Durbin s Campus Law Enforcement Emergency Response Act strengthens the safety and security of college and university campuses by requiring that each school develop and test emergency response procedures and testing of campus notification systems on at least an annual basis  Durbin s original legislation was cosponsored by Senator Barack Obama  D IL  and introduced in the wake of the Virginia Tech tragedy  The bill would    Require colleges and universities to develop and test procedures for responding to large scale emergencies and dangerous incidents on their campuses  including procedures for promptly notifying the campus community in case of such emergencies    Permit the Secretary of Education and the Attorney General to combine their knowledge and resources in order to advise colleges and universities on model emergency response procedures and to disseminate best practices    Authorize the Department of Education to create a competitive grant program for colleges and universities to develop and improve their emergency response procedures and programs
