                                                                                                                                                                                                             9 9 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING MILITARY BASE REALIGNMENT AND CLOSURES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Friday  September 9  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the recommendations of the Defense Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission       The BRAC Commission   s report is now in the hands of the President   I hope he will see this work for what it is     a short sighted and error ridden set of recommendations that will cut jobs in the communities like Springfield and Rock Island and fail to comply with the criteria established for this BRAC process under law             Senator Obama and I have encouraged the President to give this report a failing grade and return it to the Commission for changes   Absent that  I will vote against the Commission   s recommendations when they come before the Senate             I encourage Governor Blagojevich and Attorney General Madigan to continue their legal fight to preserve the 183rd Air National Guard Fighter Wing in Springfield   This BRAC report undermines the strength and effectiveness of the Air National Guard when we are all too aware of the Guard   s importance and the strains it is under             I am grateful that Scott Air Force Base will gain jobs and planes as a result of this process and that the Peoria Air National Guard unit will keep flying   But I simply cannot look the other way when it comes to BRAC decisions that will harm the Rock Island Arsenal and the 183rd Fighter Wing and fail to follow the commission   s own published standards
