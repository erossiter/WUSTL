     Durbin Announces September 2007 as  Adopt a School Library Month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 9 7 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces September 2007 as  Adopt a School Library Month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  September 7  2007   WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Dick Durbin today announced the passage of his legislation to designate September 2007 as Adopt a School Library Month  This legislation reaffirms what studies have consistently shown  children are more likely to succeed when they have access to a high quality library at school  Senator Norm Coleman  R MN  co sponsored the legislation  Some of my favorite childhood moments were spent losing myself in a book about faraway places  exciting adventures and historic figures  said Durbin  Sadly  today  many children do not have access to new books that could establish them as lifelong readers  In fact  some libraries in Illinois are unable to purchase new books and must rely on donations from the community  We can not expect our students to compete in todays global economy unless we provide them with the tools they need to succeed  As Adopt a School Library Month  September 2007 has the potential to jumpstart community efforts to replenishing book shelves  revitalizing technology and redecorating our libraries     Increasingly  school libraries are facing extraordinary obstacles in the effort to provide students with high quality resources  These circumstances are not due to lack of concern by librarians  school officials or members of the community  Rather  school leaders are working with limited budgets and unforgiving performance standards    There is  however  a strong link between school libraries and student achievement  both in the classroom and on standardized tests  When libraries are well funded  students score an average of 5 percent to 13 percent higher on their reading and writing test scores  The data shows that children are more likely to succeed when they have access to current  well funded libraries    Durbin s legislation was intended to raise public awareness about the important role that school libraries play in the academic success of every child in America  By designating September 2007 as Adopt a School Library Month  Senator Durbin hopes to raise public awareness about the important role that school libraries play in the academic success of every child in America  Adopt a School Library Month offers a great opportunity for state and local governments  nonprofit organizations  and businesses to take an active role in the success of their local school libraries by making a commitment to restock and revitalize libraries so that students have the resources they need to succeed
