     DURBIN ANNOUNCES  220 000 FOR HEALTH CLINIC AT COMMUNITY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL     11 12 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ANNOUNCES  220 000 FOR HEALTH CLINIC AT COMMUNITY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Saturday  November 12  2005    STAUNTON  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced  220 000 in federal funding to construct the Rural Health Clinic at Community Memorial Hospital   Durbin  who previously secured nearly  150 000 for the first phase of the clinic   s construction  made the announcement at a news conference with Patrick Heise  CEO of Community Memorial Hospital        The hospital was built nearly 40 years ago and lacks adequate physician office space     said Durbin      The new Rural Health Clinic will provide residents of the Staunton area with a modernized facility and more comprehensive medical care        The Rural Health Clinic will house a six physician practice that is currently occupying cramped quarters in the hospital   The hospital expects to hire additional health professionals and administrative support staff for the new clinic     The clinic will have an active role within the community  educating families about healthcare and promoting preventative care   The Rural Health Clinic is set to open during the first quarter of 2006
