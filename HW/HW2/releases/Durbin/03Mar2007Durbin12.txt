     STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DICK DURBIN REGARDING DOWNSTATE REGIONS AIR SERVICE SHUT DOWN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          3 3 2007                                                                                                                                                                            STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DICK DURBIN REGARDING DOWNSTATE REGIONS AIR SERVICE SHUT DOWN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Saturday  March 3  2007         U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  made the following statement in response to a public announcement made today by Regions Air that it  is temporarily ceasing scheduled operations until further notice    Regions Air  operating as American Connection to St  Louis Lambert International Airport  serves the Quincy  Decatur  Marion Herrin  and Springfield airports        Early this morning  Regions Air announced that it was ceasing operations until further notice due to pilot training issues with the FAA   This is yet another example of how Regions Air has failed the tests of safety and service  Our downstate communities deserve better         I ve been in contact with the U S  Department of Transportation  American Airlines American Eagle  and the affected communities  all of whom share my lack of confidence in Regions         I began face to face conversations with American Eagle in Dallas nearly two weeks ago to replace Regions with a quality air carrier as quickly as possible   And when I alerted the Department of Transportation earlier this week that Regions wasn t living up to its Essential Air Service  EAS  commitments and shouldn t have their existing Illinois contracts renewed  they put into motion a plan to replace Regions in these markets         It s unfortunate that the airline that prided itself on its dependability and professionalism has demonstrated just the opposite in recent months   It s time for an air service change         We will continue our efforts  Passengers in downstate Illinois have already experienced more than their fair share of service disruptions and inconvenience
