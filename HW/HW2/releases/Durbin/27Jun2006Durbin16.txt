     DURBIN MOURNS LOSS OF 100TH SOLDIER FROM ILLINOIS IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        6 27 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN MOURNS LOSS OF 100TH SOLDIER FROM ILLINOIS IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Tuesday  June 27  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after the 100th soldier from Illinois was killed in Iraq   The state of Illinois has lost its 100th brave service member in the war in Iraq   They join the more than 2 400 other soldiers  sailors  airmen and Marines from across America who have  as President Lincoln so eloquently stated  given their last full measure of devotion   Families in Illinois and across America have given their best and their bravest    In order to truly honor the sacrifice of our fallen heroes  we must set a clear path to bring our troops home with their mission truly accomplished   That path begins with a clear message to the Iraqis   The nation of Iraq has been the focus of our attention and our resources for more than three years  Our commitment is not forever   It is time for the Iraqi government to stand  lead and defend its own nation
