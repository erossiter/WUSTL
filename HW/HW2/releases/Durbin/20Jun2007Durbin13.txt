     Durbin Announces Nearly  1 8 Million in Commerce Department Grants for Illinois Projects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         6 20 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  1 8 Million in Commerce Department Grants for Illinois Projects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Wednesday  June 20  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded  1 755 628 in federal grant funding to development projects in Illinois  Senator Durbin worked with Congressman Don Manzullo  R IL  to secure these projects  The Economic Development Administration grant will be used to fund the following   Freeport  Illinois   1 7 million in funding to support the construction of multiple improvements to the Mill Race Crossing Industrial Park in Stephenson County which could potentially create 300 new jobs and generate  18 million in private investment   Harrisburg  Illinois   55 628 in funding for the Southeastern Illinois Regional planning and Development Commission to support long term regional economic development planning effort
