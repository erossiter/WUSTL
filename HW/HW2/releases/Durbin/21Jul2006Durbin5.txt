     DURBIN  SENATE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE APPROVES  3 8 MILLION IN HEALTH AND EDUCATION PROJECTS FOR ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      7 21 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN  SENATE APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE APPROVES  3 8 MILLION IN HEALTH AND EDUCATION PROJECTS FOR ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Friday  July 21  2006   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee  of which he is a member  has approved  3 8 million in federal funding for health and education projects in Illinois       The Fiscal Year  FY  2007 appropriations bill for the Labor  Health and Human Services Departments Appropriations includes funding for the following projects      Access Community Health Network  Chicago    450 000 to provide behavioral health services in a community health setting   Catholic Charities  Chicago   350 000 for vocational training and support programs at the St  Leo Residence for Veterans   Cristo Rey High School  Chicago    300 000 for the Cristo Rey High School library and technology center   East St  Louis High School    300 000 to improve math and sciences programs at East St  Louis High School   Golden Apple Foundation  Chicago    350 000 for a teacher training initiative for undergraduate math and science programs   Heartland Partnership  Peoria    400 000 for construction of a cancer research laboratory   Holy Cross Hospital  Chicago   1 000 000 for information system and technology improvements   Illinois Primary Health Care Association  Statewide    200 000 for information system and technology improvements for community health centers   St  Leonard   s Ministries  Chicago    250 000 for job training and placement for ex offenders   St  Mary   s Good Samaritan  Inc   Mt  Vernon   100 000 for medical equipment to promote minimally invasive surgery   Swedish American Health System  Ogle County    100 000 to construct and equip a rural health clinic in Davis Junction
