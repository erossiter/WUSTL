STATEMENT FOLLOWING ANNOUNCEMENT BY SPECIAL COUNSEL PATRICK FITZGERALD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Friday  October 28  2005    CHICAGO  IL      It is a sad day for America when a federal grand jury indicts a high ranking White House official     We now know that the White House risked our national security by leaking to the press the identity of a CIA agent in an effort to discredit and silence a critic of the President   s invasion of Iraq   The American people know the cost of this war     more than 2 000 dead  more than 15 000 wounded U S  soldiers  hundreds of billions of dollars already spent and no end in sight     The special prosecutor is continuing his investigation  and it remains to be seen if other top advisors of the President will be indicted  The President should hold to his promise and any other officials who are indicted should be removed immediately  The White House needs a new team to restore the confidence of the American people in the President   s leadership
