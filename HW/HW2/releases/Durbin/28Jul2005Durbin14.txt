STATEMENT REGARDING FAA ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT STATEMENT FOR O   HARE MODERNIZATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Thursday  July 28  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  Environmental Impact Statement  EIS  for modernization at O   Hare International Airport       Earlier today  the FAA released the final Environmental Impact Statement for O   Hare modernization   After so many years of struggle  construction can now begin as early as September             I spoke last night with Federal Aviation Administrator Marion Blakey about the modernization of O   Hare   I think it   s clear that the FAA and the Department of Transportation understand the significance of this project for building aviation capacity in the Chicago region     which will help alleviate frustrating flight delays  create jobs  further economic development in the Chicagoland area and ensure that O   Hare remains a world class airport             I look forward to the formal Record of Decision from the FAA in September and for the long awaited beginning of O   Hare modernization             Administrator Blakey and Secretary Mineta deserve credit for their willingness to keep this project on track  I will continue to work with them  Governor Blagojevich  and Mayor Daley to make O   Hare modernization a reality
