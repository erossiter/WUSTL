     DURBIN S STATEMENT IN OPPOSITION TO GULF OF MEXICO ENERGY BILL     8 1 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN S STATEMENT IN OPPOSITION TO GULF OF MEXICO ENERGY BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Tuesday  August 1  2006   Washington  D C       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after the Senate passed S 3711  the Gulf of Mexico Energy Bill          The Senate passed a bill today that is not the answer to America   s rising gasoline costs or energy needs             America   s energy security demands a comprehensive solution   Any federal action to increase fossil fuel production must be coupled with policies that reduce demand through energy efficiency  increase the use of alternative fuels and encourage energy conservation             The U S  does not have enough domestic oil to meet our demand   We will not reach energy sufficiency by drilling for more   Not only is it short sighted  it will fail  Our natural resources are finite and the success of our nation depends     as it always has     on our ability to be innovative and develop technologies to use our energy resources wisely             Americans believe that we can and we must control our energy future   With our innovative spirit and technological know how  we have an opportunity to enhance our security  create American jobs and become truly energy independent
