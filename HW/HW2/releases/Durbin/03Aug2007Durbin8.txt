     Sens  Durbin and Casey on Senate Delegation to Iraq  Pakistan  Afghanistan and Jordan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            8 3 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Sens  Durbin and Casey on Senate Delegation to Iraq  Pakistan  Afghanistan and Jordan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Friday  August 3  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senate Assistant Majority Leader Dick Durbin  D IL  and Sen  Robert Casey  D PA  will lead an official visit to the Middle East and South Asia  The primary focus of the trip will be military and security issues  The senators will also explore ways the United States can support several developing democracies around the world     The delegation will be leaving today and traveling through the next week  Due to security concerns  details of the trip will not be released in advance  but Durbin and Casey will travel to Iraq and Jordan  and Durbin will also visit Pakistan and Afghanistan     In the coming months  Iraq and the Middle East will be front and center in our national debate  This trip will allow us to meet with the region s leaders and see firsthand our progress  said Durbin  I am also looking forward to meeting with Illinois servicemen and women who are stationed in the region      Durbin and Casey will also have a chance to assess the mounting Iraqi refugee crisis that is severely impacting the region  the political progress in Iraq and Afghanistan and the current security situation
