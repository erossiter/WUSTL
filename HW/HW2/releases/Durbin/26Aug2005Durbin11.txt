     DURBIN AND OBAMA STATEMENT REGARDING BRAC COMMISSION ACTION AFFECTING ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   8 26 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN AND OBAMA STATEMENT REGARDING BRAC COMMISSION ACTION AFFECTING ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  August 26  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senators Dick Durbin  D IL  and Barack Obama  D IL  today made the following statement regarding the Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission   s vote on the U S  Department of Defense   s recommendations regarding military bases in Illinois       The BRAC Commission has completed the decision making process for this round of closures and realignments   Overall  we can be proud that no Illinois military bases will close and that original estimates of job losses at Rock Island Arsenal have been decreased by half     retaining 84 percent of the original jobs in the Quad Cities             It   s easy to be discouraged by the shifting of TACOM and DFAS jobs at the Arsenal  removal of F 16 aircraft from Springfield  and reduction in the number of additional planes assigned to the Peoria Air National Guard unit and Scott Air Force Base   But the Illinois Congressional Delegation  the State  and the affected communities put forth solid arguments  compelling evidence  and a unified front in defense of our bases and the associated jobs          While we disagree with some of the Commission   s decisions  we will continue support our Illinois installations and look for ways to retain jobs through the Defense Department   s implementation of the BRAC Commission   s recommendations
