     Durbin Commends House Passage Of Paul Simon Study Abroad Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     6 5 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Commends House Passage Of Paul Simon Study Abroad Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Tuesday  June 5  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement after the passage of the Paul Simon Study Abroad Act in the House of Representatives    The strong support that the Paul Simon Study Abroad Act received as it passed in the House was very encouraging  said Durbin  An international study abroad program will help provide the next generation of Americans a deeper understanding of the cultures and histories of other nations  I will work to see that this legislation is considered in the Senate
