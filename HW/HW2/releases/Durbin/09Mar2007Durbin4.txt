     STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DICK DURBIN REGARDING 2nd DOWNSTATE REGIONS AIR SERVICE SHUTDOWN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       3 9 2007                                                                                                                                                                            STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DICK DURBIN REGARDING 2nd DOWNSTATE REGIONS AIR SERVICE SHUTDOWN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Friday  March 9  2007         U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  made the following statement in response to a public announcement made today by Regions Air that it has ceased operations for the second time this week        Two weeks ago  I sent letters to the U S  Department of Transportation  American Airlines  and the affected communities voicing my lack of confidence in Regions Air   Three weeks ago  I met in Dallas with the CEO of American Eagle to discuss this situation and to urge a prompt replacement of Regions Air         Today s shutdown  the second in a week  confirms my fears   Regions Air cannot provide even the most basic air service  let alone the reliable service our downstate communities deserve         The U S  Department of Transportation  working with the affected communities  has accelerated their efforts to find a reliable replacement carrier and will be announcing their decision shortly
