     BIPARTISAN RESOLUTION HONORS 1 5 MILLION VICTIMS OF ARMENIAN GENOCIDE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            11 18 2005                                                                                                                                                                            BIPARTISAN RESOLUTION HONORS 1 5 MILLION VICTIMS OF ARMENIAN GENOCIDE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Friday  November 18  2005       U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today joined a bipartisan group of Senators in introducing a resolution to honor the 1 5 million Armenians killed and the half million survivors exiled by the Ottoman government between 1915 and 1923   April 2005 marked the 90th anniversary of the Armenian Genocide        The Armenian Genocide was the Twentieth Century   s first genocide  a vicious  organized crime against humanity that included murder  deportation  torture  and slave labor   U S  clarity on this historical fact is of utmost importance and long past due     Durbin said     The Resolution calls on the President to issue a yearly message to commemorate the Armenian Genocide and to ensure that U S  foreign policy    reflects appropriate understanding    concerning human rights and ethnic cleansing relating to the Armenian Genocide        Recognizing the Armenian Genocide takes on added importance in the face of the genocide occurring right now in the Darfur region of Sudan     Durbin said      We must honor those who died in the Armenian Genocide by recognizing their suffering and by dedicating ourselves to preventing human suffering and tragedy in the future
