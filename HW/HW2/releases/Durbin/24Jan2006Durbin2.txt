STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF RICHARD NORTON SMITH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Tuesday  January 24  2006    WASHINGTON  DC     Today  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  made the following statement regarding the resignation of Richard Norton Smith       Those of us who have worked on the Lincoln Presidential Library for many years owe a special debt of gratitude to Richard Norton Smith for helping in its historic launch   His credentials as an historian were essential to establishing the reputation of the ALPC   Our job now is to find a worthy successor who will build on our early success and challenge all of us to find new ways to bring the Lincoln story to the world
