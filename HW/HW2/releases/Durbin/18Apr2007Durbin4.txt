INTRODUCE RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE CHICAGO ON OLYMPIC BID                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Wednesday  April 18  2007    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and  Congressman Rahm Emanuel  D IL  are planning to introduce a resolution this week expressing the sense of Congress that the City of Chicago should be commended for being chosen to represent the United States in the International Competition for the 2016 Olympic and Paralympic Games        It has been over 100 years since a Midwestern city has hosted the Olympic Games   said Durbin    Chicago is recognized as a city with a remarkable amount of diversity  culture and history that inspires people around the world   From its beautiful downtown parks to the magnificent lakefront to its terrific sports venues  Chicago is truly a world class city that has what it takes to bring the Olympics back to the United States         Bringing the 2016 Olympics to our nation s third coast will be a boon for Chicago and a gold medal for the United States   said Emanuel       On April 14  2007  Chicago was selected by the United States Olympic Committee to represent America in its bid for the 2016 Summer Olympic and Paralympic Games   The last time a Summer Olympic Games was held in a U S  city was in Atlanta  Georgia in 1996   Today s resolution encourages the International Olympic Committee to select Chicago as the site of the 2016 Olympic and Paralympic Games       On April 15th  Emanuel and Durbin  D IL  announced plans to introduce legislation that will ensure international Olympic officials  coaches and athletes receive visas efficiently in order to compete in the 2016 Summer Olympic Games       The legislation is cosponsored by Senators Barack Obama  D IL  and Ted Stevens  R AK  and Representatives Bobby Rush  D IL   Jesse Jackson  Jr   D IL   Daniel Lipinski  D IL   Luis Gutierrez  D IL   Peter
