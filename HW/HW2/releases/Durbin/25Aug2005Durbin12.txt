     DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING SMALL COMMUNITY AIR SERVICE DEVELOPMENT GRANT AWARD TO ROCKFORD AIRPORT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               8 25 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING SMALL COMMUNITY AIR SERVICE DEVELOPMENT GRANT AWARD TO ROCKFORD AIRPORT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Thursday  August 25  2005    ROCKFORD  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement after the announcement that the Northwest Chicagoland International Airport at Rockford  RFD  has been awarded a  1 million Small Community Air Service Development Grant by the U S  Department of Transportation       Earlier this summer  Senator Obama and I met with Transportation Secretary Norm Mineta to stress the importance of this grant for the Rockford community and the Illinois economy   Today   s announcement that  1 million in Small Community Air Service Development Grant funding has been awarded to RFD is great news for the people of Rockford   According to the Department of Transportation  Rockford received the largest grant of the 37 recipients nationwide             Rockford plays an important role in Illinois    regional aviation system and economic development   Operations at the Rockford airport are key to reducing air traffic at O   Hare and Midway Airports in Chicago   This grant will be a real benefit to RFD   s customers  carriers and the community             I will continue to work with Airport Authority Chairman Mike Dunn  the City of Rockford  Senator Obama  and Congressman Manzullo to ensure that RFD receives the federal support it needs to continue to attract airlines and passengers
