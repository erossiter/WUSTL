     Durbin Announces Nearly  800 000 in DOT Grants for Illinois     8 3 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  800 000 in DOT Grants for Illinois                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Friday  August 3  2007    WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  775 146 in grants to Scott Air Force Base and the Greater Peoria Regional Airport    Federal Aviation Administration     Scott Air Force Base  Belleville   718 146 in funding to improve airport erosion control and update the airport master plan study       Greater Peoria Regional Airport  Peoria   57 000 in funding to acquire safety equipment that will assist in monitoring and preserving perimeter security around the airport      On August 1  Senator Durbin met with Lieutenant General Arthur Lichte who has been nominated to receive a fourth star and to take command of Air Mobility Command  AMC  at Scott Air Force Base  AMC is responsible for all airlift and aerial refueling for the U S  military including the Active Duty  Reserve  and Air National Guard components  Scott AFB is the largest employer south of Springfield and is the fourth largest employer in the St  Louis region  It is estimated that Scott has an annual local and regional economic impact of well over  2 billion
