     STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DURBIN ON RESIGNATION OF TRANSPORTATION SECRETARY NORM MINETA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          6 23 2006                                                                                                                                                                            STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR DURBIN ON RESIGNATION OF TRANSPORTATION SECRETARY NORM MINETA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Friday  June 23  2006    Washington  D C       Assistant Democratic Leader Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement after the White House announced that Norm Mineta will be stepping down as Transportation Secretary        Secretary Mineta is a good friend and has been a tremendous ally for over 20 years   When we served together in the House  Norm Mineta recognized the need to invest in our nation   s transportation infrastructure and fought tirelessly for funding for roads  bridges  highways  transit  and high speed rail   When he served as Commerce Secretary under President Clinton  he played an integral role in crafting an economic policy that gave this nation its strongest period of economic growth in three decades   And  as Secretary of Transportation for the current Administration  he helped Illinois make significant inroads in advancing their transportation infrastructure  leading the charge in the development of several local projects  including the O   Hare modernization program           I am honored to have worked with Secretary Mineta and wish him the best as he prepares to leave the public arena for private life
