    6 14 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Meets with Transportation Secretary Mary E  Peters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Thursday  June 14  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today met with Transportation Secretary Mary E  Peters urging the Department of Transportation to move forward on several Illinois transportation issues including downstate Illinois air service  OHare modernization  and expanded Amtrak service in Illinois  Great Lakes Airlines was awarded the Essential Air Service contracts in Marion Herrin  Quincy and most recently Decatur  Illinois  Durbin has asked repeatedly that the airline work to make sure the necessary planes  personnel and equipment are in place so that service between downstate communities and St  Louis Lambert International Airport can resume safely and quickly  I made sure the Secretary understood that downstate Illinois communities have experienced months of travel  tourism and economic disruptions as a result of the air service shut down  said Durbin  The area has been without service for far too long and there is growing frustration over Great Lakes Aviations delay in restoring service  Secretary Peters assured me that she will continue to work with FAA  the airlines and the affected communities to restore service as quickly and safely as possible  On June 12  2007  Durbin sent a letter to the CEO of Great Lakes Airlines  Chuck Howell and the Chairman of the Board of Directors  Doug Voss asking that the airline work to begin providing scheduled passenger air service from Decatur  Marion Herrin  Quincy  and Springfield  Illinois to St  Louis as soon as possible  Durbin sent a similar letter to Mr  Howell  on April 11  2007  On March 19  2007  Durbin sent a letter to the President of American Eagle Airlines  Peter M  Bowler urging that the airline work with Great Lakes to begin service immediately
