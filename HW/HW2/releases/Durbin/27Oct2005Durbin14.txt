    10 27 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT ON BIG OIL S STAGGERING PROFITS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  October 27  2005           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  issued the following statement after Exxon Mobil reported the largest quarterly profits in U S  history       Illinois consumers  businesses and farmers have spent over  2 5 billion more this year for gasoline than last year   High energy costs are pushing family budgets to the limit and becoming a serious drag on our economy             Don   t blame hurricanes or Saudi sheiks for these outrageous gasoline prices   It   s the greed of Big Oil   Today  Exxon Mobil reported the largest quarterly profits in U S  history   9 9 billion     straight from your credit card to their corporate boardroom             Americans may not be surprised that Big Oil is posting their largest profits ever  but they are losing patience with the Republican led Congress for ignoring the high cost of energy   Americans need relief at the gas pump  help paying this winter   s heating bills and a long term plan to lessen our dependence on foreign oil  Instead they are getting left out in the cold             That is why I have proposed a Windfall Profit Tax on the largest oil and gas companies   My legislation ensures that the revenue generated by the Windfall Profit Tax will be used to provide a direct rebate to consumers  fund the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program  provide financial incentives to auto makers to make more efficient vehicles and provide States with additional resources for transit and highway projects
