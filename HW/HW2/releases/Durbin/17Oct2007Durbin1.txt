     Durbin Commends Decision to Suspend Doctor s License                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             10 17 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Commends Decision to Suspend Doctor s License                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wednesday  October 17  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement on the decision by the Illinois Department of Financial and Professional Regulation  IDFPR  to suspend the license of Dr  Jose Veizaga Mendez who is being investigated in connection with a string of deaths at a VA hospital in Marion  IL  Just as Dr  Veizaga Mendez is entitled to a fair hearing  the patients being treated in Illinois are entitled to peace of mind  said Durbin  The Department took the right action in suspending his license as the investigation into the deaths at the Marion VA Medical Center continues  I will continue to work with Senator Obama and the VA to determine what went wrong at the Marion VA facility and how it can be prevented in the future  Yesterday  Durbin and Senator Barack Obama  D IL  sent a letter to the Secretary of the IDFPR  Dean Martinez expressing their concerns with Dr  Veizaga Mendez practicing in the state of Illinois  They asked to be informed of the status of Dr  Veizaga Mendezs license to practice medicine in Illinois and the steps that IDFPR plans to take to ensure that no further Illinois patients are endangered pending the outcome of the IDFPRs investigation of Dr  Veizaga Mendez
