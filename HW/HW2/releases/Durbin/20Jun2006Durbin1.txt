     DURBIN CALLS FOR MINIMUN WAGE INCREASE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           6 20 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN CALLS FOR MINIMUN WAGE INCREASE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Tuesday  June 20  2006           U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement  calling on the Senate to pass a Democratic proposal to the Defense Authorization bill that would raise the minimum wage to  7 25 an hour        The Bush Administration and the Republican Congress have repeatedly said no to a minimum wage increase   There was a time when presidents of both political parties considered increasing the minimum wage a moral obligation           Today  the party that preaches to America about family values is failing to recognize the value of the families struggling to make ends meet on just  5 15 an hour   The Republican leadership would rather eliminate taxes for the wealthiest Americans instead of working on behalf of Americans looking for a decent wage  for an honest day   s work           Since Congress last increased the minimum wage in 1997 to  5 15 an hour  the real value of that wage has decreased by 20    Raising the minimum wage to  7 25 an hour would benefit 6 5 million Americans  60  of whom are women  and give millions of workers an extra  4 000 a year that they so richly deserve
