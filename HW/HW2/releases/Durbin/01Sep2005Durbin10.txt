1 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT REGARDING ILLINOIS ATTORNEY GENERAL   S ACTION TO PROTECT    S AIR NATIONAL GUARD BASE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Thursday  September 1  2005      IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding Illinois Attorney General Lisa Madigan   s filing of a temporary restraining order against the U S  Department of Defense and the Defense Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission to prevent the transfer of F 16 aircraft from the 183rd Fighter Wing in Springfield       When the BRAC Commission upheld the Department of Defense   s recommendation to move the 183rd Fighter Wing   s F 16s to Indiana  they clearly ignored the law requiring military value to be the number one criterion for making such transfers             More importantly  the Department of Defense overstepped their authority by removing the jets and realigning the National Guard base without the permission of the Governor     the commander in chief of the Illinois National Guard             I strongly support Attorney General Madigan   s request for a temporary restraining order to halt the short sighted and legally questionable actions of the BRAC Commission   I believe the federal lawsuit she filed earlier this year to block this move should receive expedited consideration             I will continue to work with Attorney General Madigan  Governor Blagojevich and the Illinois Congressional delegation to help keep the 183rd Fighter Wing flying in Springfield
