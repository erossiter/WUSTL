REP  MANZULLO  ROCKFORD COURTHOUSE CONSTRUCTION TO BEGIN THIS SUMMER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Thursday  March 9  2006    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and Congressman Don Manzullo  R IL  announced today that construction of a new federal courthouse in Rockford will begin this summer   Durbin and Manzullo made the announcement following today   s meeting with leaders from the U S  General Services Administration  GSA  in Durbin   s Capitol office in Washington        We now have assurances from GSA that they will begin construction on the Rockford Courthouse this summer     said Durbin      A new modern federal courthouse in Rockford will not only meet the needs of the judicial system  but will be a source of pride for the entire community        In today   s meeting  Acting GSA Administrator  David Bibb committed to a groundbreaking for the Courthouse by August   The GSA will begin with the site excavation and foundation work        Today   s announcement is great news for the people of Rockford and all of northern Illinois     Manzullo said     The new federal courthouse will revitalize downtown Rockford  It will mean hundreds of much needed jobs for our workers and a much more secure facility for our judges and the visiting public        The Rockford courthouse  which was built more than 25 years ago  is filled to capacity and has structural problems that impede efficient functioning of the court and would be prohibitively expensive to repair   In addition  the lack of a separate prisoner entrance to the building compromises security at the facility     To date  Durbin and Manzullo have secured over  41 million in federal funds for the construction of the new federal courthouse in Rockford  They will continue to work together this year to secure the remaining funding necessary to complete the project
