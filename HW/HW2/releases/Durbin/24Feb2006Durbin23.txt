                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  February 24  2006    CHICAGO  IL      U S  Senator Dick Durbin released the following statement on the pension agreement reached by United Airlines and the Association of Flight Attendants  AFA        I   m pleased the pension agreement reached by United Airlines and the Association of Flight Attendants has been approved by United   s flight attendants   On several occasions  Sen  Obama and I convened meetings to encourage an agreement between AFA and United Airlines   The negotiations have been lengthy and tough  and I applaud the dedication of both parties to reaching a final agreement          We all want to help United succeed   Sacrifices have been made from every corner of the United family over the course of the last several years to keep the airline strong   United   s return from bankruptcy is remarkable
