     Durbin Announces a Nearly  900 000 DOT Grant for Decatur Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 6 8 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces a Nearly  900 000 DOT Grant for Decatur Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  June 8  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded a  900 000 grant to the Decatur Park District for the Decatur Airport  The grant will be used for the rehabilitation of a runway and two taxiways  According to the DOT  this project will restore the structural integrity of existing pavements on the runway and taxiways which have outlived their useful lives
