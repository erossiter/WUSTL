     DURBIN CALLS ON PRESIDENT TO ENFORCE DEADLINES IRAQIS SET FOR THEMSELVES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         4 18 2007                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN CALLS ON PRESIDENT TO ENFORCE DEADLINES IRAQIS SET FOR THEMSELVES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Wednesday  April 18  2007         After meeting with President Bush at the White House today  U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  said that if the President doesn t want to implement timelines created by the U S  Congress  he should enforce deadlines for political reforms that the Iraqi government has set for itself        If the President doesn t want to enforce the timelines the U S  Congress has proposed for reduction of the number of American troops in Iraq  he should at least be willing to enforce timelines the Iraqi government has set for itself   said Durbin       Senator Durbin today called on the President to join Congress in enforcing political reforms along the timelines the Iraqi government has set for itself      Approve a provincial election law by early 2007  Approve a law to regulate the oil industry and share revenues by early 2007  Approve the de Baathification law to reintegrate officials of the former regime and Arab nationalists into public life by early 2007  Approve a law to rein in sectarian militias by early 2007  Approve laws controlling militias by May 2007  Approve the amnesty agreement by May 2007  Complete all reconciliation efforts by May 2007  Hold provincial elections by June 2007  Increase the number of battle ready Army units  Take over total control of the Iraq Army by April 2007  Be given full civil control of all provinces by September 2007  Achieve total security self reliance by December 2007
