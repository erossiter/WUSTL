     Durbin and Emanuel Statement on EPA Decision to Hold Hearing on U S  Steel Permit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                12 10 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin and Emanuel Statement on EPA Decision to Hold Hearing on U S  Steel Permit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Monday  December 10  2007    WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  and Congressman Rahm Emanuel  D IL  today released the following statement just a day before the Environmental Protection Agency  EPA  is schedule to hold a hearing on Indianas draft wastewater discharge permit for U S  Steel Corporations plant in Gary  Indiana  We applaud the EPA for objecting to a permit that would allow the US Steel Gary Works facility to avoid compliance with Clean Water Act standards for another five years  Additional pollutants in Lake Michigan would threaten a national treasure that provides safe drinking water to over one million people  Because of the efforts of various groups and legislators  concerned residents of Illinois and Indiana will be heard tomorrow  Earlier this year  we brought BP to the table and asked them to stop plans to dump more pollution into the Lake  We put pressure on a company to do what is right and they responded  Now  we are asking US Steel to do the same  It should be made clear that creating economic opportunity should go hand in hand with protecting the environment  On October 17  Durbin  Emanuel and Senator Barack Obama  D IL  sent a letter to the EPA Region 5 Administrator  Mary Gade calling for a public hearing on the Indiana Department of Environmental Management  IDEM  permit for U S  Steel Gary Works
