     DURBIN ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  7 MILLION IN DOT GRANTS FOR ILLINOIS AIRPORTS     9 12 2006                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  7 MILLION IN DOT GRANTS FOR ILLINOIS AIRPORTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Tuesday  September 12  2006   WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  7 850 000 in grants to the Morris Municipal Airport  Aurora Municipal Airport and Palwaukee Municipal Airport            Illinois airports are vital arteries that support our economy and national defense     Durbin said      Today   s grants will go a long way towards ensuring that these airports continue to serve their customers with maximum efficiency          According to DOT  entities receiving grants are      Morris Municipal Airport    1 000 000 to extend the taxiway and runway at the Morris Municipal Airport   Aurora Municipal Airport    850 000 to install an instrument landing system at the Aurora Municipal Airport   Palwaukee Municipal Airport   6 000 000 to acquire land and build new a new runway at the Palwaukee Municipal Airport
