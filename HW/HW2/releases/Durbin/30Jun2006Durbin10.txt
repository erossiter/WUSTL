BIPARTISAN LINCOLN COMMEMORATIVE DOLLAR COIN PASSES SENATE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Friday  June 30  2006   WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   co chair of the Abraham Lincoln Bicentennial Commission  today announced the Senate passed legislation which directs the Secretary of the Treasury to issue 500 000 one dollar commemorative coins to honor the 200th anniversary of the birth of Abraham Lincoln   The Lincoln Bicentennial Commission was established by Congress in 2001 to organize the national observance of the 200th anniversary of Lincoln   s birth and to recommend activities that will pay tribute to Lincoln on the occasion     The  1 commemorative coins will be issued in 2009  the year of Lincoln   s bicentennial  and will feature designs emblematic of the life and legacy of President Abraham Lincoln   The design for the coins will be selected by the Treasury Secretary  after consultation with the Commission of Fine Arts and the Abraham Lincoln Bicentennial Commission and will be reviewed by the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee        Abraham Lincoln is a true American hero  and his life can provide inspiration to Americans of any age and background     Durbin said      Lincoln demonstrated enormous courage and strength of character during the Civil War and throughout his life  ensuring that the United States remained one nation  united and inseparable   To honor the 200th anniversary of his birth  this special series of  1 commemorative coins will pay tribute to his life and work        In December  the Senate passed Durbin   s Abraham Lincoln Bicentennial One Cent Coin Redesign Act which which will issue a series of one cent coins to honor the 200th anniversary of the birth of Abraham Lincoln   The redesigned pennies will be issued in 2009  the year of Lincoln   s bicentennial  and will feature four designs on the reverse side of the coins  each representing a different aspect of the life of Abraham Lincoln   The full cost of producing the coins will be paid for by funds generated through coin sales   Any additional proceeds will help fund the celebration and the continued study of the life of President Lincoln     The Abraham Lincoln Commemorative Coin Act  S 811  was co sponsored by 71 members of the
