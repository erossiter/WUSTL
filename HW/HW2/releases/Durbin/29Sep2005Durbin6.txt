     DURBIN WINS VICTORY FOR UNINSURED IN ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    9 29 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN WINS VICTORY FOR UNINSURED IN ILLINOIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Thursday  September 29  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  this week won a victory on behalf of uninsured Illinoisans who are in high risk health insurance pools   High risk pools provide health insurance for individuals with medical conditions that make it impossible for them to qualify for health insurance coverage in the regular market     For the past few weeks  Durbin has been objecting to legislation in the Senate  S  288  the State High Risk Pool Funding Extension Act  that would result in funding cuts for Illinois and other states with a large number of people without health insurance   He has been working with Senators to negotiate a formula that is more fair to Illinois and the larger states     A compromise was reached last night that would help guarantee that Illinois would receive its fair share of federal grants for the operation of high risk pools   According to initial calculations of the compromise  Illinois would receive  5 4 million   Under the original bill  Illinois was scheduled to receive just  3 8 million in federal funds        This is a victory for Illinois and the people who depend on the federal government for their health coverage   I could not stand idly by when the legislative language in the previous Senate bill would have resulted in hundreds of Illinoisans losing their health insurance  The compromise reached today is a critical step in helping to get this legislation through Congress and to the President     Durbin said     Earlier this month  Durbin sent a letter in response to criticism that he was blocking funding that would help the uninsured   Durbin wrote     The new bill cuts funding to states with large numbers of uninsured people  such as Illinois  Colorado  Indiana  Maryland and Oklahoma  and increases funding to states with a smaller number of uninsured Americans   There is no reason a state like Illinois that has 1 8 million people lacking access to health insurance should suffer a funding cut of 60 percent while a state like South Dakota with 90 000 uninsured people sees its funding more than double  The money should follow the people in need  not be determined by politics
