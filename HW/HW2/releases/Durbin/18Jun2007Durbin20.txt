     Durbin Announces Nearly  1 1 Million in Dot Grant for Springfield Mass Transit District                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          6 18 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin Announces Nearly  1 1 Million in Dot Grant for Springfield Mass Transit District                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Monday  June 18  2007   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  1 048 042 in federal grant funding to the Springfield Mass Transit District  The Federal Transit Administration grant will be used to fund the following transportation projects    300 000 in funding for preliminary engineering on the multi modal transportation center and for the development of an environmental impact statement     446 250 in funding for the purchase of one bus and one paratransit vehicle      301 792 in funding for the purchase of one replacement bus   This is good news for the residents of Springfield  Durbin said  Todays grant will help ensure that the Springfield Mass Transit District has the necessary funds to replace some buses in its aging fleet with environmentally friendly and cost efficient buses  while continuing to provide its riders with quality service  Durbin is a member of the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee
