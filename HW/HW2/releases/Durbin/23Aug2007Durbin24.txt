     Durbin  NIE Confirms Need for Change                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             8 23 2007                                                                                                                                                                            Durbin  NIE Confirms Need for Change                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Thursday  August 23  2007    Washington  D C    U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today issued the following statement following the release of the National Intelligence Estimate on Iraq    Today s National Intelligence Estimate on Iraq doesn t tell us anything new  While our men and women in uniform are doing a remarkable job making the streets they patrol safer  the only way to ensure long term security and stability is for the Iraqi government to take control of the country  Todays report clearly states that the prospects for Iraqis to effectively govern Iraq are not good    The National Intelligence Estimate also makes it clear that intensifying divisions within the Shia coalition and continuing disputes between the Shia  Sunni  and Kurd factions within the government will act as an increasingly destabilizing force and make Iraq even less secure over the next six months  Meanwhile  our military continues to be strained by 15 month deployments  and equipment that is being stretched far beyond the limits of its serviceable life     As Congress considers our future course in Iraq  we remain committed to providing our soldiers every resource they need to fight effectively and come home safely  But it is time to begin the orderly redeployment of our troops so that they can begin coming home soon
