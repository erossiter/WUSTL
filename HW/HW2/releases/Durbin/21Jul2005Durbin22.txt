     DURBIN STATEMENT ON HHS SECRETARY LEAVITT   S VISIT TO CHICAGO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     7 21 2005                                                                                                                                                                            DURBIN STATEMENT ON HHS SECRETARY LEAVITT   S VISIT TO CHICAGO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Thursday  July 21  2005    WASHINGTON  DC      U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL  today made the following statement regarding U S  Secretary of Health and Human Services  HHS  Michael Leavitt   s visit to Chicago to tout President Bush   s Medicare plan  which will take affect later this year   Durbin is the sponsor of legislation in the Senate that would allow Medicare to negotiate lower prescription drug prices on behalf of seniors       Seniors in Chicago need a Medicare plan that actually lowers the cost of their prescriptions for the next 20 years   The President   s plan  which Secretary Leavitt is promoting  is not the answer             Come November  seniors in Chicago and all across America will have to wade through at least four different Medicare HMO plans and 17 prescription drug plans     each with different premiums  deductibles and drug coverage options   Seniors will have their hands full trying to figure out which is the best deal             Instead of simply allowing seniors to take their Medicare card to the pharmacy  the Bush Administration chose to turn this program over to the private sector   Instead of allowing Secretary Leavitt to negotiate with drug companies for lower cost prescriptions  the Bush Administration prohibited Medicare from negotiating on behalf of all seniors and left the job of negotiating prices to the insurance companies             Seniors deserve better   They deserve a simple  unified and comprehensive benefit
