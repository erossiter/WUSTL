SEN. WYDEN TO HOLD TOWN HALL MEETING IN WASHINGTON COUNTY
  U.S. Senator Ron Wyden will host a town hall meeting on February 21, 2008, in Beaverton. Washington County residents of are invited to attend the open forums to ask questions and voice their concerns.
   During his 1996 U.S. Senate campaign, Wyden promised to hold town hall meetings in every Oregon county each year. The event Beaverton is in the 11th year of these meetings, which now total over 400.
   Washington County Town Hall Meeting
  Date: February 21, 2008
  Time: 1:30 p.m. to 3 p.m.
  Location: Elsie Stuhr Center
  5550 SW Hall Blvd.
  Beaverton
