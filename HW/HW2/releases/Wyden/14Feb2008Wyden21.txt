SEN. WYDEN TO HOST HEALTH CARE TOWN HALL ON FEB. 20 IN MEDFORD
  On Wednesday, February 20, 2008, Oregon Senator Ron Wyden will host a community conversation on health care in Medford. Wyden, co-sponsor of the bipartisan Healthy Americans Act, is inviting Southern Oregon residents to attend and share their personal experiences in dealing with the health care system. 
   "Health care consistently ranks at the top of the list of issues that concern average Americans," Wyden said. "Hearing the personal experiences of those who deal with today's health care system is vital to understanding what needs to be done in the future." 
   The Health Care Town Hall is part of Wyden's week-long tour of the state to talk to and with Oregonians about their health care needs and what needs to be done to fix the health care system.
   WHO: Senator Ron Wyden 
   WHAT: Health Care Town Hall 
   WHEN: 10 a.m., Wednesday, February 20, 2008
   WHERE: Smullin Health Education Center, 2825 East Barnett Road, Medford.
   (Located on the Rogue Valley Medical Center Campus. Parking available in the structure next to the Smullin Health Education Center.
