SEN. WYDEN ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF HARRIET MIERS TO THE U.S. SUPREME COURT
  Sen. Ron Wyden (D-Ore.) today released the following statement on the nomination of White House counsel Harriet Miers to replace Justice Sandra Day O'Connor on the United States Supreme Court:
   "Harriet Miers is clearly a trusted White House counsel. Her nomination presents a largely unknown quantity to the Senate for confirmation. I look forward to working with my colleagues to find out more about her record and her beliefs about the law."
