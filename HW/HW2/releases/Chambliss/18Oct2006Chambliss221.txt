SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON TO HOLD SECOND FIELD HEARING ON ARMY CORPS OF ENGINEERS' MANAGEMENT OF RIVER BASINS
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., Chairman of the Senate Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition and Forestry, and U.S. Sen. Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., will hold a Committee field hearing Tuesday, October 24, at 9:30 a.m., EST in Columbus to address the effect of the Army Corps of Engineers' operation of the Apalachicola-Chattahoochee-Flint and Alabama-Coosa-Tallapoosa River Basins on Georgia's Agriculture Community. The Senators held a similar hearing in Gainesville earlier this year and promised to hold a second hearing downstream.  
  The hearing will be chaired by Chambliss and is open to the public and press. In addition, U.S. Rep. Phil Gingrey, R-Ga., and U.S. Rep. Lynn Westmoreland, R-Ga., will attend. The witness list is limited due to time constraints; however, interested parties can submit written testimony for the record within five business days following the hearing.
