SEN. CHAMBLISS, REP. LINDER, PRICE TO HOLD FAIRTAX NEWS CONFERENCE, RALLY ON APRIL 3 AS TAX DAY APPROACHES
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss (R-GA), Rep. John Linder (R-GA), Rep. Tom Price (R-GA) and Ken Hoagland of Americans for FairTax will hold a news conference and rally on Tuesday, April 3, at 10:30 a.m. to announce that they have re-introduced the FairTax in both the U.S. Senate and U.S. House. They will be joined by hundreds of grassroots supporters of the legislation, including members of Georgians for FairTax and Americans for FairTax.
