SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE HUD FUNDING FOR CITY OF PLAINS
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., today announced the City of Plains, Ga., will receive $96,224 for the construction and facilities buildout of the Plains History Resource Center.  
   The funds will be distributed through the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development's FY 2005 Special Project Grants program. 
   "These important programs fund a wide range of community initiatives, such as housing and rehabilitation, support services, public improvements and economic development projects in Plains," said Chambliss. "I will continue to work with my colleagues in support of the programs that mean so much to so many communities." 
   "I am pleased to announce this funding for the Plains History Resource Center," said Isakson. "It is so rich with historic materials and it is important to preserve them for future generations."
   For more information please visit the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development's website at http://www.hud.gov/.
