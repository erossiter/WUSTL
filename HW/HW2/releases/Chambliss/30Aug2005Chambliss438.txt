SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE FUNDS FOR BURKE COUNTY
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., today announced that Burke County will receive a $26,519 grant from the U.S. Department of Justice's to aid in local law enforcement needs. 
   The U.S. Department of Justice's Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program (JAG) will distribute the funding. JAG allows local governments to support a broad range of activities to prevent and control crime based on their own local needs and conditions. 
   The Burke County Sheriff's Department will use the funds to purchase law enforcement equipment.
   "This important program will fund a crucial community initiative in Burke County to better equip our first responders," said Chambliss. "I am honored to assist Georgians in their efforts to make our communities better places to live."
  "This funding will go a long way to help ensure that Burke County's first responders have the resources necessary to protect their community," said Isakson. "This grant fills an important need for the Burke County Sheriff's Department."
