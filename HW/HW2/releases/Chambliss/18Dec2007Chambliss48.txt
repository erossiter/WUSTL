SEN. CHAMBLISS COMMENTS ON LOSS OF FORMER GEORGIA HOUSE SPEAKER MURPHY
  U.S. Senator Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., today made the following statement regarding the passing of former Georgia House Speaker Tom Murphy who passed away late yesterday evening. 
  "Tom Murphy was a true champion for our great state and Georgians will reap the benefits of his many contributions for generations to come. He will always be remembered for his long-standing commitment to Georgia values, and his leadership will remain an inspiration to those in public service. Tom Murphy left Georgia better than he found it. 
  "Julianne and I would like to extend our most heartfelt condolences to Tom's family and friends during this most difficult time."
