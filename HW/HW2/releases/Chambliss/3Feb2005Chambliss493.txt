SEN. CHAMBLISS ISSUES STATEMENT ON STATE OF THE UNION
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., praised President George W. Bush for his resolve against terror and commitment to America's domestic needs that he included in his State of the Union Address. 
   "President Bush clearly has an aggressive and bold agenda for his second term. And I look forward to working with the President to strengthen many of our domestic programs and continue our efforts to fight and win the war on terror." 
   "President Bush's commitment to promoting freedom and democracy for all people continues to be unwavering. Additionally, I look forward to working with President Bush to ensure we do all we can to make America safer and more secure."
