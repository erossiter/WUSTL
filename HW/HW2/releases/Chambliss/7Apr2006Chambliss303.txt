SEN. CHAMBLISS TO APPEAR ON 'FOX NEWS LIVE WEEKEND'
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., will be a guest tomorrow, Saturday, April 8, on "Fox News Live Weekend," hosted by Tony Snow. Chambliss will discuss the latest regarding immigration reform pending before the United States Senate.
  Chambliss' interview is expected to begin at approximately 1 p.m. EST. Tune into the Fox News Channel for the interview.
