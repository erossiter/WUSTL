SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE HUD FUNDING FOR CHILDREN'S SHELTER IN CANTON
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., today announced that Cherokee County will receive $248,000 for the construction of the Cherokee County Emergency Children's Shelter in Canton, Ga.  
   The funds will be distributed through the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development's FY 2005 Special Project Grants program.
   "These important programs fund a wide range of community initiatives, such as housing and rehabilitation, support services, public improvements and economic development projects in Cherokee County," said Chambliss. "I will continue to work with my colleagues in support of the programs that mean so much to so many communities." 
   "I am pleased to announce this funding for the Cherokee County Emergency Children's Shelter, which will aid many of our young people who need it most," said Isakson. 
   For more information please visit the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development's website at http://www.hud.gov/.
