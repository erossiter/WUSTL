SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE FUNDS FOR MOREHOUSE COLLEGE, MOREHOUSE SCHOOL OF MEDICINE
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., announced today that Morehouse College has been awarded a $524,590 grant and the Morehouse School of Medicine will receive a $22,500 grant from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.  
   Morehouse College will use the grant for a program to increase the knowledge and skills of disadvantaged minority students. The Morehouse School of Medicine will use the funds for its National Center for Chronic Disease Prevention and Health Promotion Conference Support Program. 
   "Morehouse College and Morehouse School of Medicine are making tremendous academic strides in the educational and medical communities," said Chambliss. "I am pleased to help secure this funding that ensures the ongoing success of these important programs." 
   "I am pleased to announce these grants for Morehouse College and the Morehouse School of Medicine," said Isakson. "The educational advancements and medical research conducted by the school benefits so many Americans."
