SEN. CHAMBLISS PAYS TRIBUTE TO FORMER REP. FOWLER
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., issued the following statement regarding the passing of former Rep.  
 Tillie Fowler. 
   "Tillie Fowler was a very good friend whom I have known for many years. It is with a heavy heart and deep sadness that Julianne and I offer our prayers and condolences to her family and friends at this very sad time. 
   "Over the years, Tillie and I have worked together on many issues. When we served together in the House, I was deeply impressed with the respect Tillie quickly earned from our colleagues because of her expertise and insights on defense and national security issues. Tillie was known for digging into issues and fighting hard for the causes she believed in. She was also deeply dedicated to the people she represented and I am thankful for having had the opportunity to serve with her.
   "Tillie Fowler was a very special person, a great mother, a smart and successful legislator, and a good friend. I will miss her very much."
