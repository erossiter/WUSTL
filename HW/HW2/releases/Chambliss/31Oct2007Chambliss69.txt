SEN. CHAMBLISS COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF ED SCHAFER FOR USDA SECRETARY POST
  U.S. Senator Saxby Chambliss (R-Ga.), Ranking Republican Member on the Senate Agriculture Committee, today issued the following statement regarding the nomination of Ed Schafer to be the next Secretary of the U.S. Department of Agriculture.  
  "I am pleased the President has nominated former North Dakota Governor Ed Schafer to lead the Department of Agriculture. He has a strong background in public service, which will certainly be a valuable asset in his new role as Secretary. I look forward to getting to know Governor Schafer during the confirmation process and will work hard to fill this critical position for U.S. agriculture as soon as possible."
