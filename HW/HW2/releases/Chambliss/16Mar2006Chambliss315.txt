SEN. CHAMBLISS ISSUES STATEMENT ON GOV. KEMPTHORNE'S NOMINATION TO BE NEXT INTERIOR SECRETARY
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., issued the following statement in support of President Bush's nomination of Idaho's Gov. Dirk Kempthorne to serve as the next U.S. Secretary for Interior.  
  "I applaud President Bush for nominating Governor Kempthorne to serve as the next Secretary of Interior. As a Senator and as a Governor, Dirk Kempthorne has a proven track record demonstrating his dedication to public service and his outstanding leadership. He has served the citizens of Idaho well and I am confident he will serve the people of America well as the next Secretary of Interior. I look forward to working with my colleagues as the United States Senate to confirm his nomination."
