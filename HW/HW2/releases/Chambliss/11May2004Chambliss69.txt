SEN. CHAMBLISS TO APPEAR ON CNN'S LARRY KING LIVE, MSNBC'S HARDBALL
  Sen. Chambliss is expected to appear on CNN's Larry King Live and MSNBC's Hardball tonight.  
   Hardball airs at 7 p.m. ETon MSNBC
   Larry King Live airs at 9 p.m. ETon CNN 
   Chambliss, a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee, will discuss Maj. Gen. Antonio Taguba testimony before the Senate Armed Services Committee. 
   Taguba is the author of a report that exposed the Iraqi prisoner abuse scandal.
