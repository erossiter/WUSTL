SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE FUNDS FOR EMORY UNIVERSITY
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., announced today that Emory University has been awarded $1,094,805 for its Centers for Public Health Preparedness program.  
   U.S. Department of Health and Human Services' CDC Procurement and Grants Office will distribute the funding.
   "Emory University is making tremendous strides in the medical field and continues to provide the state and the nation with important resources to prevent and respond to public health concerns," said Chambliss. "I am pleased to help secure this funding that ensures the ongoing success of these important programs." 
   "Emory University has one of the finest medical research facilities in the country, and I know that these funds will lead to great advancements in the area of public health preparedness," said Isakson.
