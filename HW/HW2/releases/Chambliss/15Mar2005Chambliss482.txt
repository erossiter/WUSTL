SEN. CHAMBLISS COMMENTS ON BRAC COMMISSION
  Sen. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., member of the Senate Armed Services Committee today released the following statement regarding the White House nominations to the Defense Base Realignment and Closure Commission. 
   "President Bush has recommended nine capable and highly qualified nominees for this year's BRAC commission. These individuals have a tremendous job ahead of them and I know each one is committed to being fair and objective throughout the evaluation process. I am confident in the abilities of former Secretary Principi to lead this effort. I look forward to working with the commission in the best interest of our and our national security."
   For more information contact the Chambliss press office at 202-224-3423.
