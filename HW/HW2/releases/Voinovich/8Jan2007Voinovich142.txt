SEN. VOINOVICH ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF ZALMAY KHALILZAD TO U.N.
  Sen. George V. Voinovich (R-OH), a member of the Foreign Relations Committee, on Monday, Jan. 8, released the following statement regarding the nomination of Zalmay Khalilzad to be U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations:
  "I am pleased that the administration has moved rapidly to fill this vacancy. The next ambassador to the United Nations must continue the focus on true U.N. reform - making it more transparent, efficient and beholden to higher ethical standards. I look forward to meeting with Ambassador Khalilzad and to the confirmation hearings.".
