SEN. VOINOVICH SUPPORTS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF GONZALES AS ATTORNEY GENERAL
  Sen. George Voinovich (R-OH) issued the following statement today regarding the Senate's confirmation of Alberto Gonzales as Attorney General.  
 Gonzales passed by a vote of 60-36, with Voinovich's support. Voinovich issued the following statement: 
   "Judge Gonzales will serve ably as Attorney General. I was proud to support his confirmation and believe that his distinguished education, experience in public service and impeccable character will all be valuable assets to our nation as he serves in this important job. I'm especially looking forward to working with him to enact legislation to end racial profiling and help establish programs to train law enforcement leaders and their agencies on how to eliminate this practice."
