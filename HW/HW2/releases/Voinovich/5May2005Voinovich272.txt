SEN. VOINOVICH MEETS WITH INTERNATIONAL TRUST FUND FOR DEMINING AND MINE VICTIMS ASSISTANCE DIRECTOR
  On Wednesday, Sen. George Voinovich (R-OH) met with ITF Director Dorijan Marsic and ITF Deputy Director Goran Gacnik to discuss ITF's efforts in demining in Southeast Europe. 
   Voinovich is on an eight day trip to Slovenia and Croatia to meet with government officials.
