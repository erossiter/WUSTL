SEN. VOINOVICH MEETS WITH MACEDONIAN PRIME MINISTER
  Today, Sen. George Voinovich met with Vlado Buckovski, the Prime Minister of Macedonia. They discussed Macedonia's efforts to join NATO, and the European Union and also the status of Kosovo. Voinovich, a member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, has been instrumental in assisting Eastern European countries gain admittance into NATO.
