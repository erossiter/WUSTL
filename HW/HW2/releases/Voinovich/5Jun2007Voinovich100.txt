SEN. VOINOVICH ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SEN. THOMAS
  Sen. George V. Voinovich (R-OH) today released the following statement on the death of U.S. Senator Craig Thomas (R-WY):
   "I was extremely saddened to hear about Craig's passing. I admired his perseverance and dedication to his work, especially while undergoing treatment. He truly cared about the future of our nation and was passionate about making a positive impact on all of our lives. We are all going to miss him dearly. My thoughts and prayers are with his wife Susan and their family."
