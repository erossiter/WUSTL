SEN. VOINOVICH ISSUES STATEMENT ON ELECTION OF REP. BOEHNER AS HOUSE MAJORITY LEADER
  Sen. George V. Voinovich (R-OH) released the following statement today on the election of U.S. Rep. John Boehner (R-OH) as House Majority Leader. 
   "I am delighted. John Boehner is a responsible person who understands the heavy responsibility that has been entrusted to him. His election begins a new chapter for leadership in the House of Representatives and enhances what I like to believe is one of the most powerful Congressional delegations. I look forward to working with John for Ohio and the Nation."
