                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Comments on Judiciary Committee Approval of Mukasey Nomination                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Tuesday  November 6  2007      U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments after the Senate Judiciary Committee approved the nomination of Judge Michael Mukasey as Attorney General     I am pleased that the Senate Judiciary Committee took the important step of favorably reporting the nomination of Judge Michael Mukasey to the full Senate for its consideration  Judge Mukasey is an extremely qualified nominee  He has demonstrated an adherence to the rule of law and the independence and integrity necessary to serve as our next Attorney General  Judge Mukasey has earned bipartisan support and confidence from the committee and from those who have worked closely with him during his professional career  I was happy to offer my support during the Judiciary Committee confirmation process  and I believe that as a man of good judgment  clear thinking  and broad experience Judge Mukasey should be swiftly confirmed by the U S  Senate                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
