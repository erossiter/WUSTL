                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the death of Jean Sullivan of Selma  former Republican National Committeewoman                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Wednesday  May 31  2006          Jean Sullivan was one of the most remarkable people I  ve ever met in politics  She had a ferocious interest in it  and she clearly was one of the architects of the modern Republican Party in Alabama  Not only was Jean a powerful figure in the state  but she had tremendous contacts nationally  and there were few Republicans who ever ran for president who didn  t consult her  Jean was a great personal friend of mine and was instrumental in helping me get my job as an assistant United States attorney in Mobile  And there are many others who benefited from her support and interest over the years                                 2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
