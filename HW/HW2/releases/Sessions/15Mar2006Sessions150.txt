                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Lauds HELP Committee Passage of Bill To Provide Health Insurance To Working Families                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Wednesday  March 15  2006      WASHINGTON      U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that a key Senate panel approved legislation that would allow small businesses to band together in an effort to offer affordable group health insurance to working Americans                   The Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Committee  of which Sessions is a member  voted 11 to 9 to approve the   Health Insurance Marketplace Modernization and Affordability Act                      The legislation responds to escalating health care costs that are making it difficult for small and  family owned businesses to provide medical insurance to their employees                      Today the HELP Committee approved legislation that will provide long awaited relief for small businesses that struggle to find affordable health insurance for their employees     Sessions said    Though everyone feels the pinch from higher health and insurance costs  those hurt the worst are small businesses and individuals who don t get the advantage of large group rates and don t get the same tax advantages as larger businesses                      Under the bill  employers offering small business health plans would be able to choose between two plans    one a low cost  basic insurance policy  the other a higher cost option with comprehensive coverage  These small business health plans would be licensed in each state but would be structured so that they could be offered across state lines   The plans would also be fully insured to protect consumers                      I have heard from Alabama s business community for years that this was a matter of utmost importance  and I am pleased that the HELP committee has taken this important step towards remedying the problem     Sessions said                   Sen  Mike Enzi  R WY   chairman of the HELP Committee  who introduced the bill  said the bill also would increase competition within the insurance industry                     If small business health plans can harness the power of America  s small business owners  it would force the entire marketplace to respond     Enzi said    If we transform health insurance to a market where small employers and family owned businesses can demand better benefits at better prices  insurance companies would be forced either to keep up with the competition  or lose their market share                                   2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
