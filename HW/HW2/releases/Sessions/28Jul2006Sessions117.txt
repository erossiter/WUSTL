                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions Lauds TVA Electric Rates Cut                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Friday  July 28  2006                     WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following statement today in response to the Tennessee Valley Authority  s announcement of a 4 5 percent reduction in wholesale electric rates that will take effect Oct  1                     I was pleased to hear that the new TVA board voted today for a 4 5 percent rate reduction   Many families in Alabama are dealing with the high costs of energy  and every little bit saved helps   I am hopeful that TVA  s continued dedication to new nuclear power production  including work at the Bellefonte plant in Scottsboro  will allow them to offer low prices in the Valley for years to come                                   2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
