                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions  Statement on Water Wars Meeting                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Thursday  October 25  2007         The ongoing drought has cast light on the complicated issue of water allocation in the Southeast  which is critical to continued economic growth in our region  I felt it was important that President Bush directly hear how this issue impacts Alabama when I met with him recently at the White House  I am pleased that he is sending Interior Secretary Dirk Kempthorne and several trusted advisors to meet with the governors so they have the opportunity to learn more about this situation  It is very important that the federal government not take any action that strikes an unfair balance between our states     I am also pleased that Govs  Riley and Perdue have agreed to come to Washington next week to meet with Sens  Chambliss  Isakson  Shelby and me  I will be pleased to support Gov  Riley as he and the other governors work to produce a fair water allocation plan for our states                              2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
