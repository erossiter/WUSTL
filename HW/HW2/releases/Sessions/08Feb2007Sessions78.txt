                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Northrop Grumman Team To Compete For Tanker Contract                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  February 8  2007         Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on Northrop Grumman s decision to compete for the Air Force tanker contract         The announcement by the Northrop Grumman team is good news for the warfighter  the taxpayer and for Alabama and Mobile  and I believe that the Northrop team will present a competitive proposal  Ultimately  the warfighter will have an asset that can be relied on in times of crisis  and the taxpayer will get the best value because of the competition  As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  I will do all I can to insure that the Air Force is in a position to make a final selection by October                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
