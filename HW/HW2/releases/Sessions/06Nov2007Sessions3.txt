                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions Comments on Harper Lee Receiving the Presidential Medal of Freedom                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Tuesday  November 6  2007      U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments after President Bush presented distinguished author and Alabamian Nelle Harper Lee with the President Medal of Freedom    I was pleased to be in attendance at the White House to see President Bush present Harper Lee with the Presidential Medal of Freedom  The Medal of Freedom is the highest honor that a President can bestow on a civilian  It is a well deserved honor for a woman whose literary work inspires us to stand firm for what is right  even when that sometimes means standing alone    Harper Lee  by this work  has made an historic contribution to America and the civilization of which we are a part  While the story unflinchingly describes injustice and discrimination  it also highlights and affirms the highest and best values of our people in the Christian tradition     Much has changed in the 45 years since To Kill a Mockingbird was first published  but the books themes of honor  courage  and integrity still resonate  not just in the South  but throughout the world  The work is routinely included on lists of the most influential books of the twentieth century  and remains required reading for school students and in law school     It is difficult to overstate the impact of this work on America  It remains the most read book in high schools today  Over 30 million copies have been sold with millions around the world reading it  Ms  Lee made nothing less than a great contribution to American society when she wrote To Kill a Mockingbird  and it is entirely fitting that President Bush honor her with this tremendous award  Ms  Lee is a native of Monroeville  and she has certainly made her state proud                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
