                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of Sen  Jeff Sessions on the nomination of Judge John G  Roberts Jr  to the Supreme Court                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Tuesday  July 19  2005        With the nomination of Judge John G  Roberts Jr   I believe President Bush fulfilled his commitment to appoint an individual who is committed to restraint and not legislating from the bench   I am very pleased   Judge Roberts  a Harvard Law School graduate  has served our nation superbly as a circuit court judge on the U S  Circuit Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia       I hope the Senate will approach this nomination in a fair and expeditious manner so that Judge Roberts can be ready to join the high court when it reconvenes in October  This should be a straightforward confirmation because the Senate confirmed by unanimous consent his circuit court nomination in 2003  During his confirmation  the American Bar Association rated him   well qualified     its highest rating       He was a fabulous witness when he testified at confirmation in 2003 for the D C  Circuit and has won the respect of Democrats and Republicans alike for his legal skill       Once again  President Bush has not disappointed   He has chosen as his nominee one who is probably the most capable person in America for this job                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
