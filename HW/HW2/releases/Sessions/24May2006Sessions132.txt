                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions issued the following statement after the Senate voted to waive a budget point of order on the immigration bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Wednesday  May 24  2006          I was glad to see that 30 of my colleagues joined me in support of the budget point of order  which is six more senators than opposed an earlier motion to end debate and move to final passage   Even though most members seem bent on moving the bill to conference with the House  this vote reflects  I think  a growing concern with the Senate immigration bill   The fact that we had no official estimate of the cost of the bill after the first 10 years was very troubling to many    even those who voted to waive the budget limits  I remain convinced that this bill needs to be pulled from the floor and given a complete overhaul                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
