                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions comments on base closure commission nominees                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Wednesday  March 16  2005      WASHINGTON    President Bush has nominated nine people  including former Department of Veterans Affairs Secretary Anthony Principi  to serve on the Base Realignment and Closure Commission  BRAC   The nominations will go to the Senate Armed Services Committee for consideration  and the full Senate will vote on the nominees  Sen  Jeff Sessions  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today made the following statement on the BRAC nominees         It  s good to see our friend Anthony Principi appear as the President  s nominee to lead as chairman of the 2005 BRAC Commission  I have said recently that I strongly believe Alabama s military installations are enduring  facilities that could benefit from the BRAC process  We possess in Alabama the capabilities needed not only for today  but for those roles and missions yet to be assigned by the Department of Defense         Former Department of Veterans Affairs Secretary Anthony Principi  s nomination will be well received by both sides in the Senate Armed Services Committee  However   I am disappointed that former General John Coburn  retired  will not serve as a member of the BRAC Commission   His vast military expertise and knowledge will be missed  But I am working with Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist on a replacement nominee that I believe the White House will move expeditiously         Alabama has much to be proud of as a state which historically has greatly contributed to the nation  s defense  The BRAC process can be somewhat unnerving for our communities  but working with Governor Riley  Senator Shelby and the other members of our congressional delegation we are meeting the task head on  with one purpose in mind    to make our nation strong today and into the future                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
