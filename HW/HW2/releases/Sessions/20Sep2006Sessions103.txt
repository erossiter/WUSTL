                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Applauds Senate Vote On Border Fence                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Wednesday  September 20  2006                   WASHINGTON    The Senate today voted to move forward on the Secure Fence Act of 2006  a House passed measure that would authorize the construction of 700 miles of fencing along the U S  Mexico border  The Senate already has passed amendments sponsored by U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  to build and fund a border fence                    This was a very important vote that allows us to continue moving forward to secure our borders     said Sessions    There is a danger that any amendments to the Secure Fence Act could either gut the impact of the legislation or complicate it so that it never becomes law  My eventual goal is to pass real comprehensive immigration legislation  but first we need to secure our borders  and the bill that cleared a first procedural hurdle today will move us in that direction                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
