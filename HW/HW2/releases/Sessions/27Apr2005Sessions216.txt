                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sens  Sessions And Shelby Lift Their   Hold   On Army Corps Of Engineers Nominee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Wednesday  April 27  2005      WASHINGTON    Sens  Jeff Sessions and Richard Shelby today lifted their hold on the confirmation of John Paul Woodley Jr  to be assistant secretary of the Army for civil works                 Sessions and Shelby met Tuesday with Woodley  who gave assurances that Georgia is not getting favorable treatment in a decade old water dispute among Georgia  Alabama and Florida                 The Alabama congressional delegation recently told the Corps in a letter that it was showing favoritism toward Georgia because Atlanta is taking more than its fair share of water from Lake Lanier  adversely affecting water levels downstream on the Chattahoochee River in Alabama and Florida                 Sessions and Shelby put a hold on Woodley  s nomination in an effort to ensure that the delegation  s concerns were addressed                   We had a good conversation with Mr  Woodley and he agreed not to take any action that will adversely affect any state     Sessions said    I am hopeful that the Corps   assurances will help to create a good working environment for the governors of Alabama  Georgia and Florida to negotiate in good faith to resolve this dispute                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All
