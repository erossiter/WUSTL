                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions Announces  4 2 Million In Job Training Grants For Two Alabama Community Colleges                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Grants Go To Trenholm State and Bevill State                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Monday  December 11  2006                   WASHINGTON     Two Alabama community colleges have been selected to receive  4 2 million in job training grants  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today                  Sessions  a member of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  learned of the grants in a phone call from U S  Department of Labor Secretary Elaine Chao                 According to the Labor Department  the grants were awarded under the President  s Community Based Job Training Initiative                   To remain competitive in the global economy  American industry needs a well trained workforce with the skills to meet today  s advanced industry and manufacturing challenges  These grants will help provide top notch training to Alabamians in the fields of healthcare and energy     Sessions said                    The Alabama grants were among 72 such grants nationwide announced today by the Labor Department  totaling  125 million  The department narrowed down the 72 recipients from 429 applications                 The two Alabama community colleges receiving the Labor Department grants are                                                                    H  Councill Trenholm State Technical College     2 3 million to expand the availability of nursing and health education programs in South Central Alabama  especially in economically depressed rural areas of the Black Belt                                                                   Bevill State Community College     1 9 million to offer an expanded mining curriculum in mine safety and mine systems technology                   Community colleges are vital partners in educating and preparing workers for good jobs in their local area     Labor Secretary Chao said    The  125 million these 72 community college partnerships will receive under the President  s Community Based Job Training Initiative is going to help workers succeed in careers in health care  advanced manufacturing and other growing industries                    Introduced by President Bush in his 2004 State of the Union Address  the purpose of the training grants is to build community colleges   capacity to equip workers with the skills needed by local industries                              2004
