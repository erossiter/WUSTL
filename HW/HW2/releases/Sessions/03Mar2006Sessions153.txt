                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on Senate confirmation today of Howard Thrailkill to the TVA board                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Friday  March 3  2006        I am pleased to announce that the Senate today confirmed the nomination of Howard Thrailkill and five other nominees to the Tennessee Valley Authority Board of Directors  As Alabama  s first representative on the board  Howard will bring to the position a fresh perspective  great integrity and a proven record in business  plus a real interest in the Tennessee Valley  I predict he will be a key participant as TVA prepares for a new day                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
