                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Announces Additional Alabama Counties Hit By Tornadoes Are Eligible To Receive Federal Disaster Assistance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Tuesday  March 6  2007           WASHINGTON   U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has declared Dale  Dallas  Henry  Montgomery and Wilcox Counties eligible for Individual Disaster Assistance  The declaration opens up federal disaster funding for individuals affected by severe storms and tornadoes on March 1  2007  Coffee County was previously declared eligible        FEMA also declared Coffee  Henry and Wilcox Counties as eligible for Public Assistance  The Public Assistance declaration makes funding available for state and local governments and nonprofit organizations working to repair facilities damaged by the severe weather          I toured many of the areas hit by the storms last week and I was shocked at the devastation   Sessions said   I am glad to see that FEMA has quickly responded to the disasters by assessing the damage and recognizing the areas in need of assistance         President Bush made the initial disaster declaration on March 3  2007  which triggered the FEMA announcements that funding is available                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
