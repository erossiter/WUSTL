                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  on the death of Vivian Malone Jones                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  October 13  2005        Vivian Malone Jones was a lady of grace and poise who was in every way a worthy representative of all those who had been wrongly denied the right to enter their University of Alabama  In those times of tension and hostility  she demonstrated courage and steadfastness in taking the lead to end segregated higher education in Alabama  Despite the hostility she faced  she remained a strong proponent of reconciliation and forgiveness  consistent with the best values of her Christian faith                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
