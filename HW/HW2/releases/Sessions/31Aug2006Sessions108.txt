                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Announces  2 7 Million Grant For Ozark Airport Runway Project                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Thursday  August 31  2006                WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today announced that the City of Ozark has been awarded a  2 7 million grant from the U S  Department of Transportation to complete the final phase of a runway extension at Blackwell Field      According to the Transportation Department  the grant will be used to provide lighting and signage for a taxiway and extend the existing runway and install lighting        I am pleased that the funding will allow for the completion of this important expansion at Blackwell Field     said Sessions    The airport is critical to future economic development  in the area         Ozark Mayor Bob Bunting said the extension will bring the runway out to 5 235 feet        This  will allow corporate jets to land here safely and will support the expansion of U S  Helicopter and other new aviation industries that are locating at Blackwell Field     Bunting said                              2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
