                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of Sen  Sessions on Sen  Reid s Decision to Pull Immigration Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Thursday  June 7  2007         WASHINGTONU S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  released the statement below after Sen  Harry Reid  D NV  pulled the immigration bill from the Senate floor  following a failed vote to limit debate    Im pleased that the majority leader pulled the immigration bill from the Senate floor tonight  I think tonight s cloture vote is a result of senators hearing directly from their constituents and learning of their sincere concern about this legislation  The American people have a fundamental understanding that our immigration system should be effective and lawful  and they recognized that this bill does not achieve those goals     Our immigration system is comprehensively broken  and it needs to be comprehensively fixed  The best way to achieve that is through thoughtful deliberation in the traditional committee process  I look forward to further work on this extremely important issue in the future                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
