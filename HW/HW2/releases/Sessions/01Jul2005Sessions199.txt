                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the retirement of Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O  Connor                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Friday  July 1  2005        Justice O Connor should be commended for her 24 years of service on the nation s highest court  She has served with grace and honor  As we move forward to confirm a replacement  I hope that we approach our advice and consent duty with the dignity that is representative of Justice O  Connor  s service          This confirmation should not be about politics  but should focus on a nominee  s judicial philosophy  integrity and competence   While outside groups undoubtedly will try to influence the process  I hope that we can avoid the character assassinations that have been too common   I know that President Bush will send over an extremely qualified nominee of the caliber he has nominated previously  and I look forward to the confirmation process                                2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
