                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions Lauds HELP Committee Passage of Mine Safety Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wednesday  May 17  2006      WASHINGTON    A Senate panel today approved a mine safety bill that included a provision by Sen  Jeff Sessions to establish the Brookwood Sago Mine Safety Grant Program in remembrance of the 2001 tragedy at the No  5 mine in Brookwood  Alabama   The grant program will provide funding for education and training programs to better identify  avoid  and prevent unsafe working conditions in and around mines        The legislation is called the Mine Improvement and New Emergency Response Act  MINER Act   It was passed out of the Senate Committee on Health Education  Labor  and Pensions  HELP   of which Sessions is a member       In passing this legislation  we have taken practical steps to make mines safer  while also paying tribute to the mine workers and rescue team members who died in the 2001 accident in Brookwood and the recent accident in Sago     Sessions said    The Brookwood miners lost their lives in a heroic effort to save their trapped colleagues  They represent the best of American courage and selflessness                                 2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
