                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on Supreme Court Decision Upholding Partial Birth Abortion Ban                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Wednesday  April 18  2007                     WASHINGTON U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today issued the following statement after the Supreme Court s decision in the case of Gonzales v  Carhart upholding the Partial Birth Abortion Ban Act of 2003                    As an original cosponsor of the Partial Birth Abortion Ban Act of 2003  I am especially pleased that today the Supreme Court upheld the statute as a valid  constitutional restriction on a particularly abhorrent form of abortion  Today s decision marks an important step toward restoring a culture of respect for life  It also represents a defeat for liberal judicial activism and a victory for the people  speaking through their elected representatives in Congress  Overwhelmingly  the American people oppose partial birth abortion                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
