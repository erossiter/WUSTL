                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions to Take Seat on Armed Services Committee s Seapower Panel                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Thursday  August 2  2007         WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that he has accepted a seat on a key Senate subcommittee with primary oversight over the majority of U S  Navy and Marine Corps programs   The panel  known as the Subcommittee on Seapower  is part of the Senate Committee on Armed Services  on which Sessions has served since he came to the Senate in 1997    The Seapower Subcommittee has jurisdiction over most U S  Navy and Marine Corps programs and procurement activities  as well as strategic transportation activities   I am pleased to accept this assignment on the Seapower Subcommittee  Sessions said  I look forward to working with my colleagues on the committee to make certain that the United States Navy has the resources  technology and manpower necessary to maintain its preeminent position    In addition to the Seapower Subcommittee assignment  Sessions will continue as the Ranking Member on the Strategic Forces Subcommittee and as a member of the Airland Subcommittee  making him the only member of the Senate to serve on subcommittees with jurisdiction over military activities on sea  land  air  and space    The assignment comes as a result of U S  Sen  John Ensign  R NV  leaving the Armed Services Committee for a seat on the Finance committee    In conjunction with the move  Sessions will leave the Readiness and Management Support Subcommittee                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All
