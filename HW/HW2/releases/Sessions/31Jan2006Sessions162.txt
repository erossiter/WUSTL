                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the death of Coretta Scott King                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Tuesday  January 31  2006        The death of Coretta Scott King  a Perry County  Alabama  native  marks yet another sad passing of an historic leader in the movement for equal rights in America  She courageously stood with Dr  King and Rosa Parks to commence the modern civil rights movement with the Montgomery bus boycott  Through many dangers  toils and snares  she served as a partner of Dr  King in the monumental and peaceful drive for equality  All Alabamians should be proud of Mrs  King  s role in that effort to make America and Alabama a better place                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
