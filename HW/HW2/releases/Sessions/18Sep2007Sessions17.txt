                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions  Statement on Michael Mukasey                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Tuesday  September 18  2007       WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments today after meeting with Michael Mukasey  President Bushs nominee to be Attorney General    Judge Mukasey appears to be a man of character and integrity  two qualities that I believe the next Attorney General must possess  The Attorney General must have a backbone strong enough to stand up to the President when he is wrong to avoid errors  and vigorous enough to defend the Presidents legitimate actions before tough congressional committees  Judge Mukasey has an excellent background  serving as an Assistant United States Attorney in possibly the toughest section of Manhattan and as a federal judge in the Southern District of New York for 18 years  It is important that the Attorney General have experience on the front lines of the Department of Justice  and I think his time as a federal judge will give him a certain independence that is also necessary  I look forward to the confirmation hearings and to learning more about Judge Mukasey     I am pleased that some of our democratic colleagues have indicated that they believe Judge Mukasey has the experience and independence to warrant their support  The Department of Justice is a critically important part of our government  Judge Mukaseys challenge will be to act as an effective leader of the department  immediately addressing existing issues and problems in such a way that earns the publics trust and restores credibility in the organization  It is vital that the Senate move expeditiously with the confirmation process                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
