                                                                               Sessions  comments on TVA s decision to proceed with nuclear application                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Thursday  September 27  2007      WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments today after the TVAs Board of Directors approved the submission of an application to the Nuclear Regulatory Commission for a Combined Construction and Operating License for Bellefonte Nuclear Plant near Scottsboro  Alabama    I am pleased that TVAs Board of Directors voted today to move forward with the NRCs application process for two new reactors at the Bellefonte site near Scottsboro     We  as a nation  must dedicate ourselves to a long term transition to utilizing more nuclear power  Nuclear power is our best source of clean  safe  and reliable energy  and it can be produced here at home without any further reliance on foreign sources of energy  Increasing our ability to generate nuclear power is a critical step to providing the Valley with the affordable base load power that its growing economy will require    Todays vote is a significant step toward utilizing the  4 5 billion investment that TVA made at Bellefonte in the 1980s  Putting Bellefonte to good use for ratepayers has been a priority of mine since I came to the Senate  and I applaud the Board for supporting this application                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama
