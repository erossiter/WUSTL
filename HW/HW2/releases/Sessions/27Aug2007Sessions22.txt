                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions Comments on the Resignation of Attorney General Alberto Gonzales                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Monday  August 27  2007       WASHINGTONU S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments today regarding the resignation of Attorney General Alberto Gonzales   Mr  Gonzales is a patriotic American who has dedicated over a decade of his life to the service of his country  The Department of Justice is a critical part of our government which affects the daily lives of millions of Americans  It is imperative that the public have a fundamental confidence in its management and trust in its effectiveness  Mr  Gonzales may have lacked the necessary experience to be an effective leader of the more than 90 000 Department of Justice employees  Moving forward  the focus should be on what is best for the Department  I am hopeful that President Bush will put forth a strong nominee with the experience and personal characteristics necessary to lead the Department of Justice in a manner that restores the publics confidence                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
