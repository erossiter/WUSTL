                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions Lauds Successsful THAAD Missile Test                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Missle was built by Lockheed Martin at Troy  THAAD program is executed by Project Office in Huntsville                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Thursday  May 11  2006                    WASHINGTON    The Missile Defense Agency announced that a test today of the Terminal High Altitude Area Defense  THAAD  Ballistic Missile Defense System element was successful in meeting all test objectives   Following is a statement from U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL   a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee        I am elated with this morning  s successful launch of the THAAD interceptor missile from its mobile launcher at the White Sands Missile Range in New Mexico  Alabama can take special pride in the accomplishment because the missile was built at Lockheed Martin Corporation  s plant in Troy  The Terminal High Altitude Area Defense  THAAD  missile met all of its test objectives  according to the Missile Defense Agency  THAAD is a crucial interceptor well on its way to proving it is a missile killer of the first magnitude  This successful launch should move us further towards fielding this critically important part of our defense shield  Once again  Alabama workers are proving their mettle in producing some of the world  s most sophisticated space and missile components  As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  I will continue to push for funding and continued testing of this system  emphasizing deployment to ground forces in the near future                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
