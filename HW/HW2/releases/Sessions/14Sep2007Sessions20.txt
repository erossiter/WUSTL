                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Sessions Applauds Pentagon Decision on UAV Program Management                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Friday  September 14  2007        WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  applauded a decision today by the Department of Defense to allow control of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle programs to remain with the services that use them to protect and assist troops on the ground   Senior Pentagon leadership rejected a proposal by the Air Force that would have designated that service as the Executive Agent for UAV systems  effectively granting them control over medium and high altitude UAV programs    Sen  Sessions has opposed such a proposal because it would have lessened Army and Marine Corps units of participation in UAV development and support   I was pleased to learn today that the Defense Department has recognized the importance of ensuring that our troops in the field have access to UAV support when and where they need it  Sessions said  I am proud that the state of Alabama plays such a vital role in providing our troops with the UAVs they need to fight terrorism and ensure our national security here at home  It makes sense that the men and women who directly depend on UAV support for their missions success have a say in how those systems are designed and operated  The decision is a significant step forward for the potential of joint interoperability    The Army UAV Center of Excellence  which Sessions strongly supported creating  is located at Ft  Rucker near Dothan  Much of the technical work on Army UAVs takes place at Redstone Arsenal in Huntsville    The decision was made yesterday at a meeting of a Pentagon working group known as the Deputys Advisory Working Group                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All
