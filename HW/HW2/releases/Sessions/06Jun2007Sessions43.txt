                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Sessions Comments on Improvement in Test Scores among Alabama Elementary and Middle School Students                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Wednesday  June 6  2007         WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today made the following comments on a study released yesterday by the Center on Education Policy showing that since 2002  Alabama students have improved in reading in all grades but high school    I was pleased to see that Alabama elementary and middle school students have posted improved test scores in reading and math  This is good news for our state and underscores the continued success of the Alabama Reading Initiative  The one area that showed a lack  however  was reading among high schoolers  The Striving Readers Act  a bill I have introduced in the Senate with Sen  Patty Murray  D WA   would focus more attention on improving reading in high school students  Research shows that if they are given adequate attention and intensive work  high school students can show significant improvement  Improved reading skills will help improve students performance in all courses and reduce dropout rates                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
