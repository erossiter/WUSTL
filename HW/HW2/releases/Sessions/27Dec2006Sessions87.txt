                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Senator Jeff Sessions on The Death of President Gerald Ford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Wednesday  December 27  2006      MOBILE    U S  Senator Jeff Sessions released the following statement today on the death of President Gerald Ford          Gerald Ford never sought the presidency  but once in office he used the position to help heal our nation and restore confidence in government  Gerald Ford rose to the challenge as president and will be remembered for his modesty  integrity and bedrock decency                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
