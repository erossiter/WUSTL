                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Comments On House Decision To Vote On Senate Offshore Energy Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Friday  December 1  2006         WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today issued the following statement regarding the announcement that the House of Representatives next week will consider S  3711  the Gulf of Mexico Energy Security Act  Sessions helped negotiate the proposal with fellow Gulf state senators and the Senate leadership                      At this juncture  it is vitally important that the House agree to pass the Senate bill  While its bill is even stronger  with the results of this past election  this congressional session may be the only chance we have for years to come to produce more oil offshore  This legislation opens up 8 million acres to production    all of which is at least 125 miles from the Alabama Gulf Coast    and it is historic in that for the first time the Gulf Coast states will get a share of the oil and gas revenue from production  This could mean millions of dollars in revenues for Alabama in years to come as production increases  I want to compliment Congressman Jo Bonner for his tireless work to end the stalemate by encouraging the House leaders to accept the Senate version  As a practical matter  there is nothing more Congress can do this year to help America reduce its dependence on foreign oil than passing this legislation  To let it slip away would be unthinkable                                   2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
