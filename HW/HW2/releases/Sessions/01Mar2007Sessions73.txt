                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement Of U S  Senator Jeff Sessions On Deadly Alabama Tornadoes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  March 1  2007       My deepest sympathy goes out to the families of those who have lost loved ones and to those who have suffered injuries   The losses of our children at school in Enterprise is especially heartbreaking                     I want to express my sincere appreciation to Governor Riley  the Alabama Emergency Management Agency  and National Guard and to Major General Packett and his superb team at Fort Rucker for their prompt response                       I have earlier today joined with the other members of the Alabama congressional delegation in urging President Bush to quickly declare the damaged counties as disaster areas                     I plan to tour the damage in Alabama tomorrow   Clearly this storm has affected many  many people in Alabama  and I and my office will do all we can to ensure that the full resources of the federal government are quickly deployed to the state                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
