                                                                               Sen  Jeff Sessions Announces  3 5 Million Grant For Manufacturing Workforce Training in North Alabama                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Friday  June 24  2005                  WASHINGTON      Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today announced a  3 5 million grant to establish a manufacturing workforce training program at Calhoun Community College                 Sessions learned of the grant in a phone call from U S  Department of Labor Secretary Elaine Chao                 According to the Labor Department  the grant will be used to create a Center for Manufacturing Innovation at Calhoun Community College to serve the diverse needs of North Alabama  s advanced manufacturing community  The project will provide training in advanced manufacturing processes and technologies  focusing on industrial maintenance and machine tool technology                  America  s manufacturers are undergoing significant technological and economic changes     said Sessions  a member of the Senate Labor Committee    To remain competitive in the global economy  American industry needs a well trained workforce with the skills to meet today  s advanced manufacturing challenges                 The grant will go to the Alabama Workforce Investment Board  Key partners in the project  according to the Labor Department  are  Manufacture Alabama   WIA One Stop Centers  Drake Technical College  Snead State Community College  Northeast Community College  Alabama Department of Postsecondary Education  Alabama Technology Network  ACT Work Keys Center and Chambers of Commerce        Our goal must to be provide top notch training to prepare our workers in a short period of time     Sessions said    Alabama  s excellent program does just that                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights
