                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement Of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions On Award Of Congressional Gold Medal To Tuskegee Airmen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Thursday  March 29  2007           WASHINGTON   U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today issued the following statement regarding the award of the Congressional Gold Medal to the Tuskegee Airmen  The legendary black aviators received the medal during a ceremony in the Capitol Rotunda         The Congressional Gold Medal is the most prestigious award that Congress can offer  and I am honored to recognize the Tuskegee Airmen s great achievements and bravery in fighting for our freedom in World War II  Like many of the heroes of that war  these brave men left their families at home to fight overseas for the principles of freedom and democracy  Unlike most of their colleagues  these great airmen also fought an enemy of prejudice at home  Thankfully  on both fronts  they were victorious  They executed the great challenge they faced flying fighter aircrafts in hostile airspace with exceptional skill and magnificent courage  I am proud to recognize this great accomplishment  honor their service  and thank them for their dedication to racial equality in the U S  armed services                                                      2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
