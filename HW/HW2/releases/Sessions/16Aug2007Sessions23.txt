                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of Sen  Jeff Sessions on USDA Announcement of Federal Drought Assistance for Alabama                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  August 16  2007        WASHINGTONU S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following statement in response to a decision by Secretary of Agriculture Mike Johanns to release  1 05 million in Emergency Conservation Program assistance to farmers and ranchers in 28 Alabama counties affected by the ongoing drought    I applaud the Secretarys decision to release drought assistance for qualified producers  Farmers and ranchers throughout the state are suffering from an unprecedented drought and this assistance will provide them some of the help they need to implement emergency conservation practices  Its a good first step  but unfortunately all Alabama farmers dont qualify for this assistance  I look forward to working with the Secretary in the future to ensure that any available assistance is directed to Alabamas farmers and ranchers suffering from drought                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
