                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Comments On Final Passage of Democrats  Budget                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Friday  March 23  2007                    WASHINGTON   U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today issued the following statement after passage of the Senate Democrats  budget resolution                     This budget does not meet our critical need for restraint in spending and will force tax increases  The budget increases non defense related discretionary spending by 6 1 percent which is almost three times the Consumer Price Index  the cost of living  It makes no changes in the surging mandatory or entitlement spending which now accounts for more than half of all spending  And the resolution uses a parliamentary maneuver to ensure that the extension of current low tax rates will not be possible because it will require 60 votes  This means we are facing the largest tax increase in history                    The sad fact is that the Democrats  first budget increases spending and increases taxes  It is tax and spend                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
