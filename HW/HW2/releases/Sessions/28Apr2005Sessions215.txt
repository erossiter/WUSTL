                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senate Judiciary Committee Chairman Agrees To Vote On Pryor Nomination At Next Meeting                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Thursday  April 28  2005                   WASHINGTON    Senate Judiciary Committee Chairman Arlen Specter of Pennsylvania said today that he would call for a vote on Bill Pryor  s federal judicial nomination when the panel reconvenes next month                   Pryor  s nomination had been on today  s committee agenda  but Specter moved instead to consider a bill to create a trust fund to compensate asbestos exposure victims  Specter  however  promised Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL   a panel member  that Pryor  s nomination would be the top agenda item when the committee meets again in two weeks                   President Bush has twice nominated Pryor to a vacancy on the 11th U S  Circuit Court of Appeals  After Democrats blocked the nominations of Pryor and several other appeals court nominees in the last Congress  Bush appointed Pryor to the court while the Senate was in recess  Bush renominated Pryor earlier this year  The former Alabama attorney general  will have to step down from his 11th Circuit appointment at the end of the year unless the Senate approves his nomination        I  m glad the chairman agreed to move Judge Pryor  s nomination to the top of the agenda at our next meeting     Sessions said    Judge Pryor has been performing admirably on the 11th Circuit and I believe we need to move forward on his nomination                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
