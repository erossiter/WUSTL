                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Sessions Comments on Passage of the Emergency War Supplemental                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Thursday  April 26  2007         Sen  Sessions released the following statement today regarding Senate passage of the emergency war supplemental         I am disappointed that this war supplemental has passed Congress with an arbitrary date for withdrawal from Iraq   This requirement was added  even though it will lead to a veto by President Bush         The war supplemental is critical to our efforts in Iraq  but Congress should not mandate a troop withdrawal or redeployment without regard to the situation on the ground or the advice of our military commanders         Our troops continue to selflessly fulfill their duties overseas   They are at their posts throughout Iraq this very minute doing the duty that Congress gave them   They should not have to worry about whether politicians in Washington are going to provide them with the funding they need to get the job done         After the President vetoes the war supplemental  Congress should immediately send him a bill that provides the necessary funding  but does not impose a political deadline for withdrawal   Somehow  we need to get past this political hurdle         I am going to Iraq this weekend to see the situation firsthand and to talk with U S  troops  commanders  and Iraqi leaders   We must closely watch the effects of the surge to ensure that we are moving in the right direction   I will expect and demand an honest and objective evaluation                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
