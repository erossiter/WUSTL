                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of Sen  Jeff Sessions on Senate passage of a  70 billion tax reconciliation package                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Thursday  May 11  2006        We all should celebrate the Senate  s passage today of the  70 billion tax reconciliation package   Having already passed the House of Representatives  this bill will soon be signed into law by the president  While this legislation includes a number of important changes to our country  s tax laws  such as increased expensing for small businesses and a one year extension of relief from the alternative minimum tax  it most significantly extends President Bush  s tax cuts on capital gains and dividend income   By freeing up capital for investment in businesses across the country  these cuts have helped create more than 5 million new jobs since August 2003  allowed corporations to pay more dividends to their shareholders and led to a steady growth in our nation  s gross domestic product With this legislation soon to become law  we can all expect continued economic growth                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
