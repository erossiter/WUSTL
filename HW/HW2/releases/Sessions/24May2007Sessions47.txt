                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Sessions Announces New VA Clinic Coming To Childersburg                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Commitment to Bring Health Care Closer to More Veterans Called  Good News                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Thursday  May 24  2007                    WASHINGTON  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today was notified by the Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  that it plans to open a new community based clinic in Childersburg  which will enhance access to VA health care for veterans of Alabama   The clinic was among 38 approved in 22 states by VA Secretary James Nicholson                     The announcement of a new VA clinic in Childersburg is good news for Alabama s veterans   said Sessions   I am pleased that the VA is not only committed to providing the best health care for our veterans  but to making quality care more easily accessible to our state s dedicated service men and women                       The new facilities  called community based outpatient clinics  or CBOCs  will become operational in 2007 and 2008   Local VA officials will keep communities and their veterans informed of milestones in the creation of the new CBOCs                     VA is committed to providing world class health care to the men and women who have served this nation   VA Secretary Jim Nicholson said   These new clinics will bring VA s top notch care closer to the veterans who have earned it                     With 153 hospitals and more than 700 community based clinics  VA operates the largest integrated health care system in the country   VA s health care budget of more than  34 billion this year will provide health care to about 5 5 million people during nearly 800 000 hospitalizations and 60 million outpatient visits                     There are 460 000 veterans in Alabama  all of whom are eligible for VA health care  There are currently eight CBOCs and three hospitals in Alabama  In 2006  the three veterans  hospitals saw a total of 101 580 patients  In January through March of this year  86 235 patients were treated in the three hospitals                                2004
