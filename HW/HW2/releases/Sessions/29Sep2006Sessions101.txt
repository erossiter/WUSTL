                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator Sessions Announces A  596 640 Education Grant For Central Alabama Parent Training Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Friday  September 29  2006                     WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that the Alabama Parent Education Center  APEC  in Wetumpka will receive a  596 640 U S  Department of Education grant for a program that trains parents to be actively involved in their children  s education                       The grant will enable APEC to expand its Parent Information and Resource Center of Alabama  PIRCA   a subordinate program that aims to increase active parental involvement in the education of children in low performing schools                  PIRCA assists parents by holding a variety of workshops on topics such as helping children learn to read  parenting for violence prevention and homework help for parents  Regular newsletters are distributed with tips such as summer home learning activities and helping your child do better in math                  The current program will be expanded from serving families in Central Alabama to serving families statewide  APEC estimates more than 19 000 parents will benefit from training activities in the next five years  and more than 360 000 parents and teachers annually will receive information through mail outs and e newsletters                   Funding will also help create a new parent leadership program  which will establish a statewide network of parents equipped to implement PIRCA programs locally                     Parents can have a critical impact on their children  s education  so I am pleased to announce this grant that will support the valuable programs at the Alabama Parent Education Center     said Sessions  a member of the Senate Health and Education Committee
