                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of Sen  Jeff Sessions on the Senate confirmation of John Roberts as Chief Justice of the United States                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Thursday  September 29  2005        Today  s 78   22 Senate confirmation vote of John Roberts as Chief Justice of the United States represents a victory for the rule of law  a victory for the classical and proper role of a judge in our legal system  John Roberts  s confirmation was a defeat for the politicization of the courts and the notion that a judge should be an advocate for this or that view  I don  t believe a judge should advocate my political views  and I don  t think the President believes that a judge should promote his political views  To cite the apt metaphor that John Roberts used during his confirmation hearings  a judge should be a neutral umpire                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
