                                                                               U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions Announces A  48 000 Grant For Roots Of American Music Trail Along The Natchez Trace Parkway                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Wednesday  August 30  2006             WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today announced that the Muscle Shoals Regional Center at the University of North Alabama  UNA  will receive a  48 000 federal grant to implement the Roots of American Music Trail along the Natchez Trace Parkway in Colbert and Lauderdale Counties     According to the Muscle Shoals Regional Center at UNA  the Roots of American Music Trail was conceived in 2005 by a three state committee and will ultimately connect the prominent music cities of Nashville  TN  Muscle Shoals and Tupelo  MS  The committee anticipates the Music Trail will create jobs and stimulate the economies of the communities along the route      The U S  Department of Transportation National Scenic Byways grant will go to the Muscle Shoals Regional Center at UNA through the Alabama Department of Transportation  The federal grant money will be matched by  12 000 from the Colbert and Lauderdale tourism offices and a private donation and will be used only for the Alabama portion of the Music Trail        With the combined  60 000 in project funds  the Muscle Shoals Regional Center at UNA will coordinate the project  The center plans to garner interest in the Trail by developing a comprehensive website and creating signs to place along the route  Information about the area  s music heritage will be disseminated through brochures and themed maps available in kiosks on the Trail         I am pleased to announce this grant for Colbert and Lauderdale Counties     said Sessions    The Shoals area has a rich tradition in American folk and popular music  and the Music Trail will allow Alabama to share that history with visitors from around the country                                  2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
