                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the withdrawal of Harriet Miers  s Supreme Court Nomination                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Thursday  October 27  2005        Harriet Miers  s withdrawal letter is further proof of her integrity and decency  She put the country and the President ahead of her personal interests  Indeed  I believe many of the criticisms were unfounded and unjust  What the President needs to do now is to review a list of top Supreme Court candidates and send to the Senate the one he is confident has outstanding legal skills and who shares his judicial philosophy  The confirmation process will take care of itself                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
