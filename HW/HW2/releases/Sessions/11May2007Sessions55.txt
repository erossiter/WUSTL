                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement Of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions On ThyssenKrupp Announcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  May 11  2007      WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  released the following statement today on the announcement from ThyssenKrupp AG that it will build a  3 7 billion steel plant in Alabama         Today s announcement is a tremendous affirmation that Alabama is home to the first rate workforce  infrastructure and resources that world class companies like ThyssenKrupp value   Alabama is becoming known worldwide as a place where the best companies come to do business          This fantastic news is due  in part  to a dedicated team of local  state  and federal leaders  including Mayor Jones  Governor Riley  and the state legislature   The team was committed to putting forth the most competitive proposal possible  and that hard work has clearly paid off         Alabama s congressional delegation is looking forward to working with ThyssenKrupp to make their investment a success   The ThyssenKrupp facility is certain to have a positive impact on Alabama s economy  which is already thriving   In this regard  one can only be excited about the future                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
