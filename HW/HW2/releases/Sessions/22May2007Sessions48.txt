                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions Comments On Restart Of Nuclear Reactor At Brown s Ferry Nuclear Plant                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  May 22  2007      U S  Sen  Sessions  R AL  made the following comments today regarding the restart of Unit One at TVA s Brown s Ferry Nuclear Plant in Alabama         The restart of Unit One at Brown s Ferry is an important milestone in our nation s transition to utilizing more nuclear energy production   The increased baseload capacity will help TVA meet the demands of a growing economy in the Valley  and the clean  safe and affordable energy is good news for ratepayers   We  as a nation  must continue to push for more nuclear power as it is our best source of domestically produced emissions free energy   I am proud that TVA is leading the way for a nuclear renewal in the United States                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
