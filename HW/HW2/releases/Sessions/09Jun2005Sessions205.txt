                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the Senate  s 53   45 bipartisan vote to confirm Judge Bill Pryor  s nomination to the 11th U S  Circuit Court of Appeals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  June 9  2005        Judge Bill Pryor  a supremely qualified nominee to the 11th U S  Circuit Court of Appeals  today finally got the up or down vote he deserved  It was unfortunate that the Democrats filibustered Judge Pryor  s  nomination for more than two years  Their opposition was driven by misinformation and politics  Today we moved beyond partisan politics and allowed senators to simply vote yes or no on Judge Pryor  s nomination        Bill Pryor  s career has been marked by integrity  fairness and a keen legal mind  He impressed me as Alabama  s Attorney General  administering justice in a fair and impartial manner and making decisions based on the law rather than politics  President Bush gave Judge Pryor a recess appointment to the 11th Circuit last year after being blocked by Democrats  Judge Pryor is doing a great job on the circuit court  winning praise from fair minded observers  and I am pleased that the Senate today finally got a chance to vote on his nomination                                2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
