                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sessions  ITC Determination Good   For Alabama Steel Industry                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Wednesday  July 11  2007           WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  applauded yesterdays determination by the U S  International Trade Commission  ITC  that revoking existing antidumping orders on steel concrete reinforcing bars  or rebar  imported from seven countries would likely lead to material injury to the U S  steel industry   The ITCs determination means that existing antidumping orders will remain in place on products imported from Belarus  China  Indonesia  Latvia  Moldova  Poland  and Ukraine  The ITC determination was part of a five year review process required under the Uruguay Round Agreements Act   Sessions submitted written testimony in support of maintaining the antidumping orders for an International Trade Commission hearing on May 10  2007    I am pleased that the ITC vote will result in the continuation of antidumping orders on imported rebar from seven foreign countries  The existing orders have been effective at curtailing dumped imports  signifying that these countries cannot compete with American products when the playing field is level  Sessions said  Alabama has a long and proud history of steelmaking  but our industry has suffered in the last decade as a result of dumping practices  Yesterdays announcement is good news for the thousands of Alabamians who work in Alabamas steel industry                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
