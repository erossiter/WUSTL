                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Sessions Announces  6 49 Million for Enterprise High School                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Friday  June 15  2007         WASHINGTON  U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that the Federal Emergency Management Agency has awarded a  6 49 million grant to the state of Alabama for use in rebuilding Enterprise High School in Coffee County   Enterprise High School was severely damaged after a tornado struck the area on March 2  2007  Sessions  who toured the damaged areas after the storm  applauded the announcement   I am excited that FEMA is making  6 5 million available today to help offset the cost of replacing Enterprise High School  I visited Enterprise shortly after the storm  and the damage and destruction I saw there was truly heartbreaking  Sessions said  A local high school is an important part of any community  and rebuilding this school will certainly be a boost to the people of Enterprise   According to FEMA  the estimated cost of replacing the school is  30 069 691  Insurance is expected to cover  20 810 494  The grant announced today will assist in covering the difference    FEMA obligates funding for these projects directly to the state  which is required to provide a 25 percent match for the federal funds                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
