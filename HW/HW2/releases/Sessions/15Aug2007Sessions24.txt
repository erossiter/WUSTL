                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions  comments on Boeing s decision to locate missile production in Huntsville                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Wednesday  August 15  2007      U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  made the following comments after Boeing today announced that it will transfer production supporting the Aegis Ballistic Missile Defense  BMD  program from Anaheim  California  to its facility in Huntsville   Boeings decision to move production activities for the Aegis system to Huntsville is further proof of the outstanding reputation that Huntsville has earned as a production center for advanced defense technology  Aegis is among the most successful elements of the Ballistic Missile Defense System  providing critical protection of our deployed forces  our allies and the homeland  As the home of missile defense  Huntsville is a natural place for SM 3 production  I am proud of Alabamas growing role as a supplier of advanced technology to the men and women of our armed forces  and I m pleased that an excellent company such as Boeing is choosing to increase its investment here                              2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
