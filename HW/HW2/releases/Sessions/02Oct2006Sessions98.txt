                                                                               Mobile  s Robert Sharp Appointed To Federal Biomass Advisory Committee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Monday  October 2  2006                     WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  announced today that Mobile resident Robert Sharp has been appointed to the Biomass Research and Development Technical Advisory Committee                   The committee advises the secretaries of Agriculture and Energy on strategies to develop energy from plant material and animal waste  known as biomass                  Sharp has 35 years of experience in the forest products industry  He is president of Forest Marketing Group in Centerville  Tennessee  and operates Mobile Forest Products with partner John Zukley  also of Mobile  Sharp is also a former president of the Alabama Forestry Association                      I am glad that Bob has been selected to the committee     Sessions said    He has a proven track record of success in operating forest product companies that convert renewable forest resources into energy                     Sharp  along with 11 other new members  will serve a three year term on the panel  which was established in 2000 by the Biomass Research and Development Act                     It will be good to have someone in this important position who knows Alabama and our potential to be a major player in the development of alternative energy sources     Sessions said    Our country  s dependence on foreign oil is a threat to our national security  so it is critically important that we cultivate the resources we have available to us  such as biomass  It is vital that Alabama be on the cutting edge in connecting wood  switchgrass  corn and other products to energy                                   2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama
