                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Jeff Sessions Announces Three Additions To His Washington Staff                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Thursday  May 19  2005      WASHINGTON    Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today announced three additions to his Washington D C  office  Thomas DeHaven was appointed as Legislative Assistant  Mary Beth Brednich as Constituent Correspondence Manager and James Mathews as Staff Assistant                   Thomas DeHaven will serve as Legislative Assistant for fiscal policy   Prior to joining Sessions   staff  he served as an economic policy analyst for the National Taxpayers Union and as a fiscal policy researcher at the Cato Institute   DeHaven is published in the Washington Post  Washington Times  New York Post  National Review Online  National Review magazine  FoxNews com  Tech Central Station and Human Events Online  and he has appeared on the CBS Evening News to discuss Social Security   DeHaven received a bachelor of science in business administration from Shippensburg University                 Mary Beth Brednich will serve as Constituent Correspondence Manager   Brednich received a bachelor  s degree in education from Abilene Christian University in 1964   She taught elementary school for two years in Fort Worth  Texas  and has spent the past 32 years as a full time homemaker raising her four children   She is returning to Washington  D C  after having served as an intern at the Pentagon in 1964                   James Mathews  Staff Assistant  is a native of Pinson  Alabama   Mathews received a bachelor  s degree in political science from the University of Alabama at Birmingham   Mathews was elected Chairman of the College Republican Federation of Alabama in 2004   Before coming to Washington  Mathews served as an intern for the Alabama Republican Party and for U S  Rep  Spencer Bachus  R AL                             2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
