                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Sen  Sessions  R AL  made the following comments after Sen  Reid s announcement that Senate debate on the immigration bill will extend past the Memorial Day recess                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Monday  May 21  2007          I am pleased that Senator Reid agreed with my request not to bring up the immigration bill for a vote this week   The bill is critically important to the American people  and to railroad it through the Senate would be unthinkable   Senators still have not received the final text of the legislation  which will be 800 pages or more   The recess week will provide some time for senators and the American public to become familiar with this extraordinarily complex legislation and to better understand its consequences                                 2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
