                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on the Air Force  s Selection of an Introductory Flight Screening Facility                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Tuesday  February 7  2006        I  m terribly disappointed that Selma was not selected as the site for the Air Force  s Introductory Flight Screening facility  It was one of my top wishes that the Introductory Flight Screening facility would locate in Selma because I knew it would have had an important economic impact  That  s why I did all I could to support Lockheed Martin  s selection process when it chose Selma as its designated site  That selection in itself certainly speaks well for Selma  While the Air Force  after completing its process  decided otherwise  I certainly believed that Lockheed and Selma  s capabilities were unmatched  I believe Selma is ready for economic expansion  and I am more determined than ever to see that it occurs  Progress will require a united federal  state and local effort                               2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
