                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Statement of U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions on Senate passage of the USA Patriot Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Thursday  March 2  2006      I welcome the Senate  s reauthorization today of the USA Patriot Act  It is a sound piece of legislation that provides terrorism investigators the same investigative and prosecutorial tools that are available to law enforcement officers probing bank fraud and drug dealers  Unfortunately  we let outside groups with agendas confuse people about the intent and scope of this legislation  These groups claim the Patriot Act undermines our historic civil liberties  but that is not true  In fact  this is a very restrained piece of legislation that simply allows us to better protect our country from terrorists                            2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
