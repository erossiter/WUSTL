                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions Lauds Sustained GDP Growth                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Friday  April 28  2006                    WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Jeff Sessions  R AL  today issued the following statement after the Department of Labor announced a 4 8 percent economic growth rate in the first quarter of 2006          Today s report that the gross domestic product increased at a rate of 4 8 percent during the first quarter is another good sign that the American economy continues to be strong  Our economy has added more than 5 million jobs since August 2003  with the national unemployment rate at 4 7 percent   More impressively  the unemployment rate in Alabama has fallen to 3 3 percent  These numbers are a clear indication that the country and Alabama are doing well  While we face several tough challenges  these numbers should give us confidence that our policies are working and that with increased effort we can meet and overcome the problems of high gasoline prices and budget and trade deficits                                   2004 United States Senator Jeff Sessions  Alabama                     All rights reserved
