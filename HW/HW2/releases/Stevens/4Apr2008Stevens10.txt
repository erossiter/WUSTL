GRANTS AWARDED TO ALASKA PROGRAMS
  The Alaska Congressional Delegation today announced that federal agencies have awarded a series of funding grants to Alaska programs and communities. The grants announced this week total over $115,000. 
   U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development
   * Two grants totaling $95,000 to the State of Alaska Housing Finance Corporation to help Alaskan community-based nonprofit organizations produce affordable housing in low-income communities at the grassroots level.
   National Park Service
   * $20,000 in Preserve American Grants for the Downtown Anchorage Historic Walking Tour and Education Project. The project's goal is "to explore Anchorage's history and enhance heritage tourism."
   Corporation for National & Community Service
   * Up to $4,725 to send an AmeriCorps*VISTA member to serve as a Teen Coordinator and Program Developer and provide outreach to at-risk and underserved teens in Ketchikan and Saxman.
