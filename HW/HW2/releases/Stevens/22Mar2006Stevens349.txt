SEN. STEVENS ADDRESSES ALASKA STATE LEGISLATURE
  Sen. Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) today addressed a joint session of the Alaska State Legislature. In his address, Sen. Stevens remarked that the current challenges facing Alaska are even greater than those faced during the fight for statehood. He urged all of Alaska's elected representatives to work together to develop a plan for the state's future that will foster a climate for investment, maximize Alaska's economic potential, and educate Americans about Alaska's challenges and opportunities.
