SEN. STEVENS ANNOUNCES BRAC COMMISSIONERS FOR ALASKA HEARING
  Today, Sen. Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) announced that the June 15, 2005 Base Realignment And Closure (BRAC) Commission hearing will have three of nine Commissioners in attendance. 
   The Commissioners attending the hearing will be BRAC Chairman Anthony Principi, former Secretary of Veterans Affairs; Mr. Philip Coyle, Senior Advisor to the Center for Defense Information; and Mr. James Hansen, former member of Congress and member of the House Armed Services Committee.
   There will also be a possible fourth Commissioner in attendance. That person will be announced when confirmed.
   While in Alaska the Commissioners will tour Kulis Air Guard Station in Anchorage, Elmendorf Air Force Base, Eielson Air Force Base, and will represent the entire BRAC Commission at the hearing on June 15th.
