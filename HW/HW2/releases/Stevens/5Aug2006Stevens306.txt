ALASKA MOURNS PASSING OF SUSAN BUTCHER
  At the request of David Monson, Sen. Ted Stevens is deeply saddened to announce that Susan Butcher, four-time Iditarod winner and mother of two, passed away today at 3:30 pm in Seattle, Washington.  
   "Susan was a very close personal friend, and Catherine and I are deeply saddened to learn of her death," said Stevens. "Our thoughts and prayers are with David, their two daughters, and Susan's friends and family. Susan was a remarkable and courageous woman. Alaska shall not forget her." 
   Susan Butcher was diagnosed with leukemia in 2005. She passed away following complications from a bone marrow transplant. 
   Memorial service details and additional information will be posted to www.susanbutcher.com as they become available.
