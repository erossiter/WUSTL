SENS. STEVENS, MURKOWSKI MOBILE OFFICE TO VISIT INTERIOR
  Senator Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) and Senator Lisa Murkowski (R-Alaska) today announced that members of their staffs will visit Talkeetna, Cantwell, Healy, Nenana, Fairbanks, Delta Junction, Tok, and Glennallen from August 13 to 16. 
  This "mobile office" will enable Senator Stevens' and Senator Murkowski's congressional staff to listen to constituent concerns about issues related to federal government. Public opinion messages and casework requests are also welcome. Message forms will be available on site, and all information will be directed to the Congressional Delegation and the appropriate federal agencies.
