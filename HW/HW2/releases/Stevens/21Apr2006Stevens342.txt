ALASKA CONGRESSIONAL DELEGATION ANNOUNCES GRANTS AWARDED TO ALASKA PROGRAMS
  The Alaska Congressional Delegation today announced that the U.S. Department of Justice has awarded grants to Alaska groups and communities totaling more than $1.2 million. The grants include:  
   * $142,514 to the Municipality of Anchorage for the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program (JAG). This program enables state, tribal, and local governments to support a broad range of activities designed to prevent and control crime. 
   * $1,085,951 to the Alaska Native Justice Center, Inc. The center helps Alaska Natives address challenges they encounter in the civil and criminal justice systems at the local, state, and federal levels. This grant will help fund the center's Alternatives for Justice Program.
