GRANTS AWARDED TO ALASKA PROGRAMS
  The Alaska Congressional Delegation on Friday, March 2, announced that federal agencies have awarded a series of funding grants to Alaska groups and communities. The grants total more than $2 million.  
  U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS)
   * $165,153 to Palmer's Alaska Family Services Transitional Living Program. 
   * $194,185 in Block Grants for Community Mental Health Services to the Alaska Department of Health and Social Services. 
  U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS)
   * $1,707,446 to the State of Alaska from the DHS' Emergency Management Performance Grant program. This program supports comprehensive state and local emergency management efforts by encouraging enhanced mitigation, preparedness, response, and recovery capabilities for all hazards.
