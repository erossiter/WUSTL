SEN. STEVENS DISCUSSES ALASKA, NATIONAL ISSUES AT CONFERENCE
  On Thursday, March 15, Sen. Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) spoke with Washington-based Alaska reporters about a wide variety of topics affecting the State and the nation. 
  Senator Stevens addressed the following issues during the press conference:
   * The Alaska bridge projects being labeled as "earmarks" 
   * Making ANWR part of the Strategic Petroleum Reserve 
   * The Bush Administration's aquaculture proposal 
   * Debating the Iraq resolutions 
   * The Bush Administration's cuts to federally funded Alaska education programs 
   * The earmark process 
   * The possibility of placing beluga whales on the Endangered Species list.
