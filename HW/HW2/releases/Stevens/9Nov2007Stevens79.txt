HUD AWARDS GRANTS TO ALASKA VILLAGES
  The Alaska Congressional Delegation today announced that the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) awarded grants to two Alaska villages. The grants total $432,935. ' 
   * $392,495 to the Arctic Village Council. The primary purpose of this grant is to expand available housing and economic opportunities in Native communities. 
   * $40,440 to Lime Village. This grant can be used for a variety of eligible affordable housing activities, including operating assistance, development of affordable housing, housing services, housing management for low-income housing, and crime prevention.
