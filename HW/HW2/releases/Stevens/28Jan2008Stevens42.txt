SUPREME COURT ACCEPTS, MAKES PUBLIC ALASKA DELEGATION'S BRIEF IN SUPPORT OF OIL SPILL VICTIMS
  The United States Supreme Court today accepted an amicus brief authored by Alaska's Congressional Delegation in support of the plaintiffs in Exxon Shipping Co. and Exxon Mobil Corp. v. Grant Baker, et al. The brief was filed Friday. Upon the Supreme Court's review and acceptance today, it is now available to the public.
  A copy of the brief can be found http://stevens.senate.gov/misc/amicus.pdf.
