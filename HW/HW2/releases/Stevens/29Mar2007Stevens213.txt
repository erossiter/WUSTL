SEN. STEVENS DISCUSSES ALASKA AND NATIONAL ISSUES AT PRESS CONFERENCE
  On Thursday, March 29, 2007, Sen. Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) spoke with Washington based Alaska reporters about a wide variety of topics affecting the State and the nation. 
  Senator Stevens answered questions on the following issues during the press conference:
   1. The Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act which includes the Secure Rural Schools program extension (9 minutes) 
   2. Various groups' efforts to block oil and gas leases in Bristol Bay (10 minutes) 
   3. His re-election and campaign fundraising efforts (7 minutes) 
   4. The Environmental Defense's pushing of "catch shares" (2 minutes).
