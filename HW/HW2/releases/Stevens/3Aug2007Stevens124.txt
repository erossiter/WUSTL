SENATOR STEVENS' MOBILE OFFICE TO VISIT SOUTHEAST ALASKA
  Senator Ted Stevens (R-Alaska) today announced that members of his staff will visit Sitka, Hoonah, Yakutat, Petersburg, and Wrangell from August 17 to 24. 
  This "mobile office" will enable his congressional staff to listen to constituent concerns about issues related to federal government. Public opinion messages and casework requests are also welcome. Message forms will be available on site, and all information will be directed to the Congressional Delegation and the appropriate federal agencies.
