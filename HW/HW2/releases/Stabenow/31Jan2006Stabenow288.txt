  STABENOW STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF CORETTA SCOTT KING 
   Sen.  
 Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) today made the following statement regarding the death of Coretta Scott King: 
   "I was deeply saddened this morning to hear the news of Coretta Scott King's passing. 
   "Mrs. King's dignity and grace in the face of struggle and adversity stands as an example to all of us of the true goodness that lies at the heart of our nation. 
   "Her tireless work on behalf of the civil rights movement brought her to Michigan on many occasions. While visiting our state she touched and inspired many lives, and her presence will continue to be felt in our communities for generations to come. 
   "Our thoughts and prayers are with the King family today, as well as our assurance that Coretta Scott King's dedication to the cause of equality for all Americans will never be forgotten."
