SENS. STABENOW, LEVIN ANNOUNCE $35,000 FIRE PREVENTION GRANT FOR DETROIT
  Sens. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) and Carl Levin (D-MI) announced today that the Detroit Fire Department has received a $35,000 grant under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program.  
   "Effective fire prevention includes a variety of activities, including public education and awareness, fire code enforcement, and fire inspections," Stabenow said. "The fire prevention initiative supported by this FEMA grant will help the Detroit Fire Department keep neighborhoods safe and secure."
   "Prevention is the best way to protect our homes and communities against fire disasters," said Levin. "These funds will help the Detroit Fire Department provide important fire prevention programs throughout the city." 
   This grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program, which funds the Fire Prevention Program. 
   Information on the assistance to firefighters grant program is available at: http://www.firegrantsupport.com.
