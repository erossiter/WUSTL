SEN. STABENOW ISSUES STATEMENT ON GENERAL MOTORS' NEW INVESTMENTS IN MICHIGAN
  Sen. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) today made the following statement in response to the announcement by General Motors that the company will invest more than $545 million in five Michigan manufacturing facilities in Pontiac, Lansing, Ypsilanti and Romulus: 
   "Today's announcement by General Motors is great news for Michigan and the communities that will benefit from these new jobs.
   "GM's decision to further invest in Michigan manufacturing is a recognition of our dedicated labor force, our unique resources and our great potential for future innovation."
