SENS. LEVIN, STABENOW ANNOUNCE OVER $29,000 FOR DELTA COUNTY SHERIFF'S DEPARTMENT
  Sens. Carl Levin, D-Mich., and Debbie Stabenow, D-Mich., announced today that the Delta County Sheriff's Department has been awarded $29,400 by the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) Community Facilities grant program.  
 The grant will go towards the purchase of two police vehicles and new equipment for the Sheriff's Department. 
   "This is great news for all of Delta County," said Levin. "These federal dollars will help local authorities do their jobs by providing them with the equipment and vehicles they need."
   "All preparations for emergency response come down to making sure that first-responders, including local law enforcement have the equipment they need," Stabenow said. "This is an important grant for the Delta County Sheriff's Department and the people it serves." 
   The Community Facilities grant program is run by the USDA's Office of Rural and Community Development. The program aims to assist rural areas in the development of essential community infrastructure.
