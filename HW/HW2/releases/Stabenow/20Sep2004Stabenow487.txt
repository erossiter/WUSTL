SEN. STABENOW OFFERS CONDOLENCES, STATEMENT ON DEATH OF AMERICAN CAPTIVE IN IRAQ
  Sen. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) issued the following statement today following the news that former Michigan resident Eugene Armstrong had been murdered by his captors in Iraq: 
   "I was shocked at the news that another innocent American civilian, a former Michigan resident, was brutally murdered by terrorists in Iraq," Stabenow said. "My heart goes out to his family, who have suffered the uncertainly of his fate and the grief of his death. This cowardly act only recommits our nation to bringing these killers to justice and eliminating the agents and roots of terrorism around the world."
