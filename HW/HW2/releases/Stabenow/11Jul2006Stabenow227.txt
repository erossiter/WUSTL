SEN. STABENOW ISSUES STATEMENT ON GOOGLE'S NEW MICHIGAN FACILITY
  Sen. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) today made the following statement regarding Google, Inc.'s announcement they are building a facility in Ann Arbor, Michigan with up to 1,000 new jobs:  
   "Michigan is home to some of the best and brightest workers in America. I am thrilled that Google will be making the most of our talented workforce by opening a new facility in Ann Arbor and creating 1,000 new jobs. Today's announcement is further evidence that investing in education and innovation brings jobs to Michigan." 
   "This is a major victory for Michigan, and I will keep fighting for policies that will help other high-tech employers follow in Google's footsteps and create good-paying jobs in our state."
