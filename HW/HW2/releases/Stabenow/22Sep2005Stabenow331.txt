SENS. STABENOW, LEVIN ANNOUNCE $922,064 IN FIREFIGHTER GRANTS FOR MICHIGAN
  Sens. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) and Carl Levin (D-MI) announced today that 12 fire departments in Michigan have received a total of $922,064 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 and 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Programs.  
   "This grant program provides funding directly to local fire departments to improve their ability to respond to fires and fire-related emergencies in their communities," Stabenow said. "Our state\'s first responders deserve nothing less than the best possible equipment and training, and these grants will help these 12 fire departments attain that goal."
   "These grants are a crucial part of keeping Michigan's communities safe and secure," said Levin. "Firefighters and emergency personnel perform an invaluable service and should be rewarded with the best and most state-of-the-art equipment and facilities. This federal support is a vital step toward providing these departments with the resources they need."
   The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training, equipment, personal protective equipment, wellness and fitness, and modifications to facilities.
