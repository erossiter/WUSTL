SEN. STABENOW'S ISSUES STATEMENT ON TUSKEGEE AIRMEN RECEIVING CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL
  Sen. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) on Thursday, March 29, 2007, released the following statement on the historic Tuskegee Airmen receiving the Congressional Gold Medal.  
   "This heroic group of African-American flyers fought bravely to free Europe, all the while battling racism here at home. We owe these men a debt of gratitude for their victories on both of these fronts. America is a stronger nation today because of their service.
   "These soldiers are a vital part of both America's and Michigan's history, and I am thrilled that they are finally receiving the recognition they deserve." - U.S. Senator Debbie Stabenow.
