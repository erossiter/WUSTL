SENS. LEVIN, STABENOW ANNOUNCE $420,519 IN FIREFIGHTER GRANTS FOR MICHIGAN
  Sens. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) and Carl Levin (D-MI) announced today that six fire departments in Michigan have received a total of $420,519 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program.  
   "These federal dollars will help to keep Michigan's communities safe and secure," said Levin. "This is a critical part of the ongoing effort to keep our first responders prepared and well-equipped as they confront a wide variety of threats."
   "Communities throughout Michigan rely on local firefighters to protect them when emergencies arise," Stabenow said. "These six grants will help local fire departments respond to the emergencies and hazards that threaten the safety of our local communities."
   The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training, equipment, personal protective equipment, wellness and fitness, and modifications to facilities.
