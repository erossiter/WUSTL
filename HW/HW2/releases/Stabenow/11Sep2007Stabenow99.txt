SEN. STABENOW ISSUES STATEMENT ON SIXTH ANNIVERSARY OF 9/11 ATTACKS
  U.S. Senator Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) today made the following statement regarding the sixth anniversary of the 9/11 terrorist attacks on the United States: 
   "Six years ago today the United States faced one of the greatest tragedies in our history when we were ruthlessly attacked. Like all Americans, my thoughts and prayers go out to the victims and their families on this solemn anniversary. We will never forget the sacrifices of September 11, 2001, and we must never waiver in our commitment to keeping America safe." --U.S. Senator Debbie Stabenow
