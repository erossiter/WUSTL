SENS. STABENOW, LEVIN ANNOUNCE $2 MILLION IN FUNDING FOR AIRPORT PROJECTS IN SAULT STE. MARIE, KALAMAZOO
 Sens. Debbie Stabenow (D-MI) and Carl Levin (D-MI) today announced that Kalamazoo/Battle Creek International Airport in Kalamazoo and Chippewa County International Airport in Sault Ste.  
 Marie will each receive $1 million for ongoing infrastructure projects and safety enhancements at the airports. 
   "Every improvement we can make at our Michigan airports is a step forward in enhancing the safe and efficient movement of passengers and cargo," Stabenow said. "We will continue to work to support funding for airport improvements such as these being undertaken at the Kalamazoo and Sault Ste. Marie facilities." 
   "This funding will allow these airports to make needed upgrades," Levin said. "From runway and taxiway upgrades to environmental safeguards, these improvements will enhance the quality of airport safety and service." 
   Projects at the Kalamazoo facility funded by the grant include making improvements to the airport deicing facility to dispose of deicing fluid and constructing a connecting taxiway to the hanger area. Work at the Chippewa County facility includes construction of service roads and rehabilitation of the apron and taxiway.
