SENS. LEVIN, STABENOW ANNOUNCE $6.28 MILLION FOR GERALD R. FORD INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT, $1.05 MILLION FOR DELTA COUNTY AIRPORT
  Sens. Carl Levin, D- Mich., and Debbie Stabenow, D- Mich., announced today that the U.S. Department of Transportation has awarded $6,281,400 to Kent County for the Gerald R. Ford International Airport and $1,045,000 to the County of Delta for the Delta County Airport.  
 The funding will be used for rehabilitations projects. 
   "Hundreds of planes land and take off in Michigan airports everyday and passenger and crew safety is essential," said Levin. "This funding will help with the needed maintenance of runways, taxiways and aprons at these airports." 
   Stabenow quote
   At Gerald R. Ford International Airport, the federal funds will be used to rehabilitate the east and west general aviation apron areas and associated taxiways. They will also be used to construct a taxiway connector to the east general aviation apron and construct an airport service road. 
   At Delta County Airport, the funds will be used to rehabilitate taxiway C and the terminal apron.
