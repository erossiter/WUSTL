SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON LESLIE FISHBEIN'S DEATH
  Today, United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement upon hearing of the death of Leslie Fishbein.
   "Today the Colorado community lost a generous citizen, a devoted woman and a business pioneer. Leslie's work and vibrant personality will have a long-lasting impact on our community. My thoughts and prayers are with Leslie's family and friends in this difficult time."
