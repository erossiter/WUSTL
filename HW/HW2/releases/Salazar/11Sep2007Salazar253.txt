SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT COMMEMORATING SIXTH ANNIVERSARY OF SEPT. 11TH
  Today, United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement commemorating the sixth anniversary of the terrorist attacks on September 11th, 2001.  
    "Today, we honor the thousands of innocent people who died at the World Trade Center, the Pentagon, and in Pennsylvania six years ago. Our nation will always remember the horrendous acts of September 11th. We must honor and pay tribute to those who lost their lives and support the families who live with their loss. We must continue to be vigilant to protect our homeland from such terrorist actions in the future."
