SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON ARREST IN CASE OF JONBENET RAMSEY'S MURDER
  Sen. Salazar's Statement on an Arrest in the case of JonBenet Ramsey's Murder: 
   "I am pleased that Boulder District Attorney Mary Lacy and her partners in law enforcement have continued their work to apparently solve the murder of JonBenet Ramsey. District Attorney Lacy had promised to continue to keep the case going and pursue ways of solving this murder. It appears as though progress has been made. I am proud of the continued efforts by law enforcement officials in this tragedy."
   United States Senator Ken Salazar was Colorado's Attorney General from January 1999 until January 2005.
