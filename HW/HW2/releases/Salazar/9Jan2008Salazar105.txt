SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON JUDGE FIGA'S FUNERAL TODAY
  United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement in honor of U.S. District Judge Phillip Figa's funeral today. Senator Salazar will attend Judge Figa's funeral this afternoon.
   "I knew and worked with Judge Figa for almost 30 years. Judge Figa was a strong example of a fine lawyer and a superb judge. I will miss him and my thoughts and prayers remain with his family as they mourn the loss of a great man."
