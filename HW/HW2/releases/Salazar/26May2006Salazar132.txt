SEN. SALAZAR VISIT FORT LOGAN NATIONAL CEMETERY TO COMMEMORATE MEMORIAL DAY HOLIDAY
  Sen. Ken Salazar will visit Fort Logan National Cemetery on Memorial Day to participate in its Memorial Day ceremony as a commemorative speaker. Sen. Salazar will deliver his statement in honor of Memorial Day at 11:00A MDT at Fort Logan National Cemetery. The address is 4400 W. Kenyon Avenue, Denver, CO.
   A copy of Senator Salazar's Memorial Day speech, as prepared, is available at http://salazar.senate.gov/images/pdf/060529cem.pdf.
   Contact: Cody Wertz, 303/455-7600; Andrew Nannis, 202/224-5852.
