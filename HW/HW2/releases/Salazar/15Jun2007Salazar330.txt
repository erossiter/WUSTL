SEN. SALAZAR ANNOUNCES COLORADO PILT PAYMENTS FOR FISCAL 2007
  Sen. Ken Salazar today announced the Payments in Lieu of Taxes (PILT) payments to Colorado counties for Fiscal Year 2007 (FY07). Colorado is receiving $17,352,969. This is $125,869 less than in FY06, a difference of 0.72 percent. 
   "Federal lands make-up more than one-third of Colorado," said Senator Salazar. "In many counties, PILT is what pays for our schools, emergency responders and roads and is critical to our state I remain committed to working with my Republican and Democratic colleagues to fully-fund PILT."
