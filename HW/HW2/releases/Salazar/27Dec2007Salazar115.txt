SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON BENAZIR BHUTTO ASSASSINATION
  Today, United States Senator Ken Salazar made the following statement upon hearing that former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto was assassinated earlier today. 
   "Benazir Bhutto's death is a blow to peace and stability in Pakistan and the entire region. A stable Pakistan is critical to our National security and our global fight against terror. I continue to believe that Pakistan must move forward with free, fair, and open elections in January that are democratic and include the opposition parties."
