SEN. SALAZAR COMMENTS ON GOV. RITTER AND CO LAWMAKER PLANS FOR 2008 ELECTION
  Today, United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement in response to the legislative plan released by Governor Ritter and a group of bipartisan legislators for conducting the 2008 elections.
   "It is imperative that we provide Colorado voters with a wide array of options by which they can cast their votes. I welcome the bipartisan proposal set forth by Governor Ritter and members of the Colorado Legislative Assembly, and look forward to doing everything I can to help ensure and restore the voting public's faith in the electoral process once again."
