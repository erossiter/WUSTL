SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON BLM'S DECISION NOT TO POSTPONE LEASE OF PARCELS IN SAN MIGUEL, MESA COUNTIES
  Sen. Salazar's Statement on BLM's Decision Not to Postpone Lease of Parcels in San Miguel & Mesa Counties
   "I fully support Congressman Salazar in his efforts to convince the BLM to postpone the lease of parcels in San Miguel and Mesa counties in an effort to provide local communities more time to understand the impact on water quality and individual property rights." - Ken Salazar.
