SEN. SALAZAR COMMENTS ON RETIREMENT OF NEW MEXICO SEN. PETE DOMENICI
  Today United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement upon hearing that New Mexico Senator Pete Domenici will retire in 2008.
   "Senator Pete Domenici has proudly served the people of New Mexico for the last 35 years. During that time, he has been one of the premier minds on western issues in the United States Senate. It has been an honor to serve with him the last few years and he will be greatly missed in the Senate."
