SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH'S COMMUTATION OF 'SCOOTER' LIBBY'S PRISON SENTENCE
  United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement today after President Bush commuted the prison sentence of former White House aide I. Lewis "Scooter" Libby for obstruction of justice:
   "I am very disappointed that President Bush has chosen to exercise his power to commute "Scooter" Libby's sentence. Mr. Libby was convicted by a jury of his peers and he should face the consequences of his actions."
