SEN. SALAZAR'S STATEMENT REGARDING SENATOR WARNER'S DECISION NOT TO SEEK REELECTION
  United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement after learning that Senator John Warner (R-VA) announced that he will not seek reelection for his Virginia Senate seat. 
   "I have been honored and privileged to work with Senator John Warner over the last two-and-a-half years. I have traveled with him to Iraq and the Middle East and I know his dedication to a strong and free America. His service to this country has been exemplary and second to none. He will be missed in the Senate."
