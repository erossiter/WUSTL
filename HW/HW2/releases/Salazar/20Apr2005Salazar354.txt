SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON SIXTH ANNIVESARY OF COLUMBINE TRAGEDY
  Sen. Ken Salazar today released the following statement on the sixth anniversary of the Columbine High School shootings in Littleton, Colorado:
   "Six years ago, we saw the horror that two troubled minds can inflict upon their community. The tragic events of April 20, 1999 at Columbine High School touched not only our community, but everyone across America. The victims of the Columbine shooting and their families continue to be in my prayers."
