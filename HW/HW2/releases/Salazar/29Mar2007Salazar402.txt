SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON TORNADOS IN HOLLY, COLO.
  Sen. Ken Salazar released the following statement regarding the tornados that struck the town of Holly, Colorado yesterday: 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the community of Holly, Colorado and in particular those who were injured or who lost loved ones in the devastating tornado.
   "My office has contacted the Prowers County government to offer any assistance we can to help the citizens of Holly and the surrounding area. I extend this same offer to Governor Ritter as well as state, county and local officials working to address this terrible ordeal.
   "Southeastern Coloradans are the toughest of the tough and I remain confident that local law enforcement and emergency services, bolstered by aid from the state agencies, will ensure that everyone in the area is provided shelter, security and comfort."
