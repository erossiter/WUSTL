SEN. SALAZAR RESPONDS TO DEATHS AT XCEL HYDROELECTRIC ENERGY PLANT
  United States Senator Ken Salazar released the following statement this morning following the deaths of five workers at the Xcel Energy's Cabin Creek Hydroelectric Plant yesterday.
   "I am deeply saddened by the deaths of the five workers in Cabin Creek yesterday. My thoughts and prayers are with their families, friends and coworkers."
