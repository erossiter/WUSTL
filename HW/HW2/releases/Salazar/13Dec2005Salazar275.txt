SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON EFFORTS TO REFORM 1872 MINING ACT
  Sen. Ken Salazar issued the following statement on efforts to reform the 1872 Mining Act:
   "I look forward to working on reforms to the 1872 Mining Act with Senators Craig, Reid, and others next year. It is imperative that we seek input from the public and work in cooperation with all stakeholders including mining companies, other public land users, preservationists and local governments and communities."
