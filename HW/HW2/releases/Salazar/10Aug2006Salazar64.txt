SEN. SALAZAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON FOILED TERRORIST PLOT
  Statement of Sen. Salazar on foiled terrorist plot:  
   Sen. Ken Salazar released the following comment today regarding the uncovered terrorist plot in the United Kingdom.
   "I applaud the work of British law enforcement today in foiling this terrorist plot. The tireless efforts of these investigators, like those of their American counterparts and allies worldwide, are what keep our Nations safe.
   "These terrorists will stop at nothing to kill innocent Americans, and others.
   "I support the Transportation Security Agency's move to increase security measures at our Nation's airports. Washington must provide the men and women of the Department of Homeland Security and other law enforcement with the tools they need to do their jobs."
