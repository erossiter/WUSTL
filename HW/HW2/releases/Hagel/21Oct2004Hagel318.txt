SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON DECISION BY FARM CREDIT SERVICES OF AMERICA TO TERMINATE SALE TO RABOBANK
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE), a member of the U.S. Senate Banking Committee, released the following statement today in regard to the decision by the board of directors of Omaha-based Farm Credit Services of America to terminate their acquisition agreement with the Dutch bank Rabobank: 
   "I am sure that the Board made their decision based on what is in the best interest of their shareholders. Access to credit is critical for Nebraska's agriculture producers. The Senate Banking Committee will continue to work to ensure that Nebraska's producers have broad access to a wide array of credit options."
