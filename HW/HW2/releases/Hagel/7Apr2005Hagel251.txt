SEN. HAGEL COMMENTS ON TAIWAN'S DECISION TO ACCEPT U.S. BEEF
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today after the United States Department of Agriculture announced that Taiwan is re-opening its market to beef from U.S. cattle under 30 months of age beginning on April 16th, 2005: 
   "This is important news for Nebraska's beef producers. Taiwan's decision sends a clear signal to countries like Japan about the quality and safety of U.S. beef. Secretary Johanns, Governor Heineman and State Agriculture Director Merlyn Carlson deserve enormous credit for their work to open this market."
