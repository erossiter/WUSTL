SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF EDUCATION SECRETARY VENEMAN
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today regarding the resignation of Secretary of Agriculture Ann Veneman: 
   "Secretary Veneman and the USDA have worked tirelessly on issues that are important to Nebraska such as BSE, the resumption of beef trade, soybean rust, and drought assistance. We will miss Secretary Veneman's steady leadership and inclusive style. She and her team have been very effective advocates for American agriculture. Secretary Veneman can be proud of her service to this nation."
