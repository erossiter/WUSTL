SEN. HAGEL ANNOUNCES PRESS SECRETARY
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) announced today that Beth Tiehen of Omaha, Nebraska has been promoted to Press Secretary.  
   "For two years working in my press office, Beth has developed the relationships and trust with members of the media needed to do this job. She works hard and brings a deep knowledge of Nebraska and the Senate to the job. Beth has earned these new responsibilities and will do an excellent job," Hagel said. 
   Tiehen is a 2003 graduate of the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO) with a degree in International Business and Spanish. At UNO, Tiehen was a Goodrich Scholar and was the 2001 Nene Field Ambassador to Brazil. After graduation, she began a fall internship in the Senator's office before being hired as a full-time staff member. Her parents are Mike and Mary Tiehen of Omaha, Nebraska.
