SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT OF SUPPORT FOR CONSIDERATION OF SEN. NELSON TO BE AGRICULTURE SECRETARY
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement regarding White House consideration of Sen. Ben Nelson to be the new Secretary of Agriculture: 
   "The White House would be wise to consider Sen. Nelson for Secretary of Agriculture. It would be good for Nebraska. I have let the White House know of my support for Sen. Nelson."
