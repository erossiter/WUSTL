SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF ENERGY SECRETARY ABRAHAM
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today regarding the resignation of Secretary of Energy Spencer Abraham: 
   "Secretary Abraham brought strong management and innovation to the Department of Energy. He led on important issues like nuclear non-proliferation and advanced research for renewable fuels and hydrogen vehicles. Secretary Abraham reached out to our allies and the world in cooperative ventures that enhanced America's security and energy priorities. We will miss Secretary Abraham's steady leadership and inclusive style. Secretary Abraham and his team can be very proud of their accomplishments and service to this nation."
