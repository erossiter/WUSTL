SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF REP. SMITH
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today regarding the death of Congresswoman Virginia Smith at the age of 94. Rep.  
 Smith represented Nebraska's Third District for 16 years: 
   "There is no person in public life that I have had a higher regard for than Virginia Smith. Her commitment to Nebraska and the Third District she represented is her legacy. She set a standard for individual representation in Washington. Virginia Smith worked hard, said it straight and always did what she thought was right. She was a very special and unique person. She will be missed. 
   "Lilibet and I will keep her in our thoughts and prayers."
