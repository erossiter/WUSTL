SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF TRADE REPRESENTATIVE ZOELLICK TO BE DEPUTY SECRETARY OF STATE
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today following the nomination of U.S. Trade Representative Robert B. Zoellick to be Deputy Secretary of State: 
   "Bob Zoellick has a range of experience, knowledge and judgement that will be critically important in working with Secretary of State Designate Rice. He understands the nature and importance of America's relationships around the world. I have always valued Bob Zoellick's counsel and wise advice and look forward to working closely with him over the next four years," Hagel said.
