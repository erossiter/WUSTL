SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF JUDGE ROBERTS TO BE SUPREME COURT JUSTICE
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement tonight on President Bush's nomination of D.C. Circuit Court of Appeals Judge John Roberts to be an Associate Justice on the United States Supreme Court: 
   "President Bush has made a wise choice in nominating Judge John Roberts. Judge Roberts will bring a broad base of experience and jurisprudence to the Supreme Court. He should receive widespread support in the Senate following his confirmation hearings," Hagel said.
