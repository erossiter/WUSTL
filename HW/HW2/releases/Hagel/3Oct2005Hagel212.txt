SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF MIERS TO BE SUPREME COURT JUSTICE
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today on President Bush's nomination of White House Counsel Harriet Miers to be an Associate Justice on the United States Supreme Court: 
   "President Bush has chosen someone he trusts and who has a record of accomplishment. I look forward to learning more about her as the confirmation process moves forward," Hagel said.
