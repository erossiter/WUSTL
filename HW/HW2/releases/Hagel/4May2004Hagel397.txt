SEN. HAGEL AIDE ATTENDS OMAHA TRADE CONFERENCE
  Michael Considine, Senior Legislative Assistant on Trade for Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-Neb.), spoke today at an Omaha Chamber of Commerce event, "Trade Opportunities Eurasia: A Fresh Look At Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Russia and Georgia."  
 The event also featured speakers from the U.S. Department of Commerce, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan and Uzbekistan.
   "It is important Michael was able to discuss these trade opportunities with Nebraska business leaders. These growing markets are key to Nebraska's future and Eurasia's economic development," Hagel said.
   Before joining Hagel's staff Considine worked at the U.S. Department of Commerce as an international trade specialist. Prior to that, Considine spent 10 years in the private sector working on international trade issues in the Soviet Union, Eastern Europe, North Africa, and Asia.
   The event is sponsored by the Omaha Chamber of Commerce, Nebraska Export Assistance Center, Midwest International Trade Association, Nebraska Department of Economic Development, and Metropolitan Community College.
