SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF KIM LAURITZEN
  U.S. Senator Chuck Hagel issued the following statement on Tuesday following the death of Kim Lauritzen, who passed away last night at the age of 63. 
   "Kim Lauritzen represented the best of Nebraska and the best of the human spirit. Her dedication to service has made Nebraska a better and richer place. The grace with which she confronted her illness leaves a legacy of courage and inspiration for all of us. 
   "Lilibet and I join Nebraskans in offering our thoughts and prayers to Bruce Lauritzen and the Lauritzen family," Hagel said.
