SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON BUSH'S STATE OF THE UNION ADDRESS
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) issued the following statement regarding President Bush's State of the Union Address, delivered tonight: 
   "Tonight, the President connected the great challenges of our time with the historic opportunities we have to confront them. The President focused on opportunities to move the Middle East peace process forward, immigration reform, Social Security reform and a comprehensive energy policy. 
   With hard work, strong leadership and the President and Congress working together, we have the potential to do enormous good at home and around the world. I look forward to working with the President and my colleagues in addressing these great challenges in this important year."
