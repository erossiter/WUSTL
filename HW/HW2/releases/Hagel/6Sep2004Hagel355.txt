2004 FIRE PREVENTION AND SAFETY GRANT PROGRAM IS ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) announced today that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security is now accepting applications for the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Fire Prevention and Safety Program.  
 The application deadline is September 30, 2004 at 5:00 p.m (EST). 
   "It is important that Nebraska's fire prevention and safety personnel have the tools they need to efficiently and effectively protect our state's communities. I encourage all organizations that are eligible to apply," Hagel said. 
   The Fire Prevention and Safety Program assists state, regional, national, and local organizations in developing and implementing fire prevention and safety programs. The primary goal is to reach high-risk individuals including children, senior citizens, and firefighters. Applications and program guidance can be found at http://www.firegrantsupport.com. Questions can be addressed to the fire grant help desk at 1-866-274-0960 or to firegrants@dhs.gov.
