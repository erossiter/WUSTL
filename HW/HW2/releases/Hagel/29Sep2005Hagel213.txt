SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON CONFIRMATION OF ROBERTS AS CHIEF JUSTICE
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today on the U.S. Senate's confirmation of John Roberts as Chief Justice of the United States Supreme Court: 
   \"John Roberts is a person of strong intellect, character and judgement. America will be proud of Chief Justice Roberts and the many years of service he will give to America.\"
