SEN. HAGEL COMMENTS ON CONFIRMATION OF JUDGE ALITO TO U.S. SUPREME COURT
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today after Judge Samuel Alito was confirmed as an Associate Justice of the United States Supreme Court by a vote of 58 - 42. Hagel voted for Alito and announced his support of Judge Alito following a meeting with him in November: 
   "Samuel Alito brings a long distinguished record of experience, expertise and judgement to the United States Supreme Court. He will be an important voice on the Court and will be a Justice the American people will be proud of," Hagel said.
