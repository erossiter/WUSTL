SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON NATIONAL POET LAUREATE KOOSER WINNING PULITZER PRIZE FOR POETRY
  Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) released the following statement today following the announcement that National poet laureate Ted Kooser has won the Pulitzer Prize for poetry: 
   "Ted Kooser has a unique ability to capture the essence of Midwestern life in his poetry. His poems are an inspiration and treasure to Nebraska and the nation. Ted is deserving of this distinguished honor," Hagel said.
