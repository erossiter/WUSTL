SEN. HAGEL ISSUES STATEMENT ON BUSH'S ADDRESS TO NATION REGARDING REBUILDING FOLLOWING HURRICANE KATRINA
 Sen. Chuck Hagel (R-NE) issued the following statement regarding President Bush's address to the nation this evening from New Orleans: 
   "President Bush spoke plainly and directly to the American people tonight about the enormous task in front of us. The successful rebuilding of New Orleans and the Gulf Coast is a challenge shared by all Americans. To succeed, the resources of the federal government must be effectively and responsibly combined with the energy, innovation and entrepreneurship of the private sector."
