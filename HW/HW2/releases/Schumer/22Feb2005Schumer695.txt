    Latest News             TOPICS                                                                                                           Latest              News                           FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 22  2005 Schumer Announces Over  3 6 Million In Federal Funds Headed To Westchester County Airport For Security Enhancements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that Westchester County Airport has been awarded over  3 6 Million in federal funds for security enhancements  The funds are being awarded as an Airport Improvement Program grant through the Federal Aviation Administration in the U S  Department of Transportation         This is great news for all of Westchester County   said Schumer   The Westchester County Airport welcomes thousands of travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for additional economic growth in the region        This grant will provide the Westchester County Airport with perimeter security upgrades including new fencing and cameras  which are important in protecting both the members of the Westchester community and tourists visiting the New York City area
