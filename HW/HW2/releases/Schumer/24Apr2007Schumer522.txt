IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 24  2007 Schumer Announces  529 000 In Federal Funds For The Warren County Economic Development Corporation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Funds Will Be Used To For Construction Projects Related To The Creation Of A New Industrial Park                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced today that the Warren County Economic Development Corporation has been awarded  529 000 in federal funds by the Economic Development Administration  EDA  of the U S  Department of Commerce  DOC  as an EDA Investment Announcement   The funds will be used for construction projects related to the development of a new industrial park        This is great news for Warren County   Schumer said   The new industrial park will generate jobs and economic development in the community  and I am pleased that we are making this kind of investment   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the region as it becomes a thriving center of activity and hub for economic growth        The federal funds will be used by the Warren County Economic Development Corporation to support the construction of new roads  water  sewer and storm water management facilities necessary for the development of a new industrial park in Warren County   The entire project is expected to create 800 new jobs and generate over  25 million in private investment         The EDA assistance is available to rural and urban areas experiencing high unemployment  low income  or other severe economic distress   EDA works in partnership with state and local governments  regional economic development districts  public and private nonprofit organizations  and Indian tribes  They help distressed communities address problems associated with long term economic distress  as well as sudden and severe economic dislocations including recovering from the economic impacts of natural disasters  the closure of military installations and other Federal facilities  changing trade patterns  and the depletion of natural resources
