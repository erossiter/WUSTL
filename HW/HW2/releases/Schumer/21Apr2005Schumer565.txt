IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 21  2005 Schumer Announces Up To  60 000 For Frederick Edwin Church Exhibition In Hudson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  National Endowment for the Arts  NEA  Grants will Provide Support for Arts Education Programs    Schumer  Federal Funds to Support Art Exhibit and Educational Programming in Hudson US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced up to  60 000 in federal funding for an upcoming art exhibit  New York State received four of the eleven grants allocated by the National Endowment for the Art  s  NEA  American Masterpieces  Three Centuries of Artistic Genius national initiative  totaling  408 000 in federal dollars for arts programs        This is a wonderful opportunity for the people of Hudson   Schumer said   This exhibition will display the natural beauty of the Hudson Valley and will increase tourism and enhance the cultural life in Hudson          The exhibition  Treasures from Olana  Landscapes by Frederick Edwin Church  features the work of one of the most prominent members of the Hudson River School whose painting celebrated the natural beauty of the Hudson River Valley  The exhibition will provide an opportunity to see the artist  s   hidden    treasures as well as help them understand his work more fully as a microcosm    a trove of paintings  drawings  sculptures  photographs  prints  furniture  decorative arts  books  manuscripts  and miscellaneous objects of Church  s life and times  The grant will support the exhibition with funding to the American Federation of Arts to produce teachers   resource packages and information panels for the exhibition       The NEA  s American Masterpieces  Three Centuries of Artistic Genius initiative is a multi year grant program designed to connect Americans  especially school children  with the best of their artistic patrimony  With sufficient funding  the National Endowment for the Arts hopes that American Masterpieces initiative will also include grants for touring of major dance  choral and musical theater performances in future years       The National Endowment for the Arts is a public agency dedicated to supporting excellence in the arts and bringing the arts to all Americans  Established by Congress in 1965 as an independent agency of the federal government  the Endowment is the nation s largest annual funder of the arts  bringing great art to all 50 states  including rural areas  inner cities  and military bases
