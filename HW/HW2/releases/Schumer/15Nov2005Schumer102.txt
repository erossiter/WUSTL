IMMEDIATE RELEASE  November 15  2005 Schumer  Seniors Need More Time To Sign Up For Complicated Medicare Prescription Drug Plan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Senator    These plans are so complicated and the options are so varied  that Albert Einstein couldn  t understand them   U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer joined Sens  Bill Nelson  FL  and Dick Durbin  IL  and Families USA are urging the Senate to pass the Nelson bill to extend the time seniors will get to choose their Medicare prescription drug plan because the program is so confusing and difficult to understand  Nelson  s bill  which Schumer is a lead co sponsor of  would allow seniors an additional 7 5 months to decide which drug plan to enroll in under Medicare without penalty  for a total of 1 year   It would also allow seniors to change their plan once during that year to find the plan that best suited their needs and prevent seniors from losing existing employee provided coverage     Senator Schumer is a member of the Senate Finance Committee that oversees Social Security and Medicare  and is the author of the Greater Access to Affordable Pharmaceuticals Act of 2001 with Senator John McCain  R AZ   The Act enabled generic drugs to be brought to market sooner  and lowered the cost of prescriptions for millions of American seniors     Schumer said    These plans are so complicated  and the options so varied  that Albert Einstein couldn  t understand them  Instead of giving seniors easy options for low cost drugs  the Administration has created a Wild West of drug plans  and seniors who make the wrong choice in plans will be robbed        This is just way too confusing  This bill is one of the great government fiascos of the 21st Century  We need to tear up this plan and put a real plan into effect that helps seniors  not the pharmaceutical industry  Seniors need the Cliff Notes for these choices not the Encyclopedia Britannica     Schumer concluded     The   best    way to compare plans is located on the Medicare gov website  But only 6  of seniors have ever visited the site  and 76  of seniors have never used the Internet at all     For example  if you use the Medicare website and plug in some hypothetical information  If you live in DeWitt outside Syracuse and take five commonly prescribed drugs  all on the top 50 most prescribed drugs list  here  s what you get   Plavix    heart  Celebrex  anti inflammatory arthritis  Norvasc  blood pressure  Fosamax    osteopororis  Singulair  asthma allergies     The Medicare website returns this senior from Syracuse 42 PLANS  And the senior  s annual costs range from  2300 to  11 000  Estimated co pay ranges are from  0  250  estimated drug premium ranges from  9 00 to  85  and the monthly cost share  which is what you pay after the deductible  ranges from  125 to  872      For seniors with multiple drugs  trying to understand which plans cover all of their drugs  and the different kinds of cost sharing that will be required for drugs that are placed on different tiers within a plan  will be nearly impossible     61  of seniors report that they don  t understand the drug benefit and 43  haven  t figured out whether or not to sign up  And seniors who do not decide on a plan quickly will be penalized heavily if they ever choose a Medicare drug plan in the future      For seniors who are eligible for extra financial help  the forms are so long  intrusive  and confusing that many people in New York think it  s a scam and don  t want to fill it out  forgoing potential savings     The Medicare prescription drug program is also prevented from negotiating prescription drug prices using bulk orders like the Veteran  s Administration does to save billions of dollars
