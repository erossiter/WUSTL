IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 21  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 5 Million For Long Island Macarthur Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Grant will allow construction of a snow removal equipment building US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced  1 555 150 in federal funding for Long Island MacArthur Airport in Islip  The funding will be used to construct an addition to the current snow removal equipment building         Long Island MacArthur Airport is an economic engine that attracts a diverse array of industry and corporate offices to Islip due to its close proximity to major highways and railroads     Senator Schumer said    I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region         With more and more people flying into Islip  these funds will be a big help   Senator Clinton said    Islip airport is an integral part of the Long Island economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to the airport is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region       MacArthur Airport is a vital part of Long Island s economy  On behalf of the people of Islip and all of Long Island  I thank Senators Schumer and Clinton for stepping up to secure the future of this vital resource   Islip Supervisor Peter McGowan said       In addition to the existing fleet  the airport has acquired new  larger snow removal equipment  The current building is too small to house the new equipment and its structure restricts movement of machinery  The new facility will allow room to store the additional equipment
