IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  2 7 Million For Griffiss Airpark                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Money Will Be Used For Building and Equipment Improvement U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded two grants for  1 9 million and  869 250  respectively  in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Griffiss Airpark  The grants will go towards building improvements and replacement of the aircraft rescue and firefighting vehicle          This is great news for the Mohawk Valley   said Schumer   Building improvements and state of the art rescue equipment will ensure Griffiss remains an important part of the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a safe and thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region          This is great news for Griffiss Airpark and will be a considerable boost to their ongoing infrastructure and safety upgrades and its capacity to support the region  s continued growth     Senator Clinton said    For tourism and business alike  easy access by air is an essential ingredient for economic development  so this funding is important to help keep the planes flying and the passengers coming into the Mohawk Valley     Senator Clinton said       The airpark will use the  1 9 million in federal funds for improvements to the physical facilities including roof replacement  new doors and the demolition of old structures  The additional  869 250 will go towards the purchase of a new aircraft rescue and firefighting vehicle to replace the current one which is approaching the end of its useful life
