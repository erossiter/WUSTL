IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  3 Million For Elmira Corning Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Money will be used for Rehabilitation U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  3 148 268 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Elmira Corning Regional Airport  The money will be used for the rehabilitation of the runway         This grant is great news for the entire Southern Tier  and will help Elmira Corning Regional Airport to remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region  These improvements are important for local businesses and will strengthen the local economy   said Senator Schumer          These funds will help Elmira Corning Regional Airport with their runway upgrades  which are important for maintaining safety and ongoing operations  Right now  it is more important than ever that we make these kinds of investments to maintain airport infrastructure and keep flights and passengers coming into the Southern Tier  This is especially important when the region draws tourists from all over for the annual Chemung Steuben Air Show     Senator Clinton said       Federal dollars will be used for rehabilitation of the runway at Elmira Corning Regional Airport  The airport welcomes many commercial flights as well as the annual Chemung Steuben Air show and the National
