IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 26  2005 Schumer Announces  149 023 For Canton Public Radio Station                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Canton  s WSLU FM Public Radio Station to Receive Federal Funds the U S  Department of Commerce Public Telecommunications Facilities Program U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  149 023 for WSLU FM of Canton as part of the U S  Department of Commerce Public Telecommunications Facilities Program  PTFP   which supports the expansion and improvement of public telecommunications services that deliver educational and cultural programming  The federal funds will support the purchase of a new tower and transmitter in Canton  the technological upgrade of subsidiary station  WXLU FM in Peru  and the activation of FM translators in Chateaugay  Cape Vincent and Clayton       This is great news for the North Country     says Senator Schumer    Public broadcasting relies on the support of generous listeners and public funding  This funding will ensure that WSLU FM can purchase new technology  replace existing equipment  and continue its important role in the community        Founded 30 years ago  North Country Public Radio has grown into an around the clock service reaching the entire Northern Tier of New York State  parts of Canada  and western Vermont  WSLU FM carries popular national programming and local broadcasts  as well as producing internet feeds of live and archived broadcasts     Last week Senator Schumer announced that WSLU FM was awarded  80 000 by the Corporation for Public Broadcasting to assist in the radio station  s upgrade to digital transmissions  The federal funds will be used to purchase the equipment needed to begin broadcasting using digital transmission  significantly enhancing the quality and scope of program services     Canton  s WSLU FM is one of the four New York public radio stations which are receiving funding from PTFP this year  This round includes  21 4 million dollars in federal funds for 123 public telecommunications facilities throughout the country  The federal funding is awarded as a matching grant for public facilities to support the replacement and improvement of public broadcasting infrastructure  The program also provides funds to educational institutions and non profit organizations for technology oriented distance learning projects
