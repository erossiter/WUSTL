IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 18  2006 Schumer Announces Over  399 000 In Federal Dollars For Housing Programs In Western New York                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Federal Funds Awarded To People  Inc  As Part Of The Section 202 Demonstration Planning Grant Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  399 251 has been awarded to People  Inc  in Western New York by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  under the Section 202 Demonstration Planning Grant program   The funds will be used to establish apartments for low income seniors in Lockport and Angola           This is great news for Western New York  said Schumer    For far too many seniors  the cost of living is so high that it forces them to move elsewhere  or move into substandard housing   This funding will increase the number of affordable apartments available for low income seniors so that they enjoy the quality of life and sense of community Western New York has to offer          The grant award will be used by People  Inc  for the construction of affordable apartments for low income seniors in Lockport and Angola   Many seniors in these areas are currently living in sub standard housing  without the support they need to continue living independently   Founded in 1970  People Inc  is a leading non profit human service agency serving communities throughout Western New York   The agency provides a variety of services  including residential  employment  community outreach  health care  and recreation programs   Today  the agency provides over 300 units of quality housing for low income seniors in Amherst  Blasdell  Buffalo  Cheektowaga  Grand Island  and Hamburg       The Section 202 Demonstration Planning Grant Program is designed to assist sponsors of projects that received Fund Reservation Awards pursuant to the FY 2005 SuperNOFA for the Section 202 Supportive Housing for the Elderly Program by providing predevelopment grant funding for architectural and engineering work  site control  and other planning related expenses that are eligible for funding under the Section 202 Supportive Housing for the Elderly Program   Funding under this program will provide resources for predevelopment costs that would otherwise not be reimbursable until Initial Closing or would be payable from funding resources secured outside of Section 202 Capital Advance funding
