IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 17  2006 Schumer Announces Key Senate Panel Approves Funding For Broome County CATS Unit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Aggressively Lobbied Senate Appropriations Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill   Schumer  Helping Law Enforcement Fight Crime is a Top Priority and Vitally Important for the Safety of our Communities U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced that late last week  the Senate Appropriations Committee approved funding under the FY07 Commerce  Justice and Science Appropriations Act for Broome County project Computer Analysis   Technical Services  CATS  Unit   The bill must now be approved by the full Senate before being sent to a House Senate Conference Committee and eventually being signed into law   The exact dollar amount of funding will be included in the bill in the House Senate Conference Committee   Schumer aggressively lobbied members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this year  s spending bill         This is great news for Greater Binghamton    said Schumer     This funding is an important step in developing safety technologies and services   Helping law enforcement fight crime must be a top priority   The CATS Unit will provide critical assistance to law enforcement in order to keep communities in Broome County safe            Broome County Security s CATS Unit is a shining star of Shared Services and it is an integral partner in law enforcement efforts to fight on line crime   said Broome County Executive Barbara J  Fiala   Some of the most hideous crimes occur on line and Jim Thompson and the team of investigators who work out of our CATS Lab have been involved in sending some of the most heinous criminals to prison   I want to thank Senator Schumer for securing these much needed funds to keep this lab in operation and to allow the much needed training for the investigators who work in the lab        The Broome County Government Project Computer Analysis   Technical Services  CATS  Unit serves as a regional facility providing essential video and computer forensic services to law enforcement agencies throughout Central New York   The funding will be used to expand the CATS Unit so that it can eliminate unacceptable backlogs  hire and train two new investigators  and purchase computer and video forensics equipment         Late last week  the Appropriations Committee passed in the Commerce  Justice and Science spending bill  The bill now heads to the floor for a full vote before the Senate  and then a House Senate conference committee will reconcile the differences versions before sending it to the President to become law
