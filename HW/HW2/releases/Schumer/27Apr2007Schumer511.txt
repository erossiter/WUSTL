IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 27  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Chautauqua County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Village of Cassadaga and Town of Portland Will Use Federal Funds For Improvements to their Water Systems                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that two towns in Chautauqua County will receive federal funds from the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as Water and Waste Disposal Grants and Loans  The Village of Cassadaga will receive a  13 300 grant to improve the town s water delivery system and develop a new water source  The Town of Portland will receive a  473 000 grant and a  500 000 loan for completion of Water District  7        This is great news for the Village of Cassadaga and the Town of Portland   Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents   Clean drinking water is a basic need and this funding will help the communities meet that need  improving the quality of life for everyone in the area        The Village of Cassadaga will use federal funds for a preliminary engineering report to research costs involving a possible future water project  The Chautauqua County Health Department has been advising the Village to improve its system and develop a new source water well due to concerns over potential contamination of the existing shallow wells        The Town of Portland will use federal funds to construct Water District  7  Residents in this area currently rely on private wells and experience water shortages and unacceptable water quality  The proposed service area also currently has no fire protection capabilities       The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan program provides funds to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The grant program also sends funds to help develop water and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns   Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
