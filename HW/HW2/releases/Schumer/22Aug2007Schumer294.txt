IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  4 Million For The Elmira Corning Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal Money Will Be Used to Rehabilitate the Runway                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  4 363 085 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Elmira Corning Regional Airport  Federal funds will be used to perform an important runway rehabilitation project   This is great news for Elmira  said Schumer  The Elmira Corning Regional Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region  It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient  With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region   This is welcome news for New Yorks Southern Tier  said Senator Clinton  This improvement will go a long way in enhancing air travel to and from the region and to benefit local businesses and the local economy  I am pleased that we can further support the Elmira Corning Regional Airport as it continues to serve business travelers and tourists alike   Federal funds will be used to for a runway rehabilitation construction project  The construction will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
