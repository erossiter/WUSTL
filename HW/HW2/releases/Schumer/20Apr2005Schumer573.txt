IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 20  2005 Schumer Announces Federal Hotline For Local Flood Victims To Apply For Relief                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Schumer Pushed State to Declare Disaster  Urged FEMA To Quickly Grant Aid    Schumer  Flood Victims Should Call 1 800 621 FEMA To Apply For Assistance US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced a new hotline available for flood victims to apply for federal assistance  Residents in Broome  Chenango  Cortland  Delaware  Greene  Montgomery  Orange  Rensselaer  Schenectady  Schoharie  Sullivan  Tioga  and Ulster counties should call 1 800 621 FEMA to begin the process of receiving federal aid         You can t prevent natural disasters from happening  but we can help victims recover from them     Schumer said   This is important federal assistance that s needed to help a number of counties get back on their feet and I m glad the feds will help our residents recover from this month  s flooding  It is our job to help local areas in their times of need and I can tell you that this is definitely a time of need          FEMA provides money and services to people in the declared area whose property has been damaged or destroyed and whose losses are not covered by insurance  After the disaster area is declared  the state conducts   Applicant Briefings    for state and local officials so they can inform residents of the assistance available and how to apply for it  If a county is approved for Individual Assistance  residents in that area must call FEMA at 1 800 621 FEMA Telecommunications Device for the Deaf 1 800 462 7585 to register within the next 60 days       Last night  Schumer announced that Individual Assistance and Public Assistance were granted for the following counties  Broome  Chenango  Cortland  Delaware  Orange  Rensselaer  Schenectady  Sullivan  Tioga  and Ulster  Schoharie County will only get Individual Assistance  Public Assistance was granted for granted for Green and Montgomery Counties  Individual Assistance is for citizens who suffered losses  including the Individual and Households program  disaster unemployment insurance  and Small Business Administration loans  Public Assistance is to reimburse communities for debris removal  emergency response efforts and restoration of public infrastructure       Last week  in a personal phone call  Schumer urged FEMA Administrator Michael Brown to expeditiously grant Governor Pataki  s request for federal disaster assistance and speed up their PDAs to estimate the extent of the disaster and its impact on individuals and public facilities  Earlier  Schumer urged the State to declare the region a disaster area  a prerequisite for obtaining FEMA funds
