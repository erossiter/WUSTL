    Latest News             TOPICS                                                                                                           Latest              News                           FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 23  2006 Schumer Announces  152 000 For The Floyd Bennett Memorial Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal Funds Will Be Used For Runway Safety Improvements and Rehabilitation U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  152 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Floyd Bennett Memorial Airport in Warren County   The funds will be used for runway safety improvements and rehabilitation       This is great news for the North Country     said Schumer    The Floyd Bennett Memorial Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy in the Adirondacks   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region        The Floyd Bennett Memorial Airport will use the federal dollars for the design portion of a project to rehabilitate Runway 12 30 and improve the safety area   The existing surfaces are in poor condition and require rehabilitation to restore their condition and increase overall safety at the airport
