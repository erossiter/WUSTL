IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 24  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Village Of Marcellus                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 With The Water Treatment Facility Beyond Its Useful Expected Life  Federal Funds Will Be Used For Plant Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a loan of  327 000 and a grant of  269 000 have been awarded to the Village of Marcellus in Onondaga County  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan  Federal funds will be used to improve and upgrade the present wastewater treatment facility which no longer meets state requirements          This is great news for the Village of Marcellus   Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents   Clean drinking water is a basic need and this funding will help the community meet that need  improving the quality of life for everyone in the area        The Village of Marcellus will use federal funds to upgrade and improve the existing wastewater treatment plant   The existing plant is aging and does not meet New York State Department of Environmental Conservation  NYSDEC  requirements   Proposed improvements will provide a more efficient  energy conservative operation and will meet NYSDEC requirements   Nearly 2 000 people will be served by this project         The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan program provides funds to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The grant program also sends funds to help develop water and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns   Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
