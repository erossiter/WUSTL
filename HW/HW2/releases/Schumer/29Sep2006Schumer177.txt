IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 29  2006 Schumer Announces  106 000 For The Opportunities For Chenango  Inc                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Federal Funds Will Promote Economic Development By Improving Business Opportunities for Local Entrepreneurs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  106 360 has been awarded to the Opportunities for Chenango  Inc   in Norwich  by the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC    The federal funds will help create a marketing initiative for local micro entrepreneurs to expand the market for their products both within and beyond Chenango County       This is great news for Chenango County     Schumer said     Small businesses are an essential part of a thriving local economy   This funding will allow for new economic development initiatives that will help entrepreneurs promote their products locally and throughout the Southern Tier        Federal funds will be used by the Opportunities for Chenango  Inc  to merge two local economic development efforts  thereby creating a joint marketing initiative for local micro entrepreneurs to expand the market for their products throughout Chenango County and the surrounding region   The company plans to merge Bullthistle Rag Enterprise and Bullthistle Bounty   two grassroots economic development efforts  so that local micro entrepreneurs are able to access services to help them start  sustain  and grow new or existing businesses   This merger will allow for the creation of a joint marketing initiative to promote products created by local micro entrepreneurs   An integral part of Bullthistle Products will be marketing local products to local business outlets like grocery and convenience stores  gift shops  and hospitality services   This project would also implement an e commerce program that helps micro entrepreneurs sell their products through an eBay store and on a website for high end crafts and artists       Each year ARC provides funding for several hundred projects that create thousands of new jobs  improve local water and sewer systems  increase school readiness  expand access to health care  assist local communities with strategic planning  and provide technical  managerial  and marketing assistance to emerging new businesses  ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life  The ARC awards grants from funds appropriated to the Commission annually by Congress
