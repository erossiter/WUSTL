IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 5  2006 Schumer Announces  30 000 For Southern Tier West Regional Planning Development Board In The City Of Salamanca                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Funds Will Be Used To Bring Broadband Telecommunications Services To The Area                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  30 000 has been awarded to the Southern Tier West Regional Planning Development Board in Cattaraugus County by the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC    The federal funds will support the planning and analysis of ways to bring broadband telecommunications services to the town of Salamanca         This is great news for Cattaraugus County     Schumer said     Better internet and other broadband services will make business and communications much more efficient   This new service will boost the local economy by attracting new business to the region          Federal funds will be used by the Southern Tier West Regional Planning Development Board to aid the city of Salamanca in undertaking critical planning and analysis work in bringing in broadband telecommunications services   The new work elements will include data collection  analysis  market research  and expanded planning activities including community   aggregated of demand    for services and other related analysis       Each year ARC provides funding for several hundred projects that create thousands of new jobs  improve local water and sewer systems  increase school readiness  expand access to health care  assist local communities with strategic planning  and provide technical  managerial  and marketing assistance to emerging new businesses  ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life  The ARC awards grants from funds appropriated to the Commission annually by Congress
