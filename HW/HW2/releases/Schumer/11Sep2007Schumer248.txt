IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 11  2007 Schumer Announces Over  115 000 For Jamestown Community College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Federal Funds Will Be Used to Start a New Machine Tool Technology Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  116 095 has been awarded to Jamestown Community College in Chautauqua County by the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC   The federal funds will be used to start a new machine tool technology program    This is great news for city of Jamestown and the local community college  Schumer said  This funding will allow JCC students to get the training needed to sustain and build their local enterprises  They will no longer have to leave their community to get the latest technological training   Federal funds will be used to start a new machine tool technology program at Jamestown Community College  The local industry is concerned that they will not have the workers they need in the local area to sustain and build their enterprises  New workers able to use the newest programmable mills and lathes will be critical  To meet this need  Jamestown Community College will hire a new full time faculty person to teach the course and equip a machine tool lab with state of the art equipment    Each year the ARC provides funding for several hundred projects that create thousands of new jobs  improve local water and sewer systems  increase school readiness  expand access to health care  assist local communities with strategic planning  and provide technical  managerial  and marketing assistance to emerging new businesses  The ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life  The ARC awards grants from funds appropriated to the Commission annually by Congress
