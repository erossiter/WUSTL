IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Full Senate Approval Of  500 000 For North Country Children s Clinic                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Schumer  Clinton Urged Senate to Include Funding In Spending Bill Federal Funding Will Be Used to Build a New Children s Healthcare Facility in Downtown Watertown Bill Will Now Proceed to Conference with the House  U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced full Senate approval of the Labor  Health and Human Services and Education FY08 Appropriations Bill which includes  500 000 for the North Country Childrens Clinic  These funds will be used to construct a childrens healthcare facility in downtown Watertown  The bill will now proceed to conference with the House as the next step in the appropriations process  Schumer and Clinton urged the Senate to include funding for the project in this years spending bill     This is great news for families in Watertown and all of Jefferson County  as well as a critical step in the appropriations process  said Schumer  This clinic has provided our children with the affordable specialized care they need and deserve  Quality healthcare at a young age is essential to preventing health problems later in life     This is wonderful news for the North Country Childrens Clinic and Watertown  said Senator Clinton  It is imperative that all our children receive good healthcare  and the clinic has long provided this invaluable service for North Country families  Im glad that these funds will allow the clinic to continue its work in a new and modern facility     The North Country Children s Clinic will use federal funds for capital improvements including renovations and new construction of a children s health care facility in downtown Watertown  The Clinic was founded in 1971  by a group of volunteers led by Dick Charles and Dr  George Sturtz  to fill a void in pediatric health care for children without regular access to health care  The first Well Child Clinic was held on October 12  1971 at the North Side Improvement League  a donated site in Watertown  Today  the North Country Children s Clinic is a licensed health care facility serving low to moderate income families with a primary focus on uninsured and underinsured families  Services include health  dental  nutritional  mental health  and insurance enrollment programs     Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate it will move towards Conference with the House and then to the President for signature
