IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 15  2006 Schumer Announces Over  1 8 Million In Federal Dollars For Housing Programs In Chautauqua County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Funds Awarded To The City Of Jamestown As Part Of The Community Development Block Grants Program And The Home Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  1 822 550 has been awarded to the City of Jamestown in Chautauqua County by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD   A grant of  1 428 897 has been awarded under the Community Development Block Grants program and  393 662 has been awarded under the HOME program           This is great news for Jamestown   said Schumer    For far too many New Yorkers  the American Dream of owning your own home is out of reach  By providing support for prospective first time homeowners  initiatives like this stabilize neighborhoods and revitalize communities        The CDBG program provides annual grants to states and local governments to develop viable urban communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment  and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for low and moderate  income persons  In 2005  Senator Schumer supported the amendment to the budget which restored funding to the CDBG program  The amendment  introduced by Senator Norm Coleman  R MN   expressed the sense of the Senate that CDBG should be fully funded and remains with the Department of Housing and Urban Affairs      The HOME program helps to expand the supply of decent  affordable housing to low and very low income families by providing grants to state and local governments to fund housing programs that meet local needs and priorities       Housing and community revitalization are at the heart of HUD  s mission  and these grants are part of the Department  s plan to meet local needs and expand opportunities for Americans throughout the nation   HUD aims to create more affordable housing and empower large and small communities across the country so that every citizen can live the American Dream
