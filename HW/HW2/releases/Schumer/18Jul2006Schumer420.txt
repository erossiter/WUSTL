IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 18  2006 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars For Niagara Town Hall Expansion                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                USDA Rural Development Funds Will Improve Facilities for Police Department and Local Courts U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced a  50 000 grant and a  902 000 loan to the Town of Niagara for the Town Hall expansion   The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Community Facility Loan and Grant        This is great news for the Town of Niagara   Schumer said   Safety is essential to every community and requires a facility large enough accommodate the departments that protect this standard   The Town Hall expansion will serve the entire community by improving justice and security for Niagara and the surrounding areas          The town of Niagara will use the federal funds to expand the Town Hall which houses the Police Department  Court and meeting areas in order to improve access  security and safety   The town will receive federal grants and loans which will be repaid at a low interest rate over 30 years         The USDA Rural Development Community Facility Loan and Grant program administers programs designed to develop essential community facilities for public use in rural areas  These facilities include schools  libraries  childcare  hospitals  medical clinics  assisted living facilities  fire and rescue stations  police stations  community centers  public buildings and transportation  Through its Community Programs  the Department of Agriculture is striving to ensure that such facilities are readily available to all rural communities
