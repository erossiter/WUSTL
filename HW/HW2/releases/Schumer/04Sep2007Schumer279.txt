                                    Funds from Dept of Health and Human Services Grant Programs to Go to Local Research Labs for Medical Research Brookhaven National Lab Among Awardees for Antiviral Research  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that  1 336 206 in federal grants has been awarded to five Long Island institutions  including Brookhaven National Laboratory  by the Department of Health and Human Services for medical research   This is great news for Long Island  and a huge boon to the continued growth of cutting edge medical research here  said Schumer  These funds will allow researchers on Long Island to continue to push the barriers of scientific knowledge and will promote Long Islands research facilities as thriving centers of innovation   The  1 336 206 in HHS federal funding will go to    659 944 for Brookhaven National Laboratory from the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases for antiviral research   212 813 for Feinstein Institute for Medical Research from the National Institute of General Medical Sciences for research into sepsis and mediators   213 268 for Stonybrook Technology Applied Research from the National Institute of General Medical Sciences for research into smart lidocaine gel for pain management   177 713 for Endomedix  Inc  from the National Institue of Neurological Disorders and Stroke for research into gel fillers for eliminating cerebral aneurysms   72 468 for Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory from the National Cancer Institute for CSHL integrated data analysis for cancer research
