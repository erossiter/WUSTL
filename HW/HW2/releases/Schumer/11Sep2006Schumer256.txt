IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 11  2006 Schumer Announces Over  29 000 Coming To The Cooperative Extension Association Of Madison County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Funds Will Provide Assistance To Farmers Market Managers And Vendors Through Development Of Training Materials                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that the Cooperative Extension Association of Madison County has received a  29 829 federal grant from the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  under the Farmers Market Promotion Program  FMPP    Funds will be used to improve the ability of Agricultural Extension Agents to provide assistance to farmer  s market managers and vendors in their communities through the development of targeted training materials       This is great news for Madison County     said Schumer    Farmer  s markets provide some of the freshest quality foods produced by local members of the community   This funding will contribute to the local economy by ensuring that farmers markets have what they need to continue to provide the fruits  vegetables  and other agricultural products we look forward to each season        Federal funds will be used by the Cooperative Extension Association of Madison County  located in Ithaca  to improve the ability of Agricultural Extension Agents to provide relevant assistance to farmer  s market managers and vendors in their communities       The Farmers Market Promotion Program  FMPP  was created through a recent amendment of the Farmer to Consumer Direct Marketing Act of 1976   The grants  authorized by the FMPP  are targeted to help improve and expand domestic farmers markets  roadside stands  community supported agriculture programs and other direct producer to consumer market opportunities   USDA  s Agricultural Marketing Service  AMS  administers the program
