IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 20  2006 Schumer Announces Key Senate Panel Backs  350 000 For St  Elizabeth Medical Center In Utica                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Aggressively Lobbied Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill  The Federal Funding Will Enable The Renovation  Expansion and Modernization of St  Elizabeth Medical Center U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee is expected to approve later today  350 000 in the FY07 Labor  Health and Human Services and Education Appropriations Bill for the St  Elizabeth Medical Center in Utica  Schumer aggressively lobbied members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this year  s spending bill       This is great news for the Utica area     said Schumer    These federal funds will help bring new healthcare technology to the community  There is nothing more important than the health and well being of our citizens  and the modernization of St  Elizabeth Medical Center will improve the quality of life for those who use this facility        St  Elizabeth Medical Center is an institution devoted to excellence in healthcare and education  The federal funds will go toward the renovation  expansion and modernization of St  Elizabeth  s emergency department and various administrative aspects of the hospital plant  This includes a new emergency room  ambulance bay  kitchen and cafeteria     Later today  the Appropriations Bill for Transportation  Treasure  the Judiciary  Housing  and Urban Development is expected to pass the Appropriations Committee and will head to the floor for a full vote before the Senate  The bill will then proceed to the Conference Committee and finally will be sent to the President before becoming law
