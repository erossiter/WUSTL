IMMEDIATE RELEASE  May 19  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  2 9 Million For Albany International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Money will be used for Security Enhancements and to Expand the Apron US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded a total of  2 919 264 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Albany International Airport   2 128 950 for Security Enhancements and  790 314 to expand the apron         This is great news for the Capital Region   said Schumer   The Albany International Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region          This is great news for Albany International Airport  Anything we can do to support the families  security and services at Albany International is money well spent  because it is not just an investment in bricks and mortar  it is an investment in the local economy     Senator Clinton said       This grant will provide federal funding to preserve and enhance airport safety and capacity by making security enhancements at the airport and expanding the air cargo ramp  As part of the security enhancements  the Albany International Airport will add additional lanes to the TSA security checkpoint  expand the main concession area  and enable access to a second elevator  They will also create a second apron for FedEx to park during peak hours
