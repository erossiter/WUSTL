IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 4  2006 Schumer Announces Over  351 000 For Genesee Community College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Funds Will Be Used To Assist Students Who Wish To Pursue Postsecondary Education Programs U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that Genesee Community College in Batavia will be awarded a  351 061 grant by the U S  Department of Education under the  DOE  TRIO Educational Opportunity Centers  EOC  Program   The federal funds will be used to provide counseling on financial aid options and the admissions process for students applying to higher education programs         This is great news for Genesee Community College    said Schumer     It is so important that bright and motivated students have the resources they need to continue in their education  This program provides students with the information they need to continue building a future of success for themselves and the community          Federal funds will be used by Genesee Community College to provide outreach  counseling and information on college admissions to adults who want to enter or continue a program of higher education        Students will have access to services such as    Academic advice    Personal counseling    Career workshops    Information on postsecondary educational opportunities    Information on student financial assistance    Assistance in completing applications for college admissions  testing and financial aid    Coordination with nearby postsecondary institutions       Media activities designed to involve the community with higher education opportunities    Tutoring    Mentoring
