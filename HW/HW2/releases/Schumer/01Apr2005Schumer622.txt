IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 1  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  3 3 Million For Soundproofing Measures Near LaGuardia Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Funds will be used for soundproofing project at St  Athanasius School US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton announced today that the US Department of Transportation awarded  3 381 935 to the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey for noise mitigation measures  The funds will be used for a soundproofing project for St  Athanasius School  which is located in close proximity to LaGuardia Airport        It is so important that our students who attends schools in close proximity to New York airports be provided with a noise free and optimal learning environment   Senator Schumer said   School soundproofing will be a tremendous benefit to schoolchildren whose classrooms are affected by such aircraft noise         While it is important that we support La Guardia airport as a vital transportation hub for New York  we must also ensure that we seriously address the issues impacting the surrounding communities   Senator Clinton said   Hopefully  this sound proofing project at St  Athanasius will make living and learning next to one of New York s busiest airports easier        The school soundproofing program is jointly administered by the Port Authority and the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  under the federal Airport Improvement Program  AIP   The goal of these school aircraft noise abatement projects is to create a condition in the classroom in which the voice of a student or teacher is audible and intelligible  even in the presence of aircraft takeoffs and landings and daily operations at LaGuardia Airport
