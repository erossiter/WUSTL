IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 11  2007 Schumer Announces  300 000 Coming To JobStart Chautauqua                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Federal Funds Will Be Used to Help Village of Sherman Restore Historic Downtown                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  300 000 has been awarded to JobStart Chautauqua by the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD   under their Rural Housing and Economic Development Program  RHED   The federal funds will be used to help to help the Village of Sherman restore its commercial downtown   This is incredible news for the Village of Sherman and Chautauqua County  Schumer said  These much needed funds will help bring new vitality and jobs to the Village of Sherman by funding rehabilitation of its historic building stock  I will continue to fight for these federal funds to ensure that towns across the state can thrive and grow   Federal funds will be used to help the Village of Sherman restore several commercial buildings in its downtown  In a one block target area  a collection of late nineteenth century brick buildings  built in the village tradition with retail spaces on the ground floor and residential units above  will be rehabilitated for commercial and residential development  The funds will be spent rehabbing eight commercial spaces to unveil their period historicity and bring them up to code  and will include faade restoration and replacement of over 30 period historic windows  The final portion of the grant will be put into a revolving loan fund for businesses in the greater Sherman area to create and retain jobs for low and moderate income residents   The Rural Housing and Economic Development Program  RHED  works to create capacity at the State and local level for rural housing projects and economic development  It was established to help nonprofit organizations in rural communities across the country  Funds are available in two categories  Capacity Building  and Support for Innovative Housing and Economic Development activities
