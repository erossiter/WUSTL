IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 26  2006 Schumer Urges Feds Extend Deadline On C W M Chemical Services Application And Hold Public Hearing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer urged the U S  Army Corps of Engineers to extend the deadline on the C W M  Chemical Services application which seeks authorization to fill in federal wetlands to build and upgrade three facilities on the site  Schumer also requested that the U S  Army Corps of Engineers hold a public hearing during this extended comment period       It is very important for this community to have more time to comment     Schumer said    On decisions this important  it is essential that the permitting process be transparent  and that it maximizes public participation  Extending the deadline and holding a public hearing will not only allow the community more time to submit comments  it would be a good chance to educate them about this process         The C W M  site  the only active hazardous waste facility in the Northeast  is located in an area which has contamination from years of Department of Defense weapons manufacturing and waste disposal  The planned construction of a new landfill is a complex and significant proposal that concerns residents  local officials and state representatives  The U S  Army Corps of Engineers guidelines says that a lack of response means there is no objection to the work as proposed  however  the community may not be familiar with the process nor be aware of these guidelines      In a personal letter late yesterday to Scott L  Schlueter of the U S  Army Corps of Engineers  Schumer wrote    The residents have long lived with the burdens of past environmental mistakes made by the federal government  and deserve a deliberative and thorough process for the C W M  project  Again  I urge you to extend the comment period for this project  and schedule a public hearing in the upcoming months
