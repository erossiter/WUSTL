IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 18  2006 Schumer Announces Nearly  2 Million Coming To Western New York For Lead Elimination Programs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Funds Awarded To Environmental Education Associates Inc  As Part Of The Operation Lead Elimination Action Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  1 999 997 has been awarded to Environmental Education Associates Inc   in Erie County  by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  under the Operation Lead Elimination Action Program  LEAP    The funds will be used to increase lead control activities in Western New York           This is great news for Western New York  said Schumer     Lead poisoning  which causes permanent neurological damage  is the number one preventable childhood health problem in our nation  These funds are so vital precisely because they will help prevent this pernicious toxin from getting in our children s systems in the first place            Environmental Education Associates  Inc  will use this funding to partner with public and private organizations to increase the number of contractors and community based organizations performing lead control activities in Buffalo  Jamestown  Niagara Falls  Rochester  and Syracuse   These areas are all HUD Renewal Communities Empowerment Zones that have expressed the need for greater contractor capacity       The Operation Lead Elimination Action Program is designed to leverage private sector resources to eliminate lead poisoning as a major public health threat to young children  Operation LEAP provides seed money in the form of grants to applicants that demonstrate a concrete ability to leverage substantial private sector resources to be used for lead hazard control related activities  Resources generated through Operation LEAP are used and or distributed by non profits or for profits to assist National  State and local entities actively engaged in creating lead safe housing for families with young children on both a preventive and remedial basis
