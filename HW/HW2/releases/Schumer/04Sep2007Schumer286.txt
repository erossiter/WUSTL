IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 4  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 1 Million For Griffiss Airfield                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal Money Will Be Used to Construct an Access Road  Install Approach Aid  Rehabilitate Taxiway Lighting and Construct Terminal Building                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  1 160 800 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Griffiss Airfield in Oneida County  Federal funds will be used to construct an access road  install approach aid  rehabilitate taxiway lighting and construct a terminal building   This is great news for Oneida County  said Schumer  Griffiss Airfield is an important general aviation facility in this region  It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient  With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region    This is great news for Griffiss  especially in light of the funding announcement that came at the end of last month  said Senator Clinton  These continue to be positive investments in the facility that will allow for growth and ensure Griffiss remains an economic engine for the region   Federal funds will be used to construct an access road  install approach aid  rehabilitate taxiway lighting and construct a terminal building  These projects will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
