IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 1  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce  2 6 Million In Federal Money For Plattsburgh International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Funding Included in FY05 Omnibus Appropriations bill    Airport Improvement Grant Will be Used to Construct the  New Two Level Terminal Building US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that Plattsburgh International Airport has been awarded  2 6 Million in federal funds for the airport to construct the terminal building  The funds are listed in the Transportation  Treasury  and Independent Agencies Appropriations section of the Fiscal Year 2005 Omnibus Appropriations bill        This is great news for the entire North Country   said Schumer   Upon completion  the Plattsburgh International Airport will become a landmark to welcome travelers to New York  and will strengthen the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport as it becomes a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region         Plattsburgh Airport is one of the key gateways to the North Country and as an important tool in the growth of our upstate economy  we cannot do enough to support it   Senator Clinton said   The creation of this new terminal is critical step in the establishment of a world class airport right in Plattsburgh  I am pleased that we can continue to make these kinds of investments in the region s future        The federal funds will help cover the construction of a new two level passenger terminal and improvements to roadways and parking facilities  The project will provide funding to construct a two level passenger terminal building that will contain ticketing  baggage and security services on the first floor and administration rooms and hold areas on the second floor  Funding will also be used for the construction of access and circular roadways  a parking area and utility extensions       Clinton County will be relocating the existing airport general and commercial service from its existing location to the former military airport at Plattsburgh  Plattsburgh International Airport is located three miles south of the City of Plattsburgh
