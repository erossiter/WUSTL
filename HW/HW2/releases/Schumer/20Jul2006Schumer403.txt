                                Senator Aggressively Lobbied Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill  Schumer  Programs Will Make A Positive And Substantial Impact On Youth U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee is expected to approve later today  150 000 under the FY07 Labor  Health and Human Services and Education Appropriations Bill for the Boys and Girls Club of Mount Vernon  The bill must now be approved by the House Senate Conference Committee before being signed into law by the President  Schumer aggressively lobbied members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this year  s spending bill         This is great news for Boys and Girls Club of Mount Vernon   said Schumer    This organization gives children in the community a sense of belonging  friendship and safety  Children get a safe place to grow up and have fun  The Boys and Girls Club will put these federal dollars to good use  helping the children in their program develop leadership skills  broaden their learning  and experience a strong sense of community        The federal funding will support Character and Leadership Development programs that will empower youth to support and influence their Club and community  The participants will be encouraged to develop a positive self image  sustain meaningful relationships with others  participate in the democratic process and respect their own and others   cultural identities       Later today  the Appropriations Bill for Transportation  Treasure  the Judiciary  Housing  and Urban Development is expected to pass the Appropriations Committee and will head to the floor for a full vote before the Senate  The bill will then proceed to the Conference Committee and finally will be sent to the President before becoming law
