IMMEDIATE RELEASE  January 27  2006 Reid Schumer Urge President To Release All Records Of Meetings  Calls  Correspondence Involving Jack Abramoff                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Today Senators Harry Reid and Chuck Schumer released the following letter to President Bush urging him to immediately release all records of meetings  phone calls  and correspondence involving Jack Abramoff  It is clear that Abramoff had many significant relationships with members of the Bush Administration and in order for the public s confidence in our government to be restored  the Administration must be honest and forthright about its involvement with him        The letter is below      January 27  2006      President George W  Bush  The White House  1600 Pennsylvania Avenue  Washington  DC       Dear Mr  President       We write to ask you to direct the White House to release all records of meetings  phone calls  and correspondence involving Jack Abramoff        Mr  Abramoff  s significant ties to Republican leadership in Congress have already been well documented  We remain deeply concerned about these ties  but we are equally troubled by allegations of improper activity involving Administration officials  reaching  possibly  into the White House itself  Though White House Spokesman Scott McClellan originally insisted that Mr  Abramoff  s visits to the White House involved two holiday receptions and a few   staff level    meetings  recent revelations call these explanations into doubt        Recent news reports confirm that Mr  Abramoff met you on several occasions and during some of those meetings  Mr  Abramoff and his family had their photos taken with you  Mr  Abramoff also organized at least one and possibly several meetings with White House staff for his clients  This information reveals a relationship that is significantly more extensive than the one described by Mr  McClellan        In light of the numerous ties now documented between your Administration and Mr  Abramoff  we urge you to come clean and explain to the public the true extent of not only your personal interactions Mr  Abramoff but also the interactions between Mr  Abramoff and all White House personnel and officials        To this end  we ask that you provide to Congress and publicly disclose        Emails between staff members and Jack Abramoff or his associates        Video taken by the White House press office before every meeting         Guest lists for holiday receptions         Call logs         WAVE records       The Abramoff scandal continues to shake the public  s confidence in our government  in order to truly clear the air and restore the faith of the American people  your Administration needs to be honest about its involvement with Mr  Abramoff  We look forward to the prompt disclosure of this information       Sincerely   Harry Reid   Chuck Schumer
