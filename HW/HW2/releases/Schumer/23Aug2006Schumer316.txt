IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 23  2006 Schumer Announces  150 000 For Chautauqua Opportunities For Development  Inc                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Funds Will Improve Chautauqua Opportunities For Development  Inc   s  Ability To Pursue Its Mission To Alleviate Poverty By Promoting Self Sufficiency And Creating New Business Opportunities   Chautauqua Opportunities for Development  Inc  Serves New And Existing Entrepreneurs And Start up Businesses in Chautauqua County                                                               U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  150 000 for Chautauqua Opportunities for Development  Inc   CODI  from the Community Development Financial Institutions  CDFI  Fund of the U S  Department of the Treasury  A financial assistance grant of  50 000 will allow the CODI to expand the basic banking services it provides to an underserved community  A technical assistance grant of  100 000 will be used to purchase new technology and for staff training and compensation         This is great news for Western New York     said Schumer     Programs like these provide critical services  and CODI allows organizations to help people when they need it the most   This funding will lead to improvements within CODI that will increase the efficiency and effectiveness of their services  leading to stronger communities and small businesses throughout the region        CODI will use the financial assistance grant to capitalize its loan fund   The technical assistance grant will go towards purchasing additional technology  training staff  and assisting with the compensation of two staff members   CODI  established in 2002  makes capital  training and technical assistance available to entrepreneurs and start up businesses in Chautauqua County in order to create business opportunities and employment for low to moderate income individuals  CODI offers a variety of services  including loan programs  one on one technical consulting and entrepreneurial training courses and workshops      Community Development Financial Institutions Fund uses federal resources to invest in and build the capacity of private  for  and non profit financial institutions with a primary mission of community development  known CDFI   Its mission is to expand the capacity of financial institutions to provide credit  capital and financial services to underserved populations and communities in the United States   Since its creation  the Fund has made  729 million in awards to community development organizations and financial institutions   Financial Assistance awards leverage private sector resources to help CDFI further goals of economic development  affordable housing and community development financial services   Technical assistance grants can be used to purchase needed computer hardware or software  obtain consulting services  or pay for the training of staff
