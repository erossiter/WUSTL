IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 13  2005 Schumer Announces Over  900 000 For Western New York In HUD Grants                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Grants Awarded as Part of the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  Program and the EDI Special Project Grant  Grants Will Improve Housing Options for Families in Dunkirk and Improve Facilities at Canisius High School in Buffalo US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that the US Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  has awarded  919 648 in federal funds to Western New York  The City of Dunkirk in Chautauqua County will receive  651 403 from the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG   Canisius High School in Buffalo will receive  268 245 from the EDI Special Project Grant        One of the biggest problems facing New Yorkers is the rapidly escalating cost of housing  putting a home out of reach for many hardworking families   said Schumer   I believe initiatives like the CDBG program provide critical resources that help to make homeownership a reality for many deserving families        The CDBG program provides annual grants to states and local governments to develop viable urban communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment  and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for low and moderate  income persons  This year  Senator Schumer supported the amendment to the budget which restored funding to the CDBG program  The amendment  introduced by Senator Norm Coleman  R MN   expresses the sense of the Senate that CDBG should be fully funded and remain with the Department of Housing and Urban Affairs         This is a great day for the students  parents  and faculty of Canisius High School     said Schumer   The education of our children is of critical importance to the development of our community  and improving our school facilities is one necessary step in achieving that goal           The EDI Special Project Grant will be used for facility renovations at Canisius High School in Buffalo  The HUD grant is part of the plan to meet local needs by improving the educational facilities of the Canisius High School       Housing and community revitalization are at the heart of HUD  s mission  and these grants support these goals  They hope to create more affordable housing and empower large and small communities across the country so that every citizen can live the American Dream
