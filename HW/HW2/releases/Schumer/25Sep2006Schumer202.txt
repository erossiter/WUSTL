IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 25  2006 Schumer Announces Over  624 000 In Federal Dollars For Port Of Albany   Rensselaer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Federal Funds Will Be Used To Enhance Security Throughout Port Environment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  624 750 has been awarded for the Port of Albany   Rensselaer by the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS  under the Port Security Grant Program                                                                   This is great news for the Capital Region  for far too long  Albany has been eligible for port security funds  but has received nothing from DHS   said Schumer   The Port of Albany Rensselaer continues to grow and thrive  ships and crews trading from all over the world come into the Port on a daily basis   While that is certainly good for the region  s economy  it also creates obvious security concerns   This year  DHS is finally recognizing that the Capital Region needs these security funds too          In 2005 the Port of Albany Rensselaer imported and exported a combined 579 000 tons of cargo with trading partners from countries around the globe    Through the first eight months of this year  the Port has already handled a combined 529 000 tons of cargo  and is on pace to increase last year  s total by a significant margin       The FY 2006 Port Security Grant Program provides funds to enhance the security of the Nation  s ports and port facilities  This year  the emphasis is protection from improvised explosive devices  non conventional methods of attack  and increased domain awareness in the port environment  The Nation  s most critical seaports  as identified by the United States Coast Guard  were eligible to apply for funding under this program  DHS is awarding a total of  168 052 500 nationwide under the program this year
