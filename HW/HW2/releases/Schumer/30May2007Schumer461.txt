IMMEDIATE RELEASE  May 30  2007 Schumer Announces  12 500 For The Delaware County Chamber Of Commerce                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Federal Funds Will Be Used For A Tourism Assessment For Communities Surrounding The Catskill Park                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  12 500 has been awarded to the Delaware County Chamber of Commerce by the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC    The federal funds will be used to coordinate a tourism assessment for communities surrounding the Catskill Park          This is great news for Delaware County and all the communities surrounding the Catskills   Schumer said    This funding will allow local communities to unite their efforts to improve tourism to the region which will in turn provide a huge boost to the local economy        Federal funds will be used by the Delaware County Chamber of Commerce to coordinate a tourism assessment for communities surrounding the Catskill Park   The funding will cover the training and technical assistance to assess the area s growth potential and help local communities create a shared vision for the future   Training and technical assistance will be provided by the Conservation Fund and the national Trust for Historic Preservation Heritage Tourism Program       Each year ARC provides funding for several hundred projects that create thousands of new jobs  improve local water and sewer systems  increase school readiness  expand access to health care  assist local communities with strategic planning  and provide technical  managerial  and marketing assistance to emerging new businesses  ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life  The ARC awards grants from funds appropriated to the Commission annually by Congress
