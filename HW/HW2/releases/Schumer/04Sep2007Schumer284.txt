IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 4  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 2 Million To Stewart International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Money Will Be Used to Acquire Snow Removal Equipment  Install Miscellaneous NAVAIDS and Acquire VALE infrastructure                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded three grants totaling  1 298 500 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Stewart International in Orange County  The first grant  totaling  175 000 will be used to acquire snow removal equipment  The second grant  totaling  70 000  will help install miscellaneous NAVAIDS  A final federal grant for  1 053 500 will be used for gate electrification systems for docking aircrafts  VALE infrastructure     This is great news for Orange County  said Schumer  The Stewart International Airport welcomes many thousands of travelers to Hudson Valley each year  These improvements are important for local businesses and will strengthen the local economy  These funds will be instrumental in increasing safety and capacity  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport thrive and serve as a hub for economic growth in the region   This is great news for Orange County  said Senator Clinton  Stewart International Airport continues to create more travel opportunities for the region and these funds will provide new facilities and equipment to ensure continued safe and efficient operation I am pleased to support this effort that will help the airport remain critical to the local economy   The
