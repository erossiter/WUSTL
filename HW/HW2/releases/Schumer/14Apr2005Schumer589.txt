IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 14  2005 Schumer Announces Over  1 2 Million In Federal Housing Money For Auburn                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Grants Awarded as Part of the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  Program US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that the US Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  has awarded  1 225 163 in federal funds to one community in Cayuga County  The federal dollars come from the Community Development Block Grant program         One of the biggest problems facing New Yorkers is the rapidly escalating cost of housing  putting a home out of reach for many hardworking families   said Schumer   I believe initiatives like this program provide valuable resources that help to make home ownership a reality for many deserving families        The CDBG program provides annual grants to States and local governments to develop viable urban communities by providing decent housing and a suitable living environment  and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for low and moderate  income persons  This year  Senator Schumer supported the amendment to the budget which restored funding to the CDBG program  The amendment  introduced by Senator Norm Coleman  R MN   expresses the sense of the Senate that CDBG should be fully funded and remain with the Department of Housing and Urban Affairs        Programs like these can help stabilize neighborhoods and revitalize communities  while at the same time provide an opportunity for members of the community to become first time homeowners   Schumer said       Housing and community revitalization are at the heart of HUD  s mission  and these grants are part of the Department  s plan to meet local needs and expand opportunities for Americans throughout the nation  HUD aims to create more affordable housing and empower large and small communities across the country so that every citizen can live the American Dream
