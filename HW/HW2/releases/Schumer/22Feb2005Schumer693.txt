    Latest News             TOPICS                                                                                                           Latest              News                           FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 22  2005 Schumer Announces  437 000 In Federal Funds Headed To Adirondack Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal grant will help the airport construct an apron US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that Adirondack Regional Airport has been awarded  437 000 in federal funds for the airport to construct an apron  The funds are being awarded as a grant from the Federal Aviation Administration in the U S  Department of Transportation         This is great news for the entire North Country   said Schumer   The Adirondack Regional Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region        This project will provide federal funds for the construction of a terminal apron expansion area in order to meet existing and future demand for additional aircraft parking space at the airport  The Adirondack Regional Airport serves as the key airport for the Adirondacks and Olympic region around Lake Placid
