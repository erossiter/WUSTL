IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 30  2005 Schumer Announces  1 5 Million Investment For City Of Yonkers Industrial Development                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Schumer  Economic Assistance is Important for Strengthening the Community and Creating Jobs U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced an investment of  1 500 000 for the City of Yonkers Industrial Development Agency from the U S  Department of Commerce  s Economic Development Administration  EDA   The federal funds will be used to renovate the n Valley Technology Center       This money will be a shot in the arm for Yonkers     Schumer said    Real economic development demands planning and this funding helps to ensure that any growth is sustainable  I will continue to push for funding for programs that help New York State communities becoming booming centers of business activity        This investment will help to fund renovation of the site for the n Valley Technology Center  the development centerpiece and catalyst for a major redevelopment effort in the city  s Empowerment Zone  It will create 800 new jobs in the short term and generate private investment of  30 million     EDA was established to generate and help retain existing jobs  as well as stimulate industrial and commercial growth in economically distressed communities  EDA works to empower distressed communities by encouraging development and implementation of their own economic development and revitalization strategies  Program assistance is available to areas experiencing high unemployment  low income  or other severe economic distress  EDA helps communities address problems associated with long term economic distress  as well as sudden and severe economic dislocations including recovering from the economic impacts of natural disasters  the closure of military installations and other Federal facilities  changing trade patterns and the depletion of natural resources      Last year  Schumer helped secured  280 000 million for the n Valley Technology Center in Yonkers  Those funds were used to build a technology center that includes a 32 000 square foot incubator for biotechnology  lab space  and new media space  The funding was included in the Fiscal Year 2004 Omnibus Appropriations Bill
