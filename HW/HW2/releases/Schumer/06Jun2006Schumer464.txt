IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 6  2006 Schumer Announces Over  245 000 For Eagle Creek Marina In Kendall                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                U S  Fish And Wildlife Services   Grant Will Provide Support For Boating Infrastructure Enhancements U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  245 741 in federal funding for the Eagle Creek Marina in Kendall from the U S  Fish and Wildlife Services   Boating and Infrastructure Grant  BIG   in the U S  Department of Interior  The federal dollars will provide support for continuing their boating infrastructure enhancements        With the summer tourist season just beginning  these dollars are great news for Lake Ontario boaters     Schumer said    Boating and fishing are not just recreational activities  but are a vital part of the upstate economy  It is important that we upgrade and maintain our facilities so they can continue to thrive  These federal dollars will help Eagle Creek become a destination for boaters  and will benefit the entire region         The Eagle Creek Marina will use the federal funds to help with the construction of 24 new transient slips with utilities  additional new restrooms  and the installation of an updated sewage system and a fuel dock for transient boaters  The Marina will contribute  267 756 in matching funds for the project      The U S  Fish and Wildlife Service is a public agency that works with others to conserve  to protect and to enhance fish  wildlife  and plants and their habitats for the continuing benefit of the American people  This program  s dollars comes from the Sport Fish Restoration and Boating Trust Fund  which is supported by excise taxes paid on certain fishing  boating equipment and boat fuels  The BIG grants enables large projects  such as Eagle Creek Marina  to compete for funding  with these grants matching up to 75 percent of a project  s cost
