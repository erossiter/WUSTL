                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Cedartech  Inc  Will Use Funds To Provide Law Enforcement with Handwriting Identification Software                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that  153 289 has been awarded to Cedartech  Inc   located in Williamsville  in Erie County  by the U S  Department of Justice  s  DOJ  National Institute of Justice  NIJ  under the Forensics DNA Program         This is great news for Williamsville     said Schumer     This funding will provide the needed technology to identify and apprehend dangerous criminals who would otherwise remain on the streets   Better technology for crime investigation will increase safety for the entire community        This project will enable software created with a 2004 NIH grant for Quantitative Assessment of the Discriminatory Power of Handwriting and Validating Improving Handwriting Identification Procedures to be transferred to the Question Document Community   Project objectives include  rigorous software testing  assistance on using the software  creation of a user manual  telephone support  creation of software patches as necessary  and making software updates available for download   After one year  the software is expected to be self supporting   The DOJ NIH Forensic DNA Program funds research to improve the use of forensic DNA in criminal investigations  This program s goal is to direct advances in human genetics  molecular biology  and biotechnology toward the development of novel tools and technologies that will permit DNA to be used in all areas of criminal investigation
