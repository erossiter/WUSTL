IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 5  2005 Schumer Announces  43 000 For Rural Opportunities Enterprise Center Inc                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Federal Funds Will Allow Financial Support for Farmers and Small Businesses and to Expand to More Areas in Upstate New York  Rural Opportunities Enterprise Center Currently Serves Western New York and Finger Lakes Regions  and will Expand with this Funding U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  43 000 to Rural Opportunities Enterprise Center  Inc  ROECI   based in Rochester  from the Community Development Financial Institutions  CDFI  Fund of the U S  Department of the Treasury  This Technical Assistance grant covers the costs of expanding the Center  s capacity so it can provide financial support to more regions of upstate New York        This is great news for upstate New York     said Schumer    Farming and small businesses are the backbone of our economy  The services and financing opportunities that this funding will help ROECI provide can be felt throughout upstate New York        The federal funds for ROECI will be used to obtain consulting services  staff time  training and equipment to expand capacity to expand into new territories  ROECI is a certified CDFI established in 1998 serving the Western  Central Northern Tier and Finger Lakes regions of Upstate New York by providing micro loans  business training and related services  It has since expanded into other NY state counties  and with this TA Grant will expand further serving targeted population in the state  ROECI is a subsidiary of Rural Opportunities Inc   which is a regional CED dedicated to improving the social economic and living conditions of farm workers and other rural low income individuals in a multi state area      The Community Development Financial Institutions Fund  the Fund  was created for the purpose of promoting economic revitalization and community development through investment in and assistance to community development financial institutions  CDFIs   Its mission is to expand the capacity of financial institutions to provide credit  capital  and financial services to underserved populations and communities in the United States  Since its creation  the Fund has made  729 million in awards to community development organizations and financial institutions  The Technical Assistance component of the CDFI Program provides grants to CDFIs  and entities proposing to become CDFIs  in order to build their capacity to better address the community development and capital needs of the communities they serve  TA grants can be used to purchase needed computer hardware or software  obtain consulting services  i e  development of loan policies  or pay for the training of staff
