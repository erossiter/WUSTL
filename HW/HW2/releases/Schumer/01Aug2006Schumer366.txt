IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 1  2006 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Emergency Medical Squad In Warren County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Funds Will Be Used To Purchase New Ambulances and Stretchers U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a  40 000 grant and  190 300 loan was awarded to the Lake George Post  374 American Legion Emergency Squad to purchase two new ambulances and two stretchers   The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as Community Facility Loan and Grant        This is great news for Lake George and the surrounding communities   Schumer said    We need to ensure that EMS responders have the equipment they need to do their job well   These funds are truly a life saving investment that will provide the Lake George squad with essential equipment so they can be prepared for emergencies throughout Warren County          The federal funds will be used to purchase two new 2006 Type IIIE 450 ambulances and two new stretchers   The EMS service has increased its number of calls substantially over the past few years adding to the wear and tear on the old vehicles   This has caused increasing maintenance costs   This facility serves a population of over 4 500       The USDA Rural Community Facility Loan and Grant program funds may be used to construct  enlarge  or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services   This can include costs to acquire land needed for a facility  pay necessary professional fees  and purchase equipment required for its operation
