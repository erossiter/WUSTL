IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 31  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Village Of Brocton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Federal Funds Will Be Used to Update Wastewater Treatment Plant                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a loan of  1 812 900 and a grant of  490 000 have been awarded to the Village of Brocton in Chautauqua County  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan  Federal funds will be used to update the wastewater treatment facility   This is great news for the Village  of Brocton  Schumer said  The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents  Clean drinking water is a basic need and this funding will help the community meet that need  improving the quality of life for everyone in the area    These funds will be used to repair and update the villages wastewater treatment plant  Results from recent Department of Environmental Conservation inspections show that the current facility periodically exceeds design capacity and State Pollution Discharge Elimination System flow limitations  Proposed improvements will eliminate foam currently discharged via the final effluent to Slippery Rock Creek  and will reduce extraneous flow to the collection system   The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan program provides funds to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The grant program also sends funds to help develop water and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns  Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
