IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 16  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  200 000 For Greater Binghamton Edwin A  Link Field                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Federal Money Will be Used for Airport Security Enhancements Washington  DC  U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  224 200 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Broome County for the Greater Binghamton Edwin A  Link Field  The federal funds will be used for airport security enhancements at Greater Binghamton Edwin A  Link Field    This is great news for Binghamton said Schumer  The Greater Binghamton Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region  Not only does it provide local aviators and tourists with a base of operation  but it allows businesspeople visiting New York to easily access the region  Keeping this airports running smoothly  safely and securely is crucial  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region   This is welcome news for the Greater Binghamton Edwin A  Link Field  said Senator Clinton  While we are disappointed by Delta s recent decision  this is a vote of confidence in the airport which is first class and allows for business travelers and tourists alike to visit and invest locally  We will do everything we can to keep flights coming in and out of Binghamton   Broome County will use federal funds for airport security enhancements required by Title 49 Code of Federal Regulations  Part 1542
