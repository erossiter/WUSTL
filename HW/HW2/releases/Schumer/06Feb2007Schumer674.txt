IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 6  2007 Schumer Announces WNY Counties Eligible For Federal Disaster Aid                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Farmers in Allegany  Cattaraugus  Chautauqua  Erie and Wyoming Counties Will Be Eligible For Emergency Aid Following Torrential Fall Rains                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  has designated Cattaraugus County a primary natural disaster area and Allegany  Chautauqua  Erie and Wyoming Counties as contiguous disaster areas due to losses caused by excessive rain and cool temperatures this past fall        This is great news for Western New York farmers who have had a particularly difficult year due to severe weather conditions   said Schumer    Farmers are the backbone of our economy and when they are hurting  the whole state feels the impact of the damage   Assistance from USDA is essential for farmers to cover losses  and I am pleased to see the government stepping up to provide this aid        This past fall  between August 27 and November 30 Cattaraugus  Allegany  Chautauqua  Erie and Wyoming Counties experienced excessive rain and cool temperatures leading to significant production losses which  in turn  triggered a natural disaster designation         This designation makes farm operators in both primary and contiguous counties eligible to be considered for low interest Emergency loans from USDA s Farm Service Agency  FSA   FSA will consider each application on its own merit by taking into account the extent of losses  security available  and repayment ability   These emergency loans are designed to help producers recover from production and physical losses due to drought  flooding  other natural disasters  or quarantine   Emergency loan funds may be used to restore or replace essential property  pay all or part of production costs associated with the disaster year  pay essential family living expenses  reorganize the farming operation  and refinance certain debts
