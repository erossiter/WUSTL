IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 23  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce  1 9 Million For Griffiss Airpark                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Federal Money Will Be Used For Building Improvements And Modifications                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  1 900 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Griffiss Airpark in Oneida County  under the Airport Improvement Program  s Military Airport Program  The grant will go towards building improvements and modifications          This is great news for the Mohawk Valley   said Schumer   With more improvements to Griffiss Airpark  it will have state of the art facilities and be at the forefront of safety for passengers and employees   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a safe and thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region          This is more great news for Griffiss Airpark in an ongoing effort to update and improve the safety and security of its buildings  This funding is important to maintain efficiency on the ground to maximize service for its employees and passengers     Senator Clinton said       The airpark will use the federal funds for a roof replacement for Building 100  modifications to Building 220 and for the demolition of Building 150
