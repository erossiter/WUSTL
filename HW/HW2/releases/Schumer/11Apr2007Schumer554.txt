IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 11  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Funds Coming To Firefighters In Franklin County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Federal Dollars Will Be Used to Purchase a New Pumper Truck and Refinance an Existing Tanker Truck                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced today that a  50 000 grant and two loans totaling  191 000 have been awarded to the Duane Volunteer Fire Company  Inc  in Franklin County by the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Community Facility Loan and Grant   The Duane Volunteer Fire Company  Inc  will use the loan to purchase a new pumper truck and to refinance an existing tanker truck          This is great news for Franklin County   Schumer said   We can never truly repay our first responders for their heroic efforts and dedication to duty  but we can ensure they have the equipment and resources they need to do their jobs well  These trucks will allow first responders to better serve the various rural communities throughout the county        The Duane Volunteer Fire Company  Inc  will use a  108 000 loan and a  50 000 grant to purchase a new pumper truck  A loan of  83 000 will be used to refinance a 2005 Kenworth tanker truck  already owned by the Fire Company  This would lower their payments and offer significant savings to the Fire Company         Community Programs  a division of the Housing and Community Facilities Programs  is part of the United States Department of Agriculture s Rural Development mission area  Community Programs administers programs designed to develop essential community facilities for public use in rural areas  These facilities include schools  libraries  childcare  hospitals  medical clinics  assisted living facilities  fire and rescue stations  police stations  community centers  public buildings and transportation  Through its Community Programs  the Department of Agriculture is striving to ensure that such facilities are readily available to all rural communities  Community Programs utilizes three flexible financial tools to achieve this goal  the Community Facilities Guaranteed Loan Program  the Community Facilities Direct Loan Program  and the Community Facilities Grant Program
