IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Prioritizes Funding For Batavia Wifi Project                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WiFi Network Will Provide Cutting Edge Wireless and Mobile Communication Services To Businesses And Residents    Senators Worked Closely With Committee To Include Project In The Bill    Bill will now be sent to the Senate Floor                                                                                                                                                              U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Agriculture  Rural Development  Food and Drug Administration  and Related Agencies FY08 Appropriations Bill  which includes Batavia WiFi as a project to be considered for funding from the USDAs Rural Development Telecommunications Program at a later time  The bill will now proceed to the Senate Floor as the next step in the appropriations process  Schumer and Clinton worked closely with members of the Appropriations Committee to include the project in this years spending bill   This is great news for Batavia  said Schumer  Wireless  high speed internet is the wave of the future that will connect Batavia businesses to the 21st century economy  Wireless networks will enable entrepreneurship to flourish and will attract new types of cutting edge  high tech business to the region  This green light from the Committee signifies that the project has cleared a significant hurdle and we are now a giant step closer to making a WiFi network in Batavia a reality   Access to the technology is an important tool for students and families but it is also critical for government  for small businesses as the most effective way to reach the global marketplace  and for our emergency services as they work to keep our community safe  The Batavia WiFi project represents an investment in the future of the entire community  Senator Clinton said  With technological advances being made each day we need to ensure that everyone has access to the high tech tools like Wi Fi so people can get connected and can get the job done  I am very pleased that we could secure these funds as I believe they will have benefits for the community as a whole   The Committee has designated the Batavia WiFi project as a project to be given consideration for funding by the USDAs Rural Development Telecommunications Program  The project would provide wireless and mobile communications among city departments  including emergency services to an area in need  It would also encourage economic development by offering affordable internet and phone service to local businesses and residents in a rural area  Project funds would be used to install communications towers and other equipment   Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill will move towards Conference with the House and then to the President for signature
