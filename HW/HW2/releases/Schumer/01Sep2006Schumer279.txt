IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 1  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  3 5 Million For The Chautauqua County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Funds Will Be Used For Runway Safety Area Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  3 500 515 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Chautauqua County Airport   The funds will be used to make improvements to the runway safety area         This is great news for Chautauqua County   said Schumer   The Chautauqua County Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these safety improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport become a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region          This is great news for Chautauqua County Airport     said Senator Clinton    These funds will be used to provide the necessary safety measures that are essential for efficient operation   I am pleased that we can continue to support the airport with this federal funding because more flights mean better economic opportunities for the surrounding communities            This funding is further proof that Senators Schumer and Clinton are effective advocates and supporters of the Chautauqua County Airport  Their work to deliver the funds has allowed Chautauqua County to combine two projects into one to cut costs and save the taxpayers over  400 000  while we improve our County Airport   state Chautauqua County Executive Gregory J  Edwards       The Chautauqua County Airport will use the federal dollars to for phase two of a project to improve the runway safety area
