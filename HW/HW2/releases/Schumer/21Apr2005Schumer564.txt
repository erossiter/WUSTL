IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 21  2005 Schumer Announces Over  4 4 Million For Utica In HUD Grants                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Grants Awarded as Part of the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG   Emergency Shelter Grant  ESG  and HOME Programs US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that the US Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  has awarded  4 412 371 in federal funds to the City of Utica in Oneida County  The federal dollars come from several programs  including the Community Development Block Grant   3 352 529   the Emergency Shelter Grant   129 842  and HOME   929 999         One of the biggest problems facing New Yorkers is the rapidly escalating cost of housing  putting a home out of reach for many hardworking families   said Schumer   We need to do everything possible  including supporting programs like these  to make homeownership a reality for many deserving families        The CDBG program provides annual grants to states and local governments to develop viable urban communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment  and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for low and moderate  income persons  This year  Senator Schumer supported the amendment to the budget which restored funding to the CDBG program  The amendment  introduced by Senator Norm Coleman  R MN   expresses the sense of the Senate that CDBG should be fully funded and remain with the Department of Housing and Urban Affairs       The ESG program provides annual grants to State  local and private entities to improve the quality and number of emergency homeless shelters       The HOME program helps to expand the supply of decent  affordable housing to low and very low income families by providing grants to state and local governments to fund housing programs that meet local needs and priorities       Housing and community revitalization are at the heart of HUD  s mission  and these grants support these goals  They hope to create more affordable housing and empower large and small communities across the country so that every citizen can live the American Dream
