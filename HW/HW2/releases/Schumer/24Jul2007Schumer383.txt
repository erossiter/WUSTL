IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Prioritizes Funding For The Fort Edward Health Center                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 As the Only Federally Qualified Health Center In Washington County  This Facility Will Provide Care for Local Communities So Patients Don t Have To Travel To Other Counties    Senators Worked Closely With Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill    Bill will now be sent to the Senate Floor                                                                                          U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Agriculture  Rural Development  Food and Drug Administration  and Related Agencies FY08 Appropriations Bill  which includes the Fort Edward Health Center as a project to be considered for funding from Rural Housing Service  RHS   Department Of Agricultureat a later time  The bill will now proceed to the Senate Floor as the next step in the appropriations process  Schumer and Clinton worked closely with members of the Appropriations Committee to include the project in this years spending bill   This is great news for Washington County  said Schumer  Every community deserves to have local health care facilities so that patients dont have to travel long distances to receive the care they need  This green light from the Committee signifies that the project has cleared a significant hurdle  We are now a giant step closer to making the Fort Edward Health Center a reality   This is wonderful news for everyone in Fort Edward and especially for those who are most in need  This funding will enable the establishment of a local Health Center in Fort Edward to better serve our working families and elderly citizens  Senator Clinton said  As the only Federally Qualified Health Center in Washington County  this new facility will serve several communities  eliminating the burden of traveling long distances to receive quality health care   The Committee has designated Fort Edward Health Center as a project to be given consideration for funding by RHS  This new facility would serve many communities throughout the county including the Town of Fort Edward  the Village of Hudson Falls  and the Town of Kingsbury  Currently  no Federally Qualified Health Center  FQHC  exists in Washington County  Medicaid and uninsured patients are forced to travel to neighboring Warren and Saratoga counties to receive care  while those who cannot travel are subject to long waits in the regions overburdened emergency rooms   In the past 12 months  Hudson Headwaters health centers in adjacent counties have received more than 30 000 patient visits from residents of Washington County towns  with nearly 24 000 from Fort Edward and Hudson Falls residents  more indication that the region is in need of a new facility  Currently  25 9 percent of Fort Edwards population and 32 9 percent of Hudson Falls live below 200  of the poverty level  The area also has a higher than average elderly population  The Fort Edward Health Center would become part of Hudson Headwaters Health Network  a Section 330 Federally Qualified Health Center directly meeting the charge of Health and Human Services to provide access to primary care for indigent Americans including the uninsured and those living in rural areas   Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill will move towards Conference with the House and then to the President for signature
