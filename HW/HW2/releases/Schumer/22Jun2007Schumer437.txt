IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Approves  500 000 For North Country Children s Clinic                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senators Worked Closely Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill    Federal Funding Will Be Used To Build A New Facility In Downtown Watertown    U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Labor  Health and Human Services and Education FY08 Appropriations Bill which includes  500 000 for the North Country Childrens Clinic  These funds will be used to construct a childrens healthcare facility in downtown Watertown  The bill will now proceed to the Senate Floor as the next step in the appropriations process  Schumer and Clinton worked closely with members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this years spending bill   This funding is great news families in Watertown and all of Jefferson County  said Schumer  This clinic has provided our children with the affordable specialized care they need and deserve  Quality healthcare at a young age is essential to preventing health problems later in life   The North Country Childrens Clinic has provided critical healthcare and support services for North Country children and families for more than thirty years  Building a new healthcare facility in downtown Watertown and modernizing existing facilities will enable the clinic to continue to meet the needs of area families  said Senator Clinton   The North Country Children s Clinic will use federal funds for capital improvements including renovations and new construction of a children s health care facility in downtown Watertown  The Clinic was founded in 1971  by a group of volunteers led by Dick Charles and Dr  George Sturtz  to fill a void in pediatric health care for children without regular access to health care  The first Well Child Clinic was held on October 12  1971 at the North Side Improvement League  a donated site in Watertown  Today  the North Country Children s Clinic is a licensed health care facility serving low to moderate income families with a primary focus on uninsured and underinsured families  Services include health  dental  nutritional  mental health  and insurance enrollment programs    Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill will move towards Conference with the House and then to the President for signature
