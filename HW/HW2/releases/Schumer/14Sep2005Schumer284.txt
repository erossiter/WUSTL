IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 14  2005 Schumer Announces  35 000 to Alfred Technology Resources for Local Job Creation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Federal Funds will be used in Development of an Improved Business Model to Create Three New Hi Tech Companies                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced a  35 000 award to Alfred Technology Resources  Inc   ATRI  from the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC   The federal funds will be used to develop a plan to improve the business model for the Ceramics Corridor Innovation Centers  CCIC  in Painted Post and Alfred  ATRI expects the plan to lead to the construction of three new hi tech companies that will be clients of CCIC  This partnership will create nine new local jobs in the process        This is great news for the Southern Tier     Schumer said    The comprehensive business plan will allow improvements within CCIC to generate jobs and economic development in the community  and I am pleased that we are making this kind of investment  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the region as it becomes a thriving center of activity and hub for economic growth         CCIC  which began as a partnership between Alfred University and Corning  Inc   has a long record of accomplishment of producing jobs and business success stories  Since its creation twelve years ago  CCIC clients have created or utilized over 69 patents and a variety of intellectual property  trade secret processes  techniques  and material compositions  These achievements have been recorded in over 137 papers and journals  The CCIC has also created 2 434 jobs and an estimated  485M cumulative economic benefit  Alfred Technology Resources  ATRI  is a not for profit corporation dedicated to job creation  development and research in the ceramics  glass  advanced materials  and materials science technologies in the Ceramics Corridor of New York State      The ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life  The ARC awards grants from funds appropriated to the Commission annually by Congress  ARC funding for this project is provided through a special ARC initiative that targets funds to encourage the adoption of telecommunication applications that strengthen local economies and contribute to long term self sufficiency in the region
