IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 9  2007 Schumer Announces Additional WNY And North Country Counties Eligible For Federal Disaster Aid                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Farmers in Allegany  Cattaraugus  Chautauqua  Erie  Genesee  Jefferson  Lewis  Livingston  Monroe Niagara  Ontario  Orleans  Oswego  Seneca  St  Lawrence  Steuben  Wayne  Wyoming and Yates Counties Will Be Eligible For Emergency Aid Following Summer Flash Floods                                                                                                                                           U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  has designated Allegany  Chautauqua  Jefferson  Niagara  Ontario  and Wyoming Counties a primary natural disaster areas and Cattaraugus  Erie  Genesee  Lewis  Livingston  Monroe  Orleans  Oswego  Seneca  St  Lawrence  Steuben  Wayne and Yates Counties as contiguous disaster areas due to losses caused by excessive rain and flash flooding that occurred during the period of August 1  2006 and continuing        This is great news for Western New York and North Country farmers who have had a particularly difficult year due to severe weather conditions   said Schumer    Farmers are the backbone of our economy and when they are hurting  the whole state feels the impact of the damage   Assistance from USDA is essential for farmers to cover losses  and I am pleased to see the government stepping up to provide this aid        This past summer  beginning on August 1st and continuing Allegany  Cattaraugus  Chautauqua  Erie  Genesee  Jefferson  Lewis  Livingston  Monroe Niagara  Ontario  Orleans  Oswego  Seneca  St  Lawrence  Steuben  Wayne  Wyoming and Yates Counties experienced excessive rain and flash flooding leading to significant production losses which  in turn  triggered a natural disaster designation         This designation makes farm operators in both primary and contiguous counties eligible to be considered for low interest Emergency loans from USDA s Farm Service Agency  FSA   FSA will consider each application on its own merit by taking into account the extent of losses  security available  and repayment ability   These emergency loans are designed to help producers recover from production and physical losses due to drought  flooding  other natural disasters  or quarantine   Emergency loan funds may be used to restore or replace essential property  pay all or part of production costs associated with the disaster year  pay essential family living expenses  reorganize the farming operation  and refinance certain debts
