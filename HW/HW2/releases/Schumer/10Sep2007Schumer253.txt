IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 10  2007 Schumer Announces Nearly  400 000 Coming To My Sister s Place In White Plains                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    My Sister s Place Will Use Funds to Help Immigrant Women Who are Victims of Abuse                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer announced today that  399 977 has been awarded to My Sisters Place in White Plains by the U S  Department of Justices Office on Violence Against Women under the Legal Assistance for Victims Grant Program  The agency will use these federal funds to help immigrant women who are the victims of abuse    We need to send an unequivocal message that violence against women is unacceptable  Schumer said Battered women should never have to think twice about whether or not to report their abusers  and should always have a place to go  This grant will provide needed services for victims in White Plains   My Sisters Place is a domestic violence and sexual assault victim services agency providing direct advocacy and supportive services to immigrant victims of domestic violence  sexual assault  stalking and dating violence  The agency will use these federal funds to provide civil legal representation and advice  supportive services and outreach to victims within its service area   The Legal Assistance for Victims Grant Program is designed to strengthen civil and criminal legal assistance programs for adults and youth victims of domestic violence  dating violence  sexual assault and stalking who are seeking relief in legal matters arising as a consequence of that abuse or violence  Eligible applicants include nonprofit  nongovernmental legal services organizations  nonprofit  nongovernmental victim services agencies  public entities operating in their nongovernmental capacity  and Indian tribal governments
