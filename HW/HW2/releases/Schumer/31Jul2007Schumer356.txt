IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 31  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Town Of Windsor                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Funds Will Used To Construct a Wastewater Treatment Plant For The Town s Water Supply                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a loan of  100 000 has been awarded to the Town of Windsor in Broome County  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Loan  Federal funds will be used to build a new Wastewater Collection and Treatment Plant   This is great news for Windsor  Schumer said  The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents  Clean drinking water and an effective wastewater system are basic needs and this funding will help the community meet that need  improving the quality of life for everyone in the area  The Town of Windsor will use federal funds for the construction of a Wastewater Collection and Treatment Plant to serve the residents of the Hamlet of West Windsor  Residents were relying on failing private wells and the problem has been exacerbated by heavy rains during the tropical depression which raised the groundwater table inundating the septic disposal systems and causing contaminated water supplies    The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan program provides funds to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The grant program also sends funds to help develop water and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns  Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
