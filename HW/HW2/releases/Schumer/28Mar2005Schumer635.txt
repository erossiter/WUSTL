IMMEDIATE RELEASE  March 28  2005 Schumer  1 1 Million Coming To Greater Glens Falls Development Corporation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Funds will be used for a business incubator and education facility    New Grant Will Help Create 60 New Jobs in Glens Falls US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that  1 1 million in federal funds was awarded to the Greater Glens Falls Development Corporation to assist in the funding of a business incubator and education facility  The funds are being awarded through the Economic Development Administration s Public Works Program in the U S  Department of Commerce    This is great news for Glens Falls and the entire region   Schumer said   The business incubator and education facility will bring the best and the brightest together and mean a big boost for the local economy  This is the latest example of Glens Falls developing into a thriving center of business activity        The Greater Glens Falls Development Corporation will use this investment to help fund a high tech business incubator and education facility in Glens Falls  This will create 60 permanent new jobs  and generate  8 5 Million in private investment       The Economic Development Administration s mission is to lead the federal economic development agenda by promoting innovation and competitiveness  preparing American regions for growth and success in the worldwide economy  The Public Works Program empowers communities to revitalize  expand  and upgrade their infrastructure to attract new industry  encourage business expansion  diversify local economies  and generate or retain long term  private sector jobs and investment
