IMMEDIATE RELEASE  March 22  2005 Schumer  Federal Dollars Coming To Town Of Huron                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Funds will be used to Install New Water Main US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a  110 400 grant and a  821 100 loan was awarded to the Town of Huron to assist in installing a new water main  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant        This is great news for the Town of Huron  Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of town residents and finding ways to make the system better will improve the quality of life in the area        The rural development funds will be used to install new water main for water district 3  The area is currently dependent on well water that historically has produced low yields and poor quality water       USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant program works to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The Loan and Grant program also sends funds to help develop waste and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns  Loans and grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
