IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 5  2006 Schumer Announces  1 Million In Federal Dollars Coming To The Town Of Windsor                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Town Will Use Funds For Construction Of The   West Windsor Sewer District   U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a  1 million grant was awarded to the Town of Windsor to assist in the construction of the   West Windsor Sewer District    The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as Water and Waste Disposal Grant        This is great news for the Town of Windsor and everyone involved   Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents  This funding for the water system will improve the quality of life in the area        The rural development funds will be used for the construction of the   West Windsor Sewer District    The tropical storm depression that hit this area in late 2004 caused severe damage to the septic systems  The Health Department documented over 100 surface discharges of wastewater and it remains a chronic problem due to failing on site sewage systems within the hamlet  The project will include a gravity flow wastewater conveyance system with pump stations at low points and a sequencing batch reactor wastewater treatment facility to address and correct current and future needs  The number of Equivalent Dwelling Units is 307  Additional funds are being leveraged from the New York State Revolving Loan Funs in the amount of  6 125 800        The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant program works to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The Grant program also sends funds to help develop waste and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns  Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
