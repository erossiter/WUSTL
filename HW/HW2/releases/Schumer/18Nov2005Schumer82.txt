IMMEDIATE RELEASE  November 18  2005 Schumer  Clinton  McNulty Announce Congressional Approval Of  25 5 Million For Military Construction Projects To Benefit The Capital Region                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Schumer  Clinton  Defense Projects are Vitally Important for Capital Region  the New York State Economy and National Security U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton and Representative Michael McNulty announced that the Joint House Senate conference committee has approved the FY 2006 Military Construction Appropriations Bill  including  25 5 million for projects benefiting the Capital Region         Now that we  ve successfully fought to keep Stratton Air National Guard Base and Watervliet Arsenal open through the BRAC process  we can focus on keeping the Capital Region  s bases at the forefront of our national defense efforts  Our guardsmen and reservists have been at the front line in our nation s war on terror around the world  and these men and women play a vital role in the local community  We must do everything we can to support them and ensure that they have the most up to date facilities available when they are called to duty     Senator Schumer said         The men and women of the National Guard are making sacrifices for our freedom every day    both here at home and overseas  This funding is a recognition of the heroic work they do every day and demonstrates a real commitment to ensuring they have the best equipment and training available  I am glad our colleagues in Congress agreed with us about how important this funding is     Senator Clinton said       Congressman Michael R  McNulty  D Green Island  said    Construction of these facilities is important to our national security  to the security of our State  and to our efforts to retain missions and jobs at the Stratton Air National Guard Base  The Navy Reserve Center in Glenville is expected to create 40 jobs and host hundreds of reserve and guard troops who will train at the facility on a regular basis and generate additional economic activity for Schenectady County  No one deserves these state of the art facilities more than the men and women of the National Guard and Reserve  who have sacrificed so much for us and for our country           The following projects were approved by the conference committee      Army National Guard   Readiness Center   5 580 million   The funding will go towards providing a readiness center addition  of permanent type construction  to serve the peace time missions of the assigned units and space for all personnel to perform necessary wartime missions as well      Navy Reserve   Joint Reserve Center   19 970 million   The funding will go towards the construction of a new Armed Forces Reserve Center at the Schenectady County Airport to support Navy  Marine Corps and Army National Guard drilling reservists       The FY 2006 Military Construction Appropriations Bill was approved by the Senate in September  and now having been released by the conference committee  must be approved by both chambers of Congress and sent to the President for his signature  Schumer and Clinton urged members of the Conference Committee to keep the funding for these projects in this year  s spending bill
