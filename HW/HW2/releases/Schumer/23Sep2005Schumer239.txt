IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 23  2005 Schumer Announces Over  111 000 For Community Foundation For Greater Buffalo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Funds Awarded by Project Safe Neighborhoods to Reduce Gun Violence                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       U  S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  111 987 for the Community Foundation for Greater Buffalo by the U S  Department of Justice  s Project Safe Neighborhoods  PSN   The federal funds will be used to reduce gun violence in the community        When it comes to protecting our communities  we need to be tough on crime and smart on prevention     Schumer said    Finding ways to create safer neighborhoods must be top priority  and law enforcement need the tools to keep Buffalo  New York  a safe place to live and work        The Community Foundation for Greater Buffalo is implementing the gun violence strategy of the Western District of New York  s Project Safe Neighborhood Task Force  They are continuing to fund a prosecutor at the Niagara County District Attorney  s Office  dedicated to working with federal  state and local law enforcement to deter and prosecute gun violence  They are contracting with Partners Against Violence Everywhere  PAVE  to conduct media outreach services to include billboard  radio  and television sports and promotional materials      PSN is a nation wide commitment to reduce gun crime by networking existing local programs that target gun crime and providing these programs with additional tools necessary to be successful  PSN seeks to achieve heightened coordination among federal  state  and local law enforcement  with an emphasis on tactical intelligence gathering  more aggressive prosecutions  and enhanced accountability through performance measures
