IMMEDIATE RELEASE  May 4  2005 Schumer  Federal Dollars Coming To Town Of Hanover                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Funds will be Used to Improve Town  s Water Distribution System US Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a  500 000 grant and a  100 000 loan was awarded to the Town of Hanover to assist in improving the water distribution system  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant        This is great news for the Town of Hanover   Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents  This funding for the water system will improve the quality of life in the area        The rural development funds will be used to improve the existing water distribution system which continues to experience multiple breakdowns and therefore causes health concerns for Water District 1  Leveraging funds include  4 475 000 from the Drinking Water State Revolving Fund  DWSRF        Last month  Senator Schumer wrote a letter to Glen King  Director of the New York State Governor  s Office for Small Cities  advocating for the Town of Hanover  s application for funding under the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  Program  That funding would enable the town to implement the Hamlet of Irving Water System Improvement Project         Historically  the water distribution system in the Town of Hanover  s Hamlet of Irving has experienced multiple water main breaks and is incapable of satisfying fire protection criteria  These conditions have led to health concerns  public safety issues and limited residential and economic development in the region  The proposed water system improvement project will help eliminate public health and safety risks and will reduce the number of water main breaks which will in turn eliminate the current health risks and potential ingestion of contaminants  Additionally  the proposed water system improvements will ultimately decrease the risk of property loss and reduce insurance premiums     Schumer wrote    By improving fire protection and water quality  the Town of Hanover hopes to improve the quality of life for Irving  s residents by protecting life and preserving decent  affordable housing  I applaud the Town of Hanover in its foresight and hope its application for funding meets with your approval          The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant program works to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The Loan and Grant program also sends funds to help develop waste and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns  Loans and grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
