IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 8  2006 In Light Of Hockey Mania Taking Over WNY  Schumer Lobbies For 2008 USA Hockey National Championships To Be Held In Amherst                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       The Sabres Recent Drive For The Stanley Cup Has Renewed The Region  s Enthusiasm For All Things Hockey As the Youth Council deliberates on a location for the 2008 USA National Hockey Championships  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer wrote to USA Hockey Inc  to urge the organization to choose Amherst  New York as the host of the 2008 USA Hockey National Championships  Schumer helped Amherst in their successful bid to host the 2007 Championships       Watching the Sabres make it to the Eastern Conference Finals was a thrill     Schumer said    It has reenergized Western New York  s enthusiasm for hockey  and more and more children are joining youth hockey leagues  From their state of the art facility and necessary accommodations to their passion for the sport  Amherst is the ideal location to hold this event a second time        Amherst Youth Hockey has a long  impressive history  Amherst Youth Hockey was started in 1964 and has continued to thrive since then  If Amherst is chosen as the site for this event  it will bring Metropolitan Buffalo and the State of New York national recognition as well as an economic impact of several million dollars  Amherst has demonstrated a strong devotion to the ice hockey  as the home of the Amherst Pepsi Center  a state of the art facility with four ice surfaces and a brand new fitness center  is also used for practice sessions by the Sabres  Due to its close proximity to the State University of New York at Buffalo campus  the facility is surrounded by the large numbers of restaurants and hotels that would be necessary to host the players and families of the USA Hockey National Championships  The final announcement by the Youth Council for which city will get the 2008 USA Hockey National Championship will go out Saturday  June 10th     Located right across the border from Canada  Western New York is imbued with a passion for ice hockey   from its diehard support for the NHL  s Buffalo Sabres  to the thriving college and high school programs  to the thousands of young people and their parents who dedicate so much to this sport  Western New York has long been a hotbed of hockey enthusiasm  producing scores of first rate players including Amherst native and first round draft pick  Brooks Orpik  who now plays for the Pittsburgh Penguins     In his personal letter to the National Tournament Director of Youth Ice Hockey David Klasnick  Schumer wrote    As one of the largest youth hockey organizations in the United States  Amherst Youth Hockey is well qualified to host this prestigious event and I fully support their efforts to become the host organization plays
