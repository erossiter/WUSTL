IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 30  2005 Schumer Announces  HHS To Launch Federal Investigation Of Lake Grove Schools And Treatment Centers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Earlier this Month  Senator Sent Letter to HHS Inspector General to Address Long Standing Issues with Lake Grove Centers  HHS   Shares Concerns     Agrees to Commencing IG Investigation in Response to Schumer Request  This Spring  Lake Grove Sober Homes were Shut down by NY State for Fraud Earlier this month  after news reports of a rape at a Lake Grove facility that came just weeks after New York state shut down all of the Lake Grove operated sober homes in Suffolk County  Senator Charles E  Schumer called on the Inspector General of Health and Human Services to launch an investigation into Lake Grove Schools and Treatment centers in New York  Connecticut  and Massachusetts  Schumer wrote to HHS Inspector General Daniel Levinson to look into the licensing of all Lake Grove facilities  Today  Schumer revealed that HHS is launching a federal investigation into Lake Grove Schools and Treatment centers         Federal Medicaid funding has been providing the mothers milk to perpetuate a system of abuse and neglect     Schumer said    I worry that Lake Grove facilities have been hurting more people than they have helped for a long time now  and I  m thrilled that HHS is finally going to get to the bottom of this  Its time for us to start helping the people who need treatment and the children seeking an education by offering safe facilities where they know they cannot be harmed           Community leaders welcomed the news    Senator Schumer  s quick action is paying important dividends     said the Rev  Kennedy McGowan  pastor of Brentwood Presbyterian Church and a leader in Long Island Congregations Associations and Neighborhoods    The kind of investigation HHS has announced is key to ensuring that anyone taking advantage of vulnerable people and misusing precious health care funds is held  to account           Schumer has been asking HHS to look into Lake Grove Facilities for well over a year  Last year  Schumer asked the HHS Inspector General to commence an investigation into the licensing of Lake Grove sober homes on Long Island to ensure that the good homes continued to thrive and that the bad homes were weeded out  The Agency deferred to the state to address the problems directly  which led Schumer to contact OASAS  Following Schumer  s actions Lake Grove was forced to turn over its sober homes facilities to the state to recommend whether the facilities should close or operate under new management        Following reports of sexual abuse earlier in the month Schumer called on HHS to Launch a Fedral Investigation into Lake Grove facilities in New York  Connecticut and Massachussetts       In a letter dated August 2nd  Schumer wrote    Facilities run by Lake Grove each receive tens of thousands  and in some cases  hundreds of thousands of dollars from Medicaid every year  It is my grave concern that the facilities are being grossly mismanaged  putting residents at risk and using federal money inappropriately  I hope that you share my concern and will launch a complete investigation looking into every aspect of the operations of Lake Grove facilities in New York  Massachusetts  and Connecticut  We cannot allow Lake Grove Schools and Treatment Centers to continue to neglect the care of its patients and students or to use federal money in the process        Daniel Levinson  Inspector General of Health and Human Services  wrote in a letter back to the Senator   You expressed concern regarding mismanagement of Lake Grove facilities  putting residents at risk and using federal money inappropriately  We share your concern  We will conduct a review of Medicaid funding received by Lake Grove facilities  We will begin by gathering information on the total amount of Medicaid dollars paid to each individual Lake Grove facility in New York  Massachusetts and Connecticut  Once we gather the Medicaid payment information  we will select specific Lake Grove facilities for review
