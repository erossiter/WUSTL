Hurricane Katrina and the terrorist attacks of 9 11 have taught us that our local first responders must be well equipped and well trained     Schumer said    With budget gaps worse than they have been        Two grants for a total of  120 306 were awarded to fire departments and organizations in two Central New York communities  the Clinton Fire Department   88 301  in Oneida County and the
