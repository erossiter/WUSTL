IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 15  2006 Schumer Announces Over  318 000 Coming To Agricultural Businesses In Central New York                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Funds Will Be Used By Businesses in Cayuga and Onondaga Counties under the USDA Rural Development Grant program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that federal dollars have been awarded to two organizations in Central New York by the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development Value Added Agriculture Product Market Development Grant Program   Lake Distilling  LLC  in Cayuga County  has received a grant award of  300 000 to operate a start up ethanol plant  Wake Robin Farm  in Onondaga County  has received a grant award of  18 861 to establish a marketing campaign        This is great news for Central New York  Schumer said   Many of our farms  wineries  and other agricultural businesses produce high quality products yet do not have the resources needed to market them to the public   This funding will provide our agricultural businesses with tools that will allow them to expand  profit  and provide more consumers with their quality products         Lake Distilling  LLC will use federal dollars to purchase the necessary inventory needed to operate a start up ethanol plant using locally grown wheat and corn  Wake Robin Farm will use federal dollars to establish a marketing campaign for a pasture based yogurt manufactured from milk produced by Wake Robin Farm       The USDA Rural Development Value Added Agriculture Product Market Development Grant program provides federal funds to be used for planning activities and for working capital for marketing value added agricultural products and for farm based renewable energy   Funds are made available to agricultural commodity producers who refine their products in order to increase the value of these products to users and customers   Eligible applicants are independent producers  farmer and rancher cooperatives  agricultural producer groups  and majority controlled producer based business ventures
