IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 3  2006 Schumer  Clinton  Senate Unanimously Approves Clinton Bill To Rename Averill Park Post Office After Major George Quamo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  DC    Senators Hillary Rodham Clinton and Charles Schumer announced that legislation to honor Major George Quamo was passed unanimously yesterday by the U S  Senate   The bill  which Senator Clinton sponsored and Senator Schumer co sponsored  would designate the United States Postal Service facility located in Averill Park  NY as the   Major George Quamo Post Office           A true hero and role model  Maj  George Quamo served our nation with valor  leadership  and bravery  I am sincerely thankful to my colleagues in the Senate for unanimously approving this bill  This is a mark of true respect for a man who made the greatest of sacrifices to our nation  I am so pleased that we were able to get this done for his family and his community     Senator Clinton said        Most of us will never know what soldiers endured during their service overseas   The men and women in our military risk the ultimate sacrifice to serve our country and protect our liberties  George Quamo epitomized bravery  patriotism and public service but his life was tragically cut short in Vietnam  It is only fitting that the post office in Quamo  s hometown should bear the name of this great American hero who will be honored in the Averill Park community forever     Senator Schumer said     Maj  Quamo  a member of the Special Forces Unit in the Vietnam War  was killed in 1968 at age 27 when the helicopter he was traveling in crashed outside of Laos in a heavily wooded area   Quamo commanded three reconnaissance teams while in Vietnam  leading a number of covert missions  His actions saved the lives of 14 of his men  During his distinguished career he was awarded 26 medals which included the Distinguished Service Cross  two Silver Stars  Bronze Star  Legion of Merit and Presidential Unit Citations  While conducting a mission in Vietnam  Major Quamo was killed when the helicopter he was flying in crashed  He was 27 and the youngest major ever to have served in the Special Forces Unit  Major George Quamo was a Class of 1958 graduate of Averill Park High School in upstate New York  A natural leader  he was president of his high school junior class and a quarterback on the football team  After joining the Army he attended Officer Candidate School      Companion legislation was introduced into the U S  House of Representatives by Congressman John Sweeney and has been referred to the House Committee on Government Reform
