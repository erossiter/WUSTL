IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 24  2007 Schumer Announces Federal Dollars Coming To Town Of Esperance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Federal Funds Will Be Used For The Construction Of The Wastewater Treatment Plant After Plant Had To Be Moved From The Original Site                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that a grant of  300 000 has been awarded to the Town of Esperance in Schoharie County  The funds are being awarded through the United States Department of Agriculture s  USDA  Rural Development program as a Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan  Federal funds will be used for the construction of the Esperance Schoharie wastewater treatment plant   Schumer has worked closely with Earl Van Wormer  Chair of the Board of Supervisor  to ensure the completion of this project         This is great news for the Town of Esperance   Schumer said   The water system is critical to the livelihood of the community and the health of local residents   Clean drinking water is a basic need and this funding will help the community meet that need  improving the quality of life for everyone in the area        The Town of Esperance will use federal funds for the construction of the Esperance Schoharie wastewater treatment plant   This project has experienced a significant cost overrun due to a tribal burial ground being found at the site of the proposed plant   After many months of mitigation with the nation  the plant needed to be moved   The new site had to be purchased and the treatment plant had to be redesigned to fit the new site       The USDA Rural Development Water and Waste Disposal Grant and Loan program provides funds to reduce water and waste disposal costs to a reasonable level for rural users  The grant program also sends funds to help develop water and waste disposal systems in rural areas and towns   Grants can be used to construct  repair  modify  expand  and improve water supply and distribution systems  acquire needed land and water sources  and pay for legal and engineering fees when necessary to build facilities
