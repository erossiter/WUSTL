IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 22  2006 Schumer Announces Over  221 000 In Federal Dollars For Legal Assistance Of Western New York  Inc  In Rochester                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Federal Funds Awarded as part of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Grant Programs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  221 800 has been awarded to Legal Assistance of Western New York  Inc  of Rochester  in Monroe County  by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  under the Private Enforcement Initiative Grant  PEI  Program             This is great news for Rochester   said Schumer    For far too many New Yorkers  the American Dream of owning your own home is out of reach   This funding will insure that fair housing laws are in place and they are enforced appropriately   By providing support for homeowners  initiatives like this stabilize neighborhoods and revitalize communities        The Private Enforcement Initiative Grant Program is designed to assist private  tax exempt fair housing organizations in the investigation and enforcement of alleged violations of the Fair Housing Act and substantially equivalent fair housing laws  The grant recipients of this program are Fair Housing Enforcement Organizations with at least one year of experience in enforcement related activities and Qualified Fair Housing Organizations with at least two years of experience in enforcement related activities  PEI grants are part of HUD  s Fair Housing Initiatives Program  FHIP          Housing and community revitalization are at the heart of HUD  s mission  and these grants are part of the Department  s plan to meet local needs and expand opportunities for Americans throughout the nation   HUD aims to create more affordable housing and empower large and small communities across the country so that every citizen can live the American Dream
