IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 8  2005 Schumer Announces  45 000 for Bard College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       National Endowment for the Arts  NEA  Grant will Support Bard Music Festival and Development of Literature Web Site   Schumer  Federal Funds will Allow Bard College to Expand Artistic Efforts U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced  45 000 for Bard College of Dutchess County  The College received the federal money in two grants through the National Endowment for the Art  s  NEA  Access to Artistic Excellence program  This program aims to foster and preserve excellence in the arts and increase access to the arts         This will be a real shot in the arm for Bard College     Schumer said   Bard is well known for its programs in the arts and their music festival has an international reputation  An investment in the arts will further exposure of these programs  I will continue to fight hard for programs that support artistic growth and appreciation          Bard College will use the federal money to support two different projects   10 000 will be used to support the Bard Music Festival  This festival will explore the music of Franz Liszt and his contemporaries by presenting a range of musical forms   35 000 will be used to support the continued development of Words Without Borders  an interactive web site devoted to international literature       Established by Congress in 1965 as an independent agency of the federal government  the NEA is dedicated to supporting excellence in the arts and bringing the arts to all Americans  Over 120 000 grants have brought art to all 50 states  including rural areas  inner cities and military bases and have generated an average of seven dollars for each dollar awarded  Grants provided during FY 2006 will help support projects that involve the creation and presentation of artistically excellent work       Bard College is a small  private and independent liberal arts college serving approximately 2 600 students  It is located in Annandale on Hudson in Dutchess County  New York
