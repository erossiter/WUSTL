IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 24  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  2 Million For Greater Binghamton Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Federal Dollars Will Be Used To Improve Access Road  Improve Runway Safety Area And Remove Obstructions U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Clinton today announced  2 565 000 for the Greater Binghamton Airport from the Airport Improvement Program  AIP  at the U S  Department of Transportation  Federal Aviation Administration  FAA           This is great news for the Greater Binghamton Airport     said Senator Schumer    This airport welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help this airports remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region            The Greater Binghamton Airport is an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it   Senator Clinton said    This funding is a very welcome investment and I am pleased that we can support the airport and continue investing in safe and convenient air travel throughout New York  Any opportunity we have to improve access to the airport is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region        At the Greater Binghamton Airport  this grant will provide federal funding to improve access road  improve runway safety area  and remove obstructions
