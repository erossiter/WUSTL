IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 20  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  300 000 For The Penn Yan Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Money Will Be Used To Construct a Taxi Lane at Penn Yan Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  338 271 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Penn Yan Airport in Yates County   This is great news for the Rochester Finger Lakes Region  said Schumer  The Penn Yan Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region  Not only does it provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but it allows businesspeople visiting New York to access the region easily  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region   This is a good investment in the Penn Yan Airport and in the Finger  Lakes as a whole  said Senator Clinton  This funding will help to ensure the continued safe operation of the airport for tourists and business travelers alike  supporting not only Penn Yan but the tourism industry and the economy of the entire Finger Lakes region   The Penn Yan Airport will use the  338 271 of federal funds to construct and mark a taxi lane  which is required to access the T hangar
