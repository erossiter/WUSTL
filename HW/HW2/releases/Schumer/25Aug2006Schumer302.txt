IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 25  2006 Schumer Announces Over  137 000 Coming To Rosamond Gifford Charitable Corporation For Gun Crime Reduction In The Capitol Region                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Organization Will Use Funds To Develop A Strategy To Address Gun Related Crime In Albany  Rensselaer  and Schenectady Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  Senator Charles E  Schumer today announced that  137 401has been awarded to the Rosamond Gifford Charitable Corporation by the U S  Department of Justice  s  DOJ  Bureau of Justice Assistance  BJA  under Project Safe Neighborhoods  PSN    The federal funds will be used to create a strategy for increased prevention  intervention and suppression of gun related crime in Albany  Rensselaer and Schenectady Counties         This is great news for the Capitol Region     said Schumer     This funding will allow organizations to collaborate in an effort to prevent and eliminate gun related violence   Better intelligence and educational programs will have a huge impact  making the city a safer place for everyone        The Rosamond Gifford Charitable Corporation  located in Syracuse  will use federal funds to support the PSN strategy for Albany  Rensselaer and Schenectady Counties   Rosamond will fund increased prevention  intervention  and suppression of gun related crime through several activities for a 36 month period   These activities include providing law enforcement overtime and coordinating intelligence and data sharing among the three county law enforcement agencies  contracting the services of a juvenile delinquency coordinator to enlist the support of community based organizations and schools in the these counties for at risk and court involved youth  and conducting an outreach and media campaign to raise awareness on PSN suppression  intervention and prevention activities throughout these counties       The Paul Coverdell Forensic Sciences Improvement Grant Program provides funding to State and local governments to improve the quality and timeliness of forensic science and medical examiner services and or to eliminate backlogs in the analysis of forensic evidence  including controlled substances  firearms examination  forensic pathology  latent prints  questioned documents  toxicology  and trace evidence  NIJ is the research  development  and evaluation agency of the U S  Department of Justice and is dedicated to researching crime control and justice issues  NIJ provides objective  independent  evidence based knowledge and tools to meet the challenges of crime and justice  particularly at the State and local levels
