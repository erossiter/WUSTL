    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON BRAC COMMISSION VOTE TO KEEP PORTSMOUTH NAVAL SHIPYARD OPEN                              SUNUNU STATEMENT ON BRAC COMMISSION VOTE TO KEEP PORTSMOUTH NAVAL SHIPYARD OPEN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Wednesday  August 24  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  8 24  released the following statement regarding the BRAC Commission   s vote to keep the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard open         Today is a great day for the Shipyard and its highly skilled workforce  the Seacoast region  and our nation  By removing Portsmouth from the Defense Department   s list of facilities slated for closure  BRAC Commissioners have confirmed what we   ve known all along  that our country could not afford to lose the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard as a part of its national defense infrastructure  This success is first and foremost the result of the incredible record of accomplishment turned in by the dedicated workers of the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard  With their work as our foundation  a team including the Seacoast Shipyard Association  Shipyard unions and employees  the local Chambers  individual community members  the states    governors and the members of the New Hampshire and Maine Congressional delegations came together to fight for Portsmouth and assure that the Yard   s centuries old tradition of excellence will continue far into the future           30      244892
