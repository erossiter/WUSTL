    NEW HAMPSHIRE DELEGATION ANNOUNCES FEDERAL GRANT FOR   ANTI GANG INITIATIVE                                  NEW HAMPSHIRE DELEGATION ANNOUNCES FEDERAL GRANT FOR   ANTI GANG INITIATIVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  May 3  2006       WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu and Congressmen Charlie Bass and Jeb Bradley  R NH  today announced that New Hampshire will receive an Anti Gang Initiative grant from the U S  Department of Justice in the amount of  121 955 to combat gang activity in the Granite State   The Anti Gang Initiative provides nationwide funding to support new or expanded anti gang enforcement and prevention efforts under the Project Safe Neighborhoods  PSN  Initiative  Gang activity in New Hampshire has been increasing at a steady rate  including known activity with nationally and regionally recognized gangs   The Anti Gang Initiative Grant will allow New Hampshire to implement a state wide strategy that includes specialized law enforcement training  education and youth Anti Gang messaging efforts          The presence of gangs and gang related violence is an issue of concern in New Hampshire   Law enforcement officials in New Hampshire have long been focused on this problem  and these funds will help them in this effort     stated Gregg  Sununu  Bass and Bradley                                                 255126
