                                                                                                                 Group provides a link to the technology sector on Capitol Hill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Monday  February 5  2007       WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell has selected United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  to once again serve as a member of the Senate Republican High Tech Task Force  The group helps facilitate an exchange of views between the Senate and America   s job creators in high tech industries         The High Tech Task Force represents our dedication to furthering technology development and integration throughout the country     said McConnell     Senate Republicans are committed to ensuring that excess taxation and regulation do not stifle technological innovation  I am pleased to announce Senator John Sununu is a part of our efforts to create an agenda which fosters growth for high tech job creators                     Sununu said     America is a global leader in technological innovation and the development of new products  which helps to drive our economy  By helping the Senate keep in contact with the high tech sector  the Republican High Tech Task Force has an opportunity to ensure that Congress   s policies do not get in the way of continued technological growth  In my work with the Task Force and in the Senate  I will continue to find solutions for the technology industry that encourage investment and entrepreneurship         Sununu  who joined the High Tech Task Force in 2003  is a member of the Senate Committee on Commerce  Science and Transportation  which has Senate jurisdiction over national technology and science policy  The High Tech Task Force is a leading advocate for the technology sector on Capitol Hill and was created in 1999 to advise the Republican Caucus on technology issues         30      268462
