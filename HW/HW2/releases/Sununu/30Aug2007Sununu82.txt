    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  255 000 IN HOUSING ASSISTANCE FOR COOS COUNTY                                              SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  255 000 IN HOUSING ASSISTANCE FOR COOS COUNTY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Funding to provide transitional housing  support services to victims of domestic violence                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  August 30  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  8 30  announced that the United States Department of Justice will provide Coos County Family Health Services  Inc  with a  255 000 grant for transitional housing and support services for victims of domestic violence  This funding underscores a commitment by the Department of Justice to victims of domestic violence  who when in crisis need immediate help when emergency shelter or crisis intervention is unavailable  said Sununu  a member of the Banking Committees Housing  Transportation and Community Development Subcommittee  Through Transitional Housing Grant Program funding  Coos County Family Health Services will direct housing and support services to victims of domestic violence  sexual assault  and dating violence as they transition to permanent housing  The  255 000 grant is administered by the Department of Justices Office of Violence Against Women and directed to Coos County Family Health Services  Inc    30    281430
