    GREGG  SUNUNU SECURE  500 000 FOR UPGRADES TO BERLIN WATER WORKS DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM                         GREGG  SUNUNU SECURE  500 000 FOR UPGRADES TO BERLIN WATER WORKS DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Funding contained in Fiscal Year 2006 Interior Appropriations Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Thursday  June 30  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today  6 30  announced that they have secured  500 000 for upgrades to the Berlin Water Works Distribution System in the Fiscal Year 2006 Interior Appropriations Act  The legislation passed the Senate by a vote of 94   0   Senator Gregg stated     As communities of all sizes across New Hampshire deal with changing populations  access to clean and safe drinking water is an absolute necessity   Berlin is doing an outstanding job addressing water infrastructure issues and this award will allow them to continue that work      Senator Sununu stated     As the City of Berlin continues infrastructure improvements to its water distribution system  these funds will be directed toward the replacement of unlined cast iron water mains  some of which date back more than 100 years  This work will play a critical role in ensuring the safe and reliable delivery of water to thousands of residents      Senator Gregg has previously secured  10 million for the Berlin drinking water system  including  600 000 secured
