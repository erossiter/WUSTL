                                                                                   Friday  June 17  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  6 17  commended the Department of Commerce   s appointment of Hampton Falls resident Michael Leary to serve as a member of the New England Fisheries Management Council  NEFMC   Sununu noted that Leary     an active commercial fisherman and fishing community leader     will make meaningful contributions to the Council         Michael is highly regarded among his fishing peers and has consistently worked toward innovative solutions to sustain our fisheries     said Sununu  who strongly supported Leary   s appointment to the NEFMC in an April 2005 letter to Secretary of Commerce Carlos M  Gutierrez     His knowledge and experience with fishery and related regulatory matters will be an asset to the Council   s fishery management efforts                                       The Department of Commerce announced its selection of appointments to the Department   s eight regional fishery management councils on June 16  The councils prepare fishery management plans for marine fish stocks in their respective geographical areas of responsibility  which are submitted for review by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration   s National Marine Fisheries Service and approved by the
