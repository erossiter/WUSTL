                                                                                                                       Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Friday  May 11  2007       WASHINGTON  DC   United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  announced today  5 11  that the Federal Aviation Administration is set to disburse  650 000 to the Pease Development Authority to support projects of importance to Portsmouth International Airport  Of these U S  Department of Transportation resources   500 000 will support an ongoing effort to insulate nearby residences from sound generated by local air traffic  and  150 000 is set to be used for an update of the facility s master plan study      Gregg and Sununu  commenting on funds slated for noise reduction  said   Portsmouth Airport can continue to grow without needlessly inconveniencing nearby homeowners  This grant money will help the airport and its neighbors coexist        Bill Hopper  Airport Manager  said   We are now in the third phase of this sound insulation program  and we re delighted to be able to provide this support to the neighbors of Portsmouth Airport       The airport s master plan study will explore the feasibility of establishing and developing a dedicated general aviation area at the facility       For more information about these grants  contact Bill Hopper  603 433 6536        30
