    GREGG  SUNUNU ANNOUNCE NASHUA VETERANS HOUSING INITIATIVE IN LINE FOR  350 000                               GREGG  SUNUNU ANNOUNCE NASHUA VETERANS HOUSING INITIATIVE IN LINE FOR  350 000                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    House  Senate reaches agreement on Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  November 14  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  11 14  announced that a joint House  Senate committee of conference has approved the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development conference report  The measure includes  350 000 for Harbor Homes Inc  of Nashua to help defray the costs Harbor Homes has incurred from the construction of Buckingham Place  the organizations 20 unit transitional housing development for honorably discharged homeless veterans and their families  It is expected that the House will vote on the conference report later today  allowing the Senate to consider it thereafter  Senator Gregg stated  Veterans and their families who have given so much in service to our country deserve our help in their time of need  Buckingham Place does an extraordinary job providing these critical services to many individuals and families who need help getting back on their feet  and I am pleased to support the initiative  Senator Sununu stated  Our nations veterans have made enormous sacrifices to protect our nation and the ideals for which it stands  The funding in this bill is a small but important contribution that will help Harbor Homes and Buckingham Place continue to offer housing and valuable services to veterans in need  Through Harbor Homes  veterans will have access to the resources they need to quickly and comfortably transition back into their communities  Peter Kelleher  Executive Director of Harbor Homes  stated  On behalf of Harbor Homes  Inc   we were thrilled to learn of the joint efforts of Senators Gregg and Sununu on this project  Buckingham Place offers a second chance for residents to put the pieces of their lives back together  establish and or the regain skills and confidence to return to their communities  The outpouring of support from our representatives and the community at large demonstrates to these men and women that their sacrifices are not forgotten  We are greatly indebted to the Senators  Harbor Homes specializes in helping homeless individuals with and without mental illnesses return to mainstream society and has built a transitional housing development  Buckingham Place  for homeless veterans in Nashua  This 20 unit apartment building is one of the first facilities in the country to provide services to both homeless male and female veterans and their families  In addition to housing  clients have access to meals  job training and vocational services  employment assistance  and access to medical and mental health care          287551
