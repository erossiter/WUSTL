    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  200 000 FOR SENIOR HOUSING FACILITY AT SILVER LAKE LANDING IN MADISON                      SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  200 000 FOR SENIOR HOUSING FACILITY AT SILVER LAKE LANDING IN MADISON                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senate Appropriations Committee approves funding for preservation of 20 units of affordable senior housing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Thursday  July 21  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  7 21  announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved  200 000 requested by the Gibson Center of North Conway for the preservation of 20 units of affordable senior housing at Silver Lake Landing in Madison  The funding is contained in the Fiscal Year 2006 Transportation  Treasury  Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which passed the Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday      Our senior citizens deserve the security and peace of mind of knowing that safe and affordable housing will be available to them if they need it  The many parties who have joined together to help preserve this property are to be commended for their foresight in working toward this goal     said Sununu  a member of the Committee on Banking  Housing  and Urban Affairs     These resources will help the Gibson Center and local officials finalize the purchase of 20 housing units at Silver Lake Landing that otherwise would be put on the real estate market and out of reach to those who need housing the most      Final action on the Fiscal Year 2006 Transportation  Treasury  Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill is expected this fall  Further information on the project is available by contacting Gibson Center Executive Director George Cleveland at 603 356 3231        30    241201
