                                                                                      Thursday  August 25  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  announced today  8 25  that the University of New Hampshire  UNH  is set to receive  990 028 from the U S  Department of Transportation for capital improvements to enhance an alternative and clean fuel vehicle fleet at UNH  The University plans to use the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  grant funding to continue its ongoing efforts to improve campus transportation and air quality         These resources reflect the importance of UNH   s environmentally responsible initiative to reduce emissions generated by the University   s buses     said Sununu     By helping to limit air pollution in the Seacoast  UNH is delivering benefits to the University community and those who live in the region          Specifically  UNH will use the FTA resources to improve transit maintenance and fueling facilities  acquire new alternative fuel vehicles  and emission retrofit existing buses used on the    Wildcat Transit    and    Campus Connector    transit systems  The University   s bus system includes stops in Dover  Durham  Madbury  Newington  Newmarket  and Portsmouth      For more information  call Erika Mantz at UNH  603 862 1567        30        244988
