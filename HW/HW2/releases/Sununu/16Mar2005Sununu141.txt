    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH   S NAMING OF KEVIN MARTIN TO  POSITION OF FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS C         SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH   S NAMING OF KEVIN MARTIN TO  POSITION OF FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS COMMISSION CHAIRMAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  March 16  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH   a member of the Senate Commerce Committee  today  3 16  issued the following statement regarding President Bush   s naming of Kevin Martin to the position of Chairman of the Federal Communications Commission  FCC       The President has made a strong selection for FCC Chairman  Kevin Martin brings stability  creativity  and thoughtfulness as the Commission considers an enormous backlog of issues before it   I look forward to working with him as he leads the Commission to keep new technologies  such as IP applications  away from the old regulatory structure      Kevin Martin was previously confirmed by the Senate for his current seat on the FCC  As such  Senate confirmation hearings and a Senate vote are not needed for him to assume his new position        30    233710
