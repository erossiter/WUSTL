    ME NH DELEGATION MEMBERS PRAISE LETTER WRITING CAMPAIGN TO SAVE PNS                                          ME NH DELEGATION MEMBERS PRAISE LETTER WRITING CAMPAIGN TO SAVE PNS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  April 7  2005        Washington  D C     Members of the Maine and New Hampshire Congressional Delegations today hailed the collection of thousands of letters by the    Support the Shipyard Letter Writing Campaign     organized by the local Save Our Shipyard  SOS  Task Force  area Chambers of Commerce and municipalities    Next Tuesday  April 12  the Seacoast Shipyard Association will take the letters to Washington  D C    The Members have submitted a request to the office of the Secretary of Defense to make arrangements to deliver the letters to the Pentagon             We commend the Save Our Shipyard Task Force  the Seacoast Shipyard Association  and all the area Chambers of Commerce from Maine and New Hampshire for initiating this effort and coordinating the collection of so many letters of support for Portsmouth Naval Shipyard     said the delegations in a joint statement       Most of all we want to thank the thousands of employees  friends  and families who help make Portsmouth Naval Shipyard the finest shipyard in the country    We look forward to receiving these letters and joining with representatives of the Seacoast Shipyard Association and shipyard leadership in hand delivering them to the Department of Defense                   236219
