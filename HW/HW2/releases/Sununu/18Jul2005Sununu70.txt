                                                                                               Monday  July 18  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     Continuing his commitment to our veterans  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  cosponsored legislation  S  Res  155  to designate November 6     12  2005  as    National Veterans Awareness Week        a nationwide opportunity to develop educational programs highlighting the contributions of veterans to our country         America   s cherished freedoms were earned through the bravery of our servicemen and women     said Sununu     The importance of educating all Americans about the heroism of our nation   s veterans cannot be overstated     National Veterans Awareness Week    provides a special opportunity to learn about the contributions veterans have made to protecting our way of life and spreading freedom around the globe         Senator Joseph R  Biden  Jr   D DE  introduced S  Res  155 on May 25  2005  The measure  which passed the Senate unanimously  recognizes the significant contributions America   s veterans have made to our country and draws attention to the need for future leaders of the nation to understand their
