    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES COMMITTEE APPROVAL OF  500 000 FOR RENOVATIONS TO EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN MANCHEST         SUNUNU ANNOUNCES COMMITTEE APPROVAL OF  500 000 FOR RENOVATIONS TO EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN MANCHESTER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Building suffered extensive damages during catastrophic flooding of 2005                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Friday  July 13  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  7 13  announced Senate Appropriations Committee approval of  500 000 in funding for reconstruction and repairs to the Manchester Easter Seals building  which was heavily damaged in the 2005 floods  The resources are contained in the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which now moves to the full Senate for consideration  The comprehensive services of Easter Seals in New Hampshire and across the nation allow people of all ages with disabilities and specials needs to receive quality care that they could not otherwise afford  said Sununu  This Economic Development Initiative funding will play a major role in helping the Manchester Center continue reconstruction and expand its range of services for years to come  Easter Seals NH President and CEO Larry Gammon stated  Our Auburn Street facility serves our most vulnerable citizens  We are grateful to Senator Sununu and his staff for their long time commitment to those people who are disadvantaged or who have disabilities and special needs  Gammon further noted that  The flood was a major disaster that had a significant impact in the lives of so many of our families  but Senator Sununu and his staff were on the scene from day one  providing caring support and encouragement  This funding will allow us to continue to meet the human service needs of children  seniors and their families in our state  The funding is targeted for continued renovations and repairs to the Easter Seals Center at 555 Auburn Street  Heavy rain in June  2005 dumped over four inches of water on Manchester in little over an hour  resulting in severe flash floods   30    278986
