                                                                                                 Friday  November 18  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  announced today  11 18  that Manchester   s    Families in Transition    program will receive  450 000 in funding to establish the    Mothers with Children  Staying Together Recovery Program     The funds are included in the Fiscal Year 2006 Treasury  Transportation  Judiciary  and HUD Conference Report which passed the Senate on Friday      The Families in Transition program plays an invaluable community role by providing housing and transitional support for those in need     said Sununu     The organization   s plan to create this new facility represents a commitment to providing services that help program participants achieve self sufficiency  Senate approval of these funds is an important step toward making Families in Transition   s plan a reality      With help from these Economic Development Initiative Housing and Urban Development  HUD  resources  FIT plans to create a residential treatment center that provides supportive services and independent living accommodations to homeless women with children and single women suffering from addiction and or coping with the effects of trauma   For more information about FIT  visit www fitnh org and contact FIT President Maureen
