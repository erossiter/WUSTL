    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES SENATE APPROVAL OF  500 000 FOR RENOVATIONS TO EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN MANCHESTER          SUNUNU ANNOUNCES SENATE APPROVAL OF  500 000 FOR RENOVATIONS TO EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN MANCHESTER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Building suffered extensive damages during 2005 flooding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  September 12  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  9 12  announced Senate approval of  500 000 in funding for reconstruction and repairs to the Manchester Easter Seals building  which was heavily damaged in 2005 flooding  The resources are contained in the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 88 7  The bill now heads to a joint House Senate conference committee before returning to each chamber for final passage  Easter Seals facilities in New Hampshire and across the nation offer comprehensive services that allow people of all ages with disabilities and specials needs to receive quality care that they could not otherwise afford  said Sununu  This funding will play a major role in helping the Manchester Center continue reconstruction and expand its range of services for years to come  Larry Gammon  Easter Seals President and CEO said  Senator Sununu and his staff did not stand still while we were in distress  This unprecedented response demonstrates Senator Sununu sdeep concernfor disadvantaged children and adults in our community  By securing this funding  we will be able fully recover from the flood  and improve services for chronically ill children  frail seniors and their families  The funding is targeted for continued renovations and repairs to the Easter Seals Center at 555 Auburn Street  Heavy rain in June  2005 dumped over four inches of water on Manchester in little over an hour  resulting in severe flash floods    30    283299
