    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON NEW HAMPSHIRE FLOODING                                                                   SUNUNU STATEMENT ON NEW HAMPSHIRE FLOODING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Monday  May 15  2006       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  5 15  issued the following statement regarding the flooding in the state of New Hampshire      As New Hampshire residents continue to deal with severe flooding across the state  my office is in direct contact with officials at the state and federal level to offer whatever assistance we can at this very difficult time  The Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has informed me that it has dispatched state liaison officer David Knowles to work out of the Office of Emergency Management in Concord  We will remain in touch with them and other relevant agencies so that New Hampshire individuals and families get the help they need in the fastest possible manner               30      255581
