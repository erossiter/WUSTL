    SUNUNU HONORED WITH U S  CHAMBER OF COMMERCE S    SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE AWARD                                 SUNUNU HONORED WITH U S  CHAMBER OF COMMERCE S    SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      National business advocacy organization praises    Senator s votes to create jobs  expand economy   Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Monday  April 30  2007       WASHINGTON  DC   In recognition of his strong support in the Senate for free enterprise and job creation  the U S  Chamber of Commerce has honored United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  with its  Spirit of Enterprise  award  The Chamber selected Sununu to receive the award based on the strength of his legislative record during the second session of the 109th Congress  2006                    Senator Sununu proved to be a valuable ally to the business community last year  supporting measures to stimulate job creation and spur economic growth   said Tom Donohue  Chamber president and CEO   The business community is grateful for John s dedication to a pro growth agenda and we are proud to present him with this award                   Presented annually  the  Spirit of Enterprise  award is given to members of Congress based on their voting record with regard to business issues  For the 2006 ratings  the Chamber used several votes in the Senate to produce its ratings  including  tax relief  medical liability reform  port security and free trade       The U S  Chamber of Commerce is the world s largest business federation  representing more than three million businesses and organizations  For more information  visit http   www uschamber com        30    273729
