    GREGG AND SUNUNU ANNOUNCE FEDERAL GRANT FOR ANTI GANG INITIATIVE                                             GREGG AND SUNUNU ANNOUNCE FEDERAL GRANT FOR ANTI GANG INITIATIVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  August 29  2007      WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today announced that New Hampshire will receive an Anti Gang Initiative grant from the U S  Department of Justice in the amount of  135 697 to combat gang activity in the Granite State  The Anti Gang Initiative provides federal funding to support new or expanded anti gang enforcement and prevention efforts under the Project Safe Neighborhoods  PSN  Initiative  Gang activity in New Hampshire has been increasing in recent years  including known activity with nationally and regionally recognized gangs  The Anti Gang Initiative Grant will allow New Hampshire to implement a state wide strategy that includes specialized law enforcement training  education and youth Anti Gang messaging efforts   Senator Gregg stated  New Hampshire communities have long been some of the best places to live and raise families  and that is due in large part to the outstanding work of our law enforcement officials  But New Hampshire is not immune to some of the most dangerous and widespread crime trends throughout the country  including gang violence  Law enforcement officials have been working hard to combat gang activity in the Granite State  and these funds will help them to continue this crucial fight   Senator Sununu stated  This funding underscores a commitment on the part of the Department of Justice to supporting law enforcement officers in New Hampshire and across the nation who are on the front lines dealing with increased gang activity  Through its New Hampshire Anti Gang Initiative  the New Hampshire Department of Justice will continue to direct resources to increased patrols  prosecution  and education and training  all valuable directions for this anti gang enforcement and prevention program          281414
