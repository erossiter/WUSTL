    ME NH DELEGATIONS CONTINUE FIGHT FOR PORTSMOUTH NAVAL SHIPYARD                                               ME NH DELEGATIONS CONTINUE FIGHT FOR PORTSMOUTH NAVAL SHIPYARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Friday  August 19  2005        Washington  D C      Maine and New Hampshire   s congressional delegations today spoke out against arguments contained in a letter the Department of Defense  DoD  sent to Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission Chairman Anthony Principi   The letter restates the flawed rationale behind DoD   s proposal to close Portsmouth Naval Shipyard in Kittery  Maine                The Department of Defense clearly wants to close the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard  but the facts are not on DoD   s side     said Maine Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins  New Hampshire Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  Maine Representatives Tom Allen and Mike Michaud and New Hampshire Representatives Jeb Bradley and Charles Bass      We have demonstrated that there is no excess capacity at any of our nation s four shipyards and closure of Portsmouth would irrevocably damage our nation   s naval war fighting capability   With so many of the BRAC Commissioners questioning the Defense Department   s cost savings numbers  it is increasingly clear that DoD   s rationale for closing Portsmouth cannot be substantiated                    244131
