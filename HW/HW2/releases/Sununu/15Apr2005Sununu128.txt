 Friday  April 15  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senate Commerce  Science  and Transportation Committee Chairman Ted Stevens  R AK  has named Senator John Sununu  R NH  to chair the Commerce Committee   s National Ocean Policy Study       The rich diversity of New Hampshire   s Seacoast has given me a deep appreciation for the unique value of our oceans and the need to preserve them through effective management and stewardship     said Sununu      As Chairman of the National Ocean Policy Study  I plan to turn to the many scientists  conservationists  and fisherman from my home state and around the world for their expertise as we further the study   s mission  That critical work will include examining and implementing recent recommendations to improve the status of oceans  reviewing U S  ocean management and programs  and conducting comprehensive examinations of long term policy proposals for ocean use        Sununu   s colleagues on the National Ocean Policy Study  which will function in similar fashion to a subcommittee  include  Senators Ted Stevens  R AK   Trent Lott  R MS   Kay Bailey Hutchison  R TX   Olympia Snowe  R ME   Gordon Smith  R OR   Jim DeMint  R SC   David Vitter  R LA   Barbara Boxer  D CA   Daniel Inouye  D HI   John Kerry  D MA   Maria Cantwell  D WA   and Frank Lautenberg  D NJ     Sununu will also serve on the Commerce Subcommittee on Aviation  Subcommittee on Surface Transportation and Merchant Marine  Subcommittee on Science and Space  Subcommittee on Fisheries and the Coast Guard  Subcommittee on Trade  Tourism
