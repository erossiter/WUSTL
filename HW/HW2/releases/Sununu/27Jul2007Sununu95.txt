    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  300 000 GRANT TO HELP THE HOMELESS                                                         SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  300 000 GRANT TO HELP THE HOMELESS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Contact  Barbara Riley      Friday  July 27  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  7 27  announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services has awarded  300 000 to the New Hampshire State Department of Health and Human Services for Projects for Assistance in Transition from Homelessness  PATH   This funding underscores a commitment to providing a range of supportive services that will help the homeless achieve self sufficiency  said Sununu  a member of the Senate Committee on Banking  Housing  and Urban Affairs  This particular program targets outreach assistance to those individuals who are homeless and struggling with mental health illness and substance abuse  By addressing these obstacles  these individuals will have a greater chance of success in transitioning into their communities  New Hampshire Health and Human Services Commissioner John Stephen stated  This grant will allow the state to continue to prevent homelessness among some of the most difficult to place individuals we see  It can be a challenge to find appropriate housing for people with co occurring disorders  but that s just what Project Path does  I want to thank Senator Sununu for his essential efforts to help find services and protect the safety of our state  This competitive grant awarded by the Center for Mental Health Services of the United States Department of Health and Human Services represents continued funding for an existing program  The program is administered in partnership with New Hampshires Community Health Centers   30      280000
