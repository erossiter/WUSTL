    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES MANCHESTER EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN LINE FOR  500 000 IN RENOVATION FUNDING                 SUNUNU ANNOUNCES MANCHESTER EASTER SEALS FACILITY IN LINE FOR  500 000 IN RENOVATION FUNDING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      House  Senate reaches agreement on Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  November 14  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  11 14  announced that a joint House  Senate committee of conference has approved the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development conference report  The measure includes  500 000 for reconstruction and repairs to the Manchester Easter Seals building that was heavily damaged in 2005 flooding  It is expected that the House will vote on the conference report later today  allowing the Senate to consider it thereafter  The funding was approved by the full Senate on September 12  People of all ages with disabilities and special needs receive quality  comprehensive care that they might otherwise not be able to afford at Easter Seals facilities in New Hampshire and across the nation  said Sununu  With this funding  the Manchester Center will be able to proceed with its reconstruction plans and expand its range of services for years to come  The funding is targeted for continued renovations and repairs to the Easter Seals Center at 555 Auburn Street  Heavy rain in June 2005 dumped over four inches of water on Manchester in little over an hour  resulting in severe flash floods        287552
