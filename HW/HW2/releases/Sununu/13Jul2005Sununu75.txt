    SUNUNU COMMENTS ON WHITE HOUSE ANNOUNCEMENT  OF ANTICIPATED REDUCED DEFICIT FIGURES                          SUNUNU COMMENTS ON WHITE HOUSE ANNOUNCEMENT  OF ANTICIPATED REDUCED DEFICIT FIGURES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Office of Management and Budget predicts FY 2005  budget deficit to be  94 billion less than expected                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Wednesday  July 13  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  7 13  issued the following statement regarding the White House Office of Management and Budget   s  OMB  announcement that the Fiscal Year 2005 budget deficit is expected to be  94 billion less than previously forecasted  OMB also reported on Wednesday that over 3 7 million jobs have been created nationwide since May 2003         OMB   s announcement reflects a strengthening economy     said Sununu  a member of the Joint Economic Committee     By delivering tax relief to American businesses and families  Congress and President Bush helped create a pro growth environment that is driving strong economic activity across the country  A robust economy delivers direct benefits to Americans in the form of new jobs and increased tax receipts     which helps to reduce the national deficit            Deficit reduction remains a top priority for our country  Although OMB projects a smaller than anticipated deficit for Fiscal Year 2005  the long term health of our economy is linked with controlled federal spending  As the appropriations process continues on Capitol Hill  I will continue to work with my colleagues to reduce the deficit     said Sununu      For more information about OMB   s announcement  visit www whitehouse gov omb         30        240568
