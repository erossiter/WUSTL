                                                                                                 Thursday  July 21  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  announced today  7 21  that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved  450 000 in funding for Families in Transition  FIT  to establish the    Mothers with Children  Staying Together Recovery Program     The funds are included in the Fiscal Year 2006 Treasury  Transportation  Judiciary and HUD Appropriations bill  which was approved in mark up on Thursday      Families in Transition plays an invaluable community role by providing housing and transitional support for those who are in need     said Sununu     The organization   s plan to create this new facility represents its commitment to providing services that help program participants achieve self sufficiency  Committee approval of these funds is an important step toward making Families in Transition   s plan a reality  and I will continue to advocate strongly for the project as the appropriations process continues      With help from these Economic Development Initiative Housing and Urban Development  HUD  resources  FIT plans to create a residential treatment center that provides supportive services and independent living accommodations to homeless women with children and single women suffering from addiction and or coping with the effects of trauma   For more information about FIT  visit www fitnh org and contact FIT President Maureen Beauregard at 603 641 9441        30    241200
