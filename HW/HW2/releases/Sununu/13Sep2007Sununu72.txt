    GREGG  SUNUNU ANNOUNCE SENATE APPROVAL OF  400 000 FOR VETERANS HOUSING INITIATIVE IN NASHUA                 GREGG  SUNUNU ANNOUNCE SENATE APPROVAL OF  400 000 FOR VETERANS HOUSING INITIATIVE IN NASHUA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  September 13  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today  9 13  announced that the United States Senate has approved  400 000 for Harbor Homes Inc  of Nashua to help defray the costs Harbor Homes has incurred from the construction of Buckingham Place  the organizations 20 unit transitional housing development for honorably discharged homeless veterans and their families  The funding is contained in the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 88 7 on Wednesday  September 12  The bill now heads to a joint House Senate conference committee before returning to each chamber for final passage  Senator Gregg stated  Our veterans and their families have given much to protect America and the freedoms we enjoy  We can recognize and support their efforts by providing them the resources they need to make a new start  Buckingham Place provides an important service to our veterans and I am pleased to help support the initiative  Senator Sununu stated  This funding will help Harbor Homes and Buckingham Place continue to offer housing and a range of valuable resources and services to veterans in need  Americas veterans have made enormous sacrifices to protect our nation and the ideals for which it stands  We can do no less for them than to ensure that they have the resources they need to quickly and comfortably transition back into their communities   Harbor Homes specializes in helping homeless individuals with and without mental illnesses return to mainstream society and has built a transitional housing development  Buckingham Place  for homeless veterans in Nashua  This 20 unit apartment building is one of the first facilities in the country to provide services to both homeless male and female veterans and their families  In addition to housing  clients have access to meals  job training and vocational services  employment assistance  and access to medical and mental health care  Peter Kelleher  Executive Director of Harbor Homes  stated  On behalf of Harbor Homes  Inc   we were thrilled to learn of the joint efforts of Senators Gregg and Sununu on this project  Buckingham Place offers a second chance for residents to put the pieces of their lives back together  establish and or the regain skills and confidence to return to their communities  The outpouring of support from our representatives and the community at large demonstrates to these men and women that their sacrifices are not forgotten  We are greatly indebted to the Senators   30    283296
