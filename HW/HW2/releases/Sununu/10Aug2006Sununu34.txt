    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON DISRUPTION OF AIRLINE TERROR PLOT                                                        SUNUNU STATEMENT ON DISRUPTION OF AIRLINE TERROR PLOT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  August 10  2006       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  8 10  released the following statement regarding the terror plot  which targeted airliners bound for the United States from the United Kingdom        In the wake of the discovery of the terrorist plot targeted at innocent travelers bound for the United States  United Kingdom and other international law enforcement and intelligence authorities are working together with the Department of Homeland Security  FBI  and our intelligence agencies to protect the traveling public from the continuing threats posed by terrorists  Security officials here in the United States and abroad are to be commended for their diligent work in disrupting this latest plot       Travelers can do their part to help by arriving at the airport earlier than usual and alerting law enforcement or airline personnel should they notice anything suspicious   Equally important  we all must remain patient  cooperative  and flexible as we adjust to new security procedures and restrictions          Editor   s Note  Further information from the Department of Homeland Security can be accessed at    261660
