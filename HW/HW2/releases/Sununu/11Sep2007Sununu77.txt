    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PETRAEUS CROCKER REPORT                                                                  SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PETRAEUS CROCKER REPORT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Barbara Riley      Tuesday  September 11  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH   a member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee  today  9 11  released the following statement regarding the testimony of General David Petraeus and U S  Ambassador to Iraq Ryan Crocker before the committee  Men and women serving in Iraq have done an outstanding job  made more difficult by the failure to effectively plan for dealing with the fall of Saddam Hussein  Over the last seven months  security has improved  many local leaders are cooperating with coalition forces  and some steps have been taken toward political reconciliation  General Petraeus has recommended that we begin bringing troops home this fall  with a minimum reduction of 30 000 by the middle of 2008  In March  he will determine whether even more significant reductions are possible  The time has come to shift more responsibility to Iraqi security forces and political leadership  General Petraeus and his commanders on the ground should be given the support they need to implement these withdrawals and complete the mission in Iraq    30    282306
