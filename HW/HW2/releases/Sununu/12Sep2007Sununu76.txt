    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  11 8 MILLION  FOR CONWAY VILLAGE FIRE DISTRICT                                             SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  11 8 MILLION  FOR CONWAY VILLAGE FIRE DISTRICT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   7 9 million loan   3 9 million grant targeted for improvements  expansion  of water and wastewater distribution system                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  September 12  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  9 12  announced that the Conway Village Fire District will receive  11 8 million in funding   7 9 million loan and  3 9 million grant  for improvements and expansion of the existing water and wastewater distribution system  This funding underscores a commitment to the public health and safety of residents served by the Conway Village Fire District  said Sununu  who advocated on behalf of the Conway Village Fire District for resources  Improvements and expansion of the existing water and wastewater system will allow local officials to meet the long term demands of this growing region  The resources are being administered through the United States Department of Agricultures Office of Rural Development  The terms of the loan are 30 years at 4 25 percent   30    283295
