                                                                                  Friday  September 9  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  issued the following statement on Thursday  9 8  following Senate approval of   51 8 billion in emergency federal assistance for the Hurricane Katrina relief effort      As the day to day physical  emotional  and financial toll from Hurricane Katrina continues  it is imperative that all Americans stay focused and work together to help those who need it the most     said Sununu      This emergency federal assistance will help to bolster response and recovery efforts in the Gulf Coast states  delivering benefits to victims in the form of badly needed food  medicine  water  and resources for road system and bridge repair  I have no doubt that in the weeks and months to come  more aid     both public and private   will be directed to this tragedy as citizens of good will from New Hampshire and across the nation do all they can to help out       To date  Congress has approved  62 3 billion in emergency federal aid for the Hurricane Katrina rescue and recovery effort        30    245435
