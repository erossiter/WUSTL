                                                                                                                                          Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Friday  September 16  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     The Council for Citizens Against Government Waste  CCAGW  has named United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  as a    Taxpayer Hero    for his strong commitment to cutting taxes  controlling deficit spending  and working to make government more accountable to taxpayers  Based on the strength of his legislative record in 2004  Sununu is one of ten Senators to receive this award this year from CCAGW         Anyone can talk about cutting waste and lowering taxes  Sen  Sununu is of the rare breed who    walks the walk    by voting to ease the burden of a cumbersome  bloated federal government     CCAGW President Tom Schatz said     With all the special interests in Washington clamoring for handouts from a spendthrift Congress  the fight against big government is an uphill battle  Thanks to people like Sen  Sununu  taxpayers have a fighting chance and will continue to score victories         CCAGW focused on key roll call votes taken in the second session of the 108th Congress  The group considered several measures  including votes regarding extension of the Internet tax moratorium  striking special interest energy tax breaks  and curbing medical malpractice awards and capping punitive damages         Cutting waste is a politically difficult task because almost every program has an active constituency that profits from the status quo     Schatz said      But Sen  Sununu recognizes that the federal government must set priorities  He has taken a long term view of the nation   s fiscal problems and makes a serious effort to tackle the enormous debt being left to future generations         For more information  visit
