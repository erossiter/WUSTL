    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  130 500 IN FIREFIGHTERS ASSISTANCE GRANT FOR SUGAR HILL                                    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  130 500 IN FIREFIGHTERS ASSISTANCE GRANT FOR SUGAR HILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Homeland Security Department Resources Aim to Assist Communities with Equipment Purchases  Training  and Education Programs   Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  January 5  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  1 5  announced that the Sugar Hill Fire Department will receive  130 500 in Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program funds from the Department of Homeland Security  DHS          This funding represents a commitment on the part of the Department of Homeland Security to providing local fire departments with the resources they need to perform their heroic duties day in and day out     said Sununu     Assistance to Firefighters Grant program funds are utilized in communities across the country to train first responders  fund public awareness programs  support fire based EMS units  and purchase new trucks  ambulances  and equipment                     The resources were made available as part of the 29th round of the Fiscal Year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  which is administered by the Department   s Office for Domestic Preparedness in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration  The program assists rural  urban  and suburban fire departments throughout the United States      Further details on the program can be obtained at www usfa fema gov        30        230652
