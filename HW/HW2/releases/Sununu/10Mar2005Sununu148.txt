    SUNUNU EARNS U S  CHAMBER OF COMMERCE    SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE    AWARD                                           SUNUNU EARNS U S  CHAMBER OF COMMERCE    SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE    AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                National business federation praises Senator   s votes to grow economy  create jobs   Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  March 10  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     The U S  Chamber of Commerce today  3 10  presented United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  with the group   s    Spirit of Enterprise    award for pro economic growth votes he cast during the second session of the 108th Congress         It is critical to have members of Congress fighting for American business and their employees at this time of economic expansion and opportunity     said Thomas J  Donohue  Chamber President and CEO     John Sununu is a leader who has voted time and again to create jobs  improve our domestic security  and promote economic expansion         The Chamber based its award rankings on crucial business votes during the 108th Congress   s second session  including tax relief  extension of a moratorium on state and local taxes on Internet access  pension reform  medical liability reform  and free trade  Sununu earned a 93  score on Chamber identified key votes last year  he has a 91  cumulative rating during his time in Congress      The U S  Chamber of Commerce is the world   s largest business federation  representing more than three million businesses and organizations  For more information  visit http   www uschamber com         30      233295
