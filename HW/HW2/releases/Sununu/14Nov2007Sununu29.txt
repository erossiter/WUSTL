    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES COLEBROOK S MOHAWK RIVER RETAINING WALL IN LINE FOR  300 000 IN REPAIR FUNDING              SUNUNU ANNOUNCES COLEBROOK S MOHAWK RIVER RETAINING WALL IN LINE FOR  300 000 IN REPAIR FUNDING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   House  Senate reaches agreement on Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Contact  Barbara Riley      Wednesday  November 14  2007      WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today  11 14  announced that a joint House  Senate committee of conference has approved the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development conference report  The measure includes  300 000 for the repair of the retaining wall on the Mohawk River in Colebrook  It is expected that the House will vote on the conference report later today  allowing the Senate to consider it thereafter  The funding was approved by the full Senate on September 12  Immediate repairs are needed to the Mohawk River retaining wall as a result of heavy damage from flooding in 2006 and 2007  said Sununu  Fixing severe cracks in the wall and nearby water mains will help protect Academy Street  Colebrook Academy  and downtown Colebrook and help the community avert millions of dollars in damages should the wall fail during another rainy season  Colebrook Selectboard Chairman Larry Rappaport  said  The Town of Colebrook is incredibly grateful to Senator Sununu for continuing to push for the financial assistance that will enable us to repair the Mohawk River Wall System near our school and downtown area  Sununu requested the Mohawk River retaining wall funding from the Appropriations Committee earlier this year  In August  Sununu was successful in obtaining funding through the Economic Development Administration to enable the town to begin a technical study of the damage          287550
