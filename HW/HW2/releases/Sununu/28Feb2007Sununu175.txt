  WASHINGTON  DC   United States Senator John Sununu  R NH   a member of the Senate Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs  today  2 28  announced that New Hampshire will receive  1 985 016 in Department of Homeland Security  DHS  funding to support state and local emergency management under DHS s Emergency Management Performance Grants Program  EMPG       Through strategic planning  state and local officials play a critical and often unsung role in ensuring that New Hampshire residents remain safe day in and day out   said Sununu   This Homeland Security funding will help them move forward with that mission by providing funding to prepare and implement plans for emergency situations wherever they may occur across the state     In announcing the funding for New Hampshire and other states  Department of Homeland Security Under Secretary George Foresman said   The department remains steadfast in its commitment to providing this critical assistance to the nation s emergency management community  These resources will help state and local officials to sustain or strengthen the effectiveness of emergency management programs nationwide     The New Hampshire funding will be directed to the New Hampshire Department of Safety  Bureau of Homeland Security and Emergency Management  and is part of  193 888 228 in EMPG funds announced nationwide by
