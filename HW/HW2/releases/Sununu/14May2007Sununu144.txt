    STATEMENT FROM SENATOR JOHN SUNUNU REGARDING DEATH OF CORPORAL N  BRUCE MCKAY III                            STATEMENT FROM SENATOR JOHN SUNUNU REGARDING DEATH OF CORPORAL N  BRUCE MCKAY III                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Monday  May 14  2007       WASHINGTON  DC   United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  released the following statement regarding Corporal  N  Bruce McKay III  who died on Friday  May 11 in the line of duty as a police officer in Franconia       All New Hampshire residents share in the profound sadness caused by Corporal McKay s untimely death  This shooting is a reminder of the dangers that law enforcement officers confront each day in their effort to protect and serve all of us  In our grieving  we stand recommitted to supporting our brave police officers   and to building stronger  more peaceful communities  Kitty and I extend our deepest sympathies to Corporal McKay s family and friends at this difficult time  as well as to the members of the Franconia Police Department          30      274173
