    SUNUNU BACKS LEGISLATION TO ELIMINATE ETHANOL IMPORT TARIFF                                                  SUNUNU BACKS LEGISLATION TO ELIMINATE ETHANOL IMPORT TARIFF                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Barbara Riley      Tuesday  May 9  2006       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  today announced  5 9  that he has joined Senators Dianne Feinstein  D CA  and Jon Kyl  R AZ  in supporting legislation to eliminate the 54 cent per gallon ethanol import tariff       The current 54 cents per gallon tariff on imported ethanol serves as nothing more than a barrier to lower cost fuel for consumers and deserves to be eliminated   Right now  we subsidize ethanol production  we mandate ethanol use  and we construct barriers to importing ethanol     said Sununu      If the government is going to mandate the use of ethanol  then it should not add insult to injury by forcing drivers to pay a premium price for it   We should let the free market decide what the cost of this product should be  and if we can get it cheaper elsewhere  it will ultimately help our economy      Sununu and Kyl are original co sponsors of the bill  introduced on Monday  May 8 by Feinstein  The measure  S  2760  has been referred to the Senate Committee on Finance        30    255332
