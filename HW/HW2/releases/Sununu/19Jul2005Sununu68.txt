    SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH   S NOMINATION OF   JOHN ROBERTS TO SERVE ON THE UNITED STATES SUPR         SUNUNU STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH   S NOMINATION OF   JOHN ROBERTS TO SERVE ON THE UNITED STATES SUPREME COURT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Barbara Riley                                                                                                                                               Tuesday  July 19  2005       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  issued the following statement regarding President George W  Bush   s nomination of John Roberts to serve as an Associate Justice on the United States Supreme Court         President Bush   s nomination of John Roberts to serve as a Supreme Court justice begins the Senate confirmation process  during which his record will be carefully reviewed  To ensure that a new justice is on the bench when the Court begins its next term in October  the Senate has little time to waste in its consideration of the President   s nominee  I look forward to working with my colleagues to ensure this nomination proceeds in an expeditious and fair manner          Editor   s note  Further information on this nomination and the confirmation process can be obtained at www whitehouse gov and www judiciary senate gov            30      240959
