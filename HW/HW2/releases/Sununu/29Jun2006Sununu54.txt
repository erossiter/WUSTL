    GREGG AND SUNUNU ANNOUNCE  500 000   FOR WATER PROJECT IN MANCHESTER                                         GREGG AND SUNUNU ANNOUNCE  500 000   FOR WATER PROJECT IN MANCHESTER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Contact  Barbara Riley      Thursday  June 29  2006       WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH   today announced that the City of Manchester will receive  500 000 for its Combined Sewer Overflow initiative in a bill moving through the Senate  The Senate Appropriations Committee today approved the Fiscal Year 2007 Interior Appropriations bill  which includes the award for Manchester   s CSO program  The Combined Sewer Overflow project works to separate sewer lines from storm drains  improving public health for the safety of Manchester residents  The funding measure  which also includes  7 5 million for a variety of other New Hampshire conservation efforts  now heads to the full Senate for its consideration                    Senator Gregg  a member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on the Interior  stated     As the population of New Hampshire continues to grow  our cities are experiencing an increased strain on their public water systems  It is essential to ensure that all residents in the Granite State have access to safe and clean drinking water  in addition to proper and safe disposal of waste water  Manchester is doing an outstanding job addressing water infrastructure issues and this award will allow them to continue that work                       Senator Sununu stated     Our state   s largest city continues to successfully address the infrastructure issues that come with growth  This funding will help local officials continue their mission to protect the health and well being of city residents through proper access to safe drinking water and proper disposal of wastewater                      Manchester Mayor Frank Guinta stated   On behalf of the people of Manchester  I would like to thank Senators Gregg and Sununu for securing this crucial funding for our city   s combined sewer overflow project  This appropriation will save Manchester   s taxpayers from having to shoulder the financial burden of this federal mandate which will result in a cleaner river system
