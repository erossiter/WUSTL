    SUNUNU STATEMENT REGARDING PRESIDENT BUSH   S   STATE OF THE UNION SPEECH                                      SUNUNU STATEMENT REGARDING PRESIDENT BUSH   S   STATE OF THE UNION SPEECH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Tuesday  January 23  2007       WASHINGTON  DC     United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  issued the following comments regarding the State of the Union Speech given by President Bush on Tuesday evening  1 23          Two weeks ago  the President outlined a new strategy for Iraq which recognized that our commitment in Iraq is not open ended  The burden of responsibility for providing security must shift to the Iraqi government of Prime Minister Nouri al Maliki  Tonight  the President underscored these key points and made a number of bold proposals in the areas of health care  energy  and education         We   ve had four years of economic growth and record job creation fueled by small businesses in New Hampshire and across the country  To ensure continued growth  we need a fair tax code that supports small businesses and their efforts to create jobs and improve wages         The President also talked about the need to extend health care coverage for millions of Americans through tax incentives and support for innovation at the state level  There is no single policy that can address all the concerns regarding cost and access to quality health care  however these ideas are a step in the right direction           30        267865
