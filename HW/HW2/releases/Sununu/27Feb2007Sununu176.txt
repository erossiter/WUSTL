    SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  3 9 MILLION FUNDING PACKAGE FOR NORTH CONWAY WATER PRECINCT                                SUNUNU ANNOUNCES  3 9 MILLION FUNDING PACKAGE FOR NORTH CONWAY WATER PRECINCT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Barbara Riley Jeff Grappone      Tuesday  February 27  2007       WASHINGTON  DC   United States Senator John Sununu  R NH  announced today  2 27  that the North Conway Water Precinct is set to receive  3 9 million through the U S  Department of Agriculture s Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant Program  The funding package  which includes a  2 1 million loan and a grant of  1 8 million  aims to extend service to new customers and improve water quality and reliability    These resources will help advance plans to upgrade local water and sewer infrastructure  which officials have identified as a priority   said Sununu   Approval of this funding package also brings good news for taxpayers  Combining Water Precinct projects with planned road improvements will help deliver significant cost savings to the community    Funds provided through the loan and grant will be used for a project to install gravity sewer lines on Intervale Cross Road  Kearsarge Road  Mechanic Street  Kearsarge Street  and Lane Street   delivering service to new customers and connecting to existing sewer lines    30    269779
