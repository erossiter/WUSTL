SEN. CHAFEE OFFERS ASSISTANCE TO THOSE WITH FAMILY MEMBERS STRANDED IN MIDDLE EAST
  Sen. Lincoln Chafee announced today that Rhode Islanders with family members stranded in Lebanon or elsewhere in the Middle East may contact his Washington, D.C. office for help in expediting their evacuation. The Senator offers his deepest sympathies to those who are dealing with this difficult situation and would like to do all that he can to help. To reach the State Department's hotline on the evacuations, call (202) 647-6611. You can also call Bess Russell-Gulliver at Sen. Chafee's office at (202) 224-2921 for further assistance.
