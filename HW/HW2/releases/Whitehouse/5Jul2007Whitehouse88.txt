SEN. WHITEHOUSE ISSUES STATEMENT ON CAPE VERDE INDEPENDENCE DAY
  Sen. Sheldon Whitehouse (D-R.I.) has released the following statement:
   "I'd like to wish a happy Independence Day to the Cape Verdeans of Rhode Island. Whether expressed in the sounds of traditional Morna music or in a bowl of delicious cachupa, the vibrant culture of Rhode Island's Cape Verdean community is one of our great treasures.".
