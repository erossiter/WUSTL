 Los Angeles, CA - U.S. Senator Barbara Boxer (D-CA) today announced that she has selected Adolfo Bailon Jr. to serve as a Field Representative in her Los Angeles Office. 
As a Field Representative, Bailon will work directly with the southern California community and will represent Senator Boxer in the counties of Santa Barbara, Ventura, Los Angeles, and the desert portion of Kern. 
Bailon has worked for Senator Boxer since September 2003. During his career he has gained experience at the state legislative level,  having served as a field representative for Assembly Member Judy Chu. After serving as a conversational English teacher in Suwon, South Korea in 2003, he served as staff assistant for Senator Boxer√¢s Los Angeles office and later as grassroots coordinator for the  2004, Barbara Boxer for Senate campaign. 
A long-time resident of Los Angeles, Bailon is a product of the Los Angeles Unified School District. He received his Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from California State University, Fullerton.
