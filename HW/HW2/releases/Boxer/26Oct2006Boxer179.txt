 Los Angeles, CA - Today U.S. Senator Barbara Boxer  released this statement on the Esperanza Fire:    
"I am deeply saddened at the loss of four brave firefighters who were killed in the line of duty while fighting the Esperanza Fire.  My heart goes out to their families and fellow firefighters, who I know are devastated by this loss.  I hope and pray for well-being of the injured firefighter and for all the firefighters who continue to battle this fierce blaze.  Let us all hope that the weather will allow a quick end to this terrible and dangerous fire."
