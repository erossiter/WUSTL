 Washington, DC â U.S. Senator Barbara Boxer (D-CA) today announced that the Senate legislation reauthorizing the Water Resources Development Act (WRDA) will include funding for the reauthorization of the Llagas Creek Flood Damage Reduction project in southern Santa Clara County.  The bill was approved by the Senate Environment and Public Works Committee.    
Under Boxerâs proposal, approved today as part of WRDA, the project is authorized for a total of $95 million including a federal contribution of $55 million and non-federal (local and state) contributions of $40 million.    
WRDA authorizes water supply, flood control, port improvement, and environmental restoration projects across the country.  The bill now goes to the full Senate for consideration.
