                                                                                              May 15  2006     Press Release        Reed Statement on Border Security                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Jack Reed  D RI  stated     While it is essential that we increase security along our borders  we must be careful about the usage of our National Guard troops for this endeavor  Many members of our National Guard have been deployed overseas for extended periods  and I have concerns that placement of these troops on our border would place an undue burden on these brave men and women  Rather than a short term fix we should be looking for long term solutions for our border security
