                                                                                              November 5  2006     Press Release        Reed Reacts to Saddam   s Guilty Verdict                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  DC    Today  following reports that Saddam Hussein was found guilty  U S  Senator Jack Reed  D RI   a member of the Armed Services Committee  issued the following statement        Any time a tyrant is found guilty  it is good news   But despite this verdict  the attacks against our troops in Iraq continue and President Bush still hasn   t articulated a plan to improve the situation          Saddam is gone  but Iraq remains in the grips of ethnic cleansing and chaotic violence          Justice will not be served for the Iraqi people if this verdict is not met with a concerted effort to end the violence in their country   Now is the time for the Iraqi government to step up and fill the void  confront the militias  and rebuild Iraqi society
