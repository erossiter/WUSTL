                                                                                              September 1  2006     Press Release        Statement by U S  Senator Jack Reed on the Death of U S  Army Sergeant Moises Jazmin                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               PROVIDENCE  RI    U S  Senator Jack Reed today stated     Sergeant Moises Jazmin s death is another painful loss for our state and the nation   My deepest sympathies go out to his family  friends  and loved ones             Like others before him  Sgt  Jazmin made the ultimate sacrifice for his country  giving his life  and we are all in his debt   My thoughts and prayers are with his family and all of our brave Marines and soldiers fighting in Iraq and Afghanistan             The country must honor and respect Sgt  Jazmin s sacrifice  and the hard work and valor of all our military men and women  by committing ourselves to supporting them as they continue to face danger
