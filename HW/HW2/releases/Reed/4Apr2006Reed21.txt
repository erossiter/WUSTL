                                                                                              April 4  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Jack Reed on the Death of Marine Corporal Brian R  St  Germain                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON  DC    Senator Reed stated     My deepest sympathies go to the family and friends of Corporal Brian St  Germain   His death is another painful loss for our state           Like others before him  Cpl  St  Germain sacrificed himself for his country  and we are all in his debt   My thoughts and prayers are with his family and all of our brave Marines and soldiers fighting in Iraq and Afghanistan           The country must honor and respect the sacrifice of all our military men and women by committing ourselves to supporting them as they continue to face danger
