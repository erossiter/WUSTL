                                                                                              November 2  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Jack Reed on the Death of Army Sergeant Michael R  Weidemann                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON  DC    Today  following reports that Sergeant Michael R  Weidemann  23  of Newport  Rhode Island  was killed while serving in Iraq on October 31  2006  U S  Senator Jack Reed issued the following statement        This is a moment to reflect on the courage and dedication of one brave American who has given his all for this country   Our hearts go out to Sergeant Weidemann   s family and friends as they bear this terrible loss
