                                                                                              December 27  2007     Press Release        Reed Statement on the Assassination of Benazir Bhutto                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Jack Reed  D RI   a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today issued the following statement regarding the assassination of former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto  Benazir Bhutto was a courageous woman and an inspiration to many  She took great personal risk to try and bring about democratic change in Pakistan  Her death has cast great uncertainty over the future of Pakistan  Ms  Bhuttos assassination is a terrible reminder that Pakistan remains arguably the most dangerous arena where international terror cells are currently operating  This is a nuclear armed nation on the brink  President Bush should do everything he can to promote stability in Pakistan and prevent the situation from further unraveling  There also needs to be a renewed effort to crack down on the radical militants who are fueling the violence in the region and deny safe haven to terrorists based in Pakistan
