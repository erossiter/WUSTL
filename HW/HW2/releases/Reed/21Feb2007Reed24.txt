                                                                                              February 21  2007     Press Release        Reed Reacts to British and Danish Plans for Troop Withdrawal from Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  DC    Today  following British Prime Minister Tony Blair s announcement that he will soon order thousands of British troops to withdraw from Iraq  and Danish Prime Minister Anders Fogh Rasmussen s announcement that Denmark will withdraw all of its 460 troops stationed in Iraq this summer  U S  Senator Jack Reed  D RI  issued the following statement          The British and Danish decisions to withdraw leaves us increasingly isolated in Iraq   The coalition  which was never sufficiently robust to begin with  has all but disappeared   This is not a good development when you consider that these British and Danish forces could have been used to seal the borders or shifted to other areas of concern like Baghdad or Anbar province   said Reed  a member of the Armed Services Committee
