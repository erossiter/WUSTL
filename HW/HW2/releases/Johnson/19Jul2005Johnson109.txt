SEN. JOHNSON ANNOUNCES $328,108 AWARD TO LITTLE WOUND SCHOOL FOR ALCOHOL ABUSE PREVENTION
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) announced today that the Little Wound School of Kyle will receive $328,108 for alcohol abuse prevention.  
   "We face tremendous obstacles on and near Native American Reservations, as many are facing alcohol abuse," said Johnson. "This program will take a giant leap toward helping curb alcohol abuse among juveniles." 
   Using the funds provided by the Department of Education Grants to Reduce Alcohol Abuse Program, the school will implement innovative alcohol abuse prevention programs using proven strategies for reducing underage alcohol abuse. The Department will also provide training and technical assistance to the Little Wound School to maximize the effectiveness of the program.
