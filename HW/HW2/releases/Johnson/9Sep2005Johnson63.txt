SEN. JOHNSON ANNOUNCES $1.02 MILLION IN FUNDING FOR ROSEBUD SIOUX TRIBE
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) announced today that the Rosebud Sioux Tribe will receive a $254,000 loan and a $766,000 grant to build a solid waste transfer station.  
   "I am pleased the Rosebud Sioux Tribe will receive this funding," Johnson said. "Proper infrastructure is essential for a society to operate smoothly. It is important that our small communities have the equipment they need to ensure that daily needs are met." 
   This funding will be provided by United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) Rural Development Agency. This project provides for a central collection point for sorting and reloading solid waste. The loan term is 40 years with a 4.125 percent interest.
