SEN. JOHNSON OFFICE ANNOUNCES $1.1 MILLION IN SUPPLEMENTAL FUNDING TO LOCAL HEAD START PROGRAM
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) today announced $1,129,856 in supplemental funding to the Head Start program located in Wagner. The grant will be used to help coordinate early childhood education opportunities for area kids.  
   "Every child should have the same opportunities to excel. Head Start builds a strong educational foundation," Johnson has said. "Head Start is one of the best ways to even the playing field for kids from low and moderately low-income families." 
   Head Start is a comprehensive program designed to provide all children with the necessary tools to start their learning process before entering elementary school. The program provides educational services for young children, as well as resources, training and support for parents. 
   The grant is awarded by the Department of Health & Human Services.
