SEN. JOHNSON ANNOUNCES $700,000 FOR FAULK COUNTY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) announced today that the Faulk County Memorial Hospital will receive a $700,000 low-interest loan to finance costs associated with the construction of a new clinic.  
   "Everyone deserves access to high quality, affordable healthcare," said Johnson. "This funding will bring essential services to patients throughout Faulk County and the region." 
   The Rural Health Outreach Special Initiative is a special project of the Department of Health and Human Services. This program provides funds to strengthen rural health services. 
   Faulk County Memorial Hospital has previously received a $4.5 million low-interest loan from the United States Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Rural Development Agency for this purpose. The loan will be repaid at an annual rate of 4.125% over 30 years.
