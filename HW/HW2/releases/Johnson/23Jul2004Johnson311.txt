SEN. JOHNSON ANNOUNCES ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FUNDING TO CHEYENNE RIVER SIOUX TRIBE
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) today announced that the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe will receive $44,000 in funding for economic development planning.
   "Economic development is critical to the small businesses in our community," said Johnson. "This funding will allow for ongoing planning. The more community businesses are able to thrive, the more we are able to spread economic opportunity."
   The funding is provided by the Department of Commerce's Economic Development Administration.
