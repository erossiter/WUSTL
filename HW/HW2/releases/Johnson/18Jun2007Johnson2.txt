SEN. JOHNSON ANNOUNCES RAPID CITY FIRM WINS $839,318 FEDERAL CONSTRUCTION CONTRACT
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) announced today that Mountain Movers Construction, Inc. in Rapid City will receive an $839,318 federal contract through the General Services Administration to replace the roof of Rapid City's federal building.
  "I am glad that the much-needed repairs will be made to the federal building in Rapid City," Johnson said. "It is important that the community have access to a safe and modern facility."
  The contract was awarded to the construction firm through a competitive bidding process.
