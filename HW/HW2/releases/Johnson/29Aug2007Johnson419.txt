SEN. JOHNSON TO RETURN TO SENATE
  The day after his "Thank You South Dakota" celebration, U.S. Senator Tim Johnson (D-SD) met with his state staff and was briefed on their recent work around the state. Johnson will be in the state for the next couple days before returning to Washington, DC. Johnson plans to be in his Senate office and on the Senate floor on Wednesday, September 5th.
