SEN. JOHNSON'S WIFE REACTS TO ELIZABETH EDWARDS HEALTH NEWS
  In response to inquiries, Barbara Johnson, wife of Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD), released the following statement regarding the announcement of Elizabeth Edwards, wife of former Sen. John Edwards (D-NC), that she is again battling cancer: 
   "Our thoughts and prayers go out to Elizabeth Edwards and the Edwards Family. Tim and I are impressed by her tremendous courage and are reminded of the strength that family and faith help to provide." 
   Barb Johnson is a two time breast cancer survivor.
