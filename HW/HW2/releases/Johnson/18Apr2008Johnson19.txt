SEN. JOHNSON COMMENTS ON RESUMPTION OF BEEF EXPORTS TO SOUTH KOREA
  U.S. Senator Tim Johnson (D-SD) released this statement following today's announcement that South Korea has agreed to resume trading United States beef exports: 
   We have always known that we have the safest beef in the world, and today's announcement reiterates that. South Korea is an important market for the United States, and I'm glad to see they have agreed to resume trade on our beef exports. This announcement was a long time coming, and it is good news for our nation's cattle producers.
