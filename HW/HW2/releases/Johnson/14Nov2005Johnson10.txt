SEN. JOHNSON COMMEMORATES LIFE OF DR. VINE DELORIA, JR.
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) released the following statement upon hearing of the death of Dr.  
 Vine Deloria, Jr., a distinguished Native American scholar and fellow South Dakotan whose research, writings, and teaching span history, law, religion, and politics: 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the family and friends of Dr. Vine Deloria, Jr. Dr. Deloria was an extraordinary pioneer and supporter of Native American rights. His commitment to history, understanding, and education leave a legacy that will have influence on Indian history for generations to come."
   In January of this year, Johnson honored Deloria with a statement in the Congressional Record, publicly commending him for receiving the American Indian Visionary Award from Indian Country Today. Deloria received the award at a ceremony at the National Press Club in Washington, DC in February.
