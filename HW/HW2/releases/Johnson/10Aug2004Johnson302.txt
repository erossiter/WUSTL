SEN. JOHNSON COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF REP. GOSS TO HEAD CIA
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) released the following announcement from the White House that President George W. Bush has chosen former Rep. Porter Goss to head the Central Intelligence Agency: 
   I appreciate Congressman Goss' long-time service to the United States, both as an employee of the Central Intelligence Agency and as a Member of Congress. Our nation's intelligence community is at a critical crossroads and I look forward to hearing Congressman Goss' views on implementing the recommendations of the 9/11 Commission and on reforming our intelligence agencies to help keep the American people safe.
