SEN. JOHNSON TO VISIT SEVERAL COMMUNITIES IN NORTHEAST SOUTH DAKOTA JUNE 24
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) will be available to the media either at or after three events in Northeast South Dakota next Saturday, June 24, 2006. He will make stops in Brentford, Cresbard and Britton. The events open to the media are as follows: 
   1. Johnson Participates in Brentford Parade; Presents Congressional Record Statements to Brentford, Mellette
   WHAT: Senator Johnson will walk in the Brentford parade celebrating the town's 100th anniversary. Before the parade, Johnson will present Brentford officials with a Congressional Record statement congratulating the town on being 100 years old. Additionally while in Brentford, Johnson will present Mellette officials with a Congressional Record statement congratulating the town on being 125 years old.
   WHEN: Saturday, June 24, 2006, 9:45 am to 11:15 am CT
   WHERE: Before the parade at 9:45 am:
   Brentford Fire Department, 
   301 S. Main St.
   Brentford, SD 57429
   2. Johnson Presents Congressional Record Sattement to Town of Cresbard 
   WHAT: Senator Johnson will present Cresbard officials with a Congressional Record statement congratulating the town on being 100 years old.
   WHEN: Saturday, June 24, 2006, 12:00 pm to 12:30 pm CT
   WHERE: Fest Inn Cafe & Lounge 401 Main Street 
   Cresbard, SD 57435
   3. Johnson Attends Opening of Prayer Rock Museum
   WHAT: Senator Johnson will participates in the opening of the Prayer Rock Museum in Britton.
   WHEN: Saturday, June 24, 2006, 3:00 pm to 4:00 pm CT 
   WHERE: Prayer Rock Museum
   Main Street
   Britton, SD 57430
   Contact: Julianne Fisher and Joshua Rosenblum, 202/224-5842.
