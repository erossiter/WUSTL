SEN. JOHNSON VOTES FOR WEST RIVER PROJECTS IN OMNIBUS
  U.S. Senator Tim Johnson (D-SD) voted for the Omnibus spending bill yesterday, which passed the Senate late last night. The bill funds the federal government through Fiscal Year 2008 and provides resources for projects atEllsworth Air Force Base, South Dakota School of Mines and Technology and the Community Health Center of the Black Hills. 
  "As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee, I am proud to secure resources for projects that will be beneficial to the Black Hills." said Johnson. "By building new facilities at Ellsworth to improving health care to investing in transportation infrastructure, we are building a strong foundation to continue economic growth in the coming years."
