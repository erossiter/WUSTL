SEN. JOHNSON WELCOMES GROTON NATIVE TO STAFF
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) is pleased to announce that Erica Swisher of Groton has recently joined his staff as a Research Assistant, working on agricultural, trade and nutrition issues. 
   "Erica is a great example of the South Dakotan values of hard work and service. She has added greatly to our office," said Johnson. "We are very glad that Erica is here. She grew up on a farm and has firsthand knowledge about the agricultural concerns of South Dakota."
   Erica was raised on a farm near Groton. She graduated from Groton High School. She then attended Augustana College in Sioux Falls where she received a degree in Communications. She also worked for the American Coalition for Ethanol (ACE) in Sioux Falls before joining Senator Johnson's office.
   "I consider myself incredibly fortunate to be able to serve the people of South Dakota in this capacity," Erica said. "Senator Johnson is a true public servant and I am honored to work for a man who represents South Dakotans with such honesty and passion."
