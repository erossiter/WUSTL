SEN. JOHNSON, REP. HERSETH ANNOUNCES $1.5 MILLION FOR HIGHMORE MUNICIPAL AIRPORT
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) and Rep. Stephanie Herseth announced today the City of Highmore will receive a $1.5 million grant to improve the infrastructure and runway facilities at the municipal airport.  
   "Airports serve a vital purpose for our rural communities, and I am pleased that the people of Highmore will benefit from this grant," said Johnson. "This grant will improve the safety of the airport, and assure that South Dakotans stay connected with business partners and family members in other parts of the state and nation." 
   Herseth said, "Rural airports, like Highmore Municipal Airport help sustain the businesses and livelihood of people in smaller towns across our state. I am excited about the opportunities that this grant will create for the people of Highmore and the surrounding communities." 
   The grant will be administered by the Department of Transportation as authorized by the Wendell H. Ford Aviation and Investment and Reform Act for the 21st Century.
