SEN. JOHNSON RECEIVES DISTINGUISHED AWARD FOR LEADERSHIP FROM AMERICAN CHIROPRACTIC ASSOCIATION
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) received an award this week from the American Chiropractic Association (ACA), for his leadership and distinguished service in supporting chiropractic patients and their benefits.  
   "I am pleased to receive this award from the ACA," said Johnson. "The American Chiropractic Association serves their patients well. I will continue working with the ACA to provide high quality healthcare to South Dakota chiropractic patients."
   The award is given to those who promote education, advocacy and show leadership within the chiropractic community. 
   The ACA provides education for its members and patients and is the largest professional association in the world representing doctors of chiropractic. With approximately 18,000 members, the ACA promotes the highest standards of ethics and patient care, contributing to the health and well-being of millions of chiropractic patients.
