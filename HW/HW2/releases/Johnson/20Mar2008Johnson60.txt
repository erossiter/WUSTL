SEN. JOHNSON'S MARCH TRIP TO SOUTH DAKOTA CANCELLED
  U.S. Senator Tim Johnson (D-SD) today announced that he has to cancel a planned trip back to South Dakota for the Easter recess. The Senator's mother-in-law is dealing with a medical complication and the Johnsons will be spending this recess with Barbara's family in Florida. 
   "Barb and I both apologize for our change of plans. We were looking forward to seeing friends and family back home. However, we appreciate everyone's prayers and understanding as we spend this time with family."
