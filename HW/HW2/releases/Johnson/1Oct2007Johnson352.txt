SEN. JOHNSON MEETS WITH REPRESENTATIVES FROM SOUTH DAKOTA'S HEAVILY IMPACTED SCHOOL DISTRICTS
  Senator Johnson meets with representatives from South Dakota's Heavily Impacted School Districts on Monday, October 1, 2007. Johnson briefed the group on his amendment to protect Impact Aid. 
   The Johnson amendment (S.A. 2158) to the Department of Defense Authorization bill was accepted by unanimous consent on September 25, 2007. The Senate passed the Department of Defense Authorization bill (H.R. 1585) today. Johnson voted for the bill.
   Seen in the picture from right to left are Loren Scheer, Douglas School District; Darrell Mueller, Andes Central; Maxine Schochenmaier, consultant for South Dakota's schools; and Butch Reder, Oelrichs. Not pictured: Susan Smit, Wagner.
