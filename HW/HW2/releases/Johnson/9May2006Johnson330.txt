SEN. JOHNSON COMMENTS ON SOUTH DAKOTA SOLDIER KILLED IN IRAQ
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) today released the following statement regarding Staff Sergeant Gregory A. Wagner, who died in Iraq yesterday: 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with Sgt. Wagner's family. We are enormously proud of our men and women serving in harm's way, under the most difficult conditions. Iraq remains a volatile and dangerous setting for American soldiers. We must remember those who have sacrificed and those who continue to do so."
   Staff Sergeant Wagner was assigned to C Battery, 1st Battalion, 147th Field Artillery, Army National Guard of Yankton, South Dakota. His surviving family resides in Alexandria and in Mitchell, South Dakota.
