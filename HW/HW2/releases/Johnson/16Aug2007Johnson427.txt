SEN. JOHNSON TO HOLD PUBLIC EVENT ON AUG. 28TH IN SIOUX FALLS
  U.S. Senator Tim Johnson (D-SD) and his wife Barbara will be at a public event in Sioux Falls in the evening on Tuesday, August 28th. It will be a welcome home celebration in which Senator Johnson will personally thank the people of South Dakota for their support.
