SEN. JOHNSON STATEMENT ON PASSING OF ROSA PARKS
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) released the following statement upon hearing of the passing of civil rights leader, Rosa Parks: 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the family and friends of Rosa Parks. Her brave act of protest nearly 50 years ago inspired a movement to reverse inequalities in this country. Rosa Parks serves as a tremendous role model to so many, demonstrating the importance and effectiveness of civil action."
