SEN. JOHNSON MOURNS ON PASSING OF POPE
  Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD) released the following statement in response to the passing of Pope John Paul II:
   "I was saddened to hear of the death of Pope John Paul II. He was a spiritual leader to one billion Catholics worldwide. As a world leader, he played an influence in the ending of the Cold War. He was a voice of peace in the world whose efforts reached beyond Catholicism to bring people of all faiths together. I join people of all faiths as they mourn the passing of this great leader."
