                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Releases             Barrasso Statement on the Death of Former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto  December 27  2007  CASPER  WY    U S  Senator John Barrasso  R WY  issued the following statement today on the death of former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto   I join President Bush in condemning this cowardly assassination  My prayers are with Benazir Bhutto s family and the people of  Pakistan  at this tragic time    Despite the constant threats  Benazir Bhutto was a woman of courage  she gave everything in her attempt to win democracy for her nation  The anguish felt in  Pakistan  today will be shared around the world  as terrorism attacks us all  regardless of religion  nationality or political affiliation    Pakistan has been an ally of the  United States  in the Global War on Terror  This atrocity must not weaken our resolve  rather is should serve to strengthen our determination  A democratic  Pakistan  is essential in our struggle with Islamic terrorists    The terrorists behind todays senseless attack must be held responsible  and the imminent democratic elections in  Pakistan  must proceed                                            December 2007 Press Releases
