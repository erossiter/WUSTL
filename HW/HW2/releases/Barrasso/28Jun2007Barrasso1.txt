                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Releases             Barrasso Votes No On Immigration Bill  June 28  2007        U S  Senator John Barrasso  R Wyo   today joined a majority of senators in rejecting the Senate immigration reform bill  saying the bill would have granted amnesty to millions of illegal immigrants  Fifty three senators voted against moving forward with the bill   We owe it to Americans and everyone who goes through the legal citizenship process to secure our borders and put our citizens first  Barrasso said  I am proud to have voted against this bill  Our first priority should be securing Americas borders and not providing a path to citizenship for those who have broken the law by coming here illegally   Supporters of the immigration bill failed to garner even a simple majority  53 46   signaling the end of this legislation in the 110th Congress                                              June 2007 Press Releases
