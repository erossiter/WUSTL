                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Releases             Barrasso Announces Launch of New Website  September 13  2007 WASHINGTON  U S  Senator John Barrasso  R Wyo   today announced the launch of his new permanent website       One of the greatest responsibilities I have as a United States Senator is to inform and assist folks in    Wyoming   Barrasso said  That is why I am pleased to announce the launch of my website  My site will provide links to help people get information on a variety of topics and issues   Barrasso also encouraged   Wyoming citizens to contact him with their comments and questions at   or by contacting one of his five state offices    I want to hear from people  Barrasso added  Their comments will help me be a more effective United States Senator for all of the people of   Wyoming     The website will serve as a one stop shop for constituent services  applications for Congressional internships and military academy nominations  tips on filing grant proposals  and instructions for filing casework                                            September 2007 Press Releases
