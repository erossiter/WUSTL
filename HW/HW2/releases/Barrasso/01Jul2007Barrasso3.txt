                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Releases             Barrasso Applauds U S  Job Growth in June June 2007 Marks 46th Consecutive Month of Job Growth July 1  2007 WASHINGTON   U S  Senator John Barrasso  R Wyo   applauded news from the Bureau of Labor Statistics  BLS   which reported that 132 000 jobs were created in June 2007  Since August 2003  more than 8 2 million new jobs have been created  with more than 2 million jobs created in the last year     This report is proof of Americas continued growth and vitality  Barrasso said  Our nations unemployment rate remains a low 4 5 percent  and Wyomings unemployment rate continues to be one of the lowest in the nation  Peoples income has risen nearly  3 000 per person since 2001  and the economy has now experienced over five years of uninterrupted growth   As a U S  Senator from Wyoming  I will work to sponsor and vote for legislation that will keep peoples hard earned dollars in their pockets  and keep our economy moving forward                                             July 2007 Press Releases
