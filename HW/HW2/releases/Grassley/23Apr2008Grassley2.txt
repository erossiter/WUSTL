SEN. GRASSLEY: $225,000 TO IOWA FOR DRUG ABUSE, ADDICTION RESEARCH
  Sen. Chuck Grassley today announced that the Department of Health and Human Services, National Cancer Institute has awarded a $225,000 grant to the University of Iowa .
   The University of Iowa will use the grant funds for drug abuse and addiction research. The grant will help pay for a project titled "Significance of microRNA-mediated gene regulation in chronic neuropathic pain."
