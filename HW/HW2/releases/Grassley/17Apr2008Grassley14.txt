SEN. GRASSLEY: $325,749 TO IOWA STATE UNIVERSITY FOR ALLERGY, IMMUNOLOGY, TRANSPLANTATION RESEARCH
  Senator Chuck Grassley today announced that the Department of Health and Human Services, National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases has awarded a $325,749 grant to Iowa State University.
   Iowa State Univeristy will use the grant funds for allergy, immunology, and transplantation research. The grant will help pay for a project titled "Regulation of T Cell Signaling: Structural Studies of PLCgamma1."
