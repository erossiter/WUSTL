U.S. ATTORNEY NOMINATION SENT TO CAPITOL HILL
 Senator Chuck Grassley made the following statement after the White House officially nominated Matt Dummermuth to be the U.S. Attorney for the Northern District of Iowa.
   "I'm glad that this nomination has finally been received by the Senate. Matt is a highly qualified individual who has already gotten his feet wet as the interim U.S. Attorney for the Northern District. We need to get the process moving as soon as possible so he can continue his good work for Iowa."
