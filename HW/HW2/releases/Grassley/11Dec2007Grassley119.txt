SEN. GRASSLEY: $375,000 TO THE UNIVERSITY OF IOWA
  Sen. Chuck Grassley today announced that the Department of Health and Human Services, National Heat, Lung and Blood Institute has awarded a $375,000 grant to the University of Iowa .
   The funds will help pay for a project titled "T-tubule Remodeling and Ca2+ -Dependent Arrhythmogenesis in Cardiomyopathies."
