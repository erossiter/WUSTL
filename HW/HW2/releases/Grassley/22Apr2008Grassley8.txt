SEN. GRASSLEY: $50,000 TO KID ZONE DAYCARE, IN GUTHRIE CENTER FROM USDA
  Senator Chuck Grassley today announced that the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Office of Rural Development has awarded a $50,000 grant to Kid Zone Daycare, Inc. in Guthrie Center through the Community Facility Grant program. 
   The funds will be used to purchase an existing building in Guthrie Center to be used as a childcare center.
   "It's important to develop community facilities in rural Iowa. This funding will help Kid Zone Daycare provide quality childcare in a safe setting to those in Guthrie Center," Grassley said.
   The Community Facility Grant program provides assistance for rural areas and towns in developing essential public community facilities.
