SEN. GRASSLEY ANNOUNCES $345,000 TO IOWA STATE UNIVERSITY
  Sen. Chuck Grassley today announced that Iowa State University is receiving a $345,000 grant from the National Science Foundation.
   " Iowa State has a reputation for doing excellent research," Grassley said. "These funds will help them continue that tradition.
   Iowa State will use the funds for a project titled "Conformationally Controllable Amphiphiles and their Applications in Sensor Design."
