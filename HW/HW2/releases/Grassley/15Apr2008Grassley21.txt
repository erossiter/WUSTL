SEN. GRASSLEY: $311,250 TO IOWA FOR CANCER RESEARCH
  Sen. Chuck Grassley today announced that the Department of Health and Human Services, National Cancer Institute has awarded a $311,250 grant to the University of Iowa.
   The University of Iowa will use the grant funds for cancer cause and prevention research. The grant will help pay for a project titled "Skin Cancer and Arsenic Exposure."
