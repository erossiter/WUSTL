SEN. GRASSLEY: $360,000 TO IOWA STATE UNIVERSITY FROM NSF
  Sen. Chuck Grassley today announced that Iowa State University has received a grant of $360,000 from the National Science Foundation.
   " Iowa State has a history of doing excellent research," Grassley said. "These funds will help them continue that tradition."
   The funds will be used to help pay for a project titled "GOALI: Monitoring and Reconfiguration for Fault-Tolerance of Embedded Control Software with Automotive Applications."
