SEN. GRASSLEY BEGINS ANNUAL VISITS TO EVERY IOWA COUNTY
  Sen. Chuck Grassley announced today that he will once again begin his annual tour of Iowa 's 99 counties with visits to 12 counties during the week of January 14. 
   "I'm excited to start my annual tour of Iowa and continue to have the exchange of ideas required by our representative government," Grassley said. "I've always found that the best way to work on behalf of Iowans is to maintain an open and candid conversation."
   Grassley will visit several businesses including a Wal-Mart Distribution Center in Mount Pleasant, Case New Holland in Burlington , Emerson's Fisher Valve Division, and AlphaGen Materials in Vinton. He will also speak with the Oskaloosa-Sunrise Kiwanis, the Sigourney Kiwanis, the Iowa City Rotary and will meet with patients and staff at the McCreery Cancer Center in Ottumwa .
   He will speak with students and participate in question and answer sessions at Louisa-Muscatine Jr./Sr. High School, Pekin Community High School , Williamsburg High School and City High School in Iowa City
   Grassley will participate in a community walking tour of West Liberty and meet with chamber members and the mayor.
