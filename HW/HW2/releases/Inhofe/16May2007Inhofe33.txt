May 16, 2007
WASHINGTON, D.C.  U.S. Senator Jim Inhofe (R-Okla.) made the following statement in response to the news of Rev. Jerry Falwells death. 

Today our Nation grieves the passing of a tremendous American leader, Rev. Jerry Falwell, Inhofe said. Rev. Falwell served as the cornerstone for the Moral Majority in this nation and America is stronger as a result of his service. I knew Rev. Falwell well and consider myself blessed to have called him my friend. Today I join Americans of every stripe in mourning our nations loss.

Rev. Jerry Falwell, a television evangelist who is credited with founding the Moral Majority, died on Tuesday in Lynchburg, VA at age 73. He also founded and serves as the president of Liberty University.
