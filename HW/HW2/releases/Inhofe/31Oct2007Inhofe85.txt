WASHINGTON, D.C.  U.S. Senator Jim Inhofe (R-Okla.) today made the following statement praising Oklahoma-based power company OGE Energy Corp. for their recent announcement of plans to expand the companys wind power production:

Western Oklahoma has an abundance of usable wind and OGE deserves praise for their plans to expand their energy output by taking advantage of this renewable energy source.
