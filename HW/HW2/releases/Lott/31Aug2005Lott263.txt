SEN. LOTT TO HOLD MEDIA AVAILABILITY ON HURRICANE KATRINA
  Sen. Trent Lott of Mississippi will hold a media availability on Hurricane Katrina at 2:30 p.m. Central Time on Thursday, 
   September 1, outside the Harrison County Courthouse Emergency Operations Center in Gulfport, Mississippi.
   Prior to the media avail, Senator Lott will tour the Pascagoula area by vehicle, view by helicopter Katrina's devastation along the entire coastline, and receive a briefing at the National Guard Command Center at Gulfport.
   WHO: U.S. Senator Trent Lott of Mississippi
   WHAT: Media Avail on Hurricane Katrina
   WHERE: Harrison County Courthouse Emergency Operations Center, Gulfport,
   Mississippi
   WHEN: 2:30 p.m. Central Time, Thursday, September 1, 2005
   Contact: Lee Youngblood, 601/965-4644.
