SEN. LOTT ANNOUNCES $10 MILLION TO REMOVE DEBRIS, REBUILD SCHOOLS
 Federal grants totaling more than $10 million are approved for various projects in Harrison and Rankin counties, including funds to rebuild Pass Christian schools, Sens. Trent Lott and Thad Cochran said today. The grant funding is as follows:
   $2.2 Million (federal share) to Rankin County for debris removal 
   $8.6 Million(federal share) to Harrison County's Pass Christian Elementary School to replace and repair buildings.
   The grants will be administered through the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA).
