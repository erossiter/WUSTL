SEN. LOTT: MORE DISASTER ASSISTANCE AVAILABLE FOR MISSISSIPPI
  Sen. Lott has personally urged President Bush to expand the federal disaster declaration, making more Mississippi counties eligible for both public and individual federal disaster assistance.  
 As a result, on 9/6/05 the declaration was expanded. 
   Federal disaster assistance is broken into two categories: Public assistance enables local governments to repair damaged public facilities, while individual assistance empowers individual households to apply for assistance to repair disaster related damages. 
   As a result of Hurricane Katrina, an unprecedented 2/3 of Mississippi's counties have been declared federal disaster areas, and eligible for either public or individual assistance. The county-by-county breakdown is as follows.
   Counties currently eligible for individual assistance to households are:
   Adams, Amite, Attala, Claiborne, Choctaw, Clarke, Copiah, Covington, Forrest, Franklin, George, Greene, Hancock, Harrison, Hinds, Jackson, Jasper, Jefferson, Jefferson Davis, Jones, Kemper, Lamar, Lauderdale, Lawrence, Leake, Lincoln, Lowndes, Madison, Marion, Neshoba, Newton, Noxubee, Oktibbeha, Pearl River, Perry, Pike, Rankin, Scott, Simpson, Smith, Stone, Walthall, Warren, Wayne, Wilkinson, Winston and Yazoo.
   Counties eligible for public assistance include:
   Adams, Amite, Attala, Chickasaw, Choctaw, Claiborne, Clarke, Clay, Copiah, Covington, Forrest, Franklin, George, Greene, Hancock, Harrison, Hinds, Itawamba, Jackson, Jasper, Jefferson, Jefferson Davis, Jones, Kemper, Lamar, Lauderdale, Lawrence, Leake, Lee, Lincoln, Lowndes, Madison, Marion, Monroe, Neshoba, Newton, Noxubee, Oktibbeha, Pearl River, Perry, Pike, Rankin, Scott, Simpson, Smith, Stone, Walthall, Warren, Wayne, Webster, Wilkinson and Winston 
   with the amendment also including for public assistance:
   Alcorn, Benton, Bolivar, Calhoun, Carroll, Coahoma, DeSoto, Grenada, Holmes, Humphreys, Issaquena, Lafayette, Leflore, Marshall, Mntgomery, Panola, Pontotoc, Prentiss, Quitman, Sharkey, Sunflower, Tallahatchie, Tate, Tippah, Tishomingo, Tunica, Union, Washington, Yalohusha, and Yazoo counties.
