SEN. LOTT'S WEEKLY RADIO ADDRESS, APRIL 19
  Throughout his more than three-decades of service for Mississippians in Washington, Sen. Lott has made available an informative five-minute weekly radio address to be aired by Mississippi radio stations. Topics include national issues pending before the Senate, as well as federal projects and initiatives specifically advanced by Sen. Lott on Mississippi's behalf.
   Senator Lott's April 19, 2007, Weekly Radio Address.
