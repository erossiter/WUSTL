SEN. LOTT ANNOUNCES RETIREMENT DATE FROM U.S. SENATE
  U.S. Senator Trent Lott of Mississippi has informed Vice President Dick Cheney, the President of the U.S. Senate, he is retiring as the U.S. Senator for the State of Mississippi, effective at 11:30 p.m., Tuesday, December 18, 2007.
