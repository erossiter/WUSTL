SEN. LOTT ISSUES STATEMENT ON CONFIRMATION VOTE FOR JUDGE SOUTHWICK
  Senate Republican Whip Trent Lott of Mississippi issued the following statement after the Senate voted to confirm Judge Leslie Southwick to sit on the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals: 
    "The Senate carefully looked at the record and the temperament of Judge Leslie Southwick today and agreed with the Mississippians who know him so well that he will be an outstanding addition to the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals.
   "Judge Southwick is a scholar, a distinguished veteran, a compassionate civic leader and an outstanding jurist. My colleagues in the Senate understood that, and that is why they brought bipartisan deliberation back to the Senate today with his confirmation.
   "This is a good day for the Senate and portends an air of comity I thought we had lost. I am encouraged about the body in which we serve."
