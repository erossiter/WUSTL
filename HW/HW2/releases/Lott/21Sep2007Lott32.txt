SEN. LOTT'S WEEKLY RADIO ADDRESS, SEPT. 21
  Throughout his more than three-decades of service for Mississippians in Washington, Senator Lott has made available an informative five-minute weekly radio address to be aired by Mississippi radio stations. Topics include national issues pending before the Senate, as well as federal projects and initiatives specifically advanced by Senator Lott on Mississippi's behalf.
