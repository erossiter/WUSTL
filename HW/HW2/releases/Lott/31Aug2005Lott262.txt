GOV. BARBOUR'S OFFICE ESTABLISHES 501C3 FUND TO ACCEPT HURRICANE RELIEF DONATIONS
  Sen. Lott has received many calls from Americans asking how they can help the victims of Hurricane Katrina. Gov. Haley Barbour's office has established a 501C3 fund to accept hurricane relief donations.
   Only checks can be accepted, payable to: 
   "Mississippi Hurricane Fund" 
  Office of the Governor, Attn: Hurricane Relief Fund 
  P.O. Box 139 
  Jackson, MS 39201
