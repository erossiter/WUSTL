SEN. LOTT ANNOUNCES $1.1 MILLION TO IMPROVE MADISON AIRPORT
 Federal funding totaling more than $1.1 million is approved for use at Bruce Campbell Field in Madison, Mississippi , Sens. Trent Lott and Thad Cochran said today.  
  "The City of Madison and Madison County together compose one of Mississippi 's fastest growing residential and business centers," Senator Lott said. "Improving this airport is certainly a logical progression reflecting this rapid growth." 
   Senator Cochran stated:"Expanding Bruce Campbell Field will make it easier for companies that do business in Madison County to travel to the area. I am pleased to have helped provide the funding for this important project that will hopefully aid in economic development for central Mississippi ." 
   The grant will be administered by U.S. Department of Transportation via the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   The airport is beginning a major reconstruction of the terminal area. Specifically, the funds will be used to construct a new apron. As the recipient of this grant, the City of Madison plans to construct the new terminal building fronting on this new apron.
