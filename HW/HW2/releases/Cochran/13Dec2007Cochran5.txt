IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT REGARDING HOLIDAY CARDS FOR MEMBERS OF OUR MILITARY
  For the first time, the Department of Defense is not accepting holiday cards at military hospitals. The Red Cross has recently announced that it will accept cards and deliver them to the hospitals.
   To send holiday cards to soldiers recovering in military hospitals, mail to:
   We Support You During Your Recovery!
  c/o American Red Cross
  P. O. Box 419
  Savage, MD 20763-0419
