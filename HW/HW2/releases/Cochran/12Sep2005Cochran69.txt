SEN. THAD COCHRAN REOPENS GULF COAST OFFICE
  Sen. Thad Cochran announced that his Gulfport District Office has reopened following the devastation of Hurricane Katrina on the Gulf Coast. 
   "I am pleased my Gulfport office is open to assist in the needs of the Gulf Coast citizens who were affected by Hurricane Katrina. The federal government is responding comprehensively to the disaster, and my office will be able to help constituents fully utilize the resources available to them," said Cochran.
   The Gulfport staff, under the direction of Office Director Suzanne Case, is now available to assist constituents with questions regarding all federal agencies.
