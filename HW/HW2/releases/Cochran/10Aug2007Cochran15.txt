SEN. COCHRAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON APPOINTMENT OF MAYOR BROWN TO MISSISSIPPI COMMITTEE OF FARM SERVICE AGENCY
  Cochran Statement On Appointment Of Mayor Yvonne Brown To Mississippi Committee Of The Farm Service Agency: 
   "I am very pleased with the news of Mayor Yvonne Brown's appointment to the Farm Service Agency state committee for Mississippi. As mayor of Tchula, Mayor Brown has demonstrated her talent as a great community leader. Her unique people skills and her knowledge of agriculture will make her a strong advocate and mediator for farmers across Mississippi."
   For more information on the Farm Service Agency or this appointment, visit http://www.fsa.udsa.gov and click on the "News and Events" link.
