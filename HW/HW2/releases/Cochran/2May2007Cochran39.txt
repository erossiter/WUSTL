SEN. COCHRAN REACTS TO SUPPLEMENTAL VETO
  Sen. Thad Cochran (R-MS) said the following today in response to President Bush's veto of the Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Bill:
   "We need to quickly resolve the differences between the President and the Congress on this issue and approve a bill that provides the troops with the funding needed to protect the security of our country."
