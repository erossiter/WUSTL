SEN. COCHRAN APPLAUDS CONFIRMATION OF JUDGE SOUTHWICK
  Senator Thad Cochran praised the confirmation of Judge Leslie Southwick to the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals today.  
   "I am very pleased the Senate has confirmed this nominee," Senator Cochran said. "Judge Southwick is eminently qualified to serve on the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals. Not only is he an experienced judge in an appellate court position, but he is a widely respected person of great integrity. Judge Southwick's record of service to our country and to the people of Mississippi is exemplary. The Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals will be enhanced by his presence."
   The jurisdiction of the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals covers Mississippi, Louisiana, and Texas.
