SENS. SARBANES, MIKULSKI ANNOUNCE $5 MILLION FOR HAGERSTOWN REGIONAL AIRPORT
  Sens. Paul S. Sarbanes and Barbara A. Mikulski (both D-Md.) today announced the Hagerstown Regional Airport Field will receive a $5 million aviation grant from the U.S. Department of Transportation. This federal funding will be used to construct a runway safety area at the Richard A. Henson Field.  
   "This new infusion of funds continues the commitment made in 2003 to provide $47 million in federal funding for the expansion of and improvements to Richard A. Henson Field," said Senator Sarbanes. "These improvements when completed will significantly expand airport operations in the Hagerstown area and will serve as a beacon for economic development and job growth in the area." 
   "Our regional airports play a key role in maintaining Maryland's robust economy. An efficient and safe Hagerstown Regional Airport is critical to keeping Western Maryland's economy moving," said Senator Mikulski. "This federal funding will help keep the airport's runways safe and keep businesses and Marylanders on the go. I'm proud to have worked with Senator Sarbanes to help secure this important funding."
