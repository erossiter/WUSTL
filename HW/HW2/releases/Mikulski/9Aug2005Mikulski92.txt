SEN. MIKULSKI ISSUES STATEMENT ON RETURN OF DISCOVERY
  Sen. Barbara A. Mikulski (D-MD) released the following statement this morning following the safe return of the space shuttle Discovery: 
   "I am so proud to welcome Discovery and its crewmembers back to Earth. This mission has reminded Americans what space exploration is all about - that it requires both competence and courage. It has inspired a whole new generation of Americans and shown the world that we are tenacious, intrepid and innovative."
