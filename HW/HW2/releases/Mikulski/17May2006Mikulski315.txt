ON NATIONAL IMMIGRATION LOBBY DAY, SEN. MIKULSKI MEETS WITH STATE DEL. GUTIERREZ
  Sen. Barbara A. Mikulski (D-Md.) met with Maryland State Delegate Ana Sol Gutierrez (D-Montgomery County) and advocacy groups from Montgomery and Prince George's Counties today as part of National Capital Immigration Coalition's (NCIC) Lobby Day. Sen. Mikulski reiterated her strong support of comprehensive immigration reform.  
   "I hope the Senate is able to reach a compromise that addresses my three priorities: securing our borders, keeping American jobs in America, and making sure immigrants are treated with dignity," said Senator Mikulski.
