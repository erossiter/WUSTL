SEN. MIKULSKI ISSUES STATEMENT ON NSA REPORT
  Sen. Barbara A. Mikulski (D-Md.), a member of the Senate Select Intelligence Committee, released the following statement today following's reports of the NSA's collection of phone records:  
   "Today it has been reported that the government may be collecting the phone records of millions of Americans. We do not know if this report is valid. But what we do know is that we need greater congressional oversight of the Bush Administration's actions. 
   "During General Hayden's confirmation hearing, I will press for real answers on this and other issues regarding domestic surveillance."
