SEN. HATCH: GIVE HELP TO HURRICANE VICTIMS
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) today urged Utahns to assist victims of Hurricane Katrina in Gulf Coast states. 
   To best help those who are seeking to provide support, Hatch suggests volunteers should first visit the following websites: 
   * Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) at: http://www.senate.gov/cgi-bin/exitmsg?url=http://www.fema.gov/news/newsrelease.fema?id=18473
   * American Red Cross: http://www.senate.gov/cgi-bin/exitmsg?url=http://www.redcross.org/
   * Salvation Army: http://www.senate.gov/cgi-bin/exitmsg?url=http://www.salvationarmyusa.org/USNSAHome.htm
   * National Voluntary Organizations Active in Disaster (NVOAD): http://www.senate.gov/cgi-bin/exitmsg?url=http://www.nvoad.org/
   Volunteers may also wish to review the enclosed PDF document from FEMA that provides hotlines for providing donations and reporting missing persons. 
   View related PDF: HotlinesForMissingPersonVolunteersandDonations at: http://hatch.senate.gov/index.cfm?FuseAction=DataPipes.ViewPDF&Id=1417
