SEN. HATCH COMMENDS DR. CAPECCHI FOR HIS NOBEL PRIZE
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) today released the following statement on Dr. Mario Capecchi of the University of Utah, who received the Nobel Prize for medicine. 
   "This is truly an honor of the highest degree for a brilliant scientist who has devoted his life to bettering mankind. I am honored to have had the opportuity to discuss Dr. Capecchi's work with him on many occasions, and have only the highest praise for him and for his dedication, perseverance and intellect."
