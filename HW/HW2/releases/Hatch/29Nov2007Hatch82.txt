REP. HATCH MOURNS ILLINOIS REP. HYDE
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-UT) released the following statement today on the death of former Illinois Representative Henry Hyde.  
   "I am saddened by the passing today of a true statesman, courageous public servant and great friend, Henry Hyde. Henry and I had the opportunity to chair the Senate and House Judiciary Committees at the same time, and during those years I observed his true dedication to all aspects of the legal system. He was a fierce defender of human life and was never afraid to stand up for what he believed. He was a powerful voice for many in the House of Representatives and served his District with honor and integrity."
