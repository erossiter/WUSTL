SEN. HATCH REACTS TO U.S. AIRWAYS DROPPING BID TO MERGE WITH DELTA
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) released the following statement after learning that US Airways has withdrawn its bid to merge with Delta Air Lines: 
   "We can all breathe easier now. This was the right decision, and it couldn't have come sooner. Consolidation with US Airways could have seriously harmed Delta's Salt Lake City hub and hurt Utah's economy."
