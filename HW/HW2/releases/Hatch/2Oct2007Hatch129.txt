SEN. HATCH URGES DOL TO SHARE INFO WITH UTAH MINE SAFETY COMMISSION
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) signed onto a letter wgucg urges the Department of Labor to provide the Utah Mine Safety Commission with access to information that would otherwise be available under a Freedom of Information act request. It is also signed by Sen. Bennett, and Representatives Matheson, Bishop, Wilson, and McKeon.
