MIDVALE COMPANY AWARDED $75 MILLION CONTRACT
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) announced today that the Department of Homeland Security will be awarding a contract for $75 million to The NASCENT Group located in Midvale, Utah for construction projects at multiple Federal Law Enforcement Training Centers (FLETC).  
   "Utah companies have a reputation for hard work and innovation," said Hatch. "The Department of Homeland Security is awarding these contracts to the best firms in America, those who are able to demonstrate great performance and high quality work, so I compliment NASCENT, a great Utah company, on its achievement." 
   FLETCs play a vital role in the United States' homeland security efforts, providing law enforcement with training programs that include counterterrorism, forensics, financial investigations, physical fitness, and leadership. 
   The NASCENT Group, JV, is located at 7090 South Union Park Avenue, in Midvale, Utah.
