SEN. HATCH ISSUES STATEMENT REGARDING GENWAL MINE COLLAPSE IN EMERY COUNTY, UTAH
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah) today issued the following statement regarding the Genwal Mine collapse in Emery County, Utah 
   "My heart goes out to the trapped miners and their loved ones who are anxiously awaiting their rescue. Mine rescues are dangerous, so I want to commend those who have rushed to the site - and put their own lives in jeopardy - to bring these miners back home. I pray the rescue operation is successful, not only for the miners but for those in the rescue effort as well."
