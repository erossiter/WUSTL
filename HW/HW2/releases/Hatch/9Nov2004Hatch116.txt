SEN. HATCH PRAISES ATTORNEY GENERAL ASHCROFT'S LEGACY
  Sen. Orrin G. Hatch (R-Utah), Chairman of the Senate Judiciary Committee, today issued the following statement regarding Attorney General John Ashcroft's resignation: 
   "Attorney General John Ashcroft has served our country faithfully during one of our most challenging chapters in history. I enjoyed working closely with him. His courage and leadership are second to none. 
   "I am proud to have worked with him as Attorney General, to have served with him in the United States Senate and to call him a friend. I wish him and his family all the best."
