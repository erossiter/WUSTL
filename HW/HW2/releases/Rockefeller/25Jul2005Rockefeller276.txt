SEN. ROCKEFELLER SAYS THAT HIS WIFE, SHARON ROCKEFELLER, WILL BE TREATED FOR COLORECTAL CANCER
  Sen. Rockefeller said today that his wife Sharon has been diagnosed with colorectal cancer. She will be undergoing daily chemotherapy and radiation treatment for the next three months. During this period, her schedule will be uncertain. 
   "Sharon is a fighter, and I know she's going to beat this," Senator Rockefeller said. "She is a strong and loving person, and we are fortunate to be surrounded by so many supportive friends and relatives."
