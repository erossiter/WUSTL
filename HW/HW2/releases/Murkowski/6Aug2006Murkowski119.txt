SEN. MURKOWSKI MOURNS PASSING OF SUSAN BUTCHER
  Upon learning of the passing of Susan Butcher, Sen. Lisa Murkowski offered the following comments: 
   "Susan was an Alaskan hero - not only in how she ran and won the many Iditarod races, but also in how she battled her cancer. We are deeply saddened by her death, and Verne and I send our prayers to her husband and young daughters. Alaska shares your loss."
