SEN. MURKOWSKI ANNOUNCES SCHEDULE FOR EASTER RECESS
  Sen. Lisa Murkowski today announced her public schedule for the upcoming Easter Recess. During her travel, the Senator will participate in community events, school tours and meetings with local leaders in Kotzebue, Deering, Buckland, Noorvik, Selawik, Pt. Hope, Fairbanks, Wasilla and Anchorage.  
   The following is a list of Senator Murkowski's public events. 
   Monday, April 10: Senator Murkowski will be traveling throughout the Northwest Arctic Region. She will attend community events and tour schools in Kotzebue, Deering, Buckland, Noorvik and Selawik. 
   Tuesday, April 11: Senator Murkowski will tour the Kotzebue wind farm and travel to Pt. Hope to visit subsistence whaling crews during their spring hunt. 
   Wednesday, April 12: Senator Murkowski will be in Fairbanks to speak at the 2006 Annual Spring Luncheon at the Westmark Hotel from 11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. Additionally, the Senator will visit with researchers at UAF International Arctic Research Center to talk about climate change. 
   Thursday, April 13: Senator Murkowski will provide welcome remarks to the Pandemic Influenza Summit at the Anchorage Sheraton Hotel at 8:00 a.m. The Senator will discuss transportation issues with the Alaska Tribal Technical Assistance Program from 8:30 to 9:00 a.m. at the Marriot Hotel in Anchorage. Senator Murkowski will make opening remarks to the Sexual Assault Response Team's Summit from 10:30 to 11:00 a.m. at the Mat-Su Regional Hospital.
