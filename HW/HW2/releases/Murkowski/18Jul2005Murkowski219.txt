ALASKA DELEGATION ANNOUNCES THIRD LEG OF MOBILE OFFICE
  Beginning July 25th, the Alaska Congressional Delegation will be continuing its annual Mobile Office tour to meet constituents and help Alaskans handle federal issues.  
   Alaska Senators Ted Stevens and Lisa Murkowski will have staff visiting towns across the State where the Delegation does not have permanent offices. The third leg of the Mobile Office tour begins the week of July 25 and ends August 4. 
   Congressional staff will hold office hours in Gustavus, Hoonah, Skagway, Haines, Tok, Delta Junction, Nenana, Healy, and Talkeetna. Staff also will solicit public opinion messages and handle case work concerns. 
   Subsequent legs of the Mobile Office will be announced throughout the summer.
