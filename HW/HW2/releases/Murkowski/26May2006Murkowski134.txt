SEN. MURKOWSKI'S SCHEDULE FOR MEMORIAL DAY RECESS
  Sen. Lisa Murkowski today announced her public schedule for the upcoming Memorial Day Recess. During her travel, the Senator will participate in community events, local roundtables, town tours and meetings with local leaders in Kenai, Haines, Juneau, King Cove, Unalaska, Sand Point and Anchorage.  
   The following is a list of Senator Murkowski's public events. 
   Monday, May 29: Senator Murkowski will speak at a VFW Memorial Day Gathering at Leif Hanson Memorial Park in Kenai from 1:00 - 2:00 PM. 
   Tuesday, May 30: 
   In Haines, the Senator will speak at a Noon Chamber of Commerce Luncheon at the Alaska Bald Eagle Foundation. Immediately following, there will be a media availability in the Diorama Room. 
   The Senator will participate in a community meeting in Juneau at 4:30 PM at Centennial Hall. The forum will provide the people of Juneau the opportunity to express their views on the War in Iraq. 
   Wednesday, May 31: 
   Senator Murkowski will meet with City and Borough representatives in King Cove and will have lunch with the local community. 
   The Senator will meet with the Unalaska community at a public reception at the Grand Aleutian at 5:00 PM. 
   Thursday, June 1: 
   In Sand Point the Senator will participate in a public reception at 11:30 AM. 
   Friday, June 2: 
   Senator Murkowski will address the Women's Press Club at 11:30 AM in Anchorage at the Golden Lion Hotel. 
   At 1:00 PM, the Senator will hold a media availability at the Golden Lion.
