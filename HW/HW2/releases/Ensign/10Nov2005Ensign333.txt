 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign  a member of the Veterans  Affairs and Senate Armed Services Committees  today made the following statement in honor of the 230th birthday of the Marine Corps       Every day the Marine Corps proudly defends our freedom around the world in places such as Iraq and Afghanistan   Their profession is not an easy one  They put their lives on the line every day for the United States and all that it stands for   On this 230th birthday celebration of the Marine Corps  I salute past  present and future Marines  Their bravery and dedication to our country is to be commended         Senator John Ensign
