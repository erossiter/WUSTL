 Washington  D C     A bill sponsored by Senator John Ensign honoring former Sparks Mayor Tony Armstrong was signed into law by President George W  Bush  The Mayor Tony Armstrong Memorial Post Office will be dedicated on August 15 in Sparks   Congressman Jim Gibbons introduced the legislation in the House        Tony Armstrong is greatly missed     Ensign said     His life was about his family and his community  and I am pleased that his legacy and commitment to Sparks will be remembered through this post office   It will serve as a lasting reminder to northern Nevadans of a great leader and a great friend         Armstrong served as Sparks mayor from 1999 until his death in January 2005
