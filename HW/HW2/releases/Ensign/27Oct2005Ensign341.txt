 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement this morning in reaction to the withdrawal of Supreme Court nominee Harriet Miers       I respect and admire Harriet Miers   willingness to subject herself to a grueling process and intense scrutiny from members of Congress  the press and the public   She conducted herself with poise and dignity at all times   I look forward to President Bush  s putting another nominee forward  and I hope it is someone who believes in a strict interpretation of the Constitution as the Founders intended  who believes strongly that Congress  not the courts  makes law and who will reject the current trend of judicial activism in the courts today          Senator John Ensign
