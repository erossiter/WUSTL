 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement today in response to the recent passing of J A  Tiberti       The passing of J A  Tiberti is a great loss for all Nevadans   He was not only a friend but also an inspiration to me   Mr  Tiberti personified the bold entrepreneurial spirit that makes Nevada great and throughout his remarkable career was motivated by the values of honesty and sincerity   Throughout Nevada  homes  businesses  office buildings and resorts rising out of the desert bear the Tiberti name  a testament to a man who dedicated his life to helping build his community   J A  Tiberti leaves behind a family proud to carry on the Tiberti name  As Nevadans we are saddened over his passing but proud that he chose our state to raise his family and call home          Senator John Ensign
