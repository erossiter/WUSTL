 Washington  D C     Senators John Ensign and Harry Reid today announced a federal fire prevention grant to the Shoshone Paiute Fire Department in Owyhee   The  48 000 grant includes arson prevention  inspector certifications  smoke alarm purchases and education and awareness activities        Preventing fires before they happen will keep our firefighters and citizens safe     Ensign said     These funds will provide them the necessary preventive tools to protect their community           We need all the help we can get to fight fires in Nevada     said Reid     These funds will increase public awareness of the ways in which every Nevadan can help prevent fires         The Shoshone Paiute Tribe  s grant is a part of the 2004 Assistance to the Firefighters Grant Program managed by the Department of Homeland Security   All fire department grants throughout the country will total nearly  750 million in direct assistance      The Assistance for Firefighters Grant Program is administered by the Department of Homeland Security  s Office of Domestic Preparedness   The program assists rural  urban and suburban fire departments throughout the United States   These funds are used by the nation  s firefighters for firefighting operations  firefighter health and safety programs  new firefighting equipment  emergency medical services programs and fire prevention and safety programs
