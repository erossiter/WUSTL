 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign made the following statement after learning that Southwest Airlines will expand its presence at Las Vegas McCarran International Airport to nearly 2 000 employees   Southwest Airlines expansion shows the growing importance of McCarran International Airport and the future strength of Nevadas economy  As Las Vegas continues to grow  businesses from around the world are seeing the many advantages to calling Nevada home  I am pleased that Southwest  and other businesses  have seen such tremendous success in Nevada  Our state has always valued entrepreneurs  businesses of all sizes and hard work  and this is another example of how these values continue to reward Nevadas economy and residents
