 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today released the following statement in reaction to the elections taking place in Iraq       Today the Iraqi people are taking a crucial and historic step toward democracy and freedom     Ensign said     The road to democracy after generations of oppression and dictatorship is grueling  but the opportunity for Iraqis to chart their own future is worth the hard work and sacrifice   I applaud the courage of the Iraqi people and remain extremely proud of the men and women of the American military who helped make this day possible          Senator John Ensign
