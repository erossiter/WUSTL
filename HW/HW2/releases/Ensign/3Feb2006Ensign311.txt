 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today thanked President Bush for recognizing a major disaster in the state of Nevada following the severe storms and flooding that struck several northern counties at the end of last year and beginning of this year   Ensign led the delegation effort to request assistance from the White House         The storms and flooding that struck northern Nevada wreaked havoc on many of our public facilities     Ensign said     With the President  s declaration of a major disaster  he has shown his concern for our state and has opened the opportunity for federal funds to help us rebuild        Carson City  Douglas  Lyon  Storey  and Washoe Counties and the Pyramid Lake Paiute Tribe in Washoe County and the Washoe Tribe in Douglas County are eligible for the Public Assistance program following the President  s declaration   All Nevada counties are eligible to apply for assistance under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program   Both programs are administered by the Federal Emergency Management Agency       Federal assistance means a great deal to the impacted counties in the aftermath of these storms  and I know that all Nevadans are grateful for this critical aid     Ensign said
