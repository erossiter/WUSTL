 Washington  D C    Senators John Ensign and Harry Reid announced today more than  74 000 in federal funding for the Carson City Fire Department s fire prevention program      Preventing fires is crucial to the safety of the citizens of Carson City   Ensign said    The destruction of property and the risk to human life by fires can be prevented  and this funding will help advance efforts by the brave men and women of the Carson City Fire Department       We need all the help we can get to fight fires in Carson City   Reid said    This funding will ensure that firefighters have the resources they need to be safe  protect Nevadans  and do the best job possible      The funding comes from the U S  Department of Homeland Security s Office of Grants and Training in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration and is made under the Fiscal Year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program
