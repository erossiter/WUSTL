 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign made this statement following the death of former Nevada State Senator Lawrence   Jake    Jacobsen       I am saddened by the death of Senator Jacobsen  but I am inspired by the long and productive life he lived   He served our nation and our great state in many capacities and will always be a model of the type of citizen we should strive to be   From his Navy days and bravery at Pearl Harbor  to his exemplary service in our state legislature  to his commitment to a multitude of community organizations  Senator Jacobsen will always hold a special place in the hearts of Nevadans
