 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign  following a meeting with Air Force General Michael Hayden this morning  said he will support General Hayden  s nomination to lead the Central Intelligence Agency   Senator Ensign released the following statement       General Hayden and I had a frank and very productive discussion about some of the important issues facing the CIA and America  s overall efforts in gathering and analyzing intelligence     Ensign said     General Hayden was direct and forthcoming and based on what I  ve learned through our meeting and Capitol Hill hearings  I believe he is well qualified to head the CIA and I intend to support his nomination          Senator John Ensign
