 Washington  D C     Senator Ensign made the following statement regarding the high price of gasoline that continues to burden American families and businesses        No one knows better than Nevadans how trying this time is for our household budgets  our businesses and our patience   There is no easy cure for this continuing problem  but there are solutions that can start to ease the burden   As a nation  we need to encourage conservation  reduce our dependence on foreign oil and increase exploration of fossil fuels here at home   There are solutions  and it is time to make those the priority
