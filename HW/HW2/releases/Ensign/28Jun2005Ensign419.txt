 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement in reaction to tonight  s speech by President Bush regarding the war in Iraq        Tonight the President offered the American people an important reminder of America  s goals in Iraq  to hunt terrorists  help the Iraqi people build a sovereign nation  and defend and promote freedom in the Middle East   As President Bush said  the difficult and dangerous mission being undertaken by our troops will provide a safer and more peaceful world for our children and grandchildren   Now more than ever  it is crucial that the American people stand firm in their support of our servicemen and   women  their families  and our President           Senator John Ensign
