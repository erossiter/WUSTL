 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign shared the following thoughts after learning of the passing of former United States Senator Chic Hecht       Chic Hecht will be greatly missed   He was a good friend to me and a good friend to Nevada   Chic was the kind of person who you could always count on   I will always remember him for his fierce loyalty and for the fact that he believed in me when very few did           Senator John Ensign
