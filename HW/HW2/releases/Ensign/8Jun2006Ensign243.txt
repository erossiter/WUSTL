 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement in reaction to the death of Abu Musab al Zarqawi       The death of the terrorist leader Abu Musab Al Zarqawi marks a significant victory in our war on terrorism and  hopefully  brings us an important step closer to the defeat of Al Qaida     said Ensign  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee     Iraqis stepped forward with tips and intelligence which our troops were able to use to put an end to Zarqawi  s reign of terror   This cooperative effort shows that no terrorist in Iraq ultimately can hide from justice   Al Zarqawi  s death is an important victory  but the mission remains dangerous   I ask the American people to join me in congratulating our troops for a job well done and in praying for their continued success and safety          Senator John Ensign
