 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign made the following statement after Justice Priscilla Owen was confirmed by the United States Senate for the U S  5th Circuit Court of Appeals       Justice Priscilla Owen is an extremely well qualified and well respected jurist   I am sure Justice Owen and her family are relieved that this process has finally concluded   After being nominated by the President four years ago  it is outrageous that she found herself in the middle of an unprecedented political battle before today  s confirmation   While it is more than any nominee should have to endure  I know she will serve this nation with pride and with the utmost respect for our Constitution           Senator John Ensign
