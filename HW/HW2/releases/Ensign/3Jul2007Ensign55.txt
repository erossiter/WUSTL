  Washington  D C   U S  Senators John Ensign and Harry Reid of Nevada secured a federal loan to purchase four new school buses for the Storey County School District   We have many areas in the state where students must travel long distances to get to school  said Reid  The buses in Storey County are driven thousands of miles per year and it is imperative that we provide the safest  most reliable transportation possible for these children   Many families living in Nevada  especially in the rural areas  rely on school districts to take their children to and from school safely  said Ensign  These new buses will be a tremendous asset to parents and students of Storey County   The Storey County School District will receive a  400 000 loan to purchase four new school buses  The buses perform an important function for the school district transporting over 70 percent of the students to and from school
