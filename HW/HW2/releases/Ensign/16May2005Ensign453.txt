 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign today made the following statement in response to allegations that Russia profited from the United Nations Oil for Food scandal         Alleged Russian involvement in the United Nation  s corrupt Oil for Food program is further evidence that the depth and complexity of this scandal require far more investigation  As we uncover more information  the fraudulent and corrupt nature of this humanitarian program is truly coming to light  I am troubled that Russia received more oil allocations than any other country  These new developments only raise more questions  and I look forward to getting to the bottom of this scandal           Senator John Ensign
