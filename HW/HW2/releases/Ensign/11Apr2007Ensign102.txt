 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign supported legislation today that allows stem cell research to proceed in a manner that is ethical and respects human life   The Hope Offered Through Principled and Ethical Research Act passed with more than 70 Senators in favor  and after the vote Ensign released the following statement      Many researchers believe that human embryonic stem cell research has the potential to treat and better understand numerous diseases   I support the HOPE Act because it allows embryonic stem cell research to proceed using alternative methods that avoid the moral dilemma of destroying life in the process
