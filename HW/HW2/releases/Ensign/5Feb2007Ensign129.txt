 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign released the following statement regarding his efforts to keep Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act funding in Nevada as the President announces his budget      The Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act has proven to be an overwhelming success for every part of our state   We ve helped pay for our children s education  built parks and trails  protected places like Lake Tahoe and Red Rock Canyon and preserved our precious natural resources      I fought for and received a firm commitment from the Administration that the money generated from the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act will remain in Nevada  and the President s new budget does not change that commitment
