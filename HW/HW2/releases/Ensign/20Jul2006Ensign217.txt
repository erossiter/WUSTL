 Washington  D C    Senators John Ensign and Harry Reid today announced a grant for  237 385 for utility upgrades in Lincoln County   The funding is provided through the United States Department of Agriculture Rural Development Program          These funds are essential to helping Lincoln County upgrade its utilities and other vital services   Ensign said    I will continue my efforts in Washington to protect the interests of all rural communities in Nevada      The rural development grant will be used to replace existing clay piping of original sewer lines  repair service connections  bring manholes up to current code  upgrade the sewer treatment plant and make other necessary upgrades          Strengthening our rural communities and improving the quality of life for Nevada s rural residents is a high priority for me   said Reid    I m pleased that this money will be available for the people of Lincoln County to make these much needed improvements      The utility upgrades will directly affect more than 900 hundred residents in Lincoln County
