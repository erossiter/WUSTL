 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today made the following statement regarding the guilty pleas of Iraqi American Samir A  Vincent with regard to his role in the United Nations oil for food program        We know that corruption was rampant in the United Nations oil for food program  and I am hopeful that great progress will come from today  s charges   The United States is sending a clear message that those who made money off the suffering of the Iraqi people will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law   We owe it to the Iraqi people and to the legitimacy of future humanitarian efforts to get to the bottom of this scandal and ensure that such corruption never happens again              Senator John Ensign
