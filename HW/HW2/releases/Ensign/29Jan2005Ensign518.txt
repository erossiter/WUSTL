 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today released the following statement in reaction to the death of Sparks Mayor Tony Armstrong        Nevada has lost a fine mayor  a true and dedicated public servant  and a man who many  including myself  considered a close friend   I  m deeply saddened by Tony  s death and will miss him immensely  but grateful that I had the chance to know him   While there is nothing that can console Tony  s family at this time  I hope they find comfort in the fact that Tony spent his life trying to better his community   He approached his job with optimism  determination  and humor   He will be deeply missed  and his contributions will never be forgotten           Senator John Ensign
