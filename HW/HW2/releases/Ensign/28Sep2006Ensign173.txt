 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement tonight after voting in favor of a bill to clarify the rules under which suspected terrorists in American custody can be interrogated   The bill was approved by the Senate by a vote of 65 to 34       This bill enables our intelligence community to continue its effective program of detecting and stopping terrorist plots against America and our allies   This compromise bill denies classified information to suspected terrorists and will help pinpoint the precise location of our terrorist enemies so they can be apprehended before they strike   America is safer with the passage of this legislation          Senator John Ensign
