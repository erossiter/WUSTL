 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign voted in favor of the Lobby Reform Bill that passed the United States Senate by a vote of 90 8 tonight   Ensign released the following statement on the vote       There is no question that Americans are demanding  and rightfully so  a new era of clarity  transparency and accountability in the way their government does business   They expect their elected representatives to maintain a high level of integrity  and we have a deep responsibility to do so   This bill is an important step in the right direction   I remain committed to fighting for meaningful reforms in the earmark process   Earmarks are resulting in billions of dollars of wasteful government spending  and that part of the system must be improved          Senator John Ensign
