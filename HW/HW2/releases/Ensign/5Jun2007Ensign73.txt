 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign made the following comments today regarding the passing of U S  Senator Craig Thomas   Craig Thomas was a great man who served this nation with honor and pride  He set an example for all through his commitment to conservative principles and his advocacy for public lands  resources and rural America  Craig  a true cowboy with traditional Western values  will be missed as a colleague and as a friend  I extend my deepest sympathies to his wife  Susan  their family  and the people of Wyoming  whom Craig served with passion and devotion
