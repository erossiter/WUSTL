 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign  a member of the Veterans  Affairs Committee  today made the following statement in honor of the 75th anniversary of the Veterans Administration        It is important today and every day to salute our veterans for the sacrifices they have made for our freedom   For the past 75 years  the VA has provided our veterans with quality programs ranging from health care to home loan assistance   Our veterans protected us  and through the VA we are now protecting them   It is an honor to represent Nevada s veterans  and I will continue to fight for the benefits they deserve
