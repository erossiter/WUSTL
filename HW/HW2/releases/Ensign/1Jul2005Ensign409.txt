 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today issued the following statement on the retirement of Supreme Court Associate Justice Sandra Day O  Connor        Since joining the Supreme Court in 1981  Sandra Day O  Connor has made a tremendous impact on our country   She will leave the Supreme Court with a legacy that goes beyond being the first woman to serve on the high court   Justice O  Connor served with dignity and integrity  and I join President Bush in expressing gratitude for her service           Senator John Ensign
