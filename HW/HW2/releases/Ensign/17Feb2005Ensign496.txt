 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign made the following statement after President Bush  s nomination of current U S  Ambassador to Iraq John Negroponte to become the first national intelligence director        Ambassador Negroponte brings his extensive diplomatic career and leadership into this position   Having served as a United Nations Representative  a National Security Deputy Assistant  a member of numerous Foreign Service posts and an U S  Ambassador to four countries  including his current term in Iraq  he has all the right qualities to meet our global intelligence challenges   Mr  Negroponte will be a valuable asset in this critical time as our nation  s first national intelligence director  and I congratulate him on his nomination            Senator John Ensign
