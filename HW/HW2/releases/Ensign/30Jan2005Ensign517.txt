 Las Vegas  NV    Senator John Ensign released the following statement regarding today s election in Iraq        Today s election in Iraq should bring pride and appreciation to the hearts of all Americans   We are proud for the light of freedom that has been delivered to one of the darkest and most oppressed corners of the world  and seeing the risks that Iraqis are taking to experience freedom firsthand by casting their votes should make us all appreciate our own right to vote  something we too often take for granted   There is also an important lesson being shown to the world today   People crave freedom at their core no matter what their religion or ethnic background   Regardless of the outcome of today s election  freedom is the victor
