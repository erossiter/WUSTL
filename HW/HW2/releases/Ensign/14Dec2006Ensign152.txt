 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign has been reappointed to four committees for the next session of Congress  Armed Services  Budget  Commerce  Science and Transportation  and Veterans Affairs      These committees shape policies for our national security  economic competitiveness  keeping our promise to veterans and bringing fiscal discipline back to government   said Ensign    These committees oversee policies that are critical to Nevadans  and they reflect the issues important to our state      The committee assignments will be effective when the 110th Congress meets on January 4  2007  and are in addition to Ensign s new role in the Senate Republican leadership as Chairman of the National Republican Senatorial Committee   Committee seats were announced by Senate Republican Leader Elect Mitch McConnell     The Armed Services Committee has jurisdiction over the Pentagon and related military agencies  while the Commerce Committee has authority over a range of issues including aviation  interstate commerce and tourism
