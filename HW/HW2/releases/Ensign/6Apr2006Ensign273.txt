 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign has introduced amendments to the proposed immigration bill to reimburse states so they can employ the National Guard to secure their borders and to remove provisions that provide amnesty to illegal immigrants   The amendments were proposed late this afternoon   Ensign released the following statement on the bill and his proposed amendments to it       I am working to remove amnesty from this bill   A meaningful temporary worker program must start with securing our borders and having an employer verification program   Providing amnesty without securing our borders would create a flood of illegal immigration that our country cannot afford          Senator John Ensign
