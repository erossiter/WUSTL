 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign made the following statement after the Nevada delegation met with Energy Secretary Sam Bodman in regards to Yucca Mountain        After meeting with Secretary Bodman  I am disappointed and dismayed   It is amazing that after all of the recent developments regarding Yucca Mountain  including the revelations of falsified documents and fraudulent science  the Department of Energy continues to push ahead with this misguided project   Despite the Secretary  s attitude  opposition to the Yucca Mountain project is growing  and along with the other members of our delegation  I will continue to fight for the ultimate defeat of the project             Senator John Ensign
