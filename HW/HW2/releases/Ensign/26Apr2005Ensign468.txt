 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign issued the following statement regarding the last Syrian troops pulling out of Lebanon today        Today is a wonderful day for the Lebanese people and for the cause of freedom   For nearly three decades Lebanese citizens have lived in the shadow of their neighbor and oppressor  Syria   Today  they have emerged from that shadow and will finally have the opportunity to see and experience the light of freedom   I want the people of Lebanon to know that those who love freedom stand with them on this new day
