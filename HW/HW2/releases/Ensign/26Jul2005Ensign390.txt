 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today made the following statement after the launch of the space shuttle Discovery in Cape Canaveral  Florida        The seven astronauts aboard Discovery are in my thoughts and prayers as they take part in the first trip to space in two and a half years  While space travel is one of the greatest advancements in technology  it is also one of the most dangerous  The Discovery astronauts are brave men and women  and their commitment to the advancement of the U S  space program is to be commended
