 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign made the following statement after Nevada Attorney General Brian Sandoval was confirmed to the federal bench       I congratulate Brian Sandoval on this tremendous achievement   Throughout his career  Brian has shown a deep commitment not only to Nevada but also to our nation   I am confident that he will make Nevada proud as he takes on this incredibly important role in our judicial system   I have known Brian to be a man of integrity who will work tirelessly on the federal bench  He is committed to justice and will make an outstanding federal district court judge          Senator John Ensign
