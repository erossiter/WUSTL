 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign today held another hearing on financial management accountability and business systems modernization within the Department of Defense   Ensign is the chairman of the Readiness and Management Support Subcommittee of the Senate Armed Services Committee  where the hearing was held         Over the last four years we have seen a lack of financial management accountability  but we have had great progress in creating clear and definable goals to improve management and accountability     Ensign said     I am proud of the progress we have made in these hearings  but we still have a long way to go in fixing this broken system          I want to note for the record that this subcommittee was one of the shining stars in conducting oversight in this Congress     stated David M  Walker  Comptroller General of the United States     I want to compliment you for your dedication to this effort          As chairman of the Readiness and Management Support Subcommittee  Ensign has called oversight hearings every six months for four years   The hearings are designed to improve and streamline the financial practices throughout the Department of Defense         Every six months we continue to see improvements  but we must work to ensure that we spend every dollar wisely and continue to eliminate wasteful spending in our military     Ensign said     I have great respect for the men and women serving in our military and that is why I believe it is extremely important to make sure we get the greatest return for each dollar spent funding our uniformed men and women        Witnesses attending the hearing included Mr  David Walker  Comptroller General representing the Government Accountability Office  Mr  John Argodale  representing the Army  Mr  David Wennergren  representing the Navy  and Mr  John Vonglis  representing the Air Force
