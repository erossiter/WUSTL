 Las Vegas  NV    Senator Ensign made the following statement regarding Secretary of the Interior Gale Norton  s decision to maintain Colorado water releases  rather than divert water away from Lake Mead        I applaud Secretary Norton for taking a reasonable approach to water issues in the West   She determined that contentious adjustments should not be made to an agreement that was already in place   Water is a challenging issue for all of the states that rely on the Colorado River  and Secretary Norton made a fair decision that is good news for southern Nevada           Senator John Ensign
