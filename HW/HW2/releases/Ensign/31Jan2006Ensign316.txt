 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign released the following statement today in reaction to the passing of Coretta Scott King  who died overnight at the age of 78       Coretta Scott King made an immeasurable  graceful and permanent contribution to the civil rights movement in America and throughout the world   She turned the tragedy of her husband  s assassination into an inspiration for generations   My thoughts and prayers  and those of my family  are with all those close to Mrs  King today          Senator John Ensign
