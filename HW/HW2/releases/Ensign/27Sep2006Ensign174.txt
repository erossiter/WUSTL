 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign applauded the designation of the Nevada Northern Railway  East Ely Yards in White Pine County a National Historic Landmark   The designation was made today by the Secretary of the Interior Dirk Kempthorne       I would like to thank Secretary Kempthorne for ensuring that future generations can visit and experience this truly historic landmark   The railway station in Ely is a tangible reminder of Nevada and White Pine County  s unique history   A piece of Nevada  s rich heritage will now be preserved for years to come with this new designation          Senator John Ensign
