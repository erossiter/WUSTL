 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign made this statement following the successful vote in Iraq for a new constitution      The Iraqi people have once again demonstrated their yearning for freedom and their willingness to risk their lives in order to have a democracy   They exhibited bravery in the face of a growing insurgency  and with their purple inked fingers  showed the world their commitment to a free Iraq   For Americans  it is gratifying to see such progress and to know that the courageous men and women of our Armed Forces have fought for that freedom   Their sacrifices have not been made in vain
