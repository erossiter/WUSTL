 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign released the following statement after the Senate confirmed Greg Brower to be Nevadas next United States Attorney   Greg Brower has the expertise  qualifications and reputation to lead our U S  Attorneys Office  His experience ranges from working at the Department of Justice to serving in the United States Navy to representing Carson City and Washoe County in the state legislature  Greg has a tremendous track record and is widely respected in the legal community  which is why I recommended him for this position  Im pleased the United States Senate supported his nomination  His wife Loren and family should be proud   The Senate confirmed Brower last night
