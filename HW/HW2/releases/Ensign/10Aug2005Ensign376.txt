 Reno  NV  Senator John Ensign was joined by Sparks Mayor Geno Martini  Regional Transportation Commission Chair John Mayer and community leaders today in celebrating  12 million he secured for the Pyramid Highway Corridor in Sparks   The federal funding is included in the Transportation Equity Act which was signed into law by President Bush today       The growth and development of this area has created significant traffic challenges that are affecting the quality of life for Sparks   residents     Ensign said     This funding will go a long way toward easing congestion and providing a smoother and less stressful commute for drivers        Recognizing the importance of this corridor in northern Nevada  Ensign made the improvements in the area a priority   He previously delivered  6 million for the Pyramid Highway in a transportation appropriations bill passed last year
