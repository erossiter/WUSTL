 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign made this statement following a unanimous decision by the United States Supreme Court today that stops law schools and universities across the country from denying free speech to America  s military branches   The Court upheld the Solomon Amendment that was approved by Congress in 1994 and was designed to prevent discrimination on college campuses against military recruiters         The Supreme Court has stated unequivocally that military recruiters must have equal opportunity to educate students   Free speech is vital on our campuses  and representatives of our military  the very people who fight for our freedoms  deserve the chance to be heard   Today  the First Amendment was protected on behalf of students and on behalf of our military          Senator John Ensign
