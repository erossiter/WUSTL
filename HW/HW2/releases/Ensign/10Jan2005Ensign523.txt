 Las Vegas  NV    Senator Ensign made the following statement after learning of the death of Army PFC Daniel Guastaferro  27  of Las Vegas         I extend my heartfelt condolences to Daniel Guastaferro  s family and friends   Daniel was a bright  considerate  and well liked young man with a patriotic spirit that did not waver   He was proud to be in the Army  and this nation is proud of him   He will be sorely missed  but we will always remember the legacy of freedom that he left behind
