 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign issued this statement following the death of U S  Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist       I extend my deepest condolences to the Chief Justice  s three children  the rest of his family  his friends and his colleagues   His passing is a loss for the United States and our judicial system   Chief Justice Rehnquist served this country with deep respect for our Constitution and with great devotion to the principles upon which our nation was built   His judiciousness and leadership will forever be part of his legacy
