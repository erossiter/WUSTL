 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign applauded a recent announcement that a record level of funding has been transferred to Nevada counties   More than  13 7 million has arrived in Nevada through Payments in Lieu of Taxes  PILT   which are federal payments to local governments to offset losses in property taxes due to federal lands within their boundaries         This is good news  especially for our rural counties     said Ensign  a cosponsor of legislation to fully fund PILT     No state has more of its land owned by the federal government than Nevada  and these increased payments are extremely important to our counties and their citizens   Counties across Nevada will be able to use these payments to fund critical needs in their communities          Nevada received an increase of more than  237 000 over last year   PILT payments may be used for any governmental purpose such as police and fire protection and road  water and school maintenance
