 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign released the following statement regarding the Senate s bipartisan agreement to couple a minimum wage increase with a small business tax relief package      America s small businesses keep our economy strong by creating jobs   By adding tax relief to a minimum wage increase  we re helping to preserve jobs and the entrepreneurial spirit that has always been the strength and backbone of our country       The House muscled through a stand alone minimum wage increase that would be detrimental to the small businesses that keep our economy moving forward       This bipartisan agreement in the Senate is the result of Senate Republicans insisting that we will not just hastily raise the minimum wage  which could result in layoffs   It will also encourage growth and job creation by providing much needed relief to the small businesses that employ nearly half of all Americans
