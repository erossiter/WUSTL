 Washington  D C   Senator John Ensign made the following statement in reaction to the death of Nevada Army National Guard Specialist Anthony S  Cometa  21  of Las Vegas  who was killed in Iraq        Anthony Cometa will be remembered as a courageous young man who made the ultimate sacrifice while serving our nation  I extend my deepest condolences to Anthony  s family at this time of tragedy and hope they know that all Nevadans join with them in mourning their son  My thoughts and prayers are not only with his family but also with the families of all our troops who so bravely fight for our country
