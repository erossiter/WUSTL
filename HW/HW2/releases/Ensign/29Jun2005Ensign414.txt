 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign announced today a  1 1 million grant for the Southern Nevada Workforce Investment Board to train more than 11 000 employees for work in the hospitality industry        This grant is a huge boost for those seeking the training and skills to work in southern Nevada  s hospitality industry     Ensign said     The job placement and on the job training that the Southern Nevada Workforce Investment Board and its partners provide mean expanded employment opportunities and invaluable experience   They also translate to a well trained workforce for our community         The Southern Nevada Workforce Investment Board works in close partnership with the Community College of Southern Nevada  the Nevada Hotel   Lodging Association  the Nevada Hospitality Foundation and Station Resorts and Casinos   The grant of  1 121 166 was awarded by the Department of Labor as part of the President  s High Growth Job Training Initiative
