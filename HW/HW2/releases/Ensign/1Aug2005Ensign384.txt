 Washington  D C    Senator John Ensign today released the following statement regarding President Bush s appointment of John Bolton as Ambassador to the United Nations       I am extremely pleased that President Bush is using his Constitutional right to make recess appointments to name John Bolton Ambassador to the United Nations   Mr  Bolton will be diligent in asserting U S  interests at the United Nations and will serve America well in this important post   He is the right man to provide leadership in achieving much needed U N  reform   I congratulate both the President and John Bolton on this appointment         Senator John Ensign
