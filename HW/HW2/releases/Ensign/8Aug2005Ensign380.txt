 Washington  D C     Senator John Ensign made the following statement after learning that ABC anchorman Peter Jennings died of cancer        I extend my condolences to Peter Jennings   family  friends  colleagues and viewers   For more than 40 years  Peter built a career as a respected journalist   He covered the biggest news stories of our time and earned his place as one of our top news anchors   He will be missed for his devotion to delivering news and for his relationship with the American public
