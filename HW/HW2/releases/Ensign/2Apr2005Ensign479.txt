 Las Vegas  NV  Senator John Ensign made this statement following the death of Pope John Paul II         I join the world community in mourning the loss of Pope John Paul II   The Pope was a man of deep faith  incomparable compassion and great conviction who inspired millions of people in his lifetime   He traveled the globe to share his love for God in all corners of the world   The Pope was a tremendous example for all of us with his respect for life and his spirit of mercy  humility  suffering and service to others   He sowed peace and forgiveness in this world   In the final days of his full and rich life  he exhibited a courage and dignity for which we should all strive   May God continue to watch over him and bless him           Senator John Ensign
