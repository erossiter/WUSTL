    03 16 2006   Senate Adopts Levin Amendment to Increase Funding by  6 Million for Additional Northern Border Air Wings                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 16  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senate Adopts Levin Amendment to Increase Funding by  6 Million for Additional Northern Border Air Wings                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON     The Senate today adopted an amendment by Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   to increase funding by  6 million to establish additional Northern Border Air Wings  including one in Michigan   The amendment was accepted as part of the Budget Resolution for fiscal year 2007            Establishing a Northern Border Air Wing site in Michigan is a national priority     said Levin      The Michigan site would be responsible for an area that encompasses at least three of our Great Lakes and several major ports along the St  Lawrence Seaway including Detroit  Cleveland  Chicago  Milwaukee and Green Bay   In addition  Southeast Michigan is home to three of our nation   s busiest border crossings and an unparalleled industrial base vital to our economy and national security   A Northern Border Air Wing site in Michigan will provide an additional layer of air and marine border security along a critical section of our Northern Border          The Northern Border Air Wing initiative  launched by the Department of Homeland Security in August 2004  is designed to maximize security along the U S   Canadian border   Original plans called for five Northern Border Air Wings to be established in New York  Washington  North Dakota  Montana and Michigan   The New York and Washington sites have been in operation since 2004         In testimony before the Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee this month  Secretary Michael Chertoff testified that a new Northern Border Air Wing site will open in Montana in fiscal year 2006 followed by additional sites in Michigan and North Dakota in fiscal year 2007   However  the President   s budget request for fiscal year 2007 did not include any designated funding for new Northern Border Air Wing sites   Levin   s amendment would add  6 million to the Budget Resolution for fiscal year 2007 for the establishment of the new sites
