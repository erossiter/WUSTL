    09 14 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  97 000 for Pinconning Fraser Fire Department                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 14  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  97 000 for Pinconning Fraser Fire Department                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the Pinconning Fraser Fire Department has been awarded  97 683 through the fiscal year 2005 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used for operations and safety programs          Firefighters play a critical role in the safety of Pinconning and all of Michigan     said Levin      This federal funding will help provide the resources they need to do their jobs effectively             Michigan   s first responders work tirelessly every day to protect our neighborhoods and our families     said Stabenow     This grant will help the Pinconning Fraser Fire Department to provide its firefighters the best equipment and training available          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of firefighting operations and firefighter health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process       Senators Levin and Stabenow are fighting to restore over  300 million to the AFGP which was cut under the Bush Administration s fiscal year 2007 budget
