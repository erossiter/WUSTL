                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 5  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  301 818 in Firefighter Grants for Seven Fire Departments in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that seven fire departments in the state of Michigan have received  301 818 in grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  Lincoln Park  Duncan Township  Centreville  Hillsdale  Muskegon Heights and Frederick Township Fire Departments will receive funding for fire operations and firefighter safety programs   Buena Vista Township Fire Department will receive funding for the firefighting vehicle acquisition program           Our firefighters deserve our full commitment as they risk their lives to protect our communities     Levin said     These grants will provide our firefighters with additional training and needed equipment so that they can do their jobs more safely and effectively             Assistance to Firefighters Grants play an important role in making sure that our nation s emergency responders have the equipment and training they need to respond to all hazards      Stabenow said     This round of funding will help another seven Michigan fire departments do their jobs  improve safety and save lives           These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  a joint effort of the Department of Homeland Security  the Federal Emergency Management Agency and U S  Fire Administration   The fire operations and firefighter safety programs provide firefighters with equipment  training and modifications to facilities  The vehicle acquisition program allows departments to purchase vehicles  such as tankers  rescue vehicles  foam units and ambulances
