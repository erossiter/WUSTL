    09 23 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  29 000 for Delta County Sheriff   s Department                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 23  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  29 000 for Delta County Sheriff   s Department                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that the Delta County Sheriff   s Department has been awarded  29 400 by the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  Community Facilities grant program   The grant will go towards the purchase of two police vehicles and new equipment for the Sheriff   s Department          This is great news for all of Delta County     said Levin      These federal dollars will help local authorities do their jobs by providing them with the equipment and vehicles they need             All preparations for emergency response come down to making sure that first responders  including local law enforcement have the equipment they need     Stabenow said     This is an important grant for the Delta County Sheriff   s Department and the people it serves              The Community Facilities grant program is run by the USDA   s Office of Rural and Community Development   The program aims to assist rural areas in the development of essential community infrastructure
