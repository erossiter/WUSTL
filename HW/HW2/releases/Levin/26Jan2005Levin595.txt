    01 26 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Funding for 21 Buses for Nine Michigan Transit Authorities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 26  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Funding for 21 Buses for Nine Michigan Transit Authorities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON    A  970 874 grant from the Federal Transit Administration will allow nine transit authorities in Michigan to purchase a total of 21 buses to serve their communities  U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today          Public transportation is a sensible choice for some people  but for others it   s an essential means of obtaining and maintaining quality jobs to support their families     Stabenow said     Just as we support job growth  we must support the infrastructure that allows people to get to their jobs             Each of these communities will benefit from this investment in public transportation     Levin said     These new buses will improve the rider experience for the many people who rely on the bus system to get to work or to do their shopping          The grants were awarded as follows         Community Award   Jackson 11 vehicles    City of Adrian  1 vehicle   City of Belding  1 vehicle   Care Transit Authority 1 vehicle   Cass County Transportation Authority 1 vehicle   Charlevoix County Transportation  2 vehicles   Clinton Area Transit System  1 vehicle    City of Midland  2 vehicles    Otsego County  1 vehicle
