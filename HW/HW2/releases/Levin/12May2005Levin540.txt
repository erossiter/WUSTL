    05 12 2005   Senators Carl Levin  Debbie Stabenow React to Rejection of Proposed Compromise on 6th Circuit Nominees                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 12  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Carl Levin  Debbie Stabenow React to Rejection of Proposed Compromise on 6th Circuit Nominees                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  issued the following statement today          Today we supported a good faith effort by Senator Reid to break the larger logjam on judges by agreeing to move forward on three of the four Michigan judges President Bush has nominated to the Sixth Circuit   We continue to have serious objections to one of the four judges             It was our hope that Senator Reid   s proposal would help resolve the impasse in the Senate on judicial nominations that threatens to undermine the checks and balances put in place by our founding fathers             We are astonished that the Republican leadership rejected this good faith compromise             It has become clear that the nuclear option is more about reducing the advise and consent power of the U S  Senate than it is about getting judges confirmed
