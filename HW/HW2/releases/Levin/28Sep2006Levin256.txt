    09 28 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  180 000 for West Michigan Volunteer Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 28  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  180 000 for West Michigan Volunteer Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security is awarding  180 220 to two Michigan organizations in Crockery and Emmitt Townships under the Assistance to Firefighters Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grant program   The funding will be used to support the recruitment and retention of volunteer firefighters          Volunteers firefighters provide our communities with an invaluable service     said Levin      These federal dollars will help ensure that West Michigan is prepared for fire related danger             Volunteer firefighters play a critical role in fire departments across Michigan     said Stabenow      This funding will help recruit and retain those volunteers that selflessly give their time and energy to protect our communities          The goal of the SAFER program is to help fire departments protect their communities from fire related hazards and achieve and maintain 24 hour staffing       The Crockery Township Fire Department will receive a grant of  58 700 and the Emmett Township Department of Public Safety will receive a grant of  121 520   Both of these awards will be used to create a more favorable working environment for volunteer firefighters and encourage prospective volunteers
