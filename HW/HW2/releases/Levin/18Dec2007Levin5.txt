    12 18 2007   DOD Will Not Pursue Change in Regulation Following Questions Raised by Levin                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 18  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      DOD Will Not Pursue Change in Regulation Following Questions Raised by Levin                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON   Following questioning this morning of a Department of Defense nominee by Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   the Pentagon announced this afternoon that it would not pursue a proposed regulation that would have given civilian politically appointed lawyers the authority to interfere in the promotion process for military lawyers          I   m pleased the Defense Department has backed off of this ill advised proposal to give political appointees veto authority over the recommendations of military promotion boards     Levin said      This proposal would have had an unfortunate chilling effect on the legal advice furnished by military attorneys to our military commanders          The proposed DOD instruction  first reported in the Boston Globe yesterday  would have required the approval of the General Counsel of the Department of Defense or the General Counsel of a military department before the promotion of any military lawyer   At the nomination hearing for Craig Duehring to be Assistant Secretary of the Air Force for Manpower and Reserve Affairs  Levin  chairman of the Senate Armed Services Committee  expressed concern about the proposed regulation   Senator Jim Webb  D Va   expressed a similar concern         Several hours after the hearing  Mr  Duehring informed the committee that DOD would delete that language from the draft instruction   The Department of Defense has confirmed the decision to make this deletion
