    09 09 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  1 2 Million in Funding for Grand Rapids Area Buses Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 9  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  1 2 Million in Funding for Grand Rapids Area Buses Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON    A grant of  1 214 724 from the Federal Transit Administration will allow The Rapid to purchase six new buses to serve the Grand Rapids area  U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today          Public transportation plays an important role in getting many of our citizens to work  to school  or to doctors    appointments     Stabenow said     Public transportation has allowed individuals and families to improve their quality of life  and I have worked hard to ensure that Michigan receives expanded funding for public transit services             This investment in public transportation is also an investment in the lives and livelihoods of many citizens of the Grand Rapids area     said Levin      These six new buses will provide reliable public transportation for people to get to their jobs  to the store  or to wherever they need to go          An agency of the U S  Department of Transportation  the FTA offers grants for a wide variety of bus and bus related projects  including the acquisition of buses for fleet and service expansion  bus maintenance and administrative facilities  transfer facilities  bus malls  transportation centers  intermodal terminals  park and ride stations  and other services that aid and improve public transportation
