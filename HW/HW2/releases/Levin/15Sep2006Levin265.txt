                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 15  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  84 000 to Support Two Javits Fellowships at U of M                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Education has awarded  84 448 to the University of Michigan to support two Jacob Javits Fellowships   Javits fellowships are awarded for graduate level study to students of exceptional ability   Michigan is one of just 12 universities nationwide selected by the Department of Education to support Javits fellows          The Javits Fellowship program provides valuable opportunities for graduate study to students who have demonstrated that they are capable and eager to learn     said Levin     The University of Michigan will provide the educational resources and the environment these students need to reach their full potential             I am proud that our state is once again being recognized for its commitment to higher education     said Stabenow      The Javits Fellowship program will provide these deserving students the resources they need to reach their educational goals          The Javits Fellowship Board selects panels of experts to award each fellowship in designated fields of study spanning the arts  humanities and social sciences   Students are selected on the basis of demonstrated achievement  financial need and exceptional promise       For more information  students interested in applying for the program should visit    Applications are due October 6
