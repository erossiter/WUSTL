    10 27 2005   Levin Statement on the Withdrawal of Harriet Miers     Nomination to the Supreme Court                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 27  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Withdrawal of Harriet Miers     Nomination to the Supreme Court                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement after President Bush announced that White House Counsel Harriet Miers had withdrawn her nomination to the Supreme Court         I urge the President to consult widely on his next nominee for the Supreme Court so that he can make a unifying choice that will have broad support   The President should choose a nominee who doesn   t have an ideological agenda  who is open minded  and whose values are consistent with the fundamental principles of American law   Levin said
