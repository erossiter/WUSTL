                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 7  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  900 000 for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that five Michigan fire departments have been awarded  935 155 through the Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety and to purchase firefighting vehicles          These federal dollars are an important part of preventing disaster and keeping our communities safe     said Levin     Firefighters and first responders are critical to protecting our homes and families  and we owe it to them to provide the funding and the resources they need           Keeping our communities safe begins with providing our first responders the resources they need   said Stabenow    This funding will enable these fire departments to continue their great work protecting Michigan families        The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process   More information can be found at    Individual grant
