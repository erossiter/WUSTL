                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security is awarding  101 715 to the City of Novi Fire Department under the Assistance to Firefighters Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grant program   Funds under the SAFER program support the hiring of firefighters and the recruitment and retention of volunteer firefighters          Cities across Michigan rely on their first responders to keep their communities safe and secure     Stabenow said      I   m glad these funds will go toward ensuring the City of Novi has all the personnel needed to effectively respond to emergencies              Our fire departments are able to protect our communities thanks to the dedicated men and women who step up to serve as firefighters  paramedics and emergency medical technicians     said Levin      This grant will help the City of Novi Fire Department meet its staffing needs          The goal of the SAFER program is to help fire departments protect their communities from fire related hazards and achieve and maintain 24 hour staffing       Ultimately  it is hoped that the recruitment and retention activity will create a net increase in the number of trained  certified  and competent firefighters capable of safely responding to emergencies likely to occur within the fire department   s geographic response area
