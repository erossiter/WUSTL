    04 15 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Urge the Department of Homeland Security to Locate New Northern Border Air Wing at Selfridge                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 15  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Urge the Department of Homeland Security to Locate New Northern Border Air Wing at Selfridge                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senators seek update on department   s decision         WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   sent a letter to the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security today  urging him to select Selfridge Air National Guard base as the location for the new Northern Border Air Wing  stating it is a    secure  operational facility situated on 3 200 acres of land owned by the federal government     The new facility is scheduled to open this year           A Selfridge based Northern Border Air Wing  like those operating out of New York and Washington  could operate out of Selfridge with ease  The Michigan National Guard recently completed a full upgrade of its runway and it could easily handle the needs of the entire Immigration and Customs Enforcement Service  Air and Marine Operations inventory     Levin and Stabenow wrote to Secretary Michael Chertoff  The senators last year made the same request to Secretary Chertoff   s predecessor  Secretary Tom Ridge        The senators requested that Secretary Chertoff provide them an update on the selection and opening of a Northern Border Air Wing in Michigan  which is critical to the region   s northern border security     This region is the home of three of our nation   s busiest border crossings and a massive industrial base which is vital to our national security and economy  The addition of a Marine Air Branch in this vital region would bring the technology and equipment necessary for protecting this region   s border crossings and critical infrastructure     Levin and Stabenow wrote
