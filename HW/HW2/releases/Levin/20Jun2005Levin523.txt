                                                              Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  1 700 000 for Chippewa County International                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded a  1 575 000 and a  125 000 grant to the Economic Development Corporation of Chippewa County for enhancements at Chippewa County International Airport  The funding will be used to improve the runway and security          Chippewa County International Airport serves an important function as the only commercial airport in the eastern U P      said Levin     These grants will provide needed rehabilitation and improved security so residents and travelers can continue to count on a safe and reliable airport               Michigan airports need to continually upgrade their facilities to meet new demands for safety and security     Stabenow said     With this funding  Chippewa County International will be able to make important improvements that expand commercial and tourism opportunities for the eastern U P          The  1 575 000 grant will be used to      Restore the structural integrity of the pavement  Replace the runway lighting system  Purchase an emergency generator to be used during periods of electrical outage  Conduct a cost benefit analysis of building a new cargo facility    The  125 000 grant will be used to provide security enhancements required by law        For more information on DOT grants  please visit http   www dot gov
