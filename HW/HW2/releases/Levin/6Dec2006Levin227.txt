    12 06 2006   Levin Statement on the Death of Mayor Michael Guido                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 6  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Death of Mayor Michael Guido                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made this statement following the death of Mayor Michael Guido of Dearborn          Mike Guido was a dear friend and a great leader in Michigan   I will always remember his warm greeting and his extraordinary hospitality whenever we saw each other or met in his office   Mike was tireless and always on the move  working on the issues that mattered most to the people of Dearborn   He loved his job and he loved Dearborn   Mike Guido was more than a mayor  he was truly a servant to the public   Dearborn blossomed under his leadership   I am deeply  deeply saddened by this loss
