                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 1  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  1 319 947 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that five fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  1 319 947 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program          Every fire department in Michigan faces critical challenges in protecting local residents and their property     Stabenow said     Through these grants  we help ensure that Michigan   s firefighters have the equipment  training  and personnel they need to respond to all threats and hazards              Michigan   s first responders continue to play a vital role in ensuring our safety and security     said Levin      These federal dollars are an important step in providing them with the resources they need to adapt to and manage a wide variety of potential dangers          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities  The Recruitment and Retention of Volunteer Firefighters grants are intended to increase the number of competent firefighters who are trained  certified  and capable of responding to emergencies likely to occur within the fire department   s geographic area       The following communities were awarded grants under terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program
