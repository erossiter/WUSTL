                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 29  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  1 380 690 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that 18 fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  1 380 690 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 and 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Programs            From my many meetings with Michigan firefighters  I know that our communities value this grant program and the assistance it provides in enhancing local fire protection     Stabenow said     These new grants will aid 18 more Michigan communities in the vital task of protecting life and property             This is great news for communities across Michigan     Levin said      The fire grants program plays an important role in making sure local emergency personnel are well funded and prepared for the challenges they face every day          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities        The following communities were
