    04 10 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  420 000 for Michigan Law Enforcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 10  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  420 000 for Michigan Law Enforcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice is awarding  420 103 for law enforcement programs to five Michigan cities and counties as part of the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistant Grant Program   Grant recipients include the Counties of Macomb and Saginaw and the Cities of St  Clair Shores  Farmington Hills and Grand Rapids          Adequately funding our local law enforcement agencies is one of the most important steps we can take towards protecting our homes and communities     said Levin      We depend on local police officers and emergency responders for our basic safety  and this federal funding will help provide them with the equipment and resources they need             Our law enforcement agencies constantly face new challenges and new responsibilities in protecting the people of Michigan and in fighting and preventing crime     Stabenow said     These grants represent our continued commitment to work in partnership with Michigan communities to help them meet these challenges          The Byrne grant program supports states  Indian tribes and local governments in their efforts to prevent and control crime   The program takes local needs and conditions into account and provides funding for a variety of purposes  including targeted initiatives  technical assistance  personnel  training  equipment and supplies   Please see the table below for the details regarding each individual grant        Grant Recipient  Amount Awarded  Purpose    Macomb County   109 973  to purchase new safety equipment  and to help fund a diagnostic program for non violent offenders with substance abuse problems and mental illness    City of St  Clair Shores   12 019  to purchase a new 800 MHz police radio system    City of
