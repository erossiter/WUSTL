                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 31  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Death of Coretta Scott King                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   released the following statement on the death of Coretta Scott King          We first came to know Coretta Scott King as Dr  Martin Luther King   s wife  but we came to treasure her for the more than 50 years of courageous and inspiring leadership she gave to our nation            Following her husband   s assassination  Mrs  King picked up his mantle and made clear that his famous dream of a just America was her dream too   Over the nearly 40 years that followed  her fight for that dream took her to every corner of the world and into every heart that loved justice            In September 2004  the Senate passed legislation to honor Mrs  King and Dr  Martin Luther King Jr   posthumously  with a congressional gold medal for their contributions to the nation   The design of this medal includes these words from Mrs  King  a compilation of her favorite lines from Dr  King   s speeches     I suggest that the philosophy and strategy of nonviolence become immediately a subject for study for serious experimentation in every field of human conflict  by no means excluding the relations between nations  This may well be mankind s last chance to choose between chaos and community      Mrs  King contributed these lines less than a year ago  in March 2005  reflecting her steadfast commitment to nonviolence throughout her entire life          Coretta Scott King moved our nation forward  and we owe her a debt that we cannot repay   As we mourn Mrs  King   s passing today  let us celebrate her exceptional life  and let us honor her by recommitting ourselves to the dream the Kings shared of freedom  justice and equality for all people
