    10 03 2007   Levin Statement on President Bush   s Veto of Children   s Health Insurance Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 3  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on President Bush   s Veto of Children   s Health Insurance Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement regarding the President   s veto of the State Children   s Health Insurance Program           In a country with millions of uninsured children  the President   s decision to veto the State Children   s Health Insurance Program is outrageous    We have a moral obligation to provide access to affordable health care to all Americans  but especially to the most vulnerable members of our community            I am pleased that a strong  bipartisan and veto proof majority of 67 senators voted for the bill   I hope that the House will gain the votes needed to override President Bush   s veto and show the American people that Congress is doing all we can to reduce the number of uninsured children in this country
