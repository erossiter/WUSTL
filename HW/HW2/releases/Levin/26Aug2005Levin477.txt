    08 26 2005   Senators Levin and Stabenow Announce  826 738 in Funding for Native American Law Enforcement Agencies in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 26  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin and Stabenow Announce  826 738 in Funding for Native American Law Enforcement Agencies in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  has awarded  826 738 in grants to Native American law enforcement agencies in Michigan through the COPS Tribal Resources Grant Program  TRGP        TRGP is designed to enhance the law enforcement infrastructure and community policing practices in Native American communities  and funds such items as salaries and benefits for new community policing officers  basic equipment  crime fighting technology systems  and training           The COPS program has been a great success in Michigan  where it has allowed local communities to hire nearly 3 500 police officers     Levin said      This funding for Native American law enforcement agencies will help tribes hire and train new officers and modernize their equipment   It is a significant  community based contribution to improving safety and law enforcement in Michigan   s Native American communities             These funds will help provide Michigan   s Native American communities with much needed support for their law enforcement efforts     Stabenow said     COPS Tribal Resource grants address needs that aren   t met by state  local and BIA funding and help make communities safer           The grants were awarded to 80 tribal police departments in 25 states   These grants will enable Native American law enforcement agencies to hire 73 new officers  purchase critical much needed equipment  and receive training   Since 1995  COPS has provided more than  192 3 million to Native American law enforcement
