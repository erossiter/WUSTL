                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJuly 31  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin  Over  112 000 Coming to Ingham County to Help Steer Youth Away from Gangs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that Ingham County will receive  112 142 to provide students with the skills they need to avoid gang pressure and youth violence   The funds were awarded under the Department of Justice FY 2007 Gang Resistance Education and Training  G R E A T   program          Our children need the support of parents  law enforcement  and community leaders to resist gangs and the temptation to choose the wrong path early on in life     said Stabenow     This program brings together a diverse coalition of community leaders and the proper resources necessary to administer an effective curriculum for students in high crime neighborhoods             Keeping Michigan   s future generations away from the scourge of gangs is a very important goal     said Levin     I am hopeful that this program will help law enforcement officials in Lansing guide students away from such a destructive path          The Lansing Ingham County G R E A T  program will include four components  a 6 week elementary school curriculum for 5th graders  a 13 week middle school curriculum for 8th graders  a 6 week family program  and a summer component operated in collaboration with the Boys and Girls Club of Lansing       The primary goal of the G R E A T  program is to provide a life skills competency program in which students will be able to develop values and practice behaviors that will help them to avoid destructive activities  G R E A T  trains law enforcement officers at the state and local level to administer instructive classes to school aged children  The G R E A T  program also coordinates with community and civic groups to help with the program
