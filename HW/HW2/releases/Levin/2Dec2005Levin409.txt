    12 02 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce USDA To Purchase Up To 15 Million Pounds Tart Cherries                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 2  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce USDA To Purchase Up To 15 Million Pounds Tart Cherries                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON     Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the US Department of Agriculture  USDA  will buy up to 15 million pounds of tart cherries for donation to their Food and Nutrition Service  FNS  program   The decision signals a potentially large increase in the USDA   s purchasing of tart cherries  up from 1 6 million pounds in fiscal year 2004 and 3 5 million pounds in FY2005          This is great news for Michigan   s economy     said Levin     This will provide a nice boost to our cherry growers while also allowing the USDA to provide a tasty and healthful fruit to those in need             This cherry purchase will benefit Michigan cherry growers and will provide a healthy supplement for our nation   s nutrition programs  including school lunch programs     Stabenow said     This is a win for Michigan and a win for children and families across the country          The FNS program supports a wide variety of national food assistance initiatives  including school breakfast and lunch programs  nutrition programs for the elderly and emergency food assistance services for victims of natural disasters   The USDA   s Agriculture Marketing Service  AMS  will now publicly invite bids to provide the total amount needed  which will be determined by an FNS survey of potential recipients  Michigan is the leading producer of tart cherries in the United States  growing about 200 250 of the total annual crop of 275 350 million pounds
