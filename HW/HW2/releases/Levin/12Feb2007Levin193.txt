                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEFebruary 12  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin  Stabenow Announce Dates of  Assistance to Firefighters Grant Workshops                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that the Department of Homeland Security will offer several workshops throughout Michigan to assist fire departments and non affiliated emergency medical service agencies with the process of applying for a 2007 grant through the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   The workshops are free and no pre registration is required          Hundreds of fire departments across the state have already benefited from fire grants     Levin said      These workshops offer the opportunity to learn about the Assistance to Firefighters grant process   I encourage all interested firefighters and EMS personnel to attend one of these workshops             For years  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program has served as a valuable source of funding for first responders all over our state     Stabenow said      I encourage departments across Michigan to learn as much as they can about the 2007 program to make sure they do not miss out on this important opportunity          According to the Department of Homeland Security  there have been significant changes made to the 2007 grant program  Fire department personnel and non affiliated emergency medical service organizations are encouraged to attend a workshop to learn about changes in the grant process and for insight into this year   s grant program       More
