                                                              Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  4 Million in Transportation Grants for Alpena  Houghton  Marquette and Muskegon Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  4 330 609 to Alpena  Houghton  Marquette and Muskegon Counties  The funding will be used for airport improvements           We rely on airports for business for tourism  for jobs and to visit our friends and families     said Levin     This funding will allow these airports to improve and maintain their equipment and facilities to ensure the safety of their travelers and employees             These grants will help four Michigan airports make the improvements and acquire the equipment they need to ensure safe service in all conditions     Stabenow said     These investments in our airports are important investments in commerce  tourism and family travel in Michigan          The funds from these grants will be used for the following        County  Airport
