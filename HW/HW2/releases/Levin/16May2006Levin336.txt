    05 16 2006   Stabenow  Levin Announce  4 7 Million In Grants to Help Revitalize Brownfields Sites in SE Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 16  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin Announce  4 7 Million In Grants to Help Revitalize Brownfields Sites in SE Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Grants will help put former industrial sites to new economic and commercial use      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that  4 7 million in grants from the Environmental Protection Agency will help communities in southeast Michigan clean up and redevelop abandoned and contaminated or potentially contaminated property   The state total of EPA grants is more than  9 million          We continue to make progress in southeastern Michigan in cleaning up old industrial sites and restoring the land to new commercial  industrial or community use     Stabenow said     These grants will help these communities create jobs and increase their tax base by making local abandoned residential and commercial property available for new investment             This federal funding is a significant step to help rebuild decaying and contaminated properties across southeast Michigan     said Levin      The Brownfields program is an effective way to turn some of southeast Michigan   s neglected properties into attractive and valuable parts of our revitalization effort          The cleanup grants will fund direct cleanup activities at brownfields sites  The assessment grants will permit planning for eventual cleanup  and the revolving loan fund grants will allow for low interest loans to be made for brownfields cleanups         Grant Recipient Grant Type and Project Grant
