    02 07 2005   Statement of Sen  Carl Levin on the FY 2006 Budget                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEFebruary 7  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Sen  Carl Levin on the FY 2006 Budget                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement on the President   s FY 2006 budget request         President Bush has sent us a budget that reflects the wrong priorities for America   His FY 2006 budget would deepen the deficit ditch by making tax cuts  which go largely to the wealthiest Americans  permanent   At the same time his budget proposes cuts in many programs that affect America   s families and communities  like education  environmental protection  funding for highways and sewers  small business loans  and programs important for manufacturing states like Michigan like the Advanced Technology Partnership Program and the Manufacturing Extension Partnership Program            This budget also hides the true size of the deficit because it does not include the cost of the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan  the huge cost of the President   s plans to privatize Social Security  and the exploding cost of making the tax cuts permanent
