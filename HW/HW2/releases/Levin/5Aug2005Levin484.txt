    08 05 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Federal Transit Grants of More Than  3 5 Million for Ann Arbor and Kalamazoo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 5  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Federal Transit Grants of More Than  3 5 Million for Ann Arbor and Kalamazoo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON    Two grants from the U S  Department of Transportation totaling more than  3 5 million will allow transit authorities in Ann Arbor and Kalamazoo to upgrade vehicles and facilities and improve local service  U S  Senator Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  said today          Public transportation is a sensible choice for some people  but for others it   s an essential means of getting to work  school or a doctor   s appointment     Stabenow said     These grants will enhance public transportation service for people served by the transit authorities in Ann Arbor and Kalamazoo              Investing in public transportation and maintaining that investment is just plain common sense     said Levin      These funds will help Ann Arbor and Kalamazoo keep pace with needed improvements to their public transportation systems         A grant of  1 608 012 will assist the Ann Arbor Transportation Authority in the reconstruction and renovation of its Downtown Bus Transfer Center        A  1 952 520 grant has been awarded to Kalamazoo Metro Transit and Kalamazoo County Care A Van for the purchase of two replacement buses and the ongoing construction of the intermodal bus terminal in downtown Kalamazoo  Additional Kalamazoo funds will assist in the purchase of miscellaneous bus station equipment  communications equipment  and surveillance and security equipment
