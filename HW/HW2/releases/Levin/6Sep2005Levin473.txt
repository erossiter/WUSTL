    09 06 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Grants of  1 9 Million Each for Flint  Port Huron Public Transportation Systems                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 6  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Grants of  1 9 Million Each for Flint  Port Huron Public Transportation Systems                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that the Flint Mass Transportation Authority and the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission have each been awarded grants of  1 901 803 for construction of local bus facilities          These grants will improve public transportation facilities and therefore will improve the quality of service     Stabenow said     These improvements are important to the people served by public transit in the Flint and Port Huron area             Public transportation is vital to Flint and Port Huron     said Levin      These grants will enable both communities to make sound investments in their public transit infrastructure in order to better meet their growing needs          The Flint grant will assist in funding the rehabilitation and construction work at the Downtown Flint Transfer Center  The work includes fa  ade and internal improvements to the transfer center       The grant for Port Huron will provide funds for continued construction of the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission operations and maintenance facility
