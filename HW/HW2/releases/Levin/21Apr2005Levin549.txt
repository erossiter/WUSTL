                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 21  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  79 441 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that four fire departments in Michigan have received  79 441 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program            One of the most important roles of our firefighters happens before the fire bell ever rings   Educating the public about the importance of fire safety and prevention is an irreplaceable resource for every community throughout Michigan     Stabenow said      The four grants we are announcing today will enable Michigan   s emergency workers to continue this fight for public knowledge through fire prevention programs             Fire prevention education classes and awareness activities are essential in the battle to keep our communities safe     said Levin     This funding will help elevate public awareness and allow our firefighters to enhance prevention activities            These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  which funds three areas of support for communities  The Fire Prevention Program focuses on public education and awareness activities  fire code enforcement  fire inspection certification  and purchase and installation of smoke alarms and fire suppression systems       Funding has been provided as follows         Organization Name  Program Area  Federal Share  City    Grand Traverse Band of
