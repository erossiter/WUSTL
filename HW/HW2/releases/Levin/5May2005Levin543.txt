                                                                                                                                                     Berrien Center  Bridgeport  Chassell  Flint  Hudsonville  and L   Anse to receive grants      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that six Michigan communities have received  169 435 in grants from the Department of Homeland Security through the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  The grants will help the communities of Berrien Center  Bridgeport  Chassell  Flint  Hudsonville  and L   Anse fund fire prevention programs           In addition to putting out fires  our fire departments do much to prevent fires from ever starting     said Levin     These grants  which will fund fire awareness activities  will protect people and property from the destruction that fire can cause              These grants are critical to preventing fires and keeping our communities safe     Stabenow said     The six Fire Prevention grants we are announcing today will help these local fire departments protect their communities         The Department of Homeland Security   s Office of Domestic Preparedness in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration manages the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  which awards one year grants directly to fire departments   The fire departments agree to match a percentage of the federal grant   Fire departments that serve communities of 50 000 or fewer provide a cost share of 10 percent  fire departments for jurisdictions over 50 000 provide a 30 percent cost share       The Fire Prevention Program focuses on public education and awareness activities  fire code enforcement  fire inspection certification  and purchase and installation of smoke alarms and fire suppression systems   Funding has been provided for the fire prevention program as follows        Organization Name  Federal Share  City    Public Safety
