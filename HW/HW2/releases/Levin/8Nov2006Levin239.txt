    11 08 2006   Levin Statement on the Resignation of Secretary Donald Rumsfeld                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 8  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Resignation of Secretary Donald Rumsfeld                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             The American people spoke yesterday with great force and clarity on the need to change course in Iraq   I welcome the President   s statement today reflecting a willingness to work with those of us in Congress who have had a different view from his as to what is needed for success there   The President   s willingness to do so is long overdue       I hope Secretary Rumsfeld   s departure will lead to a change in policy   I expect that the Armed Services Committee and the Senate will give careful consideration to the forthcoming nomination of Robert Gates to be the next Secretary of Defense  and I will work with Chairman Warner to see if that can be achieved during the upcoming lame duck session
