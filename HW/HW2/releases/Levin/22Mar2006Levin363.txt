    03 22 2006   Levin Hails Decision by United Solar Ovonic  to Locate New Manufacturing Facility in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 22  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Hails Decision by United Solar Ovonic  to Locate New Manufacturing Facility in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   today hailed the decision by United Solar Ovonic LLC  a subsidiary of Energy Conversion Devices  Inc   to build a new  high tech manufacturing facility in Greenville   The facility is expected to create up to 563 jobs in the community           ECD is one of the leading U S  companies developing technologies to make the U S  less dependent on foreign oil     Levin said      I   m delighted that they are expanding their operations by building a new facility in Greenville  where they will find a highly skilled workforce and receptive business environment   I commend Governor Granholm   s leadership in forging this partnership between United Solar Ovonic  the Greenville community  and the State of Michigan
