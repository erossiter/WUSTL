                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 30  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Funding for Michigan Projects  In the Interior Department Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Keweenaw  Sleeping Bear Dunes  and water and sewer projects to receive funding      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced that the funding bill for the Department of the Interior for FY06 includes millions in funding for Michigan water and sewer infrastructure projects  as well as funding for Keweenaw National Historic Park  Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore  and the Automobile National Heritage Area   The Senate approved the bill last night  and a conference committee will next consider the bill to resolve differences between the Senate and House passed versions          This bill provides critical funding to protect our ground and surface water supplies vital for these communities     said Levin     Some of our state   s most cherished landmarks   Keweenaw National Historic Park  Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore and the Automobile National Heritage Area   will benefit greatly under this bill              This bill will help protect Michigan   s environment by helping prevent sewage overflows  and it will help preserve our rich state heritage and promote tourism     Stabenow said     Michigan residents and the many visitors to Keweenaw National Historical Park  Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore and the Automobile National Heritage Area will benefit from the improvements to those facilities          Specific appropriations of interest to Michigan include
