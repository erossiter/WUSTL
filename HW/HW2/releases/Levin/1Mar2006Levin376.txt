    03 01 2006   Levin Confronts Homeland Security Secretary   on Screening of Canadian Trash Trucks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 1  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Confronts Homeland Security Secretary   on Screening of Canadian Trash Trucks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     At a Senate hearing this morning  Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   confronted Homeland Security Secretary Michael Chertoff on the screening process used to inspect trash trucks entering the United States along the Canadian border   On a weekly basis  approximately 2 000 trash trucks cross the U S  Canada border into Michigan for disposal           We   ve got a major container security issue in Michigan that is festering     Sen  Levin  a senior member of the Homeland Security Governmental Affairs Committee  said at today   s hearing      It is a big problem   It is the municipal waste trucks that come in from Canada that cannot be adequately inspected            Last month  the Department of Homeland Security issued a blistering report  which was prepared in response to an October 2003 request by Sen  Levin  Sen  Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   and Rep  John Dingell  D Dearborn  which indicated that the current screening process is inadequate   DHS labeled the document    For Official Use Only    and requested that Congress members not share any of the findings publicly          Levin also asked Chertoff to    support language in our law which will tell Canada    sorry  we are not able to practically inspect that waste  you   re going to have to keep your waste and find a dump site for it yourselves
