    04 21 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Grant of  50 715 for the North Oakland Transportation Authority                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 21  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Grant of  50 715 for the North Oakland Transportation Authority                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  50 715 to the Suburban Mobility Authority for Regional Transportation  The grant will be used to purchase a bus and replace a van for the North Oakland Transportation Authority             Providing bus and van services to our communities is essential to senior citizens  the disabled  and people using public transportation to commute to work     said Levin     This funding will add to North Oakland Transportations Authority   s fleet  enhancing its service           Public transportation is a sensible choice for some people  but for others it   s an essential means of getting to work  school  or a doctor   s appointment     Stabenow said     This grant will help enhance public transportation service for people served by the North Oakland Transportation Authority         For more information on DOT grants  please visit http   www dot gov
