    10 25 2005   Levin Statement on the Death of Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 25  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Death of Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement regarding the death of civil rights legend Rosa Parks          Rosa Parks is a true American heroine   Her moral clarity and quiet dignity helped to shape the civil rights movement and added great power to it   Her actions challenged the conscience of our nation and the world          Although Rosa Parks will be forever associated with Montgomery  Alabama  she lived most of her life in my home state of Michigan   We have always been proud to call her one of our own    The great courage and strength of spirit that she demonstrated in Montgomery and throughout her life captured the hearts of the American people
