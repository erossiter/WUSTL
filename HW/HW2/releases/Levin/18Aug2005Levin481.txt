    08 18 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  636 000 in Funding for Crime Prevention and Control in Seven Michigan Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 18  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  636 000 in Funding for Crime Prevention and Control in Seven Michigan Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice is awarding  636 911 to communities in Michigan including  Lansing  Saginaw  Muskegon and Grand Rapids to prevent and fight crime   Each community will use the funds for a variety of crime fighting initiatives             The Justice Department grants will aid these police departments in their fight against crime     said Levin      Every community has unique issues to address  and these funds will help local law enforcement tackle their particular crime prevention and control needs             Bureau of Justice Assistance grants provide state and local law agencies with the resources they need to help prevent and solve crime  based on their own local needs and conditions     Stabenow said     These grants will further our efforts to improve the skills and equipment of our state   s crime fighters and first responders         The Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program allows local governments to prevent and control crime based on their own requirements and conditions   These funds are designated for use for programs such as law enforcement  prosecution and control  prevention and education  corrections and community corrections  drug treatment and planning  and evaluation and technology improvements        Specific grants include the following        Benton Charter Township  Purchase a digital electronic fingerprinting system    19 765    Buena Vista Charter Township  Purchase portable radios and a handheld radar system    13 067    Grand Rapids and Kent County  Support three community based crime prevention policy programs  purchase equipment  support park attendant program  increase community policing efforts  and purchase an automatic vehicle locator system    299 430    Lansing  Increase the use of technology by the court system and law enforcement through purchase of a video arraignment system for the court  laptop computers for patrol officers and mobile video recorders for traffic stops    175 459    Muskegon County  Enhance the quality of life for residents by reducing crime through aggressive prevention efforts and tough prosecution of offenders    92 167    Troy  Purchase digital cameras and accessories    11 875    Ypsilanti  Upgrade computer systems  hardware and software for its mobile field reporting system    25 148
