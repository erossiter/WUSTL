    10 05 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 6 Million for Fire Safety in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 5  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 6 Million for Fire Safety in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that 20 Michigan organizations have been awarded  2 631 352 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to purchase firefighting vehicles and to support operations and firefighter safety          This federal funding is critical for the safety of communities across Michigan     said Levin     Firefighters and first responders play an important and courageous role in protecting our homes and families  and we owe it to them to provide the funding and the resources they need             Michigan   s firefighters work tirelessly to protect our communities and our families     Stabenow said      These grants will help ensure fire departments across the state have the equipment and training they need          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of firefighting operations and firefighter health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process
