    01 24 2007   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  30 000 for Fire Safety in Millersburg                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 24  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  30 000 for Fire Safety in Millersburg                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the Ocqueoc Bearinger Fire Department in Millersburg has been awarded  31 175 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety          This is good news for those who depend on the Ocqueoc Bearinger Fire Department     said Levin     These federal dollars will help to provide the equipment and the training that is so critical to keeping our communities safe and secure             Keeping our communities safe begins with ensuring that our first responders have the resources they need     said Stabenow      I   m glad these funds will enhance the Ocqueoc Bearinger Fire Department   s ability to do their job safely and effectively          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process
