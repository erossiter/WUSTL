    01 12 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  210 949 in Firefighter Grants for Fire Departments in Manton and Marysville                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 12  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  210 949 in Firefighter Grants for Fire Departments in Manton and Marysville                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that two fire departments in the state of Michigan have received  210 949 in grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security        Stabenow and Levin said the Cedar Creek Township Fire Department in Manton has been awarded  180 000 under the terms of a firefighter vehicle acquisition program  and the Marysville Fire Department has been awarded  30 949 for fire prevention programs           This grant program helps fire departments protect residents and property in several ways     through improved training  improved equipment  and proactive prevention programs     Stabenow said     The two grants we are announcing today will enable these two fire department to enhance their firefighting efforts and better serve their communities             Investing in preventative safety measures and providing funding for fire equipment and vehicles are essential to our safety efforts     said Levin     Protecting our communities is the primary job of our fire departments and these grants will help accomplish this mission          These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  a joint effort of the Department of Homeland Security  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  and U S  Fire Administration         The vehicle acquisition program allows departments to purchase vehicles  such as tankers  rescue vehicles  foam units and ambulances  The fire prevention program includes such activities as fire code enforcement  the purchase and installation of smoke alarms and fire suppression systems  and arson prevention and detection        More information on this grant program is available at
