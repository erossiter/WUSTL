    09 07 2007   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on Passage of College Cost Reduction Act of 2007                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 7  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on Passage of College Cost Reduction Act of 2007                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement following the Senate   s passage today of the conference report of the College Cost Reduction Act of 2007 by a vote of 79 12   The bill now goes to the President for his signature into law    The passage today of the College Cost Reduction Act of 2007 is a great victory for students and families in Michigan and across America   I am pleased to support this legislation because it reflects a commitment to expanding access to higher education and making it more affordable  It opens the door to those previously denied educational opportunity due to a lack of adequate financial resources         Among other things  the bill increases the maximum Pell grant award from  4 050 to  5 100 in 2008  and will give the struggling middle class much needed relief by capping monthly student loan repayments to 15 percent of the borrower s discretionary income  The bill represents the single largest federal investment in higher education since the GI Bill  including an additional  17 billion in college aid
