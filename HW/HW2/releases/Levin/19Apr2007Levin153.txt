                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 19  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin  Fire Prevention Grants Awarded to Children   s Hospital  Wayne County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that Children   s Hospital of Michigan will receive  43 847 and the Wayne County Airport Authority will receive  13 000 for fire prevention and safety programs   The funds are awarded under the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP           Community safety and fire prevention begins with education and outreach     said Stabenow     I   m pleased this funding will provide Children   s Hospital and Wayne County Airport the resources needed to prevent fire related emergencies              These federal dollars will help to keep Detroiters safe from the devastating consequences of fire and other dangers     said Levin     Educating the public about potential hazards is an important step in protecting our homes and communities          The primary goal of the AFGP Fire Prevention and Safety Program  administered by the Federal Emergency Management Agency in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration  is to mitigate or prevent the danger to the public and first responders posed by fire and other hazards   Projects supported by the program include fire prevention and public safety education campaigns  media outreach  and arson prevention and awareness programs
