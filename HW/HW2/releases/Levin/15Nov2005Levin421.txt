                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 15  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin  Federal Funds Will Aid St  Francis Hospital                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Funding included will allow Escanaba   s hospital to enlarge emergency room  relocate Urgent Care Clinic      WASHINGTON     Legislation approved by the U S  Senate and sent to the president for his signature authorizes  250 000 for St  Francis Hospital in Escanaba  U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  said today  The funds will help St  Francis Hospital make improvements to its emergency room and urgent care facilities          St  Francis Hospital has been vital part of the Escanaba community for more than 100 years and is the only source of emergency room care for Delta County     Stabenow said      These federal funds will allow St  Francis to make critical improvements to their emergency department and ensure that the hospital can continue to provide the very best care to its patients             This is great news for those who depend on St  Francis Hospital     said Levin      The emergency care provided for so many years by St  Francis is enormously important in and around Escanaba  and these federal dollars should go a long way towards keeping those facilities running smoothly          The senators noted that the emergency room at St  Francis Hospital was built to serve 10 000 patients per year  but now cares for more than 17 000 annually  The planned improvements will enlarge the emergency room and relocate the Urgent Care Clinic so they are housed within the same space  providing patients with a single point of entry and improved service  In addition  the project will provide for isolation rooms and decontamination facilities to ensure full St  Francis Hospital is able to handle a toxic chemical exposure or terrorist incident         The funding was included in the fiscal year 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill
