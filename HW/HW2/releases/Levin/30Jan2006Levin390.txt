    01 30 2006   Statement of Senator Levin on the Cloture Vote on Judge Samuel Alito                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 30  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Levin on the Cloture Vote on Judge Samuel Alito                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        While I had expected that the Senate would move directly to an up or down vote on Judge Alito   s nomination to the Supreme Court without a vote on cloture  because I strongly oppose this nomination as I explained in my remarks last week and because the filibuster has been a time honored and accepted part of the checks and balances on the President   s appointment powers  I will vote against cloture on this nomination
