    03 03 2006   Levin Statement on the Death of Dick Vander Veen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 3  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Death of Dick Vander Veen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made this statement following the death of former Michigan Congressman Dick Vander Veen           Dick Vander Veen served the people of western Michigan with civility  decency  and integrity   His kindness and willingness to listen to others were exactly what the country needed when he was elected to Congress in 1974 and remain an example to all of us in public life today   For one brief moment during the 1978 Democratic primary  Dick and I were political rivals  but our friendship was strengthened by the experience   With his passing  I lost a great friend and Michigan lost a great public servant
