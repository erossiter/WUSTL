                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 6  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  510 185 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that seven fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  510 185 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Programs            Communities throughout Michigan rely on local firefighters to protect them when emergencies arise     Stabenow said     These seven FEMA grants will help local fire departments respond to the emergencies and hazards that threaten the safety of our local communities             Each day Michigan   s firefighters go to work prepared to risk their lives to protect us     said Levin     We owe it to them to provide first class equipment and training  This funding will strengthen each department   s ability to respond to emergencies while minimizing the risks to the men and women who work so hard to keep us out of harm   s way          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities   The Vehicles Acquisition Program allows departments to purchase vehicles such as pumpers  brush trucks  rescue vehicles  ambulances and fireboats          The following communities were awarded grants under terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program        Organization Name  Program Area  Federal Share
