                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 22  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Burials to Begin at Metro Detroit   s Veterans    Cemetery on October 17th  Levin Announces                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   announced today that the Veterans    Administration will raise the flag at the new Great Lakes National Cemetery on October 15th and burials will begin on the 17th   The cemetery  which is located in Holly  Michigan  is the 122nd national veterans    cemetery          Michigan is home to almost one million veterans    and over half of them live in the Detroit area    but we have never had a cemetery in metro Detroit dedicated to the heroic men and women of our armed forces     Levin said      Construction of the cemetery has been a long road  but I am pleased that it is nearing completion and that burials will begin next month   Many families have been frustrated as the remains of their loved ones have been held in temporary facilities throughout the construction delays   The new cemetery will provide a beautiful  serene place for our veterans to be put to rest          On the 15th  the VA will hold a ceremony to mark the formal opening    Families and funeral homes wishing to arrange burials can contact the cemetery at 866 348 8603 beginning on September 26         Levin helped lead Senate passage of a bill establishing the cemetery in 1999  and he subsequently has helped to win funding for the project    Earlier this month  the VA released the contractor of the project after Levin raised concerns about repeated construction delays in an August 25  2005  letter to VA Secretary R  James Nicholson   VA employees are completing construction on the project
