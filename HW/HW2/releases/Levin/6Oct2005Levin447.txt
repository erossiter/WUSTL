    10 06 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 5 Million in Homeland Security Preparedness Grants for MSU and Eastern                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 6  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 5 Million in Homeland Security Preparedness Grants for MSU and Eastern                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that two Michigan universities have received grants totaling  2 508 093 as part of the Department of Homeland Security Competitive Training Grant program  which is aimed at preparing the nation to prevent  respond to  and recover from incidents of terrorism and natural disasters   Michigan State University will receive  1 539 461 and Eastern Michigan University  968 632 for training initiatives          This funding is a critical part of the push to improve our disaster readiness     said Levin      Both Michigan State and Eastern Michigan are at the cutting edge in this field and it is great to see their efforts being rewarded   These programs will provide the knowledge and training necessary to prepare citizens and first responders for a wide variety of potential situations             These grants will support important homeland security training  especially on our open and potentially vulnerable college campuses     Stabenow said     These grants are good news for Michigan State University and Eastern Michigan University             The FY2005 Competitive Training grant program selected 15 organizations from a pool of 267 applicants for a total of  30 million in funding   Michigan State will develop Campus Community Response Teams and Citizen Corps Councils to improve preparedness in and around colleges and universities in the Lansing area   Eastern Michigan will provide Teen Student Emergency Response Training to help students with emergency decision making skills
