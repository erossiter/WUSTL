    05 23 2006   EPW Committee Approves DeWine Levin Great Lakes Bills                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 23  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      EPW Committee Approves DeWine Levin Great Lakes Bills                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON     The Senate Environment and Public Works Committee today approved two bills by Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   and Sen  Mike DeWine  R Ohio  that would support fish and wildlife programs and restoration in the Great Lakes  the Great Lakes Fish and Wildlife Restoration Act of 2006 and Great Lakes Coordination and Oversight Act           The Great Lakes are a unique American treasure  but we must recognize that we are only their temporary stewards     Levin said      Congress must act to keep pace with the needs of the lakes in order to protect and restore them for future generations   These bills help us in that mission by supporting fish and wildlife in the lakes and by coordinating oversight of the lakes    management           The Great Lakes Fish and Wildlife Restoration Act was enacted in 1990 and in 1998  and the DeWine Levin bill would reauthorize this critical program   The program authorizes a grant program to restore fish and wildlife in the lakes  authorizes up to  6 million for newly created fish and wildlife regional projects  requires the Fish and Wildlife Service to provide information on line for fish and wildlife managers  calls for several reports on fish and wildlife in the lakes  and maintains the Fish and Wildlife Services Office  The authorization of appropriations for this bill is increased from  8 million to  20 million per year        The Great Lakes Coordination and Oversight Act would codify the Great Lakes Executive Order and establish Great Lakes Interagency Task Force and Great Lakes Regional Collaboration  This legislation will ensure the on going partnership of states  cities  tribes  and other groups will continue prioritizing the restoration needs of the lakes         Both bills now go to the floor for full Senate consideration        Other sponsors of the Great Lakes Fish and Wildlife Restoration Act include Senators Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   and George Voinovich  R Ohio   Representative Dale Kildee introduced a House companion bill   Other sponsors of the Great Lakes Coordination and Oversight Act include Stabenow  Voinovich  and Sen  Russ Feingold  D Wisc
