                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 8  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  41 Million in Department of Transportation Grants for Several Michigan Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  41 395 659 in grants benefiting several Michigan airports   The DOT grants will be used for safety improvements and development projects          As millions of people fly into and out of Michigan   s airports every year  we must continually update and improve facilities     said Levin     The funding will help ensure the continued safety of our air travelers and will support additional improvements to our airports              Like all our other transportation infrastructure  our airport facilities need constant improvement and added security     Stabenow said     This funding will improve travel for Michigan residents and visitors  and it will aid state business and commerce   building jobs and making the state an excellent place to live and work          The funds from these grants will be used in part for the following        Airport  Grant Recipient  Purpose  Amount
