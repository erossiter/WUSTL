    05 14 2007   Levin Statement on Levin Reid Iraq Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 14  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on Levin Reid Iraq Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   said the following today regarding the   PDF  that was offered to the Water Resources Development Act           The amendment to the Water Resources Development Act that was introduced on my behalf this afternoon  is the second best legislation relating to the presence of U S  Armed Forces in Iraq   the original language contained in the Emergency Supplemental bill that the President vetoed was our preferred way to address this issue                      The amendment I am offering today would build upon the provisions in the vetoed bill by providing consequences for the Government of Iraq   s failure to make substantial progress towards meeting the political benchmarks that they had agreed upon among themselves  by providing for an acceleration of the reduction of U S  Armed Forces in Iraq   This would be in addition to the economic consequences already provided for in the vetoed bill   In order to avoid a veto  however  the amendment provides for a waiver by the President of the reduction of U S  forces   By providing for the Presidential waiver  we are removing any reason for the President to veto the supplemental funding bill
