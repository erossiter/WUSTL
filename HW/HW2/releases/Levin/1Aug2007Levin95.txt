                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that five Michigan organizations have been awarded  402 360 through the fiscal year 2007 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety  public safety education and awareness  and to purchase firefighting vehicles          Protecting our communities from fires and other emergencies starts with public education and public awareness     said Stabenow     I   m grateful this funding will also provide our first responders with the equipment needed to keep our families and communities safe             This funding is critical to improving our readiness and protecting families and communities in Michigan     said Levin      These programs will help provide the knowledge and training necessary to prepare citizens and first responders for a wide variety of potential situations          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  to purchase protective equipment and response vehicles  to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States and for public safety education  Grants are awarded competitively through an application process  More information can be found at        Individual grant awards can be
