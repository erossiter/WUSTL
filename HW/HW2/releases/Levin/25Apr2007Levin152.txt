    04 25 2007   Levin Statement on the EPA Decision Regarding PCB Contaminated Sediments from the Plainwell Impoundment Cleanup Site                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 25  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the EPA Decision Regarding PCB Contaminated Sediments from the Plainwell Impoundment Cleanup Site                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement about the EPA   s decision not to dump PCB contaminated sediments in Kalamazoo     I am pleased that the EPA has decided not to dump PCB contaminated sediment at the Allied Paper site in Kalamazoo   I hope the EPA has learned a lesson and will involve the community and local leaders in its future deliberations about matters so seriously impacting local communities and neighborhoods
