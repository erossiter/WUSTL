    10 03 2007   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  300 000 for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 3  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  300 000 for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that three Michigan fire departments have been awarded  301 789 through the Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety and to purchase firefighting vehicles          Directing resources to our firefighters and first responders is an important way to ensure the safety and security of communities across Michigan     said Levin     Better training and equipment will help these brave men and women do their jobs effectively           Michigan communities depend on their first responders when emergencies arise     said Stabenow      These brave men and women deserve only the best possible equipment and training available as they face the challenge of keeping our communities safe          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process   More information can be found at
