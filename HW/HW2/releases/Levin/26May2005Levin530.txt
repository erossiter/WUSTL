    05 26 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Grant for the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission to Build New Facility                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 26  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Grant for the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission to Build New Facility                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  2 468 723 to the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission in Port Huron  The funding will be used for a new operations and maintenance facility            A new and larger maintenance facility will enable the Blue Water Area Transportation Commission to address its growing needs     said Levin     Public transportation is vital to the community and I am glad we are investing in such an important service              The Blue Water Area Transportation Commission continues to make great improvements to its facilities and its service     Stabenow said     This is an important investment for public transportation in Port Huron and St  Clair County  and an important step forward for public transportation in the area          A larger operations and maintenance facility is needed to replace the existing facility in the City of Port Huron  The grant will help fund the acquisition of land and the design  engineering and construction of the new facility  The future facility will be approximately 115 000 square feet and will be located about 2 2 miles from the current site        This grant has been provided from fiscal year 2002 and 2003 earmarks  For more information on DOT grants  please visit http   www dot gov
