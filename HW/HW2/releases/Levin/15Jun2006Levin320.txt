    06 15 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Request  2 6 Million for Emerald Ash Borer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 15  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Request  2 6 Million for Emerald Ash Borer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  today  the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on the Interior to include  2 6 million in its fiscal year 2007 budget for emerald ash borer  EAB  research and technology development   To date  EAB has destroyed 16 million trees and resulted in approximately  11 million in lost jobs and revenues in Michigan           An emerald ash borer infestation is currently plaguing Michigan  Indiana and Ohio  and has just been identified in Illinois     the Senators wrote to Sens  Conrad Burns  R MT  and Byron Dorgan  D ND   the chairman and ranking member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on the Interior      To date  federal support for combating EAB has focused on eradication and limiting the spread of EAB from affected states through the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service  APHIS  at the Department of Agriculture   Unfortunately  EAB activities at APHIS have yet to be adequately funded to control the pest  and it has continued to spread across Michigan  Indiana  Ohio and Illinois           The funding would assist the Northern Research Station of the U S  Forest Service  which is researching technology to control EAB             The research station believes that with a total investment of  2 6 million  substantial progress can be made on identifying techniques to suppress the EAB and slow its spread     the Senators wrote        Joining Levin and Stabenow in requesting the funding were Sens  Mike DeWine  R OH   George Voinovich  R OH   Richard Lugar  R IN   Barak Obama  D IL   Dick Durbin  D IL   and Evan Bayh  D IN
