    04 22 2005   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Nominations for Chairman and Vice Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 22  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Nominations for Chairman and Vice Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement today on the nomination of General Peter Pace  USMC  and Admiral Edmund P  Giambastiani  Jr   USN  to be chairman and vice chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff          President Bush and Secretary Rumsfeld have made outstanding choices in naming General Pace and Admiral Giambastiani to be chairman and vice chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff   They are both experienced and thoughtful military officers  and I   m confident they will meet the challenges of leading and transforming our armed forces to protect our nation   s interests and meet the threats of the future
