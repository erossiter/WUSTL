    05 24 2005   Statement of Senators Levin and Stabenow Regarding Michigan Nominations to the Sixth Circuit Court of Appeals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 24  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senators Levin and Stabenow Regarding Michigan Nominations to the Sixth Circuit Court of Appeals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  made the following statement today regarding the nominations of Michigan judges pending for the Sixth Circuit Court of Appeals          We supported Senator Reid when he proposed that some Michigan judges be confirmed  and we support the agreement reached by the 14 Senators last night relative to the Michigan judges            We returned blue slips supporting Judge David McKeague and Judge Richard Griffin to the Judiciary Committee today  and we will set up a very prompt review process for Judge Susan Neilson
