    12 08 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  420 519 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 8  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  420 519 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that six fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  420 519 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program          These federal dollars will help to keep Michigan   s communities safe and secure     said Levin      This is a critical part of the ongoing effort to keep our first responders prepared and well equipped as they confront a wide variety of threats             Communities throughout Michigan rely on local firefighters to protect them when emergencies arise     Stabenow said     These six grants will help local fire departments respond to the emergencies and hazards that threaten the safety of our local communities          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities      The following communities were awarded grants under
