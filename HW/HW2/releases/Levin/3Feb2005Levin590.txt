    02 03 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  454 698 in Firefighter Grants for Seven Michigan Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEFebruary 3  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  454 698 in Firefighter Grants for Seven Michigan Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security has awarded  454 698 in grants to seven Michigan fire departments under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program            Fitness and safety training  new vehicles  and improved personal protective equipment are among the enhancements to local firefighting units supported by these grants     Stabenow said     Michigan firefighters put their lives on the line every day to protect people and property in their communities  and we must ensure that they have the best equipment and training available              Through this funding  our fire departments will be able to update equipment  provide necessary training and offer public education on fire prevention     said Levin     This grant program helps to ensure that our firefighters  who risk their lives to protect our communities  have the necessary tools and resources to do their jobs          These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  a joint effort of the Department of Homeland Security  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  and U S  Fire Administration   The Operations and Firefighter Safety programs provide firefighters with equipment  training and modifications to facilities  The Fire Prevention program includes such activities as fire code enforcement  the purchase and installation of smoke alarms and fire suppression systems  and arson prevention and detection   The Firefighting Vehicle acquisition program allows departments to purchase vehicles  such as tankers  rescue vehicles  foam units and ambulances        The grants were awarded as follows        Grant Recipient  Community  Amount  Program Area    Saline Area Fire
