    06 08 2007   Lawmakers Urge Agriculture Secretary to Take Steps to Prevent the Spread of VHS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 8  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Lawmakers Urge Agriculture Secretary to Take Steps to Prevent the Spread of VHS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senator Carl Levin  D MI   co chair of the Senate Great Lakes Task Force  today released a   PDF  from a bipartisan group of 30 lawmakers urging Agriculture Secretary Mike Johanns to move forward with a final rule to prevent the spread of viral hemorrhagic septicemia  VHS  in the Great Lakes basin   VHS has been found in Michigan   s inland waters and stopping the spread is critical          We agree that the appropriate steps need to be taken to prevent the spread of such a serious disease  and we are writing to find out what progress has been made to implement a final rule     the lawmakers wrote      We are very concerned by the progress in moving forward with an interim and final rule which should have been in place this spring when the fishing season began   We therefore urge you to move forward expeditiously          Read the text of the    PDF
