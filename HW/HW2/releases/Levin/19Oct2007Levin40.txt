    10 19 2007   Levin  Stabenow Announce  460 000 to Benefit Homeless Michigan Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 19  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin  Stabenow Announce  460 000 to Benefit Homeless Michigan Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs is awarding  259 265 to Volunteers of America of Michigan in Lansing and  201 501 to Michigan Ability Partners in Ann Arbor to provide reliable housing and medical care to homeless veterans and help them achieve greater self determination   The funding comes as part of the Health Care for Homeless Veterans  HCHM  grant program          Our veterans have fought to protect the safety and the freedom that we enjoy every day  and we have a responsibility to provide them with adequate housing and medical care     said Levin      I am hopeful that these federal dollars will honor their service and their sacrifice by helping those Michigan veterans who have fallen on hard times to get back on their feet again           I m so pleased that this funding will help our veterans facing the challenges of homelessness  mental illness and addiction   Stabenow said   These brave men and women served our state and our country proudly  and they deserve our support        The HCHM grant program funds community agencies that provide services to homeless veterans   The goal is to develop and provide supportive housing and services to help homeless veterans achieve residential stability  build skills and income  and achieve a greater level of independence and self determination       For this year  VA has awarded 46 grants totaling more than  16 million to create 950 beds for homeless veterans nationwide
