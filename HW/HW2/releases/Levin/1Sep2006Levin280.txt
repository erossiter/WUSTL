    09 01 2006   Statement on Canadian Trash Agreement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 1  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement on Canadian Trash Agreement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made this statement regarding the agreement he and Sen  Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   made with Canadian officials to reduce the trash that Ontario currently dumps in Michigan        I am pleased that under the agreement we announced yesterday  2 78 million tons of Ontario s garbage that would have been shipped to Michigan between now and 2010 will now not come into our state         I am also pleased that after 2010 no shipments of garbage collected by the municipalities of Ontario will be shipped into our state  amounting to stopping 1 34 million tons of municipally managed solid waste from coming into Michigan        I also look forward to working with colleagues to address the non municipally managed waste coming into Michigan  which amounts to 2 2 million tons per year         This agreement represents the first   and so far only    successful reduction in Canadian garbage shipped into our state  and for that reason I believe the agreement is in the best interests of the people of Michigan
