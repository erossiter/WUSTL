    12 07 2005   Levin Statement on Secretary Rice   s Remarks Regarding the Convention Against Torture                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 7  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on Secretary Rice   s Remarks Regarding the Convention Against Torture                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   said the following today      Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice   s comments this morning     that the United States    responsibilities under the Convention Against Torture  which prohibits cruel  inhuman and degrading treatment  extend to U S  personnel wherever they are  whether they are in the United States or outside of the United States     represent a reversal from the administration   s position on the convention up to now  It is an important and very welcome change from their previous position  which I believe has cost us dearly in the world and does not reflect our nation   s laws or our values  I also believe that the administration   s position on this matter up to now has endangered our troops  because others might point to our practices to justify their own   This is the heart of the debate over Senator McCain s amendment  and I am glad the administration finally realizes that Senator McCain is right
