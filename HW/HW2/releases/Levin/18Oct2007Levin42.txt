    10 18 2007   Levin and Bipartisan Group of Senate Colleagues Call for Energy Conference to Address CAFE Concerns                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 18  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin and Bipartisan Group of Senate Colleagues Call for Energy Conference to Address CAFE Concerns                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   and a bipartisan group of Senate colleagues today  to Senate leaders and the chairman and ranking member of the Senate Commerce Committee to call for a House Senate conference on the energy bill and to highlight four issues related to fuel economy standards that would need to be satisfactorily resolved in the conference          We agree that CAFE standards must be increased and support aggressive yet achievable new standards  and we are wiling to work with you to find a compromise that accomplishes this goal     the senators wrote      While we support efforts to go to conference  for the same reasons we opposed the Senate provisions on CAFE  we would strongly object to a conference report that adopted these provisions because they would have a needlessly detrimental effect on the auto industry and its workers
