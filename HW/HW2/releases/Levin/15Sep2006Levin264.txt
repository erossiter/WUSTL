    09 15 2006   Levin Reaction to U S  Trade Representative Moving Forward in Case Against Illegal Chinese Tax on Auto Parts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 15  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Reaction to U S  Trade Representative Moving Forward in Case Against Illegal Chinese Tax on Auto Parts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement in reaction to United States Trade Representative Susan Schwab   s decision  along with the European Union and Canada  to request that a World Trade Organization  WTO  panel be established to rule on a dispute over Chinese tax regulations that discriminate against imported auto parts          This administration has been dragging its feet for years when it comes to using international trade laws to fight for a two way street in trade   This long overdue step will hopefully end some of the discriminatory treatment of American auto parts by China   By vigorously pressing our case against these and other egregious violations of the very WTO rules that China has agreed to abide by  we can hopefully help force an end to the one way street in trade with China
