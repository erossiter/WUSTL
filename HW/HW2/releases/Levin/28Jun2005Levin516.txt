    06 28 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Urge Base Closure Commission to Visit Battle Creek                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 28  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                              Senators Levin  Stabenow Urge Base Closure Commission to Visit Battle Creek                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  wrote a  to the Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission today  urging them to visit the Battle Creek Air National Guard Base and the 110th Fighter Wing  which are slated for closure and realignment           The Battle Creek Air National Guard Base is one of the most modern and capable Air National Guard bases in the United States     the Senators wrote      We would ask that you carefully examine the military value  readiness and cost effectiveness of the Pentagon   s recommendation to realign the 110th Fighter Wing  and suggest that a visit to Battle Creek this summer would be critical to a full appreciation of the value the base and wing provide to the U S  military           In May  the Secretary of Defense submitted a list of proposed base closures and realignments to the BRAC Commission   The commission conducts a thorough review of the list     including holding hearings  visiting sites  and receiving community feedback     before submitting a final list to the President and then the Congress in September        Senator Levin and representatives from the Battle Creek area testified at a regional BRAC hearing in St  Louis on June 20th  but the commission has not scheduled a visit to the Battle Creek facilities   The Senators believe that any site considered a    major closure    by the Pentagon     and Battle Creek was one of the 33 sites on that list     should be granted a site visit to have the opportunity to make the case for the facility
