    09 21 2006   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on  Compromise on Military Tribunal Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 21  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on  Compromise on Military Tribunal Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made this statement following the announcement that Sens  John Warner  R Va   John McCain  R Ariz   and Lindsey Graham  R S C   have reached an agreement with the Administration on legislation governing military tribunals        Senators Warner  McCain and Graham have stated persuasively that it would not be in the interest of the United States  and could endanger our troops in the field  if we were to enact legislation that undermines our commitment to the Geneva Conventions  provides for secret trials  or permits the use of testimony obtained through coercive procedures   I will be reviewing the compromise that they have reached with the Administration in hopes that it meets that test           I understand that the agreement does not address bipartisan concerns about the habeas corpus provisions in the bill   This issue will have to be addressed when the bill comes to the Senate floor
