    06 21 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 4 Million for Grand Rapids  Marquette Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 21  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 4 Million for Grand Rapids  Marquette Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  today announced that the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs has awarded  1 482 332 to the State of Michigan for upgrades and renovations to State Veterans Homes in Grand Rapids and Marquette          It is critically important that we honor our veterans    service whenever possible  and this funding is a step in the right direction     said Levin     These federal dollars will help to ensure that veterans facilities in Michigan are the best and most up to date we can provide             Just as we support our troops overseas with the equipment they need  we must do everything we can to meet the needs of the one million veterans in Michigan     Stabenow said     This funding for the Grand Rapids and Marquette facilities helps us fulfill our promise to those who have served our nation in the military          The  786 274 Grand Rapids grant will help to fund Life Safety Code renovations at the State Veterans Home   In Marquette  the  696 058 in federal assistance will be put towards Life Safety Emergency Generator and Fire Safety upgrades   Both grants will fulfill 65 percent of the anticipated cost of the work  with the remainder guaranteed by the state
