    08 03 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Funding for Charter Township of Plymouth to Hire Two Police Officers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 3  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Funding for Charter Township of Plymouth to Hire Two Police Officers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  150 000 to the Charter Township of Plymouth to hire two police officers  The grants were awarded under the terms of the Universal Hiring Program of Community Oriented Policing Services  commonly called COPS           Officers added under the COPS program have put police back on neighborhood patrols  allowed communities to develop new strategies to prevent crime  and enhanced local response to homeland security concerns     Stabenow said     These new officers will be a great asset to Plymouth Township             One of the greatest weapons against crime is a police officer who walks the beat  knows the neighborhood and works with the community     Levin said     With this grant  Plymouth Township will now have two more officers protecting their community          The COPS Universal Hiring Program is one of several programs intended to increase the number of officers on the beat  The program provides up to 75 percent of the approved entry level salary and benefits of each newly hired additional officer for three years  up to a maximum of  75 000        Since 1995  COPS has funded the hiring of more than 118 000 community policing officers through grants that have been awarded to over 13 000 of the nation   s estimated 18 000 state and local law enforcement agencies
