    05 18 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  6 Million for Gerald R  Ford International Airport and Over  1 Million for Delta County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 18  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  6 Million for Gerald R  Ford International Airport and Over  1 Million for Delta County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  6 281 400 to Kent County for the Gerald R  Ford International Airport and  1 045 000 to the County of Delta for the Delta County Airport  The funding will be used for rehabilitations projects           Hundreds of planes land and take off in Michigan airports everyday and passenger and crew safety is essential     said Levin     This funding will help with the needed maintenance of runways  taxiways and aprons at these airports              Maintaining and improving our airport infrastructure is vital for safe and efficient recreational and business travel in Michigan     Stabenow said     In addition  because these airport improvements in Grand Rapids and Escanaba also aid commerce and tourism  these grants also play an important role in supporting Michigan jobs and improving Michigan   s economy          At Gerald R  Ford International Airport  the federal funds will be used to rehabilitate the east and west general aviation apron areas and associated taxiways  They will also be used to construct a taxiway connector to the east general aviation apron and construct an airport service road        At Delta County Airport  the funds will be used to rehabilitate taxiway C and the terminal apron        For more information on DOT grants  please visit
