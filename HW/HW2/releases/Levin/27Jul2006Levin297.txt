    07 27 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 Million for Michigan Colleges and Universities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJuly 27  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  2 Million for Michigan Colleges and Universities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that U S  Department of Education has awarded  2 188 533 to seven colleges and universities in Michigan as part of the TRIO Talent Search Program  aimed at assisting individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds towards success in higher education          The TRIO program provides resources for many in Michigan who are eager for the opportunity to succeed     said Levin     Opening the door to higher education for each and every student in Michigan is a very important goal  and this funding is a solid step in that direction             Every young person in Michigan deserves access to a high quality education  and this funding will provide low income students the opportunity to succeed through college and beyond     said Stabenow      Investing in education is essential for Michigan   s economy  for our families and for our future          The TRIO program provides academic  career and financial counseling to participants identified as having the potential to succeed in higher education   The goal of the program is to increase the number of students from disadvantaged backgrounds who are able to complete high school and enroll in the postsecondary education institution of their choice   The program includes tutoring services  assistance with college entrance exams and applications  and workshops for the families of participants       The individual grants are outlined below        Recipient  Town    Amount    Grand Valley State University  Allendale  317  960    Alpena Community College  Alpena  314  365
