    05 18 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce a  35 000 Fire Prevention Grant for Detroit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMay 18  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce a  35 000 Fire Prevention Grant for Detroit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that the Detroit Fire Department has received a  35 000 grant under the terms of the fiscal year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program            Effective fire prevention includes a variety of activities  including public education and awareness  fire code enforcement  and fire inspections     Stabenow said      The fire prevention initiative supported by this FEMA grant will help the Detroit Fire Department keep neighborhoods safe and secure              Prevention is the best way to protect our homes and communities against fire disasters     said Levin     These funds will help the Detroit Fire Department provide important fire prevention programs throughout the city           This grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  which funds the Fire Prevention Program        Information on the assistance to firefighters grant program is available at   http   www firegrantsupport com
