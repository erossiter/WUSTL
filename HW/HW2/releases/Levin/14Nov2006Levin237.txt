    11 14 2006   Stabenow  Levin Announce Over  200 000 for Flint Area Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 14  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin Announce Over  200 000 for Flint Area Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that Flint Odyssey House  Inc will receive  228 000 for the renovation of a building in Flint  The renovation will create transitional housing that will provide 40 beds for homeless veterans   The funds were awarded through a grant from the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs  VA           We can never forget the men and women that devoted themselves to protecting our nation     said Stabenow     This grant will provide the Flint area with valuable resources to help those Veterans most in need             We all owe our veterans an enormous debt for their service to this country     said Levin     It is important that we do everything we can for their health and well being  and I   m pleased that these federal dollars will provide necessary resources for veterans in Flint and throughout Michigan          As a result of this grant program  approximately  11 56 million will be awarded to 52 programs in 27 states and Puerto Rico   Funds will support a total of 811 beds and the purchase of eighteen vans for outreach to or transportation of homeless veterans   Programs awarded funding will be monitored with VA inspections and reviews of the services provided
