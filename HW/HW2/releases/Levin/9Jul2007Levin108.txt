    07 09 2007   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Confirmation of Janet Neff  Robert Jonker and Paul Maloney to the Federal Bench of the Western District of Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJuly 9  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Confirmation of Janet Neff  Robert Jonker and Paul Maloney to the Federal Bench of the Western District of Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  I am pleased that the long road to confirmation for the three nominees for the federal bench in the Western District of Michigan  Janet Neff  Robert Jonker  and  Paul Maloney  is finally apparently near an end   Senator Stabenow and I worked with the White House on these nominations and last year they were unanimously reported out of the Judiciary Committee   However  confirmation of the nominations has been blocked since November        The sticking point of the Senator who objected was one of the nominee   s personal attendance at the same sex commitment ceremony of a family friend  who was a next door neighbor for 26 years   When Judge Neff was asked to deliver remarks  Judge Neff felt that it was like being asked by one of her own daughters to be part of an important event in their lives   The ceremony was entirely private  took place in Massachusetts where Judge Neff had no official capacity and the ceremony had no legal effect         Judge Neff graduated with honors from the University of Pittsburg in 1967  then graduated from Wayne State University Law School in 1970   She has had a distinguished legal career   After law school  Judge Neff served as an estate and gift tax examiner for the Internal Revenue Service and then as a research attorney for the Michigan Court of Appeals  before becoming an assistant city attorney for the City of Grand Rapids   Judge Neff has also worked in private practice  served as a commissioner for the Michigan Supreme Court and then as an assistant United States attorney   Judge Neff currently serves on the Michigan Court of Appeals   She has been granted numerous awards and honors  including the Outstanding Member for 2006 of the Women Lawyers Association of Michigan       We are fortunate to have the opportunity today to confirm Judge Neff  along with two other qualified nominees  Robert Jonker and Paul Maloney
