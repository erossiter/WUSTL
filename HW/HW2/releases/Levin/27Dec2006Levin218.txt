    12 27 2006   Levin Statement on the Passing of President Gerald R  Ford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 27  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Passing of President Gerald R  Ford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made this statement following the death of President Gerald R  Ford          Gerald Ford came to be president at a time of turmoil  when we needed a healer who would unify this nation   He rose to the occasion  and for that indispensable service  Gerald Ford will always have the gratitude and reverence of the American people          Most Americans will remember President Ford for his calm and steady leadership that ended one of the most serious domestic crises in our history   But we in Michigan hold Jerry Ford in affection and esteem for his lifetime of service  including 13 terms in the House of Representatives   We take particular pride in this son of Michigan and the manner in which he always treasured his West Michigan roots          Gerald Ford was deeply respected on both sides of the political aisle for his fairness  his bipartisanship  and his integrity   He helped smooth out the rough edges of politics   Over time  we will honor his memory in many ways  but one immediate way is to return the Gerald Ford quality of civility to the nation   s capital          Our thoughts and prayers are with his beloved wife Betty and his family as we celebrate the life and legacy of Gerald Ford
