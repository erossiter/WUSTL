    08 24 2006   Stabenow  Levin  Over  1 Million Coming to Lansing   s Capital City Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 24  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin  Over  1 Million Coming to Lansing   s Capital City Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senators have secured  7 million for the runway extension project over the past 2 years      WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that Lansing   s Capital City Airport will receive  1 268 717 for a runway extension project  The funds  awarded through the U S  Department of Transportation  are the second and final installment of the  3 million FY2006 appropriation secured by Stabenow and Levin last year           The Capital City Airport plays a critical role in Michigan   s economy  bringing business travelers and tourists  as well as cargo and products  to our state everyday     said Stabenow     The runway expansion will greatly enhance the airports capabilities  while creating and maintaining good jobs in Michigan             By expanding and modernizing airport facilities  we help airport operations to run smoothly so passengers and cargo can get to their destinations on time     Levin said     This expansion will benefit Michigan businesses  Michigan tourists  and Michigan government agencies  all of whom are served by Capital City Airport             This funding is essential to lengthen the primary runway at Lansing   s Capital City Airport     said Bob Selig  Executive Director of the Capital Region Airport Authority     The longer runway is a critical component of the Airport Authority   s development program  enabling the Airport to be serviced by larger and longer range cargo and passenger aircraft  The Airport Authority is very appreciative of the senators continued support of these development efforts          Earlier this month  Stabenow and Levin announced an additional  4 million for the project  which has been approved by the Senate Appropriations Committee  to be included in the Transportation  Treasury  Housing and Urban Development  T THUD  FY 2007 Appropriations bill  The Senate will vote on the final T THUD FY 2007 Appropriations bill this fall
