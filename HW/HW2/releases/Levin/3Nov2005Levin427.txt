                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 3  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  336 786 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that seven fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  336 786 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Programs          It   s important that we continue to strengthen our firefighter agencies     through training  new equipment  and additional trained personnel to tap in an emergency     Stabenow said     I will continue to work closely with our firefighters to help them obtain the manpower and hardware they need to respond to all threats and hazards             The firefighter grants program is invaluable to Michigan   s first responders     said Levin      This federal funding will help provide these fire departments with the best equipment and training available   It will also help to ensure that they are fully staffed in their efforts to safeguard Michigan   s communities          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities  The Recruitment and Retention of Volunteer Firefighters grants are intended to increase the number of competent firefighters who are trained  certified  and capable of responding to emergencies likely to occur within the fire department   s geographic area       The following communities
