                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 27  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  1 453 587 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that 18 fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  1 453 587 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Programs            This grant program helps fire departments protect residents and property through improved training and improved equipment     Stabenow said     The grants we are announcing today will enable these 18 fire departments to enhance their firefighting efforts and better serve their communities             In providing our first responders with the best training and equipment  we are investing in the safety and security of Michigan s communities     said Levin      It is especially important that fire departments have dependable vehicles and these grants will supply the federal dollars to make sure that need is met          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities  The Vehicles Acquisition Program allows departments to purchase vehicles such as pumpers  brush trucks  rescue vehicles  ambulances and fireboats       The
