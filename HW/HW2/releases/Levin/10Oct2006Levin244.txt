                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 10  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on North Korea   s Underground Test                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement on North Korea   s underground test           North Korea   s underground test should be condemned by the entire international community   The Bush Administration   s inaction over the years leaves us once again faced with bad choices   The Administration has consistently and unwisely rejected direct talks with North Korea outside of the six party talks even though our South Korean ally and China want us to do so   The Bush Administration should also end its inconsistent approach to North Korea   s nuclear weapons program   For example  Secretary Rice minimized the importance of North Korea   s declaration that it had nuclear weapons last year  stating that  I do think the North Koreans have been  frankly  a little bit disappointed that people are not jumping up and down and running around with their hair on fire    One other action that the Administration can take is to promptly appoint a senior coordinator for North Korea policy as called for by National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007
