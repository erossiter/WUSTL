                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEFebruary 21  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 4 Million for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that seven Michigan organizations have been awarded  1 420 222 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety          Investing in our first responders pays immediate dividends to the community     said Levin      Each of these communities will benefit from the improved equipment and the fire prevention  safety and training programs that these grants will help to fund             Michigan   s first responders deserve nothing less than the very best training and equipment     said Stabenow      I   m pleased that this funding will assist our local fire departments in continuing to keep their communities safe          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process         Individual grant awards can be found in the table below        Organization
