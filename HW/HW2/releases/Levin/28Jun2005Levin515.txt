    06 28 2005   Levin Statement on President Bush   s Speech on Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 28  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on President Bush   s Speech on Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement tonight following President Bush   s address to the nation on Iraq       The President is finally becoming a little more realistic about the challenges ahead in Iraq  but I wish he had used this opportunity to acknowledge that the status quo is not working for us and that we need to change the dynamic in Iraq            There is a consensus that military action without a political settlement will not defeat the insurgency in Iraq   The best chance to change the dynamic in Iraq is to make it clear to the Iraqis that  unless the Iraqis meet their own timetable for adopting a constitution  the United States will review our position in Iraq with all of our options open  including a reevaluation of our military commitment         See  from Senator Levin and Senator Susan Collins  R Maine
