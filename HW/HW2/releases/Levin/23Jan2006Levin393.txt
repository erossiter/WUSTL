                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 23  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Urge OMB Director to Release Funds to Fight Emerald Ash Borer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  urged the director of the Office of Management and Budget to release funds for emerald ash borer eradication from the U S  Department of Agriculture   The department has requested  7 5 million in emergency funding  but the OMB director   s approval is required prior to release           We respectfully request that you expedite the release of the emergency funds so that the State of Michigan can continue the important work of detecting and preventing the spread of emerald ash borer     the senators wrote to Director Joshua Bolten on Friday along with Rep  Joe Schwarz   R MI 07       The Michigan Department of Agriculture estimates that our state has lost  30    50 million in revenue and jobs statewide as a result of the EAB infestation   Commercial nurseries in the state have lost approximately  11 million   To date  we have been forced to destroy approximately 16 million ash trees           The funding would be used to help eradicate EAB in
