    04 06 2005   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Grant of  179 934 for Upper Peninsula Rural Health Care Network                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 6  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce Grant of  179 934 for Upper Peninsula Rural Health Care Network                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           A Rural Health Network Development Grant of  179 934 has been awarded to the Upper Peninsula Health Care Network  which partners 16 independent health care organizations working together to improve access to quality health care for residents within their communities  The Department of Health and Human Services grant was announced today by U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  who both wrote federal officials in support of the grant request          This grant will enable the participating health care organizations to reduce the much higher than average pharmaceutical costs of the Michigan Medicaid Managed Care Plan in this region through cooperative purchasing     Stabenow said     I am impressed that the members of UPHCN are working together to meet the unique pharmaceutical needs of their 15 county rural region             Small hospitals do not have the same resources available to them as larger hospitals do     said Levin     With the help of this grant  the Upper Peninsula Health Care Network will be able to help strengthen the viability of its health care facilities by addressing the rising cost of pharmaceuticals          UPHCH members are Baraga County Memorial Baraga County Memorial Hospital  L   Anse  Bell Hospital  Ishpeming  Dickinson County Healthcare System  Iron Mountain  Grand View Health System  Ironwood  Helen Newberry Joy Hospital  Newberry  Iron County Community
