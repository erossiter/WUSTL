    08 24 2005   Levin Praises Base Closure Commission s Decision on Detroit Arsenal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 24  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Praises Base Closure Commission s Decision on Detroit Arsenal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement today on the BRAC Commission   s decision on the Detroit Arsenal          I am delighted by the Commission   s decision to endorse the Department of Defense   s recommendation to expand and enhance the ground vehicle programs at the Detroit Arsenal and to designate southeast Michigan as a center of excellence for ground vehicle research and acquisition  Clustering additional ground vehicle research  development  and acquisition functions at the Detroit Arsenal is an inherently logical way to create greater efficiencies and advances for the Army  and I am pleased it will bring about 1 100 new jobs to Michigan          With the Commission   s decision  Southeast Michigan will continue to lead the way in developing the Army of the future
