    01 14 2005   Northville Student Wins Several Awards at U S  Senate Page School Ceremony in Washington  D C                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 14  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Northville Student Wins Several Awards at U S  Senate Page School Ceremony in Washington  D C                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON  Most high school awards ceremonies include a musical prelude  the presentation of awards and a speech by a student  At the U S  Senate Page School farewell reception today  one student  Jack Temple of Northville  did all three  Not only did Jack play keyboards in the Page School ensemble for the prelude  he also received three awards and gave remarks on behalf of his Page School class        Jack won the Political Science American Government Award  the Citizenship Award and the Principal   s Award  In addition  Jack gave a well received speech about the challenges and rewards of being a Senate page  Jack was selected from a group of six students to give remarks on behalf of his classmates         Jack exemplifies the best in our Senate pages     said Levin      His well rounded background of solid academic achievement and extracurricular activities will stand him in good stead as he continues his education   I am proud of his achievements and wish him much success in his future endeavors          In an ornate room in the Capitol  the 27 Senate pages and their families heard speeches from Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt   Sen  John Warner  R Va   and Sen  Mike Enzi  R Wyo   Jack   s parents  Patrick and Mary Temple  flew to Washington for the awards ceremony  which was also attended by several Levin staff members   Levin was traveling in Michigan today and unable to attend   Jack will return to
