    10 19 2007   Levin Applauds Decision to Expedite Review of Great Lakes Water Levels                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 19  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Applauds Decision to Expedite Review of Great Lakes Water Levels                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   today applauded a decision by the International Joint Commission  IJC  to expedite the St  Clair River portion of its study of Upper Great Lakes water levels   Levin and six Senate colleagues had  to the commission in September asking for an expedited process            The water levels in the Great Lakes are very important to all of Michigan     said Levin     I am pleased that  in response to our letter  the International Joint Commission will expedite its role in assessing water flows through the St  Clair River and implications that those flows might have on erosion of the riverbed and the changing water levels   The Great Lakes are a treasured natural resource that we must protect            The International Upper Great Lakes Study Board is expected to produce a draft report by February 2009  a year ahead of its original schedule   Interim progress reports are expected during 2008   The IJC   s press release announcing the expedited review can be viewed
