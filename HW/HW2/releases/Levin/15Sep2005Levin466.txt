    09 15 2005   Levin  Stabenow Announce  4 Million for Michigan in Senate Passed Commerce  Justice  Science Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 15  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin  Stabenow Announce  4 Million for Michigan in Senate Passed Commerce  Justice  Science Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     The Commerce Justice Science appropriations bill passed by the Senate today contains  3 million for the Detroit West Riverfront and  1 million for Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary  U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced   In addition  the Detroit Economic Growth Corporation has been listed as a high priority project for grant consideration under the Commerce Department   s Economic Development Administration          Detroit   s riverfront is being transformed from a neglected industrial wasteland to a beautiful space for our people and visitors to our city   This funding will greatly contribute to that transformation     said Levin      The narrative surrounding Michigan   s rich maritime history will be better appreciated with the opening of the Great Lakes Maritime Heritage Center at the Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary   Visitors will be enthralled with this underwater cultural resource              This legislation provides funding for COPS grants  as well as for significant Michigan projects     Stabenow said     In addition  this legislation carries my request that the Federal Trade Commission expedite and expand its investigation of gas prices  This is important legislation for Michigan         Detroit West Riverfront  3 million    The funds will be used to acquire land in the West Riverfront area  along the Detroit River west of Cobo Hall  for the purpose of protecting an important estuarine area with important conservation  recreation  ecological  historical and aesthetic value        Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary  1 million   The Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary and Underwater Preserve will use the funds for the completion of permanent exhibits at the new visitor   s center  the Great Lakes Maritime Heritage Center  and for gear such as submersible cameras and communications equipment that will allow visitors to talk with divers at the shipwrecks   The Thunder Bay National Marine Sanctuary protects a nationally significant collection of approximately 160 shipwrecks in the bay area spanning over a century of Great Lakes shipping history         Detroit Economic Growth Corporation   The Detroit Economic Growth Corporation is seeking to implement a Business Attraction program and a Business Retention program  both with the goal of strengthening and diversifying the foundation of the city   s economy   The Business Attraction project hinges on the concept of diversifying from a manufacturing automotive based industry to a technology based industry  which offers high growth   The project will focus on recruitment of targeted technology industry clusters through trade shows  trade missions  personal contacts and other sources   All recruitment activities will be housed in a database       The Senate passed H R  2862  the Commerce  Justice  Science Appropriations Act  by a vote of 91 4   The House passed the CJS spending bill in June   Members of the House and Senate will meet in a conference committee to work out the differences between the two bills
