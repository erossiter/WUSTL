Department of Transportation Grants for Several Michigan Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON  Senators Carl Levin  D  Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D  Mich   announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  2 780 237 in grants to communities throughout Michigan            Our roads  bridges and trails allow us to commute to and from work  visit friends and family  and travel throughout our state     said Levin     Our roads are also needed for commerce and by constantly improving the infrastructure  incorporating new technologies and adapting our roads to meet new needs we are helping to ensure safe and efficient travel for all              Michigan   s roads  airports and transit systems are the backbone of the state   s economy and its ability to maintain and create jobs     Stabenow said     Funding for Michigan transportation projects is one of my top priorities  and I will continue to fight for funding to improve travel across the state          The funds from these grants will be used in part for the following        Project  Purpose  Amount    Berrien County  I 94
