    04 21 2006   Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  339 000 in Fire Prevention Funding for Kalamazoo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEApril 21  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  339 000 in Fire Prevention Funding for Kalamazoo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Michigan Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that Kalamazoo Department of Public Safety has been awarded  339 000 in fire prevention funding from the U S  Department of Homeland Security        The funding will help pay the cost of putting sprinkler systems in downtown Kalamazoo buildings  as well as supporting fire prevention programs throughout the community  The funding assistance for sprinkler installation is a continuation of a program that has helped foster downtown development          Kalamazoo has developed an excellent program to promote both community development and community safety     Stabenow said     This grant gives Kalamazoo an important to tool continue to work to improve the quality of life of its residents             This funding will give Kalamazoo a boost in attracting residents to the downtown area and making sure they are safe     said Levin     I   m hopeful that this program will help the city to work towards the dual goals of downtown development and fire prevention          Administered by the U S  Department of Homeland Security s Office of Domestic Preparedness  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program provides direct assistance to local fire departments for general firefighter and first responder operations  in conjunction with the U S  Fire Administration
