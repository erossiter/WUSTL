    01 23 2006   Levin Statement on Ford Restructuring Announcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 23  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on Ford Restructuring Announcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement today regarding the Ford Motor Company   s announcement that it plans to close 14 facilities by 2012  eliminating up to 30 000 jobs          It is a sad and difficult day for the Ford Motor Company and the thousands of hardworking employees and their families who are impacted by today s announcement               Ford s announcement this morning illustrates the plight of our domestic manufacturers and their struggle competing on an uneven playing field with foreign counterparts             Ford s announcement further highlights the need to turn around the drain of manufacturing jobs in the U S   We must be aggressive in leveling the playing field for domestic manufacturers in areas such as health care and trade  including efforts to fight currency manipulation by other countries   We need an Apollo type federal program that includes federal R and tax incentives to encourage manufacturers to continue doing business here and to expand and re equip existing facilities to meet today s challenges   Although I am pleased to see Ford s announcement that it plans to expand its use of advanced technology  which will help to increase its competitiveness  we need to do much more as a country to encourage advanced technology to help all of our domestic manufacturers
