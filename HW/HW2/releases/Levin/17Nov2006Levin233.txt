    11 17 2006   Levin Statement on the Death of Bo Schembechler                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 17  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on the Death of Bo Schembechler                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   made the following statement today regarding the death of Michigan legend Bo Schembechler          Bo Schembechler may have been born in Ohio but he is a Michigan legend   As the coach of the University of Michigan Wolverines football team from 1969 until 1990  and a campus fixture until the day he died  Bo led a program defined by integrity and excellence   His accomplishments on and off of the field are a source of pride for all Michiganders and especially those who wear the Maize and Blue
