    11 30 2006   Levin Statement on Reported Iraq Study Group Recommendations                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 30  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on Reported Iraq Study Group Recommendations                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                The recommendations of the Iraq Study Group outlined in the press today would be a welcome change in the course of our policy in Iraq  and are similar in significant ways to the Levin Reed amendment that the Senate considered earlier this year         Although the recommendations are not as pointed or as definitive as I would like  many of them seem to be the same as the Levin Reed amendment       The Iraq Study Group reportedly advocates a gradual    pullback     which is similar to our proposal for a phased redeployment        they  would send a message that the U S  presence is not open ended  and would move away from the Administration policy that essentially provides Iraqis with a blank check on the presence of our troops       they apparently recognize that there is not a military solution to the conflict  and that the resolution must be a political settlement among the Iraqis  and     they recognize that we must communicate a sense of urgency to the Iraqis  putting pressure on them to reach the political settlement that only they can reach    The Iraqis must understand that we cannot save them from themselves      The press reports suggest that the Iraq Study Group will not specify a beginning for the pull back of troops   I think that is a mistake because I believe the announcement of a date to begin the redeployment of U S  forces from Iraq would increase the pressure on Iraqis to reach the political settlement that is essential to ending the sectarian violence   However  I welcome the direction of the Iraq Study Group   s recommendations reported today  and I look forward to reading their report
