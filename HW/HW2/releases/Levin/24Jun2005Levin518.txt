    06 24 2005   Levin Releases Letter to Bush on North Korea                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 24  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                              Levin Releases Letter to Bush on North Korea                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON    Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   along with Sen  Minority Leader Harry Reid  D Nev   and Sens  Joseph Biden  D Del   and Jay Rockefeller  D W V   the ranking members of the Senate Foreign Relations and Intelligence Committees  respectively  sent the attached letter to the President  signed one year from the day the six party talks with North Korea were last convened   The Senators urge the President to take further steps to address the threat to U S  national security and international stability posed by North Korea   s nuclear programs   The steps include appointing a special envoy  and setting priorities in order to achieve a phased  reciprocal  verifiable deal to eliminate North Korea   s nuclear weapons program
