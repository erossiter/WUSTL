Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that 8 Michigan fire departments have been awarded  1 145 564 through the Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety and to purchase vehicles        Providing first responders with the equipment they need is a critical step in keeping Michigan communities safe     said Stabenow   I   m so pleased that this funding will provide our brave men and women with the resources they need to continue protecting Michigan families and homes from danger         Directing resources to our firefighters and first responders is an important way to ensure the safety of communities across Michigan   said Levin   Better training and equipment will help these brave men and women do their jobs effectively        The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process   More information can be found at
