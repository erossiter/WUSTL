    09 23 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  700 000 Coming to Michigan Regional Community Policing Institute                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 23  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  700 000 Coming to Michigan Regional Community Policing Institute                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  is awarding  700 000 to the Michigan Regional Community Policing Institute  RCPI  at Michigan State University in East Lansing for training and technical assistance programs   This year   s grant is twice the amount that the RCPI received in 2004          Today   s law enforcement officials face more diverse threats than ever before     said Levin      The RCPI supports our efforts to ensure that every officer in Michigan is properly trained for all situations   This increased grant shows that the RCPI has been and will continue to be an effective way to secure our homes and communities             The challenges of homeland security threats and the impact of natural disasters like Hurricane Katrina reemphasize the importance of a well trained  highly professional police force working closely with each community      Stabenow said     The RCPI at Michigan State University helps train law enforcement officers to meet these new challenges          The RCPI at Michigan State is part of a national network of community policing centers receiving funding in this round of grants  The centers provide community policing training and technical assistance to both law enforcement officers and members of the community that work collaboratively with local law enforcement       Training provided by the RCPIs covers topics such as rural community policing  cultural diversity  community mobilization  conflict resolution  crime mapping and analysis  violence prevention  organization change management  and ethics and integrity within law enforcement agencies
