                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASENovember 18  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Correction on al Zarqawi Question                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     In response to a question at the press conference about Abu Musab al Zarqawi being in Baghdad prior to the war  I mistakenly responded with information about a different person about whom the Administration had made similar claims  Abdul Rahman Yasin was a suspect in the first attack on the World Trade Center in 1993  who fled to Iraq  Vice President Cheney said on January 10  2004 that there was evidence that Iraq had    put him on a payroll  gave him a monthly stipend  and provided him with a house    in Iraq      In my answer  I was referring to information that Yasin may have been under house arrest in Iraq  and that Iraq had actively offered to turn him over to the U S        With respect to Zarqawi  the Administration asserted before the war that the Iraqi government knew he was in Iraq   However  pre war intelligence reporting indicated that Zarqawi apparently used an alias to travel to Iraq  suggesting that the Iraqi regime may not have been aware of his presence  and or his real identity   I described this issue in greater detail in my October 21  2004    PDF
