    02 07 2007   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 3 Million for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEFebruary 7  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 3 Million for Fire Safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that sixteen Michigan fire departments have been awarded  1 318 654 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety          This is good news for communities and families across Michigan     said Levin     Better training and equipment will help our brave firefighters and first responders to do their jobs safely and effectively             Michigan   s first responders work tirelessly keeping our families and homes safe     said Stabenow      These grants will allow communities across the state to provide these brave men and women with the best training and equipment available          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process   Individual grant awards can be found in the table below        Organization  Location  Program
