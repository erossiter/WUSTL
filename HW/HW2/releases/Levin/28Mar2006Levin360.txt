    03 28 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  98 000 for Ogemaw County Police Training                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 28  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce over  98 000 for Ogemaw County Police Training                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice Bureau of Justice Assistance is awarding  98 723 to Ogemaw County to fund training for corrections officers to become certified  sworn police officers   The Sheriff   s Department currently has only 16 sworn officers covering an area of 575 square miles and a population of 21 729          Well trained law enforcement officials are critical to the safety of Michigan   s families     said Levin      This federal grant will help the Ogemaw County police force effectively manage the large area they have to cover             More police officers on the street means greater success in fighting and preventing crime  and quicker response to local emergencies     Stabenow said     This Department of Justice grant is good news for the people of Ogemaw County          The 17 week training program will be conducted by the Police Academy at Kirtland Community College in Roscommon   It will include instruction on  among other topics  physical conditioning  defensive tactics  operation of firearms  first aid  drug detection and identification  evidence collection and state law   In addition  training on homeland security and animal control issues will be provided to strengthen operations through the Sheriff   s Department
