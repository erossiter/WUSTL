                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that Moorestown and Walkerville have been awarded  42 541 and  40 404  respectively  through the Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations  fire prevention  and firefighter safety          Protecting our communities from fires and other emergencies is a partnership that requires public education and providing our first responders with the proper training and equipment     said Stabenow      I   m pleased this funding will provide Moorestown and Walkerville with the resources needed to keep these communicates safe             Firefighters and first responders put themselves in danger every day to protect our lives and communities     said Levin     I am pleased that these federal dollars will help provide the necessary training and equipment to keep these brave men and women safe          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  to purchase protective equipment and response vehicles  to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States and for public safety education  Grants are awarded competitively through an application process  More information can be found at
