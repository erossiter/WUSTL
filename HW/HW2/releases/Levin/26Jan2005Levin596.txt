                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 26  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  91 219 in Firefighter Grants for Three Fire Departments in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Departments in Dimondale  Concord and Bay City to receive funds      WASHINGTON   Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that three fire departments in the state of Michigan have received  91 219 in grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  Windsor Township Emergency Services  Concord Fire Department and Bangor Township Fire Department will receive funding for fire operations and firefighter safety programs            Our firefighters risk their lives every day they go to work so that they can protect our lives and communities     said Levin     The fire operations and firefighter safety program allows departments to provide the necessary training to keep these brave men and women safe              This grant program provides funding directly to local fire departments to improve their ability to respond to fires and fire related emergencies in their communities     Stabenow said     Our state s first responders deserve nothing less than the best possible equipment and training  and these grants will help these three fire departments attain that goal          These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  a joint effort of the Department of Homeland Security  the Federal Emergency Management Agency and U S  Fire Administration   The fire operations and firefighter safety programs provide firefighters with equipment  training and modifications to facilities        Funding provided to the departments are as follows         Organization Name  Program Area  Federal Share  City    Windsor
