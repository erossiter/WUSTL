    12 12 2007   Levin Statement on CAFE Provision in Energy Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 12  2007 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Statement on CAFE Provision in Energy Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON    Following is the statement of Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   regarding his efforts to amend the energy bill to clarify the roles of EPA and NHTSA in setting CAFE standards          I am trying to clarify the language to make sure there is no conflict between the role of the EPA and the role of NHTSA   The Senate and the House have both voted for an industry wide fuel economy standard of 35 m p g  by the year 2020  and we need to make it clear that what the EPA does will be consistent with those decisions
