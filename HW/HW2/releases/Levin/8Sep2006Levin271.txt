Our Schools    Grants to Eight Michigan Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  today announced that eight Michigan communities will receive  388 583 from the U S  Department of Justice to enhance security at local schools   The grants are awarded through the Secure Our Schools  SOS  Initiative  which is part of the Office of Community Oriented Policing Service  COPS  program          A safe school environment is essential to give our children every opportunity to learn and grow     said Stabenow     The Secure our Schools program provides local communities with much needed funding to keep crime out of our schools             This is good news for communities and school districts across Michigan     said Levin      The Secure Our Schools initiative helps to promote cooperation between school officials and law enforcement agencies and ensure that our children are free to learn in a safe environment          The SOS Initiative improves security at schools by assisting communities with the cost of installing metal detectors  locks  lighting  and other equipment that can help deter crime   The funding also helps cover some of the cost of school security assessments  training for students and personnel  and coordination with local law enforcement agencies       These grants will cover up to 50 percent of the total cost of these security enhancements  and each grantee is required to contribute a local match of 50 percent toward the cost of the approved projects       Organizations receiving SOS Initiative grants are as follows       Belleville Police Department    8 924  Benzie County Sheriff   s Department   11 329  Calhoun County Sheriff   s Department  62 679  Livonia Police Department    24 800  Monroe County     66 070  Mott Community College    84 032  City of River Rouge     51 722  Royal Oak Police Department   79 027
