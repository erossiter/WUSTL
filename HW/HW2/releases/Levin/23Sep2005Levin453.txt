    09 23 2005   Levin Announces USCGC Mackinaw Will Stop in Menominee During Farewell Tour                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 23  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Announces USCGC Mackinaw Will Stop in Menominee During Farewell Tour                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON  Senator Carl Levin  D Mich  announced today that the U S  Coast Guard Cutter Mackinaw will make a stop in Menominee  Michigan  on its underway schedule before retiring to its homeport of Cheboygan   The famous cutter  led by USCG Commander Joe McGinnis  will arrive on the evening of October 26th  lie in port for visitation on the 27th and depart the morning of the 28th          Throughout her illustrious 60 year career  the Mighty Mackinaw has kept shipping lanes open and commerce moving on the Great Lakes     said Levin      I am pleased that the people of the U P  have this opportunity to pay final tribute to the proud legacy of the old Mack while still afloat in the waters of Menominee          The Mackinaw  known as the largest and most powerful icebreaker in the world  has been homeported in Cheboygan for more than 60 years and will soon retire there after this final tour as a historically designated maritime museum ship   A new Mackinaw  launched in April of this year from Menominee Harbor  Michigan and Wisconsin  will take over the duties of her namesake
