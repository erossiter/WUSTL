    12 13 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 2 Million for Fire Safety in Michigan                  Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 13  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 2 Million for Fire Safety in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that ten Michigan fire departments have been awarded  1 220 928 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety and purchase firefighting vehicles          Assistance to Firefighters grants play a critical role in protecting Michigan   s communities     said Levin      Firefighters and emergency personnel perform an invaluable service and this federal support will help provide them with the resources they need to do their jobs safely and effectively             Michigan   s first responders work tirelessly every day to protect our neighborhoods and our families     said Stabenow      We owe it to these brave men and women to provide them with the best training and equipment available          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process
