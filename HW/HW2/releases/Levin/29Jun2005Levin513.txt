    06 29 2005   Stabenow  Levin React to U S  House Committee Approval of Legislation to Regulate Canadian Solid Waste                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJune 29  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Stabenow  Levin React to U S  House Committee Approval of Legislation to Regulate Canadian Solid Waste                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  issued the following comments today in response to the action of the U S  House Energy and Commerce Committee  which on a voice vote approved legislation to help block the flow of Canadian municipal into Michigan  Stabenow and Levin are authors of the companion bill in the Senate          This bipartisan legislation will empower Michigan to pass its own laws to limit trash imports and push the Environmental Protection Agency to enforce our existing treaty with Canada  and we will work the Senate to match the quick action of the House     the senators said     We will be sending a letter in the near future to Senator Inhofe and Senator Jeffords  the Chair and Ranking Member of the Senate Environment and Public Works Committee  urging them to expedite the legislation
