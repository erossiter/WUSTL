    03 24 2005   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on  3 Billion Collected By IRS Tax Shelter Settlement Offer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEMarch 24  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on  3 Billion Collected By IRS Tax Shelter Settlement Offer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON     Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   senior Democrat on the U S  Permanent Subcommittee on Investigations  led an investigation that culminated in November 2003 hearings and a bipartisan subcommittee report earlier this year about tax shelters sold by professional firms such as accounting firms  law firms and banks   One of the key tax shelters examined by the subcommittee was sold by KPMG  is known as BLIPS  and is included in the Son of BOSS settlement offer whose results were announced by the IRS today   Levin issued the following statement        The  3 2 billion collected by the IRS from users of an abusive tax shelter known as    Son of BOSS    shows how huge the tax shelter problem is and how much can be done when the IRS enforces the law   Hearings held by the Permanent Subcommittee on Investigations in 2003 exposed one version of this abusive tax shelter  called    BLIPS     as a complete sham  the IRS is now tracking down the hundreds of individuals who bought BLIPS or a similar tax dodge to duck paying their taxes   Collecting this money is a victory for all those honest taxpayers who play by the rules and are forced to shoulder the burden when others cheat on their taxes           Over a thousand taxpayers who used the Son of BOSS tax shelter took advantage of the IRS settlement offer  but about 400 taxpayers did not   It is critical that the IRS now use the full weight of the law against the folks who refused to come forward   That action is needed not only to punish past wrongdoing and deter future tax evasion  but also to ensure the success of future IRS settlement offers
