    10 05 2006   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  410 000 for Hiring at Two Michigan Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEOctober 5  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  410 000 for Hiring at Two Michigan Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security is awarding  410 500 to fire departments in Jackson and Brighton under the Assistance to Firefighters Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grant program   The funding will be used to support the hiring of additional firefighters          It is crucially important that Michigan   s fire departments have the resources they need to protect our homes and communities     said Levin      This funding will help the Green Oak and Blackman Township Fire Departments to be fully staffed and ready to do their jobs well             Firefighters are essential to ensuring all Michigan communities are safe and prepared for emergencies     Stabenow said      This funding with help local fire departments hire additional firefighters so that first responders will be on call around the clock          The goal of the SAFER program is to help fire departments protect their communities from fire related hazards and achieve and maintain 24 hour staffing       The Green Oak Township Fire Department in Brighton will receive a grant of  310 500 and the Blackman Township Fire Department in Jackson will receive a grant of  100 000   Both of these awards will be used to pay a portion of the salaries for newly created firefighter positions over a five year period
