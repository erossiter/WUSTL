                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASESeptember 7  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Stabenow  Levin Announce  667 519 in Firefighter Grants for Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     Senators Debbie Stabenow  D MI  and Carl Levin  D MI  announced today that 12 fire departments in Michigan have received a total of  667 519 in grants under the terms of the fiscal year 2005 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program            The Assistance to Firefighters Grant program enables firefighters to enhance their ability to do their jobs  improve safety and preserve lives     Stabenow said     This round of grants will enable twelve Michigan fire departments to provide better firefighting protection for themselves and the communities they serve             As we have seen so often  our firefighters are truly our first responders     said Levin      These grants will give them additional resources to carry out their mission of protecting their communities          The Operations and Firefighter Safety Program funds training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to facilities        Funding has been awarded as follows
