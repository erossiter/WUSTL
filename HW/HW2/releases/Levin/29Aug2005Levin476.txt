    08 29 2005   Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Announcement of DOJ and IRS Settlement with KPMG   and Indictments Related to Abusive Tax Shelters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 29  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Statement of Senator Carl Levin on the Announcement of DOJ and IRS Settlement with KPMG   and Indictments Related to Abusive Tax Shelters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON   Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   issued the following statement today          The actions of the IRS and DOJ in this case send a powerful message to the promoters  aiders and abettors of abusive tax shelters that they can no longer expect to be let off the hook  They are as culpable as the taxpayers who take advantage of the scams  and should be treated no better
