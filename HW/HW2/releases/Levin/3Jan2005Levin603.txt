    01 03 2005   Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  311 040 in Firefighter Grants for Fire Departments in Alger and Garden City Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEJanuary 3  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce  311 040 in Firefighter Grants for Fire Departments in Alger and Garden City Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senators Carl Levin  D Mich   and Debbie Stabenow  D Mich   announced today that two fire departments in the state of Michigan have received  311 040 in grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  Garden City Fire Department will receive  85 140 for fire operations and firefighter safety programs and Wildwood Fire Department in Alger will receive  225 900 for the firefighting vehicle acquisition program           We must ensure our firefighters have the necessary resources to do their jobs     said Levin     This funding will strengthen each department   s ability to respond to emergencies while minimizing the risks to the men and women who work so hard to protect our lives     Levin said          Every fire department in Michigan faces critical challenges in protecting local residents and their property      Stabenow said     These grants will help the Wildwood and Garden City Fire Departments obtain the equipment and training they need to meet these challenges           These grants have been provided through the 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  a joint effort of the Department of Homeland Security  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  and U S  Fire Administration   The fire operations and firefighter safety programs provide firefighters with equipment  training and modifications to facilities  The vehicle acquisition program allows departments to purchase vehicles  such as tankers  rescue vehicles  foam units and ambulances       More information on the assistance to firefighters grant program is available at
