                 Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEDecember 20  2006 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Senators Levin  Stabenow Announce Over  1 Million for Fire Safety in Michigan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON     U S  Senators Carl Levin  D MI  and Debbie Stabenow  D MI  announced today that ten Michigan fire departments have been awarded  1 062 340 through the fiscal year 2006 Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP    The funding will be used to support operations and firefighter safety and purchase firefighting vehicles          These federal dollars are an important part of keeping our communities safe     said Levin     Firefighters and first responders play a critical role in protecting our homes and families  and we owe it to them to provide the funding and the resources they need             Our local firefighters play a key role in keeping our families and communities safe  and they deserve the best training and equipment available     Stabenow said      These grants will strengthen neighborhoods across Michigan  and I am very pleased these fire departments have been selected to receive this funding          The AFGP awards grants directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to improve the effectiveness of operations and health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout the United States   Grants are awarded competitively through an application process       Individual grants awards can be found in the table below        Organization  Location  Program    Amount    Bridgeport Charter Township
