    08 23 2005   Levin Urges President to Push Oil Companies to Reduce Prices                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Carl Levin  News Release              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASEAugust 23  2005 Contact  Press OfficePhone  202 228 3685      Levin Urges President to Push Oil Companies to Reduce Prices                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     As Americans are paying record high gasoline prices and oil companies are making record profits  Sen  Carl Levin  D Mich   urged President Bush to call the major oil companies to the White House and push them to reduce oil prices           Americans are suffering from outrageously high gasoline prices     Levin wrote      Presidential leadership is essential to deal with this crisis           Levin urged the President to tell the oil companies that the White House will support an excess profits tax if the companies do not act to reduce prices                Text of Levin   s letter to Bush follows       August 22  2005        The President  The White House  Washington  DC  20500      Dear Mr  President            Americans are suffering from outrageously high gasoline prices   The massive increase in the cost of gas and other petroleum products has also severely damaged the economy  causing significant additional suffering            Price increases cannot be justified by short supply   Indeed  crude oil inventories are above normal and are higher than last year   s level              Oil companies are raking in record profits   Revenues of major oil companies are up from 30 to 50 percent            Presidential leadership is essential to deal with this crisis             I urge you to call the major oil companies into the White House  and to tell them in a clear and forceful way that  if they do not act immediately to bring down oil prices  you will support an excess profits tax   I also urge you to inform them you will ask the Federal Trade Commission  FTC  and the Department of Justice to look into the steep price increases to determine if any existing laws have been violated            Thank you for your urgent consideration            Sincerely             Carl Levin
