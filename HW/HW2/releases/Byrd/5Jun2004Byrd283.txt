SEN. BYRD ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF FORMER PRESIDENT REAGAN
  Sen. Robert C. Byrd, D-W.Va., issued the following statement after learning of the death of former President Ronald Reagan:
   "I am saddened to learn of President Reagan's passing. While I did not agree with him politically, I liked him personally. When it came to his general philosophy of life, Ronald Reagan exuded the bright side. He was filled with optimism no matter the pressures of the day."
   "Erma and I offer our sincere condolences to Nancy and their family."
