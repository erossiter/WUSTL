                                                        February 7  2007 Durbin Names McCaskill As Deputy Whip For 110th Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   Assistant Majority Leader  today announced the Senate Democratic Whip Team for the 110th session of Congress  which includes four freshman senators   The Whip Team will assist Senator Durbin on the floor and will be responsible for mobilizing votes within the Senate Democratic Caucus on major issues        A razor thin Democratic majority means that every vote counts and there is no room for error   The Whip Team for the 110th Congress is committed to delivering the votes and to helping ensure that America s priorities move forward   Durbin said        Durbin said that U S  Senator Barbara Boxer  D CA  has agreed to serve as Chief Deputy Whip  where she will be Durbin s primary assistant in managing and monitoring legislative efforts on the Senate Floor        The Deputy Whips for the 110th Congress are Sens  Sherrod Brown  D OH   Ben Cardin  D MD   Tom Carper  D DE   Russ Feingold  D WI   Amy Klobuchar  D MN   Claire McCaskill  D MO   Patty Murray  D WA   Bill Nelson  D FL   Mark Pryor  D AR   Ken Salazar  D CO   and Ron Wyden  D OR                     Questions or comments        Text Size
