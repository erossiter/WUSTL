SEN. DEMINT TO VISIT CHARLESTON AIR FORCE BASE
  Sen. Jim DeMint (R-S.C.) will visit Charleston Air Force Base on Friday, March 16th and hold a townhall with airmen. Sen. DeMint will address airmen from the 437th and 315th Airlift Wings on the War on Terror and take time to hear their stories and answer questions.  
   When: Friday, March 16th 
  Media availability at 9:30 a.m. 
  Senator's remarks at townhall 9:45 a.m. - 10:15 a.m.

  and arrive at Dorchester Road Gate before 9:00 a.m.
