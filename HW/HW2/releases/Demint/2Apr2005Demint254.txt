SEN. DEMINT ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF POPE JOHN PAUL II
  Today, Sen. Jim DeMint (R-S.C.) released the following statement: 
   "Today, the world has lost one of our strongest advocates of life and freedom. Pope John Paul II fought bravely alongside President Ronald Reagan and Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher to help bring an end to communism, and has argued forcefully to encourage a culture of life."
