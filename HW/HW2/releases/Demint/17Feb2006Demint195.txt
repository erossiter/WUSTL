SEN. DEMINT ISSUES STATEMENT ON LLOYD CONFIRMATION
  Today, Sen. Jim DeMint (R-S.C.) made the following statement after the Senate unanimously confirmed Reginald "Reggie" Lloyd for U.S. Attorney:  
   "Reggie Lloyd represents some of the best South Carolina has to offer. He has a solid reputation and is known throughout South Carolina as a fair and honest man. His experience both in the private and public sector will give him a broad perspective and serve him well in his new position. 
   "I am thankful to Reggie and his family for his decision to serve our nation and pleased that the Senate acted swiftly and unanimously to confirm him."
