SEN. DEMINT ISSUES STATEMENT ON JASPER PORT DEAL FOR SOUTH CAROLINA
  Sen. Jim DeMint (R-South Carolina) made the following statement Monday after the announcement by Govs. Sanford and Perdue to jointly develop the Jasper county port. 
   "I congratulate Governors Sanford and Perdue for their hard work on this project. This port will better connect South Carolina with the rest of the world and bring good jobs to our state." 
   "I look forward to working with the Governor and the new port authority to make this project a success."
