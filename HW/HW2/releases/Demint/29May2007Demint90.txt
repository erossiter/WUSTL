SEN. DEMINT'S 'LISTENING TOUR' COMES TO GREENVILLE
  Sen. Jim DeMint will speak to Greenville residents at a Dutch-treat breakfast at Tommy's Country Ham House where he will give a Washington update and take questions from attendees. 
   "Listening to constituents is very important. Without sufficient feedback, oftentimes elected officials will not govern effectively. I really look forward to it," said Senator DeMint. 
   Who: U.S. Senator Jim DeMint 
   Where: Tommy's Country Ham House, 214 Rutherford Street, Greenville 
   When: 8:00 am, Wednesday, May 30, 2007.
   Contact: Ryan Dawkins, 803/771-6112.
