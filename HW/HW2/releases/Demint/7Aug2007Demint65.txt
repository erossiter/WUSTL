SEN. DEMINT BRINGS 'KEEPING SC SECURE' TOUR TO SPARTANBURG
  U.S. Senator Jim DeMint will continue his third annual South Carolina on the Move tour in Spartanburg on Wednesday, August 8, 2007. The theme of this year's tour is "Keeping SC Secure" and will highlight local law enforcement and first responders around South Carolina.  
   While in Spartanburg, Senator DeMint will be the keynote speaker at the Spartanburg Chamber Issues Forum where he will present a South Carolina on the Move Award. He will also tour the Spartanburg Law Enforcement Center and the Spartanburg Regional Emergency Room where he will meet with EMS and view different emergency response procedures. 
   "I want to personally thank the brave men and women who make important sacrifices to keep South Carolina secure," said Senator DeMint. "These are everyday heroes who deserve our admiration and my goal with this tour is to highlight their positive impact on our state."
