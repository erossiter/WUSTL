SEN. DEMINT ISSUES STATEMENT ON GRANITEVILLE TRAIN CRASH
  "Today's train wreck is a heartbreaking accident and I along with all South Carolinians extend condolences to the individuals and families affected by this tragedy. Our thoughts and prayers will be with you during this difficult time. 
   "While our first priority is to care for those who have been injured, I will be working with the National Transportation Safety Board to help prevent future accidents."
