SEN. DEMINT BRINGS 'KEEPING SC SECURE' TOUR TO GREENVILLE
  U.S. Senator Jim DeMint will continue his third annual South Carolina on the Move tour in Greenville on Wednesday, August 8, 2007. The theme of this year's tour is "Keeping SC Secure" and will highlight local law enforcement and first responders around South Carolina.  
   While in Greenville, Senator DeMint will be the keynote speaker at a Young Professionals breakfast that will include participants from Greenville, Spartanburg and Anderson. He will then tour the Greenville Law Enforcement Center and meet with Greenville County Sheriff Steve Loftis and Greenville City Police Chief Terri Wilfong. 
   "I want to personally thank the brave men and women who make important sacrifices to keep South Carolina secure," said Senator DeMint. "These are everyday heroes who deserve our admiration and my goal with this tour is to highlight their positive impact on our state."
