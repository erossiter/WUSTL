SEN. DEMINT'S 'LISTENING TOUR' COMES TO CHARLESTON
  Sen. Jim DeMint will speak to Charleston area residents at a Dutch-treat lunch at Sticky Fingers Restaurant in Mt. Pleasant where he will give a Washington update and take questions from attendees. 
   "Listening to constituents is very important. Without sufficient feedback, oftentimes elected officials will not govern effectively. I really look forward to it," said Senator DeMint. 
   Who: U.S. Senator Jim DeMint 
   Where: Sticky Fingers Restaurant, 341 Johnnie Dodds Blvd., Mt. Pleasant, SC 
   When: Noon, Thursday, May 31, 2007.
   Contact: Ryan Dawkins, 803/771-6112.
