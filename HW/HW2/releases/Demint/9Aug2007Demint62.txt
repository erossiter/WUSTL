SEN. DEMINT BRINGS 'KEEPING SC SECURE' TOUR TO PEE DEE
  U.S. Senator Jim DeMint will continue his third annual SC on the Move tour in Florence on Friday, August 10, 2007. The theme of this year's tour is "Keeping SC Secure" and will highlight local law enforcement and first responders around South Carolina.  
   While in the Pee Dee, Senator DeMint will tour ESAB Welding and Cutting Products. He will then proceed to IFH Food Distribution where he will tour the facility and participate in a lunch with local Florence business leaders. During lunch, Senator DeMint will present a South Carolina on the Move Award to ESAB. He will conclude his day with a media availability where he will present additional South Carolina on the Move Awards to the Florence County Sheriff's Office and the City of Florence Police and Fire Departments. 
   "I want to personally thank the brave men and women who make important sacrifices to keep South Carolina secure," said Senator DeMint. "These are everyday heroes who deserve our admiration and my goal with this tour is to highlight their positive impact on our state."
