    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Tuesday  November 29  2005     Senator Arlen Specter made the following comments today regarding Terrell Owens      My comments were made in an early Monday morning call to Angelo Cataldi  s WIP radio talk show which I do after every Eagles game   I was very critical of Terrell Owens   conduct in ruining the Eagle  s season and then noted that there may be an anti trust violation  which I have confirmed in discussions with several experts       The key question in my mind was whether the collective bargaining agreement provided an exemption for the anti trust violation  but that occurs only if the agreement covers the specific penalty   The four game suspension without pay was specified  but the prohibition against playing for the balance of the season was not   It appears that the gag order against the other teams was not part of the collective bargaining agreement     I have discussed this matter with lawyers at the Antitrust Division at the Department of Justice and I think it is better left to them rather than to the Judiciary Committee because of our heavy workload   Notwithstanding this hullabaloo  it won  t diminish my attendance at the games or calling Angelo Cataldi the next day
