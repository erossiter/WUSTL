    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Wednesday  April 4  2007     Senator Arlen Specter  ranking member on the Senate Judiciary Committee  yesterday sent letters to the Chief Executive Officers of Pennsylvania heath insurance organizations Highmark  Inc  and Independence Blue Cross regarding their pending merger               Senator Specter wrote to Dr  Kenneth Melani  President and CEO of Highmark  and Joseph A  Frick  President and CEO of Independence Blue Cross  in anticipation of their participation in the Judiciary Committee  s field hearing in Philadelphia on Monday     I very much appreciate your willingness to provide testimony at the hearing     Senator Specter wrote     It is my hope that this hearing will provide an opportunity to thoroughly consider the possible effects of the proposed merger  particularly the impact on Pennsylvania consumers                  Highmark  a Pittsburgh based company  and Independence Blue Cross  a Philadelphia based company  constitute the two largest health care insurers in Pennsylvania   Their merger would create the third largest insurance carrier in the country  and the new company would control over half of the state  s health insurance market               On Monday  April 9th at 9 30 am Senator Specter will convene a field hearing at the National Constitution Center in Philadelphia to examine the proposed merger and potential antitrust issues
