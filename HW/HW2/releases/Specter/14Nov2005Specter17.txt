    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Monday  November 14  2005     Senator Specter  the Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education  made the following comments about the LHHS bill    For this key subcommittee to have this kind of an allocation is scandalous   Every item on our tentative conference budget is under last year  under this year   This is just not right as we approach the problems of America    I came to the Chairman  Regula  with the suggestion and the urging that there be an emergency declaration on LIHEAP because it really is an emergency caused by Katrina   It is a genuine emergency   Had we had the little flexibility of  2 1 billion  and that is only a little flexibility  we could have kept the earmarks   Or  if we had the  2 1 billion on bioterrorism  which is another emergency  we would have had a little flexibility and could have kept the earmarks      My suggestion was to find some way to make those emergency designations to keep the earmarks and to find some way out   I am very reluctant to see the earmarks and the projects not maintained      But I don  t see any way around having the earmarks for a billion dollars and coming in with a cut on health professions of  225 194 000 and a cut on Community Services Block Grants for  316 793 000 or coming in with a cut for LIHEAP of  175 000 000                                                                  Browse by
