                                                                         News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Tuesday  July 25  2006     U S  Senators Rick Santorum  R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  and Arlen Specter  R PA   Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education  announced today that over  4 million in federal funding has been approved by the Senate Appropriations Committee for hospitals in the Northeast Pennsylvania   The funding is part of the FY07 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill  which will now await action by the full Senate   Hospitals approved in this bill can use the funding to provide wage stabilization for staff   Northeastern
