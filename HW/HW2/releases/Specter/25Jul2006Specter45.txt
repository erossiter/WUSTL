    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases          Funding Approved in the Fiscal Year 2007 LHHS Approprations Bill                      Washington  D C    Tuesday  July 25  2006     U S  Senators Rick Santorum  R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  and Arlen Specter  R PA   Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education  announced today the U S  Senate Appropriations Committee has approved funding for several central Pennsylvania healthcare projects   The projects are contained in the Fiscal Year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill     I applaud my colleagues in the Committee for approving the Health and Human Services funding for projects in central Pennsylvania     Senator Santorum said     The funding will allow communities to purchase new equipment  construct new health care facilities  and provide the most advanced health care modernizations which will aid in the health and wellness of the citizens of central Pennsylvania         I am pleased my colleagues have approved this important funding for central Pennsylvania     Senator Specter said     These projects are vital to ensuring quality primary and emergency care  as well as advanced preventative care measures  for the area  s residents      The bill will now await action on the Senate floor   The projects in the bill include         250 000 for Good Samaritan Health System in Lebanon County to implement a pharmacy information system         700 000 for Penn State University  Milton S  Hershey Medical Center in Dauphin County to provide equipment that will support Penn State Heart   Vascular Institute recruit individuals with expertise in the area of population based sciences and increase the number of scientists studying cardio  circulatory and respiratory systems         400 000 for Susquehanna Health System in Lycoming County for multi leaf collimator  MLC  radiation therapy technologies         300 000 for Geisinger Health System in Montour County for construction of a facility that will examine healthcare in rural communities           250 000 for Pinnacle Health Hospitals in Dauphin County for image guided  intensity modulated radiation therapy equipment           250 000 for Wellspan Health in York County for disease management and primary care services  equipment for patient education  and purchase of a healthy community pharmacy delivery vehicle         250 000 for Memorial Hospital of York for construction  renovation and equipment         200 000 for Dauphin County Social Services for Children   Youth in Dauphin County for children and youth medical services in rural areas
