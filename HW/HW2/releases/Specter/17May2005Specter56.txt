                                                                         News Room News Releases          Funding from Hope VI Public Housing Revitalization Program                      Washington  D C    Tuesday  May 17  2005     U S  Senator Arlen Specter  R PA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  and U S  Senator Rick Santorum  R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  announced today that the Philadelphia Housing Authority will receive  17 059 932 through the Department of Housing and Urban Development  s Home Ownership for People Everywhere  HOPE VI  program   The funding will allow the Philadelphia Housing Authority to revitalize the Ludlow Scattered Sites public housing development   The original public housing development has been almost entirely demolished and will be replaced with 50 public housing rental units  50 replacement homeownership units  and 22 affordable homeownership units   The revitalized development will also provide programs aimed to foster self sufficiency among residents with a range of income levels       The HOPE VI program has been very successful in providing low income families with better housing  employment  and educational opportunities     Senator Specter said     I am very pleased to see the City of Philadelphia receive the resources it needs to provide these opportunities to residents in need        The revitalization of the Ludlow Scattered Sites development is an important initiative in the City of Philadelphia     said Senator Santorum    The HOPE VI program has been very successful in meeting the needs of low income families and providing opportunities for affordable housing   The HOPE VI program is an essential part of the effort to improve public housing within Pennsylvania      The HOPE VI program provides funding for the demolition and reconstruction of obsolete  severely distressed public housing   HOPE VI enhances communities by decentralizing poverty and giving families an opportunity to live in mixed income neighborhoods with better educational and employment opportunities                                                                 Browse by
