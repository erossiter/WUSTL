    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Monday  April 2  2007     Senator Arlen Specter  the ranking member of the Senate Judiciary Committee made the following comment today following the U S  Supreme Court s decision to not to review the cases of Guantanamo detainees seeking to invoke their habeas rights     The Supreme Court has left open later review on the constitutionality of the habeas corpus legislation on an appeal from a decision of the D C  Circuit on a case brought by someone detained by the Combatant Status Review Tribunal   Since that will take some time  Congress should act promptly to enact the pending Specter Leahy bill to reverse last year  s legislation and reinstate habeas corpus for the Guantanamo detainees                                                                    Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
