    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases           The FBI has not followed its own rules                        Washington D C    Friday  March 9  2007     Senator Arlen Specter  ranking member of the Senate Judiciary Committee  made the following comments today at a press conference with Chairman Patrick Leahy       The inspector general s report shows a massive misuse by the FBI of the national security letters for law enforcement      And in order to give greater latitude to the FBI  the law was revised    with some substantial misgivings    to require only that there be a showing of relevancy   But there had to be checks  and the FBI has not followed its own rules     And  if it weren t for the fact that  as Senator Leahy noted  we put in a special requirement for an inspector general s report  we would never have found out about it     And this is  regrettably  part of an ongoing process where the federal authorities are not really sensitive to privacy and go far beyond what we have authorized   And we have tried to give them broad authority because of the nature of the problem     But  as Senator Leahy notes  there ll be oversight hearings   And I think we may have to go farther than that and change the law to revise the Patriot Act to impose statutory requirements and perhaps take away some of the authority which we ve already given to the FBI since they appear not to be able to know how to use it                                                                    Browse by
