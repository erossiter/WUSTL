    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Friday  September 2  2005                      Senator Arlen Specter  the senior Senator from Pennsylvania  made the following comments today regarding Hurricane Katrina     Hurricane Katrina has caused a national disaster of unprecedented magnitude   It is heartbreaking to see the deaths  injuries  and massive destruction of property depicted on television   America will respond to assist and share the burden of our fellow citizens who have suffered Katrina  s wrath   As a start  the Senate acted last night to appropriate  10 5 billion in emergency aid   Americans  through Congressional action  will do whatever it takes to assist in rebuilding the lives and property of Katrina  s victims                                                                    Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
