    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C     Friday  July 27  2007     U S  Senator Arlen Specter  ranking member on the Senate Judiciary Committee  today circulated a discussion draft of an immigration proposal  The proposal seeks to provide a plausible and alternative path to achieving comprehensive immigration reform during this Congress     Detailed in a letter circulated to the every member of the Senate  the modified proposal makes two key changes to the previous comprehensive immigration reform legislation  S 1639   1  eliminates the path to citizenship for immigrants  and 2  does not tamper with the current family unity provisions     Although the defeat of the bill on the Senate floor was a major disappointment  I think that we proponents of comprehensive immigration reform have significant momentum and these changes  perhaps supplemented by other modifications  could put us over the top  Specter wrote                                                                 Browse by                                                                  Current record
