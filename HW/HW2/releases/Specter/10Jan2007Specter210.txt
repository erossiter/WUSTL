    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Wednesday  January 10  2007                      Senator Arlen Specter  the Senior Senator from Pennsylvania  made the following comments regarding President Bush s speech     After listening to the President s speech  I remain skeptical that an additional 20 000 troops will produce victory because the professional military advisors have said those troops will not win the war and the record shows that the Iraqis cannot be relied upon to uphold their obligations under the plan    I am concerned that more U S  troops will provide more targets for the insurgents and increase Iraqis  reliance on the U S   rather than compel the Iraqis to reach a political solution among the warring Sunnis and Shiites and push Iraq to provide the necessary force structure for their own security    I look forward to the President s promised full briefing by his National Security team and the hearings later this week in the Senate Armed Services Committee before the anticipated vote in the Senate next week                                                                  Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Current record
