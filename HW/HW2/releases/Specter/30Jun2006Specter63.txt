                                                                         News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Friday  June 30  2006     Senator Rick Santorum R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  and Senator Arlen Specter R PA   the Senior Senator from Pennsylvania  today announced that the Federal Emergency Management Agency has issued a disaster declaration for the following eight counties  Bradford  Bucks  Columbia  Luzerne  Northampton  Northumberland  Susquehanna  and Wyoming   For a period of up to 72 hours  assistance for debris removal and emergency protective measures  including direct Federal assistance  will be provided at 100 percent of the total eligible costs   On June 29  2006  Senators Santorum and Specter urged President Bush to make the formal major disaster declaration for the Commonwealth to trigger the release of federal funds to help communities recover from the severe storms and flooding that began June 23  2006   The letter from the Senators supplemented Governor Rendell  s request that President Bush provide federal assistance for Pennsylvania counties that suffered severe damage from the storm     Senator Santorum visited Luzerne  Bucks and Susquehanna counties and is scheduled to tour flood damaged areas in Columbia  Susquehanna  Northampton  and Bucks counties tomorrow with Governor Rendell   Senator Specter toured flood damaged areas in Bucks County on Friday                                                                 Browse by                                                 Current record
