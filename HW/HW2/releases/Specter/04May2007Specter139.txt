    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               State College   Friday  May 4  2007     Pennsylvania Sen  Arlen Specter toured Penn State s Applied Research Laboratory  ARL  to be briefed on the lab s large unmanned undersea vehicle  Specter  senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  visited ARL to see a major ongoing Department of Defense Research Project  the Large Unmanned Undersea Vehicle  LUUV     LUUVs are submerged  self propelled units that allow the Navy to reduce the vulnerability  cost and risk associated with manned  undersea missions  The LUUV Test Bed at University Park is a collaborative  ongoing project with the Navy to develop a vehicle with a mission enhancing technologies  Since fiscal year 2005  Specter has secured  7 2 million for the LUUV project     Development of the Large Unmanned Undersea Vehicle is very important for our national defense and the mission of the United States Navy   Specter said   I am pleased to see Penn State s excellent research team collaborate with the Navy on this impressive project                                                                  Browse by                                                                                   Current record
