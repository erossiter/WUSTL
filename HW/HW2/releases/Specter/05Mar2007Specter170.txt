                                                                         News Room News Releases          Coalition Will Work to Implement Annual Celebration of Month                      Washington  D C    Monday  March 5  2007                      Today  Sen  Arlen Specter  PA  and Rep  Debbie Wasserman Schultz  FL 20  applauded the formation of the Jewish American Heritage Month Coalition to shape and implement programs each May   The Jewish American Heritage Month  JAHM  Coalition will be convened by United Jewish Communities  the Jacob Rader Marcus Center of the American Jewish Archives  and the American Jewish Historical Society and will consist of a steering committee that includes a number of organizations   The unique experiences and perspectives of the Coalition members are vital to the celebration of JAHM  and the future of this national observance     The creation of the JAHM Coalition is another great step forward in the movement to celebrate Jewish contributions to American history     Senator Specter said     This Coalition will guarantee that Jewish contributions to the fabric of our country will be taught and celebrated year after year     said Rep  Wasserman Schultz     The talents that the Jewish American Heritage Month Coalition members bring to the table will provide the Month with a strong foundation upon which to grow      The JAHM Coalition will move forward in the spirit in which it was formed  as a community driven movement to institutionalize the celebration of Jewish American Heritage Month
