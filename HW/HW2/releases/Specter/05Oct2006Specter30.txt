    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Thursday  October 5  2006     U S  Senator Arlen Specter  R PA   a member of the Homeland Security Appropriations Subcommittee  and U S  Senator Rick Santorum  R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  announced today that the Department of Homeland Security has approved funding for several fire companies in northwestern Pennsylvania       Ensuring that Pennsylvania  s first responders get the support they need from the federal government is one of the most important things I do as a United States Senator     said Senator Santorum     This funding will help to provide the knowledge and skills necessary for our firefighters to better protect Pennsylvania families from fire and fire related hazards         Our Pennsylvania firefighters risk their lives every day to ensure the safety of millions of people     Senator Specter said     This grant will help assure their protection as they safeguard our communities   I commend the Department of Homeland Security for recognizing this priority and for its continued support of the nation  s fire services      These grants represent the first round of funding from the Department of Homeland Security  s Office of State and Local Government Coordination and Preparedness  SLGCP  and the United States Fire Administration  s Assistance to Firefighter Grant Program     This program is designed to provide an opportunity for the U S  Congress and SLGCP to enhance fire service in locations across the country by providing funding for firefighting operations  firefighter safety  EMS delivery  vehicle purchase  and prevention programs     The following northwestern Pennsylvania companies were approved for funding         239 400 for Lafayette Township Volunteer Fire and Rescue Department  Inc  in McKean County         122 911 for East End Volunteer Fire Department in Mercer County         80 180 for Belle Valley Fire Department Inc  in Erie County         54 075 for Sugarcreek Township Volunteer Fire Company in Clarion County         53 666 for Wurtemburg Perry Township Volunteer Fire Department in Lawrence County          49 318 for Glade Volunteer Fire Department in Warren County         17 100 for Titusville Fire Department in Crawford County   Grant recipients can visit the AFG website link for information  http   www firegrantsupport com   or call the toll free help line at  866 274 0960
