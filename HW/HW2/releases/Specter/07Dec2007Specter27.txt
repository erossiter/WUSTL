                                                                         News Room News Releases          Loan Servicers  Victims  Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac to Testify                       Middletown   Friday  December 7  2007     Senator Arlen Specter  along with Senator Bob Casey and Congressman Tim Holden  will hold a hearing on the Lancaster and Berks area Homeowners mortgage crisis    DATE TIME  3 00pm on Monday  December 10  2007     Media Availability to follow immediately after hearing  circa 4 15pm     LOCATION  Penn States Harrisburg Campus   Student Center  Room 210  Capital Union Bldg    777 West Harrisburg Pike   Middletown  PA 17057     Senator Arlen Specter  in conjunction with Senator Bob Casey and Congressman Tim Holden  will hold a field hearing in Harrisburg to examine the mortgage crisis facing many Lancaster and Berks area homeowners who were defrauded in a mortgage based Ponzi scheme    Representatives from the loan services  Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac are scheduled to testify regarding what they can do to help distressed homeowners  Several victims will also speak on their experience     Many Lancaster and Berks area homeowners were defrauded by mortgage broker Wesley Snyder when he induced them to borrow more money than necessary from a bank when refinancing their homes  Snyder then stole much of the surplus funds  using them for  among other things  to pay him and his wife millions of dollars in salary and to keep his company afloat  The homeowners only recently discovered they had large  outstanding mortgage obligations  Snyder recently pleaded guilty to one count of felony mail fraud                                                                       Browse by
