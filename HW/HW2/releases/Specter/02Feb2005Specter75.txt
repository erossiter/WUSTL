    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Wednesday  February 2  2005                      U S  Senator Arlen Specter  Chairman of the Senate Judiciary Committee  made the following comments following President Bush  s State of the Union address     I was pleased to hear the President  s emphasis on education with increasing Pell grants  and his proposals for health savings accounts   His ideas on Social Security certainly deserve a full national hearing   It is a complicated subject  but we need to consider what the President has in mind and in some detail     I believe Congress will grant his request for  350 million for the Palestinian Authority to promote peace in the Mid East   He was right on target to demand more from the Saudis in terms of helping in the Mid East peace process  to call on Egypt to do more as they have upcoming elections and to demand that Syria oust the terrorists from Damascus       Overall it was a confident speech and I think a very good one                                                                    Browse by                                                                                   Current record
