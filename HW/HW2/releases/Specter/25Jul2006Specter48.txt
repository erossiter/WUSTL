    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases          Funding is Approved in the Fiscal Year 2007 LHHS Appropriations Bill                      Washington  D C    Tuesday  July 25  2006     U S  Senators Rick Santorum  R PA   Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  and Arlen Specter  R PA   Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education  announced today the U S  Senate Appropriations Committee has approved funding for several western Pennsylvania healthcare projects   The projects are contained in the Fiscal Year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill     I applaud my colleagues in the Committee for approving the Health and Human Services funding for projects in western Pennsylvania     Senator Santorum said     The funding will allow communities to purchase new equipment  construct new health care facilities  and provide the most advanced health care modernizations which will aid in the health and wellness of the citizens of western Pennsylvania         I am pleased my colleagues have approved this important funding for western Pennsylvania     Senator Specter said     These projects are vital to ensuring quality primary and emergency care  as well as advanced preventative care measures  for the area  s residents      The bill will now await action on the Senate floor   Western Pennsylvania healthcare projects in the bill include         800 000 for Allegheny General Hospital in Allegheny County to expand its operating room suites         800 000 for Allegheny Singer Research Institute in Allegheny County for a state of the art human performance laboratory         800 000 for Carnegie Mellon University in Allegheny County to purchase a confocal microscope and to retrofit laboratory space         800 000 for University of Pittsburgh Cancer Institute in Allegheny County for equipment to complement and expand their current Clinical Proteomics Facility  CPF                  800 000 for University of Pittsburgh Medical Center in Allegheny County to continue the Allegheny County Minority Women  s Heart Initiative through the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center  This study will address the impact of depression upon the clinical care and prevention of cardiac events in African American women with at least one risk factor for cardiovascular disease and depression         500 000 for Children s Hospital of Pittsburgh in Allegheny County for construction of their new facility  a technologically advanced pediatric hospital   The new hospital will alleviate severe equipment and infrastructure shortfalls at the existing location         500 000 for Magee Women s Research Institute in Allegheny County to expand their research facility   This will house laboratories  offices  conference rooms  and support facilities of the Pittsburgh Development Center         500 000 for University of Pittsburgh in Allegheny County for rare disease therapeutic research         450 000 for Somerset Hospital in Somerset County for technical improvements and new equipment including three auto dispensing stations for pharmaceuticals and a replacement of the hospital s core information system         250 000 for Tyrone Hospital in Blair County for digital radiology imaging technology         250 000 for Pittsburgh Mercy Health System in Allegheny County for construction of a state of the art bi plane angiography suite with 3D capabilities and access to full operating room capabilities for surgical intervention  This integrated interventional and surgical suite will allow real time intra operative imaging to permit more aggressive  safer  surgical intervention for patients suffering from stroke and other intracranial vascular abnormalities         250 000 for Excela Health Latrobe Hospital in Westmoreland County to expand the emergency department         250 000 for Meadville Medical Center in Crawford County for construction and equipment to create a new cancer care center         250 000 for the Children  s Hospital of Pittsburgh to provide for space to meet the growing demands for children  s services         200 000 for Children s Home of Pittsburgh in Allegheny County for additional space to allow for a dependent infants ventilator  and for continuing care by neonatal and pediatric nurses  maintaining a low nurse to patient ratio  and continuing administration of expert family centered hospice care to infants born with congenital anomalies         200 000 for Sharon Regional Health System in Mercer County to purchase a digital mammography system         200 000 for the Dr  Gertrude Barber National Institute in Erie to expand the quality of effective service delivery for individuals with autism spectrum disorders                                                                 Browse by
