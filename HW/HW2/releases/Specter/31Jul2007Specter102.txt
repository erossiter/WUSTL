    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Tuesday  July 31  2007     U S  Senator Arlen Specter  R Pa   ranking member of the Senate Judiciary Committee  today issued the following statement regarding S  742 Ban Asbestos in America Act of 2007      I commend the Environment and Public Works Committee  which today reported S 742  the Ban Asbestos in America Act of 2007  If enacted  S  742  sponsored by Senator Patty Murray  will help protect American workers and their families from future exposure to asbestos  However  an asbestos ban does not solve the problem that has been plaguing our courts for years  forcing many employers into bankruptcy and leaving thousands of workers without compensation      A comprehensive approach is needed to resolve the asbestos exposure debacle  Last Congress  I sponsored the Fairness in Asbestos Injury Resolution  FAIR  Act  The FAIR Act created a privately funded trust to compensate victims of asbestos exposure in an equitable and prompt manner  The bill was narrowly defeated on the Senate floor by a procedural maneuver  but I will not give up the fight to resolve this problem  I remain committed to passing legislation that will ensure prompt compensation for workers with mesothelioma  including veterans who have no one to sue in the tort system  I invite Senator Murray and other members of Congress to join me in continuing to work on a comprehensive solution to this problem by supporting legislation that goes beyond a ban on asbestos
