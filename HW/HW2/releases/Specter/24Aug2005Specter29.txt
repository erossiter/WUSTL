    United States Senator Arlen Specter  Pennsylvania   News Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                 News Room News Releases                               Washington  D C    Wednesday  August 24  2005     Senator Arlen Specter  the Senior Senator from Pennsylvania  issued the following statement today on the Base Realignment and Closures Commission  s voting     While the BRAC Commission  s actions today will result in job losses for a portion of Pennsylvania  which is disappointing  there is a bright spot in today  s decisions   Letterkenny and Tobyhanna Army depots are expected to receive hundreds of additional jobs   Pennsylvania has done better on this base closing activity than in any time in the past   I applaud the state and local governments  local communities  and the Congressional delegation for the tremendous effort they put into ensuring Pennsylvania is not as severely impacted in this round of BRAC as it has been before   Secretary Rumsfeld has stated that the communities which have been economically hard hit will be given economic aid and that is something which we will be pursuing                                                                    Browse by                                                                  Current record
