SEN. BURNS ISSUES STATEMENT ON NEW BLM STATE DIRECTOR FOR MONTANA
  Today Sen. Conrad Burns (R - Mont.) issued the following statement on the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) naming Gene Terland State Director for Montana and the Dakotas: 
  "As Chairman of the Senate Interior Appropriations Subcommittee, I look forward to meeting with Gene Terland in the near future to discuss the critical public land usage and management issues facing Montana. Terland's experience as Associate State Director for Utah is encouraging, and his many years in Montana previously will be helpful."
  "Gene's background and experience form a great fit for the resource issues facing Montana and the Dakotas," said BLM Director Kathleen Clarke.
