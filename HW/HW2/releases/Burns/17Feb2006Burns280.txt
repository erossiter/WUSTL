SEN. BURNS, BOZEMAN FIELD REP. CARLSON TO HOLD HOURS IN VIRGINIA CITY
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) announced today that his Bozeman Field Rep. Dennis Carlson, will hold community outreach hours in Belgrade on Friday, February 24, 2006. 
   Carlson will be available from 11:00am - 1:30pm at the Madison County Courthouse, 110 East Wallace Street, Virginia City, MT 59755. He is available to meet with anyone who has a problem with a federal agency or questions or concerns regarding issues before Congress.
   Sen. Burns said, "I make sure my staff spends time all over the state, in small towns, reaching out so that we understand how government affects your lives and how we can help you with any concerns you may have. Dennis is there on my behalf and will relay your concerns directly to me."
   If you have any questions, please feel free to call Dennis Carlson at (406) 586-4450 or (800) 344-1513.
