SEN. BURNS ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF TRANSPORTATION SECRETARY MINETA
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R - Mont.) issued the following statement regarding the news that U.S. Secretary of Transportation Norman Mineta announced his resignation today: 
  "I would like to congratulate and thank Secretary Mineta for his public service. As Transportation Secretary, we worked closely together on a number of important issues, such as the Beartooth Highway restoration and funding for Going to the Sun Road. Although we did not always agree, we remained good friends, and I appreciated his dedication to improving our nation's transportation infrastructure. Secretary Mineta has given a large portion of his life to public service, and this country owes him a debt of gratitude. I wish him the very best in all of his future endeavors.
  Senator Burns is senior member of the Senate Transportation, Treasury, the Judiciary and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Subcommittee, which funds the U.S. Department of Transportation.
