SEN. BURNS' FIELD REPRESENTATIVE TO HOLD HOURS IN LIBBY
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) announced today that his Kalispell Field Representative, Denise Smith, will hold community outreach hours in Libby on January 23, 2006.  
   Smith will be available from 12pm - 1:30pm at the Libby Memorial Center, 111 East Lincoln Blvd. Smith is available to meet with anyone who has a problem with a federal agency or questions or concerns regarding issues before Congress. 
   Sen. Burns said, "I instruct my staff to be available for constituents all over our state, reaching out to our many small towns and rural communities. We need to constantly improve our understanding of how government affects your lives and how we can help. Denise is there on my behalf and will relay your concerns to me." 
   If you have any questions, please feel free to call Denise Smith at (406) 257-3360.
   Contact: James (JP) Pendleton, 406/252-0550.
