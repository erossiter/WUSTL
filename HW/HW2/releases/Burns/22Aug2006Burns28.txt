SEN. BURNS ANNOUNCES STAFF OUTREACH IN SEELEY LAKE
  Today Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) announced that his Missoula Field Representative, Larry Anderson, will hold community outreach hours in Seeley Lake on Thursday, August 31 from 2pm - 4pm at the Seeley Lake RS. He will be available to meet with anyone who has a problem with a federal agency or questions or comments regarding issues before Congress. 
   Senator Burns said, "With all the distances we have to travel in our state, I like to have my staff visit communities to save folks the trip to our Missoula field office. Please take a moment to stop by and visit with Larry if you have comments or questions, and feel we might be able to assist you. Larry will let me know what's on your mind and we'll do what we can to help out."
   If you have any questions, please feel free to call Larry Anderson at (406) 728-3003 or 800/344-1513.
