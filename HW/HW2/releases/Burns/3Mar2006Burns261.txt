SEN. BURNS ANNOUNCES $5.3 MILLION GRANT TO GREAT FALLS INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) is pleased to announce the awarding of $5,300,000 to the Great Falls International Airport from the U.S. Department of Transportation. 
   The money will be used to install modern approach instruments that will increase access and safety on landings in poor weather conditions. 
   Senator Burns said, "This is wonderful news for the Great Falls Airport and the people of Great Falls. Increasing safety is everyone's top priority when it comes to air travel, and this will do just that."
