SEN. BURNS WELCOMES GOV. KEMPTHORNE TO INTERIOR
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) today issued the following statement following the confirmation of Gov. Dirk Kempthorne (R - ID) to be Secretary of the Interior, by a voice vote: 
  "As Chairman of the Senate Interior Appropriations Subcommittee, I am pleased I will be able to work closely with my friend Dirk Kempthorne as Interior Secretary. We have important work to do at Interior and I've discussed with Dirk my commitment to major rehabilitation for the St. Mary diversion facilities and the full funding of the Secure Rural Schools program. I congratulate Dirk on his confirmation and look forward to working with him in the near term on the important issues facing us."
  Senator Burns is the Chairman of the Senate Interior Appropriations Subcommittee and serves on the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee.
