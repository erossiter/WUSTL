SEN. BURNS ANNOUNCES FIRE DISTRICT FUNDS FOR ENNIS
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) today announced funding for Madison Valley Rural Fire District in Ennis.  
 Through the Department of Homeland Security's Assistance to Firefighters Grant Awards program, Madison Valley Rural Fire District will receive $175,863 for the purchase of a new firefighting vehicle. 
   "Our firefighters are on the front lines responding to very dangerous situations, and they need to have everything they can to complete their job in a safe and timely fashion," said Burns. "Time and again these folks sacrifice their safety for our own, so I'm pleased to see Madison Valley Rural Fire District receive this important funding today." 
   The United States Fire Administration (USFA) is a division of the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) within the Department of Homeland Security. The mission of the USFA is to reduce life and economic losses due to fire and related emergencies, through leadership, advocacy, coordination, and support. For more information on USFA, please contact the Website at http://www.usfa.fema.gov.
