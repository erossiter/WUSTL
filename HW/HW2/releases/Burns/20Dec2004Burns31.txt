SEN. BURNS, REP. REHBERG ANNOUNCE ACADEMY APPOINTMENTS
  Sen. Conrad Burns and Rep. Denny Rehberg today announced their selections to the U.S. Service Academies for the next term. The students were selected by an advisory board comprised of retired service personnel and community leaders from across Montana. 
   "These young men and women are some of Montana's best and brightest," said Rehberg and Burns in a joint statement. "They have excelled in their studies and their communities, and will make fine leaders in the armed forces of our Nation."
