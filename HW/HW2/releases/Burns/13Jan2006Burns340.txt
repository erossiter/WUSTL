SEN. BURNS' FIELD REPRESENTATIVE TO HOLD HOURS IN DARBY, HAMILTON
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) announced today that his Missoula Field Representative, Larry Anderson, will hold community outreach hours in Darby and Hamilton on January 19, 2006.  
   Anderson will be available from 10am - 12pm at the Darby Library, 101 S. Marshall, Darby, MT 59829. He will also be available from 3-5pm at the County Administrative Office, Planning Conference Room, 215 South Fourth Street, Hamilton, MT 59840. Anderson is available to meet with anyone who has a problem with a federal agency or questions or concerns regarding issues before Congress. 
   Sen. Burns said, "I make sure my staff spends time all over the state, in small towns, reaching out so that we understand how government affects your lives and how we can help. Larry is there on my behalf and will relay your concerns to me." 
   If you have any questions, please feel free to call Larry Anderson at (406) 728-3003 or (800) 344-1513.
