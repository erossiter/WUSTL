SEN. BURNS, BOZEMAN FIELD REP. PILSTER TO HOLD HOURS IN BIG TIMBER
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) announced today that his Bozeman Field Rep. Misti Pilster, will hold community outreach hours in Big Timber on Thursday, March 2, 2006.  
   Pilster will be available from 3:00 - 4:00 at the Sweet Grass County Courthouse, 200 West First Avenue, Big Timber, MT 59011. She is available to meet with anyone who has a problem with a federal agency or questions or concerns regarding issues before Congress. 
   Sen. Burns said, "I make sure my staff spends time all over the state, in small towns, reaching out so that we understand how government affects your lives and how we can help you with any concerns you may have. Misti is there on my behalf and will relay your concerns directly to me." 
   If you have any questions, please feel free to call Misti Pilster at (406) 586-4450 or (800) 344-1513.
