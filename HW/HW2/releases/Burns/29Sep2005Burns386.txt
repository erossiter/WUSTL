SEN. BURNS PRAISES CONFIRMATION OF JUDGE ROBERTS TO SUPREME COURT
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) praised the Senate confirmation of Judge John Roberts to the Supreme Court today in a vote of 78-22.  
 Burns reaction to the confirmation will be made available via satellite feed today at 1:45pm MT. 
   "It is important for the state of Montana that we have a qualified, fair, and competent Supreme Court Justice on the bench, and this is the kind of judge we have in John Roberts," said Burns. "He is a man of strong character who has stated time and time again that his allegiance is to the Constitution, and that is what we should all look for in an individual who will be seated on the Supreme Court. I was happy to vote in favor of Judge Roberts, and I am confident he will serve our country well in this new position."
