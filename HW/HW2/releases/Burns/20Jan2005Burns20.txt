SEN. BURNS TO ATTEND PRESIDENT'S INAUGURATION
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) will attend the inauguration of President George W. Bush today, followed by his participation in the inaugural parade with Montana businessman Buford Madsen. Burns invited Madsen and his draft horses and carriage to participate in the parade along with the Montana Congressional delegation. 
   Audio of Burns' reaction to the inauguration and inaugural parade will be available after the inauguration on his website, and via voicemail at (800) 545-1267, box 333.
