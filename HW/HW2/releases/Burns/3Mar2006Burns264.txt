SEN. BURNS ANNOUNCES $2.9 MILLION GRANT TO IMPROVE FIRE, RESCUE SERVICES AT MISSOULA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
  Sen. Conrad Burns (R-Mont.) is pleased to announce the awarding of $2,950,000 to the Missoula International Airport from the U.S. Department of Transportation.  
   The money will be used to expand the aircraft rescue and fire fighting building, as well as miscellaneous improvements to the airport. 
   Senator Burns said, "This grant will help greatly in maintaining the important infrastructure of Missoula's airport. The money will upgrade and maintain one of Montana's vital links to the rest of the country, which will help keep Montana's economy rolling through business travel as well as tourism. This is great new for Missoula."
