 
		    
            Wednesday, November 1, 2006 
           

  WASHINGTON - U.S. Senator Sam Brownback today commended American men and women in uniform for their dedicated service to our country.     
"Every year I help send some of the best and brightest young Kansans to our country's military academies.  Many of the most brilliant people I have ever met wear military uniforms."     
Brownback responded to recent controversial statements regarding the intelligence of the men and women serving in Iraq and elsewhere.     
"We owe a great debt of gratitude to all our men and women in uniform for their service to this nation."     
Brownback serves on the Senate Appropriations Foreign Operations subcommittee.
