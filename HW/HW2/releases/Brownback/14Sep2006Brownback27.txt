 
		    
            Thursday, September 14, 2006 
           

  BROWNBACK ANNOUNCES $10,250 IN GRANTS TO HORSE CREEK RANCH     
WASHINGTON - U.S. Senator Sam Brownback today announced the U.S. Department of Agriculture has awarded a $10,250.00 grant to Horse Creek Ranch in Wabaunsee County, Kansas.         
"I am pleased to announce this award to Horse Creek Ranch," said Brownback.  "This important grant money will help a vital Kansas agricultural business remain competitive in the national economy."       
The money was awarded to Horse Creek Ranch under the USDA Value-Added Agricultural Product Market Development grant program.  According to the USDA, this grant program aids independent producers of agricultural commodities, agricultural producer groups, farmer and rancher cooperatives, and majority-owned producer-based business ventures.       
Senator Brownback is a member of the Appropriations Committee and the Judiciary Committee.
