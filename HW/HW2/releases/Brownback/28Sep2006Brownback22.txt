 
		    
            Thursday, September 28, 2006 
           

  WASHINGTON - U.S. Senator Sam Brownback today announced the U.S. Department of Commerce has awarded a total of $905,803 in grants for improvements to communications systems in Hutchinson and Wichita, Kansas.       
"I am pleased to see this important grant money go to Hutchinson and Wichita," said Brownback.  "As a former broadcaster, I know the importance of maintaining up to date communications technology."     
According to the Department of Commerce, $695,803 has been awarded to KHCC-FM in Hutchison for the construction of a new 1,300 foot transmission tower.  An additional $210,000 has been awarded to KPTS-TV in Wichita for an analog studio production switcher and character generator with digital equipment.       
Senator Brownback is a member of the Appropriations Committee and the Judiciary Committee.
