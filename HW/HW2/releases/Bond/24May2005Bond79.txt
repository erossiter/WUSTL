
WASHINGTON, DC U.S Senator Kit Bond issued the following statement on the deal brokered by seven Democrats and seven Republicans on President Bushs judicial nominations: It is good to know that three of the well-qualified judges nominated by President Bush will likely have a simple 51 vote majority confirmation. The jubilation over the deal, however, suggests that this is the first time a band-aid has been invented. The problem with this band-aid is that it takes out of the hands of the surgeon the scalpel needed to fix the underlying problem.
