Senate Resolution Honors Patriot Guard Riders Conrad Legislation Salutes Group for Supporting Families of Fallen Soldiers                   June 12  2006  Fargo    Senator Kent Conrad led a patriotic ceremony today as a salute to members of the Patriot Guard Riders for their efforts to protect the honor of military funerals  It is an amazing scene to see the Patriot Guard Riders standing shoulder to shoulder honoring America s fallen soldiers  Their presence provides comfort to a heartbroken family and protects the sanctity of a soldier s memorial service  Senator Conrad said  Members of an American Legion motorcycle club in Kansas formed the Patriot Riders in 2005  The bikers came together to protect families of fallen soldiers after a Kansas based church began picketing military funerals  Followers of the radical Westboro Baptist Church are known to protest military funerals because they believe U S  soldiers are fighting for an army that represents a country that accepts homosexuality  In just over a year  the Patriot Guard Riders have grown into a nationwide network of more than 45 000 people  many of who are veterans  Patriot Guard Riders attend military funerals across the country and form a human wall to shield the grieving family from protesters  They hold up large American flags to cover picket signs and rev the engines of their motorcycles to drown out the chanting of protesters outside the memorial services  Last month  the U S  Senate unanimously approved Senator Conrad s resolution honoring the Patriot Guard Riders  Senator Conrad presented a copy of his resolution today to Ron Van Dorn  a representative from the Patriot Guard Riders  Earlier this year  Senator Conrad attended the funeral services for two North Dakota soldiers killed in Iraq  Michael Hermanson in Fargo  and Paul Beyer in Jamestown  Protestors from the Westboro Baptist Church tried to disrupt the funerals but members of the Patriot Guard were on hand to protect the sanctity of the services  This Senate resolution salutes those who protect the dignity of our fallen soldiers  the Patriot Guard Riders  Senator Conrad said  They are true patriots  escorting our fallen soldiers to their final rest  Allowing loved ones to say goodbye with peace and dignity
