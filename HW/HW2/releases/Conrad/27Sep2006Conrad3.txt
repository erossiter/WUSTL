Search Begins for Community with Entrepreneurial Edge Conrad and Johnson Look for  Entrepreneurial Community of the Year                    September 27  2006  Fargo    Senator Kent Conrad and North Dakota Agriculture Commissioner Roger  Johnson are calling for nominations for the 2007 Marketplace Entrepreneurial  Community of the Year Award  The annual award recognizes a North Dakota community s achievement   excellence and efforts to integrate entrepreneurial and businesslike thinking   The prestigious award will be presented during Marketplace for Entrepreneurs   January 17  2007  at the Fargodome  The entrepreneurial spirit is a combination of ingenuity  innovation and  persistence  That spirit is alive and well in North Dakota  Senator Conrad and  Commissioner Johnson said in a joint statement  This award recognizes those  dynamic communities in our great state where an entrepreneurial environment is  being developed  nurtured  and most importantly  enacted  The communities of Bismarck Mandan and the nonprofit Dakota Heartland  Champion Community  which represents McIntosh  Napoleon  Edgeley and Kulm  were  presented with the Marketplace Entrepreneurial Community of the Year Award last  year  Nomination forms for the 2007 Entrepreneurial Community of the Year Award are  available online at http   conrad senate gov  www agdepartment com  and  www marketplaceofideas org  Forms may also be obtained by calling Marketplace at  888 384 8410  Nominations must be received by November 17  2006  Marketplace is North Dakota s premier economic development effort  The  day long event brings together aspiring entrepreneurs  venture capitalists   corporations  universities  foundations and community and business leaders to  exchange ideas  discuss the latest innovations and share skills and technology   Senator Conrad and Commissioner Johnson are the organizing sponsors of  Marketplace  In January  more than 10 000 people turned out at the Fargodome for the 2006  Marketplace for Entrepreneurs  The annual economic development summit drew  visitors from across North Dakota and the region with the opportunity to view  more than 300 exhibits of innovative products and services and participate in  hundreds of different workshops  Our goal  the goal of Marketplace  is to empower people across North  Dakota and create a climate for entrepreneurship and innovation  The  Entrepreneurial Community of the Year Award highlights those places that support  an entrepreneurial climate  resulting in jobs and training North Dakota s future  business leaders  Conrad and Johnson said  Marketplace will be returning to the Fargodome on January 17  2007
