Conrad Renews Fight for National Energy Bill  We Must Take Bold Action on America s Energy Policy   Senator says                   June 13  2007 Washington  As the U S  Senate continues debate on national energy policy  Senate Kent Conrad today called for a plan to massively increase domestic energy production and bring relief to American consumers coping with near record gas prices  He pointed to his BOLD Energy Act as an example to follow when crafting comprehensive energy legislation    Our country faces serious energy challenges  Senator Conrad said  Our dependence on foreign energy threatens both our national security and our economic security  Unless we change course  we will become even more dependent on foreign energy sources and subject to even higher prices and uncertainty
