Delegation Announces  120 000 to Complete Three Affiliated Tribes  Cultural Center                    July 17  2007  Washington  Senators Kent Conrad and Byron Dorgan and Congressman Earl Pomeroy today announced that a federal grant for more than  120 000 has been secured for the Three Affiliated Tribes  The grant will help defray the final cost of the Fort Berthold Cultural Interpretive Center    This grant provides key resources for the completion of the new Cultural Interpretive Center  the delegation said in a joint statement  The Center is a tremendous addition to Fort Berthold  providing visitors with a better understanding of the rich history and culture of the Three Affiliated Tribes while contributing to the economic well being of the community    The  120 280 grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development through funds appropriated by Congress
