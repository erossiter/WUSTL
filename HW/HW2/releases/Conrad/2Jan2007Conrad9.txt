Conrad  President Ford a  Good and Decent  Man National Day of Mourning for President Ford                   January 2  2007  Washington    On Tuesday  Jan  2  the national day of mourning for President Gerald Ford  Senator Kent Conrad issued the following statement  Lucy and I extend our sincerest condolences to the family of President Ford  He was a good and decent man who led our country with honor  We appreciate the healing spirit he brought to our land at a difficult time in our history
