Statement by Senator Kent Conrad on Record Trade Deficit                    February 13  2007  Washington    Senator Kent Conrad released the following statement today regarding the news that the US Trade Deficit increased to  763 6 billion in 2006 from  716 7 billion in 2005  Today s announcement that the 2006 trade deficit hit a record  763 billion is yet more evidence that we are on an unsustainable course   This Administration s trade and fiscal policies are an utter failure that continues to mortgage the future of hard working Americans
