Delegation to Host Military Academy Day Event Helps North Dakota Students Learn About Nation s Service Academies                   April 4  2007  Washington    Senators Kent Conrad and Byron Dorgan and Congressman Earl Pomeroy announced today they will host a special Military Academy Day in Fargo on Saturday  April 14  2007   The event was put together by the congressional delegation to help local high school students learn how they can apply to one of the nation s military academies  Nominations to the U S  Air Force Academy  the U S  Naval Academy  the U S  Military Academy at West Point  and the U S  Merchant Marine Academy come principally from Congressional nominations from either a Senator or Representative  Our nation s military academies are among the world s finest institutions offering tough challenges and great rewards  They provide young people a free  first rate education while preparing them to serve our nation as an officer in the armed forces  the delegation said in a joint statement  The admission process for military academies is extremely competitive  The congressional delegation s information session will provide students interested in pursuing a nomination to one of the service academies with the proper guidance necessary to begin the nomination process   Staff members from the congressional delegation will be on hand at the event to discuss the application process and answer any questions  In addition  high school students will be able to speak with students who currently attend each of the service academies  allowing them to ask questions about life at the academies and careers in the military  Each year North Dakota s best and brightest seek admission to this country s military academies  the delegation said in a joint statement  This event is a forum where the future leaders of our nation can get the guidance and information they need before they apply to these prestigious institutions  Military Academy Day will be held April 14  from 1 00 to 3 00 p m  at Fargo South High School  South Campus II  1305 South 9th Avenue  The delegation plans to organize a similar event in western North Dakota later this year
