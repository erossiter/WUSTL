Statement from Senator Kent Conrad on the Resignation of Secretary of Veterans Affairs Jim Nicholson                    July 17  2007  Washington  Senator Kent Conrad issued the following statement today after the resignation of Secretary of Veterans Affairs Jim Nicholson  Every day in the Senate  I am reminded of America s solemn obligation to our veterans  Veterans deserve the best health care the federal government can possibly provide  With a new and growing generation of veterans returning from Iraq and Afghanistan  it should be the top priority of Secretary Nicholsons replacement to not only maintain  but to update the care provided by the Department of Veterans Affairs
