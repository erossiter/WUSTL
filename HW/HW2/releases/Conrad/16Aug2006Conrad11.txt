Patriot Guard Riders Saluted by Conrad Senator s Legislation Honors Group for Supporting Families of Fallen Soldiers                   August 16  2006  Bismarck    North Dakota veterans joined Senator Kent Conrad today at a  ceremony to present members of the Patriot Guard Riders with a Senate resolution  honoring the national organization for protecting the funeral services of fallen  American soldiers  The Patriot Guard Riders stand watch over the families and friends of our  fallen soldiers  Their presence protects the sanctity of a soldier s funeral  service and allows loved ones to say goodbye with peace and dignity  Senator  Conrad said  Members of an American Legion motorcycle club formed the Patriot Riders in  2005 after a Kansas based radical church began picketing military funerals   Followers of the Westboro Baptist Church are known to picket military funerals  because they believe U S  soldiers are fighting for a country that condones  homosexuality  Over the past year  the Patriot Guard Riders have grown into a nationwide  network of more than 45 000 people  many who are veterans  Patriot Guard Riders  attend military funerals across the country  They stand shoulder to shoulder to  form a human wall  shielding the grieving family from protesters  They hold up  large American flags to cover picket signs and rev the engines of their  motorcycles to drown out the chanting of protesters outside the memorial  services  Last month  the U S  Senate unanimously approved Senator Conrad s resolution  honoring the Patriot Guard Riders  Senator Conrad presented a copy of his  resolution to Patriot Guard Riders State Captain Randy Lindborg today  Earlier this year  Senator Conrad attended the funeral services for two North  Dakota soldiers killed in Iraq  Michael Hermanson in Fargo  and Paul Beyer in  Jamestown  Protestors from the Westboro Baptist Church tried to disrupt both  funerals but members of the Patriot Guard were on hand to protect the sanctity  of the services  It is an amazing scene to see the Patriot Guard Riders standing  shoulder to shoulder honoring those who died defending our freedom  Senator  Conrad said  This Senate resolution salutes those who protect the dignity of  fallen soldiers  the Patriot Guard Riders
