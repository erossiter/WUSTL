Statement by Senator Conrad on Retirement of Justice O Connor                     July 1  2005  Washington    Senator Kent Conrad released the following statement today after the retirement announcement by Supreme Court Associate Justice Sandra Day O Connor  I wish to congratulate Associate Justice Sandra O Connor on the announcement of her retirement  and I wish to extend to her my deep felt thanks for her service to the nation  Justice O Connor s tenure on the Nation s highest court has been distinguished by her independent thinking  She clearly approached each case with an open mind  Her independence should serve as a model to all jurists  A Republican her entire life  appointed by a Republican president  Justice O Connor showed that it is possible for a Justice to not rule from either the right or the left    but from the word of law and the facts of each case
