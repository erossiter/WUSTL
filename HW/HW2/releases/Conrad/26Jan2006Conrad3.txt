Conrad Launches Cyber Salute to U S  Troops  North Dakotans Send Greetings for Soldiers Serving Overseas                    January 26  2006  Washington    During his annual Marketplace for Entrepreneurs business summit last week  Senator Kent Conrad gathered personal video greetings from dozens of North Dakotans as a salute to U S  troops stationed overseas  All of the clips gathered during the economic development conference in Fargo have been uploaded to the Senator s Web site  where troops with Internet access can now view them at their convenience  It was great to see so many heartfelt messages to our troops  It is important for our men and women in uniform to know how proud we are of them  Senator Conrad said  I now plan to share these messages with North Dakota military units stationed around the world  The video greetings were shot at Senator Conrad s official U S  Senate booth in the Fargodome at Marketplace for Entrepreneurs on January 18  The messages can now be viewed on the Senator s Web site at www conrad senate gov  The leadership of the North Dakota National Guard has agreed to pass word of these messages to the troops in the field  There are military personnel from North Dakota stationed all over the globe including  Soldiers from Company A  164th Engineer Battalion  Minot  are currently serving in Iraq  Soldiers of the 1 188th Air Defense Artillery  Grand Forks and Bismarck  are currently stationed at Ft  Lewis  WA  and Ft  Hood TX  preparing for deployment to Afghanistan  Soldiers of the 112th Command Aviation Battalion  Bismarck  are stationed in Bosnia Herzegovina  Soldiers of the 129th Mobile Public Affairs Detachment  Bismarck  have been alerted for a possible mobilization  Airmen from the North Dakota Air National Guard 119th Fighter Wing  Fargo  are currently serving in Iraq  Qatar  the United Arab Emirates  Germany and Japan  People from across the state participated in the taping  sharing messages for family and friends serving our nation around the world  Senator Conrad said  As you will see in the video  we are extremely proud of our troops  courage and commitment and truly grateful for their service to our country
