Energy Bill Means a Big Boost for ND  Conrad says Senator Applauds National Energy Bill But Says Even More Must Be Done                   June 22  2007 Washington  Senator Kent Conrad today applauded Senate passage of a national energy bill that contains many of the key initiatives from his BOLD Energy Act which he introduced last year to dramatically expand the production of renewable fuels  The Senate energy bill would bring a big boost to North Dakota agricultural and energy industries
