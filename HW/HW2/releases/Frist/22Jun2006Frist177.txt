SEN. FRIST PRAISES DEFEAT OF LEVIN, KERRY AMENDMENTS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after the Senate defeated the Levin and Kerry amendments to the FY07 Defense Authorization bill: 
   "Today the Senate defeated a bad policy that threatens our national security and poses unacceptable risks to Americans. Though Democrats proposed multiple and confusing strategies for withdrawal, it's clear that the Senate has rejected their plans for surrender and cut and run."
