SEN. FRIST CONGRATULATES CORKER
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) made the following statement after Bob Corker won the Tennessee Senate race: 
    "Bob Corker is a born leader, and I have every confidence he will proudly serve the people of Tennessee with honor and integrity. Congratulations to Bob, Elizabeth, and their daughters for running a strong race."
