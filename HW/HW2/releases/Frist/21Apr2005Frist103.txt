SEN. FRIST APPLAUDS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF NEGROPONTE AS DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL INTELLIGENCE
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after Senate confirmation of Ambassador John Negroponte to the post of Director of National Intelligence:  
   "I'm pleased by today's Senate confirmation of Ambassador John Negroponte to the post of Director of National Intelligence. He's widely regarded as one of America's most distinguished and respected public officials. As his background attests, Mr. Negroponte has tackled many difficult and sensitive missions, and he's earned a reputation as a skilled manager. Mr. Negroponte is superbly qualified for this new and challenging position. And we're fortunate to have a public servant of his caliber working hard to help keep America safe."
