SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON CIA DIRECTOR NOMINATION
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after President Bush announced the nomination of Gen. Michael Hayden to serve as director of the Central Intelligence Agency: 
   "General Hayden is a very qualified nominee with a long history of service to his country," Frist said. "He's run one of the most important programs in the War on Terror, a key effort to try and make America safer. I look forward to his prompt confirmation by the Senate."
