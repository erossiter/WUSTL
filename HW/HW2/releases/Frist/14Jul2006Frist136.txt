SEN. FRIST ANNOUNCES SCHEDULING FOR THE WATER RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT ACT
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today announced that, subject to a unanimous consent agreement being reached, the Senate will take up the Water Resources Development Act (WRDA) on Tuesday, July 18, following completion of action on stem cell legislation. If the unanimous consent agreement is reached, all action on WRDA will be completed on Wednesday, July 19.
