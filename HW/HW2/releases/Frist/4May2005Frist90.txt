SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON CAPTURE OF SENIOR AL-QAEDA LEADER
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) made the following statement today following the announced capture of senior al-Qaeda leader Abu Farraj al-Libbi: 
   "This arrest is a huge blow to the al-Qaeda network, and will produce tips and leads that will aid America's efforts to dismantle and destroy terrorist networks. I'm heartened by today's announcement, and confident that this arrest is another indication that the al-Qaeda network is on the defensive and that we're winning the War on Terror."
