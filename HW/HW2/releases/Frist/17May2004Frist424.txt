SEN. FRIST ISSUES STATEMENT ON APPOINTMENT OF GEN. PETRAEUS TO OFFICE OF SECURITY TRANSITION IN IRAQ
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) today applauded the Senate's approval of Army General David Petraeus to the grade of Lieutenant General and assignment as Chief of the Office of Security Transition in Iraq: 
   "I can think of no better individual to take on this important and difficult duty. General Petraeus' leadership, bravery and honor embody the values of a true American soldier. All those who have seen him can attest to his dedication and commitment to his troops. I greatly respect his leadership and the work that the 101st Airborne Division has accomplished in the war on terrorism."
