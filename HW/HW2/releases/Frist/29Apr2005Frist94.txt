SEN. FRIST HAILS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF JOHNSON AS EPA ADMINISTRATOR
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after Senate confirmation of Stephen Johnson as EPA Administrator: 
   "Stephen Johnson is a committed scientist who is passionate about our environment. I believe that he will be an outstanding administrator that will follow in the effective footsteps of Administrators Leavitt and Whitman. Mr. Johnson has unique institutional knowledge of the challenges and opportunities facing the EPA. I look forward to working with him as we continue to protect our air, land and water and promote responsible environmental stewardship."
