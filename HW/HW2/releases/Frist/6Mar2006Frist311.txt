SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON RETIREMENT OF CHAIRMAN THOMAS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after House Committee on Ways and Means Chairman Bill Thomas announced that he will retire at the end of this year:  
   "Throughout his career, Bill Thomas has demonstrated effective leadership on a range of issues of principal importance to the American people. As Chairman of the House Ways and Means Committee, he's steered to passage countless pieces of vital legislation that have reduced trade barriers, cut taxes, stimulated the economy and protected the interests of all Americans. 
   "Smart, tenacious, hard-driving and steeped in the traditions and subtle nuances of the House, he's been a crucial voice that has helped deliver on the priorities of the American people. He leaves behind a storied legacy, and his presence will be missed. I wish him well as he enters a new stage in life."
