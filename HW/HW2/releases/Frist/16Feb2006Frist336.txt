WEEKLY REPORT FROM WASHINGTON BY SEN. FRIST
  I'm proud to introduce a new feature my office is offering to help keep you up-to-date on what Congress is doing to address the issues that matter most to you. 
   Border Security, Judicial Nominations, National Security and Taxes are just some of the issues you can sign-up for in order to receive periodic updates from me.
   By selecting the issues that you care about most, I can send along information that is specifically important to you. Please use the boxes to the left to register for updates on specific issues.
   If at any time you wish to change your profile or unsubscribe, you may either use the "Update My Profile" hyperlink on these emails or simply visit my website at http://frist.senate.gov.
   I encourage you to sign-up today so you can automatically receive periodic updates from me about the issues you care about.
   I look forward to hearing from you.
   Bill Frist
