SEN. FRIST ISSUES STATEMENT FOLLOWING SENATE PASSAGE OF HIGHWAY BILL CONFERENCE REPORT
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) this evening made the following statement after Senate passage of the Safe, Accountable, Flexible, and Efficient Transportation Equity Act: A Legacy for Users (SAFETEA-LU) highway bill conference report: 
   "Roads, bridges, rails and buses pave the path towards greater economic development. This legislation will encourage investment and stimulate economic growth throughout the country. America needs a modern transportation infrastructure to support the demands of 21st century commerce, and this funding ensures that America has the necessary resources to remain a leader in the global marketplace. I'm proud that we finally delivered this long-sought piece of legislation, and look forward to seeing how this bill will create jobs, generate growth and support the backbone of our thriving economy."
