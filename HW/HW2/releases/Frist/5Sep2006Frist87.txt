SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON AUGUST JOB NUMBERS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader, Sen. Bill Frist, M.D. (R-Tenn.) made the following statement after the Department of Labor announced the latest jobs report for the month of August: 
   "Despite Democratic derision, the economy continues to grow, adding jobs month after month. Today's announcement follows other good news that GDP growth in the second quarter outpaced previous estimates and median household income received a boost as well. By eliminating unnecessary spending and keeping taxes low, we can look forward to more good news to come."
