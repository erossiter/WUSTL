SEN. FRIST ANNOUNCES SENATE SCHEDULE FOR SECOND SESSION OF 109TH CONGRESS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today announced the 2006 Senate schedule for the second session of the 109th Congress: 
   January 18: 2nd session convenes.
  February 20 - 24: Presidents' Day Recess
  March 20 - 24: St. Patrick's Day Recess
  April 10 - 21: Easter Recess
  May 29 - June 2: Memorial Day Recess
  July 3 - 7: July 4th Recess
  August 7 - September 4: August Recess
  October 6: Target Adjournment
