SEN. FRIST HIGHLIGHTS LOWER DEDUCTIBLES FOR MEDICARE DRUG PLANS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement following the announcement of nationwide availability of low-cost, high-quality prescription drug plans under the Medicare program:  
   "Empowering Medicare consumers with choice and competitive pricing helps lower drug costs and expand access to coverage for millions of Americans. Today's announcement is great news for all Medicare beneficiaries, and proves that injecting competition into the process makes care more affordable, available and dependable. The landmark Medicare reform bill will improve care, financial security and quality of life for all seniors. Seniors and their families owe it to themselves to take a close look at plans available in their area."
