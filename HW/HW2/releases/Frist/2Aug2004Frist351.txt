SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON PRESIDENT'S INTELLIGENCE ANNOUNCEMENT
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) made the following statement today in response to the President's proposals: 
   "I welcome the President's announcement and his continued commitment to this issue. Senator Daschle and I have already put in place a framework to examine how to best move forward in securing our homeland. Committee hearings and preliminary work are under way, with several Senate Committees at work this month. 
   "I look forward to coordinating our efforts with the Administration and remain committed to an October 1st deadline for Senate consideration of a legislative package."
