SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON INITIAL MEETING OF INTELLIGENCE REFORM CONFERENCE
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) today made the following statement on the initial meeting of the Intelligence Reform Conference Committee: 
   "I am gratified that the initial meeting of the 9/11 conference met today, and applaud the selection of Congressman Pete Hoekstra as chairman of this vital bicameral, bipartisan effort to improve the work of our intelligence community to face the challenges of our war on terror. I remain hopeful that the conferees will finalize intelligence reform prior to the election, and am impressed with the spirit of dedication and energy expressed today by all members of the conference committee."
