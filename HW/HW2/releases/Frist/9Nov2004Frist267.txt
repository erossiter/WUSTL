SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON ATTORNEY GENERAL ASHCROFT, COMMERCE SECRETARY EVANS RESIGNATION
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statements on the resignations of Attorney General John Ashcroft and Commerce Secretary Don Evans: 
   "Attorney General John Ashcroft worked hard to ensure the safety and security of all Americans. His service came at a challenging time in our history. His dedication and commitment to fighting the war on terror has been critical to ensuring the safety of all Americans in our homeland. I respect his leadership and appreciate his service to the country."
    "Secretary Don Evans has done a tremendous job shepherding our economy out of recession and putting it back on track. He has been a vital and crucial member of the President's economic team. With the help of his tireless leadership, our economy has seen over 2 million jobs created in the last 14 months. He has an outstanding record of achievement and boldly led our economy during the unprecedented challenges we faced over the last four years."
