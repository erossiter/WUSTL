REP. FRIST HONORS REP. HASTERT AS LONGEST-SERVING REPUBLICAN SPEAKER
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement marking tomorrow's milestone of Dennis Hastert becoming the longest-serving Republican Speaker of the House:  
   "As Speaker, Dennis Hastert has acquired a reputation for advancing common-sense legislation. He consistently identifies realistic goals, paving the way for successful Medicare reform, education reform, and landmark tax relief. The House has achieved unprecedented success under his leadership. It's no wonder Denny has become the longest-serving Republican Speaker in history. It's been great to work with him as my partner since 2003, and I look forward to his continued leadership of the House."
