SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON CONFIRMATION OF CRAWFORD AS FDA COMMISSIONER
 U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after the Senate confirmed Lester Crawford as the new Food and Drug Administration Commissioner: 
   "Lester Crawford will provide needed leadership at the FDA. The FDA currently faces a number of challenges and responsibilities to ensure the safety of our nation's food and drug supplies, and Dr. Crawford's experience as acting commissioner will help make this a smooth transition for the agency. I'm pleased that the Senate was able to complete its responsibilities and confirm a strong leader to direct this critical agency."
