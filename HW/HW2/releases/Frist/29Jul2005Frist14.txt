SEN. FRIST'S COMMUNICATION DIRECTOR ISSUES STATEMENT ON SUPREME COURT CONFIRMATION HEARINGS
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist's Communication Director Bob Stevenson today made the following statement after Senator Arlen Specter's announcement concerning the schedule for upcoming Supreme Court confirmation hearings in the Judiciary Committee: 
   "In light of Chairman Specter's statement, Senator Frist plans to begin the full Senate floor debate on the nomination of John Roberts no later than Monday, September 26, 2005."
