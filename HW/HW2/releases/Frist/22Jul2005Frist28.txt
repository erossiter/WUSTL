SEN. FRIST'S COMMUNICATION DIRECTOR ISSUES STATEMENT REGARDING NATIVE HAWAIIAN GOVERNMENT REORGANIZATION ACT
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist's Communication Director Bob Stevenson today made the following statement regarding S. 147: 
   "The majority leader continues to work with colleagues to resolve outstanding issues regarding S. 147, the Native Hawaiian Government Reorganization Act, for its timely consideration in the Senate."
