SEN. FRIST URGES PROMPT HEARING FOR KAVANAUGH
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after the Senate Judiciary Committee announced it will hold a second hearing on the nomination of Brett Kavanaugh to the D.C. Circuit Court of Appeals: 
   "The Judiciary Committee should proceed expeditiously to complete the second hearing and report out Brett Kavanaugh. After three years of waiting, Brett Kavanaugh deserves a fair up-or-down vote without further delay. We remain on track for floor action by Memorial Day."
