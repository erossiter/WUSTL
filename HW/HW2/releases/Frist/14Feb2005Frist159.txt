SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF LESTER CRAWFORD AS FDA COMMISSIONER
 U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement regarding President Bush's nomination of Lester Crawford as FDA Commissioner: 
   "Lester Crawford is an outstanding candidate to serve as commissioner of the FDA. As acting commissioner for the past year, Dr. Crawford's demonstrated the expertise and superior leadership skills necessary to oversee this organization which affects the lives of all Americans. Dr. Crawford's eminently qualified to handle the challenges and responsibilities of keeping our nation's food and drug supply safe and secure. I'm pleased by President Bush's nomination of Dr. Crawford, and look forward to seeing his nomination move quickly through the Senate."
