SENS. FRIST, GREGG CALL FOR MAXIMUM BUDGETARY SAVINGS IN DEFICIT REDUCTION CONFERENCE REPORT
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) and Senate Budget Committee Chairman Judd Gregg (R-NH) issued the following statement calling on members from the House and Senate to achieve the maximum amount of savings in the Deficit Reduction Bill Conference Report: 
   "The deficit reduction bill is the most important fiscal spending legislation Congress will address this year. Last week the Senate acted. We are confident we can achieve more savings in conference and we are committed to enacting the highest possible savings into law."
