SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON INTERIOR SECRETARY NOMINATION
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after President Bush announced the nomination of Idaho Governor Dirk Kempthorne to be Secretary of the Interior: 
   "Dirk is a strong nominee for Interior Secretary. He's an outspoken advocate for America's parks and has a wealth of public service experience at both the state and federal levels. I look forward to his swift confirmation by the Senate."
