SEN. FRIST CONGRATULATES FORMER KNOXVILLE MAYOR ASHE ON CONFIRMATION AS U.S. AMBASSADOR TO POLAND
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) today made the following statement congratulating former Knoxville Mayor Victor Ashe on his confirmation as the U.S. ambassador to Poland: 
   "As a Tennessean, I'm proud of today's confirmation of Victor Ashe as the next U.S. ambassador to Poland. He has been a leading public servant in East Tennessee for decades. I'm confident that he'll meet this new challenge in Eastern Europe with the same commitment he put forth during his 16 years as the mayor of Knoxville. I wish Victor and his family the best of luck."
