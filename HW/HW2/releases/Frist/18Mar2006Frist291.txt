SEN. FRIST ANNOUNCES NEW FIELD REPRESENTATIVE IN TRI-CITIES OFFICE
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-TN) today named Stephanie Walker as field representative in his Kingsport office, which serves the Tri-Cities region. Walker succeeds Tim Whaley in the field representative position.  
   "Stephanie has been a great asset to our team, and I know she'll serve the people of Upper East Tennessee well," Frist said. "I appreciate Tim's years of service and his many great contributions to our staff and the Tri-Cities areas. I wish him all the best in his future endeavors." 
   Prior to becoming field representative, Walker served as staff assistant in Frist's Kingsport office.
