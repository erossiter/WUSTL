SEN. FRIST COMMENDS PRESIDENT KARZAI ON AFGHANISTAN'S FIRST DEMOCRATIC ELECTIONS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) made the following statement on the election of President Hamid Karzai in Afghanistan's first democratic elections:  
   "I would like to congratulate President Karzai and the people of Afghanistan on the successful completion of the first democratic elections in their nation's history. The Afghan elections represent an historic moment for the Afghani people in their quest for freedom and peace." 
   "These elections also mark a momentous victory for the forces of freedom in the broader Middle East. Across the world's most troubled region, people can now look to Afghanistan as a symbol of hope and democracy and witness the transformational power of liberty." 
   "A democratic Afghanistan deals a tremendous blow to freedom's enemies in the war on terror. The United States stands closely with President Karzai and the people of Afghanistan in their efforts to build their democracy, and I take great pride in welcoming Afghanistan into the community of democratic nations."
