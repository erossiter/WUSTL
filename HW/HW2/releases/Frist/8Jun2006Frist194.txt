SEN. FRIST HAILS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF SCHWAB AS U.S. TRADE REPRESENTATIVE
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after the Senate voted to confirm the nomination of Susan S. Schwab to serve as U.S. Trade Representative: 
   "In this era of increasing global competitiveness, it's imperative that we have an experienced trade representative to ensure that U.S. businesses get free and fair access to foreign markets. With her extensive experience and current position as deputy trade representative, Ambassador Schwab comes with impeccable credentials. I have every confidence in her ability to effectively represent our nation."
