SEN. FRIST APPLAUDS NOMINATION OF REP. COX AS SEC CHAIRMAN
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement on President Bush's nomination of Representative Christopher Cox (R-CA) as the next chairman of the Securities and Exchange Commission:  
   "Chris Cox will bring an experienced and steady hand to the Securities and Exchange Commission. With a career dedicated to public service, Chris Cox has the expertise needed to handle the intricacies of securities regulation. I'm confident that Representative Cox has the vision and leadership to continue restoring our nation's trust in corporate America. 
   "I look forward to working with Chris Cox and expect swift Senate confirmation, so he can begin fulfilling his duties to enforce regulations that protect the businesses and consumers that are driving America's thriving economy."
