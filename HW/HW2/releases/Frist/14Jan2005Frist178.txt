SEN. FRIST REMEMBERS DR. MARTIN LUTHER KING, JR.
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement regarding the living legacy of Dr.  
 Martin Luther King, Jr. whose birthday the nation will celebrate on Monday, January 17, 2005. 
   "Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. taught that every human life is equal and that each person has a duty to help others. His compassion and vigilance stemmed the tide of intolerance and has helped our nation continue to realize the true meaning of freedom. 
   "As we reflect upon Dr. King's legacy, I pray that we allow his spirit of hope and unity to spread values of peace and liberty throughout America and around the world. We must challenge ourselves everyday to ensure that Dr. King's powerful message continues to live on for generations to come."
