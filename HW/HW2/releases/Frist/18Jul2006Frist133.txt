SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON SENATE VOTE TO EXPAND STEM CELL RESEARCH FUNDING
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after Senate passage of legislation to provide expanded federal funding for embryonic stem cell research, prohibit fetus farming and explore alternative pluripotent stem cell therapies:
   "The Senate acted today to unleash the promise of stem cell research while respecting human life at its earliest stages."
