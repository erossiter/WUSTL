SEN. FRIST HAILS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF REP. PORTMAN AS U.S. TRADE REPRESENTATIVE
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement after Senate confirmation of Rep.  
 Rob Portman as U.S. Trade Representative: 
   "America competes in a global economy and we need an experienced Trade Representative to ensure that U.S. businesses get free and fair access to foreign markets. Congressman Portman is the right man to lead our trade policies. As many of my colleagues have pointed out, he's well-known as an experienced, talented, and thoughtful leader on these issues. He's highly respected by both Republicans and Democrats, and knows how to get things done. I'm glad the Senate has acted quickly to get him on the job."
