SEN. FRIST ISSUES STATEMENT ON ONE YEAR ANNIVERSARY OF LONDON TERROR ATTACK
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement on the one year anniversary of the London bombing attacks: 
   "My heart goes out to those in London today as they remember the vicious terrorist attacks of a year ago. In our struggle for the values and freedoms we share together as democracies, we must always remember those innocents struck down by heinous acts. Our two countries stand united against those who wish to do us harm and we will continue to use every tool available in this important war on terror."
