SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON PRELIMINARY DRUG INDUSTRY PRINCIPLES REGARDING DIRECT TO CONSUMER ADVERTISING
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement regarding preliminary guidelines proposed by PhRMA: 
   "I'm pleased that PhRMA is acting so quickly following my call for greater restrictions on out of control direct to consumer advertising. I look forward to reviewing the industry's complete and final recommendations and examining areas where improvements may be needed. I also await the formal, comprehensive study that I requested from the GAO."
