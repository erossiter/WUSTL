SEN. FRIST HAILS CONFIRMATION OF MILAN SMITH AS U.S. CIRCUIT JUDGE FOR NINTH CIRCUIT COURT OF APPEALS
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after the unanimous confirmation of Milan Smith as U.S. Circuit Judge for the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals: 
   "Today I congratulate Milan Smith on his unanimous Senate confirmation as U.S. Circuit Judge for the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals. The Ninth Circuit has long been a bastion of judicial activism, and we look forward to Judge Smith bringing to the court a renewed focus on interpreting our laws rather than legislating from the bench."
   Judge Smith is the 44th circuit judge nominated by President Bush and confirmed by the Senate. The overall vacancy rate for federal judges is now 5.7%, with a vacancy rate of 8.9% for circuit judges.
