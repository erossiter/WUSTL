SEN. FRIST PRAISES CONTINUED ACTION ON U.S. INDIA NUCLEAR DEAL
U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) made the following statement after Senate conferees were named for the U.S.-India Civil Nuclear Cooperation bill: 
 "Today Senate conferees were named for the U.S.-India Civil Nuclear Cooperation bill. Work will begin immediately with the intention of completing all action by week's end. Together with Senators Allen, Hagel and Lugar, we will work across the aisle with Senators Biden and Dodd to ensure that goal is met."
