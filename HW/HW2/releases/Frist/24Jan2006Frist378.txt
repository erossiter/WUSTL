SEN. FRIST CONGRATULATES STEPHEN HARPER ON ELECTION VICTORY
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement on the victory of the Conservative Party, led by Stephen Harper, in Canada's national elections: 
   "I congratulate Stephen Harper and the Conservative Party for their victory in yesterday's national elections in Canada. A strong, cooperative bilateral relationship between the United States and Canada is vital to the continued security and prosperity of both our countries. Under Mr. Harper's leadership, I am confident that our partnership will continue to grow and strengthen, and I look forward to working closely with him on issues of mutual interest and concern."
