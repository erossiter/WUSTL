SEN. FRIST PRAISES GATES CONFIRMATION
U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn) made the following statement after Dr. Robert Gates was confirmed to serve as Secretary of Defense: 
 "I want to congratulate Robert Gates on his confirmation as Secretary of Defense. His experience, knowledge and directness will make him a great leader for the Department of Defense. The fresh insights he brings to the table are just what America needs."
