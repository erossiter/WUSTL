SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON RELEASE OF AMERICAN HEALTH INFORMATION COMMUNITY CHARTER
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) made the following statement today after the introduction of the American Health Information Community (AHIC) charter: 
   "Today's announcement is a positive step towards developing a secure and private interoperable electronic health record system. There is a great deal of work to do in order to reach the goal of an electronic health record for every American. Senator Clinton and I remain committed to working with the administration to accelerate this important work."
