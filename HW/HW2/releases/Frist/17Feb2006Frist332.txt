SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF DAVID KUSTOFF AS U.S. ATTORNEY FOR WESTERN DISTRICT OF TENNESSEE
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after President Bush nominated David Kustoff to serve as the next U.S. Attorney for the Western District of Tennessee.  
   "I'm pleased the president has selected David Kustoff to serve as the next U.S. Attorney for the Western District of Tennessee. David's personal integrity, legal acumen, and attention to detail are well-known. His lengthy history of community involvement indicates he will act as a tireless advocate for the people of West Tennessee. I appreciate his willingness to serve the region in this capacity and look forward to his confirmation."
