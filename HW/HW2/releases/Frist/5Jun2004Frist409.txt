SEN. FRIST HONORS MEMORY OF PRESIDENT REAGAN
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) made the following statement today following the announcement of President Ronald Reagan's passing: 
   "President Reagan's bold leadership in difficult times provided Americans with tremendous strength and inspiration. Above all, he was a true patriot, whose endless optimism inspired America's continued ascent to greatness. Undoubtedly, Ronald Reagan has left an indelible mark on our country and our global community. 
   "Karyn and I are saddened by the loss of this true American legend. I offer my sincerest condolences to Nancy and the Reagan family. May God bless his memory and his family."
