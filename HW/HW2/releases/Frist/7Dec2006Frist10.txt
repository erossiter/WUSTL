SEN. FRIST PRAISES VON ESCHENBACH CONFIRMATION
U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D. (R-Tenn) made the following statement after Andrew von Eschenbach was confirmed as the Commissioner of the Food and Drug Administration: 
 "I want to congratulate Andrew von Eschenbach on his confirmation as the Commission of the FDA. His expertise and experience as a nationally renowned researcher, urologic surgeon, and oncologist have prepared him for this job and the challenges ahead."
