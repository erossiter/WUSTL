SEN. FRIST APPLAUDS NOMINATION OF CONDOLEEZZA RICE AS SECRETARY OF STATE
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement on President Bush's nomination of National Security Advisor Dr.  
 Condoleezza Rice as Secretary of State: 
   "Dr. Condoleezza Rice is an outstanding choice for Secretary of State. The American people are fortunate to have a public servant of such talent and intellect. During her tenure as National Security Advisor, Dr. Rice has been a steady and trusted confidant to the President. In these difficult and challenging times, America needs a leader of her ability and skills. Dr. Rice possesses a rare combination of administrative experience, public policy expertise, high academic scholarship, and a gracious manner that will serve America's interests well. Dr. Condoleezza Rice is an outstanding choice to protect and serve our nation, and I hope to see her confirmed swiftly."
