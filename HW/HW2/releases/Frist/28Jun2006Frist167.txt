SEN. FRIST PRAISES SENATE CONFIRMATION OF PAULSON AS TREASURY SECRETARY
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after the Senate confirmed Hank Paulson to serve as Secretary of the Treasury:
   "I know Hank well and have complete confidence in his ability to lead the Treasury Department. Hank's hands-on experience in business and financial markets will prove an invaluable asset. He will serve our nation well, and I look forward to working with him in this new capacity."
