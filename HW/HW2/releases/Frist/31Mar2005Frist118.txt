SEN. FRIST ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF TERRI SCHIAVO
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist made the following statement regarding Terri Schiavo's passing: 
   "Today I join with all those mourning Terri Schiavo's passing. I am deeply saddened by her loss. I pray for her mother and father, her family, and all those involved in this regrettable loss of life. May God bless her memory."
