SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON PASSING OF JEANE KIRKPATRICK
Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn) made the following statement after hearing the sad news of Jeane Kirkpatrick's passing: 
 "I'm saddened by the news of Jeane Kirkpatrick's death. She stood up for the interests of America while at the UN, lent a powerful moral voice to Reagan foreign policy and has been a source of wise counsel to our nation since leaving government service two decades ago. She will be greatly missed."
