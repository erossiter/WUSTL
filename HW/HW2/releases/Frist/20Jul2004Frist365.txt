SEN. FRIST TO ADDRESS HEALTH INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CONFERENCE
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) will address the National Health Information Infrastructure Conference on Wednesday, July 21, to discuss the importance of information technology in health care.  
 More than 1,000 participants are expected to attend the conference, entitled "The Cornerstone of Electronic Health," to discuss and examine how information technology can improve the safety, quality and efficiency of health care. 
   * WHO: U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN)
   * WHAT: Address the National Health Information Infrastructure Conference on the benefits of health care information technology 
   * WHEN: Wednesday, July 21, at approximately 10:30 a.m. 
   * WHERE: Washington Convention Center, 801 Mt.Vernon Place NW, Washington, D.C.
