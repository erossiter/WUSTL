SEN. FRIST PRAISES GATES UNANIMOUS PANEL APPROVAL
U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D. (R-Tenn) made the following statement after Robert Gates was approved unanimously by a Senate panel: 
 "I want to commend Robert Gates for his exemplary performance answering tough questions today and thank the Armed Services committee for their expeditious action on his nomination. I'm pleased that the Senate will begin debate on his confirmation tomorrow."
