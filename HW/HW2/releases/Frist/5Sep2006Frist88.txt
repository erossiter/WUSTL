SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON RESIGNATION OF DR. MARK MCCLELLAN
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader, Sen. Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) made the following statement after the resignation announcement of Dr. Mark McClellan as Administrator of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services:  
   "While Dr. McClellan is stepping down as the head of CMS, I am confident he will remain a formidable force in advancing policies that will transform our health care system. Throughout his tenure with the Administration, his commitment to improving health care for all Americans has remained true. I appreciate his service, look forward to continuing to work with him and wish him and his family the best of luck in the future."
