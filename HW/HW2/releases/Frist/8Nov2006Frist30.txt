SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON SPEAKER HASTERT
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) made the following statement on Speaker Dennis Hastert (R-Ill) decision to not seek minority leader post next congress: 
   "Speaker Hastert is a friend, colleague and great leader. I respect his decision to not seek a leadership position, and am confident he will continue to serve the people of Illinois with the same dedication he has had for the last 20 years."
