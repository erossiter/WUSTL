SEN. FRIST REACTS ON RECOMMENDATION TO REPLACE MEHLMAN AS CHAIRMAN OF REPUBLICAN NATIONAL COMMITTEE
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn.) made the following statement regarding the announcement that Sen. Mel Martinez (R-Fl.) and Republican National Committee (RNC) General Counsel Mike Duncan will be recommended to replace Ken Mehlman as Chairman of the RNC:  
   "The vision and leadership of both Senator Martinez and Mike Duncan will help infuse the Republican Party with a renewal of energy and purpose that answers the concerns and needs of Americans, both today and tomorrow. 
   "Both Mel and Mike will be unparalleled assets at the helm of the RNC."
