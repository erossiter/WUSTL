SEN. FRIST EXPRESSES SUPPORT FOR PRESIDENT'S JUDICIAL NOMINATIONS
 U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement in support of President Bush's new judicial nominations: 
   "I'm pleased that the president has renominated these excellent women and men to serve on the federal bench. I'm hopeful that Democrats will work with me to get up-or-down votes on each nominee."
