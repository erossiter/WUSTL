SEN. FRIST APPLAUDS FORMATION OF NEW IRAQI GOVERNMENT
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today made the following statement on the formation of the new transitional Iraqi government led by Prime Minister Ibrahim al-Jaafari:  
   "I applaud President Talabani, incoming Prime Minister al-Jaafari, and the Iraqi people on the formation of a new government. This is an historic occasion for Iraq. I congratulate Prime Minister al-Jaafari on creating an inclusive cabinet that reflects the diversity of Iraq's population. And I urge all members of Iraq's new government to work together in the task of writing and ratifying a new constitution that reflects the voices of all of Iraq's ethnic and religious communities.
  "The formation of a democratic government is a testament to the bravery and determination of the Iraqi people and will serve as an inspiration to the entire Middle East. The United States will continue to support the Iraqi people and the Iraqi government as they move forward to build a free and prosperous Iraq."
