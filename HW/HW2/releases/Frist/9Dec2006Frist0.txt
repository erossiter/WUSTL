SEN. FRIST CALLS 109TH CONGRESS TO A CLOSE
U.S. Senate Majority Leader Sen. Bill Frist, M.D., (R-Tenn) made the following statement after the conclusion of the 109th Congress: 
 "The 109th Congress has adjourned sine die. 
 "I want to thank all of my colleagues and my staff for their hard work. I will truly miss each and every one of you. It has been an honor to serve the People of Tennessee and the United States of America"
