SEN. FRIST CALLS ON SEN. REID TO MEET WITH SENATE INTELLIGENCE COMMITTEE CHAIRMAN ROBERTS, RANKING MEMBER ROCKEFELLER
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-TN) today sent this letter to Senate Minority Leader Harry Reid (D-NV). The letter proposes a meeting with Senate Intelligence Committee Chairman Pat Roberts (R-KS) and Ranking Member John Rockefeller (D-WV) to discuss ending the politically-motivated tactics that are halting the committee's ability to investigate real and credible threats to America's security at home and abroad.
