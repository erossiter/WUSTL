SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON SUPREME COURT'S SCHIAVO RULING
  Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist made the following statement: 
   "I'm saddened by the decision of the court to reject Terri Schiavo's case for life despite a compelling case for reexamination of the medical evidence. It is a sad day for her loving family and for their innocent and voiceless daughter."
