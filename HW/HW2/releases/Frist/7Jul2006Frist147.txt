SEN. FRIST ISSUES STATEMENT ON MEXICAN PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION RESULTS
  U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D. (R-Tenn.) today made the following statement after Felipe Calderon was declared the winner of the Mexican Presidential election: 
   "My heartfelt congratulations go to President-elect Calderon and to the Mexican people. The real winners in this election are the Mexican people who have participated in one of the fairest and cleanest elections in the history of their great nation."
   "The United States and Mexico have many shared interests and we look forward to continuing our work with the Mexican Government and the Mexican people in the future."
