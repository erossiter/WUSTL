SEN. FRIST COMMENTS ON LOSS OF MARCELLE WILDER
  Sen. Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) today made the following statement: 
   "As with the loss of President Reagan, Mrs. Wilder's passing marks the end of another great love story. Gov. Wilder and Marcelle's devotion to each other was an inspiration. Karyn and I join with all Tennesseans in extending our condolences to the Wilder family. We will keep them in our thoughts and prayers."
