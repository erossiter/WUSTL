SENS. FEINGOLD, REPS. KOHL, BALDWIN, KIND, MOORE, OBEY LETTER ON MEDICARE PART D
  Sens. Feingold and Kohl, along with Reps. Baldwin, Kind, Moore and Obey, sent the attached letter to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services today regarding the need to fix many problems beneficiaries are having in obtaining medications under the Medicare program. 
   "We ask that you take immediate action to help individuals in need... This situation has reached emergency status, and we ask that you treat it as such."
   View a Copy of the Letter Here (http://feingold.senate.gov/~feingold/releases/06/01/McClellan_11306.pdf).
