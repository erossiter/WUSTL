SEN. FEINGOLD ANNOUNCES GREEN BAY OFFICE STAFF CHANGES
  U.S. Senator Russ Feingold today announced staff changes in his Green Bay federal office. Senator Feingold has hired Suzanne Brault Pagel as his new Fox Valley Regional Coordinator and she will be responsible for constituent outreach in northeast Wisconsin. Brault Pagel previously worked for Senator Feingold in the Green Bay office from 1996 to 1999. Since working for Senator Feingold Brault Pagel has worked for the Fox Cities-Kurgan Sister Cities Program and the Appleton Area School District.
   The changes come upon Bob Schweder retiring from Senator Feingold's office. Schweder had worked for Senator Feingold since 2000.
