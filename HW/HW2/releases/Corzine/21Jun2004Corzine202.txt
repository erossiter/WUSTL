SEN. CORZINE OFFERS CONDOLENCES TO FAMILY OF PAUL JOHNSON
  Sen. Jon S. Corzine (D-NJ) issued the following statement in response to news of the death of Paul Johnson, Jr.: 
   "I am utterly appalled at the brutal, heinous murder of Paul Johnson. All my thoughts and prayers are with his family and my office will do all it can to provide them support and comfort in the aftermath of this tragedy. Most of all, Paul Johnson will not be forgotten. As we know all too well, terrorism knows no boundaries, and the cold-blooded crime that ended Mr. Johnson's life must strengthen our commitment and resolve to win the war against terrorists."
