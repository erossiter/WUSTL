
            	Biden Issues Statement on Delaware State University shootings
             September 21, 2007 
 Washington, DC - U.S. Senator Joe Biden (D-DE) issued the following statement in light of the shootings at Delaware State University early this morning:   "My thoughts and prayers this morning are with the two victims, their families, the student body, and the entire Delaware State community.  Tragically, this community has suffered far too much violence and I urge everyone to come together to put an end to these senseless shootings and hostility."
