
            	Biden Statement on the Death of Coretta Scott King
             January 31, 2006 
 WASHINGTON, DC -- U.S. Senator Joe Biden (D-DE) released the following statement today on the passing of civil rights leader Coretta Scott King:   Today, the nation mourns the loss of Coretta Scott King. She was the keeper of Dr. Kings promise and her presence was a constant reminder of the roads weve traveled and the paths weve yet to forge. She stood for peace long before others were willing to take a stand. We will miss her strength, her wisdom, her passion and her compassion.
