                     PRESS RELEASE  NEBRASKA  S SENATOR BEN NELSON SAYS COURT DECISION AGAINST PLEDGE IS   WRONG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    September 15  2005         NEBRASKA  S SENATOR BEN NELSON SAYS COURT DECISION AGAINST PLEDGE IS   WRONG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON D C     A U S  District Court judge declared Wednesday that the reciting of the Pledge of Allegiance in public schools is unconstitutional  ruling that the pledge s reference to one nation   under God    violates school children s right to be   free from a coercive requirement to affirm God           Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement in response to the ruling          I could not disagree more with the District Court  s ruling against the Pledge of Allegiance  History will not look kindly on this ruling and the incredible waste of time and energy and taxpayers dollars that went to support the court proceedings  This judge simply got it wrong and I hope that a higher court overturns this misguided decision
