                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON SOUTHWICK NOMINATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    October 24  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON SOUTHWICK NOMINATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           October 24  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement after the Senate voted 62 35 to invoke cloture on the nomination of Leslie Southwick of Mississippi to be U S  Circuit Judge for the Fifth Circuit    Today s cloture vote on Judge Southwick sends a clear signal that there is a core group of Senators willing to work together to rebuild a spirit of compromise that is so critical to getting things done here in the Senate  Some might see this bipartisan compromise as a new development  but its the way I conduct business in the Senate  I am proud of the work Senator Lott and I and others did to avert another partisan fight over judicial nominations    Judge Southwick was confirmed by the Senate today by a bipartisan vote of 59 38    Senator Nelson was a central leader in the Senates Gang of 14 that developed an agreement to resolve a judicial nomination crisis in 2005  Nelson has played central roles in other compromises over tax cuts  economic policies  and other issues since joining the Senate in 2001
