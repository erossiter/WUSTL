                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    July 28  2006         NELSON  EUROPEAN UNION SHOULD OUTLAW HEZBOLLAH AS TERRORIST GROUP                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON  DC    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today issued the following statement on developments in the Middle East          Our Nation continues to stand with Israel as it defends itself against Hezbollah  Hamas and their state sponsors         Next week  several Senators and I will be sending a letter that calls on the European Union to place Hezbollah on the EU  s list for the Application of Specific Measures to Combat Terrorism which would effectively outlaw Hezbollah in Europe  and prohibit them from raising funds there         Hezbollah is a terrorist group that is not simply a threat to Israel and the United States  but also to Europe  Its continuing rocket attacks on Israel are fundamentally attacks on the peace process         Hezbollah must disarm so that it no longer poses a threat to the democratically elected government of Lebanon or its neighbors        I also support an international stabilization force to help the democratically elected Lebanese government s security forces disarm and replace Hezbollah forces in southern Lebanon  This international security force should also help to fully implement United Nations Security Council Resolution 1559  That s the solution to this crisis and the path to peace  Hezbollah must be disarmed so that it no longer poses a threat to Lebanon s democratic government or Israel
