                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  SENATE PASSES IRAQ WAR FUNDING BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Press Release    April 21  2005         NELSON  SENATE PASSES IRAQ WAR FUNDING BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON  D C     The United States Senate today passed an  81 billion funding bill for military operations in Iraq and Afghanistan and other hotspots around the globe that includes funds for the war on terror  the war in Iraq  more armored humvees  border protection  and tuition assistance for Army Reserve soldiers  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson voted in favor of the bill       With continuing conflict in Iraq  still more to be done in Afghanistan and the continued threat of terrorism against the United States  we need to provide our fighting men and women the resources they need to complete their mission     said Senator Nelson      Nelson is a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee and the ranking member of the Personnel Subcommittee which has jurisdiction over military personnel policies and benefits      30
