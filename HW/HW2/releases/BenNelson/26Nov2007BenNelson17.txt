                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF SENATOR TRENT LOTT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    November 26  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF SENATOR TRENT LOTT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              November 26  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today issued the following statement on the resignation of Senator Trent Lott    Over the past several years Senator Lott and I forged a friendship based on our willingness to work together to find bipartisan solutions for many of the problems facing the Senate and the country  Though I am sorry to see him leave the Senate and I will miss working with him in the future  Diane and I offer him  Tricia and his family our best wishes as he moves on to new challenges
