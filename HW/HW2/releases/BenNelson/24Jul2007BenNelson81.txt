                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON COMMENDS CHOICE TO HEAD STRATCOM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Press Release    July 24  2007         NELSON COMMENDS CHOICE TO HEAD STRATCOM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            July 24  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement on President Bush s nomination of Air Force General Kevin Chilton to be the new commander of the U S  Strategic Command at Offutt Air Force Base        General Chilton is an excellent choice to head StratCom   As a former astronaut and current commander of the Air Force Space Command  General Chilton will bring extraordinary experience in the fields of space and cyberspace to the position   I m looking forward to a continued close working relationship with StratCom  Offutt Air Force Base  the entire Nebraska Congressional delegation and the greater Omaha business community  I know I will be asking him to work with us on adding the new Cyberspace Command to the array of national security operations housed at Offutt        Senator Nelson plans to meet personally with General Chilton before his nomination goes before the Senate Armed Services Committee on which Nelson serves
