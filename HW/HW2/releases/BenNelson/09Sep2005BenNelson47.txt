                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    September 9  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE FOURTH ANNIVERSARY OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 TERRORIST ATTACKS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   OMAHA  NE    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the fourth anniversary of the September 11  2001 attacks on New York City  Washington  D C  and Shanksville  Pennsylvania        This year marks the fourth anniversary of the catastrophic events of September 11  2001  Unfortunately  the anniversary of this terrible event comes on the heels of another devastating disaster    this time a natural disaster that has claimed the lives of many Americans        September 11 is forever etched into American history  and indeed the lives we as Americans lead  As September 11 comes again this year  it brings with it the memories of that horrific day and the heroes that emerged from the rubble and the heroes that never dusted themselves off  It is a time to also celebrate a renewed American spirit and the ideals of freedom and liberty that this nation was founded on  prospered with  and sets as an example for all the world to follow        Here in Nebraska and all over America  our flag still flies proudly  America remains steadfast in fostering freedom and liberty  We have endured attack  the wrath of nature and the terrible cost of war  but our spirit is unchecked and unbroken      That strong spirit is honored when we honor the memory of those that lost their lives during those attacks on September 11 and those that perished trying to save them  I join all of you today in remembering our fallen  honoring our heroes  and rekindling the American spirit
