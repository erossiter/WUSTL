                     PRESS RELEASE  SENATE CONFIRMS IRAQ WAR COMMANDER GENERAL PETRAEUS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Press Release    January 26  2007         SENATE CONFIRMS IRAQ WAR COMMANDER GENERAL PETRAEUS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today voted to confirm General David H  Petraeus as the incoming Commander of the Multinational Force in Iraq  The Senate confirmed the nomination 81 0       Gen  Petraeus   experience and leadership in counterinsurgency signal a new direction for our military objectives in Iraq     said Nelson    During our meeting last week  I told Gen  Petraeus the expectations from Congress for his success are high  but the hopes of the American people are even higher  I feel that Gen  Petraeus wants nothing less than success in Iraq and I look forward to working with him in the coming months to meet the needs of the troops so they have the tools they need to complete this mission  I wish him Godspeed        Senator Nelson serves on the Senate Armed Services and Appropriations Committees and is the Chairman of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee
