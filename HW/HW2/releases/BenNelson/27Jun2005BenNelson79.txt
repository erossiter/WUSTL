                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON PUSHES NEBRASKA PRODUCTS  WITH CUBA TRADE OFFICIALS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Press Release    June 27  2005         NELSON PUSHES NEBRASKA PRODUCTS  WITH CUBA TRADE OFFICIALS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  D C     Cuba trade officials are looking to import thousands of tons of edible dry beans and Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson recommended they look to Nebraska as their trading partner     In meetings with high level Cuban trade officials  including Pedro Alvarez  Chairman of Cuba  s purchasing agency for farm and food products  Nelson made the case for products from Nebraska including dry edible beans and other products  Cuban officials told Nelson they are seeking a considerable amount of these beans  especially black beans       What I gathered at our meetings with Cuban officials is that there is a great opportunity for Nebraska producers and processors to expand their markets in Cuba     said Nelson  who as Nebraska  s Governor started the practice of trade missions lead by the Governor  s office    Mr  Alvarez was interested in dry edible beans and wheat for bread  I told them they could get everything they need from Nebraska        Later this year a trade delegation from Nebraska will visit with Cuban officials to discuss opportunities to expand trade of food  Nelson will share the information he received in his meetings with Nebraska Governor Dave Heineman         We  re all on the same side in promoting Nebraska products and opening markets for Nebraska producers     said Nelson      Nebraska ranks at the top in the production of many commodities  including corn  soybeans  wheat  dry edible beans  pork  beef  and many others   As Governor  Nelson lead eleven trade missions on behalf of Nebraska  s producers and products and saw the level of exports increase over the eight years from  800 million to  2 7 billion annually     Nelson is a cosponsor of federal legislation that would ease the ban on trade with Cuba and open new markets for domestic producers and products in that country
