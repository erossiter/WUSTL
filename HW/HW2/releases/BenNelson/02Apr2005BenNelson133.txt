                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON POPE JOHN PAUL II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    April 2  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON POPE JOHN PAUL II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement on the death of Pope John Paul II                        Today  the Church has lost an historic leader who spread freedom and faith behind the Iron Curtain  demanded that western nations establish higher standards for human rights  and expanded opportunities for faith in Latin America and Asia  The loss of Pope John Paul II is a loss not just for the Catholic Church but for the world community of all faiths       Pope John Paul II once said that Man  s path to happiness is through prayer and faith  His commitment to human rights and the world  s children will serve as the foundation for his legacy                                                                30
