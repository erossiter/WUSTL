                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    November 13  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE GROUNDBREAKING OF THE MARTIN LUTHER KING  JR  NATIONAL MEMORIAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today released the following statement on the groundbreaking of the Martin Luther King  Jr  National Memorial                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          A monument to honor Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr  is a fitting tribute to the leader of the civil rights movement because it recognizes the non violent struggle that forever transformed our country  but it is long overdue     More than 40 years have passed since Dr  King shared his dream of hope  freedom  equality  and justice for all  Now  a memorial  not far from where Dr  King delivered his famous   Dream    speech in 1963  will provide visitors with the opportunity to honor his legacy that all Americans deserve to be treated with dignity     As a country  we have made tremendous progress in the battle for equality but there is still much to be done   The Martin Luther King  Jr  National Memorial will remind us all that together we can transform Dr  King  s dreams into a reality
