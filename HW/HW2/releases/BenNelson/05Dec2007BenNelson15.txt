                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON SHOOTING AT WESTROADS MALL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Press Release    December 5  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON SHOOTING AT WESTROADS MALL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     December 5  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement today regarding the shooting at Westroads Mall in Omaha    Today the city of Omaha and the state of Nebraska have endured a terrible tragedy  Though some of our neighbors have suffered devastating losses today  I know that they can count on the support of the Omaha community at this time  Diane and I send our thoughts and prayers out to the families and friends of the victims of today s terrible violence
