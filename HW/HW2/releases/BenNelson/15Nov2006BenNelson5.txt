                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON QUESTIONS IRAQ TROOP READINESS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    November 15  2006         NELSON QUESTIONS IRAQ TROOP READINESS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson questioned the ability of Iraqi forces to defend their own country in today  s Senate Armed Services Committee hearing         I am concerned about what s required for Iraq  s independence in terms of standing up troops and ending the country  s cycle of dependence     said Nelson    The continuing question will always be how quickly are we moving   What s the progress   And what remains to be done          Senator Nelson has promoted establishing measurable goals to track progress on key political  economic and military benchmarks in Iraq to assist the country  s ability to independently govern and defend itself against insurgents         Although I don  t support arbitrary dates for troop withdrawal  it  s clear the stay the course approach isn  t working     said Nelson    To encourage Iraqis to take on more responsibility  we need to introduce measurable goals we expect them to meet
