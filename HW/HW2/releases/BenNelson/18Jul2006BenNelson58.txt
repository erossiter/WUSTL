                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON THE RESIGNATION OF CHIEF JUSTICE JOHN HENDRY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Press Release    July 18  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE RESIGNATION OF CHIEF JUSTICE JOHN HENDRY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   In response to Nebraska Supreme Court Chief Justice John Hendry  s resignation  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement       When I appointed John Hendry I trusted he would adjudicate from the bench and not legislate  He didn  t let me or the State of Nebraska down  and his judicial principles set a wonderful example for every judge in Nebraska        As Nebraska  s Governor  Nelson appointed Hendry to the Chief Justice seat on Nebraska  s Supreme Court in 1998
