                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON OFFERS REVISED RESOLUTION OPPOSING TROOP SURGE IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Press Release    January 31  2007         NELSON OFFERS REVISED RESOLUTION OPPOSING TROOP SURGE IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Nelson  Warner  Collins Joined by Senator Levin  Sponsor of Competing Resolution                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson and several colleagues tonight proposed a revised version of their resolution opposing the proposed troop surge in Iraq that has won support from a key Senator  Senate Armed Services Committee Chairman Carl Levin       We  ve proposed a revised version of our own resolution that addresses some of the concerns that were raised by others including calling for a specific delineation of command for operations in Baghdad and details key benchmarks for the Iraqi government to meet if our military presence is to continue    Senator Nelson said    The revised resolution is strongly worded  expresses the Senate  s disagreement with the President  s surge plan and holds the Iraqi government accountable for its responsibilities        Nelson  along with Senators John Warner of Virginia  Susan Collins of Maine  and Norm Coleman of Minnesota last week proposed a Senate resolution opposing the troop surge   Senator Levin had proposed a competing resolution earlier  Levin is now a cosponsor of the revised Warner Nelson Collins resolution     The revised resolution addresses previous questions about the chain of command for US troops in Iraq  The revised resolution calls on the Administration to   clarify the command and control arrangements in Baghdad     The revised resolution also adds details key benchmarks for progress the Iraqi government must meet        We  ve heard from a number of our colleagues and we have taken some of their recommendations and revised our resolution  Senator Warner deserves recognition for his leadership on this matter and I thank Senator Levin for his support     Nelson said
