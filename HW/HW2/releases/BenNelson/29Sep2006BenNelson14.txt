                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON VOTES FOR CHILD CUSTODY PROTECTION BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Press Release    September 29  2006         NELSON VOTES FOR CHILD CUSTODY PROTECTION BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Nebraska  s Senator disappointed critical legislation does not move forward                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Senator Nelson voted to complete work on the Child Custody Protection bill today and is disappointed this critical legislation did not move forward   The legislation would make it a federal crime to circumvent parental consent laws by transporting minors across state lines with the intent of helping such minors obtain an abortion  The procedural motion to move the bill forward failed 57 42  it required 60 votes  Nelson is a cosponsor of the Child Custody Protection Act  S  403  which first passed in the Senate in July         To protect minors and uphold state parental notification laws  it  s critical that we implement this federal law barring individuals from taking minors across state lines     Nelson said        All but six states and Washington DC have consent or notification requirements for minors seeking abortions  although nine of those laws have been enjoined by judges or the states  attorneys general  State parental consent laws  including Nebraska  s  contain  judicial bypass  provisions  as required by the U S  Supreme Court         I signed Nebraska  s parental notification law into effect in 1991 when I was Governor     said Nelson    It is essential that we ensure these laws are not circumvented   I will continue to fight for passage
