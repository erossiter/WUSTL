                     PRESS RELEASE  DUBOIS VOLUNTEER  AND ANSELMO RURAL FIRE DEPARTMENTS RECEIVE FEMA FIREFIGHTING GRANTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    February 2  2005         DUBOIS VOLUNTEER  AND ANSELMO RURAL FIRE DEPARTMENTS RECEIVE FEMA FIREFIGHTING GRANTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and the U S  Fire Administration  USFA  has awarded Assistance to Firefighters Grants to the DuBois Volunteer Fire Department  and the Anselmo Rural Fire District     Designed to strengthen and enhance basic firefighting capabilities and other emergency services across the United States  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program will give awards through four program areas   These areas include the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  the Fire Prevention Program  the Emergency Medical Services Program  and the Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program      The thirty third round of FEMA grants brings Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety funding to two rural Nebraska fire departments   The DuBois Volunteer Fire Department will receive  72 000  and the Anselmo Rural Fire District will receive  61 722 for a new firefighting vehicle      The Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for additional training  fitness equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   This grant also provides funding for new firefighting equipment   The Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program includes the purchase of  pumpers  brush trucks  tankers tenders  rescue vehicles  ambulances  quints  aerials  foam units and fire boats        These grants will assist these two rural fire departments in more efficiently protecting Nebraska families     said Nelson     With these funds  our fire departments can expand their efforts to continue to make Nebraska as safe as it can be           30
