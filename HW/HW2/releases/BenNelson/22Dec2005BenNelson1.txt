                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON CO SPONSORS SIX MONTH EXTENSION OF PATRIOT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    December 22  2005         NELSON CO SPONSORS SIX MONTH EXTENSION OF PATRIOT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senate reaches anti terrorism law compromise                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON  D C     In a compromise reached late last night  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson cosponsored legislation to preserve the Patriot Act and extend the sun setting portions of America  s anti terrorism laws by six months     The agreement  S  2167  arrived after Nelson and a number of Senators sent a letter to Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist of Tennessee asking him to allow the Senate to pass an extension of the current Patriot Act       If the Patriot Act expired  our law enforcement and anti terrorism officials would lack the tools they need to protect us     said Senator Nelson    This six month extension will give Congress more time to develop a stronger anti terrorism bill that preserves civil liberties and gives law enforcement the tools they need to protect us from those that seek to do us harm         Nelson has called for an investigation by the Senate Intelligence Committee into the Administration  s program that allows domestic surveillance of American citizens without a court order as required by law       The Constitution establishes a system of governmental checks and balances     said Senator Nelson    This is a situation when Congress needs to check that we  re not becoming the very people we  re fighting against  The American people expect us to protect their freedom and Congress has an oversight obligation to meet in this case
