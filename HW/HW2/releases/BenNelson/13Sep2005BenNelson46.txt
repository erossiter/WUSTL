                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON HONORED FOR PROMOTING RENEWABLE FUELS BY NATIONAL FARMERS UNION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Press Release    September 13  2005         NELSON HONORED FOR PROMOTING RENEWABLE FUELS BY NATIONAL FARMERS UNION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Nebraska  s Senator Honored with NFU  s   Presidential Award for Leadership    for   his efforts to pass a Renewable Fuels Standard  and ongoing support of mandatory country of origin labeling                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C     The National Farmers Union this week presented Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson with their   Presidential Award for Leadership    in recognition of his efforts to establish the Renewable Fuels Standard that was included in the recently passed Energy Bill and for his support of mandatory country of origin labeling          I am honored to receive this recognition from the National Farmers Union but the recognition is secondary to the tangible benefits and results we will see in ethanol production as a result of the new renewable fuels standard     said Senator Nelson    Hopefully we can move from high gasoline prices to high ethanol production which will create jobs and lower our dependence on foreign sources of oil and in turn reduce fuel costs           In a press release announcing NFU  s award recipients including Nelson  NFU President Dave Frederickson said    We appreciate these leaders for their support of America  s family farmers and ranchers  Their dedication to rural America is appreciated across the countryside            Ben Nelson has a strong record of supporting ethanol   During his tenure as Nebraska s governor  Nelson moved Nebraska to the forefront in ethanol production  From 1990 to 1997 Nebraska tripled its ethanol production to 300 million gallons  ranking third in the nation  Ben Nelson also formed the Governors  Ethanol Coalition in 1991 to promote increased ethanol use  Since its formation  the Governors  Ethanol Coalition has worked to expand national and international markets for biofuels
