                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    June 8  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON RECOMMENDATION OF GENERAL CARTWRIGHT TO SERVE AS VICE CHAIR OF JOINT CHIEFS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     June 8  2007  Today  Defense Secretary Robert Gates announced that General James Cartwright has been recommended to serve as Vice Chair of the Joint Chiefs of Staff  General Cartwright currently serves as commander of United States Strategic Command at Offutt Air Force Base   Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  issued the following statement on his recommendation   I spoke to General Cartwright this afternoon and offered my unqualified support  During his tenure as commander of StratCom  I worked closely with him on the creation of Global Innovation and Strategy Center  GISC  in Omaha which encourages private sector involvement in developing new strategies and innovations in support of StratComs mission  I am disappointed that we will lose him at StratCom but I know he will serve with distinction as the Vice Chair of the Joint Chiefs of Staff    Nebraska is fortunate to have a man who has a strong relationship with our state serve in such an important role  As head of StratCom  General Cartwright knows firsthand how critical Offutt Air Force Base is to our national security and to Nebraska  He maintained a strong relationship with the communities of Omaha and Bellevue and I am confident that he will continue those relationships in his new position  I look forward to hearing from him during his Senate confirmation hearings and working with him in his new capacity
