                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF FORMER CONGRESSMAN PETER HOAGLAND                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Press Release    October 31  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF FORMER CONGRESSMAN PETER HOAGLAND                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               October 31  2007  Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the passing of former 2nd District Representative Peter Hoagland     Peter Hoagland was a very special friend to all who knew him  His tenure in Congress coincided with my first four years in the governor s office and I will always remember his thoughtful advice and advocacy on issues important to his constituents  Peter always worked to do what he believed was right for his district and our state    He was a true leader  and while he left public service  he never left public life    Earlier this year  Nebraska Democrats honored Peter with the Hall of Fame Award at the Morrison Exon Dinner  I am so grateful we had that opportunity to let Peter know how much he meant to all of us    My thoughts and prayers are with his wife Barbara and their family
