                     PRESS RELEASE  SENATE ARMED SERVICES COMMITTEE APPROVES CARTWRIGHT NOMINATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Press Release    August 2  2007         SENATE ARMED SERVICES COMMITTEE APPROVES CARTWRIGHT NOMINATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     August 2  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today joined his colleagues on the Senate Armed Services Committee in unanimously approving the nomination of General James Cartwright as Vice Chair of the Joint Chiefs of Staff   Cartwright currently heads STRATCOM at Offutt Air Force Base        General Cartwright has been an exceptional leader at STRATCOM and I m sure he ll use those same skills to continue to serve our nation as Vice Chair   said Nelson    I m pleased that so many other senators on the Armed Services Committee also recognized his abilities by supporting his nomination        The nomination will now be sent to the full Senate for consideration   The Armed Services Committee also approved the nomination of General Michael Mullen to be Chair of the Joint Chiefs of Staff
