                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF SENATOR CHUCK HAGEL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    September 10  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF SENATOR CHUCK HAGEL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              September 10  2007  Nebraskas Senator Ben  Nelson released the following statement on Senator Chuck Hagels announcement  that he will not run for re election next year  Its an honor and a privilege to  serve your nation  especially representing the citizens of Nebraska  The  opportunity to serve in the United States Senate is something that many seek but  few achieve  I congratulate Chuck Hagel for his service and successes  and wish  him and his family well in the future
