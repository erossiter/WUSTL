                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON TO MEET WITH SUPREME COURT NOMINEE ROBERTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    July 26  2005         NELSON TO MEET WITH SUPREME COURT NOMINEE ROBERTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Nebraska  s Senator Says He   Owes It    to Nebraskans to Learn All He Can About Nominee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that he will meet on Thursday with Judge John Roberts  the president  s nominee to replace Associate Justice Sandra Day O  Connor on the U S  Supreme Court       I  ve made it a practice to meet with many of the judges before making a decision on the nomination  I do not consider Judge Roberts a controversial nomination  but because he  s been nominated to the Supreme Court  I think I owe it to Nebraskans to learn all I can about the nomination     said Senator Nelson      In the past  Nelson has made it a point to meet with some of the high profile judicial nominations before deciding his vote on the nominees  Nelson believes that you can  t learn everything you need to know about a person from media reports  As a former Governor who has appointed the entire Nebraska Supreme Court  the Appeals Court  and about half of the state judges in Nebraska  Nelson believes personal interviews are the best way to get to know a nominee  Nelson has met with Miguel Estrada  Janice Rodgers Brown  and Priscilla Owen among others   Nelson is meeting with Judge Roberts at the request of the White House       As I have said before and will repeat here again  at this point in time I see nothing in Judge Roberts   background that could rise to the level of extraordinary circumstances in my mind  I expect the Judiciary Committee to conduct a thorough hearing and that Judge Roberts will be given the opportunity to answer questions about his background     said Nelson     Nelson is a leader among the so called   Gang of Fourteen    moderate minded Senators who broke the logjam on judicial nominations in the Senate
