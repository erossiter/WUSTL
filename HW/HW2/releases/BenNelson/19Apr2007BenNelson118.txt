                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON HONORED WITH  ADOPTION HALL OF FAME  AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    April 19  2007         NELSON HONORED WITH  ADOPTION HALL OF FAME  AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  April 19  2007   The National Council for Adoption presented Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson with their  Adoption Hall of Fame  award yesterday to honor his fight for adoption benefits for military families        As an adoptive parent myself  this recognition is especially meaningful   said Nelson   Of all the challenges I ve experienced serving as Nebraska s Governor and Senator  none match the challenge presented and reward gained from raising a family  And I ll continue my work in Washington to make those amazing experiences possible for other families wishing to adopt        In 2005  Nelson introduced legislation that authorized up to 21 days of additional leave for service members to use when adopting a child and filled in the cracks to ensure adopted children of military families have access to quality healthcare       Nelson has also promoted adoption through adoption tax credits and is a member of the Congressional Caucus on Adoption
