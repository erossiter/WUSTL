                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH  S SPEECH ON IMMIGRATION REFORM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    June 7  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT BUSH  S SPEECH ON IMMIGRATION REFORM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  In response to the President  s visit to Omaha  Nebraska today to promote his guest worker proposals  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement       First  I  m glad the President got to hear from Nebraskans directly about their concerns regarding the amnesty plan being considered in Washington       He probably heard what I hear when I  m home  Nebraskans want to secure the border first  and then deal with the 12 million illegal immigrants already here  If we don  t secure the border first  our borders will remain open and the problem will just get worse        I offered a plan that did that with more border agents  a border barrier  more detention beds and cracked down on employers who intentionally hire illegal workers       The Senate amnesty plan now seems to be stalled  Maybe we could bifurcate the issues  and get the border security part done first  then go on with the other programs  It would be unfortunate if the amnesty plans killed any hope for securing our borders this year        Senator Nelson voted against a Senate bill that would provide amnesty to millions of illegal immigrants because he believes that we need to secure our borders first  before we discuss guest worker programs and other amnesty proposals  Nelson offered a bipartisan comprehensive border security first bill last fall  Nelson does not support the egregious provisions of the House passed bill
