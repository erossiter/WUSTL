                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    January 5  2005         PICKRELL RURAL FIRE DEPARTMENT RECEIVES  123 840 FROM FEMA FOR A NEW FIREFIGHTING VEHICLE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C     The Pickrell Rural Fire Department will receive a  123 840 grant to enhance firefighter safety  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today  The grant comes from the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and the U S  Fire Administration  USFA  as part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program     Designed to strengthen and enhance basic firefighting capabilities and other emergency services across the United States  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program will give awards through four program areas in 2004   These areas include the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  the Fire Prevention Program  the Emergency Medical Services Program  and the Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program      The grant to the Pickrell Rural Fire Department comes as part of the twenty ninth round of FEMA fire fighting grants   The  123 840 grant is awarded through the Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program     The Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program allows fire departments to obtain the needed equipment to improve the effectiveness of firefighting operations   The much needed equipment can include  pumpers  brush trucks  tankers tenders  rescue vehicles  ambulances  quints  aerials  foam units  and fireboats      This grant will greatly assist the Pickrell Rural Fire Department in acquiring the equipment they need to protect and serve their rural area     said Nelson     With these funds  firefighters in Pickrell will more efficiently protect their community            30
