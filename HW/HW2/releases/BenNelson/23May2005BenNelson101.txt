                                                         Press Release    May 23  2005         NELSON BROKERS DEAL ON JUDGES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON  D C   Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement on reaching a compromise with a coalition of 13 of his colleagues to avert the nuclear option and preserve minority rights and the power of small states in the Senate         I am assured and relieved that a compromise has been reached to avoid a Senate showdown   I have been working with a group of moderate minded senators to craft an alternative to the nuclear option because I don  t support filibustering judicial nominations and I don  t think the nuke option is the solution   Now the Senate can move forward and get to important business like the energy bill   Our compromise was crafted within the rules of the Senate  without requiring a rules change or a change in minority rights in the Senate   The negotiated and agreed upon compromise is aimed at preventing a momentous vote on whether to strip the Minority of their capacity to use the filibuster to block Bush s candidates for federal courts          The following Senators signed the agreement with Senator Ben Nelson  D NE   Senator John McCain  R AZ   Senator John Warner  R VA   Senator Robert Byrd  D WV   Senator Mary Landrieu  D LA   Senator Olympia Snowe  R ME   Senator Ken Salazar  D CO   Senator Mike DeWine  R OH   Senator Susan Collins  R ME   Senator Mark Pryor  D AR   Senator Lincoln Chafee  R RI   Senator Lindsey Graham  R SC   Senator Joseph Lieberman  D CT   and Senator Daniel Inouye  D HI
