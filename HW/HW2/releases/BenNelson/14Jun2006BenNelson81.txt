                                                         Press Release    June 14  2006         NELSON  USDA RURAL DEVELOPMENT OFFERS PROGRAM TO PROMOTE ENERGY EFFICIENT HOME OWNERSHIP                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         June is National Homeownership Month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               In response to National Homeownership Month  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson expressed his support for a US Department of Agriculture program giving rural homebuyers greater loan flexibility if they are purchasing a newer home that is energy efficient     The Home Energy Advantage program expands the loan eligibility requirements for qualified low  and middle income families wishing to buy a new energy efficient home in rural Nebraska  Loans are also available for families interested in installing energy saving features to an existing home       Encouraging homeownership in our rural communities is essential to a community  s growth     said Nelson    I hope Nebraskans will take advantage of this opportunity to invest in our rural areas while also promoting efficient energy use        For more information regarding the Home Energy Program  visit USDA Rural Development  s web site at http   www rurdev usda gov
