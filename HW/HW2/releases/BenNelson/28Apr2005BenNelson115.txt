                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON COMMENDS CABLE INDUSTRY INTIATIVE TO ADDRESS INDECENCY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Press Release    April 28  2005         NELSON COMMENDS CABLE INDUSTRY INTIATIVE TO ADDRESS INDECENCY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today issued a statement commending the cable television industry for taking the initiative to address indecency on cable television  Nelson  s statement is below       I applaud the cable industry  s efforts to address indecency on cable television  I believe these are important first steps to help parents better understand the options that are available to them to control what types of programs their children are watching  I encourage the cable industry to continue to explore ways to address indecency  and I look forward to working with them to address this issue which is of such concern to so many families         30
