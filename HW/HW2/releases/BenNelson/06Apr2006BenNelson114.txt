                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  SENATE MUST ADDRESS BORDER SECURITY BEFORE AMNESTY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Press Release    April 6  2006         NELSON  SENATE MUST ADDRESS BORDER SECURITY BEFORE AMNESTY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Nebraska   Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement after the Senate voted on a cloture motion on a guest worker and amnesty bill offered by Senator Arlen Specter of Pennsylvania  The cloture motion failed 39 60  Senator Nelson voted against it       I voted against cloture on the Specter bill because I believe the Senate must address border security first  I can  t support cloture when it blocks an up or down vote on my border security first amendment   I have proposed tough border security legislation that would strengthen America  s border by adding 8 000 border patrol agents  enhancing technology for surveillance of the border  construct a barrier at the direction of the Department of Homeland Security  and increase workplace enforcement measures
