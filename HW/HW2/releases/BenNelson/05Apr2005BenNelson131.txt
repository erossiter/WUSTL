                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    April 5  2005         NELSON  SENATE ADOPTS RESOLUTION HONORING THE LIFE OF POPE JOHN PAUL II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today voted in favor of a Senate Resolution honoring the life of the Holy Father  Pope John Paul II who passed away over the weekend at the age of 84 after serving twenty six years as the head of the Catholic Church       The resolution calls Pope John Paul II  one of the greatest spiritual leaders and moral teachers of the Modern era  who  campaigned tirelessly for human rights and human dignity throughout the world           This resolution honors the life of a remarkable man whose life work was touching the hearts and minds of millions around the world     said Senator Nelson  who is a cosponsor of the resolution honoring the Pope         The Church has lost an historic leader who spread freedom and faith behind the Iron Curtain  demanded that western nations establish higher standards for human rights  and expanded opportunities for faith in Latin America and Asia  The loss of Pope John Paul II is a loss not just for the Catholic Church but for the world community of all faiths  Pope John Paul II once said that Man  s path to happiness is through prayer and faith  His commitment to human rights and the world  s children will serve as the foundation for his legacy     Nelson said in reaction to the Pope  s death on April 2       The Senate voted unanimously  98 0  in support of the resolution        30
