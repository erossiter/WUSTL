                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON COMMEMORATES 50TH ANNIVERSARY OF    IN GOD WE TRUST   AS THE NATIONAL MOTTO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Press Release    June 16  2006         NELSON COMMEMORATES 50TH ANNIVERSARY OF    IN GOD WE TRUST   AS THE NATIONAL MOTTO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   June 15  2006    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today cosponsored a resolution commemorating the national motto of the United States    In God We Trust          Each time I enter the U S  Senate chamber to vote  I pass under the words   In God We Trust       said Nelson    The national motto serves as a reminder to my colleagues and me that we  re responsible for preserving the unalienable rights to life  liberty and the pursuit of happiness  and it deserves to be celebrated        The national motto was officially adopted July 30  1956  after first appearing on a coin of the United States in 1864     The Nelson supported resolution encourages Americans to reflect on the integral role the national motto has played in America  before and after its official adoption 50 years ago
