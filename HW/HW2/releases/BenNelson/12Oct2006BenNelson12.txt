                                                            Press Release    October 12  2006         NELSON RECEIVES   FRIEND OF FARM BUREAU    AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      The Nebraska Farm Bureau honored Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson with its   Friend of Farm Bureau    award for his support of agriculture  Nelson received a 75 percent voting record approval from the American Farm Bureau Board of Directors       I  m proud to fight in Washington for our farmers and ranchers in Nebraska     said Nelson    I have enjoyed working with the Farm Bureau and look forward to supporting our mutual priorities and Nebraska  s interests in the future          We really appreciate Ben  s leadership role he has played in the Senate on behalf of the priority issues for Farm Bureau     said Nebraska Farm Bureau President Keith Olsen    His work on legislation efforts regarding trade  estate taxes  renewable fuel standard and disaster assistance has been very important to Nebraska farmers and ranchers        Senator Nelson is a member of the Senate Agriculture Committee and has been a constant proponent of emergency drought assistance for farmers  reopening beef trade with Japan and bean trade with Iraq  and developing renewable fuels like ethanol
