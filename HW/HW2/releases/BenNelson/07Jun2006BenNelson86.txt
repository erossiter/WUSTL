                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON MARRIAGE PROTECTION AMENDMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    June 7  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON MARRIAGE PROTECTION AMENDMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Today  the United States Senate voted on a procedural motion to allow debate and an up or down vote on the Marriage Protection Amendment  The Marriage Protection Amendment would define marriage as a union between a man and a woman  The motion failed 49 48  it needed 60 votes to pass  Senator Nelson voted in favor of proceeding to the MPA and issued the following statement after the vote       I believe marriage to be a unity between a husband and a wife  I supported the Nebraska state law passed by the voters in 2000 and voted for the federal amendment because the courts have preempted the rights of the states in this matter
