                                                          Press Release    April 28  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT  S PRESS CONFERENCE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement in response to the president  s press conference on Thursday  April 28       I share the President  s goal of passing a comprehensive energy security and independence policy aimed at increasing conservation and reducing our dependence on foreign sources of energy       The House version of the energy bill includes provisions that many of my colleagues from both sides of the aisle will find objectionable  It is unfortunate that some special interest provisions continue to threaten the likelihood of passing an energy bill even as energy prices continue to soar  I support this energy bill because of important provisions like the renewable fuels standard that will significantly boost the domestic production of ethanol and other biofuels  I am working with members from both Parties and both Houses of Congress to find agreement and complete this bill as soon as possible       When I met with the president  s staff earlier this week I emphasized to them that most people are looking for a solution to the long term Social Security solvency problem  Tonight  while he did not lay out a complete plan to address solvency  I am pleased that he did present one idea for us to consider as part of a solvency solution                                                               30
