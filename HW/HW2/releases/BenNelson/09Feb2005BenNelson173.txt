                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    February 9  2005         PLYMOUTH VOLUNTEER  NAPER RURAL  AND HICKMAN VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENTS RECEIVE FEMA FIREFIGHTING GRANTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and the U S  Fire Administration  USFA  has awarded Assistance to Firefighters Grants to the Plymouth Volunteer Fire Department  Naper Rural Fire Department and the Hickman Volunteer Fire Department     Designed to strengthen and enhance basic firefighting capabilities and other emergency services across the United States  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program will give awards through four program areas   These areas include the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  the Fire Prevention Program  the Emergency Medical Services Program  and the Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program      The thirty fourth round of FEMA grants brings Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety funding to three rural Nebraska fire departments   The Plymouth Volunteer Fire Department will receive  112 500  and the Naper Rural Fire Department will receive  27 315  and the Hickman Volunteer Fire Department will receive  144  000 for new firefighting vehicles      The Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for additional training  fitness equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   This grant also provides funding for new firefighting equipment   The Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program includes the purchase of  pumpers  brush trucks  tankers tenders  rescue vehicles  ambulances  quints  aerials  foam units and fire boats        With these funds  our fire departments can expand their efforts to continue to make Nebraska as safe as it can be  said Nelson        These grants will assist these three rural fire departments in more efficiently protecting Nebraska families          30
