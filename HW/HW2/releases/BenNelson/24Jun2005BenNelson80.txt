                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    June 24  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON USDA BSE TEST RESULTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C     Today  U S  Department of Agriculture Secretary Mike Johanns announced that testing has confirmed a second case of mad cow disease in the United States  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement regarding the announced test results        Even though the test results from the Veterinary Laboratories Agency in Weybridge  England confirm the cow tested positive for bovine spongiform encephalopathy  BSE   the meat was prevented from entering the food supply    Stringent safeguards have been put in place to protect our food supply       Secretary Johanns said the food supply is safe and that the threat of BSE in the United States is remote  388 000 total tests have been made since the discovery of BSE in the United States and this is only the third animal that has tested positive  With public health our first concern in these matters  the USDA has assured all Americans that beef is safe to eat       The system we have in place to protect our food supply is working  Our firewalls and safeguards are identifying risks and stopping questionable products from reaching the market                                                            30
