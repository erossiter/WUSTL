                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON RECOGNIZED FOR LEADERSHIP ON RURAL ISSUES                                                          Press Release    September 18  2007         NELSON RECOGNIZED FOR LEADERSHIP ON RURAL ISSUES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Nelson Awarded 2007 National Farmers Union Golden Triangle Award                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   September 18  2007  The National  Farmers Union yesterday recognized the work of Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson by presenting  him with a 2007 Golden Triangle Award  Americas farmers and ranchers have  taken a central role in recent policy debates  said Nelson  Food and fuel  security are critical to the future of our nation and rural communities are an  important part of these discussions  I am honored to receive this award and I  will continue to champion issues important to rural America  The Golden Triangle Award is the  highest honor given by the National Farmers Union The award is given annually  to members of Congress who have demonstrated outstanding leadership on issues  affecting family farmers  ranchers and rural communities  Nelson was  specifically recognized for his work in support for a farm bill that addresses  the challenges and opportunities in rural America  the promotion and production  of biofuels  support for permanent emergency weather related disaster assistance  and his on going support of mandatory country of origin  labeling  The National Farmers Union is a  general farm organization with a membership of nearly 300 000 farm and ranch  families throughout the United States  Other recipients of  this award include Sen  Tom Harkin  D Iowa  Sen  Max Baucus  D Mont   and Sen   Harry Reid  D Nev
