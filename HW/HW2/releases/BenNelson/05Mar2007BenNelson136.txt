                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON   WE WANT ANSWERS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    March 5  2007         NELSON   WE WANT ANSWERS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           March 5  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today toured Walter Reed Medical Center and spoke to service members and staff about reported problems at that facility   Nelson  chair of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee  issued the following statement following his visit        What is clear to me is there is a group of professional folks working hard to repair the buildings and improve living conditions   They should be commended for their actions and the care the soldiers in the Hospital are receiving         Questions remain about the bureaucracy that led to neglect at Building 18 and delays in treatment for outpatients         We want answers  We want to know why things were overlooked and why matters were not addressed when brought to the leadership s attention        Nelson will participate in tomorrow s Senate Armed Services Committee hearing regarding Walter Reed
