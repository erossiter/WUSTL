                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON    I SUPPORT JUDGE MUKASEY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    November 5  2007         NELSON    I SUPPORT JUDGE MUKASEY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  November 5  2007  Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today met with Attorney General nominee  Judge Michael Mukasey  Following the meeting  Nelson announced that he would support the nomination and issued this statement    Judge Mukasey and I agree that a primary objective for the next Attorney General of the United States is to restore trust and credibility in the U S  Department of Justice  I plan to support his nomination     The Department of Justice has suffered a debilitating loss of credibility in recent months  Judge Mukasey has a daunting task ahead of him  to rebuild the American peoples trust in the Department of Justice and to refocus the department on upholding the rule of law  Without achieving those goals  the Department of Justice will continue to be ineffective     The Department of Justice is enduring a crisis of leadership  Judge Mukasey can provide the steady hand needed to move the Department forward
