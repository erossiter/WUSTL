                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON THE   GANG OF 14   MEETING OF July 14  2005                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Press Release    July 14  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE   GANG OF 14   MEETING OF July 14  2005                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today hosted a meeting of the so called   gang of 14   Senators who forged a compromise that broke the logjam over judicial nominations in the Senate    the first meeting of the group since Associate Justice Sandra Day O  Connor announced that she would step down from the Supreme Court     The   Gang of 14   had agreed to continue meetings and communications periodically after signing the May 24 Memorandum of Understanding on Judicial Nominations  Today  s meeting was part of that ongoing communication       After the meeting  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement       In my opinion  the   gang of 14   has no interest in becoming a shadow judiciary committee or a shadow leadership organization  In fact  I hope the gang plays no role in the confirmation process because President Bush puts forward a nominee with broad appeal who will not be a judicial activist on the Court
