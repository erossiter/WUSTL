                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    April 6  2005         Federal Freedom of Information Act  FOIA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  DC  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that he is co sponsoring a bill aimed at strengthening the Federal Freedom of Information Act  FOIA     S  394  the Openness Promotes Effectiveness in our National Government Act  is appropriately referred to as The OPEN Government Act of 2005      To quote a Supreme Court opinion  the effective functioning of a free government like ours depends largely on the force of an informed public opinion     said Nelson     This informed public opinion is largely due to the media and its effective use of tools such as the Freedom of Information Act   We need to do everything we can to make the information as accessible as possible        S  394 promotes accessibility  accountability and openness by strengthening the law through the closing of loopholes   Agencies will not be permitted to outsource recordkeeping functions and then deny FOIA requests because they are not in physical possession of the records       It will also help those making requests get timely responses by setting time limits for agencies to act on requests   It further provides tracking numbers so requesters can follow the status of their request       When I was Governor  every time I walked through the front portal of the Capitol in Lincoln I  made it a point to look up at one of my favorite inscriptions on the building     said Nelson    It reads   The Salvation of the State is Watchfulness in the Citizen     That watchfulness is possible through an independent news media aided by a free flow of information using such tools as FOIA and The OPEN Government Act of 2005
