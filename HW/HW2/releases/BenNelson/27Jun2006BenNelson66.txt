                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON SUPPORTS CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT TO PROTECT THE AMERICAN FLAG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    June 27  2006         NELSON SUPPORTS CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT TO PROTECT THE AMERICAN FLAG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Effort defeated 66 34   The Senate today voted on a constitutional amendment to protect the American flag  The amendment failed 66 34  it required 67 votes to pass  Following the vote  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson  an original cosponsor of the amendment  released the following statement       The flag is a very special symbol of liberty and freedom and should be protected  This is an issue you should think about with your heart  We owe it to our veterans to protect the flag they marched into battle under and sacrificed their lives to protect
