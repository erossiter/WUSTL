                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON CHILDREN S HEALTH LEGISLATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    November 1  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON CHILDREN S HEALTH LEGISLATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  November 1  2007   Today  the United States Senate again passed legislation to reauthorize and expand the State Childrens Health Insurance Program  S CHIP   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson voted in favor of the measure and issued the following statement    This legislation will ensure that millions of children will have access to affordable health care  Leaders from both parties have made changes to alleviate concerns about this bill and it is my hope that those changes will be enough to ensure it is enacted into law
