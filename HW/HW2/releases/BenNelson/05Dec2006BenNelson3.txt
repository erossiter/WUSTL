                                                         Press Release    December 5  2006         NELSON  SENATE DRAGGING FEET WHILE FARMERS SUFFER FROM DROUGHT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     The Senate today neglected to pass assistance for America  s farmers and ranchers suffering from drought  Again  the Senate voted 57 37 today against a bipartisan emergency aid amendment to the Agriculture Appropriations Bill  HR 5384   Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson sponsored and voted for the amendment       I  m discouraged that Congress keeps dancing around this critical aid     said Nelson    It  s unacceptable for Washington to keep dragging its feet when our farmers need action now        This year  s net farm income will be 20 percent lower than last year  s according to a report released by the U S  Department of Agriculture last week  Also  Nebraska farmers and ranchers are estimated to have lost over 340 million dollars this production year alone due to the ongoing drought       The failure to provide this needed relief threatens many small  rural businesses and communities and it threatens our nation  s food and fuel security efforts     said Nelson    We can  t stop a natural disaster like drought  but we can and should help when a disaster hits
