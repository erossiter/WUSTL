                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON MEMORIAL DAY STATEMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Press Release    May 25  2007         NELSON MEMORIAL DAY STATEMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the upcoming Memorial Day holiday        This Memorial Day is significant as we mourn and remember 49 Nebraska heroes who have died in the service of our country in Iraq and Afghanistan        We can never forget these and the millions of men and women who gave their lives in other conflicts to strengthen our nation and spread the promise of equality and liberty throughout the world        We can honor their memory by supporting our troops and thanking them for the sacrifices they have made  and those they will be asked to make in the future        Nelson is Chairman of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee and last month led a Congressional Delegation to visit with officials and Nebraska troops in Iraq       The recording can be heard by visiting   http   bennelson senate gov news multimedia cfm
