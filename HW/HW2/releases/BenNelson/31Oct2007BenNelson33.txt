                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF EDWARD SCHAFER AS AGRICULTURE SECRETARY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Press Release    October 31  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF EDWARD SCHAFER AS AGRICULTURE SECRETARY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          October 31  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement today regarding the nomination of former North Dakota Gov  Edward Schafer as Secretary of Agriculture    Ed Schafer is a good friend and a former colleague  He and I worked together as governors on transportation  agriculture and other issues  I look forward to having him into the office to grill him on his position on permanent drought disaster aid  payment limitations and the Food and Energy Security Act the Senate is about to debate  I spoke with Gov  Schafer today and congratulated him on his nomination and told him I look forward to working with him    Senator Nelson is a member of the Senate Agriculture Committee
