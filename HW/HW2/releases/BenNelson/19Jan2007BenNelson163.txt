                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    January 19  2007         NELSON TO SERVE AS CHAIRMAN OF SENATE ARMED SERVICES PERSONNEL SUBCOMMITTEE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        The Senate Armed Services Committee unveiled its subcommittee memberships  naming Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson Chairman of its Personnel Subcommittee       The men and women serving our country in uniform are the most important aspect of our military     said Nelson    As Chairman of the Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee  I will be committed to providing our troops and their families with the security they need as they make sacrifices for the security of our country        As Chairman of the Personnel Subcommittee  Nelson plans to continue working for military benefits  increasing military endstrength  equipping forces  and transitioning from a Strategic Reserve to an Operational Reserve     As Ranking Member of the Personnel Subcommittee during the 109th Congress  Nelson fought for equipment for troops fighting in Iraq and Afghanistan  adoption benefits for military families  and health coverage for Guard and Reserve families     In addition to the Personnel Subcommittee  Senator Nelson was named to the   Emerging Threats and Capabilities   and   Strategic Forces   Subcommittees
