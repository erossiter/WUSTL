                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON OUTSOURCING PORT SECURITY OPERATIONS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    February 21  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON OUTSOURCING PORT SECURITY OPERATIONS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           OMAHA  NE    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement regarding the proposed sale of port security operations to a company owned by the government of the United Arab Emirates       I was surprised and astounded to learn of the proposed outsourcing of our port security operations  I support the efforts of Senators Collins and Lieberman to seek more information about the sale and the decision to do it       We need a bunch of answers from the Administration on why they believe this proposed outsourcing will enhance our homeland security efforts and not put the nation at risk
