                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  TIME TO MOVE FORWARD ON AMT FIX                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Press Release    December 6  2007         NELSON  TIME TO MOVE FORWARD ON AMT FIX                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            December 6  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today voted to allow debate on a measure that would prevent millions of Americans from being hit by the Alternative Minimum Tax  AMT   The effort required 60 votes and was not successful    It s time the Senate moved on this issue  The proposal before us may not be perfect  but we need to get to work now  said Nelson  I want to move forward  debate and vote on this issue so we prevent a tax increase for 19 million Americans  including 96 000 Nebraskans  Political games should not result in higher taxes
