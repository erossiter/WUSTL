                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON VETO OF CHILDREN S HEALTH BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Press Release    October 3  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON VETO OF CHILDREN S HEALTH BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 October 3  2007   Today  President George W  Bush vetoed the State Children s Health Insurance Program  SCHIP   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson  who joined 66 of his colleagues in supporting the bill  issued the following statement    I am very disappointed that President Bush chose this path  It has been clear for weeks what the final bill would look like  Yet  during this time  President Bush chose to issue veto threats rather than work with the Senate and House to find common ground    The Senate passed this legislation by a veto proof margin  with 18 Republicans joining Democrats in voting for the bill  This is a program that works and helps uninsured children get health care    I hope my colleagues in the House join the Senate in voting to override this veto and renew this important program
