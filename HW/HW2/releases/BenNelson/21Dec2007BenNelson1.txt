                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON CELEBRATES AIR FORCE ANNIVERSARY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Press Release    December 21  2007         NELSON CELEBRATES AIR FORCE ANNIVERSARY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            December 19  2007  Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that his resolution recognizing the 60th anniversary of the United States Air Force as an independent military service  S  Res  416  was unanimously passed by the Senate  Nelson is the current co chair of the Air Force Caucus    The Air Force is an integral part of our nations security and defense efforts  said Senator Nelson  It is only fitting that we commemorate the proud 60 year history of this branch of the military and honor the brave men and women who have proudly served their country in the Air Force
