                     PRESS RELEASE  THE CITY OF LINCOLN  LINCOLN ARTS COUNCIL  NEBRASKA ARTS COUNCIL AND OMAHA SYMPHONY ASSOCIATION RECEIVE NATIONAL ENDOWMENT FOR THE ARTS GRANTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Press Release    April 21  2005         THE CITY OF LINCOLN  LINCOLN ARTS COUNCIL  NEBRASKA ARTS COUNCIL AND OMAHA SYMPHONY ASSOCIATION RECEIVE NATIONAL ENDOWMENT FOR THE ARTS GRANTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that the National Endowment for the Arts  NEA  has awarded grants to the City of Lincoln  the Lincoln Arts Council  the Nebraska Arts Council and the Omaha Symphony Association     Designed to support arts and education programs  these grants will give awards through arts education projects for children and youth  projects that present and or preserve artistically excellent work  and grants that distribute 40 percent of the agency  s program funds to the 56 state and jurisdictional arts agencies and six regional arts organizations  Additionally  these grants require at least a one to one match and generate  on average  seven dollars for each dollar awarded    The second round of NEA grants for FY 2005 brings support for arts and education programs to four Nebraska district organizations  The City of Lincoln will receive  20 000  the Lincoln Arts Council will receive  35 000  the Nebraska Arts Council will receive  604 800 and the Omaha Symphony Association will receive  10 000 for support to arts and education projects    These NEA grants provide funding for the East Meets West Summer Theatre Project  the Stories of Home Project  Partnership Agreement activities  and a symphonic tour to rural and underserved communities in Nebraska         These grants will assist these four organizations in more effectively and resourcefully promoting the arts to Nebraska communities     said Nelson     With these funds  these programs can expand their efforts to continue to make Nebraska as diversely educated as it can be              30
