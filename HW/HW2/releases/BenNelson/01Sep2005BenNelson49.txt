                                                         Press Release    September 1  2005         NELSON  ACT NOW ON DISASTER AID                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    OMAHA  NE    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson says Congress should move quickly to convene for an emergency session to approve  10 5 billion in disaster assistance for the Gulf Coast region to aid the rescue and recovery efforts in the wake of Hurricane Katrina       With every passing hour the situation in the Gulf Coast is worsening and we need to commit the resources needed to aid the rescue effort and begin the recovery effort     said Senator Nelson    Congress needs to support the effort and FEMA should be given resources to do everything they can to help those Americans that are suffering as a result of this disaster        The House of Representatives is expected to convene an emergency session Friday to pass the aid package and the Senate is expected to approve the package at 10 pm Thursday night according to media reports  In the interest of time  the Senate will pass the measure by unanimous consent  nullifying the need for Members to return to Washington   The package will provide  10 billion for the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and  500 million for the Department of Defense       America has suffered a great loss and I know most Nebraskans join me in extending our thoughts and prayers to the people of Louisiana  Alabama and Mississippi as they cope with this disaster     said Nelson          For information on how Nebraskans can help with the aid efforts visit Senator Nelson  s website at http   bennelson senate gov
