                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF CORETTA SCOTT KING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    January 31  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF CORETTA SCOTT KING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  DC    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today released the following statement on the death of Coretta Scott King       American has lost another civil rights icon  Coretta Scott King should be remembered as one of the most influential Americans of this era  Her impact on the country is yet to be fully measured  As Americans we owe a lot to Coretta Scott King and of course her husband  the late Dr  King  They are among the handful of Americans that have had an actual hand in shaping our country and changing it for the better
