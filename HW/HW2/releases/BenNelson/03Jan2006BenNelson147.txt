                                                             Press Release    January 3  2006         BEN NELSON  S   MAKING HEALTH INSURANCE AFFORDABLE TOUR    BEGINS TUESDAY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Nebraska  s Senator Will Visit 6 Cities to Meet with Business Leaders beginning January 3rd                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         OMAHA  NE    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson will visit with community and business leaders in 6 cities January 3   4  2006 to discuss his plan to expand health insurance coverage for small businesses as part of his   Making Health Insurance Affordable    tour       Nelson introduced legislation in November that would allow small businesses to band together to provide health insurance to their employees    a plan that would drive down health care costs for employers and expand health coverage for employees at Nebraska  s 30 000 small businesses        Nelson introduced the Health Insurance Marketplace Modernization and Affordability Act along with Senator Mike Enzi of Wyoming  the Chairman of the Senate Health Education Labor and Pensions Committee  This legislation has gained positive reviews from a number of prominent groups including the Nebraska Association of Realtors  the Associated Builders and Contractors  Inc   Association of Health Insurance Plans  Blue Cross and Blue Shield  Association of Health Underwriters  This legislation represents the first time the state insurance commissioners  insurance carriers  and small business groups have all worked together to find a workable solution
