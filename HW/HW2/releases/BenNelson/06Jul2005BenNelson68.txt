                     PRESS RELEASE  STATEMENT BY SENATOR BEN NELSON ON THE PASSING OF DON McGINLEY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Press Release    July 6  2005         STATEMENT BY SENATOR BEN NELSON ON THE PASSING OF DON McGINLEY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Don McGinley became a dear personal friend over the years beginning when I first ran for Governor in 1990 and he helped me in the campaign  He was a great Nebraskan and a great American who served in Congress  the Nebraska Unicameral  as Lt  Governor  and as head of the Selective Service  a post to which I appointed him     Don overcame a lot of personal challenges but he was always someone I could count on and I enjoyed his wonderful sense of humor   He always had a one liner or a funny story    He brought down the house with his imitation of me doing my imitation of former Governor Frank Morrison   I  ll miss him
