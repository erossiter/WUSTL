                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON VOICES OPPOSITION TO  COMPREHENSIVE  IMMIGRATION BILL ON SENATE FLOOR                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Press Release    May 21  2007         NELSON VOICES OPPOSITION TO  COMPREHENSIVE  IMMIGRATION BILL ON SENATE FLOOR                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Nebraska s Senator Votes to Allow Debate to Proceed in     Hopes of Improving Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          May 21  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today announced his opposition to the immigration reform proposal before the Senate         In a speech on the floor the Senate  Nelson reiterated his support for a  border security first  policy that would reduce illegal border crossings and improve workplace enforcements while streamlining the legal immigration process          There are three steps to resolving this problem   first is border security  second is fixing legal immigration and third is addressing those who are here illegally   said Nelson    We should give the border security provisions a chance to prove that they will work and can effectively slow and stop illegal immigration   But instead we are being asked to jump to step three before steps one and two are completed        Last Congress  Nelson introduced legislation to tackle the border security problem first   Portions of that bill were enacted and  in a February trip to the border  Nelson saw that beefed up security and construction of a border barrier were slowing the number of illegal border crossings   Today  he still believes that we shouldn t short circuit border security by discussing amnesty proposals          We must secure the borders so that we don t have millions more illegal immigrants   If we do not  we will only encourage millions more to cross the border illegally in hopes of being part of the amnesty offered under this legislation   said Nelson       Today  the Senate voted to invoke cloture and proceed to consideration of the immigration bill   Nelson voted to allow debate to continue on the legislation          I will vote for cloture on the motion proceed  but not because I support the underlying bill   said Nelson in his floor speech    I will support cloture only because I hope we can significantly improve this bill so that it addresses the problem properly   at the border first and then through fixing the legal immigration system
