                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON OPTIMISTIC ON ENERGY BILL AS SENATE PANEL COMPLETES LEGISLATION                                                          Press Release    May 26  2005         NELSON OPTIMISTIC ON ENERGY BILL AS SENATE PANEL COMPLETES LEGISLATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Bill includes Renewable Fuels Standard of 8 Billion Gallons Per Year                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON  D C     The Senate Energy Committee passed its comprehensive energy legislation that includes an historic Renewable Fuels Standard  RFS  that will boost production to 8 billion gallons per year by 2012  providing a needed lift to agriculture dependent economies like Nebraska  s  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson praised the Committee Action and expressed optimism that the Senate may pass the Energy bill again this year        It  s clear that use of ethanol and biodiesel  as part of a Renewable Fuels Standard is a win win win situation for Nebraska and the country  a win for farmers  a win for consumers  and a win for the environment     said Senator Ben Nelson    The renewable fuels standard in this bill will reduce our dependence on foreign oil  help lower gas prices in the long run  and boost the economy in rural states like Nebraska  I look forward to debating and voting on this bill this year          Current fuel ethanol production is around 3 4 billion gallons   Earlier this year  the House passed a 5 billion gallon standard   The RFS included in the Senate bill begins at 4 billion gallons in 2006 and increases to 8 billion gallons in 2012         Soon after being elected Nebraska  s 37th Governor  Nelson formed and was the first Chair of the Governor  s Ethanol Coalition to promote increased ethanol use nationwide  The Coalition began with nine Midwest states and grew to 22 states  Puerto Rico and 2 foreign countries under his stewardship   More than 6000 Nebraskans are now employed directly or indirectly in Nebraska ethanol production   One of the first bills Nelson cosponsored as Senator was a bill to boost production of ethanol and other renewable fuels  which was the basis for the agreement in last year  s Senate energy bill   Senator Nelson received the Renewable Energy Leadership Award in 2002 for his work on that bill
