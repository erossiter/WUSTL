                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON WANTS A 21ST CENTURY DEFINITION FOR THE GUARD AND RESERVES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Press Release    March 8  2006         NELSON WANTS A 21ST CENTURY DEFINITION FOR THE GUARD AND RESERVES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Nebraska  s Senator asks independent Commission on the National Guard and Reserves to examine role of Services in Pentagon  s future plans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today asked the Commission on the National Guard and Reserves to examine the past and present roles the National Guard and Reserves have played in our national defense strategy and to develop a 21st Century definition for the Guard and Reserves for the future        The role of the National Guard has changed over the years  from the local militia that fought at Lexington and Concord to the modern day fighting force that has seen battle in Fallujah and Baghdad     said Nelson  Ranking Member of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee    This Commission must examine the roles of the Guard and Reserves and how the proposed reformation of America  s fighting forces will impact their ability to meet operational duties here and abroad         During one phase of the Iraq War  nearly half of the U S  combat forces were Army National Guard soldiers  There is high demand domestically for the Guard  90 percent of the troops responding to Hurricane Katrina were National Guard members     To ensure the protection and preparedness of the National Guard  Senator Nelson has fought to properly equip the Guard  He also introduced a resolution that unanimously passed the Senate last month calling on the Pentagon to confer with Congress and the nation  s governors on any proposed reduction in the Guard  s force  structure  or personnel      The Comission on the National Guard was authorized in the National Defense Authorization act of 2005   The Commission is to examine the role of the Guard and Reserves in the modern military and make recommendations to Congress based on its findings
