                                                         Press Release    April 17  2007         NELSON RECEIVES U S  CHAMBER  SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE  AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Nebraska s Senator Recognized for Support of Key Business Issues                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON  DC  The United States Chamber of Commerce today presented Sen  Ben Nelson  D NE  with its annual  Spirit of Enterprise  award for his strong voting record on critical business issues in the second session of the 109th Congress  This is the fifth time Nelson has been honored with the award        Sen  Nelson proved to be a valuable ally to the business community last year  supporting measures to stimulate job creation and spur economic growth   said Tom Donohue  Chamber president and CEO   The business community is grateful for Ben s dedication to a pro growth agenda and we are proud to present him with this award         By supporting American businesses  we re supporting American jobs and economic growth   said Nelson   I m proud of my pro business track record and will continue working on behalf of the millions of American families who benefit from strong business        The Chamber s prestigious  Spirit of Enterprise  award is given annually to members of Congress based on rankings it gives for key business votes outlined in its yearly publication  How They Voted  Sen  Nelson compiled an 83  score with the Chamber last year and holds a cumulative 80  ranking during his tenure in Congress       The Chamber s designated  key votes  are recorded floor votes on issues established as priorities by the Chamber s board of directors  on which the Chamber informs Congress of its position prior to the vote  Senators and House members who support the Chamber s position on at least 70  of these key votes receive the  Spirit of Enterprise  award
