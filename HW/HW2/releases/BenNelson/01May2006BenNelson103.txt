                                                                                             Nelson    We need to do something about skyrocketing costs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            OMAHA  NE    46 million Americans are without health insurance   Some because they lost their jobs  others because their employers do not provide health insurance plans because the cost is unaffordable     The week of May 1st through 7th has been designated as Cover the Uninsured Week  The following week  the Senate will consider Senator Ben Nelson  s bill to expand health insurance coverage to one million employees of small businesses nationwide       We need to do something about skyrocketing health insurance costs or we will soon have an entire workforce without health insurance     said Nelson     Of the 30 000 small businesses in Nebraska  only 10 000 provide health insurance coverage for their employees  My bill will make health insurance more affordable for Nebraska  s small businesses        Nelson  s bill  S  1955  The Health Insurance Marketplace and Modernization Act  will allow small businesses and organizations to pool resources when negotiating for health insurance coverage giving them greater bargaining power to secure lower premiums
