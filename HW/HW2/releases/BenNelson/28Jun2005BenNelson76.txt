                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON REACTION TO PRESIDENTIAL ADDRESS ON IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Press Release    June 28  2005         NELSON REACTION TO PRESIDENTIAL ADDRESS ON IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement in response to tonight  s presidential address on Iraq       Tonight  s address was a solid reminder of what are the challenges our soldiers face and the accomplishments we have achieved so far in the effort to establish a free and safe Iraq        I agree  we don  t want our troops to stay one day longer than they have to stay  But tonight we didn  t hear  unfortunately  what the milestones are  what the objectives are  that the Iraqis need to meet before we can start bringing our troops home  And that  s what we need to focus on       We all support our troops and we all want to see them succeed in Iraq  Hopefully  notwithstanding the debate that rages here at home  our troops will know that Americans on both sides of our policies in Iraq support their efforts in Iraq       This is not a lost cause  it is a tough road and one that we cannot abandon until Iraq has a constitution  has a military  can stand on its own and can defend itself
