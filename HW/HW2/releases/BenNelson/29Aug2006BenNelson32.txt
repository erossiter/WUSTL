                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  DROUGHT ASSISTANCE WELCOME  BUT INSUFFICIENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Press Release    August 29  2006         NELSON  DROUGHT ASSISTANCE WELCOME  BUT INSUFFICIENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Following the U S  Dept of Agriculture  s  USDA  announcement of  780 million in drought aid for America  s farmers and ranchers  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement       This is welcome assistance for our farmers and ranchers coping with the consequences of drought  but unfortunately farmers need comprehensive drought relief along the lines of what I am trying to get passed in the Senate          Information from the national drought mitigation center at the University of Nebraska Lincoln is crucial in mitigating and assessing the damage done by drought  That  s why earlier this week I wrote a letter signed by the entire Nebraska congressional delegation  to Secretary Johanns encouraging him to expand funding for drought mitigation in next year  s agriculture budget
