                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON OPPOSES AMNESTY FOR TERRORISTS IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Press Release    June 20  2006         NELSON OPPOSES AMNESTY FOR TERRORISTS IN IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today voted against granting amnesty to terrorists in Iraq who have attacked  killed or wounded American forces        I oppose granting amnesty to terrorists    said Senator Nelson    We don  t need an amnesty plan  what we need is a pathway to prison for terrorists        Nelson voted in favor of an amendment that expresses the sense of the Congress that granting amnesty to terrorists in Iraq is unacceptable  The amendment  offered by Senator Bill Nelson of Florida  simply states that it  s the sense of the United States Congress that the new Iraqi government should not grant amnesty to persons known to have attacked  killed or wounded members of the Armed Forces of the United States and that the president should immediately notify the Iraqi government that the U S  Government strongly opposes granting amnesty to those individuals        The reported Iraqi proposal to grant amnesty to terrorists is disrespectful to the 2500 American soldiers who died to free Iraq  the thousands who have been wounded in that effort and the hundreds of thousands who continue to serve there     Nelson added    I am pleased that the government has disavowed their plan to offer amnesty to terrorists        The amendment was offered to the Fiscal Year 2007 National Defense Authorization Act  S  2766  and was adopted by a vote of 79 19
