                     PRESS RELEASE  SENATE PANEL APPROVES GEN  CHILTON TO HEAD STRATCOM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Press Release    September 28  2007         SENATE PANEL APPROVES GEN  CHILTON TO HEAD STRATCOM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                September 28  2007   The Senate Armed Services Committee last night approved the nomination of General Kevin Chilton as the head of STRATCOM  Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson joined his colleagues on the committee in supporting the nomination    General Chilton is highly qualified individual and he will bring strong leadership to STRATCOM  I know he will continue the legacy of partnership with the Omaha and Bellevue communities established by his predecessors  This is a critical time for STRATCOM as it has the potential to absorb more command responsibilities and Gen  Chilton will provide the steady leadership needed  said Nelson    Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson is the Chairman of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee  Nelson presided over Thursday s full Committee confirmation hearing    A vote by the full Senate has not yet been scheduled
