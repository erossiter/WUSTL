                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON PUSHES THREE MONTH EXTENSION OF PATRIOT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Press Release    December 20  2005         NELSON PUSHES THREE MONTH EXTENSION OF PATRIOT ACT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Nebraska  s Senator says three month extension would allow time for full renewal of anti terrorism measure                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C     With anti terrorism laws set to expire on December 31  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson is part of an effort to pass a temporary extension of the laws before congress adjourns for the year sometime this week     Nelson and a number of Senators are sending a letter to Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist of Tennessee asking him to allow the Senate to pass a three month extension of the current Patriot Act  Senator Nelson is a cosponsor of this legislation  S  2082       We need to give our law enforcement and anti terrorism officials the tools they need to keep us safe     said Senator Nelson    Extending the Patriot Act for three months would give Congress more time to develop a stronger anti terrorism bill         Last week  Nelson voted against a filibuster of controversial new anti terrorism legislation  The filibuster succeeded  leaving in place the current Patriot Act law that expires at the end of this year  The extension Nelson is pushing would push the expiration back three months      Nelson has also called for an investigation by the Senate Intelligence Committee into the Administration  s program that allows domestic surveillance of American citizens
