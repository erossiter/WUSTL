                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON 5TH ANNIVERSARY OF 9 11                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Press Release    September 11  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON 5TH ANNIVERSARY OF 9 11                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        The statement is as follows       Five years after the cowardly and brutal attack on America in many ways our country is more secure but more needs to be done   We need to tighten border and port security and our intelligence gathering capabilities need to remain as efficient as possible to prevent future attacks       The attack certainly changed many things about America but it did not change American ideals       Our flag still flies proudly for the entire world to see       The people of this great country have turned anguish to strength and pain to purpose       Yes  America is a changed nation  more somber and more steadfast   The acts of terror that pierced the calm of that beautiful fall morning 5 years ago may have smashed stone and twisted steel  but they did not break America  s spirit       This is our country  and it remains the land of the free and the home of the brave
