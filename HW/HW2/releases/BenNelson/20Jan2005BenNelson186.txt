                                                         Press Release    January 20  2005         NELSON  SENATE CONFIRMS JOHANNS FOR AG SECRETARY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON  D C     The United States Senate today by unanimous consent confirmed the nomination of Governor Mike Johanns to be U S  Secretary of Agriculture  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced     Johanns   nomination was taken up in a special session of the Senate today at 3 00 PM ET following the Presidential Inauguration ceremony and related activities  Johanns   nomination was approved by voice vote  with no objection     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the confirmation of Governor Johanns       The Senate  s unanimous confirmation affirms the president  s choice for USDA Secretary  our own Governor Mike Johanns       Governor Johanns and I have discussed and agree it is important that as Agriculture Secretary  he work in a bipartisan fashion with members of the Senate and Congress to address the challenges facing American agriculture       The confirmation of Governor Johanns as Secretary of Agriculture is good for Nebraska and Nebraska agriculture   I look forward to working with him in this new capacity
