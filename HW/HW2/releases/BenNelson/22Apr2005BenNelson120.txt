                                                         Press Release    April 22  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON PACE NOMINATION TO JOINT CHIEFS OF STAFF                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON  D C     On Friday Marine Gen  Peter Pace  USMC  who quietly helped shape the Pentagon s role in the global war on terrorism  was nominated to be the next chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff  Admiral Edmund P  Giambastiani  Jr   USN  was also nominated to be vice chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff         General Peter Pace is the right choice for this critical position     said Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson     His experience  integrity  leadership  and ability will serve all branches of the military well especially as he takes over during continuing operations in Iraq  Afghanistan and other areas around the world  I also look forward to working with General Pace as he works to meet the challenges associated with the ongoing reorganization of the military          Pace is the first Marine selected for the top military position and would succeed Air Force Gen  Richard B  Myers  He is expected to win easy Senate confirmation         Admiral Giambastiani is an experienced  thoughtful and highly respected officer and I know he will serve honorably as vice chairman     Nelson said       Nelson is a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee which will consider Pace  s nomination        30
