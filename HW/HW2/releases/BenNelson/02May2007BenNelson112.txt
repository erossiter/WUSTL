                                                         Press Release    May 2  2007         NELSON HONORED WITH  ADAM SMITH  AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Nebraska s Senator Recognized for Support of Free Enterprise                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       May 2  2007   In a ceremony last night  Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson was presented the  Adam Smith  Award for his continued support for lower taxes  business development and free markets          I ve been very proud to have a close working relationship with business leaders both in Nebraska and in Washington   said Nelson    I am honored to have been selected for this award and I will continue to push initiatives to make it easier for businesses to do business        The award was presented by the Business Industry Political Action Committee  BIPAC   a leading business group  and the Friends of Adam Smith  a non profit foundation that researches and promotes the historical and relevant link between politics  public policy and economic freedom in this nation  The organization also seeks to highlight the positive contributions of business and business leaders to the development of good government and lasting prosperity   The award is given to one elected official and one business leader each year whose life work emphasizes their commitment to promoting free enterprise       Nelson was recognized for his leadership in promoting business growth and free markets including       His leadership in passing six tax cut packages that have been signed into law        His support of legislation to curb bankruptcy abuses to protect consumers  creditors  and family farmers        His work on legislation to allow small businesses to band together to purchase health care for employees  and       His efforts  as both governor of Nebraska and senator in promoting trade policies that allow domestic products access to foreign markets        Past recipients of the Adam Smith Award include former Congressman Charles Stenholm of Texas  House Minority Leader John Boehner  and Senators Kay Bailey Hutchinson  Jon Kyl and Larry Craig
