                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON FOILED TERRORIST PLOT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Press Release    August 10  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON FOILED TERRORIST PLOT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          OMAHA  NE    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today released the following statement on the suspected terrorist plot that was disrupted           A plot of this magnitude by would be mass murderers is a somber reminder that we remain involved in a global war on terror and cannot afford to let down our defenses       I speak for peace loving people everywhere in expressing our thanks to the intelligence community for disrupting this barbarous plot and saving countless numbers of innocent lives
