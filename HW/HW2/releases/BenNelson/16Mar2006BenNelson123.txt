                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON RAISING DEBT CEILING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    March 16  2006         NELSON STATEMENT ON RAISING DEBT CEILING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  DC    Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson released the following statement today on increasing the national debt limit       Raising the debt ceiling allows Washington to continue to spend without cutting costs   Instead of forcing Washington to adopt a pay as you go budget  this proposal encourages government to spend now without even a plan to pay for it later   If we raise the debt ceiling  the nation will just go deeper in debt  Washington will never get out of the hole if it doesn  t stop digging
