                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON   NEW ATTORNEY GENERAL MUST BE INDEPENDENT  RESTORE CONFIDENCE IN JUSTICE DEPARTMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Press Release    September 17  2007         NELSON   NEW ATTORNEY GENERAL MUST BE INDEPENDENT  RESTORE CONFIDENCE IN JUSTICE DEPARTMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        September 17  2007  Today  President George W  Bush announced that he has selected former U S  District Judge Michael B  Mukasey as his nominee for attorney general  Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the announcement    For months  the Department of Justice has been rocked by controversy and conflict  With todays announcement  I am hopeful the Administration can put those days behind it  Judge Mukasey appears to be well qualified for the job and has a reputation for being fair and independent  someone who has not built a career on being a Washington insider  Above all else  we need to have an attorney general who can restore confidence in the Department of Justice  both internally and with Congress and the American people
