                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON BILL DESIGNATES SEPTEMBER AS NATIONAL LIFE INSURANCE AWARENESS MONTH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Press Release    September 7  2006         NELSON BILL DESIGNATES SEPTEMBER AS NATIONAL LIFE INSURANCE AWARENESS MONTH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        With nearly 70 million Americans lacking the life insurance coverage they need to ensure a secure financial future for their families  the Senate passed a bill  S R  448  yesterday introduced by Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson and Georgia Senator Saxby Chambliss recognizing September 2006 as   National Life Insurance Awareness Month          This legislation will help raise awareness of the importance of life insurance     said Nelson    I hope this designation will encourage more Americans to seek information and secure the lifetime protection their families need
