                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    March 16  2005         WINNEBAGO TRIBE OF NEBRASKA RECEIVES FEMA FIREFIGHTING GRANT FOR FIRE SAFETY PROGRAM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON  D C     The Winnebago Tribe of Nebraska will receive a  65 448 grant to enhance firefighter safety  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today  The grant comes from the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and the U S  Fire Administration  USFA  as part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program      The grant to the Winnebago Tribe of Nebraska comes as part of the thirty ninth round of FEMA fire fighting grants   The  65 448 grant is awarded through the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program      The Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding for additional training  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to fire stations and facilities         This grant will assist the Winnebago Tribe of Nebraska with education programs and fire prevention     said Nelson     With these funds  the Winnebago Tribe will more efficiently protect their community        Designed to strengthen and enhance basic firefighting capabilities and other emergency services across the United States  the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program gives awards through four program areas   These areas include the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  the Fire Prevention Program  the Emergency Medical Services Program  and the Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program         30
