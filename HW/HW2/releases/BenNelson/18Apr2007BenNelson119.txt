                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON SUPREME COURT RULING ON PARTIAL BIRTH ABORTION BAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Press Release    April 18  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON SUPREME COURT RULING ON PARTIAL BIRTH ABORTION BAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             April 18  2007   Today  in a 5 4 vote  the United States Supreme Court upheld a federal law banning partial birth abortions   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson voted for and was a co sponsor of the legislation   The law had previously been challenged by federal courts in Nebraska  New York and California   The decision today overturns those rulings       Nelson issued the following statement on today s decision        I agree with today s decision by the Supreme Court and I disagree with federal Judge Richard Kopf and others who have struck down the ban on partial birth abortion   I am pleased that the U S  Supreme Court s final say overturns poor decisions made by lower federal courts         When Justices John Roberts and Samuel Alito sat in my office before their confirmation hearings  they pledged they would interpret the law  not make the law   Today s decision is a victory over judicial activism
