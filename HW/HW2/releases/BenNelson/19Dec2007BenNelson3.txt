                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON SUPPORTS FUNDING FOR TROOPS IN IRAQ  AFGHANISTAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Press Release    December 19  2007         NELSON SUPPORTS FUNDING FOR TROOPS IN IRAQ  AFGHANISTAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            December 19  2007  The United States Senate last night approved a measure to provide  70 billion in emergency funding for the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan  The amendment was added to the FY08 Omnibus appropriations bill    Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson  a member of the Armed Services and Appropriations Committees  joined 69 of his colleagues in supporting the amendment  Nelson issued the following statement    It is critically important that we provide the troops in the field with the equipment and resources they need to do their jobs  The Administration had initially requested nearly  190 billion in emergency  off budget funding for Iraq and Afghanistan  I would have preferred to consider a complete package  or even better to have worked within the regular budget process  But it is crucial that we pass at least part of the funding now  I expect we will have a rigorous and necessary debate in 2008 about the rest of this package and our future mission in Iraq
