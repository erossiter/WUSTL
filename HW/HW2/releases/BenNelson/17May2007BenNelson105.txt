                                                         Press Release    May 17  2007         NELSON ATTENDS WHITE HOUSE COMMISSIONING CEREMONY FOR RESERVE OFFICERS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             May 17  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today joined President George W  Bush and Secretary of Defense Robert Gates at a Joint Reserve Officer Training Corp  ROTC  Commissioning Ceremony at the White House  University of Nebraska Lincoln graduates Second Lieutenant Michael Keblesh of Omaha  Second Lieutenant Michael Horab  and Illinois Institute of Technology grad Ensign Vu Chu of Lincoln were commissioned at today s ceremony        Lts  Keblesh and Horab and Ensign Chu are just three example of the excellent officers we have in the reserves   They have trained long and hard for the opportunity to serve their nation and I was proud to join the President  Secretary Gates and their families at today s ceremony   said Nelson       The ceremony  which commissioned fifty five officers from both the Army and Navy ROTC  was recognition of the contributions members of the reserves have made throughout American history and particularly in the recent operations in Afghanistan and Iraq          The burdens placed on members of the reserves are significant   they are often asked to leave their regular jobs and families for months at a time to serve in dangerous regions   Americans can be honored to have these men and women representing our military and our country   said Nelson       As the chair of the Senate Armed Services Personnel Subcommittee  Senator Nelson has fought to improve benefits for reservists and their families
