                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON   THE CONFIRMATION OF PRISCILLA OWEN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    May 25  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON   THE CONFIRMATION OF PRISCILLA OWEN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement on the confirmation of Priscilla Owen to U S  Circuit Judge for the Fifth Circuit         Though I voted to break the filibuster of Priscilla Owen  s nomination to the federal bench  I could not support her nomination because she has not convinced me that she will not be a judicial activist  I interviewed Ms  Owen and she did not adequately address my concerns about her past judicial activism and the possibility she could be an activist in the future          Owen was confirmed by a 56 43 vote        Along with Senator John McCain of Arizona  Senator Nelson brought together a bipartisan coalition of 14 Senators who constructed an agreement to break the logjam over President Bush  s judicial nominations  Nelson has voted five times to break the filibuster and allow Owen  s nomination to be considered by the full Senate
