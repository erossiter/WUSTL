                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON NUSSLE VOTE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Press Release    September 4  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON NUSSLE VOTE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    September 4  2007  Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson today joined 68 of his colleagues in voting to approve former Iowa Congressman Jim Nussle as Director of the Office of Management and Budget  Nelson issued the following statement  It is my hope that Jim Nussle will cooperate with Congress in a bipartisan fashion as we work to get the federal budget back on track  Over the past few years  the deficit has continued to grow and spending has continued to rise  Congress will look to him to discontinue the dishonest practice of funding the Iraq war off budget and to find ways to make permanent the tax cuts for American families  I look forward to working with him to achieve these goals
