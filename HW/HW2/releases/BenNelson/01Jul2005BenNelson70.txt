                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Press Release    July 1  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON THE RETIREMENT OF JUSTICE SANDRA DAY O  CONNOR                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  OMAHA  NE    Today  U S  Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O  Connor announced her retirement from the Court  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement regarding her retirement and the nomination of a new Supreme Court Justice       Sandra Day O  Connor has served with distinction and honor on the U S  Supreme Court and America is proud of her service  Justice O  Connor blazed the trail for women to serve as members of the Supreme Court and her contribution to history will be without question       I am honored to be serving in the United States Senate at such an historic moment  Supreme Court vacancies and nominations are a relatively rare occurrence   I look forward to working with President Bush and my colleagues in the Senate through the   advice and consent    clause in the U S  Constitution to identify and confirm a new Supreme Court Justice       Once a nominee is put forward  I  m sure the   Gang of Fourteen   Senators will meet and begin discussions about the nominee to ensure that the agreement we reached will remain intact  Any speculation on potential nominees is obviously premature  We will cross that bridge when we get there
