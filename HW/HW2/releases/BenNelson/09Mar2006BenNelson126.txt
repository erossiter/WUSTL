                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON VOTES TO ALLOW   UP OR DOWN VOTE    ON PORT SECURITY AMENDMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Press Release    March 9  2006         NELSON VOTES TO ALLOW   UP OR DOWN VOTE    ON PORT SECURITY AMENDMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Nebraska  s Senator Opposes Obstruction of Port Security Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today voted to allow an up or down vote on a controversial provision to block a deal which would turn operations at six U S  ports over to a United Arab Emirates owned company     Nelson entered the following statement into the Congressional Record       I will be voting against the motion to invoke cloture on the Lobbying Reform Bill  Typically  I vote for cloture motions because they are usually intended to facilitate an up or down vote on a piece of legislation or a nomination that is being stalled  Today  that is not the case  Yesterday cloture was filed    to prevent an up or down vote on an amendment  In this case  it is an amendment on port security  an issue of critical importance to this country right now  As a result  I will vote against cloture today to ensure that up or down votes are allowed to occur         The United States Senate voted 51 47 on a motion to invoke cloture on legislation to overhaul lobbying practices  Nelson voted   no   on the motion to invoke cloture so that debate could continue and to allow an up or down vote on the port security provision  The cloture motion required 67 votes  and thus was not passed     Nelson has consistently voted to allow up or down votes on important legislation and nominations such as judicial nominations  Nelson  s believes that the Senate needs to get things done and that measures intended solely to obstruct are counterproductive  Today  s vote was in support of securing an up or down vote for the port security provision and against efforts to block that provision from getting a vote
