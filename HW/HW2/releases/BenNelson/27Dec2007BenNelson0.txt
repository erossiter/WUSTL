                                                         Press Release    December 27  2007         NELSON STATEMENT ON ASSASSINATION OF BENAZIR BHUTTO                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                December 27  2007  Nebraskas Senator Ben Nelson today issued the following statement on the assassination of Pakistans opposition leader Benazir Bhutto   The death of Pakistan opposition leader Benazir Bhutto is a tragic loss for democracy in the world particularly for Pakistan  The assassination is a cowardly act of terrorism undertaken by extremists seeking to destabilize the region and those who planned it must be apprehended and brought to justice  I have met Ms  Bhutto and I want to send my condolences to her friends  family and supporters    In a formal meeting with President Pervez Musharraf  he assured me that Pakistans nuclear weapons are sufficiently secured  The United States must monitor the situation closely because further destabilization and the presence of terrorist forces such as Al Qaeda and Taliban in Northeast Pakistan represent a significant national security threat to the United States    In the midst of all this turmoil I am concerned about potentially having misspent billions of U S  dollars in Pakistan  I plan to ask the Senate Armed Services Committee to review our financial obligations to Pakistan and the efficiency of the use of U S  funds there   To download an audio version of this recording  visit Senator Nelsons multimedia
