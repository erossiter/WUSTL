                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON STATEMENT ON MEETING WITH JUDGE SAMUEL ALITO  NOMINEE TO THE U S  SUPREME COURT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Press Release    November 2  2005         NELSON STATEMENT ON MEETING WITH JUDGE SAMUEL ALITO  NOMINEE TO THE U S  SUPREME COURT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson today met with President Bush  s nominee to the Supreme Court  Judge Samuel Alito and released the following statement       I met with Judge Alito this morning for about forty minutes  We had a lengthy discussion on various issues  the role of the court  and his philosophy on judicial activism  It was a good meeting and I came away from it with a better knowledge and understanding of Judge Alito and his judicial philosophy        The American people deserve a fair and thorough confirmation process   Judge Alito  s meetings with members of the Senate are the beginning  not the end of this important process   It is my view that he is off to a good start  I look forward to learning even more about Judge Alito as the confirmation process unfolds  It would be unfair to prejudge the nominee  one way or the other  before he has had a fair hearing before the Judiciary Committee and had a chance to tell his story to the American people
