                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  IMMIGRATION COMPROMISE COLLAPSE LEAVES BORDERS OPEN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Press Release    June 21  2006         NELSON  IMMIGRATION COMPROMISE COLLAPSE LEAVES BORDERS OPEN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Following news reports that the U S  House of Representatives has largely given up on passing an amnesty and guest worker program this year  Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson issued the following statement        Most Nebraskans tell me they want Washington to focus on border security  If the senate had passed a bill that focused on border security first  we  d be on our way to securing the borders  Once again  the do everything approach has succeeded in doing nothing  While Washington regroups  the borders remain open  just as they have for the past decade  So that another decade doesn  t pass before our borders are secured  I will continue to pressure Washington to address border security first
