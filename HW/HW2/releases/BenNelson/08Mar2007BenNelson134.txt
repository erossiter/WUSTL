                                                         Press Release    March 8  2007         NELSON SUPPORTS LEGISLATION TO CLARIFY ENVIRONMENTAL RULES FOR LIVESTOCK PRODUCERS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bill Would Firmly Establish that Manure is Not a Hazardous Substance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               March 8  2007   Nebraska s Senator Ben Nelson today co sponsored legislation that would exempt manure from a list of hazardous substances under the Comprehensive Environmental Response  Compensation  and Liability Act  CERCLA  of 1980        Nebraska s cattlemen and pork producers add to our economy and are an intrinsic part of our rural communities   said Nelson    This legislation will ensure that they are not burdened by cumbersome regulation and litigation as a cost of running their businesses        CERCLA was established in 1980 to help clean up the most polluted areas of the country   The law  more commonly known as  Superfund   has since been interpreted by some as applying to manure produced by domesticated livestock         Today s legislation is designed to ensure that farms  ranches and other properties will not become Superfund sites   Without such protection  farms and ranches that produce large amounts of manure could be targeted for the costs of clean up       This legislation  supported by the National Pork Producers Council and the National Cattlemen s Beef Association  is sponsored by Arkansas Senator Blanche Lincoln
