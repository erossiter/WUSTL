                     PRESS RELEASE  U S  CHAMBER HONORS SENATOR BEN NELSON WITH   SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE    AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Press Release    March 10  2005         U S  CHAMBER HONORS SENATOR BEN NELSON WITH   SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE    AWARD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Nelson Recognized for His Pro Economic Opportunity Agenda                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C   The United States Chamber of Commerce today presented Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson with its   Spirit of Enterprise    award for his strong support for a pro economic growth legislative agenda in the second session of the 108th Congress       It is critical to have members of Congress fighting for American business and their employees at this time of economic expansion and opportunity     said Thomas J  Donohue  Chamber President and CEO     Ben Nelson is a leader who has voted time and again to create jobs  improve our domestic security  and promote economic expansion          It  s important to provide Nebraska businesses with the support they need to keep their doors open and to fuel the Nebraska economy     Nelson said     I am honored that the U S  Chamber of Commerce has recognized my efforts to promote economic activity  create jobs  and provide economic opportunity in the Senate          The Chamber awards the   Spirit of Enterprise    based on rankings it gives members of Congress for key business votes explained in its annual publication  How They Voted   Senator Nelson earned an 81 percent score with the Chamber last year and has compiled a 75 percent cumulative ranking during his tenure in Congress   This is Senator Nelson  s third   Spirit of Enterprise    award  He previously received the award in 2004 and 2002      Chamber designated   key votes    are recorded floor votes on issues established as priorities by the Chamber  s board of directors and on which the Chamber communicates its position prior to the vote   Members of Congress who support the Chamber  s position on at least 70 percent of key votes receive the   Spirit of Enterprise    award        For the second session of the 108th Congress  the Chamber scored 17 Senate votes and 21 House votes as key votes   Key votes for 2004 included medical liability reform  the U S  Australia free trade agreement  pension reform and tax relief     NOTE  For a complete list of key votes and ratings for individual members of Congress  visit the Chamber  s Web site at www
