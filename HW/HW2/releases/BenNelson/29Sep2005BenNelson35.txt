                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    September 29  2005         NELSON  JUDGE JOHN ROBERTS  CONIFRMED TO SERVE AS CHIEF JUSTICE OF THE UNITED STATES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             The United States Senate Votes 78 22 to Confirm Roberts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson voted to confirm Judge John Roberts as Chief Justice of the United States today  Nelson issued the following statement regarding the confirmation        From what I  ve seen  I expect Judge Roberts to serve with distinction and honor as Chief Justice of the United States  I think the Court will be enhanced despite the obvious and measurable impact of the loss to the Court of Chief Justice William Rehnquist        What came through to me  when I met with him and as I watched his confirmation hearings  was a man with great poise  a deep and thorough knowledge of the law  a love for this country and unmatched integrity  John Roberts is a man deserving of the nomination and deserving of confirmation  I was pleased to support his nomination to Chief Justice of the United States
