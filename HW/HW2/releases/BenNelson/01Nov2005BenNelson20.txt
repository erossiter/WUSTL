                                                         Press Release    November 1  2005         NELSON TO MEET WITH SUPREME COURT NOMINEE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Nebraska  s Senator Will Meet With Judge Samuel Alito                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON  D C       Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson will meet Wednesday morning with Judge Samuel Alito  the President  s nominee to replace Associate Justice Sandra Day O  Connor on the U S  Supreme Court       I am looking forward to the confirmation process and looking forward to learning more about Judge Samuel Alito   Judge Alito needs to have a fair and thorough hearing I think it should be rigorous but fair  and we should withhold judgment until that process unfolds        In the past  Nelson has made it a point to meet with some of the high profile judicial nominations before deciding his vote on the nominees   As a former Governor who appointed the entire Nebraska Supreme Court  the Appeals Court  and about half of the state judges in Nebraska  Nelson believes personal interviews are the best way to get to know a nominee  Nelson has met with Miguel Estrada  Janice Rodgers Brown  Priscilla Owen  and Supreme Court Chief Justice John Roberts during their nominations        Nelson is a leader among fourteen moderate minded Senators who broke the logjam on judicial nominations in the Senate
