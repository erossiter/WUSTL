                     PRESS RELEASE  HUMBOLDT VOLUNTEER  KEARNEY VOLUNTEER AND OMAHA FIRE DEPARTMENTS RECEIVE FIREFIGHTING GRANTS FOR FIRE SAFETY PROGRAMS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Press Release    May 18  2005         HUMBOLDT VOLUNTEER  KEARNEY VOLUNTEER AND OMAHA FIRE DEPARTMENTS RECEIVE FIREFIGHTING GRANTS FOR FIRE SAFETY PROGRAMS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Security s Office of State and Local Government Coordination and Preparedness  SLGCP  in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration  USFA  has awarded Fire Prevention and Fire Safety awards to the Humboldt Volunteer Fire Department  the Kearney Volunteer Fire Department  and the Omaha Fire Department       This award is made under the Fiscal Year 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  Program  This is round seven of the Fire Prevention and Safety Program announcements   The Humboldt Volunteer Fire Department will receive  1 710  the Kearney Volunteer Fire Department will receive  40 334 and the Omaha Fire Department will receive  51 767 for fire operations and fire safety measures         The Fire Prevention and Firefighter Safety Program provides funding to improve the effectiveness of firefighting operations  firefighter health and safety programs  and to establish or expand fire prevention programs throughout he United States         These grants will assist these three rural fire departments in more effectively and resourcefully protecting Nebraska communities by improving fire stations  facilities  training and equipment     said Nelson     With these funds  our firefighters can expand their efforts to continue to make Nebraska as safe as it can be            Additional information about the grants may be found at www firegrantsupport com   Fire departments with questions about the Assistance to Firefighters Grant program  can reach the fire grants help desk at  1 866 274 0960 or by writing to firegrants dhs gov              30
