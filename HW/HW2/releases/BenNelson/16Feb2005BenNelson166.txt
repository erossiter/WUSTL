                     PRESS RELEASE  NELSON  COLLEAGUES URGE PRESIDENT TO TIGHTEN SANCTIONS ON SYRIA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Press Release    February 16  2005         NELSON  COLLEAGUES URGE PRESIDENT TO TIGHTEN SANCTIONS ON SYRIA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON  D C     Nebraska  s Senator Ben Nelson is calling on the President to tighten sanctions on Syria following the recent assassination of former Lebanese Prime Minister Rafik Hariri in Beirut   Nelson says our message to the Syrians  and to other undemocratic regimes in the region  must be clear and direct  terrorist activity will not be tolerated       As a member of the United States Senate Armed Services Committee  Senator Nelson has taken seriously his role and his duty to help fight terrorism   In 2003  The Syria and Lebanese Sovereignty Act was passed in response to Syria  s support for terrorism  its occupation of Lebanon  and its pursuit of dangerous weapons   That legislation gives the President the discretion to adjust the level of sanctions on Syria      The United States cannot afford to let Syria go scot free and not take responsibility for their actions     said Senator Nelson     It  s imperative to send a message to enemies of the US that we take terrorism seriously and it will not be tolerated          The letter sent to President Bush outlined recent actions taken by the Syrians including the Syrians   failure to secure the Iraq border  permitting the infiltration of foreign terrorists into Iraq   Syria continues to harbor leaders who order  plan  and finance terror attacks against Israeli citizens   Operatives of the Islamic Jihad  Hamas  the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine  and the al Aqsa Martyrs Brigades regularly receive training in Syrian camps  As long as Syria continues to occupy Lebanon and train suicide bombers  the region is not safe     Along with Senator Ben Nelson  the letter was also signed by Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison of Texas  Senator Sam Brownback of Kansas  Senator George Allen of Virginia  Senator Gordon Smith of Oregon  Senator Jon Kyl of Arizona  Senator Frank Lautenberg of New Jersey  Senator Hillary Clinton of New York  Senator Ron Wyden of Oregon  Senator Bill Nelson of Florida  and Senator Charles Schumer of New York   The letter was sent on Thursday  February  17  2005
