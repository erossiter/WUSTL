SEN. SANTORUM ENCOURAGES SUPPORT FOR VICTIMS OF TSUNAMIS IN SOUTHERN ASIA, AFRICA
  Sen. Rick Santorum (R-PA), Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, made the following statement regarding disaster relief efforts as a result of the tsunamis in Southern Asia and Africa. 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the families and loved ones of the victims of the devastating earthquake and resulting tsunamis that affected several nations across Southern Asia and Africa. Following such a horrible tragedy, we must join together to help ease the loss and devastation that struck this region of the world."
   "Many constituents have contacted my office to find out how they can help the victims of the tsunamis. I would encourage all Pennsylvanians to contribute to relief efforts to the extent which they are able." For more information on how to make a donation to the ongoing relief efforts in Asia and Africa, please visit the U.S.A. Freedom Corps at http://www.usafreedomcorps.gov. Additionally, constituents can make donations to the American Red Cross at http://www.redcross.org. 
   "I extend my most sincere condolences to the people affected by this terrible disaster. I am grateful to the many Pennsylvanians, and Americans throughout the world, who have supported the relief efforts of this terrible tragedy."
