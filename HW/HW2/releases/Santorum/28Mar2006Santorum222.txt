SEN. SANTORUM ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF CARD AS WHITE HOUSE CHIEF OF STAFF
  Sen. Rick Santorum (R-PA), Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today issued the following statement regarding the resignation of Andy Card as the White House Chief of Staff: 
   "After learning of the resignation of Andy Card as the White House Chief of Staff, I would like to extend my best wishes to him. As the second longest serving White House Chief of Staff, Mr. Card has been a loyal and dedicated advisor to the President.
   "For the remainder of the President's second term, I trust that Josh Bolten - who will succeed Mr. Card - will serve the administration and country admirably. I wish Mr. Card the best as he embarks on future endeavors."
