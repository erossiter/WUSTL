SEN. SANTORUM VISITS VICTIMS OF HURRICANE KATRINA
  Sen. Rick Santorum (R-PA), Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today joined U.S. Secretary of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) Alphonso Jackson and Philadelphia Mayor John Street to meet with families from New Orleans who evacuated to Philadelphia in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina.  
 Hurricane Katrina victims are temporarily housed at the John Wanamaker Middle School in Philadelphia. 
   "I am pleased to see so many Pennsylvanians pitching in to assist the victims of Hurricane Katrina," said Senator Santorum. "The outpouring of generosity from all over the Commonwealth and across our nation is encouraging in this tragic time. I am grateful for this opportunity to join Secretary Jackson to visit and extend our prayers and blessings to the victims."
