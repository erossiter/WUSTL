SEN. SANTORUM COMMENTS ON U.S. ECONOMIC GROWTH
  Sen. Rick Santorum (R-PA), Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, commented on the monthly employment situation report released today by the Bureau of Labor Statistics. 
   "New statistics released today show that the United States economy is continuing to grow. Nearly 5 million jobs have been created since August 2003. The U.S. economy has experienced 30 consecutive months of job growth. The unemployment rate remains low at 4.8 percent, and 243,000 new payroll jobs were created in February.
   "Pennsylvania also received some good news regarding the employment situation in the Commonwealth. 4,700 jobs were created in the month of January and the unemployment rate decreased from 4.7 percent to 4.3 percent.
   "The economy is strong and continues to grow stronger each month. I will continue to work to pass legislation that will continue to lead the nation towards a stronger and more stable economy."
