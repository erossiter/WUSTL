SEN. SANTORUM MAKES STATEMENT FOLLOWING TENET RESIGNATION
  Sen. Rick Santorum (R-PA), Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, issued the following statement on the resignation of CIA Director George Tenet:  
   "I wish George Tenet the best following the news of his resignation as CIA Director. I salute his service to two Presidents. I am hopeful that the President will nominate a new candidate that will have the skills to harness the talents of the CIA to assist in the War on Terrorism." 
   "Our need for a coordinated system of defense in this country is vitally important, and I look forward to working with the next Director as he or she takes on the difficult challenge of improving our level of human intelligence to match our technical capabilities."
