SEN. LUGAR MEETS WITH UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN INDIANA PRESIDENT
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar met today with Ray Hoops, President of the University of Southern Indiana (USI) to discuss education issues and the university.
   Hoops thanked Lugar for his assistance with the Campus Perimeter Road project and discussed enrollment increases at USI. Lugar and Hoops also discussed the development of a curriculum to better prepare undergraduate education majors in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics using an inquiry based approach to learning.
