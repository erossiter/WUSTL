INDIANAPOLIS AIRPORT TO RECEIVE MORE THAN $14 MILLION IN FEDERAL FUNDS FOR AIRPORT IMPROVEMENTS
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified of $14,626,025 in grants awarded by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) to the Indianapolis Airport. 
   "This is good news for Indianapolis," Lugar said. "These funds will assist airport officials as they continue to improve the safety and capacity at the Indianapolis Airport."
   The funds are divided between the following three grants:
   $10,000,000 - Construct apron, construct service road, construct taxiway, improve airport drainage, and remove obstructions
   $4,138,000 - Construct aircraft rescue and fire fighting building
   $488,025 - Acquire snow removal equipment and install airfield guidance signs
