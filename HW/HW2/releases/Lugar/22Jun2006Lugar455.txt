SEN. LUGAR VISITS WITH ROTARY CLUB OF INDIANAPOLIS' AMBASSADORIAL SCHOLARSHIP RECIPIENT
  Sen. Dick Lugar met today with Anne Marie Guerrettz of Indianapolis, the recipient of the Rotary Club of Indianapolis' Ambassadorial Scholarship. Following the visit, Guerrettz toured the Capitol with staff from Lugar's office.  
   The Ambassadorial Scholarship is given by the Rotary Club of Indianapolis to individuals who possess excellent leadership skills and potential, have a proven record of success in academics and demonstrate a personal commitment to community service. 
   Guerretz, who received her Bachelor of Arts degree from St. Mary's College in Notre Dame, Indiana, is using the Rotary scholarship to study bilingual education in the Dominican Republic. Anne Marie also completed her Masters of Arts in Teaching from the College of Notre Dame in Baltimore, Maryland.
