FOREIGN RELATIONS COMMITTEE PASSES NOMINATIONS
  The Senate Foreign Relations Committee reported out the following nominations en bloc by a unanimous voice vote at today's hearing:  
   NOMINATIONS: 
   * Claudia A. McMurray to be Assistant Secretary of State for Oceans and International Environmental and Scientific Affairs 
   * Bradford R. Higgins to be Assistant Secretary of State for Resource Management and Chief Financial Officer of the Department of State 
   * The Honorable Jackie Wolcott Sanders to be Alternate U.S. Representative for Special Political Affairs in the United Nations, with the rank of Ambassador, and Alternate U.S. Representative to the Sessions of the General Assembly of the U.N. during her tenure of service as Alternate U.S. 
   * Representative for Special Political Affairs in the United Nations 
   * The Honorable Janet Ann Sanderson to be Ambassador to the Republic of Haiti 
   * Bernadette Mary Allen to be Ambassador to the Republic of Niger 
   * Patricia Newton Moller to be Ambassador to the Republic of Burundi 
   * The Honorable Steven Alan Browning to be Ambassador to the Republic of Uganda 
   * Robert Weisberg to be Ambassador to the Republic of Congo 
   * Michael W. Michalak for the rank of Ambassador during his tenure of service as U.S. Senior Official to the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Forum 
   * Janice L. Jacobs to be Ambassador to the Republic of Senegal, and to serve concurrently and without additional compensation as Ambassador to the Republic of Guinea-Bissau 
   * Jeanine E. Jackson to be Ambassador to Burkina Faso 
   * The Honorable James D. McGee to serve concurrently and without additional compensation as Ambassador to the Union of Comoros 
   * The Honorable Kristie A. Kenney to be Ambassador to the Republic of the Philippines 
   * Gary A. Grappo to be Ambassador to the Sultanate of Oman 
   * Patricia A. Butenis to be Ambassador to the People's Republic of Bangladesh 
   * Donald T. Bliss for the rank of Ambassador during his tenure of service as U.S. Representative on the Council of the International Civil Aviation Organization 
   * The Honorable Ben S. Bernanke to be U.S. Alternate Governor of the International Monetary Fund for a term of five years 
   FSO PROMOTIONS LIST: 
   * Anne Elizabeth Linnee, et al dated December 13, 2005 
   * Lisa M. Anderson, et al dated December 14, 2005
