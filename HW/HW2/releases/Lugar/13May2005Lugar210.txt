SEN. LUGAR SCHEDULED TO APPEAR ON CNN LATE EDITION
  Sen. Dick Lugar (R-IN) is scheduled to be on CNN Late Edition with Wolf Blitzer, Sunday, May 15, 2005. He will be joined by Sen. Joe Biden (D-DE). Possible topics include Iraq, Iran, North Korea and the nomination of John Bolton to be U.S. Representative to the United Nations. 
   Lugar is Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee. Biden is the Ranking Member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee. 
   The show begins at 12:00 noon EDT/11:00 a.m. (Indiana time).
