GREENE COUNTY AWARDED USDA RURAL DEVELOPMENT GRANT
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that Eastern Greene Schools will receive rural development funds from the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
  Eastern Greene Schools has been awarded a Community Facilities Grant of $51,900. These funds will be used to cover the installation of athletic field lighting.
  Questions regarding the grants process in general may be directed to Kim Preston, Grants Coordinator for Senator Lugar, by phone at 202-224-4814 or by email at grants@lugar.senate.gov
