NORTHWEST INDIANA PUBLIC BROADCASTING AWARDED $316,000 IN DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE GRANT
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar was notified that Northwest Indiana Public Broadcasting has been awarded a $316,215 grant from the U.S. Department of Commerce. 
   "I believe that public broadcasting provides valuable educational, historical and children's programming to a large number of viewers and listeners, as evidenced by the consistent increase in private support over the years," said Lugar.
   Funds will be used to convert the station to a digital operation.
   Please contact the Department of Commerce at 202-482-3663 for more information regarding this grant. Questions regarding the grants process in general may be directed to Kim Preston, Grants Coordinator for Senator Lugar, by phone at 202-224-4814 or by email at grants@lugar.senate.gov
