GRANTS MADE TO INDIANA ART ORGANIZATIONS
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that ten Indiana art organizations will receive funds from the National Endowment for the Arts totaling $805,700. 
   These grants will support arts education projects for children and youth and projects that present and/or preserve artistically excellent work. Grants from the National Endowment for the Arts require at least one-to-one matching and on average generate seven dollars for each dollar awarded.
