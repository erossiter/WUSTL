INDIANA SMALL BUSINESSES RECEIVE ENERGY EFFICIENCY GRANTS
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar was notified that four Indiana small businesses received grants totaling $1,099,305 from the U.S. Department of Agriculture through the Renewable Energy Systems and Energy Efficiency Improvement grant program. 
  "We must work to ensure all Hoosiers have adequate support, equipment and training in the areas of energy efficiency and biofuel technology," said Lugar.
