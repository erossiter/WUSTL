HUD GRANTS AWARDED TO INDIANA COMMUNITIES
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified that Hamilton County, LaPorte and Michigan City will receive grants totaling $2,070,451 from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). 
   The grants awarded were Community Development Block Grants (CDBG). The CDBG program provides annual grants to states and local units of government to develop viable urban communities by providing decent housing and a suitable living environment and by expanding economic opportunities, primarily for low-and-moderate-income persons. 
   HUD grant recipients:
   Hamilton County, $816,996 
  LaPorte, $511,891 
  Michigan City, $741,564
