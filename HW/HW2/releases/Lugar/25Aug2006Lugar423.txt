SEN. LUGAR SCHEDULED TO APPEAR ON CNN LATE EDITION
  Sen. Dick Lugar (R-IN) is scheduled to be on CNN Late Edition with Wolf Blitzer, Sunday, August 27, 2006. He will be joined by Sen. Carl Levin (D-MI). 
   Lugar is Chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee and Levin is Ranking Member of the Armed Services Committee. 
   The show begins at 11 a.m. EDT.
