MICHIGAN CITY AWARDED $822,000 HUD GRANT
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that Michigan City will receive a Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) totaling $821,930 from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. 
   The CDBG program provides annual grants to state and local government to develop viable urban communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment and by expanding economic opportunities for low-income and moderate-income persons.
