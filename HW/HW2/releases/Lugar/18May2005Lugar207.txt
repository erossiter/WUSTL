NORTHEAST INDIANA TO RECEIVE MORE THAN $4.7 MILLION IN FEDERAL FUNDS FOR AIRPORTS
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified of grants awarded by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) to Fort Wayne-Allen County Airport Authority and De Kalb County Board of Aviation Commissioner. These funds will be used for a variety of projects that will enhance the safety and capacity at their respective air facilities. 
   Award recipients include: 
   * Recipient - Fort Wayne-Allen County Airport Authority
   Federal Share - $4,583,490
   Project - Rehabilitate runway and runway lighting; Acquire aircraft rescue
   * Recipient - De Kalb County Board of Aviation Commissioner
   Federal Share - $150,000
   Project - Conduct environmental assessment for runway extension; Construct an aircraft apron
