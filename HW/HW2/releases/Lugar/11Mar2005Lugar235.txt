SEN. LUGAR SCHEDULED TO APPEAR ON CBS'S FACE THE NATION
  Sen. Dick Lugar is scheduled to be on CBS's Face the Nation hosted by Bob Schieffer on Sunday, March 13. He will be joined by Sen. Joe Biden (D-DE). Possible topics include Iran, Syria, Lebanon and Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice's trip to Asia. Secretary Rice will also be on the program. 
   Lugar is the Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee. Biden is the Ranking Minority Leader of the Foreign Relations Committee.
