SEN. LUGAR ANNOUNCES GRANTS FOR MUNCIE PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION CORPORATION
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that the Muncie Public Transportation Corporation (MPTC) will receive two grants totaling $1,110,544, from the U.S. Department of Transportation.  
 The funds are part of the Job Access and Reverse Commute Allocations, distributed by the U.S. Department of Transportation's Federal Transit Administration. 
   MPTC will receive a grant of $971,779, to provide funds for the purchase of six low floor 35-foot replacement buses for use in MPTC's fixed route service
   In addition, MPTC will receive a grant of $138,765 to fund the continuation of project activities started in previous grants. The project scope includes increasing service and hours to major employment centers not currently served by the MPTC.
