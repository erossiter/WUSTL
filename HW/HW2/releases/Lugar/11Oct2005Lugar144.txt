NEW ALBANY AWARDED HUD GRANT OVER $825,000
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that New Albany will receive a grant totaling $829,263 from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. 
   The grant awarded to New Albany was the Community Development Block Grant (CDBG). 
   The CDBG program provides annual grants to states and local units of government to develop viable urban communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment and by expanding economic opportunities for low-income and moderate-income persons.
