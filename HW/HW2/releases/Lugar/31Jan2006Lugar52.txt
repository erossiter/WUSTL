SEN. LUGAR COMMENTS ON JUDGE ALITO
  Sen. Dick Lugar made the following comment regarding his support for the confirmation of Judge Samuel Alito to be a U.S. Supreme Court Justice: 
   "Judge Alito is a sound choice for Supreme Court Justice with great respect from the American Bar Association and legal community. He performed well in the arduous hearings before the Senate Judiciary Committee," U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar (R-IN) said.
