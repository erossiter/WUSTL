SEN. LUGAR COMMENTS ON BUSH'S INTENTION TO NOMINATE HUGHES TO BE UNDER SECRETARY OF STATE FOR PUBLIC DIPLOMACY AND PUBLIC AFFAIRS
  Senate Foreign Relations Committee Chairman Dick Lugar made the following statement on President Bush's intention to nominate Karen Hughes to be Under Secretary of State for Public Diplomacy and Public Affairs.  
   "I applaud the President's nomination of Karen Hughes to be Under Secretary of State for Public Diplomacy and Public Affairs. Ms. Hughes will bring the strategic orientation required for this vital responsibility. She is close to the President and she has significant national campaign communications experience. 
   "The United States is involved in an international strategic communications campaign to promote and defend American interests in the world. Public diplomacy is a national security issue, and I believe Karen Hughes will bring to this job the wisdom and energy essential for this critical challenge."
