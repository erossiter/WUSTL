SEN. LUGAR NOTIFIED OF FEDERAL TRANSIT FUNDS FOR INDIANA
  Sen. Dick Lugar's office was notified that the Federal Transit Administration has awarded funds for transit systems in Indianapolis and Bloomington. 
   The grants are as follows: 
   * $1,000,000 for the Indianapolis Public Transportation Corporation (IPTC) for a Job Access and Reverse Commute program. This grant will provide funds to continue IPTC's job access services developed and implemented in the first two years of the program's operation. 
   * $1,518,853 for the Indiana University Campus Bus System. These funds will be used to help Indiana University purchase seven new 40- foot low-floor buses. 
   Funds for these grants were provided by Congress at Lugar's request.
