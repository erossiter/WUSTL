SEN. LUGAR: TOBIAS NOMINATION APPROVED BY SENATE
  Sen. Dick Lugar announced that the U.S. Senate approved the nomination of the Honorable Randall L. Tobias to be Administrator of the U.S. Agency for International Development last night. 
   Lugar's opening statement at Ambassador Tobias' nomination hearing before the Senate Foreign Relations Committee is available at http://www.lugar.senate.gov/pressapp/record.cfm?id=252221.
