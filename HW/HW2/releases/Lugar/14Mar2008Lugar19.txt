SEN. LUGAR MEETS WITH MAYOR HENRY
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar today met with Fort Wayne Mayor Tom Henry. Henry, who had previously visited D.C. with Community Health Centers, came for his inaugural visit as Mayor.
   Henry discussed the completion of a link in the Fort Wayne and Allen County Rivergreenway, the need for a flood control study to reassess the effectiveness of aging flood control infrastructure and economic growth activities in Fort Wayne.
