SEN. LUGAR ASSISTS TIPPECANOE, CLINTON COUNTY RESIDENTS IN RECEIVING BRONZE STARS
  Sen. Dick Lugar announced that Alan K. Schilling of Lafayette, Billie L. Taylor of Lafayette and Roy J. Roush of Rossville will receive Bronze Star medals on July 4, at the Stars and Stripes Concert at Slater Center in West Lafayette at 7:45 p.m. 
   Emmy Huffman, Lugar's Veterans History Project Coordinator, will be present as the Bronze Stars are awarded to the Hoosier veterans. Assisting with the presentation will be Lt. Col. Joe Bumbleburg (Ret.), Department Judge Advocate for the American Legion, Department of Indiana.
