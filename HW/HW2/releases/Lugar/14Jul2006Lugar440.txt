SEN. LUGAR MEETS WITH NORTHEAST INDIANA CHAMBER COALITION
  Sen. Dick Lugar met with the Northeast Indiana Chamber of Commerce on Thursday, July 13, 2006, in Washington, D.C. The Chamber members were in Washington from July 12-13 for their annual D.C. Fly-In.  
   Lugar and the delegation discussed issues including consumer benefits, Medicaid, Social Security, and immigration as well as topics particular to Indiana such as the implementation of E85 fuel pumps and federal and state transportation appropriations. The Chamber members also asked Senator Lugar about immigration and foreign policy issues including North Korea and the Middle East. 
   The Chamber members expressed their thanks to Senator Lugar for his efforts in securing appropriations for Elkhart County Road 17, which runs through Northeast Indiana, and for his continued efforts on their behalf.
