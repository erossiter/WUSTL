EPA REGION 5 ANNOUNCES IT IS ACCEPTING PROPOSALS FOR NATIONAL RESOURCE CONSERVATION CHALLENGE
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar announced that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is accepting grant applications for the national Resource Conservation Challenge (RCC). The EPA will award 5 grants totally more than $180,000. 
   The national RCC aims to increase the recycling of municipal solid waste; increase industrial materials recycling; and increase green electronics in Region 5. [Region 5 encompasses the states of Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, Minnesota, Ohio, and Wisconsin.] This funding supports EPA's strategic goal of land preservation and restoration. Applicants are encouraged to partner and collaborate on projects with other organizations involved in similar efforts.
   The EPA deadline for national RCC grant applications is April 16, 2008.
   To learn more about State Programs in Region 5 and grant opportunities available through the EPA for recycling and waste management please contact the RCRA Hotline at: (312) 886-7449 or http://www.epa.gov/reg5rcra/wptdiv/recycle/states.htm.
   Questions regarding the application process may be directed to Andrew Mills, Grants Coordinator for Senator Lugar, by phone at 202-224-4814 or by email at grants@lugar.senate.gov
