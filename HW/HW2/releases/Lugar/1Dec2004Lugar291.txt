SEN. LUGAR TO TESTIFY ON UKRAINE
  U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee Chairman Dick Lugar will testify on "Ukraine's Election: Next Steps" before the House Committee on International Relations. 
   The Hearing will be Tuesday, December 7, at 10:30 a.m. in Room 2172 of the Rayburn House Office Building. Assistant Secretary State for the Bureau of European and Eurasian Affairs Elizabeth Jones will also testify. 
   Lugar served as President Bush's envoy during the November 21 Ukraine election. This will be Lugar's first Washington, DC public appearance since returning from Kiev.
