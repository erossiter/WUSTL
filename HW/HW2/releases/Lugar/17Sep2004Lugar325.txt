SEN. LUGAR SCHEDULED TO APPEAR ON ABC THIS WEEK ON SEPT. 19
  Sen. Dick Lugar is scheduled to be on ABC This Week, Sunday, September 19. Appearing with Sen. Lugar will be Sen. Joe Biden. Iraqi Prime Minister Ayad Allawi is also scheduled to appear. 
   Lugar is Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee. Biden is the Ranking Member on the Foreign Relations Committee.
