SEN. LUGAR COMMUNITY CENTER TO HOLD MEDICARE PRESCRIPTION DRUG INFO SESSIONS
  On Wednesday, January 30, 2008, at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. the Richard G. Lugar Northeast Indiana Community Service Center and AARP Indiana will offer educational seminars about prescription drug coverage plans available through Medicare. The Community Service Center is located at 6384-A West Jefferson Boulevard in Covington Plaza, Fort Wayne.  
  The event will highlight the mandated coverage established with the Medicare Modernization Act of 2003, what drugs are covered, the enrollment guidelines, other sources of assistance (i.e. - Partnership for Prescription Assistance (PPARx) program that includes most of the major pharmaceutical manufacturers and Indiana State Medicaid program), and special coverage issues for veterans, etc. 
  A member of Lugar's staff will be available from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. to discuss individual Medicare and Social Security issues.
  While registration is not required to attend these sessions, seating is limited. To reserve a seat, please contact the Lugar Community Service Center at 260-422-1505.
  Additionally, Medicare offers information at: http://www.Medicare.gov or 1-800-MEDICARE. The Indiana Senior Health Insurance Information Program (SHIIP) is also available to provide guidance at 1-800-452-4800.
