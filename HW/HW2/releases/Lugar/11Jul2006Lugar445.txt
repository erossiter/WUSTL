INDIANA UNIVERSITIES RECEIVE MORE THAN $500,000 IN NURSING GRANTS
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified today that eight colleges and universities across the state received more than half a million dollars in nursing grants from the Department of Health and Human Services.  
   "I strongly believe that registered nurses are a crucial part of our health care system. The declining number of nurses is a problem of great importance, particularly as our population ages," Lugar said. "With spiraling health care costs and the shortage of professionals, the need to prepare quality, cost effective health providers continues to be acute."
