JOHNSON FIELD TO RECEIVE MORE THAN $1.2 MILLION
  Sen. Dick Lugar was notified that the Delaware County Airport Authority will receive a $1,236,847 grant from the Department of Transportation. The money will be used to rehabilitate the taxiway of Johnson Field. 
   "This is good news for Delaware County," Lugar said. "I commend our officials for their work. Airport improvements are important in our combined effort to strengthen Hoosier communities." 
   More information about this grant is available at: http://grants.ost.dot.gov/public/ViewMessage.cfm?MsgID=h49aabaqpp
