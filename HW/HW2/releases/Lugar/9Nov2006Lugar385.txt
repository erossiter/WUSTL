SEN. LUGAR ISSUES STATEMENT ON SECRETARY RUMSFELD, ROBERT M. GATES
  Sen. Dick Lugar made the following statement on the announcement of Secretary Rumsfeld's resignation and President Bush's nomination of Dr. Robert M. Gates to succeed him:  
   "I congratulate President Bush on his nomination of Dr. Robert M. Gates to succeed Secretary Donald Rumsfeld as our nation's new Secretary of Defense. I had the privilege of serving on the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence for 18 years and appreciating the remarkable testimony of Bob Gates during his service as Director of Central Intelligence and Deputy National Security Advisor. I am confident that he will bring an experienced perspective to enhance the support of our national security and of the members of our Armed Forces as they face extraordinary challenges."
