SEN. LUGAR NOTIFIED OF FEDERAL FUNDS FOR GOSHEN AIRPORT
  Sen. Dick Lugar's office was recently notified of a grant awarded by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) of $1.06 million for a runway extension project at the Goshen Airport. 
   The discretionary funds awarded by the FAA will be used to complete Phase 1 of the project which includes the reimbursement for approximately 100 acres of land and relocation of a county road.
