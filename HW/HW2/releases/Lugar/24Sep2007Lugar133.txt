CITY OF PLYMOUTH RECEIVES $1.1 MILLION ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT GRANT
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar was notified that the city of Plymouth has been awarded a $1,100,000 grant from the U.S. Department of Commerce. 
  "This funding will contribute to commercial growth and economic development in Marshall County," Lugar said. 
  Funds will be used to help with construction of a 74-acre commercial, industrial site to accommodate development of Marshall County's emerging bio-life sciences sector. 
  Please contact the Department of Commerce at 202-482-3663 for more information regarding this grant. Questions regarding the grants process in general may be directed to Kim Preston, Grants Coordinator for Senator Lugar, by phone at 202-224-4814 or by email at grants@lugar.senate.gov
