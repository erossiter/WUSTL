SEN. LUGAR MEETS WITH HOOSIER NATIONAL FFA ORGANIZATION LEADER
  U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar met today with the National FFA Organization Eastern Region Vice President, Tyler Tenbarge, who is in Washington D.C. for National Agriculture Day 2008 and an FFA rally.
   Tenbarge, originally from Haubstadt and currently a student at Purdue majoring in agricultural economics, was selected to serve in his officer position at the 80th national FFA Convention and is a graduate of Gibson Southern High School.
