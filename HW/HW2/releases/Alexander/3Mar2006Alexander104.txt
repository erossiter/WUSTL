                                                                                                                   Alexander Praises Senate Confirmation of TVA Board Members          March 3rd  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN   chairman of the TVA Congressional Caucus  made the following statement today after the Senate confirmed six new members of the Tennessee Valley Authority  TVA  Board of Directors      Behind Senator Frist s leadership  Congress has given the nation s largest public utility what it has needed for a long time  a modern governance structure and a chief executive officer      The new board members are broad gauged and committed to giving TVA customers reliable  clean power at the lowest possible cost      Confirmed today in the U S  Senate were  Dennis Bottorff  William S  Sansom and Susan Richardson Williams of Tennessee  Donald R  DePriest of Mississippi  Howard A  Thrailkill of Alabama and Robert M  Duncan of Kentucky
