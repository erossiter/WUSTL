                                                                                                                   IMMIGRATION  Alexander Votes for Isakson Amendment to Secure the Border         May 16th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  today voted in favor of Sen  Johnny Isakson  s  R GA  amendment which was intended to prohibit the federal government from adjusting the legal status of any illegal immigrant until every border security provision in the immigration bill had been implemented and the Secretary of Homeland Security certified that the border is secure      We have no business passing a comprehensive immigration bill without making sure  first  that the border will be secure     said Alexander  a cosponsor of the Isakson amendment    Upholding the rule of law on our border is as important as defending our freedom in Iraq  A nation that loses control of its own borders is a nation that is not likely to exist for long       The Isakson amendment to the immigration bill failed to pass the Senate by a vote of 40 to 55
