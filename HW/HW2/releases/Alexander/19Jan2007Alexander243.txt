                                                                                                                   Alexander Will Serve on Environment and Public Works Subcommittees Which Oversee TVA  Nuclear Plant Safety and Clean Air          January 19th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  today announced that he will serve on the Environment and Public Works  EPW  subcommittees whose jurisdiction include the Tennessee Valley Authority  TVA   clean air  nuclear plant safety and solutions to global warming  Alexander will serve as the Ranking Republican on the Public Sector Solutions to Global Warming Subcommittee       Serving on these subcommittees will enable me to work in a bipartisan manner to clean the air in the Smokies and across the state  oversee the work of the TVA and seek ways to reduce the effects of global warming     Alexander said    The EPW subcommittees on which Alexander will serve include   Public Sector Solutions to Global Warming  Oversight and Children  s Health Protection    whose issues include global warming  oversight  children  s health protection  the National Environmental Policy Act and Yucca Mountain  Alexander will be the Ranking Republican on this subcommittee   Clean Air  Nuclear Safety and Security    whose issues include the TVA and nuclear power plant safety and security   Alexander will also be the third ranking Republican on the Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Committee  and will also serve on the Appropriations Committee and the Rules and Administration Committee
