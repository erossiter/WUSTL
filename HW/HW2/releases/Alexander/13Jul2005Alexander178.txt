                                                                                                                   Alexander Names Valentine Communications Director          July 13th  2005   WASHINGTON    U S  Sen  Lamar Alexander  R TN  today named Harvey T  Valentine  who served as spokesman for former senator Fred Thompson  as his new communications director        Valentine will replace Alexia Poe  who joined Alexander  s Senate office in February 2003 and is returning to Tennessee to join the private sector          Alexia did a great job of building a press office from the ground up     said Alexander    I am sorry to see her go but wish her all the best in her new endeavors     I am pleased to have Harvey join our team     continued Alexander        He knows Tennessee  he knows the Senate  and he has a good working relationship with the media       As someone who has admired Senator Alexander s work  I m honored to be joining his staff   Valentine said   I very much look forward to helping him serve the people of Tennessee    Prior to joining Alexander  s staff  Valentine served as communications director for the Republican Governor  s Association      Prior to that  he served as U S  Sen  Fred Thompson  s  R TN  communications director from 1999 through 2002 and in a variety of roles  including press secretary  for U S  Senator Alfonse D Amato  R NY  from 1989 through 1998      He    began his career as a reporter for the    Gloversville  NY  Leader Herald        A native of Troy  New York  Valentine earned a bachelor of arts in Mass Communications    from St  Bonaventure University in Olean  New York           He assumes his post Monday  Aug  1
