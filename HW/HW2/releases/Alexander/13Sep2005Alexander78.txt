                                                                                                                   Statement of Sen  Alexander following 51 47 vote on mercury emissions         September 13th  2005     I  m disappointed by the outcome of today  s vote  The public health risks associated with mercury pollution are real and the technology needed to address them is available  We can do more and we should do more  We need to achieve mercury reductions sooner than 2018  I  m going to work with my colleagues to determine a path forward
