                                                                                                                   Alexander Praises Energy Committee s Approval of Kempthorne s Nomination for Interior         May 10th  2006   ALEXANDER PRAISES ENERGY COMMITTEE  S APPROVAL OF KEMPTHORNE  S NOMINATION FOR INTERIOR    Washington  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN   member of the U S  Senate Energy   Natural Resources Committee  made the following statement today following the Committee  s nearly unanimous approval of the nomination of Idaho Governor Dirk Kempthorne to serve as U S  Secretary of the Interior     I  m very glad the Energy Committee has given such a strong stamp of approval on Governor Kempthorne  s nomination for Interior Secretary  Dirk is experienced  he is sensible  and he cares deeply about the great American outdoors  He is ideally suited to serve on President Bush  s cabinet and to lead the Department of Interior  I hope his nomination is given swift approval by the U S  Senate        The nomination will now be sent to the full Senate for a vote
