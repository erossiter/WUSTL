                                                                                                                   Alexander Named Hero of the Taxpayer  Americans for Tax Reform Recognize Alexander with Award Based on Key Votes in 2005        June 9th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  has been named a  Hero of the Taxpayer  by Americans for Tax Reform  ATR   The taxpayer advocacy organization presents the award to members of Congress who vote with taxpayers on 85 percent of the issues tracked by ATR      I appreciate this recognition from Americans for Tax Reform     said Alexander    I was proud to side with taxpayers on key votes before the U S  Senate  and I  ll continue to support the kind of fiscal responsibility Tennesseans expect         Americans for Tax Reform  ATR  is a non partisan coalition of taxpayers and taxpayer groups who oppose all federal  state and local tax increases  For 2005  it rated Senate roll call votes on tax cuts  spending control  property rights and free trade
