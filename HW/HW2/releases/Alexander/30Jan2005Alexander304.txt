                                                                                                                   Statement Of Sen   Alexander   Iraqi Elections          January 30th  2005   We should salute the courage of Iraqi voters and of American military men and women and now see this mission through to the end   helping to preserve security while training Iraqis to defend themselves  To appreciate this inspiring day  it helps to remember that after our Constitution was adopted  it took 133 years to give women the right to vote and 190 years before African Americans could freely vote in every part of the United States
