                                                                                                                   Statement Of Sen  Alexander   President Bush s Budget Proposal         February 7th  2005   This is a tough budget  We re in a time of war  and we have a deficit  The President has given us a budget that requires fiscal discipline    His budget is entitled to respect  and I m going to give it that respect  I am carefully reviewing the budget  I still have my priorities  and I may have some different ideas than the President  As a new member of the Budget Committee  I ll be more involved in that process    The President is right to try to cut the deficit by almost half over the next three to four years    In areas where there is lower growth in federal spending  I m going to argue strongly to give states more flexibility on how to spend that money
