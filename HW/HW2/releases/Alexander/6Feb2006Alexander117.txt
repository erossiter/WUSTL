                                                                                                                   Alexander Names David Cleary Staff Director of Subcommittee on Education and Early Childhood Development         February 6th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R Tenn   today announced that David Cleary has been named staff director of the Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Subcommittee on Education and Early Childhood Development  which Alexander chairs  Cleary has nearly a decade of experience working on education issues in Washington     I  m pleased to have David Cleary leading the education subcommittee staff     Alexander said    His knowledge and experience will be a great asset as the subcommittee addresses issues such as increasing our emphasis on math and science education and the reauthorization of the No Child Left Behind Act      Cleary  32  served as a staff member on the U S  House of Representatives Committee on Education and the Workforce under Chairman John Boehner  R Ohio   and previously as a legislative analyst for the U S  Department of Education  Just prior to joining the Education and Early Childhood Subcommittee staff  Cleary was the director of policy and research for the Aspen Institute  s Commission on No Child Left Behind      It  s a great honor for me to join Senator Alexander  s team     Cleary said     Alexander is a leading voice on the importance of education and the need to improve all educational opportunities to maintain our competitive advantage as a nation   I look forward to working for Senator Alexander  and I appreciate the opportunity he has given me      A Minnesota native  Cleary earned a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Master of Public Administration from The George Washington University    Cleary  who assumes his post today  succeeds Christine Dodd  who recently accepted a position with IBM  s government relations office in Washington
