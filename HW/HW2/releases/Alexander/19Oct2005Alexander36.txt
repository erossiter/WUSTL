                                                                                                                   Alexander  Frist applaud over  1 Million in Head Start Funding for Erin         October 19th  2005   WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Bill Frist  R TN  and Lamar Alexander  R TN  today applauded a grant of over  1 million through the U S  Department of Health and Human Services  HHS  to the Highland Rim Economic Corporation in Erin  TN         I  m pleased that the Department of Health and Human Services continues to support Erin  s children and families     Frist said        We owe the gift of education to all of our children  and Head Start helps ensure that Tennessee  s underserved students are given every opportunity to succeed   Senator Alexander and I appreciate the commitment of HHS to the growth and development of all America  s children                                                         Head Start programs have been beneficial to both communities and participants across Tennessee     Alexander said    I  m very happy to see this investment in Erin  s children by the Department of Health and Human Services  and the Head Start program          The grant is  1 096 786 for the Highland Rim Economic Corporation to support Head Start programs         The Head Start program delivers comprehensive and high quality services to foster healthy development in low income children   Head Start and Early Head Start are comprehensive child development programs which serve children from birth to age 5  pregnant women  and their families   They are child focused programs and have the overall goal of increasing the school readiness of young children in low income families
