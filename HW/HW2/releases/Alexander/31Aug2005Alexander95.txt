                                                                                                                   Statement of U S  Sen  Lamar Alexander on Hurricane Katrina          August 31st  2005   Our thoughts and prayers are with everyone affected by this terrible tragedy  I  m proud that the people of the Volunteer State  many of whom are dealing with the impact of Katrina themselves  are opening their hearts to their neighbors in the hardest hit areas of the Gulf Coast region   My office has been in contact with state and local officials in Tennessee  as well as my Senate colleagues in the states most affected by Katrina  and we are closely monitoring events and potential steps the federal government might take to offer additional assistance
