                                                                                                                   IMMIGRATION  Alexander Votes For Immigration Amendment to Prevent Criminal Aliens From Obtaining Legal Status Alexander an Original Cosponsor of Kyl Cornyn Amendment        May 17th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  today voted in favor of an amendment to S  2611  the Comprehensive Immigration Reform Act of 2006  which closes a loophole that would allow criminal aliens to obtain legal status  The amendment  offered by Senators Jon Kyl  R AZ  and John Cornyn  R TX   ensures that any illegal alien who has been convicted of a felony or three misdemeanors is prohibited from being placed on a pathway to citizenship       We are a nation that is based on the rule of law  and we must uphold those laws     said Alexander  an original cosponsor of the amendment    A person who comes into our country illegally and then commits a felony should not be given a green card    that  s common sense      The amendment passed the Senate today by a vote of 99 0
