                                                                                                                   Statement of Sen  Lamar Alexander    Creation of an NCLB Commission at the Aspen Institute         February 14th  2006     I am pleased to learn of the creation of an independent  bipartisan Commission on No Child Left Behind at the Aspen Institute  Secretary Tommy Thompson and Governor Roy Barnes are strong leaders and their work together on this Commission will play a valuable role in the public discussion of how to improve the law  I share their desire to make sure that the important goals of No Child Left Behind are being met  and I agree that we should examine the law carefully before its upcoming reauthorization  I look forward to receiving their recommendations and proposals to improve No Child Left Behind
