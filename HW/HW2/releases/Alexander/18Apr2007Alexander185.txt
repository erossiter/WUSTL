                                                                                                                   Alexander Joins Tennessee Delegation in Requesting Disaster Declaration for Frozen Crops         April 18th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Lamar Alexander joined other members of the Tennessee Congressional Delegation Tuesday in asking U S  Agriculture Secretary Mike Johanns to issue an agricultural disaster declaration for all 95 Tennessee counties after a devastating crop freeze     It  s too early to know the exact extent of the damage  but it  s already clear that a wide range of farmers across the state have suffered major losses     Alexander said     That  s what disaster relief programs were created for  and it  s important to get the administrative process rolling so that farmers can be compensated in a timely way once we know exactly how many crops were lost
