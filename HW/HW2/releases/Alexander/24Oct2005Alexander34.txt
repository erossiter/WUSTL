                                                                                                                   Alexander  Frist Applaud Senate Confirmation of Sandy Mattice to the Federal Bench          October 24th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senators Bill Frist  R Tenn   and Lamar Alexander  R Tenn   today applauded Senate confirmation of Sandy Mattice to serve on the U S  District Court for the Eastern District of Tennessee     I  m immensely proud of the Senate for confirming one of Tennessee  s finest attorneys to the federal bench     Frist said     Throughout his distinguished career  Sandy Mattice has proven his merit as a skilled lawyer and talented prosecutor who is always fair and impartial   I  ve known Sandy for many years  and I am continually amazed by both his devotion to Tennessee and his rock solid work ethic   Sandy is an honest  moral person who will serve with honor as a U S  District Court Judge for Eastern Tennessee        President Bush made an excellent decision when he nominated Sandy Mattice to be district judge for the Eastern District of Tennessee     Alexander said     He has some big shoes to fill  succeeding Al Edgar  and he should fill them well  His experience  temperament and scholarship all suggest that he will be an excellent judge      Sandy Mattice has served as the United States Attorney for the Eastern District of Tennessee since October 2001   In 1997  he served as senior counsel for the Senate Committee on Government Affairs during a special investigation of the 1996 federal election campaigns   He also practiced at the law firms of Baker  Donelson  Bearman and Caldwell  now named Baker  Donelson  Bearman  Caldwell and Berkowitz  and Miller   Martin   Mr  Mattice graduated from the University of Tennessee College of Law in Knoxville and currently resides with his family in Signal Mountain  Tennessee
