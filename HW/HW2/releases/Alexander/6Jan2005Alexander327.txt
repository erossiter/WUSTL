                                                                                                                   Statement Of Sen  Alexander On Judge George Brown          January 6th  2005   George Brown made history in Tennessee   When I appointed him in 1980  he became the first African American to serve on the Tennessee Supreme Court   As the nominee of the Republican Party to continue that service  he ran a strong statewide race carrying a majority of votes in East Tennessee and in Memphis   He has served as circuit judge with distinction   He has been my friend for 30 years  and I wish him the very best in his new endeavors
