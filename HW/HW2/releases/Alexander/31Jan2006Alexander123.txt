                                                                                                                   Statement of U S  Senator Lamar Alexander On the 58 42 Senate Vote Confirming Samuel A  Alito  Jr  as Associate Justice of the U S  Supreme Court          January 31st  2006   I was proud to cast my vote to confirm President Bush  s nominee for the U S  Supreme Court  Samuel Alito understands that as a judge he is supposed to enforce the law impartially and respect the Constitution  He has demonstrated the judicial temperament ideal for a Supreme Court justice  I believe Samuel Alito will serve with distinction as an Associate Justice of the United States Supreme Court
