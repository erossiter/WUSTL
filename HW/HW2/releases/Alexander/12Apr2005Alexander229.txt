                                                                                                                   Senator Alexander Co sponsors Bill To Restore Gas Tax Money To Tennesseans         April 12th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Sen  Lamar Alexander  R TN  today announced he is co sponsoring the   Highway Funding Equity Act of 2005     which would provide more than  40 million in federal gas tax money for Tennesseans     The current distribution of federal highway dollars is unfair to Tennessee taxpayers     Alexander said    Over the past five years  taxpayers in Tennessee paid more than  4 2 billion into the highways trust fund  and only received  4 billion in return  losing more than  200 million to other states      A strong highway system brings better jobs to Tennessee  Our citizens should be getting back what they pay for instead of seeing their federal gas tax dollars go elsewhere    Currently  federal gas taxes are collected in the dedicated Highway Trust Fund and distributed to each state based on a formula which includes population  highway use and other variables    The Highway Funding Equity Act will increase the minimum share for each state from approximately 90 percent to 95 percent of the revenue paid by the state   Sen  George Voinovich  R OH  introduced the legislation in the Senate today
