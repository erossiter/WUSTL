                                                                                                                   Alexander Cosponsors Gregg Amendment to Add Funding for Border Security         April 26th  2006   ALEXANDER COSPONSORS GREGG AMENDMENT TO ADD FUNDING FOR BORDER SECURITY   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  today signed on as an original cosponsor of an amendment offered by Sen  Judd Gregg  R NH  to the Supplemental Appropriations Act that would add  1 9 billion in funding for critical one time investments in border security     The first step in comprehensive immigration reform has to be securing our borders and we shouldn  t wait to do it     Alexander said    This amendment will fund more border patrol vehicles  aircraft  new stations  and fences where most illegal border crossing occur      The Gregg amendment includes a budget offset and does not change the overall cost of the supplemental appropriations bill
