                                                                                                                   Statement of Alexander following Senate passage of supplemental appropriations to meet immediate needs of Hurricane Katrina          September 8th  2005   In Tennessee and everywhere in this country  the people who sent us here to the Senate know what to do  They are opening their hearts  they are opening their doors  they are opening their churches  and they are opening their pocketbooks  The furthest thing from their minds is political advantage  I suspect that is exactly how they would like for us to conduct ourselves here  I am glad that the House and Senate  in a speedy and bipartisan effort  have passed this additional  52 billion on top of last week  s  10 5 billion emergency package to aid in the relief of Hurricane Katrina
