SEN. REID STATEMENT ON PASSING OF NEWSCASTER BRADLEY
  Senate Democratic Leader Sen. Harry Reid today released the following statement on the passing of Ed Bradley. 
   "I am saddened to hear of the passing of CBS Newscaster Ed Bradley. Mr. Bradley was a pioneer and trailblazer for African American journalists on network television. For over 25-years, his was a powerful voice presenting the stories that shaped our world. I extend my heartfelt prayers and condolences to Mr. Bradley's family. We will all miss him."
