SEN. REID EXPRESSES DISAPPOINTMENT IN DOJ RESPONSE TO JUDICIARY COMMITTEE DEMS
  Senate Democratic Leader Harry Reid released the following statement today. 
   "I am disappointed that the Justice Department is unwilling to make available some of the documents relevant to the Roberts nomination. I urge the White House to reconsider this initial decision, and to work cooperatively with Judiciary Committee Democrats so that the Senate can do its job more effectively."
