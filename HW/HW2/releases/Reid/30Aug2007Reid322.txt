SEN. REID COMMENDS MESQUITE ON DEDICATION TO CLEAN ENERGY
  U.S. Senator Harry Reid of Nevada issued the following statement following the Mesquite City Council's unanimous vote opposing coal fired power plants. "Today is a great day for Nevada," said Reid. "Under Mayor Holecheck's leadership, the Mesquite City Council unanimously adopted a resolution stating the city's opposition to coal fired power plants.  
   The citizens of Mesquite realize the importance of protecting Nevada's resources and environment. I am so proud that Mesquite's elected leaders share my vision of creating thousands of jobs and protecting our air by making Nevada the leader in renewable energy."
