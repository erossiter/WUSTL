SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON CULINARY MGM-MIRAGE AGREEMENT
  U.S. Senator Harry Reid of Nevada made the following statement in response to the agreement reached between the Culinary Union and MGM-Mirage.
   "D. Taylor of the Culinary Union and Terry Lanni of MGM-Mirage are to be commended for reaching an agreement that benefits workers here in Las Vegas and keeps our state's top industry strong. MGM-Mirage is one of America's premier businesses and the Culinary Union is recognized as one of the finest labor organizations in the nation. When business and labor work together to reach an equitable agreement, everyone wins."
