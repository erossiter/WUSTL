SEN. REID DISCUSSES HISTORIC NEVADA RACE, FUTURE OF DEMOCRATIC PARTY
  After an historic and overwhelming victory in Nevada, Sen.  
 Harry Reid will hold a press conference today to thank Nevada voters and discuss how he will continue serving Nevada and the nation as the Democratic leader of the Senate. 
   After speaking to his long-time friend and colleague Senator Tom Daschle early this morning, Reid decided to seek the position of leader. Reid has received universal and overwhelming support from his Senate colleagues. 
   Reid will discuss his Nevada race, his campaign for leader and the direction of the Democratic party. 
   TODAY, WEDNESDAY NOVEMBER 3RD 1:00 p.m. Lloyd George Courthouse, outside the main entrance 333 Las Vegas Blvd South.
