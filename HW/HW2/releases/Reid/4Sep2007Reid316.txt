SEN. REID RECEIVES CHUTZPAH AWARD
  U.S. Senator Harry Reid of Nevada was awarded with the Jewish Friendship Club's first and only Chutzpah Award.
   "I am gracious for this recognition," said Reid. "Having been born and raised in Nevada, I am committed to working on the issues that are so important to the people of our state, including making health care more affordable, changing course in Iraq and protecting the benefits our veterans deserve"
   Senator Reid is known and appreciated for his straight talk and vision for Nevada's future.
