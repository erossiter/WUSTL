SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF CORETTA SCOTT KING
  Senate Democratic Leader Harry Reid released the following statement today on the passing of civil rights leader Coretta Scott King: 
   "We awakened this morning to news of the passing of one of our nation's true heroes in the struggle for civil rights, Coretta Scott King. Together, Americans all mourn her passing. We offer our deepest thanks for her dedication to keeping the dream of racial equality and national harmony alive, even after losing her husband, Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. I join all Americans in sending my thoughts and prayers to her family"
