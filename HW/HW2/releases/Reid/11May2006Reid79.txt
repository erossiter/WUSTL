SEN. REID: IT IS TIME TO MOVE FORWARD ON COMPREHENSIVE IMMIGRATION REFORM
Senate Democratic Leader Harry Reid released the following statement on the return of comprehensive immigration reform to the Senate Floor. 
 "I welcome the return of the immigration bill to the Senate Floor. America's immigration system is broken, and our national security depends on Republicans and Democrats finding common ground to fix it. The assurances I have received from Senator Frist make me hopeful we can finally move forward on real comprehensive reform."
