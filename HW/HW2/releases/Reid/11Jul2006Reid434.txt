SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF KATHY AUGUSTINE
  Sen. Harry Reid issued the following statement regarding the passing of Nevada State Controller Kathy Augustine.
   "I was saddened to learn of the passing of Kathy Augustine today. Kathy had a long record of service to Nevada. She served as a member of the Nevada State Assembly and Senate and went on to be the first woman elected as Nevada State Controller. My thoughts and prayers go out to Kathy's husband, Chaz, and their entire family during this very difficult time."
