SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SERGEANT ANTHONY SCHOBER
  Sen. Harry Reid of Nevada issued the following statement upon learning of the death of Sergeant Anthony Schober of Nevada. Schober, 23, had been listed as one among the missing after the squad he led was attacked last weekend.
   "This is a tragedy. Sgt. Schober served our country with honor and the news of his death saddens us all in Nevada and across the nation. My thoughts and prayers go out to Sgt. Schober's family and friends as they mourn this heartbreaking loss."
