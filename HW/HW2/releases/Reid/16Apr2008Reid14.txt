SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF BOB STOLDAL
  Nevada Senator Harry Reid released the following statement upon learning of the upcoming retirement of Bob Stoldal, Vice President of News at KLAS-TV, Channel 8.
   "For more than four decades, Bob Stoldal has been a true pioneer of broadcast journalism in Las Vegas. He is a tireless leader in our community and maintains a firm commitment to preserving Nevada's rich history. Bob's impact will be felt for years to come and I wish him nothing but the best in retirement."
