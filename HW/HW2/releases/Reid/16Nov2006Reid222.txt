SEN. REID MEETS WITH NEVADA'S PROFESSOR OF THE YEAR
  Sen. Harry Reid of Nevada met today with Dr. Cheryll Glotfelty, Nevada's Professor of the Year. Dr. Glotfelty, a literature professor at University of Nevada Reno, is the world's first Professor of Literature and Environment. In addition, she formed the nation's first graduate program in Literature and Environment.  
   "I am delighted to meet with Dr. Glotfelty," Reid said. "She serves as an outstanding example of educational leadership and innovation. Nevada's higher education system has been strengthened by her efforts." 
   The Professor of the Year is awarded by the Carnegie Foundation and the Council for Advancement and Support of Education. The Professors of the Year program is the only national awards program that recognizes college and university professors for their teaching.
