SEN. REID TO OUTLINE SMALL BUSINESS PRIORITIES
  At an event hosted by the Reno/Sparks Chamber of Commerce, Sen. Harry Reid will meet with small business owners to discuss priority legislation he will be working to pass to help them and their employees. 
   Reid will highlight legislation he recently introduced offering tax refunds to help small businesses cover the cost of health insurance for their employees.
   WHO: Senator Harry Reid
  Harry York, Executive Director of Reno, Sparks Chamber of Commerce
  Small business owners and employees
  WHERE: Payroll Systems of Nevada
  860 Tyler Way
  SPARKS
  WHEN: Tuesday, June 29, 2004
  10:00 - 10:45 a.m.
