SEN. REID COMMENTS ON AFFORDABLE HOUSING SALE UNDER SOUTHERN NEVADA PUBLIC LAND MANAGEMENT ACT
  Sen. Harry Reid of Nevada released the following statement on news that the first affordable housing property made available under the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act, is expected to close escrow today.
   "I am pleased to see that the BLM, Clark County, HUD and Nevada H.A.N.D. have successfully worked together using the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act to make BLM land available for affordable housing. There is a critical need for affordable housing in Nevada and I look forward to seeing more projects like this take shape."
