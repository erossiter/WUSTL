SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON REP. MENENDEZ'S APPOINTMENT TO SENATE
  Senate Democratic Leader Harry Reid released the following statement today on Rep. Mendez's appointment to the Senate: 
   "I welcome Governor-Elect Corzine's decision to appoint Congressman Robert Menendez. With his decision, Governor-Elect Corzine is helping the U.S. Senate better reflect the diversity of America. Congressman Menendez has been a strong leader as the Chairman of the House Democratic Caucus. His leadership skills and experience in public service will make him a great addition to the Senate Democratic Caucus. I look forward to working with him."
