SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF TRANSPORTATION SECRETARY MINETA
Senate Democratic Leader Sen. Harry Reid today issued the following statement on the resignation of Transportation Secretary Norman Mineta: 
 "I have known Secretary Mineta for 25 years, had the pleasure of serving alongside him, and send him my best wishes on his resignation. Norman Mineta is a dedicated public servant. He has had a long and successful term as Secretary of Transportation, and I wish him all the best in his future pursuits."
