SEN. REID ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF CHARLIE WATERMAN
  Nevada Senator Harry Reid released the following statement upon learning of the passing of Charlie Waterman.
  "I was saddened to learn of the passing of Charlie Waterman. In addition to a long, distinguished career as an attorney, Charlie dedicated his life to building the foundation of the Clark County Democratic Party. As a long-time friend, my thoughts go out to his friends and family during this difficult time."
