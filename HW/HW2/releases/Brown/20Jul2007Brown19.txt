            Brown Statement On Use Of Hindu Prayer In The Senate Chamber                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement On Use Of Hindu Prayer In The Senate Chamber July 20  2007  Washington  DC U S    D OH  released the following statement today in response to outrage at the use of Hindu prayer in the Senate chamber As a Lutheran  I deplore the intolerance and disrespect shown to Rajan Zed  a Hindu Clergyman who gave the opening prayer during last Thursdays session of the Senate   Honoring the expression of all religions is important to protecting the plurality of faith that strengthens our diverse nation   As a member of the House of Representatives  I was proud to invite the first Hindu priest to lead Congress in the opening prayer   I will continue to promote open dialogue among all religions and support the faith of all citizens    U S
