            Brown Statement On Peru Trade Agreement In Senate                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement On Peru Trade Agreement In Senate December 3  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  released the following statement today on the Peru Trade Agreement  How large does our trade deficit have to grow  how many jobs have to disappear  and how many imports have to be recalled before we finally get off this trade mill  Free trade shouldnt mean free of positive results  We can and should take a breath and come up with a results oriented trade model that benefits our economy  not just a handful of CEOs Part of that effort  and one that has been woefully lacking with this administration  is enforcement  If you pay lip service to the rules  they arent really rules Congress ability to improve the Peru FTA is limited because Fast Track  the process by which Congress considers this trade deal  bypasses real debate and meaningful participation  Voters in Ohio and throughout the country demanded fair trade  not for Congress to yield its authority and allow another job killing trade deal    U S
