            Brown And Voinovich Announce New VA Clinics Coming To Ohio                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown And Voinovich Announce New VA Clinics Coming To Ohio  May 24  2007  WASHINGTON  Today  United States   D OH  and George V  Voinovich  R OH  announced that the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  will open new community based outpatient clinics in Parma and Hamilton  The new clinics will enhance access to quality health care for Ohios veterans  Access to veterans health care remains a major hurdle  but these new clinics are a step in the right direction  I look forward to working with the VA as a member of the Senate Veterans Affairs Committee  to continue to improve access to care for Ohios veterans  said Sen  BrownBuilding these new clinics takes us one step closer to ensuring we keep our promise to our nations veterans  Sen  Voinovich said  The new facilities will help the VA provide better care closer to home  While we can never fully repay our veterans for their service  it is critical that we continue supporting programs that improve their quality of life The facilities  called community based outpatient clinics  or CBOCs  will become operational in 2007 and 2008  Local VA officials will keep communities and their veterans informed of milestones in the creation of the new CBOCs VA is committed to providing world class health care to the men and women who have served this nation  VA Secretary Jim Nicholson said  These new clinics will bring VAs top notch care closer to the veterans who have earned it The senators have been long time advocates of better VA care in Ohio  The new outpatient clinics in Parma and Hamilton will be part of a 30 mile radius initiative  guaranteeing that every veteran in Ohio is within 30 miles of a VA Primary Medical Care Center for regular doctor visits  The Parma facility is projected to have 37 000 visits annually and 5 000 at Hamilton With 153 hospitals and more than 700 community based clinics  VA operates the largest integrated health care system in the country  VAs health care budget of more than  34 billion this year will provide health care to about 5 5 million people during nearly 800 000 hospitalizations and 60 million outpatient visits  The clinic was among 38 approved in 22 states by the VA
