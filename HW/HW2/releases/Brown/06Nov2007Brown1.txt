            Brown Response To New Bush Import Safety Proposal                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Response To New Bush Import Safety Proposal November 6  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  today released the following statement in response to President George W  Bushs new import safety proposal Our nation has seen a flood of recalls of contaminated food that made it onto our kitchen tables and toxic toys that found their way into our childrens bedrooms  Todays White House acknowledgement that we need to strengthen the CPSC is a step in the right direction  I hope this means the president will also support the current CPSC legislation making its way through Congress  We must do more to protect 40 years of U S  safety standards  We must do more to protect our families and our children
