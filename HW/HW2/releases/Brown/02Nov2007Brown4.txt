            Brown Statement On Presidential Veto Of Water Resource Development Act                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement On Presidential Veto Of Water Resource Development Act November 2  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  released the following statement on todays presidential veto of the Water Resource Development Act I am extremely disappointed by the presidents veto today  Instead of funding improvements for Americas deteriorating infrastructure  the president used his fifth veto of the year to once again leave middle class families behind  The Water Resource Development Act is bipartisan legislation that includes many vital projects for Ohio  including flood mitigation efforts and initiatives for the Great Lakes  Today was a step backward for economic development in our country  but I hope next week Congress will move forward and override the presidents veto    U S
