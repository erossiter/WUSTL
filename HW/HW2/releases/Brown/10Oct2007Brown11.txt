            Brown Expresses Concern For Shooting Victims                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Expresses Concern For Shooting Victims October 10  2007  CLEVELAND  United States   D OH  issued the following statement in response to the school shootings today at SuccessTech Academy Todays victims and their families are in my prayers  As the father of four children  my heart goes out to each and every parent  I will work with Mayor Jacksons office to ensure that the community receives full support as the healing process unfolds   No parent should have to fear for their childrens safety at school  and representatives at all levels of government must determine how to prevent tragedies like this in the future
