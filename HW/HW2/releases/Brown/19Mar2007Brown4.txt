            Senator Brown Statement On Fourth Anniversary Of War In Iraq                                                                                                                               Press Release    Senator Brown Statement On Fourth Anniversary Of War In Iraq March 19  2007  WASHINGTON  United States  released the following statement on the fourth anniversary of the War in Iraq Our nations brave men and women serving in Iraq deserve our gratitude and our support  And they deserve a plan for redeployment  Four years after invading Iraq our troops are embroiled in a civil war while the administration advocates escalation  Voters in Ohio spoke loudly and clearly in November that they want an end to this war and it is our duty in Congress to heed their call    U S
