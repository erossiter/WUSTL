            Brown s Response To President Bush s Speech in Tipp City  Ohio                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown s Response To President Bush s Speech in Tipp City  Ohio April 19  2007   WASHINGTON  United States   D OH  released the following response to President George W  Bushs speech today at Tippecanoe High School in Tipp City  Ohio   The president refuses to listen to voters  he refuses to listen to the Iraq study group  he refuses to listen to state legislators  and he refuses to listen to Congress  The war in Iraq has cost 142 brave Ohioans their lives  and wounded another thousand  Make no mistake    every member of the Senate supports the brave men and women fighting in Iraq  We support the equipment our soldiers need and the health care they deserve  But more of the same is not a plan for our troops  and will not end the war in Iraq    If the president wont take responsibility for his failures and lead our troops home then Congress will  We owe it to our soldiers  our sailors  our Airmen  and our Marines  We owe it to their families  The President should listen to military leaders and the American people and work with Congress to change course in Iraq  not threaten vetoes     U S
