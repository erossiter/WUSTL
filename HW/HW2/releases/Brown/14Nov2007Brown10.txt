            Brown Announces Final Passage Of Head Start For School Readiness Act                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Announces Final Passage Of Head Start For School Readiness Act November 14  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  today announced final passage of the Head Start for School Readiness Act  The legislation ensures accountability  advances participation in Head Start programs  strengthens Early Head Start  and promotes quality services across early education programs  Brown is a member of the Senate Health  Education  Labor  and Pensions  HELP  Committee This is a long overdue reauthorization that will help thousands of low income children transition into school and be successful later in life  Brown said  Head Start helps children thrive  There is no greater investment we can make The reauthorization bill would expand Head Start access  focus on school readiness  and strengthen the workforce  Specific improvements in this bill include  An increase of  83 million for Early Head Start  which could serve an additional 8 000 low income children    Critical training and a cost of living adjustment in Head Start teacher salaries   Expansion of programs to allow low income children in families just over the poverty line to qualify for participation    Enhanced standards to transition Head Start children to school by promoting the development of language and literacy  math  science  and other cognitive skills   Dedicated funding for Head Start grantees for local training and technical assistance efforts    On going literacy training for Head Start teachers to promote the development of childrens pre reading skills   Yesterday the president vetoed the funding for Head Start  Budgets are about priorities  Vetoing funding for Head Start tells us something about his priorities  By passing legislation that expands Head Start to reach more low income children  this Senate signaled that its priorities are different  I hope that together we can override the presidents veto and fulfill the promise inherent in the Head Start program  Brown said
