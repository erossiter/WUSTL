            Brown Statement On Final Passage Of Water Resource Development Act                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement On Final Passage Of Water Resource Development Act September 24  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  released the following statement on final passage of the Water Resource Development Act The Water Resource Development Act includes many vital projects for Ohio  including flood mitigation efforts and initiatives for the Great Lakes  I hope that Congress will fully fund these important infrastructure programs when the Energy and Water Appropriations Act comes before us in the next few weeks  If the President decides to veto this bipartisan bill  he will hurt economic development throughout the country
