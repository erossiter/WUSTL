            Brown Applauds Passage Of Senate Budget Resolution                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Applauds Passage Of Senate Budget Resolution March 23  2007  WASHINGTON  United States   D OH  released the following statement on the Senate Passage of the Budget Resolution Today the Senate passed a fiscally responsible budget that will eliminate the deficit within the next five years while providing billions of dollars for essential programs including veterans health care  the State Childrens Health Insurance Program  and Community Development Block Grants This budget reflects the values of middle class families and invests in workers and small businesses  students and teachers  seniors and veterans  This will go a long way toward helping families throughout Ohio The House of Representatives is expected to consider its version of the budget resolution next week   Following House action  a House Senate conference committee must then reconcile the two versions of the legislation and both chambers must vote on the final resolution
