            Brown Statement On Presidential Veto Of Children s Health Insurance Bill                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement On Presidential Veto Of Children s Health Insurance Bill October 3  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  released the following statement today after President George W  Bush issued a veto of the bipartisan Childrens Health Insurance Program  CHIP  legislation I wish that before he vetoed this bill the president would have met with the children and working parents I talked to in Ohio  By abandoning these children    children who are already eligible for CHIP but not yet enrolled  he has shown that on this issue he is neither compassionate nor conservative   These children need to receive preventive care in the doctors office instead of acute care in the emergency room   I hope enough of my Republican colleagues will join our bipartisan effort and vote to override todays veto
