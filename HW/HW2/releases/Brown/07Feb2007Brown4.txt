             Appointed Deputy Whip for 110th Congress                                                                                                                               Press Release     Appointed Deputy Whip for 110th Congress February 7  2007  WASHINGTON  United States   D OH  has been appointed to the Senate Democratic Whip Team for the 110th Congress by Assistant Majority Leader Dick Durbin  D IL   As a member of the whip team  Brown will assist with mobilizing votes within the Senate Democratic Caucus on major issues  and help to manage and monitor legislative efforts on the Senate Floor It is an honor to assist the Democratic Leadership in advancing our agenda this Congress  said Senator Brown  It is vitally important that we spend the next two years moving Americas priorities forward   We have passed both ethics and minimum wage bills this Congress  and will continue to pursue legislation that supports working families  I look forward to the busy year ahead I am pleased that Sherrod Brown has agreed to do this very important job  said Senator Durbin  With his years of service in the House of Representatives  Sherrod brings a wealth of experience and knowledge on the important issues  He is respected  trusted and liked by Senators both old and new and I look forward to working with him Senator Barbara Boxer  D CA  will serve as Chief Deputy Whip  The remainder of the whip team includes Senator Ben Cardin  D MD    Tom Carper  D DE   Russ Feingold  D WI   Amy Klobuchar  D MN   Clair McCaskill  D MO   Patty Murray  D WA   Bill Nelson  D FL   Mark Pryor  D AR   Ken Salazar  D CO  and Ron Wyden  D OR
