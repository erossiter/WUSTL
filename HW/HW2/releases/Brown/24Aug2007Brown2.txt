            Brown  Voinovich Send Letter To President Requesting Flood Relief                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown  Voinovich Send Letter To President Requesting Flood Relief  August 24  2007  Washington United States Senators Sherrod Brown  D OH  and George Voinovich  R OH  along with Representatives John Boehner  R OH   Marcy Kaptur  D OH   Paul Gilmor  R OH  and Jim Jordan  R OH  today sent the president a letter regarding the tragic flooding that has occurred in Ohio    The text of the letter follows                                                                                  August 24  2007The PresidentThe White HouseWashington  D C   Dear Mr  President We are writing in support of Governor Ted Strickland declaring a state of emergency across nine counties in Ohio    Record rainfall and severe flooding in Allen  Crawford  Hancock  Hardin  Putnam  Richland  Seneca  Van Wert and Wyandot Counties have left scores of Ohioans without homes and has even tragically led to fatalities   Currently  U S  Coast Guard and state assets are assisting in disaster response operations   Governor Stricklands current state of emergency declaration makes disaster assistance available to flooding victims across Ohio  Should Ohios own assets be insufficient however  it is our hope that your office will not hesitate to provide federal assistance to help blunt the extraordinary costs and damages related to this disaster  We encourage your support of Governor Strickland and hope any request for emergency assistance will be met promptly   Thank you for your attention to this matter of great importance to the people of these counties  Sincerely Sherrod Brown    George V  VoinovichJohn BoehnerMarcy KapturPaul E  GillmorJim Jordan
