            Brown Statement on UAW Strike                                                                                                                               Press Release    Brown Statement on UAW Strike September 24  2007  Washington  DC  U S    D OH  released the following statement on the UAW strike My staff is visiting with workers on the picket line in Ohio today  Both the management and workers know that it is in their best interest to figure this out  and I hope there is a quick resolution and everyone can get back to work  Health care is a problem for American industry as a whole  coupled with the enormous challenge working families face today because of job killing trade policies and lack of a national manufacturing policy
