  Senator Bennett Speaks on the Death of Lady Bird Johnson   WASHINGTON D.C.  Senator Bob Bennett (R-Utah) issued the following statement today following the news that Lady Bird Johnson had died at the age of 94:       Lady Bird Johnson was a good friend of my mothers. They served as Senate wives together, and then continued their friendship after the Johnsons went to the White House.  Joyce and I send our condolences to the family, particularly Chuck and Linda Robb, with whom we served in the Senate.# # #
