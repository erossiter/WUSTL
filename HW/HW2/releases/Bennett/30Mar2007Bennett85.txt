  Senator Bennett's Staff To Hold Office Hours In Green River On April 17     Staff will be available to assist with federal agency issues   

  Bennett staff often hold remote office hours throughout central Utah, giving Utahns the opportunity to meet face-to-face with caseworkers who can help them with many federal agency issues. Bennett caseworkers can provide assistance with federal agencies, including: Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Department of Defense, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, and Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.
