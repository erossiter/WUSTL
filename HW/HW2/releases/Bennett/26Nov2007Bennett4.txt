  Senator Bennett's Staff to Hold Office Hours in Montezuma Creek on November 28th     Staff will be available to assist with federal agency issues  WASHINGTON,DC -- Representatives from Senator Bennetts office will be at theUtah Navajo Health Center in Montezuma CreekonWednesday,November 28thfrom9:00 to11:00a.m. to help individuals with federal agency questions or problems.  Over thepast two years, Bennett staff members have held remote office hours throughout central and southeastern Utah, giving Utahns the opportunity to meet face-to-face with caseworkers who can help them with federal agency issues. Bennett caseworkers can provide assistance with federal agencies, including: Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services,Military, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, and Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.  If individuals are unable to attend theMontezuma Creek office hours onNovember 28th, but would still like to request Senator Bennetts help, they are encouraged to call the senators Provo office, which handles constituent services for central and southeastern Utah, at (801) 851-2525. # # #
