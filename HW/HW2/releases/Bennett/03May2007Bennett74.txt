  Senator Bennett's Staff to Hold Office Hours in Heber and Wallsburg on May 10     Staff will be available to assist Utahns with federal agency issues  

  Bennett staff office hours, held throughout Utah, gives local citizens the opportunity to meet face-to-face with caseworkers who can provide assistance navigating through the federal red tape. Staff regularly works with federal agencies including: Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Department of Defense, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, and Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.    

  Heber City Main Office,   75 N. Main Street,  Heber, Utah,  Thursday, May 10, 2007, from 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m.    

  Wallsburg Community Center,   West Main Street,  Wallsburg, Utah,  Thursday, May 10, 2007, from 4:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.    
 

If individuals are unable to attend the these office hours, but would still like to request Senator Bennett's help, please call the senator's Salt Lake office at (801) 524-5933.
