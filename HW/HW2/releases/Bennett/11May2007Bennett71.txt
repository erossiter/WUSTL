  Senator Bennett's Staff to Hold Office Hours in Castle Dale on May 23     Staff will be available to assist with federal agency issues  

Bennett staff often hold remote office hours throughout Utah giving constituents the opportunity to meet face-to-face with caseworkers who can help them with many federal agency issues. Bennett caseworkers can provide assistance with federal agencies, including: Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Department of Defense, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, and Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.    
  Emery County Courthouse, Small Conference Room, 75 East Main, Castle Dale, Utah, Wednesday, May 23, 2007, 9:30 to 11:30 a.m.   
  If individuals are unable to attend the these office hours, but would still like to request Senator Bennett's help, please call the senator's Provo office, which handles constituent services for central and southeastern Utah, at (801) 851-2525.
