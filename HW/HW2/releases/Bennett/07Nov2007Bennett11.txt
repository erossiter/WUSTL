  Senator Bennett's Staff to Hold Office Hours in Ephraim on November 20     Staff will be available to answer Utahns questions about federal agencies  WASHINGTON, D.C.  Representatives from Senator Bob Bennetts office will be at Ephraim City Hall on November 20 to assist area residents with questions and issues regarding federal agencies. Over the past two years, Bennett staff members have traveled throughout central and southeastern Utah meeting one-on-one with Utahns who have questions pertaining to a federal agency. These caseworkers are available to provide assistance with issues concerning the: Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services, and other federal agencies.Senator Bob Bennett Office HoursEphraim City Hall5 South Main, Ephraim Tuesday, November 20, 2007, from 2:30 to 4:30 p.m. If individuals cannot attend these office hours, but would still like to request Senator Bennetts help, please call the senators Provo office, which handles constituent services for central and southeastern Utah, at (801) 851-2525.# # #
