  Senator Bennett's Staff to Hold Office Hours in Moab on March 21     Staff will be available to assist with federal agency issues  


Bennett staff members often hold remote office hours throughout central and southeastern Utah, giving Utahns the opportunity to meet face-to-face with caseworkers who can help them with federal agency issues. Bennett caseworkers can provide assistance with federal agencies, including: Internal Revenue Service, Bureau of Land Management, Social Security Administration, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Department of Defense, Veterans Affairs, Forest Service, and Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.    

Senator Bob Bennett Office Hours,  Moab City Mayor's Office,  217 East Center, Moab,  Wednesday, March 21, 2007, from 9:00 to 11:00 am    

To schedule an appointment, please call Carmella in Mayor Sakrison's office at 435-259-5121. If individuals are unable to attend the Moab office hours on March 21st, but would still like to request Senator Bennett's help, they are encouraged to call the senator's Provo office, which handles constituent services for central and southeastern Utah, at (801) 851-2525.
