 Washington  DC    Idaho Senator Mike Crapo commended Senate plans to call an emergency session tonight to approve appropriations to provide immediate assistance for federal and local disaster response teams   The Senate plans to send approximately  10 billion in aid to help with rescue efforts currently underway in the Gulf region        We   ve all seen pictures of the devastation in New Orleans and Biloxi and other communities throughout the South     said Crapo      It is imperative that we respond quickly and effectively to this natural disaster   Congress is planning this emergency session to expedite the financial resources needed by relief organizations and local and state agencies   I also encourage Idahoans to do all they can to assist the recovery efforts  Those could include donations of money and other necessary items  and donating blood through local hospitals and Red Cross chapters        Crapo   s Senate website contains more information on how Idahoans can assist victims at http   crapo senate gov
