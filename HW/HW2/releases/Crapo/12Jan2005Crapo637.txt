 Washington  DC     Student applications to attend one of the nation   s four military academies are now being accepted by Idaho Senator Mike Crapo   s office   Every year  Crapo nominates selected Idaho students to the following   the U S  Air Force Academy  Colorado Springs  Colorado  the U S  Merchant Marine Academy  Kings Point  New York  the U S  Military Academy  West Point  New York  and the U S  Naval Academy  Annapolis  Maryland   Interested students need to submit an application and references and meet certain scholastic requirements for consideration in the next academic year   The final admission selections are made by the individual academies        It is never too early for high school students to begin to explore their higher education options     Crapo said      The four military academies maintain some of the highest academic standards and programs in the country  and the learning environment and opportunities are unparalleled   I would strongly encourage Idaho students who have an interest in attending one of these academies to begin the application process now   Each year  we have several Idaho students who are accepted to the academies and their educational experience is one of the finest available today        Students may apply to both United States Senators and the U S  Representative from their congressional district as well as the Vice President of the United States   The names of students nominated by Crapo will be forwarded to each service academy  where final appointments are made by the individual institution   Those wishing to apply for a nomination to the next class  entering in 2006  should contact Crapo   s military representative  Linda Norris at  202  224 0851 for more information   Applications must be submitted no later than October 31  2005
