 Washington  DC     This morning  Idaho Senator Mike Crapo stood before the U S  Supreme Court to present three Idaho attorneys for admission to the Bar of the Supreme Court   Crapo  who is one of a handful of U S  Senators with privileges to appear before the High Court  introduced Bonneville County Prosecutor Dane Watkins  Jr   Chief Deputy Bruce Pickett  and Deputy Randy Neal          It is a privilege for any individual to be able to appear before the nation s highest court   Crapo said    Today was particularly significant in that I was able to introduce three Idaho attorneys for admittance to the Bar of Supreme Court   That means that they are now qualified to appear before the nation s highest court   I was honored to be asked to introduce them
