 Washington  DC     The service of a former Boise resident who served multiple tours in both Iraq and Afghanistan was praised by Idaho Senator Mike Crapo today in Washington  DC   Sgt  Christopher M  Erberich died during a training flight in Georgia earlier this month   He was laid to rest today during a ceremony at Arlington National Cemetery        Sgt  Erberich went above and beyond the call of duty   He was training with Special Operations forces when the accident which caused his death occurred  and that followed multiple tours of duty in both Iraq and Afghanistan     Crapo said     Sgt  Erberich was a man who constantly worked to better himself by advancing his education in Boise and advancing the cause of freedom overseas   His service and sacrifice stands as an example for all of us
