 Washington  DC     Idaho Senator Mike Crapo expressed his condolences today to the family and friends of Specialist Harley D  Andrews  who died when an improvised explosive device detonated near his military vehicle in Ar Ramadi  Iraq   Specialist Andrews  whose father lives in Boise  was originally stationed in Germany with the 130th Engineer Brigade  54th Engineer Battalion   He died on September 11th        My heartfelt sympathy is with Specialist Andrew   s father in Idaho and with his family and friends during this time of loss     Crapo said      I know words offer little comfort  but I do hope his father and family members know a grateful nation appreciates his service to our country and will long remember his ultimate sacrifice on behalf of the American and Iraqi people
