SEN. CRAIG HIRES NEW D.C. STAFF
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig announced the hiring of Whitney Rolig of Twin Falls as a Staff Assistant in his Washington, D.C., office.  
 Rolig will assist with data entry, constituent services and other administrative tasks in the office. 
   "Coming from the Magic Valley, Whitney will bring a strong work ethic to this position," said Craig. "She has a good grasp of the issues that are important to Idahoans, and she'll do an outstanding job on their behalf. I am very pleased to have her join my team." 
   "I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to work for Senator Craig in Washington," Rolig said. "The Senator's extensive involvement in a variety of issues offers an exciting and challenging environment, and I look forward to working with such a motivated and dynamic staff." 
   Rolig graduated from Twin Falls High School in 2000, and in 2004, she earned her B.A. Degree in Economics and International Studies from Willamette University in Salem, Oregon.
