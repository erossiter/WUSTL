SEN. CRAIG TO HOLD TOWNHALL MEETING IN MERIDIAN
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig announced today that he will hold a townhall meeting on Thursday, October 13, from Noon-1:30 p.m. at Meridian City Hall, 33 East Idaho Avenue. Sen. Craig will give a short update on affairs in the Senate and will then open the floor for questions from the audience. 
   WHO: Senator Larry Craig is holding a townhall meeting, open to the public.
   WHEN: Thursday, October 13, Noon-1:30 p.m. 
   WHERE: Meridian City Hall, 33 East Idaho Avenue 
   For more information, please contact Senator Craig's Boise Regional Office at (208) 342-7985.
   Contact: Mike Tracy, 208/342-7985; Sid Smith, 202/224-8708.
