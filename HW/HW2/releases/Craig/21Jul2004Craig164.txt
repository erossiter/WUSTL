SEN. CRAIG ANNOUNCES NEW STAFF IN BOISE REGIONAL OFFICE
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig announced the appointment of Mike Freese as the Natural Resources Field Coordinator based in his Boise Regional Office. 
   Freese is a recent graduate of Eastern Oregon University where he studied Environmental Economic Policy and Management. He served as the Student Body President of the LaGrande based school where he was recognized as the University's Outstanding Agriculture student during his senior year. He also served an internship with the Idaho Water Users Association in 2002.
   The Jordan Valley, Oregon native will assist Southwest Idaho constituents as Senator Craig's liaison to State and federal natural resource agencies such as the U.S. Forest Service, the Bureau of Land Management, the Bureau of Reclamation, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, the USDA, and others.
   Mike Freese replaces Nate Helm who left to become the Executive Director of the Idaho Chapter of Sportsmen for Fish and Wildlife.
   Freese can be reached in the Boise office at 342-7985.
