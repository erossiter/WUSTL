SEN. CRAIG TO HOLD TOWNHALL MEETING IN LEWISTON
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig announced today that he will hold a townhall meeting on Wednesday, October 12, from 3:00 - 4:30 P.M., at the Idaho Department of Fish and Game Building in Lewiston. Sen.  
 Craig will give a short update on affairs in the Senate, and will then open the floor for questions from the audience. 
   WHO: Senator Larry Craig is holding a townhall meeting, open to the public. 
   WHEN: Wednesday, October 12, 2005, from 3:00 - 4:30 P.M. 
   WHERE: The Idaho Department of Fish and Game Building, Clearwater Conference Room, 3316 16th Street (in the Lewiston Orchards).
   For more information, please contact Senator Craig's Lewiston Regional Office at (208) 743-0792.
   Contact: Mike Tracy, 208/342-7985; Sid Smith, 202/224-8708.
