SEN. CRAIG COMMENTS ON SECRETARY NORTON'S RESIGNATION
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig commented on the resignation of Secretary of Interior Gale Norton today. Norton served for five years in the position.
   "Secretary Norton played a vital role in pushing the Healthy Forest Initiative and bringing common sense to endangered species recovery," Craig said. "She has been innovative and brought a great deal of respect to Interior. Her leadership will be missed and I wish her the best of luck in her future endeavors."
