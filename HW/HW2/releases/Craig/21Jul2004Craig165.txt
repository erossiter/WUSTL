SEN. CRAIG ANNOUNCES GRANT WORKSHOP IN COEUR D'ALENE
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig today announced a workshop on federal aid for local libraries and museums, to take place on Friday, August 6, at North Idaho College in Coeur d'Alene.  
 Craig and Rep. George Nethercutt, of Washington's 5th District, are sponsoring the event along with the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS).
   "I am very pleased to be sponsoring this event in Coeur d'Alene, and I encourage library and museum officials from all over the region to come," Craig said. "The IMLS has a wealth of knowledge and resources that can benefit every library or museum official, helping them achieve their goals and serve their communities more effectively. I think it will be a very productive and informative meeting."
   Dr. Robert Martin, Director of the IMLS, will be attending and conducting a seminar on "Creating and Sustaining a Nation of Learners."
   For more information, please visit Senator Craig's website at http://craig.senate.gov/grants/, or call Senator Craig's Coeur d'Alene office at (208) 667-6130.
