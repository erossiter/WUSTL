SEN. CRAIG'S LEWISTON OFFICE HAS MOVED
  Idaho Sen. Larry Craig's Lewiston office changed locations this week.  
 While still downtown, constituents wishing to visit his office should go to:
   313 D Street 
  Suite 106 
  Lewiston, Idaho 83501 
  (208) 743-0792
   Any of Senator Craig's six offices around Idaho seek to bring the Senate to Idahoans. Idahoans can visit the offices in Coeur d' Alene, Lewiston, Boise, Twin Falls, Pocatello, and Idaho Falls to:
   * Express their opinion to Senator Craig
  * Resolve a dispute with a federal agency
  * Order a flag flown over the U.S. Capitol
  * Request tickets for tours in Washington, D.C. or congratulation letters for Eagle Scouts
  * Request greetings from the President for certain significant birthdays or wedding anniversaries
  * And just about anything else related to Senator Craig or the federal government.
   Contact: Mike Tracy, 208/342-7985; Dan Whiting, 202/224-8078.
