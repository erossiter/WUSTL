SEN. COLEMAN TO VISIT WABASHA AND WINONA ON SEPT. 29
  Senator Norm Coleman will visit the southeastern Minnesota cities of Wabasha and Winona on Saturday, September 29th. Coleman will participate in the National Eagle Center dedication ceremony in Wabasha and visit the Disaster Recovery Center in Winona during the afternoon visit.  
   Details of the day's events are as follows. Local media are welcome to attend. 
   1:20 - 2:45 p.m. 
  Coleman Speaks at National Eagle Center Dedication 
  50 Pembroke Avenue 
  Wabasha, MN 
   3:45 - 4:30 p.m. 
  Sen. Coleman Visits Winona Disaster Recovery Center 
  JC Penney Building 
  Hwy 61 & Hwy 14
