SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF REP. RAMSTAD
  Senator Norm Coleman made the following statement in response to Congressman Jim Ramstad's (R-MN) announcement that he will retire from the United States House of Representatives:  
   "Congressman Jim Ramstad has tirelessly served the constituents of his district with great passion throughout his nine terms in office. He has proven to be an effective Congressman who has always shown great commitment to the issues he cared about most, including an unwavering dedication to mental health parity and expanding access to chemical dependency treatment. Jim's service will have lasting benefits on the people of Minnesota. He truly represents the best public service has to offer and I wish him nothing but success and happiness in his future endeavors."
