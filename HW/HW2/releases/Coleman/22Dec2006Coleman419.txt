SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES HOLIDAY STATEMENT
  HOLIDAY STATEMENT FROM SEN. NORM COLEMAN:
   "Laurie and I would like to extend a Merry Christmas, Happy Hanukkah and blessed New Year in 2007 to all Minnesotans. I especially want to recognize our service men and women and their families and ask everyone to remember them in your thoughts and prayers during this holiday season. I look forward to another year of serving my constituents in 2007 and continue to be optimistic about the future of our great state and nation."
