SEN. COLEMAN STAFF TO VISIT ALEXANDRIA, WADENA, PARK RAPIDS, HENNING, HALLOCK, ROSEAU, ERSKINE
  Staff for Sen. Norm Coleman will visit seven communities in West-Central and Northwest Minnesota during a two-day visit to the region on Tuesday, September 19th and Wednesday September, 20th. The visit by Carl Kuhl and Brenda Elmer, Coleman's regional representatives in the area, will include stops in Alexandria, Wadena, Park Rapids, Henning, Hallock, Roseau and Erskine. Kuhl and Elmer will discuss the latest work by Sen. Coleman to secure disaster assistance for Minnesota farmers, while also discussing and listening to other area concerns from local officials and residents.  
   On Tuesday, Kuhl and Elmer will begin the day with a meeting at the FSA Service Center in Alexandria, followed by visits to FSA Service Centers in Wadena and Park Rapids. They will conclude Tuesday's visits at the Minnesota Association of Townships District 9 meeting in Henning. 
   On Wednesday, they will meet with local agriculture representatives at the Kittson County Courthouse, before meeting with Kittson County Sheriff Kenny Hultgren in Hallock. They will then travel to Roseau for a discussion at the FSA Service Center and a meeting with the Roseau County Sheriff, Jule Hanson. They will conclude the trip with a Minnesota Association of Townships District 13 meeting in Erskine.
