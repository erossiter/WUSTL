SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF HIS FATHER
  Statement On The Passing Of Senator Norm Coleman's Father:
   "This morning, Senator Coleman's father, Norman Coleman, Sr., passed away after an ongoing battle with cancer. As a decorated World War II veteran, he will be laid to rest at Arlington National Cemetery. During this time of grief, Senator Coleman and his family ask for your thoughts and prayers."
