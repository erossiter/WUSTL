SEN. COLEMAN ANNOUNCES GRANTS FOR SIX FIRE DEPARTMENTS ACROSS WESTERN MINNESOTA
  Sen. Norm Coleman today announced six fire departments in western Minnesota have been awarded grants under the federal Assistance to Firefighters Grant (AFG) Program. The grant will be used for various operations and safety improvements at fire departments in Beardsley, Benson, Sauk Centre, Vergas, Walnut Grove and Winger.  
   "I applaud the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program for providing important funding to these fire departments, "said Coleman. "With the help of these grants, firefighters up and down the western half of Minnesota will be better equipped to protect local residents, and I am pleased they have received these important grants." 
   Specific grant awards are as follows: 
   * Beardsley Fire Department - $43,700 
   * Benson Fire Department - $99,540 
   * Sauk Centre Fire Department - $42,476 
   * CDH-Vergas Fire and Rescue - $37,046 
   * Walnut Grove Fire Department - $34,675 
   * Winger Fire/Rescue Department - $87,507 
   The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program, administered within the Department of Homeland Security, is designed to provide resources for the firefighting and emergency response needs of fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical services organizations. The AFG program awards grants directly to local fire departments to support firefighters, first responders, and the services they deliver. 
   For more information on the program, please visit www.firegrantsupport.com/afg.
