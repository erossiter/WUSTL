SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON OUTGOING BIA HEAD
  "I want to thank Dave Anderson for his willingness to serve as head of the Bureau of Indian Affairs. I admire him for heeding the call to public service. 
   Dave is a good man whose nomination to head the BIA I was proud to support. His life story is truly a tale of the American dream come true. I admire his accomplishments and his steadfast committment to helping the Native American community. Today, I salute his service."
