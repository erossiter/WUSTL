SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON VOTING RIGHTS ACT
  Statement from Sen. Norm Coleman on Voting Rights Act: 
   "Today's vote to renew the Voting Rights Act reaffirms our core principles of equality, freedom and democracy - regardless of race, color or creed. When it first became law, this bill represented one of the major steps on our country's journey toward equality, and I was pleased to join my colleagues in voting to renew this important and historic piece of legislation."
