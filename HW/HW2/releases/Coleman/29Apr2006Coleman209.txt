SEN. COLEMAN TO HOLD PRESS CONFERENCE AT STATE CAPITOL TO DISCUSS HIGH GAS PRICES, RENEWABLE FUEL INITIATIVES
  Sen. Norm Coleman will hold a press conference on Monday, May 1st at 11:00 a.m. at the State Capitol to discuss rising gas prices, as well as his new legislative initiatives to encourage and expand the use of alternative fuels, including his bills to require 10 percent of our nation's fuel to be blended with renewable fuels in 10 years and his bill to reduce foreign oil dependence by establishing specific national goals for replacing oil imports through a significant expansion of renewable fuel and renewable fuel infrastructure, increased use of vehicle technologies such as hybrids, and conservation measures. Additionally, Coleman will discuss his commitment to ensuring that Minnesota's nation-leading renewable fuel gasoline blends will not be affected during the Senate's upcoming debate on reducing boutique fuel blends across the nation.
