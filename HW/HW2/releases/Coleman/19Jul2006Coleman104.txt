SEN. COLEMAN AGRICULTURE REPRESENTATIVE TO VISIT DODGE, MOWER, WASECA, STEELE COUNTIES
  In light of recent dry weather that is posing serious challenges for Minnesota farmers, Sen. Norm Coleman will be sending his Agriculture Representative, Matt Ketelsen, to visit Dodge, Mower, Waseca, and Steele Counties as part of a one-day visit to the region on Thursday, July 20th. With the potential for drought disaster conditions, Coleman is sending Ketelsen to get an updated assessment of the crop situation and to hear specifics problems and concerns from farmers and representatives of Farm Service Agencies (FSA) in the area.
