SEN. COLEMAN ANNOUNCES GRANTS FOR FIRE DEPARTMENTS IN BLUFFTON, TWIN VALLEY, EAGLE BEND AND BATTLE LAKE
  Senator Norm Coleman today announced local fire departments in Bluffton, Twin Valley, Eagle Bend and Battle Lake have been awarded grants under the federal Assistance to Firefighters Grant (AFG) Program. The grants will be used to fund operation and safety improvements for the respective departments.  
   "I applaud the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program for providing this important funding to these local fire departments, "said Coleman. "Local firefighters are often the first line of safety for their communities, and it is critical they have the best resources available. With the help of these grants, these departments will be better equipped to protect area residents, and I am pleased they have received this funding." 
   Specific grant awards are as follows: 
   * Bluffton Fire Department - $66,230 
   * Twin Valley Fire Department - $36,480 
   * Eagle Bend Fire Department - $45,078 
   * Battle Lake Fire Department - $27,835 
   The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program, administered within the Department of Homeland Security, is designed to provide resources for the firefighting and emergency response needs of fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical services organizations. The AFG program awards grants directly to local fire departments to support firefighters, first responders, and the services they deliver. 
   For more information on the program, please visit www.firegrantsupport.com/afg.
