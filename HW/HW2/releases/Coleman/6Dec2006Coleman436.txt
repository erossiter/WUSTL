SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON CONFIRMATION OF ROBERT GATES
SEN. COLEMAN STATEMENT ON CONFIRMATION OF BOB GATES:
 "I was pleased to join my colleagues today in confirming Bob Gates to the position of Secretary of Defense. During his confirmation hearings, I found Dr. Gates to be sincere in his testimony and I appreciate his commitment to bring an effective, independent voice to our nation's defense policy. I am confident that with this change in leadership at the Pentagon, we will move forward with an improved strategy for victory in Iraq."
