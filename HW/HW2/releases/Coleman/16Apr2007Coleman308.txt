SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON VIRGINIA TECH TRAGEDY
  Statement From Sen. Norm Coleman On The Virginia Tech Tragedy: 
   My heart goes out to the victims and their families affected by this senseless tragedy. As parents of a son in college, my wife Laurie and I feel the enormity of loss that so many Virginia Tech families are experiencing tonight. Today, our nation comes together to grieve this loss and provide support to the students, families and entire Virginia Tech community. Their loss is the nation's loss. Laurie and I will be keeping them in our thoughts and prayers during this time of grief.
