SEN. COLEMAN URGES ALL MINNESOTANS TO VOTE ON NOV. 2
  "Minnesota has a long and proud history of strong voter turnout, and I urge every Minnesotan to exercise what is perhaps their most important right as a citizen of the United States and vote on November 2. The strength of our democracy truly depends on the participation of all citizens.
   Should you need more information on your polling place, visit http://pollfinder.sos.state.mn.us/ or call the Minnesota Secretary of State's Office at (877) 551-6767."
