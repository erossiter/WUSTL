SEN. COLEMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON 35TH ANNIVERSARY OF ROE V. WADE
  "On the 35th anniversary of Roe v. Wade, it remains as important as ever to continue the fight to protect and promote a culture of life in America. Through perseverance and unwavering commitment, we have made real progress during the last 35 years. Going forward, we can build on the many successes we've had by continuing to work together to protect the sanctity of life for every human being."
