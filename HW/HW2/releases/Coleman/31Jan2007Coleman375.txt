SEN. COLEMAN ANNOUNCES $224,271 FOR WHITE BEAR LAKE FIRE DEPARTMENT
  Sen. Norm Coleman today announced the White Bear Lake Fire Department has been awarded a $224,271 grant under the Assistance to Firefighters Grant (AFG) Program.  
   "I applaud the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program for awarding this grant to the White Bear Lake Fire Department," said Coleman. "Local fire departments are the on the front lines of public safety, and it is important they have the resources they need. This grant will help improve operations for the folks in and around White Bear Lake, and I am pleased they have received this funding." 
   "We're very grateful for this grant," said White Bear Lake Fire Chief Tim Vadnais. "It's going to go a long way towards helping our paid on-call staff as we upgrade our emergency phone equipment." 
   The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program, administered within the Department of Homeland Security, is designed to provide resources for the firefighting and emergency response needs of fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical services organizations. The AFG program awards grants directly to local fire departments to support firefighters, first responders, and the services they deliver. 
   For more information on the program, please visit www.firegrantsupport.com/afg.
