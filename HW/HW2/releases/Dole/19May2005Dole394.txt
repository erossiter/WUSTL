SEN. DOLE ISSUES STATEMENT REGARDING MERGER OF U.S. AIRWAYS GROUP WITH AMERICA WEST HOLDINGS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today made the following statement regarding the merger of US Airways Group, Inc. and America West Holdings Corporation:  
   "I am pleased to see that US Airways appears to have found a way to emerge from bankruptcy and keep flying. I am also pleased to hear that as a result of this agreement, Charlotte will continue to serve as a hub for US Airways. The many folks working for US Airways have been through a very difficult time. I am glad that under this agreement there will be an emphasis on minimizing many employee dislocations. I look forward to learning more details as to how this merger will affect North Carolina and our nation's airline industry."
