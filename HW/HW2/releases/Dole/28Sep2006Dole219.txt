SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $400,000 GRANT FOR MARS HILL COLLEGE
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that Mars Hill College has been awarded a five year grant for $400,000 from the Strengthening Institutions Program at the U.S. Department of Education.  
   "North Carolina's colleges educate students to meet the challenges of new jobs and opportunities," said Senator Dole. "I commend Mars Hill College for securing this significant funding to better help students achieve their educational and career goals." 
   According to the Department of Education, the Strengthening Institutions Program provides grants to higher education institutions to improve academic quality, institutional management, and fiscal stability; to increase their self-sufficiency; and to strengthen their capacity to make a substantial contribution to the higher education resources of the nation.
