SEN. DOLE TO HOST SEMINAR FOR CONSTITUENTS ON ATTACKING IDENTITY THEFT
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole, a member of the Senate Banking Committee, and her staff will hold an "Attacking Identity Theft" consumer education seminar for constituents. The seminar will be held on Tuesday, October 11 at 9:00 a.m. at Jones Auditorium on the campus of Meredith College in Raleigh. For more information and registration, please view the attached PDF.
