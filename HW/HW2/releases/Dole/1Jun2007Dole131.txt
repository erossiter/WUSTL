SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $1 MILLION ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT INVESTMENT FOR TOWN OF HOLLY SPRINGS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced a $1 million Economic Development Administration (EDA) investment for the Town of Holly Springs. This funding will be used to make improvements to the town's wastewater system to support construction of a new manufacturing facility.  
   "It is vital that our communities have strong infrastructure to support and promote economic growth," said Dole. "This investment will help foster business development in the region, and I applaud the Town of Holly Springs for securing this funding." 
   According to the EDA, the project will create 258 new jobs and generate more than $61.96 million in private investment. For more information about this project, visit http://www.eda.gov.
