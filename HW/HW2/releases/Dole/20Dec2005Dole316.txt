SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $1 MILLION FOR NORTH CAROLINA RURAL ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT CENTER
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the North Carolina Rural Economic Development Center in Raleigh will receive a $1,000,000 grant from the U.S. Department of Commerce's Economic Development Administration (EDA).  
 This funding will be used for expansion of the Rural Center. 
   "Providing resources and advocating for rural development is critically important," said Dole. "I applaud North Carolina Rural Economic Development Center for securing this significant funding." 
   According to the EDA, this investment will help create 3,918 new jobs and generate $73 million in private investment. 
   The Rural Center serves North Carolina's 85 rural counties. For additional information, please visit www.ncruralcenter.org.
