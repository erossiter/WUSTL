SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $1,249,755 GRANT TO CITY OF HICKORY FOR AIRPORT IMPROVEMENTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the City of Hickory will receive a $1,249,755 grant for improvements to the Hickory Regional Airport. The funding is provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation through the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have adequate infrastructure," said Dole. "I commend the City of Hickory for securing these funds to make their airport as safe and efficient as possible."
   According to the FAA, the grant will be used to rehabilitate and strengthen a taxiway and fund an airport layout plan update.
