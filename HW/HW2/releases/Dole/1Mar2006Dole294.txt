ONSLOW COUNTY TO RECEIVE $327,750 GRANT FOR AIRPORT
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that Onslow County will receive a $327,750 grant from the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), part of the U.S. Department of Transportation. 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have proper infrastructure," said Senator Dole. "I applaud Onslow County for securing the funds to make their facility as safe and efficient as possible." 
   According to the FAA, the funds will be used to acquire land for airport development and to rehabilitate the runway.
