SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR SALISBURY AREA PROJECTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced the passage of the Omnibus Appropriations bill for Fiscal Year 2005. The $388 billion federal spending bill contains million of dollars designated for important projects in North Carolina, including $2 million for improving I-85 in Rowan County.  
   "I am pleased we were able to secure funding for widening I-85 in Rowan County," said Dole. "An efficient highway system is vital to a community's ability to support residents, sustain current industries and attract new businesses." 
   The Fiscal Year 2005 appropriations bill allocates funds to North Carolina for building and expanding vital infrastructure, establishing and growing educational programs and research, strengthening our law enforcement agencies, and improving health care facilities. Listed below are the appropriations for Rowan County and other large projects for North Carolina.
