SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR FAYETTEVILLE AREA PROJECTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced the passage of the Omnibus Appropriations bill for Fiscal Year 2005. The $388 billion federal spending bill contains millions of dollars designated for important projects in Robeson County and throughout North Carolina, including $1 million for improvements at the Lumberton Municipal Airport and $3 million for the US-74/Future Interstate 74 project.  
   "I am pleased we were able to secure funding for Robeson County, including $3 million to further progress on the Interstate-74 project." said Senator Dole. "The completion of this interstate will help spur economic development and will safely and efficiently connect the Southeastern portion of the state for commuters and businesses." 
   The Fiscal Year 2005 appropriations bill allocates funds to North Carolina for building and expanding vital infrastructure, establishing and growing educational programs and research, strengthening our law enforcement agencies, and improving health care facilities. Listed below are the appropriations for the Robeson County area and large projects for North Carolina.
