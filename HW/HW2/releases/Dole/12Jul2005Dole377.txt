SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $1.5 MILLION FOR BOILING SPRING LAKES
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that USDA Rural Development will loan $1,500,000 to the City of Boiling Spring Lakes. The funding will help construct a new 10,100-square foot city hall, which will house the city's municipal operations center and police department. 
   "It is critical that communities have strong infrastructure to support residents, sustain current industries and attract new businesses," said Senator Dole. "Additionally, it is important that the local government and police department have adequate facilities so that they can best serve the community. I applaud the City of Boiling Spring Lakes for securing this significant funding."
