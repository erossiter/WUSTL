FORSYTH TECHNICAL COMMUNITY COLLEGE TO RECEIVE $1.7 MILLION GRANT
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that Forsyth Technical Community College will receive a grant from the U.S. Department of Education (DoE) for $1,788,220 over five years. The funding is awarded from DoE's Strengthening Institutions Program and will be used for the college's academic advising program. 
   "North Carolina's community colleges educate and train the local workforce to meet the challenges of new jobs and opportunities," said Senator Dole. "I commend Forsyth Tech for securing this significant funding to improve its academic advising system to better help students achieve their educational and career goals."
