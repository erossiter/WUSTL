CITY OF CONCORD RECEIVES $1 MILLION GRANT FROM ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT ADMINISTRATION
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced that the City of Concord will receive a $1 million grant from the Economic Development Administration, part of the United States Department of Commerce.  
   The grant, which will help create 300 short-term jobs and generate $50 million in private investment, will be used for infrastructure improvements to support the development of a hotel and convention center located near Interstate-85. 
   "Projects that promote investment, infrastructure improvements and job creation are important to helping the North Carolina economy continue to recover and grow," said Senator Dole. "I commend the City of Concord for its successful effort to secure this significant funding for this project."
