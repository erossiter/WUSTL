SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $3,121,495 GRANT TO ONSLOW COUNTY FOR AIRPORT IMPROVEMENTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that Onslow County will receive a $3,121,495 grant for improvements to the Albert J. Ellis Airport. The funding is provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation through the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have adequate infrastructure," said Dole. "I commend Onslow County for securing these funds to make their airport as safe and efficient as possible."
   According to the FAA, the grant will be used to purchase safety equipment, improve the airport's terminal building, rehabilitate a runway and help construct a new sewer system.
