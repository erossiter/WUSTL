SEN. DOLE APPLAUDS SENATE CONFIRMATION OF JUDGE ALITO
 Sen. Elizabeth Dole today made the following statement about the Senate confirmation of Samuel Alito as Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court:  
   "I am very pleased that the Senate today confirmed Samuel Alito as Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court. His judicial record and Senate testimony demonstrate his strong respect for the Constitution and the rule of law, as well as his commitment to hear all sides of an argument with an open-mind - impartiality and fairness to all parties, big or small, powerful or powerless. Samuel Alito was among the most qualified Supreme Court nominees in history. A person of his intellect, qualifications, and integrity is precisely the type of person America needs on the Supreme Court."
