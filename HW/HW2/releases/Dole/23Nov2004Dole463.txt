SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR KINSTON AREA PROJECTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced the passage of the Omnibus Appropriations bill for Fiscal Year 2005. The $388 billion federal spending bill contains thousands of dollars designated for important projects in North Carolina, including $240,000 for Lenoir Community College.  
   "I am pleased we were able to secure funding for Lenoir Community College to train closed captioning writers." said Dole. "This is a shining example of our community colleges preparing the local workforce for highly specialized, highly skilled careers." 
   The Fiscal Year 2005 appropriations bill allocates funds to North Carolina for building and expanding vital infrastructure, establishing and growing educational programs and research, strengthening our law enforcement agencies, and improving health care facilities. Listed below are the appropriations for Kinston and other large projects for North Carolina. 
   Kinston Projects 
   Lenoir Community College $240,000 
  This will fund a specialized training program for closed captioning writers.

It is composed of the textile departments of Auburn, Clemson, Cornell, Georgia Tech, UMASS-Dartmouth, NC State, Philadelphia University, and UC-Davis.
