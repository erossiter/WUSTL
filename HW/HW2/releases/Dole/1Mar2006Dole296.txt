CHARLOTTE/DOUGLAS INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT TO RECEIVE MORE THAN $8.3 MILLION
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the city of Charlotte will receive a grant of $8,381,664 for Charlotte/Douglas International Airport. The funding is provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation through the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA).  
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have adequate infrastructure," said Senator Dole. "I commend Charlotte for securing these funds to make their facility as safe and efficient as possible." 
   According to the FAA, this funding will be used to acquire land for airport development, which includes a third parallel runway.
