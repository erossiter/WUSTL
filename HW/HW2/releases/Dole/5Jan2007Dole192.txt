SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $4 MILLION LOAN FOR WEST CARTERET WATER CORPORATION
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that USDA Rural Development has approved a loan of $4,000,000 for West Carteret Water Corporation, Inc.  
   "It is so important that communities have the infrastructure they need to support residents, sustain current industries and attract new businesses," said Dole. "This project will better serve the water needs of the people in Carteret County and help spur economic development." 
   According to USDA Rural Development, this loan will be used to make various improvements to the water system and bring it into compliance with the Safe Drinking Water Act. The project will add an additional 675 residential users to the West Carteret system.
