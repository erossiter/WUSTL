SEN. DOLE APPLAUDS BRAC COMMISSION DECISION TO KEEP JOBS AT ARMY RESEARCH OFFICE IN DURHAM
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today applauded the decision by the Base Realignment and Closure Commission to strike the Army Research Office in Durham from the realignment list. As a result of this decision, 114 jobs will be saved at this facility. 
   "I am very pleased the Commission recognized the tremendous value of the Army Research Office in Durham," said Senator Dole. "As I have reiterated to the Commission, the strong relationships this facility has with Research Triangle Park and the top research universities in the region are irreplaceable."
