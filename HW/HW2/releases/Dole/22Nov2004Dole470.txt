SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR LAURINBURG AREA PROJECTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced the passage of the Omnibus Appropriations bill for Fiscal Year 2005. The $388 billion federal spending bill contains thousands of dollars designated for important projects in North Carolina, including $50,000 for Scotland County HOSTS Program.  
   "I am pleased we were able to secure funding for the HOSTS program," said Senator Dole. "This mentoring and tutoring program is an invaluable resource for students in Scotland County." 
   The Fiscal Year 2005 appropriations bill allocates funds to North Carolina for building and expanding vital infrastructure, establishing and growing educational programs and research, strengthening our law enforcement agencies, and improving health care facilities. Listed below are the appropriations for the Laurinburg area and large projects for North Carolina. 
   Laurinburg Area Projects 
   Scotland County HOSTS Program- $50,000 
  Funds will be used for computer software, reference materials, literature and teacher salaries in the Scotland County school district.
