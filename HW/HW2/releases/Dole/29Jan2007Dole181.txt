SEN. DOLE ISSUES STATEMENT ON NORTH CAROLINA'S DECISION TO PURCHASE CHIMNEY ROCK PARK
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today made the following statement regarding the announcement that North Carolina will purchase Chimney Rock Park: 
   "I celebrate with the people of North Carolina that Chimney Rock Park will be preserved for future generations to enjoy. I saw firsthand from the many letters and phone calls to my western office how important this natural treasure is to our state. I applaud the State of North Carolina on its decision to acquire the park for addition to the Hickory Nut Gorge State Park, and I congratulate the area officials, organizations and generous donors who made this project possible."
