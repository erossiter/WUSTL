SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $4,190,868 GRANT TO BUNCOMBE COUNTY, CITY OF ASHEVILLE FOR AIRPORT IMPROVEMENTS
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that Buncombe County and the City of Asheville will receive a $4,190,868 grant for improvements to the Asheville Regional Airport. The funding is provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation through the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have adequate infrastructure," said Dole. "I commend Buncombe County and the City of Asheville for securing these funds to make their airport as safe and efficient as possible."
   According to the FAA, the funding will be used for improvements to the airport's terminal and access road.
