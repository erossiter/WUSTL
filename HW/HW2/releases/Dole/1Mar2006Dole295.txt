FAYETTEVILLE REGIONAL AIRPORT TO RECEIVE $500,000 GRANT
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the city of Fayetteville will receive a $500,000 grant for the Fayetteville Regional Airport. The funds are provided by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), part of the U.S. Department of Transportation.  
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have proper infrastructure," said Senator Dole. "I applaud Fayetteville for securing these funds to make their facility as safe and efficient as possible." 
   According to the FAA, the funding will be used to rehabilitate the regional fire training facility.
