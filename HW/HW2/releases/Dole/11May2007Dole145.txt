SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $950,000 GRANT TO PIEDMONT TRIAD AIRPORT AUTHORITY
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the Piedmont Triad Airport Authority will receive a $950,000 grant for improvements to Piedmont Triad International Airport. The funding is provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation through the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have adequate infrastructure," said Dole. "I commend the Piedmont Triad Airport Authority for securing these funds to make their airport as safe and efficient as possible."
   According to the FAA, the funding will be used to improve the airport's runway safety area.
