SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $100,000 FOR BELHAVEN HARBOR STUDY
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers has allocated $100,000 in fiscal year 2006 funding for the Belhaven Harbor Environmental Improvements project feasibility study. Dole advocated for this funding, and her representative has met with the mayor of Belhaven, local economic development officials, and the Corps about this project. 
   "Improving the Belhaven Harbor breakwater structure would benefit the local economy and environment," said Dole. "I am very pleased that the Army Corps of Engineers recognizes the need for this feasibility study."
   Background:
   The feasibility study for the Belhaven Harbor Environmental Improvements project will investigate the modification and improvement of the existing breakwater structure to provide greater benefits to the environment and estuarine habitat.
