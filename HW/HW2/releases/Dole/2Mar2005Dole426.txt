CRAVEN COUNTY REGIONAL AIRPORT TO RECEIVE $900,125 GRANT
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole announced that Craven County Regional Airport will receive a $900,125 grant from the Federal Aviation Administration, part of the United States Department of Transportation.  
 The funds will be used for the rehabilitation of the taxiway, the purchase of an aircraft rescue and fire-fighting vehicle, and other purposes. 
   "As a former Secretary of Transportation, I know how important it is for airports to have the proper infrastructure," said Senator Dole. "I applaud the Craven County Regional Airport for securing the funds to make their facility as safe and efficient as possible." 
   Additionally, the funding will allow the airport to conduct a study to identify areas in need of further maintenance and conduct other general improvements.
