SEN. DOLE PRAISES CHARLOTTE'S SUCCESSFUL BID FOR NASCAR HALL OF FAME
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today made the following statement applauding the announcement of Charlotte as the site for the NASCAR Hall of Fame:  
   "Today is a landmark day for Charlotte and for NASCAR. I applaud the many local leaders who worked so hard to bring the Hall of Fame to our great state. 
   "NASCAR and its surrounding industries have tremendous economic impact on North Carolina, and I am very supportive of increasing its presence in our state--for the jobs that depend on motorsports and the fans who enjoy it."
