SEN. DOLE ISSUES STATEMENT ON GROUNDBREAKING OF HONDAJET
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today made the following statement on the groundbreaking of HondaJet:  
   "Today's groundbreaking for Honda Aircraft Company at PTI is a landmark event for our state and our economy. This project is a shining example of the new opportunities and jobs that are being created in North Carolina's economic transition. No question this is an exciting yet challenging time for our state, and it is my top priority to do everything possible to ensure a future that is more prosperous and more secure for every North Carolinian. 
   "In addition, I applaud Honda's commitment to build an environmentally responsible and energy efficient facility. This is a forward-thinking approach that we should encourage businesses around our state and nation to imitate."
