SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES MORE THAN $300 MILLION FOR NORTH CAROLINA ELECTRIC COOPERATIVES
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced that USDA Rural Development will loan $306,147,000 to the North Carolina Electric Membership Corporation (NCEMC).  
 This significant funding will benefit the customers of the state's electric cooperatives by providing with them an economical and reliable source of power. 
   "It is critical that our electric cooperatives are able to efficiently and affordably meet the energy needs of their customers across our state," said Senator Dole. "This funding is a significant investment in our state's energy infrastructure, and I applaud NCEMC for its efforts to secure this grant." 
   According to USDA Rural Development, the loan will finance the construction of 620 megawatts of combustion turbine generating stations (CT projects). The CT projects consist of 11 Pratt & Whitney Power Systems FT-8 Swift-Pac units. Each Swift-Pac unit consists of two CT's and one electrical generator. Six units will be constructed in Anson County and five in Richmond County.
