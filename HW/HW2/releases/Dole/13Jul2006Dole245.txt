SEN. DOLE ANNOUNCES $630,000 ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT INVESTMENT FOR WILSON COUNTY
  Sen. Elizabeth Dole today announced a $630,000 Economic Development Administration (EDA) investment for Wilson County and the Upper Coastal Plain Development Corporation in Rocky Mount. This funding will be used renovate a mixed-use business incubator in downtown Wilson. 
   "It is vital that our communities have strong infrastructure to support and promote economic development," said Dole. "This investment will help foster business development in the region, and I applaud Wilson County and the Upper Coastal Plain Development Corporation for securing this funding."
   According to the EDA, the project will create 100 new jobs and generate $3 million in private investment. For more information about this project, visit http://www.eda.gov.
