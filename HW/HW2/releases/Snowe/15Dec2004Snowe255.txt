SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES EXPANDED COMMUNICATIONS TEAM
  Today Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) announced that Preston Hartman will now serve as Press Secretary in her Washington, D.C. office. Preston, who previously handled communications for U.S. Rep. Cass Ballenger (R-NC) joins Communications Director Antonia Ferrier in a reorganized press shop. Rounding out the communications team, recent hire Meghan Nutting will serve as Press Assistant. 
   "I need a competent, conscientious communications team to ensure that my constituents in Maine understand what I am doing in Washington, D.C.," said Snowe. "I am confident that the staff I have put into place will do an excellent job."
