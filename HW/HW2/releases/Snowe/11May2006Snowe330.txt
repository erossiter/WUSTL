SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $212,039 FROM U.S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) has awarded $212,039 to three groups in Maine.  
   "The USDA provides funding for essential research and project implementation throughout Maine," said Snowe and Collins in a joint statement. "Their recent grant awards will support rainbow smelt research, help to develop a lobster branding strategy, and allow the implementation of a groundbreaking business model for successful aging." 
   Harmon Brook Farm (Maine Smelt Hatchery) in Canaan has received $52,084 for a project entitled "Establish Effective Feeding and Harvest Protocol for Rainbow Smelt." 
   Saltwater Marketing, LLC in Portland has received $80,000 for a project to develop a lobster branding strategy that leverages Maine's quality reputation and exploits growing demand for ready to prepare meals. 
   DBA Global Wellness in Brunswick has received $79,955 to implement a business model for productive successful aging in place in rural Maine.
