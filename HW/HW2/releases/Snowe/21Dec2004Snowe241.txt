DEATHS OF 2 MAINE SOLDIERS CONFIRMED
  After receiving word confirming the deaths of two Maine Army National Guardsmen in Mosul, Iraq today, Sen.  
 Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement: 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the families and loved ones of the two brave Maine soldiers who died today fighting for their country in Iraq. We will forever remember the extraordinary sacrifice and courage they exhibited. 
   "To the troops from Maine and throughout America who are serving bravely and honorably, they continue to have our unwavering support. We must ensure that they have the resources they need to perform their duties and return home safely. 
   "I will continue to be in close contact with Adjutant General Libby of the Maine Army National Guard and officials at the Department of Defense, and I stand ready to assist the families in any way possible."
