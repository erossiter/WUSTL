SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $879,604 IN FUNDING TO MAINE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES
  Sens. Olympia Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) has awarded the Maine Department of Human Services in Augusta $879,604 for maternal and child health services.  
 The funds where allocated through Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) of HHS. 
   "This critical funding will give the Maine Department of Human Services an opportunity to do even more to promote and improve the health and well-being of Maine's mothers and children," Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. 
   The Maternal and Child Health Bureau (MCHB) of HRSA helps to promote and improve the health, safety, and well-being of mothers and children. MCHB works in partnership with States, communities, public-private partners, and families to improve the quality and availability of local maternal and child health services.
