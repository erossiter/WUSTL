SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES GRANT FOR ELLSWORTH FIRE DEPARTMENT
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Me) announced today that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security's Federal Emergency Management Agency in cooperation with the U.S. Fire Administration (USFA) has awarded $105,425 to the Ellsworth Fire Department as a part of the Staffing for Adequate Fire Emergency Response (SAFER) program. Funds under the SAFER program are intended to support the hiring of firefighters and the recruitment and retention of volunteer firefighters. 
   "We can never underestimate the constant challenges that our fire safety personnel face every day," Senator Snowe said. "The funds from the SAFER program will help Ellsworth fire officials strengthen their readiness, enhance preparedness and improve their ability to address fire disasters."
