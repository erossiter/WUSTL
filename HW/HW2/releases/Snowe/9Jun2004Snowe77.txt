SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES CONSTITUENT HOURS IN STANDISH, BRIDGTON ON JUNE 16
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today announced that her Maine staff will hold Constituent Office Hours in the communities of Standish and Bridgton on Wednesday, June 16th.  
   Residents of Cumberland County are encouraged to meet with Snowe's representatives to discuss specific problems, legislative issues, or any concerns regarding the federal government. For more information, call Senator Snowe's Portland State Office at 874-0883 or toll-free in Maine at 1-800-432-1599. 
   Office hours in Standish and Bridgton will be as follows:

  175 Northeast Road 
  10:30 am to 12:00 noon 
   Town of Bridgton - Town Office 
  3 Chase Street, Suite 1 
  2:00 pm to 3:30 pm
   Contact: Antonia Ferrier, 202/224-5344.
