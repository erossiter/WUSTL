SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES $77 MILLION CONTRACT FOR DDG 51, FFG 7 CLASS INTEGRATED PLANNING YARD SERVICES
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today announced that the United States Navy has awarded Bath Iron Works (BIW) in Bath, Maine a contract valued at almost $77 million to extend DDG 51 and FFG 7 Class Integrated Planning Yard Services.  
   "Bath Iron Works is known for its premiere shipbuilding and I am so pleased to announce this award for the men and women who have so diligently upheld this tradition," said Senator Snowe. "BIW has been performing the highest quality work and because of this contract our shipbuilders will continue to showcase their excellent workmanship."
