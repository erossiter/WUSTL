SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $113,000 FEDERAL GRANT, $357,000 LOAN FOR NEWPORT PUBLIC LIBRARY ASSOCIATION
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the Newport Public Library Association has received a federal grant in the amount of $113,000 and a loan in the amount of $357,000 from the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program. These funds will be used to help construct a 10,640 square foot cultural center on Main Street in Newport. The new facility will include a library, public meeting spaces, and will provide activities specifically for children. 
   "The Newport Cultural Center promises to provide residents with an array of educational, historic, and cultural services," said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement. "This funding will help the Center enhance the quality of life in the Newport community."
   According to the USDA, the Newport Cultural Center will employ four people.
