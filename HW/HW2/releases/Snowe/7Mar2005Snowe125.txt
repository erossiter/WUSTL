SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF EPA ADMINISTRATOR
  Today Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement on the nomination of Stephen Johnson to be Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrator:  
   "With his 24 years of service in the EPA, Stephen Johnson possesses a wealth of experience. I look forward to his confirmation hearing, where we will have the opportunity to learn more about his vision and priorities for the EPA. 
   "I will ensure that Mr. Johnson understands my concerns and Maine's most pressing environmental issues, including air pollution and mercury emissions. Reducing these emissions - as well as addressing climate change and health problems caused by pollution - is a goal we should all share."
