SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $150,000 IN FUNDING TO COASTAL ENTERPRISE OF WISCASSET
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Small Business Association has awarded $150,000 to Coastal Enterprise, Inc. of Wiscasset. As a part of the Women's Business Center program, the grant will offer counseling and training assistance and help remove any discriminatory barriers that are encountered by women in accessing capital and promoting their businesses.  
   "These funds will help Coastal Enterprise, Inc. better serve female, small business owners in the Lincoln County area," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "It is imperative that we help break down any barriers that keep women from expanding or building their businesses." 
   Under the U.S. Small Business Administration, the Women's Business Center Program offers funding to private, nonprofit organizations that help assist, through training and counseling, the small business concerns owned and controlled by women.
