COAST GUARD MOVING 22 JOBS FROM SOUTHWEST HARBOR
  Today Sen. Olympia J. Snowe, who chairs the Senate Subcommittee on Fisheries and the Coast Guard, released the following statement on the Coast Guard's decision to relocate 22 positions from Southwest Harbor:  
   "I am hopeful that the relocation of these 22 positions will not adversely impact the affected communities, and I stand ready to help I any way I can. The number of positions in Maine will remain the same, and the Coast Guard has assured me that there will be no degradation in services. I will continue to closely monitor Coast Guard operations in Southwest Harbor to ensure that it maintains its high level of performance.".
