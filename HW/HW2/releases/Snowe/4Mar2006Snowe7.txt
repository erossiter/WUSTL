SEN. SNOWE TRAVELS TO IRAQ THIS WEEKEND
  As a member of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence, Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) traveled to Iraq this weekend to meet with senior U.S. and Iraqi officials to learn more about the situation on the ground as the country makes the transition to self-governance. 
   Sen. Snowe is expected to meet with members of the Maine Army National Guard's 152nd Maintenance Company, which was deployed in January 2005 in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom.
