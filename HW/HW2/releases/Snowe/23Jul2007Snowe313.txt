SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF ARMY PFC. JASON DORE OF MOSCOW
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today issued the following statement on the recent death of Army Pfc. Jason Dore, 25, of Moscow who was killed Sunday in Baghdad, Iraq:  
   "Today our state mourns the loss of Jason Dore, who bravely gave his life to protect our country," said Senator Snowe. "Our nation will forever be indebted to Jason for his tremendous sacrifice and courageous service. I extend my heartfelt condolences to his family and loved ones at this difficult time." 
   Pfc. Jason Dore was assigned to the 2nd Battalion, 5th Cavalry Regiment, 1st Brigade Combat Team, 1st Cavalry Division, Fort Hood, Texas. He died Sunday after sustaining wounds from an improvised explosive device.
