SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE TRADE ADJUSTMENT ASSISTANCE FOR SAN ANTONIO SHOES WORKERS IN PITTSFIELD
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins (R-ME) announced today that the U.S. Department of Labor has certified Trade Adjustment Assistance for San Antonio Shoes (SAS) Pittsfield, Inc. employees. All workers of SAS Pittsfield, Inc., who became totally or partially separated from employment on or after February 15, 2008, are eligible to apply for the assistance. Workers can access these benefits by going to their local one-stop career center. The easiest way to locate the closest one-stop is by going to www.servicelocator.org and entering their zip code. 
   "Employees affected by this layoff now face an uncertain future, through no fault of their own," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "We will continue our efforts to ensure that they receive every available resource they need to help them move forward and become gainfully employed once again."
