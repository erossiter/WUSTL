SEN. SNOWE MOURNS PASSING OF LUCIEN 'BABE' DUTREMBLE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today expressed her sympathy to the family and friends of Lucien "Babe" Dutremble, who passed away this morning. Snowe issued the following statement: 
   "Jock and I were saddened to learn of Babe's passing, and we offer our profound sympathy to his family and friends at this time of sorrow. Babe was a dedicated public servant who, by his efforts and commitment, improved the lives of the people of Biddeford and York County. His devotion to the community continued long after retirement, and he inspired younger Mainers to follow in his footsteps."
