SEN. SNOWE REMEMBERS LEGACY OF EUGENE MAWHINNEY
  An alumnus of the University of Maine, Sen. Olympia J. Snowe today mourned the passing Eugene Mawhinney who was a University of Maine professor for 31 years. 
   Snowe issued the following statement today:
   "Eugene Mawhinney was an institution and a treasure at the University of Maine. He was a man of tremendous knowledge who was held in the highest esteem by everyone who had the privileged of knowing him. I send my deepest condolences to his friends, family and the entire University of Maine family during this most difficult time."
