SEN. SNOWE STAFF TO HOLD OFFICE HOURS IN MACHIAS ON JUNE 14
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today announced that her Maine Staff Assistant, Andrew Varisco will hold office outreach hours at the Machias Town Hall, next Thursday, June 14, 2007.  
   Office outreach hours are an opportunity for area residents to meet with Andrew to discuss specific problems, legislative issues or any concerns regarding the federal government. For more information, please call Senator Snowe's Bangor office at 207-945-0432 or toll free at 1-800-432-1599 .
