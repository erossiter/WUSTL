SEN. SNOWE STATEMENT ON DEATH IN IRAQ OF SERGEANT COREY DAN OF NORWAY, MAINE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today expressed her deepest sympathy to the family and friends of Sergeant Corey Dan of Norway. Corey died in Iraq on March 13.  
   "I was deeply saddened to learn of the death of Corey Dan as he served his country in Iraq," said Snowe. "My thoughts and prayers go out to his family and loved ones who are suffering through this difficult time. Our gratitude for his sacrifice and his selfless commitment to protecting our freedom will never be forgotten." 
   Sergeant Corey Dan, a member of the101st Airborne Division, was stationed at Fort Campbell, Kentucky. His parents reside in Norway, Maine.
