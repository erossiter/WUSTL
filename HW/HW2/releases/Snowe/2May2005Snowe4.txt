SEN. SNOWE MEETS WITH SMALL BUSINESS HONOREES
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) met recently with Maine small business honorees - Marianne Sensale-Guerin, winner of Maine Small Business Person of the Year; Cheryl Cerrier, Women's Business Award winner; and Michaella Cavallaro, Editor of Maine Biz, Maine 2005 Small Business Journalist of the Year, New England Regional Small Business Journalist of the Year, and U.S. Small Business Administration's (SBA) National Small Business Journalist of the Year for 2005.  
   "It was an honor to welcome three of Maine's finest small business leaders to my office in Washington, D.C.," said Snowe. "As Chair of the Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship, I am especially proud of their impressive accomplishments. Their exemplary work in the realm of small businesses can serve as a model for others working to start, sustain, and expand their businesses."
