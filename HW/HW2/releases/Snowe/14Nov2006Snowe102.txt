SEN. SNOWE MOURNS LOSS OF SERGEANT WILLIAM JACKSON IN IRAQ
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the death of Sergeant William Samuel Jackson II: 
   "I would like to extend my deepest condolences to the friends and family of Sergeant William Samuel Jackson who paid the ultimate sacrifice for his nation. We must never forget the tremendous service of our servicemen and women like Sergeant Jackson.".
