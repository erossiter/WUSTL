SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF EDWARD GODFREY
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe issued the following statement today on the passing of Edward Godfrey, the founding Dean of the University of Maine's School of Law and former Justice on Maine's Supreme Court:  
   "Today, Maine mourns the loss of Ed Godfrey - a man committed to the State and people of Maine. His service as the founding Dean of the University of Maine School Of Law and a Justice on the State's Supreme Court is a hallmark of his dedication to our legal system and all that it represents. Jock and I send our condolences to the entire Godfrey family and those who had the privilege of working with him over his truly remarkable life during this most difficult time."
