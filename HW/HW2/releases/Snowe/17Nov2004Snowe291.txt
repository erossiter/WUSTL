SEN. SNOWE COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF MARGARET SPELLING TO SERVE AS SECRETARY OF EDUCATION
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the nomination by President Bush of Margaret Spelling to Secretary of Education: 
   "I commend the President for selecting Margaret Spelling to serve as the Secretary of Education, and believe she has the experience and knowledge essential to continuing our nation down the path of excellence in our schools. Margaret Spelling knows how to bridge the partisan divide - an essential component to improving education for all of our children."
