SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF SOLIDER WITH TIES TO MAINE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today mourned the loss of Army Sgt. Joel A. House, who died as a result of an IED attack in Iraq on Saturday. Sgt. House's parents, Paul and Deanna, live in Lee.  
   "It is tremendously sad and tragic that another family from Maine is facing the loss of a loved one in this war," Senator Snowe said. "I join my fellow Mainers in offering my heartfelt prayers and condolences to Sgt. House's parents who live in Lee and hope that they know that their son's bravery and sacrifice will never be forgotten by our state and nation."
