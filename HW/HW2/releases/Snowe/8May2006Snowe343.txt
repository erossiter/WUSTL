SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE AGENCY DIRECTOR PORTER GOSS
  Sen. Olympia Snowe (R-Maine), a member of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence, today issued the following statement on the resignation of Central Intelligence Agency Director Porter Goss:  
   "I would like to thank Porter Goss for years of selfless service to his country and I commend him for his dedication and hard work. I have known Representative Goss since he first came to the U.S. House of Representatives in 1988, where I served with him for six years. I wish Porter well in his future endeavors. 
   "This surprise announcement comes at a critical time of transition for the CIA. The President must select a candidate for director who can bring stability and increase morale at the agency, and I look forward to working with him in the weeks to come."
