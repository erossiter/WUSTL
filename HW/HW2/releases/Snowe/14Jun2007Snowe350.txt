SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE U.S. NAVY SHIP VISITS
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that four U.S. Navy ships are scheduled to visit Maine this summer during various festivals. Sens. Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins formally requested the visits in a letter to the Navy.  
   "We are so pleased that these naval ships will grace our harbors this summer," said Senators Snowe and Collins. "We look forward to welcoming them to our treasured Maine festivals to help celebrate our state's proud shipbuilding history." 
   Schedule of festivals and ship visits: 
   Boothbay Harbor Windjammer Fest 25-29 June USS JOHN L. HALL 
   Eastport July 4th, 1-6 July USS MCFAUL 
   East Boothbay Boat Builders 26-30 July USS ELROD 
   Rockland Lobster Fest 2-6 August USS NASSAU.
