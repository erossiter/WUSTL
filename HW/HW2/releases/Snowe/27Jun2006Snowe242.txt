SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF HARRY VOSE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the passing of Harry Vose: 
   "Traveling to Eastport to march in the Fourth of July parade was how I came to know Harry Vose. Over the years, I came to respect him greatly for his remarkable service representing Washington County in the state legislature. He was a man of tremendous character and indomitable spirit - I think I speak for the many throughout our state who knew him in saying that he will be sorely missed. I would like to extend my deepest condolences to his friends and family during this most difficult time."
