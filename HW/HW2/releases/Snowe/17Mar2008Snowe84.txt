SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCES TRADE ADJUSTMENT ASSISTANCE FOR WESTPOINT HOMES, BED DIVISION, EMPLOYEES IN BIDDEFORD
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins (R-Maine) announced today that the U.S. Department of Labor has certified Trade Adjustment Assistance for WestPoint Homes, Inc., Bed Division, employees in Biddeford, Maine. All workers of WestPoint Homes, Inc., Bed Division, who became totally or partially separated from employment on or after December 6, 2007, are eligible to apply for the assistance. Workers can access these benefits by going to their local one-stop career center. The easiest way to locate the closest one-stop is by going to www.servicelocator.org and entering their zip code. 
   "Our troubled economy has had a devastating impact on a countless number of Mainers who, through no fault of their own, have lost their jobs," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "We need to provide them with every measure of federal assistance available so that they can swiftly transition into a new job that will help sustain their quality of life."
