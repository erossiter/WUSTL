SEN. SNOWE MOURNS LOSS OF MARINE ANGEL ROSA
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) on Friday morning mourned the loss of 21 year-old Marine Private First Class Angel Rosa of South Portland, who was killed Tuesday during a combat mission in Iraq. Rosa's wife and family live in South Portland.  
   "Any time we lose one of our own, it is a terribly sad day for our state and our nation," said Senator Snowe. "Like so many before him, PFC Angel Rosa courageously answered the call of duty and his heroism will always be remembered by our country. My heartfelt thoughts and prayers are with his wife, family and friends during this difficult time."
