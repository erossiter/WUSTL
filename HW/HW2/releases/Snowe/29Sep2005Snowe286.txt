SEN. SNOWE MEETS WITH WOMEN LEGISLATORS LOBBY
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) met today with Women Legislators Lobby participants Marilyn Canavan, Mary Cathcart, Lillian O'Brien, Elaine Makas, Lauren Maloney, Ann Naimie, Susan Shaer, and Janet Mills. The women came to Washington, D.C. to discuss issues of foreign policy and international security with Sen. Snowe. 
   "It is always a pleasure to meet with other legislators from the state of Maine," said Snowe. "I share many of the concerns of the women in the Women Legislators' Lobby and look forward to addressing the issues we discussed."
