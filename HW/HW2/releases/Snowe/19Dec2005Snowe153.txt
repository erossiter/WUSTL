SEN. SNOWE EXPRESSES SYMPATHY FOR DEATH OF SOLDIER FROM MAINE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today expressed her deepest sympathy to the family and friends of Joseph Lucas, a soldier from the 3rd Infantry Division, who was fatally wounded in Balad, Iraq yesterday by an Improvised Explosive Device (IED).  
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the family and loved ones of Joseph Lucas, who died fighting for his country in Iraq. We can never sufficiently express our gratitude for his sacrifice, and his courage forever be remembered. I stand ready to assist Joseph's family in any way," said Snowe. 
   Joseph Alan Lucas was a Specialist with A Troop, 5-7 Armor Squadron, 3rd Infantry Division. He grew up in Monson, Maine and lived in Georgia with his wife Heather and his 15 month old son, Joseph A. Lucas, Jr. He was 23 years old.
