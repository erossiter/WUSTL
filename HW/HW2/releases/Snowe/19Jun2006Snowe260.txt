SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $62,000 IN ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FUNDS FOR MAINE
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Department of Commerce (USDOC) Economic Development Administration (EDA) has awarded $62,000 in investments to the Eastern Maine Development Corporation in Bangor. 
   "The U.S. Department of Commerce Economic Development Administration investments provide vital financial support for rural Mainers to boost economic growth," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "We applaud this group for its initiative and commitment to improving the lives of the people in Eastern Maine."
   This funding will be used to support long-term regional economic development planning efforts initiated by the Eastern Maine Development Corporation.
   U.S. Department of Commerce Economic Development Administration investments are focused on locally-developed, regionally-based economic development initiatives that achieve the highest return on the taxpayers' investment and that directly contribute to economic growth.
