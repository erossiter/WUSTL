SEN. SNOWE MEETS WITH STUDENTS FROM FOREST HILLS HIGH SCHOOL
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) met Wednesday with students from Forest Hills High School in Jackman, Maine.  
 The students, led by teachers Harald Moore and Theresa Crawford, were visiting Washington, D.C. on a school trip. 
   "It is always a pleasure to meet with school groups that come down to D.C. and visit my office," said Snowe. "I am excited that these students from Forest Hills High School had the opportunity to experience such a historic city." 
   The students that attended were Tim Gilboe, Sean Dubois, Clay Hoyt, Ashley Krukowski, Tara Achey, Melissa Brown, Sam Holbrook, Josh Mulhall, and Stephanie Bauer.
