SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES NEW LEGISLATIVE DIRECTOR
  Today Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) announced that Ray Krncevic has been promoted to Legislative Director and Counsel.  
 He has been serving as legislative counsel in, handling telecommunications and other Commerce Committee matters, as well as judiciary and election law issues. Prior to joining Sen. Snowe's office, Mr. Krncevic practiced law as a civil litigator for five years in the private sector, specializing in commercial, telecommunications, labor, and environmental law. A native of Cleveland, Ohio, Mr. Krncevic is a graduate of Northwestern University and the University of Virginia School of Law.
