MAINE TROOPS MAY BE INVOLVED IN ROCKET ATTACK IN MOSUL
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe has received information this morning that the rocket attack in Mosul, Iraq struck a dining facility used by the 133rd Engineering Battalion of Maine and other units. Sen.  
 Snowe issued the following statement: 
   "I am in close contact with Adjutant General Libby of the Maine National Guard in order to ascertain more complete information surrounding this attack, and I will work with him to provide any assistance that may be necessary. All our thoughts and prayers go out to the soldiers and families of the 133rd Engineering Battalion and all those troops in harm's way at this difficult time."
