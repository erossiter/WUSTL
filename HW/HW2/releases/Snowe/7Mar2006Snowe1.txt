SEN. SNOWE COMMENTS ON PASSING OF PETER WILEY
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today expressed her condolences to the friends and family of Peter Wiley, Rep. Tom Allen's Chief of Staff, who passed away this morning. Snowe issued the following statement:
   "I would like to extend my deepest condolences to Peter's friends and loved ones during this most difficult time. Peter was an admirable and hardworking man who served Congressman Allen with excellence.".
