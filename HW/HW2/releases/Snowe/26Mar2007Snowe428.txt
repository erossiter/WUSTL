SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF LIEUTENANT COLONEL JAY ZEAMER
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) on Monday, March 26, 2007, paid tribute to Maine 's last surviving Medal of Honor Recipient, Lieutenant Colonel Jay Zeamer of Boothbay Harbor , who passed away Thursday.  
   "More than sixty years ago, Jay Zeamer received the highest military honor for his heroic and noble efforts to save the lives of his comrades during a mission in World War II," said Senator Snowe. "As a great son of Maine and genuine American hero, he shall always be remembered for his remarkable courage and enduring service on behalf of a grateful nation. I extend my condolences to his family and loved ones during this difficult time." 
   In honor of Lieutenant Colonel Jay Zeamer and his status as a Medal of Honor Recipient, Governor John Baldacci had directed that the United States flag and the State of Maine flag be flown at half staff from sunrise to sunset on Monday, March 26, 2007.
