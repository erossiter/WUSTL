MAINE CONGRESSIONAL DELEGATION MEETS WITH STATE DIRECTOR OF HEALTH POLICY AND FINANCE
  The Maine Congressional Delegation met today with Trish Riley, the Director of the State of Maine Office of Health Policy and Finance.  
 The Delegation discussed priorities for Maine in the President's budget proposal for Fiscal Year 2006. 
   "It is important that we maintain a close working relationship with the Governor's Office in order to coordinate our efforts to best serve Maine interests at the Federal and State level," said the Congressional delegation in a joint statement. "We pledge that we will work together to preserve federal support for certain health programs and other public services that are so important to the health and well-being of the people of Maine." 
   Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins and Representatives Tom Allen and Michael Michaud all took part in the meeting this afternoon on Capitol Hill.
