SEN. SNOWE STAFF TO HOLD OFFICE HOURS IN EASTPORT ON MAY 10
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today announced office outreach hours at the Eastport City Hall on Tuesday, May 10, 2005 from 2:00 to 3:00 p.m. with members of her staff. 
   Office outreach hours are an opportunity for area residents to discuss specific problems, legislative issues or any concerns regarding the federal government. For more information, please call Senator Snowe's Bangor Office at 1-800-432-1599. 
   Office hours are scheduled as follows:

   Eastport City Hall

   2:00 PM - 3:00 PM.
