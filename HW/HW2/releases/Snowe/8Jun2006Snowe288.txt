SEN. SNOWE CONGRATULATES PORTLAND AFFORDABLE HOUSING GROUP
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today congratulated the Logan Place development in Portland, Maine as this year's winner of the Affordable Housing Tax Credit Coalition's Special Needs Housing category annual award. The award was presented today at the 12th annual Charles L. Edson Tax Credit Excellence Awards ceremony held in Washington D.C.  
   "I would like to extend my deepest congratulations to Logan Place. Their remarkable work and exemplary commitment to Maine's homeless have bettered the lives of countless Mainers," Snowe said. "This award is a recognition of the valuable roll the Logan Place plays providing the homeless and mentally ill, a safe and stable environment where they can develop life skills and achieve self sufficiency." 
   The Affordable Housing Tax Credit Coalition's annual awards ceremony honors outstanding housing development funded by the low-income housing tax credit.
