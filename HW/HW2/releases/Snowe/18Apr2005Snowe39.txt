SEN. SNOWE CONGRATULATES ROBERT KENNEDY FOR BEING NAMED PRESIDENT OF UNIVERSITY OF MAINE, ORONO
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today congratulated Robert A. Kennedy for being named president of the University of Maine, Orono by the Board of Trustees.
   "I would like to congratulate the Board of Governors for selecting Bob Kennedy to serve as President of the University of Maine, Orono," said Snowe. "His leadership will be essential as he steers one of our state's premiere educational institutions into the future. As a graduate of UMaine, I know Bob is up to the task and am confident the entire University system will benefit from his tremendous vision."
