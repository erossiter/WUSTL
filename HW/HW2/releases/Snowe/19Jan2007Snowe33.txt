SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES $7 MILLION CONTRACT TO GENERAL DYNAMICS IN SACO
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) on Friday, January 19, announced a $6.9 million contract to the General Dynamics' Saco facility for the production of M2HB Machine guns. 
   "I am pleased to announce the $7 million contract to General Dynamics in Saco ," said Senator Snowe. "This award truly signifies Maine 's quality workforce and our state's commitment to defending our nation."
   The U.S. Army Tank Automotive Command award modifies a contract originally awarded in October 2006, bringing the total contract value to $27.5 million.
