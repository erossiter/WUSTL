SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF ANDREW SMALL OF WISCASSET
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the death of Private First Class Andrew Small of Wiscasset who was killed in combat in Afghanistan serving in the Army's 10th Mountain Division out of Fort Drum, New York : 
   "Andrew Small made the ultimate sacrifice in service to his nation fighting in Afghanistan. His selfless commitment to his country will never be forgotten," said Snowe. "My thoughts and prayers go out to his friends, family and loved ones during this most difficult time."
