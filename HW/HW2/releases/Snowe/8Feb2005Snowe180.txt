SEN. SNOWE: 'CONGRATULATIONS ON ANOTHER GREAT WIN!'
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today made the following statement on last night's win by the New England Patriots over the Philadelphia Eagles in Superbowl XXXIX: 
   "Congratulations to the Pats! For the second year in a row, and the third time in four years, they have persevered and managed to bring home the ultimate victory," said Snowe. "They performed incredibly against a strong and tough opponent in the Philadelphia Eagles. I would like to congratulate this great team on their well-deserved victory and thank them for making New Englanders proud once again."
