SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF 1 LIEUTENANT BEN KEATING
Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the death of 1st Lieutenant Benjamin Keating. Keating, who died in Afghanistan Sunday, November 26th, was a former resident of Shapleigh, Maine. He was a member of the 3rd Squadron of the 71st Calvary, 3rd Brigade Combat Team of the 10th Mountain Division based in Fort Drum, New York.  
 "Ben Keating's service to his country will never be forgotten, and his sacrifice always remembered. His courage and the courage of every soldier who has fought to defend the United States is an inspiration to us all. My thoughts and prayers are with his family, his friends, and his loved ones during this most difficult time," Senator Snowe said.
