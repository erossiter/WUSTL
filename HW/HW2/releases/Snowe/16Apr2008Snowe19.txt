SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE GRANT FOR MAINE STATE HEALTH INSURANCE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services has awarded a $305,915 grant to the Maine Department of Health and Human Services Bureau of Elder and Adult Services for its State Health Insurance Assistance Program (SHIP). 
   "In a time when the rising cost of healthcare has overwhelmed seniors across the nation, Maine's State Health Insurance Assistance Program has consistently provided helpful and informative healthcare support to the state's most vulnerable citizens," Senators Snowe and Collins. "Continued federal funding for this vital program is essential to ensure a healthy and vibrant future for Maine's senior citizens."
