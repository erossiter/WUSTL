SEN. SNOWE RECIEVES NIH CHAMPION AWARD
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) recently received National Institute of Health Champion award for their Campaign for Medical Research.  
 The award was presented by former Rep. Robert Michel, Former Rep. Paul Rogers, and Michael Goldberg. 
   "It is such an honor to receive an award from an institution as prestigious as the National Institute of Health," said Snowe. "I strongly believe that improving healthcare and health services in this country is of the utmost importance and so I am pleased that I have the support of NIH. I pledge to continue my work within the realm of healthcare and hope to achieve some substantial victories during this Congress."
