SEN. SNOWE CONGRATULATES MAINE HIGH SCHOOLER FOR INVITATION TO ATTEND RESEARCH SCIENCE INSTITUTE AT MIT
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today congratulated Stephanie Alessi from Lincoln for being selected as one of 78 outstanding U.S. high school students to attend the 21st annual "Research Science Institute", to be held on the campus of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).  
 Sponsored by the Center for Excellence in Education, MIT and the California Institute of Technology, Alessi was selected for her demonstration of superior achievement in math, the sciences and verbal arts. In addition, Alessi scored at least 75 on her math PSAT score with a combined math/verbal PSAT score over 140.
   "The leadership and outstanding academic achievements of Stephanie Alessi has been rightly recognized with her selection into the Research Science Institute program. I congratulate Stephanie and encourage her to follow her dreams - academically, professionally, and personally," said Snowe.
   Students across the country are competitively selected to attend the six-week seminar at MIT with no room, board, or food expenses to the recipient. Upon completion, the Center for Excellence in Education provides additional resources and support to the students throughout their undergraduate and graduate studies.
