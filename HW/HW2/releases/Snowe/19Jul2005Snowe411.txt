SEN. SNOWE BIDS FAIRWELL TO PORTLAND POLICE CHIEF CHITWOOD
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) today issued the following statement on the resignation of Portland Police Chief Michael Chitwood: 
   "I have enjoyed working with Mike over the years, and value his friendship. Mike's 17 year professional tenure with the City of Portland has been remarkable and accomplished. The citizens of Portland will miss Chief Chitwood's forthright manner in dealing with the issues that have been critical to Portland's public safety."
