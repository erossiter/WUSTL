SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $35,876 IN FUNDING TO BOWDOIN COLLEGE
  Sens. Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) has awarded Bowdoin College in Brunswick, Maine $35,876 in federal funding under the National Research Initiative Competitive Grants Program.  
 The funding goes to the Department of Biological Sciences and will be used for equipment to study ways of preventing disease in white and red spruce trees. 
   "This funding will allow Bowdoin College to continue it's mission as a stellar research institution while at the same time helping to solve problems faced by Maine businesses and homeowners," Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. 
   The National Research Initiative (NRI) Competitive Grants Program supports high priority fundamental and mission-linked research of importance in the biological, environmental, physical, and social sciences relevant to agriculture, food, and the environment.
