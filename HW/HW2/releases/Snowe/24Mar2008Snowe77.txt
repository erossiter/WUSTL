SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF PRIVATE SMITH OF BETHEL
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today mourned the loss of Private Tyler J. Smith, 22, who died on March 21st near Baghdad, Iraq , of injuries sustained from indirect fire. Private Smith's wife lives in Fort Stewart, Georgia and his parents live in Bethel, Maine. 
   "Today we pause and honor the service and sacrifice of Private Tyler J. Smith - a dedicated patriot whose remarkable courage was an inspiration to us all," Senator Snowe said. "His steadfast commitment and extraordinary heroism to serving his country will not be forgotten. The heartfelt thoughts and prayers of all Mainers are with Private Smith's family and friends as they grieve this unimaginable loss."
