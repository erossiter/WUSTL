SEN. SNOWE WELCOMES AIRTRAN AIRWAYS TO MAINE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine), a member of the Senate Commerce Committee that overseas our nations airlines, on Monday, March 5, enthusiastically welcomed AirTran Airways to Maine. AirTran Airways will offer low-fare flights between Portland, Maine and Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport and Orlando International Airport beginning June 7, 2007.  
   "As a long-time champion of providing reliable air service to rural America, I am pleased by AirTran Airways' decision to offer dependable and affordable flights to the people of Maine," said Senator Snowe. "This great opportunity will not only provide more choices for Maine's travelers, but will undoubtedly boost our state's economy and expand our transportation network in the years to come."
