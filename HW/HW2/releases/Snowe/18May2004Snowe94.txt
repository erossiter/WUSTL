SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE NEARLY $3 MILLION TO MAINE DOT
  Sens. Olympia Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced $2,970,087 in federal funding to the Maine Department of Transportation Awarded by the Federal Transit Authority under the U.S. Department of Transportation, the federal grant will support the purchase of 32 replacement buses for 13 sub-recipients throughout the state.  
   "This federal funding is critical to maintaining safe and dependable bus service statewide," said Snowe and Collins in a joint statement. 
   The 13 sub-recipients that will receive replacement buses include: Aroostook Regional Transportation, Coastal Trans. Inc., Kennebec Valley CAP, Regional Transportation Program, Waldo County CAP, Washington-Hancock CAP, Penquis CAP, Downeast Transportation, York County CAP, the City of Bangor, Biddeford-Saco-Old Orchard Beach Transit, Lewiston-Auburn Transit and the Greater Portland Transit District.
