SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE MORE THAN $290,000 IN FEDERAL FUNDING FOR EDUCATION
  Sens. Olympia Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced $298,230 in federal funding to the Maine Center for Educational Services. The grant is awarded by the U.S. Department of Education.
   "This federal funding is critical to ensuring our state's education is of the standards that promote rigorous academics and college preparation for all Mainers," Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement.
   The Maine Center for Educational Services addresses the needs of educators to efficiently assess tasks that allow accurate data about the performance of their students.
