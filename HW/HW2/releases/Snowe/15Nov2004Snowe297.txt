SEN. SNOWE COMMENTS ON RESIGNATIONS OF CABINET SECRETARIES PAIGE, VENEMAN, ABRAHAM, POWELL
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) issued the following statement today on the resignations of Secretary of Education Rod Paige, Secretary of Agriculture Ann Veneman, Secretary of Energy Spencer Abraham and Secretary of State Colin Powell:  
   "The United States will be losing four dedicated public servants with the resignations of Secretary of Education Rod Paige, Secretary of Agriculture Ann Veneman, Secretary of Energy Spencer Abraham, and Secretary of State Colin Powell. Each have tirelessly served their country with steadfast leadership over the past four years and leave their respective offices with the appreciation of a grateful nation. 
   "Throughout my 25 years in Congress, I have come to know Colin Powell - and the commitment and honor he has brought to his lifelong service to our nation. A true statesman, Colin Powell's diplomatic and steady voice will be truly missed both at home and, indeed, around the world. I wish him the greatest success in all his future endeavors."
