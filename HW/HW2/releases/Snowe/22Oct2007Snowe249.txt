SEN. SNOWE ANNOUNCES NEW COMMUNICATIONS DIRECTOR
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today announced that John Gentzel has been promoted from National Press Secretary to Communications Director in her Washington, DC office. John previously worked as Communications Director for Rep. Jim Gerlach (R-PA) in the U.S. House of Representatives and as a reporter for a daily newspaper in Pennsylvania.  
   "Since his arrival, John has been a tremendous asset to our communications operation," Sen. Snowe said. "His professionalism, dedication to Maine and experience working in and with the media will continue to be a beneficial and critical part of informing and helping constituents statewide."
