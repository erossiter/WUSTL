POLL FINDS SEN. SNOWE HAS HIGHEST NET APPROVAL RATING OF ALL SENATORS
  A SurveyUSA poll released today finds that at 77% of Mainers polled approve of Sen. Olympia J. Snowe's (R-ME) performance in the U.S. Senate. At 59%, Snowe has the highest net job approval of any U.S. Senator. 
   Net job approval = (% Who Approve of Senator's Job Performance) minus (% who Disapprove of Job Performance). 
   Complete survey data on Senator Snowe is available on the web at: http://www.surveyusa.com/50StateTracking.html.
