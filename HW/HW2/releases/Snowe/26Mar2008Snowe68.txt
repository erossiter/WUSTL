SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $570,000 IN FUNDING FOR TOWN OF MONSON
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins (R-Maine) announced today that the Monson Utilities District in Piscataquis County will receive a water and waste disposal loan and grant from the United States Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program. The funds consist of a $422,000 grant and $148,000 loan and will be used to make water source improvements 
   "As the Monson Utilities District serves the needs of more than 400 people in Piscataquis County, it is vital that the community receives the necessary funding to modernize and improve its water sourcing capabilities," Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "We applaud the efforts of the USDA to enhance rural Maine's aging infrastructure and ensure that its citizens receive the quality services they deserve."
