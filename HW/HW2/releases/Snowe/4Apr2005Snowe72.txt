SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF JOHN O'LEARY
  Sen. Olympia Snowe (R-ME) issued the following statement today on the passing yesterday of John O'Leary, former Portland Mayor and Ambassador to Chile under President Clinton:  
   "Jock and I are profoundly saddened by the untimely death of John O'Leary. We greatly appreciated the opportunity to come to know him both personally and professionally over the past twenty years. He brought the highest standard of professionalism and dedication not only to the legal profession, but also to the State of Maine and the country. His impressive talents and energies truly knew no boundaries. We send our deepest condolences to his wife Patricia, their two daughters, and his entire family during this most difficult time."
