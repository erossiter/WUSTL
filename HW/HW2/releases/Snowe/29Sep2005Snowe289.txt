SEN. SNOWE MEETS WITH SENATE HISPANIC LEADERSHIP NOMINEES FROM MAINE
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) met today with Senate Hispanic Leadership Nominees from Maine.  
 They were nominated by Sen. Snowe. 
   "I would like to congratulate the Maine Senate Hispanic Leadership Nominees on all of their tremendous accomplishments," said Snowe. "Their nomination is truly a testament to their hard work and community involvement." 
   The nominees from Maine are Ariel Armony of Waterville, Priscilla Doel of Vassalboro, Ben Guiliani of South Portland, Mirna Kolbowski of Waterville, Rodolfo Lara of South Portland, Maria Rave of Bangor, Gregory Rodriguez-Reed of Presque Isle, and Geraldine Sanchez of Portland.
