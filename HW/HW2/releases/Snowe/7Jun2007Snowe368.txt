SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $120,000 IN ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FUNDING FOR MAINE
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Department of Commerce's Economic Development Administration (EDA) has awarded $60,000 in funding to the Androscoggin Valley Council of Governments in Auburn and $60,000 to the Kennebec Valley Council of Governments in Fairfield. The funding will be used to implement the Councils' economic development planning programs.  
   "These EDA funds will greatly invest in more stable and diversified economies for the people of Androscoggin and Kennebec counties," said Snowe and Collins in a joint statement. "This assistance is critical to helping Mainers get the resources and opportunities they need to stay employed and keep our state economy growing. 
   U.S. Department of Commerce Economic Development Administration investments are focused on locally-developed, regionally-based economic development initiatives that achieve the highest return on the taxpayers' investment and that directly contribute to economic growth.
