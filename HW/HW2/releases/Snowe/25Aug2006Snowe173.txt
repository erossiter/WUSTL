SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE MORE THAN $35,000 IN ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FUNDS FOR PENOBSCOT INDIAN NATION IN OLD TOWN
  Sens. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the U.S. Department of Commerce (USDOC) Economic Development Administration (EDA) has awarded $35,267 in investments to the Penobscot Indian Nation in Old Town. This funding will be used to support long-term economic development planning efforts.  
   "EDA investments provide essential financial support for outstanding Maine communities," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "We applaud this group for its initiative and commitment to improving the lives of the people in Eastern Maine ." 
   U.S. Department of Commerce Economic Development Administration investments are focused on locally-developed, regionally-based economic development initiatives that achieve the highest return on the taxpayers' investment and that directly contribute to economic growth.
