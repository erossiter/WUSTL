SEN. SNOWE CONGRATULATES RECIPIENTS OF TIBBETTS AWARDS
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) congratulated the recipients of the Tibbetts Awards, Karen West of C&P Management Services, David Erb, Shirley Ashton, and Erick Ritter of Tex Tech, and Max Kidalov of the Senate Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship. The Tibbetts Awards are made annually to those small firms, projects, organizations, and individuals who exemplify the very best in small business innovation in research achievement.  
   "I was deeply honored to meet with the Tibbetts Award honorees this year," said Senator Snowe. "Small businesses are the backbone of our economy and it is through the work of Karen, David, Shirley, Erick, and Max that the importance and impact of small businesses are exposed." 
   The Tibbetts Awards focus primarily on the economic impact of technological innovation, business achievement and effective collaborations, and demonstrated state and regional impact and proven support. The recipients of this year's awards attended a ceremony in Washington, DC on September 26, 2006.
