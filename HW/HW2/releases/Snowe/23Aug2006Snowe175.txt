SEN. SNOWE ATTENDS SERVICES FOR FALLEN MAINE SOLDIER
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine) today attended the funeral services for PFC Andrew Small in Wiscasset. PFC Small, who was killed on August 11th while serving in Afghanistan, was a member of the Army's 10th Mountain Division out of Fort Drum, New York and a native of Wiscasset, Maine. 
   "Andrew Small's selfless commitment to his country will never be forgotten," Snowe said. "It was an honor to join his family, friends, and loved ones today to remember his life and his bravery. My thoughts and prayers are with them all during this most difficult time."
