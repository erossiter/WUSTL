SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $850,000 LOAN, $700,000 GRANT FOR TOWN OF DOVER-FOXCROFT
  U.S. Senators Olympia J. Snowe and Susan Collins (R-ME) announced today that the town of Dover-Foxcroft received a waster and waste disposal loan and grant from the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program. Specifically, the town will receive a loan for $850,000 and a grant in the amount of $700,000. 
   "More than 2500 people will benefit from the infrastructure upgrades the town of Dover-Foxcroft will be able to make thanks to this loan and grant," Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "This funding will ensure that the town is able provide the quality of service its residents depend on."
   The rural development funds will be used to make improvements to the Town's sewer system. These collection system improvements are needed to replace the aging lines on several streets.
