SENS. SNOWE, COLLINS ANNOUNCE $489,883 IN FUNDING TO REGIONAL MEDICAL CENTER AT LUBEC
  Sens. Olympia Snowe (R-ME) and Susan Collins (R-ME) today announced that the Regional Medical Center at Lubec has received $489,883 in funding for a Health Center Cluster from the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS).  
 The grant was allocated through the Health Resources and Services Administration of HHS. 
   "This grant award will help to increase the standard of health care in the State of Maine," Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement. "Expanding the Regional Medical Center at Lubec will make it more able to meet its patients' needs." 
   HRSA works to improve and expand access to quality health care for all. The funds allocated by this program provide primary health care services for families through state programs, as well as train a health workforce that is both diverse and motivated to work in low-income communities. HRSA assures the availability of quality health care to low income, uninsured, isolated, vulnerable and special needs populations and works to meet their unique health care needs.
