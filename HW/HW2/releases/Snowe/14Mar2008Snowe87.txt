SEN. SNOWE TO HOST PANEL ON FEDERAL CONTRACTING
  U.S. Senator Olympia J. Snowe (R-Maine), the Ranking Member of the U.S. Senate Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship, will be hosting an informative panel discussion on federal contracting on Wednesday, March 19th in Augusta. There is no fee to attend and parking is available. 
   The seminar will highlight how to utilize the U.S. Small Business Administration's (SBA) programs relating to federal contracting and other resources available. The Maine Procurement Technical Assistance Center (PTAC) will provide an overview of their programs and services. Contracting officers and representatives from the Veterans Administration, the Maine National Guard, Portsmouth Navy Shipyard, and the General Services Administration (GSA) will be on hand to provide insight on how to access and pursue contracts with their agencies.
   Pre-registration is required. Please contact Bonnie Erickson, with the Maine SBA at 207-622-8275 or via e-mail at bonnie.erickson@sba.gov
