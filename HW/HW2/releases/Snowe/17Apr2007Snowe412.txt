SEN. SNOWE ISSUES STATEMENT ON SHOOTINGS AT VIRGINIA TECH ON APRIL 17
  Sen. Olympia J. Snowe (R-ME) issued the following statement in the aftermath of the shootings today at Virginia Tech:  
   "The horrific loss of life that occurred this morning at Virginia Tech constitutes an unimaginable, national tragedy," said Senator Snowe. "America is united in its profound sorrow and its condemnation of this unspeakable crime. All of us are shocked by the news, as we struggle to comprehend these unfathomable events. Our collective thoughts and prayers are very much with the families and friends of the victims, and for those who have been injured we hope and pray for their full recovery."
