SENS. BAUCUS, TESTER ANNOUNCE $3MILLION FOR MISSOULA COUNTY
  Montana Sens. Max Baucus and Jon Tester on Thursday, Feb. 22, praised the Department of Homeland Security for awarding Missoula County $3 million to reduce the risk of flooding to more than 110 residential homes. 
   The grant will be used to construct channels and drains to lower flood elevations, and to map existing flood hazards. 
   The grant was awarded through the Federal Emergency Management Agency.
