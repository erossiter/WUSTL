SEN. TESTER TO HOST GLENDIVE LISTENING SESSION FOR SMALL BUSINESSES
  Senator Jon Tester will host a listening session for small business owners in the Glendive area this Saturday, February 9. 
  All small business owners are welcome to attend the hour-long forum between 11:00 and 12:00 Saturday morning to share concerns and ideas.
  Tester is a member of the Senate Small Business and Entrepreneurship Committee. He will share the information from Saturday's listening session with his colleagues in Washington, D.C.
   DATE/TIME: Saturday, February 9, from 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
   LOCATION: Glendive Medical Center (Carney Conference Rooms) 202 Prospect
