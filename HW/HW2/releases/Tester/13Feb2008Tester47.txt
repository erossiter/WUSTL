SEN. TESTER TO HOST GREAT FALLS, HAVRE LISTENING SESSIONS FOR SMALL BUSINESSES
  Senator Jon Tester will host a listening session for small business owners in Great Falls and Havre on Friday, February 22. 
   All small business owners are welcome to attend the hour-long forums to share concerns and ideas.
   Tester is a member of the Senate Small Business and Entrepreneurship Committee. He will share the information from Saturday's listening session with his colleagues in Washington, D.C.
   EVENT: Havre Small Business Listening Session
   DATE/TIME: Friday, February 22, from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
   LOCATION: Bear Paw Economic Development Corporation (Conference Room)
   48 Second Ave., Suite 202, Havre
   EVENT: Great Falls Small Business Listening Session
   DATE/TIME: Friday, February 22, from 2:15 p.m. to 3:15 p.m.
   LOCATION: Great Falls Civic Center (Missouri Room)
  2 Park Drive South, Great Falls
