SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SPC. DONALD YOUNG
  Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement regarding Specialist Donald Young of Helena, who died of injuries last week in Iraq:
   "Today with heavy hearts, Sharla and I join all Montanans in saying goodbye as we lay to rest another American hero. Our thoughts and prayers are with Donald's family, friends and his fellow troops in harm's way. Montana will never forget Donald's service to our country and his ultimate sacrifice."
