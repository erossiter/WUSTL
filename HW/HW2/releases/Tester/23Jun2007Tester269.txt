SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF NANCY PETERSON
  Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement in response to the passing of his friend, Nancy K. Peterson: 
   "Sharla and I are deeply saddened by Nancy's passing, and we join all Montanans in sending our thoughts and prayers to the Peterson family. Nancy was my close friend, a great neighbor, and a Montanan to the core. Her commitment and dedication to agriculture--through science, stewardship and hard work--changed Montana's family farms and ranches forever. She will be always be remembered here on the Hi-Line and across the Big Sky."
