SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF STAFF SGT. SHANE BECKER
  Montana Sen. Jon Tester on Thursday, April 12, released the following statement in response to the death of Staff Sgt. Shane Becker, who was killed earlier this month in Iraq: 
   "Sharla and I are deeply saddened by Shane's death. We are forever thankful for his service and commitment to this nation. Our thoughts and prayers go out to his wife Crystal and his young children, Sierra and Cheyenna. We will never forget what Shane gave us all."
