SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON SIXTH ANNIVERSARY OF SEPT. 11
  Montana Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement:
   "Sharla and I join all Americans in honoring and mourning those we lost six years ago. We will never forget.
   We also will never forget the firefighters and police officers who risked everything to save lives, and those who gave their own.
   With heartfelt thanks we honor those who bravely go overseas to defend this country. With heavy hearts and gratitude we remember those who will never come home."
