SEN. TESTER COMMENTS ON FIGHTING SAINTS VICTORY
  Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement after the Fighting Saints of Carroll College defeated the University of Sioux Falls, securing their fifth National Championship in six years:
   "There are few things we can count on in Montana, but it seems as if every December we celebrate another National Championship. Today is a great day for Coach Van Diest, his team, and the entire Carroll community. You've made Montana proud by marching the Saints to another victory."
   Carroll College is located in Helena, Montana.
