SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING AWAY OF SEN. CRAIG THOMAS
  Sen. Jon Tester today released the following statement in response to the death of Wyoming Senator Craig Thomas:
   "Sharla and I join all Montanans in sending our thoughts and prayers to Senator Thomas' wife Susan, his family and to all of Wyoming. Craig will always be remembered as a Marine, a public servant, a listener and a fighter for the Cowboy State. As we mourn his death, we honor the spirit of Wyoming enthusiasm, hard work and public service he left behind."
