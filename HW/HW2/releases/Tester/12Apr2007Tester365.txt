SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF ARMY PVT. 1 CLASS KYLE BOHRNSEN
  Montana Sen. Jon Tester on Thursday, April 12, released the following statement in response to the death of Army Pvt. 1st Class Kyle Bohrnsen of Philipsburg, who was killed earlier this week in Iraq: 
   "It pains me to hear of another Montanan killed in Iraq. Kyle was an all-American man devoted to serving our country. Sharla and I are deeply saddened by his death, and we mourn with Kyle's family and friends."
