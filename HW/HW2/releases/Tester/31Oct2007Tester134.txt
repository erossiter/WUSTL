SEN. TESTER RESPONDS TO AGRICULTURE SECRETARY NOMINATION
  Senator Jon Tester, a Big Sandy farmer, today released the following statement in response to President Bush's nomination of former North Dakota governor Edward Schafer to become the next Secretary of Agriculture:
   "The next Secretary of Agriculture needs to have a solid understanding of the issues and needs of family farms and ranches, and rural America. I look forward to talking with Governor Schafer and making sure his priorities are fit for Montana."
