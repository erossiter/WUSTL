SEN. TESTER RESPONSE TO DEATH OF EVEL KNIEVEL
  Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement in response to the death of Evel Knievel:
   "Heaven just got a daredevil. Evel Knievel embraced life by living it on the edge. Like the town he came from, Evel will always be a part of Montana's history and character. Tonight, many Montanans will be saying in their prayers, 'Tap 'er light, friend.'"
