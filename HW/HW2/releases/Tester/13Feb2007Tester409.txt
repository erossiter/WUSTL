SEN. TESTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF REP. NORWOOD
  Montana Sen. Jon Tester on Tuesday, Feb. 13, released the following statement on the passing of Rep. Charlie Norwood of Georgia: 
   "Sharla and I join my colleagues in the House and Senate in sending thoughts and prayers to Congressman Norwood's family, and to all people of Georgia. Charlie was a true public servant, both as a U.S. Representative and as a military officer. My staff and I are saddened by the news of his passing".
