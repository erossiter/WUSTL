SEN. TESTER TO HOST POLSON LISTENING SESSION FOR SMALL BUSINESSES
  Senator Jon Tester will host a listening session for small business owners in the Polson area on Thursday, February 21. 
   All small business owners are welcome to attend the hour-long forum between 9:00 and 10:00 a.m. that morning to share concerns and ideas.
   Tester is a member of the Senate Small Business and Entrepreneurship Committee. He will share the information from Saturday's listening session with his colleagues in Washington, D.C.
   DATE/TIME:Thursday, February 21, from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
   LOCATION: Natural Resources Department Conference Room
  316 1st Street East, Polson
