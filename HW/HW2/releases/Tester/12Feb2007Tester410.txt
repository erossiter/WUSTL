SEN. TESTER STATEMENT ON DEATH OF PVT. MATTHEW ZEIMER
  Montana Sen. Jon Tester on Monday, Feb. 12, released the following statement on the death of Pvt. Matthew Zeimer: 
   "This is another sad day for Montana as we mourn the loss of a fine man who served as a soldier in the United States Army. Matthew gave his life to this country only a few months after graduating high school. Sharla and I are forever grateful for his brave service, and our thoughts and prayers are with his family."
