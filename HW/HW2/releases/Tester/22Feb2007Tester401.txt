SENS. BAUCUS, TESTER ANNOUNCE RESEARCH GRANT FOR MONTANA STATE
  Montana Sens. Max Baucus and Jon Tester on Thursday, Feb. 22, announced that the HHS is awarding Montana State University $247,625.00 to continue research related to Neurological Disorders. 
   This funding will be dedicated to developing technology to study the brain and is considered a stepping stone to better understanding the biological basis of psychological disorders.
