SEN. TESTER CONGRATULATES MONTANA NOBEL PEACE PRIZE WINNER
  Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement after personally congratulating University of Montana researcher Dr. Steve Running. A lead author for the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change, Dr. Running is a co-recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize, awarded today in Oslo, Norway. Dr. Running will share the honor with other authors worldwide.  
   "Winning a Nobel Peace Prize is one of the greatest honors you can get. Montana couldn't be more proud of Steve. He put years of hard work into making our world a better place to live for future generations. Steve's cutting-edge research will keep Montana at the forefront in the fight against climate change, and I look forward to working with him as he continues to lead the way."
