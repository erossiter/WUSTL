Washington, D.C. - Senator Daniel K. Akaka (D-HI), sponsor of the Native Hawaiian Government Reorganization Act, will begin debate on his bill today. Debate is expected to take place on the Senate floor at 3:00 p.m. today Eastern Standard Time. Proponents of the bill will have alternating half-hours beginning at 3:30 p.m. Debate is expected to last until 6:00 p.m.Live coverage is available on CSPAN-2. It is also accessible via a link to the Senate floorfound on the home page.What: Debate on the Native Hawaiian Government Reorganization ActWhen: Today 3:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. (EST)For more information on S. 147/S.3064, please visitthe issues section of this web site.The Senate is expected to vote on the cloture motion to proceed to the bill tomorrow, June 8th. Sixty votes are required to get cloture on the motion to proceed.
