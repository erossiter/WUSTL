This legislation is a small, but important step in battling homelessness among veterans. As our servicemembers transition from military service to civilian life some will be at risk of homelessness. Any effort VA can make to assist these servicemembers will improve lives and reduce the demand for VA homeless services in the years to come, Chairman Akaka stated. We have all heard the sad and shocking statistic that one out of every three homeless persons on the street at any given time is a veteran.
