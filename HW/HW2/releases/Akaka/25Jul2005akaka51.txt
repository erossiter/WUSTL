Washington, D.C. - Senator Daniel K. Akaka is working with the Democratic Leader to file cloture on a motion to proceed tomorrow. In addition, Senator Akaka spoke with Majority Leader Bill Frist, who believes an agreement to proceed to the bill can be worked out by tomorrow. Frist is still working with his colleagues to keep his commitment of bringing the bill up for a debate and vote prior to the August recess. Senator Akaka said, I am pursuing both tracks of action because I intend to make sure that S. 147 is considered this week. Senator Akaka also continues negotiations with the White House and Senators with holds on language to address concerns.
