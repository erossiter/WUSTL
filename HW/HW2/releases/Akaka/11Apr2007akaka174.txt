Washington, D.C. - U.S. Senator Daniel K. Akaka (D-Hawaii) will enter cyberspace Friday to answer questions posed by Hawaii residents on the Honolulu Advertisers live online blog, The Hot Seat. The 1-hour chat will begin at noon Hawaii Time. Constituents are encouraged to submit a question to the forum on Friday. Akaka will answer as many as time allows.
