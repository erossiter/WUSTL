Washington, D.C. - Senator Daniel K. Akaka (D-HI) is pleased to announce today a number of health, economic development, and education programs and projects have been approved by the Senate Appropriations Committee. Senator Akaka was instrumental in securing funding for the following:I am pleased that we were successful in getting funding approval for all these programs and projects because they are essential in improving conditions for those who need it, stated Senator Akaka. Many of these projects will result in giving a necessary boost for communities that have been waiting for financial help to continue contributing to the betterment of Hawaii, whether it be in education, health, or economic development.
