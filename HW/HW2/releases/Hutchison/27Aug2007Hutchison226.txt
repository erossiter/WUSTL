SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES OVER $7 MILLION IN HEALTH CARE CENTER GRANTS FOR TEXAS COMMUNITIES
  Texas' senior Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today announced that the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) released $7,143,996 in grants to community health centers in Texas.  
   "Community health centers are an important tool to expand access to comprehensive primary and preventative health care services for Texas families," Sen. Hutchison said. "These grants will help create more of these health centers and improve health care across Texas."
