TEXAS SENATORS TO VISIT BASES ON BRAC LIST
  On Sunday, May 15, Texas Sens. Kay Bailey Hutchison and John Cornyn will meet with local officials to discuss Texas military installations affected by the Base Realignment and Closure (BRAC) list released by the Department of Defense today.  
 Following the meetings the Senators and local officials will be available for media questions and comments.
