SEN. HUTCHISON COMMENTS ON LOSS OF ROSA PARKS
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today made the following statement on the passing of Rosa Parks;
   "Rosa Parks has long been revered as the mother of the civil rights movement. She compelled change through quiet strength. Her conviction during those challenging times will forever hold a place in America's history. All Americans owe a great debt of gratitude to Rosa Parks."
