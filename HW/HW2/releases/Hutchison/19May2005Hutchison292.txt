SEN. HUTCHISON TO VISIT ABILENE, SAN ANGELO, DEL RIO
  Friday, May 20, 2005, Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) will meet with Dyess Air Force Base officials to discuss the Base Realignment and Closure (BRAC) recommendation list released May 13 by the Department of Defense. The Pentagon's proposal recommends moving the 28th Bomb Wing from Ellsworth AFB in South Dakota to Dyess AFB and relocating the C-130 Aircraft from Dyess AFB to Little Rock AFB in Arkansas. Following the meeting, Sen. Hutchison and local officials will be available for comments. 
   WHO: Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison
  WHAT: Dyess AFB BRAC Press Conference
  WHEN: 10:05 a.m. (CDT)
  WHERE: Abilene Aero
  2850 Airport Blvd
  Abilene, TX

   DEL RIO - Friday, May 20, 2005, Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) will meet with local, county and Laughlin Air Force Base officials to discuss the Base Realignment and Closure (BRAC) recommendation list released May 13 by the Department of Defense. The Pentagon's proposal recommends that Laughlin gain 182 personnel, mostly from Moody AFB in Georgia. Following the meeting, Sen. Hutchison and local officials will be available for comments.
