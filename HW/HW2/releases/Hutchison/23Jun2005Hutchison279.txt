SEN. HUTCHISON ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS' BUDGET SHORTAGE
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, today issued the following statement after learning that the VA is approximately $1 billion short in 2006.  
 Her statement follows:
   "I am informed that, after a mid-year review of the budget of the Department of Veterans Affairs, the VA will be approximately $1 billion short in 2006. I appreciate Secretary Jim Nicholson for conducting this review and coming up with a revised estimate. As Chairman, I will work with Secretary Nicholson and President Bush to meet the needs of the department and our nation's veterans. We can never fall short on our promises to those who have sacrificed so much."
