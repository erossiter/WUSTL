SEN. HUTCHISON COMMENTS ON UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS MEDICAL BRANCH PRESIDENT STOBO'S RETIREMENT ANNOUNCEMENT
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today issued the following statement on University of Texas Medical Branch (UTMB) President John D. Stobo's announcement that he plans to retire in August 2007:  
   "I thank President Stobo for his years of leadership at the University of Texas Medical Branch. He has had a significant impact on the school's advancements in education and medical research. The Biosafety Lab that we worked together to secure will serve as a tribute to his commitment to medical advancements in Texas. I look forward to working with him in this final year of his Presidency and wish him the best in his future endeavors."
