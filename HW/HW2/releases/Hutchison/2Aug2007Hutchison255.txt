SEN. HUTCHISON CALLS ETHICS REFORM AN 'IMPORTANT STEP FORWARD'
  U.S. Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today issued the following statement regarding Senate passage of S. 1, The Honest and Open Government Act of 2007, the Ethics Reform bill: 
   "There were parts of this legislation that are not what I would have written, but I thought it was an important step forward and we can continue to work for responsible laws of governance." Lamar University $679,471 
   Lamar Institute of Technology $144,288 
   Lamar State College-Port Arthur $75,951 
   Lamar State College-Orange $69,885 
   This funding is intended to partially reimburse these institutions for lost tuition revenue and other expenses associated with the hurricanes.
