SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES $13.4 MILLION IN EMERGENCY FUNDING AWARDED TO TEXAS WORKFORCE COMMISSION
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today announced the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) has a awarded a $13.4 million National Emergency Grant (NEG) to the Texas Workforce Commission (TWC). The funds will provide affected workers with employment and training services.  
   "This funding will help workers affected by last year's devastating hurricanes find new employment opportunities," Sen. Hutchison said. "Many areas are still recovering and this training will prepare new workers to contribute to the rebuilding process."
   The labor services that will be provided include the creation of temporary jobs and vocational training that will help employees return to work.
