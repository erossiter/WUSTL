SEN. HUTCHISON COMMENDS HARRIET MIERS FOR SERVICE TO NATION
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), today commended Harriet Miers for her service to the nation following the announcement that Miers has submitted her resignation as White House counsel. 
   "I commend my good friend, Harriet Miers, for serving our nation with honor and distinction. The country has benefited enormously from her wise counsel and invaluable advice. She is a pioneer among women and I know she will find success in her future endeavors."
