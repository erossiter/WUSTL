SEN. HUTCHISON 'LOOKS FORWARD TO WORKING WITH CHANCELLOR KHATOR'
  Today Texas' senior Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) released the following statement on the formal appointment of Renu Khator to be Chancellor of the University of Houston System and President of the University of Houston: 
   "Chancellor Khator has an excellent reputation as an accomplished scholar and dynamic leader, rising from instructor to Provost at the University of South Florida. I look forward to working with Chancellor Khator to continue building research and education programs at the University of Houston System. I worked closely with Chancellor Gogue on the nanohealth and wind energy consortia and we must continue those efforts."
