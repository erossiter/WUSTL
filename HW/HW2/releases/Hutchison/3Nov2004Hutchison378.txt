SEN. HUTCHISON ISSUES STATEMENT ON RE-ELECTION OF BUSH
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) issued the following statement in response to George W. Bush winning reelection to serve another term as the President of the United States of America. 
   "The American people made the right choice. President Bush will continue to provide the leadership and vision for a safer and more prosperous nation. He offers real solutions for protecting our country, growing the economy and giving Americans a better quality of life.
   "After this lengthy and divisive campaign, I urge Republicans and Democrats alike to put partisan politics aside. We must work together in a bipartisan manner to effectively fight the war on terrorism and address critical needs here at home."
