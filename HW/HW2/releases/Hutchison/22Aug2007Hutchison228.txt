SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES NEARLY $600,000 IN TEXAS FIRE GRANTS
  Texas' senior Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today announced $592,396 in U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) Assistance to Firefighters Grants (AFG) for Texas firefighters and Emergency Medical Service (EMS) personnel to improve overall preparedness and first response capabilities. 
   "These DHS fire grants will provide our brave firefighters and EMS responders with the additional resources they need to save lives," Sen. Hutchison said. "This funding will help these men and women better prepare for emergency response and recovery in our communities."
