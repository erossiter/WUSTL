SEN. HUTCHISON EL CAMINO REAL TRAIL TOUR: DAY 3
  Tomorrow, Wednesday, March 23, Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) will be joined by local elected officials, civic leaders and historical society representatives as she visits the following sites and makes remarks celebrating the history of El Camino Real de los Tejas. Sen.  
 Hutchison is traveling El Camino Real on a three-day tour highlighting the history of Texas' oldest trail and its recent designation as a National Historic Trail - a designation Sen. Hutchison fought to earn.
