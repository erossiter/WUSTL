SEN. HUTCHISON COMMENTS ON PASSING OF CORETTA SCOTT KING
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today released the following statement on the passing of Coretta Scott King:
   "Coretta and Rev. Martin Luther King devoted their lives to civil rights and made an indelible impact on our country. Her devotion to her family and to equality for all will never be forgotten. I hope all Americans keep the King family in their thoughts and prayers."
