SEN. HUTCHISON WORKING TO PROVIDE RELIEF FOR EL PASO COMMUNITY
  Following an August 7 tour to survey the flood damage in El Paso, Texas, Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) has been working closely with local, state and FEMA officials to provide relief for the El Paso community and released the following statement: 
   "The El Paso community has been working hard to recover from extensive flooding and needs support. I have been in constant contact with local, state and FEMA officials in efforts to expedite the assessment process so a federal disaster declaration can be made as soon as possible and relief can be provided."
