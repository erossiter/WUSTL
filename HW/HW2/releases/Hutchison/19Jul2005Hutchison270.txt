SEN. HUTCHISON ISSUES STATEMENT ON NEW WRIGHT AMENDMENT LEGISLATION
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today released the following statement in response to legislation introduced that would affect the Wright Amendment: 
   "I will work with Sen. Ensign to ensure we move forward appropriately so that all aspects of the Wright Amendment are examined. We must be thorough and cautious as we consider not only the future of the Wright Amendment but the protection of Metroplex taxpayers."
