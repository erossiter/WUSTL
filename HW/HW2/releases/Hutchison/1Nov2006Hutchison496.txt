SEN. HUTCHISON CONGRATULATES MCKINNEY ON SELECTION AS SOLE FINALIST FOR CHANCELLOR OF TEXAS A&M SYSTEM
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), today congratulated Michael D. McKinney, M.D. on being named the sole finalist for chancellor of the Texas A&M University System by the Board of Regents.  
   "I am pleased the Board of Regents has chosen such a qualified candidate for this important leadership position. The Texas A&M System is comprised of exemplary research institutions and I know Dr. McKinney will be an excellent leader.
   "I have enjoyed working with Dr. McKinney in the past and look forward to assisting him in promoting Texas A&M and its contributions to the medical field."
