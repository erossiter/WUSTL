SEN. HUTCHISON MEETS WITH CITY OF LAREDO
  Texas' senior Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison, today met with Laredo Mayor Raul Salinas, members of the Laredo City Council and a delegation of local representatives in her Washington, D.C. office to discuss local issues affecting the community.
   "This meeting was a good opportunity to discuss local issues with Laredo's leaders," Sen. Hutchison said. "Hearing directly from Texas communities helps me address their areas of concern here in Washington."
   Sen. Hutchison hosted the meeting to discuss priority issues in Laredo including, transportation and recreation development, public safety and border security.
