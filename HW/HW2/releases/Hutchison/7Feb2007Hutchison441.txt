SEN. HUTCHISON MOURNS PASSING OF NOBEL LAUREATE ALAN MACDIARMID
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), today commented on the passing of Alan MacDiarmid, University of Texas at Dallas chemist and Nobel Prize winner. 
   "Alan MacDiarmid was a brilliant chemist whose ground-breaking research at the University of Texas earned him the honorable recognition as the 2000 Nobel Prize in Chemistry," Sen. Hutchison said. "He shared his unique insights across the globe and persuaded diverse audiences about the need to increase innovation in the 21st century. He will be missed and my thoughts are with his family."
