SEN. HUTCHISON MOURNS PASSING OF BRIG. GEN. MCDERMOTT
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today commented on the loss of community leader and retired Air Force Brig. Gen. Robert F. McDermott: 
   "General McDermott leaves behind a legacy of distinguished service to his country and his community. The city of San Antonio has suffered the loss of a dedicated leader and I have lost a good friend. His family will be in our thoughts and prayers."
