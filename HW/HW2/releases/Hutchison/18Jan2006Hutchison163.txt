SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES FEMA HOTLINE, MOBILE CENTERS FOR AREAS AFFECTED BY TEXAS WILDFIRES
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee, today announced the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) has set up a recovery hotline and dispatched Mobile Disaster Recovery Centers (DRC) for residents in Callahan, Cooke, Eastland, Erath, Hood, Montague, Palo Pinto, Tarrant and Wise counties which have been affected by wildfires in Texas. 
   "Loan and grant assistance is an important resource to help residents in these counties recover. The FEMA hotline and mobile disaster recovery centers will help residents get the information they need," Sen. Hutchison said. "Those affected by these disastrous wildfires have our full support. I encourage them to utilize these FEMA resources to help them begin to recover and rebuild."
