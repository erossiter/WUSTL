SEN. HUTCHISON CONGRATULATES REP. SILVESTRE REYES ON APPOINTMENT AS HOUSE INTELLIGENCE CHAIR
Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today spoke with Rep. Silvestre Reyes (D-TX) and congratulated him on his appointment as chairman of the House Intelligence Committee in the 110th Congress. 
 "Silvestre Reyes is a man of great integrity and a good choice for this position. I spoke with him today and told him how pleased I am with his appointment to this critical post. The unique background and experience in law enforcement he brings to the committee will provide a valuable perspective. He understands the value and importance of good intelligence in protecting Americans' security."
