SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES NEARLY $600,000 IN TEXAS FIRE GRANTS
  Texas' senior Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX) today announced $597,639 in U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) grants for Texas firefighters to improve first response capabilities. 
   "These Homeland Security fire grants will provide our brave firefighters with the resources they need to save lives," Sen. Hutchison said. "This funding will help them better prepare for emergency response and recovery in our communities."
