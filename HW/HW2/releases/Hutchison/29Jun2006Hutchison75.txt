SEN. HUTCHISON CONGRATULATES CONEWAY ON CONFIRMATION AS U.S. AMBASSADOR TO SWITZERLAND
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today congratulated Houston resident Peter Coneway on Senate confirmation as U.S. Ambassador to Switzerland and the Principality of Liechtenstein. Sen. Hutchison's statement follows: 
   "Peter Coneway is uniquely qualified for this position. His extensive experience in financial services and his knowledge of the global economy will serve him well in his new role as U.S. Ambassador to Switzerland and the Principality of Liechtenstein."
