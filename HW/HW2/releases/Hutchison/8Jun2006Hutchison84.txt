SEN. HUTCHISON ANNOUNCES $227.5 MILLION CONTINUANCE OF C-17 PROGRAM
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison, Chairman of Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Subcommittee, today announced that the Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Bill conference report will include $227.5 million for continued production of the Boeing C-17 aircraft, largely produced in Ft. Worth.  
   "This aircraft is vital to our military operations and provides over 600 crucial, specialized jobs in the Dallas-Ft. Worth Region," Sen. Hutchison said. I am pleased that my congressional colleagues continue to support my efforts to fund the C-17 program.
   Vought Aircraft Industries of Ft. Worth is the largest sub-contractor on the C-17 and employs over 600 specialists on this project.
