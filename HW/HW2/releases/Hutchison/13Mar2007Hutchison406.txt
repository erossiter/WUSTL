SEN. HUTCHISON MEETS WITH CITY OF ARLINGTON
  Texas' senior Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison, today met with Arlington Mayor Robert Cluck, members of the Arlington City Council and a delegation of local representatives in her Washington, D.C. office to discuss local issues affecting the community.
   "This meeting was a good opportunity to discuss local issues with Arlington's leaders," Sen. Hutchison said. "Hearing directly from Texas communities helps me address their needs and areas of concern here in Washington."
   Sen. Hutchison hosted the meeting to discuss priority issues in Arlington including, transportation, energy and conservation and public safety.
