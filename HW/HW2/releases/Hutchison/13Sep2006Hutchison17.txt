SEN. HUTCHISON MOURNS PASSING OF FORMER TEXAS GOV. RICHARDS
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), Vice Chairman of the Senate Republican Conference, today commented on the loss of former Texas Gov. Ann Richards:
   "Ann Richards was a trailblazer in our beloved state of Texas. She was fun, and funny, and irreverent and loved life. My thoughts are with her family at this sad time."
