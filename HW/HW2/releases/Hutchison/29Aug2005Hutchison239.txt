SEN. HUTCHISON ADDRESSES FIRST GIRLS FUNDRAISING LUNCHEON
  Sen. Kay Bailey Hutchison (R-TX), today addressed the first Girls, Inc. fundraising luncheon.  
   "Girls, Inc. is dedicated to providing essential guidance and tools to help girls succeed. It is an honor to show my support for this organization," Sen. Hutchison said. "Helping young women develop strength and confidence during childhood and adolescence will encourage success and provide strong role models for the next generation." 
   Girls, Inc. was founded in 1864 as a national, non-profit youth organization. It is dedicated to providing encouragement and offers vital educational programs to millions of girls. The organization provides guidance nationwide and has three locations in Dallas in addition to the program in San Antonio.
