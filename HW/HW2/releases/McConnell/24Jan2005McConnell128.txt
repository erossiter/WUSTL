SENS. MCCONNELL, CRAIG, CRAPO SETTLE LIBERTY BOWL WAGER
  Sens. Mitch McConnell (R-KY), Larry Craig (R-ID) and Mike Crapo (R-ID) met on Monday, January 24 to settle their wager on the Liberty Bowl football game.
   The senators made a friendly wager on the outcome of the 46th Annual AutoZone Liberty Bowl, in which the Louisville Cardinals faced the Boise State Broncos. The Cardinals beat the Broncos 44-40. 
   Senators Craig and Crapo provided a lunch of Idaho trout and potatoes to Senator McConnell in his office.
