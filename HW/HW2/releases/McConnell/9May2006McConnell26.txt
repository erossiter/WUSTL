SEN. MCCONNELL WILL APPEAR ON KET'S 'ONE TO ONE' THIS WEEKEND
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell will appear on "One to One" with Bill Goodman on Kentucky Education Television (KET) this weekend. 
   The show will air on Friday, May 12 at 10:00 p.m. EDT on KET2 and Sunday, May 14 at 2:00 p.m. EDT on KET1.
