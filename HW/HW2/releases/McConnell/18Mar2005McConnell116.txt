SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON TERRI SCHIAVO CASE
  Sen. Mitch McConnell released the following statement today on the Terri Schiavo case: 
   "Everyone who has followed this case can agonize with the family of Terri Schiavo, and I am no different. I supported the action the Senate took this week, which gives Terri's parents access to federal court as part of their effort to protect the life of their daughter."
