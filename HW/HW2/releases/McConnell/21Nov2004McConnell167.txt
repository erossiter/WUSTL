SEN. MCCONNELL ANNOUNCES SENATE APPROVAL OF NOMINATION OF CATHY BAILEY AS U.S. AMBASSADOR TO LATVIA
  Sen. Mitch McConnell announced today that the Senate has approved the nomination of Cathy Bailey as U.S. Ambassador to Latvia. 
   "I am pleased the Senate confirmed Cathy Bailey as the next Ambassador to Latvia," said McConnell. "I have worked with Cathy for years and know that she will be an outstanding representative of American values as well as policies in her new role. Elaine joins me in congratulating this Kentucky role model and wishing she and Irv all our best. Her achievements make an entire Commonwealth proud."
