SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON BUSH'S SPEECH ON TRANSFER OF POWER IN IRAQ
  Statement Of U.S. Senator Mitch McConnell On President Bush's Speech On Transfer Of Power In Iraq 
   "President Bush once again made clear that the future of Iraq rests in the hands of the Iraqi people. A free and stable Iraq will stand as a bulwark against terror and a beacon of democracy and security in a troubled region. I support the President fully in this cause."
