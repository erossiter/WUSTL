SEN. MCCONNELL COMMENTS ON NATIONAL POLICE WEEK
  U.S. Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell made the following statement in recognition of National Police Week, which honors the men and women who risk their lives to serve and protect communities throughout the U.S.: 
   "This week we pay special tribute to the noble men and women who protect our safety, even as they risk their own. We remember those who sacrificed their lives, and honor those who continue to dedicate their lives to serving and protecting others."
