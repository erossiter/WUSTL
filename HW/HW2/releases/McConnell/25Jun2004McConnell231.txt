SEN. MCCONNELL TO APPEAR ON NBC 'MEET THE PRESS' ON JUNE 27
  Sen. Mitch McConnell will appear on Meet the Press on Sunday, June 27, 2004. The topic is the June 30th handover of Iraq. The other guests are Madeleine Albright, John Podesta and Newt Gingrich. Check local listings for broadcast times in your area.
