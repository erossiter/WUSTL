SENS. REID, MCCONNELL TO LEAD MEETING OF 9/11 WORKING GROUP ON SEPT. 15
  Sens. Harry Reid (D-NV) and Mitch McConnell (R-KY) will hold a meeting of the Senate 9/11 Working Group on Wednesday, September 15, 2004 at 9:00 a.m. in S-211.  
 The meeting is closed to the media. 
   Senators Reid and McConnell, who were asked by Majority Leader Frist and Democratic Leader Daschle to co-chair the group, will address the media at the Ohio Clock immediately following the meeting. 
   The working group is responsible for examining recommendations made by the 9/11 Commission to reform Congressional intelligence oversight, as well as other options to improve oversight of the intelligence community and the Department of Homeland Security. 
   WHO: Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell and Assistant Democratic Leader Harry Reid 
   WHAT: Meeting of the 9/11 working group (closed press) 
   WHERE: Meeting: S-211 at 9:00 a.m. and Media Stakeout: Ohio Clock -- immediately following meeting 
   WHEN: Wednesday, September 15, 2004
