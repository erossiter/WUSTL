SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON U.S. SUPREME COURT DECISION ON HAMDAN CASE
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell released the following statement today on the U.S. Supreme Court decision on terrorist detainees at Guantanamo Bay: 
   "The terrorist detainees at Guantanamo Bay are not ordinary criminals. They are some of the most dangerous people in the world who are committed to murdering Americans. The Congress will work with the Administration to develop the appropriate procedures to ensure that these terrorists are brought to justice in a way that does not endanger our national security."
