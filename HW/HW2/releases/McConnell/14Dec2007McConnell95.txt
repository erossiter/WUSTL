SEN. MCCONNELL: THAPAR CONFIRMED AS FEDERAL JUDGE
  U.S. Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell announced Friday that the Senate confirmed Amul R. Thapar to be Federal Judge of the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Kentucky.
   "Amul Thapar will make an outstanding Federal Judge. He has risen through the ranks to the top of his field to become a stellar prosecutor, all while maintaining his reputation as a man of unquestionable ethics and integrity. Judge Thapar has the wisdom and the judicial temperament to excel as a federal judge, and I appreciate my colleagues for confirming him."
