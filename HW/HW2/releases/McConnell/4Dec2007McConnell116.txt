SEN. MCCONNELL ON BIPARTISAN PASSAGE OF U.S.-PERU TRADE AGREEMENT
  U.S. Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell made the following statement on Tuesday after the Senate approved the U.S.-Peru Trade Promotion Agreement: 
   "A clear bipartisan majority in both the House and Senate approved the U.S.-Peru Trade Promotion Agreement because it benefits U.S. economic growth. Not only will the agreement reinforce the free markets of Peru, it will help grow jobs here at home and continue to open the global marketplace to American products."
