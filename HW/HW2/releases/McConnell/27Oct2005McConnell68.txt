SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON MIERS DECISION TO WITHDRAW HER NOMINATION
  Sen. Mitch McConnell released the following statement today on Harriet Miers' decision to withdraw her nomination to the U.S. Supreme Court: 
   "Harriet Miers is a woman of great ability and character and I respect her decision to withdraw her nomination. Once the President announces a new nominee, I look forward to working with my colleagues to complete the process with an up or down vote in a timely manner."
