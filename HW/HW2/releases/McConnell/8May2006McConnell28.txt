SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON PRESIDENT'S NOMINATION OF GENERAL HAYDEN AS CIA DIRECTOR
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell released the following statement today on the President's nomination of General Michael Hayden as Director of the Central Intelligence Agency: 
   "The President's choice to lead the CIA, General Michael Hayden, has strong credentials and more than 20 years of experience in the intelligence field. I am confident my colleagues on the Intelligence Committee will give his nomination a full and fair hearing."
