SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON SUPREME COURT RULING ON VERMONT CAMPAIGN FINANCE CASE
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell released the following statement today following the Supreme Court's ruling on the Vermont campaign finance law: 
   "This is an important victory for the First Amendment. I applaud the Supreme Court for recognizing that Vermont's limits on contributions and spending violate the free speech rights of those who participate in the political process."
