SEN. MCCONNELL RE-ELECTED TO SENATE LEADERSHIP POST
  Sen. McConnell (R-KY) was unanimously re-elected today as the Senate Majority Whip for the 109th Congress. As Majority Whip, Sen. McConnell is the second ranking Republican in the Senate. He was first elected to this position on November 12, 2002. 
   "It has been an honor to serve in the Senate Republican leadership and I look forward to two more years under the leadership of Majority Leader Frist," said McConnell. "I look forward to working with my colleagues from both sides of the aisle during the 109th Congress to pass legislation that will benefit all Americans." 
   The 109th Congress officially convenes on Tuesday, January 4, 2005.
