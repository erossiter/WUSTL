SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON LAND BEING DONATED FOR 21ST CENTURY PARKS PROJECT
  Sen. Mitch McConnell released the following statement today: 
   "I am excited to hear about the generous donation of over 100 acres for the 21st Century Parks project. When complete, Louisville will have an innovative system of parks unmatched anywhere in the country and I am proud to have played a part in making it happen."
