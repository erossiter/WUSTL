SEN. MCCONNELL COMMENTS ON DEATH TAX RELIEF
  U.S. Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell applauded the agreement reached by Senator Jon Kyl (R-Ariz.) and Senate Finance Committee Chairman Max Baucus (D-Mont.) to move forward with legislation to provide death tax relief: 
   "The initiative shown by Sens. Kyl and Baucus to tackle one of the most pressing issues facing America's small businesses and family farms is an example of the bipartisan spirit we strive for in the Senate. I applaud their agreement to markup legislation to provide death tax relief."
