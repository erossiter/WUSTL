SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF ROB PORTMAN AS DIRECTOR OF OMB
  Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell released the following statement today on the resignation of Rob Portman as White House Director of the Office of Management & Budget (OMB): 
   "Rob Portman has served exceptionally well as White House budget director. During his tenure at OMB, he never forgot that the money we spend is not the government's money, but the people's money. I wish him well in the future and I'm sure his family will be happy to have him back at home in Ohio."
