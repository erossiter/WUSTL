SEN. MCCONNELL INTRODUCES RESOLUTION HONORING VICTIMS OF FLIGHT 5191
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell introduced a resolution last evening honoring the lives and memory of the victims of the crash of Comair Flight 5191. 
   S. Res 558, cosponsored by Senator Jim Bunning, also extends condolences to the families and friends of those who lost their lives in the crash, and recognizes the rescue and safety workers, medical personnel and others who responded to the tragedy and are investigating its causes. 
   The resolution is expected to pass the Senate this week.
