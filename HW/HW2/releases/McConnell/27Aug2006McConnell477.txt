SEN. MCCONNELL ISSUES STATEMENT ON CRASH OF FLIGHT 5191
  U.S. Senate Majority Whip Mitch McConnell released the following statement on the crash of Flight 5191 near the Bluegrass Airport: 
   "This is a day of grief for the families of those killed in the crash and for the people of Kentucky. 
   "I know I am joined by all Kentuckians in extending our sympathy for the victims and their families and loved ones, and in appreciation for all those who are continuing to comfort the families, aiding law enforcement and coordinating transportation."
