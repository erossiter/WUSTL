    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         December 13  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Today s Defeat of the Dorgan Grassley Payment Limit Amendment to the Farm Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Its critical we strike the right balance in the Farm Bill to protect the livelihood of rural America  but also to ensure a safe  abundant and domestically produced food supply for our nation  The current legislation includes significant reforms to eliminate waste and provide farm families with an adequate safety net   Proposals for further payment limits beyond the current compromise in the bill would most certainly tip the scale in the wrong direction  and force Arkansas rice and cotton farmers to give up more than they possibly could  Over the past few days  I have met with a number of my Senate colleagues to explain the real life consequences of such drastic payment limits  and Im pleased those efforts helped lead to the defeat of this measure today  I hope the Senate will move forward with the Farm Bill to provide our farmers with the certainty  structure and investments they need to plan for future crop seasons
