    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 31  2005     Press Release        Pryor to Discuss BRAC Strategy in Texarkana   Senator to Meet with Military  Community and Business Leaders                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON D C     Senator Mark Pryor announced that he will meet with representatives from the Red River Army Depot and Lone Star Army Ammunition Plant to discuss each unit  s strengths and strategies that may be used to avert Secretary Rumsfeld  s realignment and closure recommendations        Pryor said Rumsfeld  s recommendations are only one step in the BRAC process  The BRAC Commission has until October to revise the list and the President will not certify it until November  He has pledged to play an active role in protecting Arkansas   military installations throughout the BRAC process  and he will be collecting information during the trip to submit as part of his testimony to the Commission        On Wednesday  June 1st at 3 30 pm  Pryor will hold a press conference to provide an overview of his visit to Red River Army Depot and discuss the next steps in the BRAC process   Mayor Horace Shipp and former Mayor John Jarvis will also speak  The event will take place at Arkansas City Hall  216 Walnut Street  Council Chambers  in Texarkana  AR       Earlier in the day  Pryor will meet with Colonel Michael Cervone and tour the Humvee Recap Production Line and the Rubber Products and Defense Distribution Red River Center  He will also meet with officials and union leadership from the Red River Army Depot  Lone Star Army Ammunition Plant  Defense Distribution Red River and Red River Munitions Center          30
