    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 8  2007     Press Release        Senators Lincoln  Pryor and Rep  Berry Announce  23 400 for Phillips County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC   U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  AR 01  announced today that the Mid Delta Community Services Inc  will receive  23 400 in federal funding from the USDA Rural Development program   The funding will be used to purchase equipment for local Head Start Learning Centers           Head Start programs are proven to help students stay on track throughout school   said Pryor   I m pleased these grants will allow children in Phillips County acquire critical developmental skills needed to enter kindergarten and learn         The stimulation and care children receive in Head Start programs is essential to preparing them for future success in the classroom   said Lincoln   As a native of Phillips County  I am especially proud these federal dollars will make their way to our Head Start programs in the Arkansas Delta         Head Start is a valuable program that gives children the tools they need to succeed in school   said Rep  Berry   When we help our children get the most out of their education  we are preparing them for life   I am pleased to see these funds going to such a worthwhile program
