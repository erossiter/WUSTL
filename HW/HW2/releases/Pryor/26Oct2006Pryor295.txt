    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 26  2006     Press Release        Pryor to Speak at John Paul Hammerschmidt Post Office Ceremony  Attend Arkansas Black Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON D C     Senator Mark Pryor today said he is looking forward to speaking at the John Paul Hammerschmidt Post Office naming ceremony and attending the 14th Annual Arkansas Black Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony       On Friday  October 27th at 10 30 am CT  Senator Pryor will speak  along with Congressman John Boozman and former Congressman John Paul Hammerschmidt  at the John Paul Hammerschmidt Post Office naming ceremony   The Senate approved legislation for the John Paul Hammerschmidt Post Office on August 3rd and on August 17th the President signed it into law   The event will take place at 215 West Industrial Park Road in Harrison  AR        John Paul Hammerschmidt is an icon in Arkansas  which reflects his dedication to improve the lives of others in our state and nation  I was proud to help shepherd this designation through the Senate in his honor  and I look forward to seeing his name displayed at the Post Office in Harrison     said Pryor         On Saturday  October 28th at 7 pm CT  Senator Pryor will attend the 14th Annual Arkansas Black Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony   This event will take place in the Wally Allen Ballroom of the Statehouse Convention Center in Little Rock  AR
