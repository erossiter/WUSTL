    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 8  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Petty Officer Second Class Kevin R  Bewley                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        I am deeply saddened by the loss of Explosive Ordnance Disposal Technician Second Class  Diver  Kevin Bewley of Hector  Arkansas A Sailor with the Explosive Ordnance Disposal Mobile Unit Eleven  Bewley will be remembered for this friendly demeanor and ability to stay calm and collected in times of crisis His courage and sacrifice in Iraq to our nation will not be forgotten I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Bewley family
