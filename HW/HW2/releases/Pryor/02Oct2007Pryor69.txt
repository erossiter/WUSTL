    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 2  2007     Press Release        Lincoln  Pryor  Ross Announce  690 000 for Southern Financial Partners                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and Representative Mike Ross announced today that Southern Financial Partners will receive a  690 000 grant through the U S  Department of Health and Human Services Office of Community Services    Funds from the grant will be used to build a child care facility at the Clark County Industrial Park  This facility will serve companies that are located in the park and be used as a tool to attract new companies   This child care facility will provide a great relief to our working families and help the Clark County Industrial Park bring in new business  said Lincoln  With these federal dollars  Southern Financial Partners will be able to continue its positive work in community development   This child care facility is a smart investment that will assist hardworking parents  while also increasing productivity at Clark County Industrial Park  said Pryor  I commend Southern Financial Partners for seeking this important funding and putting families first   Rural Economic Development Grants provide necessary tools to help our towns and communities grow and prosper  Ross said  I am proud to join with my colleagues to announce this vital funding for Southern Financial Partners to build a child care facility at the Clark County Industrial Park to assist our working families  and increase the economic development opportunities for the region
