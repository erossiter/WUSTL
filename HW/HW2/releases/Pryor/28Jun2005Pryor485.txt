    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 28  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor   On the Death of John Walton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     I am saddened by the tragic death of John Walton  who will be remembered for his goodwill  service to our country and passion for education  John donated not just money  but his time and energy to expand educational opportunities for low income children and to champion countless other philanthropic programs  I join Arkansans in offering my condolences and prayers to John  s family and friends  including his wife Christy and son  Luke
