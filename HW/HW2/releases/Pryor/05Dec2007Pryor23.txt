    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         December 5  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Specialist Matthew K  Reece                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       I am deeply saddened by the loss of Specialist Matthew K  Reece of Harrison  Arkansas He was a devoted son  husband and father who gave selflessly to others and our nation His sacrifice in Iraq will not be forgotten I join Arkansas in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Reece family
