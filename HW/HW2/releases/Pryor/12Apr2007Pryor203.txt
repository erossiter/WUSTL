    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         April 12  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Death of Private First Class Rodney McCandless                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                I am deeply saddened by the loss of Private First Class Rodney L  McCandless of Camden  Arkansas   McCandless was a positive role model for all those around him   We will never forget his courage  service and sacrifice to our nation   I join Arkansans and the 82nd Airborne Division in offering my prayers and expressing my deepest sympathy to the McCandless family
