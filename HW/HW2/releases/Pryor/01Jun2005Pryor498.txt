    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 1  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Specialist Dustin C  Fisher                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   I extend my heartfelt sympathy to the friends and family of Specialist Dustin C  Fisher  22  of Van Buren who lost his life on May 24  2005 while fighting to bring freedom and security to the Iraqi people    At a young age  Fisher demonstrated courage and sacrifice while serving our nation in the military   I hope Arkansans will join me in praying for the Fisher family
