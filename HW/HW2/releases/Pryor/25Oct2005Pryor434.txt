    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 25  2005     Press Release        Statement On the Passing of Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              I join Arkansans in mourning the loss of Rosa Parks  the   First Lady of civil rights    whose legacy lives on to inspire and teach others about dignity and opportunity   Her courage to sit down for equal rights ignited others to stand up for theirs   As a result of Mrs  Parks   actions  civil rights pioneers such as Reverend Martin Luther King Jr   Daisy Bates and the Little Rock Nine were lifted in their struggle to provide legal equal rights for all Americans   History will remember Rosa Parks as shaking America  s conscience and changing the course of our nation for the better   My thoughts and prayers are with the Parks family
