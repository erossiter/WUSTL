    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 25  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Passage of Immigration Reform                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     I applaud the spirit of cooperation and bipartisanship the Senate showed today in passing comprehensive and effective immigration reform   First and foremost it secures our borders  and second it allows hard working immigrants who want to become citizens the opportunity to fully participate in our economy   This legislation combined some of the best ideas from Republicans and Democrats  and as a result we have produced a tough and smart proposal  It is my hope leaders from the Senate and House of Representatives will continue on this same productive path as they work to reconcile two distinct immigration bills   Time is not on our side  Congress must act responsibly
