    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 5  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Pryor on Senator Craig Thomas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  I am deeply saddened to hear the news of the death of my fellow Senator  Wyomings Craig Thomas was an asset to his country and a resolute advocate for his state  He was a beacon of reason and a fervent supporter of rural America  His contributions to his country will not soon be forgotten and his presence in the Senate will be missed  My prayers and sympathies go out to his wife  Susan  and their family
