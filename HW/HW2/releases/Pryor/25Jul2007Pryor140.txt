    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         July 25  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Minimum Wage Increase                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        This week  the federal minimum wage will increase for the first time in a decade  This raise will make a real difference in the health  welfare and dignity of 13 million working Americans  We must make a hard days work count in America  including for the 113 000 Arkansans who go to work each day to make a better life for them and their family
