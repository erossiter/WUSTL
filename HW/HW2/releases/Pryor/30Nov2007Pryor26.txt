    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 30  2007     Press Release        Lincoln  Pryor  and Snyder Announce  250 000 Upward Bound Grant for ASU Beebe                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington D C   U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and Representative Vic Snyder  AR 02  today announced that Arkansas State University Beebe has been awarded a  250 000 Upward Bound grant    The Upward Bound program is an initiative of the U S  Department of Education that serves high school students from low income families  high school students from families in which neither parent holds a bachelors degree  and low income  first generation military veterans who are preparing for college  The goal of the program is to help students develop the skills and motivation necessary to succeed in postsecondary education  Fifty students are expected to participate in the program at ASU Beebe   Upward Bound is a valuable federal program that helps thousands of Arkansans and people across the country reach their full academic potential  Lincoln said  I commend ASU Beebe for working to secure this federal funding that will allow it to continue to expand educational opportunities to students and veterans across Arkansas   These funds will go a long way to expand educational and professional opportunities for first generation college students  said Pryor  Upward Bounds innovative curriculum is a win win for students and northeast Arkansass workforce   These well deserved Upward Bound funds provide Arkansas State University Beebe a great mechanism to help some Arkansas students reach their full educational potential  This program is a great value to our state  it positively impacts the participants  their families and our society  said Snyder
