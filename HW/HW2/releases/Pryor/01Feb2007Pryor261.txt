    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         February 1  2007     Press Release        Senator Pryor Praises Senate Passage of the Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Mark Pryor today praised Senate passage of the Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007   This legislation will raise the federal minimum wage from  5 15 hour to  7 25 hour in three steps over two years        Hard work should pay off in America  but unfortunately many workers have fallen behind   said Pryor    People who work everyday shouldn t live in poverty  but at the current wage a full time employee who works forty hours a week fifty two weeks a year only takes home  10 700   With the rising costs of day care  groceries  health care and gas  it is nearly impossible to raise a family with this kind of salary   I m very proud to cosponsor and vote for this legislation and believe it is critical to a strong and efficient workforce        The Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007 also includes tax provisions to help small businesses that hire many minimum wage workers   After languishing in the Senate for years  it was passed by the Senate by a vote of 94 to 3 in the first month of the new Congress
