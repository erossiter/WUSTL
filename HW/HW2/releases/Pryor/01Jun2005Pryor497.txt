    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 1  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Specialist Phillip N  Sayles                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Today  Arkansas mourns the loss of Specialist Phillip N  Sayles  26  of Jacksonville  AR who paid the ultimate sacrifice on May 28  2005 to bring democracy and peace to the Iraqi people    Sayles  known among his friends and family as a hard worker and team player  served our nation with honor as part of the Army s 1st Battalion  s Stryker Brigade   I extend my deepest sympathy and prayers to his family and friends
