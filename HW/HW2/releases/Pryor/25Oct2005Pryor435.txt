    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 25  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on the 2 000th American Military Death in Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Today is a bittersweet day in regards to the war in Iraq   While I applaud the official passage of the Iraqi Constitution as an important step forward in Iraq  s transition to independence  I mourn the loss of the 2 000th American soldier to have died in this conflict       American troops are responsible for the freedoms Iraqis enjoy today  including the ability to write and vote on their own Constitution  If not for our soldiers   sacrifices  we may not be applauding the landmark passage of such a document      I join all Arkansans in expressing my deepest sympathy and gratitude to all of families of American servicemen and women who have given life and limb to protect our country and liberate Iraq
