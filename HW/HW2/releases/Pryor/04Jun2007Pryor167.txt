    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 4  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Specialist Erich Smallwood                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       I am saddened by the loss of Specialist Erich Smallwood of Trumann  Arkansas  I know his family and friends will miss his sense of humor and easy going nature  His courage  service and sacrifice to our nation will never be forgotten  I join Arkansans and the 875th Engineer Battalion in offering my prayers and expressing my deepest sympathy to the Smallwood family
