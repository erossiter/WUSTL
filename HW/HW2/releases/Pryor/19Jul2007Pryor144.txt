    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         July 19  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Death of Sergeant John Massey                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I am deeply saddened by the loss of Sergeant John Massey of Higginson  Arkansas  He was a courageous soldier who honorably served with the 39th Infantry Brigade and then volunteered for another tour in Iraq with the 142nd Fires Brigade to fill a shortage  His sacrifice and service to our nation will not be forgotten  I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Massey family
