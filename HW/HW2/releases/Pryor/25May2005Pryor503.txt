    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 25  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On the Passing of Specialist Tyler L  Creamean                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     I join Arkansans in mourning the loss of Specialist Combat Engineer Tyler L  Creamean  of Jacksonville  Arkansas   At 21 years old  Creamean earned a reputation of honor and distinction  saving the lives several of his fellow servicemen and women during Operation Iraqi Freedom   Creamean earned two Purple Hearts while serving our nation with great courage and patriotism  and he will be awarded a third  posthumously        We will never forget Specialist Creamean  s sacrifice  dedication and service   I extend my deepest sympathies and prayers to his family  including his wife  KaMisha Hickman Creamean
