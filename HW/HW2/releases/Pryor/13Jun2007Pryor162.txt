    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 13  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Awarding of the Navy Cross Medal to Corporal Jason S  Clairday                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                I join Arkansans in recognizing the heroic contributions Corporal Jason S  Clairday made to his country through his service in Iraq and posthumous receipt of the Navy Cross Medal  This award is the highest honor given in the Marine Corps  Jason  a Camp Arkansas native  proudly served his country as a Fire Team Leader with the 1st Squad  2d Platoon  Company K  3d Battalion  5th Marines  Regimental Combat Team 1  1st Marine Division based in Pendleton  CA  He successfully rescued an injured comrade and led his squad through hostile environments all while being attacked and wounded by enemy insurgents  He will be remembered for his selfless dedication to his country and fellow soldiers  I share great pride with the Clairday family in the awarding of the Navy Cross Medal to Corporal Jason S  Clairday
