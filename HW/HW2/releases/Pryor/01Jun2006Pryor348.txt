    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 1  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Death of Specialist Bobby West                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               I am deeply saddened by the loss of Specialist Bobby West  a soldier from Beebe  Arkansas  who paid the ultimate sacrifice to defend democracy and freedom throughout the world   Bobby  who wanted to serve his country after the September 11 terrorist attacks  was assigned to the 1st Battalion  22nd Infantry Regiment  1st Brigade  4th Infantry Division in Fort Hood  Texas   He bravely participated in the 4th Infantry  s capture of Saddam Hussein   He will always be remembered for his unyielding patriotism  true courage and devoted service to his country   I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my deepest sympathy to the West family
