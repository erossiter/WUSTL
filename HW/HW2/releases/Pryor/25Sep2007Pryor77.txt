    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         September 25  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Specialist David L  Watson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        I am deeply saddened by the loss of Specialist David L  Watson of Newport  Arkansas A combat medic with the 2nd Infantry Division  Specialist Watson was a natural leader who dedicated his life to serving others His contributions to our country and mission in Iraq will not be forgotten I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Watson family
