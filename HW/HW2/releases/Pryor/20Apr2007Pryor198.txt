    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         April 20  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Helen Robson Walton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Helen Walton touched countless lives within her community  across Arkansas and throughout the world   Helen was a natural leader who recognized the importance of putting others before self   Her generosity of time  energy and goodwill towards others is an example we all can follow    I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and condolences for the Robson and Walton families during this difficult time
