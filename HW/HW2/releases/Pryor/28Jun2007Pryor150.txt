    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 28  2007     Press Release        Senators Lincoln  Pryor and Rep  Berry Announce  2 Million LOAN for England                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  AR 01  announced today that the city of England  located in Lonoke County has received a  2 1 million dollar loan from the USDA Rural Development program  The funding will be used to construct a new treatment plant to replace the existing 30 year old plant    Cities throughout Arkansas depend on federal dollars to improve their communities  said Lincoln  I applaud theCity of England for using these funds to build a much needed treatment plant thatwillenhance thewater qualityfor its residents   An efficient water treatment plant is an essential need for the residents of Lonoke County  said Pryor  This loan is fitting example of how federal  state and local leaders can work together to make critical investments in our infrastructure   These federal dollars will allow the City of England to replace an outdated water treatment plant and provide farms and families with clean water to sustain their community  said Berry  Ensuring our public infrastructure is up to date and running smoothly is vital for continued economic development
