    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 11  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On the Loss of 3 Arkansas Soldiers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I extend my heartfelt sympathy and condolences to the families and friends of Specialist Jeremy W  McHalffey of Mabelvale  AR  Specialist Joshua S  Marcum of Evening Shade  AR  and Specialist Jimmy D  Buie of Floral  AR who died last week in the fight to bring peace and democracy to the Iraqi people  The 39th Infantry Brigade continues to make tremendous contributions in the war in Iraq  but the loss of these three Arkansans remind us of the heavy price that is paid for freedom   I join Arkansans in mourning the loss of Specialists McHAlffey  Marcum  and Buie and pray for the safe return of all our brave and selfless soldiers
