    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 24  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Passage of the Emergency Supplemental Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        American forces in the battlefield should always be our number one priority   I m pleased leaders on both sides of the aisle finally put partisanship aside to move this funding forward   With the President s signature  our troops will receive much needed replacement munitions  body armor  IED detection equipment  vehicle and aircraft equipment  as well as five new C 130Js         Additionally  this bill includes a well deserved increase in the federal minimum wage    from  5 15 an hour to  7 25 over two years   A hard day of work should count  it s critical to a strong and efficient workforce
