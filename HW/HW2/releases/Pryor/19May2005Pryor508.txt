    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 19  2005     Press Release        Pryor  s Mobile Office Coming to Northwest Arkansas  Senator  s Staff to visit Newton  Madison  Carroll and Boone Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON    Senator Mark Pryor today announced that members of his staff will travel in his Mobile Office to four northwest Arkansas counties in an effort to reach and help constituents in the region       Mobile Office staffers are trained to assist constituents who may be having problems with the Veterans Administration  Medicare  Social Security and other services involving the federal government          My Mobile Office helps me to better serve Arkansas by keeping me closely informed about the concerns and problems residents are having in every county  every city  and every town and community     Pryor said     I hope residents will visit my Mobile Office to share their concerns and allow my staff to help resolve problems they may be having involving the federal government         Pryor said his Mobile Office will make a stops in
