    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 13  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on Senate Minority Leader Reid  s Visit to Hope                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      I  m glad Senator Harry Reid was able to join Congressman Mike Ross and me to take a look at the FEMA homes at the Hope Airport  and to see with his own eyes the bureaucratic red tape that is denying shelter to thousands of families in need in the Gulf Coast   His influential position in the U S  Senate will provide some additional momentum to our efforts to get FEMA to provide these homes to the people who need them   Senator Reid also praised the many Arkansans who welcomed evacuees with open arms and graciously provided for their needs after Hurricanes Katrina and Rita
