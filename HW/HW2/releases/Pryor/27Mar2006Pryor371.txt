    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 27  2006     Press Release        Lincoln  Pryor   Berry Announce over  2 1 Million for Area Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON  D C      Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  D AR  1st  announced today that the 1st Congressional District will receive  2 194 012 from the Federal Aviation Administration to make improvements at local airports  The grants were awarded to five airports throughout the region        The Federal Aviation Administration grants include        Arkansas County  Stuttgart Municipal will receive  1 149 500 to preserve and extend the useful life of this critical airport pavement       DeWitt Municipal will receive  126 250 to increase the airport s ability to accommodate the parking of based aircraft and itinerant aircraft      Clay County  Corning Municipal Airport will receive  150 000 to construct apron pavement needed to access an aircraft storage hangar on the airport       Lee County  Steve Edwards Field in Marianna will receive  451 250 to construct taxiway pavement that will eliminate the need for back taxiing on the runway       Lonoke County  Carlisle Municipal Airport will receive  317 012 to extend the useful life of the taxiway pavement     30
