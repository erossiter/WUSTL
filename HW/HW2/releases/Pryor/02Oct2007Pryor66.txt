    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 2  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Sergeant First Class James D  Doster                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             I am deeply saddened by the loss of Sergeant First Class James D  Doster of Pine Bluff  Arkansas Doster proudly served in our nations military for 17 years His brave contributions and sacrifice in Iraq will not be forgotten I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Doster family
