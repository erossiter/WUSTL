    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 10  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Senator David Pryor  s Health                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Last evening  Senator David Pryor was treated at UAMS for chest pain  His condition is stable  However  it is anticipated that he will undergo extensive open heart surgery tomorrow  Senator Mark Pryor  his son  released the following statement        My father s doctors are prescribing the necessary treatment for a full recovery  It is a comfort to know that he is under the care of some of best doctors in the nation at UAMS  Since Dad s last heart episode  14 years ago  he remains physically fit and watches his diet and exercise  He has been a model patient   Of course  bed rest will be the most difficult aspect for him with such an active schedule   On behalf of my mother  Barbara  and brothers  David and Scott and our families  we ask for your continued prayer  support and friendship
