    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 11  2005     Press Release        Pryor  Lincoln  and Snyder Announce More Than  247 000 For UALR                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington    U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   and Mark Pryor  D Ark    along with Representative Vic Snyder  D AR  2nd   announced today that the University of Arkansas at Little Rock  UALR  has received a  247 013 grant from the U S  Department of Education       The funds have been secured through the U S  Department of Education  s Strengthening Institutions Program  which helps eligible institutions of higher education to become self sufficient and expand their capacity to serve low income students by providing funds to improve and strengthen their academic quality  institutional management and fiscal stability  UALR will use these funds to implement new learning community programs focusing on incoming freshman
