    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         December 19  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Passage of the Omnibus Spending Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              The Omnibus Appropriations bill reflects the priorities of Arkansans by making smart investments that foster strong and healthy communities throughout our state  Because infrastructure and economic development are so often intertwined  I am particularly proud of the congressional delegations successful efforts to fund critical transit  water and military construction needs  Moreover  families from small and large towns alike will benefit from specific earmarks intended to enhance education  agriculture  health care and first responder services in our state    While I support the priorities set under this legislation  I believe passing individual appropriation bills is a more responsible path for spending taxpayer dollars  I am hopeful when Congress and the White House return for the New Year  progress will take the place of partisanship
