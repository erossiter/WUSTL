    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 9  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on the Inauguration of Governor Mike Beebe                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          From humble beginnings to high office  I am delighted that my friend  Mike Beebe  will take the oath of office as the Governor of Arkansas today   He is a living example of the American dream and what heights can be reached when one seeks to serve others   I am confident that Mike will make Arkansas proud and I look forward to his implementation of innovative programs and policies that create new horizons for the Arkansas economy  education and justice systems       I also want to congratulate my friends Lieutenant Governor Bill Halter  Attorney General Dustin McDaniel  Treasurer Martha Shoffner  and all the other constitutional officers and legislative leaders in the General Assembly   I look forward to working with them to improve Arkansas  future
