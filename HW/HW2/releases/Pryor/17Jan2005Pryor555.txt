    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 17  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Celebrating Martin Luther King  Jr  Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Martin Luther King  Jr  Day is a time to honor the greatest champion of racial equality who taught a nation    through compassion and courage    about democracy  nonviolence and racial justice   On this day  we commemorate Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr   s life and legacy  celebrate the values of equality  reconciliation and service he inspires  and recommit to fulfilling the dream he sought for every family         We are closer today to realizing Dr  King  s dream than we were 35 years ago  but even the best innovations of the 21st Century have not eliminated poverty  war and inequality   Service  Dr  King said  is the great equalizer   We honor Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr  on this day  but I also want to commend those Americans who follow his principles and are committed to strengthening our communities  empowering individuals and bridging barriers
