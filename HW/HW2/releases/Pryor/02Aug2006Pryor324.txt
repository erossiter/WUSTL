    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         August 2  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Death of Paul Eells                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           For nearly thirty years  Paul Eells had a special way of making every Razorback fan feel as if they had the best seat in the house  I will miss listening to him scream   Touchdown Arkansas    and   Holy Smokes     but even more so his genuine personality and love for Arkansans   I join families throughout the state in lifting up in our prayers his wife Vickie and their three children
