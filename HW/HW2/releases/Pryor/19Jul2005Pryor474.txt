    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         July 19  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Pryor  On the Nomination of John G  Roberts Jr  to the Supreme Court                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Judge John Roberts  Jr   if confirmed by the Senate  will make decisions as a Supreme Court Justice that will impact the lives of every Arkansan  In that vein  and according to the Constitution  I will fully consider a number of qualities a Supreme Court Justice should embody  including his credentials  judicial temperament  and ability to be fair and impartial   I take seriously the Senate  s confirmation process  and I plan to proceed with diligence and an open mind  I would like to sit down with Judge Roberts in the coming weeks to discuss his judicial record
