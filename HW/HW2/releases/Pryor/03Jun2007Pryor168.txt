    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         June 3  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on Corporal Zachary D  Baker                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         I join all Arkansans in mourning the loss of Corporal Zachary D  Baker who died in Iraq on Memorial Day  May 28th  while rescuing his fallen comrades  Zachary  a Vilonia native  proudly served his country as a member of the First Calvary Division based in Texas  He will be remembered for his selfless dedication to his country  fellow soldiers  and in bringing independence to the Iraqi nation  I extend my most heartfelt sympathies and prayers to his family and friends
