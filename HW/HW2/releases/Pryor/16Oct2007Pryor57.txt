    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 16  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Miller Nomination for the U S  District Court for the Eastern District                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        I am pleased to hear from the White House today that President Bush intends to nominate Judge Brian Miller for the U S  District Court for the Eastern District  Judge Miller brings with him the qualifications  judicial temperament and experience to serve in this position  During the course of this nomination process  I have heard nothing but good reports from the legal community regarding his reputation as a fair and impartial judge  I look forward to introducing Judge Miller to my colleagues and advancing this nomination through the Senate confirmation process
