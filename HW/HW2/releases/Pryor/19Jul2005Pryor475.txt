    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         July 19  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Lt  Gov  Win Rockefeller                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        My thoughts and prayers are with Lieutenant Governor Rockefeller  who I hope will overcome illness with strength and courage    As a cancer survivor  I understand the struggle he faces and the difficualt road to recovery  and the importance of having family and friends to lend support and encouragement    I join Arkansans throughout the state in offering my best wishes for a speedy recovery
