    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         April 17  2007     Press Release        Pryor Receives Award from U S  Chamber of Commerce                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Recognized for Economic Growth Record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON D C    Senator Mark Pryor today received the U S  Chamber of Commerce s Spirit of Enterprise Award for his strong support of a pro economic growth legislative agenda       Pryor said he is honored to receive the Spirit of Enterprise Award from the Chamber  but even more appreciative of the working relationship he has built with the Chamber of Commerce and its Arkansas membership    Pryor said his work to promote tax relief  energy independence  pension reform and port security in the Senate were considered for his nomination          I appreciate the Chamber s hard work to help Arkansas businesses succeed through legislative advocacy  training and outreach programs   Pryor said   I am honored to be recognized for this award and look forward to a continued partnership with Chamber members to bring good paying jobs and economic opportunities to our state        Pryor said the award is given out by the Chamber of Commerce to Senators and Congressmen who show a strong pro business record  voting in support of the Chamber s position on at least 70 percent of their key priorities  For the second session of the 109th Congress  the Chamber presented awards to 58 Senators  including only 5 Democratic members  Pryor compiled a 75  score with the Chamber last year        Sen  Pryor proved to be a valuable ally to the business community last year  supporting measures to stimulate job creation and spur economic growth   said Tom Donohue  Chamber president and CEO   The business community is grateful for Mark s dedication to a pro growth agenda and we are proud to present him with this award
