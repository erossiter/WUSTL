    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         February 6  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the President  s Budget                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           President Bush and Congress must return fiscal discipline to our budget vocabulary  but I am discouraged by initial observations that our farmers  seniors and children have been disproportionately targeted to pay for our nation  s debts         We are at a critical crossroads concerning the strength of our nation now and for the future  a juncture that calls for the sacrifice  compassion and responsibility of every American   While I support President Bush  s commitment to homeland security and to the stability in Iraq and Afghanistan  I will oppose his deep cuts to economic development  education and health care programs that help lift up rural America  To add salt to the wound  when our farmers were expecting disaster relief  this Administration proposes major cuts to agriculture programs that serve as their safety nets        As the budget process moves forward  I stand ready to make difficult decisions about our country  s priorities  but I will fight to ensure that sacrifices are spread evenly and not solely at the expense of our nation  s future            30
