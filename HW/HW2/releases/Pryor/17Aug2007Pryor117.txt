    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         August 17  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Sergeant Bradley Marshall                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         I am deeply saddened by the loss of Sergeant Bradley W  Marshall of Little Rock  Arkansas He honorably served with the B Battery  2nd Battalion  377th Parachute Field Artillery Regiment  25th Infantry Division He will always be remembered for his selfless sacrifice and service to our nation I join Arkansas in offering my prayers to the Marshall family
