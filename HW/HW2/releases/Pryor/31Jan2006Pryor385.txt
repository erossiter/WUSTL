    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 31  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  on the Passing of Coretta Scott King                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               I join Arkansans in mourning the loss of Coretta Scott King  one of our nation  s greatest champions of the civil rights movement   Mrs  King will always be remembered for her tireless commitment to the ideals of civil and human rights that she and her husband  Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr   personified throughout their lives   Her leadership helped our nation make tremendous social and legal strides toward equal rights over the last half century   Although we mourn her departure today  she will continue to serve generations to come as an inspiration to all those who seek to promote justice and morality in this world   My thoughts and prayers are with the King family
