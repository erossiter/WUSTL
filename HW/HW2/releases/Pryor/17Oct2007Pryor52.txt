    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         October 17  2007     Press Release        Lincoln  Pryor Meet Arkansas Students at 1st Annual Latino Youth Summit in Washington  DC                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor this week welcomed Arkansass representatives to the first annual Senate Democratic Latino Youth Summit in Washington The two day summit gave approximately 150 young Latinos from across the country the opportunity to interact withSenators and discuss issues such as education  immigration  and civil rights   Arkansans participating in this weeks events included Rafael Arciga Garcia  a student at the University of Arkansas  Fayetteville  majoring in political science  Alejandro Aviles  a student at the University of Arkansas  Fayetteville majoringin sociology  and Maricella Garcia  who is completing a joint degree program at the Clinton School of Public Service and the Bowen School of Law at the University of Arkansas  Little Rock
