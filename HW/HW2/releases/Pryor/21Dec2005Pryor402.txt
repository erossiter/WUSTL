    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         December 21  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on Opposing Cloture to the Department of Defense Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                While our troops are sacrificing life and limb in places like Iraq and Afghanistan  they are being used as political pawns in Congress to advance the priorities of big oil and drug companies   Our servicemen and women did not sign up to fill the coffers of special interests  and I cannot in good conscience sign off on this abuse of power   Raw partisan politics has no place in legislation intended to provide much needed equipment and resources to our military        Arkansas troops know that I have fought tooth and nail to provide them with the best training  equipment and quality of life possible  and I am confident my actions in the Senate reflect this commitment   Congress can and must pass a better  cleaner defense spending bill before Christmas  Senator Ted Stevens  R AK  has assured the Senate one will be presented  and this bill will not compromise our soldiers   commitment to our nation
