    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 11  2005     Press Release        AR Delegation Announce Grants for Area Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senators Mark Pryor  and Blanche Lincoln  along with Congressmen Marion Berry  D 1st   John Boozman  R 3rd   Mike Ross  D 4th  and Vic Snyder  D 2nd   today announced multiple fire departments have been awarded approximately  400 000 in grants from the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program       This program provides support to fire departments by providing the tools and resources necessary to ensure both the safety of department personnel and the general public         These grants are used for training  wellness and fitness  firefighting equipment  personal protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities              Rocky Point Volunteer Fire Department  Judsonia  AR    23 616 for operations and firefighter safety       Jonesboro Fire Department  Jonesboro  AR    156 100 for operations and firefighter safety       Bergman Volunteer Fire Fighters Association  Harrison  AR    11 295 for operations and firefighter safety       Pansy Volunteer Fire Department  Rison  AR    48 600 for a firefighting vehicle       Pine Bluff Fire Department  Pine Bluff  AR    144 251 for operations and firefighter safety       Upper White Oak Volunteer Fire Department  Chidester  AR    16 200 for a firefighting vehicle      These awards are part of the 38th round of grant announcements          30
