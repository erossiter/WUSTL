    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         September 14  2005     Press Release        On Today  s U S  District Court Ruling on the Pledge of Allegiance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON D C     A U S  District Court judge today declared Wednesday that the reciting of the Pledge of Allegiance in public schools is unconstitutional  ruling that the pledge s reference to one nation   under God    violates school children s right to be   free from a coercive requirement to affirm God          Senator Pryor responded today to the ruling        I was very disappointed to hear of this ruling and I strongly disagree  I believe this judge got it completely wrong and that this issue must be fast tracked to the Supreme Court for their immediate consideration
