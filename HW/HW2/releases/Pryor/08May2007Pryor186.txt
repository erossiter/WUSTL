    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         May 8  2007     Press Release        Pryor s Mobile Office Coming to Town                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator s Staff Headed to Northeast Arkansas to Assist Individuals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON    Senator Mark Pryor today announced that members of his staff will travel in his Mobile Office to Beebe  Heber Springs  Marshall  Salem  Cherokee Village  Batesville and Newport  in an effort to reach and help constituents in the region       Mobile Office staffers are trained to assist constituents who may be having problems with the Veterans Administration  Medicare  Social Security and other services involving the federal government         My Mobile Office helps me to better serve Arkansas by keeping me closely informed about the concerns and problems residents are having in every county  every city  and every town and community   Pryor said    I hope residents will visit my Mobile Office to share their concerns and allow my staff to help resolve problems they may be having involving the federal government       Pryor said his Mobile Office will make the following stops         Wednesday  May 23rd       9 10 am  Beebe City Hall Community Room  321 North Elm Street  Beebe  AR       11 am 12 pm  Heber Springs Municipal Building  1001 West
