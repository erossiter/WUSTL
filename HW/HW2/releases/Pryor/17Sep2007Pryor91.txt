    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         September 17  2007     Press Release        Lincoln  Pryor  Ross Announce  230 000 for Warren                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with Representative Mike Ross  AR 04  announced Monday that the city of Warren has been awarded a  230 000 grant through the Department of Commerces Economic Development Administrations  EDA  Grant Program   The funds will go towards the completion of the wastewater pre treatment facility in Warren  by increasing the capacity necessary for the retention  expansion and attraction of new businesses and jobs  This federal investment willhelp expandinfrastructure critical to the creation of new jobsin the area  said Lincoln Icommend the citizens ofWarren for seeking these additional resources to improve their community  Adequate and affordable wastewater infrastructure is key to continued economic growth  said Pryor  This critical investment is important to the standard of living for Warren residents and their surrounding environment  These vital funds will help to create 100 new jobs and bring new economic development opportunities to Warren  Ross said  I am proud of the work the City of Warren and the Southeast Arkansas Economic Development District is doing and look forward to working with the city as it continues to grow and prosper through exciting economic development initiatives like this EDA grant   The EDA serves as a venture capital resource to meet the economic development needs of distressed communities throughout the United States  EDAs mission is to lead the federal economic development agenda by promoting innovation and competitiveness  preparing American regions for growth and success in the worldwide economy
