    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 1  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Private Landon S  Giles                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       I join Arkansans in extending my heartfelt sympathy to the friends and family of Specialist Private Landon S  Giles  19  of Gurdon  AR who lost his life while fighting to bring freedom and security to the Iraqi people    At a young age  Landon dedicated himself to serving our nation in the military  and I am grateful for his courage and sacrifice
