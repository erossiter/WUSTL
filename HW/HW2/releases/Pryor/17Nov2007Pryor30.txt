    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 17  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On Second Lieutenant Stuart F  Liles                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I am deeply saddened by the loss of Second Lieutenant Stuart F  Liles of Hot Springs  Arkansas Liles was a devoted father and good friend to all those fortunate enough to know him His courage and sacrifice in Afghanistan will never be forgotten I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and expressing my sympathy to the Liles family
