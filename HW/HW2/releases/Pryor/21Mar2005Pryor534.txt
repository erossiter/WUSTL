    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 21  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor   On Congressional Action Taken on Behalf of Terri Schiavo  March 21  2005                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        I support Congressional action taken over the weekend that allowed a federal judge to review the case of Terri Schiavo  The legislation offers an opportunity to gain additional perspective on the right way to proceed and ensures that every option was explored in coming to a final decision
