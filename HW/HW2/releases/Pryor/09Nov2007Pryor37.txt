    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 9  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Nomination of Attorney General Nominee Michael Mukasey                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        America needs a strong Attorney General who will put justice above politics and who will not bow to pressure  even if it comes from the Oval Office  I want an Attorney General who takes seriously Article II  Section 3 of the Constitution  which requires the President to take care that the laws be faithfully executed  I met with Mr  Mukasey earlier this week and studied his credentials and comments about torture  While I believe he is knowledgeable and experienced  I do not have sufficient confidence in him to support him for Attorney General
