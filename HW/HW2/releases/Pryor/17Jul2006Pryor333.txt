    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         July 17  2006     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Passing of Lieutenant Governor Win Rockefeller                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Win Rockefeller set high standards for public service  philanthropy and leadership  and his long standing commitment to the educational and recreational activities of children will continue to touch generations of Arkansans to come   I pay tribute to Lt  Gov  Rockefeller  s strength of character and compassion for those in need  and I join Arkansans in offering my prayers and sympathy to the Rockefeller family
