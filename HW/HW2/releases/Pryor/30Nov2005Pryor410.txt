    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 30  2005     Press Release        Pine Bluff Arsenal to Receive  1 2 Million for Storage Facility                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON D C     Senators Mark Pryor and Blanche Lincoln  along with Congressman Mike Ross today announced  1 2 million will be allocated to the Pine Bluff Arsenal to rebuild its depot storage facility       Members of the delegation said the depot storage facility will include controlled humidity warehouse space and a fire protection system  They added the previous storage facility was destroyed by a fire  which could create a hardship on the installation  s limited storage space   The funding will come from the Army  s discretionary funds for military construction projects         Ensuring the facilities and equipment at the Pine Bluff Arsenal are in first rate condition is critical for the safety of the installation and community  and building this top notch warehouse will allow the Arsenal to continue its record of placing safety first     Pryor said     I will continue to be a strong advocate on behalf of the Arsenal to ensure it has the resources necessary to remain safe and vital             Helping provide the necessary federal resources to the Pine Bluff Arsenal so it  s a safe place to work and not a hazardous threat to the communities that surround it remains one of my top priorities   Lincoln said   I  m pleased to have played a role in securing this important funding  as it will go a long way towards better protecting our citizens         This is great news for the Pine Bluff Arsenal and the Jefferson County community   said Ross    I am pleased to have worked with Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor to help secure this funding for the Pine Bluff Arsenal   Most importantly  these funds will provide for a safe work environment for employees and will help to guarantee continued excellence second to none at the Pine Bluff Arsenal
