    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         August 26  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the BRAC Decision for the 188th                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  This is great news for Fort Smith   The Commission recognized the military value of the Fort Smith Air Guard and has given the base a new mission with aircrafts that have a critical and continuous role  ensuring Fort Smith will be active in our national defense for years to come  The A 10 Warthog is vital in supporting our warfighters on the ground today        I have said all along that the 188th is in a unique position to take on a new  long term mission  if necessary  and I  m pleased the BRAC Commission agreed with me about its versatility   I applaud the community in Fort Smith and my Congressional colleagues on today  s hard fought victory             30
