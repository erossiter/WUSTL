    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         November 9  2006     Press Release        Media Advisory   Marshals Service to Visit Fort Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senior Group to Look at City for National Museum                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   FORT SMITH  Ark      U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  and U S  Representative John Boozman are assisting with a visit by several officials representing the U S  Marshals Service  as part of the city  s effort to be selected as the site for the U S  Marshals Service national museum     The group comprised of active duty and retired Marshals  Chief Deputies  and historians  will be in Fort Smith beginning November 13        The visiting officials will be available for media interviews during a reception on Wednesday  November 15th from 5 00    7 00 p m   Attending the reception will be many local and regional officials who support having the national museum in Fort Smith      Lincoln  Pryor and Boozman have been working with the City of Fort Smith on this project since early 2004        Media Availability     WHAT  Clayton House Reception    WHO  Local  County and State government officials      Historic District and Chamber of Commerce representatives     WHEN  Wednesday  November 15  2006  5 00    7 00 p m     WHERE  Clayton House  514 North 6th Street  Fort Smith    For additional information on the visit  contacts include   Mr  Claude Legris  Chairman of the Fort Smith Marshals Museum Steering Committee     479  783 8888 and U S  Marshal Richard O  Connell  Western District of Arkansas    479  783 5215
