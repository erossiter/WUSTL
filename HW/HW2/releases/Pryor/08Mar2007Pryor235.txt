    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 8  2007     Press Release        Pryor Works Every Angle to Expedite Aid to Dumas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Coordinates Housing Efforts  Introduces Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON D C    Senator Mark Pryor today called on the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  to declare 30 trailers and manufactured homes as surplus in order to expedite their delivery to Desha County  In addition  he introduced two measures to the pending Homeland Security legislation that would release immediate disaster aid and temporary housing to the area         People s livelihoods have been destroyed and all FEMA can tell me is that their hands are tied by one regulation or another  It s not true  FEMA has the resources and the ability to help  they simply won t   Pryor said    In the long term  I plan to straighten out this agency s priorities  But today  I m focused on delivering much needed assistance to Desha County and I will continue to work every angle possible to get the job done         Pryor said he has been coordinating with several federal agencies to declare trailers and manufactured homes sitting in Hope  AR  surplus and to get them moving 166 miles away to Dumas  AR in order to provide temporary housing  Pryor added that he is coordinating these efforts with the congressional delegation  Delta Regional Authority  Governor Beebe  local officials and private industry        Pryor also introduced two amendments to the Improving America s Security Act  which is currently being debated before the full Senate  The first amendment would clearly state that the Secretary of Homeland Security has the authority to move any of the 8 000 vacant homes in Hope  AR to Dumas  AR  The second measure declares the tornados in Dumas a major disaster  enabling federal disaster aid to be allocated        It shouldn t take an act of Congress to provide emergency relief to those in need  Yet  once again  FEMA has failed in its mission  refused to listen to reason  and must be forced into doing its job through legislation   Pryor said   One way or another  we re going to help the families and businesses recover from this tragedy
