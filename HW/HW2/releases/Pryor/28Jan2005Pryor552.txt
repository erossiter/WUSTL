    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 28  2005     Press Release        A Statement by Senator Mark Pryor  On Lance Corporal Brian Chase Hopper                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             I join Arkansans in mourning the loss of 31 marines  including Lance Corporal Brian Chase Hopper of Wynne  AR  who lost their lives in a helicopter accident in western Iraq   Brian showed great courage and patriotism throughout his tour  His death reminds us of the high price our brave men and women are paying to spread democracy and freedom to the people of Iraq    I extend my deepest condolences to his family and loved ones  and I pray for the safe return of his brother  Patrick  also serving in Iraq
