    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         August 19  2005     Press Release        Pryor and Lincoln Announce Selection Process for U S  Senate Youth Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON D C     Senators Mark Pryor and Blanche Lincoln today announced that the United States Senate Youth Program  USSYP  has begun its statewide selection process for next year   Two outstanding Arkansas high school students will be selected to spend a week in Washington observing the federal government in action       The two Arkansas students will join 102 other delegates March 4 11  2006 for the program s 44th Annual Washington Week   The student delegates will visit Capitol Hill  the White House  the Pentagon  Supreme Court and other Washington institutions   They will also meet with Members of Congress  Administration officials and other leaders        Delegates must be juniors or seniors that are elected student officers for the 2005 2006 academic year   Eligibility considerations may be made for students who hold positions in district  regional or state level civic and education organizations          The U S  Senate Youth Program is a unique experience for students that are interested in politics and public service to get an up close view of the operations of the federal government     Pryor said     I encourage interested students to take advantage of this opportunity           It s essential that we involve our young people in government   Lincoln said   The U S  Senate Youth Program is important as an on site introduction to our federal government to encourage students to take a more active role in public service        Pryor and Lincoln said that each of the student delegates will receive a  5 000 college scholarship  in addition to the all expenses paid trip to the nation  s capital         The delegate selection is administered by each state  s chief school officer in cooperation with high school principals   Interested students should contact their high school principal or Ms  Maggie Herrick at the Arkansas Department of Education at  501  682 5303   The application deadline is September 30  2005   Names of students selected will be announced mid December
