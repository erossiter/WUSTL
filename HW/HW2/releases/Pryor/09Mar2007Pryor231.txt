    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 9  2007     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on U S  Attorneys                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    I am very pleased to learn the Bush administration has changed course and announced their support of the Leahy Feinstein bill which restores and requires the transparent confirmation process for all U S  attorneys   By bypassing the constitutional process  the President has cast a dark cloud over the process of replacing U S  attorneys   The Senate confirmation process is vitally important to assuring that qualified individuals are selected for a position which requires the exercise of independence and judgment   This issue is larger than party affiliation or any single appointment  it is about our Constitution and the rule of law
