    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         April 27  2006     Press Release        On Transforming FEMA into an Effective Agency                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Over the past seven months  I have worked with my colleagues on the Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee to investigate the preparedness and response failures surrounding Hurricane Katrina  For me  the twenty two hearings we held  including one in Hope  AR  were not about placing blame  but on how Congress can transform FEMA into a capable and effective agency   This undertaking will entail bold steps    requiring more than qualified leadership and adequate funding       As I continue to review the recommendations put forth by the Committee leadership today  I will also be consulting with former FEMA director James Lee Witt  who demonstrated that FEMA can provide our nation with superior emergency management before  during and after disaster strikes
