    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         August 9  2005     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on the Loss of John H  Johnson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Today the state of Arkansas mourns the loss of one of its most prominent and successful sons   My thoughts and prayers are with the family and friends of John H  Johnson  a native of Arkansas City  who passed away yesterday after a long and fruitful life       Johnson overcame a childhood of poverty to become a world renowned publishing and advertising icon   His accomplishments have been recognized through his many accolades  including the Magazine Publisher  s Association Publisher of the Year Award  the Advertising Hall of Fame Award  the National Business Hall of Fame Award  the Greatest Minority Entrepreneur Award  the Horatio Alger Award  the NAACP Spingarn Award and the Arkansas Business Hall of Fame Award   He is also the recipient of the highest civilian honor in the United States  The Presidential Medal of Freedom         John H  Johnson was an exemplar of triumph over adversity and of leadership that could move mountains  and today I join the many Arkansans that are paying tribute to his memory
