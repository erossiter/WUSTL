    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         March 29  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor on Passage of the Lobbying Reform Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              The Senate today added new layers of transparency into the relationship between Congress and lobbyists  and I was pleased to help shape this bill as a member of the Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee   Elements included in this bill to increase accountability  eliminate gifts and meals from lobbyists and require more frequent and detailed disclosure by lobbyists and Members of Congress are important steps toward cleaning up how Congress works         At the same time  the trust of the American people must be earned  and these reforms alone will not restore public confidence in our government   I am hopeful campaign finance reform will become a priority as well as a renewed spirit in the Senate  House of Representatives and White House to pass legislation that places people before politics and special interests
