    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         January 12  2005     Press Release        Pryor  Lincoln Urge President to Preserve C 130J Contracts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON    Senators Mark Pryor and Blanche Lincoln today joined a bipartisan effort to encourage President George W  Bush to reconsider his budget plans to terminate the C 130J cargo aircraft contracts for the Air Force and Marines          Pryor and Lincoln joined 22 of their Senate colleagues in a letter to the President expressing their concerns that ending the C 130J program would leave the Air Force with an aging and inefficient fleet and thereby compromise long term plans to improve military readiness and capability    Both Senators said they would continue to urge the President to reinstate these funds and ensure the modernization of the affected flying units  including the Little Rock Air Force Base
