    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         December 7  2006     Press Release        Statement by Senator Mark Pryor On the Death of J B  Hunt                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Johnnie Bryan Hunt  known to all of us as  J B    will be remembered for his innovation  determination  and realization of the American dream   His legacy of leadership and philanthropy has been instrumental to the growth and prosperity of northwest Arkansas   J B  Hunt Trucking  Inc  is not only a giant in the trucking industry  but it is a model of success in American business   I join Arkansans in offering my condolences and prayers to J B   s family and friends  including his wife Johnelle and their children  Jane and Bryan
