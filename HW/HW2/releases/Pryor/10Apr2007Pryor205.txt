    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         April 10  2007     Press Release        Pryor s Mobile Office Coming to Town                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator s Staff Headed to North Arkansas to Assist Individuals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON    Senator Mark Pryor today announced that members of his staff will travel in his Mobile Office to Greenbrier  Clinton  Harrison  Jasper  Yellville  Mountain Home  Melbourne and Mountain View  in an effort to reach and help constituents in the region         Mobile Office staffers are trained to assist constituents who may be having problems with the Veterans Administration  Medicare  Social Security and other services involving the federal government           My Mobile Office helps me to better serve Arkansas by keeping me closely informed about the concerns and problems residents are having in every county  every city  and every town and community   Pryor said    I hope residents will visit my Mobile Office to share their concerns and allow my staff to help resolve problems they may be having involving the federal government
