    Senator Mark Pryor Press Releases                                                         September 25  2006     Press Release        Pryor Celebrates Hispanic Heritage Month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             During Hispanic Heritage Month  I  d like to honor the extraordinary contributions Latino families make to the United States year round   Hispanic American  s hard work and commitment to the community make our nation stronger and more vibrant each and everyday   Many people don t realize that Latinos are the most highly decorated group in American military history   I value these contributions  and I want to provide opportunities in education  health care and the workforce to help Latino families continue to make a lasting impact on our nation  s fabric
