                                      Bunning Launches New Look Website             Tuesday  July 17  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today launched his upgraded Senate website  The new and improved site went live at noon today  The site will provide Kentuckians with a more interactive look at the latest news and information on legislation Senator Bunning is working on in Washington  With this new site constituents will be able to access information specific to each Congressional District through a new interactive map  The people of Kentucky will have instant and easy access to Senator Bunnings views  speeches  columns  legislation and press releases  as well as a new flash photo gallery  Furthermore  Kentuckians will have the unique opportunity to sign up for the Senators newsletter   I am excited today to share this new website with the people of Kentucky  said Bunning  I believe it is important that all Kentuckians have quick and easy access to what I am doing here in Washington on behalf of the Commonwealth  This new site includes several more interactive features that I hope folks will find fun and informative when they are following the latest happenings in Congress  For those needing help contacting any federal agency  applying for federal funds  or looking for help with federal legislation  Senator Bunnings site offers helpful links and tips  Finally  any Kentuckian wishing to visit our Nations capital will discover a wide variety of information on tours   To visit Senator Bunnings new website go to
