                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Class Action Reform Bill             Thursday  February 10  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement supporting the bipartisan Senate passage of the Class Action Reform Bill   The Senate overwhelmingly approved the bill by a vote of 72 26       I am proud to support this bipartisan bill today that will help put an end to frivolous class action lawsuits  while still keeping open protections and options for plaintiffs who have real grievances   This is a huge step forward in our effort to enact commonsense legal reforms here in Congress       Everyone knows that billions and billions of dollars have been wasted in fighting frivolous lawsuits  and by reforming the system we can free up more capital for businesses to spend on investment and job creation                                                         February 2005 News Releases
