                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Preserve America Grants For Northern Kentucky              Friday  September 28  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the Department of Interiors National Park Service in partnership with the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation has awarded two grants totaling  152 500 for two projects in Kenton and Boone County    Northern Kentucky is rich in history  said Senator Bunning  These funds will help preserve that history for future generations    130 000   For an urban walking and driving cultural heritage tour of Covington   22 500   For the installation of plaques  wayfinding signs  and an informational kiosk to benefit the Burlington Historic District                                                         September 2007 News Releases                                                                                                  United States Senate                                                                                                               United States Senate                                                                                                                                                                    United States Senate                   Fort Wright  Kentucky
