                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Mine Safety Law             Thursday  June 15  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement on the Mine Safety Bill that was signed into law by President Bush       This is a good day for our miners and their families   This new mine safety law is a tribute to the hard work our Kentucky miners do and the dangers they face to get coal to our nation for energy consumption   This law will offer new protections to the workers who go into the mines everyday by ensuring that they have the most up to date technology and equipment they need for a safer work environment   I was honored to stand with President Bush at the White House today as he signed the Mine Safety Bill into law                                                         June 2006 News Releases
