                                      Bunning Statement On Confirmation Of Judge Alberto Gonzales To Be United States Attorney General             Thursday  February 3  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today offered the following statement on Judge Alberto Gonzales who was confirmed in the Senate by a vote of 60 36           I am proud today to support the nomination of Judge Alberto Gonzales to be our next Attorney General   Judge Gonzales has served his country well as President Bush  s White House Counsel  and he will continue to serve his nation well as the Attorney General          Following September 11th Judge Gonzales was instrumental in formulating the legal strategy to combat the War on Terror   During these times of war  America needs a steady and honorable Attorney General and Judge Gonzales fits that mold        As the first Hispanic to serve as Attorney General  Judge Gonzales will bring with him the values and hard work he learned as a boy   I am honored to have supported his nomination and he will be an effective Attorney General who will serve the United States with great honor
