                                      Statement Of Senator Jim Bunning On President Bush  s State Of The Union Address             Tuesday  January 31  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning  R KY  offered the following comments regarding tonight  s address by President Bush to the joint session of Congress         President Bush delivered a strong message to Congress that we need to put partisanship aside and work together to meet the important challenges before our nation       We are fighting a war and we need to work together to provide our troops with the tools they need to win  We need to finish the job in the War on Terror to keep our families safe from terrorist attacks       To do this  we need to make sure the Patriot Act is renewed and that our law enforcement and intelligence agencies are able to monitor terrorist  s and Al Qaeda  s communications       And we also must continue our work in helping to secure Iraq  We have made good progress over the last year  but there is still more to be done  And tonight the president restated his commitment to helping them secure their country and continuing on the path of freedom       On the domestic side  President Bush addressed the need for permanent tax relief to help create jobs and keep our economy growing       We also need to make sure all Americans have access to affordable health care  The Medicare Prescription Drug Plan has proven to lower drug costs for our seniors and those needing them the most  But more can be done   such as providing Health Care Savings Accounts and getting rid of frivolous lawsuits which skyrockets some health care costs       The president tonight laid out some important challenges for us   And I  m ready to work together with my colleagues to accomplish them
