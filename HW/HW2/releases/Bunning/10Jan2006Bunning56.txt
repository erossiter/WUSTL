                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               PACRO Receives  248 000 Federal HUD Grant             Tuesday  January 10  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  has released a grant of  248 000 to the Paducah Area Community Reuse Organization  PACRO  to locate and prepare water wells for the Regional Industrial Park in Western Kentucky             I am happy to announce this important grant that has been released to PACRO     said Senator Bunning    The funds will help the infrastructure for the Regional Industrial Park  and will help bring in new businesses to the Purchase Area          Today  we received notice that the  248 000 grant from Senator Jim Bunning has been released     said John Anderson PACRO Director     We want to express our appreciation to the Senator and his hard working staff for a job well done  These funds will be used to locate and prepare wells that will provide water to the Regional Industrial Park located in the Purchase Region of western Kentucky  This is the first step towards attracting an industry to the region  Without the help of the Senator and his staff we could not have taken this step to help restore the economy to our region     Anderson concluded                                                      January 2006 News Releases                                                                                                                       Floor of the United States Senate
