                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Confirmation Of General Michael Hayden To Be Director Of The Central Intelligence Agency             Friday  May 26  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of Air Force General Michael Hayden who was confirmed by the Senate today to be Director of the Central Intelligence Agency   The Senate confirmed General Hayden by a vote of 78 15              General Hayden has extensive experience in the intelligence business and I am confident he will do a fine job as Director of the CIA   I can think of no one better qualified to lead the agency as we press forward in the War on Terror and prepare for both current and long term threats of the new century                                                         May 2006 News Releases
