                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over 1 4 Million In Federal Transportation Funding For E T  Breathitt Parkway Extension             Wednesday  March 8  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Transportation  DOT  Federal Highway Administration has approved a discretionary grant of  1 488 million to the Commonwealth of Kentucky for the E T  Breathitt Parkway Extension               The federal funds awarded in this grant will be a great help for this vital transportation project in Christian County     said Bunning    As everybody knows the Fort Campbell area is highly congested  with the construction of the E T  Breathitt Parkway extension the people of western Kentucky will be able to travel more safely         The Christian County  E T  Breathitt Parkway Extension will provide a direct link from the Fort Campbell military installation to Kentucky  s Interstate and Parkway System   It also provides a north south link for the motoring public within the Delta Region and between Indiana and Tennessee                                                      March 2006 News Releases
