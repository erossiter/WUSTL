                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Blasts TVA For Latest Rate Hike             Monday  February 13  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the Tennessee Valley Authority  s  TVA  decision to raise rates by 9 95 percent as of April 1  2006       I am outraged that TVA has raised its rates nearly 18 percent in less than a year   The TVA directors are living in a fantasy world and do not realize the strain their huge rate increases impose on the pockets of Kentucky  s families   TVA has some of the highest electricity rates in Kentucky and the latest rate increase of 9 95 percent comes during uncertain economic times which could seriously undermine the economies of the Kentucky TVA region         This rate increase comes just after TVA top officials received huge bonuses   TVA had promised a decade ago to reduce its debt by half this year   Today  it is nowhere near that predicted reduction with its debt now at  26 billion   I am hopeful that Congress will soon confirm the new TVA board members to help get TVA  s finances in better order                                                         February 2006 News Releases
