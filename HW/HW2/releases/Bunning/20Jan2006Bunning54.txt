                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over  8 5 Million Grant For State Veterans Cemetery in Radcliff  Kentucky             Friday  January 20  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the Department of Veterans Affairs has awarded a grant of  8 521 865 to the Commonwealth of Kentucky for the establishment of the Central Kentucky State Veterans Cemetery in Radcliff  Kentucky                 I am happy to announce this grant for our veterans and military families     said Bunning     This cemetery will serve as a place where Kentuckians can go to remember and honor our soldiers who sacrificed so much for their beloved county         The federal funds will be used for the necessary cemetery infrastructure including administration and maintenance buildings  assembly area  committal service shelter  and entrance feature   The grant will also be used for design costs and the equipment needed to operate the cemetery                                                      January 2006 News Releases                                                                                                                       Floor of the United States Senate
