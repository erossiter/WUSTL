                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Passage Of Mine Safety Bill             Wednesday  May 24  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement supporting the Mine Improvement and New Emergency Response Act that passed the Senate unanimously       As the demand for coal grows rapidly  the brunt of the work falls upon the shoulders of our Kentucky miners to get the coal to our nation for energy consumption   I am pleased the Senate passed a bill today that takes big steps to help ensure our mine workers have the technology and equipment they need for a safer work environment         I hope that the House will follow our lead here in the Senate and pass a similar bill so we can send something to the President soon that will help bring mine safety up to date before we see anymore tragic accidents like the one we saw in Kentucky this past weekend                                                         May 2006 News Releases
