                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Supports Emergency Supplemental Bill             Thursday  May 24  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of the Emergency Supplemental Bill that passed the Senate by a vote of 80 14  The bill now goes to the Presidents desk for his signature  It has been 108 days since President Bush sent this vital piece of legislation to Congress and finally my colleagues on the other side of the aisle have seen the light  said Bunning  This supplemental provides the means necessary to fund our men and women in uniform as they wage the War Against Terrorism  More importantly it does not micro manage the war  and allows our commanders in the field to make strategic decisions based on sound judgements and not partisan politics  I believe it is important that the Iraqi government is held accountable and that is why I support the use of benchmarks which are included in this bill to ensure our commitment is not open ended  Even though the supplemental still has pork projects offered by my Democrat friends I still support the overall bill because it gets the needed funds to our troops                                                      May 2007 News Releases                                                                                                         U S  Senate Floor
