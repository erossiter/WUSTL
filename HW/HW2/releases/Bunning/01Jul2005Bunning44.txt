                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On The Retirement of Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O  Connor             Friday  July 1  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement on the retirement of Justice Sandra Day O  Connor from the U S  Supreme Court        I commend Justice O  Connor on her 24 years of dedication to the court and thank her for her tremendous service to our country during that time   As the first woman to serve on the bench she has proven herself to be a brilliant jurist and strong defender of the Constitution   I wish her the best of luck as she moves forward with her life                                                         July 2005 News Releases
