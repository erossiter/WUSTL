                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement Regarding FY06 Budget             Monday  February 7  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the president  s budget for Fiscal Year 2006       President Bush today delivered a fiscally sound budget that will fund top priorities like fighting the war on terror  protecting the homeland  and promoting economic growth and job creation  while at the same time holding the line on spending in order to meet the president  s goal of cutting the deficit in half by 2009             We will have some tough decisions to make in crafting the budget in Congress this year  but I am hopeful that we can put partisanship aside and focus on what  s right for America   As a member of the Senate Budget Committee I will work hard to make sure that we can pass a responsible budget that meets our nation  s most pressing needs                                                         February 2005 News Releases
