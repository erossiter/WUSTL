                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Federal Funds For Red Lick Fire Department             Wednesday  May 24  2006                   today announced that The Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG   Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  known as SAFER  of the Department of Homeland Security  DHS  has awarded  12 250 to the Red Lick Volunteer Fire Department  In 2005  Congress implemented the SAFER program as a way to contribute federal money to fire departments across the country  Out of the nearly 3 000 applicants hoping to receive a grant  only 125 departments were awarded funding     The Red Lick Fire Department will utilize the grant money in the recruiting processes of their department  assuring that prospective firefighters are given every advantage  The grants will also help place emphasis on an integral part of the firefighting profession by ensuring 24 hour staffing for departments and meeting the basic requirements of firefighter standards       The recruiting process is an integral part of a fire department  and with the help of this grant the Red Lick Fire Department will be able to recruit more effectively     commented Senator Bunning    These funds  made possible by the SAFER program  are instrumental in the creation and preservation of our Kentucky volunteer firefighters who give so unselfishly of themselves to protect our Commonwealth                                                         May 2006 News Releases
