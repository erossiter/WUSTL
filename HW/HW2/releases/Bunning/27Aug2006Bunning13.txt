                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Crash Of Comair Flight 5191             Sunday  August 27  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the crash of Comair Flight 5191 near the Blue Grass Airport in Lexington       I want to extend my deepest sympathies to those families who lost loved ones in the tragic plane crash in Lexington   This is a very sad day for all those affected by this crash and my thoughts and prayers are with the victims and their families                                                         August 2006 News Releases
