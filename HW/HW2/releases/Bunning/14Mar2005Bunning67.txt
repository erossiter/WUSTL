                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces  6 6 Million Veterans Administration Contract For the University of Louisville             Monday  March 14  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Veteran Affairs  VA  has awarded a  6 6 million contract to the University of Louisville        Our veterans deserve the best care possible  said Bunning   The partnership between the Veterans Administration and the University of Louisville will enable Kentucky veterans to utilize the University of Louisville  s top notch staff to meet their radiological needs   This is a win for our veterans and the University of Louisville          These federal funds will be used by the University of Louisville to staff and operate the Radiology Department at the VA Medical Center in Louisville  Kentucky   The contract will provide diagnostic interpretation of radiological examinations  as well as consultation related to diagnostic imaging to the other members of the medical staff   The services will improve patient care and early diagnosis rates for cases that are referred and require immediate intervention   The Veterans Administration will realize a cost savings in excess of  168 700 over the three year period of the contract in comparison to the current costs                                                      March 2005 News Releases
