                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement Regarding FY07 Budget             Monday  February 6  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the president  s budget for Fiscal Year 2007       Overall  this is a fiscally responsible budget that continues the president  s pro growth policies that have been vital to expanding our economy and creating new jobs  while at the same time keeping us on track to cut the deficit in half by 2009   It addresses priorities such as winning the war on terror  improving health care  energy independence and strengthening our competitiveness with math and science education initiatives       As a member of the Senate Budget Committee  I will work hard to make sure that we can pass a responsible budget for FY 2007   We  re going to have some tough decisions to make  but I am hopeful we can put partisanship aside and focus on what  s right for America                                                         February 2006 News Releases
