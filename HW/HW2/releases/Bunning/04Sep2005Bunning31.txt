                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On The Death Of United States Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist             Sunday  September 4  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the death of U S  Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist         I am deeply saddened by the death of Chief Justice Rehnquist  It is an enormous loss for the High Court and for the nation  He was a great American   My thoughts and prayers go out to his family during this very difficult time            Chief Justice Rehnquist served this country with great distinction first as an Associate Justice for 14 years and then as a Chief Justice for 19 years  Appointed Chief Justice by President Ronald Reagan  Chief Justice Rehnquist was a great stalwart in preserving and protecting our Constitution   He was without question one of the most influential chief justices in our nation  s history                                                         September 2005 News Releases
