                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over  1 2 Million In Federal Funding For Madisonville Community College             Wednesday  October 19  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Labor  DOL  has approved a grant of  1 210 008  to Madisonville Community College for the nursing career ladder project       Training the next generation of heath care workers is vital as the baby boom generation grows older     said Senator Bunning     I am glad to announce these funds to help Madisonville Community College as they continue to cultivate the next generation of nurses        These federal funds will be used to prepare high school students for early entry into MCC nursing programs and add state of the art medical and instructional technology and equipment to better prepare nurses for immediate employment  as well as provide scholarship funds for low income recruits to enroll in the program                                                      October 2005 News Releases
