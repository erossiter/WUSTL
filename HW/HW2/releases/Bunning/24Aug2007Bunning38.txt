                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Nearly  1 8 Million in Federal Health and Human Services Funds for Kentucky Health Centers          Washington    Friday  August 24  2007    U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the Health Resources and Services Administration which is part of the Department of Health and Human Services has awarded four health centers a total of nearly  1 8 million in federal funding   Health centers are an important component in any community s health network  said Senator Bunning  They serve many in the community who are uninsured or underinsured  regardless of their ability to pay  This money will go a long way in helping these centers provide top quality medical care to Kentuckians  I am pleased the Department of Health and Human Services has recognized these four centers in Kentucky   Health Centers receiving funds    733 000 Southeast Kentucky Family Practice Clinic in Corbin    600 000 Triad Health Systems in Gallatin County    375 000 Fairview Community Health Center in Bowling Green    79 760 for a planning grant for the Kentucky Housing Corporation                                                             August 2007 News Releases                                                                                                           Fort Wright  Kentucky
