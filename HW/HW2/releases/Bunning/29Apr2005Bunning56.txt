                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On FY06 Budget             Friday  April 29  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the Conference Report to the Fiscal Year 2006 Budget Resolution which passed the Senate by a vote of 52 47         By passing this budget today we have taken a step forward in meeting some of the challenges facing our nation right now     said Bunning     This is a fiscally responsible budget that will cut the short term deficit in half while at the same time recognizing important priorities like fully funding the President  s defense and homeland security requests and implementing sensible  job creating tax policy   Now  we must move forward using these guidelines as road map to help make America a better  more secure country for future generations to come                                                         April 2005 News Releases
