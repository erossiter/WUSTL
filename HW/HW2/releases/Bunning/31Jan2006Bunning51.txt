                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Confirmation Of Judge Samuel Alito To The U S  Supreme Court             Tuesday  January 31  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the confirmation of Judge Samuel Alito to be Associate Justice to the Supreme Court of the United States   Judge Alito was confirmed by a vote of 58 42          Throughout his career Judge Alito has shown that he is committed to the rule of law and judicial restraint     said Bunning     He understands that each branch of government has a unique role to play and his record shows that he is a fair and impartial judge who has great respect for the law and Constitution  Judge Alito is supremely qualified and I am proud to have voted in support of his nomination to the Supreme Court today                                                         January 2006 News Releases                                                                                                         Floor of the United States Senate
