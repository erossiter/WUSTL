                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Nearly  2 5 Million In Federal Labor Grant For Kentucky Auto Workers             Thursday  February 23  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Labor  DOL  has approved a grant of nearly  2 5 million to the Kentucky Community and Technical College System        As technology progresses in the auto industry it is vital that Kentucky auto workers remain above the competition     said Senator Bunning    These federal funds will help our auto workers hone their skills in an ever evolving field  and will help continue to grow the auto industry here in the Commonwealth        The grant will allow the Kentucky Community and Technical College System to establish an Advanced Manufacturing Learning Center where a wide variety of state of the art training in all facets of automotive manufacturing will be offered to workers in central Kentucky   The project will train more than 3 600 individuals over a three year period  including 750 participants who will earn certificates and 120 secondary level students participating in dual credit classes or co op internships                                                      February 2006 News Releases
