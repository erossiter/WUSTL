                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Federal Grant For Kentucky State University              Monday  July 23  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  has approved a grant of  300 000 for the Kentucky State University Cooperative Extension Program   I am pleased to announce this grant for Kentucky State University and the farmers of the Commonwealth  said Bunning  Our farmers here in Kentucky are some of the best  and this USDA grant will help continue that great tradition   The federal funds will be used to assist socially disadvantage farmers to improve their enterprises that are direct marketed to insure a safer local food supply                                                       July 2007 News Releases                                                                                                   United States Senate                                                  United States Senate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               United States Senate                                                   United States Senate
