                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Passage Of The Port Security Act             Thursday  September 14  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of the Port Security Act  which overwhelmingly passed the Senate by a vote of 98 0   This legislation has already been approved by the House and will now go to conference where members of the House and Senate will iron out details of the final bill        The Port Security Act will help put in place new policies and tools that will provide a much needed layer of security at our nation  s ports and will allow us to more thoroughly inspect any cargo coming into this country through the international supply chain   By passing this bill today we have taken another strong step forward in our effort to make America more secure from the threat of terrorist attack                                                         September 2006 News Releases
