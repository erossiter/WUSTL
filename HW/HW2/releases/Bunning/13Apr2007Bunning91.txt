                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over  6 9 Million Grant For State Veterans Cemetery In Northern Kentucky             Friday  April 13  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  has awarded a grant of  6 926 935 to the Commonwealth of Kentucky for the establishment of the Northern Kentucky State Veterans Cemetery in Williamstown  Kentucky  The cemetery will serve approximately 40 000 veterans and dependents   I am happy to announce this grant for our veterans and military families in northern Kentucky  said Bunning  This cemetery will serve as a place where Kentuckians can go to remember and honor our soldiers who sacrificed so much for our country   The federal funds will be used for the necessary cemetery infrastructure including administration and maintenance buildings  assembly area  committal service shelter  and entrance feature  The grant will also be used for design costs and the equipment needed to operate the cemetery                                                      April 2007 News Releases                                                                                                                       Alexandria  Virginia
