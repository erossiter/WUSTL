                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Applauds China  s Decision To End Its Decade Long Practice Of Currency Manipulation             Thursday  July 21  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding China  s decision to end its decade old peg to the dollar and let the Yuan fluctuate versus a basket of currencies            This is a good first step  but it is only a first step   We  ll see if this equates to a change in the cost of goods purchased from China which has undermined our American manufacturing and products base   Hopefully  China will continue to make progress towards a free flowing currency and in fulfilling its responsibilities under the WTO to be a more fair and open trading partner in the global economy                                                         July 2005 News Releases
