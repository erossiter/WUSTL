                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Resignation Of Rob Portman From OMB              Tuesday  June 19  2007                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement on the resignation of Rob Portman as White House Director of the Office of Management and Budget  OMB      During his time as Director of OMB  Rob Portman displayed the same dedication to fiscal discipline that he has shown throughout his career in public service  He is one of the smartest guys I know and while his leadership at OMB will be missed  he has made a decision at this point in his life to spend more time with his wonderful family    his wife Jane and his three teenagers  Rob is a good friend and I wish him the best of luck in whatever challenge he decides to take on next                                                      June 2007 News Releases                                                                                      United States Senate
