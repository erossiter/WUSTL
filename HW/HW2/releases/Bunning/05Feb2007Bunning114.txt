                                      Bunning Statement Regarding FY08 Budget             Monday  February 5  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the Presidents budget request for Fiscal Year 2008   The President has presented a budget that helps continue pro growth policies to keep our economy strong while at the same time reducing spending to help balance the budget  If we are going to get our financial house in order we must reduce the unsustainable growth of entitlement spending  If we dont we are going to spend ourselves into oblivion   The Presidents budget also includes important funding for the War on Terror  particularly the Presidents new strategy for Iraq  All too often when debating the current War in Iraq and the larger War on Terror  some of my colleagues criticize the cost of the war without proposing a feasible alternative   I believe that we need to focus our complete attention on the accounting of the operations in Iraq and the wider campaign against terrorism instead of threatening to cut off funding for our troops  This does little other than provide ammunition for our enemies and lower the morale of our troops  We must ensure that our troops have the necessary means to complete their mission quickly and successfully   I look forward to working with my colleagues on the Budget Committee to make sure that our budget addresses these challenges in a fiscally responsible manner                                                      February 2007 News Releases
