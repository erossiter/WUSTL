                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On BRAC Recommendations             Friday  May 13  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement on the base closing recommendations made by the Department of Defense to the BRAC Commission        I  m happy to see that the Department of Defense continues to see solid value and future promise in Kentucky for the Army  s needs   It looks like Fort Campbell continues to stand strong and Fort Knox stands to play a larger role as the Army realigns and transforms itself for the 21st Century       Not only will Fort Knox now receive FORSCOM dollars by gaining a combat brigade  but by it becoming a fixed operational base  the surrounding communities will be enriched from soldiers and their families becoming a more permanent part of the Fort Knox area   This means more home buying  economic development  and local tax revenues for the needs of the area communities       I am also pleased that this proposal would move four C 130 s to Louisville  s Air National Guard Station   This is one of the best air guard units in the country and these new aircraft will make them even stronger                                                         May 2005 News Releases
