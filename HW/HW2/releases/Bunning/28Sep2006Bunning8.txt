                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Passage Of The Military Commissions Act Of 2006             Thursday  September 28  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of the Military Commissions Act  which passed the Senate by a vote of 65 34   The legislation will be signed by the President this week        I am pleased the Military Commissions Act has passed   This legislation will give the necessary tools to our intelligence officers so they can do their jobs more efficiently   The terrorist interrogation program has been vital to preventing a major terrorist attack on American soil  and by passing this legislation today America will be safer from future attacks                                                         September 2006 News Releases
