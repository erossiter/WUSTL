                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over  1 8 Million In FEMA Funding For Emergency Food And Shelter Program             Thursday  December 29  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has awarded 77 counties in Kentucky over  1 8 million in federal funds as a part of FEMA  s Emergency Food and Shelter  EFS  Program       I am pleased to announce these FEMA funds for Kentucky  s most needy     said Bunning     As we approach the winter months it is vitally important that Kentuckians in need of food and shelter receive the proper support that this program provides        The Emergency Food and Shelter Program  EFS  is a Federal program administered by the Department of Homeland Security  s Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  and has been entrusted through the Stewart B  McKinney Homeless Assistance Act of 1987  PL 100 77    to supplement and expand ongoing efforts to provide shelter  food and supportive services    for homeless and hungry individuals nationwide                                                      December 2005 News Releases
