                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senate Approves FY06 Interior Spending Bill Includes  500 000 in federal funds for Adair County Water Infrastructure Projects             Wednesday  June 29  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced he secured  500 000 in federal funding for the City of Columbia  in Adair County   for water infrastructure projects  The funds are from the Environmental Protection Agency  s State and Tribal Assistance Grant Program  and is part of the Fiscal Year 2006 Department of Interior Appropriations Bill  which passed the Senate today by a vote of 94 0   The bill now goes to a conference committee where final details will be ironed out            I am pleased to have secured these funds for the City of Columbia     said Bunning     The people of Adair County deserve clean and safe drinking water and these funds will provide the necessary resources to help meet those needs                                                         June 2005 News Releases
