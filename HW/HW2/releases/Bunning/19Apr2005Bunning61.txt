                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Pope Benedict XVI             Tuesday  April 19  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today offered the following statement regarding the election of his eminence Pope Benedict XVI       I am overjoyed by the news that the College of Cardinals have elected a new Pope for the Roman Catholic Church     said Bunning     Pope Benedict XVI will face many challenges ahead  but with the prayers of the faithful he will be guided by the will of God   Mary and I will be praying for our  new Pontiff during this joyous time                                                         April 2005 News Releases
