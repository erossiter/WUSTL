                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Statement By Senator Jim Bunning On The Passing Of Former Congressman Gene Snyder             Saturday  February 17  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the death of former Kentucky Fourth District Congressman Gene Snyder   I am deeply saddened by the death of my friend and mentor Gene Snyder  During his time in the U S  House of Representatives  Congressman Snyder worked tirelessly to better the lives of the people he served in the Fourth District of Kentucky  He will be greatly missed  Mary and I send our deepest condolences to Pat and the Snyder family  I hope that all Kentuckians will keep the Snyders in their thoughts and prayers during this difficult time                                                      February 2007 News Releases
