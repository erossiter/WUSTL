                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On The Nomination Of Samuel Alito To The United States Supreme Court             Monday  October 31  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding President George Bush  s nomination of Judge Samuel Alito for Associate Justice of the United States Supreme Court          Judge Alito is an experienced judge with a distinguished legal career that has prepared him well to sit on the Supreme Court of the United States   He understands the rule of law  respects the Constitution  and is known as someone who will not legislate from the bench   I am encouraged by his nomination and I look forward to his fair and dignified confirmation process  followed by a timely up or down vote on the Senate floor                                                         October 2005 News Releases
