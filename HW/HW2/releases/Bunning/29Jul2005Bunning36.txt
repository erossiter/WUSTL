                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senate Approves Conference Report To FY06 Interior Spending Bill Includes  500 000 in federal funds for Adair County Water Infrastructure Project             Friday  July 29  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced he secured  500 000 in federal funding for the City of Columbia  in Adair County  for a water infrastructure project in the Conference Report to the Fiscal Year 2006 Department of Interior Appropriations Bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 99 1   The funds are from the Environmental Protection Agency  s State and Tribal Assistance Grant Program       This money will provide key resources for the city of Columbia to ensure that the people of Adair County have access to clean  safe drinking water and I am pleased to have secured these funds in the Interior Conference Report we passed today     said Bunning      The FY06 Interior Spending Bill was approved by the House yesterday and now goes to President Bush for his signature                                                      July 2005 News Releases
