                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Federal Funds For The Paducah Police Department             Monday  June 13  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today announced that the United States Department of Justice  s Bureau of Justice Assistance awarded  31 430 in federal funding under the FY 2005 Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program to the Paducah City Government        This is good news for the people of Paducah     said Bunning     The funds contained in this grant will provide essential crime fighting tools to the Paducah Police Department         The federal funds will be used by the Paducah City Government to purchase digital in car video cameras for the Paducah Police Department                                                      June 2005 News Releases
