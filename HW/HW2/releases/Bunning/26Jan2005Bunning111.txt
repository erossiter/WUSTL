                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Confirmation Off Dr  Condoleezza Rice To Be United States Secretary Of State             Wednesday  January 26  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today offered the following statement on Dr  Condoleezza Rice who was confirmed in the Senate by a vote of 85 13       I am proud today to support the nomination of Dr  Condoleezza Rice to be our next Secretary of State   Dr  Rice is without question one of the most qualified people ever to be chosen as Secretary of State         America is currently involved in an effort to bring freedom  democracy  and individual rights to a region of the world that has never known any of those things   Dr  Rice understands that those changes will not happen in just a few months or years   It will take decades  if not generations  to see the Middle East transform into a peaceful and stable region   The next few years are critical to that effort  and I believe Dr  Rice is the right person to lead our relations with Middle Eastern nations as well as all nations around the world       Dr  Rice is a brilliant  honorable woman  and she  ll do a fine job as our next Secretary of State in helping to promote democracy and spread freedom throughout the world                                                         January 2005 News Releases                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON                 WASHINGTON
