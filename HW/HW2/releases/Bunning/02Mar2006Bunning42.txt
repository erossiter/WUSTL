                                      Bunning Hails Senate Reauthorization Of The Patriot Act As Victory For The Security Of American Citizens             Thursday  March 2  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the Patriot Act which was reauthorized in the Senate by a vote of 89 10       This is a huge victory for the security of our country and our efforts to stay ahead of the terrorists     said Bunning     After two months of Democrat obstruction tactics we have finally fulfilled our obligation as elected officials to protect our fellow citizens by renewing the Patriot Act       Since the attacks of September 11  2001  the Patriot Act has been instrumental in defending Americans from those hateful individuals who wish to harm us   In that time we have captured over 400 terrorist suspects  with more than 200 terrorists being convicted or pleading guilty in investigations helped by the Patriot Act       The list of successes in deterring terrorism under the Patriot Act is endless   There are more terrorists behind bars and not advancing plots against the United States because of the tools given to law enforcement by the Patriot Act   Because of the Patriot Act we can all continue to enjoy our freedom and liberty   I like to say that civil liberties don  t mean much when you are dead  And there are many Americans alive today who would not be if those terrorists were successful in carrying out their attacks                                                         March 2006 News Releases
