                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On The Perry County Mine Tragedy Urges Congress To Take Action             Thursday  February 16  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the mine accident in Perry County  Kentucky                I would like to offer my deepest condolences to the family and friends of the Kentuckian who was killed in the mine accident in Perry County today     said Bunning    Mining is inherently dangerous  but as we begin the 21st century we must do more to minimize those risks  Congress needs to act to help improve our mining technology and ensure that miners work in the safest environment possible                                                         February 2006 News Releases
