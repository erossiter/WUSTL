                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Confirmation Of Mike Duncan To Sit On The Board At TVA             Friday  March 3  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the confirmation of Mike Duncan to sit on the board of the Tennessee Valley Authority          Residents of the TVA region can be thankful for the Senate confirmation of Mike Duncan to the TVA Board of Directors     said Bunning     He  ll face some tough challenges  but I  ve known Mike for a long time and there is no one I would trust more to help TVA get its house back in order   He is a leader and a man of great integrity   And I know Mike will always make his decisions based on what is in the best interest of Kentuckians who rely on TVA for their services                                                         March 2006 News Releases
