                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Passage Of The Defense Authorization Bill             Thursday  June 22  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of the Fiscal Year 2007 Defense Authorization Bill  which passed the Senate overwhelmingly by a vote of 96 0          Our men and women currently fighting in the Global War on Terror deserve our full support  and with the passage of this bill our commitment to them is solidified   The recent successes in Iraq  including the death of al Qaeda in Iraq leader Al Zarqawi and the installation of Prime Minister al Maliki  s Cabinet have been positive steps in the right direction  This bill is vital in funding our troops for the challenges that still lie ahead                                                         June 2006 News Releases
