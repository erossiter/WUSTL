                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Immigration Statement             Friday  May 18  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning  R KY  today issued the following statement regarding the announced immigration deal   I did not agree to any immigration deal and was not part of the negotiations  From what I have heard about the bill  it gives amnesty to the estimated 12 5 million illegal immigrants in this country  I will carefully review the text of the bill and if it does include any form of amnesty  I will not support it  Illegal immigrants should not be able to cut in line in front of the millions of other immigrants who are waiting to become citizens lawfully  Our country desperately needs a better immigration policy  We must meet the needs of our farmers and businesses through a temporary guest worker program that protects American jobs without providing amnesty                                                      May 2007 News Releases                                                                                                                       U S  Senate Floor
