                                      Bunning Statement On The Confirmation Of General Petraeus To Be Commander Of The Multi National Forces In Iraq             Friday  January 26  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding the confirmation of General David Petraeus to become Commander of the Multi National Forces in Iraq  The Senate overwhelmingly confirmed General Petraeus by a vote of 81 0   I congratulate General Petraeus on his confirmation  I am proud to support him as our new Commander in Iraq  He is a personal friend of mine and I can think of no one better to be leading our troops as they carry out the Presidents new plan   In 2004 during a trip I took to Iraq I had the opportunity to visit General Petraeus in Mosul where he was in command of the 101st Airborne Division out of Fort Campbell  Kentucky  He is one the most impressive military leaders I have ever met  I am confident that with General Petraeus experience  leadership skills and judgment he will prove to be an outstanding Commander  Next week I will have the opportunity to meet with General Petraeus before he leaves on this important mission  I want to wish him God speed  I look forward to seeing the progress we can make in Iraq under his leadership as we continue to defeat the terrorists and win the War on Terror
