                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces Over  100 000 In Federal Funds for Fort Wright Fire and EMS Department             Wednesday  November 15  2006                   today announced that The Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG   Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  known as SAFER  of the Department of Homeland Security  DHS  has awarded  103 500 to the Fort Wright Fire and EMS Department  In 2005  Congress implemented the SAFER program as a way to contribute federal money to fire departments across the country       The hiring process is an integral part of a fire department  and with the help of this grant the Fort Wright Fire and EMS Department will have the necessary resources to hire the best candidates possible     said Bunning    These funds are instrumental in helping local fire departments meet their staffing needs to protect the citizens of the Fort Wright community                         These federal funds will pay for part of the salaries of newly hired firefighters over a 5 year program period                                                      November 2006 News Releases
