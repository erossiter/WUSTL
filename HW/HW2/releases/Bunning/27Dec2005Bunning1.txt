                                      Department Of Energy Awards Major Environmental Activities Contract At Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant             Tuesday  December 27  2005                  Senator Bunning announced today that the U S  Department of Energy  DOE  has awarded Paducah Remediation Services  LLC  PRS  a  191 6 million small business contract to perform environmental remediation and waste management activities at DOE  s Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant in Paducah  Ky         Energy Secretary Bodman called me today to give me this news   I have been working with him and the DOE to help push this process forward to get an award out in a timely and fair manner     said Senator Bunning       The contract has been awarded  and now we just need to make sure this work gets done   Those living and working in and around Paducah deserve a better environment for their families   If expectations are not met  then I will continue to use my influence on the Energy Committee to bring accountability to the process and the contract     said Senator Bunning     Senator Bunning sits on the Senate Committee on Energy which has oversight jurisdiction over the Department of Energy  including activities at the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant   He has in the past held Senate Energy Committee field hearings in Paducah on matters and activities related to the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant     According to DOE  the new small business contract will run through September 30  2009 and provides incentives to the contractor for managing costs effectively while completing cleanup work on schedule   Following a transition period  PRS will take over from Bechtel Jacobs Company  LLC whose contract expires April 23  2006     The DOE says that PRS will be responsible for groundwater and soil remedial actions  removing legacy waste  decontamination and decommissioning facilities  operating the site waste storage facilities  surveillance and maintenance activities  as well as other activities                                                      December 2005 News Releases
