                                      Bunning to Reid  Leave the Politics to the Politicians             Thursday  June 14  2007                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding reported remarks in the Politico  a Washington  D C  based publication  this morning made by Senate Majority Leader Harry Reid  D NV  questioning the competence of United States Military Commanders     The fact that Democrat Leader Reid would stoop so low as to insult our top military commanders in an attempt to gain political points with his liberal blogging friends is outrageous  The debate about this war has gone into the gutter when the Democrat Leader of the United States Senate uses disparaging remarks to describe our military leadership  Generals Pace and Petraeus are two honorable men who have performed admirably under the most challenging of circumstances  These combat hardened soldiers have both served their nation with distinction throughout their military careers  To sling political mud at these two men who are not part of the political arena is cowardly     Many people in western Kentucky and throughout the Commonwealth know General Petraeus well because of his time as Commander of the 101st  Air Assault  Division based at Fort Campbell  Kentucky  I think I speak for them when I say to the Democrat Leader    stop the political rhetoric when it comes to our men and women in uniform  Hurling stones at other politicians is one thing  but throwing them at the people who defend our freedom is another  and should never become part of the political discourse                                                      June 2007 News Releases                                                                                      United States
