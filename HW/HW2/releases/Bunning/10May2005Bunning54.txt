                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Senate Passage Of The Conference Report To The Emergency Defense Funding Bill             Tuesday  May 10  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement in support of the Conference Report to the Fiscal Year 2005 Supplemental Spending Bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 100 0        This is a win win bill for all our brave men and women who put their lives on the line every day fighting the War on Terror     said Bunning     It gives our troops the resources necessary to better protect themselves in battle and it will help allow them to continue their successful march towards victory over the terrorist thugs who threaten peace and democracy around the world         Overall  the supplemental spending bill provides  82 billion in emergency funding to help fight the War on Terror in Iraq and Afghanistan   The bill includes  75 9 billion for defense programs   4 1 billion for foreign aid   907 million for tsunami relief  and  1 2 billion in funding for resources to fight the War on Terror here at home                                                      May 2005 News Releases
