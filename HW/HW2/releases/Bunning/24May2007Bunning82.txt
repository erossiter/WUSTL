                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Announces New VA Clinics For Kentucky Clinics will be in Madison  Daviess  and Grayson Counties             Thursday  May 24  2007  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today received a phone call from Department of Veterans Affairs Secretary Jim Nicholson that the VA plans to open new community based clinics in Berea which is in Madison County  Daviess and Grayson counties will get community based clinics as well  The clinics will enhance access to VA health care for Kentuckys veterans   As Kentuckians plan their Memorial Day weekend the VA has delivered good news to the Commonwealths veterans  said Bunning  We as a nation must never forget those who have served in the armed forces  and these clinics will help ensure that those who have worn the uniform get the proper health care they deserve  Our veterans have served their country with distinction so that Americans can live in the land of liberty   The new facilities  called community based outpatient clinics  CBOCs  will become operational in 2007 or 2008  Local VA officials will keep communities and their veterans informed of progress in the creation of the new CBOCs                                                      May 2007 News Releases                                                                                                         U S  Senate Floor
