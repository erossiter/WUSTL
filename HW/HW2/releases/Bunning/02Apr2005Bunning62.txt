                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On The Passing Of Pope John Paul II             Saturday  April 2  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today offered the following statement regarding the passing of Pope John Paul II       I am deeply saddened by the passing of Pope John Paul II   We have lost a truly great man who brought much joy to the world during his twenty six years as head of the Roman Catholic Church   This is a very sad day not only for Catholics but for all those whose lives he touched with his kind heart and good works during his 84 years on earth       One of my fondest memories is visiting the Vatican in April of 2000 and taking part in a personal audience with Pope John Paul II   Mary and I enjoyed a beautiful Mass in his private chapel and afterwards we spent some time speaking with the Pope about our faith   It was an extraordinary experience that I will never forget       Pope John Paul II has been a beacon of hope for so many of us in the Catholic Church   He was a moral man of strong character and conviction  and we will greatly miss his leadership                                                         April 2005 News Releases
