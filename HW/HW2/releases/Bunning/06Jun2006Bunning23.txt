                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Blasts Bernanke For Downward Spiral In Markets             Tuesday  June 6  2006                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement regarding Chairman Ben Bernanke  s poor leadership at the Federal Reserve and the downward spiral in the markets that have come as a result of it       Chairman Bernanke needs to be more alert to the impact of what he says and the affect his words can have on our markets   He speaks off the cuff at White House dinners and his inconsistencies have turned the market upside down         He  s thrown panic into the markets when there was nothing to panic about and has raised unfounded fears of inflation   Bernanke is acting like a bad doctor who over prescribes a lethal dose of medication to a patient who is not sick       Bernanke is behaving like an amateur   He  s been in a cocoon of academia and is clearly not ready for prime time  which is exactly why I opposed his nomination in the first place                                                         June 2006 News Releases
