                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Bunning Statement On Bankruptcy Reform Bill             Thursday  March 10  2005                  U S  Senator Jim Bunning today issued the following statement supporting the bipartisan Senate passage of the Bankruptcy Reform Bill   The Senate overwhelmingly approved the bill by a vote of 74 25       By passing this legislation today we have taken a huge step forward in our ongoing effort to curb abuses in the consumer bankruptcy system and reduce the cost on America  s working families   Over the last 25 years we have seen a rapid increase in individual bankruptcy filings that have proved very costly to our economy and this bill aims to fix that   It  s a solid victory for the American consumer        The bill we passed today will help restore some personal responsibility and integrity to the bankruptcy system by ensuring that those who truly need complete bankruptcy protection receive it  while at the same time making sure that those who can afford to pay back their debts live up to their obligations         This is a good bill and I hope the House will act quickly to pass this commonsense legislation so we can send it to President Bush to sign into law                                                         March 2005 News Releases
