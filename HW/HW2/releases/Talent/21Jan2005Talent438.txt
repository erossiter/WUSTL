SEN. TALENT ENCOURAGED BY SWIFT CONFIRMATION OF NEBRASKA GOVERNOR AS AGRICULTURE SECRETARY
  Sen. Jim Talent (R-Mo.), a member of the Senate Agriculture Committee and Co-Chair of the Senate Biofuels Caucus, today issued the following statement as Nebraska Governor Mike Johanns was sworn in as the 28th Secretary of the U.S. Department of Agriculture: 
   "I am pleased with the swift confirmation of Mr. Johanns to be the next Secretary of the U.S. Department of Agriculture. I believe this signals that agriculture issues will once again be a top priority for the new Congress and President Bush.
   "I welcome Secretary Johanns' help in passing the Renewable Fuels Standard, opening up markets for our beef products and expanding opportunities for value-added agriculture in Missouri.  
   "I also serve on the Energy Committee and we are at a point where we can really make tremendous strides with biofuels. I am optimistic that Secretary Johanns' leadership will help us accomplish this goal."
