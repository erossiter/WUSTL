SEN. TALENT COMMENDS SENATE APPROVAL OF DUANE BENTON
  Sen. Jim Talent (R-Mo.) today issued the following statement on the Senate's approval of Duane Benton's nomination to the 8th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals: 
   "I am very pleased the Senate voted to approve the nomination of Duane Benton. He has served faithfully as Missouri Supreme Court Judge, he is a brilliant person and he has the right temperament to serve as an appellate court judge. It was an honor to support his nomination today."
