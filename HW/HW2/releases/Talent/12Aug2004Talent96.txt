SEN. TALENT ANNOUNCES $110,419 FIRE GRANT FOR DESOTO
  Sen. Jim Talent (R-Mo.) today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded a $110,419 grant for the DeSoto Rural Fire Protection District.  
 The funds will be used for training, wellness and fitness, firefighting equipment, personal protective equipment and modifications to fire stations and facilities.
   "I am very pleased to announce that the DeSoto Rural Fire Protection District will be receiving this funding," Sen. Talent said. "This grant will allow the department to purchase new safety equipment to enhance service for DeSoto in the event of a fire. I appreciate the Administration for providing these resources for firefighters in Jefferson County so they can do their jobs as safely and effectively as possible."
   The Assistance to Firefighters Grant program awards one-year grants directly to local fire departments, enhancing their abilities to respond to fire and fire-related hazards in their communities. For fiscal year 2004, $750 million in firefighter grants are to be distributed by the Department of Homeland Security.
