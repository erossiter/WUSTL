SEN. TALENT MEETS WITH AMERICAN HEROES AT VETERANS HOME IN WARRENSBURG
  Sen. Jim Talent (R-Mo.), member of the Senate Armed Services Committee, today visited the Missouri Veterans Home in Warrensburg where he met with some of the veterans who are receiving care at the facility.  
 The veterans home is an assisted-living facility that serves the long term needs of the veterans of Missouri.
  "It's important for us to honor all military veterans for their service and sacrifice," said Sen. Talent. "This visit is an opportunity for me to see firsthand the care our veterans are receiving and to talk one-on-one with some of America's heroes."
   The Missouri Veterans Home is a 200-bed facility operated by the state. It was dedicated July 14, 2000, and the first resident was admitted September 26, 2000.
