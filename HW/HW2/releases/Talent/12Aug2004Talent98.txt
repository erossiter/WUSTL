SEN. TALENT ANNOUNCES $225,000 FOR NEW FIRE TRUCK FOR ORAN
  Sen. Jim Talent (R-Mo.) today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded a $225,000 grant for the Oran Fire Protection District.  
 The funds will be used purchase a new fire truck.
   "I am pleased to announce that the Oran Fire Protection District will be receiving this funding for a new fire truck," Sen. Talent said. "The firefighters in Oran put their lives on the line for us everyday and that is why it is so important for the federal government to provide them with the resources they need to be safe while serving the community. I appreciate the Administration for providing resources for rural communities, such as Oran; with the funds they need to adequately protect lives, homes and business in the event of a fire."
   The Assistance to Firefighters Grant program awards one-year grants directly to local fire departments, enhancing their abilities to respond to fire and fire-related hazards in their communities. For fiscal year 2004, $750 million in firefighter grants are to be distributed by the Department of Homeland Security.
