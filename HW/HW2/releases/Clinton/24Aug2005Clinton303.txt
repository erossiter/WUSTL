August 24  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Release of the Joint New York Department of Health and Federal Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry Cancer Study for Endicott                                                                                                                                                                                                                       The report reaches a number of troubling conclusions about increased rates of certain cancers and birth defects among people who lived near the IBM plume  Although it is a step forward  it does not answer the question of what caused these problems  We need to take whatever steps are required to answer that crucial question
