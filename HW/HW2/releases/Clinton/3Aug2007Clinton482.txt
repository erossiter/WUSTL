August 3  2007 Clinton Secures Amendment for a GAO Study on Shared Border Management                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Amendment Approved by Senate as Part of the Senate Homeland Security Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced an amendment that she sponsored has been included in the Senate version of the Department of Homeland Security Appropriations Bill  which was approved by the Senate late yesterday  Senator Clinton s amendment would require the Government Accountability Office  GAO  to provide a study on the Department of Homeland Securitys  DHS  use of Shared Border Management  SBM      For nearly three years  Western New Yorkers have been waiting in limbo for a decision to be made on the issue of Shared Border Management between the U S  and Canada at the site of the Peace Bridge  said Senator Clinton  Although Secretary Chertoff and the Department of Homeland Security have concluded that Shared Border Management is not possible at that site  it is still important that we obtain a thorough review and explanation of why both parties were unable to come to an agreement on how to make the policy work     While we dont yet know what caused the breakdown between DHS and the Canadian government  we do know that their inability to reach an agreement will drastically increase the costs and the amount land needed on the Buffalo side of the border  Since the Department of Homeland Security has come to the conclusion that Shared Border Management cannot be achieved  the Department has a responsibility to provide guidance  as well as the resources  so that the city and state can continue to move forward     Senator Clintons amendment requires the GAO to study the Department of Homeland Security s use of shared border management to secure the international borders of the United States   The GAOs report shall include any negotiations  plans  or designs conducted DHS regarding the practice of shared border management and the factors required to be in place for shared border management to be successful   Senator Clinton recently addressed her concerns regarding the failure of DHS to secure a SBM agreement in a letter to Secretary Chertoff and asked the Secretary to provide reassurance that now that Shared Border Management has failed  the Administration will help local communities move forward to fix the problems at the Buffalo Niagara Border
