September 28  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Governor Pataki  s Decision to Oppose the International Freedom Center                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               I welcome Governor Pataki  s decision to oppose the International Freedom Center  heeding the concerns of family members and first responders   As I said last week  the controversy surrounding the IFC has impeded progress on the memorial and essential rebuilding efforts in Lower Manhattan
