November 2  2007 Senator Clinton Welcomes USDA Disaster Declaration for Franklin County Farmers in Response to Losses Caused by Excessive Rain                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Disaster Declaration Allows Local Producers to Apply for Low Interest Emergency Loans   Clinton  Essex  Hamilton  St  Lawrence Counties Declared as Contiguous Disaster Areas                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed the decision by the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  to declare Franklin County as a federal disaster area in response to the damage caused by excessive rain  The declaration  which was announced by Acting USDA Secretary Charles F  Conner  makes farm operators who suffered losses caused by excessive rain that occurred from June 20  2007 through July 31  2007  eligible to apply for low interest emergency loans from the USDA Farm Service Agency  FSA   The counties of Clinton  Essex  Hamilton  and St  Lawrence were also declared as contiguous disaster areas  making producers in those areas eligible for the FSA emergency loans as well     This announcement by the USDA is welcome news for our farm operators who suffered losses caused by the excessive rains that hit the area  said Senator Clinton  Our North Country farmers play a key role in the regions economy and they should not be left to shoulder the burden of recovering from the damage caused by the excessive rainfall  The USDA has taken a step in the right direction by making this disaster declaration  and the emergency loans provided by the FSA will be essential in helping local farmers continue to restore what was lost    The USDA disaster designation makes farm operators in both primary and contiguous counties eligible to be considered for low interest emergency loans from FSA  provided that eligibility requirements are met     Based on the USDA review of the Damage Assessment Report for Franklin County along with the additional information submitted by the State Executive Director  Farm Service Agency  FSA   it was determined that there were significant production losses to warrant a Secretarial natural disaster designation  In accordance with the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act  the counties of Clinton  Essex  Hamilton  and St  Lawrence were named as contiguous disaster counties     Local FSA offices can provide affected farmers with further information
