June 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  800 000 for Dansville Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced a grant of  807 500 for the Dansville Municipal Airport in Dansville  NY from the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT             This grant gives Dansville Airport the funds it needs to improve its traveling experience and better accommodate its passengers  Continuing to upgrade the Dansville Airport is key for both business and economic development and this grant will be well used     said Senator Schumer            This is an important investment in Dansville Airport and in the region as a whole  Maintaining airports that are effective and efficient is important for both the safety of passengers and the ability of our airports to sustain continued air traffic  which is good for business and economic development  Keeping passengers safe and moving smoothly means more flights arriving and leaving on time and that  s good news for business travelers  commuters and tourists alike     Senator Clinton said          The  807 500 grant will allow the Dansville Airport to construct  mark and light an apron for based and transient aircraft at the airport  The construction of the apron will provide an all weather parking surface for aircraft  According to the DOT  the existing apron space is insufficient for the current aircraft demand
