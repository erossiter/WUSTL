October 17  2007 Senator Clinton Applauds Announcement of Yucca Mountain Hearing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senate EPW Chairman Boxer Announces October 31st Oversight Hearing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton issued the following statement on the announcement that the United States Senate Environment and Public Works Committee  of which Senator Clinton is a member  will hold an oversight hearing on the proposed Yucca Mountain nuclear waste repository project on October 31  2007     I applaud Chairman Boxer for scheduling this important hearing  a step that I called for earlier this year   I am strongly opposed to Yucca Mountain because there are too many unanswered questions about both the geology of the site and integrity of the science done to support the decision to store waste there   Despite these questions  the Administration is rushing to license the project  and has even announced plans to double its size  That s why this hearing is so important   We need answers about these issues  and I will be asking for them on the 31st
