July 20  2006 Senator Clinton Secures Key Senate Panel Approval of  200 000 for Construction of the Schenectady YMCA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Funds included in Fiscal Year 2007 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed approval by the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee of the Fiscal Year 2007 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which includes  200 000 that the Senator secured for the Schenectady YMCA            The bill will proceed to the Senate floor for consideration following Full Appropriations Committee approval this afternoon   Following consideration and passage on the Senate floor  the bill will head into a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between their two bills  Following conference  the bill will again be sent to the House and Senate for final approval  after which it will be sent to the President for his signature            This development is another very positive step in the city  s push to redevelop and revitalize  This center will serve as a place for everyone in the community to use    from young people to seniors    and is a bold and innovative investment in the future of Schenectady  I am very pleased to have secured these funds so we can contribute to the building efforts and realize the vision of a new city center     Senator Clinton said          Schenectady Mayor Brian U  Stratton said    We are grateful to Senator Clinton for working to secure this important federal grant for our new YMCA project   From the very first day of my administration  Senator Clinton has been a strong partner in our effort to revitalize and rebuild Schenectady   Our new  65 000 square foot YMCA project is a vital part of Schenectady  s continuing renaissance  and we owe Senator Clinton an enormous vote of thanks for her support             Federal funds will supplement private funds being used to construct a new Schenectady YMCA  located at the site of a brownfield and encircled by a 200 000 square office park currently under construction   The new YMCA will contain a 10 000 square foot day care center  dedicated spaces for youth  teen  and senior citizen programming  community meeting space  a state of the art fitness and wellness center  an indoor running walking track  a double gymnasium  and a swimming pool   The placement of the new YMCA is designed to help spur vital economic revitalization of Schenectady
