March 3  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  285 000 For Montauk Airport In Suffolk County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    FAA Grant to provide federal funding for runway rehabilitation and wind cone installation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington  DC    Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded a  285 047 in a Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  Airport Improvement Program  AIP  grant to Montauk Airport in Suffolk County         Safe  dependable air service is crucial to New York s continued economic growth   said Senator Schumer   This funding will provide improvements to help Montauk Airport ensure safety in daily operation  and meet the challenge of increased demand            Montauk Airport is integral to the local economy   Senator Clinton said    Maintaining our critical airport infrastructure is important for both the safety of passengers and the ability of our airports like Montauk to sustain continued air traffic  This funding is a very welcome investment    not only in the airport but in Long Island s economy           This funding will provide for the rehabilitation of Runway 6 24 and the installation of a wind cone at Montauk Airport  The runway restoration will ensure the longevity of this pavement and help to improve runway conditions overall  Additionally  the wind cone installation will improve safety conditions for daily aircraft activity on Runway 24 at Montauk Airport
