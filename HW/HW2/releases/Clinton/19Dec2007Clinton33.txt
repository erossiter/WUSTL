December 19  2007 Schumer and Clinton Announce Final Congressional Passage of Millions in Funding for the Hudson Valley Region                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Bill Now Goes to the President for Signature                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Washington  DCSenators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced final Congressional approval of the Fiscal Year 2008 Omnibus Appropriations Bill  which includes funding for projects in New Yorks Hudson Valley Region  The omnibus bill  having passed both houses of Congress will now go to the President to be signed into law    These projects are crucial to fueling economic growth across the Hudson Valley and for giving local residents top notch services in education  law enforcement and health care  said Senator Schumer  By investing in these vital projects  we ensure that Hudson Valley continues to lure new businesses and tourists to the region  while ensuring families have the first class services they need    This funding is an investment in the future of the Hudson Valleys people and economy  said Senator Clinton   I welcome these investments  which will strengthen our military capabilities  support local communities  and improve the regions infrastructure     The Omnibus Appropriations bill includes funding for the following projects for the Hudson Valley Region
