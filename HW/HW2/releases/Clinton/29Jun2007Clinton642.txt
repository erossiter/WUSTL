June 29  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Thwarted London Car Bombings                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC  Today s troubling reports from London of potential car bombings are a stark reminder that the threat of terrorist attacks on our major cities is still very much a reality   Today s news reminds us that we must remain vigilant and direct our limited homeland security resources to the most vulnerable areas of our country   It s important to remain steadfast here and abroad and not become complacent when it comes to our homeland security
