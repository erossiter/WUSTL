July 6  2007 Clinton Welcomes FEMA Announcement of Federal Disaster Declaration for Sullivan County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Declaration Will Now Make Federal Funds Available to the County for Emergency Work and the Repair or Replacement of Facilities Damaged by the Flooding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today welcomed an announcement by the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  of a Major Disaster Declaration for Sullivan County   The news comes after Senator Clinton called on President Bush to make such a declaration and a few days after FEMA made a similar declaration for Delaware County  Read more here and here    Under the Disaster Declaration  state and local governments and certain nonprofit organizations will be eligible for Public Assistance from FEMA for emergency work and the repair or replacement of facilities damaged by the flooding   These Public Assistance Grants will be available for projects in Sullivan County   According to their announcement earlier in the week  Read more  FEMA will also be providing Hazard Mitigation Grants  which will provide funding for actions taken to prevent or reduce long term damage risk from natural disasters    Senator Clinton today continued her call on the President to expand the Major Disaster Declaration to provide Individual Assistance for Delaware and Sullivan Counties as requested by the Governor  Additional designations may be made at a later date after further evaluation    This is wonderful news for Sullivan County and for local officials who are dealing with the financial and the physical impact of the floods  Senator Clinton said  While Public Assistance from FEMA can help communities repair and rebuild  it cannot help those individuals and families who were impacted  so I hope that the President will soon issue a declaration to make Individual Assistance available to both Sullivan and Delaware Counties    The details of the FEMA announcement today are below      Type of Assistance                      Public Assistance
