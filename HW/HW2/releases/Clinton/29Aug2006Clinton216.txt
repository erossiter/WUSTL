August 29  2006 Senator Clinton Welcomes  585 000 in Federal Funds for Nonprofit Finance Fund in New York City                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed the announcement of  585 000 for the Nonprofit Finance Fund  NFF  in New York City from the Community Development Financial Institutions  CDFI  Fund of the U S  Department of the Treasury             We must ensure that non profits are given the means to address important issues such as youth development and education   I am proud to announce the CDFI initiative will invest in an institution that focuses on the future generations of our country     said Senator Clinton          The Nonprofit Finance Fund provides gap financing to help nonprofits maintain  expand or acquire facilities   The NFF will use the funds to increase its lending to non profits that provide youth and education services            The CDFI Fund invests in and builds the capacity of community based  private  for profit and non profit financial institutions with a primary mission of community development in economically distressed communities   These institutions   certified by the CDFI Fund as community development financial institutions    are able to respond to gaps in local markets that traditional financial institutions are not adequately serving   CDFIs provide critically needed capital  credit and other financial products in addition to technical assistance to community residents and businesses  service providers  and developers working to meet community needs
