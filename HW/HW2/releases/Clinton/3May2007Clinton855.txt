May 3  2007 Senator Clinton Welcomes Extension of Disaster Relief to More Counties Impacted by Recent Flooding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Individual Assistance now Available for Albany  Dutchess  Orange  Richmond  Rockland  and Westchester Counties   Public Assistance now Available for Albany  Columbia  Dutchess  Essex  Greene  Orange  Putnam  Rockland  Schoharie  Suffolk  Ulster  and Westchester Counties                                                                                                                          Washington  DC   Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today welcomed the announcement by the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  that the major disaster declaration has been extended to more counties that were hit hard by the recent Nor easter          According to FEMA s announcement today  Individual Assistance will now be available for Albany  Dutchess  Orange  Richmond  Rockland  and Westchester Counties and Public Assistance will now be available for Albany  Columbia  Dutchess  Essex  Greene  Orange  Putnam  Rockland  Schoharie  Suffolk  Ulster  and Westchester Counties          This week  Senator Clinton wrote to the President asking that he personally act to extend the major disaster declaration to the remaining counties that were hit hard by the recent Nor easter but have not yet received the vital assistance they desperately need   Read More    Today  Senator Clinton said that she hoped that the President would also further extend the declaration to ensure that all the counties that were impacted receive both Individual and Public Disaster Assistance           This is very welcome news for the communities  residents and business owners who have not yet received any assistance and whose costs are mounting by the day  This assistance is absolutely essential to allow people to rebuild their businesses  their homes and their lives   Senator Clinton said   I also hope that this declaration is a positive sign that all the counties that were hit by the Nor easter will soon be getting the full assistance they need             The details announced by FEMA today are below  This information also includes the assistance that was declared by FEMA earlier this week        Type of Assistance                                Individual Assistance   Assistance to individuals and households        Designated Counties   Albany  Dutchess  Orange  Richmond  Rockland  and Westchester       Type of Assistance   Public Assistance    Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for emergency work and the repair or replacement of disaster damaged facilities        Designated Counties   Albany  Columbia  Dutchess  Essex  Greene  Orange  Putnam  Rockland  Schoharie  Suffolk  Ulster  and Westchester        Type of Assistance   Hazard Mitigation Grant Program    Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for actions taken to prevent or reduce long term risk to life and property from natural hazards        Designated Counties   All counties in the State of New York are eligible to apply for assistance under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program
