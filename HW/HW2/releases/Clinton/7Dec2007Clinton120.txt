December 7  2007 House Senate Conference Committee Approves Clinton Amendment to Support Niagara Air Reserve Base                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that a joint House Senate Conference Committee has included her amendment to the Fiscal Year 2008 National Defense Authorization Act which requires the Secretary of the Air Force to develop a long range plan for the Niagara Air Reserve Base while beginning to work on important new initiatives there  The measure requires the Secretary of the Air Force to submit a detailed assessment of the current aviation assets as well as a plan for future allocation of assets to House and Senate Defense Committees by March 1  2008  The language mirrors an amendment originally proposed by Rep  Louise M  Slaughter in the House version of the bill     The bill  having been approved by the Conference Committee  will now return to both chambers of Congress for final passage  whereupon it will be sent to the President for his signature    In 2005  following the Base Realignment and Closure Commissions decision to keep open the Niagara Falls Air Reserve Station  I pledged to continue to fight for the Niagara installation and to do whatever I could to ensure its long term future  said Senator Clinton  This provision will require the Air Force to submit a report on the current and future aviation asset plan for basing at the station  The mission profile for units based at Niagara Falls is critical for future planning and local community support   In 2005  Senator Clinton  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  played a key role along with the Niagara Military Affairs Council  NIMAC   in removing the Niagara Falls Air Reserve Station from the list of recommended base closures put forth by the Department of Defense  DoD  as part of the BRAC Commission    Senator Clinton recently joined with Senator Charles E  Schumer to announce that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Fiscal Year 2008 Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Appropriations bill  which includes  27 million in military construction funding for an Armed Forces Reserve Center  AFRC  at the Niagara Falls Air Reserve Station
