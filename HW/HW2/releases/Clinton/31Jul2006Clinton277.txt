July 31  2006 Senators Murray and Clinton to Keep Hold on FDA Nominee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC    Following an 11th hour announcement on Plan B the day before a Senate confirmation hearing on the nomination of Dr  Andrew C  von Eschenbach to head the FDA  U S  Senators Patty Murray  D WA  and Hillary Rodham Clinton  D NY  released the following statement           Today s announcement by the FDA is far from a  yes  or  no  decision            Rather than moving this process forward and doing right by the American people  the Administration is continuing to play a game of smoke and mirrors the day before Dr  von Eschenbach s Senate confirmation hearing             Today s announcement is nothing more than another delay tactic   The FDA continues to shirk its duty to serve as an independent agency dedicated to no other goal than the promotion of sound science and the well being of the American people           We will maintain our hold on Dr  von Eschenbach s nomination until a decision is made
