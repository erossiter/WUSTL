May 3  2007 Senator Clinton and County Executive Levy Welcome Extension of Disaster Relief to Suffolk County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  DC   Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton and Suffolk County Executive Steve Levy today welcomed the announcement by the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  that the major disaster declaration has been extended to Suffolk County           According to FEMA s announcement today  Suffolk County will now be eligible for Public Assistance  which provides assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for emergency work and the repair or replacement of disaster damaged facilities           This is very welcome news for the communities in Suffolk County who have not yet received any assistance and whose costs are mounting by the day  This assistance is absolutely essential to allow communities that were hard hit by erosion and flooding to recover and rebuild   Senator Clinton said   I also hope that this declaration is a positive sign that all the counties that were hit by the Nor easter will soon be getting the full assistance they need            Beach erosion has a serious economic impact on our tourism and recreation industries  as well as upon the quality of life we enjoy here on Long Island   said Suffolk County Executive Steve Levy   We know that Senator Clinton shares this deep concern about our shoreline and we appreciate her hard work in getting federal assistance for us to replenish the beaches           This week  Senator Clinton wrote to the President asking that he personally act to extend the major disaster declaration to the remaining counties that were hit hard by the recent Nor easter but have not yet received the vital assistance they desperately need   Read More    Today  Senator Clinton said she hoped that the President would extend the declaration to ensure that all the counties that were impacted receive both Individual and Public Disaster Assistance          The details announced by FEMA today are below  This information also includes the assistance that was declared by FEMA earlier this week        Type of Assistance  Individual Assistance    Assistance to individuals and households        Designated Counties   Albany  Dutchess  Orange  Richmond  Rockland  and Westchester       Type of Assistance   Public Assistance    Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for emergency work and the repair or replacement of disaster damaged facilities        Designated Counties   Albany  Columbia  Dutchess  Essex  Greene  Orange  Putnam  Rockland  Schoharie  Suffolk  Ulster  and Westchester        Type of Assistance   Hazard Mitigation Grant Program    Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for actions taken to prevent or reduce long term risk to life and property from natural hazards        Designated Counties   All counties in the State of New York are eligible to apply for assistance under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program
