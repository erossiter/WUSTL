June 8  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  125 000 for the Adirondack Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Federal Money Will be Used to Improve the Drainage System at the Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  125 037 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Town of Harrietstown for the Adirondack Regional Airport   Federal funds will be used to rehabilitate existing drainage systems    This is great news for the Harrietstown  said Schumer  The Adirondack Regional Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is welcome news for the Adirondack Regional Airport  said Senator Clinton  This funding will be used to provide necessary improvements which are critical to its continued maintenance and operation   Anything we can do to support our local airports promises to be positive for the local economy and I am pleased that we can continue to support them in this way    The Town of Harrietstown will use federal funds to rehabilitate the drainage system between Runway 9 27 and Taxiway C at Adirondack Regional Airport in Saranac Lake   The existing drainage system is antiquated and requires restoration
