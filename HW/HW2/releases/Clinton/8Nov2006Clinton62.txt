November 8  2006 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Resignation of Secretary Rumsfeld                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee that will consider the nomination of Robert Gates  today made the following statement in Buffalo  NY in response to the news of the resignation of Secretary of Defense Donald Rumsfeld            The president made the right decision today  I wish he had made it earlier but now we have a new beginning  a new face at the Pentagon who doesn t carry the baggage that Secretary Rumsfeld carried   On Monday when the Army Times  the Marine Corps Times  the Navy Times  the Air Force Times all came out with the same editorial asking for Donald Rumsfeld s resignation  that was it  nobody could doubt what I and others have been saying  that we needed new leadership at the Pentagon if we were going to deal with the difficult situation in Iraq  our deteriorating situation in Afghanistan  and the threats we face around the world            Past Statement   Releases               Senator Clinton Highlights Secretary Rumsfeld  s Pattern of Painting Rosy Picture of Iraq           Senator Clinton Presses Secretary Rumsfeld on Bush Administration Policy on Iraq     Click here for more of Senator Clinton s statements concerning the war in Iraq
