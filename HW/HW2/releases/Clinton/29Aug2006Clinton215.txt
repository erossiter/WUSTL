August 29  2006 Senator Clinton Welcomes  100 000 in Federal Funds for Urban Homesteading Assistance  Inc  in New York City                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed the announcement of   100 000 for the Urban Homesteading Assistance   Inc   U hab  in New York City from the Community Development Financial Institutions  CDFI  Fund of the U S  Department of the Treasury              It is necessary that we support providers of the low income housing and loans so that more people can get access to the housing they need   Through this grant  U hab will be better able to provide lending services to people in need of affordable housing     said Senator Clinton           The Urban Homesteading Assistance  Inc  facilitates the development and preservation of housing cooperatives in New York City affordable and targeted to low and very low income households   U hab will use the federal funds to purchase and create the necessary systems and software for high volume lending          The CDFI Fund invests in and builds the capacity of community based  private  for profit and non profit financial institutions with a primary mission of community development in economically distressed communities   These institutions   certified by the CDFI Fund as community development financial institutions    are able to respond to gaps in local markets that traditional financial institutions are not adequately serving   CDFIs provide critically needed capital  credit and other financial products in addition to technical assistance to community residents and businesses  service providers  and developers working to meet community needs
