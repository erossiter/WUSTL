March 7  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 4 Million In Federal Money For Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal Funding Included in FY05 Omnibus Appropriations bill    Airport Improvement Grant Will Relocate the Taxiway at Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport has been awarded over  1 4 Million in federal funds for the airport to relocate its taxiway   The funds are listed in the Transportation  Treasury  and Independent Agencies Appropriations section of the Fiscal Year 2005 Omnibus Appropriations bill          This is great news for the entire Finger Lakes Region   said Schumer   The Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           The Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport is an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to the airport is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region
