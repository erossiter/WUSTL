July 20  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Backs  1 Million for Niagara Falls International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senators Aggressively Lobbied Senate Appropriations Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill      Improvements at NFIA will Ensure Continued Reliable Service to Western New York                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee is expected to approve later today  1 000 000 for the Niagara Falls International Airport new apron terminal under the Fiscal Year 07 Appropriations Bill for Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  Housing and Urban Development   Schumer and Clinton aggressively lobbied members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this year  s spending bill            This is great news for Western New York     Schumer said    When I was in Niagara a few weeks ago meeting about cargo  I pushed hard to promote the Niagara Fall airport as an international trade hub   Building this state of the art facility for the Niagara Falls International Airport is key to providing safe and dependable air service  These improvements will allow NFIA to continue as a critical engine of Western New York s continued economic growth               The expansion of the Niagara Falls International Airport is one of the keys to the region  s revitalization  These funds will be a tremendous boost to our efforts to expand the airport  s capacity so that it can bring in more planes  more often and it can bring cargo and freight as well as visitors and people coming for business  This is a great step forward for the airport and a strong sign that we will be able to continue to support its future development with much needed investment     Senator Clinton said          Funding for Niagara Falls International Airport will provide for the construction of a new terminal apron   The apron includes two aircraft parking positions will  storm sewers  de icing fluid control  fire protection  airfield lighting  security fencing and signage  all of which will support aircraft access to the new NFIA terminal  The Niagara Falls International Airport handles international charter and cargo service and functions as the reliever airport for the region  The lengthy runway enables it to accommodate all commercial aircraft  The new state of the art terminal and apron facilities will provide for efficient  customer friendly aircraft arrival and processing           Later today  the Appropriations Bill for Transportation  Treasure  the Judiciary  Housing  and Urban Development is expected to pass the Appropriations Committee and will head to the floor for a full vote before the Senate  The bill will then proceed to the Conference Committee and finally will be sent to the President before becoming law
