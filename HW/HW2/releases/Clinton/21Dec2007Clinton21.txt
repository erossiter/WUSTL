December 21  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on New York Power Authority and Alcoa Hydropower Agreement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  DC  Alcoa and its hard working employees have been a mainstay and anchor for the North Country economy and this agreement will help make sure this legacy continues   This agreement is great news for the North Country and I am glad Governor Spitzer has taken the initiative to work with the company and the workers to reinvigorate the Massena facilities
