August 28  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  500 000 for the Greater Rochester International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Federal Money Will Be Used to Rehabilitate Taxiway                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  533 303 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Monroe County for the Greater Rochester International Airport   Federal funds will be used to rehabilitate the taxiway    This is great news for Rochester  said Schumer  The Greater Rochester International Airport is a gateway for Rochester area residents and welcomes tourists traveling to New York each year  These improvements are important for local businesses and will strengthen the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region    This is more great news for Monroe County and the Greater Rochester Region  said Senator Clinton  As a central air hub  Rochester International is a key component to the regions continued economic success  I am pleased that we can continue to support upgrades to the airports infrastructure and any improvements that are necessary to facilitate its ongoing operations   This funding will keep the airport running smoothly and ensure travelers and tourists alike continue to visit and invest locally    Monroe County will use federal funds to rehabilitate the taxiway at the Greater Rochester International Airport
