May 8  2006 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Regarding the Death In Afghanistan of Ten Soldiers from Fort Drum                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      The death of these ten soldiers from the 10th Mountain Division out of Fort Drum in a helicopter crash is another tragic reminder of the risks and sacrifices our men and women in uniform face on a daily basis in Afghanistan in order to protect our freedom   I offer my heartfelt condolences to the families of the fallen soldiers and the entire Fort Drum community as they grieve for them
