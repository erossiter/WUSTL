May 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  280 000 for the Greater Binghamton Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Federal Money will be Used to Improve the Taxiway at the Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  280 250 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Broome County for the Greater Binghamton Regional Airport   Federal funds will be used to design a plan to rehabilitate and extend Taxiway A           This is great news for Binghamton   said Schumer   The Greater Binghamton Regional Airport welcomes tourists traveling to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region            This is welcome news for Broome County and New York s Southern Tier   said Senator Clinton   This funding will allow for continued improvements and rehabilitation measures that are critical to its continued maintenance and safe operations           Broome County will use federal funds to design a project to rehabilitate and extend Taxiway A   The existing pavement condition of the taxiway is poor and requires rehabilitation to ensure safe aircraft operations   The extension will improve taxiway patterns  runway access and exiting efficiencies
