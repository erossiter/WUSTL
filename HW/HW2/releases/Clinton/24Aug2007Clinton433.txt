August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  220 000 for Ogdensburg International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Federal Money Will Be Used to Remove Dangerous Obstructions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  223 250 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Ogdensburg International Airport   Federal funds will be used to remove dangerous obstructions    This is great news for St Lawrence County  said Schumer  The Ogdensburg International Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is great news for St  Lawrence County  said Senator Clinton   Ogdensburg International has served New Yorks North Country for over forty years  and these funds will ensure continued safe operation   Airports such as Ogdensburg International Airport are vital for travel and commerce in the region  and I will continue to work to help keep our airports safe and secure    Federal funds will be used to remove dangerous obstructions  This project will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
