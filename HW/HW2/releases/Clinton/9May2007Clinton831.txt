May 9  2007 Senator Clinton Welcomes Extension of Additional Disaster Relief to Ulster and Montgomery Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC   Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today welcomed an announcement by the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  that the major disaster declaration has been extended to include Individual Assistance for Ulster County and Public Assistance for Montgomery County           This is very welcome news for residents in Ulster and Montgomery Counties  There is still so much repair and rebuilding work to be done and these communities really need this funding   Senator Clinton said          Last month  Senator Clinton wrote to the President asking that he personally act to extend the major disaster declaration to the remaining counties that were hit hard by the recent Nor easter but have not yet received the vital assistance they desperately need   Read More            To date the assistance announced by FEMA is as follows              Individual Assistance  Assistance to individuals and households       Designated Counties  Albany  Dutchess  Orange  Richmond  Rockland  Ulster  and Westchester       Public Assistance  Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for emergency work and the repair or replacement of disaster damaged facilities       Designated Counties  Albany  Columbia  Dutchess  Essex  Greene  Montgomery  Orange  Putnam  Rockland  Schoharie  Suffolk  Ulster  and Westchester        Hazard Mitigation Grant Program  Assistance to State and local governments and certain private nonprofit organizations for actions taken to prevent or reduce long term risk to life and property from natural hazards       Designated Counties  All counties in the State of New York are eligible to apply for assistance under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program
