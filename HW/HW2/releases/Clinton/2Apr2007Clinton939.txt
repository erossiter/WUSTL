April 2  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Supreme Court s Global Warming Decision                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        The Court s decision affirms the scientific consensus that global warming is a serious threat that is caused by human activities  and it rejects the Bush Administration s  do nothing  approach to the problem   I urge President Bush to face up to global warming and take strong action to address this growing threat to our planet and our children s future
