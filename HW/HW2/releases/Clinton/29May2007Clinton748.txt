May 29  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Supreme Court Decision on Pay Discrimination                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Washington  DC    Today s decision in Ledbetter v  Goodyear Tire   Rubber Co  interprets Title VII in a way that limits a worker s right to receive equal pay for equal work   In her dissent  Justice Ginsburg invites Congress to remedy this problem   I intend to do just that by introducing legislation to clarify Congressional intent on this issue   All Americans deserve equal pay for equal work  and it is my hope that Congress can remove the technical hurdles that will prevent individuals from receiving what is rightfully theirs
