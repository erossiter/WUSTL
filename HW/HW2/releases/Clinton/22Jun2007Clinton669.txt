June 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Approves  300 000 for the Village of Owego                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senators Worked Closely with the Committee to Include Funding in Spending Bill   Federal Funds will be Used for Wastewater Treatment Facility Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Interior  Environment  and Related Fields FY08 Appropriations Bill which includes  300 000 for the Village of Owego in Tioga County to improve the wastewater treatment facility   The bill will now proceed to the Senate Floor as the next step in the appropriations process   Schumer and Clinton worked closely with members of the Appropriations Committee to include funding for the project in this years spending bill    This is great news for the Village of Owego  Schumer said   This funding will help update facilities and alleviate environmental and public health threats   These funds will help Owego comply with current mandates  making the community healthier and safer    I very also pleased that these funds have been approved for the Village of Owego to pay for upgrades to their water system  Senator Clinton said  This is an important part of the federal governments responsibility to help communities maintain their essential infrastructure    The Village of Owego will use federal funds to improve an antiquated and dangerous Wastewater Treatment Facility   The current system is experiencing structural defects and capacity deficiencies  creating environmental and public health issues   The EPA has recently introduced the cMOM  capacity  management  operation  and maintenance  program that requires the elimination of storm water from the collection system   This project will allow the village to meet this mandate and increase treatment capacity at the wastewater treatment facility     Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill goes into Conference with the House version before going back to the Senate and House and on to the President
