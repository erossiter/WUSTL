November 28  2005 Statement of Senator Clinton on the Agreement Between Magen David Adom and the Palestinian Red Crescent                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton issued the following statement today on the agreement between Magen David Adom  MDA  and the Palestinian Red Crescent            I am pleased that Magen David Adom and the Palestinian Red Crescent have signed an agreement of mutual recognition  paving the way for Magen David Adom to be admitted into the International Red Cross next week  a move that is long overdue   Only weeks ago I visited the Jerusalem regional office of Magen David Adom to show my continued support for MDA  s longstanding efforts to be admitted into the International Red Cross   At that time I joined MDA doctors  paramedics  volunteers and Israel s Minister of Health to talk about the upcoming conference convened by the Swiss government in Geneva where  I hope  after years of our advocacy and urging  MDA will finally be admitted into the International Red Cross as a full voting member             While traveling earlier this month in Israel  Senator Clinton met with Magen David Adom officials and Israeli health officials to underscore her devotion to the vital life saving work that MDA has performed for 75 years   At that meeting  Senator Clinton reiterated her commitment to ensuring that the MDA gains entry into the International Red Cross  a goal she has been pursuing since she came into office         http   www clinton senate gov Colin Powell and Red Cross officials to push for full and immediate Magen David Adom membership in the International Red Cross  They sent letters to Powell  Jakob Kellenberger  President of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent  and Dr  Astrid Hieberg  President of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Society  urging swift recognition and full membership for MDA organization does not have voting rights in the ICRC Movements or equal status with other emergency relief societies under the organization  s rules            Senator Clinton  s MDA efforts continued declared that the Red Shield of David should be accorded the same protections under international law as the Red Cross and the Red Crescent and that the United States should continue to press for full membership for the MDA in the ICRC           In January 2003  Senator Clinton along with Senator Elizabeth Dole and Senator Peter Fitzgerald  offered an amendment to withhold the United States
