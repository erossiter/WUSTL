June 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  400 000 for Sound Proofing at Local Queens School Near JFK Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced a grant of   440 310 for soundproofing at PS 195Q in Queens  NY near the John F  Kennedy International Airport  from the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT             This grant is important for both John F  Kennedy International Airport and its surrounding communities in Queens  NY  Minimizing the effects of airplane noise on surrounding communities is crucial and this grant provides local schools with the necessary funds to soundproof their buildings  Maintaining a local airport is key for both business and economic develop  but we should always make sure airplane noise doesn t infringe on the ability of our young minds to productively learn in peace and quiet     said Schumer            This is an important investment in the JFK Airport as well PS 195Q  This grant ensures that one of the world  s busiest airports will continue to bring travelers to New York while ensuring a productive learning environment for local school students     Senator Clinton said          The  440 310 grant will allow for nearby PS 195Q to be soundproofed  It will mitigate the effect of aircraft noise for schools in close proximity to the John F  Kennedy Airport
