April 26  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Welcoming Corning Inc  s Approval of a  300 Million Facility Improvement Plan for the Company s Sullivan Park Research and Development Campus Near Corning  N Y                                                                                                                                                                                                       I applaud the decision by the Corning Incorporated Board of Directors to invest in expanding Sullivan Park by building a new laboratory  These funds will help provide the precious resources needed for the vital research and development that takes place on the campus  This investment will help further research in the important fields of alternative fuels and energy conservation as well as provide many job opportunities to residents in the Corning area  This is great news for the scientific community and the Corning area  This investment promises to help bolster the economic strength of the Southern Tier while promoting important scientific research
