June 8  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Nearly  7 Million for the Buffalo Niagara International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Federal Money Will be Used for Noise Mitigation Measures for Residents and Internal Road Construction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded two grants totaling  6 955 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Niagara Frontier Transportation Authority for the Buffalo Niagara International Airport  A grant of  6 580 000 will be used to alleviate noise pollution for public buildings and homes located near the airport  An additional  375 000 will be used to construct a segment of internal perimeter service roads  This is great news for Buffalo  said Schumer  The Buffalo Niagara Airport welcomes many thousands of travelers to Western  New York each year  These funds will facilitate airport operations while also addressing the noise concerns of local residents  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region  This is great news for the Buffalo Niagara International Airport becausethis fundingwill be used to provide necessary barriers to noise pollution and improvements to internal roadways  which are critical to its continued maintenance and operation  said Senator Clinton  Air service to and from the region is critical to its continued growth  Anything we can do to support our airports promises to be positive for the local economy  The Niagara Frontier Transportation Authority will use the  6 580 000 to improve noise mitigation measures for residents and public buildings located near the airport  The Buffalo Niagara International Airport will use the  375 000 to complete a system of service roads that will facilitate the reduction of vehicle and pedestrian deviation
