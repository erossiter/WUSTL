June 8  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  75 000 for Hudson Valley Airfields                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Federal Money Will be Used for Drainage Improvements and Airport Layout Updates                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded two grants totaling  79 323 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to airports in the Hudson Valley   The Kingston Airpark in Ulster County will receive federal funding of  52 323 to update the airport layout plan for Kingston Ulster Airport   Dutchess County will receive  27 000 to improve the water drainage system at Dutchess County Airport in Poughkeepsie  NY    This is great news for the Hudson Valley  said Schumer  The Kingston Ulster Airport and Dutchess County Airport and the Sullivan County Airport are important general aviation facilities in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airports safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airports bases of operation  they must remain key components of economic growth in the region    This is welcome news for the Kingston Ulster Airport and Dutchess County Airport because these funds are an important investment in our airport infrastructure and the local economy   Air service to and from the region is critical to its continued growth  Anything we can do to support our airports promises to be positive for the local economy  said Senator Clinton     Dutchess County will use a  27 000 grant for drainage improvements at the Dutchess County Airport   The drainage improvements will prevent polluted water from entering the groundwater directly     The Kingston  Airpark will use the  52 323 of federal funds to update the airport layout plan for Kingston Ulster Airport   The airport layout plan requires updating to meet the continued future demands
