September 14  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the selection of Congressman Peter King as Chair of the House Committee   on Homeland Security                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Pete King is exactly what New York needs to help with its security challenges   He has always been a strong supporter of New York  s first responders and has been a true advocate for our state  He understands the specific homeland security needs of New York as well as the many security challenges confronting our nation  This is great news for New York
