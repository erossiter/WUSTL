June 23  2005 Schumer  Clinton Welcome Over  250 000  for Joseph Y  Resnick Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Federal Money will be used to install perimeter fencing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today welcomed the news that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  253 650 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Joseph Y  Resnick Airport in Ellenville         This is great news for Ulster County   said Schumer   This funding will help ensure that the Resnick Airport remains a safe place to fly in and out of  I will continue to fight for federal funds to assist local airports remain viable and safe as part of the overall transportation network in the Hudson Valley          This funding is a very welcome investment in the Resnick airport and in the future of the region   Senator Clinton said   For tourism and business alike  safe and affordable air travel is an essential ingredient for economic development  I am pleased that we can support the airport with this funding and continue to ensure the safety and security of planes and passengers         This grant will provide federal funding to install a perimeter fence at the Joseph Y  Resnick Airport to minimize incursions and improve overall airport safety
