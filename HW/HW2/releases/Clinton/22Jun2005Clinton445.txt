June 22  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  850 000 for Potsdam Municipal Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Federal Money will be used for various construction projects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  869 250 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Potsdam Municipal Airport          Safe and secure air service is a critical engine of the North Country  s continued economic growth     said Schumer    This funding will allow Potsdam to improve aircraft access so that it can meet today s modern aviation demands             This funding is a very welcome investment in the Potsdam Municipal Airport and in the future of the region     Senator Clinton said    For tourism and business alike  safe and affordable air travel is an essential ingredient for economic development  I am pleased that we can support the airport with this funding and continue to ensure the safety and security of planes and passengers           This grant will provide federal funding for the first phase of construction of an airport apron and taxilane  which will allow for the future development of T hangars and a fuel farm at the airport  The construction of a fuel farm will allow the airport to serve the aviation community in a safe manner   The grant will also provide funding to replace the rotating beacon  the runway end identifier lights and the precision approach path indicators to ensure safe airport operations
