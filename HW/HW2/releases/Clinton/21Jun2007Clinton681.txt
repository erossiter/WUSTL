June 21  2007 Senators Schumer and Clinton Announce Senate Committee Approval of  73 4 Million in Military Construction Funding for Long Island                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Funds for Gabreski Air National Guard Base and Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale   Funding Included in Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Appropriations Bill Approved by Senate Appropriations Committee   Bill will Now be Sent to the Senate Floor                                                                                                                          Washington  DC  Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Fiscal Year 2008 Appropriations bill which includes  8 4 million in military construction funding for the Gabreski Air National Guard Base Pararescue Facility in Westhampton Beach and  65 million for the Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale    Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill goes into Conference with the House version before going back to the Senate and House and on to the President    The funding for Gabreski would support the construction of part one of the Pararescue Facility at the Francis S  Gabreski Airport in Westhampton Beach  NY   In April 2004 as a result of unit reorganization  the 106th Air Rescue Wing more than doubled   Special requirements that could not be accommodated under the old organization were now severely strained   Space is required to perform pre mission planning and training so that missions can continue to be successfully executed without endangering crew members during challenging and high risk missions    This is great news for the 106th Air Rescue Wing  and a tremendous boon for Suffolk  said Schumer  With personnel nearly doubling at Gabreski  this funding was absolutely essential to ensure the safety and proper training of our military men and women  and will contribute to the economic growth and well being of the surrounding community    The approval of funding for the Pararescue Facility at the Frances Gabreski Air National Guard Base marks an important step for both the crew members of the 106th Air Rescue Wing and for the community  Senator Clinton said  This is a significant investment in the 106th  With this funding  the Pararescue Crew will have the tools to continue their important missions    The funds for the Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale confirm what was proposed in the Presidents Fiscal Year 2008 Budget and will help construct new facilities  which will include administrative and organizational buildings designed to house headquarters  classrooms  arms rooms  logistics supply rooms  motor pools  and maintenance bays  When these facilities are completed  units will have modern and revitalized facilities from which to support their missions   There are approximately 100 AFRC units being built nation wide to support the BRAC consolidation plan    This is a tremendous boost for Farmingdale  Senator Clinton said   These facilities will be a vital component of our military and national security and will be critical to the regions frontline defense and to local homeland and disaster preparedness  This funding will enhance and expand the regions capabilities and provide critical disaster preparedness infrastructure    Funding for the Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale is a critical step forward for Long Islands ability to respond effectively and quickly to an emergency at home  Schumer said today  Providing our men and women in the New York National Guard with the tools and resources they need to protect our communities is essential  and I will continue to fight for their interests and wellbeing
