October 17  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Senate Approval of  500 000 for the Onondaga County Interoperable Communication System                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Funding in Commerce  Justice  Science  and Related Agencies FY08 Appropriations Bill Will be Used to Upgrade Equipment to Improve Communication System for First Responders                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate has approved the Commerce  Justice  Science  and Related Agencies FY08 Appropriations Bill which includes  500 000 for the Onondaga County Interoperable Communication System   The bill will now proceed to a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between their versions  Following conference  the bill will again be sent to the House and Senate for final approval  after which it will be sent to the President for his signature    Our first responders  who put their lives on the line to keep us safe  need state of the art communications equipment  said Senator Schumer  Senate approval is great news for Syracuse and Onondaga  which will use the funds to unify and streamline the communication system for first responders  making it more efficient and effective   This system is a crucial part of keeping our communities safe by ensuring that our first responders have the equipment and technology they need to coordinate their efforts when responding to emergencies     Our first responders put their lives on the line for us every day  and we should give them the tools they need to keep us safe and to support our communities   Effective communication systems are essential for law enforcement and first responders  and these funds will help Onondaga County make critically needed upgrades  Senator Clinton said    Onondaga County will use the federal funds to improve the Interoperable Communications System for first responders in Syracuse and the surrounding areas   Funds will be used to replace incompatible 800MHz radio equipment to the proposed City County UHF Trunked Land Mobile Radio Interoperable Communication System  Infrastructure equipment currently used to support two way radio communications will not be consistent with equipment planned for the new first responder system that supports operations in the City of Syracuse and County of Onondaga    Onondaga County is in the process of building and planning to implement a trunked radio UHF Land Mobile Radio System  This system when implemented will embrace all first responders in the City of Syracuse and County of Onondaga  This project will consolidate all voice communications into one operational system     Infrastructure purchases would include  21 Channel Site Controller  Antenna System for LMR  Channel  Site Software  Channel Banks  and Channel Cards  The purchase of this equipment will be incorporated with the overall systems replacement planned for this area  Several fire departments in the County and City serving a population base of 250 000 will be affected by this change
