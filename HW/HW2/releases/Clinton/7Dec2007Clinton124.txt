December 7  2007 Senator Clinton Welcomes USDA Disaster Declaration for Clinton County Farmers in Response to Losses Caused by High Winds and Hail                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Disaster Declaration Allows Local Producers to Apply for Low Interest Emergency Loans   Essex  Franklin Counties Declared as Contiguous Disaster Areas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed the decision by the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  to declare Clinton County a federal disaster area in response to the damage caused by high winds and hail  The declaration  which was announced by Acting USDA Secretary Charles F  Conner  makes farm operators who suffered losses caused by high winds and hail that occurred on August 16  2007  eligible to apply for low interest emergency loans from the USDA Farm Service Agency  FSA   The counties of Essex and Franklin were also declared contiguous disaster areas  making producers in those areas eligible for the FSA emergency loans as well     I welcome this USDA announcement for farm operators who suffered losses from the high winds and hail that hit the area earlier this year  said Senator Clinton  Our North Country farms are vital to all of New Yorks economy and the USDA is helping ease the burden caused by these storms  The low interest emergency loans that this declaration allows will help farmers in Clinton  Franklin and Essex counties recover and continue to play their vital role in the region    The USDA disaster designation makes farm operators in both primary and contiguous counties eligible to be considered for low interest emergency loans from FSA  provided that eligibility requirements are met     Based on the USDA review of the Damage Assessment Report for Clinton County along with the additional information submitted by the State Executive Director  FSA  it was determined that there were significant production losses to warrant a Secretarial natural disaster designation  In accordance with the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act  the counties of Essex and Franklin were named as contiguous disaster counties     Local FSA offices can provide affected farmers with further information
