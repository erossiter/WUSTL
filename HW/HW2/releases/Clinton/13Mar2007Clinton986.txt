March 13  2007 In Light of Rising Gas Prices  Senator Clinton Calls for Investment in Clean Energy Technology to Reduce Dependence on Foreign Oil                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Senator Releases Analysis Showing New Yorkers Now Spending  581 Million More on Gasoline Per Month than They Did in 2001                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC   In light of rising gasoline prices  Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today renewed her call for a  Strategic Energy Fund  to develop alternative energy technologies  Yesterday  the federal Energy Information Administration released data showing that the average gasoline price in New York State is now  2 71 per gallon   Senator Clinton today released figures showing that at those prices  New Yorkers are now spending over  581 million per month more on gasoline than they did in 2001          Two weeks ago  Senator Clinton reintroduced legislation to create a  Strategic Energy Fund  to jumpstart research and investment in clean energy technologies in order to promote job growth  energy independence and a cleaner environment   News reports of increasing gas prices around New York State underscore the pressing need to develop alternative energy sources             Our dependence on foreign oil is hurting New York families while oil companies continue to book record profits   said Senator Clinton    It is time to eliminate oil company subsidies and put those funds to work developing biofuels  more fuel efficient vehicles  and other clean energy technologies   It s time to get serious about reducing our dependence on oil to help consumers  create new jobs and protect our environment      Rising crude oil prices and unexpected cold weather that has swept through the region have factored into the recent price spikes   This swell in demand  coupled with fears of annual spring shortages  has experts predicting another summer of soaring gas prices       Click here for more information on Senator Clinton s plan for a Strategic Energy Fund       Below is Senator Clinton s analysis of how much more New Yorkers are paying for gasoline per
