July 23  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Key Senate Panel Prioritizes Funding for Otsego County Broadband Telecommunications Plan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Project Designed to Modernize Otsego s Telecommunications Network   Senators Worked Closely with Committee to Include Project in the Bill   Bill Will Now Be Sent to the Senate Floor                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the full Senate Appropriations Committee has approved the Agriculture  Rural Development  Food and Drug Administration  and Related Agencies FY08 Appropriations Bill  which includes the Otsego County Telecommunications Plan as a project to be considered for funding from USDAs Rural Development Telecommunications Program at a later time   The bill will now proceed to the Senate Floor as the next step in the appropriations process   Schumer and Clinton worked closely with members of the Appropriations Committee to include the project in this years spending bill    This is great news for Otsego  said Schumer  A vast broadband network will enable entrepreneurship to flourish and will attract new types of cutting edge  high tech business to the region   This green light from the Committee signifies that the project has cleared a significant hurdle and we are now a giant step closer to making the Otsego County Telecommunications Plan a reality    Funding for the Telecommunications Plan will not only provide Otsego County with the resources to upgrade their facilities  it is an investment in the future of the community  Senator Clinton said  Access to fast  reliable telecommunications is an invaluable tool for government  students and families and it is also critical for small businesses as they try to reach the global marketplace  These enhancements are necessary to ensure continued economic growth in the community  Residents deserve an inexpensive system that meets their growing needs    The Committee has designated the Otsego County Telecommunications Plan as a project to be given consideration for funding by USDAs Rural Development Telecommunications Program   The mission of the project is to develop  equip and build a fully integrated and interoperable telecommunication network across the 1 000 square miles of Otsego County   The existing countywide backbone is nearly 30 years old with several deficiencies and needs to be upgraded   Extension of optical fiber to existing microwave stations will be necessary to provide a more reliable connection and higher data transmission capability    It is anticipated that various forms of infrastructure  including fiber optical and wireless technologies will be necessary to conform to the hilly terrain  reach sparsely populated areas and support the bandwidth required by last mile technologies   This system would greatly enhance rural development initiatives including Emergency Services  Economic Development  Telemedicine  Distance Learning Opportunities  Governmental Services and provide affordable  robust and reliable broadband coverage to all County residents    Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee  it will be sent to the Senate floor  Following approval by the Senate  the bill will move towards Conference with the House and then to the President for signature
