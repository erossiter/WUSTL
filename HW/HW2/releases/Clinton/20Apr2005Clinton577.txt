April 20  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 2 Million for North Country Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Airports in Lake Placid and Massena Will Receive Federal Money for Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded  1 253 900 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  grants to airports throughout the North Country region  The money will be used for a series of improvements  ranging from equipment purchases to apron construction        This is great news for the North Country   said Schumer   These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           These airports are an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support them   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to these airports is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region           Two North Country area airports will receive federal money under today s award   Lake Placid Airport in Essex County will receive two grants  totaling  816 900  One grant will fund apron rehabilitation  as the existing apron has not been rehabilitated since its original construction in 1980  A second grant will provide funding to acquire snow removal equipment   The purchase of a snow blower is necessary to maintain safety at the airport during periods of adverse weather   Massena International Richards Field in St  Lawrence County will receive  437 000 also for snow removal equipment   This will allow the airport to remain open and reduce delays        New York State received 15 grants this round  totaling  7 793 367 in federal funds  The grants were approved under the Federal Aviation Administration Airport Improvement Program
