October 25  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Passing of Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC    It is with a heavy heart that all Americans today mourn the passing of Rosa Parks  one of the giants of our civil rights movement   Although she will be most remembered for her brave refusal to give up her bus seat in Montgomery  Alabama on December 1  1955  hers was a life of dignity devoted to the cause of civil rights   Her sacrifice and work improved the lives of all Americans and forever changed the landscape of our society   While all of us who are committed to civil rights mourn her passing  we must not forget to pause and to observe the achievement of Rosa Parks  s life  mindful of the fact that the civil rights movement exists in large part because of her sacrifice
