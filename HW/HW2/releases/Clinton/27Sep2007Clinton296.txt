September 27  2007 Statement of Senator Clinton on Administration s Plan to Reduce Congestion and Delays at New York Area Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Washington  DC   I am pleased that the Administration is finally paying attention to an issue that continues to get worse I know New Yorkers and the rest of the country have been waiting for the president to address congestion and delays at our area airports This year  delays and congestion at our airports has been the worst on record at our nation s airports Earlier this month the Senate unanimously accepted my bipartisan amendment requiring the Department of Transportation to come up with a plan to relieve congestion and the delays at New York and New Jersey airports Im glad the president has followed the lead of Congress in taking action to address this growing problem as soon as possible
