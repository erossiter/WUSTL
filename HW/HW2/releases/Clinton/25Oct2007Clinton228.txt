October 25  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Death of Joe Crapa                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC  I was saddened today to hear of the death of Joe Crapa   I had the honor of knowing Joe as a friend and colleague during his many years of distinguished service in the Clinton Administration  in the Senate  and as Executive Director of the United States Commission on International Religious Freedom   His talents and commitment as a public servant were unparalleled   A native of Brooklyn  Joe brought an indomitable spirit and determination to his work that I saw first hand during his time as Chief of Staff to Senator Schumer when we fought together to secure funding for New York in the aftermath of 9 11   Joes passing is a tremendous loss and he will be dearly missed by all of us who had the privilege to know and work with him   I send my sympathies to his wife Barbara  son Judd and daughter in law Gretchen  grandsons Sebastian and Baird  and his entire extended family   My thoughts and prayers are with you during this difficult time
