May 11  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 2 Million for Western New York Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Federal Money will be Used for Airport Improvements in Dunkirk and Olean Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded three grants amounting to  1 217 150 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds for airports in Western New York  The City of Dunkirk will receive  274 750 for two projects at Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport and the City of Olean will receive  942 400 to reconstruct the aircraft parking apron at Cattaraugus County Olean Airport           This is great news for Western New York   said Schumer   These airports are important general aviation facilities in this region  Not only do they provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but they allow businesspeople visiting New York to access the region easily  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region            This is welcome news for Chautauqua and Cattaraugus Counties because these are important investments in our airport infrastructure and in the local economy  Air service to and from the region is critical to its continued growth  Anything we can do to support our airports promises to be positive for the local economy   said Senator Clinton             The City of Dunkirk will use the  194 750 to rehabilitate Runway 6 24 at Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport as well as  80 000 to be used to evaluate existing obstructions in support of a localizer approach with vertical guidance  LPV   Runway 6 24 s pavement is in poor condition and the new approach will make the airport more accessible to pilots by providing vertical guidance to a particular runway end          The City of Olean will use the  274 750 to reconstruct the aircraft parking apron at Cattaraugus County Olean Airport  which is in poor condition and requires reconstruction
