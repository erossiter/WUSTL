June 8  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  893 000 for Airports in the Southern Tier                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Federal Money Will be Used for Airport Improvements in Binghamton and Elmira                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded three grants amounting to  893 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds for airports in the Southern Tier   Chemung County will receive  527 250 to conduct an environmental study and for airport improvements at the Elmira Corning Regional Airport   Broome County will receive two grants for the Greater Binghamton Airport totaling  365 750   A grant of  190 000 will be used to replace antiquated snow removal equipment and  175 750 will be used for tree removal to enhance airport safety    This is great news for the Southern Tier  said Schumer  The Elmira Corning Regional Airport and the Greater Binghamton Airport are important general aviation facilities in this region  Not only do they provide local aviators and tourists with a base of operation  but they allow businesspeople visiting New York to easily access the region  Keeping these airports running smoothly  safely and securely is crucial   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region    This is welcome news for Broome and Chemung Counties  said Senator Clinton   These airports are critical to continued growth in the Southern Tier because they allow for business travelers and tourists alike to visit and invest locally  Anything we can do to support our local airports promises to be positive for the local economy and I am pleased that we can continue to support them in this way    The County of Chemung will use the  527 250 to perform an environmental assessment at Elmira Corning Regional Airport   The funding will provide for a comprehensive approach to identifying the scope of potential environmental impacts during project development indentified in the airport master plan   Funds will also be used for the design portion to rehabilitate the apron  The pavement of the apron shows significant signs of deterioration  and requires rehabilitation to ensure safe airline operations   The expansion will ultimately ease congestion and meet facility needs     Broome County will use  190 000 to replace snow removal equipment at the Greater Binghamton Airport   The equipment is 28 years old  and has reached its serviceable limits and needs replacing   The other grant of  175 750 will be used to fund the removal of trees that are obstruction the approach and transitional surfaces of two runways   This obstruction removal will enhance airport security
