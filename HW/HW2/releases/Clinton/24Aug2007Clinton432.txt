August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  73 000 for Piseco Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Federal Money Will Be Used to Install NAVAIDS  Install Weather Reporting Equipment and Remove Obstruction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  73 150 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Piseco Airport in the Town of Arietta   Federal funds will be used to install NAVAIDS  install weather removing equipment and remove dangerous obstructions    This is great news for Arietta and Hamilton County  said Schumer  The Piseco Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is great news for Piseco Airport  said Senator Clinton  The airport is critical to continued growth in the area and I am pleased that we can continue to help Piseco Airport remain a key player in bringing business travelers and tourists alike to the region    Federal funds will be used to install NAVAIDS  install weather reporting equipment and removes obstruction  These projects will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
