March 3  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  807 000 For Genesee County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Federal Dollars Will Be Used To Rehabilitate Runway                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Clinton today announced  807 500 for the Genesee County Airport from the Airport Improvement Program  AIP  at the U S  Department of Transportation  Federal Aviation Administration  FAA           This is great news for the Genesee County Airport     said Senator Schumer    This airport welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help this airports remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region             Genesee County Airport is integral to the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it     Senator Clinton said    Maintaining our critical airport infrastructure is important for both the safety of passengers and the ability of our airports to sustain continued air traffic  The more flights we have  the better the economic opportunity  so this funding is a very welcome investment    not only in the airport itself but in the region as a whole           At the Genesee County Airport  this grant will provide federal funding to rehabilitate Runway 28 and associated taxiways   The existing runway surface is cracked and worn   A pavement rehabilitation will restore the runway to a good condition and increase overall safety of the airport
