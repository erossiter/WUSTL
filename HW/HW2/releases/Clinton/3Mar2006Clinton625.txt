March 3  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 239 080 For Southern Tier Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Federal Dollars Coming for Improvements to Ithaca Tompkins Regional  Tri Cities and Corning Painted Post Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Clinton today announced  1 239 080 for three airports in New York  s Southern Tier  The three Airport Improvement Program  AIP  grants from the U S  Department of Transportation  Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  include  427 500 for Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport   150 000 for Tri Cities Airport and  661 580 for Corning Painted Post Airport          This is great news for Airports in the Southern Tier     said Senator Schumer    These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help this airports remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region             Our regional airports are integral to the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support them   Senator Clinton said    Maintaining our critical airport infrastructure is important for both the safety of passengers and the ability of our airports to sustain continued air traffic  The more flights we have  the better the economic opportunity  so this funding is a very welcome investment    not only in the airports themselves but the region as a whole           The funding for the Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport will provide for the acquisition of a snow blower  as the existing snow blower has reached the end of its useful life and needs to be replaced  This acquisition will enhance safety at the airport during inclement weather         The funding for Tri Cities Airport will go towards an airport master plan study update  which will include an obstruction study  This update is necessary to guide future development         The funding for the Corning Painted Post Airport will provide for phase I of a project to construct a parallel taxiway  as there is currently no paved taxiway  This phase of the project will allow all weather access for aircraft between the runway and apron   Additional funding for the Corning Painted Post Airport will provide for the acquisition of land for the Runway End 32 Protection Zone  which will increase overall safety at the airport
