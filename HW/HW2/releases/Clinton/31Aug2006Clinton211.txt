August 31  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  78 000 for the Village of Potsdam                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  78 850 in Federal Aviation Administration funds to the Village of Potsdam in St  Lawrence County   The funds will be used for the design of the aircraft parking apron expansion and taxilane at Potsdam Municipal   Damon Field              This is great news for St  Lawrence County   said Schumer   Potsdam Municipal   Damon Field serves numerous visitors and residents each year  and these improvements will bolster the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport become a thriving center of economic growth             This funding is a very welcome investment in Potsdam Municipal   Damon Field Airport and in the future of St  Lawrence County     Senator Clinton said    For tourism and business alike  creating more room for additional aircraft is essential for economic development  I am pleased that we can support the airport with this funding and continue to invest in upstate New York             Federal funds will be used to design the expansion of an aircraft parking apron and taxilane   The purpose of the expansion is to allow for additional aircraft tie downs and access to future hangars
