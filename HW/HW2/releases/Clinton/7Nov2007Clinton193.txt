November 7  2007 Senators Schumer and Clinton Announce Over  2 7 Million in Military Construction Funding for Camp Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Funding Included in Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Appropriations Bill Approved by Conference Committee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC  Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that a Joint House and Senate Conference Committee has approved the Fiscal Year 2008 Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Appropriations bill  which includes  2 727 million in military construction funding for a Combined Support Maintenance Facility at Camp Smith    The bill must now be approved by both chambers of Congress   If it is passed by the House and Senate  it will then be sent to the President for his signature    Weve cleared another important hurdle in the appropriations process  said Senator Schumer  who visited Camp Smith earlier this year  This funding will provide the men and women at Camp Smith with the kind of top notch facility that they deserve  I will continue to push this bill into the Presidents lap  so we can secure these vital federal dollars for Westchester    These funds will begin the process of improving infrastructure at Camp Smith  and will allow the camp to better support the New York Army National Guard units that train there  I applaud the conference committees approval of this funding  and I look forward to the President signing this bill into law  said Senator Clinton    The  2 727 million in federal funding will be used toward a replacement Combined Support Maintenance Facility at Camp Smith   The new facility will provide 87 000 square feet for maintenance  storage  arms vault  and administrative space and will replace the current undersized facility   The Combined Support Maintenance Facility will support 28 individual units of the New York Army National Guard with an authorized strength of 3 547 soldiers
