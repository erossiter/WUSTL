July 20  2006 Senator Clinton Secures Key Senate Panel Approval of  500 000 for the Hudson Valley Community College Nursing Program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Funding included in the Fiscal Year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed approval by the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee of the Fiscal Year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations Bill  which includes  500 000 that the Senator secured for the Hudson Valley Community College Nursing Program          The bill will proceed to the Senate floor for consideration following Full Appropriations Committee approval this afternoon   Following consideration and passage on the Senate floor  the bill will head into a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between their two bills  Following conference  the bill will again be sent to the House and Senate for final approval  after which it will be sent to the President for his signature            The shortage of nurses in New York and around the country has reached crisis proportions     Senator Clinton said     Hudson Valley Community College stands ready to help fill this very real need  This funding will help Hudson Valley Community College offer convenient and flexible nursing classes  which is exactly what working families need  This funding will be a real boost for Hudson Valley Community College and their program to train and qualify new nurses                Our Nursing program  like many of our allied health programs  costs much more to provide than we receive in revenue from state aid and state mandated tuition  so any expansion of the program would have to be dependent on new funding sources     Hudson Valley Community College President Andrew J  Matonak said    We are so pleased that Senator Clinton has championed our cause  and secured the funds that will allow us to begin to address this important issue             These funds will be used to help Hudson Valley Community College establish their new part time weekend nursing program   Currently  hundreds students at Hudson Valley Community College are waiting to enter the college  s Nursing Program  however  the college was been unable to expand the program without additional external funding
