July 27  2007 Schumer  Clinton   10 Million for Port of Rochester Waterfront Included in Final Water Resources Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Funding for Key Projects Included in Water Resources Development Act Approved by House Senate Conferees Today                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington  DC  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that a House Senate Conference Committee has agreed on a final version of the Water Resources Development Act  WRDA  which includes  10 million for the Army Corps of Engineers to participate in the ecosystem restoration  navigation  flood damage reduction  and recreation components of the Port of Rochester Waterfront Revitalization Project    The Water Resources Development Act  which was approved by the House Senate Conferees today  authorizes Corps of Engineers civil works projects  including navigation  ecosystem restoration and shoreline protection projects  Under the authorizations  the Corps partners with local sponsors to conduct the project  A project authorization in WRDA is a necessary first step towards securing funding in an appropriations bill at a later date  The bill now goes to the full House and Senate for final approval before heading to the President for his signature     This is great news for Rochester  said Schumer   Authorization for this funding will allow the city of Rochester to revitalize and restore the citys historic waterfront  These improvements will serve as a huge boost for the local economy      I am very pleased to support Mayor Duffys Rochester Waterfront initiative and thrilled that we could secure this authorization in the final version of the bill  said Senator Clinton   This is a critical project for the City and its overall vision for the future  This funding will not only enable the expertise of the federal government to help with this development  it will also provide a substantial financial boost for the City of Rochester as they work to restore and revitalize the Port of Rochester waterfront
