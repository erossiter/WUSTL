August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  237 000 for Buffalo Lancaster Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Federal Money Will Be Used to Acquire Land for Further Development                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  237 256 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Buffalo Lancaster Airport   Federal funds will be used to acquire land for the airports further development    This is great news for Erie County  said Schumer  The Buffalo Lancaster Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is welcome news for Erie County  said Senator Clinton  This funding will ensure travelers and tourists alike visit and invest locally to help build a strong and stable economy for New York  The Buffalo Lancaster Airport is a key component to Erie Countys continued economic success and I am pleased that we can help the airport successfully expand and grow    Federal funds will be used to acquire land for a runway extension  This project will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
