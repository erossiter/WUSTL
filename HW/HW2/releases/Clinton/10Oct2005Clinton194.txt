October 10  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Regarding the Tragic Loss of Life and Devastation Due to the Earthquake in South Asia                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 My heart goes out to the people of Pakistan and the other nations in the region affected by this weekend s devastating earthquake   Our thoughts and prayers are with the victims and their families   I am very glad that the United States has already begun to send aid and resources to the region  and I know that the American people will respond to the pleas for help with characteristic generosity    I will do everything I can to help make family members and concerned New Yorkers aware of all of the federal resources at their disposal  and to help get aid to the victims
