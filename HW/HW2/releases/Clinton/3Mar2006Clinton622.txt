March 3  2006 Senator Clinton Announces Passage of Bill Honoring Local Hero Corporal Jason Dunham                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Bill renames Scio Post Office after Iraq hero                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Washington  DC    Today Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton announced Senate passage of legislation to honor local hero Marine Corporal Jason Dunham  The bill designates the United States Postal Service facility located at 4422 West Sciota Street in Scio  New York  as the  Corporal Jason L  Dunham Post Office               I am so pleased the Senate passed this important bill that is a fitting tribute to a true hero  Renaming the Scio Post Office honors Corporal Dunham  s noble sacrifice on behalf of his Nation and the Marines with whom he proudly served     Senator Clinton said    Corporal Dunham  s actions in Iraq are truly humbling and worthy of the greatest honor              I m absolutely thrilled that the Senate has passed this bill to name the Scio Post Office after Jason Dunham   said Representative Kuhl    I sincerely thank Senator Clinton for her hard work and assistance in moving this bill through the Senate so the President can sign it   The story of Jason s heroic actions in Iraq are truly inspiring  and I look forward to seeing Jason s name up on the post office very soon           Corporal Jason Dunham was 22 years old in mid April of 2004 and serving in Husaybah  Iraq  An Iraqi terrorist attacked Dunham  and Dunham selflessly acted to shield his squad members from a hand grenade blast  The blast severely wounded Dunham and he was flown to Bethesda Naval Hospital outside of Washington  D C  where he died April 22  2004          This bill was introduced in the House of Representatives by Representative Randy Kuhl  Following House passage in December  Senator Clinton called on her colleagues to pass this bill in the Senate as well
