July 16  2005 Johnson Outdoors Present Certificate of Appreciation to Senator Clinton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Certificate awarded for Senator  s help in securing  15 9 million defense order                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Binghamton  NY   On behalf of their employees  representatives from Johnson Outdoors Inc   today presented a certificate of appreciation to Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton for her efforts and support in obtaining an   urgent need    request from the U S  Government for their Modular General Purpose Tent System  In presenting the certificate  the officials thanked Senator Clinton for securing and maintaining jobs for their dedicated and talented workforce       Senator Clinton was instrumental in facilitating the contact between Johnson Outdoors and the Defense Logistics Agency  which lead to this contract         In March 2005  Johnson Outdoors  makers of Eureka Tents  announced to Binghamton employees a pending elimination of permanent positions because of the significant decline of new military tent orders and contracts over the past year   As soon as Senator Clinton learned of the potential elimination of positions at the company  her office took immediate action   They worked with the Defense Logistics Agency to make sure it knew that one of our country  s premier tent suppliers was in a perilous position   As a result of this effort  the DLA issued an   urgent needs    contract  which Johnson Outdoors won  saving numerous local jobs          It is such an honor to meet with representatives from Johnson Outdoor and to congratulate them on their work  I am so proud that Johnson Outdoors is playing such a critical role in supporting our men and women in uniform     Senator Clinton said     I am grateful that the Defense Logistics Agency recognized the superiority of Johnson Outdoors   products and has awarded this contract  which will help our military and support the local economy          The new emergency tent order ensures production of the Modular General Purpose Tent Systems  MGPTS  at the Binghamton operation through the calendar year   The government order calls for delivery of 2 510 modular general purpose tent systems  MGPTS  over the next 8 months   Urgent need orders are awarded based on a supplier  s proven capability to ensure quality and quantity at an accelerated pace   The MGPTS  first used by U S  troops in 1999  resulted from a joint innovation effort by the U S  military and Johnson Outdoors targeted at providing superior all weather tent protection   Johnson Outdoors is one of only two U S  manufacturers qualified to produce the MGPTS    The Company  s Outdoor Equipment business  headquartered in Binghamton  is preparing to begin production against the order as soon as possible         There is no question that our military needs the best equipment and I am proud that New York companies like Johnson Outdoors are playing a role in that process and saving jobs at the same time     Senator Clinton said
