July 20  2006 Senator Clinton Secures Key Senate Panel Approval of  200 000 for Renovation of the Historic Strand Theater                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Funds included in Fiscal Year 2007 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed approval by the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee of the Fiscal Year 2007 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  which includes  200 000 that the Senator secured  for the renovation of the Strand Theatre in downtown Plattsburgh           The bill will proceed to the Senate floor for consideration following Full Appropriations Committee approval this afternoon   Following consideration and passage on the Senate floor  the bill will head into a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between their two bills  Following conference  the bill will again be sent to the House and Senate for final approval  after which it will be sent to the President for his signature            I was thrilled to be able to visit the Strand earlier this month   From the moment I walked in I could see the potential for this theater to become a premiere center for the performing arts  The Strand Theater promises to be one of the cultural jewels of the North Country    Its history and grandeur is matched only by the determination and vision of the community in Plattsburgh   The efforts to restore and redevelop this important building will help benefit the city  s downtown     Senator Clinton said    I am very pleased that I could secure these funds to help realize the city  s vision for economic development downtown and along the waterfront             The funds will support the reconstruction and restoration of the historic Strand Theater in downtown Plattsburgh as a center for performing arts in the North Country region of New York   The North Country Cultural Center for the Arts has acquired the historic Strand Theater to create the Strand Performing Arts Center as a multi purpose center for performances and cultural activities of all kinds  The Strand is the last remaining historic vaudeville house and cinema in the region  It will provide a large  1020 seats   state of the art venue for community based programs  school related programs  and for hosting outside performers and shows  When opened in 2008  it will be professionally directed and promoted
