April 20  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce  600 000 for Airports in and Around Finger Lakes Region                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Airports in Dansville  Le Roy and Seneca Falls Will Receive Federal Money for Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded  600 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  grants to airports in and around the Finger Lakes region  The money will be used for a series of improvements  ranging from equipment purchases to taxiway construction          This is great news for the Finger Lakes Region   said Schumer   These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           These airports are an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support them   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to these airports is critical  because more flights and better service means more jobs and more opportunities for the region           Three Finger Lakes area airports will receive federal money under today s award   Dansville Municipal Airport in Livingston County will receive  222 000 to construct  mark and light T Hanger access taxilanes   The taxilanes will provide access to a future T Hanger   Le Roy Airport in Genesee County will receive  150 000 to install airfield guidance signs   Installing lighted guidance signs will increase airport safety   Finger Lakes Regional Airport in Seneca County will receive  228 000 to install weather reporting equipment   An automated weather observing system will provide pilots with localized weather information   This information will improve efficiency and provide safer use of the facilities        New York State received 15 grants this round  totaling  7 793 367 in federal funds  The grants were approved under the Federal Aviation Administration Airport Improvement Program
