July 28  2005 Clinton Welcomes Entergy Announcement on Backup Power For Emergency Sirens                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed Entergy  s announcement today that they will install backup power for the emergency sirens around the Indian Point nuclear plant             I welcome Entergy  s commitment today to make this important change     said Senator Clinton     It s the right thing to do for the safety and security of the people who live around the plant              Entergy  s announcement follows several months of action by Senator Clinton  including the introduction of legislation   After the NRC rejected a citizen petition to require backup power at Indian Point Sirens on May 18  Senator Clinton wrote a letter to NRC Chairman Diaz on May 26  asking him to reconsider and reverse his decision
