June 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 3 Million for Hudson Valley Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced a grant of   1 026 000 for the Westchester County Airport in White Plains  NY  and a  332 500 grant for the Stewart International Airport in Newburgh  NY  from the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT             This is great news for our Hudson Valley airports     said Senator Schumer    These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region               This is a wise investment in Westchester County Airport  Stewart Airport and in the safety of their passengers  Ensuring that such vital emergency equipment is on hand will allow officials to respond to emergencies quickly and effectively  It is also important that chemicals are properly and safely stored  Maintaining airports that are safe  effective and efficient is important for the ability of our airports to sustain continued air traffic  which is good for business and economic development  Keeping passengers safe and moving smoothly means more flights arriving and leaving on time and that  s good news for business travelers  commuters and tourists alike     Senator Clinton said          The  1 026 000 grant will provide federal funding for Westchester County Airport to acquire an aircraft rescue and fire fighting vehicle  The Federal Aviation Regulation Part 139 requires that the airport have two fully functional aircraft rescue and fire fighting apparatuses  Stewart International Airport is receiving a  332 500 grant to construct a sand and chemical storage building  The airport currently does not have any sand  sodium acetate or urea bulk storage facilities
