July 24  2006 Schumer  Clinton Welcome Senate  s Unanimous Approval of Bill to Rename Tuckahoe Post Office After 9 11 Hero Ronald Bucca                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Schumer Spearheaded Efforts to Support Bill      Dedicated Public Servant and First FDNY Fire Marshal Ever Killed in the Line of Duty will be Commemorated by Tuckahoe Post Office                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton announced that legislation to honor hometown hero and former New York Fire Department Marshal Ronald Bucca was passed unanimously late last week by the United States Senate           Schumer and Clinton welcomed the Senate  s unanimous approval of the bill after sending a letter to the Senate Committee on Homeland Security and Government Affairs earlier this year  which Senator Schumer spearheaded  lobbying for the United States Postal Office in Tuckahoe to be designated as the  Ronald Bucca Post Office   On September 11  2001  the former Fire Marshal sacrificed his life in the line of duty while rescuing fellow New Yorkers from the World Trade Center             Ronald Bucca epitomized bravery  patriotism and public service but his life was tragically cut short     said Senator Schumer    It is only fitting that the post office in Bucca  s hometown will now bear the name of this great American hero who will be honored in the Tuckahoe community forever               This is a wonderful development and puts us well on the way to renaming the Tuckahoe Post Office after Ronald Bucca  This was the right thing for the Senate to do and the fact that they approved it by unanimous consent is even more of a tribute to this true hero  This post office renaming will be a fitting honor for a man who made the greatest of sacrifices to our nation and I am very pleased that we are making such great progress towards achieving this goal     Senator Clinton said          Mr  Bucca  who dedicated his life to serving the people of New York and the nation  was the first New York City Fire Marshal to ever die while in the line of duty  On September 11  2001  Bucca had just reached the fire on the 78th floor of the South Tower of the World Trade Centers when the Tower collapsed  Bucca served in many capacities during his career in public service  Following 29 years of proud service in the United States military  Mr  Bucca protected the people of New York for 23 years as a firefighter  the last nine of which he was Fire Marshal   Similar legislation was also introduced in the House by Congresswoman Nita Lowey
