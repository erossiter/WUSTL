September 6  2007 Senators Schumer and Clinton Announce Senate Approval of  73 4 Million in Military Construction Funding for Long Island                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Funds for Gabreski Air National Guard Base and Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale   Funding Included in Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Appropriations Bill Approved by U S  Senate                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC  Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton announced that the Senate has approved the Veterans Affairs and Military Construction Fiscal Year 2008 Appropriations bill which includes  8 4 million in military construction funding for the Gabreski Air National Guard Base Pararescue Facility in Westhampton Beach and  65 million for the Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale    Now that the bill has been approved by the full Senate  it will go into conference with the House version before going back to the Senate and House and on to the President    The funding for Gabreski would support the construction of part one of the Pararescue Facility at the Francis S  Gabreski Airport in Westhampton Beach  NY   In April 2004 as a result of unit reorganization  the 106th Air Rescue Wing more than doubled   Special requirements that could not be accommodated under the old organization were now severely strained   Space is required to perform pre mission planning and training so that missions can continue to be successfully executed without endangering crew members during challenging and high risk missions    This is great news for the 106th Air Rescue Wing  and a tremendous boon for Suffolk  Schumer said  With personnel nearly doubling at Gabreski  this funding is absolutely essential to ensure the safety and proper training of our military men and women  and will contribute to the economic growth and well being of the surrounding community    This is an important step in getting final approval for these funds so I am pleased that the Senate has acted on this bill  These funds for the Pararescue Facility at the Frances Gabreski Air National Guard Base are critical for both the crew members of the 106th Air Rescue Wing and for the community  Senator Clinton said  If we are to support the 106th as they continue on their missions  we need to give them this kind of federal boost  Gabreski continues to hold the promise of support for our men and women in uniform as well as growth for the local economy and these funds will be integral to the bases continued growth    The funds for the Armed Forces Reserve Center in Farmingdale confirm what was proposed in the Presidents Fiscal Year 2008 Budget and will help construct new facilities  which will include administrative and organizational buildings designed to house headquarters  classrooms  arms rooms  logistics supply rooms  motor pools  and maintenance bays  When these facilities are completed  units will have modern and revitalized facilities from which to support their missions   There are approximately 100 AFRC units being built nation wide to support the BRAC consolidation plan    The establishment of an Armed Forces Reserve Center at Farmingdale will be a critical new resource for homeland security and New Yorks emergency preparedness  and I am pleased to see this important funding has moved one step closer to fruition  Schumer said  This state of the art facility will provide the men and women of the Army Reserve with a central location for mobilization of guard and reserve forces that have been at the frontline in our nation s defense     This is great news for Farmingdale  Senator Clinton said  The Armed Forces Reserve Center at Farmingdale will provide critical support to the regions frontline defense and to local homeland and disaster preparedness  so it is important that we can provide the federal funds to support it  With our nations increased reliance on the reserve component  they deserve to have upgraded facilities and resources that will help them achieve their missions
