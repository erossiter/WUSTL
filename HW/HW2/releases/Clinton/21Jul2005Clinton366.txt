July 21  2005 Clinton Announces Key Senate Panel Backs  200 000 For The Stanley Theatre Expansion                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Urged Appropriations Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton announced today that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved  200 000 for the Stanley Theatre in the FY06 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill         This is wonderful news for Utica and the Mohawk Valley  especially in light of the recent recommendation to elevate the theatre from local to national significance on the National Register of Historic Places     Senator Clinton said           The Stanley Theatre has been a cornerstone of the community for decades  Generations have enjoyed remarkable plays  operas  and musicals and participated in vibrant community and educational outreach programming at this splendid Theatre  I am so pleased that the Stanley Theatre will now receive federal funding to improve its facilities which will further enhance the Theatre  s role as a centerpiece in Utica s downtown economic development strategy           The funding approved by the Committee today will allow the Stanley Theatre in Utica  NY to undergo a major expansion and renovation   The project will double the size of the Stanley stage from 29   x 77   to 50   x 110    raise the stage roof from 66   to 80    add new life safety systems to meet current safety codes  make all dressing rooms handicap accessible  support space and stage area  modernize all theatrical equipment  and preserve and restore significant and character defining historic architectural facade and interiors        In January  Senator Clinton helped to elevate the Stanley Theatre to a national level of significance on the National Register of Historical Places  This recognition allowed the Theatre to be eligible for federal funding  The  200 000 will be used with an earlier local state grant of  280 000 for the much anticipated expansion and renovation
