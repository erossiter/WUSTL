August 2  2006 Statement of Senator Clinton on Secretary Rumsfeld  s Decision to Reverse Course and Appear at Tomorrow  s Open Armed Services Committee Hearing                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   EARLIER  Senator Clinton Calls on Defense Secretary Rumsfeld to Appear Before the Senate Armed Services Committee   Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton issued the following statement after Defense Secretary Donald Rumsfeld  s 11th hour decision to reverse course and attend tomorrow  s open Senate Armed Services Committee Hearing            Secretary Rumsfeld  s eleventh hour decision to reverse course and appear at tomorrow  s open Armed Services Committee hearing is the right one  as the Committee and the American people should hear directly from the top civilian leader at the Pentagon  the person most responsible for implementing the President  s military policy in Iraq and Afghanistan
