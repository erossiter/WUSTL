August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over One Million Dollars for Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Federal Money Will Be Used to Improve Runway Safety Area                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded a grant of  1 285 779 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport  The airport will use the funds to improve their runway safety area    This is great news for the City of Ithaca and Tompkins County  said Schumer  The Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    Not only does it provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but it also allows businesspeople to access the Southern Tier area easily     I will continue to fight for federal funds to help this airport remain a key component of economic growth in the region    This is great news for Tompkins County  said Senator Clinton   The Ithaca Tompkins Regional airport has served Tompkins County and New Yorks Finger Lakes region for over fifty years  and these funds will ensure continued safe operation   This airport is vital for travel and commerce in the region  and I will continue to help ensure it remains safe and secure for business travelers and tourists alike    Ithica Tompkins Regional Airport will use these federal funds to improve their runway safety area by rehabilitating the access road  removing obstruction and enhancing general security
