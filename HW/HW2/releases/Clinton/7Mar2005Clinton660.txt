March 7  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 8 Million In Federal Money For Oneonta Municipal Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Federal Funding Included in FY05 Omnibus Appropriations bill    Airport Improvement Grant Will Rehabilitate Runways  Remove Obstruction  and Install Perimeter Fencing at Oneonta Municipal Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                      US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that Oneonta Municipal Airport has been awarded over  1 8 Million in federal funds for the airport to rehabilitate its runways  remove obstructions  and install perimeter fencing   The funds are listed in the Transportation  Treasury  and Independent Agencies Appropriations section of the Fiscal Year 2005 Omnibus Appropriations bill          This is great news for the entire Southern Tier   said Schumer   The Oneonta Municipal Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           The Oneonta Municipal Airport is an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to the airport is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region
