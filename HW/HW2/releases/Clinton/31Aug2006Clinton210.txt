August 31  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce  300 000 for New York State                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  DC   Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  300 000 in Federal Aviation Administration funds to New York State   The funds will be used to conduct a state system plan study for future aviation development            This is great news for New York     said Schumer     The development of the aviation industry is always a top priority   This study will ensure that our airports are running safely and efficiently as they continue to support the New York economy                Investing in New York  s aviation system is vital to maintaining a healthy economy statewide   I am hopeful that this study will produce valuable information for future developments in air safety and security     said Senator Clinton           Federal funds will be used to develop a state system plan study for future aviation development in New York
