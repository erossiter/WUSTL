October 6  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Heightened Security Alert to the NYC Subway System                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             I have spoken with Homeland Security Secretary Michael Chertoff  and have been briefed on the heightened security alert for New York City  s subway system   New York has the greatest police force and first responders in the world  and I know that they are doing everything they can to protect our city   There are millions of New Yorkers who ride trains  buses  and ferries everyday  and they must be adequately protected    It is imperative that New Yorkers know that our transportation infrastructure continues to operate with the highest security operations in place
