May 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 8 Million for Hudson Valley Airfields                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Federal Money will be Used for Infrastructure and Equipment Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded four grants totaling  1 850 500 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to airports in the Hudson Valley  SWF Airport Acquisition  Inc will receive  1 463 000 to rehabilitate the taxiway at Stewart International Airport   The County of Westchester will use two separate grants totaling  237 500 for rehabilitation and new equipment at the Westchester County Airport   The County of Sullivan will receive  150 000 to conduct a study for future development at the Sullivan County International Airport           This is great news for the Hudson Valley   said Schumer   The Stewart International Airport  the Westchester County Airport and the Sullivan County Airport are important gateways for this region    Not only do they provide local aviators and tourists with a base of operation  but they also allow businesspeople to easily access the Hudson Valley   I will fight for more federal funds to help these airports remain springboards for economic growth            This is welcome news for the Hudson Valley because they are not only important investments in our airports infrastructure but also in the local economy   said Senator Clinton   This funding will allow for necessary improvements and rehabilitation measures which are critical to continued maintenance and successful operation           SWF Airport Acquisition  Inc   will use federal funds for the rehabilitation of Taxiway G at Stewart International Airport   The existing pavement condition of the taxiway is poor and requires rehabilitation to ensure overall airport safety            Westchester County will use a  190 000 grant for phase I of a project to rehabilitate the terminal facility roadway at the Westchester County Airport   The surface of the terminal facility roadway network has deteriorated and is in need of resurfacing to ensure airport safety   Another grant of  47 500 will be used to acquire a wheelchair lift to accommodate passengers in need of assistance            Sullivan County will use federal funds to perform an airport master plan update at Sullivan County International   The update is needed to guide future development at the airport
