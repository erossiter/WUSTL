September 25  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 Million Coming to Long Island to Improve Transportation Options for Elderly                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Funds Will Be Used to Buy Hybrid Buses and Specialized Vans for Elderly to Increase Transportation Options in North Hempstead and Brookhaven                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has released  1 007 006 in Federal Transit Administration  FTA  funds from FY2005 and FY2006 to Nassau and Suffolk Counties to buy hybrid buses for the Town of North Hempstead  and paratransit vans for the Town of Brookhaven to increase transportation options for elderly residents  This funding is great news for Long Island  and a boon for elderly residents who would otherwise have difficulty accessing essential local services  said Schumer  These new buses and vans will increase options for elderly Long Island residents  allowing easier access to doctors appointments  grocery shopping  social centers  and other important activities that would otherwise be outside their reach  This is welcome news for the towns of North Hempstead and Brookhaven who will now be able to provide additional transportation options for the seniors in their communities  said Senator Clinton  The funding for these hybrid vehicles will not only allow their residents easier access to the essential services they rely on  but it will also offer an environmentally conscious option that ultimately benefits everybody on Long Island  Two of the grants totaling  885 534 will go to the Town of North Hempstead for the purchase of seven 30 foot hybrid buses  with room for twenty four passengers and two wheelchairs on each bus  The buses will run from 7 30 am to 3 30 pm Monday through Friday to enhance the mobility of town seniors  A third grant totaling  121 472 will go to the Town of Brookhaven for the purchase of four paratransit vans and related equipment for the replacement of existing vans in the towns Senior Citizens Program
