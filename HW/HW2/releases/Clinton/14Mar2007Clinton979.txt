March 14  2007 Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Meets Chinese AIDS Activist Dr  Gao Yaojie                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC   On March 14  Senator Clinton met with Dr  Gao Yaojie  a retired physician and AIDS activist from Henan Province in China  Dr  Gao was in Washington  DC to accept an award from the Vital Voices Global Partnership  Senator Clinton  an Honorary Co Chair of Vital Voices  was instrumental in persuading the Chinese government to allow Dr  Gao to travel to the United States and receive this award  This visit marks the first time that Dr  Gao has traveled outside of China to share her experiences in provide care  treatment  education and support to individuals living with HIV and AIDS
