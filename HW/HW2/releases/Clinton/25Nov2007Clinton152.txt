November 25  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Australian Elections                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               I want to extend my warmest congratulations to the Australian Labor Party and its leader  Kevin Rudd  on their election victory  Australia is a close ally and a longtime friend of the United States  We have important economic links with Australia  we work together on numerous issues of mutual concern  such as regional security  and our future relationship will be crucial when it comes to dealing with global problems like climate change  I am heartened by Mr  Rudds commitment to fight global warming and I hope that the United States and Australia will work together in crafting a post Kyoto agreement to tackle the climate change challenge  I know that  under this new leadership  the relationship between the United States and Australia will continue to strengthen in the years to come
