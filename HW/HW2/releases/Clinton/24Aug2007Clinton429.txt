August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 4 Million for Hudson Valley Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Federal Money Will be Used for Important Environmental Studies  Runway Rehabilitation and Obstruction Removal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded four grants amounting to  1 440 987 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to airports in the Hudson Valley  Orange County will use three separate grants totaling  1 171 187 to rehabilitate runway lighting  remove obstruction and conduct an environmental study at Stewart International Airport  Dutchess County will use a  269 800 grant to remove obstruction at the Dutchess County Airport    This is great news for the Hudson Valley  said Schumer  The Stewart International Airport and the Dutchess County Airport are important general aviation facilities in this region    Not only do they provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but they also allow businesspeople to access the Hudson Valley area easily     I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region    This is welcome news for Dutchess and Orange Counties  said Senator Clinton  Anything we can do to support our airports promises to be positive for the local economy  I am pleased that we can continue to support them in this way because our airports remain vital to business travelers and tourists alike  and I will continue to work to ensure New Yorks airports continue to thrive    Orange County will use a grant totaling  215 000 to conduct an environmental assessment for the relocation of the Tower Hill at Stewart International Airport  Another  922 600 in federal funding will go towards rehabilitating the runway lighting  A final  33 587 will be spent on removing obstruction  Dutchess County will use  269 800 to remove obstruction at Dutchess County Airport
