July 6  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Regarding FEMA Mismanagement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Reports that FEMA is responsible for wasting more than  23 million worth of ice are very concerning and are unfortunately another tragic example of failure on the part of the Administration and its response to Hurricanes Katrina and Rita   Not only was FEMA unable to deliver ice to the Katrina and Rita victims when it was needed most  but it has now wasted millions more federal dollars because of its failures   This is another sad chapter in the Administrations handling of the hurricanes   FEMA and the Administration have already wasted billions of tax payer funds because of their inept response to the hurricanes and their failed planning and recovery efforts in the wake of the disaster   Today over 70 000 victims of Hurricanes Katrina and Rita are still living in federal government provided trailers and mobile homes   It is clear that the Department of Homeland Security is ill equipped to oversee FEMA and its responsibilities and the best way to ensure the efficient and effective handling of our federal disaster efforts is through an independent FEMA    Senator Clinton has legislation to restore the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  to an independent  Cabinet level federal agency  to ensure that it has the authority it needs to effectively manage recovery efforts for future crises  Read More
