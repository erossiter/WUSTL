March 3  2006 Senator Clinton Applauds University of Rochester on Recognition as A Top School For Entrepreneurs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton applauded the University of Rochester on being named one of the top schools for entrepreneurs by Fortune Small Business            I congratulate the University of Rochester  s achievement for being named one of the top schools in the country for entrepreneurs  When you look at Rochester s history in this area it is no surprise that this honor would be bestowed upon what is one of New York s finest institutions     Senator Clinton said           The University was particularly noted for their successful initiatives within the engineering and anthropology departments  which are both working to create a pollution free school bus for the Rochester School District   Senator Clinton has also been an advocate for pollution free school buses   The senator has championed the Clean School Bus USA Program in Congress  working for the last three years with her Congressional colleagues to increase funding for the program
