June 2  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Regarding the FAA Investigation Into New York Terminal Radar Approach Control  Tracon  Facility                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       I remain troubled by the situation at NY Tracon  We have one of the busiest towers in the country and it must be adequately staffed  Our controllers must have all the resources and rest they need to safely watch over the flying public  We don t need more reports  we need amicable solutions  The FAA and the controllers must sit down  work together  and solve these issues to ensure we have enough eyes watching our skies  We have the safest air traffic control system in the world  Let s make sure we keep that gold standard  We owe it to every citizen who boards an airplane
