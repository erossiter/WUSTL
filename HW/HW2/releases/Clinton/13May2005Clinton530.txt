May 13  2005 Senator Clinton Announces Stewart  Preserved by DOD in 2005 BRAC Round                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington  DC    Senator Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Defense has recommended that Stewart Air National Guard Base in Newburgh  NY  home of the 105th Airlift Wing of the NY Air National Guard  remain open in this 2005 Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission          I m proud to announce the Department of Defense recognizes the military value of the 105th Airlift Wing at Stewart Air National Guard Base  the strength of the local community  and the important role they play in the defense of our nation  As the only C 5 equipped strategic airlift organization in the Air National Guard  they have been integral to our operations in Iraq and Afghanistan  as well as providing tsunami relief   Senator Clinton said          I have always said that if based on the merits  we would have a very strong case to make for Stewart and all of New York s bases  because of their economic impact  their strategic advantage and the overwhelming community support that they enjoy  When I visited Stewart in 2004 and met with community leaders  we talked about the importance of a team effort to ensure the base remained safe during this BRAC round  I m thrilled that our team effort and hard work have paid off and know that we will persevere until the commission delivers its recommendation to the President in September   Stewart is also home to the Marine Corp Reserve s 452nd Aerial Refueler Transport Squadron of KC 130s and the Marine Aviation Logistics Squadron 49  MALS 49          As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  Senator Clinton has worked tirelessly to secure funding and support for all of New York s military installations  Most recently  the Senator met with U S  Air Force Chief of Staff  General John Jumper  to discuss and promote New York s installations  including Stewart         Last year  Senator Clinton worked to secure  501 000 in planning and design funds for the Air National Guard to replace their fire station at Stewart International Airport so it can fully support crash  fire  and rescue missions  This year  Senator Clinton has worked to authorize  10 2 million to fund this critical project         The day to day population at Stewart ANG base is approximately 660 personnel  however  one weekend each month the population surges to 1600 in response to Air National Guard drills  The 105th Rescue Wing is an integral part of Newburgh  NY and contributes more than  213 million to the local economy annually
