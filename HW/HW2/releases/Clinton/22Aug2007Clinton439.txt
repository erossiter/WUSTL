August 22  2007 Statement of Senator Clinton on Bush Administration Support for Modernizing the Federal Housing Administration                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC   It is encouraging that the Administration is finally willing to recognize at least some of the distress in the housing market and to discuss specific steps to help homeowners I have advocated modernizing the Federal Housing Administration for well over a year I am pleased that the proposals the Administration is considering are in my legislation  the 21st Century Housing Act It is clear that ensuring a strong FHA that can provide safe and stable mortgages to more homebuyers and can help responsible homeowners refinance unworkable mortgages is critical for addressing the problems in the housing market
