September 4  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  900 000 for Massena International Airport Richards Field                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Federal Money Will Be Used to Remove Obstructions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  901 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Massena International Airport Richards Field   Federal funds will be used to remove obstructions    This is great news for St Lawrence County  said Schumer  The Massena International Airport Richards Field is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is more great news for St  Lawrence County  said Senator Clinton   This funding will allow for much needed safety improvements at the Massena International Airport Richards Field so it can continue bringing travelers and commerce to the region   I will do all that I can to ensure that the airport continues to receive the resources it needs    Federal funds will be used to remove obstructions  This project will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
