May 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  114 000 for the Oswego County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Money will be Used to Conduct an Obstruction Study at the Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  114 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Oswego County for the Oswego County Airport   Federal funds will be used to perform an obstruction study at the airport           This is more great news for Oswego   said Schumer   The Oswego County Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region             This is more great news for the Oswego County Airport   said Senator Clinton   It is critical that we do all that we can to ensure airport infrastructure is safe and secure  especially as we head into summer and into the vacation season that is so important to the local economy           Oswego County will use federal funds to perform an obstruction study at Oswego County Airport   The study will identify existing obstructions and develop a plan for remediation
