January 8  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton On EPA Decision in Favor of Children  s Health                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton issued the following statement on the Environmental Protection Agency  s announcement that they have ruled out approving for residential use of Acid Copper Chromate  ACC   a wood preservative containing hexavalent chromium  a known human carcinogen   Senator Clinton repeatedly raised concerns to the EPA as they considered allowing use of ACC in playsets  decks and other places where treated wood is used            Today  s news is a victory for children  s health   I repeatedly raised concerns to the EPA about allowing use of a known carcinogen on decks  playsets and other areas where children would be exposed to this hazardous chemical   The EPA has done the right thing
