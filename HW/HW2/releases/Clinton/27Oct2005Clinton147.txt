October 27  2005 Clinton Meets With Canadian Ambassador to Share Concerns Over U S  Border Passport Requirement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator also Expresses her Concern about the Proposal  s Impact on the Fast Ferry                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton met today with the Honorable Frank McKenna  Canadian Ambassador to the United States  to discuss their mutual concern over the proposed Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative   Senator Clinton reiterated her fear that the strict travel restrictions proposed in the Initiative could have far reaching economic implications and widespread social ramifications for both American and Canadian communities along the northern border           At the meeting  Senator Clinton stressed that although national security  particularly security at our borders  must continue to be paramount  it is crucial that any new travel requirements be sensitive to the potentially serious impact on tourism  the regional economy  and the lives of border families            I was very pleased to meet with Ambassador McKenna today and to underscore our shared view that we have to find a solution that protects our security and at the same time allows for the legal  unencumbered travel across the border that our border communities need to thrive     Senator Clinton said following the meeting           Separately today  Senator Clinton also voiced her concerns about the impact that the Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative could have on the Spirit of Ontario high speed ferry            With yesterday  s news regarding the fast ferry  we don  t need any more impediments to travel     Senator Clinton said    We should be doing everything we can to encourage tourism between upstate New York and Canada             Senator Clinton has underscored her deep reservations about the Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative since it was first previewed in April of this year   The Senator has met with community and business leaders from across New York  s Northern Border to discuss this issue  including speaking to public forums in Massena  Plattsburgh  Buffalo and Watertown  and has made her concerns known to both Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice and Homeland Security Secretary Chertoff  Senator Clinton also sponsored legislation that would have prevented funds from going to the Initiative which was unfortunately eliminated by Republicans in the House Senate Conference
