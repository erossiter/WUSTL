November 8  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Decision by U S  District Court Judge Thomas Platt                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              I remain adamantly opposed to the Shinnecock Nation s proposal to build a gaming facility in the Town of Southampton   The resulting traffic congestion and the significant environmental impact involved with the facility will have too negative an effect on this environmentally sensitive area and on the region as a whole
