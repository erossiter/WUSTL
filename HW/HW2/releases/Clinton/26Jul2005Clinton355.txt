July 26  2005 Clinton Announces Key Senate Panel Backs  250 000 For Alianza Dominicana                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Senator Urged Appropriations Committee To Include Funding In Spending Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee approved  250 000 for Alianza Dominicana Inc  in the FY06 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill  The funding will go towards the construction of the Triangle Building  Alianza Dominicana  s new facility on Audubon Avenue          I am so pleased to announce this funding for Alianza Dominicana     said Senator Clinton    For almost twenty years  Alianza Dominicana has been helping families and neighborhoods in need throughout New York City  I am proud to be able to help them with their extraordinary efforts and hope this new facility will enable the organization to continue offering services to the children and families of New York for the next twenty years and beyond           The funds will help Alianza Dominicana  Inc  construct the Triangle Building  their new facility on Audubon Avenue  which houses the Afro Quisqueya Cultural Center and includes gallery space  performance space  public assembly apace  and administrative office space  Dubbed the Triangle Building due to its shape and dimensions  the 46 000 square foot building will be located at the intersection of Audubon Avenue  St  Nicholas and 166th street        Alianza Dominicana  Inc  is a non profit community development organization that partners with youth  families and public and private institutions to revitalize economically distressed neighborhoods  Alianza s mission is to assist children  youth and families break the cycle of poverty and fulfill their potential as members of the global community   Offering services in 11 sites  Alianza annually services more than 17 000 individuals from Manhattan and the four other boroughs        The FY06 Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill was approved by the Senate Appropriations Committee last week and now heads to the Senate floor for a full vote  After final passage through the Senate  a conference committee will reconcile the differences between the House and Senate versions of the Bill before sending it to the President
