November 15  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Meeting With the McCartney Sisters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I was so pleased to welcome the McCartney sisters back to Washington  D C   Their dedication to the honor of their brother Robert is admirable and inspiring   Robert  s killers must be brought to justice and I urge everyone to cooperate with the authorities to find those responsible   It has been nearly a year since their brother was taken from them and the investigation continues   I appreciate seeing both of them here in Washington and for continuing their quest   My prayers and thoughts remain with them and their family   If there is to be lasting peace in Northern Ireland  there must first be justice
