April 20  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  300 000 for Akron Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Akron Airport Will Receive Federal Money for Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded  304 611 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  grants Akron Airport in Erie County   These funds will help the airport to acquire two nested 10 unit T Hanger buildings   This project will provide adequate interior parking for aircrafts            This is great news for Akron   said Schumer   These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           Akron Airport is an integral part of the local economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support it   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to the airport is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region           New York State received 15 grants this round  totaling  7 793 367 in federal funds  The grants were approved under the Federal Aviation Administration Airport Improvement Program
