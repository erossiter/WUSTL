April 20  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  3 Million for Hudson Valley Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Airport in Middletown and Stewart Airport Will Receive Federal Money for Improvements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded over  3 million in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  grants to airports throughout the Hudson Valley  The money will be used for a series of improvements  ranging from equipment purchases to taxiway construction to facilities upgrades          This is great news for the Hudson Valley   said Schumer   These airports welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region           These airports are an integral part of the Hudson Valley economy and it is important that we do everything we can to support them   Senator Clinton said   Any opportunity we have to improve access to these airports is critical  because more flights and better service simply means more jobs and more opportunities for the region      Two Hudson Valley airports will receive federal money under today s award   Randall Airport in Middletown will receive  36 631 for taxiway extension   This project will allow access to three T Hangars which will be constructed as an independent project   Stewart International Airport in Newburgh will receive  3 074 200 toward Phases II and III of its scheduled terminal improvements   These vital improvements will enhance and promote passenger services to ensure that the facilities will meet the needs of the growing community   Last year  Stewart Airport scored the highest growth rate of any airport nationwide  bringing in 32  more passengers than last year  a total of 526 745 passengers in to and out of the airport        New York State received 15 grants this round  totaling  7 793 367 in federal funds  The grants were approved under the Federal Aviation Administration Airport Improvement Program
