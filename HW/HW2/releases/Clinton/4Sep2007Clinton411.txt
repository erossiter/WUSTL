September 4  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  76 000 for Fulton County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Federal Money Will Be Used for Aircraft Storage Construction and NAVAIDS Installation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  76 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Fulton County Airport in Johnstown  NY   Federal funds will be used to construct an aircraft storage and maintenance hangar and install miscellaneous NAVAIDS     This is great news for Fulton County  said Schumer  The Fulton County Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is welcome news for Fulton County  said Senator Clinton   This funding will provide the Fulton County Airport with new facilities and equipment that will allow continued safe and efficient operation   Smaller airports  like the Fulton County Airport  are crucial for travel and commerce all over New York State  and I will continue to do all that I can to help our airports perform their needed service    Federal funds will be used to construct an aircraft storage and maintenance hangar and install miscellaneous NAVAIDS  This project will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
