July 26  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  106 000 for Ticonderoga Municipal Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Federal Money Will Be Used For Rehabilitation of Apron and Taxiway                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  106 827 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Ticonderoga Municipal Airport  The money will be used to rehabilitate apron and taxiway            This is great news for the North Country   said Schumer   The Ticonderoga Municipal Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    Not only does it provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but it allows businesspeople visiting International Paper to access the Ticonderoga area easily  I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a key component of economic growth in the region             Tourism is the lifeblood of the Adirondacks and Ticonderoga is at the heart of any future development of the region as a tourism destination  This funding is a welcome investment in the airport so that we can keep the flights coming in throughout the year     Senator Clinton said          This grant will provide federal funding for taxiway crack sealing and apron seal coating at Ticonderoga Municipal Airport   The current taxiway and apron pavements require crack sealing to prevent further deterioration
