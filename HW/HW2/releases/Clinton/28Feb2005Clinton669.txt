February 28  2005 Clinton Applauds City of Rochester s Acquisition of Fast Ferry                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington  DC    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton issued the following statement in response to the City of Rochester successfully purchasing the fast ferry today         Today is a new and exciting beginning for the Spirit of Ontario and the people of Rochester  Rochester has always been a leader in innovation and it proved to be nothing less today  I look forward to forging ahead with a new plan for the fast ferry and working closely with our allies in Canada to address the critical issues there so that we can continue to make Rochester a gateway to Canada and beyond
