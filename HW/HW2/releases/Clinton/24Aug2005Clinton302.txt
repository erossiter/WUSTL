August 24  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the BRAC Commission  s Addition of Preparatory School to the U S  Military Academy at West Point                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     I  m pleased that the BRAC commission today recommended that the United States Army Military Academy Preparatory School be moved to West Point    This move  which will add jobs to the Hudson Valley  will cement West Point  s position as one of the premier locations for preparing and training our nation  s future military leaders   I  m proud to represent West Point and delighted that the Academy will add this prestigious preparatory school to its mission
