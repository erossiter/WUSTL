November 16  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Senate Approval of  23 7 Million in Military Construction Funding for the Hudson Valley                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Funds Included in Fiscal Year 2007 Military Construction Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington  DC   Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton welcomed approval this week by the Senate of the Fiscal Year 2007 Military Construction Appropriations Bill  which includes  23 7 million that will benefit the Hudson Valley  including  1 8 million in Military Construction funding from the President  s Budget for Stewart  s USMCR Aviation Support Equipment Shop and  21 9 million in Military Construction funding from the President  s Budget for Camp Smith Readiness Center          The bill will now head into a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between their versions  Following conference  the bill will again be sent to the House and Senate for final approval  after which it will be sent to the President for his signature            This is great news for Camp Smith  Stewart and the Hudson Valley     said Senator Schumer     Funding for the readiness center will help upgrade an aging facility for our men and women in uniform  and help Camp Smith in its future mission               This funding is critical to the upgrading of the facilities at Camp Smith and Stewart and to their future mission and puts us yet another step closer to creating the first class quarters our brave men and women in uniform deserve  These funds will help make sure that we are doing everything we can to ensure their readiness for combat and we are also providing the necessary support structures for them when they are here at home     Senator Clinton said          Funding in the FY 2007 Military Construction Appropriations Bill includes          21 9 million in Military Construction funding from the President  s Budget for Camp Smith Readiness Center        The Military Construction Appropriations bill includes  21 9 million to fund a consolidated readiness center to support units currently stationed at the Camp Smith Training Site  the Peekskill Armory  and Valhalla Armory   The readiness center will provide the necessary administrative  training  and storage areas for multiple units consisting of over 600 personnel   It will replace facilities constructed between 1918 and 1986   Planned facilities will be state of the art and improve readiness and morale of the units benefiting from the new construction       The Military Construction Appropriations bill includes  21 9 million to fund a consolidated
