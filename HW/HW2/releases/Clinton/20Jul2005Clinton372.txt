July 20  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Death of Rochester Deputy Mayor Jeffrey T  Carlson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Today New York has lost a great citizen  a great public servant  and a great man   Jeff Carlson was a treasured friend of mine and a great friend to Rochester          I will always be grateful for Jeff  s friendship and support   He helped me enormously when I first made the transition to elected office and he remained a valued colleague   I will miss him profoundly          My thoughts and prayers are with Jeff s wife and family  At this impossible time  I hope they can take solace and pride in the work Jeff did for the people of Rochester   His lasting contribution to the community he loved will never be forgotten    The city is so much stronger for his life and I join all of Rochester in deeply mourning his death
