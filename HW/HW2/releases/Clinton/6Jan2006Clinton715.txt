January 6  2006 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton Condemning the Remarks by the President of Iran Regarding Ariel Sharon                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Once again  Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad has brought shame on his government with his recent comments expressing his hope that Israeli Prime Minister Ariel Sharon will not survive   These come on the heels of various outrageous statements about Israel in the past few months   With each new despicable comment  President Ahmadinejad further lessens his standing as a credible world leader in the community of nations
