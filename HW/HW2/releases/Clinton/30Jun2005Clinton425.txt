June 30  2005 Clinton  Senate Approves Funding For Erie Canal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Erie Canalway National Heritage Corridor Commission  slated for funding under Senate Interior Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC   Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton announced today that the Senate had approved  700 000 for the Erie Canalway National Heritage Corridor Commission  The funding was included in the FY 06 Interior Appropriations Bill         This is great news  coming on the heels of the Commission s draft plan for the future of this historic treasure  I am excited about the Canal s future and am pleased that the Senate has approved this funding   Senator Clinton said   These continuing federal investments are an important part of our ongoing efforts to promote the Canal and I will work to ensure that these funds remain in the final version of the bill         The  700 000 for the Erie Canalway National Heritage Corridor Commission will support the overall operations  planning and projects of the Commission  This funding will help the Commission complete their management plan and begin the implementation of early action items to preserve and revitalize local canal communities        The House of Representatives passed its bill on May 19th  The two bills will now go to a Conference Committee where the House and Senate will resolve the differences between them
