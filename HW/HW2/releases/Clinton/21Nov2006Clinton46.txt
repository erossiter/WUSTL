November 21  2006 Senator Clinton Meets with Cadets from the U S  Military Academy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  DC   Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton met with a group of 15 cadets last week from the United States Military Academy in West Point  NY   The junior and senior cadets were in Washington  DC to meet with legislators and learn about the work that they do             These are impressive young cadets and I was pleased to have the opportunity to welcome them to Washington     Senator Clinton said    They have my full admiration  not only for their selfless decision to serve our Nation but for their interest in understanding what makes our system of government work  I  m confident that they will continue in their studies and join the ranks of tomorrow  s leaders             The United States Military Academy  U S M A   prepares cadets for   a career of professional excellence and service to the Nation as an officer in the United States Army      Admission to the U S M A  is extremely competitive  candidates must receive a nomination from a member of Congress or from the Department of the Army   In its celebrated 200 year history the U S M A  has produced notable graduates such as former President Dwight D  Eisenhower  General Ulysses S  Grant  and H  Norman Schwarzkopf   Click here for more information about the U S M A  and its celebrated history
