November 8  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Passing of Julia Agnes Washington Bond                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Today  Senator Clinton released the following statement on the death of Mrs  Julia Agnes Washington Bond  mother of NAACP Chairman and Southern Poverty Law Center co founder Julian Bond     My heartfelt thoughts and prayers go out to the entire Bond family at the passing of the family matriarch   Mrs  Bond was a tireless woman who achieved many notable accomplishments dedicating her life to education and retiring at age 92
