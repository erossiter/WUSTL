August 30  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce  3 7 Million in Grants for LaGuardia Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Federal funds will help pay for rehabilitation of Grand Central Parkway Flyover Bridge                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington  DC    U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  3 7 million in Federal Aviation Administration funds to the New York and New Jersey Port Authority   The funds will be used for the rehabilitation of the Grand Central Parkway Flyover Bridge at LaGuardia Airport             With regional air traffic reaching an all time high  and millions of New Yorkers traveling to our local airports each year  it  s crucial that New Yorkers enjoy safe and efficient access to our local airports     said Senator Schumer    This funding will cut down congestion surrounding LaGuardia Airport and make sure it remains a hub for economic development in the New York Metropolitan area               This funding is a huge boost in an effort to keep the roadways safe and traffic flowing in and around one of New York City  s three major airports   With thousands of people traveling to and from LaGuardia Airport on a daily basis  improvements such as this can help us ensure they can do so safely and securely     said Senator Clinton           Federal funds will be used to improve existing pavement conditions to enhance the safety for airport users and facilitate the traffic flow
