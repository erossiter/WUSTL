November 6  2007 Schumer  Clinton   500 000 for North Country Children s Clinic Included in Final Health and Human Services Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Schumer  Clinton Urged Committee to Include Funding In Spending Bill   Federal Funding Will Be Used for Renovation and Construction of Children s Healthcare Facility in Downtown Watertown   Bill Will Now be Sent Back to the Senate for Final Approval                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC   Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that a House Senate Conference Committee has agreed on a final version of the Labor  Health and Human Services and Education FY08 Appropriations Bill  which includes  500 000 for the North Country Childrens Clinic in Watertown  The bill now goes to the full House and Senate for final approval before heading to the President for his signature  Schumer and Clinton urged the committee to include funding for the project in this years spending bill    This is great news for families in Watertown and all of Jefferson County  as well as another critical step in the appropriations process  said Schumer  This clinic has provided our children with the affordable specialized care they need and deserve   Quality healthcare at a young age is essential to preventing health problems later in life    The conference committees approval of this funding is great news for the North Country Childrens Clinic and Watertown  said Senator Clinton   It is extremely important that all our children receive good healthcare  and the clinic has long provided this invaluable service for North Country families   Im glad that my colleagues have approved the funding  and I look forward to seeing the clinic to continue its work in a new and modern facility    The North Country Children s Clinic will use federal funds for capital improvements including renovations and construction of a children s health care facility in downtown Watertown   The Clinic was founded in 1971  by a group of volunteers led by Dick Charles and Dr  George Sturtz  to fill a void in pediatric health care for children without regular access to health care  The first Well Child Clinic was held on October 12  1971 at the North Side Improvement League  a donated site in Watertown  Today  the North Country Children s Clinic is a licensed health care facility serving low to moderate income families with a primary focus on uninsured and underinsured families   Services include health  dental  nutritional  mental health  and insurance enrollment programs  The bill now goes to the full House and Senate for final approval before heading to the President for his signature
