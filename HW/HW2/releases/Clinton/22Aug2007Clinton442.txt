August 22  2007 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on the Effects of Hurricane Dean                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      My thoughts and prayers go out to the citizens of Mexico  Jamaica  Haiti  Martinique  Belize  St  Lucia and the Dominican Republic  as they attempt to deal with the aftermath of Hurricane Dean   I am confident that the United States will once again be at the forefront of a truly compassionate global response that brings timely and meaningful support to the children and families struggling to recover from the devastation of this storm   I know that Mexican Americans  Caribbean Americans  and all New Yorkers  will do what New Yorkers do best during a time of crisis  selflessly come to the aid of others in the spirit of good will
