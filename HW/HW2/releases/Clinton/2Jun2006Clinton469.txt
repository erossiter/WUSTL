June 2  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  5 Million for Schenectady County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced a  5 294 350 grant for Schenectady County Airport in Glenville  NY from the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT             This is great news for the Schenectady Airport     said Senator Schumer    This airport welcome travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for local businesses and strengthening the local economy   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help this airports remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region               This is an important investment in the Schenectady County Airport and in the Capital Region as a whole  Maintaining airports that are effective and efficient is important for both the safety of passengers and the ability of our airports to sustain continued air traffic  which is good for business and economic development  Keeping passengers safe and moving smoothly means more flights arriving and leaving on time and that  s good news for business travelers  commuters and tourists alike     Senator Clinton said          The  5 294 350 grant will allow the Schenectady County Airport to rehabilitate its taxiways
