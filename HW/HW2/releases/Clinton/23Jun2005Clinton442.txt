June 23  2005 Statement by Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on House Vote to Restore Funding For Corporation for Public Broadcasting                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC     Today s vote was the result of tireless work by advocates  legislators and most importantly parents  who voiced their concerns in record numbers against the devastating cuts proposed by the Republican leadership  It s a very good day in the neighborhood  but our work is not over  I will continue to fight in the Senate to ensure that funding is preserved and to restore funding for Ready to Learn  which provides one of the only outlets for high quality  educational children s programming
