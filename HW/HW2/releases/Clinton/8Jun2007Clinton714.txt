June 8  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 1 Million for the Albany International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Money Will be Used to Install Runway Lighting                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  1 149 500 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Albany County Airport Authority for the Albany International Airport   Federal funds will be used to install runway lighting approaches     This is great news for Albany  said Schumer  The Albany International Airport welcomes many thousands of travelers to the Capital District each year  These improvements are important for local businesses and will strengthen the local economy   These funds will be instrumental in increasing safety and capacity   I will continue to fight for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region    This is great news for the Albany International Airport because this funding will be used to provide necessary runway improvements which are critical to its continued maintenance and operation  said Senator Clinton  Air service to and from the Capital Region is critical to its continued growth  Anything we can do to support our airports promises to be positive for the local economy    The Albany County Airport Authority will use federal funds to install runway lighting for Runway 1 approaches   The project will preserve and enhance aeronautical safety and capacity during inclement weather conditions
