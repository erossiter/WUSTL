August 30  2006 Schumer  Clinton Announce  2 259 Million in Emergency Conservation Program Funding for Flood Affected Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Farmers in Broome  Cayuga  Chemung  Chenango  Cortland  Delaware  Herkimer  Madison  Montgomery  Oneida  Oswego  Otsego  Schoharie  Sullivan  Tioga and Ulster counties will be eligible for funds                                                                                                                                                                                                              New York  NY   Senators Charles Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton announced today that United States Agriculture Secretary Mike Johanns has allocated a total of  2 259 000 in Emergency Conservation Program funding for sixteen New York State counties that were affected by recent flooding            In a letter to Secretary Johanns dated July 12  2006  the Senators called on the USDA to make adequate funding available for existing programs that are critical for helping farmers and communities recover from the flooding event  including the Emergency Conservation Program            The Emergency Conservation Program  ECP  provides emergency funding and technical assistance for farmers to rehabilitate farmland damaged by natural disasters   ECP is administered by state and county Farm Service Agency committees   The 16 counties that are eligible for the funding include  Broome  Cayuga  Chemung  Chenango  Cortland  Delaware  Herkimer  Madison  Montgomery  Oneida  Oswego  Otsego  Schoharie  Sullivan  Tioga and Ulster            This is great news for New York farmers who suffered crop damage from summer rains    said Senator Schumer     The relief money allocated by Secretary Johanns will help our farmers recover and repair our essential farmlands               Farmers across the region have suffered terrible losses from the recent floods and continue to feel their ongoing consequences  These funds will provide the necessary resources to help them rebuild and recover from this devastating event   New York farmers play a vital role in the state s economy and I am pleased that they will receive the critical assistance they need to get back on their feet     Senator Clinton said
