October 13  2006 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton on Developments in the Saint Andrews Talks on the Northern Ireland Peace Process                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Today  s announcement of the results of multiparty talks facilitated by British Prime Minister Tony Blair and Irish Taoiseach Bertie Ahern is an encouraging sign that progress continues toward implementation of the tenets of the Good Friday Agreement   Everyone involved with the discussions at St  Andrews in Scotland has an important part in this process   It is critical that Northern Ireland move toward a full  democratic government   I encourage all parties involved to look carefully at the proposal now on the table   So much has been accomplished in this process and I am hopeful that the remaining important steps are taken toward realizing lasting peace
