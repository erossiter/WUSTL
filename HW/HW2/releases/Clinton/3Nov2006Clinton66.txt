November 3  2006 Senator Clinton Facilitates Donation of 15 Computers to Canajoharie Schools                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      New York  NY    Senator Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced the donation of 15 computers to schools in Canajoharie  NY in an effort to help the flood stricken community rebuild and recover   The computers  five of which will go to the East Hill School  Canajoharie Middle School and Canajoharie High School were made available by Senator Clinton through the United States Senate Computers for Learning program and were shipped out of Washington  DC late last week            The floods earlier this year took a terrible toll on everyone in Canajoharie  impacting many students and their families  I am very pleased that we could secure these computers to help the students and their teachers who have faced so much in the past few months  Now more than ever it is essential that we give students the right tools to learn and achieve in a global market  and I hope these computers will help the students compete in this technology rich age     Senator Clinton said            The Canajoharie Central School District works hard to prepare our students for the global marketplace  We believe that this donation makes Senator Clinton a partner in that task  She  more than most people  understands that the competition our students will face in the future will demand that they display competitive strength in understanding and utilizing technology  Computers are important tools for our teachers and students to use to gain that technological advantage     said Dr  Donald L  Bowden  Principal of Canajoharie High School           The United States Senate  s Computers for Learning  CFL  Program helps public schools take advantage of the information technology age by channeling surplus computers into classrooms across the country   Computers for Learning places computers in our classrooms and prepares our children to contribute and compete in the 21st century  CFL connects the registered needs of schools and educational nonprofit organizations with computer equipment made available free of charge from government agencies and the private sector         Click here for more information on the program
