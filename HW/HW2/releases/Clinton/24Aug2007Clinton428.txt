August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce over  114 000 for Oneonta Municipal Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Federal Money Will Be Used to Remove Dangerous Obstructions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  114 950 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Oneonta Municipal Airport   Federal funds will be used to remove dangerous obstructions    This is great news for Ostego County  said Schumer  The Oneonta Municipal Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region     This is welcome news for Oneonta Municipal Airport  said Senator Clinton  The funding will be used to provide necessary improvements to remove obstruction so that the airport can operate safely and efficiently   I am pleased that we can continue to support our local airports and our local economy in this way    Federal funds will be used to remove dangerous obstructions  This project will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely
