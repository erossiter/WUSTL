August 1  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  237 000 for Orange County Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Federal Money will be used for Improvements and Rehabilitation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the US Department of Transportation has awarded  237 500 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the Orange County Airport  The money will be used for realignment and relocation of a runway         This is great news     said Schumer   The Orange County airport is the second busiest general aviation airport in New York State and it s at capacity  In order to grow responsibly  it needs to move its runway farther from the Wallkill River and this money will help do that           This funding is a very welcome investment in the Orange County Airport  which is already at capacity     Senator Clinton said    As an important regional hub  I am pleased that we can support the airport with this funding as we continue to invest in its future along with the future of the region         This grant will provide federal funding to relocate and realign the Runway 3 21 at Orange County Airport  As of now  the Runway 3 21 has a non standard Runway Safety Area  The funding will allow the airport to restructure the runway and meet both federal regulations and the airport  s existing and future operational needs
