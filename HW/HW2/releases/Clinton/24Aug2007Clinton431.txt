August 24  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  570 000 for Western New York Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Federal Money Will be Used to Construct New Taxiway and Remove Obstruction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Washington  DC   US Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded two grants amounting to  570 000 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to airports in Western New York  The Town of Perry will use a grant totaling  475 000 to construct a new taxiway at Perry Warsaw Airport  The Town of North Dansville will use a  95 000 grant to remove obstructions at the Dansville Municipal Airport    This is great news for Perry and North Dansville  said Schumer  The Perry Warsaw Airport and the Dansville Municipal Airport are important general aviation facilities in this region    Not only do they provide local aviators  and tourists with a base of operation  but they also allow businesspeople to access Western New York easily     I will continue to fight for federal funds to help these airports remain a key component of economic growth in the region    This is great news for Wyoming and Livingston Counties  said Senator Clinton  The Perry Warsaw Airport is located only three miles northwest of the central business district and this grant will allow for much needed improvements so that the airport can continue bringing travelers and commerce to the region  The Dansville Municipal Airport continues to bring travelers and tourists alike to the Finger Lakes Region and I will do all that I can to ensure it remains safe and secure    Wyoming County will use a grant totaling  475 000 to construct a new taxiway at Perry Warsaw Airport  Livingston County will use  95 000 in federal funding to remove obstruction and perform an obstruction survey
