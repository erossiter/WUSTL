January 28  2005 Statement of Senator Hillary Rodham  Clinton on the Iraqi Elections                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               No matter what you think about the Iraq war  there is one thing we can all agree on for the next days   we have to salute the courage and bravery of those who are risking their lives to vote and those brave Iraqi and American soldiers fighting to protect their right to vote  They are facing terrorists who have declared war on democracy itself and made voting a life and death process  We hope this vote succeeds and pray for a safe election day
