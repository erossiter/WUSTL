May 22  2007 Schumer  Clinton Announce  52 000 for the Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Federal Money will be Used to Improve the Runway at the Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Washington  DC   U S  Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded  52 323 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to the City of Dunkirk for the Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport   Federal funds will be used to perform a runway length analysis           This is more great news for Chautauqua   said Schumer   The Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport is an important general aviation facility in this region    It is critical that we continue to provide the funds necessary to keep the airport safe and efficient   With so many local aviators  tourists and businesspeople using the airport as a base of operation  it remains a key component of economic growth in the region             This is more great news for Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport   said Senator Clinton   This funding will allow for continued improvements and rehabilitation measures that are critical to its continued maintenance and operation   It is also an important investment in the airport which promises to be positive for the local economy           The City of Dunkirk will use federal funds to perform a runway length analysis for Runway 6 24 at the Chautauqua County Dunkirk Airport   This analysis will better position the airport to serve the surrounding business community more effectively  efficiently and safely   Earlier this month the airport received two grants totaling  274 750 to rehabilitate this runway and do a study of existing obstructions for pilots using the runway
