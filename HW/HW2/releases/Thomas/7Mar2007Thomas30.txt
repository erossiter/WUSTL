SEN. THOMAS ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF BOB PECK
  Sen. Craig Thomas, R-WY, made the following statement today following the passing of Bob Peck: 
   "My thoughts and prayers, and Susan's, are with Bob's family and friends, as well as the people of Riverton today. Bob was a leader in the newspaper industry and a valiant member of the State Senate. Wyoming has lost a true patriot and a close friend. He will be missed."
