SEN. VITTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON INTERNATIONAL PAPERS'S DECISION TO REMAIN IN PINEVILLE
  Sen. David Vitter today issued the following statement on International Papers' decision to keep its Pineville containerboard mill operational:
   "Great congratulations go to the employees of Central Louisiana's IP mill. Their ability to pull together and work in a more efficient manner produced a positive return and saved more than 200 jobs in Pineville," said Vitter.
