SEN. VITTER HONORS VETERANS ON ANNIVERSARY OF PEARL HARBOR
  U.S. Sen. David Vitter today released the following statement regarding the 66th anniversary of the attack on Pearl Harbor.
   "Today marks the 66th anniversary of the attack on the U.S. fleet at Pearl Harbor. This is a defining event in American history and was the catalyst that brought America into World War II. Many Americans - including Louisianans - will mark today's anniversary with reverence and respect. I join them today in honoring the American men and women who fought and died in defense of our nation on December 7, 1941."
