SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES GRANT FUNDING FOR PORT VINCENT POLICE DEPARTMENT
  Sen. David Vitter announced on Tuesday, March 13, that Port Vincent will receive a federal grant of $90,000 for its police force.
   "This grant will enable Port Vincent law enforcement officials to better patrol their community, promote safer streets and create a more secure environment," said Vitter.
   The funding will help Port Vincent's police force purchase three new patrol cars, six ballistic vests and other equipment that is essential to protecting and serving the community. The grant is provided under the Community Facilities Grant Program at the U.S. Department of Agriculture Rural Development.
