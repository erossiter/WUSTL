SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES SATELLITE OFFICE HOURS FOR EVANGELINE PARISH RESIDENTS
  Sen. David Vitter today announced his schedule for satellite office hours for Evangeline Parish residents.  
 Vitter will use satellite offices around the state to make it easier for his constituents to find help in dealing with the federal government. Satellite hours for the Evangeline Parish will be held on the first Wednesday of each month, beginning May 4 at Ville Platte City Hall from 9:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. 
   Residents are invited to attend these office hours and meet with a member of Vitter's staff to discuss any problems they may have with a government agency. Constituents unable to attend these office hours can always call the Acadiana office at (337) 262-6898 to speak to a member of Vitter's staff. 
   The 2005 schedule for satellite office hours in Evangeline Parish follow: 
   Where: City Hall, Conference Room 
  126 E. Main St. 
  Ville Platte, La. 
   When: First Wednesday of each month 
  9:30 a.m. - 11:30 a.m.
