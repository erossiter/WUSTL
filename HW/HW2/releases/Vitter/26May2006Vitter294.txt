SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES GRANT FOR INDEPENDENCE
  Sen. David Vitter today announced that the U.S. Department of Agriculture Rural Development will award a $49,500 grant to the town of Independence to purchase four new patrol cars.
   "I'm pleased that USDA Rural Development made this grant available to the town of Independence to help patrol the community," said Vitter. "This federal funding will help ensure safe streets and a secure environment for community residents."
   The grant, which is provided under the Community Facility Grant program, will help the town's police force purchase essential equipment to protect and serve the community.
