SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES GRANT FUNDING FOR BOGALUSA
  Sen. David Vitter announced today that the city of Bogalusa will receive a federal grant of $150,000 for enhancements to their town square and development of walking trails.
   "These are great additions and enhancements for Bogalusa, and they will give residents of the community more options for spending time outdoors," said Vitter.
   The grant will be used to develop walking trails, an amphitheater and support facilities at a 1.3 acre park. The grant is provided through the National Park Service to the Office of State Parks at the Louisiana Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism.
