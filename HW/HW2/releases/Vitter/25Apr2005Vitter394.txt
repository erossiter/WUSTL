SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES SATELLITE OFFICE HOURS FOR ALLEN PARISH RESIDENTS
  Sen. David Vitter today announced his schedule for satellite office hours for Allen Parish residents.  
 Vitter will use satellite offices around the state to make it easier for his constituents to find help in dealing with the federal government. Satellite hours for the Allen Parish will be held on the second Tuesday of each month, beginning May 10 at Oakdale City Hall from 9:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.
   Residents are invited to attend these office hours and meet with a member of Vitter's staff to discuss any problems they may have with a government agency. Constituents unable to attend these office hours can always call the Southwest Louisiana office at (337) 436-0453 to speak to a member of Vitter's staff.
   The 2005 schedule for satellite office hours in Allen Parish follow:
   Where: City Hall, Conference Room
  333 E. 6th St.
  Oakdale, La.
   When: Second Tuesday of each month
  9:30 a.m. - 11:30 a.m.
