SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES GRANT FOR ST. HELENA PARISH
  Sen. David Vitter today announced that the U.S. Department of Agriculture Rural Development will award a $13,734 grant to the South Second Ward Fire Protection District Number 1 of St. Helena Parish. 
   "I'm pleased that USDA Rural Development made this grant available to upgrade this St. Helena Parish fire department," said Vitter. "This federal funding will ensure that residents of Greensburg will have improved access to first responder services."
   The grant, which is provided under the Community Facility Grant program, will be used to purchase essential fire fighting equipment.
