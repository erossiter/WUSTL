SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES SATELLITE OFFICE HOURS FOR JEFFERSON DAVIS PARISH RESIDENTS
  Sen. David Vitter today announced his schedule for satellite office hours for Jefferson Davis Parish residents.  
 Vitter will use satellite offices around the state to make it easier for his constituents to find help in dealing with the federal government. Satellite hours for the Jefferson Davis Parish will be held on the third Tuesday of each month, beginning May 17 at Jennings City Hall from 9:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. 
   Residents are invited to attend these office hours and meet with a member of Vitter's staff to discuss any problems they may have with a government agency. Constituents unable to attend these office hours can always call the Southwest Louisiana office at (337) 436-0453 to speak to a member of Vitter's staff. 
   The 2005 schedule for satellite office hours in Jefferson Davis Parish follow: 
   Where: City Hall, Room 7 
  324 N. Broadway 
  Jennings, La. 
   When: Third Tuesday of each month 
  9:30 a.m. - 11:30 a.m.
