HURRICANE KATRINA RELIEF FUND ESTABLISHED
  Sen. David Vitter has received many calls from people all across the country asking how they can help the victims of Hurricane Katrina. Gov. Kathleen Blanco has established the Louisiana Disaster Recovery Foundation, a 501(c)(3) fund to accept hurricane relief donations.
   Checks payable to "Louisiana Disaster Recovery Foundation" will be accepted at P.O. Box 94095, Baton Rouge, LA 70804-9095.
