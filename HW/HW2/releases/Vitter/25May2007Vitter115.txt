SEN. VITTER SENDS LETTER TO SECRETARY CHERTOFF EXPRESSING HURRICANE READINESS CONCERNS
  Please find the following attached letter from Sen. David Vitter sent to DHS Sec. Michael Chertoff dated May 24, 2007. Vitter expressed concerns about the Department of Homeland Security's hurricane readiness for the 2007 hurricane season.
