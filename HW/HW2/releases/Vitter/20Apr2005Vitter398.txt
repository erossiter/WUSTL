SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES SATELLITE OFFICE HOURS FOR WINNFIELD OFFICE
  Sen. David Vitter today announced his schedule for satellite office hours for Winn Parish residents.  
 Vitter will use satellite offices around the state to make it easier for his constituents to find help in dealing with the federal government. Satellite hours for the Winn Parish will be held on the second Tuesday of each month, beginning May 10 at Winn Parish Courthouse from 10:00 a.m. to noon. 
   Residents are invited to attend these office hours and meet with a member of Vitter's staff to discuss any problems they may have with a government agency. Constituents unable to attend these office hours can always call the Central Louisiana office at (318) 448-0169 to speak to a member of Vitter's staff. 
   The 2005 schedule for satellite office hours in Winn Parish follow:
   Where: Winn Parish Courthouse, Room 112
  119 West Main St.
  Winnfield, La.
   When: Second Tuesday of each month
  10:00 a.m. - noon
