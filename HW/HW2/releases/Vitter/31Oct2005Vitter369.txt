SEN. VITTER ISSUES STATEMENT ON U.S. SUPREME COURT NOMINEE SAMUEL ALITO
  Sen. David Vitter made the following statement following President Bush's nomination of Judge Samuel A. Alito, Jr. to the U.S. Supreme Court:
   "I'm very pleased with Judge Alito's nomination so far. He has impeccable legal credentials and a well grounded conservative judicial philosophy-just what so many, including me, consider most important. I look forward to studying his full record."
