SEN. VITTER ANNOUNCES GRANT FUNDING FOR PLAQUEMINES PARISH POLICE DEPARTMENT
  Sen. David Vitter announced on Tuesday, March 20, that Plaquemines Parish will receive a federal grant of $230,000 for its police force. 
   "As Plaquemines Parish continues to rebuild and recover, this grant will provide their law enforcement with the resources they need to continue keeping the streets and community safe," said Vitter. The funding will help the Plaquemines Parish Law Enforcement District purchase 10 new patrol cars equipped with modern radios and other communication equipment that is essential to protecting and serving the community. The grant is provided under the Community Facilities Grant Program at the U.S. Department of Agriculture Rural Development.
