SEN. JEFFORDS ANNOUNCES $26,606 GRANT FOR IRASBURG FIRE DEPARTMENT
  Sen. Jim Jeffords, I-Vt., announced today that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded the Irasburg Fire Department a grant of $26,606 to support the department's role as first responders in the community.  
   Jeffords said, "I am pleased to announce that the Irasburg Fire Department has been awarded this grant. Supporting Vermont's fire departments is essential to keeping our local communities safe. " Jeffords served as a volunteer firefighter with the Shrewsbury Volunteer Fire Department. 
   These funds are used by the nation's firefighters to increase the effectiveness of firefighting operations; bolster firefighter health and safety programs; improve wellness and fitness; defend against weapons of mass destruction; and provide new fire apparatus, emergency medical service programs, and fire prevention and safety programs.
   The Assistance for Firefighters Grant Program (AFGP) assists rural, urban and suburban fire departments throughout the United States by awarding one-year grants. Vermont fire departments have received 57 grants for a total of over $4.7 million since June 1, 2004. For more information, please visit www.firegrantsupport.com.
