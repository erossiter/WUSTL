SEN. JEFFORDS ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF FORMER PRESIDENT REAGAN
  Senator Jeffords' Statement on the Death of Former President Ronald Reagan
   "Ronald Reagan served our nation well at a time of great uncertainty in the world. I will never forget his quick wit and ability to communicate with the American public like few other politicians in history. While I did not agree with many of his policies, his devotion and love for our nation was undeniable. In addition to his legacy as President, Ronald Reagan will be remembered for his courage and dignity as he battled with his illness. Liz and I send Nancy and the entire Reagan family our deepest sympathies."
