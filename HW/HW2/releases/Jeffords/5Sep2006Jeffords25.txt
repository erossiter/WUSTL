SEN. JEFFORDS' ISSUES STATEMENT ON PETERS NOMINATION
  Statement of Sen. Jim Jeffords, I-Vt., Ranking Member, Senate Environment and Public Works Committee on nomination of Mary Peters as US Secretary of Transportation:
   "Mary Peters is a good choice to succeed my longtime friend, Norm Mineta, as Secretary of Transportation. I look forward to her confirmation hearing, and I plan to support her through the process. The job will require many tough decisions on complex transportation issues in the years ahead, and I wish her all the best."
