SEN. JEFFORDS ISSUES STATEMENT ON ATTACKS IN LONDON
  Statement of Sen. Jim Jeffords on London attacks 
   "I know I join all Vermonters in sending our deepest sympathies to the citizens of Britain during this sad and difficult time. As we learned in aftermath of September 11th, these attacks will only bring the citizens of the United Kingdom closer and strengthen the resolve of all nations in the fight against terrorism. Today we are all citizens of London, and share in their sorrow."
