                                                                             Corker Votes for Defense Authorization Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  October 01  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today voted for the Defense Authorization Bill  praising its 3 5 percent across the board pay increase for military personnel   4 billion for Mine Resistant Ambush Protected Vehicles  MRAP  that protect our soldiers against improvised explosive devices  IEDs   and wounded warrior provisions to ensure that soldiers are receiving quality medical care  I believe General Petraeus had it exactly right when he called our military men and women in uniform Americas new Greatest Generation  said Corker  It is our responsibility to provide our soldiers with the best possible resources  equipment  and care  Im particularly pleased that this bill includes a 3 5 percent across the board pay raise for military personnel to help provide better financial support to the military families across our country who sacrifice so much to serve  This bill also authorizes  4 billion to be spent on vehicles that better protect our troops on the ground against IEDs  and it establishes better assessment and oversight of the medical care our troops are receiving to ensure that we are getting our soldiers who are injured in the line of duty the quality of care they deserve  The Defense Authorization Bill passed by a vote of 92 to 3
