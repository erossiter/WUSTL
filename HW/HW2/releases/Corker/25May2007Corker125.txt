                                                                             Corker Statement In Honor of Memorial Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  May 25  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R Tenn   made the following statement in honor of Memorial Day         Memorial Day brings a yearly opportunity to honor generations of America s fallen heroes   From the American Revolution to the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan  our nation s military men and women have answered the call of duty every time  placing the interests of their country above their own   And it is their sacrifice for the sake of liberty we remember on Monday         A few months ago  I met with Tennessee soldiers and military commanders in Iraq   What struck me most about them was their incredible sense of honor  courage  and hope for completing their mission  they truly exemplify the Volunteer spirit   These brief words cannot adequately express my gratitude for their service         To the Tennesseans mourning the loss of loved ones  I hope the ceremonies and national outpouring of support will bring some comfort   American freedom has come at a tremendous cost and it is a gift we can never repay or forget
