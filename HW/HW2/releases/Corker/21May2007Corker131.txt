                                                                             Corker Votes Against Motion to Proceed to Immigration Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Urges Colleagues to Allow Time to Digest and Debate Issue of this Magnitude                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  May 21  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today voted against proceeding on the immigration bill that has been introduced in the U S  Senate and urged his colleagues to allow time to digest a bill on an issue of this magnitude        After an initial review  I have serious concerns  and I believe we need real debate on this very important issue   said Corker   We received this bill at 1 58 a m  on Saturday morning  This legislation will have a tremendous affect on citizens and institutions across the country  and we need time to digest it         I fear that tonight s vote is the first step toward trying to rush through one of the most significant pieces of legislation Congress will consider this year
