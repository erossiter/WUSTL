                                                                             Corker Statement on Passing of Ernest C  Withers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  October 16  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today made the following statement regarding the death of Memphis photographer Ernest C  Withers  With the passing of Ernest Withers  Tennessee and America have lost a master photographer  historian and humanitarian  said Corker  For more than six decades  Ernest Withers chronicled on film some of the most important moments in the contemporary history of Memphis including the rise of Elvis and the assassination of Dr  Martin Luther King  He was a pioneer in photojournalism and his legacy will continue in museums and history books for generations to come
