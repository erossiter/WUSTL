                                                                             Corker Votes Against  4 Billion Increase in Federal Spending for CJS Projects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  October 16  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after voting against the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriations bill  which includes a  4 billion  8 percent  federal spending increase over the fiscal year 2007 bill  We must bring spending under control in Washington  and while this bill includes many worthy projects I could not vote for such a dramatic increase over last years funding levels  said Corker  I expect the President to veto this legislation  and when it comes back to Congress I hope we will be able to agree on a bill that funds important projects while maintaining fiscal restraint
