                                                                             Corker Votes Against Cloture on Immigration Reform Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  June 07  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement late Thursday after voting against cloture on the immigration reform bill     I voted against ending debate on the immigration bill in its current form because I dont believe weve had sufficient opportunity to debate and improve the legislation  said Corker     I am disappointed that we were not able to reach an agreement because our illegal immigration situation is intolerable and requires immediate attention  however I dont believe we should pass this or any bill until we can ensure that we are creating an immigration policy that will make our country secure  stronger over the long haul  and will inspire confidence in future generations of Americans
