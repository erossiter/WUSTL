                                                                             Corker Statement Recognizing September 11th                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  September 11  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today recognizing September 11th  The terrorists destroyed many innocent lives on September 11  2001  but they could not destroy the American spirit  Along with the tremendous tragedy our country experienced that day  we also saw courage  compassion and the strength of a nation united  said Corker  As we reflect upon the events that occurred six years ago  we honor the memories of those weve lost and give thanks for the firefighters  policemen  and emergency personnel who respond daily to human crises and for the members of our military defending our country throughout the world
