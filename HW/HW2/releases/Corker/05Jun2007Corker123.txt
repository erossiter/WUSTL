                                                                             Corker Comments on the Passing of Wyoming Senator Craig Thomas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  June 05  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today regarding the death of U S  Sen  Craig Thomas  R WY   I am very saddened by the death of Wyoming Senator Craig Thomas  I enjoyed getting to know Craig over the past few months  Elizabeth and I send our prayers to his family  friends  and staff who are dealing with the tremendous loss of this devoted public servant
