                                                                             Corker Statement on Trent Lott s Decision to Resign from the Senate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  November 26  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement regarding Senate Republican Whip Trent Lotts  R MS  decision to resign from the Senate at the end of the year     As a new Senator  I ve benefited greatly from Trent s knowledge and experience about how to effectively make a difference in the United States Senate  said Corker  For more than three decades  Senator Lott has been a great public servant to the people of Mississippi in Congress and his strong leadership will be greatly missed  I extend my best wishes to Trent  and his wife Patricia  as they begin the next phase of their lives together
