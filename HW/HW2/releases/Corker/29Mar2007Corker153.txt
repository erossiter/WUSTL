                                                                             Corker Votes Against Arbitrary Date for Withdrawal in Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  March 29  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today voted against H R  1591  the emergency supplemental bill  because it includes language setting an arbitrary date for withdrawal in Iraq           I voted against this bill because it includes language that sets an arbitrary date for withdrawal in Iraq   said Corker   I believe we must give General Petraeus an opportunity to turn what has been a downward spiral in Iraq into an upward spiral so that Iraq has the ability to take over its own destiny and maintain its own security  I don t want to hinder General Petraeus  ability to succeed  and I believe that setting an arbitrary withdrawal date   before we have even had the proper opportunity to assess our progress   would do just that         General Petraeus has stated on numerous occasions that we will know by mid summer whether progress is being made  Instead of offering thoughts about what might happen in the future  I believe we should give General Petraeus and our troops on the ground in Iraq the time  resources and support they need and deserve
