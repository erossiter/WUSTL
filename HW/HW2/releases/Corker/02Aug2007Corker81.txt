                                                                             Corker Votes for Ethics Reform Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  August 02  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following comments today after voting for ethics reform legislation     I hope passage of this bill will be an important step toward restoring the American peoples faith and confidence in the conduct of Congress  said Corker     Abuses of the earmarking system have certainly contributed to an erosion of public trust  This bill gives the public greater access to earmark requests  details on who is asking for earmarks and what they are asking for  It is important that there is more transparency in the earmarking process and that members of Congress are more accountable for the way we spend our constituents tax dollars  As we move along  I hope we are able to strengthen this portion of the bill even further     This was one of the issues we focused on in our campaign  and Im pleased we were able to pass this legislation before I go home to Tennessee for the August work period
