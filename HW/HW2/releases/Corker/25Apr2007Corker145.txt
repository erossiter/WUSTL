                                                                             Corker Praises Senate Passage of Legislation to Keep TN  U S  Competitive in the 21st Century                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  April 25  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   cosponsor of the America COMPETES  Creating Opportunities to Meaningfully Promote Excellence in Technology  Education  and Science  Act  S  761  today praised the bill s overwhelming Senate passage and Sen  Lamar Alexander s leadership on the issue         One of the reasons I ran for the U S  Senate was to work on economic development issues on a national and global scale  and to make sure our American workforce is prepared for an increasingly competitive world marketplace   said Corker  cosponsor of the America COMPETES Act        Early in my campaign for Senate  I visited China to understand the challenges our country faces  I saw firsthand the economic growth  production and innovation taking place there  and became even more convinced that maintaining America s competitive edge in the world marketplace is vital to our country s economic and national security        I believe this legislation will strengthen our workforce and make our country stronger  By making greater investments in research and innovation and strengthening math and science education  we can ensure that our young people have the skills they need for the 21st century and that we are able to keep good jobs here at home         Tennesseans should be very proud of the tireless leadership Senator Alexander has shown on this issue  He has worked for several years to make this legislation a priority and to bring both sides of the aisle together in support of this effort to strengthen America s security and prosperity        The America COMPETES Act passed by a vote of 88 to 8
