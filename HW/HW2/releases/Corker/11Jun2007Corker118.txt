                                                                             Corker Comments on Gonzales Resolution                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  June 11  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after voting against a motion to invoke cloture on the motion to proceed to S J  Res  14  a joint resolution on Attorney General Alberto Gonzales     I certainly have questions regarding some aspects of the Attorney Generals service  but the exercise we participated in this evening is pointless and only heightens partisanship within the Senate  In fact  the only real consequence of this vote is that it delayed us from addressing critical issues facing our country  like proceeding to the important  bipartisan energy security bill we are supposed to be considering this week
