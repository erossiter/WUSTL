                                                                             Corker Supports Bill to Modernize the Federal Housing Administration                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  December 14  2007   WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today praised the Senates overwhelming 93 1 approval of S  2338  the FHA  Federal Housing Administration  Modernization Act of 2007    Passage of this bill today is a prudent government reaction to the challenges we see in the housing market  said Corker  By increasing access to FHA backed loans  we give many first time borrowers the opportunity to get into affordable  stable home loans    This bill now goes to a conference committee to resolve differences between House and Senate versions of the bill
