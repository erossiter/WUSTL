                                                                             Corker Votes for Confirmation of Judge Southwick                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  October 24  2007  Washington  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after voting for Judge Leslie Southwick of Mississippi to serve as U S  Circuit Court Judge for the Fifth Circuit     I had the opportunity to meet with Judge Southwick and believe he is imminently qualified to serve in this position  said Corker  He is a thoughtful  well reasoned judge who placed his career on hold to serve our country in Iraq  I was proud to support his confirmation
