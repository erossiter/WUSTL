                                                                             Corker Comments On Senate Passage of Energy Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Legislation is  good for our country and good for Tennessee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  June 22  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   a member of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee  today made the following statement regarding Senate passage of H R 6  the CLEAN Energy Act of 2007  The bill promotes swift and continued development of biofuel alternatives to petroleum and is the first major piece of national energy legislation since the Energy Policy Act of 2005  Corker voted in favor of the legislation that passed the Senate late last night     After lengthy debate  the Senate passed an energy package that will increase our countrys energy security by helping us reduce the growth in consumption of gasoline and reduce our dependence on foreign oil  said Corker  I think we did the right thing as it relates to focusing on expanded use of biofuels  We did the right thing in setting new fuel efficiency standards  CAFE   And I think we did the right thing to promote energy conservation     Im proud of the role Tennessee is playing in producing biofuel alternatives to petroleum  Hopefully in the near future we will additionally address the need to increase domestic production of energy  The legislation we passed last night in the Senate is good for our country and good for Tennessee
