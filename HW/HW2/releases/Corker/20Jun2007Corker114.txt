                                                                             Corker Praises President s Nomination of Tate to Serve Five More Years at FCC                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  June 20  2007  Washington  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today praised President Bushs nomination of Deborah Taylor Tate of Tennessee to be a Commissioner of the Federal Communications Commission  FCC  for a five year term expiring June 30  2012  Tate currently serves as an FCC Commissioner and previously served as Director and Chairman of the Tennessee Regulatory Authority     Im proud to have a Tennessean of Debis background and experience as an FCC Commissioner  and I applaud the Presidents decision to nominate her for five more years of service  said Corker
