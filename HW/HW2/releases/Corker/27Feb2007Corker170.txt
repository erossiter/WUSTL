                                                                             Corker Comments on Toyota s Decision to Build a Plant in Mississippi                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  February 27  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today made the following comments on Toyota s decision to build an assembly plant in northeast Mississippi         An unprecedented and aggressive effort was put forth by Chattanooga  the state of Tennessee and our adjoining neighbors to attract Toyota  I believe it is only a matter of time before Enterprise South will become the location of an entity that will create tremendous job growth for the Chattanooga area  and I will continue working with all involved until it happens
