                                                                             Corker Votes for Cochran Amendment to the Iraq Supplemental                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Says  We Need to Give Petraeus Time  Resources and Support                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  March 27  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today voted for Sen  Thad Cochran s  R MS  amendment to strip an Iraq withdrawal deadline from the emergency war supplemental bill  saying           I believe we must give General Petraeus an opportunity to turn what has been a downward spiral in Iraq into an upward spiral so that Iraq has the ability to take over its own destiny and maintain its own security  I don t want to do anything that may hinder his ability to succeed with this plan  and I believe that setting an arbitrary date for withdrawal   before we ve even had a chance to see if the new strategy is working   would do just that         General Petraeus has said that we will know by mid summer whether progress is being made  Instead of offering thoughts about what might happen in the future  I believe we should give General Petraeus and our troops on the ground in Iraq the time  resources and support they need and deserve
