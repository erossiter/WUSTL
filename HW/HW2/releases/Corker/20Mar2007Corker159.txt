                                                                             Statement of U S  Senator Bob Corker on the Death of Bishop G E  Patterson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  March 20  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement this evening after learning that Church of God in Christ leader Bishop G E  Patterson died today after a long illness         The Memphis community has lost one of its most respected and beloved religious leaders  My prayers go out to Bishop Patterson s family and to all those who have been touched by his many years of ministry
