                                                                             Corker Votes Against Ending Debate on the Farm Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  November 16  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after voting against ending debate on the Farm Bill     The Farm Bill is tremendously important to our state  so I hope we can pass a responsible piece of legislation this year  said Corker  However  until agreement is reached to allow amendments to be voted on to improve the bill  I will vote against ending debate on this important legislation     In a 55 42 vote  cloture was not invoked on the Farm Bill
