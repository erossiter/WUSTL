                                                                             Corker Praises Overwhelming Senate Approval of General Petraeus to Command Forces in Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  January 26  2007  WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today praised overwhelming U S  Senate approval of the nomination of Lieutenant General David Petraeus to command Multi National Forces in Iraq         I was very impressed with General Petraeus  success in commanding our 101st Airborne  I ve listened to his testimony before the Armed Services Committee and met with him privately  and I m convinced he is the right man to lead our forces in Iraq  I was proud to vote in support of his confirmation         All of us want to see a stable Iraq  All of us want to see our men and woman in uniform come home as soon as possible  I believe General Petraeus  confirmation today is a positive step in that direction         Petraeus was confirmed by the U S  Senate in a vote of 81 0
