                                                                             Sen  Corker Applauds Supreme Court Decision Upholding Federal Ban on Partial Birth Abortion                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  April 18  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today praised the decision by the U S  Supreme Court to uphold the federal ban on the procedure of partial birth abortion          I m very pleased the U S  Supreme Court today agreed that partial birth abortion should be outlawed   said Corker   This procedure is one that most Tennesseans agree is cruel and inhumane          The Court s 5 4 decision ruled that the Partial Birth Abortion Act  which was passed by Congress and signed into law by President Bush in 2003  is constitutional
