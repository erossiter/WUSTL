                                                                             Corker Visits All 95 Tennessee Counties                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Visit to Monroe County Marks End of 95 County Tour                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  October 11  2007  MADISONVILLE  TN  U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  will hold a town hall meeting in Monroe County this afternoon  Senator Corker is visiting all 95 Tennessee counties during his first year in office to listen to constituents concerns  provide an update on his work in Tennessee and Washington on their behalf  and to introduce his key staff members in each region of the state  Monroe will be the 95th county  Im so pleased that weve been able to visit every one of our states 95 counties this year  said Corker  I appreciate the thousands of Tennesseans who attended our town hall meetings and events to share their concerns  With their feedback  I will continue working on the issues important to Tennessee and America  Its a tremendous honor and privilege to serve in the U S  Senate and my experiences visiting with Tennesseans in every county this year have only reinforced my optimism about the future of our state and country
