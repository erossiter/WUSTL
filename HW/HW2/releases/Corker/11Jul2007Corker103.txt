                                                                             Corker Applauds Re nomination of Tennesseans to TVA Board                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  July 11  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today applauded the Presidents re nomination of Bishop William Graves  of Memphis  and Susan Richardson Williams  of Knoxville  to the Tennessee Valley Authority  TVA  Board of Director for terms expiring May 18  2012     Tennesseans should be proud of the service Bishop William Graves and Susan Richardson Williams have provided our region as members of the TVA Board  I appreciate their willingness to serve in this important way as they provide governance to TVA and help ensure that our citizens continue to have access to reliable and low cost electricity  I am hopeful that their appointments are quickly confirmed by the Senate
