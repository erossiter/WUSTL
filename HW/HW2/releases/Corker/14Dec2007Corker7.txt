                                                                             Corker Votes for Defense Authorization Conference Report                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  December 14  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today voted for the conference report to accompany H R  1585  the Department of Defense Authorization Bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 90 3  The bill authorizes  506 9 billion for the Department of Defense and the Department of Energy national security program and  189 4 billion for the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan     I believe General Petraeus had it exactly right when he called our military men and women Americas new Greatest Generation  and it is our responsibility to provide them with the best possible resources  equipment  and care both in the field and at home  said Corker  This bill includes  17 billion for MRAP vehicles to better protect our troops on the ground  a 3 5 percent pay raise to increase financial support for the military families across our country who sacrifice so much to serve  and an improved treatment system for our wounded services members to ensure they get the quality of care they deserve  Im also pleased this bill includes new oversight mechanisms for contracting in Iraq and Afghanistan to give Americans greater confidence in the process   We have the best led  best trained  and best equipped military in the world  and this bill helps ensure we stay that way
