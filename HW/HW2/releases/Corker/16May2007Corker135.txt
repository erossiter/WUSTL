                                                                             Corker Comments on Iraq Amendment Votes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  May 16  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after his votes on three Iraq amendments to H R  1495  the Water Resources Development Act  WRDA            I voted against an amendment by Senator Feingold because   as I ve said on numerous occasions   I believe we need to give General Petraeus an opportunity through this summer to turn what has been a downward spiral in Iraq into an upward spiral         I voted for Senator Cochran s amendment because we need to give General Petraeus and our troops on the ground in Iraq the time  resources and support they need to continue implementing this plan  and we need to do so without further delay         I voted for Senator Warner s amendment because I believe the Iraqi government should know we are serious about our expectation that they do those things necessary to take over their own destiny and maintain their own security
