                                                                             Corker Praises Nomination of Gus Puryear to be U S  District Judge for Middle District of Tennessee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  June 13  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today praised the Presidents nomination of Gus Puryear to be United States District Judge for the Middle District of Tennessee     I have known Gus Puryear for a number of years and believe he has great integrity and am very pleased that he has been nominated for this important role  Gus is an accomplished lawyer who would serve the citizens of our state very well as U S  district judge  and I hope my Senate colleagues will join me in giving him a swift confirmation
