                                                                             Corker Voices Strong Opposition to Card Check Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Says Taking Away Secret Ballot Will Increase Worker Intimidation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  June 26  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today argued against the so called Employee Free Choice Act  H R  800 and S  1041   which would eliminate a workers right to a secret ballot when deciding whether or not to unionize  Corker will vote against cloture on the bill  saying the legislation opens the door for increased intimidation and coercion  Speaking on the Senate floor today shortly before a cloture vote on the legislation  Corker referenced his background on the issue  As a construction worker  Corker was a card carrying union laborer  Later as a business owner  he hired both union and non union laborers and served on the pension fund board of a carpenters union  This bill would take labor laws back a hundred years  said Corker  It would allow union leaders to strong arm and pick off workers one by one  intimidating them into signing cards to organize a union  I dont believe that most Americans or most union workers who truly understand what this bill means would support it  A worker should have the right to decide in private  by secret ballot whether or not they want to join a union  and this bill takes away that basic right  I am strongly opposed to this legislation  and I will work to ensure it is defeated  The bill  sponsored by Senator Ted Kennedy  D MA  and endorsed by the AFL CIO  would overhaul labor laws  ending the process that has served both employee and employer for decades  The legislation would force the National Labor Relations Board to recognize a union based on signed authorization cards obtained by union organizers without requiring a secret ballot election of all employees  Significantly  the bill would not change the method for which employees can choose to disband a union  still requiring a secret ballot election in those events
