                                                                             Corker Applauds Alexander on Republican Conference Chairmanship                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Kyl elected whip  Alexander rises to number three in the Republican leadership from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  December 06  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R Tenn   today applauded Senator Lamar Alexander  R Tenn   for being elected chairman of the Senate Republican Conference  Since Republican whip Trent Lott  R Miss   announced his retirement at the end of the year  Senate Republicans have been vying to fill the number two vacancy on their leadership team  Members gathered today for the elections  Jon Kyl  R Ariz   emerged as Lott s replacement to be Republican whip and Alexander was elected to head the Republican conference  filling the vacancy left by Kyl  As Conference chairman  Alexander will be responsible for coordinating and communicating the Republican message in the Senate  Alexander becomes the number three member of the Republican leadership behind Kyl and Republican Leader Mitch McConnell  R Ky     Lamar has a unique skill set that will make him an outstanding Conference chairman  said Corker  He can take a complex message and distill it into terms that are easy for the American people to understand  I believe that his leadership will be good for the Senate and for our country     I also congratulate Jon Kyl whose unanimous vote today indicates the high level of respect he holds within our caucus  Jon will be a strong and effective whip
