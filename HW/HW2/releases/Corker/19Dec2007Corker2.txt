                                                                             Corker Praises Landmark Energy Bill Becoming Law                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  December 19  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   a member of the Senate Energy Committee  made the following statement today after President Bush signed into law H R  6  the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007     Today the president signed into law landmark energy legislation that strengthens and reforms fuel economy standards for the first time in more than 30 years  one of the most significant ways America can reduce its oil consumption  said Corker  The bill promotes the swift and continued development of biofuel alternatives to foreign oil  like cellulosic ethanol which has so much potential in Tennessee  and it improves energy efficiency standards     Members on both sides of the aisle had to make tough decisions in order for us to pass an energy bill this year  and I appreciate all of their efforts  This legislation is good for our country and good for Tennessee  and as member of the Energy Committee  I look forward to making additional progress next year on making our country more energy secure
