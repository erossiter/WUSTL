                                                                             Corker Rides in Prototype of the Fully Electric Tesla Roadster                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Friday  April 27  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN   member of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee  rode in a prototype of the fully electric Tesla Roadster on Capitol Hill Thursday  Developed by Tesla Motors  the vehicle is a 100 percent electric sports car with an energy efficiency equivalent of 135 miles per gallon and can travel from 0   60 mph time in about four seconds          My ride in the Tesla Roadster further convinced me that it is possible to produce a reliable  energy efficient electric car without losing the performance or style Americans value in an automobile   Corker said    While fully electric vehicles remain too costly for many Americans  I m convinced with time this technology can become more affordable         America s long term economic and national security depends upon our ability to pursue alternative sources of energy   I commend Tesla Motors and others who are leading innovation in the automotive industry that will help reduce our country s dependency on foreign sources of oil         Listed below are some of the Tesla Roadster s specifications       0 to 60 mph in about 4 seconds   200 mile EPA highway range   135 mpg equivalent  per the conversion rate used by the EPA    3 for a full charge   Fully DOT compliant  crash tested  with airbags  crash structures  etc
