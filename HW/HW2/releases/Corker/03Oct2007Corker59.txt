                                                                             Corker Votes for Border Security Funding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  October 03  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  today voted for a border security amendment  S AMDT 3117  to the Defense Appropriations Bill that appropriates  3 billion in emergency funding for personnel  equipment and employee verification to help secure the border and enforce immigration laws  Securing our borders will go a long way toward restoring public faith that the federal government is doing what it says it will do to keep America safe  said Corker  an original cosponsor of the amendment  This amendment will put the personnel  equipment  and systems in place  so we can gain operational control of our borders
