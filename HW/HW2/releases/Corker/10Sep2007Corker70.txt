                                                                             Corker Denounces MoveOn Org Ad Attacking Gen  Petraeus                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Urges Colleagues to Listen to Petraeus  Crocker                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  September 10  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following comments today in regards to the MoveOn org ad attacking Commanding General David Petraeus in todays New York Times  Corker has joined colleagues in a letter to Majority Leader Harry Reid asking him to denounce the ad  I know General Petraeus personally and believe him to be a man of great integrity  It is absolutely unconscionable that any group would try to politicize and diminish his service to our country in this disingenuous way  said Corker  Regardless of how anyone in this country may feel about our situation in Iraq and the best way forward  General Petraeus  who has served our country with courage  honor and distinction for more than three decades  deserves our utmost respect  I hope my colleagues will join me in giving him our respect and in actually listening to the testimony he and Ambassador Crocker deliver this week
