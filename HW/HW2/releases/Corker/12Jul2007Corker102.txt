                                                                             Alexander  Corker Statement Nomination Of Ed Yarbrough To Serve As U S  Attorney For The Middle District Of Tennessee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Thursday  July 12  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Lamar Alexander and Bob Corker made the following statements today regarding President Bush s nomination of Ed Yarbrough to serve as U S  attorney for Middle Tennessee     The President has made a wise choice for Middle Tennessee  Ed Yarbrough is a mature  seasoned professional with deep roots in the community and a long  distinguished legal career  I hope the Senate confirms him without delay  said Alexander     I congratulate Ed on his nomination to serve as U S  Attorney for the Middle District  and I hope he is given a swift confirmation by the Senate  said Corker
