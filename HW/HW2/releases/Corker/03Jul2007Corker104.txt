                                                                             U S  Senator Bob Corker s Statement Honoring Independence Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Tuesday  July 03  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R Tenn   made the following statement in honor of Independence Day     Since we first celebrated our independence on July 4  1776  Americans have fought many struggles in defense of freedom  both here at home and abroad  On this day when we celebrate all that is best about America  our thoughts are on the military men and women and their families who make tremendous sacrifices to serve our country     I am deeply honored to represent the citizens of Tennessee in the United States Senate  On this July Fourth  I hope Tennesseans will join me in giving thanks that we live in the greatest  freest society the world has ever known and in pledging never to take it for granted
