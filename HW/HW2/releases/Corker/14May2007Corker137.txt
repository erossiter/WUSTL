                                                                             Corker Comments on Earmarking Process for FY 2008                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Monday  May 14  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today in regards to the fiscal year 2008 earmarking process         I don t believe there are very many Americans who have faith in the way the federal government spends their money  and abuses of the earmark system have no doubt contributed to an erosion of public confidence  I share this skepticism         The earmarking process is in transition  and I want to spend this first year helping shape earmark reforms and reviewing the entire appropriations process  For that reason  this first year our office is only requesting appropriations that meet the following guidelines   1  Are in support of a clear federal mission  and  2  are explicitly authorized by federal legislation such as those Army Corps of Engineer projects that are authorized by the Water Resources Development Act  WRDA   or  3  have won multi year competitive grant awards through an authorized federal program
