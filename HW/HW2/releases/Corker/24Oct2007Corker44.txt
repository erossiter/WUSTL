                                                                             Corker Votes Against Proceeding to the Dream Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 from the Office of Senator Bob Corker  Wednesday  October 24  2007  WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Bob Corker  R TN  made the following statement today after voting against proceeding to consideration of the Dream Act  S  2205     I voted against this bill because it extends federal benefits to those who are in our country illegally  said Corker  Regardless of what our immigration policy is in the country  we dont have the means to monitor or enforce it  Thats where our focus should be  creating a system that puts us in a position to actually enforce our immigration policy
