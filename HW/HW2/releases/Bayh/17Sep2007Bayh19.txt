Statement from Senator Bayh on Nomination of Judge Michael Mukasey for Attorney General Washington-- Following is a statement from U.S. Senator Evan Bayh on todays nomination of retired federal judge Michael B. Mukasey to be the next U.S. Attorney General:   I am encouraged by what I have learned about Judge Mukasey so far. If he demonstrates at his confirmation hearing that he is competent, fair-minded and loyal to the rule of law, I will vote to confirm him.
