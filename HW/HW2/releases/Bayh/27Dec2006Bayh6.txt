Bayh Statement on the Death of Former President Gerald Ford Washington, D.C. -- U.S. Senator Evan Bayh today released the following statement regarding the death of former President Gerald Ford:  Today I join millions of Americans in mourning the passing of former President Gerald Ford. During one of our nation's most difficult times, he set an example of dignity and bipartisanship that remains equally important today. Susan and I send our thoughts and prayers to the Ford family, along with our gratitude for his service to our nation.
