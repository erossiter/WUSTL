Bayh Announces $300,000 Loan Toward a New Fire Station In Westport Washington-- U.S. Senator Evan Bayh today announced a $300,000 guaranteed loan for the Westport Community Volunteer Fire Departments construction of a new fire station.  The USDAs Office of Rural Development will guarantee the loan to be provided by MainSource Bank of North Vernon.   This loan is an important step in making sure local firefighters and first responders are well equipped to meet the safety and emergency needs of the surrounding community, Senator Bayh said.   The guaranteed loan is provided as part of the USDAs Office of Rural Development Community Facility Guaranteed Loan program, which seeks to develop essential facilities for rural areas and towns.  The Westport VFD serves the Town of Westport and portions of Decatur and Jennings counties.
