Bayh Announces $300,000 for American Treasure in Columbus Washington-- U.S. Senator Evan Bayh today announced a $300,000 grant for repairs on the North Christian Church in Columbus.  The National Parks Service awarded the grant as part of the Save Americas Treasures grants.   This is great news for the North Christian Church and the city of Columbus, Senator Bayh said. The church is a unique architectural jewel and a point of pride for Columbus and Hoosiers across the state. Im thrilled to see it recognized as one of Americas historical treasures. The National Historic Landmark Church was designed by the architect Eero Saarinen and is part of more than 125 works of modern design in Columbus.  The funds will be used to repair the roof and drainage system on the church.
