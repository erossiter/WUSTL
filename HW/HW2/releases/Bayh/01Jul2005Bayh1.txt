Statement from Senator Bayh on Retirement of Justice Sandra Day O'Connor Washington-- The following is a statement from U.S. Senator Evan Bayh regarding the retirement of Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O'Connor: Justice O'Connor's retirement presents the president with an opportunity to break away from the partisan fights that have plagued Washington and produce a nominee with true consensus support. We should strive for more than the bare minimum needed to nominate someone to a lifetime appointment. The responsibilities required of the Supreme Court and the American people deserve nothing less.
