Casey Announces Dates For "Breakfast With Bob"  April 17, 2007 Washington, DC]- U.S. Senator Bob Casey (D-PA) today announced a series of Breakfast with Bob constituent breakfasts. Constituents from across the Commonwealth are invited to visit with Senator Casey and his staff.  At the gathering, Senator Casey will give brief remarks, answer questions from the crowd and visit with Pennsylvanians. A photographer will also be on hand to take photographs of Senator Casey with constituents.  Senator Casey held a similar coffee on March 22nd.  This week the coffee will be held on Thursday, April 19th, from 8:30am to 9:30am in the Russell Senate Office Building, Room 188. The room number for future coffees will be announced at the beginning of each week.  What: Breakfast with Bob  Time: 8:30-9:30am  When: April 19th, May 3rd, May 17th
