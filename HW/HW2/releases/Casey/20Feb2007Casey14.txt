Casey To Tour Dauphin County Farm To Discuss Farm Priorities February 20, 2007 WASHINGTON, DC - U.S. Senator Bob Casey (D-PA) tomorrow will tour Stoney Lawn Farm in Middletown, PA to discuss the 2007 Farm Bill with local dairy farmers.  Stoney Lawn Farm is a family-owned working dairy farm. As a member of the Senate Agriculture Committee, Senator Casey will have a key role in helping to write the new farm bill that is up for reauthorization this year.  Who: Senator Bob Casey  What: Tour of 500 acre dairy farm and press availability  When: Tomorrow, February 21st at 1:00pm  Where: 1597 Colebrook Rd, Middletown PA 17057  Directions from Harrisburg: At the turnpike interchange north of Harrisburg, take Route 283 South toward Lancaster. Exit at the Toll House Road exit (route 341). Turn left off the ramp. Turn right at the T in the road. The farm is at a crossroads and there is a sign along the road.
