Casey on Libby Commutation  July 3, 2007 WASHINGTON, DC- After President Bush commuted the prison sentence of I. Lewis Scooter Libby, U.S. Senator Bob Casey (D-PA) released the following statement:This President and his Administration act like they are above the law. Now the President has given Scooter Libby a literal get-out-of-jail free card after he was convicted by a jury of lying to investigators.
