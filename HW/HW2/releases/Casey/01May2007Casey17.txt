Casey Announces Additional Dates For "Breakfast With Bob " May 1, 2007 Washington, DC]- U.S. Senator Bob Casey (D-PA) today announced additional dates in his series of Breakfast with Bob constituent breakfasts. Constituents from across the Commonwealth are invited to visit with Senator Casey and his staff.  At the gathering, Senator Casey will answer questions from the crowd and visit with Pennsylvanians. A photographer will also be on hand to take photographs of Senator Casey with constituents.  Senator Casey held similar coffees on March 22nd and on April 19th.  This week the coffee will be held on Thursday, May 3rd, from 8:30am to 9:30am in the US Capitol, Room S-115. The room number for future coffees will be announced at the beginning of each week.  What: Breakfast with Bob  Time: 8:30-9:30am  When: May 3rd, May 17th, June 7th, June 21st, July 12th, July 26th
