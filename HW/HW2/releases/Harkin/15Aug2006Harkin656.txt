      WASHINGTON  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Iowa Department of Corrections is receiving a  450 000 grant to implement the Iowa Prison Reentry Initiative  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Justice  s  DOJ  Serious and Violent Offender Reentry Initiative            I am pleased that the Iowa Department of Corrections is receiving these important funds     Harkin said    In order to reduce the number of repeat offenders  it is vitally important that those released from prison receive the tools and treatment necessary to reintegrate into society             The program will improve public safety by providing pre release and transition services to 200 non violent offenders being released in Polk County  A reentry case plan  based on needs assessment  will be developed for each participant prior to their release into the community  A vocational assessment will also be completed and used in developing the participant  s career plan                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
