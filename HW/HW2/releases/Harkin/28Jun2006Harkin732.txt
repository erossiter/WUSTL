      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee approved  250 000 for the Des Moines Hydrogen Vehicle Demonstration Project  The appropriations bill is expected to be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday   It will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law                 As energy costs continue to soar  we must search for new  sustainable and cost effective methods of transportation     said Harkin    Hybrid electric vehicles running on renewable hydrogen could provide a path forward to a clean fuel transportation future                    The project  s goal is to demonstrate the cost effective operation of a fleet of internal combustion engine hybrid electric vehicles  converted to run on hydrogen from ethanol   Last year  Harkin introduced the Renewable Hydrogen Passenger Vehicle Act  which would provide for a long term study of hybrid electric vehicles running on renewable hydrogen                              Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
