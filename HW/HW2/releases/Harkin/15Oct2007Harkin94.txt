 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   Chairman of the Senate Committee on Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry  today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  has awarded almost  80 000 in rural development funding for Clare Fire  Rescue  Ruthven Fire Agency and the City of Moorhead  The funding was administered through the Economic Impact Initiative Grant Program   Fighting fires is already tough businesses  with our first responders putting their lives on the line to keep our communities safe  said Harkin  But in rural areas  this task can be even more daunting  having to reach remote areas when time is of the essence  This funding will ensure local fire fighters have the resources they need to stay protected while fighting fire   Clare Fire  Rescue was awarded a grant of  29 725 to refurbish the Clare fire station   Ruthven Fire Agency was awarded a grant of  24 000 to construct an addition to the existing fire station in Ruthven  The fire agency serves residents in the City of Ruthven and parts of four townships including Hyland and Lost Island Townships in Palo Alto County and Lake and Freeman Townships in Clay County   The City of Moorhead was awarded a grant of  25 300 to purchase a fire fighting brush and rescue equipment vehicle to be used by the City s Volunteer Fire and Rescue Service  In addition to the City of Moorhead  the service area also includes 42 000 acres of grasslands and 6 800 acres of the Loess Hills State Forest       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
