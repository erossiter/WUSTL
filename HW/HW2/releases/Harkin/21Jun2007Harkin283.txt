 Washington  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Kids Corner Child Care  Inc  secured a loan of  1 million in rural development funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA   Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee   Safe  affordable child care is hard enough to find  let alone in rural areas where sometimes these resources just dont exist  Kids Corner is looking to change that and this funding will pave the way  said Harkin  I congratulate Kids Corner Child Care  Inc  on receiving this competitive funding and I look forward to the improvements it will generate   Kids Corner Child Care  Inc  will use the funding to construct a new child care facility in Oskaloosa  IA to serve 286 children with child care  Head Start and before and after school programs   Harkin included a provision in the 2002 farm bill to encourage the Community Facility Loan program to prioritize funding for child care facilities       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
