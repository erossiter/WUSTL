      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that three Iowa Communities received a total of  483 000 from the U S  Department of Justice  DOJ  COPS Secure Our Schools Grant Program   These funds will help schools improve school safety  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee            It is crucial that we make sure that Iowa schools are as safe as possible for our kids     Harkin said    I hope these funds will help communities across Iowa continue improving school safety             COPS Secure Our Schools Grant Program will cover 50 percent of the cost of installing metal detectors  locks  lighting  and other equipment that can help deter crime   The grants can also be used to cover 50 percent of the cost of security assessments and training          Harkin is a long time supporter of the COPS program  Last year  Harkin worked successfully to restore funding to COPS program  which has put more police officers on America  s streets  This year  Harkin is again fighting the President  s proposal to severely cut funding for the Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  and other important anti crime initiatives  During a roundtable with Iowa law enforcement officers in January  Harkin heard firsthand that local police forces need funding from these programs to help them continue to fight meth effectively          The individual grant awards are as follows              West Des Moines   465 048             Oskaloosa Police Department   10 950            Sergeant Bluffs   7 078                                Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
