   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Shelby County will receive a  165 000 loan to construct housing for people with disabilities in need of housing   The funds are made available through the Department of Agriculture  s  USDA  Community Facility Direct Loan Program   Harkin is the Ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee         Supporting efforts to provide housing for people with disabilities is vital in rural communities     said Harkin     Meeting the financial needs to renovate facilities can be difficult and these loans help ensure that there are accessible housing options for Iowans with disabilities             The loan will be used by Concerned  Inc   a non profit corporation  that plans to convert its former workshop into housing for disabled clients   Concerned  Inc received  400 000 in loans and grants in Fiscal Year 2005                      Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
