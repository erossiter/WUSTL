 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Keokuk received  1 062 396 to improve infrastructure at Keokuk Municipal Airport  This grant was made available by the U S  Department of Transportation  Harkin is a member of the panel which funds transportation initiatives   Obtaining this funding has been a priority of Keokuks leaders for two years  and I am pleased that the town has received funding that will enable airport improvements to go forward  said Harkin  This funding will help improve airport safety by installing perimeter fencing  Maintaining a quality airport is key to the economic health of this community  because the availability nearby airport is a valuable asset that can help draw new businesses to the area   Specifically  these funds will be used to install perimeter fencing and to rehabilitate a taxiway       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
