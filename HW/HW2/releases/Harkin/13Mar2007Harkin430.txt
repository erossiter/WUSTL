         WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  will meet with leaders from the greater Quad Cities community in Washington  D C  on Thursday  March 15 to discuss issues important to the area   Harkin meets annually with Iowa chambers of commerce and community leaders to discuss issues ranging from health care to transportation to appropriations funding                  Meeting with the Quad Cities delegation is an opportunity for me to learn first hand about pressing local issues  I appreciate the time these local leaders take to discuss how what happens in Washington can improve the quality of life in their region   Harkin said    The Quad Cities community has a bold vision for the future  and I want to help make that vision a reality                In his meeting with the Quad Cities delegation  Harkin will discuss how he can continue to work with local leaders to support the Rock Island Arsenal  In recent years  Harkin has been a strong advocate for the facility and has secured federal funding for numerous projects there                During Thursday s meeting  Harkin will also discuss how to secure additional funds needed to improve the I  74 bridge and plans to improve the sewer diversion tunnel   Harkin has already acquired preliminary funding for both projects  Harkin also successfully secured funds to make improvements to Kimberly Road and to construct a rail spur to the Eastern Iowa Industrial Center                                    Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
