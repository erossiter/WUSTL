      WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  will meet with the Technology Corridor on Tuesday  February 13  to discuss issues important to Cedar Rapids  Iowa City  and the surrounding areas   Harkin meets annually with Iowa community leaders to discuss issues ranging from health care to transportation to appropriations funding                      Meeting with the Cedar Rapids  Iowa City Technology Corridor is an invaluable opportunity for me to learn first hand from local leaders about pressing issues in their backyard   said Harkin    I appreciate the time they take to discuss how what happens in Washington can improve the quality of life in their area                      As a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Harkin has helped the Technology Corridor significantly improve local infrastructure  Harkin also helped secure funding for improvements for Collins Road  Edgewood Road and I 80  the creation of a new parking facility in Iowa City  and many other local projects   A long time advocate of improving local recreational facilities  Harkin acquired funding to create local recreational trails                  In FY 2003  Harkin secured funding to begin designing a new courthouse in Cedar Rapids  Harkin is currently working to obtain the additional funds needed to proceed with the project                                          Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
