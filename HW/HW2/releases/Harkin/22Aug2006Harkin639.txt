   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Warren County will receive an  80 000 grant for rural development projects from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    The funds will be used to help the Liberty Center area construct a central wastewater collection facility and purchase the Southeast Warren Schools treatment facility  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee             Supporting efficient and effective water and waste disposal systems is essential for economic growth in rural Iowa     said Harkin     These funds will help make necessary upgrades for the region  s water treatment infrastructure               The funds are to be given through USDA  s Water and Waste Disposal Direct Loan and Grant Program                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
