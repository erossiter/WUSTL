   WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  has awarded Kornerstone Kids Child Care  Inc  with a grant of  50 000   The funding will be used to assist with the construction and purchase of equipment for this new community based child care center in Hamburg  Iowa   The center will be licensed to serve 90 children  ages infant through 4 years  and will also provide before and after school childcare               We all know how difficult it is for working parents to find safe  affordable child care   This is even harder for parents in rural communities   said Harkin    Kornerstone Kids Child Care will help to alleviate that burden for residents of Hamburg and the surrounding communities   This competitive funding award will help them get started               Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry Committee                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
