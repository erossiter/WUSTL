 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced rural development funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for three Iowa communities  the Cities of Pioneer  Prescott and Soldier  as well as the Iowa Department of Natural Resources  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee   Ensuring residents of rural areas have basic needs like power and city maintenance or life saving medical technology in the case of an emergency is what makes rural development funding so critical to states like Iowa  said Harkin  This funding makes our residents safer and improves the lives of all members of these communities  I congratulate all recipients on receiving this competitive funding and I look forward to the improvements it will generate   The City of Pioneer received a grant of  750 to purchase a portable emergency generator to provide electricity to the water system in times of power outage  The funding was administered through the Economic Impact Initiative Grant Program   The City of Prescott received a grant of  950 to purchase emergency medical equipment for the Prescott Fire and Rescue Service  a volunteer fire fighter and emergency responder unit of the city  The funding was administered through the Economic Impact Initiative Grant Program   The City of Soldier secured a grant of  30 000 to purchase a tractor and loader for the City  The equipment will be used to maintain city streets  alleys  utility sites and parks that are essential services provided by the city for the 207 residents plus the businesses within the community  The funding was administered through the Economic Impact Initiative Grant Program   The Iowa Department of Natural Resources secured a grant of  6 500 to purchase an emergency all terrain vehicle  an automated external defibrillator  and a first aid kit to provide improved emergency response capabilities in the area of Brushy Creek State Park near Lehigh  The funding was administered through the Economic Impact Initiative Grant Program       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
