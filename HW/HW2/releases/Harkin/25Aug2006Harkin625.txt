      WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Seeds of Hope in Grundy Center has received  401 003 from the U S  Department of Justice  s  DOJ  Office of Violence Against Women   The funds will help provide quality education and comprehensive services for victims of domestic and sexual violence in Black Hawk  Hardin and Grundy counties  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee            I am pleased that Iowa has received funds to support the housing needs of victims of domestic violence     Harkin said     Violence against women and children remains a major problem  but with this survivor centered approach we can provide these individuals with the support they need to overcome these terrible acts             Seeds of Hope  along with the Black Hawk Grundy Mental Health Center  the Jessie Cosby Neighborhood Center  the El Centro Latino Community Center and Beyond Welfare have partnered up to support victims of domestic and sexual violence in Black Hawk  Hardin and Grundy counties   The funds will be used to provide advocacy and support services to marginalized women who have previously been unable to access services   The grant will also be used to develop and enhance collaborative relationships with community based agencies and develop an outreach educational and support program for underserved victims of domestic violence                                       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
