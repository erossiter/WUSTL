 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Des Moinesis receiving over  491 000 to develop and implement initiatives that will connect young people who dropped out of high school to alternative learning opportunities  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Labor  DOL   Harkin is as chairman of the Senate panel that funds labor and education initiatives   I am pleased that Des Moines is receiving funds to connect at risk youth to services that will help them succeed  Harkin said  One of the best investments our government can make is providing Iowa kids with support they need to succeed in the workplace and in life    Specifically  the Multiple Education Pathways Planning Grants will engage community organizations to develop and implement a program to help youth begin careers and pursue post secondary education       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
