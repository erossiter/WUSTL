        Lead based paint hazards pose a serious health threat to many Iowa families  particularly those who live in low income housing     Harkin said    We must do everything we can to ensure that all Iowans have an environmentally safe home             The Lead Based Paint Hazard Control Program provides funds to identify and control lead based paint hazards in eligible low income privately owned housing  Activities supported by these grants include lead evaluation and abatement and lead safe construction and maintenance work  These grants also support job training and employment for local residents and businesses working on a variety of issues involving the integration of lead safe work practices into house maintenance  repair  weatherization  and rehabilitation                        Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
