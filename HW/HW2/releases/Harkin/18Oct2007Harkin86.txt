 WASHINGTON Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Burlington is receiving a  140 000 grant from the Department of Transportations  DOT  Small Community Air Service Development Pilot Program to improve air service at Southeast Iowa Regional Airport  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee   These funds will be extremely helpful as Burlington works to boost passenger levels  Harkin said  Supporting local air travel will aid efforts to make Burlington an even more attractive place to do business   The funds provided can be used flexibly to boost air service as the airport commission determines is most effective  Southeast Iowa Regional Airport currently offers flights to and from St  Louis and Kansas City   Harkin was a cosponsor of the Community Air Service Development Pilot Program when it was first developed and has been a strong supporter of its funding on the Appropriations Committee       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
