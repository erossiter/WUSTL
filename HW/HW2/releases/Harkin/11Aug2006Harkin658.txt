   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Kossuth County Nature Center Foundation will receive a  150 000 guaranteed loan from the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA    Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee               These funds will help Kossuth county make improvements to local recreational facilities     said Harkin      Supporting parks and recreational facilities increases local tourism and provides excellent venues for people to exercise and enjoy themselves              The funds will be used for the construction of a nature center and emergency shelter in Smith Lake Country Park in Kossuth County   The funds are made available through USDA  s Community Facility Guaranteed Loan program                           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
