 WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee approved  1 5 million for the Iowa Stored Energy Plant  This funding will be part of the FY 2008 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill  which is expected be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  The bill will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   As energy costs continue to soar  we must search for new  sustainable and cost effective methods of production  said Harkin  Iowa is a leader in the production of wind energy  and the Iowa Stored Energy Plant is the first renewable energy based  energy storage system for wind energy   The Stored Energy Plant will utilize a natural underground aquifer for energy storage to provide clean  dispatchable power utilizing wind energy while simultaneously enhancing the value of stored natural gas reserves  The Stored Energy Plant project serves small communities throughout the entire state of Iowa and supports regional grid stability throughout the Midwest       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
