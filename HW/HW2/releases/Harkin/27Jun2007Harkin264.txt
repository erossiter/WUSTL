 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee approved  150 000 for a flood control study in Cedar Rapids  This funding will be part of the FY 2008 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill  which is expected be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  The bill will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   This support is vital to giving Cedar Rapids the opportunity to examine ways to significantly reduce flood dangers  said Harkin  Flood control has always been a real problem in Cedar Rapids  and I am hopeful that this project will provide needed protection for the city s Time Check neighborhood    The funds will be used to initiate a feasibility study to evaluate the integrity of the existing flood levee       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
