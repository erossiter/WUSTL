      WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced two funding awards from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for the cities of Nora Springs and Sheldahl   The Nora Springs Farm Fire Service Company received a  146 000 loan and a  14 000 grant for the purchase of a 2007 pumper truck   The truck will replace a pumper truck that was destroyed in a traffic accident             The City of Sheldahl received a  150 000 grant to construct a central wastewater collection system for the town s 336 residents  Sheldahl has entered into an agreement with the city of Slater to treat the wastewater  The project will provide 137 connections to homes and businesses in Sheldahl             Rural development funding helps our communities make investments that will last long into the future   said Harkin    Every resident in these cities benefits from improvements that strengthen our local fire departments or improve our water systems   I congratulate these communities on receiving these competitive awards and I look forward to the improvements they will generate               Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee                 Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
