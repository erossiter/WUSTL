 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced a  200 000 rural development grant from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for the Iowa Alliance for Cooperative Business Development at Iowa State University  The award will be used to continue funding the Center and  in turn  improving the rural economic conditions through cooperative and producer owned business development  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee   This funding will boost the rural economy by connecting Iowa farmers and rural business owners with the resources they need to stay competitive  said Harkin  Today  rural communities face enormous challenges  but this funding represents an opportunity to reverse that trend and make investments that will last long into the future   I congratulate Iowa State and the Iowa Alliance for Cooperative Business Development on this competitive award and I look forward to the improvements it will generate   The funding was administered under USDAs Rural Development Cooperative Program  which helps rural residents form new cooperative businesses and improve the operations of existing cooperatives  Cooperative Programs provide technical assistance  conduct cooperative related research  and produce informational materials to promote public understanding of cooperatives  Since 2001  the Rural Cooperative Development Grant Program has awarded 146 grants nationwide totaling  35 7 million       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
