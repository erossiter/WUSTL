 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee approved  1 million for the Iowa Central Community College Renewable Fuels Testing Laboratory This funding will be part of the FY 2008 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill  which is expected be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  The bill will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   There is a great need for comprehensive fuel quality testing that provides fast and reliable results  said Harkin  Iowa Central is poised to be a leader in the nation in providing high quality testing for the exploding renewable fuels industry   The project will create a national testing laboratory for renewable fuels produced in Iowa and across the United States to ensure quality standards are met       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
