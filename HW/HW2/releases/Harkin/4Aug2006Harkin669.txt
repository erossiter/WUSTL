      WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Carroll County will receive a  233 000 loan and  265 000 grant for rural development projects from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    The funds will be used by the Carroll County Board of Supervisors to install a wastewater collection and treatment system for 44 users in the village of Maple River Junction  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee         Supporting water and waste disposal systems is essential for economic growth     said Harkin     These funds will help make possible the construction of essential water treatment infrastructure               The funds are to be given through USDA  s Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant Program                       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
