   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Iowa Recycling Association in Polk County will receive a  58 000 grant to educate rural Iowans about the environment  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee         Environmental education is crucial to promoting good environmental stewardship     Harkin said    The more people know about recycling and waste management  the more likely they are to actively care for the environment          The Iowa Recycling Association will use the Rural Development funds to create educational materials for rural Iowa educators regarding solid waste  recycling  and environmental stewardship  These funds will also help increase the distribution of the 4Rs    a curriculum based newsletter    and provide a searchable online database of archived publications                     Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
