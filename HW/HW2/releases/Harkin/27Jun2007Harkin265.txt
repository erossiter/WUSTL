 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommitteeapproved  1 million for the University of Northern Iowas  UNI  National Ag based Industrial Lubricants Testing Center  NABL   This funding will be included in the FY 2008 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill  which is expected be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  It will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   I have visited NABL many times and believe it is one of the most efficient and effective producers of bio based products in the Midwest  I am extremely pleased to be able to secure these resources  Harkin said   NABL at the University of Northern Iowa will provide testing services for developers and users of biobased lubricants  NABL is also a developer of soy products like grease and hydraulic fluids and has several products on the market       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
