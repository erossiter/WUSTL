 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that he secured  13 186 000 for the construction of a new Iowa Army National Guard Readiness Center in Iowa City  This funding was included in the FY 2008 Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations bill  which was passed today by the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Military Construction and Veterans Affairs  This bill is expected to pass the Senate Appropriations Committee tomorrow and will be considered by the entire Senate later this month   The construction of this new facility will help the Iowa Army National Guard complete essential mobilization and readiness training and prepare for their missions  said Harkin  Iowa plays an important role in strengthening our nations defenses  I am pleased to have obtained this funding for our Guard    Once completed  this facility will enable the training of over 450 officers and enlisted soldiers  Harkin is a senior member of the Appropriations Committee       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
