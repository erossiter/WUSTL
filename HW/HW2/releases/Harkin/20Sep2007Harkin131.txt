 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the University of Northern Iowa  UNI  received  2 797 380 to construct a parking facility that will be a center for UNIs transit system This grant was made available by the U S  Department of Transportation  As a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Harkin supported efforts to obtain this funding   This funding will enable the University of Northern Iowa to better meet the transportation needs of thousands of students who come to this campus each day  said Harkin  I am pleased that these funds will help UNI continue to offer students first rate facilities   Specifically  these funds will be used to construct a three level parking facility and to improve an attached surface parking lot that will serve as a transit facility  Three new passenger waiting stations will also be constructed  and two existing stations will be improved  A vehicle locating system and recording fareboxes will also be purchased for use by the UNI campus transit service       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
