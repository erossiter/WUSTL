   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for Iowa workers  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Iowa Workforce Development received over  285 000 to provide employment related assistance to approximately 52 workers dislocated due the closure of Regency Thermographers  in Dubuque  Harkin is the Chairman of the Senate Appropriations panel that funds labor initiatives                     This funding will help provide these workers with the job training and placement services they need to find other employment     said Harkin     It is vital that we help Iowans who have lost their jobs find new job opportunities and develop skills that will benefit their communities and the state of Iowa                   Specifically  these funds will help provide dislocated workers with employment readiness services  job placement  case management  and career and occupational skills training                          Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
