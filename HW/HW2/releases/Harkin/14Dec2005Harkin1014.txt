         WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Eastern Iowa Regional Housing Authority received  61 083 and the Des Moines Municipal Housing Authority received  27 953 from the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD                 It is essential that decent affordable housing become available for all Iowans with low and modest incomes     said Harkin     This assistance will help low income persons and their families take on this problem and work to gain the skills to live self sufficiently   I have always believed that quality and affordable housing is the cornerstone of the American dream                The organizations will use these funds to hire a program coordinator to link participating families to the support services they need to become self sufficient  Harkin is a senior Democrat on the Senate Appropriations Committee that funds HUD                      Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
