 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that  737 577 has been awarded to Ames and West Des Moines under the Department of Housing and Urban Developments  HUD  Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  program  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee   These funds will be of great service to Iowa communities  Harkin said  These investments should give a needed boost to economic development efforts and services for low income Iowans   CDBG funds help develop viable communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for persons of low and moderate income  Harkin is a longtime champion of the CDBG program and has fought to obtain adequate funding for the program  despite President Bushs repeated attempts to cut funding in recent years   The individual awards are as follows   Ames   500 165  West Des Moines   237 412      Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
