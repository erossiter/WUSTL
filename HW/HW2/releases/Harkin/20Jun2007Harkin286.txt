 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that he has secured  750 000 for the Northern Tallgrass Prairie National Wildlife Refuge as part of the FY 2008 Interior Appropriations bill  The bill is expected to be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  It will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law    The funds will be used to buy land in Western Minnesota and Northwest Iowa to preserve native prairie land  The goal of this funding is to preserve 25 percent of the remaining prairie through permanent conservation easements  Four species of plants and seven species of animal whose natural habitat is in this area are listed as endangered or threatened  including the Powesheik Skipperling and the Piping Plover        Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
