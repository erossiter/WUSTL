               WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  will meet with leaders from the Cedar Valley Coalition in Washington  D C  on Tuesday  March 27 to discuss issues important to the Waterloo   Cedar Falls area   Harkin meets annually with Iowa chambers of commerce and community leaders to discuss topics ranging from health care to transportation to appropriations funding                                 Meeting with the Cedar Valley Coalition is an opportunity for me to learn firsthand about pressing local issues  I appreciate the time these local leaders take to discuss how what happens in Washington can improve the quality of life in their region   Harkin said    This community has a bold vision for the future  and I want to help make that vision a reality                               During their meeting  Harkin and the Cedar Valley Coalition will discuss making improvements to Highway 218 in Waterloo and moving forward with the renovation of the old Deere Tech Works  This facility is being turned into a center for bio based products  Harkin has secured funding for both projects in the past                                                   Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
