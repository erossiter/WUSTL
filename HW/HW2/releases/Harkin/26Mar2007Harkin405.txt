                  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced rural development funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for two projects in Iowa   Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee                                  Rural development funding helps our communities make investments that will last long into the future   said Harkin    Every resident in these cities will benefit from improvements to local infrastructure   I congratulate both of these communities on receiving this competitive funding and I look forward to the improvements it will generate                                      The City of Garden Grove received a loan of  350 000 and a grant of  100 000 through USDA s Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant Program   Funding will be used to replace old and leaking pipes in the City s water distribution system   New distribution lines  service lines and meters will be installed for the 95 homes and businesses in town  benefiting all of the community s 250 residents                               The City of Plano received a grant of  21 750 through USDA s Community Facility Grant Program   The funding will be used to construct a city hall  which will allow for the display and preservation of numerous artifacts and pictures celebrating the community s past mining industry                                     Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
