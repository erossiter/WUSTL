                     WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Iowa Department of Transportation is receiving over  50 000 for the Job Access and Reverse Commute  JARC  program  JARC funds are used to provide expanded transit routes or service hours to help people get to and from work  Harkin  a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  is a long time supporter of this program                               Job access assistance helps ease many of the burdens associated with Iowans  daily commute  It also provides those who work outside of the normal nine to five hours with more transit opportunities   said Harkin   People who do not have their own cars  including people with disabilities and those with limited financial resources  often depend on public transportation to get to work                                 Specifically  these funds will provide operating assistance for River Bend Transit  River Bend Transit serves Cedar  Clinton  Muscatine and Scott counties  as well as the Illinois Quad Cities Area                                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
