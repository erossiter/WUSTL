                    Washington  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Mount Vernon Community School District received  361 514 from the U S  Department of Education s Alcohol Abuse Reduction Grants Program   The funds will be used for an innovative and effective alcohol abuse prevention program                        Unfortunately  too many young Iowans continue to abuse alcohol   Harkin said    We must do our part to combat youth substance abuse in our communities   This program can help combat alcohol use among Mount Vernon youth                        This project is a comprehensive  collaborative effort among the Mount Vernon Community School District and three rural districts  It will serve eight schools and over 1 800 students and families per year  Strategies that will help reduce alcohol use in secondary school include environmental intervention to reduce access to alcohol and leadership   resiliency skill building to prevent substance use                                          Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
