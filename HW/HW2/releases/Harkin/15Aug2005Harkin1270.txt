      WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that several communities will receive a total of over  400 000 in loans for rural development projects from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    Harkin is the Ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee             Supporting community projects is essential for economic growth in rural Iowa     said Harkin     These funds will help make necessary upgrades to Iowa  s rural infrastructure                 Details of each grant award are as follows                 City of Iowa Falls     50 000 loan to Iowa Falls Community Theater  Inc  The Iowa Falls Community Theater  Inc  will use these funds to purchase a recently vacated church in downtown Iowa Falls to serve as a Center for the Performing Arts               Kossuth County     300 000 loan to the Kossuth County Nature Center Foundation  The Kossuth County Nature Center Foundation will use these funds to construct a nature center and emergency shelter on the East Shore of Smith Lake               City of Lone Rock     99 000 loan to Loan Rock Fire Protective Association  The Loan Rock Fire Protective Association will use these funds to acquire a building to house fire department equipment  The building will also have space for a meeting room for training purposes                              Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
