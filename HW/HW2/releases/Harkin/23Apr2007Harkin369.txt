         Washington  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for three communities in Iowa   Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee             These funds will help communities improve their sewer systems  a key part of local infrastructure   said Harkin    I congratulate each recipient on these receiving competitive awards and I look forward to the improvements this funding will generate              The Poweshiek Water Association received a  400 000 loan and a  200 000 grant to construct a sanitary sewer collection and treatment facility  This facility will serve 80 users in an unincorporated community of Conroy located in Iowa County            The Regional Utilities Service System Commission received a  350 000 loan and a  350 000 grant to construct and treat a sewer system for Argyle  IA  This system will serve 60 users            The Warren Water District received a  210 000 loan and a  400 000 grant to construct a central wastewater collect and treatment system in the City of Bevington  The new system will benefit 40 residences and businesses in town                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
