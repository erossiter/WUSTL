 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Iowa Workforce Development received  3 7 million to provide job training for approximately 1 332 workers who lost their jobs as a result of foreign trade  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Labor s Trade Adjustment Assistance program  These funds will help Iowa Workforce Development provide Iowans with job training that will help them pursue new opportunities  Harkin said  Job training can be crucial for workers whose skills are not easily transferable or whose industries  lack new employment opportunities  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Appropriations panel that funds workforce initiatives             Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
