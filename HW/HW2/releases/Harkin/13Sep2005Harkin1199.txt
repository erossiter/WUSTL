    Washington  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the U S  Department for Veterans Affairs  VA  has awarded a total of  461 798 to two Iowa organizations    Shelter House Community Shelter and Transition Services in Iowa City and 180 Degrees  Inc  in Des Moines    to provide transitional housing for homeless veterans   Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee             We have a duty to ensure that men and women who have risked their lives to protect America are treated fairly and generously     Harkin said    Thus  we must do all that we can to ensure that homeless veterans receive the care that they need              The Shelter House Community Shelter and Transition Services in Iowa City will use its  351 846 grant to construct a new building which will provide 14 beds for homeless veterans  180 Degrees  Inc  in Des Moines will use its  109 952 grant to renovate a building that will provide 26 beds for homeless veterans           The Department for Veterans Affairs provides grants to community based agencies for the acquisition  renovation  or construction of facilities to provide transitional housing or supportive services for homeless veterans  Organizations may also use these funds to purchase vans for outreach or transportation of homeless veterans                           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
