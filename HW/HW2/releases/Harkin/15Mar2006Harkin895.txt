         Washington  D C      Continuing his commitment to improve the safety of Iowa  s communities  U S  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the City of Agency will receive a  22 500 grant and a  117 500 loan from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA   The funds will be used to construct a new fire station and purchase a training center for the City of Agency   Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry Committee            Making sure residents are safe and secure is a top priority for Iowa communities     Harkin said    These funds will help make Agency residents safer by improving fire services through purchase of a new station and training center for the Agency Community Fire Department             The funds are made available through USDA Rural Development  s Community Facility Loan and Grant Program                                 Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
