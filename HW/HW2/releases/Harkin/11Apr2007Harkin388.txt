         WASHINGTON  D C    As part of his continuing effort to improve Iowans  access to quality  affordable health care  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Primary Health Care  Inc  received  600 000 to increase access to health services in Des Moines  These funds were made available though the Health Resources and Services Administration  HRSA   Harkin is the Chairman of the Senate appropriations panel that funds health initiatives                                  Community health centers improve access to health care for Iowans who lack adequate insurance   said Harkin   These funds will help to ensure that all Iowans get the quality  affordable care they need                                   Specifically  these funds will be used to expand operations at Primary Health Care Inc  s East Side and South Side centers                             Harkin has been an ardent supporter of Iowa s 12 federally funded community health centers  obtaining funds for the facilities  construction  equipment purchases  and dental expansions through his work on the Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations subcommittee  In FY 2005  Harkin also secured funds for a statewide Health IT system that will bring cutting edge electronic health records and e prescribing technology to Iowa s community health centers                                                    Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
