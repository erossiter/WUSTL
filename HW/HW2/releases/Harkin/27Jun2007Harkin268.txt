 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee approved more than  698 000 for the rehabilitation and maintenance of Lock and Dam 19 on the Mississippi River near Keokuk  The bill is expected to be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  It will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   Barge travel is a key means of transporting grain and other products to and from Iowa  Harkin said  The funds provided to continue making improvements to Lock and Dam 19 will benefit the entire areas economy  particularly as it concerns agriculture   Funds will be used to continue restoration of this deteriorating lock  which was placed in operation in 1957  Harkin has been working on this rehabilitation project for six years now  getting a new start for the project in fiscal year 2003  This funding will significantly advance rehabilitation work on this lock       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
