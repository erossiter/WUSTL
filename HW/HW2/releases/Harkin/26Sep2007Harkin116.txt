 Washington  DC  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that The City of Des Moines received a total of  693 000 for two local economic development initiatives  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee   This funding will help two economic development projects in Des Moines  the Riverpoint West Housing Development and the Technology Business Park  move forward  I have long supported efforts to increase housing options in the downtown area  Harkin said  Supporting economic development is one of the keys to creating new job opportunities for Iowans and making our communities a better place to live and raise families   The two grant awards are as follows       297 000 for infrastructure for the Riverpoint West housing development just south of downtown Des Moines       396 000 to acquire land for the Technology Business Park between SE 30th and 43rd St near the Burlington Northern Rail line       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
