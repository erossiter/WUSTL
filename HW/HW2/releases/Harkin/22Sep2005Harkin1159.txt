        Mentoring programs provide a valuable service to Iowa  s families and children     said Harkin     These funds will expand those services to more of Iowa  s children  helping them improve school performance  improve interpersonal relationships  and avoid dangerous and sometime criminal activities             The Iowa Mentor Center is a collaboration of mentoring programs throughout the state dedicated to empowering youth through mentoring   The funds will be used for programs and activities serving children with the greatest need living in rural areas  high crime areas  or troubled home environments   The project will target students age 6 15 attending Iowa schools  where they will be matched with screened and trained adult mentors recruited from local communities  The goal of the initiative is to serve 2150 youth by creating and maintaining these one on one mentoring relationships                         Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
