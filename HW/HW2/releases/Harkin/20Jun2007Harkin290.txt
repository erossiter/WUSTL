 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a  senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today  announced he has secured  400 000 for the  Ottumwa combined sewer system as part of the FY 2008 Interior  Appropriations bill  The bill is  expected to be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  It will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   I am  pleased Ottumwa is receiving funds to make much needed improvements to its sewer  system  Harkin said   This assistance means smaller water bills  for families in Ottumwa  a solution to environmental concerns  and improved  chances of attracting new businesses to the area   The sewer  system in Ottumwa collects and transports both sanitary wastewater and storm  water to the treatment plant in the same structures  During large amounts of  rain fall  the system discharges into the Des Moines River through combined  sewer overflows  CSOs  without being treated  This system does not meet current  regulatory standards  and the city of Ottumwa is now required by the Iowa  Department of Natural Resources  IDNR  to develop a sewer separation plan       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
