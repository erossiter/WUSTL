      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the University of Iowa received over  2 6 million for medical research programs from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services  HHS   Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate panel that funds health and education initiatives            Funding medical research is the first step toward the technological and scientific breakthroughs that provide hope for many Americans     Harkin said     This research will better our scientific understanding and improve the quality of life for Iowans and others across the country          The investment will support the following projects at the University of Iowa                       1 000 000 for the Healthier Workforce Center for Excellence                       968 973 for Arthritis  Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases Research                      368 750 for Heart and Vascular Diseases Research                      324 999 for Pharmaceutical Case Management and Living Well with a Disability         Harkin has been a long time supporter of the National Institutes of Health  the federal agency responsible for medical research  As Chairman and now ranking member of the Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations Subcommittee  Harkin led the effort to double NIH funding over five years                           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
