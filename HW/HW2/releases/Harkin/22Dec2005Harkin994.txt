      WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Harrison County received a grant of  1 314 million and a loan of  458 000 from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    Harkin is the Ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee         Improving our infrastructure is crucial to nurturing our rural economies     Harkin said    I am pleased that Harrison County has received these funds             These funds will be used to finance the costs related to the construction of a central wastewater collection and treatment system for the unincorporated area in Harrison County known as River Sioux and the incorporated community of Little Sioux   The project includes approximately 153 connections  which will serve the area  s estimated population of 300 people                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
