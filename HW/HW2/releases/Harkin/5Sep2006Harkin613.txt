      WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Iowa Regional Utilities Association will receive a  2 million loan to construct a three million gallon per day water treatment plant near Waverly  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Agriculture  s  USDA  Water and Waste Disposal Direct Loan and Grant Program  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee            Supporting efficient and effective water and waste disposal systems is essential for economic growth in rural Iowa     said Harkin     These funds will help make important upgrades for a portion of the county  s water treatment infrastructure               It is estimated that this project will provide water to approximately 1 000 current customers and allow for the addition of many new customers in North Central Iowa                                   Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
