 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that over  32 million has been awarded to Council Bluffs  Des Moines  and the Iowa State Program under the Department of Housing and Urban Development s  HUD  Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  programs  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  These funds will be of great service to Iowa communities  Harkin said  These investments will also give a much  needed boost to economic development efforts and services for low and moderate income Iowans  CDBG funds develop viable communities by providing housing and a suitable living environment and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for persons of low and moderate income  Harkin worked to restore funds for the CDBG program in the 2006 Appropriations bill after the President proposed that the program be cut  The individual grant amounts are as follows  Council Bluffs   1 million Des Moines   4 4 million Iowa State Program   26 6 million The Iowa State Program funds will go to the portions of the state that are outside of cities with more than 50 000 people  The Funds are allocated by the Iowa Department of Economic Development           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
