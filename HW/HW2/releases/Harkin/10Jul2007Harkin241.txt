 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations committee  today announced that  2 million to support transportation  economic revitalization  and housing initiatives in the Cedar Rapids area was included in the FY 2008 Transportation  Treasury  Judiciary  Housing and Urban Development  TTHUD  Appropriations measure  Both the House and full Senate must approve the measure before it becomes law   The funds will help Iowa continue to improve local infrastructure and create more affordable housing for working families  Making these important investments will also help boost the local economy  Harkin said  I am pleased to have secured these funds to continue improving the quality of life in the Cedar Rapids area   Cedar Rapids  CEMAR Trail  500 000  These funds will be used to provide a hard surface trail from Cedar Rapids to Marion  The trail will run along an abandoned railroad corridor through densely populated neighborhoods in Cedar Rapids and a large nature preserve in Marion   Coralville Intermodal Facility   1 million  This funding will help complete the initial construction of this facility  which will include 500 parking spaces  a hub for Coralville Transit  bicycle parking and a child care center  Employees at the University of Iowa and University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics will also use this facility   MARION  City of Marion   500 000  These funds will be used to rehabilitate a neighborhood by converting an abandoned railroad line into a roadway and pedestrian trail       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
