    WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Drake University  s Center for Global Citizenship received  60 000 from the Department of Education to develop curriculum focusing on ethics in a globalizing world  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Appropriations panel that funds education initiatives            By integrating international ethics into its curriculum and trainings  Drake University will provide its students with the tools needed to navigate our increasingly complex global economy     Harkin said          Drake University will use these funds to develop and revise courses exploring the theme of ethics in a globalizing world  provide training for faculty members in both ethics and international studies  integrate international ethics into foreign language instruction  and raise awareness among faculty  students  and community members about the ethical challenges and opportunities presented by globalization                      Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
