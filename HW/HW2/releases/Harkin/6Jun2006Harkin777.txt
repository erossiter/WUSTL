   WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Iowa Protection and Advocacy Services in Des Moines will receive  50 000 from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services  HHS   These funds will support traumatic brain injury protection and advocacy services  Harkin is the Ranking Democrat on the Senate Appropriations panel that funds health initiatives             I am pleased that Iowa Protection and Advocacy Services has received this grant     said Harkin     These funds will help provide needed support services to victims of traumatic brain injuries and their families                The Traumatic Brain Injury Protection and Advocacy program supports implementation and planning grants to states for the coordination and improvement of services to individuals and families with traumatic brain injuries  Such services can include  pre hospital care  emergency department care  hospital care  rehabilitation  transitional services  education  employment  and long term support                             Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
