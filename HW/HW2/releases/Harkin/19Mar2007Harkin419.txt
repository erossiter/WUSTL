      WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced federal rural development funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for two projects in Iowa   Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee             Funding rural development initiatives helps our communities make investments that will last long into the future   said Harkin    I am delighted that the Thomas Rest Haven and the Regional Utilities Service Systems Commission received these competitive loans and I look forward to the improvements this funding will generate               The Thomas Rest Haven nursing home in Coon Rapids received a loan of  700 000 through USDA s Community Facility Direct Loan Program   Funding will be used to construct a 12 unit assisted living facility addition onto the existing skilled nursing facility            The Regional Utilities Service Systems Commission received a loan of  100 500 and a grant of  99 500 for the sewer collection and treatment system in the Town of Keswick    The funding was made available through the Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant Program    In Fiscal Year 2006  the project received a loan of  537 500 and a grant of  537 500                              Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
