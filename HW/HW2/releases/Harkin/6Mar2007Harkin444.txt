            WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the city of Newton received a  250 000 grant to evaluate how local resources can help workers affected by the closing of the former Maytag plant in Newton  These funds were awarded through the Department of Labor s Dislocated Worker Demonstration funds                      I hope that conducting an inventory of local worker and economic development resources will help Newton develop new employment opportunities for dislocated Maytag workers   Harkin said   By using local resources effectively and working together  I hope that workers and their families will get the assistance and training they need to get back to work as soon as possible                      Specifically  Newton will use this grant to evaluate regional economic and worker development resources that the city can use to develop strategies to help shorten the time former Maytag workers are unemployed  prepare workers for new  high wage jobs  and promote economic recovery throughout the region                                    Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
