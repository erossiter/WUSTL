    WASHINGTON    As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Greater Oelwein Area Early Childhood Development Center will receive a  150 000 loan and a  50 000 grant from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    The funds will be used for the expansion of the childcare facility in Oelwein   The expansion is expected to add 3 500 square feet of additional space                    In many Iowa families  both parents work  Yet high quality  affordable child care can be difficult to access in Iowa     Harkin said    I am hopeful that these funds will help provide quality early care for families in our state             The funds are made available through USDA  s Community Facility Loan and Grant Program   Harkin included a provision in the Farm Bill that reserves at least ten percent of USDA Community Facility funds for day care facilities                             Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
