 WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee decided to continue providing funding for the wetland restoration project in Clear Lakes Ventura Marsh  This funding will be part of the FY 2008 Senate Energy and Water Appropriations bill  which is expected be approved by the full Senate Appropriations Committee on Thursday  The bill will then need to be approved by both the House and Senate before it becomes law   Iowas environment is one of our most precious resources  Harkin said  I am pleased that these funds will help study how we can best care for Clear Lakes Ventura Marsh and preserve it for future generations   The funding will continue a feasibility study on restoring Ventura Marshs aquatic ecosystem restoration  Rehabilitation of the marsh is essential to the health of the lake       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
