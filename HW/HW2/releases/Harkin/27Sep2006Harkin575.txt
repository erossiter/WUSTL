      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Iowa State University of Science and Technology  WOI Radio  received  85 743 and Iowa Public Broadcasting Board  Iowa Public Television  received  117 150 to replace outdated equipment  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Commerce  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee              This funding will help Iowa public radio and television continue providing quality  educational programming     said Harkin     These improvements will benefit not only the stations  but also the countless Iowans who utilize the educational and cultural programs they broadcast               Details of each award are as follows             Iowa Public Broadcasting Board  Iowa Public Television    117 150 to replace the displaced translator that serves the Fort Madison area  As part of this project  IPTV has also increased the licensed power of this facility to provide service to underserved Iowans in Van Buran  Henry and Des Moines counties             Iowa State University of Science   Technology  WOI  Radio    85 743 to replace an antenna  transmission line and related components at KPTR FM  Fort Dodge  WOI Radio will replace this equipment on a replacement tower being constructed by Iowa Public Television                                             Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
