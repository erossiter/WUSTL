      WASHINGTON  D C   As part of his on going effort to support Iowa  s first responders  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Iowa Falls Fire Department received  19 000 from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  s Assistance to Firefighters    Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grant program  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee            These funds will help the Iowa Falls Fire Department recruit the best possible personnel to protect our homes and families     said Harkin    I am pleased our firefighters are receiving the support they need to keep our communities safe              SAFER grants provide fire departments with funds to hire firefighters and recruit and retain volunteer firefighters  SAFER aims to help local fire departments increase their staffing until they are staffed around the clock  ensuring that the communities have full time protection from fire and fire related hazards                              Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
