      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Indian Hills Community College in Ottumwa received  219 997 and the University of Northern Iowa in Cedar Falls received  424 530  to help educate individuals about the financial  academic  and admissions assistance available to those applying to postsecondary education programs  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Education  s TRIO Educational Opportunity Centers Program  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the panel that funds education initiatives            A college education can open countless doors of opportunity     Harkin said     I hope that these funds will help students access resources that can help them further their education             Earlier this year  Harkin and Senator Arlen Specter  R PA  successfully sponsored an amendment to add  7 billion to the Departments of Health and Human Services  Education and Labor for education initiatives including the TRIO programs  health care and social services  President Bush  s FY 2007 budget cut or eliminated funding for several of these initiatives  including TRIO programs and over 40 other education initiatives                           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
