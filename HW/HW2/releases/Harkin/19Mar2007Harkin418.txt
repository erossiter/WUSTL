            WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  will meet with leaders from the Fort Dodge community in Washington  D C  on Tuesday  March 20 to discuss issues important to the area   Harkin meets annually with Iowa chambers of commerce and community leaders to talk about matters such as health care  transportation  and appropriations funding                         Meeting with the Fort Dodge delegation is an opportunity for me to learn first hand about pressing local issues  I appreciate the time these leaders take to discuss how what happens in Washington can improve the quality of life in their region   Harkin said    The Fort Dodge community has a bold vision for the future  and I want to help make that vision a reality                         Among the topics Harkin will discuss with Fort Dodge representatives are plans to continue expanding Highway 20 into a four lane highway across Iowa                     Harkin has actively supported economic development efforts in Fort Dodge  In FY 2005 and FY 2006  Harkin secured funding for the Lincoln Neighborhood Initiative  which is transforming rental units in a blighted area into affordable family houses                             Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
