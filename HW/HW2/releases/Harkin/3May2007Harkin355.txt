                  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  has awarded Clarke Electric Power Cooperative  Inc  with a loan of  5 85 million   The funding will be used to build 68 miles of new distribution line and make other system improvements   The project will serve 720 new consumers in Clarke  Decatur  Lucas  Madison  Ringgold  Warren  Wayne and Union counties                                                                                                  Bringing reliable  affordable electricity to rural America contributes to the economic development of these communities and the quality of life for all residents   said Harkin    This funding is critical to parts of Iowa where new distributions lines will connect hundreds of residents to the service they need   I congratulate these communities on receiving these competitive awards and I look forward to the improvements they will generate                          Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry Committee                                    Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
