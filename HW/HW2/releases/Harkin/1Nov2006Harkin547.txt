        I am pleased these brave men and women will receive the essential tools they need to keep our communities safe     said Harkin    The equipment  technology and training these grants help fund are the key to protecting Iowa  s firefighters and our homes and loved ones             The Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program  AFGP  helps communities fund the purchase of firefighting equipment  firefighter health and safety programs  emergency medical services programs  and fire prevention and safety programs  Fire departments can submit proposals for grants for such things as new fire trucks  hoses  protective equipment  rescue vehicles  and training for emergency personnel           Although the program assists rural  urban  and suburban fire departments throughout the United States  it places a particular emphasis on rural fire departments   It requires that at least 60 percent of the funding must go to volunteer fire departments   This means that towns like Hartwick and Oelwein are not competing against Des Moines or Cedar Rapids   Additionally  the program requires only a 10 percent match in local funding   In today  s economy  this makes it possible for small fire departments to take advantage of the program            The Iowa grantees are listed below          Perry Volunteer Fire Department    Perry  IA     41 325    Operations and Safety         Newton Fire Department     Newton  IA      44 757    Operations and Safety         Jackson Township   Ollie Fire Department    Ollie  IA     55 775    Operations and Safety         Hubbard Fire Department    Hubbard  IA     91 751    Vehicle Acquisition         Weldon Fire and EMS     Weldon  IA     31 017    Operations and Safety         Coggon Fire Department    Coggon  IA     20 360    Operations and Safety         Iowa Falls Fire Department    Iowa Falls  IA      64 437    Operations and Safety                               Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
