 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Upper Des Moines Opportunity  Inc  has received  500 000 from the U S  Health and Human Services to address the needs of families affected by meth  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Appropriations panel that funds health and human service initiatives   Child neglect and endangerment are two of the many tragedies which stem from Iowas meth problem  Harkin said  I hope that these funds will help safeguard our kids and ensure that their families receive the treatment and assistance necessary to recover from drug addiction   Senator Harkin has led the fight in Congress to ensure Iowa receives the support it needs to fight the meth epidemic  He recently introduced the Methamphetamine Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act that would invest in preventing meth use and treating individuals and families that have been devastated by the drug  Harkin also secured funding to lock every anhydrous ammonia tank in Iowa  making it impossible for meth cooks to gain access to this key ingredient for producing the drug       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
