 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Des Moines Metropolitan Transit Authority received over  200 000 to lease buses  These funds were made available through the Federal Transit Administration  Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee     The Des Moines bus system helps Iowans get to work and complete their daily activities  It also helps improve our environment by taking cars off the road  Harkin said  Maintaining efficient and dependable public transportation will ease Iowans daily commute and boost economic development     Specifically  these funds will be used to make lease payments for 16 buses currently used in Des Moines Metropolitan Transit Authoritys fixed route service  with an option to purchase the vehicles              Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
