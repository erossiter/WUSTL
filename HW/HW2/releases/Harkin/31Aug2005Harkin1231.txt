     WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Iowa Department of Education is receiving a  318 654 grant for the Talented and Gifted Students in Alternative Schools Project from the U S  Department of Education  s  DOE  Javits Gifted and Talented Students Education Act Program   Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the panel that funds education programs              I am pleased that the Iowa Department of Education is receiving this important funding to provide gifted and talented programs in alternative schools     Harkin said     It is important that all schools in Iowa have the resources to help gifted and talented students reach their full potential               The Talented and Gifted Students in Alternative Schools Project is a collaboration between the Iowa DOE and the Belin Blank Center at the University of Iowa to serve academically talented students in alternative programs            The purpose of the Javits program is to carry out a coordinated program of scientifically based research  demonstration projects  innovative strategies  and similar activities designed to build and enhance the ability of elementary and secondary schools to meet the special educational needs of gifted and talented students                       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
