   WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   Chairman of the Senate Committee on Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry  today issued the following statement after learning of the retirement of U S  Department of Agriculture Chief Economist Keith Collins  Dr  Collins has worked in various capacities at USDA after graduating with a Ph D  in agricultural economics from North Carolina State University in 1977  He was the first to hold the position of Chief Economist at USDA     For almost thirty years  Secretaries of Agriculture and Members of Congress from both political parties have relied on Keith Collins for his extensive knowledge of agricultural policy and his sound economic analyses  He has provided incisive and objective testimony on key agricultural policy issues facing this nation  counsel that will be sorely missed with his retirement     I commend Dr  Collins for his long and distinguished service  and wish him well in his retirement     Dr  Joseph Glauber  who has been named as Acting Chief Economist to replace Dr  Collins  has long experience in agricultural policy analysis and is also highly capable and I look forward to working with him       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
