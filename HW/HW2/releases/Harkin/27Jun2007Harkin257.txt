 WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   today announced rural development funding from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  for Iowa Regional Utilities Association  The association received a loan of  1 000 000 and a grant of  420 000 through the Water and Waste Disposal Loan and Grant Program  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee   Today  in Iowa and elsewhere  rural communities face enormous challenges  Getting basic necessities like a water distribution system can be a challenge in rural America  said Harkin  The new systems that will come about because of this funding with impact the lives of hundreds of residents in these counties  I congratulate the Iowa Regional Utilities Association on receiving this competitive funding    The funds will be used to construct new distribution lines and systems to connect 357 users in Delaware and Jasper County  including the cities of Dundee and Greeley  as well as in the construction of an elevated water storage tank near Greeley       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
