        WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Drake University will receive a  100 000 grant from the U S  Department of Education  The university will use this grant to fund scholarships for students pursuing M S  degrees in Rehabilitation Placement or Rehabilitation Counseling  with a specialization in Assistive Technology  who want to work in public or non profit rehabilitation  Harkin secured the funds as the ranking Democrat on the appropriations subcommittee that funds education initiatives                   Qualified rehabilitation counselors help open the doors of opportunity for persons with disabilities     Harkin said     By training rehabilitation counselors  Drake University will be training counselors that can help people achieve their professional and personal goals                 The grant will fund approximately 10 MS degree students who demonstrate a commitment to the public rehabilitation system  service to people with disabilities  and the ability to do graduate academic work  The project hopes to recruit students from underrepresented populations  including people with disabilities  and students who are willing to work for the VR in areas with hard to fill positions  The program  s focus is a direct response to requests from the state VR agency  s indications that this is a high need area              Iowa Vocational Rehabilitation Services  Iowa Department for the Blind  area Community Rehabilitation Programs  the Region VII RCEP  area Center for Independent Living  Iowa Deaf Services and Iowa Program for Assistive Technology were all involved in developing this proposal               Harkin was the chief sponsor of the Americans with Disabilities Act  ADA   landmark legislation which seeks equality of opportunity  full participation  independent living  and economic self sufficiency for millions of Americans with physical and mental disabilities  Harkin also co authored the Assistive Technology Act of 2004  which improves and extends federal programs that help individuals with disabilities access services and devices                                 Originating Site  Number  Site
