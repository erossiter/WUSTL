 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today issued the following statement after the White House announced a new government panel to review the safety of imported foods and recommend ways to improve U S  import monitoring  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Committee on Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry   Last year hundreds of Americans became ill and several died from an outbreak of E  coli O157 H7 in spinach  Hundreds more became sick after Salmonella was traced to peanut butter  Yet the FDA still lacks the resources to adequately monitor our domestic food supplies  and in just one example  still refuses to put in place mandatory guidelines of good agricultural practices for produce   It is encouraging to see the White House finally acknowledge the quagmire of our nations food safety system  With a growing number of imports showing food safety concerns  the U S  rightly took action  Yet in focusing only on imported food  the Administration has once again missed an opportunity to adopt reform that will mean anything for improving safety of food that Americans eat everyday       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
