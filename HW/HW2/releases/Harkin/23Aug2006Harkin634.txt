      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Belle Plaine Municipal Airport is receiving  72 295 from the U S  Department of Transportation  The funds will be used to conduct an environmental assessment on possible mitigation of wetlands in the airport region that are attracting wildlife and creating safety concerns at the airport  The study will be done in accordance with the National Environmental Protection Act of 1969            I am pleased the Belle Plaine Municipal Airport will receive this investment to study mitigation efforts to its wetlands     said Harkin    Safety at our airports is paramount  but it is possible to  at the same time  protect and preserve fragile environmental areas                                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
