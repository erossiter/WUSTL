      WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Southern Iowa Rural Water Association will receive a  1 000 000 grant and a  3 300 000 loan from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  to improve local water treatment systems   Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee            Supporting water and waste disposal systems is essential for economic growth in rural Iowa     said Harkin     These funds will help make necessary upgrades for Sioux and Lyon County  s water treatment infrastructure               The funds will be used to finance new water distribution lines in Northwest Adair County to serve approximately 300 rural users   The funds are to be given through USDA  s Water and Waste Disposal Direct Loan and Grant Program                           Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
