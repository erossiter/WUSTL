    WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Iowa Department of Economic Development has received a  496 000 grant from the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  for the  Main Street Iowa program  Harkin secured these funds as a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee that funds HUD                This August  I visited many of the Iowa communities that have benefited from the Main Street Iowa Program     Harkin said    I was very impressed by the caliber of these projects and I happy to see that more communities were able to receive these important funds              Harkin has been an active supporter of economic development in Iowa  He secured a total of  1 5 million in three prior years for the Main Street Iowa program which provides grants to Iowa communities for major community revitalization dc projects on their main streets   Harkin also recently secured  1 million for the Main Street Iowa Program through the FY2006 HUD appropriation measure which will need to be approved by the House and Senate                                Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
