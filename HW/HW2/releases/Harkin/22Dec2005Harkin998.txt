   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that Xenia Rural Water District received a  4 million loan for rural development projects in Webster  Boone and Hamilton Counties from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    Harkin is the Ranking Democrat on the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee         Improving our rural infrastructure is critical to nurturing our rural economies     Harkin said    I am pleased that the Xenia Rural Water District has received these funds             This loan will be used for 82 miles of pipeline that will serve 285 potential users in south west Webster County  The loan will also support three retrofit projects for the Boone racetrack area  Honey Creek area  and Kelley area and 2005 interest expense on projects being developed in Webster and Hamilton counties  as well as other projects in the area          The funds are made available through Rural Development  s Water and Waste Disposal Direct Loan Program                            Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
