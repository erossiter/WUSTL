 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations committee and its joint conference committee with the House  announced this morning that it has approved  1 97 million for transportation and economic development projects in Council Bluffs  The funding will be included in the conference report of the 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development  THUD  Appropriations and will now seek final approval from the U S  House and the Senate before making its way to the Presidents desk  the President has threatened to block this bill     These funds will make key investments in the Council Bluffs airport and ensure safe landings for residents  said Harkin  We have also approved funding to give more individuals the opportunity to invest in their communities  and put down roots by owning their own home  These important investments will improve the quality of life in the Council Bluffs area and ensure the city continues to move forward     Statewide  the bill provides more than  15 million for specific Iowa initiatives  including  4 69 million to replace buses in Iowas urban and rural transit system   536 000 for Harkins Main Street project to help restore historic downtown areas  and  201 000 for Homeward  a rural electric cooperative focused on providing housing construction to rural areas     A breakdown of Council Bluffs funds include      1 64 million for an instrument landing system at the Council Bluffs Airport to improve safety      335 000 to make improvements to the Sunset Neighborhood  These funds will also provide more homeownership opportunities for low income families       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
