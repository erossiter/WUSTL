 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today applauded the formation of the Iowa Biodiesel Board  The new board represents the biodiesel industry in Iowa  from production and distribution to end users and promotes the commercial success of biodiesel in Iowa  Harkin is Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee   Biodiesel is the fastest growing fuel in the country and for good reason  its production depends on renewable  agricultural sources and encouraging its use is one of the key ways to secure our nations energy independence  The formation of the Iowa Biodiesel Board unites all stakeholders  producers  distributors and consumers  to further the production and encourage consumption of this innovative fuel  This new board will complement my efforts in Congress to promote renewable fuels and I look forward to working with members in the coming months        Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
