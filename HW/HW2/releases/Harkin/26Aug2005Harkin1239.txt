      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the University of Iowa is receiving a total of  614 576 from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services for medical research  The university will use these funds to study the physiology of facial emotion processing  MSM Multi scale stimulation  gas flow  and to perform cancer biology research              Funding research at the University of Iowa is the first step towards the technological and scientific breakthroughs that provide hope for many Americans     Harkin said     The results of such research will add to our scientific understanding and will improve the quality of life for people in Iowa and across the country             As ranking Democrat on the subcommittee that funds health and education initiatives  Harkin has been a long time supporter of medical research  In 2003  Harkin achieved an historic five year doubling of the National Institutes of Health  the federal agency responsible for medical research                          Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
