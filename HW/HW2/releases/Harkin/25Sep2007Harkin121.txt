 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the Southern Iowa Electric Cooperative  which serves Appanoose  Davis  Van Buren and Wapello counties  has received  1 93 million loan guarantee from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA   Harkin is the Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry Committee      Rural electric cooperatives perform a valuable service by providing energy to our smaller communities  said Harkin  By supporting loans to rural electric cooperatives  we are helping to ensure that all Iowans have access to the infrastructure they need to raise a family and grow a business      The Southern Iowa Electric Cooperative will use the loan it secured to build new office warehouse facilities       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
