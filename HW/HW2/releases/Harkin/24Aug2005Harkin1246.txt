      WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that the City of Fairfield will receive over  1 5 million from the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  to assist in the construction of runway at the Fairfield Municipal Airport             The community of Fairfield has shown tremendous growth and economic vitality in recent years     Harkin said     Expanding the Fairfield Municipal Airport will enable the local economy to continue growing and drawing new business into the area              A runway will enable business jets to land safely  attracting more businesses to the area and allowing current businesses to expand their operations   Harkin has also designated the Fairfield airport as a priority project in various appropriations measures in recent years                                 Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
