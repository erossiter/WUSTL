        WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that two Iowa communities will receive equipment worth  26 915 for their first responders  These funds were awarded by the Department of Homeland Security  s Commercial Equipment Direct Assistance Program  CEDAP   Harkin is a senior member of the Senate appropriations committee                  I am pleased that these Iowa communities are receiving the equipment their first responders need to keep our communities safe     Harkin said    Our first responders must have the best possible equipment so that they can effectively protect Iowa families                   CEDAP provides smaller law enforcement and emergency responder agencies with equipment items that will enhance and support regional response efforts and the interoperability of responder equipment  Grant recipients are required to attend a training session prior to receiving their equipment                   Details of the grant awards are as follows             Danville Fire Department  Danville  IA  will receive a Thermal Imager for fire service responders             Iowa State Fire Marshall  s Office  Des Moines  IA  will receive a radiation detector                     Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
