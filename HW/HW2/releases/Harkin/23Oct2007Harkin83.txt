 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Council Bluffs has received  297 000 to restore buildings downtown  These funds were made available through the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Developments Economic Development Initiative  Harkin secured these funds in the Transportation  Treasury  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill of 2006   Providing funding for the restoration of these buildings will preserve Council Bluffs architectural heritage and help boost economic development  Harkin said  I look forward to seeing the improvements this funding will generate   Harkin also secured  300 000 for this project in FY 2005       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
