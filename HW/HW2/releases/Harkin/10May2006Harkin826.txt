   WASHINGTON  D C     As part of his continuing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the Wayne Community School District will receive  150 000 from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services Rural Health Care Services Outreach Grant Program  Harkin is the ranking Democrat on the subcommittee that funds health initiatives            All Americans deserve access to quality  affordable health care     Harkin said    I hope these funds will help provide Iowa families with the health care services they need              Rural Health Outreach Grants help develop health care delivery systems in underserved  rural areas  Currently these grants serve Iowans in Oskaloosa  Spencer  Harlan  Leon and Marshalltown   Unfortunately  the President  s FY 2007 budget proposes eliminating Rural Health Outreach Grants and cutting support for rural health by  133 million or 83  overall           Harkin has consistently worked to improve rural Iowans access to health care  He has been an ardent supporter of Iowa  s 12 federally funded community health centers  obtaining funds for the facilities   construction  equipment purchases  and dental expansions  In FY 2005  Harkin also secured funds for a statewide Health IT system that will bring cutting edge electronic health records and e prescribing technology to Iowa  s community health centers                                 Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
