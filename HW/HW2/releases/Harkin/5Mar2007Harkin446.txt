              WASHINGTON  D C    As part of his ongoing support for rural Iowa  Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  announced today that the city of Leighton received a loan of  30 000 from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA    This loan will be used to renovate the city s community building to make it accessible for Americans with disabilities  improve the building s lighting and wiring  and replace its heating and cooling system  Harkin is the Chairman of the Senate Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry Committee                         I am pleased that Leighton has received funds to improve their community building and make it more accessible for Americans with disabilities   Harkin said   Making public spaces more accessible for Americans with disabilities has long been one of my top priorities                        The funds are made available through USDA s Community Facility Direct Loan Program     Harkin was the chief sponsor of the Americans with Disabilities Act  ADA   landmark legislation which provides equality of opportunity  full participation  independent living  and economic self sufficiency for millions of Americans with physical and intellectual disabilities                                                Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
