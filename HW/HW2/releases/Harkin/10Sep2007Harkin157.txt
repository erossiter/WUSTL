 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Tom Harkin  D IA  today announced that Homeward  Inc  based in Allison will receive a  300 000 Rural Housing and Economic Development grant from the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD   Harkin is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee   Homeward has done a great job of helping modest income families buy homes in rural Iowa  said Harkin  I am pleased that Congress was able to fund this rural economic development program  It will give a much needed boost to Iowas economy   Homeward  Inc  is a Rural Electric Utility organization based in Allison which will use the funds to provide assistance for modest income residents to buy homes in northwest Iowa  Harkin worked to create the Rural Housing and Economic Development Program in HUD about a decade ago and has worked for its continued funding since       Originating Site  Number  Site Filter  Search
