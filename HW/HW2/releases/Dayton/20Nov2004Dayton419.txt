SEN. DAYTON SECURES $150,000 FOR MINNESOTA STATE UNIVERSITY - MOORHEAD CAMPUS SAFETY IMPROVEMENTS
  Sen. Mark Dayton announced that the Omnibus Appropriations bill for FY 2005, which passed the Senate today, includes $150,000 for safety improvements at the Minnesota State University-Moorhead. The funding, secured by Dayton, will help update fire-detection and sprinkler systems in several academic buildings on campus.
   "I am pleased that the Minnesota State University-Moorhead will now have the funding necessary to make important safety improvements on campus. These improvements are critical to providing our students with a safe learning environment," said Dayton.
   The FY2005 Omnibus Appropriations bill is expected to be signed into law by the President soon.
