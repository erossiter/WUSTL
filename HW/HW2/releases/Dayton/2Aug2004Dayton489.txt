SEN. DAYTON ANNOUNCES $1.5 MILLION FEDERAL GRANT AWARD FOR ROCHESTER INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
  Sen. Mark Dayton announced today that the City of Rochester has been awarded a $1.5 million federal grant to make improvements at the Rochester International Airport.  
 The funding, provided by the U.S. Department of Transportation, will be used to extend an existing runway and to rehabilitate a taxiway.
   "The Rochester International Airport serves as a major gateway for Southern Minnesota businesses, local and regional travelers, and international visitors, especially those seeking treatment or training at Mayo Clinic," said Dayton. "It is imperative that this airport, which is such an important crossroads, receive the funds necessary to make travel as safe and convenient as possible."
