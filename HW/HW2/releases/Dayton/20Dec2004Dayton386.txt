SENS. DAYTON, LIEBERMAN TO TRAVEL TO IRAQ, ISRAEL, JORDAN
  Sens. Mark Dayton (D-MN) and Joseph Lieberman (D-CT) will travel to the Middle East on an official Congressional delegation later this week. 
   Dayton will join Lieberman in Israel on December 27th with both Senators returning to the U.S. on December 30th. 
   Dayton and Lieberman will visit Israel, Jordan and Iraq. For security reasons, a detailed itinerary is not available. The Senators will meet with U.S. troops stationed in the region during their trip, as well as U.S. military commanders, diplomats and senior foreign officials.
