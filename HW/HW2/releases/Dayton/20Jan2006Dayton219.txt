SEN. DAYTON ISSUES STATEMENT ON PASSING OF WARREN, MINN., MAYOR
  Sen. Mark Dayton today released the following statement on the passing of the Mayor of Warren, Minnesota, Dick Nelson. 
   "I note with the deepest sorrow the death of a really good friend, and someone I greatly admired, the Mayor of Warren, Dick Nelson, at the too, young age of 47. He was a great advocate for the people of Warren, and he made tireless efforts here in Washington to obtain resources to help his city. He was a terrific human being, and I am sorry that he has left us so soon."
