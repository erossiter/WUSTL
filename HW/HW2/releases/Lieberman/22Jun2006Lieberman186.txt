                                                                                  June 22  2006 Contact                                                                                               Catherine Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Statement On Delay Of Renewal Of Voting Rights Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today regarding the decision of Republican leadership to delay consideration of renewal of the Voting Rights Act           I am frustrated with the announcement by Republican leadership in the House to cancel the vote on the Voting Rights Act extension scheduled for today   Just last month  House Republican leadership joined their Democratic counterparts and my colleagues in the Senate to announce their commitment to renewing one of the greatest pieces of legislation ever enacted by Congress   The Voting Rights Act has been pivotal to ensure the right of all citizens to register and vote as that right is guaranteed by the Constitution  Renewing all of the important voting rights protections contained in the bill is critical and I urge House Republican leadership to schedule a vote on the Voting Rights Act extension as soon as possible            30
