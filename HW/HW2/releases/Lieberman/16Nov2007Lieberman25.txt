                                                                                  November 16  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Senate Passage of Terrorism Risk Insurance Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in response to Senate passage of the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act  TRIA       I applaud the Senate s passage today of legislation to extend the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act   Responding to terrorism means not only mitigating the damage from an attack  but also recovering rapidly  Critical to rapid recovery is terrorism risk insurance   As the House and Senate work out the differences between their bills  I continue to maintain that TRIA should be extended on a permanent basis and that the program should include coverage for nuclear  biological  chemical and radiological attacks      30
