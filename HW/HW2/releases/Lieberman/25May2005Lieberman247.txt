                                                                                                                                         Dodd  Lieberman Announce More Than  23 000 In Equipment For Connecticut First Responders                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Uncasville  Putnam  Torrington and Stafford to receive equipment and training       WASHINGTON   Four Connecticut towns will receive equipment worth a total of  23 600 to help improve first responder capabilities  Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced Wednesday  The equipment will be provided through federal grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security under the Commercial Equipment Direct Assistance Program  CEDAP   The initiative provides law enforcement and emergency responder agencies with equipment to improve communications between local first responders in order to increase efficiency in regional responses           Connecticut first responders are the first on the scene of a catastrophe  They shouldn t be last when it comes to getting critically important resources     said Dodd     Thankfully this assistance     while only a start     can begin to address those tremendous shortcomings and provide our first responders with the help they need and deserve             First responders serve as the eyes and ears of our communities  and make tremendous sacrifices to ensure our safety     said Lieberman  who co sponsored a bill that would have created a statutory authorization for such a technology transfer program  to be funded at  50 million annually for 10 years     The very least we can do is provide them with the tools they need to protect us  These grants will provide local agencies with needed equipment and training  and I am pleased that Uncasville  Putnam  Torrington and Stafford are among the recipients of these awards           The following localities will receive grant awards        Mohegan Tribal Fire Department  Uncasville                    3 700       Putnam Police Department                                             12 500       Torrington Police
