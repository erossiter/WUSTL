Catherine Ribeiro                                                                                                Dodd and Lieberman Challenge Bush to Address America   s Dependency on Foreign Oil                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senators join fellow Democrats in calling for securing our nation   s energy future                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     Today  Senators Chris Dodd  D CT  and Joe Lieberman  D CT  joined other colleagues writing a letter to President George W  Bush and Vice President Richard Cheney asking them to convene a bipartisan national energy summit in order to address America   s dependence on foreign oil           With skyrocketing oil prices  people in Connecticut and across the country have been struggling to pay for gas at the pump and pay for home heating or cooling costs   Meanwhile  President Bush talks about energy independence but never acts     said Dodd      Convening an energy summit would be an important first step to develop solutions to our nation   s dependence on foreign oil I sincerely hope the president and vice president will agree to a bipartisan discussion on this critical topic which not only impacts our economy but our national security              With a large bipartisan coalition  I introduced the Vehicle and Fuel Choices for American Security Act in November  which would mandate 10 million barrels in oil savings in 25 years     Lieberman said     It is now time that everyone   including the Bush Administration     take action on one of the biggest economic and national security challenges of our generation  and holding a bipartisan summit is an important step in greater energy security for America           The full letter is available     30
