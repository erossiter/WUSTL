                                                                                  February 21  2006 Contact                                                                                               Casey Aden Wansbury                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Lieberman Statement on Teamsters Strike at Sikorsky                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman made the following statement today regarding the strike begun by Teamsters Local 1150 at Sikorsky plants in Stratford  Bridgeport  Shelton  and West Haven after the union rejected a contract that would force them to pay more for medical coverage          I sympathize with the workers     they   re facing an uncertain future  Like many Connecticut residents they are worried about how they are going to meet rising healthcare costs for their families   I urge management at Sikorsky to resume negotiations and do everything possible to reach an equitable settlement with the people who are the lifeblood of this company   This isn t about bashing anyone   it s about trying to find reasonable ways to address what I think are understandable concerns          30
