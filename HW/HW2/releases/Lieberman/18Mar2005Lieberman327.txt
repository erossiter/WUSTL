                                                                                  March 18  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Praises EPA Settlement on Clean Air Case Against Ohio Edison                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  praised today s announcement by the EPA that it has reached a tentative settlement in its Clean Air Act New Source Review case against Ohio Edison           Much of the air pollution we experience in Connecticut comes across our borders from upwind states     Lieberman said      This settlement means that Ohio Edison will install state of the art air pollution controls at plants that are directly responsible for pollution that drifts into Connecticut s air   And it shows that the New Source Review provisions of the current Clean Air Act work  and work well  for states like Connecticut           The settlement is in response to a lawsuit filed in 1999 alleging that Ohio Edison violated New Source Review provisions of the Clean Air Act   Connecticut was a co plaintiff in the suit  along with New York and New Jersey   The consent decree will lead to reduced emissions of over 200 000 tons annually of the pollutants sulfur dioxide  SO2  and nitrogen oxides  NOx  from several Ohio Edison plants by 2012           30
