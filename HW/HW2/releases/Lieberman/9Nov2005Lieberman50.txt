                                                                                  November 9  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Testimony of Oil Company CEOs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to the testimony of the Chief Executive Officers of five major U S  oil companies before the Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources         During today   s hearing  the most remarkable statement made by these oil executives is the need to make our use of energy and our transportation infrastructure more efficient   As they pointed out  the price of crude oil is set on the world market by willing buyers and willing sellers   As long as this is true  our economy and security will be vulnerable to nations and forces that are beyond our control   For this very reason  we must dramatically reduce our dependence on foreign oil   Soon  I will be joined by several of my Senate Colleagues as we introduce new legislation  which will achieve this goal by setting new standards for fuel efficiency and emphasizing the use of alternative fuels
