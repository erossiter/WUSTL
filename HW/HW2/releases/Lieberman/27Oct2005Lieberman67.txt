                                                                                  October 27  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on the Withdrawal of the Supreme Court Nomination of Harriet Miers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Washington   Senator Joe Lieberman made the following statement today on the withdrawal of the Supreme Court Nomination of Harriet Miers           With the withdrawal of the President   s Supreme Court nominee  Harriet Miers  I urge the President to select a new nominee who is in the mainstream of American jurisprudence  who would come to the Court without a divisive pre determined ideological agenda and would address each case on its own merits            30
