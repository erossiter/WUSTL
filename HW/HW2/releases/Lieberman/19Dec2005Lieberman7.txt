                                                                                  December 19  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Passage of Long Island Sound Restoration Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement lauding Senate passage this weekend of the Long Island Sound Restoration Act  LISRA   which authorizes important conservation work in Long Island Sound through FY 2010           This authorization is part of my larger commitment to protecting and restoring the ecological health of Long Island Sound  a watershed that is home to 8 000 000 people   Long Island Sound is a national treasure  and 10 percent of the population of the United States live within 50 miles of it   I celebrate this success  and look forward to continuing to work with my colleagues in the House to pass the Long Island Sound Stewardship Act             LISRA  which is based on a provision in Senator Lieberman   s Long Island Sound Stewardship Act  LISSA   will allow continued operation of the Office of Management Conference of the Long Island Sound Study  and provides grants to implement the Long Island Sound Comprehensive Conservation and Management Plan         30
