                                                                                  April 16  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Virginia Tech Shooting                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in response to the tragic shooting on the Blacksburg  VA campus of Virginia Tech          I am stunned and angered by the horrific shooting that took the lives of so many students and faculty at Virginia Tech this morning   My thoughts and prayers go out to the victims of this senseless tragedy  their family and friends and to the entire Virginia Tech community         30
