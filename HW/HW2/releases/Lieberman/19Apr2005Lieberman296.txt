                                                                                  April 19  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Pushes for Stronger Union Protections                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Senator co sponsors legislation to strengthen workers    collective bargaining rights       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  announced today that he will be an original cosponsor of the Employee Free Choice Act  which is being introduced this week by Senator Edward Kennedy  D MA  and Senator Arlen Specter  R PA    The bi partisan legislation represents the next step to strengthen protections for workers    freedom to form unions            I am proud to stand with working families in Connecticut and across the country in supporting this pro union bill     said Lieberman      Strengthening workers    rights to form unions and bargain collectively is vital to ensuring that all Americans    can reap the benefits of a growing economy             The Employee Free Choice Act co sponsored by Lieberman strengthens protections for workers to form unions by requiring employers to recognize unions after workers sign cards authorizing union representation   It would provide for mediation and arbitration of first contract disputes and authorizes stronger penalties for violation of employee rights when workers seek to form a union        30
