                                                                                  November 8  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Rumsfeld Resignation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   HARTFORD  CT  Senator Joe Lieberman made the following statement today on the resignation of Defense Secretary Donald Rumsfeld         I am glad the President is responding to the historic challenge we are facing in Iraq by bringing in someone with a fresh perspective on what we need to achieve our national security objectives and bring our troops home as soon as possible  and I see it as a hopeful development  I have said for some time that our country needs a new Defense Secretary   Our policy in Iraq has been losing public support  and this allows us to open a new discussion with the American people  our military  and our allies to find new approaches to getting the done job in Iraq quickly and in a way that does not compromise American security   I wish Secretary Rumsfeld well and thank him for his service to our nation   Former CIA Chief Robert Gates is a very fine man with solid credentials in national security   But I will withhold final judgement on the matter until we get through the hearings         30
