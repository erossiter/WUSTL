                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator Meets with Lithuanian Delegation            WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  met with a delegation from the Lithuanian Embassy Tuesday evening to receive the Grand Cross of the Commander of the Order of the Lithuanian Grand Duke Gediminas  After receiving the award  the Senator briefly discussed bilateral issues with the delegation              I am honored to receive this prestigious award from our Lithuanian allies     Senator Lieberman said     I look forward to working together to uphold the integrity and valor this medal embodies           This award honors citizens of Lithuania and foreign nationals for outstanding performance in civil and public office  Other recipients of the Order include Senators McCain  R AZ  and Biden  D DE   Juan Antonio Samaranch  former President of the International Olympic Committee  and musician Mstislav Rostripovich        Grand Duke Gediminas  the Lithuanian nobleman for whom the award is named after  came to power in 1316  ushering in a new dynasty of Eastern European leaders  He established Vilnius as the capital of the Lithuania as early as 1323 and expanded the empire as far as Kiev  During his rule  a viable  stable state was established with peoples of varied ethnicity and religions  His rule ended in 1341  The award is intended to keep the spirit of his valor and integrity alive in international policy           30
