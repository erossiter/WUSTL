                                                                                  January 18  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Welcomes Senator Menendez                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON    Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   Co Chair if the Senate Democratic Hispanic Task Force  made the following statement today regarding the appointment of Senator Bob Menendez  D NJ  to the United States Senate            It is an honor to congratulate and welcome Bob Menendez to the Senate today  Senator Menendez has served the people of New Jersey with integrity and leadership since 1992 when he was first elected to Congress  and brings to the Senate a wealth of knowledge and experience   As Bob makes history today by becoming only the sixth Hispanic to serve in this great body  he is helping to bring the promise of America closer to all            30
