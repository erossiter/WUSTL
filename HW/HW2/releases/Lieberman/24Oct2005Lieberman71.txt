                                                                                  October 24  2005 Contact                                                                                               Leslie Phillips                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Lieberman Calls for Adequate Funding for 2010 Census                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON   Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee Chairwoman Susan Collins  R Maine  and Ranking Member Joe Lieberman  D Conn  on Friday urged Senate appropriations conferees to provide adequate funding for the 2010 Census   Lieberman and Collins asked their colleagues to accept House passed funding levels  rather than the lower Senate passed level          If funded at the lower Senate approved level  the Census would be forced to eliminate the American Community Survey  ACS   a new survey designed to provide communities with an annual look at how they are changing   In their letter the Senators argue that    specifically for the Gulf region ravaged by Hurricane Katrina  the ACS is going to be essential in making sound decisions during the rebuilding of New Orleans              30
