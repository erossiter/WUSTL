                                                                                                                                             Dodd  Lieberman Announce More Than  400 000 For CT Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON    Three Connecticut fire departments will receive a total of  405 770 in federal grants to improve operations and firefighter safety  Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced Wednesday  The grants are made available through the FIRE Act of 2000  which is administered by the Department of Homeland Security                     Firefighters are indispensable foot soldiers here at home  Thankfully these grants can help ensure that we have reinforcements on our nation   s front lines     said Dodd     It   s time to provide our homeland defenders with the resources and staffing they need to do a very difficult job             Connecticut firefighters risk their lives each and every day to make sure we   re safe  The least we can do is provide them with the tools needed to do their job properly     Lieberman said     While only a beginning  these grants are a powerful first step toward making sure first responders have the education  training and equipment they need to protect communities across the country          The award was made possible by the FIRE Act of 2000  a law authored by Dodd and co sponsored by Lieberman that gives local fire departments the ability to purchase new equipment  hire new personnel  and initiate education and training programs  Since the FIRE Act was enacted  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA   now a part of DHS  has distributed 241 grants for a total of more than  22 million in Connecticut to help fire departments strengthen their response capabilities        The following localities will receive awards       East Great Plain Fire Department
