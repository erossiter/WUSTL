                                                                                  May 25  2006 Contact                                                                                               Catherine McKenna Ribeiro                                                                                                Lieberman Honors Congressman Robert Giaimo                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON    Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   made the following statement today in memoriam of Congressman Robert Giaimo            Bob Giaimo was the Congressman for New Haven when I first got involved in public service there during the 60   s    We remained friends and I considered him a valued advisor   Bob was an elegant  intelligent and constructive person who brought great dignity to Washington  He was a great Democrat who believed in fiscal responsibility and  frankly  we could use him now  I know I speak on behalf of all of Connecticut when I say we will miss this fine man             30
