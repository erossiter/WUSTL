                                                                                  April 21  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Earth Day Statement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement on the upcoming Earth Day  Saturday  April 22  2006           Earth Day gives us the opportunity to reflect on the resources our planet provides and how we can most effectively manage and conserve them in a global economy   Rising gas prices are yet another indication that we must urgently change the way we conceive of and use our resources  including oil and gas     Lieberman said     My colleagues in Congress can help bring about that change by supporting two pieces of bipartisan legislation that I have introduced  The Vehicle and Fuel Choices for American Security Act and The Climate Stewardship and Innovation Act  both of which address our nation   s need to invest in alternative sources of energy as the world   s oil supply dwindles and as we work to combat the effects of global warming           30
