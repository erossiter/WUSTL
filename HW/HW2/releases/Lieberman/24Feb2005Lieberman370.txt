                                                                                                                                              Lieberman Condemns Bush Budget Cuts That Would Put CT Community Programs in Jeopardy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator urges White House to re think proposed cuts to Community Development Block Grant program       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today condemned President Bush   s plans to cut funding for the Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  program in their FY 06 budget   During a visit to the Hartford Children   s Theater  HCT   Lieberman urged the Bush Administration to re think this proposed cut  which threatens the future of the children   s theatre and numerous initiatives throughout Connecticut           I urge the Bush Administration to rethink its priorities     Lieberman said      We can   t talk about community values without being prepared to invest in those very same communities   To abandon this successful program is more than short sighted   It short changes communities across Connecticut and the nation           The CDBG program  which is administered by the Department of Housing and Urban Development  was created in 1974   CDBG provides resources for low  and moderate income residents including support for local health and senior services  housing rehabilitation  and improvements to parks and public facilities         HCT was founded and incorporated in February 1989 with the intent of providing a variety of opportunities for area residents with a diversified selection of quality live theatre that entertains and educates   In 1988  HCT received Moss Hart Memorial  Youth   Children s Theatre Divisional Award for excellence in theatre          30
