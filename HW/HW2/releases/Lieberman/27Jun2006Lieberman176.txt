                                                                                  June 27  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman to Oppose Constitutional Amendment Banning Flag Burning                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator supports statute making desecration illegal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Washington     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  announced today he will oppose a Constitutional amendment banning flag burning   While Lieberman is opposed to amending the Constitution to prohibit flag burning  he continues to support a statutory ban on desecrating the flag           The American flag is a symbol of what is great about our country     said Lieberman      It is the standard we rally around in war and in peace  in mourning and in celebration and  ultimately  in life and in death  It unites us in our past and in our future  When someone desecrates the flag  they in a sense strike at all of those things   It is because I find desecrating the flag to be so abhorrent and infuriating an act  that I will  as I have in the past  support using statutory means to prohibit flag desecration   However  although my heart makes me want to support the proposed Constitutional amendment  my head convinces me that I cannot vote to amend the Constitution s Bill of Rights for the first time ever in a manner that would restrict freedom of expression  even this most despicable expression            30
