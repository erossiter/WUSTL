                                                                                  March 31  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Reaction to Navy Plan on Sub Base New London Counterterrorism Center                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to the Navy   s announcement that Sub Base New London will become the center of the Navy s undersea counterterrorism operations           I am thrilled to hear that Submarine Base New London will become the cornerstone of the Navy   s undersea counterterrorism operations     Lieberman said      Since September 11th  the military has relied upon attack submarines to perform important intelligence  surveillance  and reconnaissance missions   Unfortunately  without a larger fleet  our submarines can not perform all of the missions our war fighting commanders ask for   Submarines are versatile  powerful  and stealthy  which make them ideal weapons to fight the War on Terror   We need to increase production immediately so we can meet the strategic demands placed upon our submarine fleet           30
