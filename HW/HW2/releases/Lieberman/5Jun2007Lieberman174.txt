                                                                                  June 5  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Mourns Loss of Senator Thomas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  issued the following statement today mourning the death of his colleague Senator Craig Thomas  R WY          I was privileged to work with Craig Thomas for the past 12 years   He was a passionate and principled man  who faithfully represented the people of Wyoming in the House and Senate for nearly two decades   Craig embodied the best principles of public service  and he will be greatly missed by his colleagues in the Senate   Hadassah and I are saddened by this loss  and our prayers are with the Thomas family         30
