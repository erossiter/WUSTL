                                                                                                                                  Senator joins bipartisan effort to guarantee quality  affordable health care for every American       WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  joined a bipartisan Senate effort to reform the nation s ailing health care system by co sponsoring key legislation introduced by Senators Ron Wyden  D OR  and Bob Bennett  R UT      The Healthy Americans Act  S 334  is the first bipartisan  comprehensive health reform bill in more than 13 years  In addition to guaranteeing that every American  including the estimated 350 000 uninsured Connecticut residents  can afford quality  private health insurance  the legislation would  give Americans choice in where they get their health care  break the link between employment and insurance to make health care portable from job to job  promote personal responsibility and preventative medicine  provide equitable payments to health care providers  and reform the insurance market so that insurers are forced to compete on price  benefits  and quality     Americans are rightly demanding that their elected officials provide badly needed leadership in confronting the hydra headed crisis in health care  said Lieberman  At the Healthcare Roundtable that I hosted in September  and in meetings with people around our State  I continually come face to face with the gravity and complexity of the health care crisis  The kind of systemic reform proposed by Senators Wyden and Bennett offers Congress the best chance for confronting that crisis head on  The Healthy Americans Act directly addresses the challenges of rising costs  shrinking coverage  over burdened employers and persisting failure to provide for the 47 million people now completely uninsured  This legislation creates a comprehensive  fully balanced plan to tackle these challenges  By realigning key provisions in the tax code  this legislation would achieve universal coverage without adding yet another burden to the federal budget  The legislation would also shield American business from ever rising health care costs and  by unleashing market forces  protect the economy by reining in overall health care costs    all while reassuring our families that their health care will always be there   to read Senator
