                                                                                  July 10  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Electric Boat Layoffs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Washington     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to the layoff of 440 workers at Electric Boat in Groton           Today  440 members of Connecticut   s hard working and highly skilled workforce find themselves without a job and my heart goes out to each family affected by these actions   I will continue to do everything I can to ensure that these workers receive the help they need to find new jobs by fighting for federal funds for job re training  But we must also continue to work hard to maintain American dominance in submarine design and manufacturing    I was pleased to be able to secure an additional  75 million in this year   s defense Authorization bill to improve the design of our current submarines and begin the design for the next generation of our ballistic missile submarines   And we must increase our sub production to two subs per year  as soon as possible   Not only is this critical to our national security  it will also sustain the submarine building and designing work force so no more workers are forced to the unemployment lines            30
