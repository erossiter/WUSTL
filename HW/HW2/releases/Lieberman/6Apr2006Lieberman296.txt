                                                                                  April 6  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Blasts Bush Administration for Bullying Climate Scientists                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator underscores need for government to be truthful about the impact of global warming                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman today issued the following statement in response to news that the Bush Administration is censoring scientists at National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  from informing the American public about the causes and consequences of global warming           Unfortunately  we now see that  just as it has done at NASA and the EPA  the Bush administration is prohibiting scientists at NOAA from telling the American people the truth about global warming   The administration is discouraging the best scientific minds in the country from focusing their brainpower on solving the climate change problem   That is the opposite of what we should be doing           30
