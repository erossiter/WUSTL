                                                                                                                                          Lieberman Co Sponsors Bill to Stop Tax Preparers from Selling Confidential Tax Information                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator joins Colleagues in calling for privacy protection                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Late last week Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  joined U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  and other colleagues to introduce legislation that would prohibit tax preparers from selling taxpayer information to third parties like credit card companies or database marketers            The people of Connecticut deserve the piece of mind that comes with confidentiality     said Lieberman     Tax returns are personal and private  They are not meant for auction to third party pirates           The IRS has proposed new regulations that permit tax preparers to sell private taxpayer information to third parties  including marketers and database brokers  The IRS has claimed that these disclosures have been legal since 1974 and that the new regulations actually limit the scope of what third party tax preparers are allowed to disclose  However  this bill would mandate the IRS to immediately clarify its rules to prohibit disclosures of any confidential tax information except under very narrow circumstances        The Protecting Taxpayer Privacy Act of 2006  s  2484   sponsored by Obama and co sponsored by Lieberman and Senators Bob Menendez  D NJ   Tom Harkin  D IA  and Hillary Clinton  D NY   prohibits tax preparers from disclosing tax information to third parties  with certain narrow exceptions for law enforcement  fiduciaries  and other legitimate uses that are already allowed under the law          Last year Senator Lieberman joined Democratic colleagues in fighting off the insistence of the IRS to allow IRS employees to participate in the preparation of individual tax returns in conjunction with the Volunteer Income Tax Assistance  VITA  program under the pretense of making sure returns were prepared correctly         30
