                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator Lauds Caribbean American   s Contributions            HARTFORD  Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today joined Mayor Eddie Perez  Consul General of Trinidad and Tobago  Dr  Harold Robertson  members of the Hartford City Council  and representatives of the Caribbean American community in recognizing June as Caribbean American Heritage Month  The celebration  which took place at Hartford City Hall  was widely attended by members of the Greater Hartford Caribbean American community              The influence  accomplishments and contributions of Caribbean Americans spans our nation   s history     said Lieberman     As we celebrate Caribbean American Heritage Month  I think it is a chance to reflect on the marvelous diversity that spans our nation in both time and geography  and makes us a beacon whose light draws millions to our shores            In February  Senator Lieberman joined his colleagues in both Houses of Congress in voting to designate June as Caribbean American Heritage month  Senator Lieberman was a co sponsor of the bill  which also recognized the vast contributions that Caribbean Americans have made to the United States  Senator Lieberman also took this opportunity to express his support for the Comprehensive Immigration Reform Act of 2006  which the Senate passed in May        30
