                                                                                  April 17  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Sikorsky Marine Contract                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Connecticut company to build CH 53K Heavy Lift Helicopter                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman today lauded the signing of a contract between Sikorsky Aircraft and the United States Marine Corps   The contract calls for the Connecticut based company to build the CH 53K Heavy Lift Helicopter             I would like to congratulate Sikorsky Aircraft on winning this well deserved contract     Lieberman said      U S  Marines are a critical part of the world   s best fighting force and now they will be flying in the world   s best helicopters manufactured in Connecticut by the world   s most skilled workforce                  Lieberman  a senior member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  was unable to attend the signing ceremony at Sikorsky   s headquarters in Stratford  but Frank Rowe  one of his military aides  attended on his behalf             30
