                                                                                  November 25  2007 Contact                                                                                               Marshall Wittmann                                                                                                Senator Lieberman Honors Governor William O Neill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON  D C  Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement in memoriam of Governor William ONeill   Connecticut has lost a leader of our state who never lost touch with the people he led Governor ONeill always remained connected with his roots in East Hampton and never forgot the concerns  the hopes and the dreams of the people he met at ONeills Taproom   Bill ONeill was truly the peoples governor   From our days together in the Connecticut legislature  I was privileged to serve with him and get to know his humor  his dedication  his insight  and his concern for our state and her people As a leader  Bill ONeill was patient  practical  and highly productive  As Governor  Bill ONeill made our state more prosperous and dynamic  Governor ONeill promoted future prosperity for our state by launching initiatives to improve and rebuild our roads and bridges The people of Connecticut have lost a good friend and a truly great public servant   Governor ONeill will be missed by all who knew him and were touched by his leadership  Hadassah s and my prayers are with our dear friend and Bill s best friend and life partner  Nikki O Neill    30
