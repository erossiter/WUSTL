                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Site offers students a portal into the government  resources for school projects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Joseph Lieberman today announced the re launch of the student section of his U S  Senate web site entitled  The Red  White and Blue Project  RWB   RWB was designed specifically for students and educators and offers a comprehensive collection of resources and information about the Federal and Connecticut State governments in one convenient location      Along with other innovative features  the site offers an interactive section which allows students to track a bill that Senator Lieberman has introduced  The first bill featured in this section is the  Climate Stewardship and Innovation Act   s  280   As part of the re launch  Senator Lieberman will speak to a group of middle school students at the Capitol Region Education Council s Two Rivers Magnet School in Connecticut about global warming  as well as the process the bill must follow to become a law  Two Rivers Magnet Middle School focuses on environmental science and serves students in the East Hartford  Glastonbury  Hartford  Manchester and South Windsor school districts       I am pleased to be able to offer this innovative feature on my web site   Senator Lieberman said   The Red  White and Blue Project offers students in Connecticut and throughout the nation a central resource to learn about their government and how to participate in it      The Red  White and Blue Project s features include a student friendly biography   A Day in the Life of a Senator  section  a collection of sources for school projects  information about the U S  Capitol Building  links to all of the different branches and agencies that comprise the U S  government  information about the Connecticut State government  educational games and quizzes  and the  Track a Bill  section  The site will continuously add new information and pod casts to keep the content as relevant as possible      30
