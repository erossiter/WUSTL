Academy Day Saturday                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON   The Connecticut Congressional delegation will sponsor Academy Day  an informative session designed to answer the questions of students who are interested in attending one of the nation s five military service academies  Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today   The event will be held from 9 a m  to 12 p m  on Saturday  September 17  at Middlesex Community College  100 Training Hill Road  Middletown  Connecticut                       All of us share pride in our service academies  especially at this important moment in our nation s history     said Dodd     This forum will help Connecticut families learn more about them in greater detail                        I am tremendously proud of the caliber of highly qualified candidates from our state who step forward to serve in our nation   s armed forces     said Lieberman      These skilled and determined students make me hopeful that the right tools will be placed in the right hands of our best and brightest  Academy Day is a valuable opportunity to learn more about the service academies and the application process directly from academy representatives                       Representatives from the United States Military Academy  West Point  NY   United States Naval Academy  Annapolis  MD   United States Air Force Academy  Colorado Springs  CO   United States Coast Guard Academy  New London  CT   and United States Merchant Marine Academy  Kings Point  NY   will be on hand to give a brief presentation and answer specific questions from both students and parents  Representatives from the Massachusetts Maritime Academy  Buzzards Bay  MA  and Norwich University  Northfield  VT   will also be present         30
