                                                                                                                                              Dodd  Lieberman Announce More Than  1 Million For CT Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON   Eight Connecticut fire departments will receive a total of  1 268 136 in federal grants to improve operations and firefighter safety  Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today  The grants are made available through the FIRE Act of 2000  which is administered by the Department of Homeland Security                                             We can and should do much more to protect families from the devastating effects of fire  and firefighters deserve to have the resources to provide that security     said Dodd     After working hand in hand with firefighters for years to make this vision a reality  I   m pleased that these funds will be the first step in making sure these brave men and women have the training they need to keep our communities safe           Connecticut   s firefighters are part of a brave cadre of first responders who risk their own lives every day to protect our communities     said Lieberman     They make tremendous sacrifices to ensure our safety  and we must continue to do all we can to make sure they have the resources  training and equipment necessary to do their job and do it well           The following localities will receive grant awards        East               The awards were made possible by the FIRE Act of 2000  a law authored by Dodd and co sponsored by Lieberman that gives local fire departments the ability to purchase new equipment  hire new personnel  and initiate education and training programs  Since the FIRE Act was enacted  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA   now a part of DHS  has distributed 255 grants for a total of more than  24 million in Connecticut to help fire departments strengthen their response capabilities        30
