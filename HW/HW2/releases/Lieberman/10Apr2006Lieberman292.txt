Catherine Ribeiro                                                                                                Lieberman Lauds Connecticut as Leader in Global Warming Debate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator hosts conference entitled    Climate Policy  The Path Forward for Connecticut and the Nation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today hosted a climate change conference in Hartford entitled    Climate Policy  The Path Forward for Connecticut and the Nation     Senator Lieberman delivered opening remarks and moderated a panel discussion on current federal  state and local climate change initiatives  The conference featured speakers and panelists representing academia  state and local government  and environmental groups  Several participants discussed Connecticut   s recent leadership developing innovative programs for reducing global warming pollution           The rhetoric of denial we hear in Washington will not roll back rising temperatures or hold back swollen seas     said Lieberman     While the Bush Administration tries to bully and censor government scientists who dare to tell the American people the truth about global warming  states like Connecticut are rolling up their sleeves to address the problem with the ingenuity and determination  that makes this country great  Washington  D C  has a lot to learn from the states right now           Conference participants reviewed and discussed different roles that local state officials and federal officials play in moving climate change issue forward  Speakers included Dr  William Schlesinger  the Dean of the Nicholas School of the Environment and Earth Sciences at Duke University  CT State Senator Bill Finch  CT State Representative Richard Roy  Mayor Elizabeth Paterson of Mansfield  CT  Dr  Daniel Schrag of Harvard University  Bradford Gentry  J D  of Yale University  Timothy Profeta  J D  of Duke University  Adam Markham  Clean Air Cool Planet  Chris Phelps of ConnPIRG  and Dr  Margaret Rubega of UCONN         30
