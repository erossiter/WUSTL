                                                                                  December 16  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Votes Against Cloture on USA Patriot Act Reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator cites civil liberties concerns                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement after voting against cloture on HR 3199  the USA Patriot Improvement and Reauthorization Act Conference Report   A motion to invoke cloture on the Conference Report fell short of the 60 votes needed to pass  with a final vote of 52 to 47           I am deeply concerned that the Conference chose not to follow the Senate   s example and craft a bipartisan bill that was able to garner unanimous support   I believe that the government must have the tools it needs to fight the war on terror  but I am convinced that adequate protections for civil liberties will not dull the sharpness of those tools   I am hopeful that we will be able to reach a more bipartisan compromise           30
