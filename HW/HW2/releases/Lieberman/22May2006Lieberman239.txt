                                                                                  May 22  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on the Death of Pat Seremet                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON    Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to the death of Patricia Kenney Seremet  author of The Hartford Courant s popular  Java  column             I join all those throughout Connecticut in mourning the death of Pat Seremet and in expressing my condolences to her family   No matter what the huge issues of the day  a large percentage of courant readers turned immediately to the Java section of the Courant  because she was the most plugged in person to the area   s social life   I have been honored to have her cover me during the most important moments of my political life with a sense of humor that was always teasing  but never mean   Hadassah and I consider her a friend and will miss her terribly            30
