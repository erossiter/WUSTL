                                                                                  December 28  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Dodd  Lieberman Announce    110 905 for Enfield Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     The Thompsonville Fire Department in Enfield  Conn   will receive  110 905 in federal grants from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS   Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today  The fire company will get funds for new equipment and safety and training programs            The brave men and women who serve in our fire departments should have every possible resource at their disposal to keep themselves and the people they protect safe     said Dodd     I will continue to work to make sure our firefighters have the tools necessary to carry out their important duties safely and effectively               Connecticut firefighters put their lives on the line every day for the rest of us     said Lieberman     so we must be sure they have the training and equipment they need to do their jobs properly  This grant is a useful step toward ensuring that first responders are as prepared as they need to be            The award was made possible by the FIRE Act of 2000  a law authored by Dodd and co sponsored by Lieberman that gives local fire departments the ability to purchase new equipment and initiate education and training programs  DHS will award the money as part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  Program  administered by the U S  Fire Administration  Since the FIRE Act was enacted  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA   now part of DHS  has distributed 329 grants for a total of more than  32 5 million in Connecticut to help fire departments strengthen their response capabilities        30
