                                                                                  April 28  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on National Coast Guard Museum                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON Senator Joe Lieberman today made the following statement celebrating the building of the National Coast Guard Museum in New London  Connecticut           I am proud to have been able to work with my colleagues in the Connecticut delegation for nearly a decade to secure the funds necessary to bring the Coast Guard Museum to New London  particularly working with Senator Dodd to ensure the Senate version of the Coast Guard Authorization Bill required the museum to stay in New London   This museum is a tribute to the tireless work the Guard does to protect America   s shores and I can   t think of a better place for such a tribute than Southeastern Connecticut            30
