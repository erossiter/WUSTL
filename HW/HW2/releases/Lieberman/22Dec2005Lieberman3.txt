                                                                                                                                              Lieberman Hails Senate Vote Against Drilling in the Arctic Refuge                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Senator joins colleagues in blocking cloture on Defense Appropriations Bill containing drilling provision                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today hailed the Senate   s rejection of a measure authorizing drilling for oil in the pristine Arctic National Wildlife Refuge   Drilling proponents had attempted to end run the normal legislative process by attaching language authorizing drilling to completely unrelated legislation   the Defense Appropriations Conference Report   Lieberman vowed earlier this week to block the bill from coming to a vote until the drilling language was removed  and worked both on the Senate floor and behind the scenes to secure enough votes to stop cloture   Today  cloture on the bill failed by a vote of 44 to 56   60 votes are needed to invoke cloture           The proponents of drilling in the arctic don   t have the 60 votes they need to end debate on this measure and that   s why they   ve tried different ways to suspend and end run the rules     Lieberman said      But the rules are there to protect against something exactly like this from happening unless 60 people are ready to vote for it  It took a lot of guts for people to stand up and say this is wrong  but I am glad they did   Continuing to try to keep this drilling provision in the Defense Appropriations conference report would be stubbornly persisting against reality   This is a turning point and now we can go on to guarantee funding for our military and passage of the rest of what is in this bill            30
