                                                                                  June 8  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Reaction to News of WH Intervention on Climate Reports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senator Joe Lieberman made the following statement today in reaction to news reports today that White House officials edited government climate reports to downplay scientific links between carbon emissions and global warming           It is unconscionable that the White House Council on Environmental Quality would have non scientists purposely manipulating and muddying essential scientific information on such a critical problem for our country and the world    This political manipulation of science is in stark contrast to yesterday   s announcement from the scientific academies of 11 countries including our own National Academy of Science which said that the science of climate change is compelling enough to demand immediate action by the world s governments   That is why Senator McCain and I will be seeking to amend the energy bill to include our comprehensive plan for reducing greenhouse gas emissions           30
