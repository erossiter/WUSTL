                                                                                                                                        Lieberman Announces  35 000 in Grants to  Research Retail Development in Stafford  CT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today announced that the Cooperative Development Institute  Inc   in partnership with Stafford First  received a USDA Rural Business Enterprise Grant for  35 000 to research and develop a community owned discount store in Stafford  CT         This grant will benefit consumers as individuals and as part of the larger Stafford area community     Lieberman said     The community owned store has the potential to provide job opportunities  strengthen the local economy and refresh this historic community           The proposed store would provide a low priced outlet for items not currently available locally  retain community dollars in the local economy  create local jobs and support revitalization of Stafford   s historic downtown area         The Cooperative Development Institute  Inc   CDI  is the Northeast   s nonprofit center for cooperative business education  training and technical assistance  CDI s mission is to increase economic opportunities and benefits for people in the Northeast by fostering the growth and success of all types of cooperative enterprises  CDI serves communities in Massachusetts  New Hampshire  Vermont  Connecticut  Maine  Rhode Island and New York
