 Seamus Hughes                                                                                                Dodd  Lieberman Announce New Fire Grant for Connecticut                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Willimantic and Willington Hill Fire Departments Receive  203 602 in Homeland Security Funds                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington   Senators Chris Dodd  D CT  and Joe Lieberman  ID CT  today announced federal grants to the Willimantic and Willington Hill Fire Departments totaling  203 602 for improved operations  firefighter safety  and vehicle acquisition  These awards were made possible as part of the Department of Homeland Security s Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  by the U S  Fire Administration  In fiscal year 2007  the AFG program will award approximately  490 million to fire and EMS departments across America       These grants will ensure that Connecticut s brave first responders receive all of the equipment necessary to carry out their vital responsibilities   said Dodd   Firefighters provide an essential public service that these grants rightfully acknowledge       Connecticut fire fighters put their lives on the line every day for us   said Lieberman   We must ensure that our firefighters have the best training and equipment available so that they can continue to protect our community  I am pleased that the Willimantic and Willington Hill fire departments received these vital  grants      Senators Dodd and Lieberman have a long history of advocating for fire safety  In 2000  Dodd authored and Lieberman cosponsored the FIRE Act  which gives local fire departments the ability to purchase new equipment and initiate education and training programs   In 2003  Dodd authored and Lieberman cosponsored the SAFER Act  a law that gives local fire departments the ability to alleviate critical shortfalls in professional and volunteer personnel  Since the SAFER and FIRE Acts were enacted  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA   part of the Department of Homeland Security  has distributed 415 grants for a total of more than  35 million in Connecticut to help fire departments strengthen their response capabilities      30
