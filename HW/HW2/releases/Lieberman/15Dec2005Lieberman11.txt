                                                                                  December 15  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Connecticut Agreement to Join Greenhouse Gas Reduction Pact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to the state of Connecticut joining the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative  RGGI            In the absence of meaningful federal climate policy  the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative is an essential component of a national response to the threat of climate change   With Connecticut   s decision to join the RGGI program  the Northeast and Mid Atlantic states are showing essential leadership on this issue   Connecticut will benefit through increased power plant efficiencies that will lead to reduced consumer costs and cleaner air   In light of the states    leadership  the Bush Administration s stubborn refusal to cut US greenhouse gas emissions is unacceptable   The leadership shown by the Northeast and Mid Atlantic states confirms the urgency of my own commitment to enacting the McCain Lieberman climate change bill           30
