                                                                                  October 29  2007 Contact                                                                                               Erika Masonhall  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman Honors Thomas Meskill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  today made the following statement in memoriam of former Governor Thomas J  Meskill   I am saddened to learn of the passing of Tom Meskill  a devoted public servant  Governor Meskill made tremendous contributions to our state through the many elected and appointed offices he held  One hallmark of his leadership was his concern for our natural resources  which lead to the creation of Connecticuts Department of Environmental Protection  He also left a lasting impact through his thoughtful judgment and distinguished service on the Second Circuit Court of Appeals  My prayers are with Mrs  Meskill and their children     30
