and Treatment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Introduces Amendment to Increase Funding for Defense and Veterans Brain Injury Center                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT   along with Senators Boxer  Biden  Kennedy  Clinton  Cantwell  Landrieu  and Akaka  today introduced an amendment to increase funding for research and treatment on traumatic brain injury  TBI    The amendment appropriates an additional  17 million for the Department of Defense s Defense and Veterans Brain Injury Center  DVBIC   the Army s primary TBI research and treatment program         TBI is affecting an estimated 1 out of every 10 returning members of the Armed Forces   These distressing injuries can leave our wounded soldiers with short  and long term cognitive  behavioral  and physical disabilities   A TBI can affect an individual s job  family  and relationships       Lieberman has called for this much needed funding to strengthen the DVBIC s ability to provide care to suffering service members  enhance the development and dissemination of best practices for diagnosis and treatment  and improve the advancement of preventive and early interventions        Traumatic brain injury  or TBI  is becoming the signature injury of this conflict   Lieberman said    People with TBI experience very debilitating symptoms  including mood changes  memory problems  and increased impulsivity  The Defense and Veterans Brain Injury Center conducts state of the art research and treatment on TBI for the military  They have been consistently underfunded and this amendment provides the Center with the funding it needs to care for our wounded service members   Those who have sacrificed so much for us deserve the very best care         30
