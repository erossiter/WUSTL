                                                                                  October 12  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Vice President Gore s Nobel Prize Award                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT   a senior member of the Senate Environment and Public Works  EPW  Committee  today issued the following statement lauding Former Vice President Al Gore for being awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for his leadership in the fight against global warming   Long before much of the world took note  Al Gore recognized the adverse consequences that climate change will have on our lives and the life of our planet  Through his dogged persistence and herculean work  Al alerted us all to the dangers of the damage that global warming will have on our environment and summoned us to take meaningful action  I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Al Gore for being awarded the Nobel Peace Prize  Hadassah and I are very happy for Al and Tipper and are very proud of the global recognition for our friend  It is an honor that he richly deserves  Now  we must follow Als lead and take the bold steps so badly needed to address this problem  Al Gore sounded the alarm  now we must heed his call to action     30
