                                                                                  May 17  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Commemorates Brown vs  Board of Education Anniversary  Calls for Voting Rights Act Reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement on the fifty second anniversary of the landmark Brown v  Board of Education decision           As we celebrate the historic Brown v  Board decision rendered more than a half century  we honor its crucial role in our nation   s Civil Rights movement that culminated in the passage of the Voting Rights Act   I urge all of my Congressional colleagues to join me in ensuring the passage of bipartisan legislation to extend key provisions of the Voting Rights Act that are set to expire in 2007            30
