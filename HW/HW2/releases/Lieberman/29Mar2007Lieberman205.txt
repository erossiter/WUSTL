                                                                                  March 29  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Tuskegee Airmen for receiving Congressional Gold Medal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                William Bowie Jr  of New Haven and Connie Nappier Jr  of New Britain among 200 Airmen honored in Washington                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today lauding William Bowie Jr  of New Haven and Connie Nappier Jr  of New Britain  who were among 200 of the surviving Tuskegee Airmen receiving America s highest civilian honor  the Congressional Gold Medal          The Tuskegee Airmen exemplify the best of the American spirit   They served their country with excellence  while breaking racial barriers with class and dignity   William Bowie Jr  and Connie Nappier Jr  have made Connecticut and their nation proud  and I congratulate them  and all of the Tuskegee Airmen  on this well deserved honor        30
