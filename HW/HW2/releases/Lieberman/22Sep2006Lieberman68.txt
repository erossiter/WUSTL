                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Senate spokeswoman to join Children   s Defense Fund                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today announced the departure of Senate Communications Director Casey Aden Wansbury  who will leave at the end of the Senate session to become Director of Communications for the nonprofit advocacy group  The Children   s Defense Fund        Aden Wansbury joined Lieberman   s Senate office in 2001 as the Deputy Press Secretary   Lieberman promoted her to Press Secretary in 2003 and to Communications Director in 2005           For more than five years Casey has been an important part of my communications team during some critical times for Connecticut and the nation     Lieberman said     I thank her for all her work for the people of Connecticut and for her able leadership of my Senate press staff during this Congress   I wish her all the best in her new endeavor              I am thrilled to be joining the venerable Children   s Defense Fund to help communicate their incredible advocacy for all of America   s children     Aden Wansbury said      But I am tremendously grateful to Senator Lieberman for the opportunities he has given me over the past 5    years   I am so proud of the chance I   ve had to help communicate the Senator   s important work and many outstanding accomplishments for the people of Connecticut           Before joining Lieberman   s staff  Aden Wansbury served as Deputy Press Secretary at the Democratic National Committee during the 2000 presidential campaign and before that on a range of telecommunications  technology and entertainment industry issues for two public affairs firms  Podesta Associates and Simon Strategies   During her tenure in Lieberman   s office Aden Wansbury has also taken time off to lend her communications skills to Senator Lieberman   s presidential campaign  the Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee  the 2004 Democratic National Convention and the Ohio Democratic Coordinated Campaign  A native of Eureka  California  Aden Wansbury is a graduate of Smith College        Lieberman intends to name a successor for Aden Wansbury in November        30
