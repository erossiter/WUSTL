                                                                                  August 24  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Applauds Over the Counter Approval of Plan B                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Says FDA waited too long to make morning after pill available without prescription                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to news that the U S  Food and Drug Administration  FDA  has issued a decision approving sale of the morning after pill  also known as Plan B  without a prescription           For too long the FDA has delayed over the counter approval of Plan B  leaving the issue mired in politics  But  I applaud the FDA   s ruling today  which means that women will have now timely access to emergency contraceptives  Today marks a victory for women   s reproductive rights         30
