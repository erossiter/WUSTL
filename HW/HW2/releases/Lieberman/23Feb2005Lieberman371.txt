                                                                                                                                              Dodd  Lieberman Announce More Than  500 000 For Connecticut Firefighters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Six Connecticut fire departments will receive a total of  524 351 in federal grants to improve operations and firefighter safety  Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today  The grants are made available through the FIRE Act of 2000  which is administered by the Department of Homeland Security                                             Firefighters are indispensable foot soldiers here at home  Thankfully these grants can help ensure that we have reinforcements on our nation   s front lines     said Dodd     It   s time to provide our homeland defenders with the resources and staffing they need to do a very difficult job             Each and every day  firefighters across the nation make tremendous sacrifices to save lives     said Lieberman     These grants are a step in the right direction toward ensuring that Connecticut firefighters have the training and equipment they need to protect and serve their communities          The following localities will receive grant awards        West Haven Fire Department                                                      168 102      West Hartford Fire Department                                                             120 776                                                                          524 351      The awards were made possible by the FIRE Act of 2000  a law authored by Dodd and co sponsored by Lieberman that gives local fire departments the ability to purchase new equipment  hire new personnel  and initiate education and training programs  Since the FIRE Act was enacted  the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA   now a part of DHS  has distributed 261 grants for a total of more than  24 5 million in Connecticut to help fire departments strengthen their response capabilities        30
