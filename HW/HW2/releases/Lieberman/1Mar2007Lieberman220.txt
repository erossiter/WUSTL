                                                                                  March 1  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Termination of Walter Reed Commander                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in response to Maj  Gen  George Weightman being removed from his command at Walter Reed Army Hospital        Walter Reed s commanding officer was responsible for his command and therefore was properly held accountable for the disgraceful conditions suffered by our wounded veterans there   By relieving him from his command  Secretary of Defense Gates and Secretary of the Army Harvey have taken a necessary first step    But  we must not be lulled into thinking that we have reached the end of the road in identifying the problems at Walter Reed   This must be just the beginning of our corrective action   We owe it to the brave men and women in uniform to hold accountable those who allowed these conditions to persist and put in place a permanent solution        30
