                                                                                  August 24  2006 Contact                                                                                               Catherine McKenna Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on Administration   s Decision to Open Undeveloped Region of Alaskan Arctic to Oil Drilling                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in response to news that the Bush administration said it would offer energy companies the opportunity to search for crude oil and natural gas on 8 million acres in Alaska s western Arctic region           We have learned that the Interior Department cost American taxpayers billions of dollars by giving oil companies open ended royalty relief  and that an oil company failed to maintain its Alaskan pipeline in a safe and responsible manner   What is the federal government   s response   It is allowing new drilling in a sensitive Alaskan wilderness that even James Watt didn   t think should be developed  and it is leaving open the possibility that it will forego payments to the federal government of royalties for the new wells   The American people deserve better than this           30
