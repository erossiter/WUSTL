                                                                                  November 9  2007 Contact                                                                                               Erika Masonhall                                                                                                Lieberman Honors Service and Sacrifice of Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON  D C   In commemoration of Veterans Day  Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  today made the following statement   On this day  we honor our Veterans who have defended our nation and served the cause of liberty They have worn our nations uniform not to conquer  but to protect the security of our beloved country and to defend and spread the ideals of human dignity As throughout our countrys history  today  brave Americans are deployed in distant lands to protect our families while bringing the blessings of liberty to those who have only known tyranny Each and every day  we should express our profound gratitude to our veterans and those who currently wear the uniform of freedom We must ensure that our Veterans receive only superior services and care They have given so much for us It is our obligation as a free people to honor our heroes who sacrifice so that we may remain free   30
