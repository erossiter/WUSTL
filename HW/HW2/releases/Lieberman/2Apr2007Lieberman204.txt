                                                                                  April 2  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Hails Supreme Court Ruling In Global Warming Case                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT   co author with Senator John McCain  R AZ  of the bipartisan Climate Stewardship and Innovation Act  made the following statement welcoming today s Supreme Court ruling that the US Environmental Protection Agency  EPA  has authority under the Clean Air Act to mandate reductions in emissions of carbon dioxide  a global warming pollutant         In knocking down yet another empty excuse for inaction  the Supreme Court s decision puts the focus where it belongs   on the urgency of finding a path forward for a meaningful policy to reduce greenhouse gas emissions   Congress must now take the lead  and all stakeholders should come to the table  now  to make the process a constructive and successful one         30
