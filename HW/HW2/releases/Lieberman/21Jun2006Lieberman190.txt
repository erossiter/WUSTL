Catherine Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on Pfizer Layoffs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today regarding Pfizer   s announcement of layoffs from their manufacturing arm in Connecticut           I am very saddened by Pfizer   s announcement to layoff 300 workers and close down its manufacturing operation in Connecticut over the next year and a half   The Pfizer workers are an important part of Connecticut   s economic success and I hope the company will reevaluate future plans so it can provide for the growth of its research and development work in the state   The news of these manufacturing layoffs in conjunction with the news of the LEGO Group   s layoffs illustrates why it is so important for Congress to begin addressing our long term economic competitiveness and do a better job of promoting innovation in American industry as well as increasing research that will lead to economic benefits  I call on the Bush Administration to take a greater interest in America   s future competitiveness by fully funding education initiatives and several other innovative and effective programs   I also am concerned that the Bush administration   s failure to insist on adequate labor protections with our trading partners exacerbates these problems              As I did with LEGO  I offer the full resources of my office to Pfizer employees so they may apply for assistance regarding retraining options  unemployment insurance and other available resources   I would also urge Pfizer to retrain as many of their workers as possible for other jobs within the company   s Connecticut operations            30
