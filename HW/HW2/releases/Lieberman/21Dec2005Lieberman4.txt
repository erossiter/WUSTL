                                                                                  December 21  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Joins Majority of Senate in Calling for 3 Month Extension of Patriot Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman today joined a bipartisan group of 51 Senate colleagues in calling on Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist  R TN  to support legislation they sponsored that would provide a 3 month extension of the expiring provisions of the PATRIOT Act and the lone wolf provision of the Intelligence Reform and Terrorist Prevention Act in the event that the Senate is unable to gain agreement on a conference report to reauthorize these provisions   Last week Lieberman voted against cloture on the current version of the PATRIOT Act reauthorization  citing inadequate protections for civil liberties   Today   s letter to Frist is available      30
