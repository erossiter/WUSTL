                                                                                  February 26  2007 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on GAO Helicopter Ruling                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator urges Air Force to follow GAO recommendations to ensure best possible helicopter                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in response to the Government Accountability Office s  GAO  findings that the United States Air Force did not consistently apply its own standards in awarding a contract for 141 helicopters to Boeing over Sikorsky        I am pleased that the GAO upheld Sikorsky s position that the Sikorsky helicopter did not get a full and complete evaluation against the required criteria   I am hopeful the Air force will follow the GAO recommendations to ensure the nation get the best possible helicopter for this job        30
