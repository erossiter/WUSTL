                                                                                  May 12  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Press Reports of NSA Phone Record Collection                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to press reports about an NSA program to collect phone records            I am troubled by the press report that the NSA is collecting the phone numbers dialed in millions of calls made by people in our country  apparently without any judicial review or the authority of a national security letter   We must use every tool available to fight and win the war against terrorists who aim to attack us  but we must do so in a manner that is consistent with our constitution and laws   Therefore  I call on the Bush Administration to come to Congress and fully explain their surveillance programs so that we can reach a reasoned conclusion about their legality           30
