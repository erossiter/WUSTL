                                                                                  September 12  2007 Contact                                                                                               Erika Masonhall  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman Statement in honor of Ethiopia s Millennium Celebration                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in honor of the commencement of Ethiopia s Millennium celebration       I congratulate Ethiopians in the United States and abroad as they celebrate this unique milestone  the Ethiopian Millennium        I appreciate the tremendous vitality of the Ethiopian American community   a community that continues to be one of the fastest growing African immigrant communities in the United States  bringing their prodigious talents to our country  to the benefit of all our citizens      The achievements of the Ethiopian American community underscore the importance of the strong ties between the United States and Ethiopia   I am hopeful that our two nations will continue to work together to confront the crucial challenges of the 21st century  notably poverty  terrorism  HIV AIDS  and weak governance        I am proud of the robust relationship between America and Ethiopia  and am grateful for the contributions of Ethiopians to American society   I wish all Ethiopians a peaceful and joyful celebration of the second millennium of Ethiopia       30
