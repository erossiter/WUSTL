                                                                                  September 14  2005 Contact                                                                                                 202 224 9965                                                                                                Lieberman Urges Schwarzenegger to Sign Bill Restricting Sale of Mature and Adult Video Games to Children                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to reports that Governor Arnold Schwarzenegger  R CA  may veto legislation passed by the California State legislature  AB 1179  which would restrict the sale and rental of violent and sexually explicit video games to children             I commend the California State legislature for passing this legislation and sending it to the Governor   I have long been concerned about the access children have to ultra violent video games   In the scientific community  evidence continues to mount linking violent media to aggressive and anti social behavior   The interactive nature of video games makes them particularly troublesome  I would be pleased to see Governor Schwarzenegger sign AB 1179  and keep violent video games out of our kids  hands           30
