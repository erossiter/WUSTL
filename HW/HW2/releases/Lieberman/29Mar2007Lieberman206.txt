                                                                                  March 29  2007 Contact                                                                                               Marshall Wittmann  202 224 4041                                                                                                Statement by Senator Lieberman on the Supplemental Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  made the following statement today in response to the Senate vote on the Supplemental Appropriations Bill        At this critical moment  Congress should be providing all the resources that are needed by General Petraeus and our troops who are beginning to achieve success in Iraq   Instead  a narrow majority in Congress is trying to use a critical supplemental bill to deny General Petraeus the troops he says he needs to succeed and to force our military to retreat  That is why I voted against the measure  It is my hope and desire that once the President vetoes this ill advised and counterproductive legislation  cooler heads will prevail in Congress and we will truly support the troops and not undermine their mission with a deadline for defeat         30
