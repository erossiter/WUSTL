                                                                                  April 25  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Honors Holocaust Remebrance Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman today honored the memory of the millions of victims of the Holocaust by introducing a Senate resolution designating April 25  2006 Holocaust Remembrance Day  The goal of this resolution is to help teach the people of the world awareness of the danger of hatred  anti Semitism  bigotry  and racism  and the importance of compassion and understanding diversity  The resolution was adopted in the Senate by unanimous consent        With this resolution we pledge never to forget the atrocities of the Holocaust and the enormous loss of life resulting from the hatred and intolerance of its perpetrators   said Lieberman   As we reflect on the tragedy of the Holocaust  we must look forward and work together to put an end to genocide all over the world          Full text of the resolution is available    30
