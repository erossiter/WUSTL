                                                                                  December 27  2006 Contact                                                                                               Marshall Wittman  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman Honors President Ford   s Patriotic Leadership                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT   made the following statement today in memoriam of President Gerald R  Ford           Leadership has many definitions  but at its core is the ability to bring a nation together during difficult and trying times   President Ford embodied the essence of patriotic leadership   We mourn his passing as we should emulate his life   For him  bipartisanship was not an empty platitude  but a way to govern  President Ford understood that we may have our differences  but what unites us as Americans is what is truly important   President Ford made America stronger and greater and we are indebted to his patriotic leadership   My prayers are with Mrs  Ford and the family          30
