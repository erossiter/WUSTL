                                                                                  October 24  2005 Contact                                                                                               Casey Aden Wansbury  202 224 0414                                                                                                Lieberman Reaction to FERC Appointment of Judge in LICAP Proceeding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman made the following statement today in reaction to news that the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission  FERC  has appointed a settlement judge in the New England ISO LICAP proceeding          I welcome FERC s decision to appoint a judge to oversee efforts to reach a settlement of the LICAP proceeding currently pending   The current LICAP proposal by FERC results in totally unacceptable high rates of electricity to Connecticut consumers and businesses  It is my sincere hope that the settlement process may lead to consideration of all the various alternatives to LICAP and a better outcome for Connecticut   This does not  however  mean that FERC has met the language I fought for in the recently enacted federal energy bill directing FERC itself to give full consideration of alternatives to LICAP   On that  I have to wait and see         30
