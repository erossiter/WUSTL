                                                                                  May 25  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds Passage of Senate Immigration Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman made the following comment today on passage of the Senate Immigration bill           The immigration reform legislation passed by the Senate enhances our national security  promotes our economic well being and creates a fair and compassionate path to citizenship for those who came here to work hard  paid taxes  respected the law and learned English   The legislation addresses serious problems that have festered for years   Our immigration system has been broken far too long   Some thought it was broken beyond repair  but it is not   This Senate reform bill stands for the principle that government can work effectively to provide pragmatic solutions to the most critical problems facing our nation   I urge President Bush to convince House Republicans to see that this common sense approach to securing our borders is quickly moved to his desk  so it can become law            30
