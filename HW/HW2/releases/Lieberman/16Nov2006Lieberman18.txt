                                                                                                                                                     Connecticut to receive more than  770 000 for homeless veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON     Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today that the Department of Veterans Affairs will provide funds to create transitional housing and provide transportation for homeless veterans   Connecticut will receive  774 895 for the purchase and renovation of a building in New Haven that will provide 12 beds for veterans   It is projected that these programs will be operational within the next 24 to 36 months   All grants awarded by the Department of Veterans Affairs are monitored with VA inspections and reviews of the services provided            This announcement is particularly timely given our nation   s recent observance of Veterans Day     said Dodd      But our veterans need us each and every day   We should have few higher priorities than taking care of our military veterans who have served our nation with honor   Our veterans have worked tirelessly to ensure our national security     the very least we can and should do for them is to help with their basic needs              Ensuring that Connecticut veterans are properly cared for is an essential duty we as elected officials have     said Lieberman     I am pleased to have been able to help secure this grant to make certain these American heroes are given the opportunity to build a successful future in Connecticut               Senators Dodd and Lieberman have long supported efforts that provide help to veterans   Senator Dodd has repeatedly introduced legislation that would enable the families of deceased veterans to obtain a headstone or grave marker from the Department of Veterans Affairs   Senator Dodd has also authored legislation that provides federal funding for the shortfalls in health care resources for wounded combat veterans returning from Iraq and Afghanistan   In March 2006  Senator Lieberman cosponsored an amendment to increase veterans    medical services by  1 5 billion in FY2007 in order to cover services like mental health and rehabilitative care        30
