                                                                                  September 8  2006 Contact                                                                                               Catherine McKenna Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Hails Passage of Childhood Cancer Resolution                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON     Late last night  Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   an original co sponsor of the The Childhood Cancer Resolution  S R  560  made the following statement in response to news that the measure had passed the Senate            Cancer is the leading cause of death by disease in children under 15 years of age  Almost 13 000 children were diagnosed with cancer in 2005     said Lieberman     As an original co sponsor of this resolution I am proud that this measure supports efforts to increase pediatric cancer awareness  treatment  and research  The resolution also expresses the sense that the Senate should support public and private sector efforts to promote cancer awareness in child populations  increase public and private investment in preventing  diagnosing  treating  and helping children recover from pediatric cancer  implement policies that encourage health care professionals to enter pediatric oncology   implement policies that promote the development of new treatments for pediatric cancers and involvement in clinical trials  and support educational services and other resources to improve patient care and long term recovery            30
