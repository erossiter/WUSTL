                                                                                  March 10  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Urges Evangelicals to Support Global Warming Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator speaks to National Association of Evangelicals on environmental agenda       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today met with members of the National Association of Evangelicals and urged them to raise their voices in support of environmental protection and the Climate Stewardship Act  legislation co sponsored by Lieberman and Senator John McCain  R AZ  which would curb greenhouse gas emissions which contribute to global warming             In the book of Genesis  God calls upon Adam and Eve to cultivate and guard the Garden     Lieberman said      We have a moral obligation to protect our environment  to care for Creation   That is why Senator McCain and I have introduced legislation to curb global warming  and why the evangelical community should join us in this profoundly religious cause   Your voice should be heard in this important debate              Global warming is real   It is no longer a scientific theory     it is a demonstrable fact   From melting ice caps to rising tides to dislocated animal species  we can see the evidence of climate change with our own eyes     Lieberman said      The time has come to act and fulfill our moral obligation  The intractable must yield to the inevitable           In his remarks  Lieberman urged evangelicals and faith communities to become more involved in public policy debates  especially debates surrounding environmental issues      America itself is a faith based initiative   While we respect the separation of church and state  we also understand the important role faith plays in shaping our private and public lives     Lieberman said      As George Washington once said     Of all the dispositions and habits which lead to political prosperity  religion and morality are indispensable supports      Your voices should be heard not just in the pulpits and pews  but also in the councils of government           The Climate Stewardship Act  crafted by Lieberman and McCain in close consultation with industry leaders and supported by the environmental community  is modeled after the successful acid rain trading program of the 1990 Clean Air Act   It would require a reduction in carbon dioxide emission levels to 2000 levels by the year 2010 by capping the overall greenhouse gas emissions from the electricity generation  transportation  industrial  and commercial economic sectors  and creating a market for individual companies to trade pollution credits         The National Association of Evangelicals is an organization representing millions of evangelical Christians in the United States   NAE   s mission    is to extend the kingdom of God through a fellowship of member denominations  churches  organizations  and individuals  demonstrating the unity of the body of Christ by standing for biblical truth  speaking with a representative voice  and serving the evangelical community through united action  cooperative ministry  and strategic planning             30
