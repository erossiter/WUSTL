                                                                                  October 17  2007 Contact                                                                                               Erika Masonhall  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on Award of Congressional Gold Medal to the Dalai Lama                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  today issued the following statement in response to the presentation of the Congressional Gold Medal to the Dalai Lama      Today Congress rightly awards the Congressional Gold Medal to His Holiness the Dalai Lama  Through his wisdom  grace  and humility  his Holiness has not only been a source of spiritual refuge and hope for the Tibetan people through their darkest days  but also a symbol of inspiration and courage for all people everywhere engaged in the greatest of all human struggles   the struggle for dignity and freedom  From Burma to Zimbabwe to Iran to Belarus  his Holiness is a reminder to dissidents and democrats that a single human face has the power to stare down the faceless inhumanity of an entire system  In his call to conscience  the fourteenth Dalai Lama is on the right side of history  and  in honoring him today  the United States Congress stands on his side       30
