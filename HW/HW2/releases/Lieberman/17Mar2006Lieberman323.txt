                                                                                  March 17  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Statement of Senator Joe Lieberman on Nomination of Governor Dirk Kempthorne to Head Interior Department                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement on President Bush   s announcement that he has nominated Idaho Governor Dirk Kempthorne to head the Department of the Interior             The Bush Administration   s record on most critical issues facing the Interior Department has been bad     trying to drill in the Arctic Refuge  logging our national forests and yielding too often to special interests   The Interior Department needs some light and air   I have high personal regard for Dirk Kempthorne  with whom I served in the Senate for years   I look forward to the hearings on his nomination and hope he will set a new and better course for the Interior Department and usher in a new era of leadership           30
