                                                                                  November 18  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Asks Appropriators to Treat CT Vets Fairly                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Today Senator Lieberman sent to Senate Appropriators requesting that the Durbin Obama Amendment  S A  1865 to HR 2528   the Military Quality of Life and Veterans Affairs Appropriation Act  which was agreed to by voice vote in the Senate  be retained in the MILCON VA Conference   This is language that benefits CT veterans  but this language was not present in the MILCON VA bill that passed the House       Last year the Inspector General at the Department of Veterans Affairs issued a report detailing the fact that the amount of compensation a disabled veteran receives varies widely from state to state  The states of Illinois  New Jersey  Ohio  Connecticut  Michigan and Indiana were identified as the  low cluster  states in which disabled veterans receive an average annual disability payment of  7 300 or less   The national average is  8 378 with the top state averaging more than  12 000 per year         Full text of the letter
