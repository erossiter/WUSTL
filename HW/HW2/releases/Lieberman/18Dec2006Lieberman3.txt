 Catherine Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Dodd  Lieberman Announce More Than Two Million Dollars to Help Connecticut Health Care System                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Connecticut Senators work to reduce statewide shortage of nurses       WASHINGTON     Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman announced today that Manchester Community College will be awarded  2 174 000 to help the Connecticut health care system provide scholarships and career services to students in registered nursing  RN   certified nursing aide  CAN   and emergency medical technician  EMT  programs  Students will receive the financial assistance based on academic merit and financial need           Our country prides itself in providing the best medical care in the world to its citizens     said Dodd     Doctors and other medical professionals will be forced to do their jobs with a hand tied behind their back if the current nursing shortage continues throughout Connecticut and our nation  I am hopeful that this funding will aid students at Manchester Community College  which does a first rate job in teaching and placing students with training in the medical field             Addressing our nursing shortage and ensuring that Connecticut   s health care system can keep up with current medical demands and the aging baby boom population is vital    said Lieberman     I am pleased with this grant award because nursing and EMT professionals are integral to our hospitals delivering the kind of quality care Connecticut   s residents deserve           All awards are granted through the United States Department of Labor and are being applied to the Manchester Community College Career Pathways Initiative in Nursing and Allied Health  The Initiative targets African American  Hispanic  low income  and non English proficient students  It is also projected that the funds will be used to establish a virtual Career Academy that will work to supply the Connecticut health care system with the medical professionals necessary to meet the state   s future needs  Senators Dodd and Lieberman have long supported initiatives that help revamp and support Connecticut   s health care system
