                                                                                  December 15  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Outraged by Republican Effort to Bog Down Military and Katrina Relief Funds with ANWR Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to news that Senate Republicans are threatening to try to attach an amendment to open the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge to oil drilling to the FY06 Defense Appropriations bill  which will also provide funding for relief from the damage caused by Hurricane Katrina           I find it outrageous    and unacceptable    that after failing in their Budget Reconciliation ploy to open the Arctic wilderness to oil drilling  drilling proponents would now try to tamper with the Defense spending bill at a time when we have troops in combat in Iraq and Afghanistan   Drilling in the Arctic Refuge is bad policy and dragging this controversy into the Senate s conscientious efforts to ensure that our military is adequately funded at a time of war is not right by our fighting men and women   It is equally outrageous that drilling proponents are attempting to exploit the Katrina relief package included in the bill   The Senate has an obligation to care for the victims of that devastating natural disaster and our fellow citizens deserve better than to have the Senate s efforts to provide for their needs undercut by such a desperate scheme
