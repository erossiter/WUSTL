                                                                                  May 12  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Hails Introduction of Comprehensive Immigration Reform Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          McCain Kennedy bill would reduce backlogs  improve national security       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D Conn    noting the contribution of immigrants to America   s history   added his name as an original cosponsor to the Secure America and Orderly Immigration Act of 2005  introduced Thursday by Senators John McCain  R Ariz   and Edward Kennedy  D Mass  The bill would help bring law abiding  hard working immigrants out of the shadows  while focusing scarce resources on apprehending illegal immigrants who actually threaten our security          Immigrants are an integral part of the history  heritage and culture of our nation  and it is imperative that we protect the rights of those here legally and those seeking to be here legally  even as we work to reform what everyone agrees is a broken immigration system             This bill seeks to bring honest  hard working immigrants out of the shadows  so that we can focus our enforcement resources on the criminals and terrorists who pose real threats to the country  By channeling foreign workers into new work visa programs  we can reduce the often dangerous flow of illegal immigration  alleviate social problems  provide more protections for American workers  enhance our security and improve our economy  The McCain Kennedy bill is our best hope for remedying the nation   s dysfunctional immigration system           The Secure America and Orderly Immigration Act of 2005 would        The McCain Kennedy comprehensive immigration reform legislation would do the following           Allow undocumented immigrants to apply for temporary status  then legal permanent residency after six years  provided they remain employed pay taxes  clear background and medical checks  and demonstrate knowledge of English and US civics         Increase legal immigration levels and reduce backlogs in the applications for available immigration visas to reunite legal immigrants with their spouses and children         Require the Department of Homeland Security to develop a National Strategy for Border Security that would focus on curbing illegal immigration and improving information sharing  communications integration and the use of technology       30
