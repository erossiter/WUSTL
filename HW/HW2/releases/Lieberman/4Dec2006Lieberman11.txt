Siobhan Oat Judge  202 224 1839                                                                                                Lieberman Urges Speedy House Vote on Historic DC Utah Voting Rights Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee Ranking Member Senator Joe Lieberman  D Conn    issued the following statement on the passage of a new Congressional map by Utah s state legislature that would create a fourth seat for Utah in the U S  House of Representatives  The map would be considered as part of a compromise bill introduced in the House that would grant Utah an additional seat  while providing the District of Columbia with a voting representative  Senator Lieberman has been a longtime supporter of D C  voting rights  and as Chairman of the Governmental Affairs Committee in 2002  he held the first Senate hearing in a decade on the issue  Senator Lieberman   s statement is below          In the spirit of fairness and compromise  the Utah state legislature has taken a significant step toward realizing the democratic principle of one person one vote for all the nation s citizens  By creating a fourth congressional district that reflects Utah s population growth  the state has also cleared the way for compromise legislation to give District of Columbia citizens voting representation in Congress   I hope my colleagues in the House move quickly to vote on the compromise plan  introduced by Delegate Norton and Representative Davis  This legislation is fair  and it is right  and Congress should not delay in providing the residents of the District of Columbia their Constitutional right to voting representation and the residents of Utah their appropriate representation            30
