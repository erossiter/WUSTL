                                                                                  February 1  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Responds to the President   s Innovation Initiatives                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in response to President Bush   s technology and education initiatives as described in last night   s State of the Union Address            To begin addressing our long term economic competitiveness we have to do a better job of promoting innovation in American industry  increasing research that will lead to economic benefits  and pushing our education efforts  particularly in the sciences  math and engineering  I welcome that the President has taken interest   He will finally be joining many of us in Congress who have a plan of action to keep America   s economy competitive through increased research  incentives for education in the sciences  and boosting our high tech manufacturing sector   I hope the President will begin to show the same leadership in other critical areas of education such as meeting our commitment to fully fund No Child Left Behind or education funding for the disadvantaged       30
