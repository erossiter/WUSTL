                                                                                  April 27  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Oil Company Profits                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator urges Bush Administration  GOP Congress to develop serious energy plan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to news that several major oil companies are reporting substantial increases in profits           While consumers in Connecticut and throughout the nation are feeling the sharp pinch of this latest spike in gas prices  our nation   s oil companies continue to turn larger profits than ever before   We cannot continue to stand by while big oil fattens its bottom line on the backs of hard working Americans   The Bush Administration and its Republican controlled Congress must put an end to five long years of inaction and protect consumers and our nation   s economy from the strain of outrageous gas prices   And  they can start by developing an innovative energy policy for the 21st century that finally abandons drilling in favor of investing in alternative fuel sources that will break America   s addiction to oil           30
