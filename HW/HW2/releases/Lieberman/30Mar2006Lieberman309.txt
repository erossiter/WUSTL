Catherine Ribeiro                                                                                                Lieberman Encourages Passage of Immigration Reform Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Senator joins Colleagues in calling for immigration reform                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   an original co sponsor of the McCain Kennedy immigration reform legislation joined Senators Arlen Specter  R PA   Patrick Leahy  D VT   Edward Kennedy  D MA   John McCain  R AZ   Dianne Feinstein  D CA   Lindsey Graham  R SC   Mel Martinez  R FL   Barack Obama  D IL   and Ken Salazar  D CO  today in discussing the current Senate floor debate on immigration reform  The bipartisan Judiciary Committee bill that is being deliberated includes strong enforcement and border security provisions  creates legal channels for foreign workers with a new temporary work visa  and brings hard working undocumented immigrants out of the shadows by providing an 11 year pathway to earned citizenship           Are we going to speak to people   s hopes and best ideals for America or are we going to play to people   s fears     Senator Lieberman asked     The immigrants who are coming to America today are coming for exactly the same reason that my grandparents came  for freedom  opportunity and a better life for their families  And it is a source of strength and a wonderful kind of market test of the continuing vitality of America that they want to come here   I applaud the work of Chairman Specter and Ranking Member Leahy the members of the Judiciary Committee on producing the legislation introduced today  and I hope we can improve the bill further as it is considered by the full Senate           30
