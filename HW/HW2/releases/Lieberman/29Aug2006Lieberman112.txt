                                                                                  August 29  2006 Contact                                                                                               Catherine McKenna Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on Census Bureau Report                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator pledges to fight for Connecticut   s citizens in need                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in response to news that despite the Bush Administration   s so called    strong economy     the number of Connecticut families living in poverty and lacking health insurance has risen over the past five years         I am dismayed by the news that more people in Connecticut are living under the poverty level and that more people in Connecticut are uninsured  The failed policies of the Bush Administration are to blame for our citizens  working harder and receiving less  I will continue to fight for the people of Connecticut by voting against tax cuts for the wealthy  and voting for an expansion in healthcare coverage to the 46 6 million uninsured Americans and for funding programs that help bring Americans out of poverty           30
