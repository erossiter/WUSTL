Leslie Phillips  202 224 0384                                                                                                Lieberman Vows    Stringent Scrutiny    for OIRA Nominee                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON     Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee Ranking Member Joe Lieberman  D Conn   Tuesday issued the following statement in response to the White House announcement that it would nominate Susan E  Dudley to be Administrator of the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs at the Office of Management and Budget                       As Director of the Regulatory Studies Program at the conservative Mercatus Center at George Mason University  Ms  Dudley is known for questioning the economic justification for certain environmental  safety  and health protections  including ones that have proven important to safeguarding the well being of many Americans                      OIRA reviews major rules proposed by federal agencies and assesses information on risk  cost  benefits  and alternatives before the regulations can go forward   The agency  therefore  is a gatekeeper of the federal role in protecting the health  safety  and environment of the American people                      That protective role  especially when applied to the environment or the health and safety of consumers and workers  is worthy of a vigorous defense  and I intend to give this nomination my most stringent scrutiny
