                                                                                                                                           Lieberman Lauds Assistance for Connecticut Small Businesses                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator introduces budget amendment to provide funding for small business programs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT  today co sponsored an amendment to the FY 2008 federal budget introduced by Senator John Kerry  D MA  that increases funding for several small business programs  including micro loans  small business development centers  veterans  small business programs  and women s business development centers   The amendment passed the Senate by unanimous consent         There are no greater representatives of the American Dream than this country s thousands of small business owners   the principal drivers of new jobs in this country   Lieberman said   This funding will help ensure that small businesses receive the federal support they need to continue their role as the reliable engines of innovation and job creation         Among the Connecticut programs aided by this amendment is the Women s Business Development Center in Stamford  which provides entrepreneurial education and professional development services to low  and moderate income women in Fairfield and New Haven Counties         Through his work on the Small Business Committee  Lieberman has supported a range of programs and services offered by the Small Business Administration  including Small Business Development Centers  the Small Business Investment Corporation  the 504 loan program  and Women s Small Business Centers        Lieberman has also sponsored legislation on a range of issues designed to meet the changing needs of small businesses  including capital and credit assistance  business development programs  contracting assistance  and loan relief for military reservists  He has also advanced legislation to provide equity venture capital and technical assistance to small businesses in low  and moderate income areas        30
