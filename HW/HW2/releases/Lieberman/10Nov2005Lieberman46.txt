                                                                                  November 10  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Lieberman Statement on Removal of ANWR from House Budget                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in response to the removal from the House version of the Budget Reconciliation Act of a provision to open the Arctic National wildlife Refuge  ANWR  to drilling           I am pleased that the House has concluded that it makes no sense to permanently damage the pristine Arctic Refuge for oil that won t lower prices for consumers or give us true energy security   I urge my colleagues on the Conference Committee to make sure this destructive provision stays out of the final budget                  30
