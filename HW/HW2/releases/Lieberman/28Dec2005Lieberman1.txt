                                                                                  December 28  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Lauds extension of USA Patriot Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senator urges bi partisan compromise on reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement after Senate leaders agreed last night to a six month extension of the USA Patriot Act           I am pleased that the Republican leadership did not follow through on its threat to allow the Patriot Act to expire  unless we passed the version approved by the conference committee   It was my hope that the conferees would follow the Senate   s example and craft a bipartisan bill that was able to garner unanimous support  but unfortunately they agreed on legislation that would not have provided enough protections for individual liberties    I believe that the government must have the tools it needs to fight the war on terror  but I am convinced that adequate protections for civil liberties will not dull the sharpness of those tools   I am hopeful that we will be able to use this extra time to reach a more bipartisan compromise           30
