                                                                                  July 26  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Reaction to Iraqi Prime Minister   s Remarks to Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today in reaction to Iraqi Prime Minister Jawad al Maliki   s remarks today before a Joint Session of Congress           I was encouraged by Prime Minister Maliki   s stated commitment to achieving democracy  security and human rights in his country     Lieberman said     I hope that the Prime Minister takes away from his visit here that the US will continue to back him in his fight to build a stable  secure  democratic and independent Iraq  but that success in that quest will not depend on us  but on the Iraqis themselves and he must match his words today with decisive actions when he returns to Iraq              30
