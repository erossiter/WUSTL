                                                                                  October 20  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Submarine Sign Sabotage                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON  Today Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  offered the following statement in reaction to news the Groton submarine sign off Interstate 95 was vandalized           This irrational act by misguided vandals disrespects the entire community  More specifically  it disregards the sacrifice and dedication of active and former submarine sailors  their families  and our equally devoted workers at Electric Boat and the sub base   While they may vandalize this sign  they cannot tarnish the spirit which is the    Submarine Capital of the World     This sabotage simply and inappropriately defiles the legacy of a great community           30
