Marshall Wittmann  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman and Kyl Statement on Administration Announcement of Iran Sanctions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Senators Applaud Designation of IRGC Quds Force as Terrorist Organization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Joe Lieberman  ID Conn   and Jon Kyl  R Ariz   today issued the following statement in response to the announcement by Secretary of State Rice and Treasury Secretary Paulson of sanctions on the Iranian Revolutionary Guard Corps and Quds Force      We applaud the Bush administration s decision today to designate Iran s Revolutionary Guard Corps  IRGC  Quds Force as a terrorist organization  and the IRGC as a proliferator of weapons of mass destruction  The IRGC has long had American blood on its hands  most recently in supporting terrorist action against our troops in Iraq      The designation of three banks  the Bank Melli  Bank Mellat  and Bank Saderat  will take the Iranian regime s chief financiers out of the global economic system   This will make the terrorist regime s activities far more expensive and difficult      Over the preceding months  our military commanders on the ground in Iraq have been explicitly warning us about how the IRGC has been training  funding  arming  and in some cases even directing the extremist militias that are responsible for the murder of hundreds of our soldiers there      For years  the IRGC  and in particular its elite Quds Force  has been tied to terrorism across the Middle East and throughout the world   At the same time  the IRGC is also a key actor in Iran s illicit efforts to develop weapons of mass destruction  which threaten America s critical national interests       The Administration s designation of the IRGC will ratchet up the economic pressure on Iran to stop its terrorist and proliferation activities   It will force hundreds of foreign companies to stop doing business with the IRGC  which controls a shadow empire of billions of dollars of ill gotten wealth      Far from taking us closer to war with Iran  as some have irresponsibly suggested  these kinds of targeted sanctions represent our best chance to influence Iran s action so as to be able to avoid military action   In fact  anyone who hopes to prevent military action against Iran should applaud the Bush administration s decision today      30
