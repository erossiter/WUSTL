                                                                                  February 14  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Supports Safeguards for Endangered Species against Global Warming                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   made the following statement today in response to the U S  Fish and Wildlife Service announcement that it will open the formal process to list polar bears as officially    threatened    due to the unprecedented meltdown of their sea ice habitat caused by global warming          The National Fish and Wildlife Service not only is moving to protect the polar bear from a critical threat to its survival  but the Service is also illustrating by its actions what has been settled science for quite a while     global warming is an urgent problem that  is already  affecting  our entire planet    It is imperative that Service move its process expeditiously and that the Bush Administration and Congress move quickly to get to the root of the problem by supporting  a market based carbon cap system like the one set forth in the McCain Lieberman Climate Stewardship and Innovation Act          30
