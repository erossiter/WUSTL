                                                                                  October 3  2007 Contact                                                                                               Marshall Wittmann  202 224 4041                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on Senate Resolution Condemning Military Junta in Burma                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON  D C    The Senate on Monday passed a resolution  co sponsored by Senator Joe Lieberman  ID CT   in support of the Burmese dissidents and democrats under attack by the military junta there       In the past weeks  the world has watched in awe and admiration as hundreds of thousands Burmese people have risked their lives for the fundamental human freedoms that the junta has long denied them   said Senator Lieberman   These courageous individuals have shown fearlessness in the face of a regime built on fear    and the United States must stand in solidarity with them      The resolution also calls for the People s Republic of China    which is the Burmese regime s most important military  diplomatic  and economic ally    to exercise responsible leadership and use its leverage to stop the bloody and destabilizing crackdown by the junta       As China becomes a global power  so too it must assume global responsibilities   said Senator Lieberman   Beijing has unique power and authority to compel the junta to end its war on the Burmese people and enter into dialogue with the democratic opposition       30
