Catherine Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Fights to Ensure Middle Class Tax Relief                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator condemns Republican attempt to burden American people through back handed politics                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Today Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  cosponsored Senator Harry Reid   s  D NV  amendment to the Department of Defense Appropriations Bill that would force Republican leadership to tackle the issue of middle class tax relief rather than holding these benefits hostage to estate tax cuts for the wealthiest Americans   Among the issues addressed in the Reid amendment the Senator supports are deductions for higher education expenses  credits for high tech corporate research and development  and treating combat pay as earned income under the earned income credit            We cannot stand by as Republicans hold the financial concerns of American families and innovative American businesses hostage to partisan politics     said Senator Lieberman     To insist that this excessive give away should be connected to middle class tax relief is unconscionable   We must tackle these issues in Congress now  instead of sitting idly while the Republican leadership hijacks the Congressional agenda  That is why I am cosponsoring Democratic Leader Reid   s effort to short circuit the Republican ploy and find other legislation on which to attach these tax extenders            30
