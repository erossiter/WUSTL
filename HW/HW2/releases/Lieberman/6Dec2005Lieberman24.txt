                                                                                  December 6  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Electric Boat Job Cuts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today issued the following statement in reaction to Electric Boat   s announcement on Connecticut job cuts           This nation has a core national security capability in undersea warfare expertise that is centered in Connecticut   This expertise is focused around Submarine Base New London and the world   s premier designer and builder of submarines   Electric Boat   The BRAC Commission recognized this important capability and made a strong statement in support of it when they voted to overturn the Pentagon   s proposal to close Submarine Base New London   The Navy   s current plan to build one submarine per year  coupled with the future reductions in submarine maintenance  threatens the other key piece of this core national capability   The long term answer to this problem is to begin building two submarines per year and to accelerate the start of the design phase of this nation   s next submarine   I intend to do everything possible to protect jobs at EB   I will work to make sure that EB has a level playing field upon which to compete for all submarine maintenance and to make sure that developmental funds for better undersea warfare capability goes to Electric Boat to develop new capabilities for submarines   And  I will work to accelerate the production of new Virginia class submarines   The expertise represented by the workers at EB is too valuable to lose
