                                                                                                                                             Dodd  Lieberman Announce  400 000 Grant for the   Housing Authority of the City of Norwalk                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Grant award will provide Norwalk   s low income   residents improved access to computer technology                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   Senators Chris Dodd and Joe Lieberman today announced that Norwalk will receive a grant totaling  400 000 from the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development to increase access to computer technology and improve job opportunities for low income residents                       A high tech economy requires a high tech workforce  That   s why it   s critical that we provide computer training skills to people looking for employment so they have an opportunity to succeed in today   s marketplace  This grant will help low income residents in Norwalk obtain these critical skills as well as enable them to find employment by providing access to computer technology and training     said Dodd                       This money has the potential to help a lot of people across Connecticut   Lieberman said   These HUD programs represent some of the most important work we do at the federal level  providing funds to help struggling families gain computer skills and opportunities so they can stabilize and advance their lives and also help the communities where they reside                        The Ross Neighborhood Networks Grant for the Housing Authority of the City of Norwalk will be used to establish  expand or update community technology centers called Neighborhood Network centers  The centers can provide a wide range of services to help low income residents achieve long term economic self sufficiency  The funds may also be used to hire a project coordinator to manage and oversee center activities  purchase computer equipment  internet connection  provide computer training  provide job training  and literacy training             30
