                                                                                  August 17  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on NSA Wiretap Ruling                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Washington  D C      Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in response to U S  District Judge Anna Diggs Taylor   s ruling that Bush Administration   s warrantless wiretapping program is unconstitutional           I applaud the Michigan court s decision  which confirms what I have been saying since these NSA surveillance programs became public   while the ever present threat of terrorist attack requires the government to have an effective and intensive surveillance program  this type of domestic surveillance may not be conducted without court approval   That is what our laws and our Constitution demand   We distinguish ourselves from our enemies by following the rule of law even in time of war           30
