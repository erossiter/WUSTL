                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Criticizes Navy Plan to Re Locate Groton Subs to Pacific                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Cites strategic disadvantage of reducing SBNL Fleet                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman today released the following statement criticizing the Navy   s plan to shift submarines from the fleet at Submarine Base New London  SBNL  to the Pacific          I strongly disagree with the Navy   s decision to take submarines from New London rather than from Norfolk to beef up our Pacific submarine force   New London is not only the center of excellence for the world   s best submarine force but is also better located strategically to operate in the Atlantic and to send submarines rapidly to the Pacific by the polar route to augment our forces there   The Navy should take the fullest advantage of the New London submarine base and of the opportunity to reduce the large concentration of ships at Norfolk    I intend to work with my Senate colleagues to convince the Navy to change this decision and to take the fullest advantage of the unequalled qualities at New London          30
