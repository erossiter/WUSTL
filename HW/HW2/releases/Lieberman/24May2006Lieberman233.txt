                                                                                  May 24  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Commemorates One Year Anniversary   of House Stem Cell Legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON     In commemoration of the one year anniversary of House passed legislation that would reverse President Bush   s ban on federally funded embryonic stem cell research  Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  urged the Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist  R TN  to bring the legislation to the Senate floor for debate   Lieberman is a co sponsor of the Senate version of the legislation  S  471            Christopher Reeve  Nancy Reagan and countless others have begged and pleaded for this administration to give new hope and new life to the afflicted     said Lieberman     Embryonic stem cell research is still in its infancy  but these cells represent potential miracles for millions of Americans who suffer horrible diseases that have no cure  The Senate should follow suit  and honor the requests of those who have fought valiantly to keep this issue at the forefront by acting immediately to pass our matching legislation and challenge the President to sign it into law           30
