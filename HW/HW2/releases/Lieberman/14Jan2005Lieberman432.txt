                                                                                  January 14  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Welcomes Administration Plans to Extend Tsunami Warning System                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Says his bill will take next step by expanding further and enlisting other nations       WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to the announcement that the Bush Administration plans to expand the current Pacific Tsunami Warning System and build a new tsunami warning system for the Atlantic coast of the United States and the Caribbean           I am encouraged that the Bush Administration is focusing on this problem and I look forward to working with them on it   The announcement today of an expanded warning system to the Atlantic and the Caribbean is a big step in the right direction   The legislation I plan to introduce when Congress reconvenes will take the next step by expanding it further and enlisting the cooperation of other nations to ensure we have the best defense possible against future natural disasters of this magnitude          On January 6  Lieberman unveiled legislation to create a global tsunami early warning system   His bill would authorize procurement and deployment of 40 50 high tech ocean based sensors to detect disturbances that could cause tsunamis in the Atlantic  Pacific and Indian Oceans  and associated seas   It would also provide for coordination among cooperating coastal nations and international organizations to develop tsunami early warning mechanisms and forecasting systems   Lieberman   s bill authorizes  30 million for purchasing the sensors and creating the global system         30
