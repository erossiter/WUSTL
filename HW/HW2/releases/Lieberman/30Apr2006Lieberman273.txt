                                                                                  April 30  2006 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Commends Rally to Stop Genocide in Darfur                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement to commend the participants in the Save Darfur Coalition   s Rally to Stop Genocide  which took place today in Washington  D C           I commend the Save Darfur Coalition and all of the participants of today   s crucial rally to stop the genocide in Darfur   By making your voices heard  you are showing the world your resolve to protect the people of Darfur and to live up to G d   s challenge to be our brothers    keepers            Stopping the genocide in Darfur is a crucial test for the United States and other like minded nations   If we fail to do more to prevent the death and suffering of millions of vulnerable and isolated people  we are turning our backs on the fundamental humanitarian values of our societies  and forgetting the lessons of our history             I support funding Darfur peacekeeping to the maximum possible extent and the Darfur Peace and Accountability Act  and I have urged and continue to urge President Bush and Secretary Rice to accelerate their efforts to stop the Genocide in Darfur   I have also called for stepped up NATO role in bolstering the existing African Union peacekeeping force            I thank the Save Darfur Coalition and today   s rally participants for their commitment and I wish I could be there today to join them   With their continued effort  we can stabilize the security situation in this troubled region and bring urgency to the vital task of saving and protecting innocent people           30
