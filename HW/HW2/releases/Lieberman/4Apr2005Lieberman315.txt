                                                                                  April 4  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on the Death of Pope John Paul II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to the death of Pope John Paul II             I join all Americans in mourning the death today of Pope John Paul II and in expressing my condolences to Roman Catholics in Connecticut and around the world   This Pope was a courageous champion of religious freedom and human rights   His role in breaking Eastern Europe from the oppressive grip of Communism can not be underestimated   During his numerous travels  the Pontiff personally touched the lives of millions of Catholics and non Catholics alike   He built bridges to non Catholics that had never existed before and transformed forever the Church s perception of Jews from a separated people to brothers and sisters in faith   In an age of science and materialism and genocide  he was the world s most consistent advocate of spiritual and humanitarian values   Pope John Paul II leaves behind a lasting legacy of peace  faith and leadership and will be truly missed by millions throughout the world               30
