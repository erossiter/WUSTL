Catherine Ribeiro  202 224 0975                                                                                                Lieberman Statement on LEGO Layoffs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  made the following statement today regarding LEGO   s announcement of impending layoffs in Connecticut due to a decision to outsource            I am dismayed over LEGO Group Denmark   s decision to outsource 300 Connecticut manufacturing jobs to Mexico     said Lieberman      I think the LEGO Group owes its employees and the people of Connecticut a serious explanation of why these good manufacturing jobs are being taken away and why there are not alternative approaches available that could address its needs without having to simply give up and outsource these jobs   Perhaps the company could sit down with local  state and federal officials to explore innovative alternatives   If LEGO Group does go through with this outsourcing  I offer the full resources of my office to LEGO Group   s Connecticut branch so they may apply for assistance from the U S  Department of Labor for Trade Adjustment Assistance on behalf of the workers who will be losing their jobs   My office also stands ready to assist LEGO Group employees regarding retraining options  unemployment insurance and other available resources   I would also urge LEGO to retrain as many of their workers as possible for other jobs within the company   s Connecticut operations           30
