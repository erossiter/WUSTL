                                                                                                                                             Senator Hails HELP Committee Passage of Bill to Renew Community Health Centers  And Meets with CT Cancer Survivors                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON     Today  Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   a co sponsor of the The Health Centers Renewal Act of 2006  S 3771  made the following statement in response to news that the measure had passed the U S  Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee         We depend upon our health system to provide preventive and reparative medical care to all Americans     said Lieberman     Connecticut s community health centers are a vital safety net to serve the un  and underinsured  They provide high quality and cost effective healthcare services to those shut out of our current healthcare system  They provide care to one out of every eight uninsured Americans and 25  of all Americans living in poverty  I cosponsored this bill because it is critically important that we continue to fund programs such as this that provide critical healthcare services to Connecticut   s low income and uninsured families          Earlier today  Senator Lieberman met with representatives from the Connecticut Cancer Society to discuss their legislative concerns   Meeting participants were engaged in a national gathering of cancer advocates on the National Mall to protest funding cuts for cancer research  The event was hosted by the American Cancer Society Cancer Action  Lieberman spoke with the advocates about his proposal to bring new drugs to people afflicted with cancer and other diseases faster   the       Heightened awareness and greater access to treatment are working in the battle against cancer  but we can and must do more     Lieberman said      Continued funding of research projects to prevent and fight cancer  like those that could be explored thru my American Center for Cures Act  must be a top national priority  Congress must continue to adequately fund life saving research and treatment to ease
