                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Urges Reduction in America   s Dependence on Foreign Oil                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Joins Senate colleagues  Fortune 100 CEO   s to discuss economic  national security risks of failing to break oil addiction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON   Senator Joe Lieberman today joined Senate colleagues and business leaders to discuss the economic and national security risks of failing to break America   s addiction to foreign oil   Lieberman spoke at the Business Leaders Roundtable on Oil Dependence about the importance of breaking America   s oil addiction        That CEOs and other high level executives from some of our nation s biggest retail and transportation companies    many of whom are literally on the frontlines of the turbulent global oil market    have all come to Washington to meet on the emerging crisis of oil dependence serves as an urgent call to action for Congress to enact legislation that will provide real solutions to the problem     said Lieberman      My colleagues and I have no choice but to redouble our efforts to pass the Vehicle and Fuel Choices for American Security Act to put our economy on an oil reducing diet and push to market the alternative fuels and advanced technologies that will end our oil addiction        The discussion was co Chaired by Frederick W  Smith  Chairman  President and CEO of FedEx Corp    David A  Brandon  Chairman and CEO of Domino   s Pizza   and Robert D  Hormats  Vice Chairman of Goldman Sachs International   General P X  Kelley  the 28th Commandant of the U S  Marine Corps and a former member of the Joint Chiefs of Staff  served as moderator   Senators Evan Bayh  D IN   Sam Brownback  R KS   Richard G  Lugar  R IN   Ben Nelson  D NE   Barack Obama  D IL   and Jeff Sessions  R AL  also attended       Last year  Lieberman led a bi partisan group of 10 Senators who introduced the Vehicle and Fuel Choices for American Security Act  which would mandate 10 million barrels in oil savings in 25 years and would also require the mass production of cars that can burn any combination of gasoline and alternative fuels or  alternatively  cars powered by efficient hybrid engines        30
