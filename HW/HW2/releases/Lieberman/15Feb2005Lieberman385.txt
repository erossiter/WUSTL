                                                                                  February 15  2005 Contact                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Lieberman Statement on Republican Jewish Coalition Ad Critical of DNC Party Chairman Howard Dean                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON     Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT  today made the following statement in reaction to an advertisement by the Republican Jewish Coalition quoting him and criticizing Democratic National Committee Chairman Howard Dean          This Republican ad prints my critical reaction to something Howard Dean said about the Middle East in the Presidential campaign  but fails to complete the story   Governor Dean responded to me and others by calling his comments a mistake   I   m confident that  as party chairman  Howard will uphold the Democratic Party   s historic commitment to support and strengthen our democratic ally Israel            30
