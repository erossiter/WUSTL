SEN. ISAKSON STATEMENT ON PASSING OF CORETTA SCOTT KING
  Sen. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) today released the following statement concerning the passing of Coretta Scott King:
   "I extend my condolences to the children and extended family of Coretta Scott King. King was a great partner in the civil rights movement and a tireless worker in preserving the legacy of her husband, the Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr."
