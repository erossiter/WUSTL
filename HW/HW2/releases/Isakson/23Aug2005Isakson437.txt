SENS. ISAKSON, CHAMBLISS ANNOUNCE RURAL DEVELOPMENT FUNDS FOR LUMPKIN
  Sens. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) and Saxby Chambliss (R-Ga.) today announced that the City of Lumpkin will receive a $20,000 grant for repairs to City Hall from the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development funds. 
   The funds will be used to purchase materials to install a new roof on City Hall and repair interior damage caused by water leaks. 
   "This USDA grant will allow the city of Lumpkin to make vital repairs to City Hall," said Isakson. "I am very pleased the city is receiving this kind of federal support."
   "I am pleased that Lumpkin has received funding to support the local government which in-turn can continue to serve the community," Chambliss said. "I appreciate the work of USDA Rural Development for their assistance in securing this funding." 
   The City is contributing the labor needed to make the repairs.
