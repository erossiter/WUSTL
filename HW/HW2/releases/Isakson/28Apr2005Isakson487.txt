SENS. CHAMBLISS, ISAKSON ANNOUNCE USDA RURAL DEVELOPMENT FUNDING FOR MITCHELL COUNTY
  Sens. Saxby Chambliss, R-Ga., and Johnny Isakson, R-Ga., today announced that the Mitchell Electric Membership Corporation in Camilla, Georgiawill receive $740,000 in funds from the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Economic Development Loan Program. 
   The Rural Development funds will be used to expand the alligator production capacity of Glass Enterprises, Inc. The project will result in the creation of 17 additional full time jobs. 
   "I am pleased that Mitchell County has received funding to support increasing economic opportunity for the community," Chambliss said. "I appreciate the work of USDA Rural Development for their assistance in securing this funding for Mitchell EMC." 
   "The USDA Rural Development funds are so important to many areas across Georgia, and Mitchell County will benefit greatly from this latest grant," said Isakson.
