SEN. ISAKSON PRAISES CONFIRMATION OF ROBERT GATES AS SECRETARY OF DEFENSE
Sen. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) today praised the Senate's confirmation of Robert Gates to be Secretary of Defense. The Senate confirmed Gates by a vote of 95 to 2.
 "Robert Gates' background in intelligence, his long history of service to this country and his experience in leadership positions make him an outstanding choice to lead the Pentagon and bring fresh ideas to the Department of Defense. I am extremely glad the Senate approved his nomination in such a quick and efficient manner."
