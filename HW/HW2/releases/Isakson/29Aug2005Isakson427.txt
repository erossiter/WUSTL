SENS. ISAKSON, CHAMBLISS ANNOUNCE FUNDING FOR TURNER COUNTY SCHOOLS
  Sens. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) and Saxby Chambliss (R-Ga.) today announced that the Turner County Board of Education will receive a $955,400 grant to implement a comprehensive community-wide plan designed to create safe and drug-free schools and to promote social skills and healthy childhood development. 
   The U.S. Department of Education will distribute the funds through the Safe Schools/Healthy Students grant program. 
   "This grant is a great triumph for Turner County's youth," said Isakson. "Alcohol, drug and violence prevention programs should be implemented in all schools and I am pleased to aid our schools in becoming better places of learning in all areas."
   "I am pleased to see the expansion of the Safe Schools/ Healthy Students program into Turner County's schools," Chambliss said. "These funds will help strengthen the education the youths receive and ensure that many more get access to the vital services they need to live safe, healthy, productive lives."
