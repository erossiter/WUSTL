SENS. ISAKSON, CHAMBLISS ANNOUNCE HUD FUNDING FOR BRUNSWICK
  Sens. Johnny Isakson (R-GA) and Saxby Chambliss (R-GA) today announced that Brunswick will receive a $509,368 grant from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development to help meet housing needs and expand economic opportunities for low-income families.  
   The Community Development Block Grant will aid development of viable urban communities by providing decent housing and a suitable living environment, and by expanding economic opportunities, principally for low- and moderate-income persons.
   "I am pleased to announce these important funds for Brunswick ," said Isakson. "These valuable grants will help the community aid those who need it most." 
   "These important programs fund a wide range of community initiatives, such as housing and support services, public improvements and economic development projects in Brunswick," said Chambliss. "I will continue to work with my colleagues in support of the programs that mean so much to so many Georgia communities."
