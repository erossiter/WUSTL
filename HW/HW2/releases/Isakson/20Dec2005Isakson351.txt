SEN. ISAKSON CONGRATULATES ECHOLS COUNTY FOR SWEETEST CARROTS IN GEORGIA
  Sen. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) today sent a letter to Echols County Commission Chairman Robert Davis congratulating Echols County for producing the sweetest carrots in Georgia.  
 Below is a text of the letter:
   "It is with great pleasure that I send my heartfelt congratulations to the citizens of Echols County as the area is recognized as the county that produces the sweetest carrots in the state. This is quite an honor, and I am sure you, and the other folks in Echols County, are very proud that Georgia General Assembly passed a resolution commending the county for providing the citizens of our state with a delicious vegetable, which is not only good for you but tastes great, too."
