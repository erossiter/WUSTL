SEN. ISAKSON MOURNS DEATHS OF HUNTER ARMY AIRFIELD SOLDIERS
  Sen. Johnny Isakson (R-Ga.) issued the following statement on the helicopter crash Thursday morning in Colquitt County, which killed four Hunter Army Airfield soldiers who were on a training mission:
   "Any time even one of our men or women in the Armed Forces is killed on the job, it causes a great deal of pain. But to lose four of our brave soldiers at once is very, very difficult. Dianne and I extend our deepest condolences to the families, friends and fellow soldiers of those who died. We will keep them in our thoughts and prayers."
