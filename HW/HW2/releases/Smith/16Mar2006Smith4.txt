     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Phone             Smith Votes to Fortify Health Care and Education Programs Allows Congress to Spend an Additional  7 Billion on Schools and Clinics         March 16th  2006   WASHINGTON  DC    Today  U S  Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  co sponsored an amendment to the FY 2007 Budget resolution that allows Congress to spend an additional  7 billion on critical health care and education programs    Supporting schools and programs that care for our most vulnerable remains a top priority  Smith said  Increasing funding for health care and education programs ensures community health centers stay open and Oregon schools are properly funded    The amendment was authored by Senator Arlen Specter  R PA  and it increases the amount of funding available in the Labor HHS and Education appropriations bill by  7 billion  Congress uses the legislation annually to fund agencies like the National Institute of Health  the Center for Disease Control and the Department of Education    The U S  Senate adopted the amendment by a vote of 73 to 27                                                                                                         current Press Release
