     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Smith Wants Inquiry into Hanford Leak          August 3rd  2007   Washington  D C     U S  Senator Gordon Smith is calling on the U S  Department of Energy to take immediate actions to address the leak of highly radioactive material at the Hanford Nuclear Reservation  Smith called on the Department to clean up the leak and to launch a thorough investigation as to why it was not discovered earlier  He also called for increased monitoring throughout the Hanford Nuclear site to ensure that radioactive waste is not entering the groundwater or the leaching into the Columbia River    This is a risk we can not tolerate  said Smith  Proper safeguards should be in place to protect public health and safety  My concern now is for the site workers and the Columbia River  The workers  the residents  and the water all need to be protected    On Thursday  Senator Smith wrote to Energy Secretary Samuel Bodman requesting the clean up  the investigation and additional monitoring  This situation highlights the ongoing problems of the clean up of this site  wrote Smith  The Energy Department needs a complete investigation into the circumstances surrounding this leak and why it was not detected earlier   Senator Smith has also asked that he be provided with regular updates from the Department about this situation                                                                                   current Press Release
