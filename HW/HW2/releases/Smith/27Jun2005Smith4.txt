     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                    Smith Statement on the Supreme Court  s Ruling On MGM vs  Grokster          June 27th  2005   WASHINGTON  DC   Senator Gordon Smith  R OR   co chair of the Senate International Anti Piracy Caucus  issued the following statement on the U S  Supreme Court  s unanimous decision which sends MGM vs  Grokster back to the lower courts   The ruling potentially makes software companies that market file sharing technologies used to illegally replicate intellectual property  such as music or film  vulnerable to civil law suits        Today  s ruling is a victory for American artists and innovators   So much of American business is based on creators   ability to market their ingenuity and ideas to the world   The ruling establishes a critical precedent that promotes legitimate economic growth in some of America  s most competitive industries                                                                                                               current Press Release
