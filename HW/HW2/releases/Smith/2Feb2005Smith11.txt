     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                    Smith Will Chair Key Commerce Sub Committee on Trade  Tourism and Economic Development           February 2nd  2005   Washington  DC   Today  Senator Gordon Smith  R OR  was selected to be the Chairman of the Senate Commerce Subcommittee on Trade  Tourism and Economic Development which plays a vital role in furthering the competitiveness of American trade  the tourism industry and general domestic business issues        Trade and tourism make up the backbone of Oregon  s economy     said Smith     This subcommittee is an exciting assignment  and its business will be to aggressively pursue the policies that will help grow Oregon  s economy       The Subcommittee has a wide jurisdiction of influence that includes oversight of the Federal Trade Commission  FTC   The Tourism Promotion Board and the Department of Commerce trade issues  A main Smith priority includes the prompt passage of the FTC Reauthorization bill    Smith formerly chaired the Senate Commerce Subcommittee on Competition  Foreign Commerce and Infrastructure                                                                                                                                                                                                current Press Release
