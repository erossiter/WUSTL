     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Smith Seeks Answers to Coos Bay Rail Closure                December 20th  2007   Washington  D C    During a hearing of the U S  Senate Commerce Committee  Oregon Senator Gordon H  Smith questioned Surface Transportation Board  STB  member Francis Mulvey on what was being done to prevent future rail line closures  like the Coos Bay rail closure which impacted the jobs of Oregonians working in the states otherwise forest industry    Somebody has failed on the job  somebody needs to answer to the people who have lost their jobs  said Smith  This is an issue that adversely impacts rural people and rural places   Senator Smith questioned Commissioner Mulvey about what he sees as the STBs role in ensuring that lines are maintained to prevent service interruption  In September  Central Oregon Pacific Railroad closed the rail line connecting Coquille and Eugene because of unsafe tunnel conditions resulting from lack of maintenance  The closure is having a massive economic impact on the region  closing lumber yards and costing local companies thousands of dollars in losses and jeopardizing thousands of jobs    The Senate Commerce Committee recently approved a provision authored by Senator Smith instructing the Federal Railroad Administration to improve its tunnel inspection and review methods so they can better prevent future line closure                                                          current Press Release
