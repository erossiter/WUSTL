     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Smith Lauds Passage of Farm Bill Bill includes funding for farmers  ranchers and fighting hunger              December 14th  2007   Washington  D C    The first major bill in five years aimed at supporting Oregonians who make their living off the land passed the U S  Senate today  The Farm Bill includes several provisions championed by Oregon s Senator Gordon Smith to aid farmers and ranchers  encourage innovation in agriculture  and boost funding for nutrition projects   Like any valuable crop  we gave this bill time and space to develop  Farmers  ranchers and those who need the food they produce will benefit  Smith said   Senator Smith pushed for the addition of a number of provisions to benefit Oregon including conservation funding  enhanced financial security programs for farmers  and funding to encourage innovation and specialization in farming  The bill also encourages renewable energy and increases nutrition funding by  5 billion dollars   The 2002 Farm Bill expired on September 30th  The Farm Bill passed the Senate 79 14  The House and the Senate must now reconcile a final version of the bill before it goes to the President for his signature                                                                                              current Press Release
