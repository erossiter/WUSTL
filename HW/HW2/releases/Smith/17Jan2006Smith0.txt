     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Phone             Smith Statement on NOAA Decision Not to Add Oregon Coastal Coho Salmon to the Threatened Species List          January 17th  2006   WASHINGTON  DC    Today  the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  s National Marine Fisheries Service announced that it has concluded that listing Coho Salmon along the Oregon Coast as   threatened    is not warranted under the Endangered Species Act   In response  Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  issued the following statement      This decision comes after nearly a decade of constructive state and federal cooperation toward the recovery of threatened salmon in Oregon   I applaud the Bush Administration  s decision to recognize the progress of the Oregon Salmon Plan and our state  s commitment to restore salmon habitat and populations   I remain committed to working with Oregon  s congressional delegation to secure continued funding for the State of Oregon through the Pacific Coastal Salmon Recovery Fund                                                             current Press Release
