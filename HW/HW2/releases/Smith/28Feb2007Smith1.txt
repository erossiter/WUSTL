     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Kimberly CollinsPhone  202 224 5364           Smith Works to Prevent Dramatic Decline in the Labor Workforce          February 28th  2007   Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  questioned witnesses this morning in the Senate Special Committee on Aging on ways to recruit and retain older individuals to help sustain the American workforce  The hearing  The Aging Workforce  What Does it Mean for Businesses and the Economy  studied ways to combat expected dips in labor force growth rates in the coming years  Senator Smith serves as the Committees Ranking Member    The hearing included testimony from Dr  Preston Pulliams  the District President of Portland Community College  Dr  Pulliams discussed how Portland Community College is supporting older learners who want to remain in the workforce  He also described how the college is partnering with businesses to assist them in retaining older workers   In response to the urging of Senators Smith and Kohl  the U S  Department of Labor formed the Task Force on the Aging of the American Workforce  This inter agency group will develop legislative and regulatory proposals addressing the issues raised by the aging of the national labor force  The task force met for the first time in May of 2006 and plans to finalize its finding and recommendations this Spring                                                                          current Press Release
