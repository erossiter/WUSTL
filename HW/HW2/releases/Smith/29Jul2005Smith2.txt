     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                    Smith Statement on Nomination of Bill McCormick to be Ambassador to New Zealand          July 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     Today  Senator Gordon Smith  R OR  issued the following statement in response to the nomination of Oregon  s Bill McCormick to be U S  ambassador to New Zealand     Bill McCormick is a wonderful choice   He is a national business leader  a civic activist and an exemplary patron to many of Oregon  s great causes   Bill has a combination of broad intellect and strong character and will proudly represent the United States abroad        I know that Bill will continue the good work of fellow Oregonian Butch Swindells and help find common ground on the important issues that still divide America and our friends in New Zealand   Our nations have a strong relationship and I look forward to working with Bill to help bring about a resolution to these matters                                                                                      current Press Release
