     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                                    Smith Statement on President  s Provisions for Columbia River Channel Deepening          February 7th  2005   Washington  DC   Senator Gordon Smith  R OR  issued the following statement on the  15 million included for the Columbia River Channel Deepening Project in President George Bush  s proposed budget for fiscal year 2006       Deepening the Columbia River Channel is integral to keeping Oregon products competitive in the world  s marketplace   The President is maintaining his commitment to the tens of thousands of Oregonians who depend on Columbia River commerce  and I  m going to do everything I can to ensure Congress fulfills that commitment   Funding the channel deepening project is a long process  but  15 million is a very promising beginning                                                                                                                                                   current Press Release
