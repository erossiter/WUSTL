     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Senator Smith Comments on County Payments Amendment                March 29th  2007   Washington  DC   Today  U S  Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  released the following statement on the U S  Senate agreeing to extend the county payments safety net      Congress has forged a bipartisan solution that would help keep schools open  roads safe and county workers on the job  However  if payments to Oregon counties are being ramped down  it is only fair that timber harvests be ramped up  Self sustainability for rural counties means managing our natural resources in a way that works better for our economy and for our environment  That should be the aim of any long term solution                                                           current Press Release
