     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Camas Valley Fire to Replace 1959 Truck Federal Grant to Replace Fire Truck after 50 Years of Service              November 1st  2007   Washington  D C    Oregon Senators Gordon Smith and Ron Wyden announced today a new state of the art fire truck is on the way to serve Camas Valley  The new fire truck will be purchased with a  261 250 federal grant to replace a historic 1959 model  currently in service    Old fire trucks carry high maintenance costs and risks of engine failure  said Senator Smith  Responding fast to an emergency requires a dependable truck  Lives are at stake  we need to provide first responders with the best equipment possible  Furnishing Camas Valley with a new truck will make a difference during an emergency   This much needed equipment upgrade will make Oregon families safer  said Senator Wyden  With this new fire truck  our first responders will be better equipped to react at the first report of an emergency   The Camas Valley Fire Dept  has not had a new truck in nearly fifty years  The innovative features and enhancements of the new truck will help Camas Valleys 15 volunteer firefighters serve their community better  The federal grant comes from the U S  Department of Homeland Security  The Camas Valley Rural Fire Dept  serves a six hundred square mile area west of Roseburg in Douglas and Coos Counties                                                                                                                      current Press Release
