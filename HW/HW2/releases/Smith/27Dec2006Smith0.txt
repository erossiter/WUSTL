     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Phone             Senator Smith Saddened By Loss of Former President Ford          December 27th  2006   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  released the following statement today regarding the death of former President Gerald Ford   I am saddened by the news of Gerald Ford s passing  He was a friend to me and I will miss him  He was a faithful family man and he leaves to them a noble name and legacy  He was an indispensable leader who  at a crucial hour in our history  restored stability  decency and integrity to the American Presidency  He was able to do this because he so embodied these qualities himself and he was worthy to answer history s call  America has lost one of her great sons                                                          current Press Release
