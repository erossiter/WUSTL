     United States Senator   Gordon Smith                                                                                                                                                                                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Smith Wants Tunnel Safety to Receive Meticulous Review Instructs Feds Top Rail Safety Authority to Improve Safety Accounting         September 27th  2007   Washington  D C    In the wake of a short line rail closure between Eugene and Coquille  Oregon Senator Gordon Smith is instructing the federal governments top railroad safety authority to improve the methods its uses to review rail tunnel safety  You shut down a rail line you shut down jobs  Smith said  Maintaining safety requires meticulous attention and that is exactly what the public expects  There should be a new system established to review tunnel safety  This afternoon  the Senate Commerce Committee approved Senator Smiths amendment to the Railroad Safety Enhancement Act  S  1889   Through the amendment  Senator Smith is instructing the Federal Railroad Administration to improve its tunnel inspection and review methods  The Senator offered the amendment to prevent future incidents like the safety conditions that forced Georgia Pacific stop operations at their Coos Bay sawmill  Congress is expected to continue working on the legislation this fall  and Senator Smith will continue shepherding the bill until it becomes law                                                                                  current Press Release
