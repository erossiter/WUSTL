                         Contact  Gordon Smith Press OfficePhone  202 224 3753           Senate Approves Gas Price Gouging Ban and Increase in Fuel Efficiency Standards Bill also authorizes Wave Energy Research centers              June 26th  2007   Washington  DC   Several provisions proposed by U S  Senator Gordon H  Smith  R OR  were included in an energy bill recently approved by the U S  Senate  Included were the Senators proposals to prevent gas price gouging in the aftermath of a disaster  implement higher energy and water efficiency standards for major home appliances and increase fuel efficiency standards for vehicles   Oregonians are fed up with sky high gas prices  Smith said  We can spur innovation that will lead to lower energy costs  but we have a long way to go   Senator Smith worked with Senator Maria Cantwell  D WA  to establish tough new federal protections to guard against profiteering and market manipulation by oil and gas companies in the aftermath of a disaster  The bill also includes new auto fuel economy standards that will allow Americans to drive farther on a gallon of gas  The new standard will increase fuel economy by 10 miles per gallon by 2020  the first mandated increase since 1975   We need diverse supplies to reduce our dependence on foreign oil  said Smith  I think we can meet tomorrows energy demands with clean  renewable energy sources   The bill also directs the Secretary of Energy to establish research centers on college campuses for the study of wave energy  Oregon State University is already a world leader in wave energy research and would be a prime location for a center  In Oregon  it would be possible to generate and deliver over two hundred megawatts of wave energy using existing transmission lines on the coast
