SEN. DOMENICI: U.S. AIR FORCE ANNOUNCES CONTRACT AWARD TO LAGUNA CONSTRUCTION OF ALBUQUERQUE
  Sen. Pete Domenici announced today that the U.S. Air Force has awarded Laguna Construction Company, Inc., in Albuquerque with a contract to perform construction projects. 
   The U.S. Air Force awarded the contract to assist in designing, constructing and remodeling new and existing facilities and infrastructures.
   "I am pleased to see that New Mexico businesses are able to serve our military in this capacity. Laguna Construction will serve the Air Force for at least five years, which is good for our local economy and for Air Force facilities in Albuquerque," Domenici said.
   The contracts were awarded to 20 businesses throughout the country and will have a basic period of five years plus three additional one-year options with an initial funding limit totaling $6 billion nationally.
