SEN. DOMENICI CONDOLES DEATH OF SIKH RELIGIOUS LEADER BHAJAN
  Sen. Pete Domenici issued the following statement regarding the death of Yogi Bhajan, the chief religious authority of Sikhism outside of India. 
   "Yogi Bhajan was a man admired around the world. He lived a remarkable life and spread a powerful, peaceful message of equality, physical well-being and spirituality. He was a positive force in New Mexico, active in making it better place through economic development and community works. 
   "On a personal level, I will miss Yogi Bhajan and offer my sincere condolences to his wife Inderjit Kaur, and his three children."
