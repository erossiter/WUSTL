SEN. DOMENICI'S 'ACCESS' RADIO SHOW--QUESTIONS, ANSWERS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO JOURNALISTS
  Sen. Pete Domenici recorded his "ACCESS" radio news conference Friday, April 13, 2007.  
   PARTICIPANTS: 
   * Tim Keithly, KRUI Ruidoso 
   * Mike Jackson, KSVP Artesia 
   * Kevin Robbins, KSMX Clovis 
   * Gillian Sutton, KRSN Los Alamos 
   TOPICS: 
   * Don Imus 
   * FLETC-Artesia 
   * Valles Caldera Trust 
   * Carlsbad Water Projects 
   * Cannon Air Force Base 
   REVIEW: 
   01:20 - Domenici addresses the aftermath of the situation that led to the cancellation of the Don Imus radio program. 
   03:41 - Domenici discusses his FY2008 funding requests for the Federal Law Enforcement Training Center at Artesia. 
   06:50 - Domenici assesses whether the unique management structure for the Valles Caldera National Trust and Preserve is working. 
   10:05 - Domenici talks about his intent to run for re-election. 
   11:38 - Domenici reviews his funding requests for Carlsbad water projects. 
   14:50 - Domenici confirms Air Force Special Operations Command to take over Cannon Air Force Base and funding needed to accommodate this mission.
