SEN. DOMENICI ISSUES STATEMENT ON ELECTION REFORM
  In response to Gov. Bill Richardson's endorsement of bipartisan, comprehensive election reform, Sen. Pete Domenici issued the following statement: 
   "I have already spoken to Governor Richardson about real, comprehensive election reform. I am pleased that he is also committed to improving the election process and I look forward to working with him on this matter. In the 109th Congress, I intend to introduce legislation that will expand the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to ensure that elections are fairly conducted and that ballots are counted in an efficient, accurate manner."
