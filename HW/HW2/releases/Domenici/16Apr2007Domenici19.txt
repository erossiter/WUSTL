SEN. DOMENICI ISSUES STATEMENT ON VIRGINIA TECH KILLINGS
  Sen. Pete Domenici today issued the following statement regarding the shootings at Virginia Tech that ended with the murder or wounding of dozens of people: 
   "I am shocked and saddened by the horrendous mass murder at Virginia Tech. My thoughts and prayers go out to the victims and their families. Such terrible crimes touch us all and this awful tragedy must be thoroughly examined so that we can learn from it."
