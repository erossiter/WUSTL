SEN. DOMENICI'S 'ACCESS' RADIO SHOW-QUESTIONS, ANSWERS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO JOURNALISTS
  Sen. Pete Domenici recorded his "ACCESS" radio news conference Monday, April 23, 2007. 
   PARTICIPANTS:
   * Steve Isaacs, KNMX Las Vegas
   * Mike Jackson, KSVP Artesia/KEND Roswell
   * Bill Dupuy, KSFR Santa Fe
   * Renee Blake, KUNM Albuquerque
   TOPICS:
   * National Guard Youth ChalleNGe Visit
   * Senator Harry Reid and the "War is Lost"
   * Why War in Iraq
   * New Mexico National Guard Discrimination Charges
   * Violence and the Mentally Ill
   * Southeast New Mexico Regional Air Service
   REVIEW:
   00:00 - Domenici discusses his upcoming tour of the National Guard Youth ChalleNGe program in Roswell.
   02:19 - Domenici discusses a funding bill for American combat missions in Iraq and Afghanistan, as well as Senate Majority Leader Harry Reid's assertion that the war in Iraq is "lost."
   05:10 - Domenici says not passing an emergency spending bill for combat mission is not an option and that Congress should get the President a bill as soon as possible.
   06:30 - Domenici recalls that the United States and allies went to war after it was determined that the Saddam Hussein regime was a threat to regional and global stability. He acknowledges that some facts leading to the war were eventually proven wrong, but maintains the goal of establishing a free Iraq remains worthy.
   08:02 - Domenici says he is very interested in the investigation into whether New Mexico National Guardsmen were discriminated against as military officials tried to determine whether the New Mexicans were involved in gangs.
   09:31 - Domenici discusses mental health care in the wake of the massacre at Virginia Tech, and his intent to draft legislation to improve care for those with mental illness. The legislation would focus on a national need for community mental health care facilities.
   12:21 - Domenici says it will take time to carefully develop a bill, move it through Congress, and implement it. He says he and Senator Ted Kennedy will work together on this legislation.
   15:09 - Domenici suggests that Roswell, Artesia, Carlsbad, and Chaves and Eddy counties have embarked on a "terrific" plan to bring regional air service from Dallas to Roswell. He looks forward to being briefed on the plan to determine what federal actions might be needed to move the plan forward.
