SEN. DOMENICI ISSUES STATEMENT ON ELECTION OF POPE BENEDICT XVI
  Sen. Pete Domenici issued the following statement regarding the election of Cardinal Joseph Ratzinger of Germany to serve as Pope Benedict XVI. 
   "Today is a very meaningful day for millions of people around the world. Pope Benedict XVI faces a challenging task-filling the shoes of Pope John Paul the great and continuing his many good works. I believe the choice of Pope Benedict XVI brings a certain level of continuity to all the advances made by John Paul II."
