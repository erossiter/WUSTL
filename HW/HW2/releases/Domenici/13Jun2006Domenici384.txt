SEN. DOMENICI ANNOUNCES ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT ADMINISTRATION GRANT FOR SAN JUAN COLLEGE BUSINESS INCUBATOR DEVELOPMENT
  Sen. Pete Domenici announced that the Department of Commerce has announced that San Juan College in Farmington has been awarded $175,000 in federal Economic Development Administration (EDA) funds. 
   The EDA investment funds will be used to support the final stage of construction of a business incubator center at San Juan College.
   "The economic investment of the San Juan College will greatly increase the standard of education and economy for the Farmington community. The EDA funds will allow the college to build the needed infrastructure to increase economic productivity while continuing to build on its solid academic and career training programs for the region," Domenici said.
   The construction overall will cost $400,000 and will create 160 new jobs as well as generate $3.5 million in private investment.
   Domenici serves on the Senate Commerce, Justice and Science Appropriations Subcommittee which oversees funding for EDA projects.
