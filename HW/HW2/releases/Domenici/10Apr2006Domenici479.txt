SENATE ENERGY COMMITTEE SCHEDULES FIELD HEARING IN NEW MEXICO
  Senate Energy Committee Schedules Field Hearing in N.M.: 
   Who: Senate Energy & Natural Resources Committee 
   When: 10 a.m., Wednesday, April 19 
   Where: Sal?n Ortega at the National Hispanic Cultural Center of New Mexico, 1701 4th Street SW, Albuquerque, N.M.
