SEN. DOMENICI CONGRATULATES KEYS FOR BUREAU OF RECLAMATION SERVICE
  In response to news that the Bureau of Reclamation Commissioner John Keys has resigned, Sen. Pete Domenici issued the following statement: 
   "John Keys should be commended for his years of service to the Bureau of Reclamation. He has been a smart and competent civil servant. He will be leaving the Bureau in better shape than when he became commissioner.
   "John and I had a productive working relationship on issue of importance to New Mexicans. He worked hard on our collaborative program to rebuild the habitat around the Middle Rio Grande. When the Santa Fe area was struggling from drought, Commissioner Keys intervened to work with me to drill wells to create a water supply.
   "I applaud John Keys for his years of work, and wish him well in his future endeavors."
