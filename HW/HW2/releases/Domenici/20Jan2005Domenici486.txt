SEN. DOMENICI: BUSH HAS SET 'BOLD, NOBLE, HUMANE' GOALS FOR SECOND TERM
  Sen. Pete Domenici today issued the following statement in reference to the inauguration of President George W. Bush, and the reelected president's inaugural address to the nation:  
   "President Bush delivered to Americans, and the people of the world, an inspirational and beautiful message about who we are and where he wants to lead this country. The President set forth the bold, noble and humane goal of expanding freedom, not only for our own good but for that of the world. He provided us with an overarching rationale for a lot of the goals he has set for his presidency and for the nation. I was extremely pleased and impressed with the President."
