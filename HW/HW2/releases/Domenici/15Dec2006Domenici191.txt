SEN. DOMENICI STATEMENT ON INVASION OF DAUGHTER'S MARYLAND HOME
  Sen. Pete Domenici today issued the following statement regarding a home invasion that took place at his daughter's home in Silver Spring, Md., this morning: 
   "Nancy and I are relieved and thankful that no one was hurt during this frightening incident at my daughter's home this morning. I am grateful to the quick action by the Montgomery County Police to secure the house and ensure my grandchildren's safety. I also appreciate the assistance given to us by the U.S. Capitol Police. We look forward to spending time with my daughter, her husband, and our grandchildren this weekend."
