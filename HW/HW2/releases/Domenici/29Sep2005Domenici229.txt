SEN. DOMENICI VOTES TO CONFIRM ROBERTS AS CHIEF JUSTICE OF U.S. SUPREME COURT
  Sen. Pete Domenici today issued the following statement regarding the confirmation of Judge John Roberts to be Chief Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court.  
   Roberts was confirmed on a 78-22 vote by the Senate Thursday, and will be sworn in as Chief Justice at 3 p.m. Domenici voted in favor of the nomination. 
   "I believe that John Roberts will make an outstanding Chief Justice, and I am very pleased that so many of my colleagues came to the same conclusion that I did. Today's vote was a strong, bipartisan show of confidence for a man who is undoubtedly one of the most qualified nominees to ever come before the Senate. I look forward to watching him at work at the Supreme Court over the coming years."
