SEN. DOMENICI DISCUSSES GASOLINE PRICES, ENERGY POLICIES, HURRICANE KATRINA RECOVERY ON C-SPAN SHOW
  Sen. Pete Domenici is the featured guest discussing energy polices, escalating gasoline prices and Hurricane Katrina recovery efforts on a C-SPAN public affairs program that will be broadcast Sunday, Sept. 11. 
   Domenici, chairman of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee, this week conducted a hearing on gasoline supplies, prices and potential gouging. This week, the veteran lawmaker also indicated that Congress should reconsider tougher vehicle fuel standards and other fuel conservation policies, in addition to incentives to produce more domestic energy sources.
