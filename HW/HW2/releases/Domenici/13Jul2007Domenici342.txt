SEN. DOMENICI: RATON MUNICIPAL AIRPORTS WINS FED FUNDS TO EXTEND RUNWAY
  U.S. Senator Pete Domenici announced today that the Department of Transportation (DOT) has awarded $469,679 to improve New Mexico airport facilities, including the Raton Municipal/Crews Field.  
   DOT awarded $189,679 for the design project to extend the runway at Raton Municipal/Crews Field. 
   "Appropriate airport facilities are helpful in improving the economic outlook of communities," said Domenici. "I am happy to report that DOT has awarded these funds to Raton to supplement its current airport facilities. I look forward to hearing of the city's progress as it extends its runway." 
   Funds were administered by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). 
   The Clovis Municipal airport also received $280,000 for survey and runway extension purposes. 
   Domenici is a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee, which helps make federal funding allocations for nationwide, including those carried out by the Department of Transportation.
