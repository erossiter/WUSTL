SEN. DOMENICI NAMES SANTA FE NATIVE AS HIS DEPUTY PRESS SECRETARY
  Sen. Pete Domenici announced today that he has named Santa Fe High School graduate Courtney Sanders as his Deputy Press Secretary. 
   Sanders is a 1998 graduate of Santa Fe High School and holds a bachelors degree in English from the University of Virginia.
   "I am pleased to welcome Courtney to my Washington D.C. office. I know that her background and experience will help her excel in this position," said Domenici. "With her assistance, I look forward to better serving the people of New Mexico."
   Prior to joining Domenici's staff, Sanders worked as a communications associate at the White House Writers Group. Her past communication experience also includes the government, non-profit, and campaign arenas.
