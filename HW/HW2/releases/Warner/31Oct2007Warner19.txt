SEN. WARNER ISSUES STATEMENT ON MEDICAL UPDATE
  On October 16, 2007, a minor surgical procedure was performed to repair a small aperture on Senator John Warner's femoral artery in his anterior thigh.
   Today, the Senator returned to Inova Fairfax Hospital for a scheduled, follow-up appointment. During the visit, doctors determined that Senator Warner has a low-grade infection at the surgical site. He has been admitted to Inova Fairfax Hospital where he is undergoing observation and such treatment as may be required. 
   Doctors expect the Senator to fully recover and return as early as next week to his regular work schedule in the United States Senate.
