SEN. WARNER ANNOUNCES HOMELAND SECURITY AWARD FOR NORTHERN VIRGINIA COMPANY
  Today, Sen. John W. Warner, R-Va., announced that the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) has chosen a Northern Virginia company to receive an award for a project aimed at strengthening the defense of our homeland.  
   General Dynamics of Arlington, Va. will receive $469,123 for a 6-month prototype project aimed at strengthening our homeland defense in the area of chemical, radiological and biological technologies. 
   These awards are appropriated annually by Congress to DHS's Advanced Research Projects Agency. 
   In announcing the grant, Senator Warner said, "Protecting our homeland remains one of our highest national priorities. I am pleased that these Virginia companies are doing their part to keep our country safe."
