SEN. WARNER: MEDICAL UPDATE
  In response to press inquiries, Senator Warner issued the following statement:
   At the direction of the Capitol Physician, Senator Warner was hospitalized for two days, during which time he responded positively to new medications and rhythm treatments for atrial fibrillation.
   Last evening, his heart returned to a normal rhythm.
   He was released from Inova Fairfax Hospital earlier today, returned directly to the office and resumed previously scheduled engagements.
