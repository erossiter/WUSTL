       Comment Of Sen  Leahy On The NFL s Decision To Allow Local Broadcast Of   The Patriots Giants Game On Saturday                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT               Comment Of Sen      Patrick Leahy  D Vt       Chairman  Senate Judiciary Committee     On The NFLs Decision To Allow Local Broadcast    Of The Patriots Giants Game On Saturday       December 26  2007All week in     Vermont  people have been stopping me in the stores and restaurants  on     the ski slopes  and at the gas station with the same question  Will we     get the game Saturday night  I am delighted I can tell my fellow     Vermonters that they will see the game and that the Commissioner and the     NFL agree with me that there is an historic nature to the game this     weekend  As a Vermonter and a New Englander  I am extremely grateful
