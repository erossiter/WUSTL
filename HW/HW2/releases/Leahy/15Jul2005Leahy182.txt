       Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt   On Chief Justice Rehnquist s   Statement That He Is Not Retiring                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT         Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt       On Chief Justice Rehnquist s Statement     That He Is Not Retiring     July 15  2005 Following are the      comments of Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt    ranking member  Senate      Judiciary Committee  on Chief Justice Rehnquist s statement Thursday      night that he does not contemplate retiring as long as his health      permits him to continue to serve         I welcome the news that Chief Justice Rehnquist will continue to      lead the Court  and I commend him for putting an end to the guessing      games in Washington       He has served the Court and the nation ably and honorably  and he      has earned the right for his future retirement to be his own      decision  on his own terms
