       Vessel Hull Designers                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT               Senate Passes Leahy Cornyn Kohl Whitehouse Bill    To Protect Vessel Hull Designers WASHINGTON  Friday  Oct  5   The U S  Senate     Thursday night unanimously passed bipartisan legislation that will     protect the work and innovation of ship designers across the country  The Vessel Hull Design Protection Act Amendments of     2007  S 1640  helps to close loopholes often exploited to infringe on     the intellectual property rights of vessel hull designers  The bill     amends the Vessel Hull Design Protection Act of 1998 to clarify the     difference between the hull and the deck of a ship  Court cases     have revealed loopholes in the protection the original act provided to     ship designers  The bipartisan legislation was introduced and     shepherded through the Senate by Judiciary Committee Chairman Patrick     Leahy  D Vt    and was cosponsored by Sens  Herb Kohl  D Wisc    John     Cornyn   R Texas  and Sheldon Whitehouse  D R I    all Judiciary     Committee members  The measure unanimously cleared the Judiciary     Committee Thursday morning and passed the Senate Thursday night      Leahys comments on the legislation follow  Statement of     Chairman Patrick Leahy     Committee on the Judiciary     On Passage of S 1640  the Vessel Hull Design Protection Act Amendments     of 2007 October 4  2007  Today the Senate voted to pass S  1640  the Vessel     Hull Design Protection Act Amendments of 2007  after the Judiciary     Committee voted unanimously to send it to the floor  This is a small     but important piece of legislation  and I thank my cosponsors    Senator     Cornyn  Senator Kohl  and Senator Whitehouse    for all their hard     work  Last year  this bill was passed by the Judiciary Committee and by     the full Senate  but unfortunately the House held it hostage to an     unrelated bill at the end of the session  I dont want that to happen     again this year  In 1998  Congress passed the Vessel Hull Design     Protection Act to recognize the significant time  effort  and innovation     that figure into ship design  Recent courtroom experience has made it     clear that in order to be effective  this law needs to be clarified and     refined  Our bill does exactly this  and no more  by clarifying the     definition of hull and deck  This ensures that the intellectual     property rights of vessel hull designers will be protected   I look forward to this bill becoming law
