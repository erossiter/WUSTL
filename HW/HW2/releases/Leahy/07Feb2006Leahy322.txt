       Reaction Of Senator Patrick Leahy On The Senate s Vote To Invoke Cloture   On The Motion To Proceed To The Asbestos Bill                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT           Reaction Of Senator Patrick Leahy       Ranking Member  Judiciary Committee      And Co Sponsor Of The Fairness In Asbestos Injury Resolution Act      On Senates Vote To Invoke Cloture     On The Motion To Proceed To The Asbestos Bill     February 7  2006    On Tuesday      evening  the Senate moved forward on a bipartisan bill that would      provide much needed relief to victims of asbestos exposure  The      Senate voted overwhelmingly to invoke cloture on a motion to proceed      to consideration of S  852  The Fairness in Asbestos Injury      Resolution Act   Senator      Leahy  the ranking Democratic member of the Judiciary Committee  is      a co sponsor of the bill with Sen  Arlen Specter  the chairman of      the panel  Leahy issued the following comment in reaction to the      Senates vote    We have marshaled solid support in      clearing this crucial first step  but we are acutely aware that many      more hurdles remain  Chairman Specter and our bipartisan coalition      will continue to press forward in this effort to fairly and      effectively help the victims  families and businesses that have been      ravaged by the asbestos crisis
