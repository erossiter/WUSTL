       Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy To The New Bush Administration Letter  Again Denying Senate Access To A Limited Number Of John Roberts s Work Papers  From The Office Of the Solicitor General                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK       LEAHY                 VERMONT             Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy      D Vt    Ranking Member  Senate Judiciary Committee     To The New Bush Administration Letter     Again Denying Senate Access     To A Limited Number Of John Robertss Work Papers     From The Office Of The Solicitor General     Wednesday  September 14  2005    It is      regrettable that the Bush Administration persists in keeping      this information from the Senate   These documents  from the period      of Judge Robertss most substantive work experience in the      Executive Branch  would help illuminate his views and earlier      decisions on a wide range of key issues that are of vital      importance to the American people and to the Senate   Stonewalling these documents in      fact heightens the obligation that Judge Roberts has to fully      answer questions at his confirmation hearing
