       Joint Statement Of Patrick Leahy And Arlen Specter On Postponement Of   Tuesday s Judiciary Committee DOJ Oversight Hearing Until Thursday                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT                   Joint Statement Of     Judiciary Committee Chairman Patrick Leahy  D Vt      And Ranking Member Arlen Specter  R Pa      On Postponement Of Tuesdays Judiciary Committee    DOJ Oversight Hearing Until Thursday     April 16  2007  We have consulted with the Attorney     General and all understand it is appropriate to postpone Attorney     General Gonzaless appearance before the Judiciary Committee until     Thursday  in light of the tragic events that occurred Monday at Virginia     Polytechnic Institute  Our thoughts and our prayers are with the     victims of this senseless and horrific tragedy  their families and the     Virginia Tech community
