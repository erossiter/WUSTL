       Comment Of Sen  Patrick Leahy On The Attorney General s Letter Regarding   July 24  2070 Testimony Before The Senate Judiciary Committee                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT                Comment Of Sen  Patrick Leahy       D Vt       Chairman  Senate Judiciary Committee      On The Attorney Generals Letter      Regarding July 24  2007 Testimony     Before The Senate Judiciary Committee     August 1  2007       Senate Judiciary Committee Chairman Patrick Leahy  D Vt   received      the attached letter from Attorney General Alberto Gonzales late      today regarding Gonzaless testimony before the Committee on July      24  2007  Below is Chairman Leahys comment on the        The Attorney      Generals legalistic explanation of his misleading testimony under      oath before the Senate Judiciary Committee last week is not what one      should expect from the top law enforcement officer of the United      States  It is time for full candor to enforce the law and promote      justice  rather than word parsing  The Attorney      General has until the end of this week to correct and supplement his      testimony  I hope he will take that opportunity to clarify the many      issues on which he appears not to have been forthcoming and to tell      the Senate Judiciary Committee and the American people the whole      truth
