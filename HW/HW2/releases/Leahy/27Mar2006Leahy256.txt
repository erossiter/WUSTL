       Comment Of Sen  Patrick Leahy On Judiciary Committee s Passage Of   Immigration Reform Legislation                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT                Comment Of Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt       Ranking Member  Judiciary Committee     On The Judiciary Committees Passage      Of Immigration Reform Legislation      March 27  2006   This was a pivotal day in which the      Senate took the first tentative steps toward immigration reform that      is comprehensive and balanced  instead of piecemeal and punitive       My immigrant grandparents would be proud  This bipartisan package      reflects real progress toward fixing our nations broken immigration      system  The hard work from senators on both sides of the aisle has      paid off  and the Senate now has a bill before it that provides a      realistic and reasonable system for immigration consistent with      American values without compromising our security  I urge the      Majority Leader to fulfill his promise of taking up a bipartisan      immigration reform bill
