       Comment Of Sen  Patrick Leahy On The Death Of Former President Gerald R    Ford                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT                   Comment Of Senator Patrick Leahy     On The Death Of Former President Gerald R  Ford     Wednesday  December 27  2006  Gerald Ford helped to heal a nation       His was the steady  calming hand on the tiller of a nation riven by      scandal and corruption and a divisive war   Gerald and Betty Ford led through      their examples of decency and civility  Their contributions to the      nation were even more a matter of who they were than what they did   Marcelle and I are among the millions      of grateful Americans who offer our prayers  and our thanks  to      Betty and to all of the Ford family
