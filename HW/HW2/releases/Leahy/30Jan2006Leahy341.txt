       Statement Of Senator Patrick Leahy On Cloture Vote On The Supreme Court   Nomination Of Samuel A  Alito  Jr                   U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT                Statement Of Senator Patrick Leahy      Ranking Member  Judiciary Committee      On Cloture Vote On The Supreme Court Nomination Of Samuel A  Alito       Jr   January      30  2006       At a time when this President is seizing unprecedented power  the      Supreme Court more than ever needs justices who will serve to      protect the fundamental rights and freedoms of all Americans from      government intrusion  I believe strongly that Judge Alito will not      serve as that crucial check  and I will vote against limiting debate      on his nomination  which carries significant consequences for      Americans today and for generations to come
