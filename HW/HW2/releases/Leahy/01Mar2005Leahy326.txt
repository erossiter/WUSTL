       Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy To The U S  Supreme Court s Decision   Striking Down Use Of The Death Penalty Against Juveniles                  U S  SENATOR PATRICK        LEAHY                  VERMONT     Reaction Of Sen  Patrick Leahy  D Vt          Ranking Member  Senate Judiciary Committee        To The U S  Supreme Courts Decision       Striking Down Use Of The Death Penalty Against Juveniles       Tuesday  March 1  2005  The Courts decision finally brings the United States in line with all        other civilized democratic Western nations  It reflects a growing national        consensus about the use of capital punishment  and it also serves as a        sharp rebuke to this Administrations Justice Department  which has sought        to keep our society in the Dark Ages
