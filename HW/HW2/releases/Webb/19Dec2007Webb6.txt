   Senator Jim Webb   Virginia                                                                                        Press Releases                   December 19  2007 SENATOR WEBB ON AMT FIX TO PROTECT MIDDLE CLASS FAMILIES FROM HIGHER TAXES                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DCSenator Webb issued the following  statement on the passage of Alternative Minimum Tax Relief in both chambers of  Congress  I am  pleased that today the House passed Senate legislation that will provide  tax relief for millions of working families affected  by the alternative minimum tax  The AMT was originally intended to ensure  the wealthiest Americans paid a minimum amount of tax  Because the tax was not  indexed for inflation  it now threatens 650 000 Virginians with  higher taxes in the 2007 tax year  This legislation will provide a  one year AMT fix to give needed relief to these working families  While it is  disappointing that this bill only provides a one year AMT fix without an offset   it was essential to move this legislation forward  so that middle class families  are not penalized by this outdated tax
