   Senator Jim Webb   Virginia                                                                                        Press Releases                   June 25  2007 Webb Applauds Announcement of North Korean Nuclear Freeze                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC The following is a statement issued by Senator Jim Webb in response to the announcement that  North  Korea will freeze its nuclear  facility   I would like to congratulate the Administration on the  announcement that North  Korea will fulfill the February 13th six party  agreement to freeze its nuclear programs  As with the situations that once  existed in China and  Vietnam  the best way forward  when dealing with isolated regimes is to add realistic economic and diplomatic  agendas to Americas military posture  The  ultimate objective of this process should be not only the dismantlement of  North  Koreas nuclear program and the abandonment of  nuclear weapons  but also a more moderate and open North Korean regime that  might pave the way for truly peaceful relations on the Korean  peninsula
