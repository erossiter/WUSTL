   Senator Jim Webb   Virginia                                                                                        Press Releases                   March 23  2007 Bipartisan Webb Amendment Reserving Funds for Veterans Education Passes in Senate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Senator Warner s Last Minute Push Helps Secure Unanimous Vote                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Washington  DC   Senator Jim Webb of Virginia applauded his Senate colleagues today for passing a budget amendment reserving federal funds for veterans  programs that focus on education and training   With help from colleague Senator Warner  the amendment passed unanimously in the Senate          In addition to Senator Warner  Senators Reid  Durbin  Mikulski  Kerry  Boxer  McCaskill and Wyden are co sponsors of the Webb amendment        Reserving funds is an important step toward the subsequent passage of Senator Webb s G I  Bill  S 22   which he introduced on the first day of the legislative session  The  Post 9 11 Veterans Educational Assistance Act of 2007  is designed to provide veterans with a level of educational benefits similar to those provided to service members at the end of World War II          The bill provides support to veterans transitioning from active duty to civilian life  Senator Webb believes that the demands placed on men and women in our armed forces have increased significantly in the post 9 11 era         I am very pleased that the Senate passed a budget that makes educational opportunities and training a priority for our service members   said Webb   This bill is a tangible expression of the value that our nation places on the service and sacrifices of our men and women in uniform
