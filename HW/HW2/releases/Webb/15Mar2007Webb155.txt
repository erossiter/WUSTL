   Senator Jim Webb   Virginia                                                                                        Press Releases                   March 15  2007 Webb Seeks Greater Funding for Community Block Grants to Help Revitalize Virginia Communities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    President s FY 08 Budget Cuts 20  of Funding for Community Development Block Grants                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Washington  D C    Senator Webb  D VA  and a bipartisan coalition of Senators this week sent a letter to the Senate Budget Committee requesting  4 1 billion for Community Development Block Grants  CDGB  in the fiscal year 2008 budget  President Bush s   FY 08 proposed budget cuts the CDGB program by 20         Virginia received  62 million in federal grants last year to help revitalize neighborhoods  expand affordable housing and economic opportunities  and improve community facilities and services   Over the past five years  President Bush has cut the funding for development grants by  1 4 billion  which a recent CDBG Coalition survey found to be  devastating  to the millions of Americans that benefit from them        We need to fulfill our commitment to support local communities by fully funding this important grant program   said Senator Webb   Community block grants are essential to Virginia s local governments with respect to their ability to create new jobs and improve the quality of life for our citizens        A copy of the letter to the Senate Budget Committee follows
