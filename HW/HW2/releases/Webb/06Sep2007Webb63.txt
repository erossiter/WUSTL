   Senator Jim Webb   Virginia                                                                                        Press Releases                   September 6  2007 WEBB SEEKS ASSISTANCE FOR SURRY COUNTY FARMERS AFFECTED BY DROUGHT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington  D C    U S  Senator Jim Webb this week contacted Secretary of Agriculture   Mike Johanns  urging him to designate Surry County as a primary agricultural disaster  area  The disaster designation would enable farmers and livestock producers  affected by the recent drought conditions to qualify for federal  assistance   Farmers and livestock producers in Surry County have been hit hard by the recent  drought and need federal assistance to maintain their livelihood  said Senator  Jim Webb    I believe the current situation warrants this federal  assistance that these drought affected communities desperately need  It is  paramount that the U S  Secretary of Agriculture expedites the disaster  declarations required to help our local farming communities during this  difficult time  continued Webb   Under the federal disaster designation  farmers in  Surry County who lose 30  of  their farm income would be eligible to be considered for up to  500 000 in  low interest loans from the U S  Farm Service Agency for losses sustained during  2007  The Agency will individually consider each application based on the  extent of loss and other eligibility requirements    Senator Webb will continue to work diligently to secure  federal assistance for Virginia farmers in other localities who have  been affected by this years drought   Below are the letters sent to Secretary of Agriculture  Mike Johanns   September 4   2007  The Honorable Mike Johanns Secretary of Agriculture United States Department of  Agriculture Second Floor 14th  Street and Independence Avenue   SW Washington  D C  20250  Dear Secretary Johanns   I am writing to urge you to declare  Halifax  Greenville  Rappahannock  and Surry Counties  Virginia as a primary agricultural disaster  area as a result of the ongoing drought  I understand Governor Tim Kaine has  recently requested this declaration   The recent drought which has affected  Halifax  Greenville  Rappahannock  and Surry Counties has resulted in widespread damage  throughout the region  The damage done to our regions agricultural crops has  been severe  and our farmers are facing critical financial situations  Federal  disaster relief assistance is urgently needed    Thank you for your consideration of this  request  With kind personal regards and best wishes  I  remain  Sincerely    Jim Webb   United States
