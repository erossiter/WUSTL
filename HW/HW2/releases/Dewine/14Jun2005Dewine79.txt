SEN. DEWINE ISSUES STATEMENT REGARDING ANNOUNCEMENT OF MANUFACTURING CAUCUS
  As a member of the newly announced Manufacturing Caucus, Sen. Mike DeWine (R-OH) released the following statement: 
   "I look forward to working with my fellow members of the Manufacturing Caucus on many issues important to Ohio," said Senator DeWine. "I hope the members of this caucus will join me in fighting for lower energy costs, fairer trade with China, and a fully funded Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) program."
