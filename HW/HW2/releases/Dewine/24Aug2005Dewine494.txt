SEN. DEWINE COMMENTS ON LIMA TANK PLANT
  After today's vote by the Defense Base Closure and Realignment (BRAC) Commission, Sen. Mike DeWine(R-OH) released the following statement: 
   "Today's vote is great news for the residents of Allen County," said Senator DeWine. "This not only eliminates the chance for a downsizing at the Lima Tank Plant through the BRAC process, but it puts the tank plant in a position to grow in the future."
