SENS. DEWINE, VOINOVICH ANNOUNCE APPLICATION PROCESS FOR JUDICIARY RECOMMENDATIONS
  Sens. DeWine (R-OH) and Voinovich (R-OH) today announced they are accepting applications from persons interested in filling a U.S. Federal District Court judgeship opening in the Eastern Division of the Northern District of Ohio that will occur when Federal Judge Lesley Wells takes senior status on February 14, 2006.  
   Requests for applications should be directed to U.S. Senator Mike DeWine, Attention: Joshua Hahn at 37 West Broad Street, Suite 300, Columbus, Ohio 43215 or at FederalJudgeship@dewine.senate.gov. The deadline for applications is Friday, December 2, 2005. 
   The Eastern Division of the Northern District of Ohio is made up of the following counties: Ashland, Ashtabula, Carroll, Columbiana, Crawford, Cuyahoga, Geauga, Holmes, Lake, Lorain, Mahoning, Medina, Portage, Richland, Stark, Summit, Trumbull, Tuscarawas, and Wayne.
