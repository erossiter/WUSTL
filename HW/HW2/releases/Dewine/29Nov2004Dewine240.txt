SENS. DEWINE, VOINOVICH ANNOUNCE APPLICATION PROCESS FOR JUDICIARY RECOMMENDATIONS
  Sens. Mike DeWine (R-OH) and Sen. George Voinovich (R-OH) today announced they are accepting applications from attorneys and judges interested in filling a U.S. Federal District Court judgeship opening in the Western Division of the Southern District of Ohio that will occur when Federal Judge Walter H. Rice takes senior status on December 1, 2004.  
   Requests for applications should be directed to Senator DeWine at 37 West Broad Street, Suite 300, Columbus, Ohio 43215 or at josh_hahn@dewine.senate.gov, subject line: SW Judicial Vacancy. The deadline for applications is Friday, January 28, 2005. 
   The Western Division of the Southern District of Ohio is made up of the following counties: Adams, Brown, Butler, Clermont, Clinton, Hamilton, Highland, Lawrence, Scioto, Warren, Champaign, Clark, Darke, Greene, Miami, Montgomery, Preble, and Shelby.
