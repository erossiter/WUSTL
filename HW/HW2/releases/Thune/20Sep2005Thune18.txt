     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Announces  4 4 Million Rural Development Loan Approved for the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe          September 20th  2005   WASHINGTON D C    Senator John Thune announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  has approved a  4 400 000 loan for the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe  These Rural Development funds will be used to construct a nursing home     The USDA is acting to help provide resources for the construction of a nursing home that will provide quality care for the members of the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe in their senior years     Thune said                                                                                                                                                   SR 493
