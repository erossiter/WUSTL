     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune announces federal funds for South Dakota communities potentially at risk of BRAC results          June 28th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune today announced the U S  Department of Labor is granting  1 million to the South Dakota Department of Labor to help the State initiate early planning for workers who could be at risk as a result of the current Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  round   The federal grant will help the state of South Dakota ensure an effective response for workers who may lose their jobs as a result of a negative decision for Ellsworth Air Force Base in Rapid City     These federal grants will help provide a much needed solace if the BRAC closes Ellsworth     Thune said    We are doing everything possible to save Ellsworth  but we are also working with the Labor Department and Black Hills community to make sure the federal government is ready to assist communities potentially affected by base closures  In addition to the thousands of military jobs at Ellsworth  we are fighting to save hundreds of civilian jobs at stake in South Dakota                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
