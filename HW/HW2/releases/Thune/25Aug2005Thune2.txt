     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Aberdeen receives  450 000 transportation grant          August 25th  2005   Washington  D C    The U S  Department of Transportation has chosen Aberdeen for a  450 000 air transportation grant  Senator John Thune announced today  The grant will be used to increase the community  s air transportation capacity by replacing Northwest Airlink  s turboprop flights with larger regional jet aircraft     Improving flight service to Aberdeen will strengthen the economy and create new jobs     Thune said    This grant will allow local officials to upgrade airline service to Aberdeen  I am proud to work with the Aberdeen community and the Transportation Department to make this possible       The grant is part of this year  s round of Small Community Air Service Development Program  SCASDP  grants  According to the Transportation Department  this year was especially competitive  with more than 80 applications for 37 grants   SCASDP is funded by a FY2005 Congressional appropriation of  20 million to encourage commercial air service in some of America  s smaller communities                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
