     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Statement on Marriage Amendment                June 6th  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today issued the following statement in support of the Marriage Protection Amendment  which is being debated today on the Senate floor            Nothing is more central to the fabric of American society than the family  It is the glue that binds our communities  raises healthy children into successful adults  and preserves the values America was founded on   Yet today  the fabric of our society is under attack by anti family activists with a narrow agenda aimed at dissolving the definition of marriage that has existed since the birth of civilization      The Marriage Protection Amendment sends a strong message to activist judges throughout the country that it is not acceptable to legislate from the bench   Thune said   Without the Marriage Protection Amendment  there is little stopping activist judges from turning the traditional family structure into a set of rights that can be gained and lost based on special circumstances                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
