     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune statement on highway bill          April 22nd  2005   Washington  D C      I applaud the Senate Leadership for agreeing to schedule debate on the highway bill next week  With threats of a Senate shut down looming  we need to act now to pass long overdue transportation legislation  I hope the Senate will work together to quickly pass a bill that updates our nation  s transportation infrastructure  promotes ethanol and protects South Dakota  s historical share of federal funding        Senator John Thune  Background  Friday morning the Senate leadership filed a cloture motion signed by Senator John Thune and other senators on the motion to proceed to debate on the Transportation bill  This means that the Senate will begin opening debate on the transportation bill Monday afternoon  As a member of the Senate Environment and Public Works Committee  Senator Thune helped move this important legislation through the Committee in March  including  1 243 billion for South Dakota  s road needs over the next 5 years                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
