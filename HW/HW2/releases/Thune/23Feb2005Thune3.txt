     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune announces Homeland Security grant for Murdo          February 23rd  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune today announced over  40 000 in Homeland Security grant money for the Murdo Volunteer Fire Department in Murdo  South Dakota    The  43 313 grant was awarded through the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  s Operations and Firefighter Safety Program   The funds will go towards training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to fire station facilities     Federal assistance will help the Murdo Volunteer Fire Department improve the community  s public safety     said Thune  R SD    It is vital that we support South Dakota  s emergency responders and improve public safety in our communities      The 2004 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program is administered by the Department of Homeland Security  s Office for Domestic Preparedness   The program awards one year grants designed to improve the effectiveness of firefighting operations and improve fire prevention programs                                                                                                                                                   SR 493
