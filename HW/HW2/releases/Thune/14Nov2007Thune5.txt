     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Meets with Department of Agriculture Nominee Former Governor Edward Schafer          November 14th  2007   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune yesterday met with former North Dakota Governor Edward Schafer  whom President Bush recently nominated to serve as the Secretary of Agriculture  The meeting took place in Senator Thune s Washington office where the two discussed the 2007 Farm Bill  the importance of ethanol and other agriculture issues important to the Dakotas    I ve worked with Ed Schafer on several matters important to South Dakota and I believe he is a good man and is a great choice to serve as the next Secretary of Agriculture  My discussion with Governor Schafer centered on the importance of the biofuels industry in our two states  It will be a big advantage for South Dakota to have someone from the Northern Plains in that position   said Thune                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
