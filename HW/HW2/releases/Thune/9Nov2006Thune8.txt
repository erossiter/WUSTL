     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Thune  Honoring America s Finest          November 9th  2006   Washington  DC    On Veterans Day  we pay tribute to our nation s veterans  who symbolize the very best of America  honor  duty  and patriotism   We should remember the sacrifices made by these brave men and women every day  but this is a special day set aside just for them    Each of America s veterans has added a resounding drumbeat to freedom s march   This is a drumbeat that started with the Revolutionary War and can still be heard today in the War on Terror   South Dakota has a long tradition of military service  from the work of the Dakota Territory s early military men who helped to settle and govern our state to the valiant efforts of the soldiers of Charlie Battery in Baghdad     As we commemorate Veterans Day  let us try to live each day in honor of those who have fought and fallen for freedom s march   We will never forget their sacrifice  and we will always be thankful for their service                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
