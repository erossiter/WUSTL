     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune announces  360 000 federal grant to East River Electric Cooperative          June 14th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune today announced a  360 000 federal grant  awarded to the East River Electric Cooperative of South Dakota   This grant was given by the Rural Economic Development Plan on behalf of the U S  Department of Agriculture   The USDA Rural Economic Development grant program is part of the USDA  s ongoing mission to ensure that rural communities can compete in the global economy   These grant programs are aimed at providing a feasible option for organizational marketing and distributing of agriculture products     This grant will assist the East River Electric Cooperative  s ability to successfully compete in a growing market     Thune said    It is vital that these South Dakota rural cooperatives have the opportunity to strengthen their business  I am proud to work with these local communities to create new jobs                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
