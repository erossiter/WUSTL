     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune Announces USDA Grant to Improve Clay County Water Supply          January 31st  2005   Washington  D C    The U S  Department of Agriculture is awarding a grant and loan totaling nearly  290 000 to increase the water supply in Clay County  U S  Senator John Thune announced today   A  103 000 grant and a  184 000 loan from federal Rural Development funds will go to increase water supply in the southern portion of the Clay Rural Water Supply distribution area     This grant will improve water access for farmers and ranchers in Clay County  strengthening our economy and community     Thune said    I  m pleased the federal government is contributing to this important water effort      The grant and loan will help construct 14 miles of 8    pipeline  In addition  an interconnect with the City of Vermillion will be installed with a small plant booster station constructed on Coyote Street   The new grant and loan will assist cost overrun expenses of the Greenfield Supply Project  Previously  the USDA awarded the project a loan of  799 000 in FY 2005                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
