     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune Announces USDA loans for South Dakota communities          February 11th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune announced today over  1 5 million in loans to benefit two South Dakota communities   The USDA Office of Rural Development has approved a loan of  560 000 to be given to the City of Brandon   Through the Community Facility Direct Loan program  Rural Development funds will be used to aid in the construction of a new fire hall   Rural Development also approved a guaranteed loan of  1 095 000 to assist with the construction of a new fertilizer facility in the Delmont area   The funds are being lent to Agland Co op of Parkston  SD through the Business and Industry Guaranteed Loan program     This federal assistance is critical to constructing these important projects     Thune said    I  m proud to work with the Administration on behalf of people in Brandon  Delmont and throughout South Dakota                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
