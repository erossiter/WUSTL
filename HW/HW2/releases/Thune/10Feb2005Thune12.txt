     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune lauds Class Action Reform passage          February 10th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune today applauded the Senate  s passage of legislation that will begin to restore fairness to the judicial system     We have finally passed class action reform that will begin to restore fairness to the judicial system in this country     Thune said    Our tort system is broken  And without the necessary reforms beginning with class action lawsuits  we deny our citizens not only fair and efficient access to justice  but we allow this problem to pull our economy downward     This was a successful bipartisan effort and shows what can be accomplished when we work together  The Senate is delivering on the voters   expectations  I hope this vote is the first of many bipartisan accomplishments      This morning  Thune urged the Senate to pass class action reform during his first ever Senate floor speech      We have a full agenda ahead of us     Thune said in his speech    The American people have put their trust in us to make this nation even greater than it is today  And we cannot let them down      The Class Action Fairness Act of 2005 passed 72 26  Thune was a co sponsor                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
