Ranking Member of Senate Ag Subcommittee on Energy          February 13th  2007   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today issued the following statement regarding the announcement of his selection to serve as Ranking Member of the Senate Agriculture Committee s Subcommittee on Energy  Science and Technology  which will be tasked with a majority of activities relating to ethanol and renewable energy for the full committee     As Ranking Member of the Senate Ag Committee s Subcommittee on Energy  Science and Technology  I will have the unique opportunity to bring a South Dakota perspective to this lead role in discussions and decisions regarding the future of America s renewable energy industry and the rural producers who supply it   Thune said   South Dakotans know first hand the potential that alternative energy holds  and our state is leading the way in renewable energy production and research  I plan on drawing from the expertise  input and direction of South Dakota s farmers and alternative energy producers as I work on this subcommittee to further the exploration and use of home grown renewable fuels so Americans can become less dependent on foreign sources of energy    Senator Thune was also selected to serve on the Subcommittee on Production  Income Protection and Price Support  and the Subcommittee on Domestic and Foreign Marketing  Inspection  and Plant and Animal Health  One of the Senate Agriculture Committee s chief objectives this year will be writing the 2007 Farm Bill    Information on Senator Thune s Subcommittee Assignments    Subcommittee on
