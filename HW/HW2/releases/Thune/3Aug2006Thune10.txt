     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Announces  3 5M HUD Grant for Sisseton Wahpeton Sioux Tribe          August 3rd  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today announced that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development has awarded the Sisseton Wahpeton Sioux Tribe an Indian Housing Block Grant  IHBG  in the amount of  3 513 772     This important funding will increase the quality  safety  and availability of affordable housing for the families of the Sisseton Wahpeton Sioux Tribe   Thune said   Constructing new  dependable housing or helping existing renters or homeowners make repairs to their homes will boost the quality of life and safety throughout the Sisseton Wahpeton community    The IHBG funding is approved under the under the Native American Housing Assistance and Self Determination Act of 1996  These funds can be used for a variety of eligible affordable housing activities  which may include operating assistance  development of affordable housing that may include new construction  acquisition  modernization  or rehabilitation of rental or owner occupied housing  housing services  housing management for low income housing  and or crime prevention safety                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
