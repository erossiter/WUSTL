Invites South Dakotans to Apply to U S  Service Academies Applications are Due October 31  2007         September 21st  2007   Washington  D C    Senator Tim Johnson  D SD   Senator John Thune  R SD  and Congresswoman Stephanie Herseth Sandlin Sandlin  D SD  announced today that South Dakota young people interested in being nominated to one of the four U S  Service Academies for the Class of 2012 should apply by October 31  2007   South Dakota residents between the ages of 17 and 23 may apply to any of the four service academies if they meet eligibility requirements in leadership  physical aptitude  scholarship and character  The four service academies are the Military Academy at West Point  New York  the Naval Academy at Annapolis  Maryland  the Air Force Academy at Colorado Springs  Colorado  and the Merchant Marine Academy at Kings Point  New York    I am proud to see a strong level of interest from South Dakota s finest young men and women every year   said Johnson   So many of our young people choose to pursue a role in our nation s defense  and I expect this year will be the same  I am always proud to nominate students to America s service academies     The service academies provide excellent educational and leadership opportunities for a select number of young men and women each year  I look forward to the honor of nominating some of South Dakota s best and brightest to be considered for an appointment by the academies   Thune said   Herseth Sandlin said   The U S  Service Academies can offer invaluable experiences for a young person who not only wants to serve his or her country  but to grow and develop as an individual  I encourage any young person in South Dakota who is interested in attending a Service Academy to apply  and I look forward to recommending excellent candidates from South Dakota once again    Johnson  Thune  and Herseth Sandlin will each compile separate lists and make final recommendations to the academies on the most qualified candidates by January 31  2008  Applicants are encouraged to submit applications to all three Congressional offices in order to better their chances of receiving a recommendation  The academies will make offers of appointment next spring   Interested applicants should contact  Senator Johnson s office at  605  332 8896 or 800 537 0025  Senator Thune s office at  605  334 9596 or 866 850 3855  Congresswoman Herseth Sandlin s office at  605  367 8371 or 866 371 8747
