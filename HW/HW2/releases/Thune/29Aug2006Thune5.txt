     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Andi FoubergPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Announces HUD Economic Development Grants for the City of Aberdeen          August 29th  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today announced the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development has approved two Economic Development Initiative  EDI  Special Project Grants for  99 000 and  247 500 for the City of Aberdeen  as identified in the Conference Report on the Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary  Housing and Urban Development  and Related Agencies Appropriations Act of 2006    These grants will provide funding for some of Aberdeen s most important sectors   Thune said   Among other provisions  this funding will allow Aberdeen to make improvements to its roads and bridges  which will strengthen its economy  create quality jobs and increase the quality of life for Aberdeen residents                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
