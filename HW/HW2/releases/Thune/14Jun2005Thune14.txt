     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune announces  740 000 federal loan to assist financing of rural businesses          June 14th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune announced today that the U S  Department of Agriculture awarded a  740 000 loan to the Northeast South Dakota Economic Corporation  NESDEC  in Sisseton  South Dakota   The Rural Development funds will be used to assist the NESDEC in providing loans to clients who wish to start a business or expand an existing business      The NESDEC has been assisting individuals and businesses in Northeast South Dakota for over 27 years     said Thune    This loan will support their efforts to provide assistance to the economic development of the area   Many individuals and businesses will get the help they need to get on their feet or expand  I am proud to work with the Sisseton community to start new businesses and create jobs                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
