     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Andi FoubergPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Introduces Bill to Establish Disabled Veterans Memorial          March 9th  2007   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune introduced a bill yesterday to extend the authorization for establishing a memorial in Washington  DC to honor disabled veterans     This memorial would recognize the sacrifices that so many in this country have made to preserve our freedom   said Thune   Washington is home to memorials and monuments honoring the people who have made this country great  this would be an appropriate and important addition    The original authorization for this memorial was enacted on October 24  2000 and expires this year  Thune s bill would extend the authorization to establish this memorial to October 24  2015   The memorial would be paid for by donations and funds raised by the Disabled Veterans  LIFE Memorial Foundation   Senator Tim Johnson introduced this legislation last year and Senator Thune has been working with Senator Johnson s staff and the Disabled Veterans  LIFE Memorial Foundation on the legislation                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
