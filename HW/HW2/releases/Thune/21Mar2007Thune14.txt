     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Andi FoubergPhone  202 224 2321           Thune to Host Energy Subcommittee Hearing          March 21st  2007   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune announced today that he will be hosting a hearing of the U S  Senate Agriculture Energy Subcommittee on Wednesday  April 4  2007 at 10 00 AM CT in Brookings  Senator Thune is the Ranking Member on the Energy Subcommittee  This hearing will provide information for the Agriculture Committee as they prepare to draft the Energy Title of the 2007 Farm Bill    This hearing will give South Dakotans a chance to directly voice their opinions on the emerging biofuels industry for the official record of the U S  Senate Agriculture Committee without traveling to Washington   said Thune   Ethanol has transformed the landscape of corn country and we need to be preparing for the commercial production of cellulosic ethanol from diverse feed stocks  such as switchgrass  I look forward to hearing from South Dakota s farmers and ranchers as well as the renewable energy community on their needs and how we can work together to prepare the infrastructure for this next frontier in bio fuel production    Additional details regarding the meeting will be available as the date approaches  Senator Thune encourages members of the agriculture community to attend this field hearing                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
