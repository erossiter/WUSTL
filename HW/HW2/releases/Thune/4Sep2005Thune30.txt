     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Senator Thune s Statement on Chief Justice Rehnquist          September 4th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune  R SD  made the following statement today after the passing of United States Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist     William Rehnquist  s service on the Supreme Court was marked by integrity and fairness  He was a strong and steady leader on the Court  and will be remembered as one of the most influential chief justices in our history  As a justice  he made his decisions based on the Constitution and laws as they are written  rather than his own personal views  America is a better place today because of Chief Justice Rehnquist s service and legal mind  As with Justice O  Connor  s replacement  John Roberts  I expect the Senate to give the President  s nominee to replace Chief Justice Rehnquist a rigorous and fair hearing followed by an up or down confirmation vote                                                                                                                                                     SR 493
