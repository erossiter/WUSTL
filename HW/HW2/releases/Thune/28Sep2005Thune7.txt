     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Announces  769 911 Grant for Oglala Sioux Tribe Pine Ridge Indian Reservation          September 28th  2005   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune announced today that the United States Department of Education has approved a  769 911 grant for the Oglala Sioux Tribe Pine Ridge Indian Reservation      These resources will go directly toward providing adequate transportation and quality job training for South Dakotans with disabilities     Thune said    This significant funding will be instrumental in helping these individuals channel their talents and start new  rewarding careers            These resources will enable the Oglala Lakota Nation Vocational Rehabilitation Agency to expand and improve its job training and development services for persons with disabilities   Over the past 10 years  this grant has helped more than 700 people undergo job training and become gainfully employed                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
