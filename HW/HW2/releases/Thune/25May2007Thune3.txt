     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Senator John Thune  R SD  today issued the following statement commemorating Memorial Day 2007           May 25th  2007   Washington  D C     On this Memorial Day  we pause to consider the great sacrifices our servicemen and women have made throughout the course of our nation s history  Only through their selflessness are we able to enjoy unsurpassed freedom and opportunity in this country   Thune said   We think especially of the brave men and women who have made the ultimate sacrifice in our nation s latest fight for freedom  It is my prayer that God will comfort their loved ones  and that they will always take heart in the honor with which their sons and daughters served  and the great contribution their sacrifices have made to the safety and well being of every American     Let s give thanks this Memorial Day and everyday for America s bravest the men and women who have answered the calling to serve their country and make the ultimate sacrifice to keep America secure and prosperous                                                                                                                                                   SR 493
