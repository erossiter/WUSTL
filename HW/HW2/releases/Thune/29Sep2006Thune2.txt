     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune  Senate Passes Border Fencing Bill          September 29th  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today issued the following statement after Senate passage of the Secure Fence Act of 2006     The United States is a nation of immigrants  but it is also a nation committed to the rule of law  America will continue to welcome immigrants to its shores  but they must come here legally  I feel that before we can seriously address immigration reform  we have to be able to secure our borders  It is in our national security interest to secure our borders  Border fencing has proven to be an especially effective deterrent against illegal immigration and that is why I support and voted for the Secure Fence Act                                                                                                                                                   SR 493
