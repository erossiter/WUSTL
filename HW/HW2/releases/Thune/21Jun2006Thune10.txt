     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Cosponsors 10 by 10 Act to Increase Use of Renewable Fuels          June 21st  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today issued the following statement regarding the 10 by 10 Act  legislation that would amend the Clean Air Act to require that all gasoline sold for use in motor vehicles contain 10 percent of renewable fuel by 2010  Senator Thune is a cosponsor of the 10 by 10 Act  which was introduced in the Senate today      I strongly support the 10 by 10 Act to greatly increase the use of renewable fuels in this country by requiring that all gasoline used in cars contain 10 percent of renewable fuel by 2010  It s a high mark to reach but I m confident we can achieve this   Thune said   These are the kind of ambitious goals we must set in America if we are going to get serious about reducing our dependence on foreign oil and shifting to cleaner  more cost effective alternative fuels  I will continue to work to promote measures like this that increase alternative fuel production and use and reduce energy costs for all Americans                                                                                                                                                   SR 493
