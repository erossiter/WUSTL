     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Thune  Senate Armed Services Committee Authorizes  5 Million for Rosebud Electronic Center          May 11th  2006   Washington  DC   Senator John Thune today announced the Senate Armed Services Committee reported a Defense bill to the full Senate that when passed will authorize  5 million for the Advanced Electronic Rosebud Integration Center     This is fantastic news for Rosebud Sioux Tribe Electronic Center and the men and women it employs   Thune said   The Rosebud Integration Center contributes significantly to our national security and defense by conducting state of the art research in missile defense and space technologies  Funding like this would only serve to strengthen the center and improve its capabilities  I have long advocated for the center with my colleagues on the Senate Armed Services Committee  and I will continue to push for this important funding as it moves through the legislative process     Authorization by the Senate Armed Services Committee is the first step in the appropriations process  This funding must now be approved by both the Senate and the House before it is finalized                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
