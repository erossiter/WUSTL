     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune announces  400k HIV grant          March 22nd  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune today announced a  400 000 Department of Health and Human Services grant for the City of Sioux Falls Health Department   HHS  s Division of Grants Management Operations will award the  400 000 through the Ryan White Title III Outpatient EIS Program   This grant program will help provide outpatient EIS  early intervention services  with respect to HIV     Over 460 cases of AIDS HIV have been reported in South Dakota since 1985     said Thune     This important grant will help Sioux Falls Health Department provide early intervention services and promote health in South Dakota      For more information about HIV in South Dakota  visit the state Dept  of Health  Office of Disease Prevention website                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
