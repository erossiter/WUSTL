     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Jessica FergusonPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Gains Passage of Amendment to Protect Farmers  Ranchers  Small Businesses from Tax Hikes          March 23rd  2007   Washingotn  D C    The Senate today accepted an amendment to the Fiscal Year 2008 Budget Resolution  offered by Senator John Thune  which would require 60 votes in the Senate in order to pass any legislation that raises taxes on family farmers  family ranchers  or small businesses   The Democrats  proposed budget includes a tax hike of more than  700 billion three times larger than the biggest tax hike in U S  history  passed by President Bill Clinton in 1993      My amendment is a necessary modification to the Democrat Budget Resolution  which shockingly proposes the largest tax hike in U S  history   Thune said     At a time when the farm economy in South Dakota has been burdened by six successive years of drought  the last thing our agriculture producers and small businesses need is a burdensome tax increase  I m glad my amendment to protect South Dakota farmers  ranchers  and small businesses from a future tax increase was accepted  but Senate Democrats have a long way to go in improving their Budget Resolution so it doesn t burden taxpayers and slow economic growth                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
