     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune to welcome home troops from Iraq          February 23rd  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune will welcome home South Dakota troops from Iraq at a special ceremony at 11 am  Thursday in Watertown   Members of the HHS 2nd Battalion  147th Field Artillery are currently in Fort Still  Oklahoma after serving in Iraq and will return to South Dakota tomorrow     Our troops served the nation bravely and all South Dakotans are proud of them     said Thune  R SD    I look forward to welcoming them home from a job well done      As a member of the Senate Armed Services committee  Thune traveled to Iraq earlier this month to visit troops from South Dakota and meet top Iraqi and Coalition officials     Having met with some of the South Dakota troops serving in Iraq  I understand how much members of the armed services and their families are sacrificing     Thune said    I look forward to saying thank you on behalf of a grateful nation and proud state                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
