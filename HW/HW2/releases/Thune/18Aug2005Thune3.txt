     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune announces  99 000 Rural Development grant for Eureka  SD          August 18th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  has approved a  99 000 grant for the city of Eureka  South Dakota   The USDA grant will be used to construct a building for lease to small and emerging businesses in Eureka     The USDA is acting to help grow Eureka  s economy and create jobs    Thune said     Small businesses fuel job creation in America  I  m proud to help South Dakota small businesses      The grant is part of the USDA Rural Development program  which improves the economy and quality of life in rural America through loans and grants                                                                                                                                                  SR 493
