     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Up or Down  not Left or Right Thune Applauds Judiciary Committee Confirmation of Judge Roberts         September 22nd  2005   Washington  D C    Sen  John Thune  R SD  today issued the following statement applauding the Senate Judiciary Committee  s vote to confirm Judge John Roberts   nomination as Chief Justice of the U S  Supreme Court   He urged his colleagues to follow suit      The majority of Judiciary Committee Members  who studied Judge Roberts   career and ability with high levels of scrutiny and hours of questioning  have determined he is an able and fitting choice for Chief Justice     Thune said    Their vote today should set a resounding example for the rest of the Senate body     As we prepare to vote on Judge Robert  s confirmation next week  I urge my colleagues in the Senate to put politics aside   Past justices have been confirmed nearly unanimously  anything less for a qualified and talented nominee like Judge Roberts would reveal a sad state of partisanship in the Supreme Court nomination process        Let  s give Judge Roberts an up or down vote  not left or right                                                                                                                                                    SR 493
