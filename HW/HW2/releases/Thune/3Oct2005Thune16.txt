     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                           Contact  Kyle DowneyPhone  202 224 2321           Thune Announces  35 990 Grant for the Brookings County Housing and Redevelopment Commission          October 3rd  2005   WASHINGTON  D C    Senator John Thune announced today that the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development has approved a  35 990 grant for the Brookings County Housing and Redevelopment Commission      These important resources will help the Brookings County Housing and Redevelopment Commission continue to offer quality services and guidance to individuals looking to buy their first homes     Thune said     Homeownership is key to financial independence and security        This funding will be used to pay the salaries of program coordinators at the Brookings County Housing and Redevelopment Commission   These coordinators provide program participants with the training and support services needed to become homeowners and achieve economic independence and self sufficiency                                                                                                                                                 SR 493
