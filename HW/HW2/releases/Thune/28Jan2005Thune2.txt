     United States Senator   John Thune                                                                  July 31  2008                                                                         Press Releases                                                                   Press Office                                                      Thune calls upcoming Iraqi elections  a great milestone           January 28th  2005   Washington  D C    Senator John Thune  R S D   today said the upcoming elections in Iraq are a victory for Iraqi and Coalition forces     The free elections in Iraq are a great milestone in the fight to create a stable and democratic Iraq     Thune said    These elections would not happen without the bravery  commitment and sacrifice of our men and women in uniform  and the desire of Iraqis to live in freedom  Regardless of who is ahead when the polls close on Sunday  the fact that the polls opened at all is a great victory for Coalition forces and a severe blow to the insurgents                                                                                                                                                     SR 493
