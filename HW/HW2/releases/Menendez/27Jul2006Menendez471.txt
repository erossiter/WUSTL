                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ  VOTING RIGHTS REAUTHORIZATION A   PROUD DAY   FOR AMERICA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 New Jersey lawmaker attends White House bill signing ceremony                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Thursday  July 27  2006      WASHINGTON    Following a White House bill signing ceremony  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement hailing the reauthorization of the Voting Rights Act  The New Jersey lawmaker said this law will protect our nation  s democracy by continuing to make it available to all Americans      Today  s reauthorization of the Voting Rights Act is a proud day for America  This legislation pays homage to American patriots who fought to ensure our nation kept faith with democratic values and that we continue to bear true allegiance to the cardinal virtues of freedom and equality  It was an honor to co sponsor legislation so integral to our democracy and I was proud to be present as this legislation was signed into law
