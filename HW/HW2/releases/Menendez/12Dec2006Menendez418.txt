                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ COMMENDS NFL FOR TEXAS BOWL OFFER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Proposal will allow millions of New Jerseyans to cheer on Scarlet Knights                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Tuesday  December 12  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today commended NFL Commissioner Roger Goodell for extending a deal to cable operators that will allow millions of New Jerseyans to view this month  s Texas Bowl between Rutgers University and Kansas State University  Absent this offer  millions of New Jerseyans would be unable to view the gridiron match between the Scarlet Knights and the Wildcats  Menendez spoke with Goodell earlier today      Commissioner Goodell is to be commended for extending this offer to New Jersey cable operators and I urge the cable operators to accept this offer  On December 28th our state should be united in watching good football and cheering on our Scarlet Knights to victory over the Wildcats        The inaugural Texas Bowl will kick off December 28th in Houston Texas
