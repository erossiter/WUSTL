ANNOUNCE MORE THAN  270 MILLION IN NEW JERSEY DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE PROJECTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senators say funds will help protect Garden State  nation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Thursday  September 28  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Robert Menendez and Frank R  Lautenberg today announced more than  270 million in funds for projects throughout the Garden State  including millions for weapons modernization  software improvements and vaccine development  The funds  included in the Department of Defense appropriations conference report  will finance projects in all geographical regions of New Jersey  The Senate is expected to consider the conference report later this week      These funds are essential to ensure the safety of all New Jerseyans and every American     said Menendez    The Garden State is a vital player in the defense of our country  and these critical resources will make New Jersey and America better equipped and better prepared to face the national security challenges of the future        New Jersey s advances in science and technology have an impact across America   Senator Frank R  Lautenberg said    In New Jersey  they create jobs and spur commerce  Across the country  they make people safer and healthier   With this money  we ll do more to secure our nation and our state
