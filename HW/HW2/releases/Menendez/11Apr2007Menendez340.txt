                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ  A LEADING PROPONENT OF STEM CELL RESEARCH  APPLAUDS PASSAGE OF BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Senator reiterates his call for the President to sign legislation into law                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Wednesday  April 11  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a staunch supporter of stem cell research and a co sponsor of today s legislation  applauded the Senate s passage of the Stem Cell Research Enhancement Act of 2007  S 5   Menendez  whose mother suffers from Alzheimer s disease  thanked his colleagues for supporting the legislation and reiterated his call for President Bush to retract his veto threats     Today the Senate truly represented the will of the people by passing this vital legislation supported by a vast majority of Americans   said Menendez   Now it is up to the president to hear the call of millions suffering from debilitating diseases  and turn their hopes into reality     A threat to veto is a promise to kill hope  I urge the president to rescind his warnings and to embrace the possibilities of science     S 5 passed the Senate tonight by a 63 34 vote  President Bush last year used his first veto in office to strike down similar legislation that passed the Congress
