                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ RENEWS COMMITMENT TO STEM CELL RESEARCH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Pledges more determination than ever                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Wednesday  August 9  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement reiterating his support for the hope and promise offered by stem cell research  On the fifth anniversary of Bush  s ban on further federal funding for stem cell research  Menendez again expressed his full commitment to fighting for this potentially life saving research       Five years ago  President Bush placed a ban on federal funding for new stem cell research  In those five years  I have heard time and again the desperation and hope of millions of Americans who pray for a change in policy so that scientists have the resources they need to potentially save millions of lives      Those with loved ones suffering from Alzheimer  s  Parkinson  s or juvenile diabetes waited in hope that their prayers would be answered and cures will be found in their lifetime   Across America  families in which a child or a parent was paralyzed from a traumatic accident held out hope that we would do the right thing and give their loved ones back the life they knew before their injury      When we received the heart breaking news that President Bush vetoed the Senate bill that would have given these families hope  Democrats pledged not to let their voices be silenced  Bush  s grave mistake to veto the bill was not the end of this fight  but has merely fueled the fire of our determination  We will keep fighting until these millions of Americans see their hopes and prayers answered
