                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON SHUTDOWN OF OYSTER CREEK NUCLEAR POWER PLANT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Friday  December 21  2007      WASHINGTON  Earlier this week  the Oyster Creek Generating Station had to be manually shut down due to a pump trip  one day after a federal safety panel recommended the relicensing of the plant for 20 years    Senator Bob Menendez  D NJ  today released the following statement    Safety must always be our first priority  Before the NRC makes the determination about whether to relicense Oyster Creek for 20 years we must know more about this incident  I therefore call on the Atomic Safety and Licensing Board to reopen its consideration of the plant s relicensing and give everyone more time to determine whether this problem with the reactor feed pump impacts the plants long term viability
