                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON SEN  WARNER S CALL TO WITHDRAW TROOPS FROM IRAQ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Friday  August 24  2007      Washington  DC United States Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a member of the Foreign Relations Committee  today made the following statement after Senator Warner  R VA  called for a withdrawal of American troops from Iraq by Christmas    The announcement by Sen  Warner shows that the President s fragile support is crumbling and that a domino effect to change the course in Iraq is finally grabbing the attention and conscience of our Republican leaders  As Ive said from the beginning  it is time to get our troops out of Iraq
