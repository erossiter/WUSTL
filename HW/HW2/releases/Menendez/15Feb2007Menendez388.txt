                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON DUBAI PORTS WORLD DEAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Thursday  February 15  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement on reports of obstacles to finalizing the purchase of the Port Newark Container Terminal     The concerns we expressed a year ago are still valid today  A company run and controlled by a foreign government should not run and control the operations of American ports  Period  All parties involved in these negotiations should remember that this is not just a business transaction and isn t just about a quick payday  This is  most importantly  a matter of national security and about protecting the American people  If this deal falls through it would be a tremendous disservice to the American people and to our nation s security   a fate no one wants on their hands
