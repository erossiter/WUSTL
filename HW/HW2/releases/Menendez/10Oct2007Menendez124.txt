                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ  LAUTENBERG ANNOUNCE FUNDING FOR ELSINBORO FIRE COMPANY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Wednesday  October 10  2007      WASHINGTON  United States Senators Frank R  Lautenberg and Robert Menendez today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded nearly  20 000 to the Elsinboro Fire Company as part of the Assistance to Firefighters grant program  The Safety and Operations grant announced today will be distributed directly to the local fire department for the purchase of personal protection  health and wellness  and safety modification resources   Firefighters risk their lives to protect our nations communities from harm  Menendez said  The Safety and Operations grant enhances the safety protocol for local fire departments  I am pleased to support an endeavor which helps keep our local firefighters safe   Firefighters are often the first to respond to an emergency  said Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ   They put their lives on the line to keep us safe and deserve our support  This grant will go a long way toward ensuring the fire departments across New Jersey have the necessary resources to enhance their response capabilities and protect the community   Since 2001  the Department of Homeland Securitys  DHS  Assistance to Firefighters Grants program has provided funding to fire departments and other first responders  In Fiscal Year 2007 DHS Assistance to Firefighters Grants program will endow approximately  490 million in grants to increase the efficiency and wellbeing of emergency response personnel  This is the 12th round of competitive FY07 Assistance to Firefighters Grants program    Organization Name Federal Share Municipality Elsinboro
