                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ APPLAUDS APPOINTMENT OF SARLES AS NJ TRANSIT S NEW EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator expresses confidence in the future of NJ Transit s projects  appreciation for outgoing Director Warrington                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Monday  April 2  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today welcomed the news that Richard Sarles  currently NJ Transit s second highest ranking official  has been appointed to the position of Executive Director  Menendez also paid tribute to George Warrington  the man Sarles will replace     This appointment ensures that NJ Transit will continue to have a very capable and forward thinking executive director in its driver s seat   Sen  Menendez   Richard Sarles has proven his ability and has helped steer the transit system into the future on several vital projects  His leadership on the Trans Hudson Express Tunnel gives me confidence that the project will move forward without a hitch     I also want to commend George Warrington for his leadership at the helm of NJ Transit  He has been a main catalyst in ensuring that projects like the THE Tunnel have come as far as they have  I wish him well in his future endeavors
