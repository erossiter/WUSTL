                                                                                                                                                                                    ENSURING  EMBEDDED GIVING  ACTUALLY GETS TO CHARITIES  SEN  MENENDEZ TO INTRODUCE LEGISLATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Increasingly  retailers promising to make donations upon purchase                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Friday  December 14  2007      WASHINGTON  Everywhere shoppers go this holiday season  they have seen promotions from retailers promising to make a donation to a particular charity upon purchase  However  this so called embedded giving lacks transparency  in fact  some charities contacted recently by reporters were not even aware that they had been listed as recipients by retailers  Following a Senate Banking Committee hearing yesterday on Financial Literacy for the Holiday Season at which Senator Bob Menendez  D NJ  raised the issue of embedded giving  he said that he plans to introduce legislation to increase transparency surrounding this practice  He is exploring measures to require retailers to notify charities when their names are being used  to require charity approval of embedded giving and to require retailers to disclose how much they are actually giving to charities  Senator Menendez intends to introduce the legislation next week  Charitable giving is an important and noble act  and the retailers that pledge to give to charity and follow through are to be commended  said Senator Menendez  Unfortunately  too often consumers have no way to know if their dollars are actually going to the intended causes  and some charities are not aware that their names are being used by retailers  We need to ensure that charity is not being used solely as a sales pitch  When consumers open their wallets  they should have the comfort of knowing their good intentions will be protected and that their donations will benefit the intended cause  Shining more sunlight on this practice will help us confirm that retailers are giving what they promise  and I plan to introduce legislation next week to do just that
