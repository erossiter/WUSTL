                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ  VOTING RIGHTS ACT ESSENTIAL FOR PRESERVING DEMOCRACY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      We must keep faith with the highest ideals and promises of this great nation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Thursday  July 20  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today reiterated his unwavering support for the Voting Rights Act on the floor of the United States Senate  Menendez  a co sponsor of the Voting Rights Act reauthorization  underscored his fervent belief that this landmark legislation    and the provisions that were later incorporated therein    set the foundation for a more inclusive democracy and a more diverse United States Congress  The New Jersey lawmaker said reauthorizing this legislation will continue to protect the voting rights of all Americans      In enacting the original Voting Rights Act and its four reauthorizations  past Congresses have declared to the world that America stands for freedom and democracy     Menendez said     But our rhetoric of equality and freedom must be ratified by an authentic pursuit of true freedom  true equality  and true democratic ideals   If we are to be a beacon of democracy and freedom to Baghdad  Beirut and Beijing    then we must first be a beacon of freedom and democracy to Bloomfield  Buffalo  and Birmingham
