                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ  LAUTENBERG ANNOUNCE  61 798 GRANT FOR WEST DEPTFORD FIRE DEPARTMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Thursday  November 15  2007      WASHINGTON   United States Senators Robert Menendez  D NJ  and Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Security has awarded  61 798 to the Green Fields Volunteer Fire Company  West Deptford  New Jersey  as part of the Assistance to Firefighters grant program  The Safety and Operations grant announced today is distributed directly to local fire departments for the purchase of personal protection  health and wellness  and safety modification resources  Senators Menendez and Lautenberg reiterated the importance of these grants    Firefighters risk their lives to protect our nations communities from harm  said Senator Menendez  The Safety and Operations grant enhances the safety protocol for our local fire departments  Our firefighters keep our neighborhoods safe and I am proud to announce funds which help keep them safe    Firefighters are often the first to respond to an emergency  said Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  They put their lives on the line to keep us safe and deserve our support  This grant will go a long way toward ensuring the fire departments across New Jersey have the necessary resources to enhance their response capabilities and protect the community    State Senator Stephen M  Sweeney and Assemblymen John J  Burzichelli and Douglas H  Fisher of New Jerseys Third Legislative district  saw a town in their region receive an Assistance to Firefighters grant for the second consecutive month  Last month  the Elsinboro Fire Company received  19 135 for the purchase of new pumps  Sen  Sweeney and Assemblymen Burzichelli and Fisher lauded the program    This funding will help strengthen Green Fields Fire Companys overall level of preparedness and ability to respond to fire and fire related hazards  The grant will be used to purchase response equipment  protective gear  and vehicles  said State Senator Stephen M  Sweeney    I am glad that the federal government awarded this grant to Green Fields Fire Company  The awards aim is to enhance firefighters ability to protect the health and safety of the community  as well as that of West Deptfords fire responders personnel  said Assemblyman John J  Burzichelli  This money will help the fire company create more resourceful services while at the same time providing enhanced protection to the West Deptford Township community  Having well prepared and equipped fire personnel is essential to the safety of the entire county  added Assemblyman Douglas H  Fisher     Since 2001  the Department of Homeland Securitys  DHS  Assistance to Firefighters Grants program has provided funding to fire departments and other first responders  In Fiscal Year 2007 DHS Assistance to Firefighters Grants program will endow approximately  490 million in grants to increase the efficiency and wellbeing of emergency response personnel  This is the 15th round of competitive FY07 Assistance to Firefighters Grants program
