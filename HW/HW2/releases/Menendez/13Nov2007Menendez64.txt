                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ INTRODUCES LEGISLATION TO KEEP KIDS SAFE ONLINE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      10 million program would be established for education about internet safety                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Tuesday  November 13  2007      WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today introduced legislation aimed at keeping children safe in their online activities  The Internet Safety Education Act would provide funds for national  state and community programs that educate children and parents about safe internet use  If youre a parent  it doesnt take an episode of To Catch a Predator to make you understand just how real and horrific online threats to our children are  said Sen  Menendez  The threats may emerge from the virtual world of cyberspace  but they are very real and need to be taken very seriously  Congress needs to ensure that children do not fall victim to the dangers of the internet  and we can do this by educating them  as well as parents and teachers  Knowledge is the first step towards stopping these horrific crimes before they begin  Sen  Menendezs initiative would create a  10 million grant program for the Attorney General to develop and administer  Grants would be awarded on a competitive basis to organizations that provide free education about internet safety to children  parents  and teachers  According to the Internet Keep Safe Coalition  one in five children receives a sexual solicitation via the Internet over a one year period  In addition  twenty nine percent of children freely give out their home addresses when asked
