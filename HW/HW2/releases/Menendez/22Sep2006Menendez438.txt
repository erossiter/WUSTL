                                                                                                                                                                                    FDA TELLS SENATORS NJ GROWN SPINACH   NOT IMPLICATED   IN OUTBREAK                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  FDA officials indicate Jersey grown spinach was not part of E  coli outbreak    Agency working to ensure New Jersey product soon returns to store shelves                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Friday  September 22  2006      WASHINGTON    After reviewing the Food and Drug Administration  s latest advisory  in addition to recent conversations with FDA officials  U S  Senators Robert Menendez  D NJ  and Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  today said that federal officials have not implicated New Jersey grown spinach in the outbreak of E  coli  The lawmakers said government officials are working to ensure New Jersey grown spinach can return to store shelves as soon as possible  with a potential announcement expected as early as Friday evening      Based on the statement released by the Food and Drug Administration  in addition to conversations with FDA officials  we understand that spinach grown on New Jersey farms was not implicated in the E  coli outbreak     the lawmakers said    Earlier this week Menendez and Lautenberg called on the U S  Food and Drug Administration to move rapidly in identifying where spinach tainted with dangerous strains of E  coli bacteria was being grown  and to issue a new advisory as soon as possible to reassure the public that New Jersey spinach is safe  The FDA issued an updated advisory Thursday  indicating the infected spinach was confined to several California counties  FDA officials said  based on that discovery  it is clear that spinach grown on New Jersey farms was not contaminated
