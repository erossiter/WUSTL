                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ CELEBRATES HISPANIC HERITAGE MONTH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Monday  September 18  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today released the following statement in celebration of Hispanic Heritage Month  which began last Friday and ends on October 15th      This Hispanic Heritage Month  we recognize the contributions that Hispanics make to this great nation every day as well as the contributions we  ve made throughout our country  s history  America  s diversity of culture is what makes our country so unique  and the Hispanic community has long contributed to the fabric of our nation and New Jersey  Hispanics are playing a larger role in strengthening and enriching our country than ever before  making notable contributions in political  economic and cultural realms of American life  As a Cuban American  I am proud to be part of that progress  and proud of the heritage we celebrate this month
