                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON PASSING OF PRESIDENT GERALD FORD                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Wednesday  December 27  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement following the passing of President Gerald Ford      Like all Americans  I was saddened by the recent passing of President Gerald Ford  America will be forever indebted to President Ford for his selfless service and for bringing calm and stability to a nation besieged by conflict and scandal  My thoughts and prayers are with the Ford family during their time of grief and sadness
