                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ REACTS TO BUSH IRAQ SPEECH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Friday  September 14  2007      WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee  released the following statement tonight after President Bushs speech on Iraq   Tonight  President Bush presented an open ended  no exit plan for the sons and daughters of America who continue to fight and die in Iraq  This administration has tried to sell us on the search for WMDs  Mission Accomplished and last throes  and now it is pitching another proposal that is just stay the course by another name  Going down this path has not made us any safer  it has diverted attention from Osama bin Laden  it has fomented terrorism  it has stretched our military thin and it has cost us dearly in lives and treasure   Until the Iraqi government and security forces actually believe we wont be there forever  they will not take control of their country  That makes a timetable for transitioning our mission and bringing our troops home imperative  The alternative is an endless occupation in Iraq with more American blood spilled and no conclusion in sight
