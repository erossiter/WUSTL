                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON THE CAPTURE OF TERROR LEADER AL ZARQAWI                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Friday  June 9  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Bob Menendez  D NJ  released the following statement regarding the termination of terror leader al Zarqawi      The elimination of al Zarqawi is great news  He was a terrorist and brutal murderer bent on creating sectarian violence in Iraq and on killing American troops  We all commend the U S  forces who carried out the air strike for succeeding in an incredibly difficult mission and for continuing to fight with determination against terrorists like al Zarqawi  And  while this is good news  we simply don  t know right now if the killing of al Zarqawi will impact the level of violence or if his followers will regroup under new leadership  My thoughts and prayers continue to be with all of the American servicemen and women who continue to serve in Iraq
