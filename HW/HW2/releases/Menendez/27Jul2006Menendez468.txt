                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ LAUTENBERG BLAST CDC REPORT ON VX DUMPING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                New Jersey lawmakers declare   fight is not over     Thursday  July 27  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senators Robert Menendez  D NJ  and Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  today blasted a federal government report that declares safe the Army Corps of Engineer  s plan to dump VX waste into the Delaware River  The report  released today by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention  said the government  s plan was   adequately protective of human health        The two New Jersey lawmakers released a joint statement denouncing the CDC  s assessment      The CDC  s recommendation to allow VX waste to be transported into New Jersey and dumped in the Delaware River is flat out wrong  and based on a rosy best case scenario that doesn  t stand up to the Army  s continued troubles in carrying this project out  This fight is not over  We  ll wait for the GAO to complete their review  and continue to work with other members of the delegation to fight the federal government  s plan to use New Jersey  s rivers as a toxic sewer
