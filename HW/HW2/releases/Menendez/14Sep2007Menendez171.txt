                                                                                                                                                                                    IRAQ BENCHMARKS REPORT CARD SHOWS WAR POLICY IS FLUNKING  SAYS SEN  MENENDEZ                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Friday  September 14  2007      WASHINGTON  Today  the Bush Administration released the second report on benchmarks in Iraq and once again not a single benchmark has been met  Unsatisfactory progress has been made toward half of the 18 benchmarks and only one benchmark was added to the statisfactory progress list since the July report   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee  today released the following statement   This latest report card shows that the presidents war policy is still flunking  Its clear that Iraq is light years away from security or political stability  and its clear that four years of this war policy has brought us to this point  Not until the Iraqi security forces and government believe we will not be there forever will they take control of their country  Instead of the presidents endless  no exit plan  we must have a timetable for transitioning our mission and bringing our troops home
