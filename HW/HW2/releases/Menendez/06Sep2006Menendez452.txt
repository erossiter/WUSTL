 ENSIGN URGE CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL FOR JERRY LEWIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Bi partisan lawmakers introduce Jerry Lewis Resolution to honor entertainer and philanthropist for Muscular Dystrophy efforts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Wednesday  September 6  2006      WASHINGTON    Citing his tireless efforts to find a cure for Muscular Dystrophy  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   joined by Senators John Ensign  R NV  and Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ   today introduced the Jerry Lewis Resolution to honor the New Jersey native with the Congressional Gold Medal  Jerry Lewis  who was born in Newark  New Jersey  is well known for his comedic stylings as well as his long term philanthropic work raising research funds for Muscular Dystrophy   The bipartisan effort is endorsed by the Muscular Dystrophy Association      Jerry Lewis has served the United States by helping our nation smile in difficult times and by helping the Muscular Dystrophy Association find a cure for this a debilitating disease     said Menendez    For these efforts he has been nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize  has received numerous awards  and has been honored with a resolution by the United States Senate expressing appreciation for his philanthropic work  Inducting Mr  Lewis into the ranks of distinguished Congressional Gold Medal recipients would be a fitting accolade to this larger than life individual          Jerry Lewis not only makes people laugh    his serious commitment to fight muscular dystrophy has improved the lives of thousands of kids     said Lautenberg    This kind of commitment is what made America great  and it is why Jerry Lewis is a great American       Lewis   talent and enthusiasm kept America laughing during some of the most turbulent periods in American history    World War II  the Cold War  and the assassinations of President John F  Kennedy  and Dr  Martin Luther King Jr   In 1966  he began the he began the  Jerry Lewis MDA Labor Day Telethon   an annual television program that benefits children and adults affected by muscular dystrophy and related neuromuscular diseases   Monday  s telethon    the forty first of its kind    raised a record breaking  61 million
