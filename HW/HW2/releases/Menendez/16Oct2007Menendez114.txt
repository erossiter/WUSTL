                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ REACTS TO SEC  PAULSON S REMARKS ON MORTGAGE CRISIS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Tuesday  October 16  2007      WASHINGTON  Treasury Secretary Henry Paulson today spoke about the nationwide mortgage crisis in a speech at Georgetown University  outlining the issues and the administrations response  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a member of the Senate Banking Committee  released the following statement   Millions of American homeowners are getting crunched by ticking time bomb mortgages  and they have yet to see their government take the necessary action  Secretary Paulsons new found openness to loan modifications is a positive step  However  it seems that every bold action this administration has taken has been to soften the blow for investors  The people who really have something to lose  the homeowners  are still struggling in this subprime storm
