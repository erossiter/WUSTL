                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ  LAUTENBERG  ANNOUNCE FUNDING FOR FIREFIGHTERS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Highland Park Fire Department Awarded Funds for Safety Equipment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Wednesday  March 14  2007      WASHINGTON   United States Senators Frank R  Lautenberg and Robert Menendez today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded the Highland Park Volunteer Fire Department  60 800 as part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  The Highland Park Volunteer Fire Department will utilize these funds to upgrade their communications system     Our nations  firefighters risk their lives every day to protect our homes and our neighborhoods  and we  in turn  should provide them with the equipment they need to protect themselves  This is why the Assistance to Firefighters Program is so important   said Menendez   Unfortunately  the President s proposed budget seeks to cut funding for this important program   But as I have in the past  I will fight to ensure that this vital program continues to help our local fire departments       Firefighters are often the first to respond to an emergency   said Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ     They put their lives on the line to keep us safe and deserve our support   This grant will go a long way toward ensuring the Highland Park Volunteer Fire Department has the necessary resources to enhance their response capabilities and protect the community     The Department of Homeland Security s Assistance to Firefighters Grants program has been providing funding to fire departments and other first responders since 2001  To date  a total of  2 4 billion in grants through the Assistance to Firefighters Grants program have been awarded to first responder organizations to purchase response equipment  personal protective equipment  and vehicles  In Fiscal Year 2006  DHS Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program will award approximately  485 million directly to fire departments and EMS organizations to enhance their response capabilities and to more effectively protect the health and safety of the public and emergency response personnel with respect to fire and all other hazards  This is the 21st Round of Grant announcements   Other New Jersey communities that have received funding through this program include Perth Amboy  Elizabeth  Randolph  Wall Township  and Point Pleasant  among many others
