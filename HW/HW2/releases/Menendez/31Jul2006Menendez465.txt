                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ INTRODUCES   HEALTHY SCHOOL BUS BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Legislation aimed at protecting children from air pollution in school buses                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Monday  July 31  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  introduced the Healthy School Bus Bill which directs the Environmental Protection Agency  EPA  to conduct a study of the air quality inside school buses  The bill  which authorizes  750 000 for this study and  55 million for EPA  s Clean School Bus USA Program  would require the agency to report back with recommendations on how to improve air quality inside these vehicles      Our top priority should be protecting the health of our children     Menendez said     Students traveling to school should not fear for their well being because of air pollution in the buses taking them there  Studies that link the soot particles inside school buses with asthma attacks  heart disease  stroke  and premature death are disturbing  and a lack of action is criminal        The Healthy School Bus bill  first introduced by then Congressman Menendez in the United States House of Representatives  will bring more attention to the issue of pollution inside school buses  Along with the required study and the authorized monies  the bill also includes a Sense of Congress that school districts should adopt policies to discourage idling  and put less polluting buses on longer routes  to cut down on the amount of pollution children who ride those buses are exposed to
