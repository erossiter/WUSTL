                                                                                                                                                                                    Statement from US Senator Robert Menendez on the Passing of Dr  David Rebovich                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Friday  October 12  2007       Washington  DC  United States Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today made the following statement upon learning of the sudden death of Dr  David Rebovich   David Rebovich was a consummate gentleman and true student of government and politics  His love for the subject was evident in his writings and in his interactions with countless students and elected officials across the states  I am certain all New Jerseyans will miss his wit and analysis  My thoughts and prayers are with his family and friends
