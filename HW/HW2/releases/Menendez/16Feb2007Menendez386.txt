                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ HAILS FINAL PORT SALE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Friday  February 16  2007      WASHINGTON   United States Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement regarding the sale of the Port Newark Container Terminal  the last of several American port facilities transferred to American ownership      It was one year ago that I led the opposition to a deal to sell American port facilities to a company controlled and owned by a foreign government  Others joined the call and we ultimately forged an alliance of Democrats and Republicans who staunchly opposed the White House and rejected this deal     I believe now  as I did then  that this transaction was not in our nation s security interests  And I was pleased to hear today that the final deal was completed and that the ports have been turned over to an American owned corporation     This was the right thing to do for our nation s security and I was proud to have been an early voice in this debate
