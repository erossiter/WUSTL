                                                                                                                                                                                    SENATOR MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON VETERANS DAY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Sunday  November 11  2007      WASHINGTON  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  made the following statement to commemorate Veterans Day  It is so important to recognize and remember our brave veterans and to keep in our hearts the 3 904 New Jerseyans currently over in Iraq and Afghanistan  We thank them for proudly serving and sacrificing for our country whenever they are called to duty  Said Menendez  We must also remember that we honor veterans not just by going to a Veterans Day observance or marching in a Memorial Day parade  we honor them by supporting them with excellent health care  working to ensure they have a roof over their heads  and making certain they are prepared to confront the barriers to success when they return home  I will continue to stand up in Congress for what could be the largest increase in aid for our veterans in history  said Sen  Menendez
