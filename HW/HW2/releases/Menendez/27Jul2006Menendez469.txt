                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ APPLAUDS PORT AUTHORITY COMMITMENT TO TRANS HUDSON TUNNEL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Thursday  July 27  2006      WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released a statement following the announcement that the Port Authority of New York New Jersey authorized the investment of at least  1 billion and as much as  2 billion toward construction of the Trans Hudson Express Tunnel project   This project will construct an additional passenger rail tunnel connecting New York City to New Jersey      The Trans Hudson Express Tunnel is critical to ensure the safe  efficient transport of thousands of local residents who travel to and from New York City for work  school  or entertainment  and the continued economic growth of the entire region   The Port Authority  s financial commitment to the construction of this tunnel is an important step to ensuring that this essential project stays on track  I will continue to work in the United States Senate to improve transportation options for all New Jerseyans
