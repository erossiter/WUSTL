                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ BLASTS NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS BOARD RULING                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Tuesday  October 3  2006      WASHINGTON    United States Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released the following statement in response to the National Labor Relations Board ruling that health care industry workers who exercise professional clinical judgment are   supervisors    and thus ineligible to join unions  The 3 2 ruling by the board  which is dominated by Bush administration appointees  opens the door for millions of other workers to be reclassified as supervisors and therefore be denied the rights and protections guaranteed under the National Labor Relations Act      From day one  the Bush administration has shown that it does not stand with the hard working Americans who try to join unions and exercise their right to bargain collectively  The National Labor Relations Board was created to protect and uphold the rights of working men and women  yet the Bush administration has used it as a tool to place a stranglehold on Americans who want a decent wage and suitable working conditions  Today  s decision is a disservice to all Americans who work hard for a better way of life
