                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON VIRGINIA TECH SHOOTINGS  FATALITIES OF STUDENTS FROM NEW JERSEY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Wednesday  April 18  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released the following statement on this week s shootings at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and the developing news that at least three students from New Jersey were killed and another is critically injured     Our country has suffered a shocking and saddening tragedy  We have lost more than 30 young men and women in a senseless act  and my heart goes out to their families  Our nation grieves with them     This tragedy may have occurred in Virginia  but it has hit particularly close to home here in New Jersey  Several New Jersey families have been deeply and traumatically affected  and we share in their sorrow      We know now that at least three of our state s bright young students were murdered  I extend my deepest sympathies to their families during this difficult time  as do millions of others across our state  Another student from New Jersey was critically injured  and my thoughts  prayers and hopes for recovery are with him and his family
