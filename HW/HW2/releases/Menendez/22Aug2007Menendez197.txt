                                                                                                                                                                                    Senators Announce Over  650 000 In Grants for Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Wednesday  August 22  2007       Washington  DC    United States Senators Robert Menendez and Frank R  Lautenberg today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded grants to various fire departments across the state as part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  The Assistance to Firefighters program awards grants directly to fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical service  EMS  organizations to enhance first responders ability to protect the health and safety of the public  Since 2001  a total of  2 4 billion in grants have been awarded to first responder organizations to purchase response equipment  personal protective equipment  and vehicles   Our nations firefighters risk their lives every day to protect our homes and our neighborhoods  and we  in turn  should provide them with the equipment they need to protect themselves  This is why the Assistance to Firefighters Program is so important  said Menendez  I will continue to fight to ensure that this vital program continues to help our local fire departments and first responders   Firefighters are often the first to respond to an emergency  said Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ   They put their lives on the line to keep us safe and deserve our support  This grant will go a long way toward ensuring the fire departments across New Jersey have the necessary resources to enhance their response capabilities and protect the community   As part of Round 26 of the Fiscal Year 2006 awards  the Niagara Fire Company will receive  66 215 and the Alloway Township Fire Company will receive  87 737  In Round 5 of the Fiscal Year 2007 Assistance to Firefighters Grant Awards  a total of  498 879 has been awarded to the Teaneck Volunteer Ambulance Corps  the Ventnor City Fire
