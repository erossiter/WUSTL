                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON FORT DIX PLOT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Tuesday  May 8  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  released the following statement on news of a foiled plot against Fort Dix in Burlington County  New Jersey   I am very thankful   as is the rest of our state   for the good work of the FBI  local and state police and other law enforcement in efficiently thwarting what seems to have been a potentially deadly plot  I also want to thank the alert and vigilant video store employee who first raised his concerns with the proper authorities  We know New Jersey is a target rich state  and that makes Homeland Security a continuing top concern for us  We should also continue to act with alertness and vigilance in our daily lives
