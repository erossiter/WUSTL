                                                                                                                                                                                    MENENDEZ STATEMENT ON BUSH S VFW SPEECH                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wednesday  August 22  2007      Washington  DC United States Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today made the following statement after President Bushs speech at the Veterans of Foreign Wars National Convention    President Bushs history lesson this morning does little to help us bring our troops home safely  The key to getting ahead is taking whatever task you are given and doing it well  While the United States military is doing its job well    and I ve supported giving them the resources they need to do the job they ve been given    Iraqi officials don t seem to have the same level of commitment to getting the job done  Without local support  our soldiers continue to be in harm s way everyday  It is true that Iraqis  not US officials  will decide if Prime Minister Maliki remains in office  but US officials do have a moral responsibility to bring our troops home when Iraqi officials wont work together to succeed at their task  peace in Iraq
