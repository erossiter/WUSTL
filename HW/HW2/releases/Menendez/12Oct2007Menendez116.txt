                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  MENENDEZ APPLAUDS CHOICE OF AL GORE  U N  PANEL FOR NOBEL PEACE PRIZE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Friday  October 12  2007      WASHINGTON  Following this mornings announcement that former Vice President Al Gore and the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change are the recipients of the 2007 Nobel Peace Prize for their work to help solve the climate crisis  U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   a member of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee  released the following statement   Few have done more than Al Gore to help the inhabitants of Planet Earth understand that our only home is under threat  At the same time  the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change has helped explain this crisis for almost two decades   Because of their work  millions  potentially billions  of people are thinking for the first time about what they can do to protect their local wildlife  prevent sea levels from rising or reduce global emissions  This is a well deserved honor  and it reinforces the need for action to address our planetary emergency  I congratulate former Vice President Gore and the panel
