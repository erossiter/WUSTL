                                                                                                                                                                                    IRAQ VOTE  SEN  MENENDEZ SUPPORTS AMENDMENT CONTAINING TIMETABLE FOR WITHDRAWAL OF TROOPS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Feingold amendment would have transitioned troops out of Iraq by March 31  2008                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Wednesday  May 16  2007      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ  today cast another vote in favor of transitioning America s mission in Iraq and withdrawing U S  troops  He supported Sen  Russ Feingold s  D WI  amendment that would provide resources for U S  troops until March 31  2008  at which point they would have to be withdrawn from Iraq unless certain specific conditions are met       I will continue to support efforts in the Senate to hold the president s feet to the fire on Iraq   said Sen  Menendez   He cannot continue to be given a blank check to continue the war in Iraq indefinitely  The only viable solution is a political solution  not a military one       The Feingold amendment did not gain the 60 votes necessary to invoke cloture and limit debate on the matter
