	Celebrating June as National Homeownership Month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
Dover, DE- Senator Carper will head to Kent County on Monday, June 19, 2006, to celebrate National Homeownership Month.  He will join members of the Carper/City of Dover Homeownership Advisory Group and the proud new owners of the house, who will talk about their experience with the Homeownership Initiative.  Photos of the home – before and after demolition – will be on display. This is the first new home to be built under the Carper/City of Dover Homeownership Initiative and the Dover Community Partnership.  
The Senator is a long-time advocate of homeownership and hosts homeownership workshops throughout the state to provide information and support for those hoping to own homes, with the next one scheduled on Saturday, July 29th. Come take a look at the exciting things that are happening in downtown Dover through the community partnership!     
 Monday, June 19, 2006
