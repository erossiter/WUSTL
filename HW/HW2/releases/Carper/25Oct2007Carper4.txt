Sens. Carper and Alexander on Senate Agreement
to Extend the Moratorium on State and Local Taxes on Internet Access                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             For Immediate Release: October 25, 2007

	 U.S. Senators Tom Carper (D-Del.) and Lamar Alexander (R-Tenn.) issued the following response to the agreement reached by the Senate tonight that extends for seven years the moratorium on state and local taxes on Internet access.
