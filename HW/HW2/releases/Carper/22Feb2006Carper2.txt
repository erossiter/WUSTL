	Carper to Read to Fifth Grade Class on Thursday, February 23, 2006                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wilmington, DE – On Thursday, February 23, 2006, Senator Carper will visit Austin D. Baltz Elementary School in Wilmington to celebrate “I Love to Read Month.” The Senator will read to a 5th grade class and then go on a tour of the school and visit other classrooms.  There are approximately 680 students at Baltz Elementary from kindergarten through fifth grade.
