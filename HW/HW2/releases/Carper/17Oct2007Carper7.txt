Sens. Biden and Carper Announce Nearly $3 Million in Federal Funding for Delaware's Law Enforcement Community                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   For Immediate Release: October 17, 2007
		Contact: Bette Phelan (202) 224-2441
	State Police, DSU, Highway Safety Among Recipients of Project FundingU.S Sens. Joe Biden and Tom Carper (both D-Del.) announced Senate passage of $2,765,000 in federal funding for several police and justice projects to improve safety and reduce crime throughout the state. The funding was included in H.R. 3093, the Commerce, Justice, Science, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act of 2008, which passed the Senate tonight, 75-19.### Return
