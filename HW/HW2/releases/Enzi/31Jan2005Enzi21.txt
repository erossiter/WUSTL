                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi welcomes Iraqi citizens to a new world  January 31  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   said the success of the Jan  30 election in Iraq shows that the seed of freedom in that country is beginning to take root     Brave Iraqi soldiers and civilians risked their lives to participate in their fledgling democracy  in some ways not unlike brave Americans did more than 200 years ago  said Enzi  Iraqis went to the polls despite the warning of violence and they continued to vote even when violence occurred  It was a historic day as the Iraqi people set an example for the rest of the world  They voted for the promise of freedom and the future of their own nation  a future that is their own to make  Their participation showed appreciation to all the American and coalition forces who made it possible     Enzi said the world should be proud of Sunday s election and should continue to work together as Iraq continues to walk the often difficult path toward democracy                                                       January 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
