                                                                                                                                                                                      Taylor joins Enzi  s HELP team   April 18  2005 Washington  D C   Rawlins resident Ryan Taylor moved up in the ranks of U S  Senator Mike Enzis staff recently with his promotion to be the Communications Assistant for the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Committee which Enzi chairs    Taylor  a former intern for U S  Senator Craig Thomas and staff assistant for Enzi  will be assisting with matters relating to the media for the HELP Committee    The chance to work for one of the nation s greatest leaders and the state of Wyoming is not only an honor  it is the opportunity of a lifetime  said Taylor    Taylor  son of Rod and Robyn Taylor of Rawlins  graduated from Rawlins High School in 1998 and went on to receive a bachelors degree in political science from the University of Wyoming in 2004  Taylor was a staff assistant in Enzis office from November 2004 until his recent promotion in February 2005    Ryan has always been an outstanding employee and I knew he would carry that over to the committee  said Enzi  He has been a welcome addition to the HELP staff    Taylor was recently elected as the Wyoming State Society Vice President and will serve a one year term coordinating
