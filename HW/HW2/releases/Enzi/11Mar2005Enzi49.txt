                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi praises Sarbanes  service   March 11  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Paul Sarbanes  D Md   announced today that he is retiring at the end of his term  U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   who worked closely with Sarbanes as a fellow member of the Senate Banking Committee  commented on the announcement    Paul and I have served on the Banking Committee for eight years and I respect his hard work and dedication to his constituents  We worked tirelessly together completing the Sarbanes Oxley legislation and we became good friends during the process  His presence in the Senate will be missed  said Enzi  Despite our political and philosophical differences Paul has always shown a willingness to work out problems for the greater good  He is willing to navigate through partisanship and posturing in order to arrive at solutions which I appreciate and I m sure his constituents do too                                                      March 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
