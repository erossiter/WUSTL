                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi highlights economy  s strong first quarter growth  April 28  2006 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Committee  praised the strength of Americas growing economy and made the following statement on the positive first quarter gross domestic product  GDP  numbers released today by the Bureau of Economic Analysis  BEA      The numbers released today reflect a strong  growing economy across the entire country  Im pleased to see that the GDP grew at an impressive 4 8 percent in the first quarter  well exceeding the 1 7 percent during the last quarter of 2005  With the ongoing war on terrorism and higher energy prices  this is an impressive indicator of the underlying strength of our economy    This strong growth also is a good reminder of the need for Congress to speed passage of an extension to the Workforce Investment Act as well as the Presidents YouthBuild initiative  which together will spark added momentum in the economy  These programs are essential to help both workers and employers close the skills gap that is putting Americas long term competitiveness in jeopardy     In addition  I will continue to urge my colleagues to join me in providing tax relief that will keep money in the hands of working families and encourage new investment in the economy    The BEA release can be found at
