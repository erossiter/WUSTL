                                                                                                                                                                                      Senate stands with Britain in wake of terrorist attacks  July 11  2005  Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   and his senate colleagues joined together to condemn last weeks terrorist attacks in London and express their solidarity with the people of the United Kingdom     The Senate voted 76 0 on a resolution  S  Res  193  that pledges the U S  to remain shoulder to shoulder with the people of the United Kingdom to bring the terrorists responsible for these brutal acts to justice    The resolution also calls upon the international community to renew and strengthen efforts to dismantle terrorist networks  increase cooperation to advance personal and religious freedoms and combat social injustice  oppression  poverty and extremism that breeds terrorism      end                                                        July 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                Current record
