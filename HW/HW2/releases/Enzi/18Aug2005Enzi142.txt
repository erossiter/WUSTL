                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi helps Burlington High get computers  August 18  2005 Washington  D C   The office of U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo  helped Burlington High School get 10 computers as part of the Computers for Learning Program  The program was developed to allow the donation of surplus federal computer equipment to schools and educational non profit organizations     According to Burlington High Principal George Risberg  the computers will be used in the publications lab  where students produce the yearbook and learn how to use desktop publications     We greatly appreciate this  said Risberg    Burlington signed up for the program in July 2004  This is the first time they have been awarded the computers  Financial need and the length of time schools or non profits have been signed up for the program are considered when distributing computers     Enzi encourages schools that may be interested in the Computers for Learning program to register online at www computers fed gov     end                                                        August 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                   Current record
