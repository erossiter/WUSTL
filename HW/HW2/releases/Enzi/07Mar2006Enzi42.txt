                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi applauds Asian border opening for beef  March 7  2006 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   applauded the recent announcement by Agriculture Secretary Mike Johanns that Malaysia will once again import beef from the United States    The tide is turning in Asia for U S  beef  The dominoes that fell are being put upright once again as more countries realize that the quality and safety of Wyoming and all U S  produced beef is second to none  Enzi said  U S  beef producers did  1 9 million worth of business with Malaysia before they closed their borders in 2003  I am pleased American beef is being welcomed back to the dinner table    Once final documentation is completed the U S  will be able to export boneless beef from animals 30 months of age and younger to Malaysia     In 2003 Malaysia banned all U S  beef and beef products following the detection of BSE                                                       March 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                   Current record
