                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi continues federal budget hawk efforts  May 22  2007  Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   is working on a bill that would revamp the way the federal government handles taxpayer dollars  get the books on track and out of debt    Enzi is cosponsoring the SOS  Stop Over Spending Act of 2007  a comprehensive bill that would rein in spending  reduce the deficit and inject some reality into the federal budget process   American households would be bankrupt if they ran their finances the way the federal government does  It is time to stop insisting Uncle Sam pick up the tab for everything  In reality  the American taxpayers are the ones picking up the tab  Its past time we put responsible spending controls in place  Thats what this comprehensive bill is all about  said Enzi who is a senior member of the Senate Budget Committee  This bill turns red into black and puts us on solid ground with four solid budget pillars   spending restraint  budget enforcement  fiscal responsibility and deficit reduction    The bill would define deficit reduction targets and put in place budget caps  It would require emergency spending to be counted against budget caps and establish across the board cuts if the caps were exceeded  Under the SOS bill  the President would have the ability to single out specific egregious spending provisions within bills and send them back to Congress for a second look  SOS would put Congress on a biennial budget time line similar to how the Wyoming Legislature works  Appropriations would include funding for two years instead of one to give more time for oversight of that spending   Senator Judd Gregg  R N H  authored the legislation and Senator Craig Thomas  R Wyo   also co sponsored the bill                                                       May
