                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces federal contract help for small businesses  September 1  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   is pleased to announce that Wyoming small businesses will have help in selling their services and products to national  state and local governments thanks to a recently awarded federal grant     The Government Resources and Opportunities for Business  GRO Biz  in Cheyenne  Wyoming will be receiving  266 000 from the Defense Logistics Agency with the University of Wyoming and the Wyoming Business Council contributing funds for a total of  515 845 for GRO Biz    Im pleased to know GRO Biz will continue to provide assistance navigating the sometimes confusing channels of government procurement  said Enzi  a member of the Senate Small Business Committee    GRO Biz is one of 93 Procurement Technical Assistance Centers in the U S  that help small businesses that cannot afford the marketing and procurement staff that large government contractors maintain  The funds are utilized to provide assistance to small businesses that are seeking to participate in Department of Defense and other federal agency procurement contracts    The award will take effect on October 1  2005     Enzi requested funding for the 93 procurement centers in letters to the Defense Appropriations Subcommittee and also co sponsored a conference in 2002  2003 and 2004 with GRO Biz  The conferences focused on bringing Wyoming  Colorado  Southern Montana and South Dakota small businesses  federal and state agencies and contractors together to save time  money and marketing expenses for small businesses interested in selling to the government  Enzi is also planning another procurement conference for 2006 with GRO Biz     For more information about GRO Biz and its services visit
