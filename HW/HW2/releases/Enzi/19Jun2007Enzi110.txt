                                                                                                                                                                                      U S  Attorney Information   Applications Due June 26  June 19  2007 Washington  D C    Following the June 7 resignation of Matt Mead as U S  Attorney for    Wyoming   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   is accepting letters of interest and resumes from people interested in filling the spot  U S  Attorneys are nominated by the President of the    United States and confirmed by the U S  Senate for a four year term  but they serve at the pleasure of the President  The White House has asked for Senator Enzis input on nominees  Enzi plans to review information from those interested in serving and then he will submit three or more names to the White House  Applicants will be interviewed by the Administration and the President will choose his nominee  June 26 is the deadline for resumes and letters of interest to be submitted to Enzi  Items should be sent to Enzis D C  office by fax  202 228 0359   or by contacting Tara Ord  202 224 3424  tara_ord enzi senate gov  The primary job of a    United States attorney is to prosecute violations of federal law and defend the federal government in civil suits                                                      June 2007 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                               Current record
