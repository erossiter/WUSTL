                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi  Senate back Rice as Secretary of State  January 26  2005 Washington  D C   The United States Senate approved the nomination of Condoleezza Rice to be the next Secretary of State Wednesday morning     The Senate confirmed her nomination with a vote of 85 13     U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   put his full support behind the nomination of Rice and made the following comments on her expertise     With her experience the last four years as National Security Advisor to President Bush  she comes prepared for this position like no other person could  She knows our President and his foreign policy and national security issues  She will arrive at a new job with a full understanding of the President s plan for our chief diplomat     I have had the privilege of working with Dr  Rice during her tenure as National Security Advisor  In 2001  Dr  Rice played an instrumental role in the Senate s passage of S  149  the Export Administration Act of 2001  a bill I introduced in 2000  S  149 was a strong bill that would have modernized our national export control system for dual use items and technology  I look forward to working again with Dr  Rice in her new capacity as Secretary of State                                                        January 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
