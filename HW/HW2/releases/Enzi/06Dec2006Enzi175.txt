                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi comments on Gates nomination for Secretary of Defense  December 6  2006 Washington  D C   The U S  Senate approved President Bushs nomination of Robert Gates by a vote of 95 2 to be the next Secretary of Defense  U S  Sen  Mike Enzi voted in favor of the nomination  The following are comments from Enzi about the Gates nomination  United States military policy is in a period of transition and Dr  Gates is part of that transition  He will help lead it  I believe he is qualified for the job  The Armed Services Committee unanimously recommended him to the full Senate  He has experience as an officer in the Air Force and in the intelligence community  He has served multiple administrations  He has also led a major university   Effective management of our armed forces should not be executed only through a top down approach  Our field commanders on the ground are integral in decision making  From what I heard during Dr  Gates testimony before the Armed Services Committee he understands this  I am hopeful he understands he needs to be willing to consider and advise on a variety of options  Its also important in our fight against terrorism that we continually improve the relationship between our armed forces and our intelligence community  Dr  Gates has extensive experience with the CIA and experience in the Air Force   On the long list of Dr  Gates accomplishments  awards and decorations it is noted that he is a fellow Eagle Scout  I have a special appreciation for that                                                       December 2006 News Releases           Browse by
