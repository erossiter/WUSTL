                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi praises coal to liquids technology  January 12  2007 Medicine Bow Fuel  Power  LLC  a subsidiary of DKRW Advanced Fuels LLC  has reached an agreement to sell 100 percent of the ultra low sulfur diesel fuel to Sinclair Oil  Production of that fuel is slated to come from its planned coal to liquids facility in the Medicine Bow Hanna area of Wyoming  U S  Senator Mike Enzi is pleased with the progress    If we are serious about finding alternatives to foreign energy sources  coal to liquids projects should be front and center in our efforts  Todays agreement is a step in the right direction to move forward with an important project in my home state that could bring jobs to the towns of Medicine Bow and Hanna  U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R WY  said  Agreements like this make clear that private industry is serious about developing coal to liquids technologies in Wyoming  Private industry and the federal government should work together to send this technology from the drawing board to the construction phase  The use of coal to liquids technologies also provides the potential to expand usage of Wyomings coal reserves  I look forward to working with my colleagues in the Senate to continue pushing for the advancement of coal to liquids projects in the 110th Congress  Enzi said                                                        January 2007 News Releases           Browse by
