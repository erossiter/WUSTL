Riverton  Buffalo  Green River capture Cherry trophy  April 18  2005 Washington  D C   The team from Wyoming Senator Mike Enzi s office won the Capitol Hill Senate team title in the 33rd annual Cherry Blossom 10 mile run  according to official results released Thursday    Bill Novotny of Buffalo led Enzi s team Frenzi to victory over reigning champs from the office of fellow western senator Mike Crapo  R Idaho     Novotny clocked a time of 1 13 16 in the Washington  DC run     Michelle Dirst of Green River led the Frenzi women with a time of 1 16 29     Drew Smith was called in to substitute for Riverton s Reagen Bebout who was required to fulfill other obligations as Wyoming s Cherry Blossom princess  Smith finished the race in 1 20 06     Adam Briddell of Pleasantville  Iowa serves as aide for Enzi on the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  which Enzi chairs  Briddell brought in a time of 1 27 15     Riverton runner Kara Calvert rounded out the Enzi team time  3 49 51  with her mark of 1 35 20     My staff works hard  whether it s running down information for me on a bill or running a challenging 10 mile course through our nation s capital  They go the distance for Wyoming  Enzi said    About 500 staffers from the Senate and House participated in the race                                                      April 2005 News Releases           Browse
