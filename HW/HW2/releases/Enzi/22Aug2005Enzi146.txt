                                                                                                                                                                                      President releases federal disaster relief funds  Delegation  Aid will provide certainty for residents of Wright August 22  2005 Washington  D C   Sens  Craig Thomas  Mike Enzi  and Rep  Barbara Cubin announced today that the President has declared a major disaster for the State of Wyoming  which triggers the release of Federal funds to Campbell County to help them recover from damage resulting from the tornado  We are pleased this important funding is now available for Wright  We saw first hand the tremendous damage to the town from the tornado  We hope this funding provides some of the necessary assistance to families and individuals in need  especially folks with no insurance  This is great news for a community who has gone through a lot these last 10 days  Were pleased to see the Wyoming community come together for the town of Wright  the delegation said  The delegation contacted the President in support of federal assistance for Wyoming and for the town of Wright  Under the declaration  Campbell County receives benefits under the Individual Assistance program and Wyoming receives benefits under Hazard Mitigation program  Disaster victims can contact FEMA through their toll free Tele registration number  1 800 621 FEMA  3362   TTD 1 800 462 7585     end                                                        August 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                               Current record
