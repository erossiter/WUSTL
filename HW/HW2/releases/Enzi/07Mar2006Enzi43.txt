                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi supports tool for fiscal responsibility  March 7  2006 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   co sponsored legislation this week that would provide line item veto power to the President  Enzi believes this is a tool that could be used to decrease excessive government spending earmarks    As a fiscal conservative and member of the Senate Budget Committee  Enzi co sponsored the Line Item Rescission Act of 2006  S  2381  because of the positive effects it could have within the government     The line item veto can be very useful in keeping spending in check  Enzi said  The President will be able to slash inessential spending projects that crept their way into legislation  Unnecessary spending runs rampant in Washington and this is a power that  if used wisely  could curtail the culture of spending     Enzi has been an advocate for the line item veto since he was elected to the Senate in 1996     Balancing a budget and keeping the cost of government down is not and should not be a partisan issue  The potential savings are great and government owes it to the taxpayers to put in place this important tool in controlling spending                                                       March 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                  Current record
