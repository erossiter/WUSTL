                                                                                                                                                                                      HELP Committee chairman Enzi  ranking member Kennedy  urge President to nominate FDA Commissioner  February 1  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R WY  Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions  HELP  Committee  and Senator Ted Kennedy  D MA  the Committee s Ranking Member  today urged the President to nominate a permanent Commissioner of the Food and Drug Administration  FDA  and pledged to move expeditiously to hold confirmation hearings on a qualified nominee     The nomination and confirmation of a Commissioner of Food and Drugs is necessary to bring to bear the expertise  resources and authority of that agency in the most effective manner possible  Enzi and Kennedy wrote in a letter released today  An Acting Commissioner  regardless of his or her qualifications and accomplishments  simply lacks the mandate for action that comes only through the Constitutional process that includes the Commissioner s nomination by the President and subsequent confirmation by the Senate     The Senators described the FDA as at a crucial juncture in its history  and emphasized that Congress and the public are intensely scrutinizing the agency s processes for reviewing drug applications and for ensuring drug safety  At the same time  advances in genetics  pharmacology  and related fields soon will challenge conventional regulatory approaches  Enzi and Kennedy wrote     The FDA needs a leader with the vision to guide the agency through this critical period and maintain the public s confidence in its work  the Senators said  The letter also was signed by 15 other Senators  all who serve on the HELP Committee     The Senators urged the President to identify and nominate a candidate for FDA Commissioner who has demonstrated leadership capabilities  a strong knowledge of science  and an unwavering commitment to promoting and protecting the public health as soon as possible  and promised to hold confirmation hearings quickly  Placing a strong and effective Commissioner at the helm of the FDA will be essential to support the work of the President s newly installed head of the Department of Health and Human Services  Secretary Mike Leavitt  Enzi and
