                                                                                                                                                                                      Simpson joins Enzi  s DC team   April 18  2005    Washington  D C   Rapid City resident Sarah Simpson recently became the newest addition to the office of Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   with her appointment as research assistant    Simpson researches constituent concerns and issues related to technology and telecommunications  budget  taxes  transportation  banking  stock options  accounting  housing  and postal reform    Its a privilege to work for such an outstanding senator who brings a sense of Western values and ethics to Washington  D C  Joining the dedicated people of Team Enzi requires hard work  and I look forward to contributing to the team  said Simpson    Simpson  daughter of Paul and Terri Simpson of Rapid City  graduated from Stevens High School in 1999 and went on to earn a bachelors degree in communication from the University of Wyoming in 2003     Previous to joining Enzis staff  Simpson worked as the marketing coordinator for Fountain Springs Community in Rapid City and coordinated events in Western South Dakota for the 2004 John Thune for U S  Senate campaign    I knew Sarahs extensive experience would help her be a beneficial asset to our team  said Enzi  With Sarahs knowledge and experience she will be able to serve the people of Wyoming well    end                                                        April 2005 News Releases           Browse by
