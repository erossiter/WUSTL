                                                                                                                                                                                      Low interest loans available to Wright tornado victims  August 24  2005 Washington  D C   Senators Craig Thomas  Mike Enzi  and Representative Barbara Cubin announced today that due to the presidential disaster declaration for the town of Wright Small Business Administration low interest loans are available to those affected by the tornado    We are pleased that these loans will provide one more way for the citizens of Wright to get back on their feet and begin to rebuild  We congratulate Wright and all the people who live there and who volunteered  There was an incredible outpouring of support that showed the true spirit of our state  the delegation said    The Small Business Administration is accepting applications for physical and economic injury disaster loans  Applications for physical damages are due by October 21  2005 and economic injury disaster loan applications are due by May 22  2006    Applications and more information can be accessed at http   www sba gov disaster_recov index html     end                                                       August 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
