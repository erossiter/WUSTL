                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi helps Torrington High get computers  August 18  2005 Washington  D C   The office of Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo  helped Torrington High School get five computers as part of the Computers for Learning Program  The program was developed to allow the donation of surplus federal computer equipment to schools and educational non profit organizations     According to District Technology Coordinator Jamie Haymaker  the computers will be used in new labs in the high school    Torrington High School signed up for the program in May 2003  This is the first time they have been awarded the computers  Financial need and the length of time schools or non profits have been signed up for the program are considered when distributing computers     Enzi encourages schools that may be interested in the Computers for Learning program to register online at www computers fed gov     end                                                        August 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                  Current record
