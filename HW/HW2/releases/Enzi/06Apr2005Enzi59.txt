                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Sublette county airport grant  April 6  2005 Washington  D C   Sublette County is set to receive grant funding from the Federal Aviation Administration for a local airport master plan  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     The Sublette County towns of Marbleton and Big Piney  Wyoming  will receive  151 699 to update the existing Airport Layout Plan which will help future development at the airport    For more information on this project  please contact Mr  Tagg Guio  Airport Board President at Big Piney Marbleton Airport at  307  276 3714  or Craig A  Sparks the Department of Transportation Regional officer at  303  342 1254                                                       April 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                    Current record
