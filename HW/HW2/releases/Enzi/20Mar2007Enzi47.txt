                                                                                                                                                                                      President nominates Honaker Enzi applauds selection of Wyoming attorney to serve on U S  District Court March 20  2007 Washington  D C   With the support of Wyomings two U S  senators  President Bush nominated Rock Springs resident Richard Honaker to be the next U S  District Judge for the state of  Wyoming   Honaker must still be confirmed by the whole Senate in order to get the job  The following statement was made by U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   on Honakers nomination  Dick was born and raised in  Wyoming   was the first person in his family to get a college degree  has 30 years of law experience  is a former legislator and a small businessman  All of these qualities plus an admiration for the people of  Wyoming  and the rule of law make Dick an exceptional candidate to be a federal judge  Dick has what it takes to provide an impartial hand in applying the laws of the land  I look forward to seeing his nomination swiftly acted upon by the Senate                                                        March 2007 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
