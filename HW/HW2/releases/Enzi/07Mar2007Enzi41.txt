                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi comments on the life of Bob Peck  March 7  2007  Washington  D C   Bob is the ultimate person   newspaperman  legislator  historian  community activist  college supporter  philanthropist  photographer  futurist and friend    I served in the legislature with Bob  He always did his homework  He had an uncanny ability to see the big picture and the future  He was always forward looking  He wanted to make a difference   and he did  In newsman style he kept extensive notes everywhere he went   and backed it up with pictures from his ever present camera  His news background gave him focus and the ability to ask penetrating questions  He was encouraging to young people like Coy Knobel to whom he gave the privilege and responsibility to run the newspaper in Dubois  He was the first to suggest to me that I should consider the U S  Senate  He always backed his words with action  When I got in the race he was the first to do a fund raiser for me and did my nomination speech at state convention   Bob has always been a person of positive suggestions   an effective leader and a practical manager  We ll miss him  but I suspect God needed someone who could take good notes and write the ultimate story                                                           March 2007 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                    Current record
