                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Sublette county airport grant  February 11  2005 Washington  D C   Sublette County is set to receive grant funding from the Federal Aviation Administration for local airport improvements  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     Sublette County  in conjunction with the Ralph Wenz Field in Pinedale  Wyoming  will receive  450 000 to acquire land surrounding the airport  This will allow the airport to better protect the approach for airplanes     For more information on this project  please contact Ms  Lil Penton  Airport Board Treasurer at  307  367 6664  Craig A  Sparks the Department of Transportation Regional officer at  303  342 1254  or the Office of Congressional Affairs at  202  366 9714                                                       February 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
