                                                                                                                                                                                      Hedderman joins Enzi  s DC team  April 18  2005 Washington  D C   Anne Hedderman of Powell recently became the newest addition to the office of Senator Mike Enzi with her appointment as staff assistant    Hedderman  a 2000 graduate of Powell High School  greets constituents  writes letters  and processes flag requests while coordinating meeting times for the Senators conference room  In mid April  she will start coordinating tours and intern projects    It has been interesting to be in the unique atmosphere of D C  I have learned a lot about the workings of Congress and the process of democracy  I am enjoying living in such a dynamic city while still serving the state of Wyoming  said Hedderman     Hedderman  daughter of Larry and Gloria Hedderman of Powell  graduated from the University of Wyoming in December 2004 and received a bachelors degree in history     Annes extensive knowledge and her personable presence are a true Wyoming quality that all of our visitors can relate to and appreciate while visiting their nations capitol  said Enzi                                                         April 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Current record
