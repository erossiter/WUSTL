                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi looks forward to working with new Fed Chairman Bernanke  January 31  2006    Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   a senior member        of the Senate Banking Committee  is confident in the ability of Ben Bernanke        to be the new Federal Reserve chairman    The Senate confirmed Bernanke by a voice vote earlier today  Enzi made the        following comments following the vote     I am pleased that today        the Senate has confirmed Mr  Bernanke to be the new Chairman of the Board        of Governors of the Federal Reserve  I have personally met with Bernanke        and am confident that he is the right man to follow Alan Greenspan as the        Presidents top economic advisor     Mr  Bernanke is one        of the brightest economic minds today  Along with his intellect  he is also        very plain spoken  These two qualities are easy to find in Wyoming and refreshing        to see in Washington  I look forward to working with him throughout his        tenure    Alan Greenspans 18 years of service to this country        have helped America maintain the strongest and most stable economy in the        world     The Chairman of the Federal Reserve is nominated by the President  confirmed        by the Senate and serves for 14 years                                                        January 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
