                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi praises Luthi promotion  July 23  2007 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   praised the hard work of Randall Luthi and congratulated him on his new position as Director of the Minerals Management Service in Washington  D C   I know former speaker of the Wyoming House  Randall Luthi  has done great work in his current position of deputy director of the U S  Fish and Wildlife Service so I am not surprised and am very pleased that his ability  focus and other talents have gotten him moved to an even more important role by the White House  said Enzi  As director of the Minerals Management Service  Luthi will be in charge of managing the nations natural gas  oil and other mineral resources on the outer continental shelf  The agency also collects  manages and disburses revenue from federal offshore and onshore mineral leases  Luthi was named deputy director of the U S  Fish and Wildlife Service in February of this year  Prior to the position Luthi served in the Department of the Interior and at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  Luthi is a former speaker of the Wyoming House of Representatives  a rancher and an attorney in private practice from Freedom  Wyo
