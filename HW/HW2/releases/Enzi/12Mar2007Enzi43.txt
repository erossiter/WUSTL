                                                                                                                                                                                      Wheatland firefighters hearing loud and clear thanks to grant  March 12  2007   Washington  D C    The Wheatland Volunteer Fire Department is set to receive federal funding for WyoLink radios  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   that would help improve communication and help firefighters more effectively help residents  The new radios will enable the Wheatland firefighters as well as other Platte County fire departments to better communicate with the State Fire Marshall office and the state Department of Homeland Security   The funding is provided through a county wide grant administered by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office of Grants and Training in cooperation with the U S  Fire Administration   Early last year Senator Enzi encouraged Wyoming fire departments to take advantage of the millions of dollars in federal grants by attending informational workshops about the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP      It is very important that fire departments have the opportunity to expand their equipment and training  said Enzi  Local firefighters put their lives on the line to protect their community  they should have access to the best resources possible   To date  20 different grants totaling more than  2 million have been given to the state of Wyoming from the fiscal year 2005 funding    In 2006 alone  the Department of Homeland Security awarded approximately  485 million nationally to fire departments and EMS organizations through the AFGP  Grant money can be used by recipients for firefighter health and safety programs  enhancing emergency medical services  the purchase of additional personal protection equipment  or modifications to fire stations and facilities   A current list of award recipients and dates announced is posted at www usfa fema gov  A complete list of applications  financial information and state fire department data is available at www firegrantsupport com   Fire departments with questions regarding the grant program can reach the fire grants help desk at 1 866 274 0960  or by writing to firegrants dhs gov  For more information on this specific grant contact Wheatland Volunteer Fire Department Chief  Scott
