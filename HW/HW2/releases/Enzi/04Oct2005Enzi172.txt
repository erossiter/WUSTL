                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi calls for student nominees to military academies Application deadline is November 30 October 4  2005    Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo  is giving Wyoming youth considering military careers the opportunity to apply for nomination to the nations Service Academies  Schools include the Air Force Academy  Naval Academy  Military Academy  and Merchant Marine Academy     Academy service is a great way for young men and women to serve our country and develop leadership skills  The academies provide excellent training and education and I am proud to be involved in a program that helps the best and the brightest serve our country  I encourage those who are interested to make the most of this opportunity  said Enzi    For every vacancy available at each of the academies  Enzi is able to nominate 10 young people  Nominations are based on SAT ACT test scores  an evaluation of leadership and extracurricular activities  applicants personal statement of interest  and letters of recommendation  Based on the applicants interview with Enzis selection committee  applicants are recommended to Enzi for final approval  Following the Senators nomination  final selection for an appointment will be made by the academy itself    All applications for service academies require a Congressional nomination  Applications for nomination are due to Enzis Gillette office by Nov  30  2005  Final nominees will be announced in late December  The application  procedures  and specific applicant criteria are available on line at http   enzi senate gov services htm under the heading Academy Nominations  For additional application information  contact State Director Robin Bailey at  307  682 6268 or Robin_Bailey enzi senate gov     end
