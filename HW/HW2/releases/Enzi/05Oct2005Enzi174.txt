                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi to meet with Burns  Pine Bluffs  Albin  and LaGrange residents and students during work week in Southeast Wyoming  October 5  2005    Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   has town meetings and school visits that will keep him on the road around southeast Wyoming on Wednesday Oct  12    Enzi plans to visit Eileen Cushings 6th grade class at West Elementary School in Burns at 9 15 a m     Enzi is then set to hold a school assembly at Pine Bluffs Jr  High and High School from 10 30   11 15 a m  in the commons area     Enzi plans to host a town meeting and ice cream social at the Pine Bluffs Community Center from 11 30 a m    1 p m  All area residents are encouraged to attend  Enzi is scheduled to speak to Albin Elementary School students starting at 2 20 p m  in the school cafeteria     Enzi is then set to hold a town meeting and ice cream social at the La Grange Town Hall from 4   5 30 p m  All area residents are encouraged to attend     The University of Wyoming homecoming parade and game on Saturday Oct  15 in Laramie are also events Enzi plans to attend     All plans subject to change due to the Senate voting schedule    end
