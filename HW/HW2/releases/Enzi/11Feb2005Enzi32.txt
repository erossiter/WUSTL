                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Bear River fire dept  grant  February 11  2005 Washington  D C   Bear River is set to receive federal funding for its operations and firefighter safety program which includes training  equipping  protection  wellness  and facility modifications according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     The Bear River Fire Department has been awarded a  13 320 grant from the Federal Emergency Management Agency s  FEMA  Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  program  The grants are awarded to private and public organizations recognized specifically for their experience and expertise in fire prevention or safety programs and activities  This award is specifically for fire prevention efforts of the Bear River Fire Department     This grant should help increase the efficiency and accuracy of the Bear River Fire Deptartment by having up to date equipment that will ensure the safety of Wyoming and its citizens by reducing the fire related injuries in Bear River and increasing prompt response  This will start fire safety education in Wyoming this year and encourage more Wyoming based safety and fire prevention organizations to develop new fire prevention projects  said Enzi  I am confident that these grants will give Wyoming fire departments the equipment and knowledge they need to respond to any hazards they may be faced with  This grant money will help ensure the Bear River Fire Department is prepared     Fire departments with questions regarding the AFG program can reach the FEMA Grant Program staff at 1 866 274 0960  or via email at usfagrants fema gov  For current information
