                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Rock Springs fire dept  grant  January 27  2005 Washington  D C   Rock Springs is set to receive federal funding for their fire prevention safety program which involves awareness  education  enforcement  installation  and detection activities  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     The Rock Springs Fire Department has been awarded an  18 695 Fire Prevention and Safety  FPS  grant from the Federal Emergency Management Agency s  FEMA  Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  program  The FPS grants are awarded to private and public organizations recognized specifically for their experience and expertise in fire prevention or safety programs and activities  This award is specifically for fire prevention efforts of the Rock Springs Fire Department in its public education and fire code awareness  fire inspector certification activities  purchase and installation of smoke alarms and fire suppression systems  wild land mitigation  and arson prevention and detection activities     This grant should help reduce the number of fires and fire related injuries in Rock Springs and Wyoming this year and encourage more Wyoming based safety and fire prevention organizations to develop new fire prevention projects  said Enzi  I am confident that these grants will give Wyoming fire departments the equipment and knowledge they need to respond to any hazards they may be faced with  said Enzi  This grant money will help ensure the Rock Springs Fire Department is prepared     Fire departments with questions regarding the AFG program can reach the FEMA Grant Program staff at 1 866 274 0960  or via email at usfagrants fema gov  For current information regarding these grant awards  visit www usfa fema gov
