                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi lauds O  Connor  s service  July 1  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   made the following comments regarding Justice Sandra Day OConnors recent announcement of her resignation from the United States Supreme Court    Justice OConnor has served with honor and integrity during her tenure on the bench of the Supreme Court  She has left a legacy of a supreme knowledge of the law and has diligently served this country while interpreting the foundational document of our country    As the first woman nominated to the Supreme Court  she can leave the bench knowing that she has impacted the course of many lives  Her life is a fine example of a woman who  in the face of adversity  educated herself and rose to one of the highest offices in this country  She has left a positive mark on history and this country  Her thoughtful insight and substantial contributions to the Supreme Court will be missed     end                                                       July 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                    Current record
