                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi highlights U S  job creation  March 10  2006 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   praised the state of Americas growing economy and commented on positive job creation numbers released today by the Bureau of Labor Statistics    The numbers released today reflect a positive economy across the entire country  About 243 000 new jobs were created in February along with over 2 million new jobs created over the past year  Following the devastating hurricanes  the ongoing war on terrorism and high energy prices it is pleasing to see our economy continues to grow  I will continue to work on legislation that will provide tax relief for businesses and workers  affordable healthcare for our nations families and continue to encourage fiscal discipline while being a good steward of the taxpayer dollar    In Wyoming unemployment levels are low but the housing shortage is high  Now one of our tasks is to provide housing for workers we are trying to recruit  If we want Wyomingites we need to have decent forms of housing for their families  Im also working on job training legislation to help workers obtain the skills they need to move up the ladder  Next week in the Senate Health Committee I hope to finish a bill that will help hundreds of thousands of people working in small businesses get health insurance     The full Bureau of Labor Statistics release can be found at http
