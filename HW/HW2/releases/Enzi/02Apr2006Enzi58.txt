                                                                                                                                                                                      Wyoming to receive HIV AIDS funding  April 2  2006 Washington  D C   Wyoming is set to receive  360 347 in federal grant funding to help people in the state living with HIV AIDS  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     The funding comes from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services and can be used to provide medical care  support services and prescription drugs for people with HIV AIDS     Basic grants are awarded based on the estimated number of people living with AIDS in a state  States may designate a portion of their award to support AIDS Drug Assistance Programs  ADAPs   which are state run programs that assist with the purchase of medication       end                                                         April 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                      Current record
