                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi plans events in Burns  Laramie  Riverton  and Douglas  May 22  2007 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   plans to meet with residents  address various organizations and attend meetings while traveling in Wyoming over Memorial Day week    Tuesday  May 29   Burns   Enzi is set to host a town meeting and ice cream social at the Community Center at 134 S  Main Street starting at 5 p m  in Burns   Weds  May 30   Laramie   Enzi plans to attend the dedication ceremony for the Gale McGee Post Office at 1 30 p m  at the Laramie Post Office located at 152 N  5th  Enzi is expected to make remarks at the ceremony    Friday  June 1   Riverton  Enzi is slated to speak to attendees of the Wyoming Stock Growers Summer Convention at noon at the Riverton Holiday Inn on N  Federal Blvd  and Sunset Dr    Sat  June 2   Douglas  Enzi is set to speak to the Wyoming Boys State convention starting at 11 30 a m  at the Douglas Fairgrounds   All plans are subject to change  pending the Senate voting schedule  For more information on these events please contact Robin Bailey in Enzis Gillette office at 307 682 6268 or Sandy Tinsley in Enzis Casper office at 307 261 6572
