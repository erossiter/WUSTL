                                                                                                                                                                                      HELP committee chairman Enzi announces selection of subcommittee chairmen for 109th Congress  February 2  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi   R WY   Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  HELP Committee  today announced the selection of Chairmen to lead four Subcommittee panels for the 109th Congress     HELP Committee members formally elected two veterans  Senator Lamar Alexander  R TN  and Senator Mike DeWine  R OH   and two freshmen  Senator Richard Burr  R NC  and Senator Johnny Isakson  R GA  to serve as the leaders of the newly reorganized Subcommittees     It is a pleasure to have four capable and industrious Senators willing to take charge of these important panels  which I expect will play key roles in developing the legislation that moves through the HELP Committee  Enzi said  I look forward to a very productive session with the HELP Committee  and am encouraged by the selection of such outstanding members to Chair these Subcommittees     During the HELP Committee s organizational meeting Wednesday  the members formally ratified the following slate of Subcommittees and Chairmen    Education and Early Childhood Development Subcommittee    Senator Lamar Alexander  Chairman   Bioterrorism Preparedness and Public Health Subcommittee    Senator Richard Burr  Chairman   Employment and Workplace Safety Subcommittee    Senator Johnny Isakson  Chairman   Retirement Security and Aging Subcommittee    Senator Mike DeWine  Chairman    Through the work of this Committee  we have an outstanding opportunity to work with all of our members  both Republican and Democrat  to ensure a quality education  affordable  quality health care  and safe  reliable pensions for all Americans  Enzi added
