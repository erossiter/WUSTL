                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi helps Wheatland Middle School get computers  August 18  2005 Washington  D C   The office of Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo  helped Wheatland Middle School get 10 computers as part of the Computers for Learning Program  The program was developed to allow the donation of surplus federal computer equipment to schools and educational non profit organizations     The Intel Pentium III computers will be used in the schools secondary lab and in classrooms for special projects and web browsing  according to Wheatland Middle School Principal Steve Lloyd     Were very appreciative  says Lloyd  Its going to really boost our technology program here  which is nice     Wheatland signed up for the program in May of this year  This is the first time they have been awarded the computers  Financial need and the length of time schools or non profits have been signed up for the program are considered when distributing computers     Enzi encourages schools that may be interested in the Computers for Learning program to register online at www computers fed gov     end                                                        August 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                 Current record
