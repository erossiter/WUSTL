                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Vedauwoo firefighting grant   December 15  2005 Washington  D C   The Vedauwoo Volunteer Fire Department is set to receive federal grant funding to combat fires  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo    The Assistance to Firefighters Grant program  AFG  awarded the fire department  21 442 in direct assistance to be used for expanding fire prevention programs  continuing firefighter safety  and improving firefighting effectiveness    Wildfires are a problem Wyomings plains and mountains unfortunately see every year and they create a major concern for the state  Even though were getting hit with sub zero temperatures and snow right now  this grant will ensure resources are available for those dry summer months  said Enzi     This grant is part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant program for 2005  The program received 21 000 applications for over  2 9 billion  Grants are awarded on a needs basis to help local fire departments fund the purchase of firefighting equipment  fund firefighter health and safety programs  enhance emergency medical services programs and conduct fire education and prevention programs  Last year this district was awarded a grant of  160 000 to go toward purchasing a firefighting vehicle    The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program is administered by the Office for Domestic Preparedness through the Department of Homeland Security  in cooperation with the Departments United States Fire Administration  USFA   to reduce life and economic losses due to fire and fire related emergencies     Fire departments with questions regarding the AFG program can reach the FEMA Grant Program staff at 1 866 274 0960  or via email at firegrants dhs gov  For current information regarding these grant awards  visit www usfa fema gov
