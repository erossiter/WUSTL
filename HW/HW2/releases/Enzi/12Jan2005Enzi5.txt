                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi comments on Bush education initiatives  January 12  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee commented on the President s recently announced education initiatives designed to equip students with the skills necessary to succeed in today s changing workforce     I applaud the President s commitment to high school reform  High schools are an integral part of our public education system  and more effective federal support is an important part of improving student achievement  particularly with respect to math  science  and reading skills  said Enzi     I look forward to studying the President s proposals in greater detail and working closely with his Administration as we expand the national conversation about high school reform     Enzi also said it is important to continue to support vocational and technical education as a critical component of high school education                                                        January 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                  Current record
