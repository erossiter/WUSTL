                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Albany county airport grant  February 11  2005 Washington  D C   Albany county is set to receive grant funding from the Federal Aviation Administration for local airport improvements  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo     Albany County  in conjunction with the Laramie Regional Airport  will receive  320 000 to construct the third phase of an airplane taxiway that runs parallel to the current runway  This new taxiway will help improve the safety of the runway by allowing an efficient line of sight for pilots on the runway     For more information on this project  please contact Mr  Fred Okers  Chairman of the Laramie Regional Airport Board at  307  742 4161  Craig A  Sparks the Department of Transportation Regional officer at  303  342 1254  or the Office of Congressional Affairs at  202  366 9714                                                        February 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Current record
