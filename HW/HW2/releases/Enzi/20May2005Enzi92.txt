                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces new Wyoming buses  May 20  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   announced today that Jackson  Cheyenne and Casper will soon be able to purchase new buses to assist the cities with transit needs     The Wyoming Department of Transportation  WYDOT  will be awarded a total of  954 000 to purchase buses for the cities  The money comes from an allocation in the Fiscal Year 2003 Transportation Appropriations bill that Enzi helped secure for Wyoming bus and bus related facilities     These cities are growing and so are their transportation needs  said Enzi  I worked to ensure Wyoming communities were not left out of the transit funding equation because urban areas are not the only areas with transit needs    Cheyenne will be able to purchase one bus and Jackson and Casper will get three new buses     About  1 5 million remains to be allocated to WYDOT at a later date for Wyoming bus and bus related facilities from the  2 5 million original Fiscal Year 2003 bus earmark  For questions please contact the Wyoming Department of Transportation at 307 777 4375        end                                                        May 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
