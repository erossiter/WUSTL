                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi pleased with Burton pick  October 27  2005    Washington  D C   U S  Sen  Mike Enzi praised Secretary of the Interior Gale Nortons choice to become the Acting Assistant Secretary for Land and Minerals Management    Norton chose Johnnie Burton  now the Minerals Management Service Director  to act in place of the departing Rebecca Watson as the Assistant Secretary for Land and Minerals Management  until a permanent replacement can be found   I worked with Johnnie in the Wyoming Legislature and have noted her excellent work since joining the Department of Interior in 2002  Johnnie has vast experience in this area and the skills to practically apply that knowledge  Enzi said  Johnnie is a good choice to fill this position    The Assistant Secretary for Land and Minerals Management is tasked with guiding policies for and providing oversight to the Bureau of Land Management  the Minerals Management Service and the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement    Burton is the former head of the Wyoming Department of Revenue and she has also been the president of oil and gas related companies that did work in Wyoming    end                                                        October
