                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi statement on the assassination of Benazir Bhutto  former Pakistani Prime Minister  December 28  2007  In the wake of the assassination of former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto  Senator Enzi issued the following statement  I was deeply saddened by the news of former Prime Minister Benazir Bhuttos assassination  I had the pleasure of meeting her through the National Prayer Breakfast  She has attended the heads of state and former heads of state dinner preceding the National Prayer Breakfast that Diana and I have hosted  I join President Bush in condemning this cowardly act and encourage the people of Pakistan to continue the democratic process which she so strongly believed in  My thoughts and prayers are with Prime Minister Bhuttos family                                                         December 2007 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
