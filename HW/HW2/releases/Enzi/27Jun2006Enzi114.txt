                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi applauds Wyo   s 89 percent prescription drug coverage  June 27  2006 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R  Wyo   is pleased so many Wyoming seniors are taking advantage of Medicare prescription drug coverage  As of June 11  89 percent of eligible Wyoming seniors either have signed up for prescription drug coverage through Medicare Part D or already have coverage through other programs   When I visited with seniors from Cheyenne to Sheridan to Rock Springs and points in between explaining the merits of the program and urging them to sign up I was impressed with the turnout and interest  I commend Wyoming volunteers for their efforts and I urge those seniors who have not yet signed up to do so at the next available date in November  said Enzi  Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  Of the 71 866 eligible Medicare beneficiaries in Wyoming  63 695 had prescription drug coverage either through Medicare Part D or coverage through a retirement  veterans or other programs as of June 11  Seniors who did not make the May 15 Medicare Part D deadline can sign up again on November 15  Over 38 million Americans with Medicare are now receiving prescription drug coverage   Wyoming has its own Senior Health Insurance Information Program with staff trained by Medicare who can answer questions and address the concerns of Wyoming seniors  Janet Hackleman in
