                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi  Cuba report advocates status quo policy  July 10  2006 Washington  D C    The Commission for Assistance to a Free Cuba released its second report to the President today  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   made the following comments  While I am pleased this report recognizes the role that Cubans themselves must play in the transition to a new government  I believe our current policies make the transition to a free and democratic Cuba a more difficult task for the Cuban people  Our policy toward Cuba reminds me of a saying from my dad  If you keep doing what you have always been doing  you re going to wind up getting what you already got  We need to be taking action now to share ideas with the people of Cuba instead of closing the door to sharing the American experience of freedom  Senator Enzi is a cofounder of the Senate Cuba Working Group and has led the effort in the Senate to allow American citizens the freedom to travel to Cuba     end                                                        July 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                     Current record
