                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Natrona County airport grant  April 6  2005 Washington  D C   Natrona County is set to receive grant funding from the Federal Aviation Administration for airport improvements  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo    Natrona County International Airport will receive  650 000 in grant funds to design the reconstruction and rehabilitation of a taxiway and add a loading bridge to the newly modified terminal building  The construction of a new loading bridge will bring the airport into compliance with the American Disabilities Act    The grant  from the Airport Improvement Program  AIP   is part of an overall effort to assist with airport maintenance  planning and repairs in Wyoming  The AIP provides funding to airports to assist them in development and planning  Airports are able to use the money on projects that support aircraft operations including runways  taxiways  aprons  noise abatement  land purchase  safety and emergency or snow removal equipment     For more information on this project  please contact Craig A  Sparks the Department of Transportation Regional officer at  303  342 1254                                                      April 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                  Current record
