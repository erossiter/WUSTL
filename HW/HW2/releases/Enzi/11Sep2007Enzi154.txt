                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi marks six years after Sept  11   September 11  2007    Washington  D C    Following are remarks by U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   regarding the sixth year following the terrorist attacks on our nation   As the war on terrorism was in the forefront of discussion today on Capitol Hill it became apparent that our country was drastically changed the day we were attacked  We have not forgotten the fear  surprise and grief of that morning  Those who are responsible will continue to be hunted and brought to justice  We remember those whose lives were taken as they tried to save the lives of those around them in the rubble on the tip of Manhattan  in a field in Pennsylvania and in the wreckage of the Pentagon with great respect and admiration  Heroes were made on September 11  2001 and heroes were lost  We will not forget them   It is our duty to tell our children about that day and to honor those who are fighting for us now  Whether it be by joining hands in prayer at church  taking part in a community walk  writing a letter to a member of our armed services  or simply lowering your head in a moment of silence  we must never forget that day and never forget all that we are thankful for  For
