                                                                                                                                                                                      George Washington University honors Enzi  June 16  2006   Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   joined the ranks of J  Edgar Hoover and Colin Powell when he was awarded by the George Washington Alumni Association in May   Enzi received the Distinguished Alumni Achievement Award  the highest honor bestowed by     George    Washington    University to its alumni  at an annual reception held during graduation week   Ambassador Edward Skip Gnehm  Jr   a George Washington alumnus and Enzis college roommate  nominated Enzi who was picked from a number of nominations based on his lasting impact on society through outstanding professional accomplishments   I did a lot of learning and growing when I attended college and it is an honor to have such an award bestowed to me by an institution that did so much for me  said Enzi  I hope that every student who desires a college education will be able to achieve their dreams   Enzi graduated from the     George    Washington    University in 1966 with a degree in accounting  Enzi went on to the     University of    Denver where he earned a Master s Degree in Retail Marketing in 1968  and received an honorary doctorate in 2005                                                           June 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
