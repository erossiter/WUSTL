                                                                                                                                                                                      Hawk Springs Fire District receives grant for truck  December 19  2006 Washington  D C   The Hawk Springs Fire Protection District  5 has been selected to receive federal grant funding for vehicle acquisition  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R  Wyo   The Department is set to receive  171 000 for purchasing a new fire truck for their department through the Department of Homeland Securitys Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  AFGP   Hawk Springs is a small community  but their needs are big  They have applied for this grant in the past  and finally their request has been answered  said Enzi It is encouraging to see our states fire departments continually improve their services and apply for and receive these grants  The AFGP funds fire departments in an effort to strengthen the nations overall preparedness and ability to respond to fire and fire related hazards  Grant money is awarded directly to fire departments and non affiliated emergency medical service organizations to better their ability to serve and protect the public                                                      December 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Current record
