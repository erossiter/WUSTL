                                                                                                                                                                                      Presidential inauguration brings Wyoming visitors to DC  January 20  2005 Washington  D C   Today U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   attended the second term inauguration of George W  Bush  the 43rd President of the United States     Enzi commented about the week and our President     Washington is alive this week  Crowds of people from all over the country are here to celebrate a rite of our democracy  the inauguration of our President     I m happy to report that hundreds of people are here from Wyoming  We are celebrating the inauguration of a Vice President from Wyoming  My staff and I have been helping people from the state feel at home here all week  It s an exciting chance to live history and I m glad to be part of it     President Bush is a solid leader with unwavering values who puts America s interests first  I m hopeful that with cooperation from members of both parties in the Senate and House  the President will help us advance a visionary energy policy  sound Social Security reforms  address problems in our healthcare and educational systems and put in place disciplined fiscal practices                                                        January 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
