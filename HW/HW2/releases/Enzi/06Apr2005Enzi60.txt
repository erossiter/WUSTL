                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi announces Sweetwater County airport grant  April 6  2005 Washington  D C   Sweetwater County is set to receive grant funding from the Federal Aviation Administration for airport improvements  according to U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo  The Rock Springs Sweetwater County Airport will receive grant funding of  1 947 528 to be used to reconstruct the commercial service apron that has suffered from extreme weathering and deterioration  New airfield guidance signs will also be installed     The grant  from the Airport Improvement Program  AIP   is part of an overall effort to assist with airport maintenance  planning and repairs in Wyoming  The AIP provides funding to airports to assist them in development and planning  Airports are able to use the money on projects that support aircraft operations including runways  taxiways  aprons  noise abatement  land purchase  safety and emergency or snow removal equipment     For more information on this project  please contact Craig A  Sparks the Department of Transportation Regional officer at  303  342 1254                                                       April 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                   Current record
