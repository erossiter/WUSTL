                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi looks forward to working with Kempthorne  May 26  2006  Washington  D C    The U S  Senate today approved President Bushs nomination of Idaho Governor Dirk Kempthorne to be the next Secretary of the Interior  U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   voiced his support for Kempthorne following his nomination in March and is pleased he will be taking over the Department of Interior   Enzi served in the U S  Senate with Kempthorne and hailed his bipartisan efforts to modernize the Endangered Species Act   Dirk understands the complex problems western states grapple with on a daily basis concerning wildlife  public lands and natural resources  Hes grappled with them for years as a senator and governor  Ive worked with him on modernizing the Endangered Species Act and many other issues  I know his work ethic  He is what the department needs right now  said Enzi   Gale Norton resigned as Interior Secretary in March                                                         May 2006 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
