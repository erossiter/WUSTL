                                                                                                                                                                                      HELP unanimous on Spellings  January 6  2005 Washington  D C   In a unanimous vote that took place at about 2 50 p m  in the President s Room just off the Senate floor in the Capitol  the Senate Committee on Health  Education  Labor and Pensions favorably reported the nomination of Margaret Spellings  the President s nominee to be the Secretary of Education     Spellings  nomination must now go to the full Senate for consideration  No vote has been scheduled     I appreciate all the cooperation that resulted in the unanimous consent to suspend rules to get this done in such a timely fashion  said Committee Chairman Mike Enzi  R Wyo     In a very cooperative atmosphere we heard testimony and asked questions of Margaret Spellings  She was enthusiastic and knowledgeable with her responses  She has been working in the White House handling education policy and obviously working hard  said Chairman Enzi  I look forward to Secretary Designee Spellings being confirmed by the full Senate in the coming weeks                                                        January 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                    Current record
