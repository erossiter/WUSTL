                                                                                                                                                                                      Enzi hails progress of small business advisory committee  March 7  2005 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Mike Enzi  R Wyo   praised the addition of new members to the Securities and Exchange Commissions  SEC  Advisory Committee on Smaller Public Companies    I applaud SEC Chairman Donaldson for establishing the Advisory Committee on Smaller Public Companies  The Advisory Committees new members come from a cross section of the small business community and will give vital guidance to the SEC to ensure that the implementation of the Sarbanes Oxley Act does not disproportionately affect small businesses  said Enzi     Enzi urged the SEC to create an advisory committee that would specifically advocate the needs of small businesses during the establishment of national accounting and corporate governance standards    A list of the 19 new members of the Advisory Committee on Smaller Public Companies can be viewed at www sec gov                                                       March 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                 Current record
