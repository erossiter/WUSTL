                                                                                                                                                                                      Congressional delegation encourages e mail correspondence  March 16  2005 Washington  D C   Wyomings Congressional delegation said today that mail correspondence with their Washington offices may be slower this week due to security concerns about mail facilities in the area    The United States Postal Service has temporarily ceased delivery of mail and packages to all government offices including Congress  The temporary delay is caused by the detection of potentially hazardous materials at a Pentagon mail facility recently    Mail already en route to Washington government offices  although delayed  should eventually reach its destination but e mail and fax is preferred for quicker delivery  according to the delegation  All offices are still available by phone                                                       March 2005 News Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Current record
