                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Murray on Resignation of Lester Crawford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Murray  Statement by Senator Murray on Resignation of Lester Crawford  09 23 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Murray on Resignation of Lester Crawford    For Immediate Release  Friday  September 23  2005        WASHINGTON  D C      Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash   who has led the charge to return credibility to the FDA and repeatedly called for a yes or no answer from the agency on the over the counter application of Plan B  released the following statement on the resignation of Commissioner Lester Crawford           Dr  Crawford  s ability to lead a strong and independent FDA was in question from the start   Unfortunately  during his tenure the FDA  s reputation as the gold standard in public health has been tarnished          I hope the President will now take this opportunity to send us a nominee who will adhere to the procedures and protocols for which the FDA is both known and respected and show his commitment to putting science ahead of politics   The health and safety of the American people should not blow with the political winds            As the FDA tackles the considerable challenges that lay ahead  there can be no question as to the integrity or independence of the Commissioner   It is far past time to return credibility to the FDA
