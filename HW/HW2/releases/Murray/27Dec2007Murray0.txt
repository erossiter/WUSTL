                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray s Statement on the Death of Benazir Bhutto                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Murray  Senator Murray s Statement on the Death of Benazir Bhutto  12 27 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray s Statement on the Death of Benazir Bhutto      For Immediate Release  Thursday  December 27  2007     Washington D C     Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA released the following statementafter the assasination offormer PakistaniPrime Minister Benazir Bhutto  I had the opportunity many years ago to meet and talk with Benazir Bhutto  She struck me as very courageous and determined for the people and country she loved  Today  sadly  we won t know how elections in Pakistan could have changed the dynamics of this region  On a larger scale this violence and instability increases my concern that our troops and infrastructure are being used to police a civil war in Iraq  Today s tragedy should serve as a reminder that we must refocus on winning the broader war on terror
