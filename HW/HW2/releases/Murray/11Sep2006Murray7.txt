                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the Fifth Anniversary of the September 11th Terrorist Attacks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Murray  Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the Fifth Anniversary of the September 11th Terrorist Attacks  09 11 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the Fifth Anniversary of the September 11th Terrorist Attacks      For Immediate Release  Monday  September 11  2006     This anniversary is a time for us to come together as a nation to mourn those who were killed and to honor the sacrifices of those who protect us everyday   It s also a time to reflect on the shared values that make our country strong          I will never forget what it felt like to stand in the Capitol and see smoke rising from the Pentagon   I will never forget the way Americans came together in the face of tragedy   And I will never forget that will still have a lot of work to do to protect our country and our citizens          My thoughts and prayers are with everyone who has lost a friend or loved one
