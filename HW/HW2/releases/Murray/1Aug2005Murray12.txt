                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Bremerton Ferry Announcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Murray  PHOTO  Bremerton Ferry Announcement  08 01 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Bremerton Ferry Announcement    For Immediate Release  Monday  August 1  2005    State Transportation Secretary Doug  MacDonald and I rode the ferry from the Colman Dock to Bremerton and joined local leaders to share the great news about ferry funding   In Bremerton  I announced that the newly signed transportation bill makes a major investment in ferry funding    doubling our nation s investment in ferries throughout the country over the next five years   That will help our state buy and maintain new boats and make needed improvements to ferry terminals      We met just outside the Kitsap Conference Center with the Bremerton ferry and Kitsap Transit passenger only terminal in the background         Speakers included Bremerton Mayor Cary Bozeman  Dick Hayes of Kitsap County Transit  and Kitsap Commissioner Chris Endresen
