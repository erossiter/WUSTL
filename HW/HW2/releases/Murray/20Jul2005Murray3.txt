                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on WTO Case against Airbus                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Murray  Murray Statement on WTO Case against Airbus  07 20 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on WTO Case against Airbus    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  July 20  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      Today the World Trade Organization took up the United States   case against the European Union and its unfair subsidies to Airbus   U S  Senator Patty Murray  the Senate  s leading voice on ending Airbus   market distorting trade practices  released the following statement           Today  s action by the WTO is another big step in the effort to hold Airbus and its job killing  trade distorting subsidy practices accountable   Due to Europe s insincere attempts to resolve the Airbus issues  the United States will now aggressively combat launch aid subsidies before the WTO            I applaud the Administration s efforts to stand up for America s aerospace workers   I will continue to monitor the proceedings and to fight with Ambassador Portman  President Bush and anyone else willing to stand against Europe s attempts to buy global domination in commercial aerospace
