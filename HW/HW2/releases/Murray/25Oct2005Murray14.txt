                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Murray  Murray Statement on Rosa Parks  10 25 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Rosa Parks    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  October 25  2005      With the death of Rosa Parks  we have lost a true American hero   Her dignified refusal to give up her seat on the bus ignited the Civil Rights movement and challenged our society to reject segregation            This simple yet brave act forced our nation to recognize that our common values of liberty and equality leave no room for discrimination of any kind            While we mourn her passing  we must remember that the movement she began is never finished   We can all honor her legacy by continuing to fight for freedom  equal justice  and tolerance   Her life will continue to be an inspiration  and remind us all that quiet acts by everyday Americans have the power to change history
