                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Republicans to Washington State  No Sales Tax Deduction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Murray  Republicans to Washington State  No Sales Tax Deduction  12 06 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Republicans to Washington State  No Sales Tax Deduction      For Immediate Release  Thursday  December 6  2007     WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senate Republican Leadership today blocked an effort to block the Alternative Minimum Tax and extend a number of tax relief measures to help middle class Americans  including Sales Tax Deductibility for Washington state residents  Senator Patty Murray released the following statement  The Grinch came early this year for families across Washington state  Not only did the Republican minority block an AMT patch to provide tax relief to nearly 20 million Americans  but they blocked a 2 year extension of sales tax deduction for Washington state residents  Sales tax deductibility is the first step towards tax fairness for Washington state taxpayers  yet the Republicans said no  It is disappointing that with the holidays around the corner Republicans refuse to get in the spirit and continue to block reasonable tax relief for struggling Americans
