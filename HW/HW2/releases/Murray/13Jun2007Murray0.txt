                                                 News Release          Murray Announces  807 500 Grant for Friday Harbor Airport      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  June 13  2007     Washington  D C    Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  announced a total of over  8 3 million in federal support for critical airport improvement projects across Washington state  including a  807 500 grant for Friday Harbor Airport in San Juan County    Friday Harbor Airport will use this grant  provided through the Airport Improvement Program  AIP   for land acquisition that is necessary to provide access to the hangar area  I m pleased that we are investing in Friday Harbor Airport so it can continue to play a strong role in supporting the economy and transportation needs of San Juan County  said Senator Murray  The Friday Harbor Airport fills a growing need for flights to and from the San Juan Islands  and these funds will help the airport meet current and future demands   AIP grants  which are administered by the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA   provide funding for infrastructure improvements that enhance efficiency and safety at airports  Murray  the Chair of the Transportation  Housing and Urban Development  THUD  Appropriations Subcommittee that oversees funding for the FAA  helped secure this funding in the FY07 THUD Appropriations bill
