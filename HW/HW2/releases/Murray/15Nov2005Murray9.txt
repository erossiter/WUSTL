                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  GreenLane Maritime Cargo Security Act                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Murray  PHOTO  GreenLane Maritime Cargo Security Act  11 15 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  GreenLane Maritime Cargo Security Act    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  November 15  2005    Senator Murray and Senator Susan Collins  R Maine  announce their introduction of the GreenLane Maritime Cargo Security Act  Murray  a member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Homeland Security  and Collins  Chairman of the full Senate Homeland Security Committee  co authored the bill to improve the security of the millions of cargo containers that enter America s ports unchecked each year  Senators Norm Coleman  R MN  and Joseph Lieberman  D CT  are original cosponsors of this legislation
