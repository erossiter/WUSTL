                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Murray on SAIC Data Breach                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Murray  Statement by Senator Murray on SAIC Data Breach  07 20 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Murray on SAIC Data Breach    For Immediate Release  Friday  July 20  2007      It is simply unacceptable that the personal medical information of hundreds of thousands of our services members and their families has been jeopardized by security lapses at SAIC  This news is an extra burden on a military community that has already sacrificed so much for our nation     As we all learned with the data theft at the Department of Veterans Affairs  no protocol can be overlooked or underutilized when it comes to protecting those who sacrifice to protect all of us
