                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Speaking Out Against the Surge                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Murray  PHOTO  Speaking Out Against the Surge  02 06 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Speaking Out Against the Surge    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  February 6  2007    Today Senator Murray called on Republicans to stop blocking debate on the President s misguided plan to escalate the War in Iraq  Murray said she will vote for a bipartisan resolution expressing opposition to the President s surge as the first step in changing direction in Iraq     Murray spoke of the tremendous sacrifices Washington state families have made during the war     Senator Murray     Mr  President  war is the most serious and most consequential issue we can debate here in the United States Senate  American lives  American security and America s future are all on the line when our country decides questions of war and peace  For years  we have been denied a real opportunity to fully debate the War in Iraq   a war that has claimed more than 3 000 American lives with no end in sight
