                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Passing of President Gerald Ford                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Murray  Murray Statement on Passing of President Gerald Ford  12 27 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Passing of President Gerald Ford      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  December 27  2006      Our nation has lost a great man in the passing of President Gerald Ford   He served our country with honor and his contributions to the world will always be remembered        President Ford understood that despite strife and differences that our country worked best as a united force and he worked to heal our national wounds by bringing America together        My thoughts and prayers are with his family and friends during this difficult time
