                                                         Murray  Senators Murray  Cantwell  And Santorum  Offer Friendly Wager in Anticipation of Super Bowl XL  01 26 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Senators Murray  Cantwell  And Santorum  Offer Friendly Wager in Anticipation of Super Bowl XL Clara  s Pittsburgh Pierogies and Quaker Steak   Lube Chicken Wings     vs     Smoked Salmon  Washington Apples and Coffee    For Immediate Release  Thursday  January 26  2006    Washington  DC    U S  Senators Patty Murray  D WA  and Maria Cantwell  D WA  have struck a friendly wager with Senator Rick Santorum  R PA  on the outcome of the 2006 Super Bowl between the Seattle Seahawks and the Pittsburgh Steelers   The Senator whose team loses will provide a lunch of favorite food from their team  s hometown   Senator Santorum has offered up Clara  s Pittsburgh Pierogies and Quaker Steak   Lube Wings while Senators Murray and Cantwell are wagering smoked salmon  apples and coffee       After finalizing the details of the wager Senator Santorum commented    I want to congratulate the Seahawks on their first trip to the Super Bowl and their finest season yet   However  my hometown team  the Pittsburgh Steelers are back in familiar territory  making their sixth Super Bowl appearance   With four rings already in hand  this is definitely the year we get the one for the thumb   Go Steelers             The Steelers may have a few rings  but what they don  t have is the 12th man   And it  s that great Washington state spirit that will be with the Seahawks as they win big on Super Bowl Sunday     Senator Murray said     I look forward to sampling some Pittsburgh hot wings as the Seahawks taste victory in Detroit            The Seahawks have had an amazing season and fans have lived up to our legendary 12th man status     said Cantwell     Getting to Super Bowl XL shows America what we  ve always known here in the Northwest   That the Seahawks should not be taken lightly   The Steelers are a formidable foe  but I  m putting my money on the Hawks   On February 6th  fans across the Northwest will be rewarded for 30 years of unwavering support and Pittsburgh  s pierogies will be headed our way   I  m looking forward to heading to Detroit with the rest of the Seahawk  s 12th man Traveling Squad next weekend   It  s going be a great victory           Super Bowl XL will be played in Detroit on Sunday  February 5  2006 at 6 pm
