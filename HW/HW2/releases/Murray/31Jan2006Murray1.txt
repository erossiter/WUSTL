                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Coretta Scott King                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Murray  Murray Statement on Coretta Scott King  01 31 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Coretta Scott King    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  January 31  2006      With the death of Coretta Scott King  we have lost a strong and dignified voice for a better America   Despite great personal loss  she remained committed to the belief that our common values of liberty and equality leave no room for discrimination of any kind           While we mourn her passing  we must remember that the movement she championed is never finished  We can all honor her legacy by continuing to fight for freedom  equal justice  and tolerance  Her life will continue to be an inspiration  and remind us all that our everyday actions have the power to change history
