                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray  s Statement on Reverend Dale Turner                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Murray  Senator Murray  s Statement on Reverend Dale Turner  06 06 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray  s Statement on Reverend Dale Turner      For Immediate Release  Tuesday  June 6  2006      I am deeply saddened to learn of the passing of a gentle soul and quiet hero  Reverend Dale Turner          Rev  Turner touched lives throughout our community and across the country with his persistent optimism and good humor   His wisdom and conviction of spirit comforted and inspired many        While we mourn Rev  Turner  s passing  we should all remember the example he set   We can honor his legacy by continuing to fight for equal justice and tolerance  His life will continue to be an inspiration  and remind us all that quiet acts by everyday Americans have the power to change lives
