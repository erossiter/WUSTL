                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Murray Announces Plan to Protect Students                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  PHOTO  Murray Announces Plan to Protect Students  06 03 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Murray Announces Plan to Protect Students    For Immediate Release  Friday  June 3  2005    At a press conference at Woodbrook Middle School in Lakewood  Senator Murray joined 3 school superintendents  the Lakewood Mayor  teachers  counselors and students to announce her proposal to help schools cope with dramatic changes in enrollment caused by military deployments and base realignment        Currently  the Clover Park School District faces the closure of three elementary schools because of the departure of local military families   Meanwhile  the Central Kitsap and Bethel School Districts will need to accommodate thousands of incoming students because of the Pentagon s recent base realignment plan       Murray s proposal to be offered as an amendment to the Defense Appropriations bill will ensure schools are unharmed from these major changes in enrollment and it will also provide resources for school counselors to ensure students of military personnel get the support they need
