                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement of Senator Patty Murray on President Bush s Medical Malpractice Reform Efforts                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Murray  Statement of Senator Patty Murray on President Bush s Medical Malpractice Reform Efforts  01 05 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement of Senator Patty Murray on President Bush s Medical Malpractice Reform Efforts    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  January 5  2005    With President Bush today traveling to Illinois to renew his call for medical liability reform  Senator Patty Murray today called on him to support real  bipartisan reforms         Today  President Bush continued his misguided effort to curb the right of every injured patient to seek justice by pushing his flawed medical liability reform bill   That there is a problem with the nation s medical malpractice insurance market is undeniable  but giving those who have caused harm a  Get Out of Jail Free  card is not the answer   Instead of pandering to insurance companies and large pharmaceutical companies  the President should work with us to find a real  bi partisan solution that helps control the cost of malpractice insurance for doctors without undermining patient safety and protections
