                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on North Korea                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Murray  Murray Statement on North Korea  10 09 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on North Korea      For Immediate Release  Monday  October 9  2006     Washington D C     U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  today released the following statement on reports that North Korea has conducted its first nuclear test        The news yesterday that North Korea decided to move forward with testing a nuclear weapon is deeply troubling and a grave concern for our country  It threatens the stability and security of Asia and the world  and is of special concern to all of us in Washington state  We cannot accept a nuclear North Korea  The international community needs to come together now and take swift action to keep the situation from escalating  The Bush Administration needs to devote its full energy to pursuing diplomatic efforts and strong sanctions
