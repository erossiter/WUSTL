                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Murray on Confirmation of Susan Schwab as United States Trade Representative                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Murray  Statement by Senator Murray on Confirmation of Susan Schwab as United States Trade Representative  06 08 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Murray on Confirmation of Susan Schwab as United States Trade Representative      For Immediate Release  Thursday  June 8  2006      I am pleased that the Senate voted today to confirm Ambassador Susan Schwab as United States Trade Representative  As the former Deputy Trade Representative  she has the experience necessary to resolve disputes  open markets throughout the world  and create agreements that will have sustained success           I have met personally with Ambassador Schwab to discuss issues important to the economic prosperity of Washington state  and am confident from our discussions that she has our state s best interests in mind           In particular  I am confident that she will support our state and national interests in the ongoing trade dispute concerning large civil aircraft  During her time as Deputy Trade Representative  Ambassador Schwab played a critical role in protecting American aerospace employees by working to end Airbus   market distorting actions  I am sure that she will continue to be a strong advocate for American workers and fair market practices as this country s chief trade negotiator
