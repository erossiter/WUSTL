                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray s Statement on Failure of CHIP Veto Override Vote in the House of Representatives                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  Senator Murray s Statement on Failure of CHIP Veto Override Vote in the House of Representatives  10 18 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray s Statement on Failure of CHIP Veto Override Vote in the House of Representatives      For Immediate Release  Thursday  October 18  2007     Washington  D C    Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  released the following statement after the House of Representatives failed to override the President s veto of the State Children s Health Insurance Program  CHIP   The final vote in the house was 273 156  286 votes were needed for an override  Today a minority of House Republicans said no to a majority of our nation s low income children  Rather than giving them access to the health care they need to thrive and succeed  House Republicans fed our kids the partisan line and refused to override the President s veto  Democrats and a bipartisan majority in the Senate will continue to fight for our children because it is the right thing to do for our children and for our future
