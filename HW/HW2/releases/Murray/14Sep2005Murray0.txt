                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Senator Meets USO Marine of the Year                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Murray  PHOTO  Senator Meets USO Marine of the Year  09 14 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Senator Meets USO Marine of the Year    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  September 14  2005    Senator Murray meets with Staff Sergeant Matthew Anderson  who has been named Marine of the Year by the USO   Sergeant Anderson is from Poulsbo  WA    he is currently stationed in San Diego      According to his platoon  Staff Sergeant Anderson continually puts himself in a position of danger in order to be the most effective during combat operations   He is able to quickly anticipate the point of friction and eliminate any problems that may arise  consistently exceeding what is expected of him      During one ambush on his platoon he led an assault on a suspected enemy position  which lead to the discovery of an illegal weapons and ammunition cache   On another occasion he maneuvered his vehicle through a cloud of smoke and debris  ensuring that the rest of his convoy did not become trapped in a kill zone        Sergeant Anderson is participating in a USO program    Operation Care Package    that makes up care packages and mails them to soldiers serving overseas
