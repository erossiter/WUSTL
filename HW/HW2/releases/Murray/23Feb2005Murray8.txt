                                         News Release          Senator Murray Sends Report to Secretary Rumsfeld    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  February 23  2005       Washington  D C      Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray joined the Washington Congressional delegation in sending a report to Secretary Rumsfeld   The report details the military value of Washington  s military installations          The complete text of the letter appears below           February 23  2005       Donald Rumsfeld  Secretary of Defense  Department of Defense  1000 Defense Pentagon  Washington  D C  20230       Dear Secretary Rumsfeld        As members of the Washington State Congressional Delegation we would like to share with you this report on the military value of the military installations in Washington state   This paper was compiled by our offices and should serve as our joint message of support for the United States military and for those who serve in our home state        Washington state soldiers  sailors  airmen and marines are our national treasure   They are serving bravely in Iraq and Afghanistan and have recently played a central role with tsunami relief in the South Pacific   We are proud of those who serve  and we are proud of the unique network of capable installations that Washington state houses for those who serve         As you will see  Washington state is a great home for our men and women now and in the future   Please take our thoughts under consideration as you make decisions regarding the 2005 Base Realignment and Closure process   We look forward to working with you in the future on these issues        Sincerely        Patty Murray  Maria Cantwell  Norm Dicks  Jim McDermott  Doc Hastings  Jay Inslee  Adam Smith  Brian Baird  Rick Larsen  Cathy McMorris  Dave Reichert   cc  Mr  Phil Grone  Undersecretary of Defense  Installations and Environment
