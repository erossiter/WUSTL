                                                                                      News Release          Murray Gains New Position to Help Washington  s Veterans  Military Families and Housing Needs Adds sixth subcommittee  Military Construction and Veterans Affairs and also gains jurisdiction over Housing and the Judiciary on Transportation Committee    For Immediate Release  Thursday  March 3  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray today announced that she has been named to the Military Construction and Veterans   Affairs Appropriations Subcommittee          The Senate today restructured the Senate Appropriations Committee giving Murray the new subcommittee assignment          I am excited about this new opportunity to continue to fight for Washington state  s men and women in uniform and their families     Senator Murray said     With our military  s needs at the fore and veterans returning home to every corner of our state  my new position will be another opportunity to champion the needs of our state           The Senate restructuring also added Housing and the Judiciary to the Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee on which she remains the highest ranking Democrat          Housing is one of the most important but least talked about challenges here in the Senate   I look forward to being a voice for Washington state families in this tight budget year     Murray said         Senator Murray  s Appropriations Subcommittee Assignments  new assignments in red           Ranking Member   Subcommittee on Transportation  Treasury  the
