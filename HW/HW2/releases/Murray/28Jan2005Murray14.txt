                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Hails Chinese Airlines Purchase of Boeing Dreamliner                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Murray  Murray Hails Chinese Airlines Purchase of Boeing Dreamliner  01 28 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Hails Chinese Airlines Purchase of Boeing Dreamliner    For Immediate Release  Friday  January 28  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray today applauded an announcement by six Chinese Airlines of the purchase 60 Boeing 787 Dreamliners   The agreement is worth approximately  7 2 billion dollars          Today  s announcement is great news for both Boeing and Washington state          Washington state  s Boeing workers are the best  most skilled workers in the aerospace business and today  s purchase is affirmation that their plane will be a market leader for decades to come    Soon  787 passengers worldwide will be flying on planes that say   Made in Washington          I will continue to work to protect Washington state  s Boeing jobs and to ensure that American aerospace workers compete on a level playing field
