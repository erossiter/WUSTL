                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Death of Terrorist Abu Musab al Zarqawi                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Murray  Murray Statement on Death of Terrorist Abu Musab al Zarqawi  06 08 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Death of Terrorist Abu Musab al Zarqawi    For Immediate Release  Thursday  June 8  2006     The death of terrorist al Zarqawi is a testament to the skill and determination of our brave men and women in uniform and the diligence of our intelligence community         This is a good day for our military and also for the people of Iraq  who have suffered under a deadly insurgency         With sectarian violence and other destabilizing factors threatening security in Iraq  there is still much work to do as we move forward   But with the death of al Zarqawi  and the recent appointment of ministers of defense  interior and national security  this is a time to recognize the accomplishments of our military and the hope for a better future for all of Iraq
