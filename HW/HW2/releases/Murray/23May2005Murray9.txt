                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray Statement on the Deal Averting Use of the Nuclear Option                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Murray  Senator Murray Statement on the Deal Averting Use of the Nuclear Option  05 23 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray Statement on the Deal Averting Use of the Nuclear Option    For Immediate Release  Monday  May 23  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      Senator Patty Murray tonight released the following statement on the announcement of a deal averting Republican use of the nuclear option         Tonight s announcement of a deal putting an end to the nuclear option is a great victory for the rights of minorities everywhere  for the U S  Senate and our entire country   Our Founding Fathers  vision for a democracy that respects all voices in this country is safe          I hope now  with the help of the brave Republicans who said no to this radical rule change  we can get back to the business of the American people         In Washington state  we have always worked across the aisle to find qualified  independent judges for the federal bench   I look forward to working with my colleagues and our commission to continue that process in the near future
