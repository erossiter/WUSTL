                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Protecting Washington State s Uninsured Children                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Murray  PHOTO  Protecting Washington State s Uninsured Children  08 01 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Protecting Washington State s Uninsured Children    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  August 1  2007    In a passionate and personal speech on the Senate floor today  Senator Murray urged Senators and President Bush to do the moral thing and support legislation to expand and strengthen healthcare for children  The Senate is considering a bill to reauthorize the Children s Health Insurance Program  CHIP   President Bush has threatened to veto the bill  In her speech  Murray shares the story of Sydney DeBord  a nine year old girl from Yakima  Washington  Sydney s mom wrote to Senator Murray this week and said that without CHIP  Sydney would not have gotten the care she needed
