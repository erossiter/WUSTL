                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Murray Assails Administration s Response to Walter Reed                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Murray  PHOTO  Murray Assails Administration s Response to Walter Reed  03 13 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Murray Assails Administration s Response to Walter Reed    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  March 13  2007    Today  Senator Murray  the fourth ranking member of the Senate leadership  delivered a speech on the Senate floor on the administration s response to veterans  health care failures including Walter Reed           It is now undeniable that the Administration has failed our troops and veterans  What is needed  and what these men and women deserve are real solutions that will meet their needs from the battlefield to the VA and everywhere in between       Our forces in battle deserve adequate body and Humvee armor  They deserve communications gear and equipment to jam IEDS  What they don t need is another day in the field without these items       Our injured heroes returning from Iraq deserve adequate mental care  treatment for post traumatic stress disorder and traumatic brain injuries  and less bureaucratic red tape  What they don t need is another report of the Administration s failure to care for them  or a White House media strategy to cover these failures
