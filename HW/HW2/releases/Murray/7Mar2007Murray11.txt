                                                      News Release          PHOTO  Senator Murray Introduces Bill Gates at Senate Hearing    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  March 7  2007    Today Bill Gates testified before a Senate HELP Committee hearing on  Strengthening American Competitiveness for the 21st Century       Senator Murray introduced Mr  Gates  saying       When it comes to making our country more competitive  improving our schools  and preparing our workforce  we face real challenges   Those challenges require innovative solutions  and that s why I m pleased to welcome to the Senate one of the most innovative thinkers of our time  Bill Gates        From my work with him over the years  I ve seen first hand his commitment to making our country more competitive   As the leader of a high tech company  he s familiar with the challenges of finding highly skilled workers   He s supported educational programs and training partnerships with schools and the private sector   And he understands how technology can help move us toward a system of  lifelong learning  that reflects the realities of tomorrow s economy  As the head of a major foundation  he s invested in education and workforce solutions in the U S  and around the world   His analysis of our high school system has been provocative and thought provoking   And as someone who helped develop the tools of our knowledge economy  he s working to make sure that all Americans can benefit from the opportunities that technology offers   Personally  I can tell you he s done so much to support the economy and workers in my home state  where Microsoft and the Gates Foundation are pillars of the local community   I m pleased that he s agreed to share his insights with us in the Senate   I want to thank him for his leadership  vision  and eagerness to help us address the challenges facing our country
