                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Announces  1 8 million DOT Grant to Pullman Moscow Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Murray  Murray Announces  1 8 million DOT Grant to Pullman Moscow Airport  03 08 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Announces  1 8 million DOT Grant to Pullman Moscow Airport Funding will improve runway safety at Regional Airport    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  March 8  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash  today announced a U S  Department of Transportation grant of  1 8 million dollars to make improvements to the runway safety area at the Pullman Moscow Regional Airport   Senator Murray helped secure the money as the highest ranking Democrat on the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee          I  m proud to have helped get this funding for Pullman Moscow Regional Airport     Murray said     These funds will allow the airport to accommodate larger aircraft  providing economic benefits to the region  and will enhance the safety area that surrounds the airport  s runways   Southeast Washington residents and visitors will benefit from the additional level of safety and capacity that these improvements provide              The funding will help improve runway safety areas between B II and C III of the airport  and will reduce the hazard of structural damage for aircraft that deviate from runway surfaces
