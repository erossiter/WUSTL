                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Murray on the Passing of Lloyd Meeds                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Murray  Statement by Senator Murray on the Passing of Lloyd Meeds  08 18 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Murray on the Passing of Lloyd Meeds    For Immediate Release  Thursday  August 18  2005     Lloyd Meeds served Washington state honorably in Congress and for decades afterwards as a public servant and advocate for our region       Lloyd was also a personal mentor and friend  He offered a helping hand when I was first starting out  took the time to show me the ropes  and was a true role model for how to be an effective legislator       My thoughts are with his family during this difficult time  I hope they may take comfort in knowing that Washington state is a better place thanks to his service
