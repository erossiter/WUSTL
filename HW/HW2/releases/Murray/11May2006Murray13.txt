                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Phone Record Collection by NSA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Murray  Murray Statement on Phone Record Collection by NSA  05 11 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Phone Record Collection by NSA    For Immediate Release  Thursday  May 11  2006      The most important job of our government is to protect the American people from harm   As a nation we must do everything we can to prevent attacks  but Congress and the Administration should work hand in hand to ensure this safety          Today  s press reports that our government is amassing phone records of millions of ordinary Americans are very concerning   It is yet another example of the Administration keeping Congress and the American people in the dark on issues of national security          I call on the White House to be open and forthcoming in upcoming inquiries and investigations so that we can work to ensure the safety and liberty of all Americans  Our citizens deserve an honest assessment of the effectiveness of our intelligence agencies and Congress and the courts must be able to exercise oversight of the Executive branch
