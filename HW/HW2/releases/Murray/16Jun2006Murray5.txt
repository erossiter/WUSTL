                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Announces  17 6 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Boeing Field                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Murray  Murray Announces  17 6 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Boeing Field  06 16 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Announces  17 6 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Boeing Field    For Immediate Release  Friday  June 16  2006     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash   today announced a  17 6 million grant for runway improvement projects at Boeing Field        As the top Democrat on the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee  Murray helped to secure the Department of Transportation grant which will help rehabilitate runway 13R 31L and the Bravo Taxiway at Boeing Field         Maintaining the function and safety of our runways is a top priority for our region   Senator Murray said    Thanks to this grant  Boeing Field can make the improvements it needs to run safely  at full capacity  and continue to serve as one of the most important economic hubs in the Seattle area         Boeing Field s primary runway suffered significant damage during the Nisqually Earthquake of 2001   While most of the earthquake damage was repaired quickly  the pavement continues to settle and deteriorate and without these critical federal dollars these problems could lead to increasingly frequent repairs and could further limit operations at the airport
