                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Boeing Placing Bid to Replace Aging Tankers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Murray  Murray Statement on Boeing Placing Bid to Replace Aging Tankers  04 11 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Boeing Placing Bid to Replace Aging Tankers      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  April 11  2007     Today the Air Force received Boeing s bid to replace the aging KC 135 tanker fleet  America s service members deserve a new tanker that will help them complete their missions safely and effectively  and I have great confidence that Boeing workers in Washington state can build the best tankers for our military   I m pleased that Air Force is moving forward with a fair and open competition to meet the needs of our military and those who wear the uniform of the United States Armed Forces
