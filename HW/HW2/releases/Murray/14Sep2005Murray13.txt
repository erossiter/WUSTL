                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Stands in Support of the Pledge of Allegiance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Murray  Murray Stands in Support of the Pledge of Allegiance  09 14 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Stands in Support of the Pledge of Allegiance    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  September 14  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      In response to a federal district court ruling that the Pledge of Allegiance cannot be recited in public schools because the phrase   under God    endorses religion  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash   today released a statement in support of the Pledge            I strongly disagree with today  s ruling and I am proud to support the reciting of the Pledge of Allegiance in America  s schools        School children across America have grown up with the Pledge of Allegiance for generations and its recitation is an important part of our daily customs here in the United States Senate   The Pledge of Allegiance should remain an important and symbolic American tradition
