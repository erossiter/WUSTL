                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Announces  500 000 for City of Carnation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Murray  Murray Announces  500 000 for City of Carnation  06 10 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Announces  500 000 for City of Carnation Federal funding will help support sewer system    For Immediate Release  Friday  June 10  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash   today announced that she has secured a half million dollars in the Senate Appropriations bill to help the City of Carnation construct a new sewer system            I am pleased to have secured this funding for the City of Carnation     Senator Murray said     In helping the local community to redevelop and expand  we are facilitating a cleaner  healthier and more pleasant environment for Carnation families and businesses           Carnation  s current septic systems have reached capacity  resulting in a health hazard declaration by the county          The  500 000 in funding secured by Murray passed the Senate Appropriations Committee today and will now go to the full Senate for consideration
