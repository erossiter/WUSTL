                                          News Release          Murray Statement on the Passing of Karen Marchioro      For Immediate Release  Thursday  August 30  2007     Washington D C    Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  released the following statement on the passing of Karen Marchioro  the former Washington state Democratic Party chairwoman and longtime state Democratic leader  Washington state Democrats have lost a strong and steady voice in the passing of Karen Marchioro  Karen devoted her life to fighting for what she believed in  and she became both the conscience and inspiration for decades of Washington s Democratic leaders  Karens steady presence and guiding hand have been a personal inspiration to me over the years  Despite her failing health  when I recently spoke with her  she used the opportunity to remind me how important it is to keep the Democratic Party strong for average Americans  That was Karen through and through  a passionate advocate and spirited friend  My thoughts and prayers are with her family during this difficult time  Elected leaders throughout our state owe them a debt of gratitude for the sacrifices they made in allowing Karen to spend her time fighting for each of us  Karens passion will continue to resonate through the words and work of our state s leaders for years to come  She will be greatly missed
