                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on President  s Social Security Ideas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Murray  Murray Statement on President  s Social Security Ideas  04 28 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on President  s Social Security Ideas    For Immediate Release  Thursday  April 28  2005      The president has spent 60 days peddling his privatization plan  and the verdict from the American people is clear  they reject his risky scheme   Unfortunately  his remarks tonight offered no new hope to those of us who want to see Social Security strengthened  not dismantled   His ideas would ensure deep benefit cuts for middle class seniors  trillions in new debt for the next generation  and an end to a guaranteed benefit in favor of a stock market gamble          I look forward to finally seeing a detailed plan from the President in the near future  but he must know by now that the American people will not support Social Security privatization   While we wait to hear any idea that promotes real long term solvency from the President  I will continue work to support and strengthen this important social insurance program    not to privatize and imperil it
