                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray s Statement on the Extension of Army Tours                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Murray  Senator Murray s Statement on the Extension of Army Tours  04 11 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray s Statement on the Extension of Army Tours      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  April 11  2007     Washington  D C     Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  issued the following statement on the Pentagon s announcement that the Army will extend tours in Iraq and Afghanistan to 15 months for all active duty soldiers          Today s announcement means that our brave troops and the many families who await their safe arrival home are once again paying the price for this administration s refusal to change course in Iraq  We have asked everything of these men and women  and they have come through in every instance  It is time we honor their sacrifice and courage with a new direction          Of course this increased combat duty will also mean increased strains on these soldiers as they return home  We cannot continue to stretch our forces to the brink and not count them as a cost of this war  We need to provide a seamless transition of care from the battlefront  to the home front  and everywhere in between
