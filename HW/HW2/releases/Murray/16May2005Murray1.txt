                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Announces  14 7 Million in Grants To Ease South Sound Congestion                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Murray  Murray Announces  14 7 Million in Grants To Ease South Sound Congestion  05 16 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Announces  14 7 Million in Grants To Ease South Sound Congestion Grant will help improve Sounder Commuter Rail service between Tacoma and Lakewood    For Immediate Release  Monday  May 16  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      Senator Patty Murray today announced an award of  14 7 million from the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  to Sound Transit  s Sounder Commuter Rail between Tacoma and Lakewood  Murray  ranking Democrat on the Senate Transportation  Treasury  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Subcommittee  secured the funding in the Transportation Appropriations bill for fiscal year 2003          The grant will fund right of way acquisition along Sound Transit  s Tacoma to Lakewood commuter rail segment           This funding will go a long way towards helping us relieve congestion in the South Sound region   Murray said    Sounder Commuter Rail has already proven to be an important part of the South Sound  s transportation network  and this additional funding adds greatly to the effectiveness of this already very successful line  Murray said
