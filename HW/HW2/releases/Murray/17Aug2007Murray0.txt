                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Utah Mine Tragedy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Murray  Murray Statement on Utah Mine Tragedy  08 17 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Utah Mine Tragedy    For Immediate Release  Friday  August 17  2007    The rescue workers who lost their lives last night attempting to reach miners in the Crandall Canyon mine are true heroes in every sense of the word  My thoughts and prayers are with their families and the families of the trapped miners whose fates are still unknown  In light of these tragic events  I will be joining with my colleagues on the Senate HELP committee to conduct a comprehensive investigation into the causes of this tragedy  the handling of the response  and the ways we can improve mine safety and rescue efforts across our nation
