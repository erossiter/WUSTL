                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Murray on Seattle Shootings                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  Statement by Senator Murray on Seattle Shootings  07 28 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Murray on Seattle Shootings      For Immediate Release  Friday  July 28  2006     I am saddened by this terrible act of violence  My heart goes out to the victims and their families          Freedom of religion and respect for diverse communities is what America is built on and what continues to make our nation strong   We can agree to disagree on international policy but we must not compound an international tragedy with sensless violence here at home
