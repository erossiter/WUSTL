                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the 34th Anniversary of Title IX                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Murray  Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the 34th Anniversary of Title IX  06 23 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Statement by Senator Patty Murray on the 34th Anniversary of Title IX      For Immediate Release  Friday  June 23  2006     Today marks the 34th anniversary of the Title IX ruling which expanded our nation s definition of gender equality   Since 1972  Title IX has opened doors in athletics and education for millions of young women across our nation  The statistics on Tile IX are amazing  It has helped women break down barriers all across the country and has transformed the lives of women through their accomplishments in the classroom and on the playing field         The challenge for all of us today is to make sure that those doors of opportunity stay open for our granddaughters and great granddaughters  As we celebrate the anniversary of this important law  I urge President Bush and Secretary of Education Spellings to protect existing Title IX policies that give every young girl in American the chance to experience the challenges of competition  the value of teamwork  and the thrills of success
