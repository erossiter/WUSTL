                    U S  Senator Patty Murray   PHOTO  Supporting Foster Students in College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Murray  PHOTO  Supporting Foster Students in College  10 20 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          PHOTO  Supporting Foster Students in College    For Immediate Release  Thursday  October 20  2005    Senator Murray receives an   I Care    apron from Carmella Macababbad  who was in Washington  D C  supporting National Care Package Day   On this day  the Orphan Foundation of America assembled more than 2 500 care packages to send to college students who have   aged out    of foster care        Unlike most college students with loving families  former foster youth rarely receive care packages from home that are a practical and emotional lifeline      It is estimated that some 13 000 former foster youth are attending colleges and universities this school year  most without any family support  The packages assembled today send a message that we care about all students        Carmella is studying to become a registered nurse
