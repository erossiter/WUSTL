                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Alan Mulally                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  Murray Statement on Alan Mulally  09 05 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Alan Mulally    For Immediate Release  Tuesday  September 5  2006     I have known Alan for a long time  and I know his energy and spirit will be missed at Boeing   From working with the community to help secure Washington as the home of the Dreamliner  to standing up to unfair marketing practices by Airbus  Alan has been a partner and a friend         While these past few years have seen ups and downs   Alan leaves Boeing with its star on the rise and a bright future ahead for the company and its workers         I look forward to continuing a strong partnership with new CEO Scott Carson   I will work with him to ensure that Boeing  its workers and its Washington state family remains strong for years to come
