                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Presidential Inauguration                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Murray  Murray Statement on Presidential Inauguration  01 20 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Presidential Inauguration    For Immediate Release  Thursday  January 20  2005      Today President Bush spoke of the importance of freedom and security and of our role in the world   I believe we must start with a commitment to these principles here at home        Security is still the most important issue facing Americans today    the security of having a job  of access to health care  of a quality education and of the protection of our ports and borders       I look forward to working with the President on these important issues and to ensuring that the needs of Washington state are heard on a national level        The President has talked about uniting our nation and working in a bi partisan manner for the good of all Americans   It is my hope that these words can be turned into action and that together we can meet the challenges facing Washington state and the nation
