                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Announces  1 5 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Sea Tac Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Murray  Murray Announces  1 5 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Sea Tac Airport  07 13 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Announces  1 5 Million Runway Improvement Grant for Sea Tac Airport    For Immediate Release  Wednesday  July 13  2005     WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Patty Murray  D Wash   today announced a  1 5 million grant for runway improvement projects at Sea Tac Airport          As the top Democrat on the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee  Murray helped to secure the Department of Transportation grant which will help rehabilitate runway 16 34 L at Sea Tac          Maintaining the function and safety of our runways is a top priority for our region     Murray said    Thanks to this grant  SeaTac can make the improvements it needs to run safely  at full capacity and expand as an important airport facility            Through her key role on the Senate Transportation Appropriations Subcommittee  Murray has jurisdiction over funding for the Federal Aviation Administration   She has consistently secured critical federal funding for construction and security improvements at Sea Tac  including the ongoing Third Runway Project
