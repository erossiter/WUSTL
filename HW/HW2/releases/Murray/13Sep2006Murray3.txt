                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Ann Richards                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  Murray Statement on Ann Richards  09 13 2006                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Ann Richards      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  September 13  2006     The world has lost a vibrant spirit with the passing of Ann Richards   She was a passionate advocate for equality  and raised us all up with her wit  wisdom and Texas sized spirit         Ann took on life  politics and all of her battles with humor and grace and our world will be forever brighter for her laughter
