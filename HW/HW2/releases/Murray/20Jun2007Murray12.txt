                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Senator Murray Reacts to President Bush s Veto of Stem Cell Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Murray  Senator Murray Reacts to President Bush s Veto of Stem Cell Bill  06 20 2007                                                                                                                    News Release          Senator Murray Reacts to President Bush s Veto of Stem Cell Bill      For Immediate Release  Wednesday  June 20  2007    Today  U S  Senator Patty Murray  D WA  released the following statement on the Presidents veto of stem cell legislation  The President didnt just veto a bill today  he vetoed the hope of millions of Americans  Embryonic stem cell research could open the door to cures for countless diseases  but President Bush today again slammed that door shut  It is a sad day when the President puts the ideological wishes of a few in his party over the health of millions of our citizens  Democrats remain committed to hope  and we will continue working tirelessly for stem cell research
