                   U S  Senator Patty Murray   Murray Statement on Pope John Paul II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Murray  Murray Statement on Pope John Paul II  04 02 2005                                                                                                                    News Release          Murray Statement on Pope John Paul II    For Immediate Release  Saturday  April 2  2005     WASHINGTON  D C         Pope John Paul II was respected around the world as a man of deep faith and broad compassion   With a clear voice  he focused the world  s attention on those in need and consistently called for peace when nations were torn by conflict   His     message of humanity and compassion will continue in the example he set and the lives he inspired
