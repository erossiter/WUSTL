Senator Gregg Gets Top Republican Spot on State Foreign Operations Appropriations Subcommittee  Contact  Erin Rath  WASHINGTON In a switch of Senate Appropriations Subcommittee assignments today  U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  became the Ranking Member on the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on State  Foreign Operations and Related Agencies  Senator Gregg has been a member of this subcommittee since 1995  Senator Gregg will continue to serve as a member of the following subcommittees  Homeland Security  Interior Environment  Labor HHS Education  Defense  and Commerce Justice Science   Senator Gregg stated  In this time when international relations are having such a massive impact on all aspects of American lives  from our security to our prosperity  this new role will give me a direct and significant role in these issues  The Foreign Operations Subcommittee plays an integral role in the shaping of U S  foreign policy through its oversight of international affairs budgets and in assessing and prioritizing national security interests overseas and I look forward to working with Senator Leahy on this Subcommittee   Drawing on my past experience overseeing the budget of the State Department  we have made significant improvements in embassy security and construction and other economic assistance and diplomatic initiatives  In addition  I look forward to engaging in a broad discussion on the overall foreign affairs budget to ensure that we have a comprehensive and coordinated strategic plan  one that effectively utilizes federal dollars to produce the most benefit worldwide for our country
