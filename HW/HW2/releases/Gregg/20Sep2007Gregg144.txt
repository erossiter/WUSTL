Senators Gregg and Sununu Announce  967 000 Federal Grant For Rochester s Skyhaven Airport Contact Erin Rath Gregg   Barbara Riley Sununu   WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today  9 20  announced that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded a  967 399 grant to the State of New Hampshire for improvements at Skyhaven Airport in Rochester  The Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funding will allow the State to complete the parallel taxiway to the Airports only runway  eliminating the need for back taxi operations on the active runway  Senator Judd Gregg stated  Skyhaven Airport serves as a transportation and recreation hub for the Rochester area  These federal funds will allow important safety improvements to be made  ensuring the safety of employees  pilots and residents using or living near the airport facility  As Skyhaven Airport grows in popularity  it is critical that the facility be able to safely meet demand  said Sununu  This grant represents a significant federal investment in the Airports future  as well as a safety improvement for its aviation operations   Jack Ferns  Director of the New Hampshire Department of Transportations Aeronautics  Rail  and Transit  said The NHDOT  Bureau of Aeronautics  is grateful for these federal funds to improve the safety and infrastructure needs of the Skyhaven Airport
