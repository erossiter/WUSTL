Senators Gregg and Sununu Announce Senate Appropriations Committee Support For St  Joseph Hospital In Nashua  Contact Erin Rath Laena Fallon  WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee has approved a measure that includes  700 000 in federal funding for St  Joseph Hospital in Nashua  These funds will be used to continue development and expansion of their electronic medical record system to improve the quality of care provided to Nashua residents  Funding for this project is included in the Fiscal Year 2008 Labor  Health and Human Services  Education appropriations bill  which is now ready for consideration by the full Senate later this year  The funding for this project is included within the Committee allocation set out for the Fiscal Year 2008 spending measures  Senator Gregg  a member of the Senate Appropriations Labor HHS  Education Subcommittee  who has previously secured  2 5 million for St  Josephs Hospital  stated  The Greater Nashua area continues to be home to an expanding population  and ensuring quality health care is a necessity  St  Joseph Hospital has a distinguished reputation for providing excellent service and resources  making a tremendous contribution to the high quality of life in Nashua  These funds will help ensure that the hospital has the means to keep accurate and secure medical records  eliminating an element of both error and delay      Senator Sununu  stated  Nashuas St  Joseph Hospital serves thousands of patients in southern New Hampshire  delivering a comprehensive range of services in its acute care hospital and trauma center  Bringing technological advancements to our hospitals and clinics helps modernize care and controls the cost of its delivery  As part of that effort  funding included in this legislation is slated to be used to automate clinical charts  medical records  and administrative functions  generating efficiencies that are passed on to patients in the form of quality  streamlined treatment  Peter Davis  President CEO of St  Joseph Hospital stated  St  Josephs appreciates Senators Gregg and Sununus understanding of how Information Technology improves health care quality and enables future efficiencies  The Senators support is pivotal in allowing St  Joseph Hospital to move quickly in adopting the latest technology
