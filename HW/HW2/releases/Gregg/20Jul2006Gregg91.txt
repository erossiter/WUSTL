                                             GREGG ANNOUNCES  500 000              FOR                ST  JOSEPH HOSPITAL IN NASHUA                               WASHINGTON               U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced that the Senate              Appropriations Committee passed a measure which includes  500 000              in federal funding for St  Joseph Hospital in Nashua  These              funds will be used to continue development and expansion of their              electronic medical record system to improve the quality of care              provided to Nashua residents  As a member of the Senate Appropriations              Committee  Senator Gregg was able to include this funding in the              Fiscal Year 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services bill  which is              now ready for consideration by the full Senate later this fall  Senator              Gregg has previously secured  2 5 million for St  Josephs              Hospital   Senator              Gregg stated  The Greater Nashua area continues to be home              to an expanding population  and ensuring quality health care is              a necessity  St  Joseph Hospital has a distinguished reputation               which has helped make Nashua such a great place to live  These              funds will ensure that the Hospital has the means to keep accurate              and secure medical records  eliminating an element of both error              and delay  I am pleased that the Senate Appropriations Committee              has again included funds for the implementation of an electronic            medical system to assist with this project   Peter              Davis  President CEO of St  Joseph Hospital stated  These              funds will allow us to greatly improve on the already outstanding              quality of care we provide at St  Joseph Hospital  It is              in tune with the national focus on improving the countrys              health care system  We greatly appreciate Senator Greggs              support of this initiative and his recognition of the efforts we              are making to meet this critical goal
