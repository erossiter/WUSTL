                              GREGG SUPPORTS HAYDEN FOR CIA POST                WASHINGTON U S               Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today made the following statement regarding              the Presidents nomination of General Michael V  Hayden to              be the next CIA Director  General              Hayden is a strong and capable choice to lead the CIA and he brings              an excellent record of service to his country  He has the exceptional              experience needed to lead the CIA into a future of better collection               communication  coordination of intelligence  and I strongly support              his nomination  Critics are working to make his military              background an issue  but if being a General is a disqualification              for a top level government position  we would not have had Colin              Powell as Secretary of State  and we would be excluding some of          the most extraordinary and competent individuals in our country
