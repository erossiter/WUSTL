Senator Gregg s Statement on Passing of Senator Craig Thomas  Contact Erin Rath  U S  Senator Judd Gregg stated  Kathy and I are deeply saddened by the passing of Senator Thomas  a great friend  colleague and Senator  Senator Thomas made the State of Wyoming proud in the way he represented his constituents  and he brought a tremendous sense of civility and humanity to the United States Senate  He will be greatly missed for his good heartedness and the many contributions he has made to the Senate  Our thoughts and prayers are with his family  his wife Susan and his children  as well as his many friends and the people of Wyoming
