Senator Gregg Announces Federal Funds For Reconstruction Of The Robert Frost Farm On Way To Final Vote Contact  Andrea Wuebker Laena Fallon  WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced that a joint House Senate committee of conference has approved a measure containing  210 000 in federal funding to restore  repair  and preserve the Robert Frost Farm in Derry  The State of New Hampshire Department of Resources and Economic Development  which has owned and managed the Frost Farm since 1965  will oversee the future reconstruction efforts  Robert Frost  a world renowned poet  wrote many of his notable literary works while living at the Farm  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Senator Gregg was able to include this funding in the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations conference report  It is expected that the House will vote on the conference report later today  allowing the Senate to consider it thereafter  Funding for this project is included within the Committee allocation for Fiscal Year 2008 spending measures      Senator Gregg stated  The Robert Frost Farm is an important historic  cultural and educational landmark in the Granite State  Each year  many children take school trips to the Farm where they learn about the accomplished poet who has played a significant role in shaping New Hampshires rich literary tradition  I am pleased to help secure these funds which will not only preserve the Farm  but will also ensure that future generations will have the opportunity to enjoy this special place   Laura Burnham  Chairman of the Robert Frost Farm Board of Trustees stated  It is reassuring to know that critical preservation issues at the Robert Frost Farm will be address now that Senator Gregg has procured necessary funding for essential maintenance of the poets former homestead  Robert Frost once said  I always entertain great hopes  Today  I offer my sincere thanks to Senator Gregg for making my great hopes for preserving the Frost Farm come to fruition  The trustees are delighted with this fantastic news and extend our heartfelt gratitude to the Senator for his extraordinary accomplishment in acquiring congressional funds for the preservation of this unique living memorial to America s best loved poet
