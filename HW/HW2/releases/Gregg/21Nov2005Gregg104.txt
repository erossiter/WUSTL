                               GREGG ANNOUNCES FINAL CONGRESSIONAL APPROVAL OF                   400 000 FOR CONSTRUCTION OF STUDENT CONSERVATION ASSOCIATION                  CENTER                                              WASHINGTON U S               Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced final Congressional approval              of a  400 000 award for the Student Conservation Association to              build a facility and develop associated programs to train young              people to become environmental and conservation leaders  As a member              of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Senator Gregg was able              to include the funding in the final 2006 Conference Report for              the Department of Housing and Urban Development approved on Friday               which now heads to the Presidents desk for his approval               The project will result in improved training and education facilities              and new conservation education programs for the 47  year old national              environmental organization  SCA has trained over 40 000 volunteers              to deliver high quality conservation service  Senator Gregg stated  We are fortunate  as a state  to              have leaders on the local and state level who understand how vital              it is to preserve and protect our natural resources  I am pleased              that an organization which embodies those ideals like the Student              Conservation Association will continue to have a significant presence              in our state  The SCA has impacted the lives of so many young people              throughout the country  and I am confident they will carry on that              strong tradition for generations to come to increase New Hampshires              tremendous environmental legacy   Dale Penny  President of The Student Conservation Association  Senator              Greggs leadership has made a tremendous difference for our              public lands and for the lives of young people in New Hampshire               With his support of the Student Conservation Associations              Center for Conservation Service  Senator Gregg will greatly assist              the organization in improving its training and education facilities              and in providing conservation learning programs to young people              throughout the Connecticut River Valley region
