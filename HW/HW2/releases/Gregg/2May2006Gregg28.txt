                GREGG  SUNUNU  BASS ANNOUNCE                  BENEFITS FOR CONTRACT WORKERS AT FRASER  WASHINGTON               DC  United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH               and Congressman Charles Bass  R NH  announced today  5 2  that              contract employees from Gorhams 3D Materials Handling working              at Fraser Papers paper mill who are totally or partially              separated from employment because of the Berlin pulp mill closure              are now eligible to apply for Alternative Trade Adjustment Assistance               ATAA  and Trade Adjustment Assistance  TAA  through the U S  Department              of Labor   Securing federal resources for workers displaced by the              upcoming closure of Frasers Berlin facility remains an important              part of the comprehensive effort to help these individuals get              back on their feet  The Department of Labors approval of              benefits for 3D Materials Handling employees reflects the importance              of ensuring that all workers who are adversely impacted by the              companys decision to close its Berlin pulp mill are able              to get the help they need  We will continue to work with officials              at the state and local levels to ensure that affected workers have              access to critical assistance  said Gregg  Sununu and Bass  According to the Department of Labor  a company official filed              a petition for these federal benefits on behalf of 3D Materials              Handling employees who work at Frasers Gorham paper mill               Having now been approved for ATAA and TAA resources  3D Materials              Handling workers who lose their jobs as a result of the Berlin              plant closure will be able to apply for ATAA and TAA benefits               which can include reemployment services  relocation allowances               cash payments for eligible workers enrolled in full time training               health insurance coverage assistance  and classroom  on the job               or employer based training   Affected workers who are over age 50 and enter a full time job              that pays less than their previous position would have the option              to instead receive  5 000 per year in income support  for a total              of  10 000 over two years  through ATAA   Last month  Congressional delegation members announced that workers              at Frasers Gorham paper mill who became unemployed as a              result of the closure of the companys Berlin pulp mill would              be eligible for ATAA and TAA  In March  they announced similar              benefits for workers at the Berlin facility  Fraser employees who need assistance in applying for ATAA or TAA              benefits may visit the NH Works Fraser Papers Workers Assistance              Center at 55 Maynesboro Street in Berlin
