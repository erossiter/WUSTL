 Senator Gregg Comments On Supreme Court Nominee                Judge John Roberts                                                            U S               Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  stated   The President s announcement              tonight of his selection of  Judge John Roberts for nomination              to the Supreme Court will rapidly set in motion a vast and hopefully              thoughtful              debate  both within the U S  Senate and throughout the country               In the Senate  the consideration of this nomination should be conducted              with the dignity and seriousness it deserves  I look forward to              a process that will focus on the substance of the candidate  the              qualifications and abilities  and one which will not be mired in              political partisanship
