Senators Gregg and Sununu Announce Senate Support For St  Joseph Hospital In Nashua   Contact Andrea Wuebker Laena Fallon Gregg   Barbara Riley Liz Chamberlain Sununu   WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  today announced that the Senate has approved a measure that includes  700 000 in federal funding for St  Joseph Hospital in Nashua  These funds will be used to continue development and expansion of their electronic medical record system to improve the quality of care provided to Nashua residents  Funding for this project is included in the Fiscal Year 2008 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill  which now heads to a joint House Senate conference committee before returning to each chamber for final passage  The funding for this project is included within the Committee allocation set out for the Fiscal Year 2008 spending measures  Senator Gregg  a member of the Senate Appropriations Labor HHS Education Subcommittee  who has previously secured  2 5 million for St  Joseph Hospital  stated  The Greater Nashua area continues to be home to an expanding population  and ensuring quality health care is a necessity  St  Joseph Hospital has a distinguished reputation for providing excellent service and resources  making a tremendous contribution to the high quality of life in Nashua  These funds will help ensure that the hospital has the means to keep accurate and secure medical records  eliminating an element of both error and delay      Senator Sununu  stated  Nashuas St  Joseph Hospital serves thousands of patients in southern New Hampshire  delivering a comprehensive range of services in its acute care hospital and trauma center  Bringing technological advancements to our hospitals and clinics helps modernize care and controls the cost of its delivery  As part of that effort  funding included in this legislation is slated to be used to automate clinical charts  medical records  and administrative functions  generating efficiencies that are passed on to patients in the form of quality  streamlined treatment  Peter Davis  President CEO of St  Joseph Hospital stated  St  Joseph Hospital believes that strengthening our electronic medical record system is one of the keys to improving the health care system in Nashua and neighboring communities  The funds Senator Gregg and Senator Sununu have secured will be critical in allowing St  Joseph to adopt the latest Information Technology  We appreciate their recognition of how IT technology can greatly improve patient care and thank them for their commitment to helping the hospital carry out our mission
