Senator Gregg Announces Federal Funding For Boys And Girls Club In Manchester On Way To Final Vote  Contact  Andrea Wuebker Laena Fallon  WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced that a joint House Senate committee of conference has approved a measure containing  210 000 in federal funding for the expansion and reconstruction of the Manchester Boys and Girls Club  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Senator Gregg was able to include this funding in the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation  and Housing and Urban Development conference report  It is expected that the House will vote on the conference report today  allowing the Senate to consider it thereafter  Funding for this project is included within the Committee allocation for Fiscal Year 2008 spending measures      Senator Gregg stated  Boys and Girls Clubs make a tremendous difference in the lives of thousands of kids nationwide  and the clubs in New Hampshire provide exceptional service to the communities  As the Manchester community continues to grow  it is important for young people to have a place to go where they can participate in after school and summer programming in a positive and safe environment  The expansion of the Club will allow many exciting and educational opportunities to continue for children in the City  Gary Frost  Executive Director of the Manchester Boys and Girls Club  stated  On behalf of the staff and supporters of the Boys and Girls Club of Manchester  I would like to pass along my thanks to Senator Gregg for the hard work he has undertaken for this club and those throughout the country  This appropriation will help us meet our Capital Campaign goal and will help to strengthen our ability to carry out the Clubs mission  Because of Senator Greggs support  we will be able to provide opportunity for even more kids in need in our community  These funds will help with the Clubs Building Better Lives Capital Endowment Campaign and the renovation and expansion project which includes a new learning and technology center  a performing arts center  a teaching kitchen  health services center and three dedicated age appropriate centers as well as engaged physical activity areas
