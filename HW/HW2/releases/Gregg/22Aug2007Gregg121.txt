Senators Gregg and Sununu Announce Benefits For Workers Affected By Closure Of Collins   Aikman Farmington Facility Contact Laena Fallon Gregg   Liz Chamberlain Sununu   WASHINGTON  DC  United States Senators Judd Gregg and John Sununu  R NH  announced today  8 21  that workers at Collins  Aikmans Farmington facility who are totally or partially separated from employment as a result of the plants closure are now eligible to apply for Alternative Trade Adjustment Assistance  ATAA  and Trade Adjustment Assistance  TAA  through the U S  Department of Labor  The Department notified Gregg and Sununus offices of the TAA approval Tuesday morning   By approving the Farmington workers for these benefits  the Department of Labor has taken an important step to provide appropriate resources to employees who were adversely affected by the companys decision to close the facility  We will continue to work with state and local officials to ensure that displaced workers receive the benefits they need to help them get back on their feet  said Gregg and Sununu     According to the Department of Labor  a company official applied for these federal benefits on behalf of 189 employees at the automotive parts manufacturer  Having now been approved for ATAA and TAA resources  Collins  Aikmans Farmington employees who lost their jobs on or after July 9  2006 through two years from the date of certification will be able to apply for the programs benefits  which can include reemployment services  relocation allowances  cash payments for eligible workers enrolled in full time training  health insurance coverage assistance  and classroom  on the job  or employer based training   Affected workers who are over age 50 and enter a full time job that pays less than their previous position would have the option to instead receive  5 000 per year in income support  for a total of  10 000 over two years  through Alternative TAA    Employees who need assistance in applying for ATAA or TAA benefits may visit www servicelocator org  a Department of Labor Web site  to locate the nearest career one stop center
