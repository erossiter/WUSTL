               SENATOR GREGG COMMENTS ON THE PASSING OF                CHIEF JUSTICE WILLIAM REHNQUIST                 WASHINGTON                 U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today made the following statement              on the death of Chief Justice William Rehnquist   Senator Gregg stated  Justice Rehnquist s death is a loss              for the entire country  He has been an exceptional jurist  He has              brought common sense  intelligence  a good sense of humor  and              a lot of collegiality to the Supreme Court and his leadership on              the Court has been  in my opinion  nothing short of exceptional    It obviously means that we now must seek a new Supreme              Court judge position  and also a new Chief Justice  I m hopeful              that we can find somebody who will emulate the talents and abilities              that Justice Rehnquist had  and that will give this nation the              type of leadership that he gave this nation from the bench
