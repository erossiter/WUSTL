Senator Gregg CoSponsors Resolution To Commemorate 25th Anniversary Of The Vietnam Veterans Memorial  Contact Erin Rath  WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  is a cosponsor of legislation to commemorate the 25th anniversary of the construction and dedication of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial  This resolution expresses support and gratitude to all who served during the Vietnam War and their families  In 1997  Senator Gregg joined 72 cosponsors on a similar resolution commemorating the 15th anniversary of the Memorial  Senator Gregg stated  Each year  millions of Americans visit the Vietnam Veterans Memorial for the opportunity to pay their respects to the brave men and women who served in Vietnam  We will forever be grateful for the sacrifices made by those who served  and their loved ones
