Senator Gregg Honored By U S  Chamber Of Commerce For Support Of Economic Growth  Contact Erin Rath  WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  was honored today by the U S  Chamber of Commerce with its Spirit of Enterprise award for his work to support and foster economic growth during the second half of the 109th Congress  2006   The Chamber of Commerce has recognized Senator Greggs contributions to pro growth issues on multiple occasions during his time in the Senate   Senator Gregg stated  I am honored by this recognition  As our economy continues to grow  it is important that the federal government creates an economic environment that allows businesses to expand and utilize innovative ideas  The entrepreneurial spirit of our nations workers and business owners should be fostered and rewarded  I will continue to work hard to keep our economy moving forward and keep taxes low for small businesses and working families  Thomas J  Donohue  Chamber President and CEO  stated  Senator Gregg proved to be a valuable ally to the business community last year  supporting measures to stimulate job creation and spur economic growth  The business community is grateful for Judds dedication to a pro growth agenda and we are proud to present him with this award
