                                             SENATOR GREGG ANNOUNCES               5 MILLION FOR LITTLE BAY BRIDGES RECONSTRUCTION EFFORT                              WASHINGTON               U S  Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced that the expansion              and upgrade of the Little Bay Bridges in Newington and Dover is              in line to receive an additional  5 million in federal funding  The              funds for this project are contained in the Fiscal Year 2007 Transportation               Treasury  Housing and Urban Development  THHUD  bill which was              passed by the Senate Appropriations Committee today  As a              member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Senator Gregg was              able to include the funding for the Little Bay Bridges upgrades              in the measure  which is now ready for consideration by the full              Senate  expected sometime this year  Since 2003  Senator              Gregg has secured  28 million for the Little Bay Bridges project   Senator Gregg stated  The Little Bay Bridges project has consistently              been identified as a top priority for New Hampshires Seacoast              region  As New Hampshires population continues to              grow exponentially and  with more tourists visiting the Granite              State each year  it is crucial that the resources are put in place              to get this project moving forward  I am pleased that these              additional federal funds will help expand the Bridges  provide              a boost to the regional and state economies and ease the travels              of drivers in the future   Portsmouth              Mayor Steve Marchand stated    Little Bay Bridge is a critical              component of the New Hampshire Seacoast and this additional funding              is further acknowledgment of how important the expansion project              is to the residents of Portsmouth and neighboring communities Senator              Greggs continuing efforts to provide these essential financial              resources to expand the Bridge and Spaulding Turnpike are greatly              appreciated  Every bit of support towards the project helps              the City  the State of New Hampshire  and daily commuters move              one step closer towards a common goal   Dover              Mayor Scott            support and excellent representation that Dover receives from Senator              Gregg   Dick              Ingram  President of the Portsmouth Chamber of Commerce  stated  Thank
