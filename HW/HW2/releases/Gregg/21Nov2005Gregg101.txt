               SENATOR GREGG ANNOUNCES NASHUA DOWNTOWN RIVERFRONT                  OPPORTUNITY PROGRAM TO RECEIVE  400 000 IN MEASURE APPROVED BY                  CONGRESS                                WASHINGTON U S               Senator Judd Gregg  R NH  today announced that Congress has approved               400 000 for the Nashua downtown riverfront opportunity program               The funding included in the 2006 budget for the U S  Department              of Housing and Urban Development now heads to the Presidents              desk for his signature  These funds will be used to help the City              of Nashua acquire riverfront property on the Nashua River and make              necessary upgrades to the area for use as a public space  including              building the riverfront promenade  These improvements  including              outdoor parks and an outdoor amphitheater  will have a significant              positive impact for the citys downtown and inner city communities               Senator Gregg  a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee               was able to include the funding in the final Conference Report    Senator Gregg stated  Nashua continues to do an excellent              job balancing tremendous economic and residential growth without              sacrificing the high quality of life it offers  These funds will              help the City transform its waterfront to continue that growth              without sacrificing its character   Nashua Mayor Bernie Streeter stated  I am very pleased              that Senator Gregg recognizes the good work we are doing in our              downtown  and especially on our riverfront  His efforts in securing              these funds is paramount to downtown Nashua s continued viability              Kathy Hersh  Nashua Community Development Director said  The              City of Nashua has a vision for our downtown riverfront  Senator              Gregg shares our vision and has provided resources for us to make              that vision a reality  We are very grateful for his continued support              of Nashua and our downtown
