MANHATTAN RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Sarah Rice, daughter of Chuck and Sue Rice, recently completed a summer internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts. 
   Rice will graduate from Kansas State University in May with a degree in print journalism with a minor in political science. For the second half of the summer, Rice will be studying abroad in London, England at the University of Westminster taking a class in British Journalism. After her experience at the Kansas State Collegian as News Editor, Rice plans to find a newspaper reporting job after graduation. 
   Rice's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included legislative research, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
