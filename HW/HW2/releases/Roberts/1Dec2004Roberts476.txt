OVERLAND PARK RESIDENT INTERNS IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Kate O'Hara, daughter of Judge James and Robin O'Hara of Overland Park, recently completed a semester-long internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts.
   O'Hara will graduate from Kansas State University in May 2005 with a degree in Political Science and a minor in History. Following graduation, O'Hara plans to attend law school and pursue a career in public service. 
   O'Hara's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included legislative research, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
