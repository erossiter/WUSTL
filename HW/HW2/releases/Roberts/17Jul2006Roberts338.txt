SEN. ROBERTS ANNOUNCES $65,000 GRANT TO EISENHOWER STATE PARK IN OSAGE COUNTY
  Sen. Pat Roberts (R-KS) today announced a $65,000 grant to the Kansas Department of Wildlife and Parks for upgrades to Eisenhower State Park in Lyndon, KS.
   The grant will be used to develop new showerhouse facilities at the Fivestar Campground and improved asphalt roads at the Eisenhower State Park. The improved facilities will modernize the recreation facilities to increase outdoor recreational use.
   The funds were awarded by the National Park Service.
