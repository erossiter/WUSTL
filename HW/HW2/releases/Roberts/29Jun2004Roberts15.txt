PIQUA RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Damian Lair, son of Greg and Judy Lair of Piqua, recently completed a six week internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   Lair will graduate from Kansas State University in May 2005, with a degree in Finance and a minor in Political Science. Following graduation he plans to attend law school. Lair graduated from Yates Center High School in 2000. 
   Lair's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, flag requests, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
