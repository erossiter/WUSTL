OBERLIN RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON OFFICE
  Brett Moore, son of Brendan and Kathi Moore, of Oberlin recently completed a spring-semester internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   Moore, a native of Oberlin, will graduate from the University of Kansas on May 22, 2005. He has completed a double-major in Political Science and Communications Studies. 
   Moore's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included attending committee hearings and briefings, performing research on health care and military issues, writing constituent mail, and conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol. Moore previously interned for Kansas State Representative Joann Freeborn (R-Concordia) in the Topeka Statehouse.
