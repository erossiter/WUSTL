KANSAS CITY RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Patrick Coughlin, son of Shane and Beth Coughlin of Kansas City, Missouri recently completed a semester-long internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts. 
   Patrick Coughlin is a senior in Georgetown University's Science, Technology, and International Affairs (STIA) program with a concentration in Security Studies. An alumni of Rockhurst High School in Kansas City, Patrick plans on pursuing a career with federal law enforcement in the intelligence and counter-terrorism fields after graduation from Georgetown.
   Patrick's responsibilities included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent correspondence, conducting tours of Capitol Hill, and providing general support to the administrative staff.
