WESTMORELAND RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Ben Moore, son of Jim and Jenni Moore, recently completed a summer internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   Moore will graduate from Kansas State University in May with a degree in Business Administration Finance. He plans to attend law school afterwards. Moore graduated from Rock Creek High School in 2001. 
   Moore's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included legislative research, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
