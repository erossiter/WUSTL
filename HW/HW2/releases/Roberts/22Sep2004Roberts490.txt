SENATE VOTES TO CONFIRM REP. GOSS AS DIRECTOR OF CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE
  Sen. Pat Roberts, Chairman of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence, today announced that the U.S. Senate voted to confirm Rep.  
 Porter Goss' (R-FL) nomination to be Director of Central Intelligence. 
   "I am pleased the Senate voted so strongly to confirm Porter Goss," Chairman Roberts said. "Porter Goss' task will be to build; to inspire; to open a new chapter in our intelligence activities," Senator Roberts said. "As we begin structural Intelligence Community reform, Porter Goss' task will be to open the new chapter, and lead the Intelligence Community into that fresh start." 
   "This nominee is ready to go to work, and he is needed. I look forward to working with Porter Goss as the next Director of Central Intelligence." 
   The Senate vote was 77-17.
