EMPORIA RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Christin Flint, daughter of Teresa Schad of Emporia, recently completed a semester-long internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts. 
   Flint is a junior in American University's School on International Relations with a concentration in U.S. Foreign Policy and Russian language. She plans on pursuing a career with foreign policy in the defense and intelligence fields. Upon graduation in May of 2007, she hopes to attend graduate school for intelligence or become a commissioned officer in the United States Army for military intelligence. She graduated from Emporia High School in 2003. 
   Flint's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent correspondence, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol and providing general support to the administrative staff.
