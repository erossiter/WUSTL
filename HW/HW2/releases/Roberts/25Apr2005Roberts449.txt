KCK NATIVE COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON OFFICE
  Melissa Shute, daughter of Richard and Theresa Collins of Kansas City, KS and granddaughter of Muriel Stroud of Arma, recently completed a semester-long internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts. 
   Shute, who will continue working for Roberts this summer, is finishing her last year of law school as a visiting student at The Catholic University of America in DC. She will receive her diploma from the University of Tulsa College of Law in December, with an emphasis in energy and environmental law and public policy. She is also a graduate of the University of Houston and Piper High School in Kansas City. 
   Shute's responsibilities included researching agriculture, environmental and health issues, and writing constituent correspondence.
