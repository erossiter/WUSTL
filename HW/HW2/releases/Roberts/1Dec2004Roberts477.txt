UNIONTOWN RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Beth Ludlum, daughter of Joe and Ann Ludlum of Uniontown, recently completed a four-month internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen.  
 Pat Roberts. 
  Ludlum graduated from Kansas State University in 2003 with a degree in Agriculture Communications, and then taught English at a university in China for a year. She plans to continue working in Washington, D.C., while completing master's degrees in International Peace and Conflict Resolution and Theology. Ludlum graduated from Uniontown High School in 1999. 
   Ludlum's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
