FORT SCOTT RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Wendy Gorman, daughter of Kevin and Allison Gorman of Fort Scott, recently completed a six week internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   Gorman will graduate from Kansas State University in May 2005, with a degree in Public Relations/Mass Communications. She plans to attend law school. Gorman graduated from Fort Scott High School in 2001. 
   Gorman's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, flag requests, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
