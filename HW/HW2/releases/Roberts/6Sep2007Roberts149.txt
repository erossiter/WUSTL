SEN. ROBERTS ANNOUNCES $75,000 GRANT TO HOISINGTON FOR BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT
  U.S. Senator Pat Roberts (R-KS) today announced a $75,000 grant to the city of Hoisington for a new business building.
   The funds will be used to purchase and remodel a business incubator building in the main street Downtown District of Hoisington that will initially be leased to a pharmacy. This project will create three new jobs.
   The grant was awarded by the United States Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Office. Senator Roberts is a senior member of the Senate Agriculture Committee.
