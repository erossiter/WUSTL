WICHITA RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON, D.C. OFFICE
  Christopher Graham, son of Dale and Suzanne Graham of Wichita, recently completed a six week internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   Graham will graduate from Kansas State University in December 2004 with a degree in Financial Services and intends to enter the workforce upon his graduation. Graham graduated from Andover High School in 2000. 
   Graham's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, flag requests, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
