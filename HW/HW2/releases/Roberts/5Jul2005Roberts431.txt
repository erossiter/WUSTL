OVERLAND PARK RESIDENT COMPLETES INTERNSHIP IN SEN. ROBERTS' WASHINGTON OFFICE
  Ryan McKenny, son of Theresa Forgy of Overland Park, and Pat McKenny of Olathe, recently completed an internship in the Washington, D.C. office of Sen. Pat Roberts. 
   McKenny will be a senior at Kansas State University, where he is working toward dual-degrees in Accounting and Finance, with a minor in Business Economics. McKenny graduated from St. Thomas Aquinas High School in 2002. 
   McKenny's responsibilities in Senator Roberts' office included researching legislation, attending committee hearings and briefings, assisting with constituent mail and concerns, conducting tours of the U.S. Capitol Building, flag requests, and providing support to the administrative and communications staff.
