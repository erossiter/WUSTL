                               MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES FY 2007 DEFENSE PROJECTS FOR FLORIDA   September 29  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded the Senates final approval of the Department of Defense Appropriations bill for FY 2007  Senator Martinez helped secure more than  100 million in defense projects  in addition to the original budget request  benefiting Floridas military infrastructure and personnel  The measure now goes to the White House for the Presidents signature     Martinez said     Floridas military installations and personnel are essential to our national security  The dollars in this bill will help fund important priorities  said Martinez  This bill will provide our military installations with the tools they need to perform their vital role in our national security strategy     Florida projects included in the bill    Item  Precision Extended Range Munition  PERM    EFSS   Amount   8 000 000   Account  Research  Development  Navy   Service  Marine Corps    The Precision Extended Range Munition  PERM  is a developmental GPS guided  long range rocket assisted High Explosive projectile for the Marine Corpss Expeditionary Fire Support System  The baseline EFSS system will provide MEU commanders with a significant operational capability  The EFSS is deployed quickly from ship to inland operating areas via the V 22 Osprey aircraft  The PERM guided projectile will have a 30 meter CEP accuracy out to 17 Km and will incorporate the latest in insensitive munition  IM  technologies for the warhead and rocket motor    Item  Special Operations Command  SOCOM  Special Operations Forces Laser Acquisition Marker  SOFLAM    Amount   1 580 000   Account  Procurement  Defense Wide   Service  SOCOM    This system will enable Special Operations Forces to successfully engage targets from a stand off distance  Using a laser to mark the targets also provides a greater likelihood of a first round hit on high value targets    Item  Center for Southeastern Tropical Advanced Remote Sensing   Amount   4 000 000   Account  Operation and Maintenance  Navy    This request provides for transitioning the Center for Southeastern Tropical Advanced Remote Sensing  CSTARS  from its development stage to full operational capability by expanding it to accommodate new high resolution satellites  increasing the number of satellites supported to increase persistence and acquiring hardware and software to meet defense needs    Item  SecNet 54    Secure Wireless Local Area Network   Amount   1 000 000   Account  Procurement  Defense Wide   Service  SOCOM    The U S  Special Operations Command has a critical need to equip its warriors with advanced  assured communications technology in support of various tactical missions  Specifically  USSOCOM has rated Secure Wireless Local Area Network  SWLAN  access of classified data among its top 12 unfunded requirements for fiscal year 2007  SOCOM users will gain real time access to intelligence and surveillance     Item  Naval Sea Cadet Corps   Amount   300 000   Account  Operation and Maintenance  Navy     This funding will offset individual Naval Sea Cadet s out of pocket costs for summer training  The Naval Sea Cadet Corps  NSCC  program is focused upon development of young men and women ages 11 17  The program serves almost 10 000 Sea Cadets managed by almost 2 000 adult volunteers  The program promotes interest and skill in seamanship and aviation  but  more importantly  instills in every Cadet qualities that mold strong moral character in an anti drug and anti gang environment     Item  Team Performance Enhancement Through Human Agent Collaboration   Amount   1 100 000   Account  Research  Development  Army     Team Performance Enhancement Through Human Agent Collaboration program is focused on research in the area of advanced interaction and teaming of humans  robots  and software agents     Item  Nanotubes Optimized for Lightweight Exceptional Strength     Composite Materials   Amount   1 350 000   Account  Research  Development  Army     Funding will be utilized for continuation of a collaborative program between the Army Research Laboratory and Florida State University to continue a carbon nanotubes composite materials research and development program  For producing lightweight multifunctional composite structures  resins impregnated with carbon nanotubes hold the promise of creating structures which will be the strongest ever known  providing maximum personnel protection     Item  Virtual Combat Convoy Trainer  VCCT    Amount   4 400 000   Account  Procurement  Marine Corps     Funding would provide one Virtual Combat Convoy Trainer  VCCT  for Marine Corps Base Hawaii  which has submitted an Urgent Universal Need Statement for a capability to train Convoy Combat Tactics     Item  F 15 Active Electronically Scanned Array  AESA  Radar   Amount   72  000 000   Account  Procurement  Air Force   Service  National Guard     Funding would procure twelve to fourteen Active Electronically Scanned Array  AESA  Radar systems for Air National Guard F 15s     Item  National Functional Genomics Center   Amount   9 000 000   Account  Research  Development  Test and Evaluation  Army   Recipient  Moffitt Cancer Center     Funding will allow the Moffitt Cancer Center to continue their one of a kind translational cancer research to identify the molecular signatures of cancers and develop procedures for the early detection and treatments based on each individuals genetic profile     Item  Military Dependant Pediatric Brain Tumor and Neurological Disease Research   Amount   1 200 000   Account  Research  Development  Test and Evaluation  Army   Recipient  Miami Childrens Hospital     Funding would be used to purchase an intra operative high resolution 3 Tesla MRI which will enable neurosurgeons to conduct advanced treatment on brain function and mapping as well as brain scanning during surgery
