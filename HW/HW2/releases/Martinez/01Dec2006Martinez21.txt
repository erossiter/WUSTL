                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SENATOR MARTINEZ APPLAUDS DISTRIBUTION OF HUD YOUTHBUILD GRANTS   December 1  2006   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  announcement that it will distribute Youthbuild grants to three at risk youth programs across Florida  According to HUD officials  the grants distributed include  700 000 to the Housing Authority of Lakeland  Florida   400 000 to the District Board of Trustees of Pensacola Junior College  and  400 000 to the Greater Miami Services Corps   These grants help HUD keep its commitment to helping American families acquire affordable housing and support a healthy standard of living  said Martinez  Preparing Americas young people for a bright future is part of that commitment   The grants announced by HUD Youthbuild programs are designed to motivate at risk young people to earn their high school diplomas and have access to career training in construction trades  while building and renovating homes for lower income families                                                                   December 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
