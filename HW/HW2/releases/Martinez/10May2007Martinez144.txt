                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ   900 000 TSA TRAINING GRANT APPROVED FOR LYNX  May 10  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced the Transportation Security Administration  TSA  has awarded a  908 000 grant to LYNX  the Central Florida Regional Transportation Authority  The purpose of the grant is to allow LYNX to provide homeland security training to its front line staff     The leaders at LYNX are focused on the safety of its riders and its system  said Martinez  I commend TSA for recognizing the steps LYNX has already taken and their effort to enhance the protection of their riders and employees     LYNX currently conducts extensive public awareness and public involvement campaigns associated with transit related topics  The TSA grant will help the transit authority incorporate Emergency Management Preparedness and Terrorism Awareness as part of on going outreach efforts     Im grateful TSA  through the Department of Homeland Security  continues to help make our nations public transportation systems safe and secure  Martinez said  Its a demonstration of leadership at the federal and local levels that this money and the training are coming to Orlando                                                                   May 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
