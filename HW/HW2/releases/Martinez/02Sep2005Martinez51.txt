                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON SENATE PASSAGE OF  10 5 BILLION IN EMERGENCY FUNDS FOR KATRINA DISASTER RELIEF  September 2  2005   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today made the following statement regarding Senate passage of  10 5 billion in emergency funds to aid in Katrina relief efforts        The Senate has taken action to approve  10 5 billion in emergency funds to help our nation deal with what is one of the most devastating natural disasters we have ever seen        This is a good first step and more will be done in the coming weeks  months and even years ahead  Never has our country had to respond to this magnitude of destruction    both in physical structures in affected states and in the destruction of the daily lives  routines and families of hundreds of thousands of our citizens        We must help our fellow citizens rebuild their cities and their lives  Its going to take time  patience and extraordinary resources        Floridians know themselves the damage and destruction caused by hurricanes  I encourage them to continue doing what they can to help our struggling neighbors in the  Gulf states            30                                                                   September 2005 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
