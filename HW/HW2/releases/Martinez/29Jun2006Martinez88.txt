                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SENATOR MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON GUANTANAMO BAY MILITARY TRIBUNALS  June 29  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement in regards to the United States Supreme Court decision on Hamdan v  Rumsfeld  Martinez said    The Supreme Court has outlined the need for us to have congressional action to authorize the proper utilization of military tribunals to provide a vehicle to resolve the issue of the detainees    I will work with my colleagues to ensure that we resolve this issue in the venue of military tribunals where it belongs and not in any other setting  I look forward to working with the President and the Congress to create the legal framework to do this   Our national security is best protected by utilizing the confidentiality and the security that military tribunals provide these very special circumstances                                                                    June 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                        Current record
