                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ OFFERS CONDOLENCES ON LOSS OF BLUE ANGEL  April 23  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today offered condolences for the loss suffered over the weekend of Blue Angel pilot Lieutenant Commander Kevin Davis  Lt  Cmdr Davis was killed Saturday in Beaufort  S C   when his No  6 jet crashed during an air show     I was saddened to hear of the loss of Lieutenant Commander Kevin Davis  The crash during the air show at Beaufort  South Carolina and the loss of Blue Angel Number 6 was a tragedy  My thoughts and prayers are with Kevins family     The Blue Angels represent the very best in naval aviation and mean so much to many people  especially those in Northwest Florida  As a Floridian  I wish to extend my profound condolences to Kevins family  his squadron  and the entire Navy                                                                    April 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                      Current record
