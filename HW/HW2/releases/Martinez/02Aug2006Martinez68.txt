                               SENATOR MARTINEZ APPLAUDS TRANSPORTATION INITIATIVE  August 2  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded the agreement announced by the Florida Department of Transportation  FDOT  and CSX that clears the way for enhanced and expanded commuter rail in Central and South Florida   This is a fantastic opportunity to open alternative avenues of transportation for Floridians  This has been in the works for some time and I am pleased that FDOT and CSX have produced an agreement that will benefit the people of our state  said Martinez  This is a step that will eventually help alleviate traffic congestion and safely enhance commercial corridors  Im pleased and I applaud the hard work of the governors office and I look forward to working with the Federal Transit Administration to help make this private public partnership a success   In Central Florida  the agreement essentially creates a 61 5 mile rail corridor that will be used exclusively for passenger use 12 hours day  exclusive freight use for 5 hours day  and mixed passenger freight operation for 7 hours day   The agreement will also allow for the expansion of commuter rail opportunities in South Florida making it possible to extend commuter services to Miami International Airport and points south  Martinez concluded
