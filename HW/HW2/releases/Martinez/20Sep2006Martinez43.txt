                               MARTINEZ  NORTH OKALOOSA FIRE DEPARTMENT TO RECEIVE  900 000 DHS GRANT  September 20  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS  has awarded  900 379 to the North Okaloosa Fire District located in Crestview  Florida  The funds are part of the Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grants program  a program to help departments increase their number of trained firefighters   These funds are meant to bring more firefighters to the frontline  This is about making sure our local fire departments have the resources they need to achieve their critical missions  Martinez said  Todays announcement is testament to the quality of the North Okaloosa Fire District and the importance of their presence in Florida   North Okaloosa Fire District protects nearly 20 000 people living in a primarily rural area of 120 square miles  The department is a public department whose members are on a combination of paid and volunteer status   Funds under DHSs SAFER program are intended to support the hiring of firefighters and the recruitment and retention of volunteer firefighters  The goal of any SAFER grant is to enhance the ability of grantees to attain and maintain 24 hour staffing and to assure that their communities have adequate protection from fire and fire
