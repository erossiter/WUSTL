                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  FLORIDA FIRST RESPONDERS TO RECEIVE  1 MILLION IN DHS GRANTS  August 31  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that a  1 million grant has been awarded to the Walton County Fire Rescue in DeFuniak Springs department The grant is part of DHSs 2007 Assistance to Firefighters Grants program  designed to help departments and emergency medical service organizations improve their ability to protect the public and first responder personnel from fire and related hazards     Supplying Florida s first responders with top training and equipment allows our brave men and women to provide the best protections for the communities they serve  said Martinez  I commend DHS for investing this substantial amount in our first responders and helping make our communities that much safer     Funds under the Assistance to Firefighters grants program are intended to support first responder training  supply adequate firefighting EMS equipment   including rescue vehicles and protective gear  as well as provide for the wellness and fitness of firefighters                                                                   August 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                            Current record
