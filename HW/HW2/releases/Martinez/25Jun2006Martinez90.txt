                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                               MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON LATEST POMBO OCS LEGISLATION   June 25  2006   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement in regards to the pending House Resources Committee bill that contains language pertinent to oil and gas exploration off of Floridas coast   I applaud Congressman Adam Putnams efforts to protect Floridas interests  but I cannot support the Pombo bill in its current form    My objection is based on the lack of an adequate buffer afforded to Florida  Even at 100 miles  the buffer is inadequate  If the House bill is brought up in the Senate in its current form  I would be inclined to join Senator Nelson in a filibuster of the measure   The overall bill as it pertains to Florida and our nations energy needs is a step in the right direction  If Congressman Putnam and other members of the Florida congressional delegation are able to extend the buffer to 125 miles and preserve a drilling moratorium through at least the year 2022  I would support that bill                                                                 June 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                    Current record
