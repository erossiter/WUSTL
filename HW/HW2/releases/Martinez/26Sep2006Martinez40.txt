                               MARTINEZ TO UNVEIL NATIONAL HURRICANE RESEARCH INITIATIVE  September 26  2006   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  on Friday  September 29  will unveil legislation to implement a national hurricane research initiative designed to help Americans better predict and prepare for hurricanes  The initiative sets out clear goals for research gathering and the creation of a national database to help all levels of government better establish public policy in relation to hurricane planning   The legislation will be unveiled in conjunction with the release of a new draft report by the National Science Board entitled  Hurricane Warning  The Critical Need for a National Hurricane Research Initiative  The National Science Board is a 24 member independent advisory body to the President and Congress on matters of national science and engineering policy   Who  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  and members of the National Science Board   What  Report release and unveiling of national hurricane research initiative   Where  U S  Capitol  Room  SC 6   When  Friday  September 29  2006 at 10 30 am ET   Background  Since December 2005  a Science Board task force has been studying the issue of nationwide investments in hurricane science and engineering  Its report warns that relative to the tremendous damage and suffering caused by hurricanes  the federal investment in hurricane science and engineering is insufficient  and as the Board document exclaims  Time is not on our side                                                                    September 2006 News
