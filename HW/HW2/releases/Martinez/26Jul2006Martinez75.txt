                               SENATOR MARTINEZ COMMENDS PRIME MINISTER MALIKI   S COMMITMENT TO A FREE AND DEMOCRATIC IRAQ  July 26  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today made the following statement following the Joint Address to Congress given by Iraqi Prime Minister Maliki   Today  Prime Minister Maliki reaffirmed Iraqs commitment to stand with the American people in the War on Terror  I was heartened to hear him unequivocally state that freedom and respect for human rights are not just Western values  but universal and inalienable rights   Last week  Ambassador Khalilzad testified before the Senate Foreign Relations Committee and confirmed that sectarian violence was the number one threat to security in Iraq  The Prime Ministers plan for reconciliation provides the best hope to stop the spiraling violence   Our brave soldiers in Iraq and the Iraqi people continue to face security challenges  I was encouraged to hear the Prime Ministers commitment to dismantle all militias without distinction  I applaud his determination to work on strong financial controls to reinforce Iraqi economic growth   Prime Minister Maliki addressed Congress in Arabic in a speech that was broadcast live in Iraq and the region  This sent a clear message of reform  not only to Iraqis  but to those in the broader Middle East yearning for freedom  peace  and democracy                                                             July 2006 News Releases             Browse by
