                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  MORE THAN  2 MILLION IN FEMA GRANTS AWARDED FOR VOLUSIA FIRE COMPLEX  February 15  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the Florida Division of Emergency Management will receive more than  2 million in federal funds to reimburse Volusia County for costs connected to the suppression of uncontrolled wild fires  known as the Volusia Fire Complex     This is welcome assistance for Volusia County and the state of Florida  This will help reimburse the County for their efforts during the wild fires  but also improves its ability to respond in the future  said Martinez     In 2006  this wild fire burned approximately 4 200 acres of private and public forest lands and mandatory evacuations were required of residences and businesses in the communities of Tiffany Estates  Waterford Estates  and Magnolia Village in the City of Edgewater and portions of unincorporated Volusia County                                                                   February 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
