                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES  27 MILLION NASA CONTRACT AWARDED TO FLORIDA COMPANY  July 27  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that NASA has selected Iveys Construction Inc  of Merritt Island  Fla   to build a new lightning protection system for Launch Pad 39B at the Kennedy Space Center  The system will support launches of the Constellation Program s Ares I rockets     Space exploration is an important industry for Florida and our nation  I am pleased NASA has chosen a Florida company to enhance their Cape Canaveral facilities  Martinez said  This project is critical because as the next generation space exploration vehicles are developed  we simply must ensure the infrastructure is in place to protect them from natures elements     Ivey s Construction Inc  will receive a fixed price contract for  27 915 000  The system is expected to be complete by March 2010  The lightning protection system is designed to reduce the probability of a direct lightning strike to the Ares I and associated launch equipment during processing and other activities prior to flight                                                                   July 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                          Current record
