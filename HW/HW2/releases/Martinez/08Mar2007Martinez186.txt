                               MARTINEZ  FLORIDA FIRST RESPONDERS TO RECEIVE MORE THAN  460 000  March 8  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS  has awarded  460 607 in grants to five Florida fire departments and rescue services  The grants are part of DHSs 2006 Assistance to Firefighters Grants  AFG  program  designed to help departments and emergency medical service organizations improve their ability to protect the public and first responder personnel from fire and related hazards     Florida s brave men and women dedicate themselves to providing community safety  said Martinez  These grants allow our departments to equip themselves with advanced training and technology that provides the best protection to our citizens     Since 2001  Assistance to Firefighters grants have enhanced the ability of first responders to protect the communities they serve from fire and fire related hazards  Funds under this program are intended to support first responder training  provide adequate firefighting EMS equipment  including rescue vehicles  protective gear  as well as support the wellness and fitness of firefighters     Floridas first responders receiving grants    Department  City of Melbourne Fire Department   Amount    266 650   Location  Melbourne  FL    Department  City of St
