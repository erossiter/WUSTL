                               MARTINEZ AND FOLEY ANNOUNCE  14 MILLION FOR COMMUNICATION IMPROVEMENTS IN PORT ST  LUCIE  December 12  2005   WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  and U S  Representative Mark Foley  R 16  today announced the U S  Department of Agriculture has approved a  14 470 000 loan for the improvement and further establishment of communication equipment and systems in Port St  Lucie    These funds will allow the city of Port St  Lucie and surrounding areas to implement much needed communications upgrades  I also want to recognize the hard work of Congressman Mark Foley in making this opportunity possible  Martinez said  The new system will enhance public safety  improve health care communications systems  and broaden the reach of Floridas emergency response network and information system   St  Lucie County is one of the fastest growing localities in the country and with more people moving in each year  the need for enhanced communications has grown exponentially  said Foley  The internet represents access to jobs and education for millions and providing easier access to the residents of St  Lucie will benefit the whole community   The USDA loan will allow the creation of a fiber network that will serve more than 15 000 homes  Homeowners will have access to basic cable services  high speed internet  and additional enhanced communications
