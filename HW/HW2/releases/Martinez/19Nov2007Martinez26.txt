                               MARTINEZ  DURING THE HOLIDAYS  THINK OF MILITARY FAMILIES  November 19  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  has joined colleagues in introducing a sense of the Senate resolution recognizing the tremendous sacrifices and contributions military families have made to our Nation     As we enter the holiday season  its important that we remember not only the men and women serving in the military  but their families who often bear the most immediate and profound burden of the absence of their loved one  said Martinez  Our military families serve this country with an equal amount of dedication as those fighting in the field and we owe them a debt of gratitude     In speaking this past weekend to the Military Officers Association of America  Senator Martinez commended organizations that support military families with emergency assistance including the Navy Marine Corps Relief Society  Army Emergency Relief  Air Force Aid Society  Coast Guard Mutual Relief  and Wounded Warrior Project  agencies that specifically exist to provide grants or no interest loans to military families     These privately funded service organizations assist our military families in times of financial distress  providing money for food  rent  and utilities  said Martinez  The holidays can be especially stressful on military families and I encourage Floridians to show our troops and the families they leave at home that were thinking of them during this time of war                                                                   November 2007 News Releases
