                               REMINDER TO SUBMIT APPLICATIONS FOR MILITARY ACADEMY NOMINATIONS   September 10  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  reminds Floridians that Sunday  September 30  2007 is the deadline to submit applications to Senator Martinezs Office for a congressional nomination to attend U S  Service Academies  Service academies requiring a nomination include the Air Force Academy at Colorado Springs  the Merchant Marine Academy at Kings Point  the Military Academy at West Point  and the Naval Academy at Annapolis  the Coast Guard Academy does not require a nomination  Applications must be postmarked or shipped through a delivery service no later than September 30 and sent to     Office of U S  Senator Mel Martinez   Attn  Military Academy Coordinator   315 East Robinson Street   Landmark Center 1  Suite 475   Orlando  FL 32801    Interviews for qualified candidates will be held on November 7  2007 in Orlando  Tampa  Miami and Tallahassee  Selected applicants will be contacted by the Orlando office with their interview time and location     The entire application packet and general information regarding the process can be found on the Web at       Applicants may contact the Orlando office at 407 254 2573 or toll free 866 630 7106 with any questions                                                                   September 2007 News Releases
