                               SENATOR MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES COMMUNITY OFFICE HOURS IN CENTRAL AND SOUTH FLORIDA   April 17  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced Community Office Hours in Central Florida to be held in Lake  Sumter  Volusia  and Seminole counties on Wednesday  April 18th and Thursday  April 19th     In addition  South Florida Community Office Hours will be held in Broward and Palm Beach counties on Wednesday  April 25th and Thursday  April 26th     At Community Office Hours events  caseworkers from the office of Senator Martinez are available to assist constituents with concerns and issues ranging from veterans services to healthcare  Social Security  Medicare  and other services provided by the federal government     Community Office Hours allow me and my staff to better serve Floridians by staying more informed and up to date about current questions and concerns  Martinez said  With nearly 18 million constituents in Florida  a traveling team of caseworkers ensures that more constituents needs are met in every region of the Sunshine State     WHAT  Senator Martinez Community Office Hours   DATE  Wednesday  April 18th
