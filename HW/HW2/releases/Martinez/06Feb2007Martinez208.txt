                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ OPPOSED TO NEW EFFORTS TO WEAKEN CUBA SANCTIONS  February 6  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement addressing newly introduced legislative proposals seeking to weaken U S  sanctions towards Cuba    Senator Martinez said    Unfortunately  these agricultural trade bills are misleading as to Cubas political and economic reality  They attempt to look at Cuba like doing business with the Dominican Republican or any other free nation in our hemisphere  The truth is that Cuba is one of the worlds few remaining totalitarian states and among the five remaining state sponsors of terror  I will continue to oppose all openings with a dictatorship until there are conditions in place that support the right of the Cuban people to live in freedom                                                                 February 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
