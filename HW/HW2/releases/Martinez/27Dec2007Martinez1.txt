                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON ASSASSINATION OF FORMER PRIME MINISTER BENAZIR BHUTTO   December 27  2007   ORLANDO  FL   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued a statement following the assassination of former Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto     Senator Martinez said     This is a very sad day for the people of Pakistan  Prime Minister Bhutto was the best hope for Pakistan and she would have been a much needed  moderate leader and a helpful partner on the war on terror     This is not only a setback for democracy in Pakistan  but it also creates uncertainty and has serious ramifications for our own national security  I am concerned about the violence and hope it does not continue  This cowardly act only reinforces our need to remain steadfast in defeating Islamic extremism     I extend my deep condolences to her family  her supporters  and to the people of Pakistan as they continue to work against the forces of terror and in favor of democracy                                                                   December 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                              Current record
