                               MARTINEZ PRAISES VA SELECTION OF LOCATION FOR ORLANDO VETERANS HOSPITAL Lake Nona site chosen for new state of the art facility March 1  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today received word from the U S  Veterans Affairs  VA  Administration that Lake Nona will be the site of the new Orlando Veterans Affairs Hospital  VA Secretary Jim Nicholson notified the senator of the site selection decision this evening     This is the most positive and important development for Central Florida veterans this decade  said Martinez  Now that an actual site has been chosen  the project moves forward into design  planning  and construction  I thank Secretary Nicholson for his work on this project  Our veterans have waited a long time for this day and I look forward to seeing this facilitys opening     Senator Martinez and VA Secretary Jim Nicholson spoke today on the subject  Nicholson said design and site preparation could start next year with construction starting in 2009  The expected completion date for the veterans hospital is mid 2012  The estimated cost of the hospital is  560 million     The next step from Congress perspective is to ensure the VA has the funds they need to build this facility  the Florida delegation is united on seeing this project completed  Martinez said  This is more than a hospital  its a promise to take care of those who protect and serve our country                                                                    March 2007 News Releases             Browse by
