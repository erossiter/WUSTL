                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  ORLANDO VA HOSPITAL LAND ACQUISITION FUNDS PASS SENATE   September 7  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  this week announced that the Senate has approved  35 million dollars for the land acquisition of the new Veterans Affairs  VA  hospital in Orlando  FL  The funds are contained in the Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriation Act of Fiscal Year 2008  The bill passed the Senate by a vote of 92 to 1     With this funding  the VA can finally begin site and utility work on this new and essential hospital  Martinez said  This is great news for Central Floridas veterans  who have waited too long for a new facility  This progress brings us one step closer to providing our veterans with the quality care they deserve  With the acquisition of the land  design and site preparation could start next year with construction starting in 2009  The expected completion date for the veterans hospital is mid 2012  The estimated cost of the hospital is  560 million     The bill now goes to conference to be reconciled with the House version of the bill                                                                    September 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
