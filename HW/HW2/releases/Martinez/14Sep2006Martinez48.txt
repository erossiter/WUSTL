                               MARTINEZ  GROWING ALLIANCE BETWEEN CUBA  IRAN  VENEZUELA ALARMING Calls on NAM participants to disavow anti American rhetoric  September 14  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today said the strengthening strategic alliance between Cuba  Iran and Venezuela is an increasing danger to U S  national security and the security of the Western Hemisphere  Noting the dangers of such an alliance  the senator called on countries participating in this weeks Non Aligned Movement summit to denounce and reject the growing anti American rhetoric being espoused by these totalitarian regimes   Martinez said   The United States has many allies within this group who share the values of freedom and democracy  But the axis between Cuba and Iran  where the rule of law is arbitrary and there is growing anti American sentiment  has been disturbing for some time  Now with Venezuela teaming up with these regimes  there is a dangerous alliance strengthening that is a direct threat to U S  security and the principles of democracy and freedom   I call on the nations participating in NAM to adhere to its principles of democratic rule  to denounce Islamic radicalism  and to understand that the U S  is a constructive and positive force in the world  We need to call on participating nations of goodwill not to let this event be hijacked by hateful rhetoric                                                                    September 2006 News Releases             Browse by
