                               MARTINEZ URGES COLLEAGUES TO REAUTHORIZE CHILDREN   S HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM  September 26  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today addressed the Senate to discuss the State Childrens Health Insurance Program  S CHIP   Senator Martinez announced he is in favor of reauthorizing the current program with a  5 billion to  10 billion dollar expansion  but registered his opposition to the Democratic alternative that is expected to be brought up later this week     Senator Martinez said     I support S CHIP  I support the program with the original mission of covering low income children who do not have health insurance  This bill that were debating today is not that program  Mr  President  It isnt even close     It is bad policy to take a program designed to help poor children and create a new entitlement for families making  80 thousand dollars a year     By expanding coverage further up the income scale and to new populations  this bill takes away needed resources for those most vulnerable  low income children     You can view Senator Martinezs speech at this link     http   www youtube com user jkq2ery8046     A transcript
