                               MARTINEZ  HISTORIC GONZALES NOMINATION SHOULD NOT BE OVERSHADOWED BY PARTISAN POLITICS  February 2  2005   WASHINGTON   In his first remarks on the Senate floor  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today rose to defend the nomination of Alberto Gonzales to be the first Hispanic American U S  Attorney General   Martinez said      As a freshman Senator  I was hoping to wait a little longer before speaking for the first time on the Senate floor   However  I could not fail to speak in defense of Judge Alberto Gonzales  I am disappointed that he has been the subject of partisan attacks         Al Gonzales is a dedicated public servant and exceptionally qualified to be our nation   s next Attorney General  I know that a lot has been said about Judge Gonzales    life story  It is a story of the fulfillment of the American Dream      As a fellow Hispanic American  I want to put the nomination of Judge Alberto Gonzales to be Attorney General in perspective  No Hispanic American has ever served at this level of government   No Hispanic American has ever served in one of the four top Cabinet positions in the U S  government     not at Treasury  DOD  State or as Attorney General       Judge Gonzales will be the first Hispanic American to serve in one of the top four Cabinet positions when he becomes our nation   s Attorney General  This is a breakthrough of incredible magnitude for Hispanic Americans that should not be diluted by partisan politics      Delivering a message to all Hispanic Americans in Spanish  Martinez said      From our school children  to our law students  to the parents and grandparents who came to America to create a better life for themselves and their families in the United States   I have this message for you today      Judge Gonzales is one of us  He represents all of our hopes and dreams    for our children  Let us acknowledge the importance of this moment for     especially our youth  We cannot allow petty politicking to deny us this moment that fills us all with such pride  Let us all support Alberto Gonzales                                                                February 2005 News Releases
