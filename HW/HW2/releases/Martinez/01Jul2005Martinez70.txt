                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF SANDRA DAY O CONNOR  July 1  2005   WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today made the following statement regarding the resignation of Justice Sandra Day O   Connor      Justice Sandra Day O   Connor has served on our nation   s highest court with great distinction for twenty three years  As the first woman to serve on the Supreme Court  she established herself as an independent thinker and was a pivotal swing vote on some of our nation   s most critical rulings  She has my deepest appreciation for her years of public service to our country and her contributions to our Courts  I wish her all the best in retirement      I look forward to President Bush sending to the Senate a well qualified nominee with high ethics  judicial temperament and a strong understanding of the important role of the Court   I believe it is critical that the process going forward be fair  devoid of political assassination  and conducted with fairness and finality                                                                        July 2005 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
