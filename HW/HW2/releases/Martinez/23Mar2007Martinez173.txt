                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  USS JOHN F KENNEDY GAVE US GREAT SERVICE  March 23  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement on the official decommissioning of the USS John F Kennedy     Senator Martinez said     The John F Kennedy has been of great service to our nation and proved time and again to be a vital component to our national security  In times of conflict  she has served us well and protected the sailors that served aboard  In times of peace  she worked as a deterrent to attack and a reminder of the reach of our military might  From the Vietnam war to the fight against al Qaeda  this is a carrier that will always be remembered for the battles she fought and the battles she prevented     As we move forward from this day  I look forward to seeing Mayport replenished with the ships and vessels that will compliment the service the JFK has given us  I want to thank all those who have served aboard and maintained the USS John F Kennedy to make her the presence that she was  and the freedom she made possible                                                                     March 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                    Current record
