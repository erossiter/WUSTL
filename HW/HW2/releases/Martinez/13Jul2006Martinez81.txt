                               SENATOR MARTINEZ CONDEMNS RECENT ATTACKS BY HEZBOLLAH  July 13  2006   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement condemning the brazen attacks of Hezbollah  calling for the immediate and unconditional release of two kidnapped Israeli soldiers and for swift action by the Lebanese government to crack down on Hezbollah  Senator Martinez said   This week  Hezbollah terrorists operating out of Lebanon  boldly attacked Israeli forces  killing eight soldiers and kidnapping two others  I condemn this unprovoked act of terrorism that can potentially exacerbate the already high tensions in the region   This attack is a grim reminder of Israels vulnerability  and how important it is that we help our reliable and steadfast ally guarantee the security of its territory and citizens  It underscores Israels right to protect itself in the midst of multiple threats   These actions do not serve the interest of the Lebanese people and greatly undermine Lebanese sovereignty and security  I hope the Lebanese government will move swiftly to control this unacceptable situation  As long standing supporters of Hezbollah  Syria and Iran should be held equally responsible for this violence    For these reasons  I am adding my voice to the many calling for the immediate and unconditional release of the two Israeli soldiers  I urge the international community to encourage all the parties in the region to abide by their obligations under United Nations Security Council resolutions 1559 and 1680 and cease all support for Hezbollah
