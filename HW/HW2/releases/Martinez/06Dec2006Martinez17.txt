                               MARTINEZ APPLAUDS COSTA RICA   S MESSAGE OF FREEDOM AND DEMOCRACY  December 6  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  welcomed the Costa Rica President Oscar Arias and his delegation this week     Martinez said     I congratulate President Arias for his continuous pursuit of freedom and democracy in Costa Rica and the Western Hemisphere  Most notably  I applaud his outspoken criticism of the Castro dictatorship and desire to support democratic change  Costa Rica has worked to promote democratic principles that will truly support the Cuban people  as opposed to allowing a succession within the same dictatorial regime  The U S  should continue to work closely with Costa Rica  with similar values and principles  to voice the message of democracy     I commend President Arias steady  long term commitment to investing in education  health and housing as vehicles to enhance the quality of life of the Costa Rican people and strengthen their economic competitiveness  Our country should explore programs in education and economic development  in addition to securing Costa Ricas participation in the Central American Free Trade Agreement  to assist the Costa Rican people  I look forward to working with the government of Costa Rica to promote new programs and bring further growth to their country
