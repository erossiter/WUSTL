                               MARTINEZ VOTES TO IMPROVE AMERICA   S TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION Bill encourages more education  research and innovation in fields of science and technology April 26  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today praised the Senates passage of the America COMPETES Act  a bill to improve education opportunities for students in the areas of math  science  technology and engineering  The act  which Senator Martinez co sponsored  passed by a vote of 88 to 8     America has been the global leader in innovative technology  and we must ensure that we continue to hold that position in our competitive global market  said Martinez  We must increase our research and development programs and we have to prepare and encourage our children to enter fields of math  science  engineering  and technology     Over the next decade  the America COMPETES Act doubles the investment in basic research at leading federal scientific research facilities  It strengthens educational opportunities in science  technology  engineering  and mathematics from elementary through graduate school  This includes expanding access to programs for low income students and increasing teacher training in these fields  In addition  the bill will focus on developing an infrastructure to enhance innovation and competitiveness in the United States     These initiatives will help to keep the United States at the forefront of the science and technology fields on the increasingly competitive global stage  Martinez said
