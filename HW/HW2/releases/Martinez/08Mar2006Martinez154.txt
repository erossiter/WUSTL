                               MARTINEZ VOWS TO TAKE OCS LEASE 181 FIGHT TO SENATE FLOOR  March 8  2006   WASHINGTON  DC U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today said he was disappointed with todays passage of a bill authored by Senators Pete Domenici  R NM  and Jeff Bingaman  D NM  that would open a sizable portion of Lease Area 181 to offshore oil and natural gas exploration and drilling  The Domenici   Bingaman bill would allow drilling to occur within a hundred miles of Floridas coastline    Florida needs assurances that we wont have to fight this battle year after year  Unfortunately the Domenici bill does nothing to address that issue  said Martinez  We need to debate this issue in a forum where lawmakers will be more sensitive to a balanced approach to energy exploration and thats why I have decided to take this fight to the Senate floor   The Domenici   Bingaman bill passed the Senate Energy Committee today by a vote of 16 to 5 with one senator voting present  The measure now moves to the full Senate for consideration   Florida needs and demands better protection  A hundred mile buffer is not enough  said Martinez  Im encouraged that even some of those who may have voted for the bill in committee left the meeting I think with a better understanding of Floridas concerns and the need for permanence
