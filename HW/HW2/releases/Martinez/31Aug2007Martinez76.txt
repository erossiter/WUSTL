                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SENATOR MARTINEZ COMMENTS ON THE RETIREMENT OF SENATOR JOHN WARNER  August 31  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement on retirement of U S  Senator John Warner  R VA      Senator Martinez said     Senator Warner  through his years of public service and hard work  has greatly benefited Virginia  the Senate  and our entire nation  As a member of our armed forces  Secretary of the Navy  and member of the United States Senate  Senator Warner has exhibited a tireless lifelong dedication to making sure our nation is safe and secure     On a personal note  Ive always considered John to be the epitome of what a senator ought to be  I only hope that in some small way Ive learned from him so that my career can help to build on the great legacy he leaves with this institution     I wish John well in his future endeavors  We will miss his presence in the Senate                                                                   August 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                              Current record
