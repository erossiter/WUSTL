                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ CONDEMNS FRAUDULENT USE OF RELIGIOUS TRAVEL LICENSES  February 22  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today condemned the fraudulent use of religious travel to Cuba as a business enterprise    U S  Attorney for the Southern District  Alexander Acosta  today announced the arrest of two individuals who fraudulently obtained  marketed and sold religious travel licenses which resulted in over 4 000 people illegally traveling to Cuba    Such abuse diminishes the importance of religious travel to Cuba during this critical time on the island  said Martinez    Martinez stressed that the actions by the United States government today should not discourage those seeking legitimate religious travel but should serve as a deterrent to those who seek to commit fraud    We should continue to encourage travel to Cuba for genuine religious purposes for individuals wishing to practice their faith said Martinez                                                                 February 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                      Current record
