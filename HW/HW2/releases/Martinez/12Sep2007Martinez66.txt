                               SENATOR MARTINEZ   26 MILLION FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF PANAMA CITY BAY COUNTY AIRPORT  September 12  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced the Department of Transportation has approved a  26 264 212 grant for the Phase 1 construction of the new Panama City Bay County International Airport     This grant will allow for the construction to begin on the new Panama City Bay County airport  which will increase airport capacity and provide the Panhandle region the economic development that its residents are seeking  said Martinez  I congratulate the working relationship between Bay Countys local leaders  the FAA  business community and environmentalists who have worked tirelessly to make this project a successful addition to this region of Florida     This project will place the existing airport site that is constrained and cannot accommodate the forecast traffic growth  The new airport is necessary to accommodate the 6800 foot runway identified by the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  as being needed for regional jet and narrow body jet aircraft operations through the current planning period  2018   It will also allow for standard Runway Safety Areas that are not practical to construct at the existing airport site                                                                   September 2007 News Releases
