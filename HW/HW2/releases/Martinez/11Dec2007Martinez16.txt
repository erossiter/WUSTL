                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ STOPS ATTEMPT TO WEAKEN CUBA EMBARGO  December 11  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today successfully stopped an effort to liberalize agricultural trade between the U S  and Cuba  Senator Max Baucus  D Mont   offered and then withdrew a U S  Cuba trade proposal after Senator Martinez and several of his colleagues made clear that consideration of the underlying Farm Bill would be stalled indefinitely if the Cuba language were moved forward for consideration     It is time for change in Cuba  People on the island are increasingly pushing for change and that is the desire of the Cuban people  But the Cuban regime  true to its nature  continues to repress the people  This is why we should not reward the Cuban government with a change in United States policy  said Martinez  I thank the senator from Montana for withdrawing this ill timed and ill advised amendment     The Baucus amendment  cosponsored by Senator Mike Crapo  R ID   would have opened agricultural trade between the U S  and Cuba and allowed direct monetary transfers from American banks to Cuban banks                                                                  December 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
