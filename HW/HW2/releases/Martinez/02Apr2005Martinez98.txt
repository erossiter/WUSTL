                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF POPE JOHN PAUL II  April 2  2005   WASHINGTON     U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today made the following statement about the passing of Pope John Paul II  Karol Wojtyla       Today we have lost the spiritual leader of my Church and one of the most remarkable people of our times  Pope John Paul II ushered profound changes in the Roman Catholic Church and his leadership has left a strong and lasting legacy      The influence of Pope John Paul II extends well beyond the boundaries of the Roman Catholic Church  He has taken remarkable steps to achieve peace  democracy and freedom worldwide  He crusaded against communism throughout Eastern Europe and beyond  unbridled capitalism and political oppression  and he worked to unite people of all different faiths      Kitty and I are deeply saddened but remain strong in our faith and know that Pope John Paul II is at eternal rest with our God                                                                       April 2005 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                Current record
