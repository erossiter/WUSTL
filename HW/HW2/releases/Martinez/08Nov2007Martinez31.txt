                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  FLORIDA NATIONAL CEMETERY IN BUSHNELL TO EXPAND   November 8  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  has awarded a  20 2 million construction contract to expand the Florida National Cemetery in Bushnell  The contract has been awarded to QGS Development Inc   of Lithia  FL to build additional burial sections and associated facilities at the VA cemetery     This expansion will ensure that the brave men and women whove served our nation in the military will have a proper burial  honoring their dedication to our country  Martinez said  It will also give families in Florida a better opportunity to be close to their loved ones that have passed     The project will encompass nearly 70 acres and will provide approximately 41 000 additional gravesites  These will include 20 000 pre placed crypts for caskets  4 000 other casket gravesites  9 000 in ground cremation sites and 8 000 columbaria niches for cremation remains  The project will also include two new committal service shelters  a public restroom  a storage facility  and other improvements  Construction is expected to be finished by late 2009                                                                   November 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                              Current record
