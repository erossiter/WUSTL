                               MARTINEZ  FEDERAL ASSISTANCE APPROVED FOR FLORIDA TORNADO RECOVERY   February 3  2007   Lady Lake  FL   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement after touring tornado affected areas of Central Florida and meeting with Florida Governor Charlie Crist and Federal Emergency Management Agency Director David Paulison  Director Paulison announced that President Bush has issued a disaster declaration for four Florida counties  freeing federal assistance for Lake  Seminole  Sumter  and Volusia counties     Senator Martinez said     Im delighted to know the federal government is going to provide the assistance that it can  I commend the governor for pulling everyone together  Governor Crist has responded ably and capably  bringing together the local  federal and state entities together to get this job done   In the days to come  our offices will work together as problems arise and people need help  our caseworkers will be available for people in the affected areas to respond to their needs     We know that this effort doesnt end today  After the cameras go away and attention is focused elsewhere  people will still be hurting  Theres going to be a long term recovery and I look forward to helping and working with all levels of government to see that people get the help they need
