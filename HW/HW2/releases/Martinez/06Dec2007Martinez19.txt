                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              Martinez Calls Army Corps Bomb Search Expansion    Positive Step      December 6  2007   WASHINGTON   Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today commended the Army Corps of Engineers for expanding their search for unexploded ordinance connected to the Pinecastle Jeep Range  The Corps announced today its plans for investigating portions of the Warwick subdivision  immediately adjacent to the eastern boundary of the Pinecastle Jeep Range     This is a step in the right direction  I commend the Corps for expanding their search  said Martinez  In the interest of public safety we need the Corps to continue searching areas where bombs are being found  regardless of boundaries or procedural hurdles     Since last summer  Senators Martinez and Bill Nelson  D FL  and Representative Ric Keller  R Orlando  have continued an open dialogue with Corps officials regarding the search for unexploded ordinance and expansion to include areas outside of the Pinecastle Jeep Range  a World War II era bombing practice range                                                                   December 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
