                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ COMMENTS ON FOURTH ANNIVERSARY OF 9 11  September 9  2005     WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today released the following statement honoring the memory of those lost in the tragic terrorist attacks of September 11  2001  We are four years from that fateful day but the pain is still fresh in our nations heart  I am grateful that since that tragic day  our country has demonstrated resolve  compassion  and a commitment to retaliate against those who attacked us  We are still at war with those forces  they wish to destroy our country  our citizens  and our way of life  We must remain firm in our commitment to winning the War on Terror  Let us pray and remember those loved ones lost on September 11  2001 and those lost in the War on Terror  It is fitting that we also pay tribute and send support to those who continue fighting on the front lines in Iraq     and elsewhere  I have met with a number of military personnel who have served overseas since 9 11 and their words and actions are heartening  They know their mission is important and critical to defending and protecting our families and our liberty                                                                September 2005 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
