                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SENATOR MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES ESTABLISHMENT OF PALM BEACH OFFICE  August 3  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced the opening of a regional office in Palm Beach  The office becomes the senators seventh regional office and is intended to centrally serve the residents of Okeechobee  St  Lucie  Martin  Palm Beach  and Broward counties  The office is located at 7711 N  Military Trail  Suite 1014 in Palm Beach     Im always looking for ways to better serve constituents  Establishing a brick and mortar presence in the region gives constituents a convenient location where they can seek assistance with issues regarding a federal agency or program  said Martinez     Senator Martinez also announced the installation of Steve Martino as his new Southeast Regional Director     Steve is a native Floridian born and raised in this region  Martinez said  His wealth of knowledge about the area and its history and his experience will serve Floridians well     Prior to being appointed Southeast Regional Director  Martino served as the Federal Emergency Management Agency s Congressional Liaison in Lake Mary  FL                                                                   August 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
