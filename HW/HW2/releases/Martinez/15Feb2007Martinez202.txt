                               MARTINEZ  FLORIDA FIRST RESPONDERS TO RECEIVE NEARLY  1 MILLION  February 15  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Security  DHS  has awarded  999 925 in grants to four Florida fire departments and rescue services  The grants are part of DHSs 2006 Assistance to Firefighters Grants  AFG  program  designed to help departments and emergency medical service organizations improve their ability to protect the public and first responder personnel from fire and related hazards   Florida fire fighters are committed to providing essential protection and safety services to their communities  said Martinez  With these grants  our fire departments are better equipped and have access to better training and technology so they can protect our neighborhoods and keep our citizens safe     Since 2001  Assistance to Firefighters grants have enhanced the ability of first responders to protect the communities they serve from fire and fire related hazards  Funds under this program are intended to support first responder training  provide adequate firefighting EMS equipment  including rescue vehicles  protective gear  as well as support the wellness and fitness of firefighters     Floridas first responders receiving grants     Department  City of Miami Department of Fire Rescue   Amount
