                               MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF ADMIRAL    JACK    FETTERMAN  March 31  2006   WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Martinez  R FL  today made the following statement on the Senate floor regarding the passing of Vice Admiral John Jack Fetterman    Mr  President  I rise today to recognize the passing of an exceptional leader  as well as a respected Floridian  Vice Admiral John Jack Fetterman passed away last Friday in his home in Pensacola  Florida  at the age of 73   Following graduation from Albright College in Pennsylvania and Aviation Officer Candidate School in Pensacola  the Admiral began his career as naval aviator  He became the Pacific Fleets Naval Air Force Commander in 1987 and was promoted to vice admiral    Mr  President  I had the pleasure of meeting and working with Admiral Fetterman during the base closure and realignment process last year  and I found him to be a fierce and eloquent defender of the Navy and of the military    In 1991  Admiral Fetterman became Chief of Naval Education and Training at Pensacola Naval Air Station  He created and was the father of the core values training program which earned him the title of the Father of Navy Ethics    Admiral Fetterman retired as a three star admiral in 1993  but upon his retirement he didnt just retire  he continued his love of the Navy and his service to the nation by becoming the President and CEO of the Naval Aviation Museum Foundation    Mr  President  Ill remember Mr  Fetterman with a great deal of love and care  He guided and directed the Museum of Naval Aviation in Pensacola  which is truly a wonderful and remarkable place where the many heroic feats of people over the years connected to naval aviation are recorded and appreciated    Admiral Fetterman to the very last continued to serve his nation and his country well  I would like to extend my condolences to the members of his family  to his beloved wife and to all those in the community  in the naval community who came in contact with such a fine American who served this country so well    At times like this I know were always reminded that life is finite and that we also have to hearken and always appreciate a life well lived  as was Admiral Fettermans    Mr  President  I yield the floor                                                                 March 2006 News Releases
