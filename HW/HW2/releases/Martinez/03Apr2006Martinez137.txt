                               NCAA FINAL FOUR    ORANGES VERSUS OSCAR NOMINEES     Senators Martinez and Feinstein Strike a Friendly Wager on the Final Championship April 3  2006   WASHINGTON  DC  In support of their respective teams  U S  Senators Mel Martinez  R FL  and Dianne Feinstein  D CA  laid a wager on tonights NCAA National Championship game between the University of Florida Gators and the UCLA Bruins  Senator Martinez has put forth a box of Florida oranges and Senator Feinstein has offered up a set of DVDs nominated for Best Picture in the 2006 Academy Awards   The Bruins might be counting on tradition to pull them through  but in Florida  its about playing basketball  The Gators strong offense and its team of record winning players that have already put them at the top of the charts will leave the Bruins stuck waist high in the middle of the Swamp  Martinez said  Our combination of big men inside and terrific three point shooting will chomp away any hope the Bruins have of winning this game   There wont be a recount tonight after UCLA brings home the NCAA championship  said Senator Feinstein  With 11 National Championships under their belt  the Bruins will defeat the Gators in this battle between the Golden State and the Sunshine State  And well prove that not only do we have better beaches and better oranges  but we also have better basketball   The 2006 NCAA National Championship game will be played in Indianapolis  Indiana today  April 3rd at 9 21 pm  The winner of tonights game will claim the 2006 National Champion title
