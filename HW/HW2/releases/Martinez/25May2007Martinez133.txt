                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  VENEZUELAN GOVERNMENT RESTRICTING FUNDAMENTAL DEMOCRATIC FREEDOMS  May 25  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today condemned the Government of Venezuela for their decision to not renew the broadcast license for Radio Caracas Television  Senator Martinez was an original co sponsor of Senate Resolution 211  approved by the Senate late last night  expressing concerns over the transgression of freedom of thought and expression that is being carried out in Venezuela     Martinez said     The Venezuelan government continues to move away from fundamental democratic principles and freedoms  shutting down Radio Caracas Television  the countrys oldest TV station and one of few independent media outlets  for clearly political reasons echoes the behavior of authoritarian regimes that protect themselves by censoring alternate views  It is in the interest of the Venezuelan people that we speak out about this additional restriction being placed on them                                                                   May 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                      Current record
