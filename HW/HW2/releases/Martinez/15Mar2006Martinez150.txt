                               MARTINEZ  IMMIGRATION REFORM MUST ADDRESS AGRICULTURAL WORKERS  March 15  2006   WASHINGTON  DC U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today participated in a news conference with Senator Larry Craig  R Idaho  and members of the Agriculture Coalition for Immigration Reform  Martinez spoke about the importance of comprehensive immigration reform to the agricultural industry   For our economy  our food supply  and fairness  it is essential that we address the needs of our agriculture industry  said Senator Martinez  There are reforms we can include in the comprehensive immigration reform bill that will give the agriculture industry the assurance they need that they will have a stable and reliable workforce  Without such a workforce  crops will stay in the field  they wont come to market  food prices will go up  and Floridas economy will take a huge hit    Martinez also discussed the need to reform the H 2A guest worker program and praised Senator Craigs AgJOBS legislation    I have been a strong supporter of AgJOBS legislation because it is an approach that will result in a more stable  secure  safe  and legal American agricultural work force  That is exactly what the agriculture industry in Florida needs  Martinez said                                                                   March 2006 News Releases             Browse by
