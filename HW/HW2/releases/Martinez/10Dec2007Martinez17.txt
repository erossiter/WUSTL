                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ COMMEMORATES HUMAN RIGHTS DAY   December 10  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today commented on Human Rights Day  which is observed by the international community every year on December 10th     Martinez said     As we commemorate the International Day of Human Rights  we must specifically remember the people of Cuba  Not only have they been denied human rights for more than 45 years  but in recent days there has been a severe crackdown against the youth of the country  One of the most egregious human rights violations happened last week when the Cuban government dragged 18 dissident youth from inside a church  violating that sacred ground  and hauling them off to jail     Today when the world comes together to seek human rights for all people  we must remember the people of Cuba  We must ask the international community to band together and with one clear voice say to the totalitarian regime in Cuba enough is enough  Let your people go  let your people be free  It is time for the people of Cuba to also enjoy the fruits of human rights                                                                   December 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
