                               MARTINEZ  JUDGE HOWARD WILL SERVE WITH DISTINCTION AS U S  DISTRICT JUDGE FOR FLORIDA   February 15  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today voted to confirm the nomination of Magistrate Judge Marcia Morales Howard to serve on the United States District Court for the Middle District of Florida  The Court s jurisdiction includes the Ft  Myers  Tampa  St  Petersburg  Orlando  and Jacksonville areas  The Senate voted to confirm the nomination by a vote of 94 to 0     In a floor statement commending the nomination  Martinez said     I m delighted to join my colleague  Senator Nelson  in advancing the nomination of Marcia Morales Howard as a federal district court judge for the middle district of Florida     This outstanding woman  a lawyer from Florida  has not only a distinguished academic background  graduated from the University of Florida College of Law and was an editor of the law review  but she also has been an accomplished litigator for 13 years with two very fine law firms in Florida  Foley  Lardner and McGuire Woods  In 2003  she became a federal magistrate     I know what an outstanding American she is  And I m very proud that we have someone of this dedication and this quality to serve in this important post     I m delighted not only for the opportunity to advance this nomination  but also to tout the judicial nominating commission that Senator Nelson and I have formed in the state of Florida  I believe it is a model of bipartisan cooperation  seeking the best and most qualified on the merits by positioning the nomination before a group of distinguished individuals who then see the applicants  study their qualifications and make recommendations
