                               SENATOR MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES  2 MILLION FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF MIAMI     COOPERATIVE INSTITUTE FOR MARINE   ATMOSPHERIC STUDIES  July 20  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced  2 041 299 in grants awarded to the University of Miami  UM  Cooperative Institute for Marine  Atmospheric Studies  Two of these grants will support research activities through 2008  A third grant will fund the Institute s use of high resolution models to study the Gulf of Mexico     These dollars will advance a better understanding of the Earth s oceans and atmosphere  said Martinez  These grants are a welcome resource that will help to educate students looking for careers in marine and atmospheric science     The funding will allow UM s Cooperative Institute for Marine  Atmospheric Studies to combine resources with the National Oceanic  Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  to develop a center of excellence in research for better understanding the Earth s oceans and atmosphere  This funding will also enable simulation experiments of high resolution oceanographic models in the northern Gulf of Mexico    Grant funding    2006 2008 Workplan for Task I Research Activities  Awarded to  University of Miami Cooperative Institute for Marine and Atmospheric Studies  Amount   843 678    2006 2008 Workplan for Task II Research Activities   Awarded to  University of Miami Cooperative Institute for Marine and Atmospheric Studies  Amount   1 030 871    Enabling and Initiating Observing System Simulations Experiments of a Coastal High Resolution Oceanographic Model in the Northern Gulf of Mexico   Awarded to  University of Miami Cooperative Institute for Marine and Atmospheric Studies  Amount   166 750
