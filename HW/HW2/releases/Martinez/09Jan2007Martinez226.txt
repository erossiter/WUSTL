                               MARTINEZ CONGRATULATES UNIVERSITY OF FLORIDA FOOTBALL TEAM ON 2006 NCAA CHAMPIONSHIP   January 9  2007   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today joined his Senate colleague and fellow Florida Senator Bill Nelson  D FL  in issuing a resolution honoring the University of Florida Gators for becoming 2006 NCAA College Football National Champions  The Gators defeated the Ohio State Buckeyes 41 14 in the NCAA Championship Game played in Glendale  Arizona last night  January 8  2007     Senator Martinez said of the Gators victory     This impressive win reflects well on the University of Florida  and the entire state of Florida should be proud  This team  led by head coach Urban Meyer  played hard  smart football  They brought down the undefeated favorite with a creative offensive structure and dominating defense  Their outstanding accomplishment is something that even this Florida State Seminole can appreciate     University of Florida President  Dr  J Bernard Machen  has much to celebrate  as does the Universitys athletic director  Jeremy Foley  Floridas 2006 SEC and NCAA championships are quite a feat  and will be remembered for a long time to come     Coach Meyer led a talented group of student athletes to the pinnacle of their sportwhat a tremendous accomplishment  Everyone in Gainesville and throughout the state should know how impressed we are here in the nations capitol  Congratulations  Gators  Your hard work paid off  We commend you for a job well done     It is worth noting that  in April of 2006  Senators Martinez and Nelson had the opportunity to honor the Florida mens basketball team for their first national championship  with this  the Gators second national championship in football  the University of Florida has now become the first university to simultaneously possess both the men s basketball and football national championships
