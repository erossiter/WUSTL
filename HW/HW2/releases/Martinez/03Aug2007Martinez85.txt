                               MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES  260 MILLION IN FLORIDA PROJECTS SECURED IN FY    08 BILLS  August 3  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that  260 million in Florida projects have been approved by the Senate Appropriations Committee  The projects are included in various Fiscal Year  FY  2008 appropriations bills that now move to the full Senate for consideration starting in September     This is just one step in the process  but its a positive step  My hope is that well finalize these bills by October so that when the federal governments new fiscal year begins  so too can these projects that are so critical to Floridas future  Martinez said  Im especially pleased with the funds secured for creating a regional fugitive task force to address the alarming rise in Floridas regional crime rates  Im also pleased with the transportation and port enhancement funds  the investment in Everglades restoration  and the research dollars going to Floridas colleges and universities  Florida has a diversity of needs and these projects will help to meet those needs     If approved by the Senate  the FY 08 appropriations bills will have to be reconciled with their House counterparts  Final versions must then be approved by both the House and Senate before being sent to the White House for the Presidents signature     Projects Secured for
