                               MARTINEZ   586 800 AWARDED TO FLORIDA COMMUNITY FINANCIAL INSTITUTION  September 14  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the U S  Department of the Treasury has awarded  586 800 to the Florida Community Loan Fund in Orlando  Florida  Through the Departments Community Development Financial Institution  CDFI  Fund  groups receive resources to serve low income citizens and communities lacking adequate access to affordable financial products and services     These resources will help an underserved population find the means for acquiring credit  capital and financial services  Martinez said  We need to continue to reward organizations that work to give more citizens financial opportunities for investment within their communities     The Florida Community Loan Fund is a certified CDFI  It provides financing and technical assistance to nonprofit organizations working in low income communities throughout the state  The award is intended to help the Loan Fund recapitalize its resource pool and provide loans to build or rehabilitate affordable housing and community facilities     The CDFI Fund was created to promote economic revitalization and community development through investment in and assistance to community development financial institutions
