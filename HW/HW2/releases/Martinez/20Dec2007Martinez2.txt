                               MARTINEZ SETTLES WAGER WITH MENENDEZ ON USF   RUTGERS GAME  December 20  2007   WASHINGTON   This week  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  delivered flats of fresh picked strawberries from Plant City  Florida to the office of Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   The strawberries settle a friendly wager made between the senators on the outcome of the October football contest between the University of South Florida Bulls and the Rutgers Scarlet Knights     As promised  these strawberries are intended to help remind Senator Menendez that Florida is the sweetest place to live  visit  and play sports  Martinez said  Picked yesterday in Plant City  Florida  this is one of this seasons first batches of strawberries and I hope my good friend enjoys what Florida has to offer     Getting to enjoy those Florida strawberries made our victory last October even sweeter  Senator Menendez said  It was another good season for Rutgers football and it s obviously a good season for Florida fruit too  With a friendly wager like this  everybody wins     I thank the Florida Strawberry Growers Association for their assistance in delivering the strawberries from Plant City  Florida to the nations capital  said Martinez  The FSGA does an outstanding job of representing producers who generate a wealth of economic activity for our state     On October 18  the Rutgers Scarlet Knights defeated the University of South Florida Bulls  30 to 27  Had USF beat Rutgers  Sen  Menendez would have settled with New Jersey saltwater taffy and cranberries
