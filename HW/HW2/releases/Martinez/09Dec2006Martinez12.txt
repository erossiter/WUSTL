                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  FLORIDA SALES TAX DEDUCTION EXTENDED TWO YEARS  December 9  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today voted in favor of extending the ability for Floridians to deduct state and local sales tax from federal tax returns  The extension  contained in H R  6111  passed the Senate by a vote of 79 to 9  The measure now moves to the White House for President Bushs signature    This is a matter of fundamental fairness  said Martinez  Floridians shouldnt be penalized because our state chooses to use a sales tax for general revenue as opposed to a state income tax  Floridians ought to have the ability to deduct sales tax and passage of this bill ensures that    The deduction is worth an estimated  1 5 billion to Floridians over a two year period  This extension applies to taxable years beginning after December 31  2005  and expires December 31  2007                                                                  December 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
