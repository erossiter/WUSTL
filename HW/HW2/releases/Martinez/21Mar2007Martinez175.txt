                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ PRAISES  2 5 MILLION FEMA GRANT FOR WUESTHOFF HEALTH SYSTEM  March 21  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today praised the U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  for awarding more than  2 5 million to the not for profit Wuesthoff Health System in Brevard County  The grant will help fund the installation of hurricane shutters at three healthcare facilities that would protect the buildings during a storm     Upgrades provided by the grant will give the Wuesthoff Health System the ability to protect patients and medical staff during a storm  Martinez said  In addition  taking precautions and preventive measures in preparation for a storm will decrease potential damage  injury  and loss of life     Funds for the project were provided under FEMAs Hazard Mitigation Grant Program  HMGP   The grant will pay for 75 percent of the cost to install more than 100 shutters at Wuesthoff Medical Center in Rockledge  The Town Square assisted living facility in Viera and the Wuesthoff Progressive Care Center in Viera  The roof at Wuesthoff Medical Center will also get a major upgrade to make it more wind resistant                                                                   March 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                Current record
