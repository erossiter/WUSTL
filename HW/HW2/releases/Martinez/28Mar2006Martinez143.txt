                               MARTINEZ APPLAUDS JUDICIARY COMMITTEE FOR WORK ON COMPREHENSIVE IMMIGRATION REFORM MEASURE   March 28  2006   Washington  DC  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today welcomed the action of the Senate Judiciary Committee in approving an immigration reform measure and sending it to the Senate floor for consideration   Senator Martinez made the following statement   I am very pleased with the comprehensive package of immigration reforms passed today by the Senate Judiciary Committee  I commend Chairman Specter and the members of the committee for their hard work in crafting a bill that addresses the important issues of border security  a feasible guest worker program and a realistic way to recognize the 12 million illegal immigrants already living in the U S  without offering amnesty     As we go forward with debate on the floor of the U S  Senate  specifics of the bill will change  but this is a great place to start  We are a nation of laws and also a nation of immigrants  We must recognize the contributions of our immigrant community without rewarding illegal behavior  I look forward to being an active part of this debate on the Senate floor in the coming days to ensure that the Senate passes a comprehensive package that strictly enforces our border while implementing a viable guest worker program that our economy demands
