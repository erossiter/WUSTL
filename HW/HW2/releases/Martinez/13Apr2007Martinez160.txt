                               SENATE PRAISES GATORS FOR WINNING 2007 NCAA BASKETBALL CHAMPIONSHIP   April 13  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  this week along with Senator Bill Nelson  D FL  introduced a Senate resolution commending the University of Florida mens basketball team for winning the 2007 National Collegiate Athletic Association  NCAA  Division I Basketball Championship on April 2  2007  The resolution was approved by unanimous consent Thursday night     Martinez said of the passage of the resolution and the Gators achievement     Its fitting Congress recognize the Gators second consecutive championship as a great achievement  Through hard work  great skill  and commitment to the sport  the Gators were able to defend their national championship title  I congratulate Head Coach Billy Donovan for leading this team to victory and accomplishing his own feat  as one of only four active coaches to win multiple NCAA titles     I commend University of Florida President  Dr  J  Bernard Machen and the Universitys athletic director  Jeremy Foley  The Gators athletic program is one to be admired with both NCAA football and basketball championships this year  Keep up the good work  Go Gators     University of Florida mens basketball team made history with their 84 75 win over the Ohio State University Buckeyes  becoming only the seventh school to repeat as national champions in mens basketball                                                                   April 2007 News Releases             Browse by
