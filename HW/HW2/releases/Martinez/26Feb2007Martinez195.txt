                               MARTINEZ  YMCA WORKFORCE EMPOWERMENT PROGRAM PUTS FOSTER YOUTH ON THE RIGHT PATH  February 26  2007   TAMPA  FL    U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today spoke with foster youth participating in the Tampa Metropolitan Area YMCAs Workforce Empowerment  WE  program  This program trains and educates young people  ages 16 22  who are aging out of the foster care systems to find employment in the banking and finance industry     Education and job training are critical components for a bright future  Through this program  young people are able to put themselves on a path towards career success  Martinez said  The Tampa Metropolitan Area YMCA is creating a national model of excellence by giving these teenagers the life skills they need and doing it by partnering with an industry that is fast growing and vital to the regions economy     The WE program provides assistance with and access to the following services  education and employment placement  individual counseling  affordable housing  transportation  childcare  health care  mental health services  life skills training  financial support  and mentorship  In the next year  the program will assist 100 youth by addressing employment needs and job readiness through training in one of the highest growth industries in the Tampa area  banking and finance  The program was made possible by a grant from the U S  Department of Labor                                                                   February 2007 News Releases             Browse by
