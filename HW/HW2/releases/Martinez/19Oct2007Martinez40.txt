                               MARTINEZ  SENATOR MENENDEZ TO TASTE SWEET FLORIDA STRAWBERRIES Martinez and Menendez made friendly wager on USF   RUTGERS game  October 19  2007   WASHINGTON   Last night  as the Rutgers Scarlet Knights defeated the University of South Florida Bulls  30 to 27  U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  lost a wager on the game made with fellow Senator Robert Menendez  D NJ   The terms of the agreement call for Martinez to provide Menendez with one of the first batches of fresh Tampa strawberries  Sen  Menendez had put New Jersey saltwater taffy and cranberries on the line     It was a tough and well played game  The Bulls put on a great game and fortunately  this is just a minor set back in whats been a great season so far  Senator Martinez said  To help remind Senator Menendez that Florida is the sweetest place to live  visit  and play sports  Im giving him one of this seasons first batches of strawberries from our great state     It was definitely a scarlet night  said Senator Menendez  Coach Schiano and the boys once again have our spirits sky high after another big win  the whole state is proud of our team  I do congratulate South Florida on what has been and Im sure will continue to be a terrific season     The first batch of fresh Tampa strawberries will likely be ready for delivery to Sen  Menendez next month
