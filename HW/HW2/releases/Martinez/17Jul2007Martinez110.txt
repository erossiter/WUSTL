                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ COMMENDS JIM NICHOLSON FOR SERVICE AS SECRETARY OF VETERANS AFFAIRS  July 17  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today commended Jim Nicholson for his service as Secretary of the Veterans Affairs  VA  department  Nicholson announced he will step down from his post no later than October 1st     I thank Secretary Nicholson for the great service he has provided the veterans of our nation  said Martinez  He has been a great help all across our country  and I particularly thank him for helping secure veterans hospitals and clinics throughout Florida  We owe our veterans a debt of gratitude and Secretary Nicholson helped us meet that obligation     In March of this year  Secretary Nicholson informed Senator Martinez that Lake Nona will be the site of the new Orlando Veterans Affairs Hospital  In May  the VA announced plans for two new community based outpatient clinics in Jacksonville and Putnam counties     Nicholson has served as VA Secretary since 2005                                                                   July 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
