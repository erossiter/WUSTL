                               MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES  72 MILLION FOR PANAMA CITY BAY COUNTY INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT REPLACEMENT PROJECT  May 25  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has approved a  72 million grant allocation for the Panama City Bay County International Airport Replacement Project     This is great news  Im pleased the FAA recognizes the quality and necessity of the replacement project  said Martinez  Federal partnering is critical to moving the project forward  Later this year  the first installment of funds will become available     The  72 million comes through the FAAs Airport Improvement Program  The FAA projects a discretionary grant payment stream of  25 million this fiscal year with an additional  27 million in FY 2008   15 million in FY 2009  and  5 million in FY 2010  In addition  the airport will be able to use its annual entitlement funds for this project as they become available     The Airport Improvement funds are a clear commitment by the FAA to work with the airport district to help see this project through to its successful completion  said Martinez  I commend our local officials and FAA Administrator Blakey for helping make this relocation a reality                                                                   May 2007 News Releases             Browse
