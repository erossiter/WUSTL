                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ APPLAUDS NOMINATION OF EMILIO GONZALEZ TO BE DIRECTOR OF BUREAU OF CITIZENSHIP AND IMMIGRATION SERVICES  September 1  2005    Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded President Bushs nomination of Emilio Gonzalez to be Director of the Bureau of Citizenship and Immigration Services at the Department of Homeland Security and pledged to work actively for Gonzales confirmation in the U S  Senate         Martinez  said        The President has chosen a top notch individual to lead our nations citizenship and immigration services  Emilio Gonzalez brings a great depth of international and domestic policy experience to his new position  As the Director for Western Hemisphere Affairs for President George W  Bush  he served as a key national security advisor to both President Bush and Dr  Condoleezza Rice  He already has the trust of this President and will do well in his new position        I am proud that President Bush has once again looked to  Florida  to find the most qualified candidates for key positions in his Administration  I urge my colleagues in the Senate to swiftly confirm Emilio Gonzalez          30                                                                   September 2005 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
