                               MARTINEZ  GULF PROTECTIONS  FLORIDA SALES TAX DEDUCTION SIGNED INTO LAW   December 20  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded President Bush for signing H R  6111  the Tax Relief and Health Care Act of 2006 into law  Senator Martinez voted in favor of the wide ranging legislative package that includes historic protections against oil and natural gas drilling in the Gulf of Mexico  a two year extension of the state and local sales tax deduction from federal income taxes  and several health care reimbursement measures  The law also includes several free trade measures important to Floridas economy     This law secures historic protections for Floridas waters while promoting responsible domestic energy production to help meet the demands of our nation  said Martinez  one of the architects of the language keeping offshore energy exploration at least 125 miles from the Gulf coast of Florida  This law extends tax fairness to the people of Florida and provides great opportunities for economic growth through free trade  I am grateful for this day and look forward to future opportunities to strengthen all of these areas in the coming years     The Tax Relief and Health Care Act of 2006 also includes tax related tuition deductions  research and development tax credits for business innovation  clean coal incentives  free trade agreements for Vietnam  Colombia  Peru  Ecuador  and Bolivia  The measure also reverses the scheduled cut in Medicare physician reimbursement rates for 2007 and it increases the Medicare dialysis reimbursement rate by 1 6 percent                                                                   December 2006 News Releases             Browse by
