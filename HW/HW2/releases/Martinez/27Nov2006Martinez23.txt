                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ APPLAUDS CECIL FIELD C 27J ANNOUNCEMENT   November 27  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded the news that the C 27J Joint Cargo Aircraft  JCA  Team has selected Jacksonvilles Cecil Field as the site for the production and final assembly of the C 27J for the U S  Army and U S  Air Force Joint Cargo Aircraft program  should they be awarded the military contract  The C 27J JCA team includes Alenia North America  Inc   Boeing and L 3 Communications   This is one more example of how Florida can meet the needs of defense contractors who are seeking a friendly state in which to conduct business  said Martinez  Cecil Field offers the right combination of space  facilities and well maintained infrastructure to meet the final assembly needs of this important joint program that will benefit the men and women of our military   This new aircraft will be used for cargo and troop transport  material and paratroop air drop  tactical operations  medical evacuation  fire fighting and aerial spraying  The JCA program is expected to replace the Armys C 23 Sherpas  C 12 and C 26 aircraft and enhance the Air Forces current fleet                                                                   November 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                            Current record
