                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ  SENATE APPROVES  50 MILLION FOR NEW VA CEMETERIES IN FLORIDA   September 7  2007   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  this week announced that the Senate has approved  50 million for the development of two national military cemeteries in Jacksonville and Sarasota  FL  The funds are contained in the Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriation Act of Fiscal Year 2008  The bill passed the Senate by a vote of 92 to 1     With this funding  we take another step toward honoring the service of the military men and women who risked their lives protecting our nation  Martinez said  This also helps ensure that we can honor the wishes of veterans  and more families will be able to be closer to their loved ones that have passed  The appropriations bill contains  22 4 million for phase I development of the Jacksonville Area National Cemetery  The bill also contains  27 8 million for phase I development of the Sarasota Area National Cemetery     The bill now goes to conference to be reconciled with the House version of the bill                                                                    September 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
