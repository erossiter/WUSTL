                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES LOCATION OF NEW USCIS REGIONAL OFFICE  August 17  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced that the U S General Services Administration  GSA  has awarded the lease for the new U S  Citizenship and Immigration Services  USCIS  Southeast Regional Office in Orlando  The Bank of America Center located at 390 North Orange Avenue in Orlando  Florida was the successful offeror     I am pleased the USCIS is one step closer to opening this facility  one that will provide better and more efficient services to Floridians and residents of other Southern states  Martinez said  Located downtown  the office will be in an ideal location and I look forward to its services being available to Floridas residents     USCIS Director Emilio T  Gonzalez notified Senator Martinez of the decision this afternoon  The facility is estimated to be ready to occupy in spring of 2008  The new Regional Office will provide office space for 94 employees in approximately 30 000 square feet of space  This facility is directly across from the Orange County Courthouse and within walking distance to area hotels     The new USCIS Regional Office will be responsible for managing all district and field offices in the southeastern region of the United States                                                                    August 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                                                    Current record
