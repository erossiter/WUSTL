                               MARTINEZ ANNOUNCES  1 MILLION FOR FLORIDA INSTITUTIONS  September 6  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today announced  1 023 311 in grants awarded to Florida AM University  FAMU  in Tallahassee  Florida International University in Miami  Indian River Community College in Fort Pierce  and the Museum of Science  Inc  in Miami  This funding is made available through the U S  Department of Educations Upward Bound Math and Science program for educational programs that lead to careers in the fields of math and science    These investments will help encourage and train our young people for careers in math and science  said Martinez  The future of our nation depends on these kinds of critical investments that ensure we remain competitive in the global economy     The Upward Bound Math and Science programs provide summer intensive math and science training  year round counseling and advisement  contact with mathematics and science research faculty members  computer training  and mentor guided   participant conducted scientific research     The following institutions will receive grants    Florida AM University   Tallahassee  FL    273 348   Florida International University   Miami  FL    249 963   Indian River Community College   Fort Pierce  FL    250 000   Museum of Science  Inc    Miami  FL    250 000
