                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SEN  MARTINEZ STATEMENT ON SIGNING OF MILITARY COMMISSIONS ACT   October 17  2006   Washington  DC   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today applauded President Bushs signing of S  3930  the Military Commissions Act of 2006  The measure  now law  outlines how to treat and interrogate terrorists in a fair and just manner without compromising intelligence sources and methods   Senator Martinez said   We must remember the detainees this law affects are terrorists engaged in an ongoing war against the United States  These terrorists will now be tried in military commissions that afford a fair and reliable prosecution consistent with our national security interests   This law protects the integrity of U S  intelligence and the methods used for gathering it  It ensures the safety of soldiers in the field  and our citizens at home  This sets a proper balance between two important American values  security and liberty                                                                    October 2006 News Releases             Browse by                                                                          Current record
