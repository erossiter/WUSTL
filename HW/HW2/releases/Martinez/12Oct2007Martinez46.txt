                               MARTINEZ CONGRATULATES U S  NAVY ON THEIR 232nd BIRTHDAY  October 12  2007   Orlando  FL   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL   the ranking member of the Senate Armed Services Committees Subcommittee on Seapower  today congratulated the U S  Navy on their 232 years of service     Senator Martinez said     On October 13th  the U S  Navy celebrates its 232nd birthday  In 1775  the Continental Congress voted to send two sailing vessels to intercept munitions and supplies headed for the British military forces in America  From that moment  the Navy was born to proudly protect the people of this nation and its surrounding seas     As the military branch dedicated to serving our country s interests on the sea  the U S  Navy has given us more than two centuries of strength and commitment on behalf of all Americans  Members of our armed services valiantly strive to protect our way of life and defend freedom  This anniversary should serve as a reminder of all those patriots who have served our country  past and present  and the importance of the Navy in securing our peace and security     As the Navys birthday is celebrated across the country and the world  we should once again pause to remember the dedication and bravery of each and every sailor  From the American Revolution to the present war on terrorism  their service is
