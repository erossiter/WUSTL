                                                                                                                                                                 Filter by                                                              SENATOR MARTINEZ COMMENTS ON THE RESIGNATION OF U S  ATTORNEY GENERAL  August 27  2007   Washington   U S  Senator Mel Martinez  R FL  today issued the following statement in regards to the resignation announcement by U S  Attorney General Alberto Gonzales     Alberto Gonzales is an honorable man whose life history speaks volumes about whats best about America  General Gonzales has a long history of public service  first in the U S  Air Force  later as a Justice of the Supreme Court of Texas  then as counsel to the President and ultimately serving as Attorney General of the United States     Alberto Gonzales has conducted himself always with honesty  dedication and integrity  He is my friend and he is a good man  He and his family have my thanks for their sacrifice for serving during difficult times  and my best wishes for the future                                                                   August 2007 News Releases             Browse by                                                                                        Current record
