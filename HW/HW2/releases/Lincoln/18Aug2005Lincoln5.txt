Katie Laning  202 224 4843 WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  along with U S  Representative John Boozman announced today that fire departments in the Third District of Arkansas have been awarded  1 197 848 from the Department of Homeland Security Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program       The program supports fire departments by providing the tools and resources necessary to ensure the safety of department personnel and the general public   These grants will be used for Operations and Safety  as well as Vehicle Acquisition       The following Third District
