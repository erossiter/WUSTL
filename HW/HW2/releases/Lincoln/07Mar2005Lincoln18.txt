                    Lincoln Statement on Vote to Raise the Minimum Wage                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Vote to Raise the Minimum Wage                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  March 07  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843  Increasing the minimum wage is long overdue in this country   Senator Blanche Lincoln said    Workers  wages have remained stagnant for seven years while they   ve seen nothing but increases in their costs of living  including utilities  gasoline  housing  groceries  higher education  child care and health care   I believe that any American who works 40 hours a week should not have to raise a family in poverty   However  Congress must also be mindful of the effects a minimum wage increase will have on our small businesses and agricultural producers   Any future attempts at increasing the minimum wage should be done in a manner that includes targeted tax cuts so our small businesses and producers can offset the increased labor costs       30
