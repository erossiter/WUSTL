                    Members of Arkansas Congressional Delegation Commend Magnolia on Progress  EDA Recognition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Both                                                    Members of Arkansas Congressional Delegation Commend Magnolia on Progress  EDA Recognition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 08  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with Representative Mike Ross  AR 04  Wednesday commended the City of Magnolia for being one of seven cities nationwide selected to receive a  50 000 performance award through the U S  Department of Commerce s Economic Development Administration  EDA   The award is based on the city having exceeded goals for the completion of construction for the city s workforce development center such as working to meet both job and private investment expectations  I commend Magnolia s local leaders and workers whose efforts to strengthen their community have earned the city this important recognition and investment  Lincoln said  The workforce development center is an exciting endeavor to prepare job seekers for new opportunities in the manufacturing industry  I commend the City of Magnolia for moving swiftly on this project and am pleased this award funding will help propel its efforts forward  Pryor said  I m proud of the hard work and dedication Magnolia officials have put into this important economic development project  Ross said  By completing the workforce development center early  the city has shown a deep commitment to its residents and this additional funding award is a well deserved honor  Fiscal Year 2007 will be the first year EDA is using performance awards which can be used to fund EDA approved economic development activities  Performance Incentives for Public Works and Economic Adjustment Investments are distributed to entities that demonstrate outstanding performance  Performance indicators of outstanding performance would include private investment  job creation  timely project completion  organizational leadership  innovation  regional collaboration  and economic strategy to compete in the global marketplace
