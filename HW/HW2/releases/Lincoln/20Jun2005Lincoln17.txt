                                                                                                                                                              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 20  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 WASHINGTON D C      Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and Congressman Mike Ross will meet with BRAC Commission Chairman Anthony Principi tomorrow to discuss the Red River Army Depot and the critical role the facility plays in our overall national defense strategy  They will be joined by Congressman Ralph Hall  R TX       Tuesday   s meeting is part of the Arkansas delegation   s continuing effort to demonstrate the depot   s military value  The meeting will mark the second time in a month Lincoln  Pryor and Ross have sat down with Principi to discuss Red River      In addition  representatives from their respective offices will be in Texarkana for tomorrow   s BRAC Commission site inspection     Lincoln  Pryor and Ross have met multiple times with south Arkansas community leaders to discuss the strengths and strategies that may be used to avert the BRAC   s closure recommendation  They will also submit testimony to the BRAC Commission in the coming weeks
