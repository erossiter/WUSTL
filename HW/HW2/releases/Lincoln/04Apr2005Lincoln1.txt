                                                                                                                                                      FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 04  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  along with Congressman Marion Berry  D AR  1st   announced grants to Arkansas  Baxter and Searcy County airports for various improvements     The grants come from the Federal Aviation Administration and cover a variety of improvements     Arkansas County     DeWitt Municipal Airport will receive  133 000 to improve runway lighting  install visual navigation aids to assist landings  and replace the beacon used to guide aircraft     Baxter County     Baxter County Regional Airport will receive  1 178 742 to create an airport road out of one of the runway approaches  improve runway lighting  acquire lands in the runway approach area  install wind cones  relocate the T Hangars  make grading improvements in the runway safety area and remove obstructions from the runway areas     Searcy County     Searcy County Airport will receive  200 900 in install perimeter fencing to reduce wildlife access to the airfield
