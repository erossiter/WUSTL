Lincoln Celebrates Hispanic Heritage Month  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 18  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   issued the following statement in celebration of Hispanic Heritage Month  which lasts from September 15     October 15    During Hispanic Heritage Month  we honor the Hispanic community and pay tribute to the extraordinary contributions that Hispanics make to the United States year round       There are more than 43 million Hispanic Americans in our country  and they make up the fastest growing ethnic group  Their hard work  strong values and closely knit families have made the United States a better and stronger country  Hispanics are the most decorated group in the history of our military  Additionally  their strong influence on popular culture is evident in music  film  food  and other areas  Latinos are an integral part of our nation   s economic  cultural and political life      Unfortunately  despite their vast contributions  Latino families face enormous challenges  It is unacceptable that one out of every three Hispanics does not have health insurance  Hispanics also face the highest school dropout rate in the nation      I will continue to address the issues that are important to Hispanic families in Arkansas  including improving education to open the doors of opportunity  providing access to affordable health care  and giving Hispanic families the tools they need to succeed  In addition  because the Republican Party cannot agree on how to proceed on immigration reform  yet another year will pass without addressing this critical issue  Americans need a comprehensive  two pronged plan to secure our borders and place undocumented workers on a pathway to earned citizenship      While we take the time this month to honor the achievements of Hispanics  we also know that celebrating the Hispanic community for one month a year is not enough  I remain committed     now and in the future     to ensuring that all Latinos have the opportunity to achieve the American Dream      La Senadora Lincoln Celebra Mes de la Herencia Hispana Washington
