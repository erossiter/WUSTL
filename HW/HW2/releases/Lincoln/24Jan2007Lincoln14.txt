                    Lincoln Announces Energy and Natural Resources Subcommittee Assignments                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln Announces Energy and Natural Resources Subcommittee Assignments  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  January 24  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today announced that she will serve on three subcommittees as a member of the Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources  With these assignments  Lincoln will help oversee a number of issues important to Arkansans including wildlife preservation  national parks  and electric power      As a member of the Energy and Natural Resources committee  I have the opportunity to address the energy crisis that is facing this nation while protecting our country   s priceless historic landmarks and precious natural resources   said Lincoln   I look forward to working with my colleagues to help our country move towards energy independence and to ensure that our nation   s history and natural resources are preserved for future generations        Lincoln will sit on the Subcommittee on National Parks  Historic Preservation and Recreation  which includes oversight and legislative responsibility for our nation   s monuments and historic sights  She will also serve as a member of the Subcommittee on Forest and Public Land Management which oversees the management of wilderness areas  wildlife refuges and national mining policy  Finally  Lincoln will have the opportunity to work on issues affecting our nation   s water supply and our use of electric power as a member of the Subcommittee on Water and Power
