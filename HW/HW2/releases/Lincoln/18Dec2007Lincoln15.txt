Katie Laning  202 224 4843 WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Vic Snyder  AR 02  announced today that Winrock International will receive grant funding through the U S  Department of Agricultures Rural Development Program    The Winrock International organization in Little Rock will receive a  234 643 rural community development initiative grant for their Nonprofit Improvement Program  These federal dollars will be used to assist nonprofit organizations in undertaking more complex  long term projects to increase their capacity  This program hosts workshops to assess needs and tailor solutions for improving the organizations management and enhancing the organizations operations    Community development in rural Arkansas helps strengthen our state s economy and enhance the quality of life for our families  said Lincoln  I commend Winrock International for seeking these federal funds to continue their successful work assisting rural Arkansas communities   Arkansas nonprofits provide diverse and invaluable services to our community  said Pryor  Winrocks expertise will ensure that these vital organizations realize their full potential and touch as many lives as possible   The Nonprofit Improvement program is a great program to strengthen nonprofit organizations in Arkansas  said Congressman Snyder  This funding will help nonprofits evaluate and build logistical support for their initiative
