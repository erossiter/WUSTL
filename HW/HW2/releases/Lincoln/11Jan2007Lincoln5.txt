                    Lincoln Reaction to Bush Speech                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln Reaction to Bush Speech  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  January 11  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   released the following statement following President Bush   s speech regarding U S  policy in Iraq     I am glad the President has recognized the need for a new strategy in Iraq  and I truly hope for the sake of our soldiers and the Iraqi people that the President   s next steps result in success   However  against the advice of our military leaders on the ground in Iraq and the concerns of his own Joint Chiefs  the President has chosen an extremely high risk strategy that escalates U S  involvement in what has become a violent civil war          We know that real progress ultimately lies with Iraqis taking responsibility for their own future   In the coming weeks of debate  I want to ensure that as the President asks additional brave Americans to sacrifice  he also presents Congress and the American people with a clear plan that measures progress and indicates how long our troops will remain in Iraq
