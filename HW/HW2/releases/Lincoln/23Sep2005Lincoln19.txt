                    Senators Lincoln  Pryor Join Rep  Boozman in Announcing  213 978 in Grants for Third District Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Both                                                    Senators Lincoln  Pryor Join Rep  Boozman in Announcing  213 978 in Grants for Third District Fire Departments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 23  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln  D AR  and Mark Pryor  D AR   along with U S  Representative John Boozman  R AR   announced today that two Third District fire departments have been awarded a total of  213 978 in grants from the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program     The Van Buren Fire Department received  137 608 and the Elkins Fire Department received  76 370   Both grants are to be used operations and safety      The hardworking Van Buren and Elkins fire departments are depended on by countless families to keep their communities safe   Sen  Lincoln said   Therefore we should do all we can at the federal level to supplement their needs and see to it that they have the necessary equipment and resources they deserve    Our firefighters should never lack the necessary resources to save lives and protect their communities   Sen  Pryor said    These grants will help ensure that the firefighters in Van Buren and Elkins are well equipped to perform this vital task       These large grants will help the Van Buren and Elkins fire departments provide essential services for their communities   Smaller communities like Van Buren and Elkins truly benefit from this grant program because it allows them to provide training  obtain necessary equipment or modify their facilities   In many cases  they would not be able to make these upgrades without these funds   said Rep  Boozman           The Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program provides awards one year grants directly to fire departments to support the nation s firefighters and the services they deliver   These grants are used for training  wellness and fitness  firefighting equipment  personal protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities
