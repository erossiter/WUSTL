                    Lincoln Remembers Helen Walton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Both                                                    Lincoln Remembers Helen Walton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 20  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843            Helen Walton was an extraordinary woman who dedicated her life to serving others   said U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln   She touched the lives of many through her philanthropic activities and charitable organizations  Helen s devotion to education and learning was evident through her work with many Arkansas educational institutions and the instrumental role she played in the creation of The National Museum for Women in the Arts in Washington  D C  The leadership Helen exhibited in the business community and the contributions that she made to Arkansas and to the world will preserve her legacy for generations to come  Helen will be greatly missed  and my thoughts and prayers are with the Walton family at this difficult time
