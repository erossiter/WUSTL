                    Lincoln Statement on Minimum Wage Passage                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Minimum Wage Passage  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  February 01  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   released the following statement regarding the Senate   s passage of the Minimum Wage Bill    Increasing the federal minimum wage is long overdue in this country and I am proud that the Senate has finally passed legislation that gives American workers the increase they deserve   said Lincoln    Wages have remained stagnant for too many years while workers have seen nothing but increases in their cost of living  including utilities  gasoline  housing  groceries  higher education  child care and health care  I believe that any American who works 40 hours a week should not have to raise a family in poverty         In addition  just as our workers face obstacles as a result of stagnant wages  our small businesses have their fair share of challenges whether it be rising health care costs or staying competitive in the ever changing global market place  I applaud the Senate for passing a bill that gives American workers a much needed minimum wage increase while protecting the small businesses that our vital to the American way of life
