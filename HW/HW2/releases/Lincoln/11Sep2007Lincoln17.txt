                    Lincoln Statement on September 11 Anniversary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on September 11 Anniversary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 11  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today issued the following statement with regard to the sixth anniversary of the September 11  2001  terrorist attacks  Today  the United States Senate paused for a moment of silence to honor the victims of the September 11 terrorist attacks  We continue to remember and pray for the families and loved ones of the innocent men and women who lost their lives in New York  Virginia  and Pennsylvania  The tragedy of these killings is still felt today  six years later  and serves as a reminder that terrorism is real and can happen on American soil  With the memories of September 11  2001  I am grateful to the men and women fighting terror around the world on behalf of our freedoms here at home
