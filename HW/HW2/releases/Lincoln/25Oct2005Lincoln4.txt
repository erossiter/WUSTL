                    Lincoln Statement on Death of Civil Rights Legend Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Death of Civil Rights Legend Rosa Parks                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 25  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843    Today the nation has lost one of its pioneers in the fight for racial equality     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln said       In 1955 Rosa Parks decided that she would claim the benefits of full citizenship when she refused to give up her bus seat to a white passenger   Through her brave act  she helped galvanize a movement that would ultimately change America   Her life is an inspiration to anyone who values decency  equality and the promise of America   My heartfelt condolences go out to the Parks family and all of those who were inspired by her act of courage
