                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce  271 000 Grant for Heber Springs Municipal Airport                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce  271 000 Grant for Heber Springs Municipal Airport  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 20  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  AR 01  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration has awarded the Heber Springs Municipal Airport a  271 851 grant through the U S  Department of Transportation     The grant will provide funds to acquire land in the Runway 23 approach to the airport for the protection of people and property on the ground       Our local airports are a vital economic development tool for our communities   said Lincoln   I am pleased that Heber Springs Municipal Airport will receive these critical funds in order to make necessary improvements to its facilities       These funds will provide a critical barrier of protection between the municipal airport and Heber Springs residents and property   said Pryor   It is a fitting example of how the FAA and local government can work together for the benefit of the wider community       The Heber Springs Municipal Airport is an important economic resource for the community   said Berry   I m pleased the airport received this vital funding and I will continue my work to find more ways to support economic development in the region
