                    Lincoln Statement on Iraq Study Group Report                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Iraq Study Group Report  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 06  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   released the following statement regarding the bipartisan Iraq Study Group report      The Iraq Study Group   s report underscores the fact that our current policy in Iraq is not working and that it is time for a new direction  We know that a    silver bullet    does not exist and that progress requires many hard decisions to be made  The stability and security of Iraq and the entire region demands that we change course now  Our American troops in harm   s way     many of them Arkansans     and the Iraqi people deserve a new approach
