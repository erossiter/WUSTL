                    Lincoln Hails Senate Passage of Comprehensive Immigration Reform Bill                                                                                                Both                                                    Lincoln Hails Senate Passage of Comprehensive Immigration Reform Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  May 25  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today called the Senate   s passage of a comprehensive immigration reform bill an important step towards ultimately fixing the nation   s broken immigration system      Lincoln said she was proud that the Senate took the rare opportunity to conduct a thorough debate and reach a bipartisan  compromise on such a difficult issue       Every part of our immigration system is broken and this reform bill takes a necessary two pronged approach towards strengthening our borders and dealing with those illegal immigrants who are already here   Lincoln said   This bill mandates an earned path towards citizenship  not amnesty  and targets much needed resources to strengthen our porous borders       Lincoln said the Senate passed bill contains tough border enforcement provisions including  the hiring of 4 000 new border security agents  the construction of a virtual fence which would be comprised of unmanned aerial vehicles  cameras  sensors and other technologies to track movement across the border  the hiring of 1 000 new inspectors to investigate violations of immigration law  and making it a felony punishable by 20 years in prison for constructing a tunnel under the border     Lincoln said that the Senate bill puts in place a system to bring undocumented workers out of the shadows by establishing a path toward earned citizenship after they have paid fines and back taxes  passed background checks  learned English and waited 11 years instead of five
