                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 31  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today issued the following statement on Judge Samuel Alito  President Bush   s nominee to replace retiring Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O   Connor       I look forward to carefully reviewing the qualifications of Judge Samuel Alito in the weeks ahead as I evaluate his ability to serve on the U S  Supreme Court   Lincoln said   It is my hope that Judge Alito will be thoroughly and fully reviewed by the U S  Senate  and through this process I hope to gain a better understanding of his skills  experiences  intellect  and ability to understand and apply established judicial precedent       In addition  I look forward to closely monitoring Judge Alito   s hearings in the Senate Judiciary Committee  to sitting down and meeting with him personally and to heavily weighing the views of Arkansans to help me reach a final decision on his nomination  Above all  I believe that the people of this country deserve a Supreme Court Justice who will fulfill the fundamental responsibility to interpret and uphold the Constitution fairly and without bias  and this is the standard I will use throughout my consideration of Judge Alito   s nomination
