                                                                                               Both                                                    Lincoln to Receive American Farm Bureau s Golden Plow Award                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  January 11  2008Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  D C   U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   has been named by the American Farm Bureau Federation to receive its Golden Plow Award for 2007  The prestigious national honor is awarded to only one U S  Senator and one Member of the U S  House of Representatives each year for demonstrating superior commitment to American agriculture and Farm Bureau policy   The announcement was made at an event in Little Rock today by the President of the Arkansas Farm Bureau  Stanley Reed  Lincoln is only the second Arkansan to receive the award since its initiation in 1988  Former Senator David Pryor received it in 1989  Lincoln is also a five time recipient of the Friend of the Farm Bureau Award    I am honored to receive the American Farm Bureau s Golden Plow Award  said Lincoln  As part of a seventh generation Arkansas farm family  I know firsthand the challenges our producers face each and every day  I am committed to working with the Arkansas Farm Bureau and the American Farm Bureau Federation to ensure that our government stands behind our farmers so that they can continue to produce the safest  most abundant  and most affordable supply of food and fiber in the world    Lincoln  a Helena native  is a senior member of the Senate Committee on Agriculture  Nutrition  and Forestry  which has jurisdiction over the Farm Bill Sheserves as Chair of the Subcommittee on Production  Income Protection  and Price Support
