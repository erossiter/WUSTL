                    Lincoln Applauds Senate for Restoring Veterans Health Care Funding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Both                                                    Lincoln Applauds Senate for Restoring Veterans Health Care Funding                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 29  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln helped pass emergency legislation today that will allow the Veterans Administration  VA  to continue providing critical services at VA clinics across the county       Our veterans should be the last constituency on earth in need of an emergency spending measure to keep them afloat   Lincoln said   These men and women have sacrificed their lives for our country and it   s inexcusable that this administration overlooked something as important as funding the health care they were promised and have earned       After a disclosure by the Bush Administration yesterday that it had severely underestimated the level of funding needed by the VA this year  Lincoln co sponsored legislation to the Interior Appropriations Bill to cover the VA   s funding shortfall for 2005 by restoring  1 5 billion  The proposal passed today with resounding bipartisan support      Lincoln pointed to her efforts earlier this year as part of a group of Senate Democrats to give the VA an additional  2 billion in the  82 billion emergency spending bill for the war in Iraq  At that time  however  the VA claimed it had all the money it needed  Lincoln blamed the Bush Administration for poor budget planning and said that the President is obligated to ensure that veterans benefits receive the highest priority  particularly in war time       We made true on our obligation to our veterans today  but it shouldn   t have come to this   Lincoln said   Because of this blatant oversight and mismanagement  I will be more skeptical of this Administration   s assurances regarding funding for veterans in the future
