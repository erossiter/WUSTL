                                                                                                                                                           FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 06  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today said that Congress  under Democratic leadership  has achieved results for Americas families and continues to push ahead on critical priorities such as tax relief for middle income families and farm legislation for Arkansass farm families   In recent weeks  the President has tried to paint congressional Democrats as failing to complete our business on behalf of the American people  Yet again  the Presidents words simply dont match reality  Lincoln said   A brief recap of some of the bills Congress has passed this year alone include the 9 11 Commissions recommendations to increase our first responders capabilities and protect our nation from terrorism  Congress also passed a lobbying and ethics reform bill to increase transparency in the legislative process  At a time when the Veterans Administration is seeing an influx in veterans returning home from Iraq and Afghanistan  we passed the largest funding increase for veterans health care in the history of our country  In addition  we passed a long overdue increase in the federal minimum wage to  7 25 per hour  And we passed the largest increase in college aid to students since the G I  Bill so that more Americans can afford a quality education and help our country remain competitive in the global economy for years to come  While questioning Congress work ethic  the President has vetoed bills on childrens health  funding for health and education programs  stem cell research  and water resources  These are bills which received overwhelming bipartisan support  In fact  Congress successfully voted to override the Presidents veto of the water resources bill  The President is simply out of touch with the priorities of American families and communities  Congress has demonstrated our ability to compromise  best seen through our commitment to the childrens health bill  but the President would rather draw a line in the sand  Further  the Presidents allies inthe Senate have halted progress several times in the past few weeks on critical legislation  such as a patch to fix the Alternative Minimum Tax as well as the Farm Bill  which was unanimously approved by theSenate Agriculture Committee  It is unfathomable that we have not been able to move forward on these bipartisan bills  and I will continue to fight to achieve results for our farmers and families in Arkansas
