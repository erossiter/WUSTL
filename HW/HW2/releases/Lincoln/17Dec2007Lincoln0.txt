                    Senators Lincoln and Pryor and Rep  Berry Announce  91 605 for Arkansas Land and Farm Development                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Both                                                    Senators Lincoln and Pryor and Rep  Berry Announce  91 605 for Arkansas Land and Farm Development                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 17  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and Congressman Marion Berry today announced  91 605 in funding for Arkansas Land and Farm Development in Brinkley  Arkansas  This money will be used to help further Arkansas Land and Farm Development s mission of land based economic development    Communitiesthroughout Arkansas depend on federal dollars to improve the quality of lifefor their residents  said Lincoln  I commend Arkansas Land and Farm Development for seeking these funds to revitalize and developcommunities in the Delta region  As aDelta native  I know firsthand the real difference federal investments can make in a community   Rural communities must overcome multiple economic development challenges  said Pryor  This smart investment will enable Brinkley to address housing infrastructure needs  attract new jobs and improve quality of living for residents   For over 25 years  Arkansas Farm and Land Development has been committed to increasing the quality of life in rural communities for disadvantaged people  said Berry  These federal funds will help them continue their work  which will not only benefit local communities  but also the state of Arkansas  These dollars will have a positive impact on economic development in the Delta region and I commend Arkansas Land and Farm Development for securing these funds   The Rural Development funds will be used to revitalize local communities by addressing housing conditions  which include the promotion energy efficiency  the reduction of neglected and abandoned properties  and development of under utilized structures into needed community facilities  In addition  the program will also work to develop innovative rural transportation programs for local communities
