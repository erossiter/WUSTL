                    Arkansas Congressional Delegation Announces  105 000 for local law enforcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Both                                                    Arkansas Congressional Delegation Announces  105 000 for local law enforcement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 14  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and U S  Representative Marion Berry today announced that two local law enforcement agencies have received grants from the United States Department of Agriculture Rural Development program  The city of Holly Grove  AR  will receive  75 000 to make renovations to the City Police Department to improve safety services  In addition  Woodruff County will receive  30 000 to purchase vehicles for the Sheriff s Department   Local law enforcement must have the proper resources  equipment  and technology to ensure safety in our communities  said Lincoln  I commend the Holly Grove City Police Department and the Woodruff County Sheriff s Office for seeking additional funding so they can continue to protect local families   It is critical that the federal government do all that it can to provide our law enforcement officials the tools they need to protect themselves as they work to keep our communities safe  said Pryor  I commend local officials for seeking this funding and know it will have a significant effect on the safety and well being of eastern Arkansas   This grant will help local law enforcement in their efforts to keep communities safe  said Berry  These brave men and women are the first to respond to emergencies  and they need to have the best resources available to protect and serve local residents
