Arkansas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 10  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Little Rock     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today stressed the need for more affordable housing in Arkansas and the need to extend opportunities to Arkansans looking to own their own home at a legislative housing symposium she hosted in Little Rock        Home ownership is the pinnacle of the American dream  representing for most families a measure of financial security  independence and stability     Lincoln said     We must work to give people the tools they need to achieve this dream so that more Americans can benefit from owning their own homes        Lincoln  host of the symposium entitled     Making A Change  Redefining the Affordable Housing Landscape     brought together community leaders  affordable housing practitioners and housing counselors to develop an action plan to improve affordable housing throughout Arkansas   Lincoln stated the importance of coordinating with each sector of the community to achieve the goal of helping more Arkansans become homeowners          To develop more affordable housing in the underserved areas of Arkansas  we must create a partnership between individuals and the public and private sectors     Lincoln said      Individuals must take the initiative and begin educating themselves on ways to access affordable housing   In addition  the public sector must create opportunities for individuals to expand their knowledge of ways to access affordable housing and help to expand the availability of affordable housing   Private companies must be given the incentives to create affordable housing  as well           Lincoln   s housing symposium is part of the week long Community Development Training Conference presented by the Arkansas Coalition of Housing and Neighborhood Growth for Empowerment
