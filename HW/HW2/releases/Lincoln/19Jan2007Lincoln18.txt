                    Lincoln  Pryor  Snyder Announce Grant For Searcy Fire and Rescue Department                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln  Pryor  Snyder Announce Grant For Searcy Fire and Rescue Department  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  January 19  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Searcy    U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  and U S  Representative Vic Snyder announced today that the Searcy Fire and Rescue Department has been awarded a grant in the amount of  66 500 from the Department of Homeland Security   s Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program          The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program gives support to fire departments by providing tools and resources to ensure the safety of fire department personnel and the public  The Searcy Fire and Rescue Department received funding through the Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  which provides for training  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to fire stations and facilities            Our firefighters work hard each and every day to keep our communities safe and they deserve the best resources available   Senator Blanche Lincoln said   That s why I m proud to announce this important federal funding to help address the needs of the Searcy Fire and Rescue Department            The dedication displayed by the Searcy firefighters is above and beyond  and I m pleased this investment will further enhance the department s ability to prepare and respond to emergencies   Pryor said     It s important that we help provide them with the equipment  training  and facilities they need to stay safe as they bravely protect our communities                The safety of our firefighters is very important     said Congressman Snyder     This money will go a long way to get the tools necessary to keep them  and Searcy residents safe
