                    Lincoln Statement on Gates Nomination                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Gates Nomination  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 06  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   released the following statement regarding the nomination of Robert Gates as Secretary of the Department of Defense      In his confirmation hearing  Robert Gates showed that he possessed a realistic view of the situation on the ground in Iraq  As Secretary of Defense  I hope Dr  Gates remains committed to exploring all options with regards to our strategy in Iraq  For far too long  the civilian leadership at the Pentagon has ignored the seriousness of the situation and placed our men and women in uniform in unnecessary danger without the proper resources to finish the mission  I hope Dr  Gates    tenure at the Pentagon begins a new phase of success in Iraq  We owe it to our American troops and the Iraqi people
