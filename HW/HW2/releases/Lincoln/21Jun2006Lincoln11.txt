                    Lincoln Honored with Internet Keep Safe Award                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Both                                                    Lincoln Honored with Internet Keep Safe Award                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 21  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today received the Internet Keep Safe Coalition   s award for her efforts to protect children from online dangers       We know that the Internet provides a wealth of opportunities for our young people to learn  communicate with friends and family  and prepare for an increasingly digital and global tomorrow   Lincoln said   But we also know that we cannot ignore the dangers that can be merely a click away  Educational materials like those provided by the Internet Keep Safe Coalition are a critical part of our efforts  and it is important that more and more children can access these resources       The Internet Keep Safe Award is given annually to leaders who have shown an extraordinary commitment to protecting children from the growing and dangerous threats they face on the Internet and through other connected technologies     In 2005  Lincoln  the co founder of the Senate Caucus on Missing  Exploited and Runaway Children  introduced legislation in an effort to stand up to those who profit on the Internet by exploiting children and promoting indecent and obscene material  Lincoln   s bill   The Internet Safety and Child Protection Act   aims to relieve the anxieties experienced by parents due to the lack of controls they have over what their children view online  The bill also shifts the costs of protecting our children online from the American taxpayer to the actual purveyors of online pornography      The Internet is America   s new main street   Lincoln said   Unfortunately  on this new main street  children are susceptible to viewing illicit material and encountering sexual predators  We all know that parents are the front line of defense in protecting our children  and we as legislators must do everything that we can to help parents keep their children safe      According to the Internet Keep Safe Coalition  more than 30 million children currently access the Internet  with the number of kids spending two or more hours online per day doubling during summer months      The Internet Keep Safe Coalition is a non profit organization dedicated to helping parents  educators and caregivers by providing guidelines and tools to teach children the safe and healthy use of technology and the Internet  For more information  visit www ikeepsafe org
