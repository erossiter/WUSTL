                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce More Than  360 000 For Phillips Community College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Both                                                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce More Than  360 000 For Phillips Community College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  March 11  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   and Mark Pryor  D Ark    along with Representative Marion Berry  D AR  1st   announced today that Phillips Community College of the University of Arkansas has received a  364 667 grant from U S  Department of Education     The funds have been secured through the U S  Department of Education   s Strengthening Institutions Program  which helps eligible institutions of higher education to become self sufficient and expand their capacity to serve low income students by providing funds to improve and strengthen their academic quality  institutional management and fiscal stability   Phillips Community College will use these funds to increase student success and retention
