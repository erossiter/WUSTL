                                                                                               Both                                                    Lincoln Honors Arkansas Adoption Hero                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 10  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today saluted Norine Pippin of Rogers  nominated by Lincoln as a Congressional Angel in Adoption  for her work on behalf of the adoption community in Arkansas   Mrs  Pippin has worked as a child welfare advocate for more than 15 years  During the last 10 years  Mrs  Pippin has worked at the Arkansas Department of Human Services providing protective and foster care as well as investigative and adoption services  She has served as the Northwest Arkansas Adoption Specialist for the pasttwo years and has worked to decrease the average length of time children spend in transitional housing  Prior to her service in Arkansas  Mrs  Pippin spent five years with the Louisiana Department of Human Services  Mrs  Pippin holds degrees in Nursing  Psychology  and Business  The Angels in Adoption campaign raises awareness about adoption by honoring individuals for their efforts in helping children and families through adoption  This year  U S  Senators and Representatives selected more than 180 Congressional Angels from around the country for their outstanding contributions toward the welfare of foster children and orphans  The Angels in Adoption campaign is sponsored by the Congressional Coalition on Adoption Institute  CCAI   CCAI was created in 2001 by the bicameral  bipartisan Congressional Coalition on Adoption  CCA   of which Lincoln is a member  CCAI is a nonprofit  nonpartisan organization dedicated to raising awareness about the thousands of foster children in this country and the millions of orphans around the world in need of permanent  safe  and loving homes   Mrs  Pippin and the other Angels were recognized October 4 in Washington at an awards ceremony and gala event held in their honor  For more information on the Angels in Adoption campaign  visit
