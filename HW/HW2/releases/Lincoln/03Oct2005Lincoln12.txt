Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today issued the following statement on Harriet Miers  President Bush   s nominee to replace retiring Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O   Connor       I look forward to carefully reviewing the qualifications of Harriet Miers in the weeks ahead as I evaluate her ability to serve on the U S  Supreme Court   Lincoln said   I plan to closely monitor the approaching hearings in the Senate Judiciary Committee and to heavily weigh the views of Arkansans to help me reach that decision       I believe that above all  the people of Arkansas and our nation deserve a Supreme Court Justice who will fulfill his or her fundamental responsibility to interpret and apply the Constitution fairly without political favor or bias  While I have yet to make a decision on Ms  Miers  I appreciate that President Bush has recognized the importance of diversity in filling this vacancy to our nation   s highest court
