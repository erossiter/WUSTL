Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  October 28  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     Members of the Arkansas Congressional Delegation today announced that Congress is expected to finalize next week the Fiscal Year 2006 Agriculture Appropriations Conference Report which includes  42 378 929 million for various Arkansas projects   Following the Senate vote  the spending bill is expected to be signed into law by President Bush                                                    The six members of the Arkansas Congressional Delegation who fought for the projects announced today are as follows   Senators Blanche Lincoln  D  and Mark Pryor   D   Representatives Marion Berry  D 1st   Vic Snyder  D 2nd   John Boozman  R 3rd   and Mike Ross  D 4th      The FY 2006 Agriculture Appropriations Conference Report includes the
