                                                                                               Both                                                    Lincoln Makes Trip to Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 06  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   traveled through Iraq Thursday to gain a first hand perspective of the U S  war effort and the Iraqi governments progress meeting benchmarks mandated earlier this year by Congress  I am very appreciative of the opportunity to visit Iraq and to have seen the incredible work our military is doing  Lincoln said  The troops deserve our utmost gratitude  and importantly  a strategy that is worthy of their sacrifice and better reflects the reality that progress ultimately lies with Iraqis taking responsibility for their own future  I will continue to fight for that new direction  Lincolns trip came a week before General David Petraeus is to deliver the Administrations report to Congress on the Iraqi governments progress  an assessment that will help shape the Iraq debate in Congress  The timing of Lincolns trip is also significant as approximately 3 200 Arkansans are set to deploy to Iraq in January when the Arkansas National Guards 39th Infantry Brigade Combat Team begins its second tour of duty in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom  Lincoln  traveling with Senator Joseph Biden  D Del    Chairman of the Senate Committee on Foreign Relations  met with U S  Ambassador to Iraq Ryan Crocker and the respective Sunni and Shiite Vice Presidents of Iraq  as well as other Iraqi officials  The congressional delegation also received a Mine Resistant Ambush Protected  MRAP  vehicle demonstration in Ramadi  the capital of Iraqs Al Anbar province  and attended the Anbar Economic Forum  Additionally  Lincoln visited with several Arkansas troops currently deployed in Iraq
