Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Little Rock     U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and U S  Representative Vic Snyder will host a military service academy information night on Thursday  April 20th from 5 00 until 7 00 pm at the Legends Room at UALR   s Jack Stephens Center in Little Rock  Congressman Snyder will be in attendance to welcome interested high school students and their families  along with representatives from both Senators    offices and the five service academies     The Academy Night will offer high school students and parents interested in the U S  service academies information on the academies  how to apply to receive an academy nomination  and more   Brief presentations from service representatives  and informational material for all five academies  the Military Academy in West Point  NY  the  Naval Academy in Annapolis  MD  the Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs  CO  the Coast Guard Academy in New London  CT  and the Merchant Marine Academy in Kings Point  NY will be available     Each year  Members of Congress have the privilege of recommending a select group of people for admission to the U S  service academies  To be considered for an appointment to a service academy  an applicant must be between the ages of 17 and 23  25 for Merchant Marines   a citizen of the United States  unmarried with no dependents  medically and physically fit  and have a strong academic background and test scores   Graduates of the service academies are required to serve as a commissioned officer for at least five years upon graduation
