                    Lincoln Statement on Death of Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Both                                                    Lincoln Statement on Death of Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 04  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843  Today marks a sad day in our nation as we now grieve the passing of Chief Justice William Rehnquist       I am grateful to Chief Justice Rehnquist for his thirty plus years of public service to our country and I extend my deepest condolences to his family at this time of loss  I enjoyed the opportunity to become acquainted with Chief Justice Rehnquist during the impeachment trial and was impressed by the strength of his knowledge and his dedication to the judiciary  Chief Justice Rehnquist served our nation   s highest court with honor and distinction  and in the days to come  there will be much discussion as to how to fill this enormous void  Now is the time to mourn Chief Justice Rehnquist   s death  honor his dignified service and celebrate his life
