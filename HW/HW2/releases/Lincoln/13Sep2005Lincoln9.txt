                                                                                               Both                                                    Lincoln Honors Arkansas Adoption Hero                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  September 13  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   announced today that Alden Roller of Little Rock has been selected as a Congressional    Angel in Adoption    for his work on behalf of the adoption community in Arkansas       Mr  Roller has worked for 32 years as an adoption champion at the Arkansas Department of Human Services   He has served as Adoption Specialist  Placement Specialist  Adoption Coordinator  and is currently the Adoption Support Specialist at the department   Mr  Roller is known by many as the    Grandfather of Adopted Children in Arkansas        The Angels in Adoption campaign raises awareness about adoption by honoring individuals for their efforts in helping children and families through adoption   This year  U S  Senators and Representatives selected more than 180 Congressional    Angels    from all 50 states and the District of Columbia for their outstanding contributions toward the welfare of foster children and orphans     The Angels in Adoption campaign is sponsored by the Congressional Coalition on Adoption Institute  CCAI    CCAI was created in 2001 by the bicameral  bipartisan Congressional Coalition on Adoption  CCA   of which Lincoln is a member   CCAI is a nonprofit  nonpartisan organization dedicated to raising awareness about the thousands of foster children in this country and the millions of orphans around the world in need of permanent  safe  and loving homes       Mr  Roller and the other Angels will be recognized today at a national press conference in Washington and later at an awards banquet held in their honor   For more information on the Angels in Adoption campaign  visit http   www ccainstitute org
