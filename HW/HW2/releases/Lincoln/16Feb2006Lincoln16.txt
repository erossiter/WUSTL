and Representative Vic Snyder   2 00     3 30 p m   LRCHS Visitor Center site at Daisy L  Gatson Bates Drive   Park in Little Rock  Monday  February 20th     Newport  Jonesboro Newport Lunch with your Arkansans and their families to meet and visit with Lincoln over a Dutch treat style lunch at one of the community   s favorite eateries   11 30 a m   Kelley   s Restaurant  815 Hwy 67 North in
