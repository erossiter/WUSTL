                                                                                                                                                          FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  December 21  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington D C    In her annual holiday gift to her U S  Senate colleagues  Senator Blanche Lincoln this year has chosen to support the Arkansas Arts Center  one of the largest and most active museums in the Mid South and Arkansass leading cultural institution    This year  I have chosen to support the Arkansas Arts Center because of the extensive educational and artistic experiences it provides for Arkansans  said Lincoln  The Arts Center is dedicated not only to providing a rich cultural experience to patrons who visit its facilities  but also to reaching out to communities throughout Arkansas in order to ensure children and families all across our state have greater access to cultural and artistic opportunities  I am committed to ensuring this local treasure continues to attract economic development  enhance education  and create a better quality of life for all Arkansans   Lincolns gift will help the Arkansas Arts Center fulfill its mission of ensuring that learning  inspiration  and creative expression in the arts flourish throughout Arkansas for people of all ages and backgrounds   The Arkansas Arts Center is accredited by the American Association of Museums and serves over 700 000 Arkansans each year  The institution features a museum and a studio school  as well as a childrens theatre  Additionally  families throughout the state are able to benefit from the Arts Centers touring programs  including a traveling Artmobile  the Tell A Tale Troupe  and traveling exhibitions that bring educational and cultural work to over 440 000 Arkansans in rural areas each year
