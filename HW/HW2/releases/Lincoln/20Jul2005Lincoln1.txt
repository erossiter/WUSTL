Grant for Jackson County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  July 20  2005Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln  D  and Mark Pryor  D   along with Representative Marion Berry  D 1st  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded  1 million to the community of Newport in Jackson County through its Economic Development Administration  EDA       Specifically  the EDA funding being announced today will be used to construct a new railroad spur and industrial access roads to the existing Jackson County Industrial Complex  According to the Department of Commerce  this investment would create 240 new jobs in the region and spur  25 million in private investments       This federal investment is terrific news for the community of Newport and for the residents of Jackson County   Lincoln said   I   m pleased to have worked alongside the leadership of Newport and Jackson County to secure this funding  and I   m impressed by their efforts to leverage such a substantial private investment        This is fantastic news for the people in Jackson County who have worked hard to secure this money   said Berry   A new rail spur is going to open up a realm of job opportunities for the industrial complex that would have otherwise passed us by  I am pleased to see federal resources going towards such a worthy economic development project          This investment will yield significant economic benefits in Jackson County  expanding business opportunities and generating much needed jobs     Pryor said      I share my enthusiasm for this funding with many community and business leaders who have worked tirelessly to spur economic growth in northeast Arkansas
