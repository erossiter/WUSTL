                    Lincoln Praises Economic Growth and Job Creation in Sebastian County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Both                                                    Lincoln Praises Economic Growth and Job Creation in Sebastian County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  November 13  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington D C   U S  Senator Blanche Lincoln  D Ark   today released the following statement in response to news that Mars Petcare US Inc  plans to build a pet food plant at Fort Chaffee  in Sebastian County  which is expected to create approximately 200 new jobs in the area   I am proud of the hard work and proactive efforts that have culminated in substantial industrial development and job creation at Fort Chaffee  I commend Governor Beebe and the Arkansas Economic Development Commission for working closely with the Fort Smith Regional Chamber of Commerce  the Fort Chaffee Redevelopment Authority  local officials  and other community and business leaders to help bring vital jobs and infrastructure that will help this area continue to grow
