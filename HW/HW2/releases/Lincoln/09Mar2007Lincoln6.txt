                                 Lincoln  Pryor  Snyder Announce     Fire Grants for Perryville  Cammack Village   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  March 09  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington   Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor and Representative Vic Snyder announced today that the Perryville Volunteer Fire Department and the Cammack Village Fire Department in Little Rock have been awarded grants through the Department of Homeland Security s Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This funding will help the fire departments enhance current operations and improve firefighter safety           Perryville Volunteer Fire Department was awarded a grant in the amount of  42 805   Cammack Village Fire Department will receive  17 813   The purpose of the grant program is to provide first responders with training and equipment in order to enhance their ability to protect the health and safety of the public  and to protect firefighters from job related hazards            Our first responders deserve the best safety equipment available as they continue to risk their lives to protect our communities   said Lincoln   That s why I am proud to help announce this important federal funding to help address the needs of our Arkansas fire departments            Uncontained fires can cause devastating damage to homes and families   said Pryor   This grant will ensure that the brave Perryville and Cammack Village firefighters are trained and equipped to effectively respond to such emergencies            The Perryville Volunteer Fire Department has worked hard to acquire this federal funding to make Perry County and its residents safer   said Congressman Vic Snyder   These are a great group of volunteers  and I m glad their hard work has paid off   I am also pleased to see the Cammack Village Fire Department receive this necessary funding           The Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  Program has been providing funding to fire departments and other first responders since 2001  To date  a total of  2 4 billion in grants through the AFG program has been awarded to first responder organizations to purchase response equipment  personal protective equipment  and vehicles
