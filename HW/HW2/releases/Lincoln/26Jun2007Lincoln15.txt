Scholarships for East Arkansas Community College                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 26  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  AR 01  announced today that East Arkansas Community College has received a  13 746 00 grant from the Department of Health and Human Services  The funding will be used to provide scholarships for disadvantaged students   Every studentdeserves access to an affordable  quality education  said Lincoln With these federal funds  East Arkansas Community College can continue to offerstudents in the Deltaan opportunityto reach their academic goalsand provide financial assistance to those who need it most  Everyone should have access to a solid education no matter what their financial background  said Pryor  This grant will allow students  who might not otherwise benefit from the outstanding faculty and resources of East Arkansas Community College  to enhance their career opportunities and contribute to their community  A quality education from East Arkansas Community College can pave the way for a successful future for both students and the community  said Berry  These scholarships will help students turn dreams of college into a reality
