                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce  200 000 Grant for Corning Municipal Airport                                                                              Both                                                    Lincoln  Pryor and Berry Announce  200 000 Grant for Corning Municipal Airport  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  April 20  2007Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington  U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor along with U S  Representative Marion Berry  AR 01  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration has awarded the Corning Municipal Airport a  204 119 grant through the U S  Department of Transportation     The grant will provide funds to construct additional aircraft parking apron space and to construct fencing that will enhance the safety and security of the airport       Our local airports are a vital economic development tool for our communities   said Lincoln   I am pleased that Corning Municipal Airport will receive these critical funds in order to make necessary improvements to its facilities       These funds will provide needed aircraft parking space as well as enhance overall safety and security of the Corning Municipal Airport   said Pryor   It is a fitting example of how the FAA and local government can work together for the benefit of the wider community       The Corning Municipal Airport is an important economic resource for the community   said Berry   I m pleased the airport received this vital funding and I will continue my work to find more ways to support economic development in the region
