Defense Authorization Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  June 23  2006Contact  Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Washington     U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor today announced that the Senate authorized funding for two significant defense projects in Arkansas  The funding  part of the Fiscal Year 2007 Defense Authorization Bill  moves the 189th Airlift Wing closer to securing a new maintenance facility and the Pine Bluff Arsenal closer to constructing new housing units     Lincoln and Pryor secured  3 6 million for the 189th Airlift Wing to build a new 13 000 square foot Engine Inspection and Maintenance Facility designed to support the maintenance and training mission of C 130 aircraft  The proposed facility will replace the current building built in 1975 and provide the 189th Airlift Wing with the necessary engine shop space that meets today   s fire  safety and health standards  The functional areas will include aircraft engine inspection and maintenance areas  tool and equipment storage  and appropriate office space      The Senators also ensured that  2 9 million from the President   s budget remained in the Defense Authorization Bill to construct 10 family housing units at the Pine Bluff Arsenal  including neighborhood amenities and supporting infrastructure  to replace 10 existing yet inadequate units that will be demolished       I am pleased that we have been able to work together to secure funding for the Little Rock Air Force Base and Pine Bluff Arsenal because we must ensure that our men and women in uniform have adequate training and housing facilities   Lincoln said   These investments will ensure that Arkansas    military installations continue their long tradition of being part of our national defense         The 189th provides such an important role in keeping aircraft well maintained and personnel safe  despite the cramped and inefficient workspace they work in everyday  Securing this funding has been a tough fight given this year   s tight budget restrictions  but I believe this facility is critical to maintain an efficient and effective operation at the Little Rock Air Force Base and improve the quality of life for military personnel     Pryor said     Replacing the worn down and inadequate housing at the Pine Bluff Arsenal is also a high priority  and a positive first step toward providing the quality living conditions our soldiers and their families deserve       The Senators added that the Defense Authorization Bill is the first step in the appropriations process   To be included in the Fiscal Year 2007 Military Construction Appropriations Bill  these projects required the authorization provided by this legislation
