Katie Laning  202 224 4843 Greenwood  Ark      U S  Senators Blanche Lincoln and Mark Pryor  and Representative John Boozman announced Friday the City of Greenwood will receive an  865 990 SAFER Grant from the Department of Homeland Security     The money will be used to hire at least 10 new firefighters in Greenwood  according to Mayor Gary Campbell      The purpose of the Staffing for Adequate Fire and Emergency Response  SAFER  grants is to help fire departments increase the number of frontline firefighters  The goal is for fire departments to increase their staffing and deployment capabilities and ultimately attain 24 hour staffing  thus assuring that their communities have adequate protection from fire and fire related hazards      The SAFER grants support two specific activities   1  the hiring of firefighters  and  2  recruitment and retention of volunteer firefighters  The hiring of firefighters activity provides grants to pay for part of the salaries of newly hired firefighters over the 5 year program  SAFER is part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grants and is under the purview of the Office of Grants and Training of the Department of Homeland Security      This funding is great news for the people of Greenwood   Lincoln said   The safety of our communities depends on the invaluable service that our first responders provide us each and every day  and that   s why it is critical that the federal government continue to support our cities and towns through SAFER grants       The dedication displayed by our Greenwood firefighters is above and beyond  and I m pleased this investment will further enhance the department s ability to prepare and respond for emergencies to an even higher degree   Pryor said      I wish to congratulate Mayor Campbell and the city on the receipt of this money which will help protect the residents of Greenwood   Boozman said   Firefighters occupy a special place in our society  and I strongly support any efforts by local governments to hire and properly train those who we look to protect our lives and property
