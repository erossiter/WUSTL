SEN. ALLEN APPLAUDS CHOICE OF CONNAUGHTON AS HEAD OF MARITIME ADMINISTRATION
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) today applauded President Bush's nomination of Sean Connaughton to be the new head of the U.S. Department of Transportation's Maritime Administration. 
  "I have known Sean for years and have every faith in his ability as a leader and administrator. His vast experience as both an elected official in Virginia and as a graduate of the Merchant Marine Academy make him an outstanding choice to head the Maritime Administration. I am confident that President Bush and the nation will be well served with his insight and management skills. Sean has my steadfast support and I believe that my colleagues will join me in my enthusiasm for this nomination," said Senator Allen.
