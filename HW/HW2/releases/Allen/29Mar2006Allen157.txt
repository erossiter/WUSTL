SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES RURAL DEVELOPMENT GRANT AWARDED TO CUMBERLAND COUNTY
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development office has awarded a grant of $555,000 to Cumberland County.  
   "These important funds will allow the folks in Cumberland County to update and improve water services for the individuals in their community," said Senator Allen. "I am pleased that the USDA is once again able to provide support to improve the lives of Virginians." 
   Rural Development funds will be used to establish a public water system for the Courthouse area of Cumberland County. The project includes, but is not limited to, the development of a new well; water lines; a 250,000 gallon storage tank; valves and meters; and fire hydrants. For more information, please contact the County Administrator: Ms. Judy Ownby at (804) 492-3625.
