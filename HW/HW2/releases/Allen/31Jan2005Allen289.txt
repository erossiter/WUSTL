SENS. ALLEN, LANDRIEU TO INTRODUCE RESOLUTION FORMALLY APOLOGIZING FOR FAILURE TO PASS ANTI-LYNCHING LEGISLATION
  Sens. George Allen (R-VA) and Mary Landrieu (D-LA) tomorrow morning will host a press conference at the National Press Club to announce the introduction of a Senate resolution formally apologizing for the failure of the U.S. Senate to pass any anti-lynching legislation during the height of these crimes. Allen and Landrieu will be introducing their resolution in the U.S. Senate on Tuesday, February 1st, the first day of Black History Month.
