SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES FUNDING TO SIX VIRGINIA AIRPORTS
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the U.S. Department of Transportation has awarded grants to six Virginia airports for projects that will enhance the efficiency, safety and value of each airport.  
   "Many local airports are vital to the economy of their surrounding areas," said Senator Allen. "It's important to keep these airports in top condition so that they can continue to generate business and create jobs while maintaining a standard of excellence in safety and service." 
   The following Virginia airports received funding: 
   * Roanoke Regional Airport Roanoke, VA $1,608,156 
   * Virginia Tech/Montgomery Executive Airport Blacksburg, VA $1,373,000 
   * Leesburg Executive Airport Leesburg, VA $300,000 
   * Mountain Empire Airport Marion, VA $1,197,000 
   * William M. Tuck Airport South Boston, VA $1,339,500 
   * Tappahannock Airport Tappahannock, VA $400,159 
   The Airport Improvement Program is a federal grant-in-aid program that represents a major source of funding for airport development and planning. These airports will use the funds for various projects which include construction, maintenance and safety improvements for runways and aprons.
