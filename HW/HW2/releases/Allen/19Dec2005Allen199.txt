SEN. ALLEN PRESENTS PURPLE HEART AWARDS
  Sen. Allen and fans at FedExField applaud Staff Sgt. Todd Watson, Sgt. Shane Cieszeski, Sgt. Joshua Heskett and Spc. Richard Hursh at halftime of the Redskins-Cowboys game on December 18, 2005. The Senator presented the four soldiers who were wounded in combat with the Purple Heart award for their service in Iraq.
