SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES FEDERAL CONTRACT AWARDED TO VIRGINIA BEACH CORPORATION
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) has awarded an initial contract of $41,708 to Tesoro Corporation in Virginia Beach to refit the DHS food services facility in Glynco, Georgia. The total contract value with all options being exercised is estimated to be $4,960,000.  
   "This important contract will allow DHS to update and improve its food service facility," said Senator Allen. "I am pleased that Tesoro Corporation, a Virginia company, has been given the opportunity to provide this service." 
   Tesoro Corporation will furnish all labor, supplies, materials, equipment and supervision necessary to retrofit the kitchen area of the Department of Homeland Security's Federal Law Enforcement Training Center (FLETC) to include: removal and replacement of existing vent hoods with a new make-up air system; removal and replacement of ceiling tiles at the Salad Bar/Serving Area and North and South Dish Return Rooms; removal and replacement of the existing roof system membrane and spayed foam insulation; removal and replacement of high windows at the Kitchen, Vegetable Preparation Room, Dry Storage Room and Shop.
