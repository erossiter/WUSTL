SEN. ALLEN VISITS INFINEON SEMICONDUCTORS
  Sen. Allen speaks with employees of Infineon Semiconductors during his tour of the facility in Sandston on March 20th. The Senator was traveling throughout the Commonwealth this week, visiting: Richmond, Chesterfield, Charlottesville, Stuarts Draft, Staunton, Mount Crawford, Ruckersville, Madison and Culpeper. This Sunday, he will be the Grand Marshal at NASCAR's Food City 500 in Bristol.
