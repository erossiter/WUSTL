SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES FUNDING TO LOCAL FIRE DEPARTMENTS IN WESTERN VIRGINIA
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security Fire Administration's Assistance for Firefighters Grant Program has awarded $603,789 in federal grants to nine Western Virginia fire departments. 
   "Improving the safety and security of Virginians and our communities is one of my top priorities. By enhancing the training and support of our emergency crews, we are making great strides in this important mission," said Senator Allen. "I am pleased that Virginia's firefighters will be better equipped and prepared. Our communities will be safer because of this assistance." 
   Eight fire departments received funding from the Fire Operations and Firefighter Safety Program: 
   * Walkers Creek Fire and EMS: $78,799, Middlebrook, VA (Augusta Co.) 
   * Shenandoah Shores Volunteer Fire Department: $57,420, Front Royal, VA (Warren Co.) 
   * Fincastle Volunteer Fire Department: $126,315, Fincastle, VA (Botetourt Co.) 
   * Shawnee Volunteer Fire Department: $61,200, Winchester, VA 
   * Edinburg Volunteer Fire Company, Inc: $21,150, Edinburg, VA (Shenandoah Co.) 
   * Dooms Volunteer Fire Department, Inc: $45,000, Waynesboro, VA 
   * Clover Hill Volunteer Fire Company, Inc: $66,330, Dayton, VA (Rockingham Co.) 
   * Bergton Volunteer Fire Company, Inc: $60,075, Bergton, VA (Rockingham Co.) 
   One fire department received funding from the Firefighting Vehicle Program: 
   * Roanoke County Fire and Rescue: $87,500, Roanoke, VA 
   The Assistance for Firefighters Grant Program is administered by the U.S. Fire Administration and assists rural, urban and suburban fire departments throughout the United States. These funds are used by the nation's firefighters to increase the effectiveness of firefighting operations and for firefighter health and safety programs, new fire apparatus, emergency medical service programs, and fire prevention and safety programs.
