SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES OVER $1 MILLION IN FUNDING FOR NEWPORT NEWS-WILLIAMSBURG INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has awarded a total of $1,010,298 in grant funds to Newport News/Williamsburg International Airport to construct an apron. The construction project will provide additional apron and parking positions for aircraft at peak hour gate operations. 
   "The Newport News/Williamsburg International Airport is vital to the success of the Hampton Roads economy," said Senator Allen. "I'm pleased these federal funds will help keep this important regional airport in top condition so that it can continue to generate business and create jobs while maintaining a standard of excellence in safety and service for its passengers."
