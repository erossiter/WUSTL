SEN. ALLEN OFFERS SUPPORT FOR STORM DAMAGED AREAS
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) released the following statement about the recent emergency declaration for Tazewell and Russell counties: 
   "The recent storms in Tazewell and Russell counties have had a terrible affect on families that incurred damage to their businesses and their homes," said Senator Allen. "My heart goes out to the communities that have been affected. I will do whatever I can on the federal level to insure that these fine folks are able to get help and get back on their feet as quickly as possible."
