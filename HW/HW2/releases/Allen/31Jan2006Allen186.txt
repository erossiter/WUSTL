SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES DEPARTMENT OF HOMELAND SECURITY FIRE GRANT WORKSHOP IN VIRGINIA BEACH
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) will be holding a Fire Grant Workshop in Virginia Beach, Virginia.  
 The workshops will be held on Saturday, February 25, 2006, at 1:00 and 3:00 PM at the Virginia Beach Fire Expo in the Doubletree Hotel, 1900 Pavilion Drive.
   "Our Virginia emergency personnel should have every opportunity to compete for additional federal funding to support them in their important homeland security work. I would heartily encourage local fire departments, EMS , and their associated personnel to take advantage of this informative workshop," said Senator Allen. "Our Commonwealth relies on the bravery of our dedicated firefighters, EMS and those that work with them, and I am altogether very pleased for them to receive help in acquiring much-needed federal funding through these workshops." 
   Please be sure to visit the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program website (http://www.firegrantsupport.com/Workshop/display/workshops.aspx) for more information.
