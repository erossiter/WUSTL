SEN. ALLEN ANNOUNCES $450,000 IN FUNDING FOR HAMPTON ROADS AIRPORT
  Sen. George Allen (R-VA) announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has awarded a total of $450,000 in grant funds to Hampton Roads Executive Airport for phase 3 of a project to rehabilitate a runway, bringing it up to runway-taxiway separation standards.  
   "The Hampton Roads Executive Airport is vital to the success of the Hampton Roads economy," said Senator Allen. "I'm pleased these federal funds will help keep this important regional airport in top condition so that it can continue to generate business and create jobs while maintaining a standard of excellence in safety and service for its passengers." 
   The Airport Improvement Program is a federal grant-in-aid program that represents a major source of funding for airport development and planning.
