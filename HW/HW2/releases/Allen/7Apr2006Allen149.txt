SEN. ALLEN SPEAKS ON U.S. - INDIA CIVIL NUCLEAR AGREEMENT
  Sen. Allen spoke Thursday, April 6th at a press conference held by the Congressional Task Force on U.S.-India Trade on the recent U.S.-India Civil Nuclear Cooperation Agreement. Sen. Allen, who was joined by Under Secretary of State Nicholas Burns, India's Deputy Chief of Mission R.S. Jassal and the CEO of the U.S.-India Business Alliance Sanjay Puri called the agreement a significant foreign policy achievement.
