SEN. FEINSTEIN TO VOTE NO ON CLOTURE FOR NOMINATION OF JUDGE ALITO TO BE ASSOCIATE JUSTICE OF SUPREME COURT
  Sen. Dianne Feinstein (D-Calif.) today announced that she will vote no on cloture regarding the nomination of Judge Samuel A. Alito, Jr. to be an Associate Justice of the Supreme Court. 
   "Based on a very long and thoughtful analysis of the record and transcript, which I tried to indicate in my floor statement yesterday, I've decided that I will vote no on cloture."
