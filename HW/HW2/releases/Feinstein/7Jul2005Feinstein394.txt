SEN. FEINSTEIN COMMENTS ON TERRORIST ATTACKS IN LONDON
  Statement of Senator Dianne Feinstein on the terrorist attacks in London
   "My heart goes out to the British people. They are a wonderful, strong people, and I know that all Americans stand with them on this tragic day. This Senator offers her help in any way possible. 
   Once again, we see that terrorists are willing to strike anywhere, anytime. But let there be no doubt - we will prevail in this war on terror."
