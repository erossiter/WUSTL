SEN. FEINSTEIN ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF PALESTINIAN PRESIDENT ARAFAT
  "My condolences go out to the Palestinian people and the family of President Yasser Arafat. I am hopeful that after his death, new leadership will emerge that has as its goal the establishment of a Palestinian state living with acceptance of its neighbor, the state of Israel. It is all-important that this new leadership be dedicated to achieving peace between the two parties."
