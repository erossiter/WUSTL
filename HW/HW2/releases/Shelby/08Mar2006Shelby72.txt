SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 TO CITY OF EVERGREEN FOR MIDDLETON FIELD     March 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  472 625 to the City of Evergreen for Middleton Field   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  400 000 for Middleton Field  This funding will allow the airport to extend existing runway protection zone and improve visibility between runways  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians                                                           March 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
