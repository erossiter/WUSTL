SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 TO THE UNITED WAY OF SOUTHWEST ALABAMA     May 5  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice has awarded  119 293 to the United Way of Southwest Alabama   I am pleased to announce  119 293 for United Way of Southwest Alabama  said Senator Shelby  I believe gang related violence is a real and immediate problem in Alabama  To effectively curb gang activity  our programs must be specifically designed to prevent the new members from joining  while prosecuting those already involved   The Southwest Alabama United Way will initiate programs aimed to curb gang related violence  The programs will focus on education  prevention and community partnerships  enforcement and prosecution investigation and prosecution of gang members  and technology   These funds are released from the fiscal year 2006 Anti Gang Initiative  which supports new or expanded anti gang enforcement and prevention efforts under the Project Safe Neighborhoods Initiative  The Anti Gang Initiative is led by the United State Attorney in each of the 94 federal districts in the United States                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
