Shelby Announces Over  117 000 to the Enterprise Fire Department    December 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  117 168 to the Enterprise Fire Department  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This grant will enhance the Enterprise Fire Departments operations and safety programs  said Senator Shelby  Our nations first responders must have the resources necessary to keep our communities and themselves safe  This funding is an essential component in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year
