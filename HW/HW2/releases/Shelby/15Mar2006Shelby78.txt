SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 MILLION FOR STATEWIDE ANTI DRUG EFFORTS          March 21  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee for Commerce  Justice  and Science today announced that the U S  Department of Justice will release  3 178 628 to the Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs Law Enforcement and Traffic Safety Division for statewide anti drug efforts   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs  Alabama will use the funds to support the 33 drug task forces currently in place throughout the state  as well as crime prevention programs  such as neighborhood watch and police training sessions   Sen  Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  3 178 628 to support Alabama law enforcement efforts to combat the growing drug problems in our cities and towns  The work of these drug task forces helps keep drugs out of the hands of our children and protect us from violent crimes that can result from drug use
