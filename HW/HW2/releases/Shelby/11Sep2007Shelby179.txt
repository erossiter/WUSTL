Shelby Announces  100 000 to the City of Russellville   September 12  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  100 000 to the City of Russellville Water and Sewer Board for water service repairs along Franklin County Highway 60  east of U S  Highway 43   It is critical that the residents of Russellville have adequate water pressure that will not only enable them to conduct day to day activities  but provide protection in the event of a fire  said Shelby  This important funding will assist the city as it makes much needed improvements to its sewer lines and fire hydrants  We must ensure that our cities are sufficiently equipped to respond to fire emergencies   The repairs along Franklin Highway 60 are necessary to improve water service and fire protection to approximately twenty customers  In addition to ARC funds  the total expected project cost of  153 203 includes  53 203 from the City of Russellville Water and Sewer Board                                                         September 2007 News Releases
