SHELBY ANNOUNCES NOMINEE TO U S  SERVICE ACADEMY FROM CHILTON COUNTY   Chilton County Student Receives Nomination to U S  Air Force Academy  February 9  2005  Washington  D C     U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   today announced that Daniel Bevers of Clanton  AL has been nominated to the United States Air Force Academy  Senator Shelby nominates students every year to the U S  Naval Academy  U S  Air Force Academy  U S  Military Academy  and the U S  Air Force Academy  Daniel  son of Bruce and Katye Bevers  is a student at Chilton County High School   Senator Shelby said  Furthering ones education at our nations service academies is remarkable  Daniel has shown exemplary performance in the classroom and in his extracurricular activities  The selection process for the academies is extremely competitive  and I was pleased to have been able to nominate Daniel    Service academy nominees must be residents of Alabama and must complete the required ACT and SAT exams  Applicants are also required to provide letters of recommendation  their school transcript  completed medical examination  and the required application form    Although Senator Shelby nominated Daniel  the United States Air Force Academy will make the final selection                                                        February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
