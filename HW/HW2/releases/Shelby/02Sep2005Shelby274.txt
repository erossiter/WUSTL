SHELBY STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF JUDGE ROBERTS TO CHIEF JUSTICE     September 5  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  issued the following statement upon learning of President Bushs nomination of Judge John G  Roberts  Jr  to be Chief Justice of the United States Supreme Court     I am pleased with President Bushs decision to nominate Judge Roberts to be Chief Justice of the United States Supreme Court   Judge Roberts has a long and distinguished career of public service  serving our country in a number of positions  including his most recent tenure on the D C  Circuit Court of Appeals   His experience and demonstrated leadership make him a strong candidate to be Chief Justice of the highest court in the land             I believe the Presidents decision to swiftly select a Chief Justice nominee serves the nation well  Our country has a number of challenges ahead of us  and having a Chief Justice in place when the Supreme Court reconvenes in October is extremely important  I am also hopeful that the President will move quickly to announce another nominee for associate justice      As we move forward  I intend to carefully review Judge Roberts judicial philosophy and qualifications for this important position  I look forward to the confirmation process  and I am hopeful that the Senate will operate in a fair manner and afford him an up or down vote        30                                                          September 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
