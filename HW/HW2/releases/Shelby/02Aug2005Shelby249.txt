SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  590 000 TO UAB TRANSIT ACTIVITIES      August 9  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will be releasing  590 291 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  Center for Composite Manufacturing     These funds will be used to support the continuing efforts of UABs Center for Composite Manufacturing to develop lighter weight and lower cost materials that will increase the performance and durability of buses and other means of mass transit        Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  590 000 for the important work being done at UAB   These funds will allow the UAB Center for Composite Manufacturing to develop new technology which will be used to make substantial improvements to the performance  durability  safety and manufacturing of various forms of mass transit          30                                                               August 2005 News
