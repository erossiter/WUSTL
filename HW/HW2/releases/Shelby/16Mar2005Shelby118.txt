      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 2 MILLION FOR CENTER FOR INJURY SCIENCES AT UAB    March 21  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  1 211 786 will be released from the Department of Transportation for the Injury Control Research Center at the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to continue the important work being done at UABs Injury Research Center  The research at the center will allow us to improve and expand the Alabama Trauma Care System and reduce mortality due to trauma related injury    Senator Shelby secured these funds through the federal appropriations process                                                        March 2005 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
