      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  176 000 for Chambers County    April 24  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  176 000 to the Chambers County Commission to fund a water line extension   Chambers County is in a great geographical position to benefit from the new Kia plant in Georgia  said Senator Shelby  Theses upgrades to the infrastructure are essential to recruiting Tier 1 distributors in the surrounding area  Other regions in Alabama have seen substantial improvements to their economies through the automotive industry and I hope we will see the same in Chambers County   The project will extend the water line to the Cusseta Industrial Park  which is located on I 85 between the Kia plant in Georgia and Hyundai plant in Montgomery  Alabama  The water line extension is expected to create 50 jobs and  3 8 million in private investment                                                          April 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                    Current record                                                                                                         Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
