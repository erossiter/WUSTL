SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  2 6 MILLION FOR THE CITY OF MONTGOMERY       March 21  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the City of Montgomery will receive  2 602 387 in federal disaster funds as reimbursement for part of the cost of debris removal in the aftermath of Hurricane Ivan  These funds will be administered by the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA    The  2 6 million grant represents a 90 percent federal share of project costs  The remaining 10 percent will be covered on a 10 percent state cost share basis  Additional Public Assistance grants will be announced in the 51 designated eligible counties as the recovery continues    Sen  Shelby said  I am pleased to announce that the City of Montgomery will receive this much deserved funding as a result of the devastation caused by Hurricane Ivan  These funds are critical for our communities to recover and rebuild to pre hurricane operation levels
