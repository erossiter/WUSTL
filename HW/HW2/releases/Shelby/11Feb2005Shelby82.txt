SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  82 000 FOR TWO FIRE DEPARTMENTS IN JEFFERSON COUNTY   Hoover Fire Department and Cahaba Valley Fire and Emergency Medical Rescue Will Receive Funding  February 16  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that two fire departments in Jefferson County will receive federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities        38 920   Hoover Fire Department          43 200   Cahaba Valley Fire and Emergency Medical Rescue      These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year     This is great news for these Jefferson County fire departments  These grants provide critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen
