Shelby Announces  175 000 for North Central Alabama Council of Governments   March 6  2007 U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission will release  175 000 for the North Central Alabama Council of Governments  NARCOG  Revolving Loan Fund   I tell people all over the world that Alabama is open for business  said Senator Shelby  This funding will allow the area to continue to take part in the growing economy  NARCOGs revolving loan fund will support small businesses  an integral part of our economy  and will also further our growth through investment in new businesses  Stronger small businesses  in addition to new companies  mean more jobs for the citizens of Alabama   In addition to ARC funds  local sources will provide  175 000  bringing the total project funding to  350 000  The funding will enable NARCOG to further their revolving loan program to invest in small and emerging businesses in Cullman  Lawrence  and Morgan counties  It is anticipated that this capitalization and the leveraged non ARC funding will result in the ability to fund four loans that could create 30 new jobs  NARCOGs revolving loan fund has leveraged over  27 million into the area from outside funding sources   Senator Shelby secured these funds through the annual appropriations process
