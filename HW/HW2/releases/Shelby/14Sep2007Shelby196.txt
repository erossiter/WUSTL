Shelby Announces  200 000 for Decatur Morgan Port Authority   September 27  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  will award  200 000 to the Decatur Morgan Port Authority for water and sewer extensions   It is critically important that local communities have the infrastructure necessary to encourage economic growth and attract new businesses  said Shelby  The extension of water and sewer lines will better serve the needs of the new petrochemical plant in Decatur  which is expected to bring new high wage jobs to the area   The Decatur Morgan Port Authority water and sewer extension project will create 100 jobs and bring  160 million in private investment to the port  In addition to ARC funds  the total expected project cost of  422 000 includes  222 000 from the Port Authority                                                        September
