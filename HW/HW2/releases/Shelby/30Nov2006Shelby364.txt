Shelby Announces More Than  73 000 to the Winterboro Volunteer Fire and Rescue    November 30  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  73 340 to Winterboro Volunteer Fire and Rescue in Talladega County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Alabamas first responders must have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  The Winterboro Volunteer Fire and Rescue will use this funding to improve and sustain their operations and safety  This grant is evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                        November 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
