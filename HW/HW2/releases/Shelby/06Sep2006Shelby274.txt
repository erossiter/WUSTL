SHELBY ANNOUNCES  716 000 FOR AUBURN OPELIKA ROBERT G  PITTS AIRPORT    September 6  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  716 000 to Auburn University for Auburn Opelika Robert G  Pitts Airport  The funding will allow the airport to construct a new apron   Auburn  Opelika  and Lee County are growing at a tremendous rate  said Senator Shelby  The industrial development throughout the area is bringing new and better jobs to the community everyday  The proposed expansion and upgrades to the airport will play an important role in the communitys ability to continue to recruit and develop its industrial and commercial business sector   With the funding provided  Auburn University will prepare the new terminal apron site at the airport  Currently  the apron is too close to the taxiway system and is too small to for the increased jet aircraft utilizing the airport  Both safety and efficiency will be improved at the airport with the completion of the new terminal apron                                                          September 2006
