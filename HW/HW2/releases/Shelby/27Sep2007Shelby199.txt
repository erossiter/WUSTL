Shelby Announces  262 000 for the Town of Pine Hill   September 28  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Delta Regional Authority  DRA  will award  262 000 to the Town of Pine Hill  Alabama  for sewer line replacement  It is critical that the Town of Pine Hill replace its current sewer lines so that they may comply with standards set forth by the Alabama Department of Environmental Management  said Shelby  It is important that we support our communities infrastructure so that they can effectively support residents and businesses  In addition to DRA funds  the total expected project cost of  350 000 includes  88 000 from the Town of Pine Hill
