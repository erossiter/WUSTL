SHELBY ANNOUNCES CONFIRMATION HEARING FOR TVA BOARD NOMINEES   Howard Thrailkill nominated as first Alabamian to board                                                                                                                                                                                                          January 27  2006  Washington  D C      U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   today announced that the U S  Senate Environment and Public Works Committee has scheduled the confirmation hearing for the Tennessee Valley Association  TVA  Board nominees  The hearing will take place on Wednesday  February 8  2006 at 9 30 a m   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the Committee is working in a timely manner to confirm Howard Thrailkill to the TVA board  Following his confirmation  Howard will be the first Alabamian to serve the TVA in this capacity  and his appointment marks the beginning of what I am confident will be a permanent tradition  I have long advocated for an Alabamian on the TVA board  and I am pleased the President chose Howard for this important position   Mr  Thrailkill was nominated by the President for a term which will expire in May of 2011
