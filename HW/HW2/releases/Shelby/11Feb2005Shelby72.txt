SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 FOR FRANKLIN FIELD IN UNION SPRINGS     February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that Franklin Field in Union Springs will receive  403 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements to the airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Franklin Field  These funds will be used to acquire land needed for approach protection at the airport to enhance safety of operations  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so Franklin Field may make these necessary improvements    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
