      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 MILLION FOR TALLADEGA     January 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Health and Human Services will release  3 305 732 to the Talladega  Clay  Randolph  and Coosa Childcare Programs  The funding will go towards the Head Start program   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available for Head Start projects in Talladega  Clay  Randolph  and Coosa  Head Start is an important program that prepares students for their formal education  and ensures strong overall development in these young children   Head Start is a school readiness program for preschool aged children of low income families  The funding was awarded by the Region IV Office for Children and Families                                                             January 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Current record                                                                                                                                       Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
