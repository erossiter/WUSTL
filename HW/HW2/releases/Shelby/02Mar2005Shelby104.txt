SHELBY COMMENDS DECLARATION TO ADJUST COST SHARE FOLLOWING HURRICANE IVAN   President Announces Federal Government will Cover 90  in Disaster Assistance  March 2  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today commended the announcement today by President George W  Bush to amend the disaster declaration following Hurricane Ivan    Specifically  President Bush amended the disaster declaration to authorize 90  federal funding for all categories of assistance under the Public Assistance Program   This will be retroactive to the date of declaration  which was September 15  2004     Senator Shelby said  In most disaster situations  the federal government will cover 75  of the cost  leaving the remaining up to state and localities to pay   I am pleased that the federal governments share has increased to 90   easing the burden of localities  who have already seen great financial strain as a result of Hurricane Ivan
