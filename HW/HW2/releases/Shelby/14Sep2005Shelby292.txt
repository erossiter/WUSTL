SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  917 000 FOR UA CENTER FOR ADVANCED VEHICLE TECHNOLOGIES      September 14  2005                WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will soon be releasing  917 898 to the University of Alabama Center for Advanced Vehicle Technologies                      Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that over  917 000 will be coming to the University of Alabama Center for Advanced Vehicle Technologies   These funds will be used to support the efforts of the center to research and develop new nano structured catalysts for hydrogen production and fuel cell applications                     Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds in the federal appropriations process
