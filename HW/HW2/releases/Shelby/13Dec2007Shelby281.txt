Shelby Announces  53 000 for Economic Development in Mobile County   December 13  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    ranking member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice and Science  today announced that U S  Department of Commerces Economic Development Administration  EDA  will invest  53 000 in the South Alabama Regional Planning Commission for economic development in Mobile County  It is critical that Alabamas government and businesses work together to strengthen the economies of our local communities  said Shelby  These funds will assist in bringing together government entities and businesses so that they can work toward a stronger economy for Mobile County  The Commission serves municipal and county governments and their citizens to promote the area  aiming to improve quality of life  create new opportunities and aid economic development  The Commission  through planning and advocacy  helps bring the region together through the common goal of bringing jobs and growth to the area                                                         December 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
