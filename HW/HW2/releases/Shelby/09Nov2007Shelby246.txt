Shelby Announces Disaster Assistance for Small Business Owners in Chambers  Lee and Russell Counties   November 14  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala   today announced that small businesses in three Alabama counties    Chambers  Lee and Russell    will be eligible for federal disaster loans from U S  Small Business Administration  SBA   Eligible small businesses in these counties will be afforded assistance through the SBAs Economic Injury Disaster Loan program to help with financial losses resulting from the drought  This terrible drought has not only affected Alabamas farmers and ranchers  but also our small businesses  said Shelby  It is critical that small business owners have the financial assistance to help them recover from this disaster  I will continue to do everything I can to ensure that assistance is available and I encourage eligible small business owners to take full advantage of this funding  To apply  small businesses can visit the SBAs website at  to download an application  or they may contact the agencys Customer Service Center at 1 800 659 2955  Completed loan applications must be returned to the SBA no later than June 18
