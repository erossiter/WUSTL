SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 TO THE MONTGOMERY AREA UNITED WAY     May 5  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice has awarded  117 324 to the Montgomery Area United Way for anti gang initiatives   I am pleased to announce  117 324 for United Way in Montgomery  said Senator Shelby  I believe gang related violence is a disturbing problem in this country  It is my hope that this project will help keep youth in Montgomery out of gangs and on a better path to their future  while prosecuting those who perpetuate gang violence in the area   The Montgomery Area United Way will initiate programs aimed to curb gang related violence  which will focus on education  prevention and community partnerships  enforcement and prosecution investigation and prosecution of gang members  and technology   These funds are released from the fiscal year 2006 Anti Gang Initiative  which supports new or expanded anti gang enforcement and prevention efforts under the Project Safe Neighborhoods Initiative  The Anti Gang Initiative is led by the United State Attorney in each of the 94 federal districts in the United States                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year
