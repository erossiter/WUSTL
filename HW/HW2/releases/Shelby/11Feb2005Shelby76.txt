SHELBY ANNOUNCES  547 000 FOR SHELBY COUNTY AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that Shelby County will receive  547 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at Shelby County Airport in Alabaster  AL   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Shelby County Airport  These funds will be used to rehabilitate the existing runway to provide a safe and high quality landing surface for the patrons of the airport  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                         February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
