SHELBY ANNOUNCES  18 3 MILLION IN HURRICANE RECOVERY FUNDS      May 17  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that Alabama will receive  18 3 million from the U S  Department of Transportation in federal hurricane recovery funds   These funds will be used for the repair or reconstruction of federal aid highways and roads on federal lands which have suffered serious damage as a result of the natural disaster    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce that Alabama will receive this much deserved funding as a result of the devastation caused by Hurricane Ivan   These funds will be used to cover the states costs to rebuild and repair highways and bridges that serve as a vital lifeline to Alabama residents   These funds are critical for our communities to return to pre hurricane operation levels following this storm        30                                                          May 2005 News Releases                      Year
