      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  1 198 096 for Alabama Department of Economic Affairs    April 25  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Labor  Health  and Human Services  today announced that Alabama will receive a  1 2 million federal grant designed to help individuals with disabilities gain information about employment  The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Labors Employment and Training Administration  ETA  to the Alabama State Department of Economic Affairs to support its Disability Program Navigator  DPN  initiative  Alabama received these funds through a competitive grant program   This program assists disabled individuals in accessing employment opportunities  said Senator Shelby  The DPN initiative creates conditions where people with disabilities can begin to help themselves and earn a good living  This initiative increases employment opportunities and self sufficiency for individuals with disabilities                                                         April 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                   Current record                                                                                          Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
