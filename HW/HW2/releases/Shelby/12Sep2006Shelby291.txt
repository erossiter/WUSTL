SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 TO YWCA OF CENTRAL ALABAMA    September 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  449 845 to the YWCA of Central Alabama for their Family Law Center  The YWCA will use the funding to provide legal services to victims of domestic violence  sexual assault  and stalking   Domestic violence  stalking  and sexual assault are brutal crimes that terrorize the lives of innocent victims  said Senator Shelby  The Family Law Center is operated by the YWCA of Central Alabama  our states largest provider of domestic violence services  They will use this funding to ensure that those who commit these horrible crimes are held accountable  By bringing their assailant to justice  victims will be able to move on with their lives  The YWCA of Central Alabama will use the funding provided to continue to operate the YWCA Family Law Center  a comprehensive civil legal services system for victims of domestic violence within Jefferson  St  Clair  and Blount Counties  The Family Law Center serves at least 100 clients per year by employing two full time attorneys  The attorneys will continue providing legal services to sexual assault victims at the Birmingham Crisis Centers Rape Response Center  The Family Law Center also works with Cumberland School of Law students who volunteer their legal services                                                         September 2006 News Releases
