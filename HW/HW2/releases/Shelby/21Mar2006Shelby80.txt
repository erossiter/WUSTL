SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  50 000 FOR CALHOUN COUNTY  CITY OF OXFORD    CITY OF ANNISTON     March 23  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  57 942 to Calhoun County  the City of Oxford  and the City of Anniston  Calhoun County will use the funding to purchase 12 new portable computers  which will allow officers to write reports in their vehicles  The City of Oxford will use their funding to support their Criminal Investigation Division and their Drug Investigation Unity  as well as to purchase GPS systems  The City of Anniston will purchase 7 digital camera systems   I am pleased to announce  57 942 for Calhoun County  the City of Anniston  and the City of Oxford  I believe it is our responsibility to support those men and women who risk their lives everyday for our safety  said Shelby  It is critical that the Alabama law enforcement has the most technologically advanced crime fighting equipment available   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs                                                           March 2006 News Releases                      Year
