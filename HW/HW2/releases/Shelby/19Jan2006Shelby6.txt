      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY ANNOUNCES  200 000 FOR TOWN OF EPES     January 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Delta Regional Authority  DRA  will release  200 000 to the Town of Epes in Sumter County for sewer line adjustments  The funding will increase public works for 30 households in Epes   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to the town of Epes  It is so important that our communities have the strong infrastructure needed to provide the necessary resources for their citizens   The DRA is funded through the federal appropriations process  Senator Shelby was pleased to assist in securing  12 000 000 to fund the DRA in the fiscal year 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill                                                           January 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                  Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
