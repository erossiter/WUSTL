SIGN UP FOR HURRICANE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM     May 3  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture 2006 Hurricane Assistance Program sign up will begin May 17  2006  The  250 million in funding will go towards four crop and livestock programs providing aid to producers affected by hurricanes in 2005  To sign up  producers may visit their local farm service agency on or after May 17  2006    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA is providing this assistance to farmers affected by last years hurricane disasters  As we enter this years hurricane season  we must continue to find ways to rebuild those areas affected by these catastrophic natural disasters  I encourage all those Alabamians eligible for this funding to take advantage of this opportunity and sign up    The four programs  funded by Section 32  are for livestock  feed  hurricane  and tree indemnity  Producers in the following counties who experienced hurricane damage are eligible  Autauga  Baldwin  Bibb  Butler  Chambers  Choctaw  Clarke  Clay  Cleburne  Coffee  Colbert  Conecuh  Covington  Crenshaw  Cullman  Dallas  Escambia  Geneva
