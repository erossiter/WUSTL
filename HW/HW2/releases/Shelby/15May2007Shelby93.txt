Shelby Announces  98 980 for Alabama Humanities Foundation    May 16  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the National Endowment for the Humanities has awarded  88 980 to the Alabama Humanities Foundation in Birmingham to support the We the People history and civics initiative  The National Endowment for the Humanities will match state funding with an additional  10 000   The funding will allow We the People to continue several of its programs  including speakers bureau presentations on Alabama history and culture  an initiative where teachers will learn about the Black Belt  the New Harmonies traveling exhibition  and a We the People grant initiative   The We the People program teaches students valuable information about the founding of our nation and state  said Shelby  Our children are the future leaders of our country  and I firmly believe that they must learn about our past to effectively lead us into the future                                                           May 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
