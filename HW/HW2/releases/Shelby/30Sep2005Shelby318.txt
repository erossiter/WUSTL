SHELBY STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF HARRIET MIERS TO SUPREME COURT     October 3  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement upon learning of President Bushs nomination of Harriet Miers to the United States Supreme Court     I am pleased with President Bushs nomination of Harriet Miers to the United States Supreme Court   I intend to carefully review her judicial philosophy and qualifications for this important position   I believe it is imperative that the Senate continue to confirm judges who will interpret the Constitution and the law without prejudice and with the utmost respect for the rule of law        Further  I was pleased with the civility in which Judge Roberts nomination was considered   I am hopeful that Ms  Miers is afforded the same respect  and that the Senate will operate in a fair manner and afford her an up or down vote  Shelby added            30                                                          October 2005 News
