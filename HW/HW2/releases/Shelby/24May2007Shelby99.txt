Shelby Announces  838 000 for City of Leeds   June 5  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Commerce Departments Economic Development Administration will invest  838 000 in the Water Works Board of the City of Leeds in Leeds  Alabama  The investment will support water and sewer infrastructure necessary for the development of the new commercial enterprises along Interstate 20   This investment is good news for the city of Leeds and the Birmingham metro area  said Shelby  As new businesses come to the city of Leeds  it is critical that the Citys infrastructure be ready to support the additional users   Construction of the Bass Pro Shop  Bass Pro Shop Hotel and a Golden Rule Barbeque on Interstate 20 in Leeds is expected to be completed in 2008  The new development is expected to create 298 jobs and generate  71 2 million in private investment                                                          June 2007 News Releases
