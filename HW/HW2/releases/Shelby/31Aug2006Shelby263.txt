SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 FOR SHELBY COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY    August 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice will release  148 084 to the Shelby County District Attorney for a prosecutor dedicated to crimes against children cases  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   Crimes against children are some of the most disturbing cases facing our judicial system today  said Senator Shelby  We have a responsibility to protect children  our most vulnerable citizens  from these crimes  A full time prosecutor for crimes against children will allow Shelby County to provide the focus necessary to prosecute these heinous crimes and protect our children  With the funding provided  the Shelby County District Attorneys office will employ specialized prosecution efforts to target crimes against children  The District Attorneys office will also be able to utilize a more effective and streamlined case management system  thereby more effectively prosecuting
