SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR ALABAMA DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION            June 14  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  226 710 to the Alabama Department of Transportation  ALDOT  for the City of Gulf Shores   I am pleased to announce  226 710 for Gulf Shores  said Shelby  This funding will allow the city to purchase five vehicles  which will be used for transportation of both senior citizens and youth in the area to and from local programs   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding through the federal appropriations process                                                           June 2006 News Releases                      Year
