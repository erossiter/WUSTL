SHELBY ANNOUNCES  64 500 FOR GREEN  S CROSSROADS VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT                       February 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office has awarded a grant of  48 375 for the Greens Crossroads Volunteer Fire Department in Louisville  In addition  USDA approved a loan of  16 125 for the Volunteer Fire Department   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA has released these funds to the Greens Crossroads Volunteer Fire Department for their new fire station  It is critical that our first responders have the tools to face an emergency quickly and effectively should one
