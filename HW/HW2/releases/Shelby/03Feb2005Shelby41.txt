SHELBY ANNOUNCES  4 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM AIRPORT     February 7  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Transportation  Treasury  and General Government  announced today that the Birmingham Airport Authority will receive  4 million from the Department of Transportation for improvements at the Birmingham International Airport  The funding will be used to acquire land for noise compatibility improvements   Sen  Shelby said  I am pleased to announce these funds for the Birmingham Airport Authority  It is an important priority for me that Alabama s airports are given the needed resources so they may remain safe and convenient for the passengers and pilots who use them    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                         February 2005 News
