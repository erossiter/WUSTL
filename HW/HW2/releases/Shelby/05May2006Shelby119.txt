SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  40 000 FOR FOLSOM FIELD IN CULLMAN     May 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  42 750 for Folsom Field   I am pleased to announce  42 750 to Cullman for Folsom Field  said Senator Shelby  This funding will be used to expand and improve the airport  These improvements are essential to meet the increasing demand at the airport  will enhance the airports ability to meet the growing needs of the community  and encourage economic development   Folsom Fields clientele has outgrown the runways and approaches currently used at the airport  The funding will allow the airport to prepare a study for runway length justification and an approach procedure upgrade                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
