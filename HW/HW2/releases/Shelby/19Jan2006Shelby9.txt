      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY ANNOUNCES  363 750 FOR UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM          January 20  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services has released  363 750 for the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB   The funding comes from the National Heart  Lung  and Blood Institute and will be used for heart and vascular disease research   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to UAB  These funds will allow UAB to continue its important research on heart disease  the number one killer of Americans  It is my hope that their research will lead to more effective treatment heart disease  and ultimately the elimination of this deadly disease                                                          January 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                               Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
