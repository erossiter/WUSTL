Shelby Announces Over  43 000 for Burnsville Volunteer Fire Department    November 17  2006  Washington  D C      Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  43 329 to the Burnsville Volunteer Fire Department in Selma  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   The Burnsville Volunteer Fire Department will use this funding for operations and safety  said Senator Shelby  First responders are on the front lines of our local communities everyday  working to keep our citizens safe  We must provide them with the funding necessary to protect themselves from the dangers inherent in their jobs  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide
