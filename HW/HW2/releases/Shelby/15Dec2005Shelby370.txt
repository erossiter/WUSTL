SHELBY ANNOUNCES  44 270 FOR THE FACKLER VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN JACKSON COUNTY     December 15  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Fackler Volunteer Fire Department in Jackson County will receive  44 270 in federal funds for operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities              These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                  This is great news for the Fackler Volunteer Fire Department  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said                                                           December 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
