SHELBY ANNOUNCES  300 000 FOR CAMDEN MUNICIPAL AIRPORT                      February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  300 000 to the City of Camden for the Municipal Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  300 000 for the Camden Municipal Airport  The funding will go towards the purchase of new land for approaches  improvement of airport drainage  removal of obstructions from the landing strip  and to update the Airport Master Plan  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructure so that we can ensure the safest and most efficient air travel possible for Alabamians
