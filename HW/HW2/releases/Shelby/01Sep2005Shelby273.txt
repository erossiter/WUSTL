SHELBY STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SUPREME COURT CHIEF JUSTICE WILLIAM REHNQUIST     September 4  2005   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today issued the following statement regarding the passing of Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist      I was saddened to learn of the death of Chief Justice William Rehnquist   His passing is an enormous loss for the High Court and for the nation      Chief Justice Rehnquist was a brilliant jurist who faithfully interpreted the Constitution throughout his 33 year tenure on the Court   Despite his ailing health over the last several months  he remained a strong and impartial voice on the Court   Chief Justice Rehnquist leaves behind a legacy as one of the most influential chief justices in our nations history   His leadership and judgment will be missed        30                                                          September 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
