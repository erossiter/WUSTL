SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 000 000 FOR PROJECTS AT THE UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM             June 27  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services will award a total of  1 043 479 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for disease researching programs   This funding is essential to ensuring that UAB maintains many cutting edge research efforts on diseases that not just affect Alabamians but all Americans  said Shelby  I am pleased to see UAB once again at the forefront of emerging medical research   Programs Funded   Allergy  Immunology  and Transplantation Research  funded by the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases   352 375   Diabetes  Endocrinology  and Metabolic Research  funded by the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases   269 175   Blood Diseases and Resources Research  funded by the
