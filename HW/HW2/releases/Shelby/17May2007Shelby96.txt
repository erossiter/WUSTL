Shelby Announces Over  3 Million for Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority     May 22  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation will release  3 1 million to the Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority  This funding will be used to purchase snow removal equipment  expand the terminal building  rehabilitate the runway apron and taxiway  and enhance security  Due to the Base Realignment and Closure Commission decision to relocate thousands of jobs to the Redstone Arsenal  North Alabama will grow exponentially in the coming years  said Shelby  These improvements to the airport will allow Huntsville to meet the needs of the increased population traveling to  from  and through the State  Efficient transportation systems are critical to attracting new citizens  tourists  and businesses
