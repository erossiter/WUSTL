      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    Shelby Announces  412 500 for New Health Center in Cleburne   August 27  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala   today announced that the U S  Department of Health and Human Services Health Resources and Services Administration will release  412 500 to Quality Of Life Health Services to establish a permanent health care site in Cleburne County   This announcement is great news for Cleburne County  said Shelby  Unfortunately  many Alabamians residing in rural areas do not have access to quality health care  This funding will allow a one stop center that will help to ensure that residents in Cleburne County have convenient access to a broad range of health care services   This grant is awarded pursuant to President Bushs High Poverty County Initiative which seeks to increase access to primary health care services in 1 200 communities by supporting new or expanded health center sites                                                        August 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                               Current record                                                            Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
