SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 MILLION FOR HUNTSVILLE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT    February 17  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority will receive  1 294 034 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at the Huntsville International Airport    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Huntsville International Airport   These funds will be used to construct a taxiway and enhance security at the airport   I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts     This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP         30                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year
