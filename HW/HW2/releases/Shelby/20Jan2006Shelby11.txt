SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  8 MILLION FOR KATRINA RELIEF                   January 24  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  will release  8 575 826 25 to the Alabama State Port Authority to rebuild Gaillard Islands berm   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to the Alabama State Port Authority for Gaillard Island  It is important that we not let Hurricane Katrina fade from our memories  but continue the rebuilding efforts until our Gulf Coast is as it was   Gaillard Islands levy and berm system were eroded and destroyed by Hurricane Katrina  The island was built to store dredge spoils from Mobile Bay and provide a protected wildlife refuge area for brown pelicans                                                          January 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
