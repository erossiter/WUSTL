SHELBY ANNOUNCES  195 000 GRANT FOR TOWN OF RUTLEDGE     February 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office has awarded a  119 000 grant for the Town of Rutledge  In addition  USDA has approved a  76 000 loan for the Town of Rutledge   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA awarded these funds to the Town of Rutledge to purchase a new pumper tank truck  It is critical that our communities have the strong infrastructure necessary to support residents and businesses                                                           February 2006 News Releases                      Year
