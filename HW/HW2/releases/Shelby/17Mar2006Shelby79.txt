SHELBY ANNOUNCES  30 858 FOR FRIENDSHIP VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN LEE COUNTY      March 22  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Friendship Volunteer Fire Department in Smith Station will receive  30 858 in federal funds to assist with fire prevention   Fire prevention education is an invaluable tool for our nations communities  and I pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through with its commitment to the safety of our citizens  Sen  Shelby said   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide
