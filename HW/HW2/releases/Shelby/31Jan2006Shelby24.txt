SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 FOR ALABAMA        February 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development program will release  143 165 for the North Central Alabama Regional Council of Governments  NARCOG    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that these funds are being released to North Central Alabama Regional Council of Governments  It is critical that we support small and new businesses to further the growth of our states economy   The funding will enable NARCOG to further their revolving loan program to invest in small and emerging businesses in Cullman  Lawrence  and Morgan counties  The project is expected to create approximately 70 new jobs  Senator Shelby secured these funds through the annual appropriations process                                                           February 2006 News Releases
