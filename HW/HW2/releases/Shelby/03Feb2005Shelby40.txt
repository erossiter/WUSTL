SHELBY ANNOUNCES  2 5 MILLION FOR MOBILE AIRPORT    February 7  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Appropriations Subcommittee on Transportation  Treasury  and General Government  announced today that the Mobile Airport Authority will receive  2 5 million from the Department of Transportation  These funds will be used for a number of projects at the Mobile Regional Airport including service road expansion  security enhancements  terminal building improvements  taxiway rehabilitation and paving and lighting improvements   Sen  Shelby said  I am pleased to announce these funds for the Mobile Airport Authority  It is an important priority for me that Alabama s airports are given the needed resources so they may remain safe and convenient for the passengers and pilots who use them    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement
