SHELBY ANNOUNCES  200 000 FOR TALLADEGA MUNICIPAL AIRPORT     March 24  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  200 000 to the Talladega Municipal Airport for taxiway extension   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  200 000 for Talladega Municipal Airport  This funding will allow the airport to extend the parallel taxiway to the end of the runway  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians   The extension of the taxiway is needed to bring the airport up to code  With the extension of the runway  they will now be able to install an Information Landing System  ILS   which allows pilots to execute a more precise landing                                                           March 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
