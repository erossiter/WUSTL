SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 TO MORGAN AND LIMESTONE COUNTIES FOR PRYOR FIELD REGIONAL AIRPORT     March 24  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  150 000 to Morgan and Limestone counties for Pryor Field Regional Airport in Decatur  The funding will be used to improve the runway safety area   I am pleased to announce  150 000 to Morgan and Limestone counties for Pryor Field  said Shelby  This funding will improve safety for pilots  passengers  and road travelers near the airport  I believe it is our responsibility to support our airports and ensure the safest and most efficient travel for Alabamians   Construction will relocate County Road 45 outside of the runway safety area  The improvement will allow the airport to meet the design criteria                                                           March 2006 News Releases
