SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  200 000 FOR ALABAMA AREA AGENCIES ON AGING     May 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  198 000 to the Alabama Department of Transportation for senior the Alabama Area Agencies on Aging   I am pleased to announce nearly  200 000 for important transportation services for senior centers throughout Alabama  said Shelby  This funding will be used to purchase seven vehicles for senior centers in our state  These critical resources will allow the Alabama Area Agencies on Aging to continue providing much needed transportation options for Alabamas senior citizens   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding
