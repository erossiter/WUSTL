SHELBY ANNOUNCES MILLIONS FOR   ALABAMA EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT     February 3  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Security Preparedness Directorate has released  2 960 389 for the state of Alabama  This funding is provided through the Departments Federal Emergency Management Performance Grant Program   I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to Alabama  It is critical that our state and local governments have the tools necessary to face emergencies quickly and effectively when they occur  Senator Shelby said   The funding will support state and local emergency management and will encourage the improvement of mitigation  preparedness  response  and recovery capabilities for all hazards                                                          February 2006 News Releases                      Year
