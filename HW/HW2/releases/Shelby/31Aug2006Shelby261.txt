SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  1 MILLION FOR MENTORING IN MOBILE    August 31  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice will release  987 228 to Team Focus in Mobile for mentoring and leadership camps  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process     A positive role model is a critical component to developing good leadership skills  said Senator Shelby  Team Focus will provide boys with mentors  from whom they can learn important skills and good character traits that can be utilized throughout their lifetime  In fact  80 percent of young men who participate in mentoring programs increase their leadership skills and 90 percent improve grade point averages    With this funding  boys will participate in both mentoring and leadership camps  where they hear lectures  participate in sports  learn job interview techniques and parenting skills  and are exposed to cultural activities                                                          August 2006 News Releases
