SHELBY ANNOUNCES  44 875 FOR TOWN OF HAYNEVILLE        March 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office has awarded the Town of Hayneville a  44 875 grant   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA has awarded these funds to the Town of Hayneville to purchase equipment that will assist in street maintenance and water and sewer line repairs  I believe it is critical that we support our communities infrastructure so that they can effectively support residents and businesses                                                            March 2006 News Releases                      Year
