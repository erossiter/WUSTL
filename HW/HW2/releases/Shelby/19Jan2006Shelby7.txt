SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 FOR AVIAN FLU RESEARCH AT AUBURN UNIVERSITY     January 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Agriculture will grant  480 834 to Auburn University for a project to protect commercial chickens from the avian influenza virus   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to Auburn University  We have become more aware of the risks involved with the Avian Flu over the last several months  and it is my hope that this project will aid in reducing flu incidents before it adversely affects Americans health or our poultry industry   Avian influenza outbreaks have increased significantly since 1997  This project will work to find a genetic predisposition to the influenza virus in chickens and has the potential to greatly reduce the risk of avian influenza outbreaks in the United States and the world  Senator Shelby secured these funds through the annual federal appropriations process                                                           January 2006 News Releases                      Year
