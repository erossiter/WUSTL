SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  63 000 FOR COOSADA VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN ELMORE COUNTY      February 23  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Coosada Volunteer Fire Department in Elmore County will receive  63 315 federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    This is great news for the Coosada Volunteer Fire Department  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said        30                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
