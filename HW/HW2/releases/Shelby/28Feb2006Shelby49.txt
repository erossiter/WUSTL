SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 8 MILLION FOR HUNTSVILLE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT                                   February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  3 824 325 to the Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority for the Huntsville International Carl T  Jones Field   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  3 8 million for the Huntsville International   Carl T  Jones Field  The expansion of the terminal and baggage claim will help the airport more efficiently accommodate passengers  This funding will also allow for necessary improvements to pavement conditions to enhance the safety of aircraft operations  These improvements will help to ensure the Airport can meet the transportation needs of business and industry throughout the Tennessee Valley Region
