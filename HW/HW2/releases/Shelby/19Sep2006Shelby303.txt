SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  290 000 TO POARCH BAND OF CREEK INDIANS    September 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  290 170 to the Poarch Band of Creek Indians for their Youth Prevention Program   The Poarch Band of Creek Indians Youth Prevention Program will allow youth to receive preventative programs directly tailored to their needs within the tribal community  said Senator Shelby  A strong sense of family is reinforced by the program and is one of the best deterrents against delinquent behavior in youth  This will help to arm youth with the skills and values they need to grow into responsible adults  The Poarch Band of Creek Indians will use the funding provided to target 660 youth within the Tribal Service Area with their Poarch Band of Creek Indians Youth Prevention Program  The program works to reduce juvenile delinquency and enhance prevention services for tribal youth                                                          September 2006 News Releases                      Year
