Shelby Announces  48 518 for Alabama Criminal Justice Information Center    May 14  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   ranking member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the U S  Department of Justice released  48 518 to Alabamas Criminal Justice Information Center to continue upgrades on the states criminal justice data system   The funding will be used at Alabamas Statistical Analysis Center  which provides annual statistical reports to state and local officials and the Federal Bureau of Investigation that improve the states ability to address criminal justice problems   This type of statistical analysis allows Alabamas lawmakers to better understand the way changes in policy affect the criminal justice system  said Shelby  As we work to address criminal justice problems in the state  it is important that we always examine the results and consequences of new laws or policies
