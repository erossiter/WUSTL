SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  300 000 FOR AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM IN ORANGE BEACH    August 31  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice will release  296 169 to the City of Orange Beach for the After School Teen Program     After school programs provide students with critical alternatives to crime and mischief  said Senator Shelby  In many cases  a positive distraction is all students need to avoid a life of criminal behavior  By pairing this entertaining program with tutoring opportunities  the City of Orange Beach is also ensuring that participating students will also meet their academic potential    The After School Teen Program  targeted to 12 19 year olds  will provide age specific recreation opportunities  tutoring  and mentoring to underserved youth  By participating in these safe  entertaining  and engaging activities at the After School Teen Program  students will have less time to be steered into risky behavior  Students who are at risk for failing at school or dropping out will be provided with tutors
