SHELBY ANNOUNCES FEMA MEETING FOR DISASTER CONTRACTORS IN MONTGOMERY       October 3  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  will host a meeting for disaster contractors in Montgomery   The meeting will explain the types of disaster work opportunities that may be available following Hurricane Katrina     Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that FEMA is hosting this important meeting for interested contractors   Following Hurricane Katrina  there have been many contractors interested in assisting in the recovery efforts  and this meeting will provide necessary information to learn the appropriate application process for the recovery efforts   I hope that those who are interested take advantage of this opportunity       The meeting will be held at the Frazer Memorial United Methodist Church  6000 Atlanta Highway in Montgomery on Thursday  October 6 at 1 00 p m   Due to limited seating  interested contractors are requested to confirm their attendance by calling 334 244 5917  Monday Friday from 8 a m  to 5 p m  CT         At the session  FEMA contract specialists will provide a basic disaster process overview  outline bidding procedures and answer audience questions             30                                                          October 2005 News Releases
