Shelby Announces  4 Million for Dallas County    May 10  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development has approved a grant of  1 4 million and a loan of  2 6 million to improve the south Dallas County water system   It is critical that Alabamas local communities have a strong infrastructure in place to support area businesses  said Shelby  Improvements and upgrades to the Dallas County water system are essential for local businesses as they expand  grow and create more jobs  In addition  strengthening the infrastructure will make south Dallas County more attractive to other businesses looking for new locations  South Dallas County will improve its water system by constructing a water storage tank  water facility and modifying its existing wells                                                          May 2007 News
