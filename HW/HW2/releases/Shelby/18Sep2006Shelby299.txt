SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  246 000 TO GULF SHORES    September 19  2006  Washington  D C         U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  246 807 to the City of Gulf Shores for essential technology upgrades for the police department  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   Our communities are the safest when police officers are on patrol and working in the field  said Senator Shelby  This new equipment will reduce the amount of time officers spend in the office by allowing them to complete essential paperwork from their patrol cars  These enhancements will allow officers to spend the majority of their time working to ensure the safety of the citizens of Gulf Shores  The funding provided will be used for the Gulf Shores Police Department to purchase laptop computers  mobile wireless capability  and reports software  This new equipment will allow police officers to complete reports while on patrol  while also increasing the availability and visibility of local law
