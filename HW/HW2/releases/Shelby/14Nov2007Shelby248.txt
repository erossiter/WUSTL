Shelby Announces  10 529 for Kellyton Volunteer Fire and Rescue   November 16  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  10 529 to the Kellyton Volunteer Fire and Rescue in Coosa County  The funding is provided through the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding will help the Kellyton Volunteer Fire and Rescue fund its operations and safety programs  said Shelby  Firefighters are our nations true first responders  and as such must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  The Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency awarded these funds as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  490 million in direct federal assistance to fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical service organizations nationwide this year
