SHELBY ANNOUNCES  160 000 FOR ALABAMA COALITION AGAINST DOMESTIC VIOLENCE         February 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Commerce  Justice  and Science Appropriations Subcommittee  today announced that  160 000 will be awarded from the U S  Department of Justice to the Alabama Coalition Against Domestic Violence  ACADV    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  160 000 for this important program on sexual assault and domestic violence  These funds will help enhance services for abuse victims and ensure those who commit these crimes are held accountable   The ACADV is a network of domestic violence shelters  organizations  and allied professional all advocating for the safety and rights of battered and sexually abused women and their children  The program will aid sexual assault and domestic violence victims through its support of rape crisis centers and battered womens shelters  In addition  ACADV will also focus on crimes committed in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina                                                            February 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
