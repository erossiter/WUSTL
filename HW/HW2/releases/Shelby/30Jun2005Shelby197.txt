SHELBY STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF JUSTICE SANDRA DAY O  CONNOR       July 1  2005   WASHINGTON  DC  U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today made the following statement regarding the retirement of Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day OConnor    I want to thank Justice OConnor for her twenty four years of service on the United States Supreme Court   Justice OConnor has served the Court with fairness and impartiality  and she has marked her place in history as the first woman to serve on the Supreme Court       There is no question that filling this vacancy is extremely important for the future of the Judiciary and the future of our nation   I am hopeful that as we move forward with the nomination process  the Senate will operate in a fair manner  and that the nominee  whomever he or she may be  ultimately receives an up or down vote          30                                                          July 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
