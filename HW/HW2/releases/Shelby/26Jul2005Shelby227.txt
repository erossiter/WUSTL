SHELBY APPLAUDS DISCOVERY  S RETURN TO FLIGHT     July 26  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriations Subcommittee with jurisdiction over the National Aeronautics and Space Administration  NASA   today applauded the Space Shuttle Discoverys successful return to flight   Senator Shelby made the following statement upon the shuttles lift off      I am extremely pleased that NASA has returned to flight with the successful launch of the Space Shuttle Discovery   After more than two years of being grounded  NASA continues to focus on the promise of space exploration and todays successful launch is a testament to that vision   I want to applaud the hard work of all those at NASA who have made this possible  and specifically the men and women at Marshall Space Flight Center for playing an integral role in helping NASA return to flight            30
