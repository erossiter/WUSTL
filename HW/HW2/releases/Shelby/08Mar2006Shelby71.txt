SHELBY ANNOUNCES  97 401 FOR MUSCLE SHOALS FIRE RESCUE         March 8  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Muscle Shoals Fire Rescue in Colbert County will receive federal funds for fire prevention  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year  This is great news for the Muscle Shoals Fire Rescue  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said                                                           March 2006 News
