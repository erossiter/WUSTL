SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  26 000 FOR CRIME CONTROL AND PREVENTION PROGRAMS IN ESCAMBIA COUNTY     August 25  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that U S  Department of Justice will be releasing  26 409 to Escambia County for The Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG                      JAG allows states  tribes  and local governments to support a broad range of activities to prevent and control crime based on the needs of the communities   These funds can be used for technical assistance  training  equipment and information systems for criminal justice for any one or more of the following purpose areas                Law Enforcement Programs               Prosecution and Court Programs             Prevention and Education Programs                                                               Corrections and Community Corrections Programs             Drug Treatment Programs             Planning  Evaluation and Technology Improvements Programs                        Escambia County will be purchasing a radio repeater for the Escambia County Sheriffs Department to improve radio reception and transmission   The City of Atmore and the City of Brewton will each use these funds to purchase a server and other office equipment to improve the storage and maintenance of arrest records  incident records and accident reports                      Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  26 000 for law enforcement throughout Escambia County   These funds will help improve communication among law enforcement officers and equip the various police departments with the most up to date technology  ensuring the safety and protection of our officers and our citizens
