Shelby Announces More Than  871 000 for Birmingham Fire and Rescue Service    December 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  871 084 to Birmingham Fire and Rescue Service  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   It is essential that Alabamas first responders have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  Birmingham Fire and Rescue will use this funding for operations and safety  This grant is further evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year
