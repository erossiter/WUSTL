SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  6 1 MILLION FOR ALABAMA CRIME VICTIMS FUND     May 3  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  6 108 000 to the Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs for the Crime Victims Fund   I am pleased to announce more than  6 000 000 to the crime victims fund  said Shelby  These funds will be used to enhance crime victims services in Alabama  including counseling  shelter  medical and legal advice  as well as other services for victims and their families  It is important that we provide the necessary tools to victims and their loved ones to enable them to recover after a crime has been committed against them   This award provides funds from the U S  Office for Victims of Crime to enhance crime victim services within the state of Alabama  The Victims of Crime Act  VOCA  assistance funds are competitively awarded by the state of Alabama to local  community based  organizations that provide direct services to crime victims
