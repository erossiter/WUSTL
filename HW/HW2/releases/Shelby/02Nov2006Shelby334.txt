Shelby Announces Over  38 000 for New Site Volunteer Fire and Ambulance Service    November 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  38 486 to the New Site Volunteer Fire and Ambulance Service  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program and will be used for operations and safety   We have a responsibility to ensure that our first responders have the most up to date equipment available  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters and paramedics are in the field everyday protecting our citizens  It is critical that our nation follow through on its commitment to ensure the safety of our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         November 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
