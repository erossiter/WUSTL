SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  240 000 TO MARSHALL COUNTY VISITATION PROGRAM    September 7  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  246 530 to the Marshall County Commission for the Marshall County Visitation Program  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process     Many times  families who have experienced victimization need a secure location to visit and rebuild their relationship  said Senator Shelby  The Marshall County Visitation Program will provide victimized or abused adults and children with a safe haven for families as they work to regain trusting relationships  A strong family bond gives children the nurturing environment they need during their formative years to become contributing members of society     The Marshall County Visitation Program works to establish the best policies and procedures for working with victims of domestic violence  child abuse and neglect  sexual assault in adults and children  and stalking  The program will use the funding provided to purchase equipment  personnel costs  supplies  training  and travel                                                         September 2006 News Releases
