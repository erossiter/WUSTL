Shelby Announces  233 225 for County 17 Fire   Rescue    November 13  2006 U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  233 225 to the County 17 Fire  Rescue  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding will help the County 17 Fire and Rescue fund its operations and safety programs  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters are a key component to our nations first response efforts  and therefore they must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide
