SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  16 000 FOR THE WEST SHELBY FIRE AND RESCUE DISTRICT IN SHELBY COUNTY     February 9  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the West Shelby Fire and Rescue District in Montevallo  AL will receive  16 344 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to the West Shelby Fire and Rescue District  It is critical that our federal  state and local governments work together to ensure that our first responders have the necessary tools to face an emergency quickly and effectively should one occur  Sen  Shelby said                                                        February 2005 News Releases
