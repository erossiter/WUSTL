Shelby Announces  250 000 for Colbert County   December 13  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  will award  250 000 to the Colbert County Commission for the Bart Riverfront Industrial Park Wastewater System  It is important that local communities have the infrastructure necessary to encourage economic growth and attract new businesses to Alabama  said Shelby  The wastewater system will serve the needs of the new National Steel Car manufacturing plant  which is expected to bring 1 800 new high wage jobs to the area   In addition to ARC funds  the total expected project cost of  2 085 050 includes  1 418 040 from the State of Alabama Community Development Block Grant program and  417 010 from the Colbert County Commission                                                        December 2007 News Releases                      Year
