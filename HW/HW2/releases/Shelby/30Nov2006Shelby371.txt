Shelby Announces  400 000 for Sixteenth Street Baptist Church in Birmingham    December 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate AppropriationsU S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of the Interiors National Park Service will release  400 000 to the Sixteenth Street Baptist Church in Birmingham   The Sixteenth Street Baptist Church has an important place in the history of the Civil Rights Movement  said Senator Shelby  Maintaining its integrity ensures that future generations will be able to look to it as an important symbol of the struggle to ensure equal rights for all  It is important that we make the investments necessary to preserve and protect our nations historic landmarks  I am proud that the National Park Service is maintaining the Sixteenth Street Baptist Church   The funding will allow the Sixteenth Street Baptist Church to replace the
