SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  18 000 FOR CITY OF DOTHAN AND HOUSTON COUNTY      May 5  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice has awarded  18 948 to the City of Dothan and Houston County for local law enforcement   I am pleased to announce  18 948 for the City of Dothan and Houston County law enforcement  said Senator Shelby  I believe we should make every effort to support our first responders  who risk their lives everyday for our safety  It is critical that they have the appropriate equipment before we send them into harms way   Both the City of Dothan and Houston County will purchase mobile data terminals and laptops  which will allow officers on the street to send and receive information electronically to complete their reports  Other information  such as city maps  city ordinances  and traffic codes  will also be available through this equipment   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs  In this case  the departments have agreed to share the funding                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year
