SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  65 000 FOR SCHOOL SECURITY PROGRAM IN MONTGOMERY    September 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  will award the Montgomery County Sheriffs Department  65 032  This funding will be provided by the Secure our Schools Grant Program   Our children are our most vulnerable and valuable citizens  and it is critical that we protect them  said Senator Shelby  We must ensure that our children feel safe in their schools  The technology and equipment purchased with this funding will allow Montgomery County schools to make certain that students have a safe and violence free learning environment  The Secure our Schools Grant Program helps schools establish and enhance safety efforts  The funding provided will help Montgomery County schools address a variety of existing and emerging problems related to school security by purchasing new equipment and technology                                                           September 2006 News
