SHELBY ANNOUNCES APPROVAL OF UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH ALABAMA  S UKRAINIAN BIRTH DEFECTS MONITORING PROGRAM   Senate Approves FY 2006 Foreign Operations Appropriations Bill Conference Report  November 10  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced Senate approval of the University of South Alabama s Ukrainian Birth Defects Monitoring Program  This program will now be eligible for funding from the United States Agency for International Development  USAID    The Foreign Operations Appropriations bill will now go to the Presidents desk  which he is expected to sign into law                  Senator Shelby said  I am very pleased that the Senate has again recognized the importance of this program and has shown continued support for the work of the Ukrainian Birth Defects Monitoring Program at the University of South Alabama  The program will continue its studies of environmental causes of birth defects in the Ukraine  while also working to implement a nationwide birth defects prevention program            30                                                              November 2005 News Releases
