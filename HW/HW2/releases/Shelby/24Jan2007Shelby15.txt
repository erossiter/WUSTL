Shelby Announces Over  33 000 for Orange Beach Public Transportation    January 25  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration will release  33 784 to the Alabama Department of Transportation  This funding will be used to purchase a new Senior Activity Center bus for the City of Orange Beach   The Orange Beach Senior Activity Center provides important classes and events for our Gulf Coast seniors  said Senator Shelby  The Activity Center offers seniors informative classes ranging from seminars on identity theft protection to nutritious cooking classes  This funding will help seniors get to and from these programs safely and conveniently  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2005 appropriations process                                                          January 2007 News
