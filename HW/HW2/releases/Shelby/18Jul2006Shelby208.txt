SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  100 000 FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN SOUTH ALABAMA     July 18  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice and Science  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerces Economic Development Administration  EDA  will release  92 638 to the South Alabama Regional Planning Commission for economic development in Mobile County  The Commission  which works with Mobile  Baldwin  and Escambia Counties  assists local governments in community development   Mobile County has made significant strides in attracting new international industries to the area  said Senator Shelby  Mobile Countys ports  tourism industry  and strong infrastructure make it a prime location for growth and a positive environment for doing business  As more emphasis is placed on expansion  I am certain the area will continue to strive to meet its economic potential   The Commission is a non partisan group utilized by local governments to gain expertise in planning  policymaking  coordination  advocacy  and technical assistance  as well as providing a nonbiased resource for problem solving  The Commission specializes in community development and works to gain federal funding for such projects                                                            July 2006 News Releases                      Year
