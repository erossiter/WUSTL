SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  1 MILLION FOR THE JEFFERSON COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION     June 5  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Education has awarded  993 888 to the Jefferson County Board of Education   I am pleased to announce nearly  1 000 000 for teacher training in Jefferson County  said Shelby  I believe that a strong knowledge of our nations history is an important part of a solid education  and I am pleased that many of Alabamas teachers are continuing a lifetime of education to better impart their knowledge to our students   The program will allow faculty from the Jefferson County School District to participate in summer field studies to further their knowledge of American history  including the American Revolution  historical sites in New York  Philadelphia  Gettysburg  and Washington  D C   and Alabamas role in the Civil Rights Movement  The funding is distributed through the Department of Educations Teaching American History Grants Program  which works to raise student achievement by improving teachers knowledge  understanding  and appreciation of traditional American
