SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  487 000 FOR HOME OWNERSHIP PROGRAMS IN FLORENCE    May 19  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the City of Florence will receive a Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  in the amount of  487 933    The CDBG program is administrated by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  to provide home ownership opportunities to all Americans   These grants allow state and local governments to develop urban communities by providing housing and economic opportunities for low and moderate income individuals     Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  487 000 for this important home ownership program in Florence   These funds will provide more low income families in the area with the opportunity to own a home and achieve a piece of the American dream        30                                                          May 2005 News Releases                      Year
