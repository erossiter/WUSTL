SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  99 000 FOR TWO FIRE DEPARTMENTS IN WASHINGTON COUNTY     Millry and Leroy Volunteer Fire Departments Will Receive Funding  January 26  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that two fire departments in Washington County will receive federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities      57 235   Millry Volunteer Fire Department  42 540   Leroy Volunteer Fire Department   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    This is great news for these Washington County volunteer fire departments  These grant provide critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said
