      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces Funding for Coffee County   County will be Reimbursed for 100  of Eligible Expenses Incurred during 48 hour Period after Tornado   March 30  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has agreed to reimburse Coffee County for 100 percent of eligible expenses incurred during the first 48 hours after the storms on March 1  2007  Coffee County  and in particular the City of Enterprise  was devastated by the tornados that ripped through Alabama causing more than  400 million dollars in damage  said Senator Shelby  In the aftermath of the storms  I visited Enterprise twice  and what I saw there was devastating  Lives were shattered and the county is struggling to rebuild  This federal funding will help the residents of Coffee County recover from this terrible disaster                                                          March 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record                                                            Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
