Shelby Announces  35 150 for Pine Hill Volunteer Fire Department    February 8  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  35 150 to the Pine Hill Volunteer Fire Department in Wilcox County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Alabamas first responders must have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  The Pine Hill Volunteer Fire Department will use this funding for their operations and safety programs  This grant is evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year
