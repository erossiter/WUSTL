Shelby Announces  1 276 250 for Albertville Municipal Airport   June 20  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Commerce Departments Economic Development Administration  EDA  will invest  1 276 250 in the Albertville Municipal Airport for infrastructure improvements  The airports infrastructure is being enhanced and expanded in preparation for Aerospace Integration Corporations new Blackhawk helicopter maintenance facility that will be built at the airport  The 100 000 square foot plant is expected to bring 500 aerospace assembly jobs over the next three years to the Albertville Municipal Airport  This investment supports construction of infrastructure improvements to accommodate the new growth  generating  16 2 million in private investment   I am glad the City of Albertville is taking the steps necessary to prepare their infrastructure for the new helicopter facility  said Shelby  A strong infrastructure is one of the best ways to attract new business and bring jobs to a community  It is critical that the City has the tools in place to support this important new investment in North Alabama
