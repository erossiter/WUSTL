SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  500 000 FOR UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM                      June 22  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  495 000 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for the expansion of activities at the Center for Injury Sciences   I am pleased to announce this important funding for the Center for Injury Sciences at UAB  The Center has made significant contributions to the prevention and treatment of injury victims  specifically those associated with motor vehicle collisions  said Senator Shelby  Motor vehicle accidents are the leading cause of trauma incidents throughout the nation  and this project will allow the Center to continue their focus on research surrounding the prevention and treatment of these injuries   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding through the federal appropriations process
