SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  2 7 MILLION FOR BLACKWELL FIELD IN DALE COUNTY    August 31  2006  Washington  D C         U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  2 701 649 to Blackwell Field in the City of Ozark   With these improvements  Blackwell Field will experience growth due to an additional investment by BellAero  which has decided to expand their operations at the airport  said Senator Shelby  I am confident that other businesses will follow suit and utilize these improvements to the airport infrastructure to locate  grow  or expand to bring additional jobs to the Wiregrass area  BellAero has decided to expand their operations at the Blackwell Field and will utilize the improvements made with this funding  including the construction of a taxiway  complete with lights and signage and a runway extension with new pavement and lighting  The project will better equip the airport to meet the needs of BellAero and other local businesses by supporting a full range of general aircraft  bringing the airport up to meet current design standards  and improving the airports safety   In addition  BellAero will coordinate with this project to add a three phase approach for two 90 000 square foot production hangars  a 30 000 square feet flight operations hangar  new paint facility  new de fuel area  new wash rack  a new fire station  new parking apron  and an internal access road  BellAeros investments total approximately  35 million
