SHELBY APPLAUDS CONFIRMATION OF CONDOLEEZZA RICE AS SECRETARY OF STATE    January 26  2005  Washington  D C     By a vote of 85   13  Dr  Condoleezza Rice was confirmed by the United States Senate to serve as Secretary of State  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement upon her confirmation   I am pleased that Dr  Condoleezza Rice has been confirmed by the Senate to serve as our nations next Secretary of State  Her expertise on a broad range of national security issues and her extensive foreign policy experience will serve her well in this critically important post  As our country continues to face opportunity and challenges ahead on the international stage  I am confident that Dr  Rice will be a strong leader and extremely effective as America s chief diplomat
