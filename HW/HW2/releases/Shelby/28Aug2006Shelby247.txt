SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 FOR FAMILY VIOLENCE PREVENTION IN MONTGOMERY    August 29  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  349 889 for the Montgomery Area Family Violence Program   Family violence is a horrible crime  affecting  in many cases  come of our youngest citizens  said Senator Shelby  It is critical that we work to prevent these crimes through educating our communities and empowering victims with the ability to escape before they are abused or before abuse escalates further   The Montgomery Area Family Violence Program  which operates under the name of Family Sunshine Center  works to end family violence by offering immediate assistance in a crisis  providing advocacy and counseling to families  and promoting community awareness through education and training
