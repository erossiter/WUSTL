SHELBY COMMENDS SUPREME COURT NOMINEE     October 31  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement upon learning of President Bushs nominee to the United States Supreme Court                  I am extremely pleased with President Bushs nomination of Judge Samuel Alito to the United States Supreme Court   Judge Alito has a long and distinguished career of public service  serving our country in a number of positions over the last twenty nine years  including his most recent tenure on the Third Circuit Court of Appeals                       As we move forward  I intend to carefully review his judicial philosophy and qualifications for this important position   I was pleased with the civility in which Judge Roberts  nomination was considered   I am hopeful that Judge Alito is afforded the same respect  and that the Senate will operate in a fair manner and give him an up or down vote  Shelby added              30
