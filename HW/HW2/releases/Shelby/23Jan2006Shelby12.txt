SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  600 000 FOR AUBURN UNIVERSITY                    January 24  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Agriculture will release  690 000 for Auburn Universitys Department of Fisheries and Allied Aquacultures   Senator Shelby said  Auburn Universitys Fisheries and Allied Aquacultures Department is one of the best programs of its type in the world  and I am pleased that this funding will help them to continue their important work   The funding will support the catfish genome project conducted at Auburn University in cooperation with the USDAs Agriculture Research Service  The project seeks to provide researchers with a physical map of a catfishs genetic structure  When completed  the map will allow scientist and producers to better understand how disease and the environment can affect catfish                                                           January 2006
