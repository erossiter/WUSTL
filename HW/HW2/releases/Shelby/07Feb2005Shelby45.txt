SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 800 FOR THE CITY OF CORDOVA VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN WALKER COUNTY     February 9  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the City of Cordova Volunteer Fire Department in Walker County will receive  1 890 in federal funds to assist with fire prevention activities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to the City of Cordova Volunteer Fire Department  It is critical that our federal  state and local governments work together to ensure that our first responders have the necessary tools to face an emergency quickly and effectively should one occur  Sen  Shelby said
