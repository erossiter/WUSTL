SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  818 000 FOR THE ANDALUSIA OPP AIRPORT       June 23  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded  818 216 in Economic Development Administration  EDA  funds to the Andalusia Opp Airport and City of Andalusia  This funding will provide for the building of an aircraft hangar facility   I am pleased to announce these funds for the expansion of infrastructure at the Andalusia Opp Airport  said Senator Shelby  These improvements will enhance the airports ability to meet the growing needs of the community  allow for important business expansion and encourage economic development   This investment will allow for the expansion of operations at Airport Industrial Park  The project is expected to maintain 51 jobs and create an additional 350 jobs  while generating over  3 7 million in private investments    30
