SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 FOR DISEASE RESEARCH AT UAB    August 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Health and Human Services National Institute of Arthritis and Muscoskeletal and Skin Diseases has released  323 010 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB    UABs has time and again proven itself as one of the premier research institutions in our country  said Senator Shelby  Groundbreaking projects  such as this  will not only have a profound effect on the lives of the many patients suffering from immunologic diseases  but ensures UABs reputation as a leading authority on medical research  The provided funding will allow researchers at UABs Division of Clinical Immunology and Rheumatology to study cell receptors and their relationship to immune complexes in patients in different stages of illnesses  such as systemic lupus erythematosus  in an effort to find effective treatment  The project will also develop new ways to deliver vaccinations to patients                                                           August 2006 News Releases
