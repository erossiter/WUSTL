      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    Shelby Announces  500 000 for the University of South Alabama   August 21  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    ranking member of the Senate Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice and Science  today announced that the U S  Department Commerce will release  500 000 to the University of South Alabama  USA  for hurricane intensity and landfall research   The 2005 hurricane season reminded us that the Gulf Coast is vulnerable to destruction caused by hurricanes  said Shelby  As this years hurricane season continues  we find ourselves still looking for answers to the questions that those devastating storms left us with  I am hopeful that we will find these answers in the research conducted at USA  so that we can provide coastal communities with a better picture of what to expect from tropical events  These funds have been awarded by the U S  Department of Commerce National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA                                                            August 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                 Current record                                                                                          Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
