SHELBY ANNOUNCES  350 000 FOR ENFORCEMENT OF UNDERAGE DRINKING LAWS      May 19  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs will receive  350 000 from the U S  Department of Justice   These funds will be used for the Enforcing Underage Drinking Laws  EUDL  Program    The goal of this program is to support and enhance State efforts  in cooperation with local jurisdictions  to enforce laws prohibiting the sale of alcoholic beverages or the consumption of alcoholic beverages by those under the age of twenty one    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce that Alabama will receive these funds to enforce underage drinking laws   We must do everything we can to teach our children about the dangers of underage drinking  and these funds will help combat this problem        30                                                          May 2005 News Releases                      Year
