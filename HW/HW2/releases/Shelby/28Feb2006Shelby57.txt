SHELBY ANNOUNCES  500 000 FOR ST  CLAIR COUNTY AIRPORT IN PELL CITY      February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  500 000 to Pell City for the St  Clair County Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  500 000 for the St  Clair County Airport  This project will fund phase II of the expansion of the terminal apron  replacing a small and unsafe apron  We must continue to invest in our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient travel for Alabamians                                                             February 2006 News Releases                      Year
