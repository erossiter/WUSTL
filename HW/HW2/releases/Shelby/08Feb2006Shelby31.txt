SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  800 000 FOR DAUPHIN ISLAND                     February 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has released  833 740 for the Dauphin Island Water and Sewer Authority  The funding will restore water service to the west end of Dauphin Island  which was damaged by Hurricane Katrina   I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to Dauphin Island  We must continue to restore damaged infrastructure in hurricane affected areas so that communities can rebuild and support local businesses and residents  said Senator Shelby   The funding was awarded through FEMAs Public Assistance  PA  program and is 75 percent of the total project cost                                                          February 2006 News Releases                      Year
