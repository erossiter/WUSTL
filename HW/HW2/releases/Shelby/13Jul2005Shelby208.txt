SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  2 2 MILLION FOR HOME OWNERSHIP PROGRAMS IN HUNTSVILLE      July 15  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  will soon be releasing  2 260 501 for housing and community revitalization programs in Huntsville   This grant is part of HUDs commitment to provide home ownership opportunities for all Americans      The funds will be awarded as follows          1 476 774 for Community Development Block Grants   This allows state and local governments to develop urban communities by providing housing and economic opportunities for low and moderate income individuals   These funds may also be used for road construction  water and sewer projects  demolition  and other economic development activities           744 647 for the HOME Investment Partnership   This program helps expand affordable housing opportunities for low and very low income families by providing grants to state and local governments to fund housing programs to meet the needs and priorities of the local community           39 080 for the American Dream Downpayment Initiative   These funds will promote home ownership opportunities by providing necessary resources for downpayment and closing cost assistance to low income households      Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  2 2 million for these important home ownership programs in Huntsville   These grants will provide more low income families in the area with the opportunity to own a home and achieve a piece of the American dream                              30                                                          July 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
