Shelby Announces  1 4 Million for Intermodal Facility at the University of Alabama in Huntsville   July 24  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala   today announced that the U S  Department of Transportations  DOT  Federal Transit Administration will release  1 360 260 for the Intermodal Facility at the University of Alabama in Huntsville  UAH   UAH needs this facility to meet the needs of its expanding campus  said Shelby  This facility will provide both an important link to Huntsvilles public transportation system and reduce congestion on campus  This funding will ensure that the campus transportation infrastructure meets the needs of those living  working and attending classes at UAH  UAH will use the funding provided for design  engineering and construction of the Bus Transfer and Park n Ride facility  which will ease traffic through campus  The City of Huntsvilles transit system will use the facility as a stop on its line  and UAH will utilize the facility as a transition area for its on campus shuttle service  The facility will also house the campus police department  provide parking for campus police vehicles and serve as a collection point for parking
