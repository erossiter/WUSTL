      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  15 875 for Rockford Volunteer Fire Department    October 26  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  15 875 to the Rockford Volunteer Fire Department  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   The Rockford Volunteer Fire Department will use this funding for operations and safety  said Senator Shelby  First responders are on the front lines of our local communities everyday  working to keep our citizens safe  We must provide them with the funding necessary to protect themselves from the dangers inherent in their jobs  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         October 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                     Current record                                                                                          Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
