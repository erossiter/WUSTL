SHELBY ANNOUNCES  147 000 GRANT FOR CITY OF HARTFORD                February 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office has awarded the City of Hartford a grant of  65 000  In addition  USDA has approved a loan of  82 000 to the City of Hartford   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA has awarded these funds to the City of Hartford for a new roof for both the police department and the community center  I believe it is critical that our communities have the infrastructure necessary to support residents and businesses                                                           February 2006 News
