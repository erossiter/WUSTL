SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  87 000 FOR MOODY FIRE AND RESCUE IN ST  CLAIR COUNTY     February 9  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that Moody Fire and Rescue in Moody  AL will receive  87 138 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    This is great news for Moody Fire and Rescue  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said        30
