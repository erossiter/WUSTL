SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 FOR CITY OF GUIN       June 19  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Highway Administration  FHWA  has awarded  129 195 for the City of Guin Industrial Park  Funding will be used for the planning  design  and construction of a main entrance on existing undeveloped property owned by the City of Guin   I am pleased that the Department of Transportation has released  129 195 for the City of Guin Industrial Park  said Senator Shelby  This entrance will improve the Industrial Commercial Park  and it is my hope that this park will attract new businesses and jobs to the City of Guin and Marion County   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding through the federal appropriations process                                                           June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
