SHELBY ANNOUNCES CONFERENCE APPROVAL OF REDSTONE ARSENAL PROJECT       December 19  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced conference committee approval of  20 million for the Software Engineering Directorate  SED  at Redstone Arsenal in the fiscal year 2006 Department of Defense  DoD  Authorization Bill   The bill passed the House by a vote of 374   41   The Senate will now consider the bill  after which it will go to the Presidents desk for his signature      Senator Shelby made the following remarks about the project  I am pleased that the conference committee recognized the value of the SED building for our Armed Forces   This two phase project will provide support facilities for the Armys expanding reliance on software automation and rapid data transfer across the full spectrum of materiel systems   As the number and complexity of unique software systems developed and supported at Redstone Arsenal continues to increase  so does the requirement for adequate facilities to support them        I believe this project will allow our warfighters to be more versatile by enabling them to trouble shoot and operate any comparable weapon system with a minimum of training  SED is the development  analysis  training  and proving ground for software in accomplishing this mission and is crucial in meeting the requirements associated with a ready force  Senator Shelby concluded                                                           December 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
