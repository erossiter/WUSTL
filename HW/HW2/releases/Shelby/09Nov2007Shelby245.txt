Shelby Announces Over  54 000 for Wicksburg Volunteer Fire Department   November 9  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  54 150 to the Wicksburg Volunteer Fire Department in Newton  Alabama  The funding is provided through the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Firefighters are a key component of our nations first response efforts  and therefore they must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  said Shelby  These funds will help the Wicksburg Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  The Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency awarded these funds as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  490 million in direct federal assistance to fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical service organizations nationwide this year                                                         November 2007
