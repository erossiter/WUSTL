Shelby Announces Over  123 500 for Thach Volunteer Fire Department    November 13  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  123 500 to the Thach Volunteer Fire Department  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding will help the Thach Volunteer Fire Department purchase a new vehicle  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters are an integral component of our nations first response efforts  and therefore we must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         November 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
