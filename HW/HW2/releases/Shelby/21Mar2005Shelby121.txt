SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  2 4 MILLION FOR INTELLIGENT TRANSPORTATION SYSTEM ON U S  280 IN JEFFERSON COUNTY     March 21  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  2 423 571 will be released from the Department of Transportation for the Intelligent Transportation System on U S  280 in Jefferson County   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the DOT will soon be releasing these funds for the Intelligent Transportation System  ITS  on US 280 in Jefferson County  These funds will be used to upgrade traffic monitoring systems  including traffic light improvements and installation of additional camera systems to monitor high traffic areas particularly during emergency situations  This project is of critical importance for this very congested artery in Jefferson County    Senator Shelby secured these funds through the federal appropriations process
