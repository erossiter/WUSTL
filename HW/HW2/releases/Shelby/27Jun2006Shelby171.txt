SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  2 4 MILLION FOR TUSKEGEE UNIVERSITY                            June 27  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services will award  2 420 926 to Tuskegee University for the Centers of Excellence Program   This funding will allow Tuskegee to continue to expose students to opportunities in the health care field who might not have otherwise considered the possibility  said Shelby  Through this program  Tuskegee will be able to launch interested students on a path with careers that will not only be rewarding to the students  but also greatly beneficial to patients treated by well educated health care providers   The Centers of Excellence  COE  Program aims provide more training for under represented minority students in health professions through its programs at Historically Black Colleges and Universities  and Hispanic and American Indian COEs at health professions schools that train a significant number of minority students                                                           June 2006 News Releases
