SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 FOR UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA IN HUNTSVILLE    August 30  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration  FTA  will release  482 130 to the University of Alabama in Huntsville  UAH  for Transportation Infrastructure and Logistics research and development   As the volume of freight traffic within the United States and Alabama increases  it places significant pressure on our transportation infrastructure  said Senator Shelby  Increased traffic congestion surrounding our airports  ports  and truck rail transfer terminals and the additional trucks on our highways make road traffic more difficult for travelers  I am confident that UAHs transportation strategies will ease the difficulties on todays roadways and improve our states transportation system to make it safer and increase efficiency   Over the last 30 years  the volume of freight moved within the United States has nearly doubled the rate of population increase  more than 11 billion tons of freight moves annually over the nations transportation system  With the funding provided  the University of Alabama in Huntsville will develop effective freight forecasting methodologies to improve transportation networks in Alabama
