Shelby Announces Over  226 000 for U S  Space and Rocket Center   March 15  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration will release  226 710 to the U S  Space and Rocket Center  USSRC   The funding will improve transportation between the USSRC and the City of Huntsvilles Botanical Garden   Both the Space and Rocket Center and the City of Huntsvilles Botanical Gardens are important components of North Alabamas tourism industry and economy  said Senator Shelby  This funding will provide a tramway connecting the Center and the Botanical Gardens  As North Alabama continues to grow  we must ensure that the area is prepared for the increasing number of residents and visitors to the region  The tramway will provide visitors access to both attractions safely and easily while reducing traffic congestion in the area  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process                                                          March 2007 News Releases                      Year
