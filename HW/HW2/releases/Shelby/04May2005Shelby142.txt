SHELBY  MOBILE ON SHORT LIST   Shelby applauds EADS North America selection of Mobile as possible site  May 5  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today applauded an announcement by EADS North America that the Mobile Airport is one of four possible locations for a future aerial refueling tanker industrial facility    Senator Shelby said  I am extremely pleased that EADS North America has selected the Mobile Airport as one of its final possible sites for their tanker industrial facility   Mobile would be an excellent site for this project  and I believe that as the selection process continues  EADS North America will see the great benefit that the Mobile Airport can offer them     The Mobile Airport has been issued a Request for Proposal by EADS North America and has three weeks to respond with additional information     In addition to the Mobile Airport  the following other sites have been selected as finalists   Melbourne International Airport  Melbourne  Florida   Stennis International Airport  Kiln  Mississippi  and  Charleston International Airport  North Charleston  South Carolina     EADS North America is expected to complete the selection process by July 2005        30                                                          May 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
