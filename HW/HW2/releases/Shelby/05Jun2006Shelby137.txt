SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  1 MILLION FOR MONTGOMERY PUBLIC SCHOOLS      June 5  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Education has awarded  970 655 to Montgomery Public Schools   I am pleased to announce nearly  1 000 000 for teacher training in Montgomery  said Shelby  I believe that a strong knowledge of our nations history is an important part of a solid education  and I am pleased that many of Alabamas teachers are continuing a lifetime of education to better impart their knowledge to our students   The program will allow faculty from the Montgomery Public School District to create a professional development program to teach traditional American History to their history teachers  The Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr   Rosa Parks Liberty Fellowship will work together with the Montgomery Public School District to emphasize the importance of seeing American History as a continuum  rather than a series of events  The funding is distributed through the Department of Educations Teaching American History Grants Program                                                           June
