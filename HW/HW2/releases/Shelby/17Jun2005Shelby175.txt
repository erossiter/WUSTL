SHELBY ANNOUNCES  250 000 FOR HOMELESS VETERANS PROGRAM IN JEFFERSON COUNTY       June 17  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Labor will be releasing  250 000 to the Aletheia House in Birmingham to address the problem of homelessness among Veterans    These funds will allow the Aletheia House to enroll homeless veterans into a program that will provide emergency shelter  substance abuse counseling  job counseling  employment assistance  transportation  subsistence  and medical referrals   The ultimate goal is to place these veterans into meaningful employment that will end homelessness       Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  250 000 to help our veterans get back on their feet   Our nations veterans deserve to be treated with the respect they have earned during their selfless service to our country   I will continue to work to ensure that veterans get the support they deserve          30
