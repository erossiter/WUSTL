SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 MILLION FOR HOME OWNERSHIP PROGRAMS IN MOBILE COUNTY       June 17  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  will soon be releasing  3 338 511 for housing and community revitalization programs in Mobile County   These grants are part of HUDs commitment to provide home ownership opportunities for all Americans      The funds will be awarded as follows          2 296 565 for Community Development Block Grants   This allows state and local governments develop urban communities by providing housing and economic opportunities for low and moderate income individuals           88 769 for the Emergency Shelter Grant Program   This program provides grants to state  local and private entities to improve the quality and number of emergency homeless shelters           929 515 for the HOME Investment Partnership   This program helps expand affordable housing opportunities for low and very low income families by providing grants to state and local governments to fund housing programs to meet the needs and priorities of the local community           23 662 for the American Dream Downpayment Initiative   These funds will promote home ownership opportunities by providing necessary resources for downpayment and closing cost assistance to low income households        Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  3 million for these important home ownership programs in Mobile County   These grants will provide more low income families in the area with the opportunity to own a home and achieve a piece of the American dream                            30                                                              June 2005 News Releases
