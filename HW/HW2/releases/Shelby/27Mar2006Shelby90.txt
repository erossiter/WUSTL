SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 TO CITY OF TUSKEEGE FOR MOTON FIELD      March 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  150 000 to the City of Tuskegee for Moton Field   I am pleased to announce  150 000 for Moton Field  said Senator Shelby  This funding will allow the airport to install fencing around the perimeter of the operational areas of the airport and to remove terrain obstructions  I believe it is important to support our airports and ensure the safest and most efficient travel for Alabamians   The perimeter fencing is necessary to prevent unauthorized access to the airport  and the obstruction removal will improve operational safety
