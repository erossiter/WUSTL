SHELBY ANNOUNCES  1 3 MILLIONFOR KATRINA DAMAGES TO DAUPHIN ISLAND AIRPORT     August 22  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportations Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  1 300 000 to  Mobile County  for renovations to portions of the   Dauphin Island Airport   damaged in Hurricane Katrina  Easy and safe access for travelers to  South Alabama  is critical to sustaining a successful tourism based economy  said Senator Shelby  Restoring damaged infrastructure in hurricane affected areas will help increase travel to and from  Alabama s coast  We must continue to work to bring the   Dauphin Island Airport   back to pre hurricane operation levels     The   Dauphin Island Airport   s headwall and runway lighting system were damaged during Hurricane Katrina  This funding will make necessary repairs  bringing the airport back up to code
