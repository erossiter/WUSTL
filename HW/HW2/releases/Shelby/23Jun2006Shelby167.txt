SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 5 MILLION FOR INDUSTRIAL PARK DEVELOPMENT     June 23  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded  1 577 910 in Economic Development Administration  EDA  funds to Clarke County and the Thomasville Water Works and Sewer Board   The North Clarke County Industrial Park is a good example of what our rural counties can do to create jobs and economic growth  said Senator Shelby  These funds will provide the critical infrastructure necessary for the recruitment of additional industry for the County  This investment supports infrastructure improvements necessary for development of the North Clarke County Industrial Park  and is expected to create 16 jobs and generate  23 9 million in private investments     30                                                            June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
