SHELBY COMMENDS THRAILKILL CONFIRMATION  First Alabamian Appointed to TVA Board  March 3  2006  Washington  D C      Senator Richard Shelby today announced that the United States Senate voted by unanimous consent to confirm Howard Thrailkill to be a member of the Tennessee Valley Authority  TVA  Board for a term that is set to expire in May 2011  Mr  Thrailkill will be the first Alabamian to hold a seat on the TVA Board  Mr  Thrailkill must now be sworn in by the President   I have long advocated for an Alabamian on the TVA board  and I am pleased Howard has now been confirmed for this important position  Howard is well qualified and holds a distinguished record as a successful businessman in North Alabama  I believe TVA needs board members with a good business background  like Howards  who have the knowledge and skills necessary to move TVA forward  Wise investment  debt reduction  and more efficient service to its customers  must be in TVAs plans for the future  I believe that Howards service on the board will help TVA reach these goals  said Shelby       30                                                          March 2006 News Releases                      Year
