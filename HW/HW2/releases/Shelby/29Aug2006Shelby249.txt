SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  100 000 FOR THE TWENTY THIRD JUDICIAL CIRCUIT OF ALABAMA    August 29  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  98 721 to the Twenty Third Judicial Circuit of Alabama for the implementation of the Drug Court Enhancement Project  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   By stressing the fundamentals of The Drug Court Enhancement Projectaccountability  supervision  and treatment  we can work to end the cycle of substance abuse and incarceration in our communities  said Senator Shelby  Drug abuse is a serious problem in our society that extends beyond the drug user  This program will work to end drug abuse and addiction by its participants through strict supervision and consistent attention from parole officers   The Drug Enhancement Project will ensure that all participants speak with their parole officer each week and increase the frequency of drug tests for Adult Drug Court and Family Drug Court participants  The program will also require participants to complete Drug Court Journals and workbooks as a punishment for violating the requirements of the program
