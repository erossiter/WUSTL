SHELBY ANNOUNCES  44 000 TO SOUTH CENTRAL ALABAMA DEVELOPMENT COMMISSION FOR THE CITY OF TUSKEGEE    June 12  2006 U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   announced today that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  will release  44 000 to the South Central Alabama Development Commission for the City of Tuskegee Utilities Board  The funding will allow the Commission to develop a geographic information system for the City of Tuskegee     I am pleased to announce this funding  said Senator Shelby  The geographic information system will allow the City of Tuskegee to manage their water and sewer systems in a more cost effective way     As Tuskegee grows and develops  the geographic system will allow the City to keep up with increasing requests from planners  homeowners  and developers
