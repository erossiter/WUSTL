Shelby Announces  101 003 for the U S  Space and Rocket Center   June 15  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  101 003 to the U S  Space and Rocket Center for the Adventure to Space in Franklin County  This funding will allow educators to participate in a hands on instructional workshop and students to take part in a simulated space mission  The Space and Rocket Center provides excellent professional and educational programs for our teachers and students  said Shelby  For America to remain competitive in the 21st Century  it is critical that todays students be skilled in the areas of math  science  and technology  This funding will allow teachers to obtain the tools needed to present these subjects to their students in an engaging manner  The Adventure to Space program will be conducted within Franklin County and Russellville City Schools  with a field trip to the U S
