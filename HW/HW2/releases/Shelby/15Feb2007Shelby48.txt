Shelby to Visit Blount  St  Clair  Etowah  Calhoun  and Cleburne Counties    February 15  2007 U  S  Sen  Richard Shelby  R Ala   will join the residents of Blount  St  Clair  Etowah  Calhoun  and Cleburne Counties for his county visits on Thursday  February 22  2007  The county visits are open to the public   Meeting Schedule   Thursday  February 22  2007  Blount County  8 00 a m  CT  Twin Oaks  1364 Heritage Road in Oneonta    St  Clair County  10 00 a m  CT  Pell City Center  120 Williamson Drive in Pell City    Etowah County  12 00 noon CT  Gadsden Senior Activity Center  623 Broad Street in Gadsden    Calhoun County  2 30 p m  CT  City Meeting Center Meeting Room A  1615 Noble Street in Anniston    Winston County  4 00 p m  CT  Volunteer Fire Department  Ross Street in Heflin   Sen  Shelby will discuss his legislative agenda for the 110th Congress as well as important issues facing the nation and the state of Alabama  Following his remarks  Sen  Shelby will open the floor to questions and comments from those in attendance  Sen  Shelby said  Over the years  these meetings have provided an excellent opportunity for area residents to listen and voice their opinions on the activities of our government  This will be a great chance to discuss the important issues facing our state and our nation as the 110th Congress reconvenes  Sen  Shelby has held over 1300 county visits since being elected to serve the state of Alabama  As part of his commitment to Alabamians  Sen  Shelby holds a meeting in each county once a year  This meeting is open to the public  Members of the media are encouraged to attend                                                           February 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
