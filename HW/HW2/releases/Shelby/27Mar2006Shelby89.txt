SHELBY ANNOUNCES  450 000 FOR GADSDEN MUNICIPAL AIRPORT     March 27  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  450 000 to the City of Gadsden for the Gadsden Municipal Airport   I am pleased to announce  450 000 for the Gadsden Municipal Airport  This funding will facilitate the growth of the airport by allowing for construction and rehabilitation of new and existing taxiways  said Senator Shelby  I believe the rehabilitation of this airport is essential to the area  and will attract new businesses and jobs to Etowah County   The current taxiway needs rehabilitation to prevent pavement breakup  which damages aircraft  The funding will also construct a new T hanger access  which will allow aircraft into the new general aviation area  Both projects are needed to accommodate the increasing number of aircraft based at the airport
