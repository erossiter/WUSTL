SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR PROJECTS AT THE UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM        June 27  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services will award a total of  220 072 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for nursing related program   The United States is in the midst of a nursing shortage  making it essential that we continue to educate  recruit and train nurses  said Shelby  This shortage will continue to intensify as baby boomers age and their need for health care grows  Nurses are critical to maintaining the best and most effective health care system for this country and we must continue efforts to support nursing related programs   The Advanced Education Nursing program will receive  189 033 and the Nurse Anesthetist Traineeships program will receive  31 039  Both projects are funded through the HRSA Division of Grants Management
