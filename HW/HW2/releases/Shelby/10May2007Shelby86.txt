Shelby Announces  5 Million for Huntsville International Airport   Carl T  Jones Field    May 10  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Aviation Administration will release  5 million for Huntsville International and Carl T  Jones Field  Huntsville International will purchase surrounding land to further expand the airport   Huntsville is one of the fastest growing cities in the South  said Shelby  With the Base Realignment and Closure Commission decision to relocate thousands of jobs to the Redstone Arsenal  North Alabama will grow exponentially in the coming years  The airport expansion will help meet the needs of the increased population traveling to  from  and through Huntsville   The Huntsville International Airport serves an 18 county surrounding airport in Alabama  and provides non stop flights to 10 destinations
