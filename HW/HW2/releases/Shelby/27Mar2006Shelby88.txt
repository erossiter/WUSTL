SHELBY ANNOUNCES  300 000 FOR GUNTERSVILLE MUNICIPAL      March 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  300 000 to the City of Guntersville for the Guntersville Municipal Airports Joe Starnes Field   I am pleased to announce  300 000 for Joe Starnes Field  This funding will allow environmental mitigation efforts in preparation of the airports new runway  said Senator Shelby  This is an important step towards allowing additional runway expansions   Approximately 42 acres of wetlands will need mitigation to meet the National Environmental Policy Act requirements before plans for the new runway can be completed
