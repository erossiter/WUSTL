SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  900 000 FOR MONTGOMERY INTELLIGENT TRANSPORTATION SYSTEMS    May 23  2006  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  906 840 to the City of Montgomery for its Intelligent Transportation Systems  ITS  project   I am pleased to announce  906 840 to the City of Montgomery for this important ITS project  said Shelby  These funds will be used to upgrade traffic monitoring systems  including traffic light improvements and installation of additional camera systems to monitor high traffic areas particularly during emergency situations   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding through the federal
