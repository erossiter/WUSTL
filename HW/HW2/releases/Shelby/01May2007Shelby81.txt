Shelby Announces Over  7 Million in Loans for Tornado Damage    May 7  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Small Business Association has released more than  7 million in Disaster Assistance Loans for residents and business owners in areas of Alabama affected by the March 1st tornadoes   The tornadoes that hit South Alabama were devastating  said Senator Shelby  While we will never recover from the loss of life  I am certain that the people in Coffee  Dale  Dallas  Henry  Montgomery  and Wilcox Counties will work to rebuild even stronger communities  I encourage anyone who suffered financial damages during the March 1st tornadoes to apply for these low interest loans   Homeowners are eligible for up to  200 000 in loans to repair or replace damaged or destroyed real estate  Businesses and private non profit organizations may borrow up to  1 5 million dollars for damaged or destroyed real estate  machinery  equipment  inventory  and other business assets  For further information  qualified applicants should visit
