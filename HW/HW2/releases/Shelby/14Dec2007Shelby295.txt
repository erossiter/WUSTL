Shelby Announces  174 539 for New Hope Volunteer Fire Department   December 14  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  174 539 to the New Hope Volunteer Fire Department in Needham  Alabama  The funding is provided through the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Firefighters are a key component of our nations first response efforts  and therefore they must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  said Shelby  These funds will allow the New Hope Volunteer Fire Department purchase vehicles  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  The Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency awarded these funds as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  490 million in direct federal assistance this year to fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical service organizations nationwide                                                         December 2007 News Releases
