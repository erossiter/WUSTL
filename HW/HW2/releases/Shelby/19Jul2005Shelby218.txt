SHELBY ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR MUSCLE SHOALS FERTILIZER CENTER   Senate Approves FY 2006 Foreign Operations Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                   July 20  2005   WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced Senate approval today of  4 million in funding for the International Fertilizer Development Center located in Muscle Shoals  Alabama   As a member of the Foreign Operations Appropriations Subcommittee  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding   The Foreign Operations Appropriations bill  which passed the Senate by a vote of 98 to 1  will now go to a conference committee with the House for consideration    Senator Shelby said  I am very pleased that the Senate has once again recognized the merits of this funding request and agreed to provide  4 million to further the work of the International Fertilizer Development Center in Muscle Shoals  In addition to the important research conducted at this facility  the Center employs
