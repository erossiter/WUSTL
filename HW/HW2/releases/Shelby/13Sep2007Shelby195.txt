Shelby Announces  4 76 Million for Alabama State Port Authority   September 19  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  4 760 000 to the Alabama State Port Authority in Mobile  These funds will be used for the Alabama State Docks Intermodel Facility Project   The Alabama State Docks has the potential to become one of our nations most economically viable ports due to its proximity to emerging trade routes in the Gulf of Mexico  said Shelby  These funds will allow Alabama to modernize and expand its current dockside facilities to ready the docks for increased traffic and freight cargo  In addition to this funding  Senator Shelby was able to secure  5 173 million for the Alabama State Docks in the fiscal year 2008 Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill  This legislation was approved by the Senate and will now go to a conference committee with the House of
