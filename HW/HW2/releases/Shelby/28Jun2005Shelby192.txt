SHELBY ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR MUSCLE SHOALS FERTILIZER CENTER  FY 2006 Foreign Operations Appropriations Bill Passes Subcommittee                                                                                                                                                                                               June 29  2005   WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced subcommittee approval today of  4 million in funding for the International Fertilizer Development Center located in Muscle Shoals  Alabama   As a member of the Foreign Operations Appropriations Subcommittee  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding   The Foreign Operations Appropriations bill will now go to the full Appropriations Committee for consideration    Senator Shelby said  I am very pleased that the subcommittee has once again recognized the merits of this funding request and agreed to provide  4 million to further the work of the International Fertilizer Development Center in Muscle Shoals  In addition to the important research conducted at this facility  the Center employs numerous Muscle Shoals area residents
