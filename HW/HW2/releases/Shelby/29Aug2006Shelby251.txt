SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  1 MILLION FOR ALABAMA BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION    August 29  2006  Washington  D C         U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  987 228 to the Alabama Department of Public Safetys  ADPS  Bureau of Investigation  ABI  for a computer based case management system  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process  This new system will allow the ABI to develop an automated  fully integrated central repository of investigative information  said Senator Shelby  Our law enforcement agencies must have the up to date technology available to do their work effectively  With this computer system as a resource  investigators will have all information pertinent to a case at their fingertips  The case management system will provide case investigations and intelligence information accessible from secure internet locations  The system will also provide the ABI with statistical information on the total numbers of arrests  prosecutions  seizures  and demographical data regarding investigations  ABI will purchase updated computer hardware necessary to effectively run the program  the management systems software  and provide training to all
