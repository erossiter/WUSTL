SHELBY ANNOUNCES  50 000 FOR FREEDOM QUILTING BEE IN WILCOX COUNTY     January 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Delta Regional Authority  DRA  will release  50 000 to the Freedom Quilting Bee in Wilcox County  The funding will go towards improvements to the facility  including roof repairs  handicapped access  and ceiling repairs  as well as to showcase one of the Blackbelts most celebrated art forms   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to the Freedom Quilting Bee  The Freedom Quilting Bee is an important part of the history of Alabama and I believe that a much improved facility will allow them to share their art form with more Americans   The DRA is funded through the federal appropriations process  Senator Shelby was pleased to assist in securing  12 000 000 for the DRA in the fiscal year 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill
