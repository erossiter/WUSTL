SHELBY COMMENDS JUDICIARY COMMITTEE APPROVAL OF JUDGE BILL PRYOR    May 12  2005   WASHINGTON  DC   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  reacted today to Senate Judiciary Committees approval of Judge Bill Pryors nomination to the 11th Circuit Court of Appeals   Judge Pryors nomination will now go to the Senate floor for consideration   Senator Shelby made the following statement    I am pleased that the Senate Judiciary Committee approved Judge Bill Pryors nomination   He is deserving of confirmation by the Senate to the 11th Circuit Court of Appeals   Since his recess appointment  Judge Pryor has served on the federal bench with impartiality and fairness   He understands and respects the constitutional role of the judiciary and specifically  the role of the federal courts in our legal system     I am hopeful that the Senate will afford Judge Pryor the up or down vote he deserves  Shelby added        30                                                          May 2005 News
