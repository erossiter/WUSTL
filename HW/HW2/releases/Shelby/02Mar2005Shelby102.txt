SHELBY ANNOUNCES  135 000 FOR ADGER VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN JEFFERSON COUNTY     March 2  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Adger Volunteer Fire Department in Jefferson County will receive  135 000 in federal funds to assist with the purchase of firefighting vehicles  These funds are available for a variety of vehicles including trucks  ambulances  aerials and fireboats    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year     This is great news for the Adger Volunteer Fire Department  This grant will provide critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen
