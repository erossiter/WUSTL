SHELBY ANNOUNCES  548 000 TO CITY OF GREENVILLE FOR MACCRENSHAW MEMORIAL AIRPORT     March 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  548 000 to the City of Greenville for MacCrenshaw Memorial Airport   I am pleased to announce  548 000 to Greenville for MacCrenshaw Memorial  said Shelby  This funding will allow the airport to construct a new t hanger  as the current aircraft storage hangers are full or in need of repairs  I believe we must support the expansion of our airports  as they are instrumental in spurring economic growth in this area   This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Program  AIP                                                            March 2006 News Releases                      Year
