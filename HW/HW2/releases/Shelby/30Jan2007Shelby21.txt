Shelby Announces  53 015 for Coppermine Volunteer Fire Department    February 1  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  53 015 to the Coppermine Volunteer Fire Department in Clay County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding will help the Coppermine Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters are our nations true first responders  and as such must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  This funding is an essential component in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                           February 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
