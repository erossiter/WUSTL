Alabama Now Closer to Being Home to KC 330 Facility    February 8  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following comments today regarding the Northrop EADS teams decision to move forward in the tanker acquisition process  My primary interest is to ensure that the capabilities of our tanker fleet are the very best and that our armed forces receive the equipment they need to complete future operations  I am pleased that the Northrop EADS team chose to move forward in the tanker acquisition process  and I am proud that Alabama is positioned to play a role as the future home of the KC 330 facility  I fully support a fair and open competition that will give the Air Force the ability to obtain the best platform for our servicemembers at the best price for
