Shelby Announces Over  60 000 for Tarrant Fire Department    January 24  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  60 232 to the Tarrant Fire Department in Jefferson County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program and will be used for operations and safety   Firefighters are in the field everyday protecting our citizens  said Senator Shelby  We have a responsibility to ensure that our first responders have the most up to date equipment available  It is essential that our nation follow through on its commitment to ensure the safety of our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide
