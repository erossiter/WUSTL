SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  100 000 FOR FAMILY CONNECTION IN ALABASTER    June 21  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  98 723 to Family Connection  Inc  in Alabaster   I am pleased to announce  98 723 for Family Connection  Inc  said Senator Shelby  Family Connection will work with non violent youth who have committed their first offense and their families to strengthen individual accountability and improve the families bond  The program will work with the entire family to preempt further delinquent behavior and to put these at risk youth on the right track   Family Connection  Inc  will use communication and decision making skill training to teach offenders  coupled with a Parenting Wisely training for their parents  The program promotes family bonding and offender accountability and will take place on four consecutive Saturdays as part of an offenders sentence   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds in the FY2006 appropriations process                                                           June 2006 News
