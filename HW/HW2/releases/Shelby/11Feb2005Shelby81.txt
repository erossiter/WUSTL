SHELBY COMMENDS APPROVAL OF MICHAEL CHERTOFF TO LEAD HOMELAND SECURITY DEPT    February 15  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today commended Senate approval of Judge Michael Chertoff as the new Secretary of Homeland Security  replacing outgoing secretary Tom Ridge   Senator Shelby made the following remarks upon Senate approval of Judge Chertoffs nomination    I am pleased that the Senate voted overwhelmingly in favor of Judge Michael Chertoff to serve as our nations next Secretary of Homeland Security   This cabinet position is of utmost importance as we continue to fight the war on terrorism and protect our homeland   I have known Judge Chertoff for many years and believe he is up to the task     The Department of Homeland Security unifies a vast number of organizations and institutions involved in the efforts to secure our nation   This department  comprised of 180 000 employees  requires strong leadership at the helm   From border security to emergency preparedness to immigration  this department is diverse in its mission  and I am confident Judge Chertoff will provide strong and determined leadership in this post        30
