SHELBY ANNOUNCES  232 436 FOR UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM                February 16  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services National Cancer Institute will award  232 436 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for its Cancer Biology Research Program   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for this important program  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging cancer research  which furthers the education of our students and makes important strides for the health of Americans                                                           February 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
