SHELBY  THRAILKILL NOMINATED FOR LONGER TERM     December 21  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   today announced the re nomination of Mr  Howard Thrailkill to serve as a member of the Tennessee Valley Association board for a term to expire in May 2011   Senator Shelby had the following reaction to Mr  Thrailkills re nomination    Senator Shelby said  This is a historic day for the State of Alabama as Howard Thrailkill of Huntsville has been nominated to serve on the TVA board   Following his confirmation  Howard will be the first Alabamian to serve the TVA in this capacity  and his appointment marks the beginning of what I am confident will be a permanent tradition   I have long been an advocate for the need of an Alabamian on the board  and I am pleased the President chose Howard for this important position   The Presidents re nomination of Howard to a longer term is important to the state as it will allow Alabama to have a strong voice on the board for years to come        Mr  Thrailkill was previously nominated for a seat on the TVA board for a term to expire in May of 2007   At the request of Senator Shelby and Senator Sessions  the President nominated Mr  Thrailkill for a longer term  which will expire in May of 2011             30                                                          December 2005 News Releases
