SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 FOR TUSCALOOSA REGIONAL AIRPORT    February 11  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the City of Tuscaloosa will receive  150 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements to the Tuscaloosa Regional Airport    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Tuscaloosa Regional Airport   These funds will be used to help the airport upgrade its fire response capabilities by expanding the airports rescue and fire fighting building   I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts     This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP        30                                                         February 2005 News Releases
