SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  4 MILLION FOR ALABAMA EDUCATIONAL TELEVISION FOUNDATION      July 26  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that U S  Department of Education will be releasing  4 467 146 to the Alabama Educational Television Foundation for the Ready to Teach Program    These funds will be used to for the Learning for Educators Initiative under the Ready to Teach Program   This program helps educators address state wide teacher quality issues by using electronic learning courses to give teachers the resources to help meet Alabamas educational priorities         Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  4 million for the Alabama Educational Television Foundation   By continuing to educate and equip our teachers with new skills  we are not only improving the education our children will receive  we are investing in the future of our state                                                  30
