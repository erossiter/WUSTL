SHELBY ANNOUNCES  2 MILLION FOR MONTGOMERY AIRPORT     February 11  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Montgomery Airport Authority will receive  2 million from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements to Montgomery Regional Airport  Dannelly Field     Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Montgomery Airport Authority  These funds will be used for phase III terminal renovation and expansion  which will consist of aircraft parking apron improvements  access road improvements and additional terminal design and construction needs  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts     This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP         30
