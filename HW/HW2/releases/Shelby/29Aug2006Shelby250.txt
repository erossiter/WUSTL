SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR CITY OF BESSEMER    August 29  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  246 807 to the City of Bessemer for Operation Making Officers Deployable for Law Enforcement  MODEL   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   Operation MODEL will update the way the Bessemer Police Department handles their cases by utilizing new technology  said Senator Shelby  Officers will become more efficient by utilizing technological resources to deploy their officers  These important technology upgrades will allow the department to become more efficient by better using their resources and manpower to reduce crime   The Patrol Division  Corrections  Criminal Investigations  and the Records Division will use this funding for upgraded computer systems  Laptop computers will be placed in patrol vehicles  along with digital audio and video cameras  which will allow officers to record interviews with victims  witnesses  and offenders                                                         August 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
