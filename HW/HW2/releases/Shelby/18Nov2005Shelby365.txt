SHELBY ANNOUNCES FEMA MEETING FOR DISASTER CONTRACTORS IN MONTGOMERY        December 2  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  will host a meeting for disaster contractors in Montgomery   The meeting will explain the types of disaster work opportunities that may be available following Hurricane Katrina and encourage businesses to apply for available contracts      Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that FEMA is hosting this meeting for companies seeking disaster contracts   Although it has been three months since Hurricane Katrina  we must continue to find ways to rebuild those areas affected by this horrible natural disaster  This meeting will provide necessary information to continue important recovery efforts  and I encourage those interested to take advantage of this opportunity      At the session  FEMA contract specialists will provide a basic disaster process overview  outline bidding procedures and answer audience questions      Thursday  December 8  2005     11 00 a m   1 00 p m  Quality Inn and Suites Convention Center 2705 East South Boulevard Montgomery  AL 36109        FEMA requests that companies interested in contracting go to Rebuilding the Gulf Coast at www rebuildingthe gulfgost gov  FedBizOpps at www fbo gov  or e mail nancy costello dhs gov to find the Requests for Proposal  RFP   Those without internet capabilities should fax written requests to Nancy Costello at  202  646 3846
