SHELBY ANNOUNCES  450 000 FOR CRAIG FIELD AIRPORT IN SELMA                              February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  450 000 to the Craig Field Airport and Industrial Authority in Selma   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  450 000 for Craig Field in Selma  This project will rehabilitate the apron and runway  as the existing concrete has begun to deteriorate  We must continue to invest in our airport infrastructure to provide the safest and most efficient travel for Alabamians                                                            February 2006 News Releases
