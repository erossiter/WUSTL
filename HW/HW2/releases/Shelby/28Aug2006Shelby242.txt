SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  400 000 TO THE CITY OF MONTGOMERY FOR THE CLEVELAND AVENUE YMCA    August 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  394 891 to the City of Montgomery for the Cleveland Avenue Young Mens Christian Association  YMCA   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   For many years  the Cleveland Avenue YMCA has been an educational center for at risk youth in Montgomery  said Senator Shelby  Through this funding  the YMCA will serve more youth in the area through educational and cultural opportunities  After school programs have proven  effective ways to reduce violence and crime in youth  and I am confident that this program will do the same   The City of Montgomery and the Cleveland Avenue YMCA will use these funds for an after school intervention and prevention program  At risk students from ages 5 15 will learn about independent education  literacy  communication  and social and nutrition skills  The program also aims to enhance academic achievement through homework help and educational workshops                                                         August 2006 News Releases
