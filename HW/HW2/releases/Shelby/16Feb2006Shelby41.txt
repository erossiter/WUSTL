SHELBY ANNOUNCES  700 000 FOR VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN PREVENTION PROGRAM                                   February 17  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that  700 000 will be released from the U S  Department of Justice to the Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs for the STOP Violence Against Women Formula Grant Program   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  700 000 for this important violence against women prevention program in Alabama  These funds will help enhance services for abuse victims and ensure those who commit these crimes are held accountable  The STOP Violence Against Women Formula Grant Program encourages the development and implementation of effective  victim centered law enforcement  prosecution  and court strategies to address violent crimes against women  This program envisions a partnership among law enforcement  prosecutors  courts  and victim services organizations to enhance victim safety and hold offenders accountable for their crimes against women                                                           February 2006 News
