SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  445 000 FOR EAST ALABAMA WATER SEWER AND FIRE PROTECTION DISTRICT IN CHAMBERS COUNTY     February 16  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the East Alabama Water Sewer and Fire Protection District in Valley  AL will receive  445 500 in federal funds to assist with the purchase of firefighting vehicles  These funds are available for a variety of vehicles including trucks  ambulances  aerials and fireboats    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    I remain committed to working in Congress to ensure that our nations first responders have the necessary resources to rapidly respond in the event of an emergency   These funds will allow the men and women of the East Alabama Water Sewer and Fire Protection District to be better prepared to respond to the needs of their community  Sen  Shelby said      30                                                          February 2005
