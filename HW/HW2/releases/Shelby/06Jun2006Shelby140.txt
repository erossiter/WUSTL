SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM     June 7  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services will award  340 000 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for Autism research   I am pleased that UAB has received funding for this important program  said Senator Shelby  An estimated 1 5 million people struggle with Autism in this country  and I am glad to see UAB continue its important research on the forefront of the diagnosis  prevention and treatment of this disease   The funding  distributed by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention  will be used for a project to research Autism Spectrum Disorders  as well as other developmental disorders                                                            June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
