SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  49 000 FOR THE BURKVILLE VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN MONTGOMERY COUNTY    January 26  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Burkville Volunteer Fire Department in Hope Hull  AL will receive  49 950 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to the Burkville Volunteer Fire Department  It is critical that our federal  state and local governments work together to ensure that our first responders have the necessary tools to face an emergency quickly and effectively should one occur  Sen  Shelby said                                                        January 2005 News Releases
