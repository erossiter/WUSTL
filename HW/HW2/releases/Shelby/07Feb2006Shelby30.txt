SHELBY  STATEMENT ON THRAILKILL CONFIRMATION HEARING                 February 8  2006   The Senate Environment and Public Works Committee today held the confirmation hearing for the Tennessee Valley Authority  TVA  Board nominees  including Howard Thrailkill of Huntsville  U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  made the following statement of support for Mr  Thrailkill   I have long advocated for an Alabamian on the TVA board  and I am pleased the President chose Howard for this important position  Howard is well qualified and has a distinguished record as a successful businessman in North Alabama  I believe TVA needs board members with a good business background  like Howards  who have the knowledge and skills necessary to move TVA forward  Wise investment  debt reduction  and more efficient service to its customers  must be in TVAs plans for the future  I believe that Howards service on the board will help TVA reach these goals   Mr  Thrailkill was nominated by the President for a term which will expire in May of 2011  He is the first Alabamian nominated to the board                                                          February 2006 News Releases
