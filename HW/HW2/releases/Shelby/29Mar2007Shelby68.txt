Shelby  Applications Available for Firefighter Grant Program    March 30  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the application period for the fiscal year 2007 Assistance to Firefighters Grants  AFG  Program opened yesterday  Thursday  March 29  and will close on Friday  May 4  2007  The grant program is offered through U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Office of Grants and Training   I encourage fire departments that seek federal assistance to complete an application to the Assistance to Firefighters Grants program  said Senator Shelby  It is critical that our first responders receive the necessary training and are equipped with the tools they require to address the needs of our communities  I am pleased that these grants will be available  and I am hopeful that many fire departments across Alabama will take advantage of this opportunity  The AFG program funds projects to purchase new equipment  improve firefighter health and safety programs  and improve the effectiveness of firefighting and emergency response operations  The AFG Program assists rural  urban  and suburban fire departments throughout the United States  Non fire based EMS organizations may also apply for grants   For more information or to obtain an application for the AFG Program visit                                                           March 2007
