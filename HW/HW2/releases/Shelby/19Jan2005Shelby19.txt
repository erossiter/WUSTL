SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  54 000 FOR THE CORINTH VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN RANDOLPH COUNTY     January 19  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Corinth Volunteer Fire Department in Wadley  AL will receive  54 720 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year   This is great news for the Corinth Volunteer Fire Department  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said                                                         January 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
