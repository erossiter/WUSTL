SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 8 MILLION FOR VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN PREVENTION PROGRAM      June 16  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that  1 895 000 will be released from the U S  Department of Justice to the Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs for the STOP Violence Against Women Formula Grant Program    The STOP Violence Against Women Formula Grant Program encourages the development and implementation of effective  victim centered law enforcement  prosecution  and court strategies to address violent crimes against women   This program envisions a partnership among law enforcement  prosecutors  courts  and victim services organizations to enhance victim safety and hold offenders accountable for their crimes again women                    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  1 8 million for these important violence against women prevention programs in Alabama   These funds will help enhance services for
