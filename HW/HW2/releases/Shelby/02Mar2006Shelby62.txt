SHELBY ANNOUNCES APPLICATIONS AVAILABLE FOR FIREFIGHTER GRANT PROGRAM        March 3  2006  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the application period for the fiscal year 2006 Assistance to Firefighters Grants  AFG  Program will open on Monday  March 6 and close on Friday  April 7  2006  The grant program is offered through U S  Department of Homeland Security   Sen  Shelby said  I encourage those fire departments seeking assistance to complete an application to the Assistance to Firefighters Grants program  It is critical that our first responders have the necessary skills and equipment to address the needs of our communities  I am pleased that these grants will be available  and I am hopeful that many fire departments across Alabama will take advantage of this opportunity   The AFG program funds projects to purchase new equipment  improve firefighter health and safety programs  and improve the effectiveness of firefighting and emergency response operations  The AFG Program assists rural  urban  and suburban fire departments throughout the United States  Non fire based EMS organizations may also apply for grants   For more information or to obtain an application for the AFG Program  please visit www firegrantsupport com                                                          March 2006 News Releases                      Year
