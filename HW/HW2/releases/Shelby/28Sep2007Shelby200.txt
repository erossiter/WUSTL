Shelby Announces  81 800 for Perry County   September 28  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Delta Regional Authority  DRA  will award  81 800 to Perry County  Alabama  for water system improvements  It is essential that the residents of Perry County have access to a safe and reliable water system  said Shelby  This funding will not only allow the county to improve its water system  but install several fire hydrants for the safety of its residents   The water line extension will provide service to 57 households  allowing approximately 128 people to receive public water service for the first time  The project will also interconnect the Perry County Water System to the Carterville Water System so that they will serve as a backup for each other                                                          September 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
