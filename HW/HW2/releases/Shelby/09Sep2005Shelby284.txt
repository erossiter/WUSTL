SHELBY ANNOUNCES  175 000 FOR THE KING  S RANCH AND HANNAH HOMES IN CHELSEA      September 12  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Justice will be releasing  175 000 for the Transitional Housing Grant Program to the Kings Ranch and Hannah Homes Incorporated in Chelsea                  These funds will be used to provide temporary housing assistance for victims of domestic violence and their children   These victims may be homeless or in need of transitional housing or assistance as a result of fleeing a situation of domestic violence   These individuals will also have access to services such as transportation  counseling  child care services  case management and employment counseling                                                                                                     Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  175 000 for the Kings Ranch and Hannah Homes in Chelsea   These funds will help give victims of domestic violence  along with their dependents  transitional housing and assistance while helping them find and secure a permanent home                                        30
