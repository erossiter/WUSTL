SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  88 000 TO THE GENEVA POLICE DEPARTMENT    August 29  2006  Washington  D C          U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  88 851 to the Geneva Police Department for a drug elimination program  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process     Drug use is an ever growing problem in our society  and it is often those selling or manufacturing the drugs that are involved in the violent crimes  said Senator Shelby  The Geneva Police Department will work to stop drug use and drug crime at the source  improving the safety of all area residents  By uniting the community in a fight against drugs  I am confident that the Department will be successful     Drug trafficking and the illegal manufacture of controlled substances in public housing areas are both problems in the City of Geneva  The police department hopes to increase the accessibility of police officers through the implementation of this program and by opening planning meetings to community leaders and local residents                                                           August 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
