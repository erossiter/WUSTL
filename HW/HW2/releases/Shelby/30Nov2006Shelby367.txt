Shelby Announces Over  112 000 for the Belgreen Fire Protection District    November 30  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  112 670 to the Belgreen Fire Protection District in Franklin County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program and will be used to purchase a new vehicle for the department   Firefighters are in the field everyday protecting our citizens  said Senator Shelby  We have a responsibility to ensure that our first responders have the most up to date equipment available  It is critical that our nation follow through on its commitment to ensure the safety of our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                          November 2006 News Releases                      Year
