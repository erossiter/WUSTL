SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  220 000 FOR THE YWCA OF CENTRAL ALABAMA      August 24  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that U S  Department of Justice will be releasing  220 359 to the Young Womens Christian Association  YWCA  of Central Alabama in Birmingham   The YWCA will use these funds for the Legal Assistance for Victims Grant  LAV  Program   The LAV Program supports projects that provide legal service to victims of domestic violence  sexual assault and stalking                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  220 000 for the YWCA of Central Alabama   This nonprofit organization has been a longtime advocate for women and families of all races and religions and has worked hard to identify and respond to their needs   These funds will help the YWCA continue to expand their outreach by supporting projects that will provide legal services to victims of domestic violence and sexual assault               30
