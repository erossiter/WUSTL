SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  100 000 TO CITY OF EUFAULA FOR WEEDON FIELD     March 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  99 180 to the City of Eufaula for Weedon Field   I am pleased to announce  99 180 for Weedon Field  said Senator Shelby  This project will allow the airport to improve drainage and rehabilitate the runway  making the airport safe for pilots and passengers   Rehabilitation to the runway will bring the airport up to design standards  while the airfield drainage improvements will enhance removal of storm waters from the airport  These changes will enhance the safety of operations throughout Weedon Field
