SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 TO THE CITY OF THOMASVILLE     January 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Delta Regional Authority will release  132 311 to the City of Thomasville in Clarke County  The funding will help to expand the sewer system to Clarke Industrial Park  and the project will bolster the Clarke County economy with 130 direct jobs   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to the City of Thomasville  It is important that our cities and counties have the strong infrastructure necessary to attract new businesses   The DRA is funded through the federal appropriations process  Senator Shelby was pleased to assist in securing  12 000 000 to fund the DRA in the fiscal year 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill                                                           January 2006 News Releases                      Year
