Shelby Announces Over  57 000 for Cottonton Volunteer Fire Department     November 20  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  57 085 to the Cottonton Volunteer Fire Department inRussell County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding will help the Cottonton Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters are an integral component in our nations first response efforts  and as such must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  This funding demonstrates our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         November 2006 News Releases                      Year
