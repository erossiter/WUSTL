SHELBY ANNOUNCES  50 000 FOR CITY OF BOAZ TO ENHANCE SCHOOL SAFETY EFFORTS    September 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  will be awarding the City of Boaz  50 000  This funding will be provided by the Secure our Schools Grant Program   The Secure Our Schools Grant Program is designed to address the safety and security needs of Boazs schools  said Senator Shelby  Children are our future and we must ensure that they receive a strong educational foundation if they are to succeed in life  Schools should be a safe haven for young people  a place where they are free from violence and able to learn and grow into productive members of society  This funding will make important strides toward providing that save haven for our school children   The Secure our Schools Grant Program helps schools establish and enhance school safety efforts  The City of Boazs allotment will allow area schools to address a variety of existing and emerging problems related to
