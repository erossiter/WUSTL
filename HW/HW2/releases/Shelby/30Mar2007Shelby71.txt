      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  396 000 for New Life for Women Shelter    April 16  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  Economic Development Initiative will release  396 000 to the New Centurions New Life for Women shelter in Gadsden  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   New Life for Women will use this funding for the construction of its new facility  said Senator Shelby  The new building will house 36 women while they undergo treatment for addiction  This shelter is a safe haven for women where they can work through their problems in a safe and secure environment   The New Life for Women shelter will likely open by the beginning of 2008                                                        April 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                       Current record                                                                                                                                                      Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
