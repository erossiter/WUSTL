SHELBY ANNOUNCES  1 8 MILLION TO BIRMINGHAM INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT    August 22  2006  Washington  D C         U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration will release  1 800 000 to the Birmingham International Airport for security enhancements   Security enhancements to our airports are an essential step towards ensuring the safety or our airline passengers  said Senator Shelby  September 11  2001 taught us that we cannot be too cautious regarding the safety of airplane travel  It is essential that Birmingham International Airport receive the support necessary to increase the security of travelers through the airport  m This funding will provide an upgrade of the airports closed circuit television system  providing security airport officials with views of each part of the terminals                                                          August 2006 News
