SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 FOR CITY OF ENTERPRISE         March 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  369 777 to the City of Enterprise for Enterprise Municipal Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  300 000 for Enterprise Municipal Airport  This funding will allow the airport to install a fence around the perimeter to ensure safety and security of airport equipment  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians                                                            March 2006 News Releases                      Year
