SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 TO CITY OF FORT PAYNE FOR ISBELL FIELD            March 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  144 445 to the City of Fort Payne for Isbell Field  The funding will expand areas of the airport to accommodate growth   I am pleased to announce  144 445 for Isbell Field  This funding will help the airport keep up with growth by constructing a new T Hangar access taxiway  said Senator Shelby  As airport traffic increases  it is important to provide the appropriate funds to support Isbell Fields expansion   The funding will allow the airport to construct T Hangar access taxiways  The new taxiways will provide access to the terminal area south of the current apron  which is the only area left around the airport for expansion                                                            March 2006 News
