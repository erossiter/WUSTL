SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  87 000 FOR REGIONAL CULTURAL ALLIANCE OF GREATER BIRMINGHAM    June 12  2006 U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   announced today that the Regional Cultural Alliance of Greater Birmingham will receive  87 500 from the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC      I am pleased to announce this funding  said Senator Shelby  The Regional Cultural Alliance of Greater Birmingham will train area artists and craftsmen in business skills  enabling artists to fully capitalize on their trades and the markets that support them     The ARC funding is provided through a regional initiative to encourage and support locally based entrepreneurship  creating jobs and strengthening local economies  This funding is expected to help create 50 new jobs over the next three years  while providing business skills to 150 area residents                                                             June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
