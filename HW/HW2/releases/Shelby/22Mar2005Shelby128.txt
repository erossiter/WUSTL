SHELBY STATEMENT ON DEATH OF FORMER SENATOR HOWELL HEFLIN    March 29  2005   Senator Shelby said  I was deeply saddened to hear of Senator Heflins recent passing   Howell and I were friends and colleagues for many years  and it was a pleasure to serve with him in the United States Senate      Judge Heflin was one of the most respected figures in Alabama politics   He truly loved his state and served it well for many years   As Chief Justice of the Alabama Supreme Court  he helped to modernize Alabamas legal system     Senator Heflin was devoted to his family  his state and his country   As a World War II hero  he put his love of country above all else   He made remarkable contributions to Alabama and the nation  and his warm hearted personality will be remembered by all who knew him       Annette and I will keep Mrs  Heflin and their family in our thoughts and prayers   We will all miss him        30                                                          March 2005 News
