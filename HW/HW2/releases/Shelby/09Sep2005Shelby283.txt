SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  28 MILLION FOR ALABAMA STATE PORT AUTHORITY    September 12  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will be releasing  28 382 225 to the Alabama State Port Authority in Mobile   These funds will be used for the Alabama State Docks Intermodel Facility Project  and account for funding included in numerous transportation bills from Fiscal Year 2002 through Fiscal Year 2005                 Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce the release of over  28 million for Alabama State Docks Intermodal Facility Project   The Alabama State Docks have the potential to become one of America s most economically important ports because of the proximity to emerging trade routes on the Gulf of Mexico   These funds will help modernize and prepare the docks to handle increased traffic as well as more freight cargo                                                30
