SHELBY ANNOUNCES  665 000 FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA TRANSPORTATION CENTER     August 19  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportations Research and Innovative Technology Administration  RITA  will be releasing  665 000 to the University of Alabamas University Transportation Center for Alabama  UTCA    These funds will be used to apply research and the latest technologies to improve the management and safety of transportation systems                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  665 000 for the University Transportation Center for Alabama   UTCA engages talented researchers and students in creating the transportation system of tomorrow which will continue to spur economic growth well
