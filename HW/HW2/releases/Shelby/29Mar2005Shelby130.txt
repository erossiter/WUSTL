      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    SHELBY ANNOUNCES  3 35 MILLION FOR ANDALUSIA OPP AIRPORT     April 1  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  3 35 million will be released from the Department of Transportation for improvements to be made to the Andalusia Opp Airport    Senator Shelby said  These funds will allow for the Andalusia Opp Airport to make several improvements including apron and taxiway construction  runway taxiway extension design  land acquisition  runway overlay  and road relocation   I believe these improvements will enhance the airports ability to meet the growing needs of the community and encourage economic growth  Shelby concluded     Senator Shelby secured these funds in the fiscal year 2005 federal appropriations process          30                                                          April 2005 News Releases                      Year            County                        Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
