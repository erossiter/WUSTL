SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 3 MILLION FOR CITY OF MUSCLE SHOALS      Funding will support economic development programs  June 23  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded  1 330 000 in Economic Development Administration  EDA  funds to the City of Muscle Shoals and the Muscle Shoals Utilities Board   The automotive industry continues to be a valuable segment of Alabamas economy  Senator Shelby said  These funds will give Muscle Shoals the opportunity to take advantage of the good jobs and economic growth that the industry produces   This funding supports water  sewer and road improvements to assist in the development of a new industrial park and an automotive lighting manufacturing facility which is expected to create 232 new jobs and generate  15 million in private investment      30                                                            June 2006 News
