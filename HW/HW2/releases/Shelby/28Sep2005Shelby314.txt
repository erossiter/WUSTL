SHELBY COMMENDS NOMINATION OF TWO ALABAMIANS TO THE FEDERAL BENCH    September 29  2005   WASHINGTON  DC  Late yesterday  President Bush nominated two Alabamians to serve as United States District Judges   The nominees are       Kristi DuBose to be United States District Judge for the Southern District of Alabama  W  Keith Watkins to be United States District Judge for the Middle District of Alabama       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  issued the following statement upon learning of their nominations      These two individuals are remarkable candidates   Both Ms  DuBose and Mr  Watkins bring tremendous experience to the bench and will serve our legal system well   I am hopeful that they receive swift confirmation from the United States Senate            30
