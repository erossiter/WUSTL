SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  490 000 FOR FRANKLIN FIELD IN BULLOCK COUNTY    September 6  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  496 596 to Bullock County for runway and apron rehabilitations at Franklin Field  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process  I am proud to have helped secure this funding for Franklin Field  said Senator Shelby  Bullock County has traditionally been one of our most impoverished counties and this project will serve as an important boost to the economy  A strong transportation infrastructure is key to attracting new businesses and industry to a community  and I am confident that these improvements will help Bullock County compete  The runway and apron pavements  which are more than 20 years old  are in need of rehabilitation  The pavement rehabilitation will improve airport operations safety for traffic at the airport  as well as extend the life of the pavement                                                           September 2006 News Releases
