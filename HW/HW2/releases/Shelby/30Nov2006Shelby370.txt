Shelby Announces  400 000 for the Hale Empowerment and Revitalization Organization    December 1  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  will release  400 000 to the Hale Empowerment and Revitalization Organization  HERO   The funding is provided through HUDs Youthbuild program   HERO and the Youthbuild program serve dual purposes for the citizens of Hale County  said Senator Shelby  First  high school drop outs are armed with GED classes and ultimately certificates  while they simultaneously receive on the job training  Students renovate or build houses and apartments that are then sold to low income families in Hale County  HEROs program provides students with a marketable skill and Hale County residents with affordable housing   Families who purchase homes from HERO pay the organization an affordable fee  which is paid directly back to HEROs loan program  This investment in the loan program allows even more families to experience home ownership  According to the U S  Census  in 2003 20 6  of Hale County residents are below the
