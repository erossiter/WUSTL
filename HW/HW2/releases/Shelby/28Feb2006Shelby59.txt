SHELBY ANNOUNCES  218 250 FOR UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA AT BIRMINGHAM              March 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases will award  218 250 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB  for its Digestive Diseases and Nutrition Research Program   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for this important program  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging medical research  which furthers the education of our students  and makes important strides for the health of Americans
