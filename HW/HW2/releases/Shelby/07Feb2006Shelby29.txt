SHELBY ANNOUNCES FUNDING FOR HANOVER VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT     February 8  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Hanover Volunteer Fire Department in Coosa County will receive  7 190 in federal funds to assist with fire prevention  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year   This is great news for the Hanover Volunteer Fire Department  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Senator Shelby said                                                          February 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
