      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces Over  1 4 Million for Alabama Crime Victims Compensation Commission    May 16  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   ranking member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the U S  Department of Justice released  1 448 000 to the Alabama Crime Victims Compensation Commission  which reimburses Alabamas crime victims for monetary loss incurred as a result of a crime committed against them   Many times  victims lose personal property or are injured as a result of a crime  said Shelby  In either scenario  innocent people lose their own assets and incur crime related expenses such as medical costs and lost wages  It is important that we compensate victims to help them recover after a crime has been committed against them                                                         May 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record                                                                                                                                                      Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
