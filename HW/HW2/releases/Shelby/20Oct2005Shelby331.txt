      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    SHELBY STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF ROSA PARKS      October 25  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   released the following statement on the death of Rosa Parks    Senator Shelby said  I was saddened to hear of the passing of the courageous civil rights pioneer  Mrs  Rosa Parks   Mrs  Parks took a public stand for civil rights and played a pivotal role in helping our country move forward to ensure equal rights for all Americans   Alabama and the nation have made great strides since Rosa Parks first refused to give up her seat on a Montgomery bus in 1955   Mrs  Parks  the mother of the new civil rights movement  will never be forgotten  as the influential role she played changed America and the world                                                          October 2005 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
