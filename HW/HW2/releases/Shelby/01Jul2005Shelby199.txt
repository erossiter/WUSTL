SHELBY STATEMENT ON LONDON BOMBINGS      July 7  2005   LONDON  U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   while traveling in London on matters related to the Senate Banking Committee  made the following statement regarding todays bombings in London      I want to express my sincere condolences to the victims and families of this terrible tragedy   As Americans  we stand united with the British people in the struggle against terrorism as they hunt down those responsible and bring them to justice   These malicious attacks only strengthen our resolve to defeat those who wish to harm the United States and our allies      Todays attacks also serve as a stark reminder that terrorists can hit anywhere and at anytime  until we completely destroy their networks   By working to spread democracy across the globe  the United States and other nations are crippling these networks   We must remain committed to this fight and committed to winning the War on Terror       30
