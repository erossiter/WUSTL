Shelby Announces  81 495 for Madison Fire Rescue    November 13  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  81 495 to the Madison Fire Rescue  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   It is essential that Alabamas first responders have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  Madison Fire Rescue will use this funding for operations and safety  This grant is evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         November
