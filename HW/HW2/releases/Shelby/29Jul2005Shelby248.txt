SHELBY APPLAUDS SHUTTLE DISCOVERY  S SAFE RETURN TO EARTH     August 9  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriations Subcommittee with jurisdiction over the National Aeronautics and Space Administration  NASA   today applauded the Space Shuttle Discoverys successful mission and safe return to earth   Senator Shelby made the following statement upon the shuttles landing                  I am extremely pleased that the Space Shuttle Discovery completed a successful mission and safely returned to earth   After more than two years of being grounded  NASA returned to flight and completed an important mission to resupply the International Space Station   Discoverys return to flight reminds us  that despite the risk  space travel holds great promise for our nation  and we must continue to focus on the future of the space program                     I want to applaud the hard work of all those at NASA who made this possible  and specifically the men and       women at Marshall Space Flight Center for playing an integral role in ensuring that NASA and the Space Shuttle Discovery had a
