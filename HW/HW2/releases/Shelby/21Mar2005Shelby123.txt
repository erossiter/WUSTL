SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  600 000 FOR INTELLIGENT TRANSPORTATION SYSTEM IN MONTGOMERY    March 22  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  605 893 will be released from the Department of Transportation for acquisition and implementation of an Intelligent Transportation System in Montgomery    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available for the acquisition of an Intelligent Transportation System  ITS  in Montgomery  These funds will be used to upgrade traffic monitoring systems  including traffic light improvements and installation of additional camera systems to monitor high traffic areas particularly during emergency situations     Senator Shelby secured these funds in the fiscal year 2005 federal appropriations process        30                                                          March 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
