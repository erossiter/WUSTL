SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  140 000 FOR ALTOONA VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN ETOWAH COUNTY     March 16  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Altoona Volunteer Fire Department in Etowah County will receive  140 432 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness activities  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    This is great news for the Altoona Volunteer Fire Department  This grant provides critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said        30                                                          March 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
