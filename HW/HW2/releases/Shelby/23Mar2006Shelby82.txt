SHELBY ANNOUNCES  300 000 FOR MONTGOMERY REGIONAL AIRPORT AUTHORITY         March 24  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  300 000 to the Montgomery Regional Airport Authority for runway rehabilitation at Dannelly Field   I am pleased to announce  300 000 to the Montgomery Regional Airport Authority for Dannelly Field  said Shelby  This funding will improve safety for pilots and passengers and begin the process of clearing the runway from extraneous debris  which has become a hazard to aircraft landing and taking off   Rehabilitation will extend the life of the pavement on the runway  Funding will be used to prepare plans and specifications for the
