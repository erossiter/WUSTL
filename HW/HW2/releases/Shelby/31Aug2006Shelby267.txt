SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  700 000 FOR UNIVERSITY OF MOBILE    August 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice will release  740 421 for the University of Mobiles Ram Kids Project  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   Our nations young people need tools and training to become contributing members of society  said Senator Shelby  Programs such as Ram Kids provide youth with alternatives to crime and mischief  which in many cases  is all they need to stay out of trouble  With the funding provided  the University of Mobiles Ram Kids Project will target at risk high school students in Prichard  Alabama by providing them with one on one mentoring  The program aims to increase the students service and leadership skills  thereby preventing delinquent behavior
