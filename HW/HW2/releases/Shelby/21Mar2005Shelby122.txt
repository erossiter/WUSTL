      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 9 MILLION FOR WIDENING OF VALLEYDALE ROAD IN SHELBY COUNTY      March 21  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  1 984 000 will be released from the Department of Transportation for widening of Valleydale Road in Shelby County   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that these funds are being released to continue the widening of existing pavement from three lanes to five lanes to accommodate increased traffic flow on Valleydale Road    Senator Shelby secured these funds through the federal appropriations process                                                         March 2005 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record                                                                                                                                                                     Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
