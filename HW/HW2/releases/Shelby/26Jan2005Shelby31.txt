SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  92 000 FOR EAST CHILTON FIRE AND RESCUE IN CHILTON COUNTY         January 26  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that East Chilton Fire and Rescue in Clanton  AL will receive  92 584 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    These funds will allow the men and women of East Chilton Volunteer Fire and Rescue to be better prepared to respond to the needs of their community  I will continue working in Congress to ensure that our nations first responders have the necessary resources to rapidly respond in the event of an emergency  Sen  Shelby said                                                        January 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
