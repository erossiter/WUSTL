SHELBY ANNOUNCES ADDITIONAL FUNDS FOR INTELLIGENT TRANSPORTATION SYSTEM ON U S  280 IN JEFFERSON COUNTY     March 22  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  484 714 will be released from the Department of Transportation for the Intelligent Transportation System on U S  280 in Jefferson County   Yesterday  Senator Shelby announced that  2 423 571 would be made available for this project    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the DOT will be making these additional funds available for the Intelligent Transportation System  ITS  on US 280 in Jefferson County  These funds will be used to upgrade traffic monitoring systems  including traffic light improvements and installation of additional camera systems to monitor high traffic areas particularly during emergency situations  This project is of critical importance for this very congested artery in Jefferson County       Senator Shelby secured these funds through the 2005 federal appropriations process        30                                                          March 2005 News Releases                      Year
