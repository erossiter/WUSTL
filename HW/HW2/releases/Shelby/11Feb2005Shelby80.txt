SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  198 000 FOR SCOTTSBORO MUNICIPAL WORD FIELD    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that City of Scottsboro will receive  198 477 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at Scottsboro Municipal Word Field   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Scottsboro Municipal Word Field  These funds will be used for land acquisition  pavement improvements  and construction of a maintenance hangar  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
