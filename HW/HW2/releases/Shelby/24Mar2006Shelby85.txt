SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  600 000 FOR THE CENTRE PIEDMONT CHEROKEE COUNTY REGIONAL AIRPORT IN CENTRE     March 24  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  673 000 to the City of Centre to construct portions of the new Centre Piedmont Cherokee County Regional Airport   I am pleased to announce over  600 000 to the City of Centre for construction of the Regional Airport  said Shelby  This funding will further construction of the runway and other important components of the Centre Piedmont Cherokee County Regional Airport  I believe this airport will be an asset to the area  bringing new businesses and jobs to Cherokee County   The funds will allow the City of Centre to construct a runway  navigational and approach aids  runway lighting  and a runway vertical visual guidance system
