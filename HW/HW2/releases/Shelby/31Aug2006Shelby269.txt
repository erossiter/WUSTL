SHELBY ANNOUNCES  2 4 MILLION FOR SECURITY ENHANCEMENTS AT BIRMINGHAM INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT    August 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  2 400 000 to the Birmingham International Airport for security enhancements   September 11  2001 taught us that we cannot be too cautious regarding the safety of airplane travel  said Senator Shelby  It is essential that Birmingham International Airport receive the support necessary to increase the security of people traveling to and through the airport  It is critical that we make every effort to protect the safety of our citizens                                                          August
