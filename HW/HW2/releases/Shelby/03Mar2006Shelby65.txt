SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  3 MILLION FOR PROJECTS AT UAB        March 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services will award  2 988 458 to three projects at the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for these important programs  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging medical research  which furthers the education of our students  and makes important strides for the health of Americans   Projects Funded Include   National Cancer Institute to Cancer Treatment Research  Armed Replicating Ad for Breast Cancer Bone   232 436    National Institute of Child Health and Human Development  ATM Coordinating Center   2 471 565   National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases to Digestive Diseases and Nutrition Research  Long term Follow up of Postobese
