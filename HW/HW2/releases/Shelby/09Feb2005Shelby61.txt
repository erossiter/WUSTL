SHELBY ANNOUNCES NOMINEES TO U S  SERVICE ACADEMIES FROM THE WIREGRASS AREA   Students from the Wiregrass area have been Nominated  February 9  2005  Washington  D C     WASHINGTON  D  C      U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   today applauded several Wiregrass area students who were nominated to the United States Service Academies  The service academies include the U S  Naval Academy  U S  Air Force Academy  U S  Military Academy  and the U S  Merchant Marine Academy   Senator Shelby said  Furthering ones education at our nations service academies is remarkable  These students have shown exemplary performance in the classroom and in their extracurricular activities  The selection process for the academies is extremely competitive  and I was pleased to have been able to nominate a number of students from the Wiregrass area    Service academy nominees must be residents of Alabama and must complete the required ACT and SAT exams  Applicants are also required to provide letters of recommendation  their school transcript  completed medical examination  and the required application form    The following Wiregrass area students have been nominated     Leandro Fugate  son of Gary and Fatina Fugate of Enterprise  and a student at the United States Air Force Preparatory School  has been nominated to the United States Air Force Academy     Matthew Canales Grooms  son of Clinton and Lydia Grooms of Daleville  and a student at Daleville High School  has been nominated to the United States Military Academy     Jessica Ciluffo  daughter of Scott Ciluffo and Pamela McArdle of Dothan  and a student at Northview High School  has been nominated to the United States Military Academy     Kathryn Erker  daughter of Erich and Michele Erker of Enterprise  and a student at Enterprise High School  has been nominated to the United States Military Academy     Daniel Whitehurst  son of Cecil and Ruby Whitehurst of Ashford  and a student at Ashford High School  has been nominated to the United States Military Academy     Joseph Ferguson  son of Wayne and Karen Ferguson of Dothan  and a student at Northview High School  has been nominated to the United States Military Academy    Although Senator Shelby nominated these students  the academy to which they were nominated will make the final selection                                                         February
