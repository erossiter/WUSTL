SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  7 MILLION FOR BALDWIN COUNTY HURRICANE SHELTER       May 17  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has released  7 534 073 to the Baldwin County Cattle and Fair Association  The Association will use the funds to construct a hurricane shelter   I am pleased to announce over  7 million to the Baldwin County Cattle and Fair Association  said Shelby  The construction of this shelter will significantly improve the shelter space in Baldwin County  an area that has been hard hit by hurricanes and other natural disasters  It is critical that we make every effort to prepare for upcoming hurricane seasons now to ensure the safety of Alabamians along the Gulf Coast   The Shelter will be built to accommodate 1900 occupants  and will be built in accordance with FEMA guidelines to withstand 200 mph peak wind gusts                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
