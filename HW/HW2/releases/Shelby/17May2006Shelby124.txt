SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  2 MILLION FOR BIBB COUNTY TORNADO SHELTER      May 17  2006  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  has released  1 942 378 to Bibb County  The county will use the funding to construct three tornado shelters   I am pleased to announce nearly  2 million for Bibb County  said Shelby  The construction of these shelters will significantly improve safety in Bibb County from tornadoes and other natural disaster  It is critical that we make every effort to prepare for these disasters before they occur   A shelter will be built in Woodstock  West Bacon  and Randolph  Each shelter will be built to comply with FEMA guidelines  Bibb County will own each shelter and provide maintenance and operations support                                                            May 2006 News Releases
