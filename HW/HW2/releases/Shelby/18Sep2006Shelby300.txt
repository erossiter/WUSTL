SHELBY ANNOUNCES  247 000 TO THE TALLADEGA POLICE DEPARTMENT    September 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  247 000 to the Talladega Police Department for technology upgrades  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process  Local law enforcement officers work on the front lines everyday to ensure the safety of our communities and our citizens  said Senator Shelby  It is crucial that they have the most up to date equipment available  With these technology upgrades  the Talladega Police Department will be able to better maintain public safety in Talladega  The funding provided will be used for the purchase of in car camera systems  mobile data terminals  vehicles  and other equipment upgrades  In addition  a storage server will be purchased to allow the department to keep data in chronological order for playback during court  complaints  and investigations                                                          September 2006
