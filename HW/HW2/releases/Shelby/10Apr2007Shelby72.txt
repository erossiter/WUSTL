      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  30 000 for the City of Cullman    April 18  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  30 000 to the City of Cullman  The funding will be used to provide free Wi Fi access in the City   Quick and easy internet connectivity will allow Cullman to be on the cutting edge of technology  said Senator Shelby  Wi Fi is important to our cities infrastructure as it helps attract and support jobs and businesses   The City of Cullman will match the ARC funds at  30 000 to fund the total project cost of  60 000  Wi Fi will allow Cullmans citizens  visitors and businesses to connect to access points for wireless internet  This project is part of the ARCs Telecommunications Initiative  which will assist the region in improving broadband access to promote economic and human resource development                                                         April 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                      Current record                                                                                                                                       Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
