SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR MONTGOMERY TRANSIT SYSTEM       May 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  236 490 to the City of Montgomery for the areas transit system   I am pleased to announce over  200 000 to the Montgomery Area Transit System  said Shelby  As people experience higher energy costs  they will be more likely to take advantage of public transportation  We must make every effort to improve our public transit system so that Alabamians may continue to depend on and benefit from its services   The funding will allow the city to purchase five new buses  which will replace buses that are no longer usable  This award will allow the city to utilize the remaining funds provided in the FY05 Transportation  Treasury  and General Government Appropriations Bill  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the federal appropriations process                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
