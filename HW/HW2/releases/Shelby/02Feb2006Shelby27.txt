SHELBY STATEMENT ON INTRODUCTORY FLIGHT TRAINING SITE SELECTION                       February 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement on todays Introductory Flight Training  IFT  program site selection   Senator Shelby said  I am disappointed that the Air Force did not choose Selma as the site for their Introductory Flight Training program  I believe Selma is an excellent location for IFT  as it meets all the education and training requirements set forth by the Air Force  Although Selma was not awarded this contract  I look forward to finding other opportunities for Craig Field and the Selma Community   Selma  partnered with Lockheed Martin  was in competition to receive the Air Force Introductory Flight Training program to provide ground school and flight training for Air Force Officers  It was announced today that the program was awarded to Pueblo  Colorado  teamed with Doss Aviation
