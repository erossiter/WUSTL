SHELBY ANNOUNCES  45 000 GRANT FOR TOWN OF DETROIT     February 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office awarded a  45 000 grant to the Town of Detroit   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA has awarded these funds to the Town of Detroit for the renovation of the existing Town Hall building  The community will use the renovated facility as a medical clinic  I believe it is critical that our communities have the infrastructure necessary to support residents and businesses                                                           February 2006 News Releases
