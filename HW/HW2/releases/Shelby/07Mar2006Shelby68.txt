SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  800 000 FOR CITY OF TUSKEGEE              March 8  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Highway Administration has released  825 000 for US Highway 80 in the City of Tuskegee   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce that the City of Tuskegee will receive over  800 000 from the Federal Highway Administration  It is critical that we support our infrastructure to ensure that our roads are safe for travelers   This funding will repair drainage failure and install a new storm sewer system  Currently  roads are frequently closed or cause delays and driving hazards due to storm runoff
