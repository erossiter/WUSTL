SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  6 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM JEFFERSON COUNTY TRANSIT AUTHORITY   August 18  2006 U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  5 947 323 to the Birmingham Jefferson County Transit Authority for Jefferson County Job Access Reverse Commute Projects  The projects aim to develop new transportation services  including shuttles  vanpools  new bus routes  or connector services to mass transit  and guaranteed ride home programs  for welfare recipients and low income persons      One of the most necessary tools to obtain and keep a steady job is transportation to and from work  said Senator Shelby  This program ensures that     Jefferson    County residents who want to work have a way to affordably get to their shifts on time      Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds during the federal appropriations process
