SHELBY ANNOUNCES 2005 COPS GRANT APPLICATIONS ARE AVAILABLE     May 13  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that the Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  that applications are available for 2005 grants   The following three grants are currently open for select law enforcement agencies      Interoperable Communications Technology Programs  Nearly  100 million is available to law enforcement to improve interoperability between law enforcement agencies an other first responders   Deadline for applying is July 15  2005      Secure Our Schools  SOS  Grant Program  Nearly  15 million is available to law enforcement agencies to purchase school safety equipment and technology   Deadline for applying is June 15  2005      Tribal Resources Grant Program  TRGP    Up to  20 million is available to address the needs of tribal law enforcement by providing a variety of funding options   They include salary and benefits for new sworn law officer positions  law enforcement training  uniforms  basic issue equipment  department wide technology  and vehicles for new and existing officers   Deadline for applying is May 31  2005      Senator Shelby said  I encourage law enforcement agencies to examine these different programs to determine if they are eligible   It is important that we continue working to improve our various law enforcement programs   By investing in their continued growth  we are making a commitment to the safety of our citizens   I believe these are worthwhile programs  and I am pleased the Department of Justice has given agencies the opportunity to apply for these grants  Shelby concluded     For more information on these grants programs  visit         30
