      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces Over  90 000 for Oakwood College    January 25  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration will release  90 572 to the Alabama Department of Transportation  This funding will be used to purchase two 21 passenger buses for Oakwood College  These buses will provide transportation for students on campus  said Senator Shelby  Allowing students to travel to and from classes via bus  rather than driving  will greatly reduce traffic congestion in the area  This facility will truly benefit the students  faculty and staff of Oakwood College  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2005 appropriations process                                                          January 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record                                                                                                                                       Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
