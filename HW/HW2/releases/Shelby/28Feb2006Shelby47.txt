SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 FOR BIBB COUNTY AIRPORT IN CENTREVILLE     February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  146 711 to Bibb County for Bibb County Airport in Centreville   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  100 000 for the Bibb County Airport  This funding will reimburse Bibb County for the purchase of two pieces of land within the protection zones of two runways  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructures to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians                                                           February 2006 News Releases
