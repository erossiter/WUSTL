SHELBY ANNOUNCES  4 MILLION FOR MUSCLE SHOALS FERTILIZER CENTER  FY 2007 State and Foreign Operations Appropriations Bill Passes Senate Appropriations Committee    June 30  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced committee approval today of  4 million in funding for the International Fertilizer Development Center located in Muscle Shoals  Alabama  As a member of the State and Foreign Operations Appropriations Subcommittee  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding  The State and Foreign Operations Appropriations bill will now go to the Senate floor for consideration   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that we were able to provide  4 million to further the work of the International Fertilizer Development Center in Muscle Shoals  In addition to the important research conducted at this facility  the Center employs numerous Muscle Shoals area residents
