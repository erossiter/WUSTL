SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 FOR RESEARCH AT UAB           February 21  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services has awarded a total of  485 060 for two projects at the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for these important programs  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging scientific research  which not only furthers the education of our students  but also allows for important discoveries   The projects were funded through the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development  and the National Center for Research Resources   Funded Projects Include  University of Alabama at Birmingham  Research for Mothers and Children  Genomic Proteomic PTB Network  UAB Clinical   181 250   University of Alabama at Birmingham  Biomedical Research
