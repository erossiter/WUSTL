SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  2 3 MILLION FOR MOBILE REGIONAL AIRPORT     March 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  2 382 780 to the Mobile Airport Authority for the Mobile Regional Airport   I am pleased to announce over  2 3 million for Mobile Regional  said Shelby  This funding will allow for significant expansion to the Mobile Airport  I believe we must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians   The funding will go towards the purchase of new land to continue an ongoing development project for the Mobile Airport  The project includes funding for land to build a new runway  to rehabilitate the terminal building  and to clear growth and buildup under the main runway                                                          March 2006 News Releases
