SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  25 000 FOR HUBBERTVILLE VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT IN FAYETTE COUNTY       February 16  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the Hubbertville Volunteer Fire Department in Fayette County will receive  25 577 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities    These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year     These funds will allow the men and women of the Hubbertville Volunteer Fire Department to be better prepared to respond to the needs of their community   I will continue working in Congress to ensure that our nations first responders have the necessary resources to rapidly respond in the event of an emergency  Sen  Shelby said        30                                                          February 2005 News Releases
