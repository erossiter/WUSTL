SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 TO UAB FOR CANCER RESEARCH    August 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the United States Department of Health and Human Services National Cancer Institute has released  316 265 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham  UAB   The funding will support a cancer treatment research project   So many Americans have struggled with various forms of cancer  said Senator Shelby  As a cancer survivor myself  I believe that research for new treatments is an important priority for medical research community  I am proud that UAB  one of the premiere research institutions in the nation  has taken on this challenge and I am hopeful of the findings that may come out of this project  The project will investigate the use of the E  coli PNP gene to treat certain cancers  including glioma  colon  and head and neck tumors  Specifically  the research aims to develop a way to deliver the gene directly to tumor cells  in hopes to find a way to kill the entire tumor                                                             August 2006 News Releases
