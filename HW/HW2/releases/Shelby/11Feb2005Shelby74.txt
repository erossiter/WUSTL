SHELBY ANNOUNCES  1 MILLION FOR DOTHAN REGIONAL AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Dothan Houston County Airport Authority will receive  1 million from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at the Dothan Regional Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Dothan Regional Airport  These funds will be used for improvements to the runway which is in poor condition due to severe cracking of the pavement surface  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
