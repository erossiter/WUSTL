SHELBY ANNOUNCES  4 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM AIRPORT AUTHORITY      July 27  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will be releasing  4 million to the Birmingham Airport Authority   These funds will be used to remove obstructions at the Birmingham International Airport                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  4 million for the Birmingham Airport Authority   It is an important priority for me that Alabama s airports are given the needed resources so they may remain safe and convenient for the passengers and pilots who use them   The Birmingham International Airport will be able to use these funds to upgrade their facilities and remove obstructions from the area
