SHELBY ANNOUNCES  860 000 FOR THE UNIVERSITY TRANSPORTATION CENTER FOR ALABAMA       June 27  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportations Research and Innovative Technology Administration  RITA  has awarded  860 000 to the University Transportation Center  UTC  for Alabama in Tuscaloosa  The UTC program combines education  research  and technology transfer at its several dozen university based centers across the country   The University Transportation Center at the University of Alabama works to collaborate ideas between the University of Alabama  University of Alabama at Birmingham and University of Alabama in Huntsville to provide technology solutions to the nations transportation problems  said Senator Shelby  These efforts improve the transportation systems across our nation and provide an interactive learning experience for our students  This funding will allow the University of Alabama system to continue to be a leader in our countrys transportation technology   The UTC for Alabama aims to advance technology and expertise in the management and safety of transportation systems  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding through the federal appropriations process                                                           June 2006 News
