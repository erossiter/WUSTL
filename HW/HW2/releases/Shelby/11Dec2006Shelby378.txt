Shelby Announces  2 3 Million for H  Councill Trenholm State Technical College    December 12  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the U S  Department of Labors Presidents Community Based Job Training Initiative has awarded  2 300 000 to H  Councill Trenholm State Technical College   H  Councill Trenholm State Technical College will significantly expand its current nursing program and bring courses to off campus sites in rural counties  said Senator Shelby  Education and training programs are necessary to improve the lives of Alabamians struggling to make ends meet  particularly in the Black Belt region  The United States is in the midst of a nursing shortage  making it essential to educate  recruit and train nurses  This program will bring education services to an underserved area  while providing for significant need in the workforce   The Presidents Community Based Job Training Initiative is a competitive grant program through which proposals are chosen from a nationwide pool  This year the Initiative received 430 applications for this program and selected only 72 winners  H  Councill Trenholm State Technical College is expected to see 485 students enroll in nursing and allied health field programs and 450 incumbent workers receive industry driven training
