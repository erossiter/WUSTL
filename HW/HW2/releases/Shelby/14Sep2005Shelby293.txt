SHELBY  FIRST TO TOUR GULF COAST   Congressional Delegation to tour damaged areas                                                                                                                                                                                                                   September 14  2005   WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that he  along with Majority Leader Bill Frist  R TN   Minority Leader Harry Reid  D NV  and other members of the Senate  will tour damaged areas of the Gulf Coast affected by Hurricane Katrina on Friday  September 16                  Senator Shelby said  I believe this is an important opportunity to show my colleagues the extent of the devastation that Alabama and the Gulf Coast experienced from Hurricane Katrina   As we consider additional relief packages to meet the specific needs of communities across the Gulf Coast  it is critical that Senators witness the devastation first hand                     In addition to Senators Shelby  Frist and Reid  the following Senators will visit the region  Senator Max Baucus  D MT   Senator Thad Cochran  R MS   Senator Susan Collins  R ME   Senator Dick Durbin  D IL   Senator Mike Enzi  R WY   Senator James Inhofe  R OK   Senator Ted Kennedy  D MA   Senator Mary Landrieu  D LA   Senator Joe Lieberman  D CT   Senator Chuck Schumer  D NY   Senator Debbie
