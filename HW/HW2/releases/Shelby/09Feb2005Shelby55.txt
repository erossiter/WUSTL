SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  66 000 FOR THE CITY OF CHELSEA FIRE AND RESCUE IN SHELBY COUNTY     February 9  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that the City of Chelsea Rescue Fire and Rescue in Shelby County will receive  66 960 in federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety  These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities   These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year    These funds will allow the men and women of the City of Chelsea Fire and Rescue to be better prepared to respond to the needs of their community  I will continue working in Congress to ensure that our nations first responders have the necessary resources to rapidly respond in the event of an emergency  Sen  Shelby said                                                         February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
