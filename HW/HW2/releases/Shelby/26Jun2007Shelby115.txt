Shelby Announces  300 000 for Homeless Veterans   Reintegration Program   June 26  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Department of Labors Veterans Employment and Training Service has released  300 000 for Homeless Veterans Reintegration Program at Aletheia House in Birmingham  Alabama   Our nations veterans deserve to be treated with the respect they have earned during their selfless service to our country  said Shelby  This important funding will allow Aletheia House to reach out to our homeless veterans and help them get back on their feet  This funding will provide homeless veterans with customized employment training and support services to assist them in becoming self sufficient and independent through employment
