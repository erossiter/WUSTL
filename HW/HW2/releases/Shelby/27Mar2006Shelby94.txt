SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 3 MILLION TO MOBILE AIRPORT AUTHORITY     March 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has released  1 350 000 to the Mobile Airport Authority for Mobile Regional Airport  These funds will be used to acquire land surrounding the airport for noise abatement for area residents and businesses   I am pleased to announce  1 350 000 for Mobile Regional Airport  said Shelby  I believe it is important to continue to improve the airport and surrounding areas so the Mobile area can maintain its efforts to attract new residents and businesses  while at the same time providing the best transportation services for Alabama   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the federal appropriations process                                                           March 2006 News Releases
