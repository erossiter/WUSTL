SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  140 000 FOR ELBA AIRPORT AUTHORITY      May 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  144 305 to the Elba Airport Authority for Carl Folsom Field   I am pleased to announce  144 305 to Elba Airport Authority for Carl Folsom Field  said Senator Shelby  By purchasing new land  the airport will be able to meet the growing needs of the community  It is important that we support improvements to our airports to ensure the safest and most efficient travel for Alabamians   The funding will allow to airport authority to acquire of land for approaches and remove obstructions from the new land  These improvements are needed to bring the airport up to FAA design standards                                                           May 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
