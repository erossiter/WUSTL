SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  300 000 FOR UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH ALABAMA               March 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services National Heart  Lung  and Blood Institute has awarded  365 000 to the University of South Alabama   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the University of South Alabama has received funding for their Lung Disease Research Program  Strong research universities not only further the education of our students  but also make important strides for the health of Americans                                                           March 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
