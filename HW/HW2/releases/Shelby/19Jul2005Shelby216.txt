SHELBY COMMENDS SUPREME COURT NOMINEE       July 19  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement upon learning of President Bushs nominee to the United States Supreme Court    I am extremely pleased with President Bushs nomination of Judge John G  Roberts  Jr  to the United States Supreme Court   Judge Roberts has a long and distinguished career of public service  serving our country in a number of positions  including his most recent tenure on the D C  Circuit Court of Appeals         As we move forward  I intend to carefully review his judicial philosophy and qualifications for this important position   I look forward to the confirmation process  and I am hopeful that the Senate will operate in a fair manner and afford him an up or down vote  Shelby added          30                                                            July 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
