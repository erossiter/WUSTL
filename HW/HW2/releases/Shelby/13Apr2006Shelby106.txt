SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  16 000 FOR CITY OF HOOVER LAW ENFORCEMENT       May 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice has awarded  16 631 to the City of Hoover for the Hoover Police Department   I am pleased to announce  16 631 for the Hoover Police Department  said Senator Shelby  I believe it is critical that our law enforcement have the best technology so that they can effectively do their jobs  These funds will allow the Hoover Police Department to purchase needed equipment to increase public safety   The Hoover Police Department will use the funding to purchase handheld radios  replacing their current system  which is over 20 years old  The improved technology will increase officer and public safety  as well as ensure communication between the department and its surrounding areas   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs
