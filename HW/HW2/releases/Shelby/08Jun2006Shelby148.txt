SHELBY ANNOUNCES  500 000 FOR MUSCLE SHOALS      June 12  2006   U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   announced today that the City of Muscle Shoals will receive  500 000 from the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC   The funding will support development of an industrial park   I am pleased to announce this funding  said Senator Shelby  This project will allow Muscle Shoals to take advantage of the great benefits the automotive industry has provided to many other parts of Alabama  I believe it is critical that local communities provide the necessary infrastructure to attract new businesses and bring more jobs to the area   The project will widen a street  and construct an access road  water lines  sewer lines  and a 500 000 gallon water storage tank in anticipation of a manufacturing firm coming to the area  Upon arrival  the firm is expected to create 320 jobs and invest  21 million in private capital within two years  As the industrial park and neighboring land are further developed  additional firms are expected to create more jobs and investment in the area                                                           June 2006 News Releases
