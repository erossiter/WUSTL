Shelby Announces  54 245 for Beulah Volunteer Fire Department   November 9  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  54 245 to the Beulah Volunteer Fire Department in Boaz  Alabama  The funding is provided through the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Alabamas first responders must have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Shelby  Beulah Volunteer Fire Department will use this funding for their operations and safety programs  This grant is evidence of our nations commitment to our first responders  The Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency awarded these funds as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  490 million in direct federal assistance to fire departments and nonaffiliated emergency medical service organizations nationwide this year                                                         November 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
