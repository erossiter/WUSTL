Shelby Announces  495 630 for University of Alabama Transportation System    March 9  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration will release  494 630 to the University of Alabama to expand transportation options in West Alabama  particularly for the disabled  The funding will be distributed by the Alabama Disabilities Advocacy Program  ADAP    Not having access to transportation can make everyday tasks  such as running errands  going to work  or going to the doctors office nearly impossible  said Senator Shelby  This funding will help West Alabamians  particularly the disabled  overcome barriers by providing safe and convenient transportation  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2004 appropriations process  These funds will be matched by local sources  resulting in a total of nearly  1 million for projects providing direct transportation                                                         March 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
