SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 TO ANDALUSIA OPP AIRPORT AUTHORITY    August 22  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Aviation Administration will release  150 000 to the Andalusia Opp Airport   Expanding the infrastructure at the Andalusia Opp Airport will allow the airport to further meet the needs of the growing community  said Senator Shelby  The improved airport will also help spur economic growth throughout the community by attracting new businesses and residents   The funding awarded in this grant will allow the airport to construct a hanger to accommodate business aircraft  Currently  the airports hangars are full and there is a waiting list to accommodate additional aircraft  The airport will also fund a new fuel pumping system                                                         August 2006 News Releases
