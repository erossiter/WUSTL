Shelby Announces Over  109 000 for Falkville Volunteer Fire Department    February 1  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  109 459 to the Falkville Volunteer Fire Department in Morgan County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Firefighters are a key component to our nations first response efforts  and therefore they must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  said Senator Shelby  These funds will help the Falkville Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                           February 2007 News Releases                      Year
