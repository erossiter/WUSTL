SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  500 000 TO AUBURN UNIVERSITY FOR FORENSIC SCIENCE RESEARCH    September 11  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  533 746 to Auburn University for forensic science research   Forensic science testing can provide our crime fighters with indisputable results  said Senator Shelby  Whether working to solve a case or determine a cause of death  forensic testing can provide insight into an investigation that can be critical to law enforcement officers  I am proud that this important research is taking place at Auburn University  which is on the forefront of this field  Auburn University will use these funds to further their forensic science research and training programs  Specifically  this project will work to improve analytical methods used to identify certain drugs  As some controlled substances appear the same after testing  this research will seek to unequivocally determine which substance is found in the specimen                                                          September
