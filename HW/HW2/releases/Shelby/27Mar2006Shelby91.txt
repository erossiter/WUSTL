SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 FOR ROANOKE MUNICIPAL AIRPORT      March 27  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  103 356 to the Roanoke Municipal Airport for the purchase of additional land   I am pleased to announce  103 356 for Roanoke Municipal Airport  said Senator Shelby  This funding will allow the airport to purchase additional land to extend the runway protection zone  I believe we must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians   Funds provided by this project will improve the safety for pilots in take off and landing by lengthening the approach area
