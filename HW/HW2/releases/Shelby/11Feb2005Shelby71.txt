SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 FOR DEMOPOLIS MUNICIPAL AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the City of Demopolis will receive  150 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at the Demopolis Municipal Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Demopolis Municipal Airport  These funds will be used to construct additional hangars needed to accommodate an increased number of aircraft at the airport  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                          February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
