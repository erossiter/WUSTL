SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  570 000 FOR TUSCALOOSA REGIONAL AIRPORT    August 22  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Aviation Administration will release  570 567 to the City of Tuscaloosa for Tuscaloosa Regional Airport  This funding will be used to upgrade airport rescue fire fighting equipment   It is critical that our airports be prepared to respond to disaster  should it strike  said Senator Shelby  A new firefighting vehicle will help the Tuscaloosa Regional Airport protect the pilots and passengers utilizing the airport   The airports current firefighting vehicle is nearly 30 years old  and the new vehicle will allow the airport to come into compliance with federal safety regulations                                                         August 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
