SHELBY ANNOUNCES  500 000 FOR TALLADEGA MUNICIPAL AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that City of Talladega will receive  500 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at Talladega Municipal Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Talladega Municipal Airport  These funds will be used to relocate the taxiway which is currently too close to the runway  The relocation of the taxiway will enhance the safety of operations at the airport  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                          February 2005 News Releases
