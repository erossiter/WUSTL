Shelby Announces More Than  38 000 for Maplesville Volunteer Fire and Rescue    November 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  38 916 to the Maplesville Volunteer Fire and Rescue  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Alabamas first responders must have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  The Maplesville Volunteer Fire and Rescue will use this funding for operations and safety  This grant is evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year
