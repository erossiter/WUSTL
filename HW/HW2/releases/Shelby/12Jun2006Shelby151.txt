SHELBY ANNOUNCES  100 000 FOR THE CITY OF ALICEVILLE                        June 12  2006   WASHINGTON  D  C      U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   announced today that the City of Aliceville will receive  100 000 from the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC   The funding will be used for water system improvements to a city owned industrial property in Pickens County   I am pleased to announce this funding  said Senator Shelby  It is critical that local communities have the infrastructure necessary to support area businesses  This funding will make significant improvements to the areas water supply  which will better serve the Aliceville and Pickens County area industry   The project will upgrade a high capacity well and a water storage tank by replacing pumps  controls  and treatment facilities at the wellhead  as well as cleaning and repainting the storage tank  The project will also construct a backup waterline connection to the Pickens County Water Authoritys system  This project is expected to create 14 new positions                                                          June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
