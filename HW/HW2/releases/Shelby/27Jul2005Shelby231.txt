SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 MILLION FOR GUNTERSVILLE MUNICIPAL AIRPORT     July 27  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will be releasing  1 073164 to the Guntersville Municipal Airport   These funds will be used to acquire land for the Guntersville Municipal Airport                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  1 million for the Guntersville Municipal Airport   These funds will be used to acquire land for the development and expansion of the airport so that it may better accommodate the needs of the fast growing Guntersville area   After this expansion takes place  larger business jets will be able to safely land on the airports runway
