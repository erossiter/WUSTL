Shelby Announces  152 536 for the University of Alabama    May 3  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  152 536 to the University of Alabamas Export Trade Financing Program  EXTRA   EXTRA will use the funding to assist small businesses throughout the Appalachian region of Alabama in generating  9 million in new export sales and creating 40 new jobs   EXTRA is a powerful resource for businesses in economically distressed regions of Alabama  said Senator Shelby  This funding will address the lack of export financing and information available for Appalachian Alabamas small businesses  The Alabama International Trade Center  part of the University Center for Economic Development  will deliver the funding to the most economically distressed sections of the Appalachian region of Alabama  The University of Alabama will also provide  115 250  which brings the total project award to  267 786                                                         May 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
