Shelby Announces Reopening of Disaster Loan Program for Small Business Owners   June 27  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Small Business Administration will again offer Economic Injury Disaster Loans  EIDL  to Gulf Coast small business owners who were affected by the 2005 Gulf Coast hurricanes   The 2005 Hurricane Season was one of the most destructive on record  said Shelby  It is critical that we aid the people  businesses and communities of Alabama and the Gulf Coast who were affected by these natural disasters  Pre existing small businesses that were located in declared disaster areas after Hurricane Dennis  Katrina  Rita and Wilma can apply for EDIL until December 31  2007  For a list of declared counties  go to   To apply  small businesses can visit the SBAs website at  to download an application  or they may contact the agencys Customer Service Center at 1 800 659 2955  Small business may also request an
