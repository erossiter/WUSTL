SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  3 3 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM INTERMODAL FACILITY    September 8  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  3 398 058 to the City of Birmingham for its Intermodal Facility  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2005 appropriations process  It is imperative that we work to improve Birminghams current public transportation system  said Senator Shelby  As the population of the Birmingham metropolitan area has expanded  inadequate public transportation options and lengthy commutes have had an impact on productivity  Upon completion  Phase II will transform the current facility into a fully integrated multi modal center   The funding provided will go towards Phase II of the Intermodal facility project  expanding the Morris Avenue bus transfer facility in downtown Birmingham  Once implemented  this service would enhance the Intermodal center as the major hub for an improved mass transit system for the entire Birmingham region  The center will accommodate various modes of transportation  local public transit  intercity bus  intercity train  hotel and airport shuttles  bicycle  taxi  passenger car  and van pool services
