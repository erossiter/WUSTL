SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  163 000 FOR COMMERCIAL EQUIPMENT DIRECT ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS    May 20  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that  163 240 will be released from the U S  Department of Homeland Security for Commercial Equipment Direct Assistance Programs  CEDAP  throughout Alabama    These funds will be used to provide smaller law enforcement and emergency responder agencies with equipment items that will enhance and support regional response  mutual aid  and interoperability of responder equipment   CEDAP recognizes the importance of new equipment education and training to ensure the proper operation and deployment of the equipment         Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  163 000 for Commercial Equipment Direct Assistance Programs across Alabama   It is important that we work to ensure law enforcement and first responders have the necessary knowledge and equipment to keep Alabamas communities safe       The funds will be allocated as follows        Alexander City Fire Department will receive  12 500 for Thermal Imaging Equipment        Atmore Police Department will receive  14 620 for a Search Camera Victim Locator System        Brick Hatton Volunteer Fire Department will receive  12 500 for Thermal Imaging Equipment        Calera Fire Department will receive  12 500 for Thermal Imaging Equipment
