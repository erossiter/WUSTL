SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  750 000 FOR THREE FIRE DEPARTMENTS IN JEFFERSON COUNTY      February 23  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today announced that three fire departments in Jefferson County will receive federal funds to assist with operations and firefighter safety   These funds are available for training and fitness  protective equipment  and modifications to fire stations and facilities        622 975   Birmingham Fire and Rescue Service         103 050   Mountain Brook Fire Department         31 575     Argo Fire and Rescue      These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program   This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year     This is great news for these Jefferson County fire departments  These grants provide critical support for our firefighters and local law enforcement  and I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security is following through on its commitment to our nations first responders  Sen  Shelby said        30
