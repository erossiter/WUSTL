Shelby Announces  6 5 Million for Rebuilding High School in Enterprise   June 15  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  will release  6 498 031 24 to the state of Alabama to replace Enterprise High School  which was destroyed by tornadoes on March 1  2007  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding   After the tornadoes hit  I visited Enterprise twice  said Shelby  What I saw there was devastating  I know that the people of Enterprise will never recover from the loss of life  However  I also saw a resilient community that will rebuild from this terrible disaster  I look forward to seeing a new  strong  and safe Enterprise High School
