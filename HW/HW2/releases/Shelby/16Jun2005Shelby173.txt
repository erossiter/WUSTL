SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  804 000 FOR HOME OWNERSHIP PROGRAMS IN BESSEMER     June 17  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the City of Bessemer will receive a Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  in the amount of  804 662    The CDBG program is administrated by the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  to provide home ownership opportunities to all Americans   These grants allow state and local governments develop urban communities by providing housing and economic opportunities for low and moderate income individuals       Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  804 662 for this important home ownership program in Bessemer   These funds will provide more low income families in the area with the opportunity to own a home and achieve a piece of the American dream          30                                                            June 2005 News Releases                      Year
