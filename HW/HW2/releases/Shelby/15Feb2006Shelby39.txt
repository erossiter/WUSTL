SHELBY ANNOUNCES  9 5 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM AIRPORT                                 February 16  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will release  9 5 million to the Birmingham International Airport  These funds will be used to acquire land surrounding the airport for noise abatement for area residents and businesses   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  9 5 million to assist the Birmingham International Airport  I believe it is important to continue to improve the airport and surrounding areas so the Birmingham area can maintain its efforts to attract new residents and businesses  while at the same time providing the best transportation services for Alabama
