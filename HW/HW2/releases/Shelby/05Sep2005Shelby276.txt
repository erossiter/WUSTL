SHELBY ANNOUNCES  49 000 FOR ALABAMA CRIMINAL JUSTICE INFORMATION CENTER       September 7  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that U S  Department of Justice will be releasing  49 899 to the Alabama Criminal Justice Information Center in Montgomery for the State Justice Statistics  SJS  Program for Statistical Analysis Centers  SAC       The SJS Program will enhance Alabamas capacity to address criminal justice issues through the collection and analysis of data   These funds will enable Alabama to conduct research to estimate impacts of legislative and policy changes within the State   This project will be completed by the Alabama Statistical Analysis Center  SAC  which is located within the Alabama Criminal Justice Information Center        Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  49 000 for the Alabama Criminal Justice Information Center   These funds will be used to facilitate the SJS Program which will allow Alabama to better understand the impact of policy changes as they work address the criminal justice issues of the State                                                 30
