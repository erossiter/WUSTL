SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 MILLION FOR HUNTSVILLE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority will receive  3 396 302 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at the Huntsville International Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Huntsville International Airport  These funds will be used to expand and improve the terminal building to more efficiently accommodate passengers  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                         February 2005 News Releases
