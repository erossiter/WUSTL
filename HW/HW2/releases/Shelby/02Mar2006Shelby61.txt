SHELBY ANNOUNCES  2 MILLION FOR HUNTSVILLE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT       March 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  2 000 000 to the Huntsville Madison County Airport Authority for Huntsville International Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce  2 000 000 for Huntsville International Airport  The funding will go towards the construction of a taxiway to improve pavement and taxiway clearances  It is critical that we ensure the safest and most efficient air travel possible for Alabamians                                                             March 2006 News Releases
