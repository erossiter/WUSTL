      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  660 000 FOR THE AMERICAN VILLAGE IN MONTEVALLO     April 27  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that  661 333 will be released from the Department of Transportation for transportation improvements at the American Village in Montevallo  AL    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the Department of Transportation is releasing these funds so the American Village can make necessary improvements at their educational campus in Montevallo   Specifically  these funds will be used for the construction of an access road  bus lanes  and a parking facility   The American Village helps educate its visitors on the foundations of our country  and I was pleased to support this project     Senator Shelby secured these funds in the fiscal year 2005 federal appropriations process        30                                                          April 2005 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                Current record                                                                           Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
