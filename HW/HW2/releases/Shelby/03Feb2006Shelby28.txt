SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  400 000 FOR NEUROLOGY RESEARCH AT UAB                   February 7  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services has released a total of  411 038 for two projects at the University of Alabama at Birmingham   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for these important programs  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging neurological research  which furthers the education of our students  and makes important strides for the health of Americans   The projects were funded through the National Institute of Neurological Disorders and Stroke and the National Institute of Mental Health   Funded Projects Include  University of Alabama at Birmingham  GSK3  Neural Modulators and Mood Disorders  Mental Health Research Grants   247 350   University of Alabama at Birmingham  The Role of ING4 in Modulating the Tumorigenic Contribution of NF KB in Gliomas  Clinical Research Related to Neurological Disorders   163 688
