SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 FOR AUTAUGA COUNTY AIRPORT    February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that Autauga County will receive  150 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at the Autauga County Airport   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for the Autauga County Airport  These funds will be used to construct a new fuel farm in the terminal area as well as continue expansion of the apron to meet the expected future demands of the airport  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                         February 2005 News Releases                      Year
