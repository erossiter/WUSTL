Shelby Announces  34 200 for Magnolia Springs Volunteer Fire Department    January 11  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  34 200 to the Magnolia Springs Volunteer Fire Department in Baldwin County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   Firefighters are a key component to our nations first response efforts  and therefore they must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  said Senator Shelby  These funds will help the Magnolia Springs Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  This funding will assist in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                          January 2007 News Releases
