SHELBY ANNOUNCES  300 000 FOR WEEDON FIELD IN EUFAULA     February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the City of Eufaula will receive  300 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at Weedon Field   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Weedon Field  These funds will be used to construct a hangar needed to accommodate an increased number of aircraft at the airport  I was pleased to play a role in securing these funds so the airport may continue work on its improvement efforts    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program
