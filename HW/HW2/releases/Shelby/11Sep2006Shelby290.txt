SHELBY ANNOUNCES MORE THAN  160 000 FOR DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND SEXUAL ASSAULT PROGRAMS    September 15  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice  DOJ  will release  83 041 to the Alabama Coalition Against Rape and  83 041 to the Alabama Coalition Against Domestic Violence  The funding will provide support to rape crisis centers     Many times  victims of domestic violence stay in unhealthy relationships because they are economically dependent on their abuser  said Senator Shelby  The Alabama Coalition Against Domestic Violence and the Alabama Coalition Against Rape provide shelter to allow victims to escape from their abusers  Once they are out of their abusers reach  victims can begin to rebuild their lives  safely and independently    The funding provided will allow the Coalitions to train and provide technical assistance  as well as increase public awareness about these crimes  This funding is provided by the Grants to Sexual Assault and Domestic Violence Coalitions Program  which supports state coalition efforts  and encourages collaboration between federal  state  and local rape response centers  The Alabama Coalition Against Domestic Violence maintains a 24 hour crisis phone line and provides safe shelters across the state  among other services for victims and their children  The Alabama Coalition Against Rape treats sexual assault victims  works to increase the number of reported assaults  and improve the investigation and prosecution of sexual assaulters                                                          September 2006 News Releases                      Year
