SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  327 000 FOR UAB CENTER FOR COMPOSITE MANUFACTURING      September 14  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R Ala  announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation will soon be releasing  327 607 to the University of Alabama at Birmingham Center for Composite Manufacturing   These funds will be used to develop lighter weight  lower cost  higher performing materials for buses and other mass transit applications                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that over  327 000 will be coming to the University of Alabama at Birmingham Center for Composite Manufacturing   These funds will enable the center to accelerate applications of new technologies which will provide substantial improvements to performance  durability  safety and manufacturing of various forms of mass transit                     Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds in the federal appropriations process              30
