SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR GIRLS INCORPORATED OF HUNTSVILLE    June 21  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  246 807 to Girls Incorporated of Huntsville   I am pleased to announce  246 807 for Girls Incorporated of Huntsville  said Senator Shelby  This program will encourage girls to keep their focus on schoolwork and improve their family relationship  which will help them resist the risky behaviors that can adversely affect our youth   Through the implementation of this program  Girls Incorporated of Huntsville will help approximately 300 girls from ages 5 15 and their families to improve school performance  improve family relationships  and address risk factors among girls in this age group  such as alcohol and substance abuse  obesity  and teenage pregnancy   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding in
