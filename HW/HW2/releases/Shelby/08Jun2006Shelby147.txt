SHELBY ON THE DEATH OF ABU MUSAB AL ZARQAWI         June 8  2006   U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL  today made the following statement about the death of the terrorist Abu Musab al Zarqawi   Al Zarqawi was the operational brains of the Al Qaeda network in Iraq  He repeatedly encouraged violence against Americans and Iraqi citizens  His death marks the silencing of a brutal terrorist  Our military forces worked tirelessly with their Iraqi counterparts to locate Al Zarqawi and should be commended for their dedication and efforts  Although the Global War on Terrorism is not over  this is a significant development as we continue to fight this ruthless enemy                                                           June 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
