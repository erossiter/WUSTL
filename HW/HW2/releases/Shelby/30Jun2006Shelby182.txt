SHELBY ANNOUNCES  198 000 FOR MONTGOMERY AREA TRANSIT SYSTEM    July 5  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration  FTA  has awarded  198 000 to the City of Montgomery for the Montgomery Area Transit System  MATS   The funds will be used to improve the bus system in the Montgomery area   Citizens throughout Alabama rely on public transportation to get to work  to receive medical care  and to visit with family and friends  said Senator Shelby  With rising gas prices and Alabamians working to reduce their energy usage  the bus system is more important than ever  As more people begin to utilize public transportation  it is imperative we work to improve the current system   The funding will allow the City of Montgomery to construct and purchase bus stops and shelters throughout the MATS service area  Montgomery will also purchase a next bus real time global positioning system  GPS  or automatic vehicle locator system for the MATS fleet of buses  This GPS system will allow MATS to improve its performance and scheduling requirements
