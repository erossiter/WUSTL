      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     Shelby Announces  273 570 for Alabama Parent Education Center     May 17  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Education has granted  273 570 to the Alabama Parent Education Center for a program that helps parents of disabled children understand their childs disability and learn ways to obtain government assistance to ensure that their children receive proper education   The Alabama Parent Education Center will assist families with disabled children in accessing educational opportunities  said Shelby  This program will help support parents of children with disabilities by providing training and information to parents to help them work collaboratively  communicate effectively  and become informed about available resources                                                         May 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record                                                                                                                        Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
