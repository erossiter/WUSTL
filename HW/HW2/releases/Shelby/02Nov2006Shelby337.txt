Shelby Announces Over  850 000 for US 80 Near Demopolis    November 2  2006 U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportations Federal Highway Administration  FHWA  will release  855 050 to widen the existing US 80 to four lanes   US 80 is a major east west corridor through West Alabama that places significant pressure on our transportation infrastructure  said Senator Shelby  Increased traffic congestion slows travel and clogs roads  leading to increased accidents and lengthened travel time  This funding will ease traffic congestion by expanding Highway 80 to four lanes between I 20 59 and Demopolis   US 80 from SR 17 to CR 71  Bellamy Road  will become four lanes through the construction of new lanes and bridges parallel to the existing lanes  This construction  one of four projects to complete the four laning  will include grade  drain  base  pave and bridge work  This funding will allow the expansion of 7 31 miles of the 22 5 miles that will be developed by the completion of all four projects                                                         November 2006 News Releases
