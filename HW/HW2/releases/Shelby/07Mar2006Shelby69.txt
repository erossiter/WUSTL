SHELBY ANNOUNCES  800 000 FOR BUTLER COUNTY            March 8  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Department of Transportation Federal Highway Administration has released  800 000 for US Highway 31 and SR 55 in Butler County   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce that Butler County will receive  800 000 from the Federal Highway Administration  It is critical that we support our infrastructure to ensure that our roads are safe for travelers and the efficient flow of commerce   This funding will complete a new 4 lane north and south corridor from Andalusia to I 65 west of Georgiana  The corridor will provide safer and more efficient access to I 65
