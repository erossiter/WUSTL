Shelby   North Korea  s continued cooperation critical for successful disarmament    February 13  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following comments today regarding the tentative nuclear disarmament agreement reached with North Korea  Senator Shelby said  While this accord is an important step towards a denuclearized North Korea  it is only the first step  We must remain vigilant to ensure that North Korea follows through on the agreement to abandon their nuclear weapons program and allow IAEA inspections  North Koreas actions over the last decade underscore the fact that those who are determined will find a way to threaten the world  regardless of the agreements in which they participate  Their continued cooperation is critical for successful disarmament of the Korean Peninsula                                                          February 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
