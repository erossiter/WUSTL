SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 3 MILLION FOR CITY OF ASHLAND    August 29  2006  Washington  D C       U  S  Sen  Richard C  Shelby  R Ala   announced today that the City of Ashland will receive  1 397 000 from the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC   The funding will be used to upgrade a water transmission line in Ashland  Millerville and Meadow Valley     It is critical that local communities have a strong infrastructure in place to support area businesses  said Senator Shelby  An upgraded transmission line will allow the City of Ashland to better support the three large industrial employers in the area  Such upgrades are essential for these companies to expand  grow  and create more jobs  In addition  strengthening the infrastructure will make Ashland more attractive to other businesses looking for new locations    The current water transmission line is old  in poor condition  and far too small to effectively serve the Citys water volume needs  The line currently serves approximately 1 665 residential  commercial  and industrial users in the area  The expansion planned by one of the water lines industrial users is expected to create 40 new jobs
