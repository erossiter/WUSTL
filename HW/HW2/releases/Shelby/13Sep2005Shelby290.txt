SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  970 000 FOR ORANGE BEACH       September 14  2005   WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science and Related Agencies Appropriations Subcommittee  CJS   today announced that the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  will soon be releasing  976 479 for the City of Orange Beach to assist with their acquisition of Robinson Island                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available for the City of Orange Beach to acquire Robinson Island in order to preserve and protect the islands natural habitat and resources   Robinson Island is home to beaches  marshes  and wooded areas and these funds will help ensure it is protected                     Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds in the fiscal year 2005 appropriations process            30                                                            September 2005 News Releases
