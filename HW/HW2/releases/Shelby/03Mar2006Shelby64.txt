SHELBY ANNOUNCES ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT MEETING FOR OIL AND GAS LEASING PROGRAM IN MOBILE     March 6  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the United States Department of the Interior will host a meeting in Mobile to discuss the environmental impact of the proposed Oil and Gas Leasing Program  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that the Department of the Interior is holding this public meeting  Those citizens interested in learning more about the Oil and Gas Leasing Program should attend this important meeting  This will provide individuals an opportunity to express their views and I encourage Alabamians to let their voices be heard   The meeting will provide an opportunity for interested citizens and organizations to provide input on the scope  significant issues  and alternatives to be considered in the environmental impact statement for the proposed Oil and Gas Leasing Program   WHO  Interested Citizens and the U S  Department of the Interior WHAT  Public Meeting WHEN  March 30  2006  7 00 p m  WHERE  Riverview Plaza Hotel  64 South Water Street in Mobile  For more information or to submit your comments electronically  please
