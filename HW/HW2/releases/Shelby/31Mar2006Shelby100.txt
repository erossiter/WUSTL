      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY OPPOSES HAGEL MARTINEZ IMMIGRATION PACKAGE    April 6  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  made the following statement expressing his opposition to the Hagel Martinez Immigration package   I believe this bill asks us to turn a blind eye to those who have broken the law  It rewards illegal immigrants by granting them amnesty and allowing them to cut in line ahead of millions of people who have followed the law and waited patiently in their home countries  I believe this bill sends the wrong message and it is nothing more than a repeat of the past    which was a failed attempt to reform our broken immigration system by providing amnesty to illegal immigrants  When we look back on this bill 20 years from now  I believe we will conclude that this effort only emboldened those coming to the United States illegally   In short  we will never be successful in our efforts to eliminate illegal immigration as long as Congress continues to pass legislation that rewards it                                                              April 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                       Current record                                                                                                                                       Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
