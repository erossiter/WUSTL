SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  200 000 FOR FAMILY SUCCESS CENTER OF ETOWAH COUNTY    August 31  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice will release  246 807 for the Family Success Center of Etowah County  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing these funds during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process   Strong family relationships give children a secure  nurturing environment during their formative years  said Senator Shelby  The Family Success Center of Etowah County works to decrease violence  child abuse  and neglect  The absence of these horrible acts allows children  our most vulnerable citizens  to grow up safely and become contributing members of society  The Family Success Center of Etowah County works to promote the well being of children and improve the lives of adults by strengthening the family  With the funding provided  families will receive resources and direct services to decrease the risk of child abuse and other violent behavior                                                        August 2006
