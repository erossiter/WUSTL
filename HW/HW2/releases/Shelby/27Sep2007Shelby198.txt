Shelby Announces  200 000 for the Town of Gainsville   September 28  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Delta Regional Authority  DRA  will award  200 000 to the Town of Gainsville  Alabama  for the renovation of a wastewater treatment lagoon  This renovation is the first phase of developing a sewage system for north Sumter County  This funding is good news for the residents of Gainsville and the other towns in north Sumter County  said Shelby  For far too long  residents in north Sumter County have not had access to sanitary sewage facilities  Upon completion of Phase I and II  this project may provide several hundred households  presently being served by septic tanks  with sewage service DRA grants target projects that will improve infrastructure in distressed counties  develop transportation infrastructure that will facilitate economic development and job training programs that work with existing public education institutions  The DRA serves 240 counties and parishes in parts of Alabama  Arkansas  Illinois  Kentucky  Louisiana  Mississippi  Missouri and Tennessee
