SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 6 MILLION FOR PROGRAMS AT UAB     February 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the United States Department of Health and Human Services has awarded  1 660 164 for five medical programs at the University of Alabama at Birmingham   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that UAB has received funding for these important programs  UAB continues to be on the forefront of emerging medical research  which not only furthers a quality  hands on education for our students  but also allows for important medical breakthroughs  Projects funded include   Allergy  Immunology  and Transplantation Research  Biophysical Studies of HIV Assembly and Maturation   327 375    Allergy  Immunology  and Transplantation Research  Ecology of Encephalitis Viruses in the USA   547 650   Allergy  Immunology  and Transplantation Research  Regulation of B Cell Clonal Diversity   363 750   Vision Research  Planning a Clinical Trial on Low Vision Rehabilitation   145 500   Kidney Diseases  Urology  and Hematology Research  Regulation and Signaling of PKD2   275 889
