      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY ANNOUNCES  550 000 TO EAST ALABAMA CONSOLIDATED AIRPORT AUTHORITY FOR LANETT MUNICIPAL      April 13  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has awarded  550 000 to East Alabama Consolidated Airport Authority for Lanett Municipal   I am pleased to announce  550 000 to East Alabama Consolidated Airport Authority for Lanett Municipal  said Shelby  I believe it is important to continue to improve the airport and surrounding areas so that the Lanett area can maintain its efforts to attract new residents and businesses  while at the same time providing the best transportation services for Alabamians   The project will rehabilitate the pavement on Runway 8 26  Over the past 20 years  the runway pavement has experienced sever weathering  requiring rehabilitation to maintain safety and operations at the airport                                                          April 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                     Current record                                                                                                         Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
