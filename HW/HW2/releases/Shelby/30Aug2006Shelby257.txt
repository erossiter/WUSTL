SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  200 000 FOR HAYNEVILLE LOWNDES COUNTY PUBLIC LIBRARY    August 30  2006  Washington  D C       U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development has released  198 000 to the Hayneville Lowndes County Public Library  The funding will be used for preliminary construction of the library  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process  Public libraries are an invaluable resource to our communities  said Senator Shelby  Parents and students alike can access important learning tools at our public libraries  such as the internet  encyclopedias  reference materials  and research texts  Having a strong  vibrant public library will allow generations of Alabamians to have a lifetime of learning and enjoyment through reading  The current Hayneville Lowndes County Public library is in disrepair  and community officials will use the funding provided to construct a new facility  The Hayneville Lowndes County Public library is the central facility for the library system that serves Lowndes County  so it is important that it meet the needs of the entire community                                                          August 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
