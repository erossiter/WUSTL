SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  40 000 FOR SELMA AND DALLAS COUNTY LAW ENFORCEMENT      May 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the Department of Justice has awarded  40 093 to Dallas County and the City of Selma for their local law enforcement   I am pleased to announce  40 093 for Selma and Dallas County law enforcement  said Senator Shelby  This funding will allow Selma and Dallas County to target drug and high crime areas  which is essential for citizens to feel safe and secure in their homes and communities   Both the Selma Police Department and Dallas County Sheriffs Office will use these funds for police overtime  Officers in both jurisdictions will patrol and target drug and high crime areas during overtime hours   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs  In this case  the departments have agreed to share the funding                                                           May 2006 News
