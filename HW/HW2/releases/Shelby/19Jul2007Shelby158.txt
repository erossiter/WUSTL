Shelby Announces  1 2 Million for Cummings Research Park in Huntsville   July 24  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportations  DOT  Federal Transit Administration will release  1 133 550 for the Intermodal Facility at Cummings Research Park   The Intermodal Center at Cummings Research Park is a vital addition that will help meet the needs of those working and living near the research park  said Shelby  The Center will provide alternate means of transportation and serve as a regional transit transfer facility for those traveling to and from Cummings Research Park   Cummings Research Park is one of the worlds premier science and technology parks  Home to more than 200 companies  including 20 FORTUNE 500 companies  it is the second largest park in the U S  and the fourth largest in the world                                                         July 2007 News Releases
