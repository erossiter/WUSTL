SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  50 000 FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN EAST ALABAMA      July 18  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  today announced that the U S  Department of Commerces Economic Development Administration  EDA  will release  53 500 to the East Alabama Planning and Development Commission for economic development in Calhoun County  The Commission works with the local governments of 10 east Alabama counties to assist in expanding the areas economy   Through Anniston Army Depot and other strong industries  Calhoun County has the substantial infrastructure and well trained work force in place to attract new businesses and industry  said Senator Shelby  Further investment in preparing the area for incoming businesses will go a long way in drawing the type of good paying quality jobs East Alabama needs   The Commission serves municipal and county governments and their citizens to promote the area  aiming to improve quality of life  create new opportunities  and aid economic development  The Commission  through planning and advocacy  helps bring the region together through the common goal of bringing jobs and growth to the area                                                            July 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
