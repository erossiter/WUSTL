SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 7 MILLION FOR BIRMINGHAM PUBLIC TRANSIT    September 26  2006  U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release a total of  1 721 438 to the Birmingham Jefferson County Transit Authority  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2003 appropriations process    As the population of the Birmingham metropolitan area has expanded  inadequate public transportation and lengthy commutes have burdened area residents  said Senator Shelby  It is imperative that we continue to improve the public transportation system  These new buses will provide citizens in Birmingham and the surrounding areas with the means to efficiently and safely move throughout the region     The Birmingham Jefferson County Transit Authority will use  983 679 to purchase three new buses  The buses will be used in Hoover and Vestavia Hills and will utilize alternative fuels     The Transit Authority will use  737 759 to purchase two diesel hybrid electric buses for Jefferson County
