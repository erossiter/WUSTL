SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  100 000 TO LAWRENCE COUNTY      March 9  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  will release  137 450 to Lawrence County for the Lawrence County Airport in Courtland   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  100 000 for the Lawrence County Airport  This project will rehabilitate the apron and runway  as the existing concrete has begun to deteriorate  The funding will also conduct an Airport Master Plan Study  We must continue to improve our airport infrastructure to ensure the safest and most efficient air travel for Alabamians                                                            March 2006 News Releases
