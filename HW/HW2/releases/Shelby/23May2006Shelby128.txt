SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  400 000 FOR ARGO FIRE AND RESCUE IN ST  CLAIR COUNTY     May 24  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  today announced that the U S  Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  ODP  has released  399 197 to Argo Fire and Rescue in St  Clair County   I am pleased that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded these funds to the Argo Fire and Rescue  said Shelby  It is critical that our federal  state and local governments work together to ensure that our fire departments are appropriately staffed to effectively respond to emergencies in the communities they support  Senator Shelby said   This funding is awarded through the ODPs Assistance to Firefighters Grant  AFG  program  which provides direct grants to local fire departments for hiring and volunteer recruitment  The SAFER grant program  a division of AFG  serves to enhance the capability of local fire departments to respond to fire and other emergency incidents  along with other community related activities that further the traditional mission of local fire departments                                                           May 2006
