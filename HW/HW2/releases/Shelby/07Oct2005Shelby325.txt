SHELBY PLEASED BY GROUNDBREAKING OF PHASE II OF VON BRAUN COMPLEX     October 11  2005   WASHINGTON  DC    U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Defense Appropriations Subcommittee  issued the following statement after the groundbreaking of Phase II of the Von Braun Complex at Redstone Arsenal in Huntsville                  Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that todays groundbreaking ceremony marks the beginning of the construction for Phase II of the Von Braun Complex   Upon the completion of this building  the Missile Defense Agency will be able to consolidate personnel in a state of the art facility   By investing in the growth and improvement of Redstone Arsenal  we continue to renew our commitment to national security and to the men and women who serve our country           This new building will be the home of the Missile Defense Agency  MDA    Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing more than  38 million in the fiscal year 2004 and fiscal year 2005 appropriations bills for the construction of Phase II of the Von Braun Complex           The construction of this building is scheduled to be finished in May 2007 and upon its completion will have office space for approximately 950 people   More specifically  the offices of the Missile Defense Agencys Ground based Midcourse Defense  Joint Program Office  GMD JPO   the Missile Defense Data Center  MDDC   the Optical Discrimination Analysis Offices  ODA   as well as direct support contractor personnel will be housed there   This five story building will be 234 000 square feet and will be located next to Phase I of the Von Braun Complex  which houses the Space and Missile Defense Command                     30                                                                October 2005
