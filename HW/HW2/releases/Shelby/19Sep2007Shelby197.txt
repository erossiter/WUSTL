Shelby Announces  320 236 for the Town of Carrollton   September 27  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  will award  320 236 to the Town of Carrollton  Alabama  for renovation of its number one lagoon at the towns wastewater facility  It is critical that the residents of Carrollton have access to clean and safe water  said Shelby  Carrolltons current wastewater treatment facility is outdated and in poor condition  This important funding will allow the town to reconstruct the facility so that it will meet the higher standards for wastewater discharge set forth by the Alabama Department of Environmental Management  The renovation of the wastewater facility will immediately impact approximately 400 customers in the area  In addition to ARC funds  the total expected project cost of  800 000 includes  479 464 from the U S  Department of
