SHELBY ANNOUNCES  150 000 FOR GRAGG WADE FIELD IN CHILTON COUNTY     February 11  2005  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that Chilton County will receive  150 000 from the Federal Aviation Administration for improvements at Gragg Wade Field in Clanton  AL   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to make this announcement for Gragg Wade Field  These funds will be used for land acquisition for existing and future runway protection zones in order to meet FAA design standards  These improvements will also increase safety of operations at the airport    This funding  which was secured in the federal appropriations process  will be administered through the Federal Aviation Administrations Airport Improvement Program  AIP                                                         February 2005 News Releases                      Year            County
