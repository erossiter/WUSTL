Shelby Announces More Than  110 000 for Cowikee Volunteer Fire Department    February 1  2007  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  110 257 for the Cowikee Volunteer Fire Department in Barbour County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   This funding is an essential component in our continued efforts to adequately prepare for any event  should one occur  said Senator Shelby  Firefighters are our nations true first responders  and as such must have the resources necessary to keep our communities safe  This funding will help the Cowikee Volunteer Fire Department fund its operations and safety programs  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                          February 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
