Shelby Announces  159 000 for Economic Development in Colbert County   December 13  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala    ranking member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice and Science  today announced that U S  Department of Commerces Economic Development Administration  EDA  will invest  159 000 in the Northwest Alabama Regional Council of Local Governments for economic development in Colbert County  It is critical that Alabamas government and businesses work together to strengthen the economies of our local communities  said Shelby  These funds will assist in bringing together government entities and businesses so that they can work toward a stronger economy for Colbert County  The Northwest Alabama Council of Local Governments is the regional planning and intergovernmental coordination agency created by the local governments in the counties of Colbert  Franklin  Lauderdale  Marion and Winston
