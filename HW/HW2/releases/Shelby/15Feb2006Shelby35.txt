SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  143 000 GRANT FOR NORTH CENTRAL ALABAMA REGIONAL COUNCIL OF GOVERNMENTS       February 15  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Commerce  Justice  Science Subcommittee on Appropriations  announced today that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development Office has awarded a  143 165 grant for North Central Alabama Regional Council of Governments  NARCOG    Senator Shelby said  I am pleased that USDA awarded NARCOG this important grant  It is important that we invest in small businesses throughout our communities to create jobs and foster economic development   The funding will expand NARCOG to invest in small and emerging businesses in Cullman  Lawrence  and Morgan counties and is expected to create approximately 70 new jobs                                                           February 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
