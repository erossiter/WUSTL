Shelby to Visit Choctaw  Washington  Clarke  Monroe  Wilcox  and Montgomery Counties     February 5  2007  Washington  D C      U  S  Sen  Richard Shelby  R Ala   will join the residents of Choctaw  Washington  Clarke  Monroe  Wilcox  and Montgomery Counties for his county visits on Saturday  February 10  2007 and Monday  February 12  2007  The county visits are open to the public   Meeting Schedule    Saturday  February 10  2007  Choctaw County  7 30 a m  CT  Choctaw County Senior Citizens Center  110 Tom OrrDrive in Butler  Washington County  10 00 a m  CT  Chatom Town Hall  27 Cochran Avenue in Chatom  Clarke County  12 00 noon CT  Glorias Caf  135 South Jackson Street in Grove Hill  Monroe County  2 00 p m  CT  Monroeville City Hall  125 East Claiborne Street in Monroeville  Wilcox County  5 45 p m  CT  Alabama Tombigbee Regional Commission Building  107 Broad Street in Camden  Monday  February 12  2007  Montgomery County  7 30 a m  CT  RSA Activity Center  201 Dexter Avenue in Montgomery Sen  Shelby will discuss his legislative agenda for the 110th Congress as well as important issues facing the nation and the state of Alabama  Following his remarks  Sen  Shelby will open the floor to questions and comments from those in attendance   Sen  Shelby said  Over the years  these meetings have provided an excellent opportunity for area residents to listen and voice their opinions on the activities of our government  This will be a great chance to discuss the important issues facing our state and our nation as the 110th Congress reconvenes  Sen  Shelby has held over 1300 county visits since being elected to serve the state of Alabama  As part of his commitment to Alabamians  Sen  Shelby holds a meeting in each county once a year  This meeting is open to the public  Members of the media are encouraged to attend                                                              February 2007 News Releases                      Year            County
