SHELBY ANNOUNCES FUNDS FOR U S  SPACE AND ROCKET CENTER IN HUNTSVILLE       July 19  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that National Aeronautics and Space Administration  NASA  will be providing nearly  1 million  to the U S  Space and Rocket Center  USSRC  in Huntsville   These funds will be used for equipping the joint NASA USSRC Education Training Facility  EFT  and for making improvements to the Space and Rocket Center Museum    These funds will allow the Space and Rocket Center to provide the tools and furnishings necessary to conduct training and professional development activities for various adult education levels to include educators  Marshall Space Flight Center  MSFC  employees and clients  contractors and others   In addition  these funds will be used for facility upgrades to make improvements to the Space and Rocket Center Museum which will include exhibit updates necessary to highlight the importance of MSFC to space exploration     Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce these funds for the U S  Space and Rocket Center for important education training equipment and museum exhibit improvements   These improvements will allow the Space and Rocket Center to provide the knowledge and tools necessary to highlight the impact that MSFC has had on the space program                           Actual amount
