      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                    Shelby Reminds Small Business Owners to Apply for Economic Injury Disaster Loans   August 2  2007  Washington  D C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R Ala   today announced that the deadline for the Small Business Administrations Economic Injury Disaster Loans program to business owners affected by the November 16  2006  tornadoes and storms is August 29  2007  The loans are available to small businesses located in the counties of Autauga  Bullock  Crenshaw  Elmore  Lowndes  Macon  Montgomery and Pike      Many small businesses in Alabama suffered economic losses as a result of the storms on November 16  2006  said Shelby  It is critical that we aid the people  businesses and communities of Alabama who were affected by these natural disasters  I encourage any affected business owners to take full advantage of the assistance available before the deadline  To apply  small businesses can visit the SBAs website at  to download an application  or they may contact the agencys Customer Service Center at 1 800 659 2955                                                         August 2007 News Releases                      Year            County                        Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                   Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
