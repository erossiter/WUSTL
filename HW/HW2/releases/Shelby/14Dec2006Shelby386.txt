Shelby Announces  78 660 for Ridge Grove    December 14  2006 U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  78 660 to the Ridge Grove Fire Department in Chambers County  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   It is essential that Alabamas first responders have the tools and training necessary to safely protect our communities  said Senator Shelby  Ridge Grove will use this funding for operations and safety  This grant is evidence of our nations continued commitment to our first responders  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                         December
