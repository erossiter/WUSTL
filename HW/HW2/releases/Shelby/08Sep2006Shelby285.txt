SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  3 8 MILLION TO UAH FOR INTERMODAL FACILITY    September 8  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Transportation will release  3 887 113 to the University of Alabama in Huntsville  UAH  for its new Intermodal Facility  Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the fiscal year 2006 appropriations process  As UAH and its campus expand  this facility will provide an important link to the citys public transportation  while reducing congestion on campus  said Senator Shelby  The co location of the facility with the campus police department will greatly increase the safety of the students  faculty  and researchers who take advantage of the campus and city transportation system  UAH will use the funding provided for design  engineering and construction of the Bus Transfer and Park n Ride facility  which will ease traffic through campus  The City of Huntsvilles transit system will use the facility as a stop on their line  and UAH will utilize the facility as a transition area for their on campus shuttle service  The facility will also house the campus police department  provide parking for campus police vehicles  serve as a collection point for parking fees and fines  and enhance the general safety of the
