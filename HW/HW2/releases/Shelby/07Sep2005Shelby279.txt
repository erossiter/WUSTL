SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  186 000 FOR COPS PROGRAM IN DECATUR       September 8  2005   WASHINGTON  D  C     U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL  announced today that the Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing Services  COPS  will be awarding the City of Decatur  186 422 for the COPS Secure Our Schools  SOS  Initiative                  The SOS Initiative helps schools establish and enhance school safety efforts   SOS gives schools the opportunity to address a variety of existing and emerging problems related to school security ranging from purchasing new equipment to development community policing programs                          Senator Shelby said  I am pleased to announce over  186 000 for the City of Decatur for the COPS Secure Our Schools Initiative   This initiative will be tailored specifically to address the safety needs and concerns of Decaturs schools   By implementing this program  we are making a commitment to the safety and well being of our
