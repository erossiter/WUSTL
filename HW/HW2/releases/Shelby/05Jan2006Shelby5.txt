      United States Senator Richard Shelby   Press Room                                                                                                    Press Room News Releases                     SHELBY ANNOUNCES  75 000 FOR BLACKBELT TREASURES IN WILCOX COUNTY      January 19  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Delta Regional Authority will release  75 000 to Blackbelt Treasurers  Inc for business development  The funding will provide Blackbelt Treasurers a tourist trail development consultant   Senator Shelby said  I am pleased these funds are being made available to Blackbelt Treasurers  It is my hope that further investment of the tourism industry will spur economic growth and development throughout Wilcox County   The DRA is funded through the federal appropriations process  Senator Shelby was pleased to assist in securing  12 000 000 to fund the DRA in the fiscal year 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill                                                           January 2006 News Releases                      Year            County                                                                                                   Current record                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Washington                                                  Email                                                            Email                                                       Email                                                       Email                                                            Email                                                       Email
