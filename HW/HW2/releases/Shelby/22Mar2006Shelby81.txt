SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  15 000 FOR LAUDERDALE COUNTY     March 23  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   Chairman of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Commerce  Justice  and Science  announced today that the Department of Justice has awarded  14 612 to the Lauderdale County Commission to purchase laptop computers with mapping capabilities for patrol cars  which will improve response time to the countys rural areas   I am pleased to announce  14 612 for Lauderdale County said Shelby  This technology will allow law enforcement to quickly and efficiently reach citizens in rural areas during their times of need   These funds are released from the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program  JAG  and allow law enforcement to use the resources to prevent and control crime based on their own needs                                                           March 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
