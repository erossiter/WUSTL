SHELBY ANNOUNCES OVER  1 000 000 TO DOTHAN HOUSTON COUNTY AIRPORT AUTHORITY     March 28  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that the Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  has released  1 160 900 to the Dothan Houston County Airport Authority for Dothan Regional Airport   I am pleased to announce  1 160 900 for Dothan Regional Airport  said Shelby  This funding will allow the airport to purchase land to extend the runway safety area  improving safety for both pilots and passengers  These funds will also be used to mark and light the parallel taxiway  which will allow the airport to meet FAA requirements   Senator Shelby was instrumental in securing this funding during the federal appropriations
