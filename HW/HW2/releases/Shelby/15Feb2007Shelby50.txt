Shelby to Visit Crenshaw  Pike  Barbour  Bullock  and Madison Counties    February 15  2007 U  S  Sen  Richard Shelby  R Ala   will join the residents of Crenshaw  Pike  Barbour  Bullock  and Madison Counties for his county visits on Saturday  February 24  2007 and Monday  February 26  2007  The county visits are open to the public  Meeting Schedule    Saturday  February 24  2007  Crenshaw County  10 00 a m  CT  John Harrison Cultural Center  Crenshaw County Industrial Development Building on Forest Avenue in Luverne    Pike County  12 00 noon CT  Julias Restaurant  809 Highway 231 South in Troy    Barbour County  2 00 p m  CT  Farmers Exchange Bank  1920 Main Street in Louisville   Bullock County  4 00 p m  CT  Red Door Theater  corner of North Prairie Street and Highway 82 in Union Springs   Monday  February 26  2007  Madison County  7 30 a m  CT  Von Braun Center  700 Monroe Street SW in Huntsville  Sen  Shelby will discuss his legislative agenda for the 110th Congress as well as important issues facing the nation and the state of Alabama  Following his remarks  Sen  Shelby will open the floor to questions and comments from those in attendance  Sen  Shelby said  Over the years  these meetings have provided an excellent opportunity for area residents to listen and voice their opinions on the activities of our government  This will be a great chance to discuss the important issues facing our state and our nation as the 110th Congress reconvenes  Sen  Shelby has held over 1300 county visits since being elected to serve the state of Alabama  As part of his commitment to Alabamians  Sen  Shelby holds a meeting in each county once a year  This meeting is open to the public  Members of the media are encouraged to attend
