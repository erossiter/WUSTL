Shelby Announces More Than  63 000 for Saragossa Volunteer Fire Department    November 2  2006  Washington  D C      U S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Security will release  63 118 to the Saragossa Volunteer Fire Department  The funding is provided by the Assistance to Firefighters Grants Program   The Saragossa Volunteer Fire Department will use this funding for operations and safety  said Senator Shelby  First responders are on the front lines of our local communities everyday  working to keep our citizens safe  We must provide them with the training and equipment necessary to protect themselves from the dangers inherent in their jobs  These funds have been awarded by the Department of Homeland Securitys Office for Domestic Preparedness  as a part of the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  This program will distribute  750 million in direct federal assistance to firefighters nationwide this year                                                          November 2006 News Releases                      Year            County
