CONFERENCE COMMITTEE STRIPS BILLBOARD AMENDMENT FROM SUPPLEMENTAL APPROPRIATIONS BILL         June 8  2006   U  S  Senator Richard C  Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and
