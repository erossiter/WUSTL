SHELBY ANNOUNCES NEARLY  2 MILLION FOR HUNTSVILLE INTERMODAL TRANSIT FACILITY      July 18  2006   U S  Senator Richard Shelby  R AL   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  today announced  1 941 747 for the City of Huntsville  Department of Parking and Public Transit  Huntsville will use this funding to construct the Intermodal Transit Facility  ITF  at the Cummings Research Park Commercial Project  The ITF  which will be part of the Bridge Street Town Centre  will become the downtown of Huntsville Cummings Research Park  expected to be the fourth largest research park in the world   Huntsville is expected to gain at least 10 000 new residents from the recent BRAC decisions alone  which will significantly change traffic patterns in the area  said Senator Shelby  As North Alabama grows  this type of development is crucial to meeting in the needs of new residents  The inclusion of the ITF will provide alternate means of transportation to the retail and housing areas of the Cummings Research Park development  easing the pressure of the many added motorists to the area   The ITFs addition to the town center will integrate public transportation into the development  easing access to the approximately 2 000 000 square feet of development  The complex will include retail shops  restaurants  office buildings  recreation amenities  condominiums  apartments  and a hotel  The full complex will invest  250 000 000 in the area  and the ITF is expected to cost  9 045 665                                                             July 2006 News Releases
