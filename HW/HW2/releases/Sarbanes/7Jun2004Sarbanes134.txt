SEN. SARBANES ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF PRESIDENT REAGAN
  "Ronald Reagan was a voice for freedom and democracy around the world. He had a strong belief and an abiding confidence in America and its people. He practiced his politics without any element of personal animosity.
   "As our Nation mourns his passing, my sympathies go out to his family and friends."
