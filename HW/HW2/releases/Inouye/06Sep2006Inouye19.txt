HONOLULU WILL RECEIVE  3 04 MILLION TO PURCHASE 18 BUSES  INOUYE AND AKAKA ANNOUNCE          HONOLULU WILL RECEIVE  3 04 MILLION TO PURCHASE 18 BUSES  INOUYE AND AKAKA ANNOUNCE  Grant Is Part of  9 12 Million Secured for the City for Bus Service  Wednesday  September 6  2006           For Immediate Release         WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Daniel K  Inouye and Daniel K  Akaka  longtime partners in securing federal funds for Hawaii  announced today that the City and County of Honolulu will receive a federal transportation grant of  3 04 million to purchase 14 paratransit buses and four 60 foot long buses    Coupled with our announcement yesterday  the City and County of Honolulu will be receiving more than  9 12 million to upgrade its bus service  Senator Inouye said    Senator Akaka added  TheBus is a vital public service for the residents of the Island of Oahu  especially for those on fixed incomes  The new buses and the new transit center in Wahiawa will do much to enhance the service for riders of TheBus    Yesterday  Senators Inouye and Akaka announced that the City and County of Honolulu will receive  3 14 million for partial reimbursement for maintenance of its buses  and more than  2 94 million to construct a bus transit center in Wahiawa and purchase six paratransit buses        30
