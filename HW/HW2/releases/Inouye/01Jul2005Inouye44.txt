STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR DANIEL K  INOUYE REGARDING THE RETIREMENT OF JUSTICE SANDRA DAY OCONNOR         STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR DANIEL K  INOUYE REGARDING THE RETIREMENT OF JUSTICE SANDRA DAY OCONNOR              July 1  2005        For Immediate Release  WASHINGTON  Justice Sandra Day OConnor served our nation with distinction as the first woman on the Supreme Court   She was a thoughtful voice of moderation   Justice OConnors decision to retire creates an opportunity for the President to consult with the Senate on a nominee who will have the broadest possible support of the Senate and the American people  and who will bring to the High Court integrity and a firm commitment to the Constitution     30
