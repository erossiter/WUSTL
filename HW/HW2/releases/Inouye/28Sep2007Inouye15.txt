INOUYE APPLAUDS FEDERAL CONTRACT FOR MUNITIONS REMOVAL FROM MOLOKAI LANDFILL         INOUYE APPLAUDS FEDERAL CONTRACT FOR MUNITIONS REMOVAL FROM MOLOKAI LANDFILL       Friday  September 28  2007           For Immediate Release     U S  Senator Daniel K  Inouye said today he is pleased that a certified environmental restoration company has been awarded a federal contract to remove military munitions from the Molokai landfill   The contract reflects the commitment of the U S  and Maui County governments to ensure that the Molokai landfill is safe and does not pose a threat to the community  Senator Inouye said   Maui Mayor Charmaine Tavares had asked that Senator Inouye provide federal assistance to help remove stray ordnance from Molokais west end     The contract for the Molokai landfill cleanup was awarded to American Technologies Inc   ATI   by the U S  Army Corps of Engineers for nearly  185 500  ATI is certified by the Environmental Protection Agency and the Occupational Safety and Health Administration    30
