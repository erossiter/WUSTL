STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR DANIEL K  INOUYE ON THE NOMINATION OF JUDGE SAMUEL A  ALITO  JR   TO SERVE ON THE U S  SUPREME COURT         STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR DANIEL K  INOUYE ON THE NOMINATION OF JUDGE SAMUEL A  ALITO  JR   TO SERVE ON THE U S  SUPREME COURT           October 31  2005        For Immediate Release  WASHINGTON  I have never met Judge Samuel A  Alito  Jr  I look forward to meeting him and learning in detail about his record  and his qualifications to serve on the United States Supreme Court  I will keep an open mind as the Senate considers Judge Alitos fitness to serve on our nations highest court  and I hope he will be forthright and cooperative in answering questions from Senators  especially during the Senate Judiciary Committees hearings on his nomination    30
