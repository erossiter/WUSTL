LIHUE AIRPORT WILL RECEIVE  4 27 MILLION FOR PERIMETER SECURITY FENCING AND SERVICE ROAD IMPROVEMENTS          LIHUE AIRPORT WILL RECEIVE  4 27 MILLION FOR PERIMETER SECURITY FENCING AND SERVICE ROAD IMPROVEMENTS   Tuesday  August 1  2006           For Immediate Release      WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Daniel K  Inouye announced today that Lihue Airport on Kauai will receive  4 27 million to install perimeter security fencing and to improve the airports service road    Both projects will strengthen the security of the airport  said Senator Inouye  a senior member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  The plan for the installation of 11 300 feet of perimeter fencing was coordinated with the U S  Transportation Security Administration  Moreover  airport security and emergency vehicles will be better served when the service road is improved and realigned    The federal grant will be disbursed by the Federal Aviation Administration       30
