SENATOR INOUYES STATEMENT ON THE NOMINATION OF ADMIRAL FALLON TO HEAD THE U S  PACIFIC COMMAND         SENATOR INOUYES STATEMENT ON THE NOMINATION OF ADMIRAL FALLON TO HEAD THE U S  PACIFIC COMMAND  January 27  2005 For Immediate Release          WASHINGTON  Admiral William Fallon is well qualified to be the next Commander of the U S  Pacific Command   He has extensive experience as a flight officer  commander  and administrator  and his service in Washington and throughout the world gives him impressive credentials that indicate he will be able to assume the important responsibilities that go with being the Commander of PACOM   I am certain Admiral Fallons confirmation will proceed without any problems                   30
