FEDERAL DISASTER DESIGNATION ISSUED TO ASSIST HAWAII FARMERS         FEDERAL DISASTER DESIGNATION ISSUED TO ASSIST HAWAII FARMERS       Tuesday  July 31  2007           For Immediate Release                       WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Daniel K  Inouye announced today that Secretary of Agriculture Mike Johanns has issued a secretarial disaster designation for all of Hawaiis counties as a result of the ongoing drought that has parched isle agricultural lands    The Secretarys disaster designation means local farmers who meet certain eligibility requirements will be able to apply for low interest  emergency loans from the federal Farm Service Agency  Senator Inouye said  These loans will greatly help our hard working farmers  who  due to no fault of their own  are in need of a helping hand    Secretary Johanns issued the disaster designation after a departmental review concluded that production losses warranted a disaster designation    30
