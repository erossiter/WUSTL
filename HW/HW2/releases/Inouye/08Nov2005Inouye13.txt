MEMORIAL SERVICE IN WASHINGTON SET FOR HENRY K  GIUGNI         MEMORIAL SERVICE IN WASHINGTON SET FOR HENRY K  GIUGNI It Will Be Held on November 15th  2005            November 8  2005        For Immediate Release  WASHINGTON  November 8  2005  A public memorial service for Henry K  Giugni  the Senates 30th Sergeant at Arms and the former top aide to U S  Senator Daniel K  Inouye  will be held on Tuesday  November 15  2005  in the 340 seat auditorium  SD   G50  on the ground floor of the Dirksen Senate Office Building  Senator Inouye announced today   The service will begin at 4 15 p m   Speakers will include Senator Inouye and Gerald S J  Cassidy  the founder and Chairman of Cassidy   Associates  one of Washingtons leading public policy consulting firms   Mr  Giugni was Vice Chairman of Cassidy   Associates  which he joined in 1991   A private reception will follow the service   Arrangements for a service in Hawaii are pending     Henrys life touched thousands of people from all walks of life  said Senator Inouye   The service next week will be a meaningful way for his family  friends  and former colleagues and employees in Washington to honor the memory of this special man   He was the consummate professional in all he did  and he will always be remembered for his compassion and generosity of spirit   Mr  Giugni  80  died on Thursday  November 3  2005  of congestive heart failure in a Rockville  Maryland  hospital   A legendary figure in Hawaii politics  Mr  Giugni forged an unbreakable professional and personal bond with Senator Inouye that lasted nearly 50 years   It began during Senator Inouyes re election campaign to the Hawaii Territorial House of Representatives in 1956  and Mr  Giugni dedicated himself to serving Senator Inouye as the Senator rose through the legislative ranks as a Territorial Representative  Territorial Senator  U S  Representative  and U S  Senator   After leaving Senator Inouyes staff  Mr  Giugni served as the Senates Sergeant at Arms from January 6  1987  to December 31  1990     30
