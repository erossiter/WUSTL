SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS ABOUT HIS LEGISLATION TO BOOST BIOFUEL USE
  During his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters Sen. Jeff Bingaman today talked about a bill he introduced to increase the country's use of biofuels. Bingaman also answered questions on a variety of other topics.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about legislation he has introduced that aims to expedite the entry of home-grown, economical, feedstock-diverse, biologically-derived fuels into America's transportation fuel mix. 
   02:12 - Bingaman talks about the Iraq supplemental appropriations bill currently on the Senate floor. 
   05:50 - Bingaman talks about the planned New Mexico Spaceport. 
   08:05 - Bingaman talks about his biofuels legislation.
