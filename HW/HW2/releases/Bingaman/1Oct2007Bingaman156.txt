SEN. BINGAMAN: TRUTH OR CONSEQUENCES EDUCATIONAL CENTER TO BENEFIT FROM FEDERAL GRANT
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Southwestern Regional Education Center in Truth or Consequences has been awarded a $265,348 grant through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program.  
   Funding will be used for a distance learning project at the education center. The project will help provide local residents with the opportunity to pursue college degrees and job training through the use of state-of-the-art video conferencing equipment. 
   "This funding will provide T or C residents with an incredible opportunity to advance their careers through advanced education and job training," Bingaman said.
