SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered question on a variety of topics discussed today during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   * 00:20 - Bingaman talks about Condoleezza Rice's nomination to replace Colin Powell as Secretary of State.
   * 01:25 - Bingaman talks about Sen. McCain's immigration proposal.
   * 02:35 - Bingaman talks about the Senate Energy Committee's Conference of the topic of natural gas.
   * 04:14 - Bingaman talks about hopes for bi-partisan cooperation on the writing of a new national energy bill. 
   * 5:23 - Bingaman talks about the comprehensive education bill he introduced yesterday.
   * 6:47 - Bingaman talks about social security.
   * 8:45 - Bingaman talks about two pieces of legislation he co-sponsored yesterday that would protect beef consumers and promote New Mexico's cattle industry.
