SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO COP-OP AWARDED $5.52 MILLION FEDERAL LOAN
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that Sierra Electric Cooperative, Inc. been approved for a $5.52 million loan from the federal government.  
   Funding will help the cooperative build 45 miles of new distribution line and other improvement. These upgrades will expand services to 483 new customers. Sierra Electric serves the counties of Catron, Luna, Sierra, and Socorro. 
   "I am pleased that this loan will be used to expand electrical services to more New Mexicans," Bingaman said. 
   Funds were provided through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program.
