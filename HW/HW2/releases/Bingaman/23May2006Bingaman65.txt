SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MAXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about two amendments he introduced, which have been adopted as part of the immigration legislation currently on the Senate floor.
   02:04 - Bingaman talks about an amendment he plans to introduce to the immigration bill.
   04:40 - Bingaman talks about his amendment to set the cap for the Senate immigration bill's guest worker program at 200,000. 
   06:24 - Bingaman answers additional question on the immigration bill.
   10:34 - Bingaman comments on concern expressed by Santa Fe law enforcement officials regarding their role in the enforcement of immigration laws. 
   11:54 - Bingaman talks about the nomination of Air Force Gen. Michael Hayden to be the new director of the CIA.
