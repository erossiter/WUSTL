SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS ABOUT ECONOMY WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters about an economic stimulus package being debated in Congress and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman gives an overview of what the Senate is working to accomplish during its first week of the second half of the 110th Congress. 
   03:12 - Bingaman talks about an economic stimulus package being debated in Congress. 
   05:53 - Bingaman comments on the rebate portion of the economic stimulus package being cdebated. 
   08:03 - Bingaman talks about $11.7 million he helped secure to fund improvements to levees in Do\xF1a Ana County.

   10:56 - Bingaman talks about his letter to the Department of Interior seeking funding for an archeological study of the Galisteo Basin.
