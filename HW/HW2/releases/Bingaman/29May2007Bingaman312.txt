SEN. BINGAMAN'S ROSWELL OFFICE MOVES TO NEW LOCATION
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that his Roswell office is moving to a new location.  
   The new office will be located at 200 East Fourth Street, Suite 300 near the Courthouse. The office's phone number - (505) 622-7113 - will stay the same. 
   "The new office will be located on the first floor of this convenient new location," Bingaman said. 
   The Roswell office serves Southwestern New Mexico and includes the counties of: Chaves, Curry, De Baca, Eddy, Lea, Lincoln, Quay, and Roosevelt.
