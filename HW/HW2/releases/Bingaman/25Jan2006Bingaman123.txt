SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NM RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about new legislation he is introducing with Senator Pete Domenici aimed at helping America maintain its leading edge in science and technology.
   03:07 - Bingaman comments on plans to build a spaceport in southern New Mexico and about proposals to build a commuter train.
   05:57 - Bingaman comments on the Bush Administration's use of wiretaps without warrants. (Also see 10:58)
   07:45 - Bingaman talks about the nomination of Judge Alito to the U.S. Supreme Court.
   15:35 - Bingaman talks about the need for lobbying reforms.
  19:18 - Bingaman talks about his meeting with U.S. Health and Human Services Secretary Mike Leavitt regarding the Medicare prescription drug bill. 
   23:18 - Bingaman talks about upcoming issues in the second half of the 109th Congress.
