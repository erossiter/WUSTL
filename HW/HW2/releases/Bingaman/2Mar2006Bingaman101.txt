SEN. BINGAMAN: UNION COUNTY FARMERS ELIGIBLE FOR DISASTER AID
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that Union County has been declared eligible for federal disaster assistance as a result of continuing drought in the area. 
   Farmers and ranchers in Union County are now eligible to be considered for low interest emergency loans from the Farm Service Agency, which is part of the U.S. Department of Agriculture.
   "I am pleased the USDA is lending a helping hand to the farmers and ranchers of Union County who have been economically harmed due to the ongoing drought in the area, ," Bingaman said.
   For more information or to determine eligibility for a low interest loan contact the local USDA Farm Service Center.
