SEN. BINGAMAN TO JOIN IN INTRODUCING SCIENCE AND TECH LEGISLATION
  This week Sens. Jeff Bingaman, Pete Domenici, Lamar Alexander (R-TN) and Barbara Mikulski (D-MD) will introduce legislation aimed at helping America maintain its leading edge in science and technology. 
   On Wednesday, Bingaman, Domenici, Alexander and Mikulski will hold a press conference to announce their plans to introduce the Protect America's Competitive Edge (PACE) Act, a package of three bills that would implement 20 recommendations contained in a recent report by the National Academy of Science titled "Rising Above the Gathering Storm."
   Bingaman schedule is as follows:
   Wednesday, January 25
  PACE Act Press Conference
  10:15 a.m. - Dirksen Senate Office Building, Rm. 366
