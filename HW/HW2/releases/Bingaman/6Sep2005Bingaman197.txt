ENERGY COMMITTEE TO EXAMINE HIGH GAS PRICES
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today will take part in an Energy and Natural Resources Committee hearing to examine gasoline prices and factors contributing to current high prices.  
 Bingaman is the top Democrat on the Energy Committee. 
   The committee will hear from experts who will discuss factors contributing to record prices, including world oil demand, refining industry issues, speculation in oil trading markets and new problems which have risen as a result of Hurricane Katrina. 
   On Thursday, the Health, Education, Labor and Pensions (HELP) Committee will hold a roundtable discussion to look at healthcare, education and workforce issues associated with Hurricane Katrina. 
   Also on Thursday, Bingaman will take part in a HELP Committee meeting to consider the Higher Education Reauthorization.
   Bingaman's schedule is as follows:
