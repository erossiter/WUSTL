SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS ABOUT HIS EFFORTS TO EXTEND DENTAL CARE COVERAGE TO MILLIONS OF CHILDREN
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about his provision to the State Children's Health Insurance Program (SCHIP) that would expand dental care coverage to millions of children. 
   01:47 - Bingaman talks about the continuing debate on the Senate floor on a change in course in Iraq. 
   04:35 - Bingaman talks about his letter to President Bush urging him to keep National Guard along the New Mexico-Mexico border until an adequate number of Custom and Border Protection agents have been trained and assigned to the region. 
   05:52 - Bingaman talks about his continued efforts to ensure funding and other resources are made available to help keep the country's borders secure. 
   07:33 - Bingaman talks about the SCHIP bill being considered in the Senate Finance committee today. 
   09:10 - Bingaman talks about his efforts to ensure the entire state of New Mexico does not suffer as a result of dairy cattle in Curry County testing positive for bovine tuberculosis. 
   10:38 - Bingaman talks about the SCHIP bill being considered in the Senate Finance committee today.
