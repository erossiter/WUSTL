SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO N.M. RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters. 
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about funding for New Mexico included in several spending bill which will soon become law.
   02:15 - Bingaman comments on the reauthorization of the Patriot Act.
   03:32 - Bingaman talks about the 2006 Defense Authorization bill that passed the Senate this week.
   06:05 - Bingaman talks about the Senate's push for a plan to end occupation of Iraq.
   08:43 - Bingaman talks about his provision to the Defense Authorization bill that deals with the Constitutional right to Habeas Corpus.
   11:17 - Bingaman comments on talk that Majority Leader Bill Frist (R-TN) intends to bring up the Fairness in Asbestos Injury Resolution Act (FAIR) Act of 2005 for Senate consideration in January.
   12:32 - Bingaman talks about the development of Santa Teresa.
   13:48 - Bingaman talks about his meeting with U.S. Supreme Court Nominee, Samuel Alito.
   16:00 - Bingaman comments on question of whether Congress had access to the same pre-war intelligent the White House did.
   18:44 - Bingaman talks about his recent tour of areas that will be affected by the San Juan Settlement and proposed Navajo-Gallup Water Supply Project.
   20:37 - Bingaman talks about a letter he and Sen. Domenici sent to oil company executives asking for clarification of their testimony before the Energy Committee last week.
