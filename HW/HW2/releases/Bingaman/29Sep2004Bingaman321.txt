SEN. BINGAMAN: LAS CRUCES FIRM TO BENEFIT FROM $100 MILLION CONTRACT
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman announced today that EMI Technologies, Inc. in Las Cruces has been awarded a $100 million contract from the U.S. Army. 
   Under the contract, EMI Technologies will manufacture vans, trailers and shelters. Additionally the firm will assist with the installation of equipment and the renovation of used trailers and shelters. All work will be performed in Las Cruces.
   "New Mexico is fortunate to be the home of businesses recognized for their quality work and experience, which is so often sought by government agencies like the Department of Defense. I'm pleased to see that this Las Cruces firm is being tapped to produce equipment for the U.S. Army," Bingaman said.
