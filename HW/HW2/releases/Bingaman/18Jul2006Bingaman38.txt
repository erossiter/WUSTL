SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about his bill to help seniors who are trapped in a prescription drug coverage gap period when most Medicare Part D policies provide no coverage. 
   03:12 - Bingaman talks about the legislation on the Senate floor, which would make federal funding available for stem cell research. 
   05:45 - Bingaman talks about a provision he worked to include in the 2007 Department of Homeland Security Appropriations bill that will benefit border law enforcement communities. 
   08:58 - Bingaman talks about the legislation on the Senate floor, which would make federal funding available for stem cell research. 
   11:27 - Bingaman says he continues to work to get commercial hours of operation at the Santa Teresa Port of Entry extended. 
   14:07 - Bingaman talks about the current situation in the Middle East. 
   18:15 - Bingaman talks about legislation that cleared the Senate last week aimed at helping families affected by methamphetamine use. 
   20:25 - Bingaman talked about the 2007 Department of Homeland Security Appropriations bill that passed the Senate last week.
