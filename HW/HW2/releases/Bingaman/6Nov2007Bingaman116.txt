SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman comments on proposed energy exploration in New Mexico's Galisteo Basin. 
   03:30 - Bingaman talks about the Farm Bill that is currently on the Senate floor and about President Bush's statements saying he will veto the bill. 
   06:10 - Bingaman talk about legislation working its way though Congress that update the 1872 Mining Law. 
   08:13- Bingaman says he has not made a decision on whether he will support the nomination of Michael Mukasey to be U.S. Attorney General.
