SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO TECH TO BENEFIT FROM EPA FUNDS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced the New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology has been awarded $222,222 from the Environmental Protection Agency.
   The funding awarded to Environmental Finance Center (EFC) at New Mexico Tech will be used to help develop and deliver assistance, training and educational services on sustainable water and wastewater systems to state and local governments. 
   "This funding will help New Mexico Tech provide a vital service to communities throughout the country," Bingaman said.
