SEN. BINGAMAN: BELEN & SANDOVAL COUNTY AIRPORTS RECEIVE FEDERAL FUNDING
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that Alexander Municipal Airport in Belen will receive $683,988 in federal funding.  
   Bingaman said the grant will be used to construct a portion of a taxiway for the existing runway, improving safety by reducing aircraft taxi time. 
   "This grant will make it possible for Belen to significantly improve its airport," Bingaman said. 
   Bingaman also said federal funding that he announced had been approved last year for Sandoval County is now being released. The county will receive $82,668. 
   Finally, Bingaman said the State of New Mexico will receive $109,920 to collect pavement condition data on airports. 
   The funding comes from the U.S. Department of Transportation.
