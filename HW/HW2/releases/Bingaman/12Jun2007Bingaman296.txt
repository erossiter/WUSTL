SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS ABOUT ENERGY BILL WITH NEW MEXICO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today talked about an energy bill being debate before the Senate during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters. Bingaman also answered questions on a variety of other topics.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talk about the energy bill currently being debated on the Senate floor.
   01:40 - Bingaman comments on proposed cuts to U.S. nuclear weapons programs that could affect New Mexico's two federal labs.
   04:30- Bingaman says he has been working for several years to help diversifying the work at the state's two laboratories.
   08:48 - Bingaman comments on Congress's 27 percent approval rating and what Congress can do to fix this.
   11:19 - Bingaman talk about the energy bill on the floor.
   13:38 - Bingaman talk about an amendment he will introduce to require electric utilities to generate 15 percent of their power through renewables by 2020.
   15:04 - Bingaman talks about the Corporate Fuel Efficiency Standards provision in the bill.
