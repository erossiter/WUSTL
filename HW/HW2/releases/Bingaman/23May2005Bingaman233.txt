SEN. BINGAMAN: ALBUQUERQUE GROUP AWARDED $2.696 MILLION TO AID VICTIMS OF CRIME
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Department of Justice (DOJ) has awarded the New Mexico Crime Victims Reparations Commission $2.696 million to assist victims of crime.   
   The grant comes from DOJ's Crime Victims Fund, which is used to support the efforts of domestic violence shelters, rape crisis centers, child abuse programs, hospitals, prosecutors' offices and social service agencies that directly assist crime victims. 
   "Victims of crime are many times left to battle the psychological trauma. The funding awarded today will help provide important services to victims of crimes and help them get the help they need," Bingaman said.
