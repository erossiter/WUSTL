SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the following link:  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about his Senate Committee assignments for the next Congress and gives an overview of the schedule for the lame duck session. 
   03:27 - Bingaman talks about what his top priorities will be as Chairman of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee. 
   05:06 - Bingaman makes a statement on the DWI tragedy that claimed the lives of five members of a Las Vegas, NM family. 
   06:11 - Bingaman talks an Energy Committee hearing today to look at the status of Puerto Rico. 
   08:00 - Bingaman talks about bill that will be considered during the lame duck session. 
   10:37 - Bingaman comments on today's Armed Services Committee hearing on Iraq. 
   12:53 - Bingaman talks about the border fence bill that was signed into law last month. 
   15:18 - Bingaman talks about job reduction at Los Alamos National Labs. 
   18:13 - Bingaman says he hopes to work across party lines as the incoming Chairman of the Energy Committee to set an agenda for the next Congress. 
   19:04 - Bingaman comments on the nomination of John Bolton to be U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations.
