SEN. BINGAMAN: LOS LUNAS TO BENEFIT FROM LOW-INTEREST LOAN
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Center for Ageless Living in Los Lunas has been approved for a $588,651 low-interest loan. 
   Funding will be used to construct a new 6,000 sq. ft. adult residential care facility. The current facility houses 30 clients. The new construction will allow for the accommodation of 15 new clients, allowing the facility to care for a total of 45 clients.
   "Assisted living centers help residents maintain independence. I am pleased the funds awarded today will the Center for Ageless Living accommodate more clients," Bingaman said.
   The loan was provided through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development program and will be repaid over 40 years at the low interest rate of 4.5 percent.
