SEN. BINGAMAN: N.M. TO RECEIVE NEARLY $2.7 MILLION TO ASSIST VICTIMS OF CRIMES
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today reported that the New Mexico Crime Victims Reparation Commission will receive $2.699 million in federal grant funding.
   Bingaman said the funding comes from the U.S. Department of Justice.
   "This federal grant will be used to assist New Mexicans whose lives have been touched by violent crime," Bingaman said.
