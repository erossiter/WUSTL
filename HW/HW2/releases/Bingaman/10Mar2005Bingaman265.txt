SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO TECH AWARDED FEDERAL FUNDS TO PROMOTE COAL PRODUCTION
 Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology has been awarded a $703,923 federal research grant to promote the efficient use of coal. 
   "I am pleased that the federal government has recognized New Mexico Tech's work in the area of energy research and has awarded them this federal grant," Bingaman said.
   The grant, awarded through the U.S. Department of Energy, will go to the school's Petroleum Recovery Research Center to help fund energy research and development activities that promotes the efficient, sound production and use of coal. The project will contribute to technological advancement by supporting research and development of energy resources needed to ensure the availability of affordable energy for the nation's future.
