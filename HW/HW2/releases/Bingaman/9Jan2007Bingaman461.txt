SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the ethics reform legislation currently on the Senate floor and about the Energy Committee hearing on oil supply. 
   02:08 - Bingaman talks about the Energy Committee hearing on the global supply of oil and the effect it has on the United States. 
   03:32 - Bingaman says the President has not made the case for sending additional troops to Iraq. 
   06:05 - Bingaman talks about the President's speech tomorrow night on Iraq. 
   07:07 - Bingaman talks about the administrations lack of commitment to invest in renewable energy. 
   08:52 - Bingaman talks about his border water bill that that was recently signed into law. 
   11:04 - Bingaman comments on the U.S. air strikes against al-Qaida in Somalia. 
   12:34 - Bingaman talks about hearings this week in the Finance Committee on the Medicare Prescription Drug Plan. 
   14:136 - Bingaman talk about the House of Representative 100 hour plan.
