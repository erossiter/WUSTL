SEN. BINGAMAN: FUNDING TO BENEFIT NEW MEXICO SMALL BUSINESSES
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Women's Economic Self-Sufficiency Team (WESST) Corp. in Albuquerque will benefit from a $50,000 federal grant.  
   Funding, which was awarded through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program, will be used to provide technical assistance to rural entrepreneurs in establishing web based Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for their e-commerce marketing. The project will assist 25 businesses. 
   "WESST Corp. provides financial resources to many of our states entrepreneurs who are trying to start a small business. The funding awarded today will help them continue their work," Bingaman said. 
   WESST Corp. provides financial backing to traditionally underserved communities and encourages the development of community-focused businesses. Their primary focus of its services is on individuals and organizations that create positive social benefits in their communities through job creation, affordable housing development, and the provision of basic services in disadvantaged communities through the state of New Mexico.
