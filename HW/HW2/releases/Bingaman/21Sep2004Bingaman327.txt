SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL GOVERNMENT AWARDS GRANT TO BERNALLIO COUNTY METROPOLITAN COURT
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today reported that the Bernalillo County Metropolitan Court will benefit from $494,732 in federal funds he secured.  
   The funding, which is coming from the fiscal year 2004 Commerce, Justice, State spending bill, will be used by the court to reduce the incidence of domestic violence by providing court services to repeat offenders, by meting out consequences for program violations, and providing treatment to the offender and his/her family.
   "The goal of this program is to put an end to domestic violence, which touches the lives of too many women and children in our state. The Bernalillo County Metropolitan Court has developed an effective way to deal with this serious problem, and I'm very glad that I was able secure this funding to help," Bingaman said.
