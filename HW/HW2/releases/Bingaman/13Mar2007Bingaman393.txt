SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:28 - Bingaman talks about the Iraq resolution being debated in the Senate. 
   01:58 - Bingaman comments on calls by legislators for the resignation of Attorney General Alberto Gonzales. 
   05:50 - Bingaman talks about asking for a six-month delay in initiating a federal investigation of Los Alamos National Laboratory operations in order to allow the lab's new management team to make more progress in implementing new operating and security procedures. 
   08:17 - Bingaman talks about the 911 Commission bill currently on the Senate floor. 
   10:06 - Bingaman talks about the appropriations process. 
   14:10 - Bingaman says he is pleased that the state of New Mexico is committing funds for the Navajo-Gallup water settlement.
