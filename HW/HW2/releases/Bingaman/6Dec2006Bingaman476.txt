SEN. BINGAMAN VOTES TO CONFIRM ROBERT GATES AS DEFENSE SECRETARY
Sen. Jeff Bingaman today voted to confirm Robert M. Gates to the position of defense secretary. 
 "Robert Gates is obviously qualified for the job. His testimony before the Senate Armed Services Committee was impressive. He seems determined to be his own man, and to do all he can to salvage a bad situation in Iraq," Bingaman said.
