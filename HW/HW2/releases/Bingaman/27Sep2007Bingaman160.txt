SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO TECH AWARDED DOE FUNDS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman announced today that New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology has been awarded a $160,000 federal research grant.  
   "This funding will allow New Mexico Tech to continue its important work in the area of energy research," Bingaman said. 
   The grant, awarded through the U.S. Department of Energy, will fund research efforts involving grid reliability. Research at New Mexico Tech, in conjunction with work being preformed at the University of Hawaii, will help develop a microgrid through the integration of renewable and distributed energy systems.
