SEN. BINGAMAN WILL CHAIR ENERGY COMMITTEE IN 110TH CONGRESS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today was officially named chairman of the Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee for the 110th Congress, which begins in January 2007.  
   Bingaman also will continue to sit on the Senate Finance Committee, which has jurisdiction over Medicaid, Medicare and Social Security, as well as the Senate Health Education Labor and Pensions Committee and the Joint Economic Committee. 
   Bingaman also was tapped today to be the Democratic Chairman of Committee Outreach, which means he will be the voice in Democratic leadership for the Senate's committee chairs. 
   "I look forward to the 110th Congress and working on issues that are important to the New Mexicans and the country, including the expansion of alternative energy sources, improving public education, and making health care more affordable and accessible," Bingaman said.
