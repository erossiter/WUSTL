SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL FUNDS TO BENEFIT RURAL NEW MEXICO BUSINESSES
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the New Mexico Rural Development Response Council (NMRDRC) in Las Cruces has been awarded $200,000 in federal funding provided through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development program.  
   The NMRDRC, which assists rural communities with economic development project, will use the funding to provide technical assistance to rural entrepreneurs looking to apply for federal financial support. Assistance includes helping communities with the completion of applications, the preparation of business plans, marketing studies, strategic planning, and leadership/management training. 
   "Small businesses are an important part of our state's economy. I am pleased this funding will provide rural New Mexico communities with help in seeking federal support for their economic development efforts," Bingaman said.
