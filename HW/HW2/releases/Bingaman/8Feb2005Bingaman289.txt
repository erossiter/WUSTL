SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered question on a variety of topics discussed today during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   * 00:00 - Bingaman talks about the president's budget proposal for fiscal year 2006.
   * 02:20 - Bingaman talk about the effects the president's budget cuts to Medicaid will have on New Mexicans.
   * 02:32 - Bingaman talks about proposals to drill in Otero Mesa and Valle Vidal.
   * 04:50 - Bingaman talks about the out look of the 2006 Senate Budget resolution.
   * 06:33 - Bingaman shares his thoughts on Medicaid reform. 
   * 09:19 - Bingaman talks about the possibility of a cease fire in the Middle East.
   * 11:30 - Bingaman talks about the president's request for $80 billion for ongoing efforts in Iraq and Afghanistan. 
   * 12:39 - Bingaman talks about his reasons for opposing the confirmation of Alberto Gonzales. 
   * 14:28 - Bingaman talks about ongoing bi-partisan efforts to draft an energy bill.
   * 15:45 - Bingaman shares his thoughts on election reform.
