SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the following link: 
   http://bingaman.senate.gov/mediafiles/bingaman071007m.mp3 
   00:16 - Bingaman comments on the president's decision to assert executive privilege in the U.S. Attorney firings case. 
   02:10 - Bingaman talks about the different Iraq withdrawal bills being debated in the Senate. 
   05:30 - Bingaman talks about his request that FEMA slow down a process that could force some homeowners in southern New Mexico to have to buy expensive flood protection insurance. 
   06:46 - Bingaman talks about the different Iraq withdrawal bills being debated in the Senate. 
   07:43 - Bingaman talks about the fiscal year 2008 budget that funds Los Alamos National Labs.
