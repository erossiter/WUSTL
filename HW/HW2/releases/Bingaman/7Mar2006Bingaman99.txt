SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL FUNDS TO AID DIABETES RESEARCH
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman announced today that the University of New Mexico (UNM) has been awarded a $320,000 grant from the federal government to support kidney disease, urology and hematology research.
   "The University of New Mexico does groundbreaking medical research, and I'm pleased that this federal funding will be used to support the life-saving work it is doing," Bingaman said.
   The funding was awarded through the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.
