SEN. BINGAMAN: SILVER CITY NONPROFIT TO BENEFIT FROM FEDERAL FUNDS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that Life Quest, Inc. in Silver City has been awarded a $50,000 grant from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.  
   The funding will help Life Quest, a non-profit organization, implement a best practices full community inclusion program for adults with disabilities and is aimed at helping them build their organization's capacity through training and resource development. 
   "Life Quest provides important services to persons with disabilities and I am pleased the funding awarded today will help them continue their good work," Bingaman said. 
   Life Quest provides services to persons with disabilities in Grant County. Programs include: early intervention for parents with children with disabilities, residential services for adults living on their own, life skills training, and vocational services, among others.
