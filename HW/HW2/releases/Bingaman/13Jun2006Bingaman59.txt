SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the fiscal year 2007 Defense Authorization bill currently on the Senate floor.
   01:18 - Bingaman talks about President Bush's visit to Iraq.
   02:20 - Bingaman talks about the President's visit to Artesia last week.
   03:36 - Bingaman talks about his provision that was included in the Senate immigration bill, which authorizes $20 million for infrastructure needs along the New Mexico/Texas - Mexico border.
   05:25 - Bingaman answers a question about the White House CIA leak investigation. 
   06:50 - Bingaman talks about the Federal Law Enforcement Training Center (FLETC) in Artesia.
   08:18 - Bingaman talks about the immigration legislation currently pending in Congress.
   10:00 - Bingaman talks about a solar energy grant that was awarded to New Mexico State University.
   11:50 - Bingaman comments on the death of terrorist leader Abu Musab al-Zarqawi.
   14:20 - Bingaman talks about the president's visit to Iraq. 
   15:15 - Bingaman talks about the high price of gas.
   17:00 - Bingaman talks about ongoing efforts to address the Iranian Nuclear threat.
