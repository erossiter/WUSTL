SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed yesterday during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.   
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about his amendment to secure an additional $4.8 billion to support No Child Left Behind and restore funding for other critical education initiatives that were cut by the White House. Bingaman offered the amendment to the 2006 Budget Resolution, which is currently on the senate floor.
   02:36 - Bingaman talks about an amendment he is cosponsoring that would prevent billions of dollars in cuts to Medicaid.
   03:45 - Bingaman addresses concerns regarding Title I funding in Silver City. 
   05:20 - Bingaman talks about President Bush's visit to New Mexico to promote his plan to privatize social security. Bingaman pointed out the lack of support for the plan in Congress.
