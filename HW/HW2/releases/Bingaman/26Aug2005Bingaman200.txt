SEN. BINGAMAN COMMENTS ON BRAC DECISION TO CREATE 'ENCLAVE' AT CANNON AIR FORCE BASE, SEEK NEW MISSION
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today commented on the Base Closure and Realignment Commission (BRAC) decision to put Cannon Air Force Base at Clovis into an "enclave status" so new missions might be found for the Curry County base.
