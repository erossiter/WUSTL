SEN. BINGAMAN: EPA AWARDS $110,000 TO PUEBLO OF ISLETA
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency has awarded a $110,000 grant to help the Pueblo of Isleta manage several environmental programs. 
   Isleta will use this funding to improve the environment and public health of the tribal community. The tribe will also conduct outreach on environmental issues, train staff and to develop a dredge and fill program, 
   "I am pleased that the federal government has made these funds available to help Isleta Pueblo continue its environmental efforts," Bingaman said.
