SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS HIGHER EDUCATION WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters today about a bill that will benefit New Mexico Colleges and Universities. Bingaman also answered questions on a variety of other topics.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about legislation he helped write to make a college education more affordable and accessible to New Mexicans. 
   02:11 - Bingaman talks about General Petraeus's testimony before the Congress this week on the situation in Iraq. 
   05:42 - Bingaman talks about an Energy Committee hearing he is chairing tomorrow on energy efficient lighting. 
   07:37 - Bingaman makes a statement on the six year anniversary of the 9/11 terrorist attacks. 
   09:12 - Bingaman comments on the latest Osama bin Laden tape to come out. 
   10:37 - Bingaman says he feels there are safety concerns that must be addressed as part of a pilot program that allows Mexican commercial truck onto U.S. highways for the fist time as part of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA). 
   13:40 - Bingaman comments on the construction of the Desert Rock coal-fired power plant. 
   15:53 - Bingaman talks about the farm bill that is working its way through Congress.
