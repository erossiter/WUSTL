SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL FUNDS TO BENEFIT FARMINGTON
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that San Juan Junior College will receive a $175,000 investment from the U.S. Department of Commerce's Economic Development Administration. 
   Funding will be used for the final stage of construction of a business incubator center on the San Juan Junior College campus, which is estimated to create 160 new jobs and generate $3.5 million in private investment.
   "Business incubators provide businesses with the resources and tools they need to succeed," Bingaman said. "The funding awarded today is a great investment for Farmington and all of San Juan County."
