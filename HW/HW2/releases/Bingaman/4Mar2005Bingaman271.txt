SEN. BINGAMAN TO DELIVER SPANISH LANGUAGE RESPONSE TO BUSH'S WEEKLY RADIO ADDRESS
  On Saturday, March 5, 2005 at 9:06 a.m. (MT) Sen.  
 Jeff Bingaman will deliver the Democratic Hispanic radio address, which will air on Spanish language stations across the country. Sen Bingaman's remarks will immediately follow President Bush's weekly radio address. 
   Bingaman will outline the reasons he opposes privatizing Social Security, and highlight a report that shows that of all Americans, Hispanics would be most hurt by privatization.
   The address will be available on http://democrats.gov/actualities/hispanic/hispanic050304. mp3 Saturday at 9:06 a.m. MT. . 
   Bingaman is the co-chair of the Senate Democratic Hispanic Task Force and is one of the few members of the Senate who speaks Spanish.
