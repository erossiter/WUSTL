SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO COMMUNITIES TO BENEFIT FROM FEDERAL FUNDS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that three New Mexico communities will benefit from federal funding awarded through the U.S. Department of Justice (DOJ).  
   "The funding awarded today will help law enforcement efforts to reduce crime and assist victims," Bingaman said. 
   The following organization/communities were awarded: 
   * Morning Star House, Inc.: $255,000 to help provide transitional housing assistance, such as rent or utilities, to victims of domestic violence; 
   * Valencia County: $150,000 from DOJ's Gang Resistance Education and Training program to help provide gang awareness education to middle school students; 
   * Pueblo of San Felipe: $150,000 to support the community's Traditional Tribal Court.
