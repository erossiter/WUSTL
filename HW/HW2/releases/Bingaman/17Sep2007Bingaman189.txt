SEN. BINGAMAN: FUNDING TO BENEFIT NEW MEXICO SMALL BUSINESSES
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Women's Economic Self-Sufficiency Team (WESST) Corp. in Albuquerque has been awarded a $200,000 grant from the federal government. 
   Funding will help the WESST Corp. continue its work to help entrepreneurs start and build their businesses. 
   "WESST Corp. provides New Mexico entrepreneurs with the information and resources they need to succeed in the marketplace. This funding will help WESST Corp. continue its efforts," Bingaman said. 
   WESST Corp. provides financial backing to traditionally underserved communities and encourages the development of community-focused businesses. The primary focus of its services is on individuals and organizations that create positive social benefits in their communities through job creation, affordable housing development, and the provision of basic services in disadvantaged communities throughout the state of New Mexico. 
   WESST Corp has regional offices in Gallup, Las Cruces, Roswell, and Santa Fe.
