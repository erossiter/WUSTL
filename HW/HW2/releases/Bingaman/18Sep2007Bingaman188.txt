SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman gives an overview of what the Senate is debating this week. 
   02:09 - Bingaman talks about the energy and water spending bill that funds New Mexico's national laboratories. 
   04:25 - Bingaman talks about General Petraeus's testimony before Congress last week. 
   05:52 - Bingaman says he looks forward to learning more about Attorney General nominee Michael Mukasey during the Senate Judiciary Committee hearings. 
   06:46 - Bingaman talks about the reauthorization of the No Child Left Behind bill. 
   08:28 - Bingaman talks about legislation Congress passed to renew the government's electronic surveillance program.
