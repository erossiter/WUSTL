REP. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the Supreme Courts ruling on Guantanamo detainee prisoners. 
   02:10 - Bingaman talks about the high cost of prescription drugs in New Mexico. 
   04:45 - Bingaman talks about how the Bureau of Land Management's planed energy corridor route will affect the local habitats and landscapes in Chaves County. 
   05:35 - Bingaman talks an amendment to the Department of Homeland Security appropriations bill currently on the Senate floor. 
   08:13 - Bingaman talks about border security. 
   09:26 - Bingaman talks about the current status of immigration legislation. 
   11:20 - Bingaman talks about his bill - the "Taxpayer Protection Act of 2005".
