SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS ABOUT INDIAN HEALTH BILL WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters about an Indian Health bill that passed the Senate yesterday. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right. 
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the Indian Health Care Improvement Act that passed the Senate yesterday. 
   02:00 - Bingaman talks about his meeting with southern New Mexico law enforcement officials about violence along the New Mexico-Mexico border. 
   04:54 - Bingaman talks about the Redeployment of United States Troops from Iraq bill currently being debated on the Senate floor. 
   11:29 - Bingaman comments on plans to construct a new plutonium pit manufacturing facility at Los Alamos National Labs. 
   13:58 - Bingaman comments on proposals for tougher state rules for oil and gas pits. 
   14:25 - Bingaman talks about yesterday's Energy Committee hearing on the country's Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR).
