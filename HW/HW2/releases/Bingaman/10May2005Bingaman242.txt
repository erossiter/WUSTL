SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed yesterday during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about a bill he is cosponsoring with Senator Domenici that would help New Mexico communities meet their water needs.
   02:06 - Bingaman states his concerns regarding the Central American Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA). 
   04:43 - Bingaman said he hopes New Mexico's bases will not be on the BRAC list, which will be released on Friday.
   07:20 - Bingaman talks about the federal government's role in reimbursing border hospitals and health care centers for the medial treatment of undocumented workers. 
   08:52 - Bingaman talks about the transportation bill currently on the Senate floor and language that has been included to increase funding for school bus roads on the Navajo reservation.
  13:31 - Bingaman explains the purpose and function of a filibuster.
   16:16 - Bingaman talks about the future of a bill to privatize Social Security. 
   19:38 - Bingaman talks about the Republican's proposal to kill the filibuster and to end debate on judicial nominees. 
   22:26 - Bingaman talks about a bill he is cosponsoring with Senator Domenici that would help New Mexico communities meet their water needs.
