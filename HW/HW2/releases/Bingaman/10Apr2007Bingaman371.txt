SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of other topics during his weekly radio press conference.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the stem cell legislation currently on the Senate floor. 
   03:12 - Bingaman talks about the energy legislation he has authored to expand the use of renewable energy and about his plans to introduce climate change legislation. 
   06:44 - Bingaman says he supports legislation to require that the oil and gas industry pay its obligated royalties. 
   09:14 - Bingaman says he hopes Congress can pass an immigration bill that will strengthen the country's borders and work to address illegal immigration. 
   10:52 - Bingaman talks about the Iraq supplemental appropriations bill that passed the Senate last month. 
   13:55 - Bingaman talks about the Renewable Replacement Warhead (RRW). 
   15:22 - Bingaman comments on Iran and North Korea. 
   17:42 - Bingaman talks about an amendment that was included in the Iraq supplemental appropriations bill that would fully fund the Payment In Lieu of Taxes (PILT) program.
