SEN. BINGAMAN: ANGEL FIRE WASTEWATER PROJECT TO BENEFIT FROM FEDERAL LOAN
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Village of Angel Fire has been approved for a $2.855 million federal low-interest loan for a wastewater project. 
   Funding will be used for the acquisition and construction of water and wastewater pipes, sewer pump stations, manholes, fire hydrants, and fencing.
   "This loan is an important investment in Angle Fire and in its residents," Bingaman said.
   Funds were provided through the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Rural Development Program. The loan will be paid back over 20 years at an interest rate of 4.125 percent.
