SEN. BINGAMAN: EPA AWARDS $100,000 TO ACOMA PUEBLO
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Pueblo of Acoma will receive $100,000 from the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency to address the problem of illegal dumping of solid waste. 
   "This funding will be put to good use to both implement a cleanup plan and prevent illegal dumping on the pueblo," Bingaman said.
