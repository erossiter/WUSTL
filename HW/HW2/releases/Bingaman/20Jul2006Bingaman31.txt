SEN. BINGAMAN: LAS CRUCES TO BENEFIT FROM HUD FUNDS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the City of Las Cruces has been awarded over $1.5 million in grant funding from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD).  
   Funds were awarded under the following HUD programs: 
   * The Community Development Block Grants Program (CDBG) - $1.019 million CDBG helps communities fund economic development initiatives. 
   * HOME - $502,636.00 The HOME program provides grants to fund housing programs that meet local needs and priorities. 
   "This funding will be put to good use expanding economic development opportunities in Las Cruces and provide housing to those who need it," Bingaman said.
