SEN. BINGAMAN: FUNDING WILL BENEFIT NEW MEXICO TEACHERS, STUDENTS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman announced today that the four New Mexico higher learning institutions have been awarded federal grants to support programs aimed at improving instruction to students with limited English proficiency (LEP). 
   Funding will be used to develop and design activities to improve a teacher's education program in order to better prepare them to provide effective instruction to LEP students.
   The following colleges and universities will receive funding:
   * University of New Mexico $299,965
   * New Mexico Highlands University $264,971
   * Santa Fe Community College $259,488
   * Northern New Mexico College $210,659
   "This funding will help provide teachers with the skills they need to help students become proficient in English," Bingaman said.
   Funding was provided through the U.S. Department of Education. Bingaman is a senior member of the Senate Health, Education, Labor and Pensions (HELP) Committee, the panel that oversees the department.
