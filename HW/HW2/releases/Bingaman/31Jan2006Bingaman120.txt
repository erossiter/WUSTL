SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
 Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about new legislation he is introducing with Senator Pete Domenici aimed at helping America maintain its leading edge in science and technology.
   01:52 - Bingaman talks about his decision to vote no on the confirmation of Sam Alito to the U.S. Supreme Court.
   06:44 - Bingaman comments on reports that Members of the Mexican military have crossed into the United States more that 200 over the last nine years.
   08:12 - Bingaman talks about the 58-42 vote to confirm Alito. 
   10:11 - Bingaman comments on a proposal to create a separate pension program for Los Alamos National Laboratory employees and retirees, as proposed by the lab's new management team.
  11:53 - Bingaman talks about his proposals aimed at addressing the numerous problems associated with the Medicare prescription drug plan.
   14:28 - Bingaman talks about the president's State of the Union Address tonight.
   17:41 - Bingaman talks about the budget reconciliation bill that will be before Congress this week.
   19:20 - Bingaman talks about his bill to combat meth.
