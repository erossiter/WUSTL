SEN. BINGAMAN MEETS WITH 2007 NEW MEXICO TEACHER OF THE YEAR
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today met with Tamra Tiong, New Mexico's 2007 Teacher of the Year.
   Ms. Tiong is a special education teacher at Dulce Elementary School and was also a 2007 National Teacher of the Year Finalist.
