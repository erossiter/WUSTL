SEN. BINGAMAN: PORTALES, ALBUQUERQUE BENEFIT FROM HUD FUNDING
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman announced today that two New Mexico organizations have been awarded Economic Development Initiative (EDI) grants through the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development.  
   Eastern New Mexico University has been awarded $613,800 and will use the funding to upgrade computer equipment throughout the university, including outfitting the science labs with computers. 
   The YMCA of Central New Mexico in Albuquerque has been awarded $495,000 to begin construction of a westside facility. 
   "These federal funds are an important investment in these two communities and I have no doubt that they will be put to good use," Bingaman.
