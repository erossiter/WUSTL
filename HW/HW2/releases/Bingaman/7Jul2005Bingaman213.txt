SEN. BINGAMAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON CHANGING OF COMMAND AT KIRTLAND AIR FORCE BASE
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today released the following statement regarding the change of command at Kirtland Air Force Base (AFB).  
 Colonel Henry L. Andrews, Jr. is the outgoing Commander of the 377th Air Base Wing at KAFB. Colonel Terrence A. Feehan is the incoming commander.
  "I worked with Col. Andrews on many issues important to Kirtland Air Force Base, including quality of life improvements, strengthening mission capabilities, and improving minority recruitment and hiring practices," Bingaman said. "Col. Andrews has been a great asset to Kirtland Air Force Base and the Albuquerque community. His dedication, commitment and leadership to Kirtland will leave a lasting impression and there is no doubt that he will be missed." 
  "I would also like to welcome Col. Terrence Feehan, incoming commander of the 377th Air Base Wing at Kirtland. I look forward to working with him and helping him meet the base's important missions," Bingaman said.
