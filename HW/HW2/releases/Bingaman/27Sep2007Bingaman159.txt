SEN. BINGAMAN: NAVAJO TECHNICAL COLLEGE AWARDED $2.88 MILLION FEDERAL GRANT
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that Navajo Technical College in Crownpoint has been awarded a $2.88 million grant from the U.S. Department of Education.  
   Funding will help the college improve many of its programs. Proposed projects include: expand the culinary arts program, provide advanced certification for the automotive/diesel program, develop a plumbing certification program, increase standards for the information technology program, launch a new computational science program, achieve accreditation for degrees in environmental studies, upgrade the legal advocate program and increase the standards for the veterinary technicians program by developing it into an animal science associates degree. 
   "I am pleased the funding awarded today will help Navajo Technical College expand their curriculum and help ensure students have access to the education and skills they need to compete in today's job market," Bingaman said.
