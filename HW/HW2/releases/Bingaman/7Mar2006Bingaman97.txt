SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about today's Energy Committee hearing on energy independence.
   01:54 - Bingaman comments on the administrations request for a line item veto.
   04:48 - Bingaman talks about increasing energy efficiency in the transportation sector.
   08:03 - Bingaman talks about the future of NADBank.
   09:42 - Bingaman talks about President Bush's fiscal year 2007 proposal to make deep cuts to public broadcasting.
   11:40 - Bingaman talks about the Navajo-Gallup Water Settlement.
   14:20 - Bingaman talks about the administration's proposal to sell-off public lands.
