SEN. BINGAMAN: EPA FUNDS AWARDED TO SANTA CLARA PUEBLO
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman announced today that the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency has awarded $110,000 to the Santa Clara Pueblo to support the development of a tribal environmental protection program. 
   Santa Clara Pueblo will use the funds to maintain its environmental office, participate in environmental meetings and training, as well as conduct tribal community outreach. 
   "I am pleased that the federal government has made these funds available to help Santa Clara Pueblo continue environmental efforts," Bingaman said.
