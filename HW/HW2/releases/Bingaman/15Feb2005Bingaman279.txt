SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered question on a variety of topics discussed today during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about two bills he introduced last week. The first deals with Medicaid while the second works to strengthen Hispanic Serving Institutions.
   03:14 - Bingaman talk about his decision to run for a fifth tern in the Senate.
   05:32 - Bingaman talks about his concerns regarding the president's 2006 budget.
   08:18 - Bingaman comments on the president's proposal to cut forest service funds.
   12:25 - Bingaman shares his concerns on the president's budget cutting funding for colonias in southern New Mexico.
   14:10 - Bingaman talks about bi-partisan legislation he introduced to create a Medicaid commission. 
   18:05 - Bingaman shares his views on Social Security reform.
   20:38 - Bingaman comments the president's plan to make the tax cuts permanent.
   22:26 - Bingaman talks about passing a transportation bill this congress.
