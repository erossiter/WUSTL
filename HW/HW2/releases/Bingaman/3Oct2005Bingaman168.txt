SEN. BINGAMAN: ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT GRANT AWARDED TO EASTERN NEW MEXICO PLAINS COUNCIL
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today announced that the U.S. Economic Development Administration (EDA) has awarded the Eastern Plains Council of Governments a $70,539 grant.  
 EDA is part of the U.S. Department of Commerce. 
   Bingaman said the funding would be used to develop an economic development plan.
   "It is a good idea to have a regional strategy for job creation. This $70,539 federal grant will be put to good use by the Eastern Plains Council of Governments to develop an economic development plan that will greatly benefit the region," Bingaman said. "This is a very good investment in eastern New Mexico."
