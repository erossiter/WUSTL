ENERGY AND NATURAL RESOURCES COMMITTEE, THE COMMITTEE ON COMMERCE, SCIENCE AND TRANSPORTATION TO HOLD A JOINT HEARING
  On Wednesday, Sen. Jeff Bingaman will take part in an Energy Committee and Commerce Committee joint hearing to look at energy pricing and profits. 
   The committees will hear testimony from the chief executive officers of major integrated oil and gas companies, who will discuss record prices of oil, gasoline, and natural gas and look at factors affecting those prices. The committees will also look at issues relating to global demand, resource development strategies and windfall profits taxes and the effectiveness of federal and state consumer protection laws to prevent occurrences of price gouging during supply disruptions. 
   The committees will also hear from the Chairman of the Federal Trade Commission and from state attorneys general from the states of New Jersey, South Carolina and Arizona. They will discuss the effectiveness of federal and state consumer protection laws designed to prevent occurrences of price gouging during supply disruptions.
   On Thursday, the Energy Committee will hold a hearing to consider the nominations of Jeffrey D. Jarrett to be Assistant Secretary for Fossil Energy at the Department of Energy (DOE) and Edward F. Sproat, III to be Director of the Office of Civilian Radioactive Waste Management at DOE.
