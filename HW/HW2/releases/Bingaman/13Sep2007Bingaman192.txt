SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL FUNDS WILL HELP REDUCE CRIME AND ASSIST VICTIMS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that three New Mexico communities will share $1.3 million in grant funding from the U.S. Department of Justice.  
   "The funding awarded today will help law enforcement agencies in New Mexico reduce crime and assist victims of domestic violence," Bingaman said. 
   The following organization/communities were awarded funds: 
   * New Mexico Department of Public Safety: $539,000 to help improve state and local criminal justice systems and reduce crime. 
   * North Central Community Based Services in Chama: $467,217 to provide additional victim services, enhance education and awareness and develop a violence prevention task force. 
   * Fiore Industries, Inc. in Albuquerque : $299,974 to develop a new tool that would help police officers stop a vehicle at a standoff distance, to avoid high speed chases.
