SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS HIGH GAS PRICES WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  During his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters Sen. Jeff Bingaman talked about an Energy Committee hearing he held today on high gas prices. Bingaman also answered questions on a variety of other topics.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talk about today's Energy Committee hearing on high gas prices. 
   04:40 - Bingaman talks about the recent surge in violence along New Mexico's border with Mexico. 
   06:25 - Bingaman comments on the president's executive order directing federal agencies to find a way to begin regulating vehicle emissions by the time he leaves office. 
   08:35 - Bingaman talks about immigration reform legislation that is expected to come to the floor this week. 
   12:59 - Bingaman talks about the Iraq Supplemental Spending Bill that the president vetoed. 
   15:37 - Bingaman comment on the recent election in France. 
   17:15 - Bingaman gives a general outlook on what the Senate will consider after the Memorial Day recess.
