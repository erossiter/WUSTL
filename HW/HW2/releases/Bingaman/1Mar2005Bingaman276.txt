SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
 Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed today during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website.
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about his participation in a panel at the National Governors Association's 2005 National Education Summit on High Schools on Sunday.
   03:19 - Bingaman comments on the Senate leadership's proposal to do away with filibusters. 
   06:20 - Bingaman talks about ways to improve the transition between high school and secondary education.
   07:42 - Bingaman comments on his legislation he is cosponsoring to keep the administration from opening the northern border to Canadian cattle imports.
   09:37 - Bingaman talks about the bankruptcy reform legislation currently on the Senate floor. 
   11:30 - Bingaman talks about his concern at the administrations proposal to cut oil and gas research and development.
   11:30 - Bingaman comments on his opposition to opening ANWR to oil drilling. 
   13:17 - Bingaman talks about his opposition to the extension on the Milk Income Loss Contract (MILC) program. 
   14:15 - Bingaman talks about teaming up with Sen. Domenici in calling for proposals to address the challenge of meeting the nation's ever increasing demand for water.
   18:17 - Bingaman talks about Social Security reform coming to the floor.
