SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right:  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about President Bush's request for an additional $46 billion for the war in Iraq. 
   03:14 - Bingaman comments on immigration raids in Otero county and about law suited that have been filed in response to the raids. 
   06:30 - Bingaman says New Mexican's continue to express concerns about the country's current situation in Iraq. 
   09:43 - Bingaman talks about legislation he recently introduced to address the country's water resources. 
   12:30 - Bingaman talks about his legislation to limit greenhouse emissions through a cap and trade system. 
   14:09 - Bingaman comments on the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act. 
   18:23 - Bingaman talks about the Labor, HHS, and Education Appropriations bill currently being debated on the Senate floor. 
   21:00 - Bingaman says he support allowing Border Patrol and Homeland Security make the informed decisions about how to keep our borders safe.
