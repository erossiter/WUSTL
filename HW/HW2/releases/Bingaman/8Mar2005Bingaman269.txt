SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed yesterday during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters. 
   Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by clicking on the following link:
   http://democrats.gov/actualities/bingaman/bingaman 030805mm.mp3 
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the cuts to Title I schools in New Mexico.
   02:05 - Bingaman talks about efforts in the Senate to increase the minimum wage. 
   04:10 - Bingaman talks about the lack of cutting for No Child Left Behind.
   09:04 - Bingaman comments on his opposition to opening ANWR to oil drilling. 
   11:15 - Bingaman talks about the bankruptcy reform legislation currently on the Senate floor. 
   13:53 - Bingaman comments on the rise in gas prices and what the United States should do to bring it down.
   17:42 - Bingaman talks about his trip to ANWR in 2002.
   20:35 - Bingaman talks about his objection to Social Security privatization.
   22:31 - Bingaman talk about the Navajo-Gallup water settlement.
   23:32 - Bingaman talks about an Energy Committee hearing last week where he expressed to Secretary Bodman his concerns surrounding the process to seek bids for the contract to manage Los Alamos National Laboratory.
