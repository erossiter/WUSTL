SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NM RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the President's visit to the U.S.-Mexico border.
   02:40 - Bingaman talks about the Pentagon-appointed team that will visit Cannon Air Force Base this week.
   04:09 - Bingaman talks about his disappointment in a provision included in major legislation that sets aside $1 billion for an unnecessary milk subsidy that benefits farmers in only a few states
   05:22 - Bingaman talks about the federal government's need to strengthen security along the U.S.-Mexico border. 
   11:48 - Bingaman talks about the need to resume U.S. beef exports to Japan. 
  13:03 - Bingaman talks about upcoming Iraqi elections.
