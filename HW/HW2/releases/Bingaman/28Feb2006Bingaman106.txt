SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about today's Health, Education, Labor and Pensions Committee hearing on the educational component of the PACE Act. New Mexico's Secretary of Education Veronica Garcia was among those testifying.
   02:00 - Bingaman comments on the possibility of additional hearings on the Presidents wiretapping program. 
   03:08 - Bingaman talks about the administrations controversial decision to allow a state-owned Dubai company to manage major U.S. seaports.
   05:42 - Bingaman talks about a U.S. House of Representatives immigration bill that calls for the building of a wall on the U.S.-Mexican border.
   06:38 - Bingaman talks about the administration's proposal to sell-off public lands.
   08:01 - Bingaman talks about the administrations controversial decision to allow a state-owned Dubai company to manage major US seaports. 
   11:30 - Bingaman talks about border security and his view on the building of a wall on the U.S.-Mexican border.
   14:38 - Bingaman talks about the administrations proposal to cut funding for Wildfire prevention and how it will hurt in New Mexico.
   16:00 - Bingaman talks about immigration reform.
   18:48 - Bingaman talks about the Zuni, McKinley and Grant County school Districts' Impact Aid lawsuit. 
   20:49 - Bingaman comments on continuing report of Guantanamo detainee abuses.
   22:15 - Bingaman comments on uranium mining on the Navajo reservation.
