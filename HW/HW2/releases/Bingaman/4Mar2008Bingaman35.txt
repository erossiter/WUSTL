SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about today's Energy Committee Hearing on the Energy Information Administration's revised Annual Energy Outlook, specifically the high price of oil. 
   04:02 - Bingaman comments on the Fort Sill Apache Tribe Casino debate. 
   05:38 - Bingaman comments on the role of super delegates in the democratic presidential election. 
   06:58 - Bingaman talks about a letter he wrote to the Mexican Ambassador about violence along the New Mexico-Mexico border. 
   09:00 - Bingaman said the Senate is expected to begin debating the fiscal year 2009 Budget Resolution, which outlines Congress's spending priorities. 
   11:04 - Bingaman talks about research funding that was awarded to New Mexico State University.
