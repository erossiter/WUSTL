SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman answered questions on a variety of topics discussed yesterday during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the need for legislation to address global warming and his plans to offer an amendment to the energy bill this issue.
   01:55 - Bingaman talks about his meeting with Prime Minister Tony Blair to discuss climate change.
   04:36 - Bingaman said he looks forward to debating the energy bill on the Senate floor in the coming weeks.
   06:28 - Bingaman comments on reports that a Los Alamos National Laboratory whistle-blower was assaulted in Santa Fe.
   07:49 - Additional comments on meeting with Prime Minister Tony Blair to discuss Climate change.
   10:25 - Bingaman comments on proposals to enact biennial budget reform, which would enact budgets and appropriations bill every other year.
   11:38 - Bingaman talks about the Supreme Court's decision yesterday which determined that federal law supersedes state laws allowing marijuana for medical purposes.
   16:25 - Bingaman talks about the Senate voting today to end debate on the nomination of Janice Rogers Brown to the U.S. Appeals Court.
