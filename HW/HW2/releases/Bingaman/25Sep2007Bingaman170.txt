SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the Water Resources Development Act that passed the Senate yesterday. 
   01:43 - Bingaman comments on proposed plans for the construction of the first 100 miles of fencing authorized by Congress along the U.S.-Mexico border. 
   03:11 - Bingaman talks about his hearing in the Energy Committee yesterday to look at the affects of climate change on wildfire activity in the U.S. 
   05:55 - Bingaman talks about the energy and water spending bill that funds Los Alamos National Labs.
