SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today talked to New Mexico radio reporters and answered questions on a variety of other topics. Bingaman's remarks can be retrieved by going to his website or clicking on the link to the right: 
   00:00 - Bingaman says efforts continue to try to pass legislation to cover America's uninsured children. 
   01:02 - Bingaman talks about the Amtrak Authorization bill currently on the Senate floor. 
   03:54 - Bingaman comments on a provision in a Senate spending bill that would expand hate crimes legislation. 
   06:20 - Bingaman talks about an Inspector Generals report that found that Los Alamos National Laboratory's largest subcontractor routinely overcharged for its work 
   07:58 - Bingaman talks about funding being requested by the president to continue efforts in Iraq and Afghanistan.
