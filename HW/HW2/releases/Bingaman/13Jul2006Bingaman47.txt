SEN. BINGAMAN-SECURED FUNDS FOR DEMING FOOD PANTRY ARE RELEASED
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today said he is pleased that the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) is releasing $247,500 he secured to construct a new food bank in Luna County.  
   Bingaman reported the funds will be used by the Deming Helping Hand Emergency Food Bank 
   "The Helping Hand Emergency Food Bank provides an extremely important service to residents of Luna County and I was very pleased to secure this much-needed funding to help the organization expand operations," Bingaman said. 
   Bingaman secured the funding in the fiscal year 2006 spending bill that funds HUD and other federal agencies.
