SENS. BINGAMAN TALKS TO NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS ABOUT BIPARTISAN, BICAMERAL PLAN TO COVER AMERICA'S UNINSURED
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman talked to New Mexico radio reporters today about legislation he has introduced aimed at helping cover the country's uninsured. Bingaman also answered questions on a variety of topics discussed during his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the Health Partnership Act, legislation he introduced today aimed at extending health care to the nearly 50 million who lack coverage. 
   02:03 - Bingaman comment on an a proposal to fund government programs at last years levels. 
   05:17 - Bingaman talks about how the Health Partnership Act would benefit New Mexico. 
   08:35 - Bingaman says the President has not made the case for sending additional troops to Iraq. 
   10:42 - Bingaman talks about how the Health Partnership Act would benefit help states provide health coverage to their citizens. 
   11:59 - Bingaman talks about the ethics reform legislation currently on the Senate floor and about the Energy Committee hearing on oil supply. 
   17:15 - Bingaman talks about the current situation in Iraq. 
   22:10 - Bingaman comments on proposals in Congress to curtail funding for the Iraq conflict.
