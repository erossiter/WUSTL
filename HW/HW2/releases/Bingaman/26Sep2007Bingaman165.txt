SEN. BINGAMAN: NEW MEXICO STATE UNIVERSITY AWARDED FUNDS TO STUDY FOOD SAFETY
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that New Mexico State University has been awarded $599,691 to help improve food safety.
   Funding was awarded through the U.S. Department of Agriculture and will help NMSU make advancements in the study of ways to reduce incidents of food borne illness among children and adults. 
   "Americans should be able to have confidence that their food is free from contaminants and safe to eat. This funding will help NMSU continue to study this important heath issue," Bingaman said.
