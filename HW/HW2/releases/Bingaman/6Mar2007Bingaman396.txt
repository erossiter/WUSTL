SEN. BINGAMAN TALKS WITH NEW MEXICO RADIO REPORTERS ABOUT LEGISLATION TO BOOST U.S. COMPETITIVENESS
  During his weekly radio press conference with New Mexico radio reporters, Sen. Jeff Bingaman today talked about legislation he helped write that aims to boost investment in science and tech education.  
   00:00 - Bingaman talks about the America COMPETES Act - legislation he is cosponsoring to help keep America competitive in the global market place. 
   02:11 - Bingaman comments on Judiciary Committee hearings into the firing of U.S. Attorneys. 
   04:21 - Bingaman talks about a provision in the U.S. Patriot Act that allows the president to appoint new US attorneys without confirmation hearings. 
   06:27 - Bingaman talks about the America COMPETES Act. 
   08:38 - Bingaman talks about how the America COMPETES Act will affect New Mexico. 
   10:13 - Bingaman talks about investigations into conditions at Walter Reed Army Medical Center. 
   16:11 - Bingaman reacts to Scoter Libby being found guilty in the CIA leak case.
