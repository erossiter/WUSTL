SEN. BINGAMAN: DOT AWARDS $6 MILLION TO ALBUQUERQUE SUNPORT FOR IMPROVEMENTS
  U.S. Senator Jeff Bingaman today announced that the Albuquerque International Sunport has been awarded a $6 million grant to rehabilitate the airport's parking apron.
   The funding comes from the Department of Transportation.
   "Airport improvements can be extremely costly, but they're necessary to keep passengers safe and planes on schedule. I'm very glad that the federal government is making a $6 million investment in the Sunport, ensuring that it remains in top shape," Bingaman said.
