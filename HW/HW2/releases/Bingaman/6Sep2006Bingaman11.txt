SEN. BINGAMAN: FEDERAL FUNDS AWARDED FOR SUBSTANCE ABUSE PREVENTION CONFERENCE
  Sen. Jeff Bingaman today said he is pleased the federal government is making funding available for a conference focusing on substance abuse prevention. 
   The U.S. Department of Heath and Human Services has awarded $25,000 to the National Indian Youth Leadership Project in Gallup. Funding will make it possible for the project to plan and host a substance abuse prevention conference. 
   "Prevention education is key in helping curb substance abuse problems in our communities. I am pleased the funding awarded today will make it possible for the National Indian Youth Leadership Project to put on this important conference," Bingaman said.
