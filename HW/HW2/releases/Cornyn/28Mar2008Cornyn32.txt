SEN. CORNYN ISSUE STATEMENT ON $10.4 BILLION CONTRACT FOR V-22 AIRCRAFT PRODUCTION
  U.S. Sen. John Cornyn, a member of the Armed Services Committee, made the following statement Friday regarding a $10,399,123,179 Defense Department contract for Bell Helicopter V-22 Osprey production. Part of the work will be done in Amarillo and Fort Worth.  
   "The V-22 Osprey is a cutting-edge aircraft that has already proven its tremendous worth on the field of battle. This contract will ensure that our troops have the benefit of this state-of-the-art aircraft for decades to come. It is also a huge victory for the Amarillo and North Texas communities that continue to support V-22 production and winning the war on terror." 
   Sen. Cornyn serves on the Armed Services, Judiciary and Budget Committees. In addition, he is Vice Chairman of the Senate Select Committee on Ethics. He serves as the top Republican on the Judiciary Committee's Immigration, Border Security and Refugees subcommittee and the Armed Services Committee's Airland subcommittee. He served previously as Texas Attorney General, Texas Supreme Court Justice, and Bexar County District Judge.
