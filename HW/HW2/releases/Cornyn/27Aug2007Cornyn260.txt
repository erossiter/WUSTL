SEN. CORNYN TOURS EXPANDED BAYLOR TRAUMA CENTER & VIEWS HIGH-TECH MEDICAL UPGRADES
  U.S. Sen. John Cornyn, R-Texas, toured the Baylor University Medical Center Emergency Department today, Tuesday, Aug. 28, 2007. The department is currently undergoing expansion to more than double the capacity for providing emergency care to area patients. The emergency department expansion includes several high-tech upgrades that Sen. Cornyn viewed, including enhanced diagnostic equipment and an electronic medical records system to improve efficiency and alleviate the current overcrowding of the emergency department.
