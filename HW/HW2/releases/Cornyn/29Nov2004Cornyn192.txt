SEN. CORNYN COMMENTS ON PRESIDENT'S NOMINEE FOR SECRETARY OF COMMERCE
  Sen. John Cornyn, a member of President's Export Council, made the following statement Tuesday regarding Carlos Gutierrez, the President's nominee for Secretary of Commerce:
   "Carlos Gutierrez is a true American success story, and I believe his will be an important voice in continuing the administration's efforts to grow good jobs and spread economic success to every corner of our country, and an inspiration to all striving to achieve a better life for their families.
   "As a longtime leader in business and the Latino community, Gutierrez brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the challenging job of keeping America competitive in the global economy."
