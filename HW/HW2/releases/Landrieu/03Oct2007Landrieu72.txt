Landrieu Secures  1 6 B  for Louisiana Defense Projects         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 03 2007    Landrieu Secures  1 6 B  for Louisiana Defense Projects Secures funds for new Avondale ship  Cyber Command and nearly doubles funding for troop protection from IEDs     WASHINGTON    From her seat on the powerful United States Senate Appropriations Committee  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today secured more than  1 6 billion in the Defense Appropriations Bill for projects in Louisiana   The bill is now headed to a conference with the House   Louisiana provides essential vehicles  troops and support for our men and women serving in uniform   Sen  Landrieu said    I am proud to have secured necessary funding give our troops  fighting bravely for our country  the necessary resources to keep us protected at home   In the bill  Sen  Landrieu secured nearly  129 million more than the President s budget request for Armored Security Vehicles  ASVs  to be produced by Textron  Inc   in the New Orleans area   The total appropriation for ASVs is  293 9 million   Sen  Landrieu also secured  3 5 million for the ASV Turret Upgrade Kit   The Pentagon s shortsightedness threatened the whole ASV program  and I am proud to have secured the funding to keep the vehicles rolling out of the Textron plants in Louisiana   Sen  Landrieu said    While even the most armored of Humvees leave our soldier s vulnerable to roadside bomb attacks  ASVs are saving lives every day   The Senate today unanimously agreed to an amendment Sen  Landrieu introduced that will provide  4 million for the 8th Air Force Cyberspace Innovation Center for Cyber Combat Development at Barksdale Air Force Base in Louisiana   The project will support cyber research focused on developing new capabilities for the United States to defend against cyber attacks   This funding is a key step toward improving our capabilities to fight new wars in cyberspace  focusing on destroying those that pose the greatest threat to the United States   Sen  Landrieu said  Sen  Landrieu also secured  1 4 billion to build LPD 26  the tenth ship of the LPD 17 Class Expeditionary Warfare Ship   The ship would be built at Avondale Shipyard near New Orleans   Avondale is one of Louisiana s biggest employers  and this funding will help promote job growth in Louisiana while we work to provide for our Sailors in the Navy   Sen  Landrieu helped to secure  1 billion for National Guard and Reserve equipment after no funding had originally been included in the budget submitted by the President  Sen  Landrieu also secured funding for the following projects   10 million for the SPAWAR Systems Center ITC   4 million for the Alternate Carbon Stationary Fuel Cell Demonstrator   7 5 million for the Air Force Minority Leaders Program   5 million for Advanced Nano Engineered Composities   2 5 million for Biosensors for Defense Applications   2 million for a Lightweight Water Purifier   2 million for the Afikim Holocaust Education Project   7 million for the Procurement Technical Assistance Program   150 million for the Peer Reviewed Breast Cancer Research Program  and  19 million for B 52 upgrades
