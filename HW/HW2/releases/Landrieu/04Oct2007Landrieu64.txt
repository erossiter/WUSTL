Landrieu Statement on Domenici Announcement         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 04 2007    Landrieu Statement on Domenici Announcement     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following statement regarding the decision of Senator Pete Domenici  R N M   not to seek reelection to a seventh term in the United States Senate    The historic Domenici Landrieu Gulf of Mexico Energy Security Act  enacted last year  expanded oil and gas production in the Gulf of Mexico by 8 million acres and shares the revenue with Louisiana and other coastal states for coastal restoration and protection projects  Sen  Landrieu said   A loving husband to his wife  Nancy  Pete Domenici is the father of eight and grandfather of 14   His faith  family and open heart to countless friends have sustained and supported his remarkable public career for many decades  and will continue to do so in the years ahead     An avid champion for our nation s energy security  he is the Senate s leading authority on nuclear energy and the leader of its recent reemergence   Having experienced the pain of one of his own children facing the challenge of mental illness  Senator Domenici has also been one of the foremost advocates for mental treatment   He has my deepest admiration for his service  my support for his decision not to seek reelection  and my prayers for many healthy  robust years ahead   He has been a good friend to Louisiana and the Gulf Coast states  and a respected colleague and mentor in the Senate
