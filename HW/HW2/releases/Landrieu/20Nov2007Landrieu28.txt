Landrieu Comments on Southwest Adding Flight Between NOLA and Nashville         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  11 20 2007    Landrieu Comments on Southwest Adding Flight Between NOLA and Nashville     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on Southwest Airline s recent announcement that the company will add one additional nonstop flight between New Orleans and Nashville for a total of three daily   It is great news that Southwest Airlines has added this additional flight   Sen  Landrieu said   It will help the economic recovery of the New Orleans region by driving more tourism traffic to the city  and opening a new channel for interstate commerce     It also reinforces the good news that New Orleans is open for business  Our hospitality and tourism industries are strong  welcoming thousands of visitors each day
