Landrieu Announces Grant for School Safety         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 08 2006    Landrieu Announces Grant for School Safety West Monroe to receive nearly  40 000     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today announced that the West Monroe Police Department will receive a school safety resource grant totaling  39 489 from the United States Department of Justice Office of Community Oriented Policing  COPS    Our schools must provide a safe environment for Louisiana s children   Sen  Landrieu said    These grants will help secure the necessary equipment so that our students can focus on learning instead of protecting themselves   The grants were awarded through COPS  Secure Our Schools program and will support efforts to improve safety at schools   They will provide up to 50 percent of the total cost of installing metal detectors  locks  lighting  and other equipment that can help deter crime   The grants can also be used to cover 50 percent of the cost of security assessments and training
