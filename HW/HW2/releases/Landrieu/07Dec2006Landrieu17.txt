Landrieu Comments on  100 Million La  Small Business Grant Program         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  12 07 2006    Landrieu Comments on  100 Million La  Small Business Grant Program Pledges to work with Blanco  LRA  and HUD to fast track program implementation     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following statement regarding Louisiana Governor Kathleen Babineaux Blanco s announcement of the Small Business Retention Program   Community Development Block Grant money totaling as much as  100 million  which Sen  Landrieu worked to secure in federal emergency supplemental appropriations funding from her seat on the Senate Appropriations Committee  will restart and sustain small businesses impacted by Hurricanes Katrina and Rita and the levee breaks that followed  Sen  Landrieu said   In my many meetings with Louisiana small businesses  they stress time and again the need for this type of grant funding  Many of our businesses desperately need this help  After first surviving Hurricanes Katrina and Rita and the devastating levee breaks that followed  these business owners then had to deal with another disaster    one of federal bureaucracy  red tape and regulations   I commend Governor Blanco  Secretary of Economic Development Michael Olivier and Matt Stuller  Chair of the Louisiana Recovery Authority  LRA  Task Force on Economic Development and Workforce Development  for launching this important program crucial to our struggling small businesses   In the coming weeks  I will work closely with state officials  the Department of Housing   Urban Development and the local business community to make sure this money gets into the hands of Louisiana businesses as soon as possible
