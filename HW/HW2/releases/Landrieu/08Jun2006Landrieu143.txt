Landrieu Statement on Death of Abu Musab al Zarqawi         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  06 08 2006    Landrieu Statement on Death of Abu Musab al Zarqawi     WASHINGTON     United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following statement regarding the death of Abu Musab al Zarqawi   The war against terrorism  like any war  will have victories and defeats   Today  we celebrate a victory   American armed forces  working in coordination with our allies  successfully located the al Zarqawi s hiding place and attacked him   Osama bin Laden called al Zarqawi the  Prince of Iraq    Through the exemplary bravery of our men and women in uniform  this is one al Qaeda prince that will no longer threaten the lives of innocent people around the globe    General George Casey  our military commander in Iraq  deserves praise for this victory   Additionally  I would like to say a word of gratitude to the people who are most responsible for this success    the rank and file intelligence officers and analysts that were able to provide this vital information to our military   It is a difficult and laborious task that requires tremendous intellect  cunning and insight  but rarely provides much glory   Yet the success of these young men and women is vital to our effort to combat global terrorism   The People of Louisiana and people all over the world thank you for the work you are doing and look forward to further success
