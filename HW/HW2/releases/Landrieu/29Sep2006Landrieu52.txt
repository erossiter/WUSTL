Landrieu Announces Funding for New Orleans VA Hospital         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 29 2006    Landrieu Announces Funding for New Orleans VA Hospital Senate authorizes  636 million for hospital reconstruction     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today announced the passage of S  3421  the Senate VA Medical Facility Authorization Bill   The bill authorizes  636 million for the reconstruction of the New Orleans VA Hospital  which was destroyed during Hurricane Katrina and the subsequent flooding   Funding had previously been appropriated in two emergency supplemental appropriations bills but still needed authorization for the money to be released   The House must now also pass an authorization for the hospital   Our veterans have put their lives and safety on the line to protect all Americans   Sen  Landrieu said    We must provide adequate medical facilities upon their return    Hurricane Katrina and the overwhelming flooding that followed destroyed the New Orleans veterans  hospital  but this funding will ensure the continued protection of our veterans  healthcare   I want to thank Senate Veterans Affairs Committee Chairman Larry Craig and the Ranking Member  Sen  Daniel Akaka  for working with me to authorize this crucial funding for the New Orleans veterans  hospital
