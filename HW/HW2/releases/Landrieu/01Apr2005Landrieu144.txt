Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Health of Pope John Paul II         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  04 01 2005    Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Health of Pope John Paul II     WASHINGTON    The following is a statement by U S  Sen  Mary Landrieu  D La   regarding the health of Pope John Paul II   Sen  Landrieu said   Pope John Paul II will be in the prayers tonight of thousands of Lousianians  as I know he will be in those of millions of others around the world who share our hopes for his recovery   Should his passing be near  we hope it will come with the tranquil peace befitting his lifelong devotion to faith  love and fellowship
