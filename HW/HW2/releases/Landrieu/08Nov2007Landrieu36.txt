Landrieu Comments on Mukasey Confirmation to be Attorney General         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  11 08 2007    Landrieu Comments on Mukasey Confirmation to be Attorney General     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on Judge Michael B  Mukasey s Senate confirmation as Attorney General  Sen  Landrieu said   Judge Mukasey has strong credentials to be the top law enforcement official in our nation  This confirmation gives us hope that we will have an attorney general interested in looking out for the American people  rather than the interests of the Administration s party    This will be a welcome change in leadership at the Justice Department  I look forward to working with Judge Mukasey to combat crime in New Orleans  fight insurance fraud along the Gulf Coast and ensure we are giving proper focus to civil rights cases
