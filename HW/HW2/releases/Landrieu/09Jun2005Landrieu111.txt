Sen  Landrieu on Nomination of William Pryor         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  06 09 2005    Sen  Landrieu on Nomination of William Pryor     WASHINGTON    The following is a statement by U S  Senator Mary Landrieu  D La   regarding her vote against the nomination of William H  Pryor  Jr   to the U S  Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit   As Attorney General of Alabama  William Pryor has called into question the validity of federal laws created to protect Americans  including the Voting Rights Act  Violence Against Women Act  the Americans with Disabilities Act  the Age Discrimination in Employment Act  and the Family and Medical Leave Act    I am concerned that as a Federal Judge he will continue to question these laws and the very basis of our American legal system   In light of Judge Pryor s record  I had to follow my conscience and vote against his confirmation today
