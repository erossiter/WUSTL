Landrieu Comments on New Crop Insurance for La  Sugarcane Farmers         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 19 2007    Landrieu Comments on New Crop Insurance for La  Sugarcane Farmers     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation s approval of the Sugarcane Group Risk Plan Crop Insurance Program that was submitted by the Louisiana Farm Bureau Federation and the American Sugar Cane League  Sen  Landrieu said   This is welcome news for our sugarcane farmers  who like all Louisianians are working hard to recover from the 2005 hurricanes  This new insurance plan will protect our farmers from risks varying from hurricanes to deep freezes that could wipe out a year s worth of crops and deal a devastating financial blow
