SEN  LANDRIEU CONTINUES  INVESTING IN SUCCESS  INITIATIVE AT EVF SCHOOL         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  August 26  2005    SEN  LANDRIEU CONTINUES  INVESTING IN SUCCESS  INITIATIVE AT EVF SCHOOL  Visits Medard Nelson UNO Charter School in New Orleans    WASHINGTON    With the start of the new school year  U S  Senator Mary Landrieu  D La   today continued her year long  Investing in Success  education initiative by touring the Medard Nelson UNO Charter School in New Orleans   The Investing in Success initiative will focus on innovative education programs taking place throughout the state   Medard Nelson UNO Charter School is participating in the recently formed Education Venture Fund  EVF   a non profit statewide public private partnership that harnesses the principles of successful investment and attracts educational entrepreneurs from the business community to lead in the effort to develop high performing schools   Working together we can do more than any one entity can do alone   said Sen  Landrieu    This is a new and bold approach  for which the possible returns are unlimited   By bringing new vision to our classrooms  we can prepare the next generation of Louisiana leaders   For the 2005 06 school year  the goal is to raise  4 million through EVF to seed turnaround efforts for at least 10 schools in Orleans Parish   The following school year  EVF will be working to raise  7 million to assist 18 schools statewide   Thereafter  EVF is hoping to raise  10 million annually for 25 schools statewide     30
