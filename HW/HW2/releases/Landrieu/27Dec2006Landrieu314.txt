Landrieu Statement on the Death of President Gerald Ford         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  12 27 2006    Landrieu Statement on the Death of President Gerald Ford     WASHINGTON    The following is a statement by U S  Senator Mary Landrieu  D La   regarding the death former President Gerald Ford  Sen  Landrieu said   Gerald Ford s career of service to our nation spanned generations  beginning in World War II and continuing through three decades in government   He will  however  be remembered most for his leadership as our President in the difficult months following the Watergate scandal and the Vietnam War   His courageous  if unpopular  decision to pardon President Nixon healed many deep wounds and allowed our nation to move forward    President Ford is deeply respected and will be remembered fondly by the people of Louisiana  and he  his wife Betty  and their family will remain in our hearts and in our prayers
