Sen  Landrieu Congratulates C  Ray Nagin on Reelection         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  05 20 2006    Sen  Landrieu Congratulates C  Ray Nagin on Reelection Pledges Partnership for New Orleans  Future    NEW ORLEANS    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released a statement tonight following the reelection of New Orleans Mayor C  Ray Nagin  Sen  Landrieu said   I congratulate Mayor Nagin on his election victory   Tonight  we all embarked on the next leg of our journey to recovery together  and I look forward to working with him as we build back our hometown and all of south Louisiana better and stronger than before   In me  Mayor Nagin continues to have a willing partner in the United States Senate   I will continue to do all that I can to make sure that our Louisiana communities and their leaders have the federal tools  resources and funding necessary to rebuild and recover smartly and safely
