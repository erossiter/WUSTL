Sen  Landrieu Remebers Coretta Scott King         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  1 31 06    Sen  Landrieu Remebers Coretta Scott King     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released the following statement today on the passing of Civil Rights Leader Coretta Scott King  Sen  Landrieu said   Coretta Scott King dedicated her life to helping America be a more just nation   Her work will always be remembered and reflected in the extraordinary progress of not only African Americans  but all Americans toward our pursuit of economic justice in the workplace and equality under the law     Our nation is a better place today thanks to the work of Mrs  King  her late husband  the Rev  Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr   and the millions of people who joined them in the belief that America can do better      I join all Americans in offering my thoughts and prayers to her family and friends at this time     30
