Sen  Landrieu Introduces Budget Amendment to Help Close Military Pay Gap         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  03 16 2005    Sen  Landrieu Introduces Budget Amendment to Help Close Military Pay Gap     WASHINGTON     U S  Sen  Mary Landrieu  D La   tonight introduced an amendment to S  Con  Res  18  the Budget Resolution  that would reserve a portion of the federal budget to provide funds for Sen  Landrieu s Helping Our Patriotic Employers at Helping Our Military Employees Act   The HOPE at HOME Act  which has been introduced in the House of Representatives by Rep  Tom Lantos  D Calif    would provide a tax credit to businesses who continue to pay the salaries of employees who are activated for Guard or Reserve duty     Our Guard and Reserve put their lives on the line to fight for each of us  our freedoms and our livelihood   Sen  Landrieu said    They should be the first in line when it comes to tax relief   Surely we can afford to fight for them and help secure their livelihood   They already volunteer for the ultimate sacrifice   They shouldn t have to sacrifice their financial stability to do so   The amendment would also give a tax credit to businesses needing to hire a temporary replacement for a deployed Guardsman or Reservist  More than 40 percent of our troops in Iraq and Afghanistan are members of the Guard and Reserve  and according to the Government Accountability Office  41 percent of them take a pay cut to serve   In October  the Senate passed a similar measure following a three day filibuster by Sen  Landrieu during debate over a corporate tax relief bill   Unfortunately  time ran out on the 108th Session of Congress before the measure could be brought all the way to being signed into law   We have voted time and again to hand out relief to corporations for far less important reasons   Sen  Landrieu said    Let us now take this opportunity to honor our troops by honoring those businesses who work to keep Guard and Reserve paychecks whole   Sen  Landrieu was last week honored with the G V   Sonny  Montgomery Eagle Award for congressional leadership by the Enlisted Association of the National Guard of the United States  EANGUS    The Eagle Award is the association s highest tribute and is awarded each year to Members of Congress who have made strong contributions to national defense  especially through promoting a strong National Guard   Sen  Landrieu was recognized for her work on National Guard issues  including the efforts to close the Guard and Reserve pay gap
