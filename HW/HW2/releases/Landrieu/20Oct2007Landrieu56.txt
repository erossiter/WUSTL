Statement on Bobby Jindal Election         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 20 2007    Statement on Bobby Jindal Election Senator secures leadership  key committee commitments tomeet with Governor elect and advance Louisiana s needs     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released a statement tonight following the election of Congressman Bobby Jindal  R La   as Louisiana s next Governor  Sen  Landrieu said   I congratulate Governor elect Jindal and look forward to working together with him to move Louisiana forward   We campaign as Democrats and Republicans  but we serve as Louisianians   Our state continues to face incredible challenges that require us to work together across party lines  racial divides and geographic boundaries to advance Louisiana s cause   Together  we can create hope  promote reform and deliver opportunity across our state   Louisiana s leaders have no greater responsibility      I have contacted key Senate leaders and committee chairs and secured commitments to meet with Governor elect Jindal in the coming weeks to advance our state s needs   There are a range of vital issues on which our Louisiana team will have to work together  including appropriations  tax incentives  hurricane recovery  disaster preparation  transportation  housing  energy  health care  veterans  facilities  ports and waterways  education  levee protection and coastal restoration   By speaking with one strong voice  we greatly enhance our chances for success       I stand ready to work with Governor elect Jindal as we champion our great state and her interests   Louisiana s fights are our fights  and we must fight them together
