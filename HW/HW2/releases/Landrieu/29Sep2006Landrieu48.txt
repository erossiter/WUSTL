Response to Vitter Statement on OCS Legislation Status         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 29 2006    Response to Vitter Statement on OCS Legislation Status     WASHINGTON    Adam Sharp  Communications Director for U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following response to a statement issued earlier by U S  Senator David Vitter  R La   regarding the status of oil and gas revenue sharing legislation  Sharp said   Senator Vitter is entitled to his own opinion on tactics  but the people of Louisiana know that Senator Landrieu s voice has been strong and effective in fighting for Louisiana s coast   Senator Landrieu believes that our delegation has been a powerful team over the past year  and will continue working together with her colleagues to accomplish their united goal of significant revenue sharing for Louisiana and the Gulf Coast
