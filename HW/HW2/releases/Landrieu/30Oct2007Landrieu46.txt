Landrieu Statement on Eddie Jordan Resignation         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 30 2007    Landrieu Statement on Eddie Jordan Resignation Stepping down of NOLA District Attorney is  a right and necessary step  to fighting crime wave  Senator says     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on the resignation of New Orleans District Attorney Eddie Jordan  Sen  Landrieu said   I support this decision and think this is the right and necessary step in order to rebuild confidence in the office of the District Attorney  which is a crucial element of the criminal justice system that has been badly broken and is in need of repair   This resignation will clear the way for new leadership that is necessary   The mayor and coalition of business leaders will have my full support in helping to provide necessary resources to help with this important effort
