Landrieu Comments on Mukasey Nomination to be Attorney General         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 17 2007    Landrieu Comments on Mukasey Nomination to be Attorney General     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on President Bush s nomination of Michael B  Mukasey to be the next Attorney General  Sen  Landrieu said   Judge Mukasey has had a distinguished  18 year career on the federal bench  bringing admirable credentials to the nation s top law enforcement job  This nomination holds the promise for an attorney general who will defend the law while ignoring partisan politics in his decision making  I look forward to examining his record further and to talking with my colleagues on the Judiciary Committee as they complete their important work considering his nomination
