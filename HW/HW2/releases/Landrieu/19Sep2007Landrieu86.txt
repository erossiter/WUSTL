Landrieu Comments on WRDA Conference Vote in the Senate         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  09 19 2007    Landrieu Comments on WRDA Conference Vote in the Senate     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today commented on the Senate leadership s decision to schedule a floor vote on the Water Resources Development Act  WRDA  for Monday  September 24  Sen  Landrieu said   We have been waiting seven long years for a new WRDA bill  and this year  the House and Senate finally came together in agreement on a bill that will authorize  3 6 billion in crucial flood control  navigation and hurricane protection projects for Louisiana   But a presidential veto threat still looms heavy over this accomplishment  I stand united with the Louisiana delegation in our effort to override a veto and ensure this bill is passed with or without administration approval
