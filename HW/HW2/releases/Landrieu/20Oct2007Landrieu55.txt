Statement on Lt  Governor s Reelection         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 20 2007    Statement on Lt  Governor s Reelection     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released a statement tonight following the reelection of her brother  Lieutenant Governor Mitch Landrieu  D La  Sen  Landrieu said   I congratulate my brother  Lieutenant Governor Mitch Landrieu  on his reelection tonight   He continues to serve Louisiana with distinction  promoting economic development across the state and championing our vibrant cultural heritage and tourism sector   His efforts to heal the wounds of racial division and economic inequality are particularly important at this time  as we must all come together to move Louisiana forward   I am committed to standing by him in this pursuit  and look forward to working with him to champion our state and her interests
