Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Death of Pope John Paul II         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  04 02 2005    Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Death of Pope John Paul II     WASHINGTON    The following is a statement by U S  Sen  Mary Landrieu  D La   regarding the death of Pope John Paul II   Sen  Landrieu said   Today is a day of great sadness as we mourn the death of His Holiness  Pope John Paul II   He was an inspirational leader whose message of peace and fellowship touched the hearts of millions around the world and in Louisiana    When I met the Pope in 2001  I was moved by his devout faith and charitable heart   He leaves behind a legacy of love and devotion for which he will always be remembered
