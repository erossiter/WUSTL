Landrieu Honors America s Veterans         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  11 10 2006    Landrieu Honors America s Veterans Visits with troops at Walter Reed Army Medical Center     WASHINGTON    In honor of Veterans  Day  United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today visited with wounded troops who have returned from Iraq and Afghanistan at Walter Reed Army Medical Center   On the anniversary of the ceasefire of World War I  Armistice Day  we honor America s veterans  the defenders of our freedom   Sen  Landrieu said   Veterans  Day 2006 falls while our nation is at war  Our soldiers fighting in the Middle East follow in the honorable footsteps of our war veterans throughout American history    the great men and women who have served and protected our nation in every conflict since our founding   We as a nation are forever grateful for their sacrifices  Our veterans have put their lives on the line to ensure the safety of the American people and the safety of people all over the world
