Landrieu Statement on Rumsfeld Resignation         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  11 08 2006    Landrieu Statement on Rumsfeld Resignation     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released the following statement today regarding the resignation of Secretary of Defense Donald Rumsfeld  Sen  Landrieu said   Secretary Rumsfeld s resignation is a clear signal that the mandate for change in Iraq has been heard loud and clear   Our goal remains the same    to be victorious    but it is obvious that a change in strategy is needed to make it happen  A new Defense Secretary gives the administration a chance to look at the war in Iraq with a fresh perspective    It is time to put partisan politics aside  to reject the old slogans  to face new realities and to change war tactics that don t work  Congress needs to work together on a bipartisan basis to help come up with a responsible solution to the situation in Iraq    one that allows us to protect our troops  defeat terrorism  stabilize the region and equip the people of Iraq to defend themselves
