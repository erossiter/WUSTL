Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Resignation of Transportation Secretary Norman Mineta         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  06 23 2006    Sen  Landrieu Statement on the Resignation of Transportation Secretary Norman Mineta     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following statement regarding Secretary of Transportation Norman Mineta s announcement of his resignation  Sen  Landrieu said   I m sorry to see Secretary Mineta go  His wisdom and compassion made him one of Louisiana s strongest partners in the Administration following hurricanes Katrina and Rita and the devastating levee breaks that followed  and we are all grateful for his leadership   The Secretary is a strong  intelligent and honorable leader whose career has been one of notable firsts  having broken many barriers for Asian Americans in government    first as the Mayor of San Jose  then as the Chairman of the House Transportation Committee and as a Cabinet secretary under two presidents   But much more than that  he is a good friend   I thank him and wish him nothing but the best as he embarks on the next stage of his career of service
