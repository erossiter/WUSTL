Sen  Landrieu Statement on Vote Supporting Condoleezza Rice         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  01 26 2005    Sen  Landrieu Statement on Vote Supporting Condoleezza Rice      Our responsibility is to determine whether a Cabinet nominee possesses the necessary qualifications for the job and can be expected to execute her duties responsibly   I believe Dr  Rice meets this standard  and have today voted to approve her nomination as Secretary of State     Dr  Rice s journey from childhood in a racially segregated South to her confirmation today as our nation s highest diplomatic officer illustrates the power of the American system of education and opportunity  While I may take issue with some elements of the President s foreign policy plan  Dr  Rice s credentials are impressive and her record of hard work and achievement cannot be denied
