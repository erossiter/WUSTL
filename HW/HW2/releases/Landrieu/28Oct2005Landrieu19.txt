Sen  Landrieu Statement on Libby Indictment         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  10 28 2005    Sen  Landrieu Statement on Libby Indictment     WASHINGTON    United States Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   released the following statement today regarding the indictment of Vice President Dick Cheney s Chief of Staff  I  Lewis  Scooter  Libby  Jr   on charges of obstruction of justice  perjury and making false statements to federal investigators  Senator Landrieu said   Over the last five years  Vice President Dick Cheney and his now indicted Chief of Staff   Scooter  Libby  have been at the center of a number of questionable and now perhaps criminal decisions and strategies   They have hosted secret meetings to direct our nation s energy policies  politicized the intelligence behind the two and a half year   200 billion war in Iraq  and steered countless no bid contracts to the Vice President s former employer  even as the company pushes aside American workers in favor of undocumented  low wage labor in rebuilding the Gulf Coast     Throughout these and other misguided strategies  this Administration has frequently hidden behind its veil of secrecy and distorted facts to meet its political objectives   As the scripture says  you reap what you sow  and today it appears some of these actions will finally be prosecuted   But many more questions must be asked and answered before we get to the real truth behind this particular incident
