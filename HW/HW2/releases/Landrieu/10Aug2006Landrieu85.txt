Landrieu Statement on Foiled Terror Plot         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  08 10 2006    Landrieu Statement on Foiled Terror Plot     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Mary L  Landrieu  D La   today released the following statement regarding the announcement by British officials that they have apprehended 21 suspects in connection with an alleged plot to detonate explosives aboard U S  bound airliners  Sen  Landrieu said   Today s news is a stark reminder that nearly five years after the tragic events of September 11th  the United States is still a prime target for acts of terrorism  and we are still vulnerable in many ways   I commend the British intelligence and law enforcement communities for successfully intercepting this threat and for potentially saving hundreds of American lives   We have spoken a great deal over the past several years about our nation s preparedness for disaster  and lack thereof   But while our recent attention has been focused on natural disasters like hurricanes Katrina and Rita and the devastating levee breaks that followed  this potential attack again reminds us that the next disaster may be man made   Our disaster management agencies will need the resources  authority and support to respond effectively  and I intend to continue working with my colleagues to ensure that these tools are in place
