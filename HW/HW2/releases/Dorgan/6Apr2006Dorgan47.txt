SEN. DORGAN PRAISES MICROSOFT ANNOUNCEMENT OF 100 NEW QUALITY JOBS IN FARGO
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) said the announcement Thursday that Microsoft will add at least 100 new support engineering positions at its Fargo campus is great news and more evidence that his Red River Valley Research Corridor initiative is helping to build a high-tech economy that attracts research and quality jobs to North Dakota. 
   The text of Dorgan's statement is below.
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   April 6, 2006
   "The announcement that at least 100 new support engineering positions will be created at Microsoft's Fargo campus is great news for the city and for North Dakota. Microsoft is a great company and this announcement is further evidence of their commitment to our region.
   "These are good-paying, professional jobs that will complement our efforts to build on the Red River Valley Research Corridor and foster a reputation as a hub of cutting-edge research and technology."
