SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF CPL. CHRISTOPHER KLEINWACHTER
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Friday on the death of Cpl. Christopher Kleinwachter of Wahpeton, North Dakota, who died of injuries sustained during combat operations in Afghanistan. 
   The text of the statement is below. 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN 
   December 1, 2006
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the family of Cpl. Christopher Kleinwachter tonight. 
   "Cpl. Kleinwachter served his country with honor. We owe him a debt of gratitude and I join all North Dakotans in grieving his loss."
