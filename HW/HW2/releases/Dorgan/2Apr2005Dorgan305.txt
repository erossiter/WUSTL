SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF POPE JOHN PAUL II
  Sen. Byron Dorgan issued the following statement Saturday, following the announcement that Pope John Paul II had passed away: 
   "I join the millions throughout the world mourning the passing of Pope John Paul II. The world has lost a great man, and a great force for freedom. He will be remembered as one of the great world leaders of the 20th century, both in matters of spirit and of state."
