DELEGATION ANNOUNCES MORE THAN $1.2 MILLION TO ASSIST VICTIMS OF CRIME
  Sens. Kent Conrad and Byron Dorgan and Rep.  
 Earl Pomeroy today announced that North Dakota has been awarded $1,243,000 for programs to enhance crime victims assistance programs. 
   The grant will be used by the North Dakota Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation. The funds are targeted toward law enforcement and private programs that provide shelter, counseling and other assistance to victims of crime. 
   "A violent crime can have a profound impact on a person's life," the delegation said in a joint statement. "This grant will support programs in the community that help victims and families recover in the aftermath of violent crime." 
   The grant was made available by the U.S. Department of Justice with funds appropriated by Congress.
