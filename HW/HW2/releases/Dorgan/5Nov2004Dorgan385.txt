SEN. DORGAN TO SERVE AS CHAIRMAN OF DEMOCRATIC POLICY COMMITTEE IN NEW SENATE
  Sen. Byron Dorgan issued the following statement on Friday regarding his continuing leadership role in the new U.S. Senate which will convene next year: 
   Text of Statement by U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan 
   I have accepted an invitation by Senator Harry Reid to remain in the Democratic Leadership as Chairman of the Democratic Policy Committee in the next Congress. 
   Our Democratic Caucus needs to be united and aggressive in working on the issues that we know will strengthen this country. I look forward to working with Senator Reid and others in our caucus to achieve these goals.
