SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SGT. NELSON
  U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Friday on the death of Sgt. Andrew Nelson, a 22-year-old Moorhead, Minn., native and Fargo Shanley High School graduate who was killed while on duty in Iraq. 
   The text of the statement is below.
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   August 31, 2007
   "I was deeply saddened to learn of the death of Sgt. Andrew Nelson while serving our country in Iraq.
   "Andrew was a Minnesotan, but he was a part of our community and attended school in Fargo. I join all of our citizens in mourning his loss and offering thoughts and prayers to his family."
