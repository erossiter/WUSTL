SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON RETIREMENT OF UNIVERSITY OF NORTH DAKOTA PRESIDENT CHARLES KUPCHELLA
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Thursday reacting to University of North Dakota (UND) President Charles Kupchella's announcement that he will retire next year.  
   The text of the statement is below. 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   January 25, 2007
   "President Kupchella's announcement that he will retire next year is sad news for UND, Grand Forks, and the state of North Dakota. 
   "I have worked with President Kupchella for years and have been impressed with his leadership and dedication. 
   "He has simply been an outstanding university president. Under his leadership, UND has grown as one of the nation's finest institutes of higher education and a major engine of the Red River Valley Research Corridor. 
   "I wish President Kupchella the very best in his future endeavors."
