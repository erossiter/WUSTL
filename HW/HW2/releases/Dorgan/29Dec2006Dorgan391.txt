SEN. DORGAN CALLS MEETING WITH FARGO CITY OFFICIALS TO DISCUSS FEDERAL PRIORITIES
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) will host a meeting on Tuesday, January 2 with Fargo Mayor Dennis Walaker and city commissioners to discuss the federal priorities that Fargo has for the upcoming year. The meeting will be held at 1:30 PM at Fargo City Hall at 200 3rd Street North.  
   "I look forward to meeting with Mayor Walaker and city commissioners to discuss what federal projects that the city will be working on in 2007," said Dorgan. 
   When the 110th Congress starts in January, Dorgan will continue to serve on the Appropriations Committee and will become chairman of the Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee. He will transition from being ranking member to becoming chairman of the Indian Affairs Committee, and will serve as chairman of the Energy Subcommittee within the Energy and Natural Resources Committee. In addition, Dorgan is expected to become chairman of a subcommittee within the Commerce, Science and Transportation Committee.
