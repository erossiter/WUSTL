SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON NOMINATION OF SCHAFER TO BE U.S. SECRETARY OF AGRICULTURE
  U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued the following statement Wednesday regarding the nomination of former North Dakota Governor Ed Schafer to be U.S. Agriculture Secretary: "Governor Schafer called me this morning and told me he will be nominated by President Bush to be the next Secretary of Agriculture. I congratulated him and told him I look forward to meeting and visiting with him as he begins the confirmation process here in the Senate. I think it's a real honor to have a North Dakotan nominated for a Cabinet position."
