SEN. DORGAN STATEMENT ON DEATH OF CPL. PAUL BEYER OF JAMESTOWN
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) released the following statement Saturday on news that Cpl. Paul Beyer, 21, of Jamestown, was killed in Iraq when a bomb went off near his Humvee. 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   June 24, 2006
   "We were notified today by the Pentagon of the death of Cpl. Paul Beyer of Jamestown.
   "My thoughts and prayers are with his family today. He died serving his country, and all North Dakotans join his family in grieving the loss of this courageous young man."
