SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON COLLAPSE OF I-35W BRIDGE IN MINNEAPOLIS
  U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Thursday on the tragic collapse of the I-35W bridge in Minneapolis, Minnesota. 
   The text of the statement is below.
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   August 2, 2007
   "I was deeply saddened to learn of the tragedy caused by the bridge collapse in the Minneapolis-St. Paul area. My heart goes out to all of those families in our neighboring state whose loved ones have been injured or killed in this tragedy.
   "I know that officials in Minnesota are even now still searching for bodies in that area and I imagine they will move very quickly to try to evaluate what has happened, why it happened, and what can be done to make sure it never happens again. 
   "I join all North Dakotans in praying for those who have been affected by this disaster."
