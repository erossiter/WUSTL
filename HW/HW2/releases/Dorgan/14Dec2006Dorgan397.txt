SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON SEN. JOHNSON
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Thursday on the health condition of Sen. Tim Johnson (D-SD). 
   The text of the statement is below. 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
  BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN 
  December 14, 2006
   "We are all praying for a full recovery for Tim and his family. Tim is a good friend, a neighbor and a wonderful U.S. Senator. I know all North Dakotans will join in offering prayers for Tim's recovery."
