SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON NEW RECORD TRADE DEFICIT OF $69.9 BILLION
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Thursday on the announcement that the United States reached a new record trade deficit of $69.9 billion in August 2006. 
   The text of Dorgan's statement is below.
   TEXT OF STATEMENT BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   October 12, 2006
   "How long will the free trade crowd keep making excuses for an out of control trade deficit?
   "This month's $69 billion deficit undermines American workers and the American economy, and yet the blind architects of this policy act as if it isn't happening."
