SEN. DORGAN TO MEET WITH JUDGE SAMUEL ALITO DEC. 14
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) said Tuesday he will meet with Judge Samuel Alito, the President's nominee to fill the Supreme Court vacancy of Justice Sandra Day O'Connor, in his office tomorrow afternoon. 
   "I have not met Judge Alito before, but I look forward to doing so and to discussing his nomination and the confirmation process with him," Dorgan said.
