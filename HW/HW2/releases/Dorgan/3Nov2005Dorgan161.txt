SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF SPC. DENNIS J. FERDERER
  Sen. Byron Dorgan (D-ND) reacted Thursday to the death of a North Dakota soldier in Iraq. 
   The text of Dorgan's statement follows: 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN 
  November 3, 2005
   "I am saddened today to learn of the death of North Dakota soldier Spc. Dennis Ferderer, who lost his life while serving his country with the U.S. Army in Iraq. My thoughts and prayers are with the Ferderer family. I want them to know that a grateful country honors the sacrifice of their son."
