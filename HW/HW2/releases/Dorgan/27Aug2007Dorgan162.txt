SEN. DORGAN ISSUES STATEMENT ON TORNADO IN NORTHWOOD
  U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan (D-ND) issued a statement Monday on the tornado that killed one, injured 18 and caused significant damage to the city of Northwood, North Dakota.  
   The text of the statement is below. 
   TEXT OF STATEMENT BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN 
  August 27, 2007M/center 
   "My thoughts and prayers are with the victims of the tornado that struck Northwood last night. 
   "I will work to make sure that the federal agencies capable of helping Northwood and its residents rebuild are working with the community immediately."
