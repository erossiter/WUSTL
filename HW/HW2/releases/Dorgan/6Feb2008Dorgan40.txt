SEN. DORGAN CONGRATULATES GOV. SCHAFER ON SWEARING-IN AS SECRETARY OF AGRICULTURE
  U.S. Senator Byron Dorgan (D-ND) congratulated former North Dakota Governor Ed Schafer on Wednesday after a ceremonial swearing-in for him in Washington, D.C. 
   The text of Dorgan's statement is below.
   TEXT OF STATEMENT
   BY SENATOR BYRON DORGAN
   February 6, 2008
   "Congratulations are in order for Secretary Schafer as he takes over this important new post. His family and the entire state of North Dakota have reason to be proud.
   "I look forward to working with Secretary Schafer as we put together a farm bill that will provide a strong safety net for family farmers in North Dakota and across the country. 
   "This is an important piece of legislation that will have a significant impact on the economy of rural America, and it's good to see another North Dakotan take a seat at the negotiating table."
