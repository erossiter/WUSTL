SENS. DODD, LIEBERMAN URGE PRESIDENT BUSH AND APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE TO FUND AMERICA\'S ENERGY NEEDS
  In a bid to help Americans stave off expected high winter energy costs, Connecticut Sens.  
 Dodd (D-CT) and Joe Lieberman (D-CT) yesterday joined a bi-partisan group 35 Senators in sending a letter to President Bush and leaders of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Energy and Water Development, strongly urging for additional funding for the Department of Energy\'s energy conservation and management programs. They ask for current and supplemental comprehensive supplemental funding to help American families, schools, hospitals, the agricultural sector, commercial enterprises, and industry reduce energy consumption through public education campaigns.
