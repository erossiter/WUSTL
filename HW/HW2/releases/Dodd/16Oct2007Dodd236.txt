SEN. DODD ISSUES STATEMENT ON DALAI LAMA'S RECEIPT OF CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL
  Senator Chris Dodd (D-CT), a senior member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, today congratulated the Dalai Lama on being awarded the Congressional Gold Medal. The Dalai Lama, who met with President Bush today, will accept the award at a public ceremony tomorrow.  
   "I am pleased that the Dalai Lama will be receiving the Congressional Gold Medal for his tireless advocacy on behalf of the people of Tibet and his commitment to non-violence and human rights. It is my hope that through constructive and peaceful dialogue the people of China and Tibet can soon resolve their long-standing conflict."
