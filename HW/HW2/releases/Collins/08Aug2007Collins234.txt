                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Federal Share of Damage Expected to be  1 37 Million August 8  2007  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins were notified today by the White House that the President has declared a major disaster in Oxford County  resulting from severe storms and flooding on July 11th and 12th  The storms left six to eight inches of rain in the area  impairing Bethel Water Districts filtration system and devastating the communitys infrastructure  In a recent letter to the administration  Senators Snowe and Collins asked President Bush to quickly approve this request to help residents in Oxford County rebuild their lives     Federal FEMA disaster funding is available to states and local communities on a cost sharing basis for emergency work and for the repair or replacement of damaged facilities  The federal share for this disaster declaration is expected to be about  1 37 million     In a joint statement Senators Snowe and Collins said  It is good news that these much needed emergency dollars for Oxford County were swiftly approved  It is our hope that the Bethel Water District can now be quickly restored to its pre storm state  These dollars will help the community recovery from this devastating storm                                                                         Browse by                                                                                                                                          Current record
