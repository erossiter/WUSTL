                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               July 7  2005  Washington  DC   Senator Susan Collins  R ME  today released the following statement regarding the series of bombings in London  England          I was briefed this morning by DHS Secretary Michael Chertoff who informed me that DHS was raising the alert level to orange for the public transit transportation sector  including intercity mass transit systems and Amtrak   Under an Orange Alert  more federal homeland security dollars can be devoted by urban areas to pay for increased security on public transportation systems  such as overtime costs associated with providing increased security at critical infrastructure sites  including mass transit          These attacks remind us how high the stakes are in the global war against terrorism   We must remain vigilant and continue working with our international partners to stop those who target innocent civilians and commit mass murder          We will support the people of the United Kingdom as they stood by us when our country was attacked on September 11th   We must maintain a unified front against those who intend to harm the countries and people who support freedom and democracy   My hearts and prayers are with the victims and their families           Senator Collins is the Chairman of the Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee
