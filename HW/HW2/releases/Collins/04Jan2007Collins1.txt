                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senators Snowe  Collins say decision paves way for redevelopment plan January 4  2007 Washington  DC         Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  are pleased to announce that the Department of Defense has issued its Determination of Surplus Property for the Topsham Annex portion of the Brunswick Naval Air Station    We are pleased that the Department of Defense has issued its Surplus Determination for the Topsham Annex  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This decision is critical step in the successful redevelopment of the approximately 74 acres of land and facilities that make up the Topsham Annex  We look forward to continuing to work with the Department of Defense and the Topsham Local Redevelopment Authority as they create a community driven  comprehensive reuse plan                                                                            Browse by                                                                    Current record
