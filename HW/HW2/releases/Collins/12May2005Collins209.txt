                                      May 12  2005  WASHINGTON  DC     Senator Susan Collins today announced that General Dynamics  which has a facility in Saco  has been awarded a  10 6 million contract from Lockheed Martin Aeronautics for production of ammunition handling systems for the F A 22 Raptor aircraft   Maine will benefit directly from this contract as General Dynamics has chosen their Saco facility to perform the assembly work for this contract that extends through April 2007   These ammunition systems are found on many of the U S  fighter aircraft on the front lines          The contract awarded to General Dynamics is especially good news for the workers in the Saco facility who will be responsible for assembly of the ammunition handling systems for the F A 22 Raptor aircraft   I have been a longtime supporter of F A 22 production and am pleased that General Dynamics has chosen the dedicated workers at their Saco facility to carry out this important work that contributes directly to our nation   s homeland security   This contract is also a testament to the talent and skills of the General Dynamics workers in Saco     Senator Collins said
