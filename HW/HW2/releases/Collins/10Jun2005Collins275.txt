                                      June 10  2005  WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Susan Collins today announced that Luke deNatale  a Pittsfield native  has been awarded a summer internship in her Augusta office   deNatale is a 2003 graduate of Maine Central Institute and the son of Joseph and Terri deNatale          Luke is a good student and a hard worker   I am pleased to welcome him to my Augusta office     said Senator Collins       I am always delighted to give students an opportunity to become actively involved in the legislative process and to serve the citizens of Maine          deNatale will be entering his third year of undergraduate studies in the fall   He will be studying political science at the University of Maine at Orono   After he graduates  deNatale hopes to pursue
