                                     Collins pleased to have helped Maineiacs obtain needed visas May 10  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senator Susan Collins is pleased to congratulate the Lewiston Maineiacs  the first American based hockey team to win the Presidents Cup  The Maineiacs beat the Val dOr Foreurs last night in Game 4 of the finals    In just a few short years  the Lewiston Maineiacs have become fan favoritesdrawing crowds from all across Maine  Today  those fans and the team are celebrating an outstanding achievement  said Senator Collins  I join all the others in offering my congratulations to the team on its victory in the Presidents Cup and wishing it continued success in the years to come    Senator Collins authored legislation  that was signed into law last year  that expanded the number of P 1 visas available for athletes who play on high level amateur teams such as the Lewiston Maineiacs and the Portland Sea Dogs  Senator Collins legislation levels the playing field for minor league sports teams that depend on getting the best athletic talent  Before Senator Collins legislation was signed into law  the Maineiacs franchise faced tremendous difficulties obtaining the necessary visas and  as a result  the team had to reschedule or cancel several early season games placing a hardship on the team  its talented players  and threatened the significant revenue the team generates for the Lewiston Auburn area                                                                             Browse by
