                                      July 20  2005  Washington D C    U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced  452 025 in funding for an alcohol abuse reduction program in Lubec  Funding was awarded by the U S  Department of Education         Educating Maine   s youth about the dangers of alcohol abuse is critical to their health and well being     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      We applaud Lubec   s forward thinking in implementing an alcohol abuse reduction program         The Grants to Reduce Alcohol Abuse Program provides resources to local educational agencies  LEAs  to develop and implement innovative and effective alcohol abuse prevention programs for secondary school students  Projects must include one or more of the proven strategies for reducing underage alcohol abuse as determined by the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration  SAMHSA
