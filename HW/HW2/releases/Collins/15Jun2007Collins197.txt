                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              New Postage Stamp Honors Former Maine Senator U S  Postmaster  Jack Potter  presents Senator Collins with a special framed copy of a new postage stamp honoring former Maine Senator Margaret Chase Smith  June 15  2007 Washington  DC     U S  Senator Susan Collins was pleased to accept a framed copy of a new postage stamp honoring former Maine Senator Margaret Chase Smith from John Potter  United States Postmaster General  during a brief ceremony in her Washington  D C  office  This presentation coincides with the official First Day of Issue ceremony being held today at the Margaret Chase Smith Library in Skowhegan    Senator Margaret Chase Smith will always remain an inspiration to me  said Senator Collins  The First Day of Issue of the Margaret Chase Smith stamp is a fitting remembrance and thoughtful way to celebrate this remarkable woman in our history    As Ranking Member of the Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee  Senator Collins has oversight of the U S  Postal Service         Click picture to view video                                                                         Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
