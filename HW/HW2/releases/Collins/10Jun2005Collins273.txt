                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               June 10  2005  Washington  DC   The Maine Congressional Delegation today released the following statement in support of the action by the Maine State Legislature and Governor Baldacci to provide  100 000 to support efforts to fight proposed closings and realignments of military bases in Maine            This show of unified  bipartisan  bicameral support from the State Legislature and the Governor will help to bolster our efforts in Congress and in the affected communities to fight the proposed base closings in Maine     Support from the state is necessary at all levels   from State and local government  community groups  and citizens   in order to send the strongest message possible to the BRAC Commission about the value of Maine  s military   facilities to national security and to our state   We must keep working together to fight the ill advised proposal to shut down Kittery Portsmouth Naval Shipyard and DFAS Limestone and drastically change the mission of Brunswick Naval Air Station   We will continue to expend every effort possible to ensure the Commission is educated and aware that these facilities are among the most efficient and effective in the various defense missions that they serve  and that the only reasonable conclusion must be to keep them open
