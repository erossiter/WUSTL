                                      June 14  2005  Washington  DC     In honor of the United States Army   s 230th birthday today  Senator Susan Collins  who serves on the Senate Armed Services Committee made the following statement commending the men and women of the U S  Army            I commend the men and women of the U S  Army on 230 years of dedicated service to our nation   Throughout our nation   s history it is the unwavering commitment of our servicemen and women stationed around the globe that has allowed Americans to continue to enjoy the freedom that makes this country great   I am particularly proud of the men and women in the Maine Army National Guard who have carried on the longstanding military tradition of the Guard   s service to our state and country          In celebration of the birthday the Army National Guard in Maine held a cake cutting ceremony at Camp Keyes in Augusta this afternoon         In its 230 year history  the U S  Army has played a vital role in more than 10 wars  from the American Revolution through the Cold War  the Gulf War  to the current Global War on Terrorism
