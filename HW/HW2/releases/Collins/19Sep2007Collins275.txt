                                      September 19  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that the Maine Department of Resources will receive a grant of  330 321 from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA   The funding will be used to continue the Maine New Hampshire Inshore Bottom Trawl Survey    We are pleased to announce this NOAA funding for the Maine New Hampshire Inshore Trawl Survey  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  These surveys provide critical information on the state of the Gulf of Maine fisheries    The Maine New Hampshire Inshore Bottom Trawl Survey is a fishery independent survey conducted in the spring and fall to complement the surveys of the National Marine Fisheries Service  This seasons survey will consist of approximately 115 tows in the Gulf of Maine                                                                            Browse by
