                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               January 7  2005  WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that  1 750 811 in federal funding has been awarded to Maine by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services  HHS    The funding has been provided to assist the Maine Immunization Program  MIP  by the Immunizations and Vaccines for Children program          These funds will give the state of Maine the vaccination resources needed to protect children  whose immune systems are more vulnerable than adults  from illness     Senators Snowe and Collins said        The Maine Immunization Program  MIP  provides vaccines  education  technical assistance  as well as support services through partnerships with public and private health services in the state of Maine   The Immunization and Vaccines for Children program will provide funding to purchase vaccines  which will ensure that children throughout Maine will be protected from vaccine preventable disease                                                                                      Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
