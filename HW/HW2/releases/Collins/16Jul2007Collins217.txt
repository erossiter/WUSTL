                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               July 16  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Army awarded General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products a contract in the amount of 25 987 217  The contract is for 1 909 M2 machine guns that will be produced at its facility in Saco    The Army continues to look to Saco Defense for state of the art quality equipment on which our service members rely  I applaud the men and women of Saco Defense as they  once again  work to supply our troops with the very best arms and equipment  said Senator Collins   The contract is estimated to be completed by February 28  2009    Senator Collins serves on the Senate Armed Services Committee  which has oversight over the U S  Army and other military branches                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
