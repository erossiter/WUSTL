                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               March 8  2005  Washington  DC Senator Susan Collins met today with Jennifer Stegeman of Dayton  who received the VFW Military Family Member Volunteer Service Award for the Army   While Jennifer   s husband  Rick  was serving a 20 month deployment in Iraq with the 94th Military Policy Company  she supported the loved ones and family members of the soldiers in the 94th   Senator Collins wrote a letter to the VFW strongly supporting Jennifer   s nomination          I congratulate Jennifer on receiving this award  and commend her on her patriotism  courage  and compassion   Jennifer was a bedrock of stability for the other family members of the 94th during the company   s lengthy 20 month deployment     Senator Collins said      The often forgotten heroes of war are those loved ones that stay behind at home   Jennifer   s husband and his fellow soldiers should be proud of the manner in which she supported the family members that stayed on the homefront          The VFW Military Family Member Volunteer Service Award is only distributed to one family member from each of the five military branches   The award recognizes family members who
