                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Collins praises King   s commitment to equal rights January 31  2006  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Susan Collins today released this statement on the death of Coretta Scott King        I am saddened by the passing of Coretta Scott King   I had the pleasure of meeting Mrs  King during her first visit to Maine in October 2004 when she delivered the keynote address at the 40th anniversary celebration of the Portland chapter of the NAACP   Following the death of her husband  Mrs  King dedicated her life and her energy to continuing the fight for racial equality   She will be remembered for her dedication  determination  and her grace and she will be deeply missed                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
