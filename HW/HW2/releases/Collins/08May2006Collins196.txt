                                      May 8  2006 WASHINGTON  D C U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  has awarded airports and towns throughout Maine  1 920 008 in federal funding for airport related projects    Airport infrastructure is a critical part of our transportation system  Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement  Funding from the Department of Transportation will allow recipient airports to make much needed improvements to their facilities and thereby increase the operational safety of their services    The following will receive funding     1 000 000 to the Northern Aroostook Regional Airport in Frenchville to remove obstructions to approach and transition surfaces   600 000 to Portland International Jetport for noise mitigation improvements  lighting and electrical vault upgrades  and the acquisition of an interactive driver training system to boost operational safety   170 000 to the Millinocket Municipal Airport for the acquisition of two hangars and associated land  which will allow for the development of revenue producing facilities at the airport   150 008 to the Town of Wiscasset for continued construction of a taxiway for the Wiscasset Airports single runway The Department of Transportation  DOT  aims to improve safety  increase mobility in support of the nations economy  protect human and natural environment  achieve organizational excellence  and support the national security strategy  DOT serves the United States by ensuring a fast  safe  efficient  accessible and convenient transportation system that meets our vital national interests and enhances the quality of life of the American people
