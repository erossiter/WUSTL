                                      July 28  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of the Army has awarded General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products a  5 583 963 million contract for production of M2 receiver cartridges  The work will be performed at Saco Defense in Saco    We are pleased that the U S  Army continues to recognize the quality work of the employees at Saco Defense and we are delighted that this contract will help to maintain the current workforce of more than 320 employees at the facility  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  We congratulate Saco Defense on this contract and applaud the men and women who work there who help supply our troops with the very best arms and equipment    General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products produces the M2  50 caliber crew operated machine gun that may be mounted on ground mounts and most vehicles as an anti personnel and anti aircraft weapon  The weapon provides automatic weapon suppressive fire for offensive and defensive purposes and can be used effectively against personnel  light armored vehicles  low  slow flying aircraft  and small boats                                                                            Browse by
