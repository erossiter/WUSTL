                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               February 8  2007 Washington  DC       Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today expressed their concern and pledged their support for the workers who will lose their jobs at Moosehead Manufacturing Company  Inc  in Monson     Officials at Moosehead Manufacturing Company  a manufacturer of hardwood household furniture  announced today that the company will cease operations    Our domestic furniture manufacturers face extreme  and often unfair  foreign competition  It is always troubling when workers lose their jobs through no fault of their own  This is a tough blow to the families who are directly affected and to the entire community  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement    We will do everything possible to assist the workers during this difficult time  We expect to work together with state and community agencies and the local transition team to help provide job training and support services  In addition  we will support any effort by the company to obtain federal emergency funds to provide workers who are losing their jobs with Trade Adjustment Assistance                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                              Current record
