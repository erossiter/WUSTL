                                      September 18  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that three Maine small businesses will be receiving a combined  433 840 from the U S  Department of Agriculture as part of the USDA Rural Development Value Added Producer Grant Program  These grants provide small  developing businesses with the working capital needed to expand    The Peaked Mountain Farm in Dedham will receive  114 240 for the marketing campaign for wild blueberry Bite o Blue dog treats  Linkletter Timberlands in Athens will receive  300 000 to fund the acquisition of feedstock to enhance the availability of local biomass  Painted Pepper Farm in Steuben will receive  19 600 for the planning of a goat ice cream production facility   This grant funding will provide these three businesses with the funding they need to grow  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  It is exciting to watch the development of Maines small businesses  We are pleased to announce this funding                                                                            Browse by
