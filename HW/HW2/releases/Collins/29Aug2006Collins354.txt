                                      August 29  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the Federal Aviation Administration has awarded the Northern Aroostook Regional Airport Authority a grant in the amount of  375 250 to aid with the acquisition of equipment that will help improve the airports snow removal process    It is important to maintain a safe operational environment at the Northern Aroostook Regional Airport year round  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This funding from the Department of Transportation will enhance the efficiency of snow removal from the airports runways  providing a safe operational environment and reducing the potential for aircraft accidents    The Department of Transportation  DOT  aims to improve safety  increase mobility in support of the nation s economy  protect human and natural environment  achieve organizational excellence  and support the national security strategy  DOT serves the United States by ensuring a fast  safe  efficient  accessible and convenient transportation system that meets our vital national interests and enhances the quality of life of the American
