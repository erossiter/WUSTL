                                      June 3  2005  WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that a  1 464 735 grant has been awarded to the Bigelow Laboratory for Ocean Sciences in Boothbay Harbor from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration   The funds will be used for development of the research laboratory  which will create office and education spaces of a staff of 100 employees           Much of Maine   s economy relies on its vast marine resources     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      Expanding the Bigelow Laboratory for Oceanic Sciences will advance research that improves quality of life in Maine and across the country           The Department of Commerce   s National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  conducts research and gathers data about the global oceans  atmosphere  space and sun  and applies this knowledge to science and service that touch the lives of all Americans   NOAA is the Nation   s premier science agency  a highly trained team of dedicated scientists and personnel working together through the agency   s seven main line offices to monitor and probe the mysteries of the natural world                                                                                          Browse by
