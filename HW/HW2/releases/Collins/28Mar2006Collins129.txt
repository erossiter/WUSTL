                                     Former Defense Secretary Dies in Bangor at age 88 March 28  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  released this statement following the death of former U S  Defense Secretary Caspar Weinberger  who died today at Eastern Maine Medical Center in Bangor at age 88    I am saddened to learn of the passing of Caspar Weinberger  Cap will be remembered as a capable  effective  and well respected  leader who was dedicated to ensuring that our nations military was strong    Caps connection to Maine goes back 30 years when he and his wife  Jane  bought their home in Somesville  In recent years  it was not uncommon to spot him strolling the sidewalks of Mount Desert Island where he was respected as a kind and unassuming man     I wish to extend my deepest condolences to Jane and his entire family                                                                       Browse by
