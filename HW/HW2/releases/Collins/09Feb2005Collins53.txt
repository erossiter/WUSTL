                                      February 9  2005  Washington DC     Senator Susan Collins today congratulated Benjamin Cote of Scarborough  Maine for receiving the George J  Mitchell Scholarship for 2005 2006  Senator Collins has remained a critical supporter of the scholarship  leading a Senate wide request to fund the program              The George J  Mitchell Scholarship is a valuable international fellowship promoting the alliance between the United States and Ireland     said Senator Susan Collins      This truly is a great honor and Benjamin should be commended   for his work              The George J  Mitchell Scholarship  named for Senator Mitchell   s pivotal role in the Northern Ireland peace process  is administered by the US Ireland Alliance a non partisan non profit organization  The program is supported by   the US  Irish and British Governments and provides a year postgraduate study at a university in Ireland or Northern Ireland  Recipients of the prestigious scholarship have demonstrated the highest standards of academic excellence  leadership and community service   Benjamin is one of two recipients from Maine to receive the award since it was
