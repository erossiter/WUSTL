                                      May 31  2006 WASHINGTON  D C U S Senators Olympia J Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development program has awarded the St  Agatha Housing  Inc  a loan of  206 125 for repairs and renovations to its 19 unit elderly property in St  Agatha   The USDA Rural Development program provides vital opportunities for rural Mainers to enhance community services and boost economicgrowth  Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement  This funding will make possible critical improvements at St  Agatha Housing  Inc  facility for the elderly   The USDA Rural Development program provides financial loans and grants to help improve the economy and quality of life in all of rural America  The funds are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services
