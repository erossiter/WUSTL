                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 16  2006 WASHINGTON  D C U S Senators Olympia J Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the U S  Department of Agricultures  USDA  Rural Development program has awarded a  69 000 grant to the MaineStream Finance Company  which works to promote community development for the low income populations in the State of Maine  The grant will be used to fund educational programs for homebuyers and post purchase counseling services in the Knox  Penobscot  Piscataquis  and Waldo Counties    The USDA Rural Development program helps Maine communities improve essential services and boost economicgrowth  Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement  This funding will benefit Maine residents by enhancing rural programs and increasing public safety throughout the state     The USDA Rural Development program provides financial loans and grants to help improve the economy and quality of life in all of rural America  The funds are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
