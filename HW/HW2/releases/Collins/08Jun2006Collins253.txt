                                     Partnership to create work in Portland  Bath  and Brunswick June 8  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today congratulated officials at Cianbro Corporation in Pittsfield and Bath Iron Works  BIW  after the two companies announced that they have entered into a partnership to complete the retrofit and conversion of two sulfur tankers into U S  flagged  new generation  370 foot  multi purpose supply vessels  Cianbro was awarded the contract by Hornbeck Offshore Services  LLC of Covington  Louisiana  Cianbro has awarded BIW a subcontract to assist with the project  This will represent the first commercial shipwork performed at BIW in 20 years    The companies say work will take place at Cianbros facility on Cassidy Point in Portland  and at Bath Iron Works facilities in Bath and Brunswick    This contract is exciting news for two of Maines leading employers and a unique opportunity for the highly skilled workforce at Cianbro and Bath Iron Works to work together on this conversion project  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  Maine has a long and proud history in shipbuilding and maritime construction  We applaud this partnership and its positive impact on Maines shipbuilding industrial base                                                                       Browse by
