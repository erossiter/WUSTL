                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Port Visit Will Honor the Maine Celtic Celebration July 12  2007 Washington  DC        Senator Susan Collins announced today that a U S  Coast Guard has agreed to her request that a ship pay a port visit to Belfast during the annual Maine Celtic Celebration to be held between July 17th and 22nd  2007    I am thrilled that the Coast Guard will once again honor our great state of Maine with this port visit during Maines Celtic Celebration  Senator Collins said  This visit will afford those who serve the U S  Coast Guard  and the citizens they protect  an opportunity to come together during these wonderful summer festivities  I am time and again moved by this proud American tradition  and I thank the U S  Coast Guard for making this visit possible    The Maine Celtic Celebration honors and celebrates the rich Celtic heritage of Maine  This years events include Celtic dinners  dancing  storytelling  and games                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
