                                      July 26  2005  Washington D C    U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that towns throughout the State of Maine have received  8 689 835 in funding from the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD   The funds were allocated as part of a Community Development Block Grant  CDBG  award  Indian Housing Block award  and Capital Fund Program Award              Housing is one of the basic necessities of life     something that no Mainer should have to do without     said Senators Snowe and Collins      Funding awarded by the Department of Housing and Urban Development allows cities and towns in Maine to provide for citizens and ensure that they have access to safe and affordable places to live Authority      The CDBG program provides annual grants to States and local governments to develop viable urban communities by providing decent housing and a suitable living environment  and by expanding economic opportunities  principally for low  and moderate  income persons       Indian Housing Block Grant Program  IHBG  awards can be used for activities that may include operating assistance  development of affordable housing that may include new construction  acquisition  modernization or rehabilitation of rental or owner occupied housing  housing services  housing management for low income housing  crime prevention  safety or model activities as described in the recipient   s Indian Housing Plan       The Capital Fund is a formula grant that is awarded on an annual basis to 3 150 public housing authorities  PHA   s  nationwide  The Capital Fund grant may be used for a variety of activities including modernization  development  financing and management improvements       The Department of Health and Human Services is the United States government  s principal agency for protecting the health of all Americans and providing essential human services  especially for those who are least able to help themselves   HHS works closely with state and local governments and administers more grant dollars than all other federal agencies combined
