                                      March 21  2007 Washington  DC      U S  Senators Susan Collis and Olympia J  Snowetoday announced that the U S  Department of Commerce has awarded a  1 1 million Economic Development Administration  EDA  grant to the Houlton Water Company in Houlton  The grant was announced early today by Secretary Carolos Gutierrez    The investment will help build a new electrical sub station and secondary distribution system to support the expansion of Louisiana Pacific Corporation  The investment is expected to create 39 jobs and generate  104 million in private investment     This is fantastic news  notedSenators Collins and Snowein a joint statement  This funding will help a major employer retain current employees and will help create new jobs in an area of our state which has had difficulty in creating and retaining manufacturing jobs  We are pleased that we were able to help bring this funding to Aroostook County    Americas competitiveness and innovation flourish in vibrant  economically growing communities that are creating jobs and opportunities  said Gutierrez  Successful economic development is driven at the local level  by leaders like Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins  It takes both smart national policies and smart local initiatives to ensure that the American dream reaches all parts of our great nation  This investment will help Houlton generate a commercial environment that will attract entrepreneurs  businesses and capital    EDA serves as a venture capital resource to meet the economic development needs of distressed communities throughout the United States  EDAs mission is to lead the federal economic development agenda by promoting innovation and competitiveness  preparing American regions for growth
