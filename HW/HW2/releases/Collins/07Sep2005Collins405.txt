                                     Delegation and Director O   Brien to Discuss Aid to Greater Brunswick Community September 7  2005  WASHINGTON  D C      Maine Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins  and Maine Representatives Tom Allen and Mike Michaud met today in Snowe   s office with Patrick O   Brien  Director of the Department of Defense   s Office of Economic Adjustment to discuss aid to help the Brunswick area recover from the closing of Brunswick Naval Air Station   The delegation issued the following statement                      Maine   s congressional delegation and local leaders are committed to securing aid for the economic recovery of Brunswick and the surrounding community     Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins  and Maine Representatives Tom Allen and Mike Michaud said      Brunswick has given so much to the Navy     it is only fitting that the Department of Defense offer every resource at its disposal to help our community recover   Our meeting with Director O   Brien leads us to believe that he agrees  and we look forward to working with him to ease the shock of this painful transition
