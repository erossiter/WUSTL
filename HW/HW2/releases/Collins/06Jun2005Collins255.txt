                                      June 6  2005  WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that a  6 748 grant has been awarded to Saint Joseph   s College in Standish from the Department of Heath and Human Services    HRSA Division of Grants Management Operations   The funds will be allocated toward a Scholarship for Disadvantaged Students            Providing disadvantaged students with access to educational funding is one of the first and most important steps toward ensuring equal opportunity and education for everyone     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      We are sure that the recent grant from the Department of Health and Human Services will be put to good use by Saint Josephs College and that it will greatly aid those students that need it most          The Department of Health and Human Services  HHS  is the United States government  s principal agency for protecting the health of all Americans and providing essential human services  especially for those who are least able to help themselves   The Health Resources and Services Administration  HRSA  improves the nation  s health by assuring equal
