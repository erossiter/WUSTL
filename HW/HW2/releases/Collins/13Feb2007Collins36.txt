                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Katie McDonough  February 13  2007 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins was pleased to recently meet with Wells High School junior Katie McDonough in the Senators Washington  D C  office    McDonough is in our nations capital this spring working as a congressional page  She is the only student from Maine currently serving as a page    Senate Page duties consist primarily of delivery of correspondence and legislative material within the Congressional complex  Other duties include preparing the chamber for Senate sessions  and carrying bills and amendments to the desk  Pages also attend classes in the early morning at the United States Senate Page School                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                        Current record
