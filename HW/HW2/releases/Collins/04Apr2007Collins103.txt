                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Guard agrees to Senator Collins request for 30 day delay April 4  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that the National Guard has agreed to her request for a 30 day extension of the public comment period on its plan to lower the flight ceiling for training missions over western Maine    I am pleased that the National Guard has agreed to delay its public comment period  said Senator Collins  I was just in Western Maine yesterday and heard concerns from those who would be directly affected by low level training flights over that part of our state  It is important that those voices be heard in this debate    The National Guard has agreed to extend the public comment period from April 13 to May 14  2007 for the general public to review and provide comments on the environmental assessment  A second public comment period for the aeronautical impact ends on May 3rd                                                                            Browse by                                                      Current record
