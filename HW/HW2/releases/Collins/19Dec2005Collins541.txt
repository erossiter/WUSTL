                                     Senator Collins Contacts Senate Leadership to Express Strong Opposition to Provision December 19  2005 Washington  D C      WASHINGTON  DC     Senator Susan Collins is strongly urging Senate leadership to exclude from an important defense spending bill a provision that would allow oil and gas drilling in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge  ANWR   Senator Collins  who is a long time opponent of drilling in ANWR and voted against the budget reconciliation legislation earlier this year  which included the ANWR provision  argues that it would be highly inappropriate to include such a provision in the spending measure      In a letter to Senate Leadership that was also signed by a bipartisan group of her colleagues  the Senators wrote      We understand that the Department of Defense Appropriations conference report may include legislation allowing oil and gas drilling in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge  While our individual views may differ on this major energy and environmental policy matter  we strongly object to including such a controversial provision in this conference report since this provision is so clearly and significantly outside the scope of this conference      Securing funding for our Nations military and national security must be the priority in this important conference report that supports the well being of our troops      We believe including this provision in the Department of Defense Appropriations conference report would violate Senate rules  and we strongly urge you to oppose any effort to permit its inclusion      The Defense Appropriations bill is scheduled to be considered by the full Senate on Wednesday                                                                       Browse by
