                                      May 6  2005  Washington  DC    Senator Susan Collins today applauded the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  for taking action that will assist domestic apple growers recover from difficult economic times   Senator Collins joined her colleagues in April requesting that the USDA consider an apple commodity purchase to assist America   s apple industry   Due to an excess supply of apples this season and the closure of the Taiwan export market  apple prices have fallen dramatically causing reduced incomes for apple growers   The USDA has announced that it will purchase up to 78 million additional pounds of apples and apple products                      Difficult market conditions that are beyond the control of Maine  s apple farmers have caused very tough economic times for an industry that is very important to our state   I am pleased that the USDA is taking action to assist our nation   s apple growers through this significant commodity purchase   This purchase will provide some relief to our apple farmers  while supplying healthy products to schools  soup kitchens  nursing homes  and other organizations     Senator Collins
