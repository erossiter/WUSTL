                                      September 13  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the U S  Department of Justice  DOJ  has awarded  1 130 000 to the City of Portland to purchase security equipment for port safety          Portland is the gateway to much of the state of Maine and so it is vital that it   s port remains adequately protected     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement       During this time of increased terrorism and world unrest  we must be especially vigilant about protecting our homeland  This Port Security Grant is necessary to fortify Portland   s harbor and ensure the continued safety of all Maine residents              This Port Security grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Homeland Security   s Office of State and Local Government Coordination and Preparedness  SLGCP   The Port Security Grant program provides financial assistance to owners and operators of ports  terminals  U S  inspected passenger vessels and ferries  as well as port authorities and State and local agencies to improve security for operators and passengers through physical security enhancements  The fiscal year Port Security Grant program will provide over  140 million for port security grants
