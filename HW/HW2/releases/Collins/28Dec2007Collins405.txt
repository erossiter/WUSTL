                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Collins Voting Streak Extends to 3 679 Roll Call Votes to Date December 28  2007  Senator Susan Collins has continued her perfect voting record and ended this legislative session with a consecutive voting streak that extends to 3 679 roll call votes  Senator Collins has been present for every roll call vote that has occurred in the Senate since she was sworn into office in January 1997     Senator Collins said  It continues to be my great honor and privilege to serve in the U S  Senate representing the people of Maine  I take my responsibilities in the Senate seriously and know that Mainers expect me to be in the Senate chamber for roll call votes  Senator Collins said that she continues to be inspired by former Senator Margaret Chase Smith who never missed a vote in 13 years before surgery forced her to miss her first vote     In addition to not missing any recorded votes  Senator Collins continues to return home to Maine for weekends and during congressional recesses to meet with her constituents  visit communities throughout the State  and to spend time in her home in Bangor                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
