                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               December 10  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the City of Brewer has been awarded  6 500 000 in loan funding for the construction of a new public safety complex  The funding comes from the USDAs Rural Development program   The new 30 000 square foot complex will be located on Parkway South  allowing for improved police and fire department response  Brewers existing public safety building was constructed in the mid 1950s and is not centrally located in the city  The city currently employs 41 public safety workers   This funding is great news for the City of Brewer  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This will allow for the construction of a public safety building that the city strongly needs  The new complex will allow the City of Brewer to better serve its citizens                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
