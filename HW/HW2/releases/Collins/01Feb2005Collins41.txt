                                      February 1  2005  Washington  DC   During a Senate Armed Services Committee hearing   today  Senator Susan Collins urged military leaders to improve current death   benefits and services available to survivors of military and defense   personnel   Senator Collins is a cosponsor of legislation to increase the   amount of compensation from  12 000 to  100 000           When we send a young man or woman into harm   s way  our nation has in   return a sacred obligation to them and to their families     Senator Collins   said      We must ensure they go forth with the complete confidence  should the   worst happen and they are called upon to make the ultimate sacrifice  that   their country will care for their families and honor their service             I have long supported an increase in the payment made to the families of   those who have lost their lives in service to our country  and I am very   pleased to see the growing consensus that we should enact significant   legislation in this regard   It is terrific to hear the Administration   s   support for doing this promptly as well     continued Senator Collins       In the 108th Congress  Senator Collins was the Senate sponsor of   legislation to double the death gratuity to  12 000 and make it tax free         During today   s Senate Armed Services Committee hearing  members heard
