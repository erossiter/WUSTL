                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 12  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins today released this statement following the death of Army Specialist Dustin Harris of Patten  Specialist Harris  who was 21 years old  died April 6  2006 in Bayji  Iraq after being injured when an improvised explosive device  IED  detonated near his patrol  Specialist Harris was assigned to the 172nd Brigade Support Battalion  Fort Wainwright  Alaska  His parents live in Patten    I am truly saddened to learn of the death of Specialist Dustin Harris  Specialist Harris was dutifully serving his country when he was killed and we honor his sacrifice in his dedication to serve his country and defend freedom across the world                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
