                                      March 28  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  announced today that U S  Navy ships will make port visits to Maine during six festivals this summer  Details regarding the names of the particular ships will be released in the near future    I am thrilled that the Navy will once again honor our great state of Maine with these port visits during these annual summer celebrations  Senator Collins said  These visits afford those who serve the U S  Navy and the citizens they protect an opportunity to come together during these wonderful summer festivities  As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  I am time and again moved by this proud American tradition  and I thank the U S  Navy for making these visits possible    At Senator Collinss request  U S  Navy ships will visit the following locations    Belfast during the 59th annual Bay Festival  to be held July 17 21  Boothbay Harbor during the 44th annual Windjammer Days Festival  to be held June 26 30  Bucksport during the Downeast Gateway Festival  formerly the Fort Knox Bay Festival   to be held July 27 31  Camden during the 12th annual Windjammer Weekend  to be held August 31 September 5  Eastport during the 4th of July Festival  to be held from June 30 July 5  Rockland during the 59th annual Maine Lobster Festival  to be
