                                      June 6  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins is pleased to announce that Jeffrey Alden  a Caribou native  has been awarded a summer internship in her Aroostook County state office     Jeffs academic profile and strong work ethic will be a great asset to my Aroostook County office  said Senator Collins  Employing a student intern that was raised in Northern Maine is especially important in meeting the needs of Aroostook County residents  As always  I am delighted to provide students with an opportunity to become actively involved in the legislative process and serve Maine citizens     Alden is a 2002 graduate of Caribou High School and the son of Roy and Beth Alden   This fall  Alden will enter his final year of undergraduate studies at Colby College in Waterville  His studies are focused in mathematical economics and government  He says that he is looking forward to serving Northern Maine citizens and businesses
