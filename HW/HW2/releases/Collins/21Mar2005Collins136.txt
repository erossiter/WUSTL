                                     Small business   economic development programs in Kennebec  Somerset  and Western Waldo Counties to benefit March 21  2005  WASHINGTON  D C   Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the Kennebec Valley Council of Governments will receive a total of  152 000 in federal funding to assist small businesses and local economic development programs   The funding will come in two parts   100 000 from the U S  Small Business Administrations  SBA  and a  52 000 grant from the Economic Development Administration  EDA    The SBA funding will provide small businesses  particularly small manufacturers  with management and technical assistance to help them grow their businesses   The EDA grant will provide technical assistance to municipalities to help implement the communities   comprehensive economic development strategy          This funding will prove instrumental for the communities of the Kennebec Valley to expand and assist their small businesses     Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement      Economic development programs are important to ensuring our small businesses have the tools they need to grow and prosper          The federal funding will be administered by Kennebec Valley Council of Governments  the economic development district for the region   The programs will benefit businesses and the communities of Kennebec  Somerset and Western Waldo Counties   Senators Snowe and Collins have worked closely with local administrators of these programs to obtain federal support and resources for
