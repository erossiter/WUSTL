                                      May 8  2006 Washington  D C    Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee Chairman Susan Collins issued the following statement on the resignation of Pam Turner  the Assistant Secretary for Legislative Affairs at the Department of Homeland Security    Pam Turner has been an affective advocate for the Department of Homeland Security since its inception  She began her tenure the month DHS opened its doors  served under the first two secretaries of the Department  and built from scratch a professional congressional relations staff  said Chairman Collins  The Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee has worked closely with Pam on numerous matters as the Department has grown  We will miss her diligence  competence  and civility  And I wish her well as she moves on to new challenges                                                                        Browse by
