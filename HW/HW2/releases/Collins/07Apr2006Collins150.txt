                                      April 7  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  Chairman of the Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee  today released the following statement after President Bush nominated acting Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  director David Paulison to be permanent head of the agency  The Administration also announced its nominations of Vice Admiral Harvey Johnson as Deputy Director and Chief Operating Officer of FEMA  and Deidre Lee as Deputy Director of Operations for FEMA    David Paulison has 30 years of fire and rescue services experience  including his position as Administrator for the U S  Fire Administration  It is clear that Mr  Paulison has a good understanding of first responder issues and is well respected within the fire and rescue community  Mr  Paulison will face many challenges within FEMA as he undertakes the difficult task of trying to repair the nations emergency management structure    Vice Admiral Johnson has had a long and successful career in the United States Coast Guard where he is admired for his dynamic  results oriented personality    Deidre Lee is one of the most well respected procurement officials in government and I am pleased that she has been chosen to become the next Deputy Director of Operations for FEMA
