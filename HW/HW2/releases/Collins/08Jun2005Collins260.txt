                                      June 8  2005  Washington DC  Senator Susan Collins today announced that Jamie Nissen of Portland has been awarded an internship in her DC office this summer   Nissen   s main responsibility will be to assist the Senator with daily office procedures and help respond to constituent concerns           I began my political career as an intern and realize how rewarding and stimulating the experience can be     said Senator Collins      It will truly be a pleasure to have Jamie in the office this summer   His ambition and excitement will be a wonderful contribution to my staff           Nissen graduated from Tabor Academy in Marion  Massachusetts and currently is entering his senior year at Bates College  where he is highly active in student politics   He will graduate from Bates in 2006 as a Political Science major with a concentration in American Government and a minor in Religion   He hopes that his experience this summer will help him
