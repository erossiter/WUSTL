                                      March 15  2005  Washington  DC Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins met today with members of the Brunswick Naval Air Station  s  BNAS  Task Force to discuss the value of BNAS to our nation  s defense   The Senators have been strong advocates for BNAS in Congress   The Senators met with Rick Tetrev  Committee Chair for the BNAS Task Force and Ralph Dean  Captain  U S  Navy Retired  a member of the BNAS Task Force   Following their meeting  the Senators made the following joint statement            Brunswick Naval Air Station is vital to our nation  s defense efforts at home and abroad   With a long and proud history of outstanding performance  BNAS is the only fully capable active duty operational military airfield in the Northeast    With the establishment of a Joint Armed Forces Reserve Center  it is clear that Brunswick is an indispensable asset to our nation  s military not just for the present  but for the future as well   We remain committed to working with the BNAS community to ensure that the Navy is fully aware of the invaluable contributions that BNAS is making to the security and defense of Maine  New England and our
