                                      June 28  2005  WASHINGTON  DC    At the request of members of the Maine Congressional delegation  Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission Member General Lloyd W  Newton visited the Defense Finance and Accounting Services  DFAS  site in Limestone  Maine today  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins and Representatives Tom Allen and Michael Michaud  have released this joint statement          We are grateful that General Newton has agreed to visit DFAS Limestone  It is important for him to be able to see first hand why we are so proud of the facility and its workers and why Limstone has played such a key role in the Pentagon   s accounting services           DFAS Limestone is one of the most effective and cost efficient Department of Defense sites in the nation  Its highly skilled workers are able to not only meet but also exceed expectations of the Pentagon  As a result  the Pentagon has shifted and added work to Limestone over the past few years          DFAS Limestone is one of the lowest cost facilities in the U S   has an extremely low employee turnover rate  low overall operating costs  and the ability to take on even more work for the Pentagon          In addition  this facility is crucial to the community as it was initially activated in part to ease the devastating economic impacts caused by the closing of Loring Air Force Base during the last round of BRAC closures          We believe that General Newton will leave Limestone today with a recognition of how valuable this DFAS facility is to the Department of Defense and that it would be a mistake to close it                                                                                         Browse by
