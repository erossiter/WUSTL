                                       May 11  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins today was pleased to accept an award from the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation  JDRF  for her work on diabetes  Mary Tyler Moore  JDRFs International Chairman  and George Rothweiler of Brunswick presented her with the award  Georges daughter  Steffi  testified before the Senate Homeland Security  Governmental Affairs Committee last year in Washington during JDRFs fourth annual Childrens Congress  Steffi spoke about her experience living with diabetes   Earlier this month  Senator Collins  co chair of the Senate Diabetes Caucus  sent a letter to the Secretary of Health  Human Services with the signatures of 68 Senators that called attention to new technologies  known as continuous glucose sensors  that help those with diabetes to monitor their glucose levels continuously and thus reduce incidences of extreme insulin and blood sugar levels   Senator Collins  center  is shown here with
