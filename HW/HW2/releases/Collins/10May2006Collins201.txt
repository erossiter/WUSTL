                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                May 10  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Collins met with Maines 2006 Prudential Spirit of Community Honoree  Kathleen Casasa Blouin  of Portland to congratulate her and discuss her volunteer work in Maine  Kathleen  18  is a senior at Deering High School and helps plan and lead counseling programs to ease the emotional trauma that children typically experience when their parents divorce or separate  Shown here is  from left to right   Kathleen Casasa  Senator Collins  Kathleen Casasa Blouin  and Terry Blouin  The Prudential Spirit of Community Awards represent the United States largest youth recognition program based exclusively on volunteer community service  The awards were created in 1995 by Prudential in partnership with the National Association of Secondary School Principals to honor middle school and high school students at the local  state and national level for outstanding service to others
