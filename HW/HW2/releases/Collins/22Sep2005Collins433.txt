                                      September 22  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that three Maine schools have won the U S  Department of Education   s Blue Ribbon School award   This award recognizes schools that make significant progress in closing the achievement gap or whose students achieve at very high levels                      We commend the faculty and staff of Yarmouth High School  Strong Elementary School and Plummer Motz School for their commitment to ensuring that each and every child receives a quality education     said Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      Every day  in every school in Maine  teachers are working hard to give our children the tools they need to succeed in the 21st Century           Following are the three schools that won the U S  Department of Education   s Blue Ribbon School award           Plummer Motz School  Falmouth  ME  Mrs  Karen Boffa  Principal        Strong Elementary School  Strong  ME   Mrs  Felecia L  Pease  Principal         Yarmouth High School  Yarmouth  ME   Mr  Ted Hall  Principal
