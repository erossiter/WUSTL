                                     Colgate agrees to purchase majority stake and keep jobs in Maine March 22  2006 WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Susan Collins released this statement following the announcement that Colgate Palmolive has agreed to purchase a majority stake in Tom s of Maine for  100 million  Tom s of Maine  based in Kennebunk  is the leading natural personal care product company in the U S  and employs nearly 200 workers  Tom and Kate Chappell started Tom s of Maine with very little money but big dreams  Today  their company continues to be guided by the personal beliefs and corporate values they established for it nearly four decades ago  Tom s of Maine built its reputation on Maine s unique qualities andI am pleased that Colgate Palmoliveexecutivesrecognize this and have committed to continuing the companys operations in Maine  I want to congratulate Tom and Kate Chappell  and the dedicated employees of Tom s of Maine  on this deal
