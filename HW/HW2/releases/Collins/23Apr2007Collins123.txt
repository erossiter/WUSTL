                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 23  2007 Washington  DC      U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today that the Maine Department of Labor has been awarded a National Emergency Grant of  395 678  The funding comes as part of wrap around services to workers who were recently laid off by Moosehead Manufacturing  Inc  in Monson     We are greatly pleased to hear about this federal assistance  noted Senator Snowe and Senator Collins in a joint statement  This funding will greatly benefit the Moosehead workers by providing the services the workers need to get back on their feet    Moosehead Manufacturing closed in mid February  laying off over 50 workers  The National Emergency Grant comes from the U S  Department of Labor and is designed to provide services not provided under the Trade Adjustment Assistance program  Moosehead had long been under intense competition from much cheaper Chinese imports                                                                           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
