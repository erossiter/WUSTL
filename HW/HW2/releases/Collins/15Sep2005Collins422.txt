2007 and barrel only deliveries through June 2006   Saco Defense is a division of General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products   Production work will be performed at the company   s plant in Saco        Senator Collins visited and toured the Saco Defense facility last month           I am extremely impressed with the work being done at General Dynamics    Maine facility  a Center of Excellence   The skilled workforce at Saco Defense will help make sure our troops have the best quality weapons systems in the world     Senator Collins said        Saco Defense employs approximately 267 people
