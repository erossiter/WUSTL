                                        May 10  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Collins met with Maines 2006 Prudential Spirit of Community Honoree  Kerry Kay  of Bath to congratulate her and discuss her volunteer work in Maine  Kerry  14  is an eighth grader at Bath Middle School and volunteers at a local nursing home  collects donations for a nearby animal shelter  and makes sandwiches and cakes for local firefighters and policemen  Senator Collins is shown here with Kerry  center  and her mother  Patricia Kay  The Prudential Spirit of Community Awards represent the United States largest youth recognition program based exclusively on volunteer community service  The awards were created in 1995 by Prudential in partnership with the National Association of Secondary School Principals to honor middle school and high school students at the local  state and national level for outstanding service to others                                                                       Browse by
