                                      September 14  2007 Washington  DC      U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of Health and Human Services has awarded two Maine programs with a combined  398 059 in grant funding to assist in refugee resettlement    The Maine Department of Health and Human Services will receive  168 059 to facilitate the organization of ethnic communities  Additionally  Catholic Charities of Maine will receive  230 000 to assist with their care of unexpected arrivals   This funding for the Department of Health and Human Services will greatly assist Maines caregivers as they lend a helping hand to new arrivals  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  With better support  new immigrants will find it
