                                      September 24  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that three Maine businesses will be receiving a combined  945 112 from the U S  Department of Agriculture as part of the USDA Rural Development Renewable Energy Systems and Energy Efficiency Improvement Grant program  These grants provide rural small businesses in developing renewable energy systems  and in making energy efficient improvements to their operations    Backyard Farms  LLC of Madison will receive  500 000 and has been approved for a loan of  500 000 for a wood fired boiler system  Maine Wood Pellet Company  LLC will receive  400 000 for the production of fuel pellets  Hancock Lumber of Bethel will receive  45 112 to upgrade lighting and compressor system   This grant funding will provide these three businesses with the funding they need to become far more energy efficient  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint
