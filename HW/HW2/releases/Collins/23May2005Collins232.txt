                                      May 23  2005  Washington  DC     Senator Susan Collins  R ME  and a group of 13 centrist Senators tonight reached an agreement that will avert the impending showdown in the U S  Senate over the use of filibusters to block judicial nominations   Senator Collins was essential to negotiating the compromise between seven Republicans and seven Democrats       Following tonight   s announcement  Senator Collins released the following statement          This compromise is a great victory for the United States Senate as an institution          This agreement is based on trust   And most important  it helps preserve the unique culture of this institution   It is a culture that is built upon a foundation of collegiality and cooperation that transcends partisanship   It is a culture in which legislative goals are reached with patience and perseverance  and through the art of negotiation and compromise   This agreement preserves that Senate culture and shows a respect for the important principles that make the Senate such a great institution          I am proud of our work to pursue a spirit of bipartisanship and cooperation to avert this crisis   It was only possible because of the good faith  mutual respect  and trust that characterized our negotiations   We hope our work here today can be a model for us as we move forward to address the important issues facing our country                                                                                      Browse by
