                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               October 15  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins  who is a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  announced that the Navy has awarded BIW a contract totaling  76 613 742  The contract will provide additional dollars for BIW to continue work as the planning yard for DDG 51 and FFG 7 class ships  Under the contract  BIW will provide expert design  planning  and material support services for both maintenance and modernization of these vessels  All of the work will be performed at BIW    The skilled workers at Bath Iron Works continue to play a key role in our national security and Naval ship programs  This contact announcement is very good news  said Senator Collins                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
