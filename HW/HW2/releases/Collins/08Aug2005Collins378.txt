                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 8  2005   Washington  D C     U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that a  100 000 grant has been awarded to the University of Southern Maine   s Center for Business and Economic Research  CBER  from the Economic Development Administration  EDA    The funds will be used to support projects specifically targeted to distressed regions  and to support and foster economic development statewide               The University of Southern Maine is the largest university in the State of Maine and a vital education resource for Maine residents     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      By conducting research on the distressed areas in Maine  we can work toward helping to address the economic needs of communities throughout our state             The EDA an element of the U S  Department of Commerce was established to work with states and localities to generate new jobs  retain existing jobs  and stimulate industrial and commercial growth in economically distressed areas and regions of the United States  The purpose of its program investments is to provide economically distressed communities with a source of funding for planning  infrastructure development  and business financing that will induce private investment in the types of business activities that contribute
