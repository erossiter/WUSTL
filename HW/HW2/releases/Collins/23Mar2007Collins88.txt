                                      March 23  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Susan Collins and Olympia J  Snoweannounced today that the Group Home Foundation in Belfast  Maine will receive a loan of  1 750 000 from USDA Rural Development for the construction of an addition to the Little River Apparel facility in Belfast  Approximately 75 percent of the 200 employees of Little River Apparel have some form of disability  The facility currently produces chemical warfare suits for the Department of Defense     These funds will greatly benefit the Group Home Foundation and the Little River facility  saidSenators Collins and Snowe in a joint statement  The growth of the facility will allow more citizens to be assisted by the Group Home Foundation and its programs     The Group Home Foundation  Inc  was organized in 1975 to promote the general welfare of handicapped adults in the Waldo County area  Little River Apparel is targeting the construction of a 25 000 square foot addition  It currently produces approximately 120 000 chemical warfare suits per year                                                                             Browse by
