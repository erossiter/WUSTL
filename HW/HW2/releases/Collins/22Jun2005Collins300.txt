                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               June 22  2005  WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  and U S  Representatives Tom Allen  D ME  and Mike Michaud  D ME  today praised Secretary of Commerce Carlos Gutierrez for today declaring a disaster in the Maine shellfish fishery due to the current red tide outbreak           This year   s devastating red tide has left many in the shellfish industry unable to work  and every day it persists worsens their plight     Senators Snowe and Collins and Representatives Allen and Michaud said in a joint statement      We are pleased that the Commerce Department recognizes the urgency of the situation and have taken the first step toward providing desperately needed relief           On June 17th  Senators Snowe and Collins and Representatives Allen and Michaud sent a letter to Secretary Gutierrez appealing for aid to Maine shellfish fishermen under Section 312 a  of the Magnuson Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act and Section 308 b  of the Interjurisdictional Fisheries Act   Assistance available through the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  for fisheries declared    commercial fisheries disasters    includes compensation for lost fishing time and lost equipment     buy back    of vessels and equipment  job retraining and incentives for economic diversification
