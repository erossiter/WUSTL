                                      January 3  2005  Washington  DC     Senator Susan Collins announced today that a Maine based company has received an award from the Department of Defense for military aviation support services   Telford Aviation Services  a subsidiary of The Telford Group in Bangor  has been awarded more than  110 million over five years          I am pleased that the Defense Department has recognized the invaluable work of Maine based Telford Aviation Services by awarding the company a military aviation maintenance contract     Senator Collins said      Telford   s efforts to earn this contract will help the company grow by sustaining and creating jobs           The Telford Group is based in Bangor  Maine and employs 125 Mainers   It operates maintenance facilities for commercial and corporate aircraft at Bangor International Airport  The company is also involved in a government research and development project involving airships at the Loring International Airport in Limestone  Maine          Senator Collins was instrumental in helping The Telford Group navigate the procurement process and win this competitive contract that will bring additional work to our Maine facilities     Bob Ziegelaar  President of The Telford Group said       The Telford Group   s core activities are aircraft maintenance  aircraft parts provision  aircraft
