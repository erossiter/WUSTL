                                      April 18  2005  AUGUSTA   Late last week  Senator Susan Collins was presented with the    Friends of the Farm Bureau    award for her work and advocacy in Congress for farmers and issues that affect the agricultural industry in Maine   Senator Collins was the only member of the Maine delegation to receive the award for the 108th Congress            Agriculture has a long tradition in Maine and is a vital part of our state   s heritage  With over 7 000 farms in Maine  it is also an important component of our economy  contributing more than  463 million annually to our state   s economy     said Senator Collins during an awards ceremony earlier today             I am proud of the work of the Maine Farm Bureau  which has helped to spread the word that Maine products represent the best quality     she added             I am honored to receive this award and look forward to continuing to work on behalf of Maine   s agricultural industry during the 109th Congress     said Senator Collins           Senator Collins was presented with the award for her support on issues such as working to increase agricultural exports  tax issues to benefit small businesses  and agriculture research                                                                                     Browse by
