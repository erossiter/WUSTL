                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Collins Releases Statement March 21  2007 Washington  DC        The Navy has notified Senator Collins that it has awarded Bath Iron Works  12 550 000 for work related to research  development  test and technical services for the DDG 1000 program  The majority of the work will be performed at BIW  DDG 1000 is the Navys next generation destroyer and was previously known as DD X    Release of these dollars is welcome news for BIW and its skilled workers  It also represents the Navys ongoing commitment to the DDG 1000 program  which is crucial for the future of the Navy and of our national security  said Senator Collins    BIW has been working on detailed design work since last summer  It is expected that the construction contract will be awarded sometime this spring                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
