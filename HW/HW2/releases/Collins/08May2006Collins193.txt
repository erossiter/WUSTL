                                     A third Maine soldier is seriously injured May 8  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins today released this statement following the announcement by the Governors office that two Maine soldiers were killed Saturday in an attack in Iraq  Staff Sergeant Dale James Kelly  Jr  of Richmond and Staff Sergeant David Michael Veverka  of Jamestown  Pennsylvania  a student at the University of Maine  both died in the attack  A third soldier  Private Christopher Fraser  of Windsor  was seriously injured in the attack and has been transferred to an Army hospital in Landstuhl  Germany    Today is a sad day for the state of Maine as we mourn the loss of two brave soldiers who died in the attack  My deepest sympathies go out to the family  friends  and loved ones of Staff Sergeant Dale Kelly  Jr  and Staff Sergeant David Veverka  We are all proud and grateful of the service and dedication these two men gave to our nation    My thoughts and prayers also remain with Private Christopher Fraser and his family  I wish him a full and speedy recovery                                                                       Browse by
