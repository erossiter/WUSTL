                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               October 26  2005  Washington  DC    Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R Maine  and Susan M  Collins  R Maine  announced that Kennebec Valley Community College has been awarded  955 831 in Community Based Job Training Grants  CBJTG  to enhance the college   s healthcare programs            Kennebec Valley Community College offers valuable training to students and professionals in Maine     Senator Collins said      These funds will allow KVCC to enhance its health care program  through additional faculty and specialized courses  Further  incentives will be provided to encourage young adults to pursue training and employment in the health care industry         The primary purpose of the Community Based Job Training Grants is to build the capacity of community colleges to train workers to develop the skills required to succeed in high growth  high demand industries   Community Based Job Training Grants support workforce training for high growth industries through the nation   s community and technical colleges  Projects that have received funding through this program include industries from health care and construction to advanced
