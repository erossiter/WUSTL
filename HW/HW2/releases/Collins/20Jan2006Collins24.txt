                                      January 20  2006  WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  is pleased to welcome Secretary of the Navy Dr  Donald Winter to Maine today   Secretary Winter is visiting Bath Iron Works  the Brunswick Naval Air Station  and the Kittery Portsmouth Naval Shipyard during todays visit   During a private meeting with Secretary Winter in September  Senator Collins addressed the need for the Navy to permanently abandon its one shipyard acquisition strategy  provide increases and stability in the shipbuilding budget  and the need to proceed with building the DD X  destroyer at levels that will strengthen our national security and ensure a robust and skilled industrial base  During that meeting  Senator Collins invited Secretary Winter to visit Maine for a first hand look at naval operations and shipbuilding activity in Maine    I am pleased that Secretary Winter decided to see for himself what we in Maine already know  said Senator Collins  I am confident that this visit will convince Secretary Winter how important it is  for our national defense  that we maintain a skilled industrial base  like the workforce at Bath Iron Works and the Kittery Portsmouth Naval Shipyard                                                                       Browse by
