                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               June 2  2005   Washington  D C      Today BRAC Commission Chairman Anthony Principi and BRAC Commissioners Lloyd W  Newton  James H  Bilbray and Philip Coyle visited Brunswick Naval Air Station   Maine Senators Olympia Snowe and Susan Collins and Maine Representatives Tom Allen and Mike Michaud issued the following statement              We thank BRAC Commission Chairman Principi and Commissioners Newton  Billbray and Coyle for coming to the last remaining fully capable naval air station in the Northeast   Today they had the opportunity to see firsthand the strengths and value of this air station  and thereby the weaknesses in the Navy s arguments for realignment   We hope that they will take from this visit an understanding of Brunswick   s indispensable value in surveillance and interdiction of our northeast borders and maritime approaches along Atlantic shipping lanes     and that abandoning the air station would leave an entire region less protected                                                                                        Browse by
