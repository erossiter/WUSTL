                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 13  2006 BANGOR  MESenator Susan Collins today released this statement following the death of Herbert Herb Sargent  Sargent  who started the successful H E  Sargent construction company in 1926  died Sunday at age 100  Funeral services will be held today in Stillwater    Herb Sargent was a wonderful man who cared deeply about his family  his community  and his state  A successful business leader who never forgot his roots  Herb made a difference in the lives of so many people and he especially loved children  I last talked with Herb on his 100th birthday in February and thought at the time about the extraordinary impact of his life  He will be missed not only by his family but also by all of us lucky enough to have known him                                                                        Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
