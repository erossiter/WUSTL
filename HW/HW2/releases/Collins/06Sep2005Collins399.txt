                                      September 6  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced the Defense Logistics Agency  DLA   an agency of the U S  Department of Defense  DoD   has awarded  365 000 in funding to Eastern Maine Development Corporation in Bangor for a cost sharing Procurement Technical Assistance Cooperative             This funding will be particularly helpful in giving the local businesses of Eastern Maine guidance on how to market their goods and services to Federal agencies  thereby making them more competitive with businesses throughout the country     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement        The Department of Defense Procurement Technical Assistance  DoD PTA  Program assists state and local governments and other private nonprofit entities to establish and maintain activities to help business firms market their goods and services to Federal agencies
