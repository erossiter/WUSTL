                                      June 8  2005  Bangor  Senator Susan Collins today announced that Michael Aube of Bangor has been awarded an internship in her Bangor office   Aube will be assisting Senator Collins and her staff with the daily operations of the office          It is very important for students to gain first hand experiences in politics by participating in the daily operations of a congressional office     said Senator Susan Collins      It is truly a pleasure to have Michael join the Bangor office for the summer             Aube graduated from Bangor High School and is now a double major in Political Science and History at Boston College   He became interested in applying for this internship in order to learn the process taken to ensure constituents    concerns and requests are answered to on a daily basis   Michael will graduate from BC in 2007 and is interested in having a career in public service
