                                      May 17  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today announced that a contract of  25 593 208 has been awarded to Creative Apparel of Belfast from the Defense Logistics Agency  DLA  to make chemical protective suits for the military    I am pleased that  once again  Creative Apparel has received this contract and that this Maine company is contributing to our national defense  said Senator Collins  It is absolutely vital that the men and women serving in our armed forces have access to the most advanced protective gear possible so that they are better protected during combat   Creative Apparel  co owned by the Passamaquoddy Tribe Indian Township and J S Apparel  will use the funds to manufacture chemical suits for the Army  Navy  Air Force  and Marine Corps     The Joint Service Lightweight Integrated Suit Technology  or JSLIST  consists of a two piece garment that features state of the art protective lining technology which provides increased protection against chemical attacks    DLA is a component of the Department of Defense and provides worldwide logistics support for the
