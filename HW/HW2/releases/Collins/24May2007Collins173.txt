                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Mary McAleney to retire with distinguished record of public service May 24  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins today praised Mary McAleney  District Director of the U S  Small Business Administrations  SBA  Maine District Office  who has announced that she will retire from her position on June 30  2007  McAleney was named SBAs District Director in July 1998  Prior to joining the SBA  McAleney worked for U S  Senator George Mitchell    Mary has dedicated herself to a life of public service  She has been a true professional throughout  Thanks to her hard work and leadership at the SBA  many Mainers now have growing and thriving businesses in our great state  She will be missed  said Senator Collins  who served as New England Administrator of the SBA from 1992 to 1993                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
