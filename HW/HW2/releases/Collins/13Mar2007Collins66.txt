                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               March 13  2007 Washington  DC      Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today announced that Bushmaster Firearms International  LLC  Inc  of Windham has been awarded a contract from the U S  Army totaling  5 411 132  The contact is for the production of 5 500 Bushmaster rifles and additional equipment  All work related to this contract will be done in Maine  The contract is expected to be completed by November of this year    I am pleased that the Army has awarded this contract to a Maine company  said Senator Collins  The Army continues to look at Bushmaster for state of the art equipment on which our military relies    Bushmaster Firearms is headquartered in Windham  and employs more than 140 people                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
