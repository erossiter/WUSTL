                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               July 9  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  has awarded three grants totaling  759 625 to the Maine Department of Marine Resources  The funding will be used for a variety of new and ongoing aquaculture management related projects  including better species tracking  species conservation  and fishery monitoring    Our state relies heavily on the many fishermen who help make the fishing industry a cornerstone of our states economy  said Senators Snowe and Collins  This grant funding is essential to helping the Maine Department of Marine Resourcesimplement successful projects that are critical to keeping our fisheries alive and prosperous                                                                            Browse by                                                                    Current record
