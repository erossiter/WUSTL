                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               November 13  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that Habitat for Humanity 7 Rivers Maine in Bath will receive  545 000 in loan funding from the U S  Department of Agricultures Rural Development program  The low interest loan funding will be used to help the group purchase and renovate its office building at 108 Centre Street in Bath   We are excited to announce this funding noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This funding will allow Habitat for Humanity 7 Rivers Maine to grow therefore allowing it to better serve low income families in Sagadahoc County by building and renovating decent and affordable housing                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
