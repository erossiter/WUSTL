                                      September 26  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today announced that the U S  Army has awarded General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products  GDATP  a contract in the amount of  44 519 583 for the procurement of 2 988 MK19 grenade machine guns  The work will be performed at Saco Defense in Saco and is expected to be completed by 2009    I am very pleased that the U S  Army has chosen to continue its ongoing relationship with the dedicated workers of Saco Defense  said Senator Collins  This contract will ensure that our special operations forces have the highest quality weaponry in their continued efforts to keep Americans safe  As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  I applaud the men and women of General Dynamics as they once again work to supply our troops with the very best arms and equipment    Senator Collins met with Linda Hudson  President of General Dynamics Armament and Technical Products Ordnance Programs  in May to discuss General Dynamics operations  specifically those of its Saco facility
