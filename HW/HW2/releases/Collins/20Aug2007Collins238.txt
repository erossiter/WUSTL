                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 20  2007   U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today that the Maine Department of Transportation will receive  250 000 for the Boom Birch Bridge on Rt  116 over Birch Stream from the Federal Highway Administration as part of continued work to upgrade and improve Maines bridges    The project will use an accelerated technique to update the existing bridge built in 1938 to a much greater range of bridge length  Using pre cast concrete caps instead of the conventional cast in place concrete will accelerate construction and minimize the road closure time required for this bridge replacement    It is important that we continually invest in and improve Maines bridges  both big and small  so that we may enhance and maintain critical infrastructure to ensure the continued safety for the people of Maine  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
