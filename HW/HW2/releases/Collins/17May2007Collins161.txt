                                      May 17  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today praised FEMAs decision to provide federal disaster assistance for areas of Washington County that were impacted by the Patriots Day storm  Todays amended disaster declaration will release public assistance funds to help repair damaged public facilities and infrastructure  President Bush has already declared 12 other Maine counties eligible for public assistance    We are pleased that FEMA has made the decision to include Washington County in its disaster declaration  said Senators Snowe and Collins  FEMA is currently reviewing incoming damage assessments to help restore our public infrastructure that was damaged during this terrible storm    The public assistance funds will provide aid to state and local governments for emergency work and the repair or replacement of disaster damaged facilities  In addition  all counties in Maine are eligible to apply for assistance under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program  Earlier this month  FEMA announced that it will provide Individual Assistance for residents of Cumberland and York Counties who were impacted by
