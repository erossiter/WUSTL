                                      October 19  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the United States Economic Development Administration  EDA  has awarded  50 000 in funding to support the Midcoast Economic Development District  MCEDD  in Bath   The funds will be used to develop a strategy and implementation plan that will respond to the economic impact of recent layoffs and plant closings             Unfortunately the Bath area has recently experienced a series of layoffs and plant closings     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement     In order to recover  they will need to develop an economic plan that will allow the area to offer increased economic opportunities  Funding from the Economic Development Administration will support the community in doing this and help it to get back on its feet                      The Economic Development Administration  EDA  aims to generate jobs  help retain existing jobs  and stimulate industrial and commercial growth in economically distressed areas of the United States  EDA assistance is available to rural and urban areas of the nation experiencing high unemployment  low income  or other severe economic distress                                                                            Browse by
