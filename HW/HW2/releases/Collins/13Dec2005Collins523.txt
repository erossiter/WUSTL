                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Collins joins Senate colleagues requesting support for  100 million rural health amendment December 13  2005 Washington  D C      WASHINGTON  D C  Senator Susan Collins has joined colleagues urging members of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee to retain the Senate passed funding levels for rural health programs when the conference committee resumes negotiations on the FY2006 Labor  Health  Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill      Collins  a member of the Senate Rural Health Caucus  sent a letter signed by 41 other Senators to the subcommittees chairman and ranking member  Arlen Specter and Tom Harkin respectively  In it  they urge the Senators to support a  100 million rural health amendment which restored full funding for several important rural health initiatives                                                                          Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
