                                     Designation will assist area   s economic development efforts May 25  2005  Washington  DC     Senator Susan Collins today announced that Maine   s Midcoast region has been designated as an Economic Development District by the Economic Development Administration  EDA    Senator Collins was instrumental in efforts to obtain this designation  which offers federal support to the Midcoast region   s economic development efforts   The district includes all of Lincoln County  Sagadahoc County  and the Town of Brunswick             This designation is critical to the economic development of Maine   s Midcoast communities   Lincoln County  Sagadahoc County  and the Town of Brunswick will now have access to programs and funding that will allow them to advance their economic development priorities and create new job opportunities     Senator Collins said       The Economic Development Administration  EDA  was established to generate jobs  help retain existing jobs  and stimulate industrial and commercial growth in economically distressed areas of the United States  EDA assistance is available to rural and urban areas of the Nation experiencing high unemployment  low income  or other severe economic distress   EDA works in partnership with state and local governments  regional economic development districts  public and private nonprofit organizations  and Indian tribes to develop and implement economic development and revitalization strategies        The Economic Development Administration offers a number of investment programs to help Economic Development Districts  including  Trade Adjustment Assistance  Economic Adjustment Assistance  Research and National Technical Assistance  Local Technical Assistance  Partnership
