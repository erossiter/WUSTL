                                      September 11  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that the Department of Homeland Security has awarded  190 000 in grant funding to the Pittston Fire Department and  The funding is provided through the Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program  or FIRE Act  The Pittston Fire Department will receive the funding through the Vehicle Acquisition program  which helps local departments purchase equipment such as pumpers  brush trucks  tankers tenders  rescue vehicles  ambulances  quints  aerials  foam units  and fireboats    The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program is an extremely critical source of funds for Maines fire and rescue personnel  noted Senator Collins  This grant will help provide the Pittston Fire Department with the equipment they need to better serve their community                                                                       Browse by
