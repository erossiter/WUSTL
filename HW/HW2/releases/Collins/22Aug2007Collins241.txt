                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 22  2007   U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today the Sanford School Department in Sanford has been awarded a grant of  714 325 from the U S  Department of Education as part of the Safe Schools Healthy Students Initiative  The grant program is designed to help create safe school environments through violence prevention programs  Additionally  students will benefit from alcohol and drug prevention programs   This funding will greatly benefit the Sanford School Department and its students  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  Every student in Maine  and the nation  should be able to benefit from a safe school with a healthy student body                                                                               Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
