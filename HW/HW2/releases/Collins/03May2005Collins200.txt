                                      May 3  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Susan Collins today announced that Ian Swanberg  a Caribou native  has been awarded a summer internship in her Aroostook County office   Swanberg is a 2002 graduate of Caribou High School and son of John and Andrea Swanberg of Caribou            Ian is a good student and hard worker   I am very pleased to welcome him to my Caribou office     said Senator Collins     I am always delighted to give students an opportunity to become actively involved in the legislative process and to serve the citizens of Maine  It is especially important that a student who was raised in Northern Maine will be helping to serve the needs of Aroostook County residents           Swanberg will be entering the final year of his undergraduate program in the fall  He is currently studying both economics and political science at McGill University in Montreal  Quebec  He says that he looks forward to assisting the citizens and
