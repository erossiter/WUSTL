                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               May 9  2007       U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today the University of Maine has been awarded a grant of  763 279 from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  as part of the Maine Sea Grant Program  The funding will be used for UMaines comprehensive program of ocean related research  education  and outreach    This grant is exciting news  noted Senator Snowe and Senator Collins in a joint statement  The continued funding provided by these Sea Grants will increase UMaines ocean research and education capacity    The Maine Sea Grant Program assists a program of research and education aimed at better understanding the Atlantic Ocean  Current areas of emphasis are ecosystem health  aquaculture  costal community development  and fisheries                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
