                                      May 18  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today that Womancare  a Piscataquis County based domestic violence support cooperative  has been awarded  170 000 in grant funding through the USDA Rural Developments Community Facility Grant Program  The funding will be used to help construct a new facility that will house Womancare  its staff  and its clients    This grant is fantastic news  noted Senator Snowe and Senator Collins in a joint statement  This funding will provide needed assistance to a community cooperative that provides a number of services to victims of abuse    Womancare currently provides services to families affected by domestic abuse  including a 24 hour helpline  safe homes  support groups  advocacy services  court accompaniment  transitional housing  transitional housing  and community and school based education programs  Womancare works closely with local law enforcement  the medical community  schools  and social service agencies  helping create a collaborative response to issues of domestic violence
