                                     Deadline is May 4  2007 April 6  2007 Washington  DC         U S  Senator Susan Collins  Ranking Member of the Senate Homeland Security Committee  today announced that the Department of Homeland Securitys  DHS  Office of Grants and Training is now accepting applications for the Fiscal Year 2007 Assistance to Firefighters Grants    In 2006  Maine received 50 separate Fire Act grant awards  totaling  5 860 578  In 2007  DHS will award more than  492 million in Fire Act grants around the country    We are all indebted to Maines firefighters for their tireless commitment to protecting our communities  and I have long supported programs  such as the Assistance to Firefighters Grants  that provide vital resources to our firefighting community  said Senator Collins  These resources will help enable fire departments to obtain updated firefighting equipment and improve firefighting training so that they are able to fulfill their responsibilities to our communities    Maine fire departments wishing to apply for these funds can do so at    The deadline for receipt of applications is no later than May 4  2007 at 5 00 p m
