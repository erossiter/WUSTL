                                     Ship to be docked in Portland July 1 5 May 16  2005  Washington  DC    Senator Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Navy is sending the U S S  Iwo Jima  LHA 7  to the Portland 4th of July Celebration this summer   The ship is scheduled to be in Portland from July 1st     5th                      It is an honor for the entire state of Maine and the city of Portland to host a Navy ship during the Fourth of July Celebration   This ship visit will add a unique experience for all who attend the celebration     said Senator Collins      Further  this ship visit will provide the opportunity for the dedicated members of the U S  Navy to meet the citizens they protect                      Senator Collins is a Member of the Seapower Subcommittee of the Senate Armed Services Committee  which has oversight over many naval programs including shipbuilding
