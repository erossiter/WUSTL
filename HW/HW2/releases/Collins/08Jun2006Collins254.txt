                                       June 8  2006 WASHINGTON D C   Senator Susan Collins today was pleased to present the Charles L  Edson Tax Credit Excellence Award to Logan Place of Portland  Mr  Dana Totman  President of Avesta Housing in Portland  accepted the award  Mark Swann  director of Preble Street  was unable to attend the ceremony  The award is given by the Affordable Housing Tax Credit Coalition and honors exemplary housing developments funded in part by Low Income Housing Tax Credits  Awards are given in four categories  Logan Place received the award for Special Needs Housing    Logan Place does not provide just housing and services  It builds a community and creates hope  Tenants become partners in individualized plans so that they may gain the skills and confidence they need to achieve their own goals  Said Senator Collins  I will continue to seek additional funding at the federal level to support the important work that Logan Place does   Logan Place is a development that provides 30 efficiency apartments for chronically homeless individuals in Portland  Supportive services for Logan Place are provided by Preble Street  This  5 000 award will enable Logan Place to bring additional facilities  services  and amenities to the development for the benefit of its residents
