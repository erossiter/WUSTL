                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Nomination will now be considered by full Senate Dr  Robert Gates  Nominee to become Secretary of Defense  December 6  2006  WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today voted to confirm Robert Gates as the next Secretary of Defense  The Senate Armed Services Committee voted unanimously to approve Gates nomination  The full Senate is expected to vote on the nomination this week    Following todays committee vote  Senator Collins released this statement   I have great respect for Robert Gates experience  ability  and intellect and I believe he will serve the nation very well as Secretary of Defense  I worked closely with Mr  Gates when I was developing the Intelligence Reform Act in 2004  At the hearing today  I was impressed with his insight  his straightforward style  and his willingness to fashion a bipartisan approach to national security issues  Mr  Gates open minded approach is refreshing    This change in leadership at the Pentagon is the right opportunity for the Administration to develop a new strategy for Iraq and Afghanistan  I am confident that Mr  Gates will work hard to address the important issues facing our military  our dedicated troops  and our country                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                            Current record
