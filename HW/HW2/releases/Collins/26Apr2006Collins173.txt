                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                April 26  2006  WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins today accepted the 2006 Friend of the Outdoor Industry Award from the Outdoor Industry Association for her support of the Land and Water Conservation Fund  LWCF  stateside program and for her commitment to parks and conservation in Maine and throughout the nation  Senator Collins is currently working with the Outdoor Industry Association to secure continued funding for LWCF stateside program  Since its creation by Congress in 1964  the LWCF stateside grants program has helped states and local communities build parks and playing fields  carve out trails  and preserve open spaces in more than 94 percent of Americas counties    Pictured  from left to right   Peter Devin  Group Show Director for VNU Expositions  Chris Ann Goddard  President of CGPR  Jay Steere  Vice President of Timberland  Will Manzer  President  CEO of Eastern Mountain Sports  Senator Collins  David Kulow  President of All Terrain  and Neal Caplowe  President of Sports Science                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
