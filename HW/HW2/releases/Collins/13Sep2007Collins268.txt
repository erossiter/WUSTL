                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               September 13  2007 Washington  DC      U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of Justice has awarded the Maine Drug Enforcement Agency  224 621 in grant funding as part of the Community Oriented Policing Service  COPS  Methamphetamine Initiative  Funding from the COPS program is designed help targeted jurisdictions across the nation combat the manufacture  use  and distribution of meth  as well as to collaborate with other entities in the prevention and treatment of meth abuse   The Department of Justice funds will help Maine combat the scourge of methamphetamine  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  Meth destroys lives  communities  and families  We are pleased that Maine will be the beneficiary of this funding                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
