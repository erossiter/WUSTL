                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               October 24  2006 WASHINGTON  DC  Senator Susan Collins today announced that General Dynamics was awarded an  18 418 800 contract from the U S  Army  The contract is for 1 300 M2 machine guns that will be produced at Saco Defense    The Army continues to look to Saco Defense for state of the art quality equipment on which our soldiers rely  The workers are making important contributions to our military services  said Senator Collins    This project includes options for the Army to extend the contract to include a total  27 627 600 in work for Saco Defense  The contract is estimated to be completed by the end of next year    Senator Collins serves on the Senate Armed Services Committee  which has oversight over the U S  Army and other military branches                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
