                                     Collins helps to unveil Mitchell portrait to hang in the U S  Capitol May 24  2005  Washington  DC     Senator Susan Collins today helped to unveil a new portrait of former Senator George Mitchell which will hang in the U S  Capitol building   The special ceremony took place in the Old Senate Chamber        Senator Collins released the following statement           George Mitchell is a peacemaker  a statesman  and a loyal representative of the State of Maine   The completion of this portrait to hang in the halls of the United States Capitol will serve to remind all of us who walk in his footsteps of the legacy he left and the great things that we can achieve     said Senator Collins      Senator Mitchell has said     I believe deeply that every single person in this nation has an obligation  a positive duty  to actively participate in preserving and improving our form of government and our way of life      We are so grateful that he took this challenge for the good of Maine and the country                                                                                        Browse by
