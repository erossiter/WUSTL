                                      September 11  2007 Washington  DC         U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of Commerces Economic Development Administration  EDA  has awarded  59 741 in funding to the City of Biddeford  The funding will be used to create a redevelopment strategy for the 35 acre former mill district in Biddeford  and is expected to be a catalyst for establishing public private partnerships with mill owners  developers and other stakeholders    We are pleased the City of Biddeford is receiving this important investment that will provide the necessary resources to create a redevelopment strategy for the mill complex  establish the framework for increased partnerships  and enhance the economic vitality of the city said Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This is a critical step in helping to create jobs and economic opportunity for our citizens and small businesses in the region     U S  Department of Commerce Economic Development Administration investments are focused on locally developed  regionally based economic development initiatives that achieve the highest return on the taxpayers investment and that directly contribute to economic growth                                                                              Browse by
