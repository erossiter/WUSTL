                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               January 19  2006 Washington  D C          WASHINGTON  DC President Bush today announced his intent to nominate Vice Admiral Thad Allen to be Commandant of the United States Coast Guard  Vice Admiral Allen currently serves as Chief of Staff to the Coast Guard and recently returned to Washington after overseeing Hurricane Katrina response and recovery efforts in the   Gulf Coast     Senator Susan Collins  who Chairs the Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee and is leading the committees Hurricane Katrina investigation  released this statement      Vice Admiral Allen is a well respected and dynamic leader  Throughout his career  he has earned the trust of everyone who has worked with him  He has also demonstrated exceptional leadership ability in his assignment in the   Gulf Coast   in the wake of Hurricane Katrina      I applaud the President for this selection and look forward to working with Vice Admiral Allen in his new position once he is confirmed                                                                          Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
