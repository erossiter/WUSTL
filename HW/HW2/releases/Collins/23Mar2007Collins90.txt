                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Maine s last suriviving Medal of Honor recipient March 23  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today released this statement following the death of Lieutenant Colonel Jay Zeamer  Jr  Lt  Col  Zeamer  who was 88  died last night at St  Andrews Village in Boothbay Harbor  He was Maines last surviving Medal of Honor recipient    Lt  Col  Zeamer was a true patriot  His heroic and selfless acts during World War II will long be remembered  He was most deserving of our nations highest military honor  and we continue to owe so much to him and all of the brave men and women who defended us in the past and continue to defend our freedom today  His courage and leadership saved the lives of his flight crew and he set an example of bravery for generations to come                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
