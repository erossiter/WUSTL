                                      March 8  2005  Washington  D C    Senator Susan Collins has been honored with the Distinguished Community Health Award by the National Association of Community Health Centers  She was recognized for her legislative efforts on behalf of America   s Health Centers and the nearly 15 million uninsured and medically underserved Americans that rely on them for vital primary care services          Ensuring that health care centers are better able to serve their communities with adequate and affordable health care services is extremely critical     said Senator Susan Collins     It is a truly an honor to receive this award from such an important and prestigious organization          The National Association of Community Health Centers  NACHC  is the national trade association serving and representing the interests of America   s community health centers   These not for profit providers of health care for America   s low income families serve community  migrant and homeless health centers   NACHC works to promote the provision of high quality  comprehensive health care that is accessible  coordinated  culturally and linguistically competent  and community directed for all underserved populations                                                                                     Browse by
