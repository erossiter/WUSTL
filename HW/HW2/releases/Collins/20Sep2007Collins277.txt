                                     Turner Letter Carrier Credited With Saving Elderly Woman s Life Senator Collins recently joined in honoring the National Association of Letter Carriers  Hero of the Year   Wayne Viger of Turner      Senator Collins is shown here with NACL President William Young and Wayne Viger  September 20  2007  U S  Senator Susan Collins was pleased to recently join in honoring a Maine letter carrier who was named the National Association of Letter Carriers Hero of the Year  Wayne Viger of Turner is credited with saving an elderly womans life while walking his mail route last year     Viger  smelled smoke at an apartment building while he was on his route on April 3  2006  Checking more closely  the 30 year veteran letter carrier heard a smoke alarm sounding inside  and then noticed smoke pouring through cracks in the windows and door of a ground floor apartment  Viger pushed open the door to find an elderly woman with her clothes aflame and her hair singed by the fire  He pulled the woman outside and extinguished her burning clothing  Then he reentered the building to check on other residents as he climbed to the second and third floors  knocking loudly on apartment doors and shouting to alert residents to the danger    Click picture to view video     Senator Collins recently joined in honoring the National Association of Letter Carriers  Hero
