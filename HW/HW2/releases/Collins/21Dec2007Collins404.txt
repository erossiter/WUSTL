                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Target donates  5 000 to help those in need Senator Susan Collins recently attended a press conference  at which Target presented the Bangor Homeless Shelter with a holiday donation of  5 000  December 21  2007  U S  Senator Susan Collinsrecently participated in a ceremony as Target presented a  5 000 donation to the Bangor Area Homeless Shelter  The Bangor Area Homeland Shelter is a 24 7 facility that provides emergency shelter and supportive services to the homeless and economically disadvantaged of the Greater Bangor area     This very generous contribution truly exemplifies the spirit we celebrate in this season of giving  said Senator Collins  Homelessness is a growing crisis  especially among those struggling with mental and physical disabilities  Dennis Marble and his devoted staff at the Bangor Area Homeless Shelter have consistently met this crisis with compassion and respect for our neighbors in need  and with the efficient and wise use of resources that have earned the confidence of supporters throughout the Bangor region                                                                        Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
