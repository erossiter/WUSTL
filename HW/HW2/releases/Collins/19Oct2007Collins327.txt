                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Team Designed Ballistic Protection System for American Troops October 19  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins today congratulated the University of Maines Advanced Engineered Wood Composites Center  AWEC  for their recent success at the American Composites Manufacturers Associations Awards for Composite Excellence  The team was awarded the Best of the Best award which recognizes the top composite product for 2007  as well as the Peoples Choice Award as voted on by more than 4 000 industry representatives  The team was awarded for their design of the Modular Ballistic Protection System which is designed to armor military tents from small arms and mortar fire    I am pleased to congratulate the Advanced Engineered Wood Composites Center for their design and innovation  noted Senator Collins  The team is truly deserving of this commendation  I have long supported this program and am more than pleased with the results                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
