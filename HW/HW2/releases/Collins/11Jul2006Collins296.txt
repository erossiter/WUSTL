                                       July 11  2006 Senator Collins attended the 2006 Dirigo Girls State conference held at Husson College in Bangor on June 18th  The program is sponsored by the American Legion Auxiliary  It is a youth citizenship program for young women who have completed their junior year of high school and shown leadership  citizenship  and cooperativeness throughout their high school career  The week long session offers training in the process of self government and good citizenship as practiced in a democratic society  Senator Collins spoke to the 400 girls that were chosen from around the state of Maine    This was the 12th time that Senator Collins has addressed the Girls State delegates on opening night of the session  Senator Collins was a delegate to Dirigo Girls State in 1970 as a student from Caribou High School   Shown in the photo with Senator Collins from left to right are members of the Honor Guard  Jasmine Bruno  Rebecca Tilley  Sarah Kennedy  Allyce Pepin  Vanessa Bush  and Catherine Collins                                                                       Browse by
