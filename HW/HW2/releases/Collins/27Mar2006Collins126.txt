                                      March 27  2006 WASHINGTON  D C U S Senators Olympia J Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced a  59 000 Rural Development grant from the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  to the Eastern Maine Development Corporation for community and economic development in traditional fishing communities of Downeast Maine  The funding will  in turn  be delivered to the Penobscot East Resource Center  PERC  in Stonington   We are pleased to see federal support from the USDA Rural Development program for Maines Downeast fishing communities in their ongoing efforts to ensure the long term health and vitality of Maines fishing industry and way of life  Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement    The USDA Rural Development program provides financial loans and grants to help improve the economy and quality of life in all of rural America  The funds are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services                                                                       Browse by
