at University of Maine at Farmington will be assisting the Senator with daily office operations  giving tours of the capital  and assisting with constitution concerns             I am glad that I am able to offer students internships in D C so they can see the day to day operation of a Senate office     said Senator Susan Collins      Shawn has been a great addition to our office and I know he will be very successful in his future endeavors          Shawn is a political science major at UMF and became interested in applying for an internship because he wanted to get first hand experience working in our Nation   s Capitol  He enjoys working on political campaigns and one day hopes to run for political office   Shawn is going into his senior year at UMF and after graduation plans on
