                                     Sen  Collins Applauds Decision by Company and Community March 24  2005  Washington  DC     Following an announcement that L L  Bean is opening a new call center in Bangor  Senator Susan Collins today welcomed L L  Bean to the area   The call center in Bangor will be the fourth year round call center for L L  Bean in the state of Maine   According to company officials  the Bangor location is expected to open in August of this year  with hiring beginning in June or July   The facility is expected to employee more than 100 workers year round  with a capacity to grow to 600     800 employees during peak season             L L  Bean has been a Maine institution since 1912  and I am delighted that they have chosen to expand operations to Bangor     Senator Collins said      L L  Bean can be assured that the Bangor community offers an impressive workforce that will be able to contribute to the company   s success in the area
