                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Collins was original cosponsor of McCain amendment to set clear standards for treatment of detainees December 15  2005 Washington  D C     WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today released this statement following the announcement that President Bush and Senator John McCain  R AZ  have reached an agreement on legislation banning inhumane treatment or punishment of prisoners in  U S   custody        As an original cosponsor of Senator McCains amendment  I am pleased with the agreement that has been worked about between the President and Senator McCain that bans cruel  inhuman  and degrading treatment of prisoners and sets clear standards for interrogations     I oppose the use of torture  whether a detainee is held by the Department of Defense or any other  U S   government agency  A clear and legal framework consistent with American values is needed for all detainees                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
