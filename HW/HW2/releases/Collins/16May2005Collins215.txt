                                     Ship to be docked in Boothbay June 20   24 May 16  2005  Washington  DC    Senator Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Navy is sending the U S S  Vicksburg  CG 69  to Boothbay Harbor during this year   s Windjammer Days Festival   The annual event is called     A Down East tradition celebrating the Boothbay Harbor Region  s rich shipbuilding heritage      The ship is scheduled to be in Boothbay from June 20     24th           The Windjammer Days Festival is dedicated to celebrating the area   s rich maritime heritage and to pay tribute to the sailing tradition of windjammers   Navy ship visits have become a memorable experience for all who attend the celebration     said Senator Collins      It is an honor for the entire state of Maine and the Boothbay community to once again host a Navy ship during this event   Further  this ship visit will provide the opportunity for the dedicated members of the U S  Navy to meet the citizens they protect           Senator Collins is a Member of the Seapower Subcommittee of the Senate Armed Services Committee  which has oversight over many naval programs including shipbuilding
