                                      April 25  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins is currently accepting applications from Maine high school students for nominations to be considered for an appointment by the four United States service academies  the Air Force Academy at Colorado Springs  Colorado  the Merchant Marine Academy at Kings Point  New York  the Military Academy at West Point  New York  and the Naval Academy at Annapolis  Maryland     I am always proud to have the honor to nominate our outstanding Maine students to our nations distinguished service academies  said Senator Collins The young people we will nominate have earned this honor through a commitment to strong scholarship  leadership  and character    Interested students should apply for a nomination in the spring and summer of their junior year  Senator Collins is currently accepting applications from students for admission to the academies in the year 2008  The deadline for completed applications is November 1  2007    For further information or an application  visitSenator Collins  website or contact   Sara Holmbom Lund c o Senator Susan Collins One City Center  Stop 23 Portland
