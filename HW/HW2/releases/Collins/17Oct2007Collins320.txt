                                      October 17  2007 Washington  DC       In response to a request from U S  Senator Susan Collins  the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administrations National Marine Fisheries Service  NMFS  today reversed its decision to establish a Dynamic Area Management  DAM  zone in an area off the coast of Machias after a recent sighting of endangered Northern right whales in the area     Senator Collins wrote to Dr  William Hogarth  Assistant Administrator  earlier this week after learning that NMFSs decision would have devastating consequences for local lobstermen and Maines lobster industry    I am pleased that officials at National Marine Fisheries Service listened to the concerns of the lobstermen who would have been severely affected by the agencys decision to essentially shut down prime fishing ground during the peak of the lobster fishing season  said Senator Collins  I appreciate that NMFS responded as quickly as possible to my request to conduct an immediate aerial surveillance of the area to ensure the accuracy of its original sighting data                                                                            Browse
