                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               June 13  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Susan Collins today released this statement following the passing of former State Representative Roger Landry of Sanford    Roger Landry was a devoted husband and father  a dedicated public servant  and a good friend  Roger always put his constituents priorities ahead of partisan politics  A Vietnam veteran  he became a tireless advocate for our veterans and worked hard to ensure that they received all of the benefits that they have earned  His passing is a loss for our state but Rogers contributions will be remembered for years to come    My thoughts and prayers are with Rogers wife  Jane  his children  and all of those who loved him  He will be missed                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
