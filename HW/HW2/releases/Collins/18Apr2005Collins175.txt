                                      April 18  2005  Washington  DC   Senator Susan Collins announced today that a permanent Port Director has been appointed to serve at Bangor International Airport  BIA    Officials at Customs and Border Protection  CBP   the agency responsible for border safety within the Department of Homeland Security  have selected John Nadeau to serve in the position   Nadeau is currently serving for CBP as a Chief Customs and Border Protection officer in Seattle  WA                      I am pleased that the Department has selected a permanent director for the vital position of Port Director at BIA   John Nadeau   s experience will serve BIA well   His work at Customs and Border Protection has given him a full understanding of the importance of homeland security responsibilities at the airport   As Port Director  he will also play a critical role in the successful execution of the airport   s daily operations         CBP was created under DHS to combine the inspectional workforces and broad border authorities of U S  Customs  U S  Immigration  Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service  and the U S  Border Patrol   Senator Collins pressed CBP to appoint a permanent Port Director in response to concerns by BIA officials that a permanent leader was necessary to guide the transition at the airport to operations under the new agency
