                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 30  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins released the following statement today after learning of the death of Specialist Matthew Evan Schneider  an active duty soldier from Gorham  New Hampshire  whose family lives in Maine  Specialist Schneider died in Ramadi  Iraq after suffering from cardiac arrest  He was not engaged in hostile activities at the time  He was assigned to the 141st Signal Battalion  1st Brigade  1st Armored Division    My thoughts and prayers go out to the family of Specialist Matthew Schneider  I was saddened to learn of the passing of a dedicated service member and I know the citizens of Maine join me in thanking Matthew Schneider for selfless his service to our nation                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
