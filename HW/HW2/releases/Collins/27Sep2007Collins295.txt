                                      September 27  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that Costal Enterprises  Inc  in Wiscasset has been awarded a loan of  750 000 through the U S  Department of Agricultures Rural Development program  The loan will be used by Coastal Enterprises to re lend for local business and community development    We are pleased to see this loan funding go to Coastal Enterprises  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  This funding will allow for low interest loans to Maine businesses and entrepreneurs  We are pleased to announce this funding    It is estimated that these Rural Development funds will create 105 jobs and retain 120 jobs in the local community    Coastal Enterprises  Inc  is a private  nonprofit Community Development Corporation and Community Development Financial Institution  Founded in 1977  the organization providesfinancing and support in the development of job creating small businesses  natural resources industries  community facilities  and affordable housing                                                                        Browse
