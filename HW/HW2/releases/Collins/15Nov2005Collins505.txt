                                      November 15  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced the Department of Homeland Security has awarded  1 818 307 in Emergency Management Performance Grants  EMPG  to Maine           We are pleased the Department of Homeland Security has agreed to provide these critical funds to Maine   This funding is important to strengthen and coordinate state and local efforts to protect our communities  airports  seaports  and international border not only from terrorists but also from threats posed by nature such as blizzards and floods     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      Congress provided  185 000 000 for the EMPG program in FY 2006   The program is designed to help state and local emergency managers develop  maintain and improve their emergency management capabilities  key components of a comprehensive national emergency management system for all hazards   This grant program provides states the flexibility to allocate funds according to risk and to address the most urgent state and local needs in disaster mitigation  preparedness  response  and recovery
