                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 13  2005  WASHINGTON  DC   Senator Susan Collins has released the following statement endorsing President Bush   s nominee for United States Trade Representative  USTR   Congressman Rob Portman                   President Bush has made a great choice in Congressman Portman to head the USTR  As a former international trade attorney and Member of the Ways and Means Subcommittee on Trade  he has a deep understanding about the importance of greater market opportunities for American businesses  He believes that strong trade relations are crucial to a more peaceful and prosperous world   He also has a strong record with reaching out to individuals on both sides of the aisle                  It is vital that the Senate confirm him as soon as possible to be our nation   s trade advocate  and I oppose any efforts to delay his confirmation                                                                                           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
