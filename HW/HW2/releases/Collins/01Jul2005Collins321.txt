                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               July 1  2005  WASHINGTON  DC    Senator Susan Collins today commented on the resignation of Associate Justice Sandra Day O   Connor  a passionate public servant who devoted her life to the rule of law            As the first woman to serve on the Supreme Court  Sandra Day O   Connor has been a brilliant Justice who has always brought integrity and common sense to her role on the bench     Senator Collins said     She has consistently demonstrated the highest standards of legal excellence   Her outstanding service on the Court has made her a role model for women everywhere   On a personal note  I would like to add that I have found her career to be inspiring   I will miss having her in Washington and wish her the best in her retirement                                                                                         Browse by                                                                    Current record
