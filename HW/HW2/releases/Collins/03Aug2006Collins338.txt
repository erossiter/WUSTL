                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 3  2006 WASHINGTON  DCSenator Susan Collins  who is a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today questioned top U S  defense officials  including DoD Secretary Donald Rumsfeld  about the status of the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan  and the global war on terrorism  In addition to Secretary Rumsfeld  witnesses included General Peter Pace  Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff and General John P  Abizaid  Commander of U S  Central Command     During her questioning of General Abizaid  Senator Collins pointed out that the premise to the U S  exit strategy in Iraq is that as the size and capability of the Iraqi military and security forces increase  the more opportunity there should be for a draw down of U S  forces  She reiterated her long standing position that a political solution to the conflict was equally important to a military solution                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                    Current record
