                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Collins says it is an appropriate time for an independent investigation March 30  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins today applauded a decision by Major League Baseball Commissioner  Bud Selig  to hire Former Maine Senator  and Majority Leader  George Mitchell to lead an independent investigation into alleged steroid use in baseball    Senator Mitchell brings an outstanding track record of independence and will conduct a fair and thorough investigation  This investigation is not just about protecting the integrity of records in a game that America loves  It is about protecting the health and values of a generation of American children who idolize major league baseball players  Former Senator George Mitchell is an excellent choice to lead this probe                                                                        Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
