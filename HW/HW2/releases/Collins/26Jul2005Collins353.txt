                                      July 26  2005   Washington  D C     U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that a  122 682 Museums for America grant has been awarded to the Maine Historical Society  MHS  from the Institute of Museum and Library Services  IMLS         The funds will be used to convert the Maine Historical Society   s current card based catalog to a digital collections management system through Maine InfoNet  a statewide  web accessible cataloging system   The project will assist MHS with exhibit design  development of education resources  acquisitions decisions  and publications while providing the public with easy access to research collections                       It is essential to encourage life long learning by funding and adequately equipping research institutions such as the Maine Historical Society     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      Maine InfoNet will serve as a valuable communication tool for Mainers  allowing them to easily access information about Maine and its history  along with current educational programs  exhibits and activities        IMLS is an independent federal agency that supports all types of museums  from art and history to science and zoos  and all types of libraries and archives  from public and academic to research and school   Museums for America Grants strengthen museums    ability to serve the public more effectively by supporting high priority activities that advance the institution   s mission and strategic goals
