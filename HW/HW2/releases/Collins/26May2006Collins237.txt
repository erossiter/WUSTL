                                     Senate votes to approve Hayden 78 15 May 26  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins  Chairman of the Senate Homeland Security Committee  today released the following statement following the full Senate vote to confirm General Michael Hayden as the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency  CIA     I am pleased that General Hayden received such a strong vote by the United States Senate  I think he is going to do a very good job as the CIA director    I got to know General Hayden when I was working on the Intelligence Reform bill in 2004 and what I most admired about him was that he was so candid in the advice that he gave to me in drafting the bill  At times  he took a position that differed from Secretary Rumsfeld  I felt that showed great courage and it showed that he was willing to do what he thought was right     The CIA faces a lot of important challenges  We need to rebuild the agency  We need to make sure it is operating only
