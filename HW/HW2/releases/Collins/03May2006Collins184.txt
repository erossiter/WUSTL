                                      May 3  2006 Washington  D C   Senator Collins released the following statement on the postal rate increase announced today   This rate increase highlights why postal reform legislation is necessary  The U S  Postal Service is the lynchpin of a  900 billion dollar industry  providing 9 million jobs nationwide and playing a critical role in our economy  Yet under its current business model  its financial future simply is not viable  said Senator Collins  The Collins Carper postal reform bill would modernize the rate setting process to provide more predictability for its users and help ensure a stronger financial future for the organization  Absent this reform  the Postal Service will now face ten months of expensive and cumbersome litigation  and more importantly  postal ratepayers will face billions of dollars in costs over the next several years  I am hopeful that the House will name conferees soon to ensure that this legislation moves
