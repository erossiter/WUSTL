                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               September 9  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Susan Collins met with Judge John G  Roberts Jr  today in her Washington  D C  office   President Bush nominated Judge Roberts to become the nation   s 17th chief justice following the death of William H  Rehnquist          Following her meeting with Judge Roberts  Senator Collins released this statement      My meeting with Judge Roberts today was a very good and informative one  He is straight forward  has an extraordinary legal mind  and a respect for the rule of law  I also believe that he is an individual who approaches the law with great humility          I am impressed with Judge Roberts    straightforward responses to a broad range of questions that I discussed with him   He has a deep respect for the Judiciary and the rule of law  and I appreciated the opportunity to explore his judicial philosophy          While I will reserve final judgment until his hearings have been completed   Judge Roberts has the judicial temperament  professional excellence  and integrity required for the Supreme Court           The Senate is scheduled to begin confirmation hearings for Judge Roberts on
