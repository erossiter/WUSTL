                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               March 9  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that  135 972 was awarded to the Medway Fire Department and South Bristol Volunteer Fire Department   The funding is provided by U S  Department of Homeland Security through the    Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program     or FIRE Act           The Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program has been an extremely critical source of funds for Maine fire stations     Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement      The grants will provide the South Bristol and Medway first responders with the equipment and training they need to better serve their communities          The following departments will receive funding            57 780 to the South Bristol Volunteer Fire Department        78 198 to the Medway Fire Department        The funds allocated to the South Bristol Volunteer Fire Department will be used to for an Operations and Firefighting Safety Program which  consists of training  equipment  wellness and fitness as well as modifications to the fire station   s facilities   The Medway Fire Department will be receiving The Firefighting Vehicles Acquisition Program  it provides various equipment  such as pumpers  brush trucks  tankers tenders  rescue vehicles  ambulances  quints  aerials  foam units  and fireboats       FIRE Act Grants were created with strong endorsement from Senators Snowe and Collins  These grants are awarded to fire departments across the United States to increase the effectiveness of firefighting operations  firefighter health and safety programs  Emergency Medical Service programs  and Fire Prevention and Safety programs and to purchase new fire equipment
