                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               January 26  2005  Washington  DC     Following Condoleezza Rice   s Senate confirmation to be U S  Secretary of State  Senator Susan Collins released the following statement          I congratulate Dr  Condoleezza Rice on being confirmed today to be America   s next Secretary of State   She has both the professional experience and the personal integrity to be a strong Secretary of State   Dr  Rice has served the President as a loyal and trusted advisor   When she speaks to foreign leaders as Secretary of State  they will know that Dr  Rice is speaking on behalf of the President          I have had the honor of working closely with Dr  Rice over the past four years   Her support was critical in seeing the Collins Lieberman intelligence reform bill become law  and she has earned my utmost confidence and trust   Condoleezza Rice is the right person for the job and she will make an outstanding Secretary of State   I look forward to working with her in the coming years                                                                                          Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Current record
