                                     Collins Worked with Maine   s Falcon Shoes to Keep Production Local March 2  2005  Washington  D C      Senator Susan Collins today applauded the sale of Falcon Shoe to Magnum USA  a division of Hi Tec Sports USA  Inc   which will keep shoe making production in Lewiston   Senator Collins has worked for the past year with Falcon Shoe Manufacturing regarding this deal   Through the acquisition Magnum has retained the current employees  hired of two additional management personnel  and will re hire twelve former Falcon employees once the transfer is complete   Lewiston will be the first Magnum facility in the United States          This is great news for Lewiston and Falcon Shoe   Lewiston has a proud history of shoe making  and that history will continue at the Continental Mill   It is the reputation of Falcon Shoe   s employees as hard workers and skilled laborers that made this sale a success  and they will be a tremendous asset to Magnum Boots     Senator Collins said      This sale is a success for the area because private industry  local economic developers  and all levels of government came together to ensure that a skilled manufacturing force remain strong and intact in the city of Lewiston   I welcome Magnum Boots to the city of Lewiston and look forward to its success in Maine
