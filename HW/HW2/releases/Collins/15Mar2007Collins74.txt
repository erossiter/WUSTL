                                      March 15  2007 Washington  DC         U S  Senators Susan Collins and Olympia J  Snowe today requested that the U S  Department of Labor approve a National   Emergency Grant for the 126 workers who lost their jobs after Moosehead Manufacturing closed two plants in Dover Foxcroft and Monson in February     Senators Snowe and Collins wrote a letter to Labor Secretary Elaine Chao requesting immediate approval of a grant for  395 678 for the employees  90 of   which were laid off from their jobs immediately  The funding will be used to provide support for reemployment efforts  including recruitment  outreach    assessment  business outreach  job development  and placement for the affected workers     This National Emergency Grant is critical to helping the former employees of Moosehead Manufacturing receive the assistance that they need    noted Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  It is our hope that the Department of Labor can immediately approve this request for these workers who   lost their jobs through no fault of their own                                                                                Browse by
