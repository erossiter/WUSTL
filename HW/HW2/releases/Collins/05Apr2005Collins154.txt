                                      April 5  2005  Washington  DC     Senator Susan Collins has been named a member of the Senate delegation that will travel to Rome to attend the funeral of Pope John Paul II  The delegation  which includes a total of 14 Senators  will depart from Washington  DC Wednesday evening and return late Friday evening             It is a high honor to be part of the delegation representing the United States at the Pope   s funeral     said Senator Collins     His remarkable courage  deep faith and enduring belief in redemption were an inspiration to people of all faiths  He was an extraordinary individual who had a profound impact on world events     she added              One of the highlights of my life was my opportunity to meet him when I traveled to the Vatican in 2001 to present him with a Congressional gold medal  I feel blessed to be able to travel to Rome to pay final respects to this great leader             Also included in the delegation trip to Rome are Senators Frist  Biden  Bunning  DeWine  Dodd  Domenici  Durbin  Kennedy  Kerry  Leahy  Martinez  Mikulski  and Santorum
