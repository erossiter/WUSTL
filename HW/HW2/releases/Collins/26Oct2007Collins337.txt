                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               October 26  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senator Olympia J  Snowe and U S  Senator Susan Collins today announced that two Maine native American tribes are the recipients of a combined  1 127 241 in grant funding as part of the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Developments Indian Community Block Grant Program  The funds will be used to provide a variety of affordable housing activities   Specifically  the Penobscot Indian Nation will receive  527 241 while the Pleasant Point Passamaquaddy Tribe will receive  600 000   This grant funding will greatly benefit our Native American communities by assisting with the creation and maintenance of safe and affordable housing noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  We are excited to announce this funding because it will do much good for so many people                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
