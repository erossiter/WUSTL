                                      July 26  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of Agriculture  USDA  Rural Development program has awarded a  500 000 loan to the Town of Dresden  These rural development funds will be used to construct a new municipal fire station public safety building that is better able to serve the public with a fully ADA handicapped accessible building  a centralized location and have meeting training room space    The USDA Rural Development program provides the opportunity for rural community services to be enhanced  Senators Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement  This funding will help the Town of Dresden build a new  more effective  and efficient fire department facility to better serve and protect the residents of the community    The USDA Rural Development program provides financial loans and grants to help improve the economy and quality of life in all of rural America  The funds are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services
