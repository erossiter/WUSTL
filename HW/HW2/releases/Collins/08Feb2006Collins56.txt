                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               February 8  2006  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Susan Collins today released the following statement on the death of Lucien    Babe    Dutremble         Today  York County lost a devoted friend and loyal public servant   Babe Dutremble  was a tireless advocate for the people of his community having served as a State Representative  a York County Commissioner for 15 years  and as Mayor of Biddeford for six years   He also instilled in his children his commitment to public service and was so proud when his son  Duke  became the first Franco American President of the Maine Senate in the state   s history   I extend my condolences to Babe   s wife  Cecile  and his entire family                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
