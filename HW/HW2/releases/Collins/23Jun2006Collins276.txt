                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               June 23  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins is pleased to announce that the U S  Senate has approved by unanimous consent the promotion of Captain Sean Crean  of Freeport  to the rank of Rear Admiral in the U S  Navy  Crean  who is the Small Business Administration Procurement Center Representative in Augusta  is the first reservist in New England to achieve this status    I want to offer my congratulations and best wishes to Sean for this important and well deserved promotion  I have known Sean for many years and we worked closely together at the Small Business Administration  Sean has consistently demonstrated strong leadership qualities that serve to enhance the Navys mission  and I am proud that he has been chosen to receive this honor                                                                        Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
