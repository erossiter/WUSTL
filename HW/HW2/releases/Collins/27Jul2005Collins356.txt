                                      July 27  2005   Washington  D C     U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan M  Collins  R ME  today announced that a grant totaling  983 300 has been awarded to the University of Maine Orono by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  as part of the Sea Grant program           As residents of a state with 3 500 miles of coastline  it is essential we continue to preserve  protect  and explore our coastal ecosystems     said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement      There is no better institution to carry out this research  education and outreach than the University of Maine Orono  which has a proven track record of positive community interactions and groundbreaking research         The Maine Sea Grant College Program carries out a comprehensive program of research  education and outreach  Major areas of emphasis include  ecosystem health  aquaculture  and fisheries  Major emphases of the outreach program are  coastal community development  aquaculture  ecosystem health  fisheries  and teacher and youth education
