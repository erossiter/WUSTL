                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 20  2007 Washington  DC       U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today that the Maine State Ferry Service has been awarded a grant of  643 500 for the Rockland Ferry Pier  The funds come from the U S  Department of Transportations Federal Transit Administration  They will be used to rehabilitate and expand the Pier in Rockland  Additionally  the projects funds will improve the timeliness and connectivity of the intercity and local bus service    This is fantastic news  noted Senator Snowe and Senator Collins in a joint statement  The Rockland Ferry provides a critical service to the residents of Vinalhaven  Northhaven  and Matinicus  This grant funding will greatly benefit the users of the Ferry    From the terminal in Rockland  the Maine State Ferry Service offers daily and year round service to the islands of Northhaven  Vinalhaven  and weekly service to Matinicus                                                                           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Current record
