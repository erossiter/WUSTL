                                       March 12  2007 WASHINGTON  D C   Senator Susan Collins met with members of the Bridgton Academy Mens Basketball team during their recent visit to Washington  D C  The team was in Washington competing in the First Annual National Prep School Championships at the University of the District of Columbia    Senator Collins met with the team in the Senates historic Reception Room  Senator Collins spoke with the students about a number of current issues facing Maine and the nation  Members of the team also met briefly with Senator Barack Obama    The team won its semi final game  defeating Hargrave Academy 121 118 in double overtime  Bridgton lost the championship final game to Notre Dame Preparatory 114 94    Bridgton Academy Mens Basketball Roster   Paul
