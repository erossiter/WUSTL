                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               February 27  2007 Washington  DC    Senator Susan Collins today introduced legislationwith Senator Ted Kennedy of Massachusetts to assist commercial fisherman purchase   life saving safety equipment  The bill would provide tax credits to help make safety equipment more affordable    The Commercial Fishermen Safety Act is designed to help fishermen who have numerous pressures experienced by most small businesses  including rising   insurance costs  maintaining their vessels  and burdensome regulation  Unfortunately  the cost of safety equipment adds to these challenges  This legislation would provide a 75  tax credit for the purchase and maintenance of the numerous pieces of safety equipment needed on the average commercial   fishing vessel  This tax credit would be capped at  1 500    In a moment of crisis on the open ocean  safety equipment is priceless  noted Senator Collins  The financial pressures on the average commercial fisherman can be prohibitive on the purchase and maintenance of safety equipment  The fishing industry has experienced far too many tragedies in its   history  It is my hope that this legislation can make the industry safer and save lives                                                                       Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Current record
