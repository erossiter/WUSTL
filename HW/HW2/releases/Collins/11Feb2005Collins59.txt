                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               February 11  2005  Washington  DC   Senator Susan Collins announced today that Cianbro of Pittsfield will help construct the new Southern Maine Postal Processing and Distribution facility   The new 429 000 square foot facility is going to be   built on the border of Scarborough and South Portland   The U S  Postal Service has approved  82 million in total funding for the project     Construction is expected to begin this summer and to be completed by April 2007   Cianbro is expected to complete approximately thirty percent of the project                 I am pleased that a Maine company was chosen to take part in this major project   This project will provide good construction jobs for Mainers and a boost to the local economy     said Senator Collins              Senator Collins is the Chairman of the Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee  which has jurisdiction over the U S  Postal Service                                                                                 Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
