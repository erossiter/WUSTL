                                      December 20  2005 Washington  D C     Washington D C    U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  has awarded  973 777 in housing funding to entities throughout  Maine         The various services funded and supported by HUDs grants are essential to communities throughout  Maine   said Senators Snowe and Collins  These programs serve some of the most vulnerable populations and provide housing to those who would not be able to afford it otherwise         500 000 has been awarded to the Aroostook Band of Micmac Indians as part of an Indian Community Development Block Grant  ICDBG   The ICDBG Program for Indian Tribes and   Alaskan Native Villages   is a competitive assistance program  The primary purpose of this program is the development of viable Native American communities  including decent housing  a suitable living environment  and economic opportunities  principally for persons of low and moderate income         396 281 has been awarded to the   University  of  Maine System   as part of a Community Outreach Partnerships Center  COPC  New Grants Program  These grants provide funds to two year and four year colleges and universities to establish and operate COPCs to address the problems of urban areas         45 574 has been awarded to the Housing Authority of the City of  Brewer    15 398 to the Lewiston Housing Authority  and  16 524 to the Portland Housing authority as part of HUDs Family Self Sufficiency  FSS  Program  The Public Housing Family Self Sufficiency program provides funding for Public Housing Authorities and tribes to hire a program coordinator to link participating families to the supportive services they need to achieve self sufficiency        The U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  funds private housing developments to assist in housing opportunities for low income residents  HUD provides rental assistance to approximately 1 5 million low income households  It also funds community revitalization projects to meet local needs and expand opportunities for Americans
