                                      February 23  2005  WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that Jackson Laboratory in Bar Harbor has received  84 500 in funding from the U S  Department of Health   Human Services  HHS    The grant was allocated to Jackson Laboratory through the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development to begin a research project for mothers and children          Jackson Laboratory is one of the Nation   s finest research institutions     Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement     It is critical that the Federal Government aid cutting edge research and development at places such as this  The funding for this particular project is especially vital as there is nothing more important than the health of mothers and children          The National Institute of Child Health and Human Development  NICHD  is part of the National Institutes of Health  NIH   which is the biomedical research branch of the U S  Department of Health and Human Services   NICHD aims to ensure that all women and children achieve and maintain healthy  productive  independent lives through optimal rehabilitation
