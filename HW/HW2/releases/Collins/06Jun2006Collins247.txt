                                       June 6  2006 WASHINGTON  D C Senator Susan Collins is pleased to announce that Lindsey Alexander  an Augusta native  has been awarded an internship in her Augusta state office  Alexanders primary duty will be to assist the Senator with constituent relations    Lindseys academic profile and strong work ethic will be a great asset to my Augusta office  said Senator Collins  Internships are extremely beneficial in providing students with the opportunity to gain real life experiences  During Lindsey s internship she will be able to learn the inner workings of a Congressional office by experiencing it first hand     Alexander  the daughter of Susan Alexander  is a 2005 graduate of Cony High School in Augusta  She is currently a sophomore at Gordon College in Wenham  Massachusetts where she is studying History and International Affairs  Alexander serves as Class President and as Chairwoman for the Gordon College Republicans  Alexander also recently completed a successful two year internship with the Maine Republican Party                                                                       Browse by
