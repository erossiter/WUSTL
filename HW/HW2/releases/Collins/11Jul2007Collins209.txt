                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Funding will be used to help treat Alzheimer   s disease in rural Maine July 11  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today the Maine Department of Health and Human Services has been awarded a grant of  300 000 from the U S  Department of Health and Human Services as part of the Alzheimers Disease Demonstration Grants to States program  The funding will be used to assist the treatment of Alzheimers disease in the rural areas of Maine   This funding will greatly benefit those suffering from Alzheimers disease and their families  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  Over 30 000 Mainers currently suffer from Alzheimers  This funding will provide them with better treatment and remove some of the burden placed on their families                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                        Current record
