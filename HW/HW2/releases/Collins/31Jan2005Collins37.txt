                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               January 31  2005  WASHINGTON  DC  Senator Susan Collins today announced the U S  Environmental Protection Agency  EPA  has selected the Caribou Maine Utilities District for a New England Regional Operations and Maintenance Wastewater Excellence Award   The Plant won in the medium secondary plant category and was one of six facilities recognized in the New England Area          The individuals operating the Caribou Utilities District play an important role in ensuring Maine   s water reservoirs stay protected from pollution     Senator Collins said      It is an honor to recognize Caribou for their efforts in keeping our environment safe and clean          The EPA Regional Operations and Maintenance Excellence Award was established to recognize staffs of Public owned wastewater treatment facilities for their commitment to improving water quality with outstanding operation and maintenance  often times with limited resources                                                                                     Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Current record
