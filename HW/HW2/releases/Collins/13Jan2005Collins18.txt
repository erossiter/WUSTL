                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               January 13  2005  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that the United States Department of Agriculture  USDA  has approved a Community Facility Guaranteed Loan of  250 000 for the Northern Maine General Hospital   The funds will be used for the development of a 4 bed assisted living home in Mapleton  Maine   The residence will house two to four individuals with mental illnesses and will create four full time jobs          Continually increasing the standard of care provided to needy Mainers is of utmost importance     Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement     We are pleased that the USDA was able to provide this loan and support the development of the Mapleton home          The USDA Rural Development mission provides Community Facility Guaranteed Loans   The loans are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services                                                                                        Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
