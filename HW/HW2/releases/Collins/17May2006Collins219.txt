to right  Tim Darnell  Michele Haggan  and Susan Bard of the Lewiston District Court during their recent visit to Capitol Hill  The group was in Washington  D C  for National Police Week and National Peace Officers Memorial Day  This commemoration began in 1962  when President John F  Kennedy signed into effect Public Law 87 726 and thereby made May 15th Peace Officer Memorial Day to honor those peace officers who gave their lives in the line of duty  This day has since been expanded to National Peace Officer Memorial Week
