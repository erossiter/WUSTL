                                     Funding to used to construct a head start facility in Farmington April 27  2007 Washington  DC         U S  Senators Susan Collins and Olympia J  Snowe announced today that Community Concepts  Inc  has received  450 000 in funding from the USDAs Rural Development program  The funding will be used to construct a new head start facility in Farmington  The center will continue to provide much needed low income day care services to Farmington and the surrounding communities    One grant of  50 000 and one loan of  400 000 comprise the funding    Studies repeatedly show that Head Start helps promote a childs growth and development  said Senators Collins and Snowein a joint statement  We are pleased that this funding will provide assistance to low income families and their children in the Farmington area by promoting further access to important head start programs that promote school readiness    Since 1965  Community Concepts  Inc  has been providing a variety of social assistance services to the citizens of Oxford  Franklin  and Androscoggin Counties  The organization is widely recognized as one of Maines best providers of social
