                                      February 14  2005  WASHINGTON  DC   Senator Susan Collins  who is a Member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  today announced that four Navy ships will be participating in events in Maine throughout the year           The presence of a Navy ship is an exciting part of Maine festivals  and I am pleased that the Navy once again has honored our great State by planning ship visits during holidays throughout the summer     Senator Collins said      These ship visits will help bring together those who serve the U S  Navy and the citizens they protect   As a Member of the Senate Committee on Armed Services  I am always moved by this proud American tradition          The following ship visits to Maine in 2005 have been scheduled by the U S  Navy          Eastport 4th of July Celebration from July 1     July 5  the USS Ramage         Belfast bay Festival  July 20     July 24  the USS Leyte Gulf        Bucksport Fort Knox Bay Festival  July 29     August 1  the USS Nashville        Rockland 58th Maine Lobster Festival  August 2     August 7  the USS Gunston Hall
