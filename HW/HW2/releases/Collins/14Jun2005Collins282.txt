                                      June 14  2005  WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and Susan Collins  R ME  today announced that  1 094 000 has been awarded to Maine from the U S  Department of Agriculture Rural Development program            Supporting vital infrastructure in Maine   s small towns is one of our greatest priorities     Snowe and Collins said in a joint statement     Therefore we are pleased that the USDA Rural Development program is able to provide these loans to the Town of Jay and Elmhurst  Inc             The funds have been allocated as follows            494 000 approved loan to the Town of Jay to assist in replacing aging sewer mains and manhole structures            600 000 approved loan to Elmhurst  Inc  of Bath to assist in the construction of a new administrative and programming facility          The USDA Rural Development mission provides guaranteed loans  approved loans  and approved grants   Rural Development is committed to helping improve the economy and quality of life in all of rural America   The loans and grants are used for community programs  which seek to construct or improve community facilities for health care  public safety  and public services                                                                                   Browse by
