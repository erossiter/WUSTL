                                      March 2  2007 Washington  DC        Senator Susan Collins  a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  released this statement following the announcement that the Department of Defense has announced the resignation of Army Secretary Francis Harvey         Harveys resignation comes following reports of substandard conditions at   Walter Reed Army Medical Center            I am very concerned about the reported conditions at   Walter Reed Army Medical Center     The conditions suggest inexcusable mismanagement of a facility that should be providing the very best care and rehabilitation of wounded veterans         Secretary Harveys resignation and the dismissal of Major General Weightman from his command overseeing the veterans hospital will help begin to restore the trust and confidence that has been lost in the wake of the revelation of these unacceptable conditions         As a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  I have asked committee leaders to thoroughly investigate conditions at Walter Reed  and a hearing has been scheduled for next week  In addition  I am pleased that the President will announce the formation of a bipartisan Presidential Commission that will review and make recommendations to improve the care we are providing our wounded service members         We must ensure that the brave men and women who return from Iraq and  Afghanistan  are provided with the medical and psychological support they need and are treated with the respect and dignity that they deserve  which certainly includes receiving excellent care and living conditions as they recover                                                                                    Browse by
