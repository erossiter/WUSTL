                                     Funding will provide improved training for personnel working with disabled children May 4  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins announced today the University of Maine has been awarded a grant of  200 000 from the U S  Department of Education as part of the Personnel Preparation program  The funding will be used to provide enhanced training for those working with children under five who suffer from disabilities    This funding will greatly benefit the University of Maine  its students  andmost importantlythose with disabilities they serve  noted Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  We are excited to announce this funding    The U S  Department of Educations Personnel Preparation program assists states in meeting their responsibility for providing personnel to serve children with disabilities  The program awards grants to assist in the training of qualified personnel in topics related to special education  early intervention  and related services                                                                           Browse by
