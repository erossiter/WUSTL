                                      May 27  2005  Washington  DC    Senator Susan Collins is cosponsoring legislation that will eliminate the unfair practice of requiring Medicare patients to pay copayments of 50 percent for mental health services  as compared to copayments of 20 percent for physical health services   The Medicare Mental Health Copayment Equity Act will phase down the copayments for mental health care services over the next six years from 50 percent to 20 percent   Senators Olympia Snowe  R ME  and John Kerry  D MA  are the bill   s sponsors             One of the most daunting public health challenges facing our nation today is how to increase access to quality mental health services for the more than 44 million Americans with severe mental disorders   This disparity in coverage is another barrier to people suffering from mental health conditions  keeping many Medicare recipients from receiving necessary mental health treatment     Senator Collins said
