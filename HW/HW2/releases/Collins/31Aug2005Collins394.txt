                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               August 31  2005  Washington  DC   Senator Susan Collins today applauded President Bush for approving the release of oil from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve to help Americans cope with the rising costs of fuel  following the effects of Hurricane Katrina on our nation  s oil production and transportation capabilities             I am pleased that President Bush has released oil from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve  so we might ease the burden of high gasoline prices on individuals and families throughout the country   Senator Collins said    Gasoline prices were too high before Hurricane Katrina  and this disaster has made the situation worse   The SPR is intended to provide relief during periods of crisis  at times when working families are struggling to make ends meet  That time is now            The President  s announcement follows a request by Senator Collins to increase the supply of oil by maximizing the use of the SPR                                                                             Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Current record
