                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               April 9  2007 Washington  DC        U S  Senators Susan Collins and Olympia J  Snowe today announced that the Department of Defense  DoD  Office of Economic Adjustment  OEA  has approved Calendar Year 2007 funding in the amount of  718 951 to the Brunswick Local Redevelopment Authority for organizational and planning activities at the Brunswick Naval Air Station  The additional money will help fund additional technical work as well as additional community outreach  With this announcement  DoD has committed a total of  1 575 083  to date  for this project    We are pleased that the Office of Economic Adjustment has agreed to provide this additional funding to assist the Brunswick Local Redevelopment Authority  said Senators Collins and Snowe in a joint statement  This decision is another positive step forward to help the Brunswick LRA perform additional technical work and community outreach to help ensure a successful community driven  redevelopment of the Brunswick Naval Air Station                                                                            Browse by                                                                                                                                                        Current record
