                                      September 22  2006 WASHINGTON  DCSenator Susan Collins  who is a Member of the Senate Armed Services Committee and has joined forces with Senators John Warner  R VA   John McCain  R AZ   and Lindsey Graham  R SC  in legislation to address military commissions  has released the following statement on an agreement reached this afternoon between the Senators and White House   I have talked with Chairman Warner and am pleased that an agreement has been reached on both the military tribunals issue and on the application of Common Article 3 of the Geneva Conventions  This agreement will allow the Senate to move forward with legislation that provides the government the tools necessary to fight terrorism  while holding us true to core American values   I commend Senators Warner  McCain  and Graham  for their persistence  tenacity  and commitment that led to agreement on this important issue
