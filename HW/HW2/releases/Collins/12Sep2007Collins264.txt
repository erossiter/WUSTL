                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               September 12  2007 Washington  DC      U S  Senators Olympia J  Snowe and Susan Collins today announced that the U S  Department of Justice has awarded the Maine Department of Public Safety  5 625 000 in grant funding as part of the Community Oriented Policing Service  COPS  Technology Program  Funding from the COPS program is designed to provide funding to law enforcement agencies for the procurement of technology that focuses on data sharing and interoperability   The Department of Justice funds will help improve Maines ability to address criminal justice issues  said Senators Snowe and Collins in a joint statement  The grant funding going to the Maine Department of Public Safety will help enhance services that will make communities of Maine safer                                                                              Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Current record
