SEN. BURR ANNOUNCES AUGUST RECESS VISITS IN NORTH CAROLINA
  Sen. Richard Burr today announced his plans to visit Bertie, Chowan, Currituck, Dare, Edgecombe, Gates, Halifax, Hertford, Northampton, Pasquotank, Perquimans, Tyrell and Washington Counties. The visits will provide Burr the opportunity to meet with North Carolinians at schools, businesses, civic groups and health care facilities.  
   Any media wishing to cover one or more of Senator Burr's visits MUST contact the host site or organization prior to the event. Please contact Senator Burr's office to schedule separate interviews.
