SEN. BURR ANNOUNCES VISITS OVER FEBRUARY RECESS
  Sen. Richard Burr will visit Anson, Duplin, Forsyth, Greene, Hoke, Jones, Pender, Richmond, Scotland, and Union Counties during the February Recess. Burr will visit schools, businesses, and civic groups.
