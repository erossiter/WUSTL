SEN. BURR ISSUES STATEMENT ON DEATH OF AL-ZARQAWI
  Sen. Richard Burr (R-NC) today made the following statement on the death of terrorist Abu Musab Al-Zarqawi: 
   "I am proud of our men and women in uniform for their hard work and dedication in removing such a ruthless, violent terrorist leader. His actions from assassinations to suicide attacks have taken the lives of many American soldiers and thousands of innocent Iraqis. Today's announcement is a step toward a free and stable Iraq."
