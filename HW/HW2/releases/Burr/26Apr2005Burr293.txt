SEN. BURR ASHEVILLE OFFICE OPEN FOR BUSINESS
  Sen. Richard Burr today announced that his office in Asheville is open for business.  
 Contact information for the Asheville office is: 
   Federal Building 
  151 Patton Avenue 
  Suite 204 
  Asheville, North Carolina 28801 
  Phone: (828) 350-2437 or (800) 685-8916 
  Fax: (828) 350-2439 
   The office will be open on Mondays from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM. All other times, Senator Burr's North Carolina staff will be traveling throughout Western North Carolina, covering a total of nineteen counties. Senator Burr's Asheville office will be staffed by Joe Brannock. 
   "With an office in Asheville, constituents throughout Western North Carolina will have access to my staff and essential government services," Senator Burr said. 
   Senator Burr's Asheville office will serve as a resource for residents of Western North Carolina. Senator Burr's North Carolina staff will be available to meet with constituents to listen to their concerns and will assist those with issues before the federal government.
