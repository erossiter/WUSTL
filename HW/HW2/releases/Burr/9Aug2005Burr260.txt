SEN. BURR ANNOUNCES AUGUST RECESS VISITS IN NORTH CAROLINA
  Sen. Richard Burr today announced his plans to visit Carrboro, Greensboro and Winston-Salem this week. Sen.  
 Burr will attend a National Health Care Week celebration at Carrboro Community Health Center, tour Syngenta Crop Protection, Inc., and present a constituent with a Congressional Award Bronze Medal. 
   Any media wishing to cover one or more of Senator Burr's visits MUST contact the host site or organization prior to the event. Please contact Senator Burr's office to schedule separate interviews.
