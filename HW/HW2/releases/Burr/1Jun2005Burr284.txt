SEN. BURR ANNOUNCES MEMORIAL DAY RECESS VISITS IN NORTH CAROLINA
  Sen. Richard Burr will visit Atlantic Beach, Goldsboro, High Point, Kinston, Morehead City, Research Triangle Park, Smithfield, Wilson and Winston-Salem during the Memorial Day Recess. Burr will visit health care centers, businesses and civic groups.
