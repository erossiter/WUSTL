                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD S STATEMENT ON CURRENT MIDDLE EAST CONFLICT          July 18th  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator   R CO  made the following statement today regarding the current Middle East Conflict     I am deeply disturbed after watching the situation near Northern Israel continue to escalate over the last couple of days     said Senator Allard       I strongly support Israel  s response to the unprovoked kidnapping of three Israeli soldiers and the unprecedented rocket bombardment of northern Israel   The current Israeli action is justified and action is necessary to punish those who are responsible for these despicable acts of terror     Allard said        At the same time  I continue to remain extremely concerned about the loss of innocent life in Israel  Lebanon  and Gaza    It is my strong belief that the United States should do everything in its power to assure Israel  s right to exist and its right to protect its borders   Israel must be allowed to live without fear within its own borders  and it is my hope that this conflict will be resolved peacefully in the coming days     Allard said     Ultimately  I believe outside actors that continue to support terrorist organizations like Hamas and Hezoballah are the main culprits here   These nations have done nothing to promote peace in the region    I believe the United States and the community of nations should put these nations on notice that their support for terrorism is unacceptable and will not be tolerated     Allard said                              July 2006 Press Releases
