                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD PRAISES ANNOUCEMENT OF NATIONAL HISTORIC LANDMARK IN BOULDER          February 10th  2006   Washington  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  praised the announcement made today by Secretary of the Interior  Gale Norton  to designate the Colorado Chautauqua as a National Historic Landmark     As a native to Colorado  I take pride in the national and historical significance of our state     said Senator Allard     I have supported the Colorado Chautauqua throughout this process to attain national recognition  and I  m very pleased with today  s announcement         As a National Historic Landmark the Colorado Chautauqua Association will enhance heritage tourism along the Front Range and bring a unique Western experience to travelers from all over the world     Senator Allard said   The Colorado Chautauqua consists of 40 acres located along the foothills of the Rocky Mountains in Boulder   The area contains five public buildings  residential cottages  and several landscaped open spaces                              February 2006 Press Releases
