          SENATOR ALLARD SECURES  56 MILLION FOR ANIMAS LA PLATA PROJECT IN ENERGY AND WATER APPROPRIATIONS BILL Bill goes to President s desk for signature         November 14th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee  on Tuesday announced that he has secured  56 million for the Animas La Plata project in Southwestern Colorado in the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations Bill  which received final approval in the Senate on Monday   The bill will now go to the President  s desk for his signature     The  56 million approved by the Senate today will keep the Animas La Plata project on track  and I  m pleased that the funding has received final approval by the U S  Congress     Senator Allard said    The Animas La Plata project  which was first started by Senator Ben Nighthorse Campbell  is extremely important to Southwest Colorado  and I will continue working in the Senate to see this project through to completion         This project is allowing us to finally resolve the water rights issues raised by the Colorado Ute tribes  without penalizing existing users  and provide a healthy water future for the region     Senator Allard said   The Animas La Plata project was established as part of the Indian Self Determination and Settlement Act of 2000   The project is in the midst of a multi year construction schedule  and the  56 million in funding is necessary to continue the project on schedule  The ongoing work includes the construction of a pumping station  construction of the Ridges Basin Dam  the implementation of wetlands and fish mitigation programs  and the relocation of a natural gas pipeline                              November 2005 Press Releases
