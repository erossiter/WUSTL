          ALLARD SAYS PRESIDENT  S BUDGET INCLUDES  52 MILLION FOR NEW VA HOSPITAL AT FITZSIMMONS          February 6th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced today that President Bush  s budget contains a  52 million budget request for a new  1 4 million square foot veterans hospital to be located adjacent to the Fitzsimons campus in Aurora     I have worked closely with the Veterans Administration on the development and funding for this new veterans hospital  so I was pleased to see the President included this  52 million request to build this new facility     said Senator Allard  a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee    This is of paramount importance to the thousands of Colorado veterans in need of accessible  quality medical care  The funding will allow us to obtain the building site  provide additional design work and begin to prepare the site for construction        As a member of the Military Construction Veterans Administration Subcommittee  I plan to fight very hard to ensure this funding request remains in the budget     Senator Allard said     Colorado veterans now face inadequate  aging facilities  Building this new VA hospital is of paramount importance to me        It is my understanding that in future years  the Department of Veterans Affairs plans to ask for the full  539 million that will be needed to build and equip this facility     Senator Allard said    I look forward to working with my colleagues to ensure we are able to get our veterans the highest quality  state of the art health care that they deserve                                 February 2006 Press Releases
