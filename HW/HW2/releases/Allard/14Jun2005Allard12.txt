          Senator Allard Secures Funding for West Slope Energy and Water Projects          June 14th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee  on Tuesday announced that he has secured funding for several West Slope projects in the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations Bill     I am pleased that the Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee has funded these critical programs for the West Slope     Senator Allard said    Water is a vital element  both for Colorado  s prosperity and for our continued quality of life   Energy  likewise  plays a huge role in our well being  and will play an even bigger role in our future      The Bureau of Reclamation  BOR  West Slope water projects approved by the Subcommittee for funding next year include    166  000 for resources management at the BOR water control project in Collbran   1 277 million for facilities operation and maintenance at the BOR water control project in Collbran   20 128 for operations and maintenance of Fruitgrowers Dam in Delta County   233 670 for facilities operations and maintenance at the BOR  s Grand Valley water control unit   2 055 million for facilities operation and maintenenace at the BOR  s Paradox Valley water control project near Montrose   62 000 for resources management at the BOR water control project in Paradox Valley   172 106 for the Uncompahgre project in Western Colorado between Montrose and Delta   In addition  in the energy portion of the bill  the Subcommittee approved  3 million for oil shale activities in Colorado  Utah and Wyoming  The full Senate is expected to act on this legislation by the end of June                              June 2005 Press Releases
