                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD TO MEET THURSDAY WITH ACTING SECRETARY OF THE ARMY TO DISCUSS PINON CANYON EXPANSION          June 20th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   will meet tomorrow with Pete Geren  acting secretary of the Army  regarding the expansion plans for the Pinon Canyon Maneuver Site      I look forward to addressing several important concerns regarding the proposed expansion of Pinon Canyon and presenting Acting Secretary Geren with a letter outlining these issues     said Allard    I expect the meeting will be productive and informative      Last week  the U S  House of Representatives added language in the Fiscal Year 2008 Military Construction   Veteran Affairs Appropriations bill to restrict the Army from spending any money that is related to  or promotes  the possible expansion of the current Pinon Canyon Maneuver Site     Continued congressional action on the FY08 Military Construction Appropriations bill is expected to occur in the near future  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  Allard is still considering whether or not to support similar language in any future appropriations bill                               June 2007 Press Releases
