          Senator Allard Praises BRAC Decision Confirming Plan to Move 4th Infantry Divison Combat Brigade to Fort Carson          August 24th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  on Wednesday praised the Base Realignment and Closure Commission  BRAC  vote in support of the Pentagon  s decision to station an Army 4th Infantry Division combat brigade and headquarters team at Fort Carson in Colorado Springs     This is something I  ve been working on since I came to the United States Senate by providing military construction funding for the growth and improvement of Fort Carson     Senator Allard said      I am pleased the BRAC recognized the value of this facility  and the potential it has for strengthening the Army  s training and mobilization capacity     he said     This is outstanding news for Fort Carson  Colorado Springs and the entire state     Senator Allard said    I also want to extend a warm welcome to those soldiers and their families who will be transferring to Colorado  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  I am going to make sure we have the training facilities  housing and other amenities needed to accommodate these new soldiers at Fort Carson       In July  Senator Allard secured  70 6 million for improvements at Fort Carson  including  14 6 million to continue work on a troop deployment facility  at Peterson Air Force Base    25 million for the barracks complex   28 million for the Combined Arms Collective Training Facility and  2 5 million for two urban combat training centers  one for regular troops and one for Special Forces  This funding is in addition to the  26 million Senator Allard obtained for the construction of new barracks at Fort Carson in the 2005 Supplemental Appropriations bill enacted in May
