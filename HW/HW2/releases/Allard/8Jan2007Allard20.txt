                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD MEETS WITH PRESIDENT BUSH AT THE WHITE HOUSE TO DISCUSS THE SITUATION IN IRAQ Colorado Senator joins Fellow Senators in Discussing Iraq Strategy         January 8th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   met Monday afternoon with President Bush at the White House along with other fellow Republican Senators to discuss the President  s new plan for Iraq    After the meeting  Allard made the following comments     President Bush today outlined the objectives of his new plan for Iraq with me  He informed me that the plan was developed with Iraqi Prime Minister Noura al Maliki  Its goal is to get the Iraqi government to play a much stronger role in terms of their share of force strength and their financial share of the costs of the war  and included new thresholds for the Iraqis to meet     I am supportive of this new strategy because it contains a much stronger commitment from Iraqis  and that  s been missing to date as they  ve become too reliant on the United States     The cost of failure in Iraq is too great as far as our future long term national security  Too much is at stake to simply abandon Iraq at this point                                 January 2007 Press Releases
