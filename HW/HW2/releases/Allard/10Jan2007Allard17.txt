                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENTS ON PRESIDENT BUSH S NEW PLAN FOR IRAQ Colorado Senator Met with President on Monday to Discuss Iraq Strategy         January 10th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    Following the President  s speech on Wednesday evening about his new strategy for the Iraq war  U S  Sen    R Colo   released the following comments      Tonight President Bush gave specific details on the objectives of his new plan for Iraq  which he shared with me earlier this week  He developed this plan with Iraqi Prime Minister Noura al Maliki to get the Iraqi government to play a much stronger role in the destiny of Iraq      I am supportive of this new strategy because it contains a much stronger commitment from Iraqis  in terms of their share of force strength and their financial share of the costs of the war  and includes new thresholds for the Iraqis to meet  To date  the Iraqis have become too reliant on U S  troops and the U S  dollar  This plan shows a new commitment from the Iraqis to step up to the plate and fight for the country  s future     The cost of failure in Iraq is too great as far as our future long term national security  It is in America  s security interests to have an Iraq that can sustain  govern and defend itself  Too much is at stake to simply abandon Iraq at this point      On Monday afternoon  Allard met personally with President Bush at the White House along with other fellow Republican Senators to discuss the President  s new plan for Iraq and learn the specifics of the new strategy                               January 2007 Press Releases
