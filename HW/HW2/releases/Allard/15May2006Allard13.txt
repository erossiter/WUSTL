                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD  S RESPONSE TO PRESIDENT  S IMMIGRATION SPEECH           May 15th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement after the President  s speech Monday evening on immigration      I am pleased to see that the President is serious about securing our border     said Senator Allard     Every border security measure the President described is worthy of consideration  including temporarily deploying the National Guard to support the Border Patrol         The addition of new border patrol agents  utilization of advanced technology  and construction of virtual and physical barriers in targeted areas  is  however  the only real  long term solution  The President  s border security plan is certainly a step in the right direction     Senator Allard said     While we must eventually address the current illegal population  we have to do so in a way that does not grant amnesty  Granting amnesty rewards breaking the law  encourages more illegal immigration  and penalizes millions of law abiding immigrants     Senator Allard said    We cannot reasonably expect to eliminate a problem by rewarding it                                 May 2006 Press Releases
