                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD TO TOUR SUPERMAX ON MONDAY          October 27th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   will take a tour of the U S  Penitentiary Administrative Maximum Facility  ADX  in Florence  also known as Supermax  on Monday  Oct  30    In light of the recent Department of Justice Inspector General and the Arbitrator  s reports  Allard thought it was important to see the operations first hand  After the tour  he will meet with local law enforcement officials  guards  county commissioners  the Sheriff of Fremont County  hospital officials  and the Sheriff of El Paso County who have expressed concerns about ongoing operations at Supermax     Allard wants to ensure that resources that were approved by Congress didn t get diverted away from Supermax  He believes it is important to visit the facility and meet with law enforcement officials prior to returning to Congress on Nov  13 to determine whether Congress will need to take action before the end of the year    Supermax houses 400 of the nation  s most dangerous criminals and needs the proper resources and personnel to keep the facility secure                               October 2006 Press Releases
