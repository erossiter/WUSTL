                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD ANNOUNCES  240 000 FEDERAL GRANT FOR SELF HELP HOUSING IN SOUTHERN COLORADO          March 12th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced today that the Upper Arkansas Council of Governments in Canon City will receive a  240 000 federal grant for self help housing technical assistance which will allow 12 new houses to be built over the next two years     This self help housing grant will help low income households in rural Colorado construct their own homes     said Allard    All Coloradans should have access to decent and safe housing  Programs such as this provide significant savings in labor costs  which helps rural families own their homes      Funded through the U S  Department of Agriculture Rural Development  the Upper Arkansas Council of Governments will fund public or private non profit organizations and Indian Tribes to provide assistance in recruitment  screening  loan packaging and related activities for prospective self help housing applicants   Self help housing funds are provided by national and regional non profit organizations and consortia to purchase home sites and develop or improve the infrastructure needed to set the stage for sweat equity and volunteer based homeownership programs for low income persons and families  Homebuyers must be willing to contribute significant amounts of their own sweat equity toward the construction of the housing units                               March 2007 Press Releases
