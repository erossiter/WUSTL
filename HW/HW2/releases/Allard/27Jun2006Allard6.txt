                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD DISAPPOINTED IN SENATE  S FAILURE TO PASS FLAG PROTECTION AMENDMENT           June 27th  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator   R CO   co sponsor of the Flag Protection Amendment  made the following statement Tuesday regarding the vote on the Constitutional amendment to ensure the right of the Congress to pass legislation protecting the American flag     I  m very disappointed in the Senate  s failure to pass the Flag Protection Amendment     said Senator Allard     All 50 states have passed resolutions calling on Congress to protect the flag  and it  s high time that we restore the voice of the American people in this debate          This right can only be restored by a Constitutional amendment that will ensure that future generations  if they choose to do so  will be able to protect the American flag without interference from the courts     Allard said   In 1989  the Supreme Court struck down long standing state laws that protected the physical integrity of the flag   In response  Congress passed the Flag Protection Act of 1989  which was subsequently struck down by the Supreme Court in 1990  thus leaving the only option of a Constitutional amendment to counter the decision                              June 2006 Press Releases
