          SENATOR ALLARD SECURES  1 3 MILLION FOR CHILDREN  S HOSPITAL AT FITZSIMONS          July 24th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator   R Colo    a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced he has secured  1 3 million in funding for the new Children  s Hospital facility at the Fitzsimons Medical Campus in the 2007 Labor  Health and Human Services  and Education Appropriations bill that passed out of committee late last week     I am pleased to secure  1 3 million for the new Children  s Hospital facility at Fitzsimons     said Senator Allard     Not only will this funding support one of the finest pediatric hospitals in the nation  it will also help create jobs and spur economic development by revitalizing the former Fitzsimons Army Medical Center      Construction of the new Children  s Hospital at Fitzsimons is already underway   The nearly 1 2 million square foot facility will include a new hospital  outpatient care building and parking facility       We are thrilled about being included in the appropriations bill   This will benefit our patients and their families for decades to come     said Dori Biester  hospital president and CEO and a registered Ph D  nurse         Once completed  the Fitzsimons Campus will be the crown jewel of Colorado  s medical community   The cutting edge patient care and medical research that will be done at new Children  s Hospital and CU Health Sciences Center will not only benefit patients  it will be a great asset to the entire community     said Senator Allard   This funding builds upon an additional  1 8 million that Senator Allard secured for Children  s Hospital in last year  s Appropriations bill                              July 2006 Press Releases
