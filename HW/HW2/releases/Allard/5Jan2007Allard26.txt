                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD BRIEFED ON STATUS OF PRESIDENTIAL DISASTER DECLARATION Disaster Declaration Decision Likely Next Week         January 5th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   was briefed on Friday by Federal Emergency Management Agency  FEMA  Director R  David Paulison regarding the state of Colorado  s request for a presidential disaster declaration      I was assured by Director Paulison that FEMA will be working through the weekend on this request and they hope to have the paperwork on the President  s desk early next week     said Allard    He understands the urgency of this disaster declaration to Colorado and to our farmers and ranchers  and that  s why they will work through the weekend to try to get it done sooner than usual      Allard sent a letter to President Bush immediately after Governor Owens asked for the emergency declaration  On Thursday and Friday he worked with White House officials to help speed the designation   Once FEMA completes the paperwork and the disaster designation is approved by President Bush  then FEMA and other federal agencies will be available to help those in need                              January 2007 Press Releases
