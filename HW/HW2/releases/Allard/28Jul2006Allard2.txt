                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD PUSHES FOR PILT AMENDMENT          July 28th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   filed an amendment to the Gulf of Mexico energy bill earlier this week that would help fund the Payment in Lieu of Taxes Program  PILT  program       The PILT program is extremely important to states such as Colorado that have a high percentage of federally owned land     said Allard    Federal ownership of these lands has its benefits  but there are unseen costs  Local governments collect reduced property taxes  but are still burdened with costs to meet the needs of the federal agencies      Allard  s amendment would provide an additional funding source for the PILT program   Based on Congressional Budget Office estimates of receipts  the additional funding for Colorado could be as much as  93 million per year   Because the program is generally funded at  110  150 million under the authorized level  the Allard amendment would help make up the annual funding gap     Every year we struggle to properly fund PILT  but unfortunately  the program is chronically underfunded  While we face an uphill challenge  I look forward to working with my colleagues to help Colorado receive its fair share from the federal government                                 July 2006 Press Releases
