          SENATOR ALLARD ANNOUNCES BALL AEROSPACE HAS WON CONTRACT TO DEVELOP NEXT GENERATION WEATHER SATELLITE TECHNOLOGY          February 9th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U  S  Senator  on Thursday announced that Ball Aerospace   Technologies Corp  of Broomfield has been awarded a  2 million contract to work on development of the next generation of weather satellites for the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA  and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration  NASA     Specifically  Ball Aerospace will be developing a geostationary lightning mapper  GLM  for the new Geostationary Operational Environmental Satellites  GOES R       I am extremely pleased and proud that Ball Aerospace  one of Colorado  s most outstanding companies  will be working to develop this 21st century weather tracking technology     Senator Allard said    These new satellites will provide Americans with vital    perhaps lifesaving    information that will be used to develop improved safety warning forecasts across the nation       NOAA  s National Weather Service estimates that lightning strikes within the United States an average of 25 million times each year  killing an average of 67 people and causing property losses  damage to aircraft and electronics  and can trigger wildfires  a major concern in Colorado    The GLM consists of an optical system  high speed optical pulse detectors and processing electronics  which will provide real time measurement of lightning activity  The GLM will also detect individual lightning pulses and provide the location of lightning flashes  continuous lightning measurements for thunderstorm and tornado warnings issued by NOAA  s National Weather Service  NWS   and accumulate a long term database to determine decadal changes in lightning activity   NOAA manages the GOES program and establishes the requirements  provides funding and distributes environmental data for the United States  NASA procures and manages the acquisition of GOES for NOAA                              February 2006 Press Releases
