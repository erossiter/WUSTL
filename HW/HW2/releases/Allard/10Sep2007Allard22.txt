Contact  Steve WymerEmail             U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENTS ON IRAQ PROGRESS REPORT FROM GEN  DAVID PETRAEUS          September 10th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   commented today on the progress report and Congressional testimony provided by Gen  David H  Petraeus  commander of the Multi National Force Iraq  on the U S  effort in Iraq  Allard  s comments are below     I did not expect that General Petraeus would come to Congress today and tell us that we have won  that liberty and safety have arrived in Iraq  and that our troops will be home next week      What he told Congress today is that the   surge    which began three months ago  is working  The U S  military has dealt significant blows to Al Qaeda  disrupted Shia militia extremists  helped reduce ethno sectarian violence  and lowered civilian casualties  He pointed out that the tribal rejection of Al Qaeda that started in Anbar Province has spread to other locations around Iraq   The Iraqi Security Forces have been standing and fighting and are even sometimes taking the lead on security operations  As a result  he said he believes that we will be able to reduce our forces to the pre surge level by next summer     Clearly our military is doing an outstanding job in Iraq  but I remain concerned about the progress the Iraqi government is making  Regardless of how much improvement the military makes  the Iraqi government must step forward and be able to effectively govern and secure their own country       At this point  I believe we should follow General Petraeus   recommendation and begin drawing down to the pre surge level with an eye towards setting further troop reductions as our military leaders on the ground in Iraq deem appropriate                                 September 2007
