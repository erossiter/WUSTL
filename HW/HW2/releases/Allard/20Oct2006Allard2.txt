                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD REQUESTS NIH SECOND OPINION FOR ARCHITECT OF THE CAPITOL TUNNEL WORKERS          October 20th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    Chairman of the Legislative Branch Appropriations Subcommittee  officially requested that the National Institutes of Health  NIH  offer its expertise to assess the medical conditions of the Architect of the Capitol  AOC  tunnel workers     It  s clear that the AOC tunnel crew still has concerns about the health assessment provided to them regarding asbestos exposure through the Attending Physician  s office     said Allard    Because these workers were not required to wear personal protective equipment prior to this year  I believe that a second medical opinion should be provided to these employees if they so choose      The written request was forwarded to the Director of NIH  Dr  Elias Zerhouni  earlier today  In the letter  Allard asks the NIH to assist in providing a full pulmonary evaluation to the AOC tunnel employees who believe their health has been adversely affected from asbestoses exposure  Allard expects that the AOC will cover the costs associated with the tests                                October 2006 Press Releases
