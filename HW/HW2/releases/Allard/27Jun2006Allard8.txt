                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD SECRUES  65 MILLION FOR ANIMAS LA PLATA PROJECT Funding Seen as Major Victory in Tight Budget         June 27th  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee  announced Tuesday that he has secured  65 million for the Animas La Plata project in Southwestern Colorado in the FY 2007 Energy and Water Appropriations bill  The funding is to be provided through the U S  Bureau of Reclamation      I am pleased that I have been able to secure  65 million in funding to keep Animas La Plata on schedule  this project is extremely important to Southwest Colorado     said Senator Allard   The Animas La Plata project was established as part of the Indian Self Determination and Settlement Act of 2000   The project is currently under a multi year construction schedule  and the  65 million in funding is necessary to continue the project on schedule  The ongoing work includes the construction of a pumping station  construction of the Ridges Basin Dam  the implementation of wetlands and fish mitigation programs  and the relocation of a natural gas pipeline     The  65 million in funding is a major step forward  and very good news in a very tight funding year     said Allard     I will continue to work with the Committee to put any available funds toward moving the schedule forward even faster                                 June 2006 Press Releases
