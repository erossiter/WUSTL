                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENDS AUTHORIZATION FOR DAILY NON STOP FLIGHTS BETWEEN WASHINGTON AND BEIJING Colorado Senator Cites Advantages for Denver Based United Airlines         January 9th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   applauded the decision announced today by the U S  Department of Transportation  DOT  to connect two of the world  s most significant capitals with non stop service between the United States and China      Direct service between the capitals of the United States and China has been long overdue  This new service will provide a great service to businesses  trade associations  the diplomatic community  academics and opinion leaders with the purpose of promoting direct access between Washington  D C   and Beijing     said Allard    Linking the capital cities of two of these two important nations spans a critical geopolitical divide and positively impacts governmental  business  academic and cultural relations      Allard sent a letter of support to Acting Secretary of Transportation Maria Cino on September 21  2006  citing the benefits of establishing this service and advocating for the acceptance of United Airline  s application to provide the service    United Airlines  with a major hub located at Denver International Airport and Dulles International Airport in Washington  D C   will provide the route service between the Washington and Beijing  Service connecting Dulles and Beijing Capital International Airport will launch within 90 days of DOT  s final order                              January 2007 Press Releases
