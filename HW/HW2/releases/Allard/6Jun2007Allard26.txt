                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF U S  SENATOR CRAIG THOMAS          June 6th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator  made the following statement today regarding the passing of U S  Senator Craig Thomas  R Wy       The Senate has lost a gentle giant who served his state and nation with honor and distinction  Joan and I are keeping Susan and the family in our thoughts and prayers     It was always a pleasure serving with Senator Thomas  first in the U S  House of Representatives  and then in the U S  Senate where we collaborated on a whole range of issues  The proximity of our home states and our shared interest and passion for natural resources and energy issues provided many opportunities to partner on legislative efforts  During the 2001   anthrax attack    on the Hart Senate Office Building that pushed several Senators out of their offices  I was happy to offer Senator Thomas   staff space in my office for several months until his office was deemed safe again     He will be remembered for being the quintessential Wyoming cowboy  a gentleman with a quick wit and humility of spirit that endeared him to his colleagues and made him a joy to work with  I will miss my friend Craig Thomas                                 June 2007 Press Releases
