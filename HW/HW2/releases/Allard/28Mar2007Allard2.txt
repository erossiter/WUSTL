          U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD KICKS OFF FINANCIAL LITERACY MONTH          March 28th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    Because April is Financial Literacy Month  U S  Sen    R Colo   is kicking off a financial literacy initiative to help raise public awareness of financial education and the importance of understanding personal finance    On Tuesday  the U S  Senate passed a resolution introduced by U S  Sen  Daniel Akaka of Hawaii and sponsored by Allard to officially designate April 2007 as Financial Literacy Month  The resolution calls on the federal government  states  localities  schools  nonprofit organizations  businesses and the people of the United States to observe the month with appropriate programs and activities     Many college students or recent grads face tremendous financial hardships whether it is pertaining to college loans or mismanagement of credit and money     said Allard    That  s why I want to help college students focus on life after graduation      In light of Financial Literacy Month  Allard will host and moderate a financial literacy panel discussion on Thursday  April 5 for students at the University of Colorado at Boulder  Allard invited panelists from the government and financial services industry to speak to help educate and prepare college students for life after college      A solid financial education can help people own a home  live a comfortable retirement  and finance an education for themselves or their children     said Allard    By improving financial literacy and its awareness we have the potential to improve the lives of millions of Americans      The Featured Issue on Allard  s Web site during the month of April will be Financial Literacy  On the site  Allard offers some links to some helpful Web sites where Coloradans can learn more about financial education and money management   In addition  Allard has compiled packets of information on financial literacy for Colorado residents that can be obtained by calling any of Allard  s offices in Colorado or Washington  D C  Contact information for any of his offices can be found at http   allard senate gov                               March 2007 Press
