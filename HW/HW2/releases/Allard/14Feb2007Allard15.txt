                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD STATEMENT ON DOD ANNOUNCEMENT ON PINON CANYON          February 14th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   has met with Assistant Secretary of the Army Installations and Environment Keith Eastin to discuss the U S  Department of Defense  DOD  announcement approving the U S  Army  s recommendation to expand the Pinon Canyon Maneuver Site  After the meeting  Allard made the following statement     The Department of Defense has approved the Army  s recommendation to expand the Pinon Canyon Maneuver Site  While this comes as no surprise  the expansion is still far from a done deal     Today  s announcement by DOD does not short circuit the process  a process that includes Congressional approval of the funds to expand the site  It is important to note that the Army  s Fiscal Year 2008 budget request does not include any funds for the expansion of Pinon Canyon     There is still a long way to go as the Army needs to identify potential willing sellers and complete a new environmental impact statement on the proposed acreage for expansion  The Army has assured me that they want the expansion to continue only with willing sellers                                 February 2007 Press Releases
