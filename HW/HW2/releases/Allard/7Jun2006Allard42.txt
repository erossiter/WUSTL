                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD PLEASED THAT MAJORITY OF SENATORS VOTING SUPPORT MARRIAGE AMENDMENT          June 7th  2006   Washington D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement after a majority of U S  Senators voting on the Amendment voted in support of the Marriage Protection Amendment in the U S  Senate on Wednesday     We have made history today     said Senator Allard    For the first time a majority of U S  Senators voting on the Marriage Protection Amendment  voted in support of the legislation  this is a clear sign that the protection of the traditional definition of marriage remains an important issue to the American people        I  m pleased with the progress we  ve made since the last vote in 2004   Gaining a majority support of those voting is a significant milestone in the fight to protect marriage  and demonstrates we are making progress on this issue     added Senator Allard                              June 2006 Press Releases
