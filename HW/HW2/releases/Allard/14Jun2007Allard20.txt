          ALLARD SECURES MORE THAN  61 MILLION IN MILITARY CONSTRUCTION BILL FOR DENVER VA HOSPITAL AT FITZSIMONS          June 14th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced that he has secured  61 3 million in funding for the new Denver VA hospital at Fitzsimons in Colorado as a part of the 2008 Military Construction Appropriations bill  Allard helped secure this funding in the bill through his work on the Military Construction   Veterans Affairs  VA  Appropriations Subcommittee  on which he serves      The continued success we have experienced in securing funding for the new VA hospital at Fitzsimons is critical to the thousands of Colorado veterans in need of accessible  quality medical care     said Allard      I have worked closely with the Veterans Administration on the development and funding for a new veterans hospital at Fitzsimons  and I am pleased that we have continued to secure the critical funded necessary to build the facility     said Allard   Allard has worked closely with the Fitzsimons Redevelopment Authority  Aurora Mayor Ed Tauer and Veterans Affairs Secretary  Jim Nicholson  to build the new replacement VA hospital at Fitzsimons     Mayor Ed Tauer expressed his appreciation for Allard  s continued work    Senator Allard continues to deliver the necessary funding to bring Colorado veterans this new state of the art medical facility  It s great for the vets  it s great for Aurora and it s great for the entire region      The Senate Appropriations Committee approved the funding today after the Military Construction and Veterans Affairs subcommittee passed the measure on Wednesday                              June 2007 Press Releases
