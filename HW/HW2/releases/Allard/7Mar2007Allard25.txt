                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD ANNOUNCES VAIL POST OFFICE NAMED IN HONOR OF GERALD FORD Legislation Signed into Law by President Bush Today         March 7th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced that today President Bush has signed legislation into law honoring former President Gerald R  Ford by naming the Vail  Colorado U S  Post Office in his honor      We are all aware that when Gerald Ford passed away late last year our country lost a great man  Much has been said recently about President Ford selflessly came to the aid of this country in one of its most trying times  how he governed through his beliefs about what was the best decision for the nation regardless of the personal consequences  and his lifelong pursuit of bipartisanship and debate     said Allard     As a lasting tribute to this tremendous man  I can not think of a more appropriate honor than to have Vail  s Post Office bear the name of Gerald R  Ford  Jr  A post office is the point in every community that brings all people together  and there is no better way to symbolize the virtues President Ford demonstrated through his public and private life     said Allard    I encourage the Senate to pass this legislation in recognition of President Ford and his contributions to Vail  Colorado      Allard and U S  Sen  Ken Salazar  D Colo   cosponsored the legislation earlier this year  U S  Rep  Mark Udall  D El Dorado Springs  introduced similar legislation in the U S  House of Representatives                              March 2007 Press Releases
