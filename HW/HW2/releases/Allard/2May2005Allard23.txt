                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Statement by U S  Senator  in Response to Interior Secretary s Decision on Water Releases from Lake Powell          May 2nd  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement Monday after U S  Secretary of the Interior Gail Norton today announced she has decided to maintain Colorado River water releases from Lake Powell at their scheduled level for the next five months     The Secretary s decision makes it clear that the Department of the Interior has the authority to make adjustments to releases from Lake Powell as part of annual operations     While I had hoped that releases would be cut back this year  I am encouraged that the Secretary has acknowledged the possibility of future curtailments  and has outlined a process for a mid year review in 2006  This is critical  While the drought may have eased somewhat  we are clearly not yet out of the woods  A future examination of this decision is warranted given the drought conditions of the past several years     I look forward to working with Secretary Norton and the Compact states to help develop a plan that protects the Upper Basin states and as well as ensures adequate water for the Lower Basin                                 May 2005 Press Releases
