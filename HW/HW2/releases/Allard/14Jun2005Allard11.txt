                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures Animas La Plata Funding  60 million provided for project         June 14th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee  on Tuesday announced that he has secured  60 million for the Animas La Plata project in Southwestern Colorado in the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations bill  The funding is to be provided through the U S  Bureau of Reclamation     I am proud to have obtained the funding to keep work going on this critical project  one extremely important to Southwest Colorado     Senator Allard said    This project allows us to finally resolve the water rights issues raised by the Colorado Ute tribes  without penalizing existing users  and provide a healthy water future for the region      The Animas La Plata project was established as part of the Indian Self Determination and Settlement Act of 2000   The project is in the midst of a multi year construction schedule  and the  60 million in funding is necessary to continue the project on schedule  The ongoing work includes the construction of a pumping station  construction of the Ridges Basin Dam  the implementation of wetlands and fish mitigation programs  and the relocation of a natural gas pipeline     This project was started and pushed forward by Senator Ben Campbell  and is dear to his heart  I am pleased that we will be able to continue his hard work on it  and see it through to completion     Senator Allard said                              June 2005 Press Releases
