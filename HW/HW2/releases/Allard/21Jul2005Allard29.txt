                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Adds Amendment to Defense Bill to Transfer Title to Air Force Property at La Junta Airport to City of La Junta          July 21st  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  has introduced an amendment to the 2006 Defense Authorization bill that will transfer surplus Air Force property at the La Junta City Airport to the city   The property was formerly referred to as the La Junta Strategic Training Range Complex Air Force installation site  and was used for electronic scoring  It now sits vacant and has been declared surplus     The Air Force no longer needs this property  and La Junta can put it to good use  There is no reason to delay this transfer  and I am optimistic the Senate will agree with my amendment     Senator Allard said    The property is located at the La Junta Airport and Industrial Park adjacent to the Otero Junior College Police Academy training area  The City of La Junta plans to use part of it as a training facility for the police academy  and for economic development    Although the property would be transferred to the city at no cost  La Junta would be responsible for any survey  environmental assessment and conveyance costs   The 2006 Defense Authorization bill is expected to pass the Senate next week                              July 2005 Press Releases
