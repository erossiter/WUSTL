                                                                                                                                                                                                                   STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD REGARDING SENATE CONFIRMATION OF GENERAL HAYDEN TO BE NEW CIA DIRECTOR Colorado  s Senior Senator Votes for Confirmation         May 27th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following comment Friday after the U S  Senate voted 78 15 to confirm General Hayden to be the new director of the Central Intelligence Agency  CIA    Allard joined the Majority in voting in favor of General Hayden  s confirmation     After General Hayden  s recent Senate hearings  I remain convinced that he is the right person to lead the CIA     Senator Allard said     The overwhelming bipartisan support to confirm General Hayden is a clear indicator that he has the confidence of the United States Senate        Cleary  General Hayden  s experience leading the NSA and being the Deputy Director of National Intelligence has prepared him for the challenges confronting the Central Intelligence Agency   I continue to remain strongly in support of General Hayden  s past decisions regarding the NSA terrorist surveillance program   I believe this program is critical to our national security and is saving American lives     said Senator Allard                                May 2006 Press Releases
