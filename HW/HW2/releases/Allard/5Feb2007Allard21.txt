                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENTS ON PRESIDENT S BUDGET          February 5th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    member of the U S  Senate Committees on Appropriations and the Budget  made the following comments after President Bush  s Fiscal Year 2008 Budget was released     I am pleased to see that the President  s budget  which includes the extension of current tax cuts  reduces the deficit every year and balances the budget in five years by 2012  His budget also slows the growth of spending and keeps the economy strong with low taxes     The President  s budget is only the beginning of the budget process to fund the federal government for Fiscal Year 2008  Now that the President has outlined his spending priorities  the Congress will do the same through the budget and appropriations process     As a member of the Senate Budget Committee  I look forward to working with my colleagues to create a budget that addresses our nation s needs while being fiscally responsible     And as the only member of the Colorado Congressional Delegation serving on the Appropriations Committee  I will work to see that Colorado priorities  like transportation  renewable energy and energy security  health care and education are included in the plan presented by Congress                                 February 2007 Press Releases
