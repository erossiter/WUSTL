          Senator Allard Secures Walden Water System Funding in Final Interior Appropriations Bill          July 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced that he has secured  800 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for the Town of Walden  s water treatment system in the final version of the 2006 Interior Appropriations bill  which was approved by the Senate on Friday   The House of Representatives approved the legislation Thursday night  the bill will now go to the White House for the President  s signature     More than a year  s hard work has finally paid off  and this long awaited funding for Walden is now a reality     said Senator Allard     I am pleased that we found a way for Walden to make the necessary upgrades to comply with new water quality regulations without passing on prohibitive costs to its residents        As a member of the conference committee that worked to iron out the differences between the House and Senate versions of the Interior Appropriations bill  I was in a position to ensure that this funding for Walden stayed in the final version of the legislation     added Senator Allard    Walden  s current treatment plant and filtration module was built in 1980   The plant  s current processes are insufficient to filter and treat newly regulated agents such as cryptosporidium   Replacement parts and equipment are becoming scarce because of the age of the system  and the plant is also in need of providing safety redundancy                              July 2005 Press Releases
