                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD COMMENTS ON THE RESIGNATION OF VA SEC  JAMES NICHOLSON           July 17th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   commented today regarding the resignation of VA Sec  James Nicholson     I was surprised to hear about the resignation of my friend and fellow Coloradan  Secretary Jim Nicholson  Having assumed control of the VA at an extremely difficult time  he has been a tireless advocate for veterans  and his efforts have helped to bring the VA up to speed in order to meet the current needs of our veterans     I would especially like to commend Secretary Nicholson for the instrumental role he played in reinvigorating the construction of a new VA hospital in Aurora on the Fitzsimons campus  Without his support  this project would not have progressed to the point were it is today  I will certainly miss Secretary Nicholson  and I thank him for his exceptional service on behalf of our veterans                                  July 2007 Press Releases
