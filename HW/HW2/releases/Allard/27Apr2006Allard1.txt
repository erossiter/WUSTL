                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD WILL PRESENT AIR FORCE ACADEMY CADET WITH CITATION AT AFA BOARD OF VISITORS MEETING FRIDAY          April 27th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  is scheduled to attend a meeting of the Air Force Academy Board of Visitors Friday at the academy  during which he will present Cadet Joseph R  Tomczak with a framed copy of an essay he wrote as part of his course work which Senator Allard had read into the Congressional Record   The essay  entitled   Winter Break     has been reprinted all over the country and is an internet wonder  bringing up thousands of hits on Google  Senator Allard had the essay entered into the Congressional Record in January   Cadet Tomczak is the son of Retired Air Force Col  Robert J  Tomczak and Nathalie Tomczak of Tampa  Florida  He is a graduate of Durant Senior High School in Tampa   When  presentation at 2 15 p m  Friday  April 28  Where  U S  Air Force Academy   Colorado Springs  Colorado  Contact  Johnny Whittaker at  719  333 7714                             April 2006 Press Releases
