                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD APPLAUDS PUEBLO AIRPORT PARTNERSHIP WITH THE AIR FORCE          September 26th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   praised the agreement reached earlier this week by the Pueblo City Council and the U S  Air Force on operation support for Air Force planes at Pueblo Memorial Airport     The citizens of Southern Colorado stand to gain from the agreement reached by the Air Force and Pueblo regarding the airport     said Allard    This partnership is the product of much discussion and I am pleased to have played a role in bringing the parties together so they could resolve this issue      The agreement between the City of Pueblo and the Air Force will provide the City with operational support and funding for crash  fire and rescue response capabilities related to Air Force flight operations conducted at the Pueblo Memorial Airport     The U S  Air Force presence has been here at the airport for some time  and the City is honored to have them     added Allard    The impacts and costs involved establish a partnership between the City and the Air Force that will last for years         At the request of Senator Allard  the Air Force Reserve Command Real Property Manager met with the City Manager  Airport Manager  City Fire Chief and a few others on July 30  The work on the agreement was completed as a result of this meeting                               September 2007 Press Releases
