                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD ANNOUNCES  2 5 MILLION GRANT FOR PAGOSA SPRINGS SENIORS HOUSING          November 1st  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced today that the Housing Solutions for the Southwest will receive more than  2 5 million from the U S  Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  for a new low income senior housing complex in Pagosa Springs     This affordable housing for Colorado seniors will help those that have very limited financial resources     said Allard  Chairman of the Banking Subcommittee on Housing and Transportation    I appreciate the Housing Solutions for the Southwest for its effort to provide seniors in Pagosa Springs with an affordable place to call home      The  2 515 600 grant will be used to construct a 20 unit  two story building for 19 low income elderly residents and a resident manager  The site for this project is six blocks from an existing Senior Center and is located near a grocery story and the downtown area  which offers shopping and a variety of restaurants and commercial services   In addition  there is a public bus stop and a senior bus service  which is available four or five times per day  to take the residents to medical facilities and to adjoining towns for other services   This federal funding was acquired through HUD  s Section 202 Program  which provides grants to non profits to acquire  build or rehabilitate rental properties for senior citizens                              November 2006 Press Releases
