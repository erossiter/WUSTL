                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces  3 Million for Jeffco Airport          February 17th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  on Wednesday announced that the Jefferson County Airport has been selected to receive a  3 million grant from the U S  Department of Transportation     I am pleased to announce this grant for the Jefferson County Airport     Senator Allard said    Jeffco is the fourth busiest airport in Colorado  and these funds will help to improve the quality and safety of the facility      The funds from this grant will be used to finance the second phase of taxiway construction at the airport   The Jefferson County Airport has been in operation since 1960  and is located near Broomfield  Colorado                              February 2005 Press Releases
