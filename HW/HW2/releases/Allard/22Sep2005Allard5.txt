                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures  149 Million in Military Construction Funding for Colorado Military Bases          September 22nd  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced on Thursday that he has secured  149 million in funding for military installations in Colorado as a part of the 2006 Military Construction Appropriations bill  which was unanimously approved by the Senate on Thursday     Our military installations in Colorado are performing vital missions both at home and abroad     Senator Allard said     This funding will ensure that Colorado  s military bases are in a stronger position than ever  and it will specifically prepare us for the influx of new personnel into the state from the Base Realignment and Closure process      The  149 million in Colorado military construction funding approved by the Senate includes    122 million for improvements in the Colorado Springs area  including  70 million for Fort Carson  and  52 million for projects at Peterson Air Force Base and the Air Force Academy  20 million for projects at Buckley Air Force Base  6 4 million to construct a new building for the 137th Space Warning Squadron in Greeley    Colorado projects are a priority in the Military Construction Appropriations bill  and I am pleased that these projects were retained in the bill passed by the Senate today     added Senator Allard                              September 2005 Press Releases
