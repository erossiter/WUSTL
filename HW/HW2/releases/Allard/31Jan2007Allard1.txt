                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD NAMED TO HIGH TECH TASK FORCE          January 31st  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    This week U S  Senate Republican Leader Mitch McConnell named U S  Sen    R Colo   to the Senate Republican High Tech Task Force  The task force helps facilitate an exchange of views between the Senate and America  s job creators in high tech industries     High technology plays a significant role in the Colorado economy     said Allard    Colorado gets high marks in venture capital investments and high tech average wages  If we can get government regulations out of the way  reduce taxes and solve the crippling problem of over litigation  Colorado  s marks may rise even higher      Allard has served as a member of the task force since its inception in 1999  In this role  he has been an advocate for telecom reform  broadband deployment to rural areas and ensuring that federal regulations and the tax code do not hinder technological growth   The High Tech Task Force is the leading advocate for the technology sector on Capitol Hill and was created to advise the Republican Caucus on technology issues  Allard joins 13 of his Senate colleagues in serving on the task force for the 110th Congress                              January 2007 Press Releases
