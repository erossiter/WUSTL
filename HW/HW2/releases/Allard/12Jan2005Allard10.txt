                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Wanye Allard Comments on Pentagon s Plan for Pueblo Depot          January 12th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement Wednesday regarding the cleanup of chemical weapons at the Pueblo Depot     The Department of Defense and the Army have an obligation to meet the 2012 deadline for disposal of chemical weapons  and I expect them to meet that obligation     The Army is well aware of my interest in getting the chemical weapons at Pueblo disposed of as safely as possible  and as quickly as possible     Right now there is money set aside for 2005 fiscal year work on the project  I have worked on this problem for quite a few years now  and will continue to make sure that the Department of Defense and the Army live up to their obligations to the Pueblo community     Last year we had to protect this program legislatively  and I am committed to doing so again  As a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  I look forward to finding solutions to this issue in the upcoming session of Congress                                  January 2005 Press Releases
