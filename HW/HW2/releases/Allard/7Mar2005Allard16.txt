                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Statement of U S  Senator  Regarding Resignation of CU President Betsy Hoffman          March 7th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement Monday regarding the resignation of University of Colorado President Betsy Hoffman     President Betsy Hoffman is to be congratulated for putting the best interests of the University of Colorado first and foremost     My desire is for the university to continue to move forward and grow  and to provide a first rate education to the young people of Colorado                                  March 2005 Press Releases
