                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces  300 000 Grant for Restoration of Elitch Gardens Theater          September 13th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced on Tuesday that the historic Elitch Gardens Theater is set to receive  300 000 for renovations through a Save America  s Treasures grant     I was pleased to support this grant for the renovation of the historic Elitch Gardens Theater     said Senator Allard     The theater has been a Colorado landmark for over a hundred years  and was the site of many happy memories for me and many other Coloradoans   These funds will help restore the theater to its former glory       The Elitch Gardens Theater was built in 1891  and is the oldest summer stock theater in America   Placed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1975  the building has fallen into disrepair since its closing in 1991   The grant will fund the renovation of the theater  s deteriorating roof structure  the first step in the building  s restoration     I commend the Historic Elitch Gardens Theatre Foundation  as well as many others in the community  for their hard work in restoring a valuable community asset in the Denver area     added Senator Allard                              September 2005 Press Releases
