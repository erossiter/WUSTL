                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD SPONSORS SANGRE de CRISTO HERITAGE AREA LEGISLATION Allard Joins Bill After Stronger Water Rights Language Included         September 6th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    Washington  D C     U S  Sen    R Colo   has agreed today to cosponsor a bill introduced by U S  Sen  Ken Salazar  D Colo   to create the Sangre de Cristo National Heritage Area after the inclusion of new water language to the legislation     I  m pleased to join my colleague from Colorado in moving this legislation forward     said Allard    This new language will protect and strengthen water rights within the Sangre de Cristo National Heritage Area boundaries      Allard met with local constituents earlier this year and agreed to support this legislation if the new water language was added to the bill  After drafting the language and receiving an assurance from Salazar that this language would be included  Allard announced Tuesday that he will cosponsor the bill   The Sangre de Cristo National Heritage Area would encompass Alamosa  Conejos and Costilla counties and the Sand Dunes  Baca Wildlife Refuge  and the Monte Vista National Wildlife Refuge  The following counties and municipalities have passed resolutions of support the bill  Alamosa  Conejos and Costilla counties  Alamosa  Antonio  Blanca  Manassa  San Luis and Sanford                                September 2006 Press Releases
