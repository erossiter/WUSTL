          Senator  Endorses Oil Shale Development Legislation          May 19th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   Chairman of the Congressional Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus  said at a Capitol press conference today that development of America  s oil shale resources has a very significant role to play in the nation  s effort to achieve energy self sufficiency      I am supporting Senator Hatch  s efforts to encourage oil shale development because I believe our smartest strategy is to develop  as rapidly as we can  more diversified domestic energy sources     Senator Allard said    Our economic well being  as well as our national security  is at stake        New technologies have made the development of oil shale more environmentally friendly  and more economically feasible    especially in light of what we are currently paying for a barrel of imported oil     Senator Allard said     Testing in western Colorado has produced a light crude suitable to fill our demand for jet fuel  and similar pure fuels  with very little surface treatment     he said      As Chairman of the Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus  a bi partisan caucus with 34 members  I am convinced that our energy plan should involve oil shale  as well as solar  wind  biomass  and geothermal  along with traditional sources such as coal and natural gas     the Senator said     We need to continue moving forward with a sense of urgency  and  to that end  I am pleased to support Senator Hatch  s efforts to formulate comprehensive legislation     the Senator said     Projects of this magnitude take years to develop    this legislation will help to begin to move the process forward                                 May 2005 Press Releases
