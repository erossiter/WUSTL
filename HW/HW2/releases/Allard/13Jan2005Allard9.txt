                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces  53 000 Grant to Southern Colorado Economic District          January 13th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced on Thursday that the Economic Development Administration has awarded a  53 000 grant to the Southern Colorado Economic Development District  SCEDD      The Southern Colorado Economic Development District has been instrumental in making the 13 counties of Southeastern Colorado a better place to do business     Senator Allard said    This grant will be of great assistance to the SCEDD as they plan the future of economic development in the region      The SCEDD is a non profit organization based in Pueblo  it has 13 member counties in Southeastern Colorado from Lake County to Baca County  The district seeks to expand economic opportunity through facilitating SBA loans  planning and funding infrastructure improvements and attracting business to member counties and communities                               January 2005 Press Releases
