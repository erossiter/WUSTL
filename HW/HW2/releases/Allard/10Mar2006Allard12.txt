                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD PRAISES NORTON  S LEADERSHIP AT HELM OF INTERIOR DEPARTMENT   Colorado and the West have lost a valuable voice in the White House           March 10th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  said Friday he is sorry to hear that Secretary of the Interior Gale Norton has decided to step down     Colorado and the West have lost a valuable voice in the White House     Senator Allard said    We have been blessed to have someone heading the Department of the Interior who understands Western issues and concerns        Her leadership on water issues has been critical to Colorado and the other states in the Colorado River Compact     Senator Allard added     I  ve also enjoyed working with her on creating parks  wilderness areas and wildlife refuges in Colorado     Senator Allard said     Being a cabinet secretary is extremely demanding  and it takes a strong commitment for anyone to stick it out as long as Gale has     Senator Allard said    She has served her country faithfully and well  and I want to wish her the best of luck in the future      Senator Allard worked with Secretary Norton on creating the Great Sand Dunes National Park in the San Luis Valley and the Rocky Mountain National Wildlife Refuge in Commerce City                              March 2006 Press Releases
