                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD STATEMENT ON ROCKY FLATS ADVISORY BOARD RECOMMENDATION          June 12th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   commented today regarding the Rocky Flats   Advisory Board recommendation      Today  s decision by the Advisory Board is frustrating on many levels     said Allard    The vast majority of the Rocky Flats workforce and their families have a right to be disappointed with the decision  Those of us in public office who have a strong sense of public duty to the Rocky Flats workers and their families share in that disappointment        Congress  s intent when passing the Energy Employees Occupational Illness and Compensation Program Act was to ensure that the men and women who knowingly or unknowingly put themselves in harm  s way had a clear  just and timely process for applying for adequate financial and medical compensation  Today  s decision  847 days after the petition was filed  was anything but timely  I have been supportive of the Rocky Flats Workforce and will continue to be an advocate of their efforts                                 June 2007 Press Releases
