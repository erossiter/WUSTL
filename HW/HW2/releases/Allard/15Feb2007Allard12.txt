                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD NOMINATES GREELEY WEST STUDENT TO AIR FORCE ACADEMY          February 15th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced he has nominated Derrick Rowe for admission to the U S  Air Force Academy as one of his 51 nominations of Colorado  s best and brightest young people for appointment to our nation  s military service academies  Rowe and other nominees would join the graduating class of 2011  selected from hundreds of students based upon criteria such as their academic record  class standing and potential for leadership   In Weld County  Derrick Rowe  a Greeley West High School student  has been nominated by Allard for admission to the U S  Air Force Academy  Rowe is the son of Bruce and Debbie Rowe of Greeley and serves as the President of his senior class and a member of the Greeley West swimming and football teams      I am very impressed by the leadership record and academic credentials of our young people in Colorado     Allard said    These are talented students who have chosen to dedicate their lives to defend the values and liberties we all cherish through service in our nation  s Armed Services  I am honored to nominate Derrick Rowe to the Air Force Academy and I wish him great success                                  February 2007 Press Releases
