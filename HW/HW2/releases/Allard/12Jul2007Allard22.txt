                                                                                                                                                                                                        Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD SECURES  1 MILLION FOR AURORA HIGHWAY IMPROVEMENTS Funding will provide easier access to the Fitzsimmons redevelopment project         July 12th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    a member of the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Transportation and Housing and Urban Development  announced today that he has secured  1 million for highway improvements along I 225 as part of the Fiscal Year 2008 Transportation and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill     This important funding will help meet the metro area  s transportation and infrastructure needs as it continues to grow     said Allard    These funds will significantly improve access to the Fitzsimmons redevelopment project by facilitating interchange construction at the intersections of I 225 and Colfax Avenue and 17th Avenue    This is good news for Aurora and I applaud Senator Allard on his efforts     said Aurora Mayor Ed Tauer    This campus is a hub for employment and health care  and providing easy access is critical         We expect greatly increased traffic heading into Fitzsimmons and it is important to make sure our road infrastructure is ready     added Allard   Allard said the funding was approved today by the full committee and he will continue to work with his colleagues on the Senate Appropriations Committee and in the House Appropriations Committee to ensure that this important project remains funded                              July 2007 Press Releases
