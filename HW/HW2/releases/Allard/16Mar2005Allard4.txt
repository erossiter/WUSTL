                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Allard Statement Regarding ANWR Vote          March 16th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following statement Wednesday after voting against an amendment to the FY 2006 Budget Resolution that would have removed a provision regarding drilling in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge  ANWR      It is not in our country  s best interest to bar the development of ANWR  OPEC today is meeting in Iran to set the price of oil  Venezuela is threatening an oil embargo against the United States  I believe drilling in ANWR can be carried out in an environmentally responsible manner  while augmenting America  s vital oil supplies  making us less dependent on foreign oil     The drilling that will take place would affect a mere 2 000 acres in a refuge that is comprised of more than 19 million acres  We cannot continue to lock up our own energy resources and leave ourselves at the mercy of unstable  if not downright hostile  regimes                                 March 2005 Press Releases
