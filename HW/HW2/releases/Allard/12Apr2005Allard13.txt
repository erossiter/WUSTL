                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces  3 1 Million in Rural Development Funding for Water System in Cedaredge          April 12th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R CO  announced on Tuesday that the Town of Cedaredge was selected to receive a  900 000 grant and a  2 2 million loan from USDA Rural Development  The funds will be used to upgrade the town  s water distribution system and add fire hydrants in the town     I am pleased that USDA Rural Development has provided this critically needed funding for Cedaredge     Senator Allard said    Recently  Cedaredge has faced an increasing amount of leaks and failures in its water lines  With this funding  Cedaredge will be able to upgrade its deteriorating system to ensure that its residents will have a safe  clean water supply     The USDA Rural Development funding will be supplemented by an additional  730 000 from sources in Colorado  including  500 000 from the Colorado Department of Local Affairs   200 000 from Delta County and  30 000 from the Town of Cedaredge                              April 2005 Press Releases
