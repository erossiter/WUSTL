                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces  400 349 for Breast Cancer Reserach at CU Health Sciences Center          January 12th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced on Wednesday that the National Cancer Institute has awarded a  400 349 grant to the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center in Aurora for breast cancer research     I am proud that the National Cancer Institute continues to recognize the quality research being conducted at UCHSC     Senator Allard said    Breast cancer cases have been declining in the United States  and we can attribute this to the commitment of public and private entities to research and prevention      This grant will fund research in the role of progesterone receptors in breast cancer  For more information  please contact David Sprenger  Director of Federal Relations at the University of Colorado at  202  577 6177                               January 2005 Press Releases
