                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD APPLAUDS THE FEMA RESPONSE TO VICTIMS OF HOLLY TORNADO          April 11th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    reacted today with appreciation to the U S  Dept  of Federal Emergency Management  FEMA  to supply temporary housing for victims of the March 28  2007 tornado that touched down in Colorado  s eastern plains     I applaud the immediate assistance offered by FEMA to assist the Holly community as they work to rebuild their lives after the tornado struck last month     said Allard    I was pleased to send a letter to U S  Dept  of Homeland Security Secretary Michael Chertoff supporting Governor Ritter  s and my request for mobile homes or other temporary housing in response to this terrible natural disaster      On April 10  2007  FEMA Region VIII received a letter from both Ritter and Allard requesting 50 temporary housing units for some of the Holly tornado victims  FEMA Region VIII approved and responded to the governor  s request immediately  FEMA is prepared to transfer the 50 mobile homes travel trailers to Colorado    Allard dispatched staff to survey the damage immediately after the tornado struck and continues to worked in coordination with Colorado Gov  Bill Ritter  s office provide aide to victims                              April 2007 Press Releases
