                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD SECURES  324 000 IN APPROPRIATIONS FOR BACA NATIONAL WILDLIFE REFUGE          June 29th  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator   R CO   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced Thursday that he has secured  324 000 in Interior Appropriations for the Baca National Wildlife Refuge     These funds will help the Baca National Wildlife Refuge maintain trails  manage existing elk herds and other wildlife  as well as  allow them to put up signs which will greatly aid visitors to the Refuge     said Senator Allard      Any time we can make public lands more user friendly we have taken a good step forward   I also see this as a great opportunity to provide public education on the good work that is being done at the Refuge and about the plant and animal species that reside there     said Senator Allard   The recently completed purchase of the Baca National Wildlife Refuge was a grassroots effort by San Luis Valley residents and their Congressional Representatives   It will act as both a refuge for wildlife and a buffer for the Great Sand Dunes National Park                              June 2006 Press Releases
