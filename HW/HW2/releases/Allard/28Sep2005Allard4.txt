                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures  2 Million for Center at CSU          September 28th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced on Wednesday that he has secured  2 million in funding for the Center for Geosciences and Atmospheric Research at Colorado State University  CSU  in the 2006 Defense Appropriations bill     I am pleased to secure  2 million for CSU  s Center for Geosciences and Atmospheric Research in the 2006 Defense Appropriations bill   I have secured annual funding for the center since 2001  and this is the latest investment     said Senator Allard       The center performs a key research mission for the Department of Defense by improving our understanding of nuclear  biological and chemical agent dispersion   The current technology being used to forecast the spread of these agents is over 30 years old  and this research will enhance both the safety and effectiveness of our Armed Forces     added Senator Allard   The Center for Geosciences and Atmospheric Research has been located at CSU since 1986   The center has conducted meteorological and hydrological research for the Department of Defense since its inception                              September 2005 Press Releases
