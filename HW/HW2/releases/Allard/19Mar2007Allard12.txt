          U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD ANNOUNCES COLORADO ACADEMY INFORMATION DAY Representatives from the Five U S  Military Service Academies to Attend and Provide Information         March 19th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced the Eighth Annual Colorado Academy Information Day and encouraged high school students interested in attending a United States military service academy to attend this event on March 25  2007  Students can meet representatives from the five academies and learn about the nomination process     As our brave young men and women are currently serving with distinction in the Middle East  high school students are becoming increasingly aware of the important role our nation  s military serves around the world     said Allard    Receiving a nomination to attend any one of the academies is a top honor  and a very exciting opportunity to serve our country      Colorado Academy Information Day will be held at the Ritchie Center for Sports and Wellness  on the University of Denver campus at 2240 E  Buchtel Blvd in Denver on Sunday  March 25  from 1 00 p m  to 4 00 p m  with the program starting promptly at 2 00 p m  Representatives from the Air Force Academy  Naval Academy  Military Academy at West Point  Merchant Marine Academy and the Coast Guard Academy will all be available to meet interested candidates for an academy nomination     Allard is honored to nominate outstanding high school students each year from across the state to attend service academies  To be considered as a candidate  students must meet the specific qualifications and submit an application to the Senator any time from January of their junior year through September of their senior year in high school  To learn more about the process  please attend the Academy Information Day                              March 2007 Press Releases
