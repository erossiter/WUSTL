                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD COMMENTS ON NOMINATION OF SAMUEL ALITO TO U S  SUPREME COURT          October 31st  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  issued the following statement today regarding President George W  Bush  s nomination of Samuel Alito  Jr  to be Associate Justice of the U S  Supreme Court     With the selection of Judge Alito  President Bush has nominated a well qualified judge in the mold of Chief Justice John Roberts   Having spent nearly 15 years with the Department of Justice  and the past 15 years on the bench with the Third Circuit Court of Appeals  Judge Alito has had a distinguished career in public service        Twice confirmed unanimously by the U S  Senate  Judge Alito has the reputation of being an intelligent  fair minded jurist who respects the rule of law        I look forward to meeting personally with Judge Alito soon to discuss his nomination  as well as his knowledge of legal issues important to the West  such as water  public lands and environmental law        As with all of the President  s nominees  I expect a fair and dignified confirmation process  ending in a timely up or down vote                                 October 2005 Press Releases
