                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Praises Chief Justice Rehnquist s Long Service on United States Supreme Court  Americans should be grateful for the dedicated work he performed on their behalf          September 4th  2005   Washington  D C     Colorado U S  Senator  on Sunday praised U S  Supreme Court Chief Justice William Rehnquist  who died Saturday evening  for his years of service on the bench of our nation  s highest tribunal      It was with great sadness that I learned of Chief Justice William Rehnquist  s death  He was one of the great men of our time  and he served his country honorably and well  The Supreme Court has lost one of its most respected jurists  the quintessential example of what a Supreme Court justice should be     Chief Justice Rehnquist will be remembered for his brilliant intellect  legal scholarship and judicial fairness      Chief Justice Rehnquist spent most of his adult life in service to his country  first in the military  then as an assistant attorney general  and finally during his long and remarkable stint on our nation  s highest court  Americans should be grateful for the dedicated work he performed on their behalf                                    September 2005 Press Releases
