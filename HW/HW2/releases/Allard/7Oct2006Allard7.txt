                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD SECURES INVITATION FOR COLORADO OFFICIALS TO ATTEND PRESIDENT S SCHOOL SAFETY CONFERENCE          October 7th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced Friday that he secured an invitation for Colorado officials to attend the President  s Conference on School Safety to be held in the Washington  D C   area on Tuesday  October 10     I am horrified by the recent acts of violence in schools in Colorado and around the nation  Unfortunately  the subject of school violence is especially important to our state     said Allard    While it will be tough work to develop strategies to stop such violence  it  s an issue that must be addressed      Allard has secured an invitation for Micheal Acree  deputy director of the Colorado Department of Public Safety and Susan Payne  executive director of Safe 2 Tell  Colorado Student Crime Stoppers Program   The White House plans to bring educators and law enforcement officials together to sit down and come up with strategies to prevent such violence and help communities deal with the aftermath of such tragic events                              October 2006 Press Releases
