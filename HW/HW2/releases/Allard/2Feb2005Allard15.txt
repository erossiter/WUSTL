                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator  Comments on State of the Union Address          February 2nd  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  made the following comments Wednesday evening after attending President Bush  s State of the Union Address     President Bush tonight continued to challenge Congress to address the critical issues of our time  Social Security  the war on terrorism  and strengthening our economy     I agree with him that we need to continue the fight for freedom and democracy in Iraq and Afghanistan  We need to ensure the long term solvency of the Social Security program for our children and grandchildren  and we need to strengthen our economy by addressing the deficit     He has asked Congress to put aside partisan posturing and deal with Social Security  Those who were in favor of Social Security reform under the Clinton administration  yet are opposing it under the Bush administration  are not cooperating in a bipartisan manner for the good of our country     I also agree we need to continue to simplify the tax code  work on legal reform  bolster our energy supplies and strengthen education  especially vocational education                                  February 2005 Press Releases
