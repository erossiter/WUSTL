                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD ANNOUNCES  77 161 GRANT FOR PAGOSA SPRINGS RADIO STATION           August 15th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced today that KPGS FM  licensed by KUTE  Inc   in Pagosa Springs will receive a digital radio grant of  77 161 from the Corporation for Public Broadcasting  CPB        This grant will help KPGS in the transition to digital radio     said Senator Allard    Digital radio holds the promise of providing more local content for local communities  and the digital transition of KPGS can be a valuable resource for Pagosa Springs        Digital technology will enhance significantly the quality and scope of radio program services  and provide richer quality sound than is currently available  To date  CPB has distributed grants to 540 public radio stations to help purchase the equipment needed to transition to digital radio                                August 2006 Press Releases
