                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures Yuma Water System Funding in Final Interior Appropriations Bill          July 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced that he has secured  100 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for the City of Yuma  s wastewater treatment system in the final version of the 2006 Interior Appropriations bill  which was approved by the Senate on Friday   The House of Representatives approved the legislation Thursday night  the bill will now go to the White House for the President  s signature     In order to comply with federal and state regulations  Yuma  s 80 year old wastewater treatment system facility needs to be replaced     said Senator Allard     I was pleased to include this funding to help reduce the financial burden on the city as they build a new treatment plant         As a member of the conference committee that worked to iron out the differences between the House and Senate versions of the Interior Appropriations bill  I was in a position to ensure that this funding for Yuma stayed in the final version of the legislation     added Senator Allard    The City of Yuma  s existing wastewater treatment facility has exceeded the parameters it was designed for  and has also had problems with leakage   Construction of the new facility is already underway                              July 2005 Press Releases
