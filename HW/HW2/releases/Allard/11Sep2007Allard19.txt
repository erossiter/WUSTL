                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD SECURES  10 MILLION INCREASE IN PRESIDENT  S BUDGET REQUEST FOR PUEBLO DEPOT Total of  142 Million Included for Depot in Senate DoD Appropriations Bill         September 11th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced today that he has secured an increase of  10 million to President Bush  s 2008 budget request  In total   142 million dollars is included for the Pueblo Depot in the 2008 Defense Appropriations bill     It is critical that we meet the deadlines set forth in the 2012 Chemical Weapons Convention deadline agreement     said Senator Allard    I  m thrilled that these funds will give an added boost to the Pueblo Chemical Agent Destruction Pilot Plant  It is reassuring that my colleagues in the Senate recognize the importance of destroying all chemical weapons at the Pueblo Chemical Depot      An additional  10 million to the  132 million requested in the President  s budget will expedite the purchase of demilitarization equipment at the Pueblo Chemical Depot   The Defense Appropriations Bill will proceed to the full Senate Appropriations Committee for consideration tomorrow                              September 2007 Press Releases
