                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SENATE PASSES MILITARY CONSTRUCTION FUNDING BILL   INCLUDES MORE THAN  17 MILLION FOR BUCKLEY AFB          November 14th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced Tuesday that the U S  Senate passed the FY 2007 military construction funding bill  which included more than  17 million for improvements at Buckley Air Force Base that Allard helped secure as a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee      These new facilities are a reflection of support at the highest levels for the excellent work performed by our Air National Guard in the state of Colorado    said Allard    I will also continue to work in the Senate to ensure that Buckley AFB  one of our premier space operations centers  receives the support it needs to carry out its mission of providing a reliable early warning system for the entire nation      The funding for Buckley Air Force Base will provide      10 7 million for a consolidated fuels facility  and      7 million for the Air National Guard F 16 Fighter Squadron Operations Center      This news is great for Aurora and great for the country     Aurora Mayor Ed Tauer said     The people at Buckley keep us safe everyday  This is a smart investment that will help them keep doing it      The military construction funding bill passed the U S  House of Representatives earlier this year and will now move to a House Senate Conference Committee where a final version will be produced  Allard  as a member of the Appropriations Committee  will serve on the Conference Committee                              November 2006 Press Releases
