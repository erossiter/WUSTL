          U S  SENATE APPROVES ALLARD S PINON CANYON AMENDMENT TO DOD AUTHORIZATION BILL          June 16th  2006   Washington  D C    The U S  Senate on Friday unanimously approved an Amendment offered by Colorado U S  Senator  that will require the Pentagon to answer a number of questions asked by the residents of southeastern Coloardo before any expansion of Pinon Canyon can proceed        The expansion of Pinon Canyon is a major issue for the people of southeastern Colorado     said Senator Allard     They are frustrated because there has been an absence of information pertaining to the Army  s proposal    I share their frustration and that is why I offered this amendment          I believe Fort Carson has done a great job of reaching out and providing what information it can to the local communities   However  the Pentagon has not been as forthcoming to the residences that have a number of questions regarding the future expansion of Pinon Canyon     said Senator Allard      Congress and  more importantly  the local communities in Southeastern Colorado need more information before we can decide whether this proposed expansion is necessary and appropriate     Senator Allard said     I  m very pleased that my amendment to the Fiscal Year 2007 Defense Authorization bill has been approved by the U S  Senate     Senator Allard said     I hope this amendment gets the Army  s attention     said Senator Allard     My amendment prohibits the Army from going forward with any proposal that would expand Pinon Canyon until the Army provides the information that I and many community leaders have requested        Now that the U S  Senate has spoken the Pentagon needs to provide this information as soon as possible so we can decide whether the project is in the best interests of the Army and the people of Southeastern Colorado     Senator Allard added   Allard  s Amendment to the 2007 DOD Authorization bill requests the following information                  Fort Carson  s current and future training requirements               Whether the Army can complete the expansion with willing sellers              Whether alternatives were considered that do not require the expansion of Pinon Canyon              The economic impact on local communities              The environmental impact of the expansion              The estimated costs of the expansion              Whether the options for compensating local communities for the loss of property tax revenues were considered              Whether options for the expansion can be carried out with transactions such as land exchanges  leases  or the              purchase of easements with willing sellers                              June 2006 Press Releases
