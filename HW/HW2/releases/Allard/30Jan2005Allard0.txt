                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard s Statement on Iraqi Elections          January 30th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   a former member of the Senate Armed Services Committee  issued the following statement on Sunday regarding Iraq  s successful democratic election    Today  the Iraqi people have spoken  Risking their very lives in some cases  they chose the right of self determination over tyranny and oppression     said Senator Allard    This is a huge step for the nation of Iraq as its people express their newfound freedom to determine their own future        Initial reports indicate that over 60 percent of eligible voters in Iraq chose to defy those who have sought to impose their will upon this courageous nation  Despite the threat of terrorist attacks  Iraqis have surpassed the world  s expectations by turning out in the millions     added Senator Allard                                  January 2005 Press Releases
