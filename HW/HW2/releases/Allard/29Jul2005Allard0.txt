          Senator Allard Secures Jefferson County Stormwater System Funding in Final Interior Appropriations Bill          July 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced that he has secured  500 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for improvements to Jefferson County  s stormwater collection system in the 2006 Interior Appropriations bill  which was approved by the Senate on Friday   The House of Representatives approved the legislation Thursday night  the bill will now go to the White House for the President  s signature     Jefferson County faces a combination of challenges  including new federal regulations and explosive growth   Because of these factors Jefferson County needs to make county wide upgrades to its stormwater collection system     said Senator Allard     I was pleased to include this funding to help reduce the financial burden on the county as they upgrade their stormwater infrastructure         As a member of the conference committee that worked to iron out the differences between the House and Senate versions of the Interior Appropriations bill  I was in a position to ensure that this funding for Jefferson County stayed in the final version of the legislation     added Senator Allard    New regulations  system conditions and growth throughout Jefferson County have created the need for a comprehensive plan to make improvements to the county  s stormwater collection system   The improvements will also be funded in part by the Colorado Department of Local Affairs                              July
