                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD SECURES  1 MILLION FOR STATE HIGHWAY 13 Funds for Repaving and Safety Improvements         July 25th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator   R Colo   announced that the Senate Transportation  Treasury  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill includes  1 million to improve State Highway 13 from Craig to the state line   Allard is a member of the Appropriations Committee which approved the funding      This is a very important project     said Allard    I  m pleased to have been able secure funds for Highway 13 from Craig to the Colorado state line      State Highway 13 is one of the only north south routes in northwestern Colorado to connect I 70 to I 80 in Wyoming  Because of increases in energy development in this region of the state  there has been an influx of heavy truck traffic along the corridor  The bill includes  1 million for safety improvements  including shoulders  and general reconstruction of State Highway 13 from Craig to the state line   The Transportation  Treasury  and Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Bill is expected to move to the U S  Senate floor for a vote later this year                              July 2006 Press Releases
