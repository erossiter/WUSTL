                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Announces State Department to Open New Direct Custormer Service Passport Office in Aurora  Colorado          May 19th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  was informed by Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice on Thursday that the U S  Department of State plans to open a new passport office to provide direct customer service in Aurora  Colorado   This will be the first on site service passport facility that the State Department has opened nationwide in the last quarter of a century     This is great news for travelers in Colorado and the Rocky Mountain Region   It is a major coup that the Denver metro area will be the site of the State Department  s first direct customer service passport facility in more than 25 years     Senator Allard said     This new facility will be close to DIA  and will be much more convenient for travelers throughout Colorado when emergency travel needs arise      Secretary Rice informed Senator Allard that the office should be opened and operating by this summer with a staff of 23 employees  including 15 State Department of State staff  and will be capable of providing passport services for up to 400 customers every day     I am very grateful to Secretary Rice for recognizing the need for a full service passport facility to serve Colorado and the entire Rocky Mountain region     said Senator Allard                               May 2005 Press Releases
