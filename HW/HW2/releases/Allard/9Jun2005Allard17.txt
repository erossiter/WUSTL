                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures Brush Water System Funding in Interior Appropriations Bill          June 9th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Interior Appropriations Subcommittee  on Thursday announced that he has secured  100 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for the City of Brush  s wastewater treatment system in the FY 2006 Interior Appropriations bill     Brush is working to address the requirements of both increased regulation and population growth as they replace their wastewater treatment facility     Senator Allard said     I  ve worked to provide this funding to help reduce the financial burden on the city as they upgrade their infrastructure for the future      The City of Brush  s wastewater treatment facility was constructed in 1965   New discharge standards require that Brush remove nitrogen and phosphorus levels from their wastewater   The city has determined that a new facility will be more cost effective than making further upgrades to the old system     I will continue my efforts in the Senate to make sure that rural communities like Brush receive the federal support that they need as they try to upgrade aging facilities  and I will be working hard to make sure that the Interior Appropriations bill will be approved by the full Senate as soon as possible     added Senator Allard                              June 2005 Press Releases
