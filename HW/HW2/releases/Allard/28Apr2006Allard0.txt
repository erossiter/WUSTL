                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD TO UNVEIL COLORADO STATE JUNIOR DUCK STAMP WINNERS          April 28th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  will be keynote speaker at 2 45 p m  Saturday  April 29th at the Colorado State Junior Duck Stamp awards ceremony at the Arvada Center  He also will unveil the Colorado Junior Duck Stamp winning entry that will compete with other state entries to be chosen as the national Junior Duck Stamp by the U S  Fish and Wildlife Service later this year  The stamps are sold by the U S  Post Office to raise funds for conservation and wildlife refuges        The Junior Duck Stamp contest is an environmental education program designed to teach wetlands  habitat and waterfowl conservation to students in kindergarten through 12th grade  The program encourages students to communicate what they have learned visually by drawing or painting native waterfowl species in their habitat  and by writing a conservation message   Mitch King  regional director for the Mountain Prairie Region of the U S  Fish and Wildlife Service  also will participate in the ceremony   When  2 45 p m  Saturday  April 29  Where  Arvada Center 6901 Wadsworth Blvd  Arvada  Contact  Angela de Rocha at 202 341 9341 Courtney Staatz at 303 220 7414                             April 2006 Press Releases
