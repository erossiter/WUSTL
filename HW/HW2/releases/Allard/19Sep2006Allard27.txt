                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENDS COLORADO SCHOOLS FOR EDUCATING STUDENTS ABOUT THE CONSTITUTION          September 19th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   commends Colorado school districts  including Cherry Creek School District and Adams  14 School District  for educating their students about the U S  Constitution in recognition of Constitution Day     The Constitution of the United States is among the most historically significant documents ever written     said Allard    The Constitution remains at the heart and root of our society and serves as a foundation for democracy and freedom all around the world  And it  s important that Colorado students  and students around the country  know and understand the rights given to them under the Constitution      Allard provided about 500 pocket copies of the U S  Constitution to schools in Colorado in preparation for this year  s event    Constitution Day celebrates the signing of the U S  Constitution on September 17  1787  The Constitution officially took effect on March 4  1789  Colorado was the 38th state to enter the Union on August 1  1876                              September 2006 Press Releases
