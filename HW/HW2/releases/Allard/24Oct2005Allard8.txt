                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR WAYNE ALLARD PRAISES NOMINATION OF BEN BERNANKE TO HEAD FEDERAL RESERVE Anticipates stability  consistency in Fed policies         October 24th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   a member of the Senate Banking Committee  on Monday said he is confident that President Bush  s nominee to serve as Chairman of the U S  Federal Reserve will continue the policies that have kept the Unites States prosperous  and on an even economic keel     I have been impressed with Ben Bernanke  s competence and knowledge since meeting him when he first was nominated to chair the President  s Council of Economic Advisers     Senator Allard said     I believe he will continue the positive  productive policies that have been put forward under Chairman Greenspan  such as moderation and transparency     Senator Allard said     His expertise in monetary policy  both hands on and theoretical  will serve this country well     Senator Allard concluded                               October 2005 Press Releases
