                                                                                                                                                                                                                   CONGRESS APPROVES ALLARD  S FUNDING FOR FOUNTAIN CREEK PROJECT STUDY IN ENERGY AND WATER APPROPRIATIONS BILL Bill goes to President s desk for signature         November 14th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     The U S  Congress on Monday approved funding included by Senator   R Colorado  that provides  125 000 for a study of the Fountain Creek watershed in the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations Bill Conference Report   The bill now goes to the President  s desk for his signature     Ever since major flooding occurred in 1999  it has been clear to me that federal support was needed     said Senator Allard     The  125 000 that I included as a House Senate conferee will keep the watershed study moving forward  despite this year  s tight budget        The funding this bill provides will pay for the preparation of a detailed analysis of flood control in the Fountain Creek watershed  and I believe this study will further demonstrate the need for environmental restoration on a regional scale to address all 150 miles of Fountain Creek and its tributaries     added Senator Allard   Senator Allard already has ensured more than  930 000 in funding for the Fountain Creek project since 2002                              November 2005 Press Releases
