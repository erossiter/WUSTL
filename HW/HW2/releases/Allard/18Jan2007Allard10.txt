                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD COMMENTS ON HANK BROWN S RETIREMENT          January 18th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   expressed his regrets today upon learning of the announcement that Hank Brown  current president of the University of Colorado  plans to resign his post at the end of the year     Hank Brown is a great Coloradan and has left his mark on the state in many ways over the years  I always appreciate working with Hank and expect that he will continue to be a leader in whatever new endeavor he chooses to pursue     said Allard   Brown is the former president of the University of Northern Colorado  and represented Colorado in the U S  Senate after five consecutive terms in the U S  House representing Colorado  s 4th Congressional District  He also served in the Colorado State Senate from 1972 76     We  ve worked together on projects over the years  but it is always great to watch the   Rocky Mountain Showdown   with Hank     said Allard    I will miss our days at the CSU CU football game                                 January 2007 Press Releases
