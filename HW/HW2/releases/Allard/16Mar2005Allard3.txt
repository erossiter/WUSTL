          Allard Urges Secretary of State Rice to Put Beef Import Embargo at Top of Economic Agenda During Visit to Japan          March 16th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  said Wednesday that he has asked U S  Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice to press the Japanese government to lift its embargo on beef imports from the United States during her visit to Japan this week     I have urged the Secretary to make the case for beef produced by America  s farms and ranches  and to put it at the top of her economic agenda during these talks with the Japanese     Senator Allard said     The State Department has reviewed my February 18 letter to the Japanese ambassador  and has been briefed on our meeting with Ambassador Ryozo Kato last week     the Senator said    I have made the Secretary aware of the growing unrest in Congress regarding the Japanese beef embargo  and requested that she take our message to Tokyo      Last week  Senator Allard met with the Japanese Ambassador to the United States to discuss how to bring the Japanese beef embargo to a quick conclusion  Thirteen other senators and congressmen participated in that meeting as well   In a letter to Secretary Rice  outlining the situation  Senator Allard wrote that        continuation of the beef ban could possible result in retaliatory economic sanctions  While I hope such measures can be avoided  I ask that you convey this sentiment so that policymakers in Japan realize what is at stake      In December of 2003  a single  foreign born  cow tested positive for bovine spongiform encephalopathy  BSE  in Washington state  As a result of this finding  Japan immediately closed its markets to U S  beef  After much negotiation  the U S  and Japan have agreed in principal to the resumption of trade  Unfortunately  an independent body of the Japanese government  the Food Safety Commission  has yet to approve this decision  keeping the market of what used to be the largest importer of U S  beef closed                              March 2005 Press Releases
