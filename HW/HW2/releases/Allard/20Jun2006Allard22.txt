          ALLARD CHAMPIONS RENEWABLE ENERGY AT TUESDAY EXPO          June 20th  2006   Washington  D C    U S  Senator  made the following statement at the 2006 Clean Energy EXPO Tuesday   The EXPO is co sponsored by the Senate Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus and is a showcase for renewable energy and energy efficient technologies that are being developed by private and government entities     As a founder and current co chairman of the Senate Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus  I certainly appreciate the contributions that renewable energy sources and energy efficiency technology make     said Senator Allard     National security  increased energy costs and tighter supplies of fossil fuels are forcing us to look in the direction of a more balanced energy policy     Senator Allard said     More attention is being given to clean energy technologies by industry and consumers        Great opportunities exist for solar  wind  geothermal  biomass  fuel cells and hydro to make significant contributions   But we should also focus on increasing energy efficiency   saving a megawatt of energy is faster  cleaner and more effective than producing one     Senator Allard said     I have long been supportive of federal renewable energy research dollars   for NREL and others to continue their great work in trying to develop both new technology and ways to bring down the cost of current technologies     Senator Allard said   The Senate Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Caucus is a bi partisan caucus   There are currently 36 Members  more than one
