                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD INFORMED BY THE VA OF PLANS TO OPEN CLINIC IN NORTHWESTERN COLORADO Clinic to Open in September in Craig         April 18th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced that the U S  Department of Veterans Affairs  VA  will open a community based outreach clinic in Craig  Colo  by September 30  2007  Allard had pushed for a VA clinic to be located in northwestern Colorado to serve a growing veterans population     This new clinic in Craig will provide greater care for western Colorado  s increasing veteran population     said Allard    The more than 400 current veteran users in the area and the approximately 4 200 veterans in northwestern Colorado have earned this convenience       The parent facility for the new clinic will be in Grand Junction  serving a total veteran population of 37 000   Allard has also been advocating for the establishment of a VA clinic in eastern Colorado and expects a decision in the next month by the VA                               April 2007 Press Releases
