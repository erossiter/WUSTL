          ALLARD SECURES FUNDING FOR MISSILE DEFENSE ACTIVITIES IN COLORADO Defense Bill Includes  15 Million for Colorado  s Joint National Integration Center         July 21st  2006   Washington  D C    Senator Allard announced today that the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Defense included at Senator Allard  s request  15 million for the Missile Defense Agency  s Joint National Integration Center  JNIC    The JNIC is based at Schriever Air Force Base in Colorado Springs  Colorado   The Joint National Integration Center is the Department of Defense  s premier site for the missile defense command and control and missile defense modeling and simulation functions      As evidenced by the recent North Korean ballistic missile tests  the threat of a missile attack to our country  our allies  and our troops deployed overseas is very alive and very real     said Senator Allard    Colorado National Guardsmen at the JNIC are on the front line defending our country against this form of attack every day        The funding provided at the request of Senator Allard will be used specifically to increase data acquisition  improved operations  and allow for better battle management decisions       Colorado  s Joint National Integration Center has been a key hub in the operations of the missile defense system  as well as a center of excellence for the missile defense modeling and simulation activities        This additional money will help the Center improve our operational capability and increase our ability to defeat ballistic missile attacks
