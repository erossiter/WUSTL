                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator  Congratulates Southwest Airlines on Establishing Denver International Airport Routes          October 20th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator   R  Colorado  made the following statement regarding Southwest Airlines   announcement Thursday that it plans to initiate service from Denver International Airport early next year     Today  s announcement by Southwest Airlines is great news for Denver International Airport  This new service will give Coloradoans and others traveling in and out of our state more travel options than ever  Dallas based Southwest no doubt had Texas skiers    who love Colorado    in mind when deciding to expand to DIA  Competition is good for business and good for consumers  I have no doubt our entire state will benefit from Southwest  s new  enhanced routes as well                               October 2005 Press Releases
