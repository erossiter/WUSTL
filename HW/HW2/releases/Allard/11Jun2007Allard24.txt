                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD STATEMENT ON THE ALBERTO GONZALES VOTE Calls today  s Senate vote a   disappointing political stunt           June 11th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   commented today regarding the Senate procedural motion today to move to a no confidence vote on U S  Attorney General Alberto Gonzales     Today  s vote on Attorney General Alberto Gonzales was a   disappointing political stunt   by the Democratic leader to embarrass the Attorney General and the President     said Allard      It is very disappointing that the Majority Leader would cut off debate on the important immigration issue to waste valuable time to stage this non binding vote on a procedural motion that did not even garner the necessary votes to allow the resolution to be brought to the floor of the Senate     added Allard    It is my hope that we can get back to the serious issues the American people sent us to Washington to address                                 June 2007 Press Releases
