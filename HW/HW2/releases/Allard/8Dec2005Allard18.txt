          SENATOR ALLARD SAYS JAPANESE COMMISSION DECISION IS AN EARLY CHRISTMAS PRESENT FOR U S  BEEF          December 8th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  on Thursday hailed a decision by Japan  s Food Safety Commission ruling that beef produced in America is safe for consumption by the Japanese people as an early Christmas present for U S  beef producers     The Food Safety Commission in Japan is finally saying what I have been saying for nearly two years now  and what American consumers have known all along   That beef produced in the United States is safe and of the highest quality     Senator Allard said    I am glad the Japanese food safety authority has finally seen the light in time for Christmas        This is progress  but there still are a couple of steps to be taken toward our final goal of complete normalization of U S  beef exportation to Japan     Senator Allard said    I am cautiously optimistic that we are on the verge of resolving this long running dispute      The Japanese Food Safety Commission agreed unanimously on Thursday to send its conclusions regarding the safety of American produced beef to that country  s health and agriculture ministries  which will make a final recommendation to the Japanese government on whether to re open the country  s ports to U S  beef imports   Senator Allard has been one of the most outspoken defenders of beef produced in the United States  and has repeatedly pressed the Japanese to reconsider their two year old decision to close their ports to American beef imports  Earlier this year Senator Allard headed up a bipartisan coalition of 14 Senators who met with Japanese Ambassador Ryozo Kato to discuss the import ban  In addition  he also encouraged President Bush and Secretary of State Rice to make the reopening of Japan to American beef imports one of their top priorities in talks with the Japanese government  He also is an original cosponsor of legislation that would place tariffs on Japanese products in response to the beef import ban   Following Thursday  s announcement  it is expected that Japan will announce its conditions for the resumption of imports of beef from the United States  perhaps as soon as Monday                              December 2005 Press Releases
