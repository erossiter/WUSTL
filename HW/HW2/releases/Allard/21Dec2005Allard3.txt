                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD SECURES  1 8 MILLION FOR CHILDREN  S HOSPITAL AT FITZSIMONS              December 21st  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Appropriations Committee  announced on Wednesday that he has secured  1 8 million in funding for the new Children  s Hospital facility at the Fitzsimons Medical Campus in the 2006 Defense Appropriations bill passed by the Senate on Wednesday and the U S  House last week     I am pleased to secure  1 8 million for the new Children  s Hospital facility at Fitzsimons     said Senator Allard     Not only will this funding support one of the finest pediatric hospitals in the nation  it will also help create jobs and economic development by revitalizing the former Fitzsimons Army Medical Center      Construction of the new Children  s Hospital at Fitzsimons is already underway   The nearly 1 2 million square foot facility will include a new hospital  outpatient care building and parking facility      Once completed  the Fitzsimons Campus will be the crown jewel of Colorado  s medical community   The cutting edge patient care and medical research that will be done at new Children  s Hospital and CU Health Sciences Center will not only benefit patients  it will be a great asset to the entire community     said Senator Allard                              December 2005 Press Releases
