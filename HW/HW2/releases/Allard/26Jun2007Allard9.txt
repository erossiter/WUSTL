We applaud Senator Allard and thank him for his efforts in securing this necessary funding for the Chatfield Reservoir     said Greg Hertzke  water acquisitions manager with the Central Colorado Water         The Greenway Foundation greatly appreciates the support of Senator Allard in securing the much needed appropriation for the Chatfield Reallocation Project     said the Greenway Foundation  s Jeff Shoemaker    Chatfield is a perfect example of how often competing forces in the world of water can come together in an engaged and cooperative manner and craft solutions that are beneficial to
