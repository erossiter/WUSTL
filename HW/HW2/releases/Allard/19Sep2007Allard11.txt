Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD TO PRESENT ROCKY FLATS WORKERS WITH A   SAMMIES    AWARD Sen   Nominated Workers for Recognition         September 19th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator   R Colo   will present the U S  Department of Energy Rocky Flats management team with the Service to America Medals  Sammies  award in the Science and Environmental category at tonight  s 2007 Sammies Awards Gala in Washington  D C      I look forward to presenting this well deserved Sammie award to the Rocky Flats management team     said Allard    The success of the Rocky Flats site represents the best of Federal Site Clean ups and the best of federal employee work        Rocky Flats serves as a daily reminder of what can be done when local communities and governments unite behind a common cause     added Allard    Our Nation will be forever indebted to the men and women who worked at Rocky Flats and played a role in the site  s unprecedented clean up and transition      The Rocky Flats DOE team was tasked with the management and oversight of one of the Nation  s largest  most controversial clean up projects  The Rocky Flats clean up included remediation and removal of approximately 3 million square feet of structures  shipment of over 600 000 cubic meters of radioactive waste  and investigation and environmental remediation of 360 potentially contaminated areas  The Rocky Flats Technology Site  s management transferred from the U S  Dept  of Energy  DOE  to the U S  Dept  of the Interior on July 12  2007  officially establishing the Rocky Flats National Wildlife Refuge   The Sammies awards program pays tribute to America s dedicated federal workforce  highlighting those who have made significant contributions to our country  Honorees are chosen based on their commitment  innovation  and the impact of their work on addressing the needs of the nation                               September 2007 Press Releases
