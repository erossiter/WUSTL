                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD INTRODUCES LEGISLATION TO COMMEMORATE ANNIVERSARY OF PRESIDENT REAGAN  S LANDMARK BRANDENBURG GATE SPEECH          June 13th  2006   Washington  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  today introduced legislation in the U S  Senate to commemorate President Ronald Reagan  s groundbreaking   Mr  Gorbachev  tear down this wall     speech at the Brandenburg Gate in Berlin on June 12  1987     It is a great honor for me to introduce this legislation     said Senator Allard    This will be a fitting tribute to the closure of a dark chapter in history          Under Reagan  s leadership  and through his dedication and conviction  he helped bring about the fall of communism  and ushered in a new era of freedom and democracy     Senator Allard said   The bill would authorize an artistic tribute to President Reagan  s famous speech at the Berlin Wall to be placed in the United States Capitol Building      The inspirational words    Mr  Gorbachev  tear down this wall    will forever resonate in our minds as a sign of America  s victory over tyranny     Senator Allard said                              June 2006 Press Releases
