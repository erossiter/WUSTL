          Senator Allard Secures Englewood Littleton Wastewater Treatment Funding in Final Interior Appropriations Bill          July 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced that he has secured  500 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for improvements to the Englewood Littleton Wastewater Treatment Plant Phase 2 expansion in the 2006 Interior Appropriations bill  which was approved by the Senate on Friday   The House of Representatives approved the legislation Thursday night  the bill will now go to the White House for the President  s signature     Higher than expected growth has created the need for greater capacity for wastewater treatment at the Englewood Littleton facility     said Senator Allard     I was pleased to include this funding to reduce the costs of the expansion  and help mitigate the rate increases that could be passed on to residents         As a member of the conference committee that worked to iron out the differences between the House and Senate versions of the Interior Appropriations bill  I was in a position to ensure that this funding for the Englewood Littleton facility stayed in the final version of the legislation     added Senator Allard    New regulations  system conditions and growth throughout Jefferson County have created the need for a comprehensive plan to make improvements to the county  s stormwater collection system   The improvements will also be funded in part by the Colorado Department of Local Affairs                              July 2005 Press Releases
