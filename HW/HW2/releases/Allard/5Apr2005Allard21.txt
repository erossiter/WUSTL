                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Named to Budget Conference Panel          April 5th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   a member of the Senate Budget Committee  has been appointed a conferee on the House Senate Conference Committee  The conference committee will meet to work out differences between the U S  House and Senate versions of the FY 2006 Budget Resolution     I look forward to working with the House and Senate leadership to getting a Budget Resolution passed that sets firm spending limits in order to responsibly address the budget deficit     Senator Allard said     It is our job to ensure that the nation  s priorities  including our energy needs  are adequately funded     the Senator said    And it is our responsibility to make sure that the country  s fiscal health is safeguarded  Discipline will be the watchword      Senator Allard was appointed to the Budget Conference Committee by Majority Leader Bill Frist                               April 2005 Press Releases
