                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD PLEASED WITH PRESIDENT  S NOMINATION OF DIRK KEMPTHORNE TO BECOME NEXT SECRETARY OF THE INTERIOR          March 16th  2006   Washington D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  said Thursday that he believes President Bush made a good choice in nominating Idaho  s Governor  Dirk Kempthorne  to replace Gale Norton as Secretary of the Interior     Dirk Kempthorne is a great choice     said Senator Allard     I had the opportunity to work with him in the Senate  and I believe he is one of the most qualified candidates to fill this position        I  m pleased the President chose someone with such great knowledge of western issues like public lands  water  and Indian Affairs     Senator Allard said       He will bring legislative experience from his time in the Senate and administrative experience from his time as Governor of Idaho     said Senator Allard                              March 2006 Press Releases
