                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Wins Assignment to Key Appropriations Subcommittees          March 3rd  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced Thursday that he has won assignment to the Senate Appropriations Subcommittees on Energy and Water  Homeland Security  Interior and Military Construction     I am honored to have been selected to serve on these key subcommittees  These assignments will allow us to do a lot of good for Colorado in the coming two years     Senator Allard said     Energy and water issues are critical to our state  Energy production is a significant segment of the Colorado economy     the Senator said    And I don  t think I have to remind Coloradans how important water  water projects and water agreements are to our continuing growth and prosperity        Guaranteeing the security of our homeland is important to every American  and of intense interest to Coloradans  I look forward to working to define our country  s homeland security priorities  and to make sure that our state  s public protection agencies receive the federal support they deserve        Finally  more than   5 5 billion was appropriated by the Military Construction Subcommittee for FY 2005  In a time of war  I anticipate that amount will increase during the next fiscal year     Senator Allard said    I plan to work as closely with the other subcommittee members on defense policies as I did when I was on the Senate Armed Services Committee                                  March 2005 Press Releases
