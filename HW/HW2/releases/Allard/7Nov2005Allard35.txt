          SENATOR ALLARD SECURES FUNDING FOR FOUNTAIN CREEK PROJECT STUDY  125 000 for regional flood control study in final appropriations bill         November 7th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Energy and Water Appropriations Subcommittee  announced that he has secured  125 000 for a study of the Fountain Creek watershed in the final version of the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations Bill  which was approved by a House Senate conference committee Monday     For several years now  Fountain Creek has been among my top energy and water funding priorities  The House version of this legislation did not contain any funding for the Fountain Creek study  so I am pleased  as a House Senate conferee  I was able to keep some  funding in the final bill     Senator Allard said    This has been a very tight budget year  so we had to fight for every dime  and although it was not as much as we hoped  this funding shows a clear and continued commitment on the part of Congress to this project        I have been seeking federal support for the Fountain Creek watershed project since major flooding occurred in 1999  causing approximately  100 million in damages     Senator Allard said    The funding this bill provides will pay for the preparation of a detailed analysis of flood control in the Fountain Creek watershed        I believe this study will further demonstrate the need for environmental restoration on a regional scale to address condition of the 150 miles of Fountain Creek and its tributaries     Senator Allard said     My office has been in constant contact with the Army Corps of Engineers  urging them to move this study along     the Senator added    The Corps re programmed an additional  449 000 toward completion of the study this year  so this  125 000 in next year  s appropriation will allow us to move forward in determining exactly what needs to be done on Fountain Creek        To adequately address this problem  the study needs to focus on not only Colorado Springs  but the area of southern Colorado that has been adversely impacted  as well     the Senator said   Senator Allard already has ensured more than  930 000 in funding for the Fountain Creek project since 2002   The conference version of the bill will be voted on by the Senate later this week                              November 2005
