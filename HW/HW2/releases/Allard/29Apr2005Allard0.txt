                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Introduces National Trails System Willing Seller Act Legislation expedites completion of National Trails System         April 29th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R CO  on Thursday introduced S  974  the National Trails System Willing Seller Act  in the U S  Senate  This legislation will pave the way for the completion of our nation  s most outstanding national trails     Growing up in Colorado  I had the opportunity to spend time in some of the nation  s most breathtaking country     Senator Allard said    It  s my hope that this legislation will help complete trail systems around America that will leave a lasting natural legacy for generations to come      The legislation will clarify the National Trails System Act of 1968 to allow the federal government to purchase land from willing sellers  providing a mechanism to complete several national trails  The Continental Divide trail  stretching from Mexico through Colorado to the Canadian border  is among the trails that await completion   Last year  Senator Allard was successful in gaining Senate passage of his legislation  but the House of Representatives failed to act on it prior to its adjournment  This year  Colorado Seventh District Representative Bob Beauprez  R CO  will introduce the companion bill in the House of Representatives     I look forward to working with my colleagues in the Senate  as well as Congressman Beauprez in the House of Representatives  to make this bill a reality     said Senator Allard                              April 2005 Press Releases
