                                                                                                                                                                                                                   SENATOR ALLARD PRAISES RETIRING CONGRESSMAN JOEL HEFLEY          February 17th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado made the following statement after Colorado 5th District Congressman Joel Hefley announced Thursday that he will not seek re election     Colorado is losing an extremely valuable member of its congressional delegation  Joel Hefley  s absence from the House Armed Services Committee will be a tremendous loss     said Senator Allard  who served with Hefley in the U S  House for six years     For two decades  he protected Fort Carson and Peterson Air Force Base  shepherding them safely through a time when several rounds of the Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  process closed dozens of bases across the country  It speaks to his effectiveness that his district  s major military installations remained intact     Senator Allard said                              February 2006 Press Releases
