Contact  Steve WymerEmail             ALLARD SECURES  150 000 FOR METRO STATE COLLEGE          June 21st  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    a member of the Senate  s Appropriations Committee  announced today that he has secured  150 000 in funding for Metro State College  MSC  in Denver as a part of the 2008 Labor  Health and Human Services Appropriations bill     The Metro State College project that I included in this bill will support flight simulation technology to enhance education and job training at the World Indoor Flight Simulation Lab at MSC     said Senator Allard    Higher education is the future of our state and I am pleased that my seat on the Appropriations Committee puts me in a position to help secure this important funding for Colorado  I look forward to continuing to work with my colleagues on the Senate and House Appropriations Committees to ensure that this project remains funded       Metro State would like to thank Senator Allard for his leadership on initiating this important funding for the College   said Metro State President Stephen Jordan   This federal funding will allow us to enhance our primary flight simulator with the latest technology and best practices    The World Indoor Airport is an essential element to Metro State  s Aviation and Aerospace Science program  which is the fifth largest in the country offering the only four year degree in the state  Graduates of this program fly for all the major airlines and have critical roles at Denver International Airport  including airport operations manager and director of security                              June 2007 Press Releases
