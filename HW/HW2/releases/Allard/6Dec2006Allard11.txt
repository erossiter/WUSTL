                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD STATEMENT ON IRAQ STUDY GROUP REPORT          December 6th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   issued the following statement about the release of the Iraq study group report earlier today     Many of the ideas in the report have been discussed previously  either with our allies in the region or in Congressional Committees  I believe the president will take the recommendations in the report seriously and be flexible in our approach going forward     Wars are not static events  and we  ve been making changes in Iraq policy since the beginning of this conflict     As this war has progressed  it has concerned me that the Iraqis remain too dependent on us  I  m pleased that the Iraq Study Group emphasizes the increased responsibility and initiative that Iraqis must be willing to accept to be self supportive  That  s going to be a tough love message from President Bush to the leadership in Iraq     Whatever decisions we make in Iraq the next year must be made with America  s national security as our top priority                                  December 2006 Press Releases
