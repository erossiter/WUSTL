                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Senator Allard Secures Yuma Water System Funding in Interior Appropriations Bill          June 9th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado   member of the Senate Interior Appropriations Subcommittee  on Thursday announced that he has secured  100 000 in State and Tribal Assistance Grant  STAG  funding for the City of Yuma  s wastewater treatment system in the FY 2006 Interior Appropriations bill     Yuma has been working with a wastewater treatment system that is over 80 years old     Senator Allard said     In order to comply with federal and state regulations  the facility badly needs replacement  and this funding will help defray some of the costs for the city      The City of Yuma  s wastewater treatment facility has exceeded the parameters it was designed for  and has also had problems with leakage   Construction of the new facility is already underway     I will continue my efforts in the Senate to make sure that rural communities like Yuma receive the federal support that they need as they try to upgrade aging facilities  and I will be working hard to make sure that the Interior Appropriations bill will be approved by the full Senate as soon as possible     added Senator Allard                              June 2005 Press Releases
