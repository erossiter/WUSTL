                                                                                                                                                                                                                   ALLARD ANNOUNCES  GRANT TO CENTRAL MOUNTAIN TRAINING FOUNDATION          August 18th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced today that the Central Mountain Training Foundation  CMTF  in Ca    on City will receive  296 168 from the U S  Department of Justice to continue training law enforcement officers     This grant will help improve the training of local law enforcement officers and help them make their communities safer     said Senator Allard    This project is good for the Central Mountain Training Foundation and for all of southwest Colorado      This funding will go toward developing training for 28 areas of concern in southwest Colorado  including handling internet crimes against children  managing criminal intelligence information and investigating methamphetamine labs and identity theft  Currently  the CMTF focuses its training for line officers on the day to day encounters they may face  but will also include additional training opportunities for mid level police staff and upper level command personnel for the seven county region                              August 2006 Press Releases
