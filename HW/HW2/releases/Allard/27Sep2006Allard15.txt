                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD ANNOUNCES LA VETA LIBRARY GRANT          September 27th  2006   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo   announced today that the La Veta Regional Library District will receive a rural development grant of  25 000 from the the U S  Department of Agriculture to improve library programming     Library services are a public good and their importance as vital cultural and educational institutions should be recognized     said Allard    I know that the library will make good use of these funds for the citizens of Huerfano County      The grant has been approved to purchase furnishings  including tables and chairs for the La Veta Library serving Huerfano County  The library intends to assemble and organize services  collections of materials and other resources to meet the information needs and interests of the residents it serves  The grant will also help the library offer special programs for kindergartners  film showings and other gatherings for children                               September 2006 Press Releases
