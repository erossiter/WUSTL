                                                                                                                                                                                                                   U S  SEN  WAYNE ALLARD SUPPORTS COLORADO REQUEST FOR FEMA TRAILERS FOR VICTIMS OF HOLLY TORNADO          April 4th  2007   WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Sen    R Colo    sent a letter today to U S  Dept  of Homeland Security Secretary Michael Chertoff in support of temporary housing for victims of the March 28  2007 tornado that touched down in Colorado  s eastern plains     I am aware that the State of Colorado has been in discussions with FEMA regarding temporary housing for the victims of the Holly tornado  I wholeheartedly support this request and ask for your immediate consideration     wrote Allard to Chertoff    Providing mobile homes or other temporary housing would meaningfully assist Holly residents as they seek to rebuild their lives following this terrible natural disaster    The tornado damaged more than 160 homes  or one third of the residences in Holly  Allard pointed out to Chertoff in his letter   Allard dispatched staff to survey the damage caused by the tornado and has worked in coordination with Colorado Gov  Bill Ritter  s office provide aide to victims                              April 2007 Press Releases
