          ALLARD SECURES  1 25 MILLION FOR I 25 IMPROVEMENTS THROUGH THE PIKES PEAK REGION Allard secures funding for badly needed project          November 18th  2005   WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator   R Colorado  announced Friday he has secured  1 25 million for improvements to I 25 through the Pikes Peak region of Colorado in the 2006 Transportation Appropriations Bill approved by a House Senate conference committee Friday  Senator Allard is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee that put together the bill  Colorado is to receive  23 85 million in highway funding for specific projects  The bill is now in its final form and cannot be amended  Final passage is expected before the Thanksgiving holiday     This is good news for Colorado Springs and all Coloradoans who drive that part of the I 25 corridor  These improvements will make that part of the interstate highway safer and smoother     Senator Allard said    I requested specific funding for this project because it is a high priority item for the Colorado Department of Transportation  Work will now be able to proceed on schedule      Also provided with funding for FY 2006 were    Highway 56 and Quebec interchange     2 8 million  Thornton Grade Separation     500 000  Colorado Boulevard in Denver     2 million  Ports to Plains Corridor     3 million  Garfield County Rio Grand Bike Trail     1 million  U S  287 corridor     2 7 million   Wadsworth State Highway 128     1 5 million I  76 from Fort Morgan to the Nebraska state line     1 6 million  Pikes Peak     1 2 million  State Highway 145  Dolores to Stoner     4 8 million  Frisco West Main Street safety study     1 million  West Vail wildlife overpass     500 000   Funding for these specific projects is being provided in addition to Colorado  s share of FY 2006 highway funding under the Safe  Accountable and Efficient Transportation Equity Act  which passed earlier this year  That share is expected to total in excess of  490 million in 2006                              November 2005 Press Releases
